Proposed Framework to Strengthen the Process of Determining of SGS and MAS Bills Closing Prices
93FAA968-09E7-4657-AE7D-D3076BD9B01C
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today published a consultation paper proposing a framework to strengthen the process of determining Singapore Government Securities (SGS) and MAS Bills end-of-day prices (Closing Prices). This is in line with MAS’ broader efforts to enhance the integrity of the processes for setting financial benchmarks.
