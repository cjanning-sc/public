MAS today announced the implementation of the final phase of the Securities and Futures Act (SFA) and the Financial Advisers Act (FAA), and issued subsidiary regulations to fully implement the 2 pieces of legislation. This new regulatory regime governing Singapore?s capital markets will take effect on 1 October 2002.
