The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) has published a public consultation paper (PDF, 58.4KB) with proposals on the setup of a Financial Industry Disputes Resolution Centre (FIDReC). The Centre will provide consumers with an independent and affordable avenue for resolving retail disputes with financial institutions in the banking, insurance and capital markets sectors.
