Bank Indonesia and Monetary Authority of Singapore Further Extend Bilateral Financial Arrangement
BB720721-4EF1-4A6E-BF67-E863D266AB28
Bank Indonesia and MAS announced the extension of the USD10 billion bilateral financial arrangement to 4 November 2022. This arrangement will continue to support monetary and financial stability in both countries amid the on-going recovery from the COVID-19 pandemic.
