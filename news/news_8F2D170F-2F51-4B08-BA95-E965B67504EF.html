MAS hosted the 55th SEACEN Governors’ Conference / High-Level Seminar and the 39th Meeting of the SEACEN Board of Governors on 13 and 14 November 2019 in Singapore. Governors, Managing Directors and delegates from SEACEN member central banks and monetary authorities participated in the event, and discussed about digital financial innovation.
