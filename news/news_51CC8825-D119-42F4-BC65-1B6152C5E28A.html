MAS Issues Revised Housing Loan Rules
51CC8825-D119-42F4-BC65-1B6152C5E28A
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today announced that banks may grant housing loans of up to 90% of the property value. Currently, MAS' housing loan rules cap the loan-to-value (LTV) limit at 80%.
