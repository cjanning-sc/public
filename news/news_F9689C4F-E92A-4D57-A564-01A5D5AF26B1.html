MAS Announces US$60 Billion Swap Facility with the US Federal Reserve as part of Coordinated Central Bank Actions
F9689C4F-E92A-4D57-A564-01A5D5AF26B1
MAS announced the establishment of a US$60 billion swap facility with the US Federal Reserve. The swap line arrangements will contribute significantly to ensuring stable liquidity conditions in the USD funding markets in Singapore and globally. MAS intends to draw on this swap facility to provide USD liquidity to financial institutions in Singapore.
