MoneySense Campaign to empower Singaporeans with knowledge and skills on financial and retirement planning
F2F333A4-3DC3-4ABC-9FF5-78D1B40C01A8
The MoneySense Campaign 2022, “Level Up Your Financial Health”, aims to empower Singaporeans with knowledge and concrete steps to take charge of their financial well-being and plan for their retirement. This year's campaign will run till end November, comprising a mix of in-person and mass media education programmes, as well as complimentary services and tools to aid in financial planning.
