The Tat Lee Bank Group (TLB Group) has reported an after-tax loss of $38.7m for 1997. The MAS is satisfied that the TLB Group is financially sound. The Group is solvent and fully capable of meeting all the obligations of TLB and its finance company subsidiary. Depositors need not worry about the safety of their deposits.
