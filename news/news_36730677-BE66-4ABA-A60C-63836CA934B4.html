The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today issued Guidelines on Switching of Designated Investment Products ("Guidelines"). The Guidelines aim to provide guidance to financial advisers on the controls, processes and procedures MAS expects them to put in place to monitor and deter undesirable switching activities. They also include disclosure requirements to ensure that consumers are fully informed of the costs and implications of switching.
