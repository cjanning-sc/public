MAS Issues $2 Polymer Notes
F1D28A41-1892-4F65-94A7-2E4AC28B5945
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today announced that $2 polymer notes will be issued from 12 Jan 2006, when the $2 paper notes stock is expected to be depleted. The $2 polymer notes will be introduced in phases. The paper notes will circulate alongside and continue to be legal tender.
