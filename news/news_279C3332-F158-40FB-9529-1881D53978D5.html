Anti-Money Laundering/AML/CFT
This Guidance was developed by the FATF, with the purpose of supporting the development of a common understanding of what the risk-based approach involves. It outlines the high-level principles in applying the risk-based approach, and shares good public and private sector practice in the design and implementation of an effective risk-based approach.
