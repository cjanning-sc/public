US-based investment association, Russell 20-20 ("R20-20"), arrived in Singapore yesterday as part of its 2000 Southeast Asia Investment Trip. Representing some of the biggest pension funds and money managers in the world, R20-20 is visiting Southeast Asia for the first time, and will be spending two days in Singapore (19 - 20 October).
