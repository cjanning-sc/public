Officers from the AGC, CAD and MAS met the Malaysian 1MDB Taskforce led by Tan Sri Abdul Gani Patail in Singapore yesterday. The meeting was held at the invitation of the Singapore authorities, and followed an earlier meeting with the Taskforce last week in Kuala Lumpur.
