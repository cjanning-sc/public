MAS Takes Civil Penalty Action against Lim Oon Cheng and Lim Huey Yih for Insider Trading
817EB18C-4AA9-4BEC-B989-4F2CF0FF6BD4
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) has imposed civil penalties on Mr Lim Oon Cheng and his niece, Ms Lim Huey Yih, for contraventions under the Securities and Futures Act (SFA). Both Mr Lim and Ms Lim have admitted to committing insider trading. Mr Lim has also admitted to false trading.
