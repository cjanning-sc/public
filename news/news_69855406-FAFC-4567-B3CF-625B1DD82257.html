MAS Welcomes Launch of Asian Bond Fund

The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) welcomes today's launch of the Asian Bond Fund (ABF) by the EMEAP group of central banks. As a member of the EMEAP grouping, Singapore will be investing US$100 million in the ABF. The initial size of the ABF will be about US$1 billion, and will be invested in a basket of US$ denominated sovereign and quasi-sovereign bonds issued by countries in the region. The ABF will be managed by the Bank for International Settlements (BIS).
