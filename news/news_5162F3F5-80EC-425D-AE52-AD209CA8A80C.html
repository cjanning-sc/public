MAS releases findings from its review of switching of investment products

The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today released the key findings from its review of financial institutions' controls on improper switching[1] of investment products. The review covered 23 financial institutions (FIs) from the banking, insurance and capital market sectors. It assessed the extent to which FIs had put in place controls in accordance with the Guidelines on Switching of Designated Investment Products (PDF, 114KB) issued by MAS in October 2004.
