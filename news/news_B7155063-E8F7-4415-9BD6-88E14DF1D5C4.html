Speaking at the Asia Pacific Anti-Money Laundering & Financial Crime Conference on 8 Apr 2019, Ms Ho Hern Shin, Assistant Managing Director (Banking and Insurance), highlighted the close partnership MAS enjoys with the financial sector. She also highlighted the 4 key elements of MAS’ AML/CFT framework – progressive regulations, intensive supervision, rigorous enforcement and effective partnerships.
