MAS Releases Guidelines for Property Funds
37BE2568-08EC-4213-8169-A21BC1F7EEEF
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today issued a set of regulatory guidelines for property funds. The guidelines give guidance to fund managers on the operation of such funds offered for sale to retail investors in Singapore. The introduction of property funds here will widen the range of financial products available to investors, consistent with the efforts to make Singapore a world-class financial centre.
