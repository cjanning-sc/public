Anti-Money Laundering/AML/CFT
This set of regulations is issued pursuant to section 27A(1)(b) of the MAS Act and gives effect to asset freezing requirements under the UN Security Council Resolutions that relate to Sudan, which all financial institutions in Singapore must comply with.
