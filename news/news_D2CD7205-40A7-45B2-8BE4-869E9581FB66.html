Seasoned financial sector personnel are found among the new faces in the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS). The influx of mid-career professionals is part of MAS' strategy to strengthen its capabilities in the process of spearheading the financial sector reforms aimed at establishing Singapore as a world-class financial centre.
