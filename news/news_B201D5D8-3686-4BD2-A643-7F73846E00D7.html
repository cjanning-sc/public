MAS Launches Doctorate Scholarship Programme

MAS has launched a Doctorate Scholarship Programme (DSP).  The Programme aims to further develop specialised skills in areas such as financial risk management, financial engineering and economics to support the long-term growth of Singapore’s financial services sector.
