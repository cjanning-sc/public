MAS and Financial Industry Provide Additional Support for Individuals
7B2584A0-F5BE-4CA6-89B9-D0F242658503
MAS, ABS and FHAS have announced a second package of measures to support individuals facing financial difficulties due to the COVID-19 pandemic. This second package will extend the scope of relief for individuals to other types of loan commitments, and allow them to continue to have access to affordable basic banking services.
