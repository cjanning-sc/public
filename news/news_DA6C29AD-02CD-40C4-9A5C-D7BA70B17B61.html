In the Second Reading Speech for the Exchanges (Demutualisation and Merger) (Amendment) Bill, Mr Alvin Tan, Minister of State, Ministry of Culture, Community and Youth & Ministry of Trade and Industry, and MAS' Board Member, highlighted the main amendments in the Bill, including allowing SEL Holdings Pte Ltd (SEL), with the Minister-in-charge’s approval, to participate in SGX’s corporate actions under which SEL may opt to receive additional SGX shares.
