Singapore's Treasury Market Turnover Increases for the Fifth Consecutive Year Foreign Exchange Trading Reaches Seven Year High
Singapore's Treasury Market Turnover Increases for the Fifth Consecutive Year Foreign Exchange Trading Reaches Seven Year High
E7D84B1F-AFF5-4224-A313-DF6740370086
For the fifth year in a row, average daily trading volumes of treasury activities in Singapore have risen, reaching a high of US$204 billion in 2004, a 21% increase over 2003. This is the largest increase experienced by Singapore's treasury market since 2000, according to the Monetary Authority of Singapore's (MAS) Survey of Singapore Treasury Activities 2004.
