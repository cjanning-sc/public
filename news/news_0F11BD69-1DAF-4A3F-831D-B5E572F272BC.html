MAS Receives 21 Applications for Digital Bank Licences
0F11BD69-1DAF-4A3F-831D-B5E572F272BC
MAS announced that it has received 21 applications for digital bank licences as at the close of application on 31 December 2019. This comprises 7 applications for the digital full bank licences, and 14 applications for the digital wholesale bank licences. Successful applicants will be announced in June 2020 and are expected to commence business by mid-2021.
