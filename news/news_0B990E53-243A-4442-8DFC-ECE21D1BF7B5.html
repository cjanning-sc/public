In response to media queries on FTX.com, MAS said that FTX.com does not operate in Singapore. FTX.com is neither licensed nor exempted from licensing in Singapore. It is not possible, however, to prevent Singapore users from directly accessing overseas service providers. FTX.com was therefore able to onboard Singapore users. MAS has consistently reminded the public of the risks of dealing with unlicensed entities.
