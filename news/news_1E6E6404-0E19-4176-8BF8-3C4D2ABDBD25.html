MAS brings forward the date of disclosure of its foreign exchange intervention operations
1E6E6404-0E19-4176-8BF8-3C4D2ABDBD25
MAS will commence disclosure of its FX intervention operations in April 2020, instead of July 2020 as previously announced. The date has been brought forward to provide more timely information to the market, and align with MAS monetary policy cycle.
