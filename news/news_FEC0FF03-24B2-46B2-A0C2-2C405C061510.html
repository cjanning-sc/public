Mr Tay Yew Khem and Ms Hui Choy Leng have been convicted and sentenced to 12 weeks’ imprisonment and 4 months’ imprisonment respectively for insider trading in the shares of Broadway Industrial Group Limited, and for other offences under the Securities and Futures Act, and the Corruption, Drug Trafficking and Other Serious Crimes (Confiscation of Benefits) Act.
