At the Singapore Sustainable Investing and Financing Conference, Ms Indranee Rajah, Minister in the Prime Minister’s Office, Second Minister for Finance and National Development, announced that the Singapore Government has published the Singapore Green Bond Framework. The Framework lays the foundation for the issuance of green bonds by the Singapore Government and outlines the Government’s ambition and commitment to high-quality green bond issuances.
