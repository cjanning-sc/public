Media Release on S$ 300 Million Bond Issue by International Finance Corporation (IFC)
07AF1202-B29C-4121-9AA4-D07EC77561E7
In August this year, MAS announced a series of measures to liberalise the use of Singapore dollars outside Singapore. These measures would make it easier for foreign entities to list Singapore dollar denominated shares and issue Singapore dollar bonds, where the proceeds will be used outside Singapore. The aim was to develop broad and deep capital markets to attract a broad range of international issuers, investors and intermediaries.
