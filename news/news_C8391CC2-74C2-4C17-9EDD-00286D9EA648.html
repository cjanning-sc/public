In response to a forum letter suggesting that MAS improves the ease with which charities can subscribe for Treasury Bills (T-bills), MAS said institutions such as charities can apply for T-bills through any of the 13 banks which are SGS primary dealers. Some banks are willing to accept smaller institutional applications, including by charities.
