MAS Press Statement on Structured Products
93E7DC92-1B99-44C0-9608-A81795172EA3
In consultation with MAS, financial institutions (FIs) that have sold the DBS High Notes 5, Lehman Minibonds and Merrill Lynch Jubilee Series 3 LinkEarner Notes have decided to appoint independent parties to oversee their complaints handling processes and investigations into any mis-selling of these products.  The independent party is intended to assure investors that their complaints will be heard, documented and reviewed fairly and swiftly.
