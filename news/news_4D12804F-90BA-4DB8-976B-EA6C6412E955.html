MAS and CAD Commence Joint Investigation into Officers Responsible for Managing Eagle Hospitality Trust
4D12804F-90BA-4DB8-976B-EA6C6412E955
MAS and the Commercial Affairs Department (CAD) of the Singapore Police Force have launched a joint investigation into current and former directors, and officers responsible for managing Eagle Hospitality Trust (EHT), in connection with suspected breaches of disclosure requirements under section 203 of the Securities and Futures Act (Cap. 289).
