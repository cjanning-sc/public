Civil Penalty Enforcement Action for False Trading
69C81403-4379-4DA1-BDA4-F62EF973548B
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) has taken civil penalty enforcement action against Mr Wong Chow Lin @ Benjamin Wong for contravening the false trading provisions under section 197(1)(b) of the Securities and Futures Act (SFA).  Mr Wong was an appointed representative and the head of business development for corporate broking in CIMB Securities Pte Ltd at the time of the contravention.
