The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today launched the 2004 Sterling Silver Proof Coin Set in the Cultural and Heritage Site series. The Sterling Silver Proof Coin Sets first issued in June 2000, features the cultural and heritage series in Singapore. The current set is the fifth and last set in the series. It features the Singapore River - Boat Quay in the mid-1900s.
