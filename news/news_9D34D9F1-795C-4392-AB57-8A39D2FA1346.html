At the fireside chat at Singapore FinTech Festival 2020, Mr Mark Carney, United Nations Special Envoy for Climate Action & Finance, and Mr Ravi Menon, Managing Director of MAS share their perspectives on how economies can balance the transition away from high-carbon energy sources and lead themselves to more sustainable and self-sufficient energy future, how technology can be used to achieve greater data transparency and mobilise greater green financing, and whether global cooperation is key to achieving a greener and sustainable future.
