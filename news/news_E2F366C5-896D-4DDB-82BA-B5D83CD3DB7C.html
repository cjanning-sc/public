MAS Urges Customers to Minimise Visits to Premises of Financial Institutions

MAS has urged customers of financial institutions to minimise visits to their premises during the current circuit breaker period. This is in line with the guidance issued by the Ministry of Health on 14 April 2020 to minimise people moving around.
