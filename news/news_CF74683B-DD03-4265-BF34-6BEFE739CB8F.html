In 2016, MAS embarked on a review of the regulatory framework governing payment services in Singapore with a view to modernising and streamlining the existing frameworks to encourage the wider adoption of e-payments in Singapore. As part of the review, one key recommendation to encourage the use of e-payments was to enhance consumer or account user protection. On 13 February 2018, MAS proposed measures to protect funds belonging to account users and merchants in the Payment Services Bill.
