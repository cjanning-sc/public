MAS Accepts Recommendations by Committee on Efficient Distribution of Life Insurance
9583DB75-A46E-48A9-9C54-A01115A7180C
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) has accepted the recommendations by the Committee on Efficient Distribution of Life Insurance (CEDLI) to enhance the sales advisory process, product disclosure, and the professional training requirements for intermediaries selling life insurance products.
