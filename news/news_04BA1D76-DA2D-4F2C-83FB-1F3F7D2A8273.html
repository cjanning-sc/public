Distribution of Traded Endowment Policies to Retail Investors Allowed
04BA1D76-DA2D-4F2C-83FB-1F3F7D2A8273
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) published today its response to comments it received from its public consultation on the regulation of traded endowment policies (TEPs) and traded life policies (TLPs). In its response MAS said that it agreed with proposals to apply separate rules to the distribution of TEPs and TLPs.
