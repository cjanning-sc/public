MAS Consults on Proposed Changes to Regulate Payment Token Derivatives traded on Approved Exchanges
E31CD8B5-E8AE-4820-8BFE-1E26B4CD2DE2
MAS has issued a consultation paper proposing to allow payment token derivatives to be traded on Approved Exchanges and to regulate the activity under the Securities and Futures Act. Interested parties are invited to submit their comments on the proposed regulation of payment token derivatives on Approved Exchanges to capital_markets@mas.gov.sg by 20 Dec 2019.
