New $20 Note to Commemorate Singapore’s Bicentennial

President Halimah Yacob launched a $20 currency note to commemorate Singapore’s Bicentennial. The note depicts Singapore’s journey to nationhood and pays tribute to our forebears who laid the foundations for modern Singapore.
