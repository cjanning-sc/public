Warning on Fraudulent Websites Soliciting “Cryptocurrency” Investments
17E6BA85-E5D8-42EA-AFCB-57FDBFACDA44
MAS has warned members of the public on 29 January 2019 not to be misled by fraudulent websites that solicit investments in cryptocurrencies using fabricated information attributed to the Singapore Government. Such websites falsely claim that Singapore is adopting a cryptocurrency as its official coin and ask readers to provide their personal and financial details to purchase the cryptocurrency. Members of the public are advised to avoid providing any financial or personal information on the forms linked from the website and report such cases to the Police.
