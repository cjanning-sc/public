Sixteen Financial Institutions Accorded Approved Bond Intermediary (ABI) Status
92D854CC-8811-454F-A970-7FC7CE333523
Minister for Health and Second Minister for Finance, Mr Lim Hng Kiang, today announced the names of the first group of sixteen financial institutions that have been accorded the Approved Bond Intermediary (ABI) status. Mr Lim also announced the formation of a multi-agency Debt Market Committee chaired by MAS Managing Director Mr Koh Yong Guan.
