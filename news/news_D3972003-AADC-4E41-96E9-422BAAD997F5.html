Promoting responsible recruitment in the financial advisory industry
D3972003-AADC-4E41-96E9-422BAAD997F5
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) has proposed measures to address the risks associated with large scale movement of financial advisory (FA) representatives from one FA firm to another. These measures, set out in a paper for public consultation, seek to safeguard the interests of consumers by promoting more responsible recruitment practices in the FA industry.
