Allocation and Exchange of Singapore Bicentennial $20 Commemorative Notes

MAS announced today that all Singapore citizens and permanent residents who applied for the Singapore Bicentennial $20 commemorative notes were allotted the full quantity requested. In total, MAS received over 200,000 applications for about 1.8 million pieces of notes.
