MAS Seeks Public Comment on Amendments to Securities and Futures Act and Financial Advisers Act
68C01DD3-A51B-4C79-A8A6-087E3F3AC88A
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) invites public comment on two consultation papers released today on proposed amendments to the Securities and Futures Act (SFA) and the Financial Advisers Act (FAA) respectively. The amendments are targeted for first reading in Parliament during the second half of 2003.
