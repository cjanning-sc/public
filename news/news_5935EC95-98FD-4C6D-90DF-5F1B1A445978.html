Singapore Savings Bonds to be opened to Supplementary Retirement Scheme funds; Individual Limit to double to S$200,000
5935EC95-98FD-4C6D-90DF-5F1B1A445978
MAS announced that investors will be able to invest in Singapore Savings Bonds using their Supplementary Retirement Scheme funds starting from 1 February 2019. The Individual Limit for SSB will be raised to S$200,000 on the same date.
