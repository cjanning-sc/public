Response to two letters on Singapore Bicentennial $20 commemorative note - Lianhe Zaobao, 13 June 2019
0C698EF6-8691-46F6-A493-C86A6C407429
In response to two forum letters in Lianhe Zaobao calling on MAS to issue more Singapore Bicentennial commemorative notes and suggesting that the note be added to circulation, MAS said that it will be issuing an additional two million pieces of the note to meet strong public interest. MAS added that it will consider reintroducing the $20 denomination in a future note series.
