MAS to strengthen individual accountability of senior managers in financial institutions

The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) has proposed guidelines to strengthen individual accountability of senior managers and raise standards of conduct in financial institutions (FIs). The guidelines are a key part of MAS’ broader efforts to foster a culture of ethical behaviour and responsible risk-taking in the financial industry.
