In the Second Reading Speech on the Deposit Insurance and Policy Owners' Protection Schemes (Amendment) Bill 2018, Mr Ong Ye Kung, Minister for Education said the maximum level of coverage under the Deposit Insurance Scheme will be increased from S$50,000 to S$75,000. This is to ensure that a vast majority of depositors be fully covered.
