MAS Press Statement on The Gazette Of The Insurance (Amendment) Regulations 1998
1FE4278A-5EFC-44DC-812C-B45CEDD91FDA
The Insurance (Amendment) Regulations 1998 takes effect on 27 Feb 1998. The regulations prescribe revisions to the investment limits, introduce new permitted counterparty exposure limits, and provide clarification on the valuation of unit trusts. The amendments, which apply to the Singapore general insurance and non-linked life insurance funds of registered insurers and reinsurers, have been finalised after feedback obtained from the insurance industry.
