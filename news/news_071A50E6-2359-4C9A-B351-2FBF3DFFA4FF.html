The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) and the Economic Society of Singapore (ESS) today launched the fourth MAS-ESS Essay Competition. The title of this year's competition is "Is Our Education System Able to Meet the Challenges of Globalisation?" This topic aims to examine Singapore's education system against a backdrop of rapid globalisation and assess if it is able to meet the challenges and opportunities of an increasingly integrated and competitive world.
