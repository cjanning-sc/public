MAS Releases Guidelines for Capital Guaranteed Funds and Futures & Options Funds
B95E3AD0-DA6A-4BFD-8236-61B7C13ACEB8
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today issued two new sets of guidelines to regulate capital guaranteed funds, as well as futures and options funds sold to retail investors in Singapore. The guidelines, which take effect immediately, give guidance to fund managers on the operation and marketing of such specialised unit trusts in Singapore. MAS said that both sets of guidelines are in line with international practice and were formulated after taking into account comments received from the local fund management industry.
