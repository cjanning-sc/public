MAS Launches First Colour Commemorative Coin
A9718CED-4A02-4B3E-9AEA-A46847377A00
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) will issue a series of coins from 2004 to 2008 to commemorate the Urban Redevelopment Authority's (URA) Identity Plan. The first series is launched today in conjunction with URA's Old World Charm Heritage Trail.
