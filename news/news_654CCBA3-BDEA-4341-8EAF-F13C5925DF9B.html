Developing Singapore as the Marine Insurance Centre for Asian Business
654CCBA3-BDEA-4341-8EAF-F13C5925DF9B
The Monetary Authority of Singapore ("MAS") announced today that it has awarded new Approved Marine Insurer ("AMI") statuses to a first batch of three insurance companies, in its move to develop Singapore as the marine insurance centre for Asian business. The three companies awarded the inaugural AMI status are Gerling Global Re, HSBC Insurance (Asia) and Swiss Re.
