Y2K Liquidity Scheme for Banks and Finance Companies
E69F43B2-30A0-4E88-B78F-38C0B1ACA00A
MAS today announced that it will establish a Y2K liquidity scheme to help meet temporary Y2K-related liquidity needs of banks and finance companies. The liquidity scheme will be available for a two-month period, from 29 November 1999 till 28 January 2000.
