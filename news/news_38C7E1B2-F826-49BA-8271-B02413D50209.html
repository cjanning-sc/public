ESMA and MAS Sign MOU on Singapore’s Financial Benchmarks
38C7E1B2-F826-49BA-8271-B02413D50209
The European Securities and Markets Authority (ESMA), the European Union’s (EU) securities markets regulator, and MAS today signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU), completing the process to allow the use of Singapore’s financial benchmarks in the EU. Under the MOU, ESMA and MAS will share information and supervisory activities on Singapore-regulated financial benchmarks.
