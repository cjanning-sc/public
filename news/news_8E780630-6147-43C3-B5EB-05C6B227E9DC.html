Ms Jacqueline Loh, MAS' Deputy Managing Director, announced the official launch of Phase 2 of the ASEAN Disaster Risk Financing and Insurance Programme (ADFRI). ADRFI Phase 2 will be impact-focused, and targeted at strengthening ASEAN’s capabilities in ex-ante risk financing and risk transfer strategies.
