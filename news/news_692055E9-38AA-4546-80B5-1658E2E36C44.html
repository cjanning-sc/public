The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) and the Economic Society of Singapore (ESS) have launched their sixth MAS-ESS Essay Competition. This year's essay title, "The Asian Financial Crisis 10 years on: What has changed and what have we learnt?", is especially significant as 2007 will mark the 10th anniversary of the Asian Financial Crisis. The topic was chosen to encourage debate on a historic event that had far-reaching consequences for the regional economies including Singapore.
