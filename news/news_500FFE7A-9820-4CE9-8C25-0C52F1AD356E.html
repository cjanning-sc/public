Financial Services Remain Open and Available
500FFE7A-9820-4CE9-8C25-0C52F1AD356E
MAS affirmed that financial services will remain open and available to all customers and counterparties in Singapore and globally. All financial markets in Singapore remain open, and payment services are unaffected. Financial services is one of the essential services exempted from the suspension of activities at workplace premises under the elevated safe distancing measures announced by the Ministry of Health (MOH) today.
