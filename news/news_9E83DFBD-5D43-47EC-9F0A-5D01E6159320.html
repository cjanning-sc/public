The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) announced that Mr Heng Swee Keat, Managing Director, MAS, and Mr Koh Yong Guan, Chairman, Central Provident Fund Board, have been re-appointed to its Board of Directors for a further period of two years. Their re-appointments take effect from 1 June 2009.
