Bank Indonesia and the Monetary Authority of Singapore Further Extend Bilateral Financial Arrangement
F7477E75-D06A-441D-96EC-053A7BA602F0
Bank Indonesia (BI) and the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) announced a further extension of bilateral financial arrangement to 3 November 2023. This arrangement was endorsed by Indonesia President Joko Widodo and Singapore Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong to reinforce the ongoing financial cooperation in preserving monetary and financial stability in both countries amid global macroeconomic uncertainties.
