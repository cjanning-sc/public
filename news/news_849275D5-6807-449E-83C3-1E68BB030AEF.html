MAS Issues New Portrait Notes
849275D5-6807-449E-83C3-1E68BB030AEF
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today announced that it will gradually introduce new portrait notes into circulation from early August 2004 onwards. The design of the new notes is similar to the current portrait notes, except that MAS' symbols replace those of the Board of Commissioners of Currency, Singapore (BCCS).
