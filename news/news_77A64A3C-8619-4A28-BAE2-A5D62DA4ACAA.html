The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) announced that Mr Lim Chee Onn, Senior Advisor of Keppel Corporation Limited, and Mr Lucien Wong Yuen Kuai, Managing Partner of Allen & Gledhill, have been re-appointed to its Board of Directors for a further period of two years.  Their re-appointments take effect from 1 January 2010.
