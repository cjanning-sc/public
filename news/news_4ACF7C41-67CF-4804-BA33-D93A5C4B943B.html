MAS and the Association of Banks in Singapore (ABS) jointly issued a Study to enhance the resilience of future workplaces of financial institutions (FIs) in Singapore. The Study involved a consortium of consultants with experience in redesign of workplaces, as well as a multi-disciplinary expert panel to provide views on infectious disease management and workplace safety. The Study sets out good practices and recommendations for FIs to review and redesign their workplaces, to better support the well-being and safety of their employees and enhance workplace resilience to future pandemics.
