MAS and IBF adjust training scheme funding parameters amidst stepped up skills development support for Finance Sector

MAS and the Institute of Banking and Finance announced changes to provide more targeted support under the IBF training schemes. This follows earlier announcements to enhance funding for talent development programmes in growth and priority areas and to return subsidy rates under the IBF training schemes progressively to pre-pandemic levels.
