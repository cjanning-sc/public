Offers of Investments/Offers of Securities or Securities-Based Derivatives Contracts,Exemptions
Sets out the exemptions from prospectus disclosure requirements, such as, disclosure of the names and occupations of each of the directors or equivalent persons of the sponsor, for an offer of asset-back securities under section 240(1) of the SFA.
