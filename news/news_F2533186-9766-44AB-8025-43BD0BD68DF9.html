MAS Lifts Car Loan Guidelines

The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) announced that from today, it will be lifting its 1995 car loan guidelines which restricted the maximum financing for the purchase of a car to 70% of its purchase price, including the price of its Certificate of Entitlement (COE), to be repaid over a period of not more than seven years.
