The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) has released a policy consultation paper on proposed amendments to the Securities and Futures Act (SFA) and the Financial Advisers Act (FAA). This is the second in a series of policy consultations MAS is conducting following a review of the SFA and FAA.
