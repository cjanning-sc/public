Speaking at the 19th Asia CEO Insurance Summit on 12 March 2019, Mr Daniel Wang, Executive Director (Insurance), said that though digitalisation may transform the industry, the key ingredients for success in a digitalised world is to “know your customer and do right by them”. As insurers strategically position themselves for the digital economy and invest in technological innovation, they should continue to build customer trust and loyalty.
