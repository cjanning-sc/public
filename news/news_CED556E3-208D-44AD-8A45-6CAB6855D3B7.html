Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) Announces New Framework For Developing Manpower Excellence
CED556E3-208D-44AD-8A45-6CAB6855D3B7
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) announced today a framework of strategies to strengthen and sustain excellence in financial manpower will be established. The new two-pronged framework comprises the setting up of a Financial Network for Excellence in Training, and the deployment of the Financial Sector Development Fund (FSDF) to support programmes to enhance the financial sector capabilities in Singapore.
