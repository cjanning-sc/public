European Commission and Monetary Authority of Singapore: Concurrent adoption of equivalence decision for certain derivatives trading venues in the EU and Singapore
27C249AC-E5DE-4E3C-876C-7BA9D9901415
On 1 Apr 2019, the European Commission recognised a number of Singapore trading venues authorised by MAS as eligible for compliance with the EU trading obligation for derivatives. This decision will strengthen cross-border trading, enabling businesses in the EU and Singapore to access deeper pools of liquidity and hedge risks more efficiently allow EU counterparties.
