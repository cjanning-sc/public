MAS' International Advisory Panel Discuss Strategies for Singapore's Financial Sector
F0ABFBF5-EFD3-43D8-A741-2778665F0B0F
The International Advisory Panel (IAP) of the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) met today to discuss the challenges and opportunities for Singapore against global trends in the financial industry and regional developments. The IAP, chaired by Mr Lim Hng Kiang, Minister, Prime Minister's Office, Second Minister for Finance, and Deputy Chairman of MAS, comprises business and finance leaders from the international community.
