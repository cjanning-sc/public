MAS International Advisory Panel Endorses Singapore's Financial Sector Reforms and Strategies
BCCBABF6-4F4F-42FA-9EFA-B36CDC1D9DF9
Members of the Monetary Authority of Singapore's International Advisory Panel (MAS IAP) endorsed Singapore's financial sector reforms and strategies at the close of their one-and-a-half day inaugural meeting at the MAS. The panel, chaired by Mr J Y Pillay, comprised renowned leaders in international finance drawn from the US, Europe and Japan. (Please see Appendix.) The IAP discussed MAS' overall financial sector reforms, directions in banking, and specific strategies for the debt capital market and the equity and derivatives exchange. They also discussed the economic situation in Asia.
