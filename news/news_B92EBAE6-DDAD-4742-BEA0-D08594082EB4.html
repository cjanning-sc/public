MAS and IE Singapore Invite Comments on the Proposed Transfer of Regulatory Oversight of Commodity Futures
B92EBAE6-DDAD-4742-BEA0-D08594082EB4
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) and International Enterprise Singapore (IE Singapore) issued a consultation paper that proposes the transfer of regulatory oversight of commodity futures from IE Singapore to MAS. Commodity futures are currently regulated under the Commodity Trading Act (CTA) administered by IE Singapore.
