MAS Unveils Hawker Culture in Singapore Commemorative Coins
MAS Unveils Hawker Culture in Singapore Commemorative Coins
67CE985C-7DC2-4C3D-8A30-A30C4F951C9E
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today unveiled the Hawker Culture in Singapore Commemorative coins, to celebrate the inscription of Hawker Culture in Singapore into the UNESCO Representative List of the Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity on 16 December 2020. Two versions of the coin, a nickel-plated zinc coin and a silver coin, will be issued on 12 November 2021.
