Changes to AIA Critical Year Support Package
35556ED3-A2A0-4030-BFB3-8FCFF82441F2
Over the last few months, MAS has received complaints from the public about life insurance policies with Critical Year features. In response, MAS has encouraged companies that had issued such policies to deal fairly with the complaints, and has been monitoring their actions to address the concerns of affected policyholders. One company which sold a large number of Critical Year policies was the Singapore branch of American International Assurance (AIA).
