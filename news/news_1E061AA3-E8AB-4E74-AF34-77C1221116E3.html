Singapore Confers Honorary Citizen Awards on Professor Sir David Philip Lane and Mr Walter Kielholz
1E061AA3-E8AB-4E74-AF34-77C1221116E3
Singapore has awarded Professor Sir David Philip Lane, Honorary Senior Fellow at A*STAR, and Mr Walter B. Kielholz, Honorary Chairman of Swiss Re Ltd (Swiss Re), with the Honorary Citizen Award for their invaluable contributions to Singapore.
