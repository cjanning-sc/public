Senior Minister and Chairman, MAS Goh Chok Tong Calls on Luxembourg Prime Minister and Finance Minister Jean-Claude Juncker
1DFDD646-7517-4F20-B725-82BDE6A86C02
Senior Minister Goh Chok Tong met with Luxembourg Prime Minister and Finance Minister Jean-Claude Juncker today from 5.10 to 5.50pm. It was a warm and friendly meeting.  Senior Minister and Prime Minister Juncker exchanged views on a wide range of issues, including cooperation between Asia and Europe, and the situation in Asia and Southeast Asia.
