Singapore’s 2007 Average Daily FX Turnover Volumes Reach US$231 billion
43FFC1F4-1399-404A-ADE7-0FA34AA6FFDF
According to the latest survey by the Bank for International Settlements (BIS), average daily foreign exchange (FX) turnover volume in Singapore jumped 84% from the previous survey in 2004, to reach US$231 billion in 2007.  The survey ranks Singapore as the fifth largest FX centre in the world and the second largest in Asia, closely behind Tokyo.
