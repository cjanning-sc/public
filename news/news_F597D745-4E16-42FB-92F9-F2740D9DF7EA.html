MAS to Launch Inaugural Singapore Sovereign Green Bond Issuance
MAS to Launch Inaugural Singapore Sovereign Green Bond Issuance
F597D745-4E16-42FB-92F9-F2740D9DF7EA
MAS announced the upcoming issuance of Singapore’s inaugural sovereign green bond – known as Green Singapore Government Securities (Infrastructure). This green bond will be launched via a book-building process within the week, and will be denominated in Singapore Dollars with a tenor of either 30 or 50 years, at a minimum issuance size of about S$1.5 billion.
