MAS Responds to Debt Capital Market Working Group Recommendations
254E086F-1131-4357-9D9A-E4000B7D0C21
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today released its responses to the recommendations made by the Debt Capital Market Working Group (DCMWG) in June this year to further develop Singapore as a leading international hub for debt market activities. This includes a move to exempt banks from maintaining Minimum Cash Balance (MCB) and Minimum Liquid Assets (MLA) requirements on S$ funds received from non-bank customers via foreign currency swaps which have original maturities of one year or more.
