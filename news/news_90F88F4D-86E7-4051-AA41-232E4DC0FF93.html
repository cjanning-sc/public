Wirecard Complies with MAS Directions to Keep Customer Funds in Banks in Singapore
90F88F4D-86E7-4051-AA41-232E4DC0FF93
MAS said today that Wirecard entities in Singapore (“Wirecard”) have complied with MAS’ directions to hold customers’ funds in segregated accounts with banks in Singapore. MAS is closely monitoring the operations of Wirecard.
