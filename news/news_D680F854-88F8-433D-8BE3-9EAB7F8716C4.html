Appointment/Re-appointments to MAS Board of Directors Singapore, 02 Jan 2002. The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) has appointed Mr Chan Seng Onn, Solicitor-General of the Attorney-General's Chambers (AGC), to its Board of Directors. Mr Chan's appointment will be for a term of 2 years, with effect from 1 Jan 2002....
