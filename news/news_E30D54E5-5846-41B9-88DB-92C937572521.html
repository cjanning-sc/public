Singapore Hosts Regulators Forum on Risk Management
E30D54E5-5846-41B9-88DB-92C937572521
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) and the International Swaps and Derivatives Association (ISDA) jointly hosted the MAS-ISDA forum in Singapore on 22 to 23 October 1999. The forum was opened by Mr Tharman Shanmugaratnam, Deputy Managing Director, MAS, and Mr Ernest T. Patrikis, Senior Vice-President and General Counsel, AIG and ISDA Director. Topics discussed this year included the supervision of risk management and the evolution of techniques used to manage risks.
