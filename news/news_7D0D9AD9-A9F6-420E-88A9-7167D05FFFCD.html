Fit-for-Gifting Notes for Lunar New Year Available from 5 January

"Fit-for-Gifting" currency notes (Fit notes) for Lunar New Year will be available from 5 January 2023. MAS and the Association of Banks in Singapore strongly encourage members of the public to use Fit notes or e-hong baos this coming Lunar New Year.
