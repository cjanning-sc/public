Moneysense Launches Financial Education Outreach to Polytechnic Students
Moneysense Launches Financial Education Outreach to Polytechnic Students
0E450CDC-A4F7-4E00-9752-C32B1043752C
The MoneySENSE Financial Education Steering Committee and the Central Provident Fund Board (CPF Board) today organized the first of activities to bring financial education to polytechnic students. This is the first time that MoneySENSE is organizing a financial education programme for students at all five polytechnics in Singapore.
