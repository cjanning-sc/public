Public Consultation on Legislative Amendments to Enable the Conduct of General Meetings by Electronic Means
89AF2D4A-0D1F-481F-B7C0-84645237E85A
ACRA, MOF and MAS invite the public to provide feedback on proposed legislative amendments, which will provide companies, variable capital companies and business trusts the option of conducting fully virtual or hybrid general meetings when the COVID-19 (Temporary Measures) (Alternative Arrangements for Meetings) Orders are revoked with effect from 1 July 2023.
