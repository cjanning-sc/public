MAS Invites Comments on Proposed Regulatory Framework for Mortgage Insurance Business

The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) issued a consultation paper that sets out the proposed regulatory framework for mortgage insurance business.  The paper also describes how mortgage insurance will be treated in the calculation of the capital requirements for locally incorporated banks.
