BIS Innovation Hub and Monetary Authority of Singapore develop prototype supervisory analytics platform

BIS Innovation Hub Singapore Centre and MAS have developed a new prototype platform that integrates regulatory data and analytics. Known as Project Ellipse, the platform successfully demonstrates how regulatory and other data can be integrated into a single platform to help regulatory authorities identify potential risks to individual banks and the banking system.
