MAS Launches Commemorative Coins for Singapore's 40 Years of Independence
8DBAB2F8-D4E2-4F53-BC53-72DE9C407255
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today launched a set of coins to commemorate Singapore's 40 years of independence. The commemorative coins celebrate our nation-building, the indomitable spirit of our citizens to strive for success despite tough challenges, and our unity as a multi-racial society.
