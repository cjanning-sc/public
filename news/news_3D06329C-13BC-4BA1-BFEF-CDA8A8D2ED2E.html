MAS Statement on Pinnacle Notes Series 9 and 10
3D06329C-13BC-4BA1-BFEF-CDA8A8D2ED2E
MAS has been informed by the arranger of the Pinnacle Notes Series 9 and 10, Morgan Stanley Asia (Singapore) Pte, that a number of credit events have occurred in relation to the underlying securities of the notes, triggering an early redemption. MAS understands noteholders would be concerned about their investments.
