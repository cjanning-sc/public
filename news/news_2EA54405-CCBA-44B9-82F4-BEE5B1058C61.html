At the 5th Joint Meeting of the ASEAN Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors, the Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors reiterate their commitment to the pursuit of an integrated ASEAN to support economic growth and strengthen financial stability in the region amidst heightened uncertainties arising from trade tensions and policy adjustments of advanced economies.
