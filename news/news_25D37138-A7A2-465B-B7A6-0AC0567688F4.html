MAS Launches The 2008 Heritage Orchids of Singapore Coin Set
25D37138-A7A2-465B-B7A6-0AC0567688F4
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today launched the 2008 Heritage Orchids of Singapore Coin Set. This is the third set of coins issued in tribute to one of Singapore’s national treasures, orchids. Orchids are a significant feature of Singapore’s heritage and previous orchid coin sets have been popular with collectors.
