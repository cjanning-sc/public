Singapore Hosts Meeting On The Asian Growth and Recovery Initiative (AGRI)
82160B5B-9927-4B99-82BA-FBC7294FA408
Singapore will host a one-day meeting on the Asia Growth and Recovery Initiative (AGRI) at the Ritz-Carlton Millenia Hotel on 12 April 1999. The AGRI is a US$10 billion multilateral initiative by the US, Japan, the World Bank and the Asian Development Bank for crisis-hit countries in Asia. The meeting, co-chaired by the US and Japan, brings together various APEC member countries, as well as the International Monetary Fund, the World Bank and the Asian Development Bank.
