New 20-Year SGS to Further Develop Singapore's Debt Market
4247BBC2-6050-4783-BA1B-7F79B40ED475
The Singapore Government will issue for the first time, a 20-year Singapore Government Securities (SGS) bond in March 2007. This was outlined in the latest SGS issuance calendar released today. Currently, the 15-year SGS is the longest maturity bond that the Government started issuing in 2001.  The issue of the 20-year bond is part of the Government and MAS' efforts to develop the Singapore debt market.
