MAS Accepts Committee's Proposals to Improve Transparency, Competition and Efficiency of Life Insurance Distribution System
00CA25C2-24B9-40C8-B4E5-E743AFE178EE
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today announced that it has accepted most of the recommendations made by the Committee on Efficient Distribution of Life Insurance (CEDLI) in its Supplementary Report, paving the way for a more transparent, competitive and efficient life insurance distribution system in Singapore. This final report by the CEDLI covered three key areas: the agency structure, the roles and responsibilities of sales advisers and their supervisors, and the development of alternative distribution channels for life insurance distribution.
