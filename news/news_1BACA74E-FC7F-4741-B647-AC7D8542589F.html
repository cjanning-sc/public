MAS issues consultation papers as part of SFA and FAA and extends grace period to industry on implementation of two initiatives
1BACA74E-FC7F-4741-B647-AC7D8542589F
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today released a number of initiatives as part of its ongoing implementation of the Financial Advisers Act (FAA) and Securities and Futures Act (SFA). This includes the release of three consultation papers and the extension in the grace period for implementation of two initiatives.
