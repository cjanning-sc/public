Safe Transition to Phase Two of the Re-opening for the Financial Sector

In line with MOH's announcement on 15 June 2020 and MOM's requirements for safe management measures, financial institutions in Singapore that re-open more branches and customer service locations, and resume more in-person financial services from 2359 hours on 18 June 2020, must ensure that Safe Management measures are in place.
