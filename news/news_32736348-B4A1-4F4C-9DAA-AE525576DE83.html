MAS Launches Second Phase of Finance Scholarship Programme
MAS Launches Second Phase of Finance Scholarship Programme
32736348-B4A1-4F4C-9DAA-AE525576DE83
MAS has launched the second phase of the Finance Scholarship Programme (FSP). This phase caters to outstanding individuals who are not sponsored by financial institutions. It will provide scholarships for these individuals to pursue specialised Masters programmes in top-ranked universities.
