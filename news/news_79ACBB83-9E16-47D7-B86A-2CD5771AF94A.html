New Deputy Chairman for MAS

Mr Lim Hng Kiang, Minister for Trade and Industry, has been appointed Deputy Chairman of the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) with effect from 1 June 2006. Mr Lim's appointment will be for two years. Mr Lim takes over from Mr Tharman Shanmugaratnam, Minister for Education and Second Minister for Finance. Mr Shanmugaratnam will remain a member of the MAS' Board.
