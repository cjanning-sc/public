MAS Releases Second Consultation Paper on Deposit Insurance Scheme
08F5BA28-1529-42D0-87FB-93C60CB8FC95
MAS today released the second consultation paper on the establishment of a deposit insurance ("DI") scheme in Singapore. The key objectives of the DI scheme are to provide an adequate level of protection for small depositors and to dispel any mistaken perception of an implicit government guarantee on deposits.
