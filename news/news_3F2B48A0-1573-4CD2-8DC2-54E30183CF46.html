The Ministry of Finance (MOF), the Inland Revenue Authority of Singapore (IRAS), and MAS today announced new measures to provide real estate investment trusts listed on the Singapore Exchange (S-REITs) with greater flexibility to manage their cash flows and raise funds amid a challenging operating environment due to COVID-19.
