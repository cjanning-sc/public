MAS Launches Electronic Trading Platform for SGS, and Introduces New Standing Facility for S$ Transactions
460B8097-2106-445C-95D1-CBDD1D1DF7EA
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) has launched an electronic bond trading platform for Singapore Government Securities (SGS), and introduced a new standing facility for Singapore dollar transactions.  These initiatives are part of MAS' efforts to enhance the sophistication and efficiency of Singapore's financial markets.
