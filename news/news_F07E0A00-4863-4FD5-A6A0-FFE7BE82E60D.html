Government of The Republic Of Singapore and The International Monetary Fund The IMF and Singapore Launch the IMF-Singapore Regional Training Institute and Hold Inaugural International Seminar
F07E0A00-4863-4FD5-A6A0-FFE7BE82E60D
On Monday, May 4, 1998 the International Monetary Fund (IMF) and Singapore will inaugurate the IMF-Singapore Regional Training Institute (STI) in Singapore. A ribbon-cutting ceremony will take place at the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS), 14th Storey, 10 Shenton Way, Singapore at 10:00 a.m. and will be attended by Mr Michel Camdessus, Managing Director of the IMF, Dr Richard Hu, Minister for Finance, Republic of Singapore, Mr Koh Yong Guan, Managing Director, MAS, and Central Bank Governors and Finance Ministers from a number of Asian countries.
