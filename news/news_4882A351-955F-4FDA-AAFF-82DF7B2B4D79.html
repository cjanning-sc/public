Existing Support Measures will Help SMEs Impacted by Legislation to Facilitate Restructuring of Contracts

MAS said that the extended credit relief measures announced by MAS and the financial industry on 5 Oct 2020 will provide support to SMEs affected by the Re-align Framework passed in Parliament on 3 Nov 2020 to help businesses severely impacted by COVID-19 to renegotiate selected types of contracts.
