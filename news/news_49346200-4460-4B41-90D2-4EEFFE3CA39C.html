Singapore to Host the 8th Asean Finance Ministers' Meeting
49346200-4460-4B41-90D2-4EEFFE3CA39C
On 7 April 2004, Singapore will host the 8th ASEAN Finance Ministers Meeting. The Finance Ministers and their deputies from the ASEAN countries meet annually to discuss the latest economic developments and outlook for the region. This is the first time that Singapore is hosting this event since the Finance Ministers Meeting started in 1997.
