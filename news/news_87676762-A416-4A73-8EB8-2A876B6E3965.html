SMS one-time passwords diverted to perform fraudulent card payments
87676762-A416-4A73-8EB8-2A876B6E3965
MAS, IMDA and SPF said malicious actors overseas had diverted and used SMS OTPs to perform fraudulent credit card transactions affecting 75 bank customers in Singapore. Customers had reported that they had not initiated the transactions nor received the SMS OTPs required to perform these transactions.
