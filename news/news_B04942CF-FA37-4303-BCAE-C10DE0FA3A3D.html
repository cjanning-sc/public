Discontinuation of $1,000 Note Issuance to Pre-empt Money Laundering and Terrorism Financing Risks
B04942CF-FA37-4303-BCAE-C10DE0FA3A3D
MAS announced today that it plans to discontinue the issuance of the $1,000 note from 1 January 2021. From now until December 2020, a limited quantity of $1,000 notes will be made available each month. This is a pre-emptive measure to mitigate the higher money laundering and terrorism financing risks associated with large denomination notes.
