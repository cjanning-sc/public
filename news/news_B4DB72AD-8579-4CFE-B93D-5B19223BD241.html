Anti-Money Laundering/AML/CFT
This set of regulations is issued pursuant to section 27A(1)(b) of the MAS Act and gives effect to asset freezing requirements and other prohibitions under the UN Security Council Resolutions that relate to Eritrea, which all financial institutions in Singapore must comply with.
