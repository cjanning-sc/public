Singapore's Payment Systems Successful in Global Test
B74B5D32-6911-4A31-B072-6573C4BA29C0
Singapore successfully completed its part in the Global Payment Systems Test (GPST) initiated by the New York Clearing House (NYCH) on 13 June 1999. This is a global test involving 34 national and international payments systems and networks to demonstrate the safe transfer of funds across the world for the Year 2000.
