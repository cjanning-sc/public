Provides guidance to financial institutions on how to use the Data Validation module in the Representative Notification System to review and validate the mappings of their current activities and products under the Securities and Futures Act (SFA) and Financial Advisers Act (FAA) to those under the amended SFA and FAA. Please note that the Data Validation module was closed on 3 October 2018. This set of FAQs is no longer relevant.
