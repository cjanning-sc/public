MAS Launches the 2007 Singapore 42 Years of Independence Commemorative Coins

The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today launched a set of coins to commemorate Singapore’s 42 years of independence. The coins capture the optimism and pride among Singaporeans from all walks of life as they come together to celebrate our nation’s independence.
