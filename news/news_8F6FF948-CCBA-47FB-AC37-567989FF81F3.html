Improving Transparency on Short Selling Activities in Singapore
8F6FF948-CCBA-47FB-AC37-567989FF81F3
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) will require investors to report their short positions and short sell orders in securities listed on the Singapore Exchange (SGX), with effect from 1 October 2018. This will improve transparency on short selling activities in the securities market and enable investors to make more informed trading decisions.
