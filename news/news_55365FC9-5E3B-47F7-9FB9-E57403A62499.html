Re-appointments to the MAS Board of Directors
55365FC9-5E3B-47F7-9FB9-E57403A62499
MAS announced on 28 May 2019, the re-appointment of Mr Lim Hng Kiang, Special Advisor, Ministry of Trade and Industry and Mr Ong Ye Kung, Minister for Education, as members of the MAS Board for a further term of three years, starting from 1 June 2019. Mr Ravi Menon has also been re-appointed as a member of the MAS Board and as Managing Director of MAS, for a further term of two years.
