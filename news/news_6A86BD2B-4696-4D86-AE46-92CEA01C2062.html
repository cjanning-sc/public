Last Week for Online Application to Exchange for Singapore Bicentennial $20 Commemorative Note

MAS commented today that over 180,000 individuals have applied for more than 1.5 million pieces of the Singapore Bicentennial $20 commemorative notes. The public has until this Sunday, 13 October 2019 11.59pm to apply online for the notes at go.gov.sg/mas.
