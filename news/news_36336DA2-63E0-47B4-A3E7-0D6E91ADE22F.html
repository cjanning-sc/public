MAS Consults on Requirements to Strengthen Financial Institutions’ Identity Verification Process
36336DA2-63E0-47B4-A3E7-0D6E91ADE22F
MAS issued a consultation paper on the types of information required for non-face-to-face verification of an individual’s identity. These proposed requirements come against the backdrop of rising impersonation scam cases, and seek to address the risks arising from theft and misuse of an individual’s personal particulars.
