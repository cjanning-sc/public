MAS Sets Out Approach for Publishing Market Conduct Regulatory Actions
C04F1E3D-5F63-4096-8980-0BE6180C064A
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) will publish details of market conduct regulatory actions where it is in the public interest to do so. This new approach is part of our ongoing enhancements of the supervisory framework and takes effect immediately.
