In response to a forum letter on the risk of individuals' financial data being exposed should the Singapore Financial Data Exchange (SGFinDex) be compromised, MAS and SNDGG explained that SGFinDex will not consolidate individuals' financial information in one place. There are also stringent security measures to safeguard the data as it transits through SGFinDex.
