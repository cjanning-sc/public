MAS Extends Digital Bank Assessment Period in view of COVID-19 Pandemic
0A34EC47-E225-48CD-BFB4-76762CAB8AEA
MAS announced that it will extend the assessment period for the award of digital bank licences in view of the COVID-19 pandemic. Successful applicants will be informed in 2H 2020 instead of June 2020 as originally intended.
