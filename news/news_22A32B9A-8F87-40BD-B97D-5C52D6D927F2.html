Brunei Darussalam Central Bank and Monetary Authority of Singapore Deepen Cooperation in Financial Supervision
Brunei Darussalam Central Bank and Monetary Authority of Singapore Deepen Cooperation in Financial Supervision
22A32B9A-8F87-40BD-B97D-5C52D6D927F2
The Brunei Darussalam Central Bank (BDCB) and MAS reaffirmed the close ties between the two authorities at the fourth BDCB-MAS Bilateral Roundtable, and discussed new areas of collaboration. At the Roundtable, BDCB Managing Director, Rokiah Bakar and MAS Managing Director, Ravi Menon signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to deepen cooperation in banking and insurance supervision.
