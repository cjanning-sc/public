MAS Launches 2005 Sterling Silver Proof Coin Set Conservation of Natural Landscape

The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today launched the 2005 Sterling Silver Proof Coin Set. This set features the conservation of Natural Landscape as its theme. Minted in 925 silver proof condition, the set consists of 5¢, 10¢, 20¢, 50¢, $1 coins and a 12-sided scallop-edged $5 coin.
