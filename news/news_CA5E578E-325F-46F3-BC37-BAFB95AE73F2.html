MAS, Bank of Ghana and Development Bank Ghana to develop an Integrated Financial Ecosystem to Support Growth of Asia-Africa SME Trade Corridor

MAS, Bank of Ghana and Development Bank Ghana signed an MOU to develop the Ghana Integrated Financial Ecosystem (GIFE). The GIFE aims to enhance financial capabilities and access for MSMEs in Ghana and generate greater opportunities for trade and financial services cooperation between Singapore and Ghana.
