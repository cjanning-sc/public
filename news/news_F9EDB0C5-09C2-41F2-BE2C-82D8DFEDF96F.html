The World Bank's Economic Development Institute (EDI) and the Monetary Authority of Singapore, with the support of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Singapore and the Swiss Agency for Development and Co-operation, jointly organised a regional seminar on banking crisis resolution on May 12-13, 1998 at the Marina Mandarin Hotel in Singapore.
