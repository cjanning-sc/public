Offers of Investments/Offers of Securities or Securities-Based Derivatives Contracts,Offers of Investments/Offers of Collective Investment Schemes
Sets out the information that provisions relating to collective investment schemes (Div 2 of Part XIII of the SFA) shall not apply to offers of debentures or any derivatives in respect of any debentures of a real estate investment trust.
