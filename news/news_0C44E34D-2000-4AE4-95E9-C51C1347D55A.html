Response to Letter "Singapore Savings Bonds – Look at raising the individual limit of $200,000" – The Straits Times, 5 May 2023

In response to a forum letter suggesting that MAS raises the individual limit of $200,000 for the Singapore Savings Bond (SSB) programme, MAS said the individual limit ensures that more individuals have access to SSBs, and for SSBs to complement other savings and investment options as part of a diversified portfolio. Investors that have reached the $200,000 limit may wish to consider other products such as Treasury Bills or SGS bonds.
