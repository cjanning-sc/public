MAS Announces Results of Foreign Bank Applications Four Foreign Banks named Qualifying Full Banks
879F8563-AE7D-46A9-AC33-C29449C67B3B
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today announced that it has awarded four foreign banks with Qualifying Full Bank (QFB) privileges and another eight banks with Qualifying Offshore Bank (QOB) privileges. Eight new Restricted Banks (RB) were also announced today.[Details of MAS' earlier announcement of QFB / QOB privileges and RB licences as well as MAS' assessment criteria are at Annex 1.]
