MAS Welcomes Announcement on Asian Bond Fund 2

The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) said today that it is pleased with developments in the local currency Asian Bond Fund 2 (ABF 2) initiative by the Executives' Meeting of East Asia and Pacific Central Banks (EMEAP)1 group. As a member of EMEAP, Singapore participates in steering the group's efforts to promote the development of bond markets in the region.
