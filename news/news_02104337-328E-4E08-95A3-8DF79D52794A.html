MAS Issues $10 Polymer Portrait Notes
02104337-328E-4E08-95A3-8DF79D52794A
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today announced that it will introduce $10 polymer portrait notes into circulation on 4 May 2004. To preserve public familiarity and minimise modifications to note handling machines, the design of the new polymer notes is similar to the current $10 paper portrait notes except for a few changes to incorporate security features unique to polymer technology. MAS will continue to issue the current $10 paper notes as part of a phased introduction of the new $10 polymer notes.
