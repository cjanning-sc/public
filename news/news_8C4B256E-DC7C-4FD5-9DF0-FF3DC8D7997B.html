New MAS Measures To Separate Financial And Non-financial Activities Of Banking Groups
8C4B256E-DC7C-4FD5-9DF0-FF3DC8D7997B
The Monetary Authority of Singapore has announced new regulations requiring the separation of financial and non-financial activities of Singapore banks. Speaking at the annual dinner of the Association of Banks Singapore, MAS Chairman DPM Lee Hsien Loong said that the measures are a necessary part of Singapore's ongoing reform initiative to strengthen the domestic banking sector.
