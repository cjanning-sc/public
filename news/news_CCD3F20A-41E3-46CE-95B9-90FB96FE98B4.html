The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today released a consultation paper on the guidelines on outsourcing. These guidelines set out MAS' expectations for financial institutions that have entered into outsourcing arrangements or are planning to outsource one or more of their business activities to a service provider in Singapore or elsewhere.
