Consultation on Adjustment Spreads for the Transition of Legacy SOR Contracts in Wholesale Markets

MAS and SC-STS jointly announced an SC-STS consultation on adjustment spreads that will apply to legacy SOR business loans and derivatives. The consultation covers the setting of the MAS Recommended Rate to provide contractual certainty for SOR contracts that remain outstanding after 31 December 2024, and supplementary guidance to support the on-going transition of legacy SOR contracts in wholesale markets.
