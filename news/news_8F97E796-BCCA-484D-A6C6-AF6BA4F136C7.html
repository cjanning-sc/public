MAS Opens New Promotional Offices In U.K. And U.S.
8F97E796-BCCA-484D-A6C6-AF6BA4F136C7
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) officially opened its new London representative office and its promotional office in New York yesterday. The Deputy Prime Minister of Singapore and Chairman of MAS, Mr Lee Hsien Loong, was in London to officiate the event. His speech was video linked live to the New York office where Mr Koh Yong Guan, Managing Director of MAS, hosted lunch.
