Singapore FinTech Festival 2022 returns as an in-person event from 31 October to 4 November
23240FE4-57FA-4D3D-B506-A6708D65CACC
MAS and Elevandi announced the return of the Singapore FinTech Festival (SFF) as an in-person event to be held from 2 to 4 November 2022, with industry events and the annual Innovation Lab Crawl being held on 31 October and 1 November. The theme of SFF 2022 will be ‘Building Resilient Business Models amid Volatility and Change’.
