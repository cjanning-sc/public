MAS Proposes Extending the Corporate Governance Framework to all locally incorporated direct insurers and reinsurers
4F44DE40-DD56-4C10-A897-B6658D1B3DBE
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) has issued a consultation paper proposing an extension of the MAS Corporate Governance (CG) Framework, which comprises the Regulations and Guidelines1, to all direct insurers and reinsurers incorporated in Singapore. This is in line with MAS’ emphasis on the importance of effective corporate governance, given insurers’ responsibilities to safeguard policyholders’ interests and the important role they play in the financial system and economy.
