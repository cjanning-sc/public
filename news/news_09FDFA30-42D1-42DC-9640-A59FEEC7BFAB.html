MAS Unveils Measures to Further Grow the Debt Market
09FDFA30-42D1-42DC-9640-A59FEEC7BFAB
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today announced new initiatives to further boost the growth of the Singapore Government Securities (SGS) market. Speaking at the FIA Asian Financial Markets 2000 Conference held in Singapore this morning, Second Minister for Finance Mr Lim Hng Kiang revealed that MAS will adopt a three-pronged strategy to strengthen the market-making mechanism and improve price transparency in the SGS market.
