MAS Issues Information Paper on Good Practices for Financial Advisers

The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) issued an Information Paper on Good Practices for Licensed and Exempt Financial Advisers. The paper aims to assist financial advisers (FAs) enhance their advisory and sales process, complaints handling, compliance function and competency level of their representatives.
