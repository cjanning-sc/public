Response to "Can remittance companies lend money" - The Straits Times, 18 January 2019

In its response to a forum letter on remittance companies' lending practices, MAS clarified that it is not the intent of MAS to allow remittance licensees to conduct consumer lending. MAS added that licensees under the new Payment Services Bill will be prohibited from conducting consumer lending when the Bill comes into force later this year.
