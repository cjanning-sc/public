Your opportunities for achievement are limitless with ACME Engineering Space Systems.

An internship at ACME Engineering is unique. Sure, you’ll learn new skills, explore our enterprise, network with experts, connect with thought leaders, and finish with a resume that opens doors. But you’ll gain something even more valuable: pride in what you’ve done. Join us and launch your career. We’ll support you with training, employee resource groups, and our shared vision of global security.

ACME Engineering Tactical Space Division in Des Moines, VA specializes in the Integration, Test and Launch of Space Systems for Commercial, DOD and NASA customers, and has started an internship program for Technician level positions.

The Des Moines Integration and Test Team is seeking the following individuals to join our team:

1. Individuals that are planning on going to a 2 year or more college program following graduation from High School in fields that have a direct relation to the broad range of job descriptions that are performed at ACME Engineering.

2. Have a desire to work in field(s) of Manufacturing, Mechanical Integration, Electronic or Environmental Testing.

3. Must have a superior work ethic. Willing to be on time each day to support the team, be able to interact with a diverse work force of Technicians, Engineers and Managers that are working toward a defined goal each day. Must be enthusiastic about the potential to work in the exciting Space Industry.

Duties will include shadowing and supporting experienced technicians performing electrical, mechanical and manufacturing tasks on Ground Support equipment and Space Flight Hardware.

Positions may be in close contact with large machines such as cranes, forklifts, manlifts, manufacturing systems such as milling and CNC machines, welders, electronic assembly pick and place machines and other machinery.

Basic Qualifications:

A candidate, regardless of age and hiring source, must meet ALL of the below criteria. The candidate must:

  • Be a student who is enrolled in an accredited high school

  • Have an overall cumulative GPA of 3.0/4.0 or higher (unweighted)( (unofficial academic transcripts must be provided at time of application by uploading the documents to your application or profile)

  • Plan on attending a 2-year college, post high school graduation in a STEM or manufacturing degree.

  • Available transportation and ability to work in shift rotations, including weekends, nights and holidays

  • Commute less than 50 miles of work location   

  •  U.S. Citizenship required  

  • Preferred areas of study include, but not limited to:

    • AET Engineering

    • AET Information Technology

    • Network, Computer or Digital technologies

    • Auto Service

    • Heating and Ventilation

    • Machinist

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Have an overall cumulative GPA of 3.7 out of 4.0 or higher

Intern Pay Range:

High school: $17.25- $20.75

At ACME Engineering, our internship pay rates are standardized and take into account three main factors: the anticipated graduation date of the individual, the cost of labor of the geographic location, and whether the internship is considered technical or administrative.
