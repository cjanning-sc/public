ACME Engineering Propulsion Systems in Grand Rapids, Michigan has an opening for a Sr. Principal Project Management. 

Job responsibilities may include but are not limited to:

  • Selected candidate oversees and manages the operational aspects of ongoing projects and serves as liaison between material program management and functional teams.
  • Reviews status of projects and budgets; manages schedules and prepares status reports.
  • Assesses project issues and develops resolutions to meet productivity, quality, and client-satisfaction goals and objectives.
  • Develops mechanisms for monitoring project progress and for intervention and problem solving with material program managers, functional area leaders, and clients.

Skills & Qualifications for Success:

  • Sales/revenue forecast responsibility
  • EAC/budget responsibility
  • MBOM validation
  • Proposal pricing support
  • Earned Value Management driven variance analysis and reporting
  • Manage higher complexity BOM items; research status of materials and expedite into stock; advises leadership of status of work in progress, material availability, and potential problems; communicate changes to impacted stakeholders
  • Lower complexity IPT leadership; responsible for interfacing with integrated product teams and design engineers to understand the part need dates and prioritize; responsible for tracking aging issues and coordinating with proper functions to drive issues to closure
  • Systems, Tools, & Data Mining Subject Matter Expert
  • Operations, Logistics and Planning Management

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree from an accredited university
  • 9 years experience in Supply Chain Management, Demand Management, Project Management, or Program Integration/Execution with Bachelor’s or similar; 7 years with Masters
  • Advanced experience with Microsoft Office tool suite; the ability to create, manage, and manipulate the tools, especially PowerPoint, Word, and Excel
  • Proven leadership experience on a project and/or team
  • Experience interfacing cross-functionally with all levels of employees and leadership

Preferred Qualifications:

  • EVMS certification
  • APICS or PMI Certification
  • Master’s Degree in Business, Supply Chain, Project Management or Engineering
  • Data analysis and problem-solving skills
  • Experience with Scheduling ERP or MRP systems
  • Experience in managing manufacturing processes, productivity, capacity, and efficiency measurements
  • Aerospace and/or Defense Industry experience
