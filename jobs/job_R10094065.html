Northrop Grumman is seeking a Logistics Planner to join our team supporting our SMORS project.  The position is based at a facility in Colorado Springs, Colorado.

This role supports business analysts and developers on project teams as they document their data requirements and establish thorough specifications.

Job responsibilities will include, but not be limited to, the following:

  • Conducts facilitation sessions to gather requirements.
  • Builds logical models.
  • Works with database administrators to build the physical model and overall data architecture.
  • Reviews and reports on project metrics to benchmark effectiveness.
  • Works closely with database administrators and developers to ensure that business models are accurately implemented in application systems.
  • Provides education on the naming and modeling standards; assists with modeling specific constructs.
  • Reviews project models to ensure adherence to standards.
  • Ensures data quality alignment.
  • Works on and across multiple projects to ensure consistency in naming conventions and promotes the sharing and reuse of data across applications and business areas.
  • Ensures that data analysis goals are met.
  • Contributes to data management internal projects as well as the upgrading of standards and best practice guidelines.

Basic Qualifications

  • 5 years of related experience with a Bachelor’s degree; 3 years of related experience with a Master’s degree; 0 years of related experience with a Ph.D.; 9+ years of related experience may be considered in lieu of a degree
  • Must have current/active DoD Secret Security Clearance or higher and be able to maintain
  • Experience with Diminishing Manufacturing Sources and Material Shortages (DMSMS)
  • Ability to self-motivate and complete assigned tasks

This role is open to supporting a hybrid work schedule.  Monday and Friday could support telework, but being onsite Tuesday through Thursday would be required.

Salary Range:  $86,300 - $129,500
