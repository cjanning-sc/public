Job Description:

Work with site team in coordinating daily maintenance with ROK Military. Delegating work assignments to employees and FSRs as require. Insuring employees follow all ground operation procedures. Provides daily reports and pass down to Programs Managers. Approve employee’s timecard and provide guidance to staff members. Follow and help insure employee follows all LSI procedure. Oversees maintenance servicing, repairing and overhauling aircraft and aircraft engines to ensure that aircraft are in good mechanical condition and safe for flight. Ensures FAA maintenance requirements are met. Schedules maintenance and inspections. Obtains clearance from ROKAF for all US only work making sure proper sign and posting are display. Recommends procedure changes to improve maintainability of aircraft and timely inspections. Consults technical manuals for specifications and determines feasibility of repair. Help to clarify FSAR with engineering team on daily basis.

Basic Qualifications:

Bachelor's Degree with 5 years experience OR 9 years experience in lieu of degree.

Active/Current Secret Level DoD clearance

Maintain all annual qualification (FOD, aircraft servicing, aircraft jacking, ESD, aircraft towing, aircraft fueling operation).

Familiar with AFTO Form 781 for signing of discrepancy.

Airframe and Powerplant license or equivalent FAA aircraft license

Must be able to maintain 7 level or equivalence to sign off Red X. 2 years’ experience in management.
