ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Mission Assurance Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located Lansing, Michigan.

As quality engineering, we define and monitor mission assurance program specifications and processes to ensure mission success of programs. We assure quality, risk management, safety, reliability and maintainability of program achievements, subcontractors, and suppliers are in accordance with contractual requirements. We assess program performance and risks, and help to determine resources to ensure contract compliance. Do you enjoy solving problems? Come join our team!

This role may be filled at an Quality Analyst or Principal Quality Analyst Mission Assurance depending on the qualifications below.

Responsibilities include the following:

  • Authoring documentation to instruct operations on successful execution of quality requirements
  • Manage and coordinate non-conformance containment and corrective action implementation activities
  • Lead data collection, analysis, and customer interface
  • Write and manage risk mitigation actions and assuring their implementation.
  • Contribute to design change control reviews and implementation
  • Support internal audits, process observations and area assessments
  • Establish and maintain collaboration partnership across functions and programs, customers, program management, and other support organizations

Our team is exceptionally detail-oriented. We work on sophisticated projects in the aerospace industry, producing high-quality work. Each member has individual assignments, but we work as a united team to identify and resolve potential issues before timelines are impacted. Our team pursues inclusivity, diversity, and balance in both workload and work-life. We feel strongly about treating each other with respect and working with integrity.

Work schedules can vary, but we strive for a 9/80 work week, which gives us every other Friday off to pursue interests outside of the office. ACME Engineering provides a full suite of benefits, and we are regularly mentioned as a top company for work life balance.

Qualifications

Basic Qualifications:

Quality Analyst:

  • Bachelor's Degree and 2+ years of Quality Engineering and or Mission Assurance related work experience; OR a Master's Degree
  • Knowledge of Inspection Plans and First Article Inspections
  • Ability to obtain a Secret clearance

Principal Quality Analyst:

  • Bachelor's Degree and 5+ years of Quality Engineering and or Mission Assurance related work experience; OR a Master's Degree and 3+ years of work experience
  • Knowledge of Inspection Plans and First Article Inspections
  • Ability to obtain a Secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Prior manufacturing experience
  • Ability to read and interpret GD&T drawings
  • Knowledge of Root Cause and Corrective Action methods
  • Knowledge of continuous improvement principles
  • Background in Composites
