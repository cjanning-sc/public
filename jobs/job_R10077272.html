Job Description:

This position is for the role of a Program Integrator. The selected candidate will be part of a diverse organization within the Program Integration Operations Homeroom onsite in Palmdale, California.

The candidate will be responsible for overseeing and managing the complex operational aspects of ongoing projects and serve as primary liaison between an Integrated Product Team (IPT), support organizations, and Program Management. With oversight, this individual contributor will manage various projects to cost, schedule, and quality goals, and compliance requirements. This includes developing methods for creating and managing schedules, preparing status reports, assessing project issues, developing resolutions, and being the IPT’s primary focal point for capturing risks, issues and opportunities, and other tasks, as assigned, such as preparation and/or presentation of executive level presentations. Some travel may be required.
