ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking an Operations Project Manager 2. This position will be located in Detroit, MI and will support Strategic Deterrence Systems (SDS) Division.  

This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package. 

What You’ll Get To Do:

  • Works with the Site Operations Manager leading a team of Facilities Planners, Facilities Coordinators and Administrative Staff. 
  • Provides direction for all phases of Site Operations projects from inception through completion.
  • Assists with the development of SOWs, purchase requests (PRs), I-Buy, ADR requests and Facilities consumable procurement actions.
  • Ensures projects are defined, operating budgets established, and sustaining service contracts are initiated and modified. 
  • Establishes milestones and monitors adherence to master plans, budgets, and schedules. Identifies problems and develops solutions, such as allocation of resources or changing project scope.
  • Works with other Sentinel Division Sites to integrate facility requirements, modifications, capability stand-ups and sustainment actions.
  • Experience working with construction projects, facilities drawings, and building/design layouts. 
  • Interfaces with Sentinel and ICBM Sustainment IPTs, Division and Sector entities to facilitate a collaborative team environment across all levels.

 Basic Qualifications: 

  • Bachelors Degree and 6 years of related experience or 4 additional years of related experience in lieu of degree to equal 10 years of related experience
