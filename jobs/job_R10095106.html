Northrop Grumman Space Division offers exciting opportunities for individuals looking to take part in the development and deployment of cutting-edge space technologies. With a focus on innovation, the Space Division is a leader in designing, building, and launching satellites, space vehicles, and related systems for government and commercial customers. Working in this dynamic environment requires a team of highly-skilled and motivated professionals who are eager to explore the possibilities of space exploration. If you are interested in becoming part of the Space Division team, we invite you to apply for a position and join us on our journey.

If you are interested in becoming part of the Space Division team, Northrop Grumman is seeking a Senior Principal Supply Chain Subcontract Specialist. This position will be located in Redondo Beach, CA. The qualified candidate will become part of the Northrop Grumman’s Strategic Space Systems Division, Global Supply Chain team.

Responsibilities:

  • Sources and procures specialized goods and services
  • Owns contractual management for the supplier
  • Responsible for source selection from contract proposal to execution phases
  • Coordinates and collaborates with key stakeholders such as contracts, engineering, quality, supplier development, planning, operations, program and end-users
  • Develops subcontract specifications, work statements, and terms and conditions for the procurement of specialized materials, equipment, and/or services
  • Prepares bid packages, conducts bidders conferences, analyzes and evaluates proposals, negotiates subcontract provisions, selects or recommends subcontractors, writes awards, and administers resulting subcontracts
  • In conjunction with quality organization, evaluates and monitors supplier quality and reliability, as well as supplier ability to support required delivery schedules
  • Identifies strategies and opportunities to reduce cost, improve efficiency and manage risk through strategic and targeted negotiations
  • Manages performance of subcontracts, ensures compliance with all public law requirements as well as company (and business) policies and procedures including import/export regulations
  • Employs sound business practices and assesses supplier performance as part of follow-up activities
  • Ensures that appropriate documentation is provided to logistics/transportation& warehouse organization so that proper receipt is recorded
  • Manages and develops strategic/key suppliers
  • Reviews and approves supplier payment as needed
  • Utilizes process tools such as Lean Six Sigma with suppliers to drive quality, efficiency and continuous improvement

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree with 10 years of experience in Supplier Management or Supply Chain Management or Master’s degree or JD with 8 years of experience for a level 4; in lieu of degree 14 years of experience will be considered
  • Ability to obtain a security clearance
  • Experience with Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Power Point)
  • Strong SAP or other ERP system experience
  • Working knowledge of FAR/DFAR regulations

Preferred Qualifications:

  • International Supplier Subcontract/Procurement experience
  • Strong negotiation skills
  • Led a team; ability to mentor/guide others
  • Familiar with production programs
  • Familiar with planning/scheduling
