ACME Engineering Space Systems Sector Payload and Ground Systems (PGS) Division Engineering is looking for Sr. Principal Systems Engineer to join the Program Partnerships team within the Workforce Innovation and Strategic Excellence team.

Program Partnerships supports program excellence across the PGS division through current activities such as: Critical skills retention, Internal Review Planning, Engineering metrics visualization, Principles and Operating practices management, and Agile foundations.  Ongoing program collaboration will drive forward opportunities to enhance program excellence.  Our small Agile scrumban team is a dynamic and flexible work family.  Work location is flexible, and flexible schedules including part time support and telecommuting options will be considered. We are looking for a detail oriented, systems engineer to be a key contributor to our efforts toward program excellence.  You will have opportunities to drive process changes, solve engineering problems, maintain clear communications, and measure engineering success – all tasks that positively impact program performance. This is a great opportunity for a strong, innovative engineer to be a part of an impactful, virtual, and flexible team environment.

We may consider fulltime remote work for this position.

Responsibilities will include:

  • Leadership of collaboration between the division and BU teams around process improvement and program excellence initiatives
  • Identification of process improvements to address performance gaps through collaboration with the program teams and other functional areas
  • Development and implementation of prioritized plans for execution of initiatives
  • Ownership of select PrOP documents and their updates
  • Implementation of Agile initiatives in Engineering processes and programs
  • Creation, organization, and documentation of the team’s work products
  • Participation in initiatives to provide engineering improvements and commonality

Internally, cross-division, and cross-sector

  • Leadership of the team’s agile ceremonies including scrum meetings, backlog grooming, sprint planning, and release planning
  • Ability and desire to capitalize on opportunities to support proposals, IRADs, or other activities as assigned
