ACME Engineering Mission Systems is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security. Our Engineering and Sciences (E&S) organization pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, knowledge, and expertise to architect, design, produce and sustain advanced Navigation, Integrated Avionics, and Applied Sciences capabilities critical to providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter.  Come be a part of our mission!

The Engineering & Sciences (E&S) Leadership is seeking an Engineering Operations Manager 1/2 to join our team located in Athens, CA.  This position will report directly to the E&S Site Director. Applicants must be organized, detail focused, and results driven, and will be responsible for translating the organization’s vision into an operational reality. Applicants are also expected to have excellent written and oral communications skills and be able to create and deliver presentations to various levels of leadership.

The selected candidate will:

  • Partner with Division E&S Operations Manager to ensure alignment to Division Leadership strategies and priorities
  • Coordinate with E&S Discipline Leads and engineering OU business partners to ensure effective planning, staffing, and efficient functional engineering operations.
  • Collaborate with the engineering, BU and OU operations managers to ensure a strong partnership between E&S and Program leadership.
  • Collaborate with multiple functional organizations (Human Resources, Talent Acquisition, Business Management, Mission Assurance, Enterprise Services, Program Management, Facilities and Security) to define strategies that enhance operational effectiveness and efficiency.
  • Identify focus areas for improvement/streamlining, development and execution of engineering initiatives, and elimination of non-value-added activities in all levels of the E&S organization.

The candidate will support Site-level operations and requires a broad base of engineering knowledge in Systems Engineering, Integration & Test (SEIT), Software Engineering/Digital Technology (SWDT), and Hardware Engineering (HWE).  The ideal candidate will also have a strong background in business management & finance, program management, proven strategic planning and business execution capabilities, as well as the ability to work collaboratively across the OUs and other Division sites.

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Establish & maintain the organization business rhythm - includes management reporting and information flow.  Improve operational systems, processes, and policies in support of the E&S mission.
  • Develop and support collaboration tools required for efficient and effective communication across all levels of the organization
  • Support staffing efforts including demand summarization, supply forecast planning, and retention activities to meet the labor goals required by the Business Units
  • Lead indirect budget management to include allocation, monitoring, reporting, and resolving discrepancies
  • Support or lead workforce development projects including training, mentoring programs, and technical growth initiatives
  • Coordinate and track all E&S capital projects from planning through project close out.
  • Coordinate and track engineering IRAD/ITA/CI projects from planning through project close out
  • Lead internal special projects to include Employee Engagement action planning and tracking, developing and monitoring performance objectives, personnel highlights, etc.
  • Serve as the Athens E&S training lead and support engineers and scientists with training matters
