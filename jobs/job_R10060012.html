Northrop Grumman's Enterprise Services organization is seeking a Database Architect. This position can be hired as a level 2 or level 3, depending upon the candidate's education and experience.

Job Responsibilities include:


•    Design, develop, and maintain scalable Database solutions
•    Data preparation/cleaning, integration, and automation from heterogeneous sources
•    Ensure data integrity and system availability
•    Identify, evaluate, and recommend core technologies and strategies
•    Monitor and optimize system performance
•    Decompose user requirements into logical functions/components

Basic Qualifications Data Architect (Level 2):

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) field with 2+ years of experience

  • Experience working with various DBMS solutions including Oracle

  • Experience with integration of data from multiple sources

  • Basic knowledge of SQL Server administration

  • Experience with Windows and Linux

  • Experience with Cloud Technology

  • Understanding of Agile Software Development

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Security Clearance AND Special Program Access in a reasonable amount of time as determined by business needs

Basic Qualifications Principal Data Architect (Level 3):

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) field with 5+ years of experience OR Master's Degree with 3 years of experience

  • 3+ years of experience working with multiple DBMS Solutions including Oracle and SQL Server

  • Basic knowledge of DBMS solutions PostgreSQL, Mongo, ESSBase, MySQL

  • Experience with Windows and Linux

  • Experience with AWS and/or Azure Cloud Solutions

  • Experience with integration of data from multiple sources

  • Understanding of Agile Software Development

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Security Clearance AND Special Program Access in a reasonable amount of time as determined by business needs

Preferred Qualifications:

  • CompTIA Security+ Certification

  • Active DoD Secret Clearance

  • Special Program Access
