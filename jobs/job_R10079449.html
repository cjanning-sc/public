Principal Engineer/Sr. Principal Engineer Mechanical (T3/T4)
Northrop Grumman is seeking Mechanical Design Engineers to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. The Marine Systems organization, part of Northrop Grumman Mission Systems, is searching for Mechanical Design Engineers in support of rapid growth in the Launcher Hardware organization. You will have the opportunity to be part of an expanding team of dedicated engineers directly supporting the engineering development of innovative launcher hardware for weapons systems on a variety of Navy platforms.

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Develop designs concept and generate solid models that reflect those concepts
  • Collaborate with internal stakeholders balancing technical, schedule, and cost requirements
  • Conduct trade studies to select and evaluate best design approaches to fulfill requirements
  • Oversee the generation of engineering drawings using common GD&T practices
  • Identify procurement needs and support fabrication
  • Provide assembly checkout and test support
  • Generate task plans and support proposal efforts
  • Occasional domestic travel may be required

The qualified candidate will possess the following skills:

  • Good communication skills, both oral and written
  • Ability to work autonomously and in a team environment

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Engineer (T3):

  • Solid background in machine design and drawing generation
  • Ability to plan tasks and execute to the established schedule
  • Must be a US citizen with the ability to obtain a DOD secret clearance
  • Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) discipline with 5 or more years engineering experience; Master of Science degree in a STEM discipline with 3 or more years of experience

Basic Qualifications for a Sr. Principal Engineer Mechanical (T4):

  • Solid background in machine design and drawing generation
  • Ability to plan tasks and execute to the established schedule
  • Must be a US citizen with the ability to obtain a DOD secret clearance
  • Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) discipline with 7 or more years engineering experience; Master of Science degree in a STEM discipline with 5 or more years of experience

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with CAD modeling (NX, CREO, SolidWorks, or equivalent)
  • Experience in ASME Y14.5 GD&T Standard and ASME Y14.100 Dimensioning, Tolerancing, and Engineering Drawing Practices
  • Experience in developing design concepts, performing trade studies, and conducting evaluations and analyses
  • Strong background in machine design, strength of materials, and materials technology
  • Experience in technical presentation and interfacing with customers
  • Experience in working with vendors and suppliers
  • An active DOD Secret clearance
