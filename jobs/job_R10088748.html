ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal Flight Simulation Trainer/ Sr. Principal Flight Simulation Trainer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Philadelphia, FL for the Product Support Group. Operating on our 9/80 schedule meaning you will get every Friday off. The position has no opportunity for remote or telework.

*This position may be filled by either a Principal Flight Simulation Trainer or a Sr. Principal Flight Simulation Trainer level based on the qualifications listed. **

Job Duties Include:

  • Develops training curriculum, study outlines, and orientation materials to be employed during procedures, simulation, and/or flight training.

  • Conducts instructional sessions, briefings, follow-ups, and proficiency checks on flight simulators in accordance with federal air regulations and company policies and procedures.

  • Evaluates trainee progress and recommends qualification or additional training. Reviews simulator design and training effectiveness to provide maximum proficiency.

  • Coordinates utilization schedules for training flight crews and necessary engineering support.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Flight Simulation Trainer:

  • Bachelor's Degree with 6 years’ experience in training and development materials; OR a Masters' Degree with 4 years’ experience in training and development materials; OR High School Diploma with 10 years’ experience in training and development materials in lieu of degree

  • Rated Aircrew (Pilot, navigator, Electronic Warfare Officer (EWO), Weapons System Operator (WSO), Naval Flight officer (NFO).

  • 1 year of experience with Microsoft products such as PowerPoint and Word

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret or Top-Secret Clearance

  • Ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access

Basic Qualifications for Sr. Principal Flight Simulation Trainer:

  • Bachelor's Degree with 10 years’ experience in training and development materials; OR a Master's Degree with 8 years’ experience in training and development materials; OR High School Diploma with 14 years’ experience in training and development materials in lieu of degree

  • Rated Aircrew (Pilot, navigator, Electronic Warfare Officer (EWO), Weapons System Operator (WSO), Naval Flight officer (NFO).

  • Experienced/ combat mission ready in one or more combat mission design series (MDS) with A, F, or B designations

  • 1 year of experience with Microsoft products such as PowerPoint and Word

  • Mission evaluation (MSN Eval) within the past 5 years

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret or Top-Secret Clearance

  • Ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience in task analysis procedures and training systems products

  • Experience with databases

  • Experience using courseware development tools

  • Experienced military courseware development 

  • A current active in-scope DoD Secret clearance

  • B-1, B-2, or B-52 experience

  • Instructional System Design (ISD) education, training, and /or experience from an accredited institution, ACC ISD principles course, ACC classroom instructor course, training support squadron (TRSS) detachment experience
