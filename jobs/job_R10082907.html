Northrop Grumman Space Systems (NGSP) seeks to fill a Principal / Sr. Principal Digital Engineering role in the Payload and Ground Systems Division Engineering organization supporting the Integrated National Systems (INS) Business Unit (BU).  We are seeking Digital Engineers for a small FPGA team in the implementation/creation of VHDL and an embedded ARM development framework inside a Xilinx UltraScale+ FPGA.  The successful candidates should be familiar with the UltraScale 7 Xilinx FPGA, DSP techniques, and IP protocols.  This position can be filled at our Morrisville, NC, or Fairfax, VA office.

The Principal / Sr. Principal Digital Engineer, designs, develops, modifies, and evaluates digital electronic parts, components or integrated circuitry for digital electronic equipment and other hardware systems. Determines design approaches and parameters. Analyzes equipment to establish operating data, conducts experimental tests, and evaluates results. Selects components and equipment based on analysis of specifications and reliability. May also review vendor capability to support product development.
