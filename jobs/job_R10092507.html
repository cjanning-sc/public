Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking to add a Principal Systems Integration Engineer to its Specialty Engineering team in support of the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.  Northrop Grumman supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.  This position is located in Roy, UT or Huntsville, AL.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

  • Apply their experience and knowledge in technical design, build, and verification of complex systems to the full integration of specialty engineering (Nuclear Hardness & Survivability, System Safety, Electromagnetic Environmental Effects, Parts Materials & Processes, Human Factors Engineering) responsibilities to the GBSD system.
  • Collaborate with both internal and external teams to integrate specialty engineering domains across all phases of the systems lifecycle including concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance, and disposal.
  • Maintain technical cognizance of subsystem interdependencies in support of maturing Specialty Systems Engineering analyses/products and identifying and resolving gaps.
  • Engage in formal and informal mentoring to support knowledge transfer and talent development.
  • Implement/Support MBSE strategy for the GBSD Architecture
  • Implement/Support Requirements Verification strategy for the GBSD architecture.
  • Plan with IPTs to ensure the proper testing and qualification/verification efforts are in place, planned for, and budgeted appropriately.
  • Align specialty engineering to the GBSD technical planning, system integration, requirements verification, modeling, and validation efforts.
  • This position will utilize excellent communication, mentoring, and interpersonal skills to interface with senior management, peers, and employees.
