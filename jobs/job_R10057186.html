ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Major Maintenance Vehicles Team Program Manager 3. This position will be located in Detroit, UT and will support the Sentinel Program.  Learn more about the Sentinel program here.  ACME Engineering supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What you will get to do:

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Manages and coordinates with a supplier team composed of engineers, production, quality and procurement personnel
  • Leads solicitations for proposals, review of proposals, and supports supplier negotiations
  • Defines and manages cost and schedule targets to achieve program and business objectives
  • Ensure suppliers are within budget and staying on schedule
  • Work with program business management and contracts to deliver required reporting to the customer
  • Deliver variance reporting on supplier cost accounts
  • Ensures accurate Estimates at Complete (EACs) and material receipt forecasts
  • Develop and track risks and opportunities and manages mitigation plans
  • Monitors daily supplier operations and actively assists/provides in-scope, technical direction to suppliers as required
  • Coordinates/leads supplier design, manufacturing, and program reviews, including the necessary levels of ACME Engineering Space Sector (NGSP) participation
  • Ensures supplier compliance with all elements of the SOW and subcontract
  • Ensures recovery plans are put in place for any / all erosion to schedule milestones
  • Responsible for the direct supervision/management of a team of engineers and support staff

As a leader, you always act with integrity, and the highest ethical standards characterize everything you do.
