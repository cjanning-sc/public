New Facility at interest rate of 0.1% to help banks and finance companies lower cost of loans to SMEs
3294177D-7074-4667-BDA3-266DBED8D47A
MAS has launched the MAS SGD Facility for ESG Loans in partnership with Enterprise Singapore (ESG), to lend SGD at an interest rate of 0.1% per annum to eligible financial institutions, to support their lending to SMEs under the ESG Loan Schemes.
