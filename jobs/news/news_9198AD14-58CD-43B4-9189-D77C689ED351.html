MAS and IMF to Host Second High-Level Seminar on Asian Regional Financial Integration
9198AD14-58CD-43B4-9189-D77C689ED351
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) and the International Monetary Fund (IMF) will host a high-level seminar on Asian regional financial integration in Singapore on Thursday, May 25, 2006. This second high-level seminar is a follow-up to the inaugural event held in Singapore on September 3, 2005.
