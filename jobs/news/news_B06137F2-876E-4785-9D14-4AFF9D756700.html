“Leverage Technology, Build Trust” – Remarks by Ms Ho Hern Shin, Assistant Managing Director (Banking and Insurance), Monetary Authority of Singapore at LIA Annual Luncheon on 6 March 2019
B06137F2-876E-4785-9D14-4AFF9D756700
Speaking at the LIA Annual Luncheon on 6 March 2019, Ms Ho Hern Shin, Assistant Managing Director (Banking and Insurance), said that the fast evolving digital economy - availability of big data, ubiquitous computing powers in the hands of every consumer, and technological advancements in areas such as artificial intelligence, biotechnology and internet of things - have opened up new possibilities for insurers. She called on insurers to tap these new initiatives and innovations to better serve customers while maintaining their trust by handling their data responsibly and by prioritising their interests.
