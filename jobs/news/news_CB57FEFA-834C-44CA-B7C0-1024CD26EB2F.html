MAS is pleased to support the inaugural G20 TechSprint initiative, launched by the Saudi G20 Presidency and the BIS Innovation Hub on the APIX innovation platform. The TechSprint initiative will enable central banks to collaborate with FinTechs on technology solutions to strengthen regulatory and supervisory practices.
