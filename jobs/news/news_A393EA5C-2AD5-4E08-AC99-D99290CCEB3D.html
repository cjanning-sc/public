Joint - US- Singapore Forex Committee Meeting in Singapore

Singapore today hosted the Third Joint Meeting of the New York Foreign Exchange Committee (NYFEC) and the Singapore Foreign Exchange Market Committee (SFEMC). This meeting also marked the first time that representatives from the Tokyo Foreign Exchange Market Committee (TFEMC) participated as an observer.
