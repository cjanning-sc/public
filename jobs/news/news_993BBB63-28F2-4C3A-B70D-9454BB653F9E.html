Singapore FinTech Festival and SWITCH come together – to bring the global innovation community to Singapore
993BBB63-28F2-4C3A-B70D-9454BB653F9E
MAS, Enterprise Singapore and the National Research Foundation announced that the Singapore FinTech Festival (SFF) and the Singapore Week of Innovation and TeCHnology (SWITCH) would come together as SFF x SWITCH to synergise across the themes of FinTech and Deep Tech. SFF x SWITCH will run from 11 to 15 November 2019.
