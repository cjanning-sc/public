Bank Indonesia and Monetary Authority of Singapore Establish US$10 Billion Bilateral Financial Arrangement

Bank Indonesia and MAS have established a bilateral financial arrangement of USD 10 billion equivalent. The arrangement will enable the two central banks to access foreign currency liquidity from each other to preserve monetary and financial stability.
