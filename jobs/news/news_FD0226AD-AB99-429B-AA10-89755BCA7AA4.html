MONEYSENSE-CPF-NTUC Collaboration To Bring Financial Education To Workers
FD0226AD-AB99-429B-AA10-89755BCA7AA4
MoneySENSE, CPF and NTUC announced today a new partnership to bring financial education programmes to NTUC members and the working population over the next two years. The tripartite collaboration aims to equip workers with the necessary knowledge and skills to become more self-reliant in managing their financial affairs and plan for their longer term needs.
