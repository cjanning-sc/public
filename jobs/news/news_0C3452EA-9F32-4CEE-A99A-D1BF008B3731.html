In response to a letter in The Straits Times calling on MAS to impose regulations pertaining to sales practices for investment products sold by banks to consumers, specifically by having a cooling-off period, MAS said this is covered by existing rules on sales practices under the Financial Advisers Act (FAA). MAS added that it has and will take supervisory actions against financial institutions and their representatives who fail to comply with these rules.
