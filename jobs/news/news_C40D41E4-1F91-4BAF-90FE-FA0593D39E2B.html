MAS Launches The 2009 Heritage Orchids of Singapore Coin Set
C40D41E4-1F91-4BAF-90FE-FA0593D39E2B
The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) today launched the 2009 Heritage Orchids of Singapore Coin Set. This is the fourth set of coins issued in tribute to one of Singapore’s national treasures. Orchids are a significant feature of Singapore’s heritage and the orchid coin series have proven to be popular with coin collectors.
