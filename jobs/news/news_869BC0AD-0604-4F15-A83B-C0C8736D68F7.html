Central Bankers and Banking Supervisors Discuss Practical Application of Basel II

The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS), in its capacity as Chair of the EMEAP1 Working Group on Banking Supervision, and the Financial Stability Institute (FSI) jointly hosted a meeting on the Practical Application of Basel II. The meeting took place on 5 and 6 July 2004, in Singapore.
