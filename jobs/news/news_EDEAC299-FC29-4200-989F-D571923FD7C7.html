Singapore to Chair Asean + 3 Finance Ministers Meeting in Jeju, Korea

The Finance Ministers of ASEAN, China, Japan and the Republic of Korea (ASEAN + 3) will meet in Jeju, Korea on 15 May 2004. This is the first time that Singapore is chairing this event since the ASEAN + 3 Finance Ministers process was officially established in 2000.
