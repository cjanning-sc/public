Securities Industry Council Issues Consultation Paper on Revision of the Singapore Code on Take-Overs and Mergers
A7A45314-BFD9-4F98-96BB-774D253184FD
The Securities Industry Council ("SIC" or the "Council") today issued a consultation paper on proposed revisions to the Singapore Code on Take-overs and Mergers (the "Singapore Code") to keep pace with market innovations and international practices. SIC invites the public to give their views and comments on the consultation paper. A copy of the consultation paper is available on the MAS website.
