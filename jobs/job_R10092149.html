Northrop Grumman Defense Systems (NGDS) is seeking an Sr. Principal Configuration Analyst. This position will be located in Orlando, FL. NGDS is an industry leader in developing, integrating, and sustaining complex systems for the US Department of Defense. Our Team performs a variety of tasks leading to the successful integration, test, and deployment of complex DoD systems worldwide.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • In this position, the configuration analyst plans and coordinates preparation of project documentation, such as engineering drawings and technical plans and procedures to ensure customer contract requirements are met. 
  • Reviews contract to determine documentation required for each phase of project, applying knowledge of engineering and manufacturing processes.
  • Maintains CDRL documentation records and provides reporting and metrics to Management and Mission Assurance.
  • Collaborates with the team in the review and analysis of released engineering change data and coordinates changes with engineering, quality, mission assurance, and engineering data control activities.
  • Maintains the as-built configuration records for hardware and software.  
  • Maintains the CM repositories. 
  • Assists with development of configuration documentation.

Job Duties include:

  • Leading the Software Configuration Management (SWCM) team performing configuration identification, change control, auditing and reporting, and the receiving and delivery (both internally and externally) of developed software products, including commercial off-the-shelf (COTS) and government off-the-shelf (GOTS) software. 
  • Establishing and maintaining product baselines
  • Maintaining the CM repositories
  • Managing change records and facilitates ERB and CCB meetings
  • Maintaining the as-built configuration records
  • Supporting and collaborating with the Software lead, Lab Operations, and QA teams by appropriately controlling (both internally and externally developed) software and associated documentation as products go through the software development cycle.  
  • Supporting Test Readiness Reviews, and CDRL releases and deliveries. 
  • Collecting and analyzing CM metrics to identify areas for process improvement and to measure throughput and team and individual performance.
  • Establishing a culture of innovation and automation & propagating this vision to other teams
  • Contributing to the development of software configuration standards for company-wide use.  
  • Training software developers in the use of configuration management tools and the implementation of software quality standards.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree with a minimum of 10 years of experience in hardware and software configuration/data management experience or OR Master’s Degree and 8 years of experience in hardware and software configuration/data management experience
  • Ability to obtain and maintain Secret clearance
  • Ability to perform and complete tasks with minimal or no supervision.
  • Demonstrated knowledge of CM principles, concepts, practices, and standards.
  • Experience working within defined engineering & development processes
  • Willing to work extended hours occasionally to meet deadlines.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Previous experience in DoD programs.
  • Previous experience with Six Sigma process improvement techniques.
  • Experience with International standards organizations bodies (i.e. IEEE, SISO, AS9100).
  • Experience with Agile development
  • Experience with the Atlassian Tool Suite
  • Proficiency in Microsoft office products (Word, Excel, PowerPoint etc.)
  • Experience with SW release and Build process
  • Experience with commercial hardware and software configuration management products
  • Experience with SQL queries
  • Experience with SharePoint
