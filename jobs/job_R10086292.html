  • Provide support for day-to-day operations using Agile practices, and adhere to Agile methodology to enforce Scrum compliance among the teams.
  • Act as an Agile coach and mentor to other scrum masters, Product Owners, and team members to impart or reinforce knowledge of Agile concepts, tools and practices.
  • Facilitate sprint ceremonies, such as Sprint Planning, Review, Retrospective, Daily Scrums, and Backlog Refinement.
  • Work with team leads and Product Owners on schedule and Program Increment planning to ensure user stories and sprints align with deadlines.

  • Coordinate with integrated product teams for establishing content within the architecture.
  • Interface with customers, program management and technical staff.
