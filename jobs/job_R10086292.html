Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Systems Engineer or Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package. This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

What you’ll get to do: 

  • Provide support for day-to-day operations using Agile practices, and adhere to Agile methodology to enforce Scrum compliance among the teams.
  • Act as an Agile coach and mentor to other scrum masters, Product Owners, and team members to impart or reinforce knowledge of Agile concepts, tools and practices.
  • Facilitate sprint ceremonies, such as Sprint Planning, Review, Retrospective, Daily Scrums, and Backlog Refinement.
  • Work with team leads and Product Owners on schedule and Program Increment planning to ensure user stories and sprints align with deadlines.
  • Use Agile tools such as Atlassian Jira to aid in Scrum coordination.
  • Aid in providing additional Agile metrics, including collating and deriving insights from data.
  • Support systems engineering activities including use case development, requirements definition/allocation, functional decomposition, interface definition, verification & validation, and requirements traceability to the test plans
  • Coordinate with integrated product teams for establishing content within the architecture.
  • Interface with customers, program management and technical staff.
