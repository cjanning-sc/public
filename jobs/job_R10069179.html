ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Manager of General Manufacturing 1 - Front Line Manager to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Cincinnati, OH. Candidates must be willing to work any of the following schedules: Monday - Thursday - 1st shift (5:00 am - 3:30 pm) OR 2nd shift (4:12 pm - 2:42 am).

The General Manufacturing Manager - Front Line Manager has the responsibility, authority, and accountability for leading the manufacturing activities of a given cost center including safety, quality, cost and schedule objectives and factory accountability along with other duties.

Essential Functions:

  • Providing direction and coaching to technicians and other production personnel in the assembly and manufacturing areas.

  • Ensures team members receive the required training and prepares the daily work plans in support of overall program objectives.

  • Encourages and provides direction for career development of employees, including educational options for highly motivated employees.

  • Provides leadership/direction to ensure all affordability initiatives and opportunities for process improvements are systematically identified, pursued, and supported.

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.
