Aeronautics Systems sector is seeking a Designer 3 – Packaging Specialist to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is located in Rancho Bernardo, CA.

  • 1st shift, 4/10s schedule Monday – Thursday, with EVERY Friday off!
  • Position is onsite, unable to accommodate a telecommute/hybrid schedule.

​Essential Functions:

  • Design components or portions of packaging, material handling, storage and transportability (PHS&T) systems as well as modifying existing designs with the intent of developing or improving products to simplify manufacturing, production and logistical engineering support operations.
  • Develop packaging instructions for the shipping of military and commercial parts utilizing military and commercial packaging standards to include hazardous materials shipping.
  • Further to identify, plan, resource and acquire packaging/preservation, handling, storage and transportability (PHS&T) constraints which maximize the availability and usability of DOD assets ensuring survivability for either training or mission support requirements.
  • ﻿﻿Develop material handling and Parts Protection Specifications, Special Packaging Instructions and Coded Packaging Data
  • ﻿﻿Determine packaging specifications according to the nature of the product, cost limitations, legal requirements, and the type of protection required, considering need for resistance to such external variables as moisture, corrosive chemicals, temperature variations, light, heat, rough handling, and tampering
  • ﻿﻿Support the Package Engineer in the development of supplier packaging requirements
  • ﻿﻿Contribute to the completion of milestones associated with specific projects
  • ﻿﻿Function as a Liaison primarily to internal company contacts, (inter-organizational teams) and occasionally with outside customer contacts on routine matters

Basic Qualifications:

  • ﻿﻿High School Diploma or equivalent and at least 4 years of additional education and/or related experience
  • ﻿﻿Military and/or commercial packing/shipping and handling/hazmat experience
  • ﻿﻿Experience creating documents, reports, spreadsheets and slides to present data and requirements using Word, Excel and PowerPoint
  • ﻿﻿Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret level security clearance

Preferred Qualifications

  • Active DoD Secret level clearance
  • CFR 49 Certification
