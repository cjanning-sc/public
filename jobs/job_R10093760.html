ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for an Associate Project Management/Project Management for Global Operations (OPM) for the Manned Aircraft supporting our St Augustine, Pennsylvania site.

This is an individual contributor, non-manager role with no direct reports.

Job Responsibilities:

In this analyst role, the candidate will collaborate closely with Engineering and other key functional disciplines towards the goal of efficiently assessing all change management vehicles, from inception to completion.

The candidate shall plan, prepare, issue and control production schedules and coordinate with Engineering, Manufacturing and Supply Chain to ensure a controlled flow of approved materials timed to meet production requirements. In addition, candidate shall negotiate engineering drawing release schedules and manage production change incorporation plans to effectively mitigate assembly disruption. The candidate shall advise management of the status of work in progress, material availability and potential production constraints to ensure that personnel, equipment, materials, and services are provided to support master production schedule, shop load and inventory requirements.

 

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, and collaborative/team settings across all levels.

Basic Qualifications for Level 1:

  • Bachelor’s Degree with 1 years of experience in project management or related
  • Ability to obtain and maintain DOD Secret Security Clearance within a reasonable amount of time as required by the business
  • Experience with Microsoft Suite (Power Point, Project, Excel, etc.).

Basic Qualifications for Level 2:

  • Bachelors with 3 years of experience in project management or related or Masters and 1 year
  • Ability to obtain and maintain DOD Secret Security Clearance within a reasonable amount of time as required by the business
  • Experience with Microsoft Suite (Power Point, Project, Excel, etc.).

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active, In-Scope, DOD Secret Security Clearance with Program Access
  • Prior experience working with complex databases SAP, Oracle, SQL relational etc
  • Experience in or knowledge of a manufacturing environment
  • Experience working with cross-functional teams in a manufacturing or business environment
  • At least 1 year of demonstrated experience leading project teams
  • Experience with shop floor control systems
  • Experience/certifications in lean/process improvement
  • Experience with manpower planning.
  • Experience interfacing with suppliers and leadership.
  • Experience with schedule creation and briefing
  • Experience using a data analytics visualization software package (e.g., Tableau, Power BI, Looker, Lumira).
