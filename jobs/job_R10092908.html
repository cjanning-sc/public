SSSD has an opening for a Database Architect (Level 2) or a Principal Data Base Architect (Level 3) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located at our Space Park facility in Redondo Beach, California.

The Database Architect or Principal Data Base Architect will report to the Manager of Program Control under Business Management to Design, model, document and guide the logical and conceptual relationship of data and database changes for complex applications.  This role also requires the incumbent to analyze needs and requirements of existing and proposed systems, and develops technical, structural, and organizational specifications and may create standards and/or do modeling to monitor and enhance capacity and performance.

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Design, develop, and maintain a scalable ETL pipeline.
  • Design, model, document, and guide the logical and conceptual relationship of data and database changes for complex applications
  • Analyze needs and requirements of existing and proposed systems
  • Develop technical, structural, and organizational specifications
  • Create standards and/or do modeling to monitor and enhance capacity and performance
  • Design for relational and columnar databases such as SQL Server
  • Develop data specifications (technical, structure and organization);
    Planning, Programming, Budgeting and/or Execution DoD processes

This position requires the incumbent to possess a Department of Defense (DoD) active Top Secret (TS) security clearance or access to Special Compartmentalized Information (SCI).

THIS POSITION CAN BE FILLED AT EITHER A LEVEL 2 OR LEVEL 3.  SEE QUALIFICATIONS OUTLINED BELOW FOR EACH LEVEL.   

Basic Qualifications – (Level 2):

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematic (STEM) field with 2+ years of experience in database development, administration, integration of data and data visualization tools and framework OR Master’s Degree with 0 years of stated experience
  • Active Top Secret (TS) security clearance or access to Special Compartmentalized Information (SCI)

Basic Qualifications – (Level 3):

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematic (STEM) field with 5+ years of experience in database development, administration, integration of data and data visualization tools and framework OR Master’s Degree with 3 years of stated experience
  • Active Top Secret (TS) security clearance or access to Special Compartmentalized Information (SCI)

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Experienced with SQL Server
  • Knowledge of MPM and/or COBRA
  • Special Program Access
