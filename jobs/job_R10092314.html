Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Sector Strategic Sourcing Manager 1 or Manager 2 to join our Sector Strategic Sourcing Team. This position can be located in Baltimore, MD, Dulles, VA, Roy, UT, Redondo Beach, CA or Chandler, AZ. PT remote work may be available.

Position Summary/Responsibilities:

The Sector Strategic Sourcing Manager role within the Sector Strategic Sourcing organization is a collaborative role responsible for the development and implementation of advanced supply chain strategies in support of technology maturation, operational deployment and program performance.  As a GSC leadership position, the role will focus on developing and managing tools and systems to provide supplier and category intelligence, supply risk management, strategic and collaborative agreements, make/buy strategies and overall project management.  This position will interface with all NGSP organizations as well as provide engagement opportunities with enterprise sectors, corporate staff and senior level leadership.

Responsibilities include:

  • Facilitate and lead GSC Sector Risk Board, including risk reporting to Sector and Corporate management

  • In collaboration with Divisions and Small Business, develop methods to increase small business utilization in support of Sector objectives

  • Utilize data and intelligence to develop and implement category management strategies in support of sector growth

  • Develop and implement commodity management and distribution strategies

  • Identify, develop and implement strategic collaboration agreements in support of sector priorities

  • Lead projects to frame, develop and deliver solutions to meet the sector’s long term strategic goals.

  • Support special projects within Sector Strategic Sourcing Team, as needed

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelors' Degree

  • US Citizenship and ability to obtain government security clearance

  • Minimum of 6 years demonstrated success/problem solving skills in Supply Chain, Operations, Logistics, Engineering or Program Management; 4 years with a Master’s for a MGR level 1

  • Minimum of 8 years demonstrated success/problem solving skills in Supply Chain, Operations, Logistics, Engineering or Program Management; 6 years with a Master’s for a MGR level 2

  • Demonstrated working knowledge of data analytic applications and techniques and methods for strategy development and deployment.

  • Understanding and experience with Supply Chain policies, process, tools, regulations and protocols including capacity planning, materials forecasting and make/buy analysis

  • Demonstrated working knowledge of risk and opportunity management

  • Proficiency in MS Office Suite

  • Demonstrated leadership success and ability to influence and interact with peers and all levels of management in the organization

  • Highly effective interpersonal skills, commitment to fostering an engaging environment for others and excellent communication skills

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with sourcing activities, including source selection, high-level capacity and capability analysis and alternate sourcing

  • Demonstrated experience with developing complex agreements such as long term agreements, teaming agreements, strategic alliances, MOA's, overriding Terms and Conditions

  • Experience with the use of agile methods for project planning and execution.

  • Experience with and a working knowledge of Space Systems product lines and technology building blocks.
