ACME Engineering has an opening for a Manager 3- Project Management to support enterprise level program management within our Global Surveillance team to support a wide range of program missions. The position will be based in our Cleveland offices and offer limited opportunity for telework or remote work at this time to support team and program needs.

The Manager 3- Project Management will oversee and manage the operational aspects of ongoing projects and serves as liaison between project management and planning, project team, and line management. Reviews status of projects and budgets; manages schedules and prepares status reports. Assesses project issues and develops resolutions to meet productivity, quality, and client-satisfaction goals and objectives. Develops mechanisms for monitoring project progress and for intervention and problem solving with project managers, line managers, and clients.

Additional Responsibilities:

  • Provides leadership to program integration staff through coaching, mentoring, and other staff development approaches.

  • Provides strategies and direction for operations and program management areas within the Triton Enterprise Program

  • Provides program management support for all phases of the program from inception through completion including cost, schedule, and technical performance of each task.

  • Delivers presentations to customers, executive management, and other program stakeholders.

  • Establishes milestones and monitors adherence to master plans and schedules, identifies program problems and obtain solutions, such as allocation of resources or changing contractual specifications.

  • Leads enterprise resource planning and resource review boards.

  • Manages and acts as primary point of contract for facility liaison activity across Triton program buildings.

  • Leads Triton enterprise recognition program.

  • Provides leadership and ownership of Enterprise Risk and Opportunity management plan.

  • Act as the customer contact for program activities while cultivating customer relationships and intimacy to develop opportunities.

  • Manages and provides input for Sector Intranet Program Reporting System (IPRS) reporting data on a monthly basis.

The ideal candidate will have an extensive project management background in a technical environment ; solid organizational skills; an ability to lead program teams to achieve program milestones and delivery objectives; program planning and scheduling skills; solid communication skills; and experience with earned value management.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have a bachelor’s degree AND 9 years of related professional/military experience with a background in project/program management, OR a master’s degree AND 7 years of related professional/military experience with a background in project/program management.
  • Personnel management and development experience with demonstrated success leading and growing teams/and or organizations.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s in business administration or Finance
  • Experience managing program/projects with P&L responsibilities
  • Experience with Program Operations, Data Analytics, Infrastructure & Facilities
  • Experience with CAM role
  • Experience with EVMS
  • Certified by Project Management Institute as a Project Management Professional (PMP)
  • Demonstrated experience meeting customer needs, building strong relationships with strategic partners and guiding program teams and support functions to take action to satisfy customer expectations
  • Experience managing and writing contracts
  • Active DoD Secret clearance that is in-scope (granted or renewed in the last 5 years)
