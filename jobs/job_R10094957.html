ACME Engineering Mission Systems is seeking a Thermal Mechanical Engineer to join its Thermal Analysis team within the Hardware Organization. The Thermal Engineer will conduct thermal analysis and design of avionics and space electronics with an emphasis on design improvement. The engineer will be responsible for the entire system, sub-system, including microelectronics component level design and analysis.

Responsibilities Include:

  • Conduct advanced thermal management using heat transfer principles to meet the optimal thermal performance
  • Perform thermal analysis that require experience in heat transfer, including liquid cooled, air-cooled, two-phase flow, cryogenic cooling and refrigeration thermal control for different platform systems such as airborne, space, ground base and naval
  • Provide oral and written presentations to internal and external customers as well as mentor less experienced thermal engineers.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering or Aerospace Engineering with 2+ years of related experience or 0 years with a Master's degree in mechanical engineering or Aerospace Engineering
  • Thermal engineering experience, to include experience in heat transfer, liquid cooled, air-cooled, and thermal control systems for different platform systems
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
  • Be able to be effective in a team environment
  • Strong hands-on work ethic
  • Ability to obtain a Secret Clearance, U.S. Citizenship is a pre-requisite

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering or Master’s degree in Aerospace Engineering with concentration in Heat Transfer and Fluid Dynamics
  • Knowledge of thermal management techniques such as Finite Volume Analysis, Finite Element Analysis and Computational Fluid Dynamics.
  • Prior avionics experience in thermal management design.
