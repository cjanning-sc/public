Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Manager Systems Engineering to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in San Diego, CA.

   

The selected candidate will work closely with the RQ-4 Program Team across the Enterprise in various engineering functions to ensure the overall system meets all performance requirements and manage the Technical Baseline across RQ-4 programs.

Essential Functions:

  • Ensure successful requirements definition, decomposition, allocation, and final verification across a complex system.
  • Manage the technical baseline of multiple RQ-4 variants at different levels of technical maturity, ensuring successful milestone reviews (e.g. SRR, SFR, PDR, CDR, FCA).
  • Tailor Requirements and Verification processes to ensure appropriate rigor is applied to a program’s size/scope/complexity.
  • Lead a multidisciplinary team to decompose and define customer CONOPs, Performance Specifications, and Use Cases/OpsCon scenarios into robust, verifiable requirements at the System and Segment Level.
  • Define and maintain standard R&V products and processes, ensuring appropriate usage across all programs.
  • Define and maintain standard MBSE products and processes, ensuring appropriate tailoring and usage across all programs.
  • Work with RQ-4 SE&I Lead to develop and implement long term strategies for improving requirements management and use of MBSE.
  • Utilize Agile methodologies which will enable rapid capability insertion and reduce time to field Implement tools, technologies, and procedure to enable more efficient integration. 
  • Manage and lead R&V and MBSE teams across all variants within the RQ-4 Enterprise.  
  • Oversee appropriate implementation of below-Segment level requirements (i.e. Subsystem/Element or Unit HW and SW) within other IPTs
  • Presentation skills to effectively provide status to internal executive management and customers.

Basic Qualifications:

  • BS degree in engineering or science and 9 years of related experience or 7 years with a Masters.
  • DOORS experience with an emphasis on requirements management and metrics generation.
  • Previous Experience leading Milestone Reviews (e.g. SRR, PDR, CDR, FCA)
  • Previous Experience in managing Technical Baselines (Functional and Allocated Baselines)
  • Previous experience in defining and developing system architectures in and MBSE architecture tool.
  • Active DoD Secret Clearance.
  • Experience leading a team of 10+ individuals in a technical environment.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • MS degree in a STEM Field
  • Experience with interfaces ICDs
  • Experience in developing proposals for R&V and MBSE efforts
  • Requirements verification and validation experience including generating Requirements Verification Plans (RVPs), generating Requirements Verification Reports (RVRs), verification sell-off.  Preferred Functional Configuration Audit (FCA) experience.
  • MBSE experience and Use Case generation per SYSML/UML standards with an understanding of OV-1 down to SV-4 diagrams. DODAF architecture knowledge.
  • Experience as Segment-Level Systems Engineer
  • Experience managing suppliers
  • Experience in Agile Development
  • Experience in Autonomous Systems Programs
  • Experience working on US Air Force Programs
  • Experience working on FMS or Export controlled Programs
  • Active TS/SCI.
