ACME Engineering Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) has an opening for a Principal Structural Design Engineer/Sr. Principal Structural Design Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Columbus, OH.
 

The Structural Design Engineer will be responsible for engineering the preliminary, conceptual, and detailed design of Spacecraft components, sub-systems, and systems, including the creation of detailed simulation models and drawing release, and the coordination of manufacturing, integration, test, and delivery of the space vehicle structures.
 

Additional responsibilities include (but are not limited to):

  • Lead, mentor, and train a team of engineers
  • Provide technical oversight
  • Responsible for cost and schedule associated to SOW
  • Collaborate with IPTL in the development of the integration schedule
  • Resolve system-level issues and constraints, such as stress, structural integrity, thermal, dynamics, and critical clearances
  • Perform requirements definition, conceptual design, preliminary / layout, and detail design, drawing release, design review presentations, and support to fabrication/integration
  • Perform bus equipment layout, payload layout, preliminary cable and harness routing (DC, RF, Waveguide)
  • Work closely with stress, manufacturing, M&P, and test to develop design requirements, define structural load paths, develop CAD models and prepare engineering drawings to fabricate flight and structural test articles.
  • Interface with other cross-functional groups, customers, and vendors
  • Provide I&T Sustaining support
  • Responsible for source control drawings, interface control drawings, & installation drawings.
  • Lead and participate in system-level trades to ensure the vehicle design meets all physical, mechanical, and environmental requirements while accommodating the required payload, power, propulsion, thermal, communications, and attitude control system requirements
  • Supports Program milestone reviews (SRR, PDR, CDR, IDR, etc.) and Gate Closure/Lien Closures
  • Create a culture of trust
     

This requisition may be filled at either a Principal Structural Design Engineer level or a Sr. Principal Structural Design Engineer level based on the qualifications listed below.
 

Basic Qualifications for Principal Structural Design Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university with 5 years of engineering experience OR Master’s Degree in STEM with 3 years of engineering experience OR Ph.D. in STEM with 0 years of engineering experience
  • Prior work experience in space subsystems design, mechanical design integration, or related engineering experience.
  • Proficiency in a CAD system.
  • Candidate must currently have, and be able to maintain, a Department of Defense Top Secret clearance OR SCI access with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI)
     

Basic Qualifications for Sr. Principal Structural Design Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university with 9 years of engineering experience OR Master’s Degree in STEM with 7 years of engineering experience OR Ph.D. in STEM with 4 years of engineering experience
  • Prior work experience in space subsystems design, mechanical design integration, or related engineering experience.
  • Proficiency in a CAD system.
  • Candidate must currently have, and be able to maintain, a Department of Defense Top Secret clearance OR SCI access with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI)
     

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with spacecraft design, integration and test
  • Active DoD Top Secret Clearance with current Polygraph OR SCI with current Polygraph
  • Proficiency in CATIA V5 and/or CATIA 3DX
  • Master’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering or Aerospace Engineering
  • EVMS/CAM, and Project Management experience
