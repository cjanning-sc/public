ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal Supply Chain Subcontracts Specialist (level 3)  to join our team of qualified, Diverse individuals in Louisville, Kentucky.

Job responsibilities may include but are not limited to:

  • Sources and procures specialized goods and services.
  • Owns contractual management for the supplier.
  • Responsible for source selection from contract proposal to execution phases.
  • Coordinates and collaborates with key stakeholders such as contracts, engineering, quality, supplier development, planning, operations, program and end-users.
  • Develops subcontract specifications, work statements, and terms and conditions for the procurement of specialized materials, equipment, and/or services.
  • Prepares bid packages, conducts bidders conferences, analyzes and evaluates proposals, negotiates subcontract provisions, selects or recommends subcontractors, writes awards, and administers resulting subcontracts.
  • In conjunction with quality organization, evaluates and monitors supplier quality and reliability, as well as supplier ability to support required delivery schedules. Identifies strategies and opportunities to reduce cost, improve efficiency and manage risk through strategic and targeted negotiations.
  • Manages performance of subcontracts, ensures compliance with all public law requirements as well as company (and business) policies and procedures including import/export regulations.
  • Employs sound business practices and assesses supplier performance as part of follow-up activities. Ensures that appropriate documentation is provided to logistics/transportation & warehouse organization so that proper receipt is recorded. Manages and develops strategic/key suppliers.
  • Reviews and approves supplier payment as needed.
  • Utilizes process tools such as Lean Six Sigma with suppliers to drive quality, efficiency and continuous improvement.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree with at least 6 years experience, or a Master’s Degree with at least 4 years experience or High School Diploma/equivalent with at least 10 years of   combined experience in Subcontracts, Procurement and/or Supplier Management
  • Experience with Subcontracts, Procurement and/or Contracts
  • Experience with SAP or related business application software
  • Experience with FAR (Federal Acquisition Regulations) and DFARS
  • Ability to obtain DoD Secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s Degree in Business, Global Supply Chain or Project Management
  • Experience with management of complex subcontracts.
  • Experience issuing and managing purchase orders/subcontracts from beginning to end of life cycle.
