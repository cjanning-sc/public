ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems is looking for an Electrical Engineer to support the Circuit Design organization in Cleveland, CA.


As a member of the circuit design team, you will be responsible for circuit development for the program enterprise. You will perform detailed 
electrical circuit design, circuit level analysis, and LabView Design. You will support qualification testing by preparing test procedures and reports. 
Finally, you will support hardware production manufacturing.

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Bachelors in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math) discipline with 2 years engineering related experience, OR Master’s in STEM with 0 years of experience
  • Experience with schematic capture and board layout using Altium Designer
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Secret Clearance


Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Active Secret DoD Clearance 
  • Degree in Electrical Engineering with a focus on mixed circuit design
  • Experience with OrCAD, Pspice, or LTspice for circuit simulation
  • Experience with IPC and Military standards, and ACME Engineering Circuit Card Design specifications
  • Statement of work includes detailed electrical circuit design, circuit level analysis, support qualification testing, preparing of test procedures and reports, and supporting hardware production manufacturing.
