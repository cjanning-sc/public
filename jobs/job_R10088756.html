ACME Engineering Mission Systems is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security. Our organization pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the spectrum of Electronic Warfare providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter.  Come be a part of our mission!

ACME Engineering Mission Systems (NGMS) seeks an RF Electronics Engineering Manager 1 or RF Electronics Engineering Manager 2 in the Amherst Systems business unit within the Navigation, Targeting, and Survivability division.  This position is located in Buffalo, NY. This is a dual-hat position responsible for both personnel management (~ 50% of time) for a multi-disciplined team of 10 engineers and technical/program responsibilities (~50% of time). This position is located in Buffalo, NY.

The successful candidate will clearly demonstrate the skillset of a LEADER. He/she will have a track record as an effective communicator and problem solver who is able to develop and maintain good working relationships with internal and external stakeholders to achieve sustainable top performance. This candidate will demonstrate a commitment to ethics and integrity, and the capability to innovate while challenging traditional ways of doing business.  Demonstrated abilities to build diverse and inclusive teams with high levels of engagement, including mentoring and coaching, are a must.

 The RF Electronics Engineering organization located in Buffalo, NY researches, designs, builds, and tests a variety of electronic components and systems used in the development, test, validation, and training of Electronic Warfare hardware, software, and personnel to ensure the safety and success of the warfighter.

 The current organization consist of 10 RF Engineers ranging from entry level through highly experienced staff. In addition to managing the team, the Manager will be required to support and as needed design and implement specialized test equipment used in the testing of Military Radar, Radar Warning Receivers (RWR), and Jamming systems.

The team with manger support will design and implement integrated microwave assemblies or sub-components utilizing transmission line, MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit), and packaged RF devices.

 What You'll Get To Do:

The team with manager assistance will be required to review and derive requirements, develop block diagrams, and perform simulation utilizing software tools such as HFSS, GENESYS, and ADS (Advanced Design Systems). The team with manger support will also be required to brass-board the design and make adjustments. Following successful design reviews, the team will formally document the design for production, support procurement/production, and integrate and test the final product.

The manager will be required to communicate with internal customers as well as external customers and suppliers. In addition, he/she must be able to support his/her team with writing test procedures and supporting technicians during the testing of sub components and finished product. He/she must be able to work as part of a multifunctional team working within schedule and budget constraints.

  Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Technical contributor / leader in a program role
  • Functional oversight for a team of RF Engineers
  • Staffing and workload forecast; employee inclusion, engagement, mentoring and career development; identification and training of employee skills required to successfully execute on programs.
  • Execution of employee performance review processes and performance improvement plans.
  • Recruiting, sourcing, onboarding, performance management and contract maintenance for contractors working in the business area as required.
  • Issue identification/reporting as well as status reporting up the engineering and program management chains.
  • Engage in the preparation of department-related meetings, proposal work statements and operating budgets.
