ACME Engineering Aeronautics System Sector is seeking a Manager of Process Improvement 3 to join our team of qualified, diverse, individuals in the Aerospace Structures Business Unit (ASBU). This position will be located primarily in Lansing, Michigan, with expected travel up to 25%.

This manager is accountable for the strategic planning and integration of Production Operations and Manufacturing Leadership objectives, as defined by the ASBU Director of Operations. This role will lead and direct a cross-functional operations team adhering to manufacturing safety, quality, schedule delivery, and cost.  The selected candidate will collaborate effectively and partner across a diverse set of stakeholders and organizations to develop, implement, measure, communicate, and oversee manufacturing operation’s strategic execution.

Roles and responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Working side by side with Operations Directors/VPs by developing tools to help them achieve all business targets
  • Creation and management of the Industrial Engineering department at ASBU’s geographically diverse facilities across the country
  • Sourcing key engineering and technical talent to ensure success of the Business Unit goals
  • Oversight of the team accountable for integration and utilization of common facilities, capacity analysis, integration of resources and implementation of improvement initiatives across the Business Unit
  • Working in concert with other functional homerooms and shared service organizations to coordinate, direct, and support the continuous improvement of people, products, processes, systems and services
  • Oversight of projects and tasks assigned to the cross functional ASBU organization and providing direction to ensure quality, schedule and cost are achieved
  • Providing direct supervision and management of employees from technical, manufacturing, and administrative areas to ensure business targets are met
  • Creating long range planning & strategies for the operations team (eg: staffing forecasting, rate change planning, execution strategy of new initiatives, pull ahead strategies, line balancing assessments, EAC / Budget forecasting)
  • Partnering with site leadership to develop executive planning and strategic vision that help achieve sector goals & objectives

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree with 9 years of experience in high rate production environments OR a Master’s Degree and 7 years of experience in high rate production environments
  • At least 2 years of experience directly managing employees and supervisors
  • Experience leading Continuous Improvement initiatives resulting in cost savings/cost avoidance greater than $5M (results can be calculated in schedule avoidance)
  • Experience effectively developing business cases, briefings and communication – both written and verbal – at a senior leadership level to inform and drive decisions
  • Business Management, Earned Value Management and EAC experience
  • Industrial Engineering, Master Scheduling, and Shop Floor Control experience
  • Digital Production Management (DPM) systems experience managing shop-integrated data visibility metrics and data scientists/data analysts (Tableau, Business Objects, PowerBi, etc.)
  • The ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Security Clearance and Special Program Access (PAR) within a reasonable period of time as determined by the company to meet its business need 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD Secret Security Clearance
  • Experience in an aerospace environment
  • Experience as a Master Production Scheduler, accountable for developing Shop Operating Plans (SOPs)
  • Demonstrated work experience leading and building diverse teams in fast-paced, quality-centric environments
  • 4 years of leadership experience; at least 1 year experience in Shop Floor Operations
  • Bachelor of Science Degree with 8 years of experience in Industrial Engineering in aerospace high production environments
  • Experience with multiple program environments utilizing common infrastructure and facilities
  • Proven experience in composite lay-up and cure processes, testing, machining methods, precision bonding and assembly processes
  • Experience and expertise managing Shop Floor Control (SFC) systems and MES/SAP environments within aerospace for both fabrication and final assembly
  • Experience with Project Management and the execution of large scale, integrated projects across multiple functions and organizations
  • Experience working on a DoD/PAR Manufacturing program
