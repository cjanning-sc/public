1st Shift (United States of America)
Northrop Grumman Tactical Space Systems Division designs, builds and delivers space, defense and aviation-related systems to customers around the world. Our main products include launch vehicles and related propulsion systems; missile products, subsystems and defense electronics; precision weapons, armament systems and ammunition; satellites and associated space components and services; and advanced aerospace structures.

The Tactical Space Systems Division is seeking an experienced Mechanical Engineer to assist a team of mechanical engineers to perform mechanical, systems, mechanisms, and design engineering tasks on one or more spacecraft systems or subsystems.

Responsibilities may include the following:

  • Work on Spacecraft Structures, Payload, Mechanisms, and Antenna mechanical hardware from requirements all the way through integration and test. 

  • Perform detailed design tasks using Computer Aided Design (CAD) and other software tools to develop and document designs for manufacturing and ultimately assembly into spacecraft. 

  • Interfacing and communicating with customers, program and functional management, other spacecraft engineering disciplines, and component suppliers. 

  • Develop test plans and procedures and support structural testing at the Mechanisms component and spacecraft levels.

  • Perform cost and schedule planning for projects and proposals. 

  • Expected to be self-motivated and provide technical solutions to a wide range of difficult problems under limited.

  • Skills include, but not limited to, Computer Aided Design (CAD), knowledge of Geometric Dimension and Tolerancing (GD&T), 3D Printing, Design for Manufacturing, and Mechanics of Metallic and Composite Materials.

This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

Basic Qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either a Principal Mechanical/Mechanisms Engineer 3 or Sr. Principal Mechanical/Mechanisms Engineer 4.

Basic Qualifications: Principal Mechanical/Mechanisms Engineer 3

  • Bachelor’s Degree in STEM - Science, Technology, Engineering, Math or related area (Degrees in Aerospace or Mechanical Engineering highly preferred) and 5 to 9+ years of experience, Master’s Degree and 3 to 7+ years of experience; 0 years of experience with a PhD.

  • Must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain government clearance.

  • Ability to perform engineering tasks associated with design, manufacturing, and testing of space mechanisms.

  • Previous experience with the specification, layout, design, and build of moving mechanical assemblies and/or mechanism components for space.

  • Experience with Computer Aided Design (CAD) software, preferably Creo Parametric.

  • Experience with cost and schedule management.

Basic Qualifications: Sr. Principal Mechanical/Mechanisms Engineer 4:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in STEM - Science, Technology, Engineering, Math or related area (Degrees in Aerospace or Mechanical Engineering highly preferred) and 9+ years of experience, Master’s Degree and 7+ years of experience; 4+ years of experience with a PhD.

  • Must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain government clearance.

  • Ability to perform engineering tasks associated with design, manufacturing, and testing of space mechanisms.

  • Previous experience with the specification, layout, design, and build of moving mechanical assemblies and/or mechanism components for space.

  • Experience with Computer Aided Design (CAD) software, preferably Creo Parametric.

  • Experience with cost and schedule management.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T) standards and preparation of manufacturing documentation.

  • Experience with mechatronic and/or precision electromechanical devices.

  • Experience with springs, moving parts, and coatings.

  • Experience with pressure systems desired but not required.

  • Experience with PTC Creo.

  • Experience processing drawings in Dulles Windchill.

  • Demonstrated leadership experience.

  • Experience with 3D Printing, Design for Manufacturing, and hands-on hardware fabrication.

  • Proficiency with analysis and simulation tools such as MATLAB, Mathcad, LabView, VB, etc.
