The Engineering, Operations, and Programs (EOP) function within Defense Systems sector is seeking an innovative, self-starter leader who is enthusiastic about change, always looking for ways to get work done more efficiently and more effectively. This role will be in Falls Church, VA or Linthicum, MD and will offer a hybrid schedule.

The Manager Project Management role serves as the business operations face of the Sector Vice President, Engineering, Operations, and Programs and the EOP leadership team and serves on the EOP Leadership Team (EOPLT).   

The candidate must possess strong leadership abilities, excellent communications skills, and effective problem-solving capabilities.  They must be knowledgeable of Engineering, Operations, or Program Management functions, as well as have experience in any of the following: program life cycles, engineering, manufacturing, global supply chain, quality, scheduling, continuous improvement, budgeting, executing cost and schedule objectives and implementing strategic initiatives.

Key responsibilities include:

  • Manages performance, schedule and execution of ongoing projects and serve as liaison between the EOPLT and counterparts in other functions and sector/divisions to share information and provide support to benefit overall operations execution. 
  • Assesses project issues and develops resolutions to meet productivity, quality, and client-satisfaction goals and objectives.
  • Develops mechanisms for monitoring EOP goal progress and for intervention and problem solving with EOP staff and support functions. 
  • Identifies and leads functional focus areas initiatives for improvement/streamlining, development and execution of EOP initiatives driven by operational goals and sector-specific objectives; assists with initiatives that eliminate non-value-added activities at all levels in the organization.
  • Responsible for preparing for and facilitating a broad range of EOP organization meetings and events including leading and managing weekly/monthly/quarterly reviews, the senior leadership team’s biweekly staff meeting agenda, the bi-annual leadership summit, site visits, and other meetings/reviews.
  • Integrates day-to-day operational objectives to meet organizational goals.
  • Serves as a model and leader of DEI&B as a representative of the EOP Vice President and the EOPLT.

Ability and willingness to travel to the various sites within the Sector as a representative of the EOP Vice President.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree with at least 7 years of work experience in project/program management or relevant business experience and a wide variety of project types (professional services, systems engineering/integration, and software development, engineering, and operations).
  • Excellent organizational, verbal, and written communication skills, as well as project management, analytical, planning, and presentation skills
  • Demonstrated strong leadership and teamwork skills
  • Ability to travel domestically (up to 25%)
  • Must be eligible for Security Clearance (Secret)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • MS or MBA degree in a technical, business, or related field
  • Strong NG understanding and proven experience effectively working within and through NG (and/or a similar) corporate culture
  • Demonstrated ability to understand detailed program financial performance; strong problem solving and analytical skills; ability to make recommendations and provide guidance to effectively bring projects to completion
  • Demonstrated ability to support complex programs while collaborating with multiple stakeholders including EOP, human resources, financial management, strategic planning
  • The ability to balance and influence multiple competing priorities and individuals
  • Comprehensive knowledge of company and sector engineering, operations, and programs policies, procedures, and command media.
  • Minimum Security Clearance: Secret
