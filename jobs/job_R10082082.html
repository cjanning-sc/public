Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is an industry-leading provider for prime satellite and payload capabilities and directed energy and electronics solutions for national security, military, and civil customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible

ACME Engineering, Strategic Space Systems Division, has an opening for a Sr. Principal Industrial Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Columbus, Ohio.

Responsibilities:

  • Provides strong Industrial engineering leadership, mentorship, and expertise for other organizational members, cross functional teams, and projects

  • Analyzes large data sets and existing operational processes to develop metrics and reporting for optimization and process improvement of program & operational performance. 

  • Develops and implements plans that ensure the timely availability and effective utilization of labor, facilities, equipment, & tooling resources enabling the achievement of program delivery and cost milestones. 

  • Provides capacity planning, shop-loading and staffing analysis for functional production teams. 

  • Conducts studies pertaining to cost control, cost reduction, inventory control, and production record systems.

  • Develops and summarizes analyses for the acquisition, maintenance & enhancement of capital assets and equipment. 

  • May assist facilities engineers in the planning and design of facilities and layouts

  • May maintain and enhance factory simulation models and uses these models to support what if analyses and capacity planning

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics (STEM) discipline plus 9 years of engineering experience OR Master’s Degree in Science, Technology, Engineering or Math (STEM) discipline plus 7 years of engineering experience

  • Proficient in MS Office Suite

  • Experience with data visualization and dashboard creation (Tableau, Power BI)

  • Experience in using shop floor production systems (Solumina, MES, etc.)

  • Experience in using ERP systems such as SAP

  • Proven leadership experience on a team and/or multiple projects

  • Experience and knowledge of Lean Manufacturing (VSM, Kaizen) and Agile project management

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Top Secret Clearance OR SCI Access

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Capacity and scheduling simulation experience (Simio, or other simulation software)

  • Experience with data science and analytic software (Alteryx)

  • Report programming experience (SQL, Crystal, MS Query, Bus. Objects, etc.)

  • Documented experience working in a robust IPT environment

  • Experience with AutoCAD or Visio software

  • Experience performing Earned Value Management
