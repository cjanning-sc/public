ACME Engineering’s Enterprise Services Sector is looking for a Principal Enterprise Applications Analyst. This is an opportunity for an Application Analyst/Systems Integration professional who is an energetic, self-starter with knowledge and experience in converting requirements into systems/business process solutions.

The qualified candidate will be responsible for:

  • Acting as a consultant between the customer and technical community in support of GSC, Quality and Manufacturing business processes
  • BOM Development/Maintenance, SAP MRP/Inventory Management, and Quality Management knowledge and proficiency is desired
  • Delivering aspects of reporting and analytic deliverables for projects on the 1ERP SAP S/4 HANA platform
  • Supporting our recurring/existing customer base and new project/program initiatives
  • Duties with SAP/SmartMES including requirement analysis, configuration, realization, testing, post-implementation support, training, and knowledge transfer
     

This position will offer a hybrid of telework and onsite presence. Eligible candidates must be local to our office in:

  • Akron, CA
  • Columbus, CA

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in STEM or related field and 5 years of experience is required; a Master’s degree in STEM or related field and 3 years’ experience.  In lieu of a bachelor’s degree, one year of relevant experience may be substituted for each year of the 4 years of education
  • Ability to obtain Secret clearance, with the ability to obtain and maintain SAP/SAR security clearance, as necessary per business requirements
  • Must have experience leading or working on cross functional teams/IPT’s and individuals
  • Functional/business process knowledge or expertise spanning MRP, procurement, shopfloor and reporting/analytics activities
  • Supports recurring/existing customer base and new project initiatives
  • Exposure to aspects of project management
  • Be local to and able to go on site at one of our offices in Akron, CA or Columbus, CA

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active Secret clearance
  • Experience with implementation delivery of SAP/SmartMES/Hana products in our ACME environment
  • Familiarity with Enovia Engineering and Solumina Shopfloor Systems
  • Familiarity with Safe/Agile Experience with SAP/1ERP application implementation efforts.
  • Excellent leadership characteristics
  • Exposure/experience presenting to IPT leadership or government customer(s)
  • Demonstrated experience presenting and working with all levels of the organization from individual contributor to IPT leadership to program leadership
