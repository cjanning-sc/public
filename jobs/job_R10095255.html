Sr Principal Property and Asset Management Analyst (Active Secret Level Clearance REQUIRED)
R10095255
1st Shift (United States of America)

The Sr Principal Property and Asset Management Analyst will oversee, manage and analyze activities associated with asset business systems to ensure company, customer and US Government property located on site and at subcontractors are managed in accordance with applicable regulations and policies to achieve best value and operational excellence. Will maintain property accountability records and determine condition and arrange disposal of surplus or obsolete material or equipment. Will conduct internal and external property assessments and analyze results for financial and operational purposes; provide guidance on contracts and regulations pertaining to property life-cycle management; develop, recommend, and implement property management policy; and prepare periodic and special purpose reports relative to matters such as taxes, leasing, insurance, facility usage, etc.

Day-to-day responsibilities will include:

  • ongoing inventories across the Xetron Operating Unit's portfolio,

  • interaction with program management and engineering personnel to ensure proper tracking of incoming and outgoing CAP/CAM/GFE

  • maintaining program paperwork including DD1149s, DD250s and bailment agreements as needed

  • ongoing activities to ensure comprehensive tracking of inventory and data fidelity via the Asset Smart tool

This job requires regular interaction with Government customers, including DCMA, as part of normal program operations and program closeout. Will serve as the site lead for regular DCMA/USG agency property system audits and program audits, as well as the site's annual AS9100 audit.

Basic Qualifications:

- Bachelors Degree and a minimum of 10 years related work experience or 8 years with a Masters. Will consider 14 years of related experience in lieu of a degree.

- Knowledge of FAR Parts 52 and 45, industry leading practices, techniques, and standards as they pertain to Property Management.

-Active Security level clearance with the ability to obtain a TS/SCI level security clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:

- NPMA membership and Certification a plus.

- Current active TS/SCI level security clearance.

- Certified Quality Auditor or internal auditor certifications a plus.

- Experience with identifying root cause and implementing corrective actions.

-  Knowledge of the DoD’s Procurement integrated Enterprise Environment (PIEE) as it relates to Property Management is a plus.
