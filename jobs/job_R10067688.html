ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal / Sr Principal Systems Engineer, Mission Systems Integration to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

The ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems (NGAS) Systems Engineering Team (SET) Cross Segment Integration (CSI) Integrated Product Team (IPT) is seeking a Principal / Sr Principal Systems Engineer to derive and capture requirements for an innovative model-based system. The selected candidate will utilize Systems Engineering practices to provide support to the Mission Systems (MS), Air Vehicle (AV) and Product Support (PST) IPT's and act as an Embedded Systems Engineer (ESE) for cross-IPT collaboration within a Digital Engineering Environment.   As an ESE, the candidate will focus on system architecture, model interface definition, supplier model integration, Technical Data Packages (TDPs), technical integration and coordination with internal and external engineering teams, and support Program leadership regarding closure of program milestones.  The candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, and collaborative/team settings across all levels.

Essential Functions:

- Act as cross-IPT technical integrator between the SEI IPT and MSS IPT

- Support requirements allocation and interface definition support to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications.

- Participate in Allocation Control Boards (ACBs), Technical Review Boards (TRBs), and other program reviews and boards, as required

- Communicate effectively and clearly present technical approaches and findings at technical and program reviews and boards.  

- Applies extensive technical expertise in the development of technical solutions to complex problems which require the regular use of ingenuity and creativity.  

- Provide cross-IPT support for Capture efforts, SSOW/SDRL/CDRL, and trade studies as required

- Work closely with peers and managers of other engineering disciplines, program managers, and IPT leads

Basic Qualifications:

- Principal Systems Engineer: Bachelor's Degree in Science, -Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) discipline with 5 years professional/military experience OR Masters Degree in STEM discipline with 3 years professional/military experience

- Sr Principal Systems Engineer: Bachelor's Degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) discipline with 9 years professional/military experience OR Masters Degree in STEM discipline with 7 years professional/military experience

- Experience with aircraft avionics, RF sensors, EO/IR sensors, and communication systems.

- Working knowledge of Microsoft Office applications, including Word, Excel, and PowerPoint

- Must have an active DoD Secret clearance or higher (with a background investigation completed within the last 6 years or currently enrolled into Continuous Evaluation)

- Must adhere to Program security requirements while performing work

- Must be able to effectively communicate with engineers and management from multiple disciplines

Preferred Qualifications: 

- Degree in Systems Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, or Computer Engineering with MBSE experience

- Experience in the military aerospace development environment is preferred.
- Experience developing various DODAF view artifacts, especially Operational and functional viewpoints, using MBSE tools like CAMEO. 

- Strong understanding of common SE process, experience writing ICD’s

- Applies wide-ranging technical expertise and has knowledge of other related disciplines.

- Able to communicate effectively and clearly present technical approaches and findings

- Demonstrated ability to work in a diverse, multi-disciplinary team environment.

- Excellent leadership, communications, and interpersonal skills.  

- Ability to interface with all levels of the workforce and management. 

- Develop technical solutions to complex problems which require the regular use of ingenuity and guides the successful completion of major programs and may function in a project leadership role.  

- Proven experience with team engagement experience in a leadership/Team

- Perform work with no appreciable direction. Exercises considerable latitude in determining technical objectives of assignment

- Proficiency using Microsoft Office applications, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint and MS Project.
