1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman is seeking a Material estimating professional, administrative level 2/3, supporting the Maritime/Land & Systems & Sensors business.  The primary location for this position will be in Charlottesville, Va.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Prepare material estimates for proposal submittals byUnderstanding customer requirements through RFPs and other requests

  • Collaborating and coordinating with proposal technical teams in developing the material estimating strategy by interfacing with Business Management, Procurement, Supply Chain, Program Management, Program Office, and Pricing Organizations

  • Selecting appropriate pricing methods (PO History, vendor quote, etc) for each item included in the proposal

  • Ensuring compliance with Customer and  FAR Regulatory Requirements

  • Ensure schedule adherence

  • Support proposal reviews with detailed Bills of Materials and Cost Estimating Relationship (CERs) Estimates including accompanying supporting data

  • Compile costs, schedules, technical elements and hardware/sub-contractor costs in accordance with request for proposal (RFP) specifications

  • Coordinate the collection and accumulation of cost, scheduling, and performance elements from internal customers

  • Research and analyze historical data

  • Interact with other functional areas to troubleshoot issues in support of proposal requirements and deadlines

  • Develop cost models to support proposal efforts and overall pricing strategy

  • Support risk and opportunity assessment, quantification, and analysis during the proposal process

  • Present and brief material pricing, cost issues and risks to senior leadership

  • Prepare material price analysis to support business unit and GSC leaders in decision making as it pertains to specific pricing proposals

  • Support fact finding, audits, customer negotiations, and certifications while ensuring TINA compliance during all aspects of the proposal process

  • Support Pricing and Estimating Team Initiatives

  • (A02)The candidate must have a Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited institution with 3 years of professional experience in business management, finance, accounting, or related fields – OR –Master’s Degree from an accredited institution with 1 year professional experience as described above

  • (A03) The candidate must have a Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited institution with 6 years of professional experience in business management, finance, accounting, or related fields – OR –Master’s Degree from an accredited institution with 4 years professional experience as described above

  • Proficiency in MS Office Suite, including Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook - Demonstrated leadership, critical thinking and, innovative behaviors in past projects, roles, or experiences

  • The willingness and ability to travel domestically up to 10% of the time.

  • The ability to obtain and maintain a US Secret level security clearance is required

  • US citizenship is a required

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Demonstrated strong leadership skills as well as the ability to multi-task in a challenging environment

  • Professional experience in a regulated industry: to include Aerospace & Defense, Insurance, Financial Services, Telecommunications, or Legal/Regulator

  • SAP experience

  • Earned Value Management Customer Reporting experience

  • Experience Reading and Interpreting legal and contractual requirements
