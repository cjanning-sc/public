Northrop Grumman Aeronautic Systems has an immediate opening at our Palmdale, California facility for a Systems Test Engineer within the Production Test Organization. The primary job responsibilities include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Contribute to the development of acceptance test plans, test schedules, and test procedures and provide support for integration testing.
  • Support and host test planning working groups. 
  •  Interface with design and analysis functions to develop test and test data requirements.
  • Coordinate test equipment and personnel requirements for safe and efficient execution of tests.
  • Provide test execution support and make real-time decisions regarding safety, efficiency, and effectiveness.
  • Participate in post-test data evaluation and discrepancy resolution. 
  • Serve as the subject matter focal point to interface with internal and external organizations to develop test and test data requirements. 

This requisition may be filled at the higher grade depending on the requirements listed below.

  • Principal Systems Test Engineer: 5 years or related experience with Bachelor's degree in STEM ; 3 with MS.; 0 with PhD,   
  • Sr Principal Test Engineer-  9 years of related experience with a Bachelor’s Degree in STEM 7 Years with Masters in STEM;    4 years with a Ph.D. in STEM
  • Current Active Secret clearance and the ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access prior to start is required.

  • SAP program access
