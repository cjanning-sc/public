Northrop Grumman is seeking a Facilities Engineering Manager to: lead and supervise site Facilities Engineering technical team and/or outside contractors who construct, maintain, and repair utilities systems, buildings, and equipment via one or more of the following trades: electrical, plumbing, carpentry, air-conditioning, heating, painting, and/or general maintenance. The Facilities Engineering Manager will lead the execution and management of facilities projects that support both sustaining infrastructure and new program facilitation requirements in the areas of facilities equipment reliability, facilities maintenance including heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC); structural and civil engineering requirements; lighting; roofing; fire protection; and other specific structural needs. Responsible to assist in all aspects of the organization's physical infrastructure needs, coordinates with architecture/engineering firms in developing design criteria and preparing layout and detailed drawings.

Experience Requirements:

  • Interact will all levels of the organization and external representatives
  • Strong oral and written communication skills
  • Interpreting building and utility drawings and making the revisions as required
  • Developing and updating Preventive and Predictive maintenance initiatives
  • Developing short and long term corrective actions for existing and/or repeating issues
  • Developing energy conservation initiatives, cost improvements and efficiency improvements
  • Have applied knowledge of Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Microsoft Project) and AutoCAD capabilities

Basic Qualifications:

  • 9 years of facilities maintenance and engineering experience with a Bachelor’s Degree in an Engineering related field (i.e. Civil, Mechanical, Industrial or Electrical); or 5 years experience and a Master’s Degree; or 13 years exp total in Lieu of degree.
  • 3-5 years of project, program, and/or people leadership experience
  • Strong mechanical skills and exposure to a manufacturing environment
  • Basic knowledge of mechanical, electrical, and plumbing
  • A broad knowledge of environmental, health and safety regulations
  • Strong communication skills, both oral and written
  • Ability to obtain a Confidential clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Solid background in Facilities Maintenance and Repair
  • Ability to network and negotiate at all levels within an organization to accomplish objectives
  • Knowledge of a Computer Maintenance Management System, i.e., Maintenance Connection
