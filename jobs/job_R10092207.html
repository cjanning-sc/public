ACME Engineering Space Systems is currently seeking a Section Head for the Digital Integrated Circuits and Electronics (DICE) Department. This role will be based in Dayton, Ohio.

The candidate will be responsible for leading a 10-12person organization whose focus is ASIC and FPGA product development for our nation’s most critical Space Systems. As a key member of the Digital Integrated Circuits and Electronics Department leadership team, the selected candidate will oversee the day-to-day operations of the functional team including employee development & mentoring, performance management, personnel recruiting & retention, staffing, communications, technical support to programs and technology/business development efforts. - Must have the ability to obtain/maintain a DOD Secret/SCI clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

- Prior leadership experience

- Recognition as a digital hardware Subject Matter Expert

- Active DoD Secret/SCI clearance
