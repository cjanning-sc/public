ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking multiple Software Engineering Managers with the desire to learn new technologies to join the Remote Sensing Programs Software team.  The manager will be responsible for providing technical oversight of design, development, and integration of software components along with support of system integration and test. Additional responsibilities include proposal generation, software process leadership, executing performance appraisals, staffing allocations, and career development. Strong communication and people leadership skills are vital to this position.
