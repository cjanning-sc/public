Essential functions include:

Maintain a smooth operation of multi-user computer systems, including coordination with network administrators. The SE will Interact with users and evaluate vendor products. They will make recommendations to purchase hardware and software, coordinates the installation / integration of the equipment and provides backup recovery. Will develop and monitor policies and standards for allocations related to the use of computing resources. Ancillary duties may include setting up administrator and service accounts, maintaining system documentation, tuning system performance, installing system wide software and allocating mass storage space.
