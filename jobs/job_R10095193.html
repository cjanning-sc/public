ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Systems Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

This Systems Engineer will be a member of the RT&E Team in the MAS COE. The team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to design, develop, produce and sustain product lines across the IPT while providing a decisive advantage to our customer’s missions.

Essential functions include:

Maintain a smooth operation of multi-user computer systems, including coordination with network administrators. The SE will Interact with users and evaluate vendor products. They will make recommendations to purchase hardware and software, coordinates the installation / integration of the equipment and provides backup recovery. Will develop and monitor policies and standards for allocations related to the use of computing resources. Ancillary duties may include setting up administrator and service accounts, maintaining system documentation, tuning system performance, installing system wide software and allocating mass storage space.

Basic Qualifications:

• 2 years of experience with Bachelor's in Science degree; 0 years' experience with Master's degree

•  Ability to obtain and maintain DoD secret clearance

• Experience writing scripts (e.g., Batch, Bash, PowerShell) to automate routine tasks

• Experience with networked data backup and recovery solutions such as Cohesity, Backup Exec, or Networker.
• Assist in the implementation of the required government policies such as RMF (Risk Management Framework) and CMMC (Cybersecurity Maturity Mode)
