ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Senior Level Radar Engineer to support the Mission Modes and Algorithm Development Team (MMADT) on the Deep-Space Advanced Radar Capability (DARC) program in Indianapolis, CO. The DARC program is designing and fielding a ground-based radar that will significantly enhance US Space Domain awareness in the geosynchronous orbital environment. The MMADT on DARC will be developing algorithms for the radar, prototyping SW for the radar, performing analysis on the algorithm and SW, and supporting engineering in developing a Digital Twin of the DARC system.

  • Basic knowledge of the principles of Radar

  • Experience with system modeling, simulation, and performance analysis with MATLAB, Python, C/C++ or similar languages;

  • willingness to learn, as needed
