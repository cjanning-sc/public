Put your skills to the test by pushing the boundaries of what’s possible.  From global defense to sustainment and modernization to mission readiness, your experience and ability will make it a reality.  Our programs are built on equal parts of curiosity and collaboration.  Our combined effort means our customers can connect and defend millions of people around the world.  With ACME Engineering, you’ll have the opportunity to be an essential part of projects that will define your career, now and in the future.

ACME Engineering Defense Systems sector (NGDS) is seeking a Principal Intelligence Systems Analyst to join our growing team of diverse professionals supporting our III Corps Mission Command Training program at Fort Hood in Killeen, Texas. The Intelligence System Administrator maintains all Intelligence Simulations Systems for the FHTX Mission Training Complex (MTC).

Responsibilities:

  • Operates and Maintains the Intelligence Fusion Servers for the Fort Hood MTC

  • Operates the WARSIM Intelligence Module Lower and Upper Enclaves to include the Tactical Communications Support Processor and Radiant Mercury

  • Serve as the System Administrator for various computer systems

  • Operates the Virtual Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS) and Multiple Unified Simulation Environment (MUSE) simulation systems

  • Possess a working knowledge of Army Mission Command Information Systems and be able to integrate all intelligence warfighting function simulations with the Intelligence Fusion Server, which serves as the primary interface between echelon Distribution Common Ground Station - Army (DCGS-A) sub-systems, external Intelligence Fusion Server (IFS), and other Army mission command information systems

  • Provide expertise in the development, execution, and assessment of tailored and dynamic simulation stimulated training scenarios and preparatory events

Basic Qualifications:

  • One of the following:

    • High school diploma or GED with a minimum of 9 years of Computer Science (CS), Information Technology (IT), Cybersecurity & Information Assurance (IA), or related technical experience

    • Bachelor of Science degree in CS, IT, Cybersecurity & IA, or related discipline with a minimum of 5 years of CS, IT, Cybersecurity & IA, or related technical experience

    • Master of Science degree in CS, IT, Cybersecurity & IA, or related discipline with a minimum of 3 years of CS, IT, Cybersecurity & IA, or related technical experience

  • Possess a U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) Top Secret / Sensitive Compartmented Information (TS/SCI) security clearance with Special Background Investigation

  • IAT Level II Certification (e.g. CompTIA Security+ CE)

  • Experience as an IFS Field Software Engineer

  • A minimum of 3 years’ experience as a System Administrator

  • Must be able to travel to CONUS locations as needed (up to 25% of the time)

  • Must be able to work on-site at Fort Hood in Texas 

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • A minimum of 5 years’ experience in Military Intelligence/Targeting operations at the BDE/DIV/CORPS level

  • Experience operating intelligence simulation systems to include Warfighting Information System (WIM), MUSE, Intelligence Electronic Warfare Tactical Proficiency Trainer (IEWTPT), and Virtual UAS

  • A minimum of 2 years of experience in Networking
