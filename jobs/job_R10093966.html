The Integrated National Systems (INS) Business Unit is seeking a Strategy Development Director for ground support to space markets focused on the Department of Defense (DoD), Intelligence Community (IC), and Civil agencies to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals.  The position can be in Aurora/Colorado Springs, CO; Redondo Beach, CA; Baltimore MD; or Fairlakes, VA and will report to the INS Director of Strategy and Business Development within the Payload and Ground Systems Division of Northrop Grumman’s Space Sector. The primary responsibility is to lead the INS strategy development team in the creation and execution of a comprehensive strategy to capture new business opportunities and implement best practices across the BU to produce compelling capture approaches.  Programs of interest focus on DoD, IC, Civil and adjacent opportunities to include both new starts and development/expansion of existing programs. 

The selected candidate will be responsible for the development, assessment, and cultivation of near/midterm strategies for DoD, IC, and Civil agencies supporting ground systems that interface with space elements.  Working with four Mission Area Chief Engineers, the individual is responsible for implementing strategies for identifying and maturing initial capture targets, market/mission expansion, and effectively marketing the business unit’s products and capabilities to meet customer needs, thereby translating business opportunities into contact plans, strategic win themes, and supporting capture teams and wins. The individual will work and collaborate with a team of business developers, INS leadership, and other integrated stakeholders to implement win strategies, compliant with Northrop Grumman’s business acquisition process.

Employees may be eligible for a discretionary bonus in addition to base pay. Annual bonuses are designed to reward individual contributions as well as allow employees to share in company results.

Northrop Grumman Corporation is a leading global security company providing innovative systems, products, and solutions in aerospace, electronics, information systems and technical services to government and commercial customers worldwide.

The position can be in Aurora/Colorado Springs, CO; Redondo Beach, CA; Baltimore MD; or Fairlakes, VA.

 Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree, or Masters, or PhD in technical area
  • Must have a current and active Top Secret and SCI security clearance
  • A minimum of 15 years’ experience in DoD and/or IC markets
  • An understanding of U.S. Government customer requirements, acquisition processes, and organizations.
  • Candidate should have proven experience in developing strategic approaches with the objective of identifying customer needs and developing technology roadmaps to establish new opportunities for market/business expansion
  • Experience with ground system development for space-related applications

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master of Science in STEM, or MBA Degree
  • A minimum of 20 years experience in DoD and/or IC markets with focus on ground support to space programs (one or more of the following:  Mission Management/Mission Control, Mission Data Processing,  and Space Domain Awareness)
  • Experience in U.S. Government customer requirements development, program management, and acquisition
  • Significant experience developing strategic approaches to include technology investments to execute capture strategies to win greater than $100M new business with the DoD, IC, and Civil agencies.
  • Proven track record of business growth with the Space Intelligence Community and DOD programs across a broad range of customer
  • Experience leading and managing multi-disciplined teams
