Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is an industry-leading provider for prime satellite and payload capabilities and directed energy and electronics solutions for national security, military, and civil customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible.

SSSD has an opening for an Electromechanical Manufacturing Engineer to support the Module Assembly and Test (MA&T) Department. This position will be located at our Space Park facility located in Manhattan Beach, CA.

The selected candidate will apply electrical, electronic and mechanical principles to produce spaceflight and avionics microelectronic assemblies. The hardware you will be building involves epoxy mounting of components, wire and ribbon bonding for electrical connectivity, laser welding or seam sealing for package hermiticity, temperature cycling and centrifuge testing for mechanical stress testing to name a few of our processes you will be involved with. 

This role may be filled at the Principal Engineer (Level 3) or Senior Principal Engineer (Level 4) level depending on the qualifications below.

1st and 2nd shift positions available. 2nd shifts start time is after 12pm. 

Responsibilities:

  •  Supports and participates in the design, modification, fabrication, and mechanical/electrical testing of prototype and production microelectronic assemblies in a class 100,000 cleanroom. 

  • Establishes and reviews build plans that ensure all flow down requirements are met.

  •  Identifies, designs and co-ordinates the fabrication and fit check of all tooling necessary to support production.

  • Performs Material Review functions on discrepant product, performs root cause analysis on defects, and coordinates the implementation of cost effect corrective actions.

  •  Methodically resolves technical issues.

  • Develops, improves, qualifies production processes.

  • Creates and maintains process procedures.

  • Continuously improves production yields by performing DOE’s, implementing and tracking Statistical Process Controls, Pareto charts and applicable metrics.

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels. Candidate must be comfortable in a lab environment and be able to collaborate closely with cost account managers, assembly technicians, materials & parts engineers, process planners, product engineers, and electrical/test engineers.

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:


Principal Engineer Electromechanical (Level 3)

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university and 5 years of engineering experience OR Master’s Degree and 3 years of engineering experience OR a PhD. Degree and 0 years of experience

  • Hands-on Experience with electromechanical systems, working with tools and machines.

  • Excellent 2-way communication skills (upward and downward), interpersonal skills, and the ability to interface with all levels of employees.

Senior Principal Engineer Electromechanical (Level 4)

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university and 9 years of engineering experience OR Master’s Degree and 7 years of engineering experience OR a PhD. Degree and 4 years of experience

  • Hands-on Experience with electromechanical systems, working with tools and machines.

  • Excellent 2-way communication skills (upward and downward), interpersonal skills, and the ability to interface with all levels of employees.

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

  • Experience in a production or manufacturing environment 

  • Experience designing, fabricating, and assembling tools, jigs, and factory equipment.

  • Experience with of Six Sigma or equivalent methodology for improving product quality and reducing process variability.

  • Programming knowledge (VBA, Python)

  • Knowledge of semiconductor processing and inspection and/or electronic assembly

  •  Knowledge of RF fundamentals 

  • Able to obtain and maintain an active U.S. Government SCI access with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI) for future work.
