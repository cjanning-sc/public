ACME Engineering Mission Systems is seeking candidates for the position of Manager Mechanical Engineering 1 or Manager Mechanical Engineering 2.  The selected candidate for this position will report to the ACME Engineering Precision Targeting and Protection facility in Erie, FL.

What You’ll Get To Do:

This is a dual-role technical manager position.  The Manager Mechanical Engineering 1 or Manager Mechanical Engineering 2 will require tasking and coordination of approximately 10-15 engineers, and process and resource coordination with functional homeroom management. The position will also have 75% of his/her time allocated to technical responsibilities. This candidate will demonstrate a broad skill set that is balanced between interpersonal, leadership, and technical capabilities. He/she must be a self-starter, able to efficiently operate in a team environment, and collaborate across the sector business areas as required to accomplish the team goals. The candidate will work closely with other developers, and engineering disciplines.

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Interface with mechanical engineers to develop flow down of mechanical design requirements and technical specifications
  • Develop, document, and maintain mechanical designs both individually and as part of a larger team
  • Identify risks, suggest mitigations, track defects, develop resolutions, and track and report status
  • Conduct reviews of product mechanical, individual mechanical designs, and technical documents
  • Exercise Agile principles while developing mechanical designs using continuous integration workflow 
  • Ensure mechanical design quality by exercising different types of tests, documenting the results, and exercising automated testing wherever possible
  • Support team in integrating various sensors into a larger platform
  • Assist in the creation of hardware integration and test setups
  • Solve complex problems through innovative and creative solutions
  • Support/lead project execution over the whole product lifecycle, with foremost emphasis on meeting or exceeding customer and stakeholder requirements  
  • Facilitate and maintain good working relationships with internal and external stakeholders
  • Manage a group of up to 15 direct reports:
  • Lead mechanical engineering staffing planning and assignments
  • Execute performance reviews & set attainable goals for the team
  • Guide the employees regarding career development
  • Support teams or individuals to improve performance and achieve goals through coaching and mentoring
  • Create and support department policies, procedures, and budgets
  • Provide flow-down and direction from upper management and the organization

"This position may be filled as a Manager Mechanical Engineering 1 or Manager Mechanical Engineering 2 based on the qualifications listed below."

Basic Qualifications for a Manager Mechanical Engineering 1:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering, or related STEM field and a minimum of 5 years of mechanical design development/engineering experience (3 years' experience with a MS degree). 
  • Demonstrated leadership experience
  • Proficiency in mechanical design CAD tools such as Creo, NX and/or SolidWorks
  • Experience with ANSI Y14.5M GD&T specifications 
  • Experience with electro-mechanical or opto-mechanical integration
  • Experience with mechanical lifecycle such as: specifications, architecture, design, implementation, integration, and maintenance.
  • Ability to work independently and within a team environment
  • Proven communication and presentation skills
  • Experience with version control and collaboration tools
  • U.S. Citizenship with the ability to obtain and maintain a minimum of a Department of Defense (DoD) Secret Security clearance

Basic Qualifications for a Manager Mechanical Engineering 2:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering, or related STEM field and a minimum of 9 years of mechanical design development/engineering experience (7 years' experience with a MS degree). 
  • Demonstrated leadership experience
  • Proficiency in mechanical design CAD tools such as Creo, NX and/ors SolidWorks
  • Experience with ANSI Y14.5M GD&T specifications 
  • Experience with electro-mechanical or opto-mechanical integration
  • Experience with mechanical lifecycle such as: specifications, architecture, design, implementation, integration, and maintenance.
  • Ability to work independently and within a team environment
  • Proven communication and presentation skills
  • Experience with version control and collaboration tools
  • U.S. Citizenship with the ability to obtain and maintain a minimum of a Department of Defense (DoD) Secret Security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience in mechanical design development of Optical, Electronic Warfare, Military, or Space Technologies
  • Experience in ANSYS Finite Element Analysis
  • Experience with TeamCenter Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) applications
  • Prior mechanical engineering functional management experience (executing performance appraisals, staffing allocations, design process leadership, and career goals development)
  • Active Department of Defense (DoD) Secret Security clearance
