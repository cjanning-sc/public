Northrop Grumman Enterprise Services is seeking an NGSP Operations Extended Support Services Manager 1 within the Chief Information Office Space Sector PMO IT Operations Organization. This role can be performed at any major Northrop Grumman location. This role is 100% onsite. The selected candidate's responsibilities will include leading the Extended Support Services team that assists end users, IT Operations, and InfoSec with the setup and installation of technology tools and products.  This is a working manager position, so advanced desktop technical support skills and networking knowledge is mandatory.  Works with stakeholders and end users to plan and configure systems and to develop solutions that will achieve needed objectives. Oversees the deployment of process controller or server projects, coordinating all parties involved to ensure a smooth process with minimal disruption. Coordinates emergency after-hours support and major troubleshooting efforts and may also develop or supervise programs to educate users. The manager leads, evaluates, provides resources, and drives a team towards realistic goals. Applies technical expertise to strategize for current/future technological developments and serves as point of contact for IT questions. Applies knowledge of Northrop Grumman IT policies and procedures to ensure risk to the business is minimized while also thinking creatively to resolve issues. The Operations Extended Support Services Manager is responsible for delivering results that keep Northrop Grumman technology assets secure, compliant, and operational.

Job duties:

  • Ensure ongoing compliance with regulatory requirements, evaluate controls to help mitigate negative outcomes through prevention, detection and correction​​​
  • Expected to offer depth and growth of responsibilities and challenges to team members, coaching, immediate constructive feedback, praise, goal-setting, and regular one-on-one meetings
  • Own the process and outcomes for resolving issues
  • Coordinate and lead team meetings and meetings with the business
  • Create a trusted partnership with ESPMs and business leaders
  • Assist the team and/or individual team members in managing priorities
  • Providing hands-on Extended Support Services, working trouble tickets and issue resolution
  • Monitoring activities of Extended Support Techs and ensure support queues are being managed appropriately
  • Demonstrate appropriate judgment and frugality when authorizing the use of corporate funds
  • Ensure that the team adheres to safety policies and procedures
  • Follow quality service standards and comply with policies, procedures, contracts, and regulations
  • Escalate roadblocks and major issues while managing risks and dependencies
  • Persuade and influence others through verbal and written communication skills; may negotiate with external parties
  • Acts as SME and point of escalation for stakeholders, business leaders and other team members
  • Confidently makes decisions in accordance with the Northrop Grumman values
  • Track delivery of IT services by all heritage ES service lines and outsource providers
  • Identifying areas for sector-wide service improvement and standardization

  • Demonstrated leadership experience.
  • Problem solving and negotiation skills.
  • Time management and prioritization skills

  • Extensive recent experience with a wide range of technology tools and systems (Windows, networks, servers, PCs, printers, ServiceNow, software, etc.)
