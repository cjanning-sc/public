ACME Engineering Mission Systems sector is seeking a Quality Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals at our Milwaukee, WI location.  The qualified applicant will become part of ACME Engineering's Production Quality Assurance team that interacts with Suppliers, Program Quality Engineers and Manufacturing Operations on products to meet our customer’s mission needs. The candidate must have the ability to learn the Vendor Material Disposition Area (VMDA) process as it relates to the Receiving Inspection area for processing material across all product lines.

The candidate must have the ability to read / interpret engineering drawings, assembly inspection instructions and must be able to use all types of inspection tools.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Support for the Milwaukee Receiving Inspection VMDA area for supplier non-conforming supplier received material
  • Evaluates and reports on incoming material, develops and maintains metrics for monthly reporting.
  • Interfaces with the supplier mission assurance team to ensure identified program issues are resolved in a timely manner.
  • Works with the team to establish root cause on identified issues and ensures corrective and preventive actions are documented, implemented, and verified effective.
  • Coordinates efforts to ensure the quality engineering team meets goals and closes actions on time.
  • Conduct audits/assessments to ensure effective processes are well documented and updated as needed
  • Supports the teams Continuous Improvement activities

Basic Qualifications:    

  • Bachelor's degree in a STEM discipline (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) field with related internship experience
  • Able to multitask, prioritize, and manage time efficiently
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Able to analyze problems and strategize for better solutions
  • Ability to obtain a DoD Secret clearance (US Citizenship required).

Preferred Qualifications

  • Knowledge of SAP/MES, experience in MS Word, MS Excel, and Microsoft Teams
  • Experience in analyzing problems, identifying root cause and corrective actions
  • Experience comprehending / interpreting regulatory compliance, such as ISO 9001/AS9100 Quality Management System
