ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Systems Engineer/Principal Engineer –OPSCON. This position will be located in Detroit, MI or Indianapolis, IN and will support Sentinel (Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD)) program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What you’ll get to do:

This role will support the development of the C&L Operations Concept (OPSCON) and related products including Use Cases, MBSE architecture diagrams, and related technical artifacts. The OPSCON SE will:

Take ownership of OPSCON products and lead their development, review, and maintenance across C&L Element teams, GBSD teams, and with the customer.

Research and understand technical and non-technical aspects driving the development of system functionality and seek innovative solutions to meeting those needs.

Maintain technical cognizance of C&L interdependencies and the impact of the OPSCON and related products across the program.

Support other teams across C&L and GBSD to mature and realize related OPSCON and use case products.

Identify gaps in architecture or design that prevent the realization of the OPSCON and engage the appropriate teams to drive to resolution.

Develop and maintain positive working relationships with teammates and program leaders to achieve OPSCON objectives.

Seek and incorporate critical feedback of OPSCON products from SMEs and technical leaders across the program to support the development and approval of these products
