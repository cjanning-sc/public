Northrop Grumman Defense Systems (NGDS) is looking for a Principal Software Developer based out of Hollywood, MD

 

The Software Developer will implement solutions across the full stack (.NET C# application, windows services, and Web Services) to develop new application features in an Agile development environment. 

  

Design and implement creative solutions, while working in a collaborative team environment.

 

 

Responsibilities Include: 

 

  • Design and implement solutions for Aircraft Mission Planning Systems. 

  • Develop, document, test, optimize, and debug software applications and systems that contain advanced algorithms and data structures. 

  • Develop and debug server-client communication. 

  • SQL database development and interaction. 

  • Research and collaborate with other engineers in planning, design, and implementation. 

 

 

 Basic Qualifications:  

 

  • 5 Years with Bachelors degree; 3 Years with Masters degree; High School Diploma / Equivalent with 9 Years of experience may be accepted in lieu of degree. 

  • 3 years of work experience in any of the following: Java, C, C++, or C# 

  • Secret Clearance with the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Top Secret Clearance and Special Access Program (SAP) is required. 

 

Preferred Qualifications: 

 

  • Proficiency in C#, WPF, XAML, and MVVM 

  • Experience with Atlassian Suite (Jira/Confluence/Crucible/Bitbucket) 

  • Excellent communication and organizational skills; strong interpersonal and analytic skills; Works well in a team-environment 

  • Previous in-depth domain knowledge of software design and development 

  • Familiarity with XML, XSLT, and XSD schemas 

  • Familiarity with Object Oriented Design, Software Design Patterns, and UML diagrams 

  • RESTful API development experience, with unit testing 

  • Security+ Certification 

  • Current DoD Top Secret Clearance  

  • Current SCI/SAP access
