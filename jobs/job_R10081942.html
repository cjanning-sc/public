Construction Engineer (3906-1)
R10081942
1st Shift (United States of America)

The Construction Engineer will be familiar with the construction subcontracts, cost estimating, scheduling,  safety, and quality.

Daily tasks include but are not limited to:
• Effectively process subcontractor submittals in accordance with both the Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR) and the Sentinel Infrastructure protocols
• Be very familiar with the quality inspections process
• Be very familiar with the site-specific Accident Prevention Plan (APP)
• Closely track the construction project schedule
• Interface with customer representative as required
• Maintain a daily construction log
• Encourage, lead, and/or participate in staff training and development

Basic Qualifications
• U.S. Citizenship and ability to obtain a DoD Secret Security Clearance with the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP).

• Documentable training and field experience on construction products including industrial, aerospace or defense projects.

Typical Minimum Education or Experience
2 Years with Bachelor of Science; 0  Years with Masters Degree in Engineering, Construction Management or Engineering Management

Preferred Qualifications

• Experience in large scale construction management or construction trade preferably on Department of Defense and/or missile contracts.
• Must be able to present a professional demeanor while dealing with both subcontractors, clients and peers.
• Ability to process a large volume of construction submittals and Requests for Information (RFI).
• Prior experience on large-scale projects with tight deadlines.
• Prior experience on projects with limited work space available.
