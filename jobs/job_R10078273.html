Sr Principal Engineer Systems - AI/ML
R10078273
1st Shift (United States of America)

NG is looking for a full-time Senior Engineer with a strong background in artificial intelligence/machine learning algorithm. There are two potential focus areas for this position: automatic target recognition and location estimation from terrain/imagery. A background in one of those two focus areas is preferred.

The candidate will perform in a team of cross-discipline engineers and be responsible for requirements, integration, and test planning for complex AI/ML solutions. The candidate will collaborate with hardware engineers/equipment providers in the establishing hardware baselines; with software engineers on algorithm design and implementation; and with test engineers to provide lab testing and field demonstration capabilities.

Other activities will include:

  • Utilize experience & domain knowledge in AI/ML to lead, manage, & guide a small team of engineers to improve existing capabilities and benchmark performance

  • Rapidly prototype and demonstrate solutions to customers

  • Iteratively incorporate feedback from live test events to improve the solution

  • Work with outside teams to incorporate AI/ML solutions in larger programs/systems

Minimum Requirements

  • A Bachelors Degree in Science, Technology, Engineering or Math (STEM Degree) with a minimum of 9 years of applicable experience with a BS or 7 years with a MS

  • Demonstrated ability to perform innovative, applied research, and to develop sophisticated algorithms under computational or other resource constraints.

  • Experience with algorithm implementations in MATLAB and software development in C, C++, and/or Python 

  • Experience working directly with stakeholders to develop/test a product

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Security Clearance

Desired Requirements

  • Experience formulating innovative solutions to algorithm development, initiating and fostering client relationships, and executing projects in compliance with cost, schedule, and technical requirements.

  • Demonstrated experience with automatic target recognition or location estimation

  • Experience with taking AI/ML solutions from a lab to fielded environment

  • Current DoD Secret Security Clearance
