Northrop Grumman Defense Systems is actively seeking Systems Engineers based out of Manhattan Beach, CA. This Engineer will support a mix of traditional systems engineering while also performing as a Product Owner for an Agile Scrum team. We are looking for a highly motivated individual with a strong ability to work in a collaborative fast-moving dynamic team environment that creates and supports state-of-the-art mission planning systems.

Responsibilities Include:

  • Developing system and subsystem requirements from user mission needs and tracking requirements throughout the development process.
  • Working closely with developers to ensure software satisfies requirements.
  • Well versed in agile software development and performing collaboration between self-organizing cross-functional scrum teams.
  • Participate in risk analysis and mitigation formation.
  • Support program reviews and creating and/or updating requirement specifications and technical baseline documentation
  • Support functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications. 

Basic Qualifications Principal Systems Engineer:

  • BS or higher degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math (STEM), MIS, or related field
  • 5+ years of experience, and significant direct experience in the field of Systems Engineering OR 3 years experience with a master’s degree in a related discipline.
    • Experience in Systems Engineering, e.g., CONOPS development, requirements analysis, decomposition and allocation, interface definition, and trade studies.
  • Experience as a Systems Engineer on a software-based effort
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Have an active Secret Clearance, adjudicated within the last 5 years
  • Ability to obtain and maintain Top Secret Clearance and Special Program Access

Basic Qualifications Sr. Principal Systems Engineer:

  • BS or higher degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math (STEM), MIS, or related field
  • 8+ years of experience, and significant direct experience in the field of Systems Engineering OR 6 years experience with a master’s degree in a related discipline.
    • Experience in Systems Engineering, e.g., CONOPS development, requirements analysis, decomposition and allocation, interface definition, and trade studies.
  • Experience as a Systems Engineer on a software-based effort
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Have an active Secret Clearance, adjudicated within the last 5 years
  • Ability to obtain and maintain Top Secret Clearance and Special Program Access

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current DoD Top Secret Clearance
  • Current SCI/SAP access
  • Experience in an Agile/SAFe leadership role (Scrum Master, Product Owner, Release Train Engineer, e.g.)
  • Experience with IA/Cybersecurity and industry platform hardening practices, such as DISA STIG Implementation
  • Current DoD 8570 IAT Level II certification (such as, Security+)
  • Experience with Cloud infrastructure technology such as Azure.
  • Experience with containerization, including Docker and Kubernetes
  • Exposure to Infrastructure as Code (IaC) development
  • Experience with UI/UX development
  • Experience with MBSE Tools
  • Experience in scripting/programming languages such as Python, PowerShell, Visual Basic, .Net, Java, C, and C#
  • Knowledge of military mission planning systems
