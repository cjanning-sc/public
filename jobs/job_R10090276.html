ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking Principal Systems Administrator to join the Command and Launch Team (CLT).   This position is in Madison, AL and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.   

What You’ll Get To Do:

Maintain smooth operation of multi-user computer systems, including coordination with network administrators. Interacts with users and evaluates vendor products. Makes recommendations to purchase hardware and software, coordinates installation and provides backup recovery. Develops and monitors policies and standards for allocation related to the use of computing resources. Ancillary duties may include setting up administrator and service accounts, maintaining system documentation, tuning system performance, installing system wide software and allocating mass storage space.

  • Configures, operates, and maintains networking and computer systems within closed area

  • Solid understanding of hardware infrastructure including servers, networking, and storage components (SAN and NAS)

  • In depth knowledge of Red Hat software packaging, distribution, and configuration

  • Implements and manages VMware vSphere 7.x infrastructure

  • Provides DevOps and source code management in support of software development

  • Responsible for STIG (security technical implementation guide) application, testing, and compliance

  • Assists in design and creation of overall system in accordance with customer requirements

  • Maintains operational security posture to ensure information systems (IS) security policies, standards, and procedures are established and followed

  • Ability to identify and summarize findings, assess root cause, and recommend solutions

  • Participates in requirements derivation and refinement.
