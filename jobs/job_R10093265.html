ACME Engineering Defense Systems (NGDS) is looking for you to join our team as a Sr Program Manager for SHORAD Short Range Air Defense Operating Unit in Madison, AL.  SHORAD integrates disparate radars and weapons to construct a far more effective air defense solution. ACME Engineering has been a prime in developing critical capabilities for the U.S. Military for over 35 years and anticipates further growth as our systems are adapted to additional customer assets and expanded to serve as the Joint All Domain Command and Control. The Tactical Operations business area has a portfolio of programs that are related to Command Post Shelters, Air Defense Related Hardware, Ground Vehicles and our sustainment support teams worldwide.

As the Program Manager, you will report to the SHORAD Operating Unit Sr Director and be responsible for technical, cost and schedule performance of a multi-disciplinary team that designs, develops, documents, tests, and integrates and fields the SHORAD layered defense solutions to our customers. This role will also have accountability for and participate in the strategy to grow our business.  We are looking to continue to nurture customer intimacy and develop solutions for DoD, and international DCS and FMS customers.
