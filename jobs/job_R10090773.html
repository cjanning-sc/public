Northrop Grumman Space Components Business Unit designs, manufactures and operates small and medium-class spacecraft for communications, imaging, scientific, exploration and national security space applications. The Group is leading supplier of space components that power and enable satellites of all classes, provides technical engineering services to government agencies and laboratories, and is developing exciting new satellite servicing and advanced space flight technologies.

Northrop Grumman Space Components has an opening for a Quality Inspector 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in San Diego, CA. for 1st (5:00 am - 2:30 pm) AND 2nd shift (1:00 pm -10:30 pm)

The Quality Inspector 2 will have the relevant aerospace industry experience in the Quality Assurance department.  This employee will be working on small, medium and/or large government and commercial programs.

Job Summary

  • Performs dimensional inspections per work order and drawing requirements

  • Ability to utilize precision hand tools and surface plate setups

  • Proficient blueprint reading experience

  • Other duties as assigned

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School Diploma and 2 years of additional education/or related experience

  • Proficiency with Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing per ASME Y14.5

  • Experience inspecting tight tolerance machined components with complex geometry

  • Experience with dimensional inspection using precision hand tools and surface plate setup

  • Proficient blueprint reading skills

  • Familiarity with AS9100 or ISO 9001 Quality Management Systems

  • Familiarity with aerospace standards (ex. AS9100, ISO9001)

  • Competent in Microsoft Word & Excel

  • Excellent communication skills and ability to work in a team environment
