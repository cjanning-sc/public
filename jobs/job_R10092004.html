Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking an AVE Lab Operation Manager 1 to join our team of highly qualified and diverse individuals. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Learn more about the GBSD program here. Northrop Grumman supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What you will get to do:

The Aerospace Vehicle Equipment (AVE) Lab Operations Manager 1 will report to the AVE Integration and Test Lab Manager. This individual will be responsible for the overall Sentinel Missile hardware-in-the-Loop (HWIL) lab operations to include day-to-day operations, safety, network and cybersecurity, configuration management, shipping and receiving, and coordination with facility maintenance. To succeed in this role, the ideal candidate must be a driver and leader for collaboration across many different sub-IPT teams for a successful integration test of the missile hardware and software configuration. The candidate must also have familiarity with a lab environment.

In this position, the AVE Lab Operations Manager will be responsible for, but not limited to, the following:

  • Lab Safety – Lead the lab team in the implementation of environmental safety, occupational safety, and health policies, establish and implement emergency procedures, ensure proper storage and disposal of hazardous materials in accordance with applicable laws, codes, and regulations, and coordinate with facility maintenance.
  • Configuration Management - Maintain the configuration baseline for all lab configurations, configure the lab to support test objectives, create and maintain a hardware/software configuration matrix for inclusion in turnover-to-test packages, ensure appropriate documentation is received for all element hardware/software received, support lab change requests, maintain standard operating procedures, and perform asset tracking and management.
  • Lab Networking and System Administration - Coordinate with cybersecurity personnel for all equipment entering or exiting the lab, perform data transfers, maintain lab networks, and coordinate with COMSEC custodian as required.
  • Equipment Maintenance, Manufacturing and Purchasing - Coordinate with supply chain management to track receipt of purchase orders and purchase requests, purchase tools and ancillary equipment, prepare kitting as required, implement calibration procedures and tracking, and fabricate fixtures and cabling as required.
  • Employee Management – Oversee the performance, progression and mentoring of the Integration, Assembly, Test and Checkout engineers on the team.  Authority to manage personnel and direct activities required to accomplish the statement of work.
  • Schedule and Cost Reporting Management – Accountable for timely cost and schedule status and reporting to upper-level management to support program management reviews.
  • Travel – May be required to travel up to 10% of the time.
