Northrop Grumman Mission Systems Sector is seeking a Budget Manager 2 to lead and support Compliance, Tools, Training, and Reporting for the overall Mission Systems Rates and Budgets organization within the Sector Controller Function.  This Manager position requires the ability to work effectively as a team member within both the Rates and Budgets function and the overall Northrop Grumman organization.

This preferred location for this position is onsite in Linthicum, MD with a hybrid work schedule. This position may also be worked as a fully remote position for a highly qualified candidate, at the discretion of the hiring manager

Responsibilities Include:

  • Develop and maintain the Rates and Budgets procedures, documentation, and training content to ensure employee awareness and compliance.
  • Partnering with Regulatory Compliance, provide robust oversight to ensure that the Sector meets its internal and external compliance requirements
  • Serve as a focal point for internal and external audits to ensure coordination and that communication is consistent across all functions
  • Rates & Budgets tool resource management lead (Analytics Toolsets and Digital Transformation)
  • Work with cross functional teams to find ways to improve access to data and data analytics within the department.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree from an accredited university and 9 years of experience in Business Management, Economics, Accounting, Finance, or a related field - OR - Master’s degree from an accredited university with 7 years of experience as described above.
  • FAR, DFARS, and CAS proficiency
  • Technical skills and working knowledge in budgeting, forecasting, and financial analysis
  • Proficient with MS Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word, Teams)
  • Experience leading dispersed teams with success building employee engagement

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with all aspects/phases of Rates and Budgets
  • People leadership experience
  • Experience with Data Analytics/Digital Tool Management
