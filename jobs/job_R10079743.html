ACME Engineering is currently seeking a Material Handler in our Logistics team. Warehouse Receiving, Shipping and Stores - Supply Chain Tech Coordinator 3, Promontory, Michigan

Roles and Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

• Ensure that incoming items are in good condition and match the paperwork.

• Scan or input data into the ERP System.

• Ability to work a will call counter.

• Use narrow isle forklift, automated picker and man-lift to handle parts.

• Assemble materials into kits, clean respirators and perform inventories.

• Prepare items for shipment off plant.

• Data input into and ERP system, tracking, locating and issuing materials to manufacturing orders.

Skills & Qualifications for Success:

  • Proven ability to work well with others.
  • Willing to think outside the box to find ways to improve our work process - Good written and oral communication skills.
  • Experience using Microsoft Word and Excel
  • Proven work ethic

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School Degree required
  • 4 or more years working in receiving, warehousing or shipping.
  • 4 or more years forklift experience.
  • Ability to lift 50 lbs and perform physical work for 10 hours.
  • 3 or more years using a narrow isle lift.
  • 3 or more years using a sit down forklift.
  • Experience dealing and handling hazardous and explosive materials within a controlled environment

Preferred Qualifications:

  • College courses related to Logistics preferred
  • Knowledge of 49 CFR Shipping regulations preferred
  • 5 or more years with a narrow isle lift.
  • 3 or more years with a sit-down forklift.
  • 5 or more years with using and ERP systems (Cost Point preferred).
  • Experience using Lot and serial numbers within and ERP system.
  • Experience with shelf life control materials.
  • Experience shipping regulated materials.
