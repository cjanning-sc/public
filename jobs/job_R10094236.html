ACME Engineering Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Principal Configuration Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located out of Indianapolis, CO.

Position Overview:

In this position, you will Manage the C2BMC Technical baseline in accordance to all program requirements in accordance with the program configuration and data management plan. Additionally, you will be responsible for all software builds and releases, administering Program Review Boards, maintaining program hardcopy library, performing COTS administration and supporting baseline verification activities.

Basic Qualifications:

If applicable, please note your updated security clearance and IAT/relevant certifications on your resume.

  • An active Secret clearance is required.
  • 3 Years with Bachelor’s degree in a related field; 7 years’ experience in lieu of a degree.
  • Experience with Configuration/Data Management requirements/procedures.
  • Ability to perform the following tasks:
    • Analyzes proposed changes of product design to determine effect on overall product and system.
    • Coordinates modification records for management control.
    • Establishes change orders and prepares for change authorization and documentation by company and subcontractor.
    • Prepares reports of change effect on overall product.
    • Reviews and analyzes released engineering change data and coordinates changes with engineering, quality, support, manufacturing, and engineering data control activities.
    • Ensures that customer requirements are implemented and reviews change accounting activity to ensure compliance with configuration management policies.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Familiar with DoD Programs.
  • Strong written and verbal communications skills along with the ability to understand and communicate technically oriented material utilizing the various electronic tools relevant to the role.
  • Strong social skills, and ability to build solid relationships with team members.
