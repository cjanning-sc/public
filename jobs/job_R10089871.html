Northrop Grumman Enterprise Services is seeking a Sr. Principal Software Development Analyst (CAD design); this position will be located at Clearfield, Utah Northrop Grumman facility. Roles and responsibilities will include, but not be limited to, the following:

  • Administering CAD and CAD Model Management Systems.
  • Providing end-user technical support of CAD software including in development and production environments.
  • Performing root cause analysis, debugging problems within the CAD environment, and working with the appropriate technical teams to develop, test, and implement fixes to prevent recurrence.
  • Monitoring, addressing, and resolving production CAD environment incidents, service requests, workflows, client install packages, etc., collaboratively with other support teams.
  • Documenting all aspects of system support so that knowledge is readily shared among all those tasked with supporting CAD instances.
  • Supporting SIT (System Integration Testing) and UAT (User Acceptance Testing) with business users as necessary.
  • Supporting business readiness and change management activities prior to go-live and in production.
  • Developing and maintaining CAD automation and macros to assist CAD users.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Associate degree with 11 years of experience, or Bachelor’s degree with 9 years of experience, or Master's degree with 7 years of experience; a High School diploma or equivalent with 13 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a completed degree.
  • 5 years minimum CAD support experience with CATIA V5, NX or other 3D CAD applications with similar functions and capabilities.
  • Proven track record supporting major CAD software in an enterprise environment.
  • Experience with CAD support and incident resolution. 
  • A current DoD Secret security clearance, and the ability to maintain the required security clearance as a condition of continued employment.
  • Ability to obtain a Security+CE certification within 6 months of hire date (this time frame may be accelerated based on customer and/or program requirements); the ability to maintain the required certification will be a condition of continued employment.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Familiarity with the functions, processes, and capabilities in native CAD applications and Data Management.
  • Experience supporting CAD in Windows and Linux environments.
  • Fluency in at least one computer programming language.
  • Ability to apply engineering techniques and analytical methods to make recommendations for improvements and provide information and assistance to end-users.
