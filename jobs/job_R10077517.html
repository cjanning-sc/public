ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a principal Systems Test Engineer / Sr Principal Systems Test Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals within our Systems Test Engineering organization. 

The NASA Sky Range program is developing and integrating new capabilities to support flight operations for advanced sensors on a fleet of unmanned aircraft providing range support for hypersonic capability development. The Sky Range team is looking for an engineer to support flight and ground control room data systems as well as real-time satellite communications. We are looking for a flexible, motivated individual to be based at the NASA Armstrong facility at Edwards Air Force to help maintain and grow our capabilities as well as support integration of new sensors.

Position Description:

This is an experienced DATA/IT Systems Test Engineer, which encompasses aspects of communications, software, network, and/or DevOps engineering. This individual will be expected to work with a small team to develop and provide operational support for satellite and network communications between operators in a control room, and the unmanned aircraft. In addition, they will be expect to provide support and troubleshooting as necessary for real-time data acquisition, data processing, and situational awareness display tools running on embedded Linux systems.

The Data Systems Engineer will be expected to:

  • Support data collection and communications systems aboard unmanned aircraft

  • Assist integration of sensor teams with aircraft systems, including coordinating and implementing pre-installation “System In the Loop” (SIL) testing

  • Staff field campaigns (2 – 4 weeks at a stretch) to provide operational support and troubleshooting.

  • Maintain and deploy primarily Linux-based systems aboard aircraft and ground-based infrastructure

  • Create documentation and training material

  • Work with Sky Range, NASA and customer/sensor teams to ensure system security and compliance with Agency and Federal IT security standards

  • Assist with the development of new software and communications capabilities aboard the SkyRange fleet

  • Work closely with sensor teams to define mission requirements and implement solutions

  • Troubleshoot hardware and/or software.

This position involves up to 25% travel, and includes shift work in support of missions/deployments. This position is based out of Edward Air Force base in Southern Ohio, and includes outdoor, plane-side testing.
