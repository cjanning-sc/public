Principal Network Engineer – RMF & Cyber (23-101)
R10094225
Days (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Principal Network Engineer – RMF & Cyber to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located out of Schriever Space Force Base, Colorado Springs, CO.

Position Overview:

  • Candidate will be a primary Network Engineer supporting the program.
  • The candidate will be responsible for providing technical support and expertise for the assessment of compliance of information systems with DoD RMF standards (review, verify and validate required DoD IA RMF documentations and artifacts using DoDI 8510.01/DoD Risk Management Framework).
  • Candidate must have proven experience in developing Cyber-secure Network device configurations (Firewalls, switches, routers).
  • Some travel may be required depending on the work to be performed.
  • This position will also support Network deployments as necessary.

Basic Qualifications:

If applicable, please note your updated security clearance and IAT/relevant certifications on your resume.

  • An active Secret clearance is required.
  • 5 Years with Bachelor’s degree in a related field; 9 years’ experience in lieu of a degree.
  • Must possess a DoD 8570 certification at IAT Level II or higher (Security+, GSEC, SCNP, SSCP, CISSP, CISA, GSE, SCNA)
  • The candidate must be a proven network engineer with hands-on mid-level experience and knowledge of network design, development and testing methodologies.
  • Specific technical skills required include:
    • A solid understanding of the OSI Model, TCP/IP, routing protocols BGP and OSPF.
    • Proven network traffic analysis, diagnostic and troubleshooting experience.
    • Experience in UDP multicast, SONET, MSPP, VLANs, GRE Tunnels, and TCP/IP network designs.
    • Hands on experience with multi-vendor switching routing (Juniper), WAN optimizer, TACLANEs, and optical transport equipment.
    • Understanding of standard DOD security practices such as, STIG compliance and system accreditation implementation efforts.
    • Familiar with RMF including hands on work with RMF topics, the assessment of compliance of information systems with DoD RMF standards (review, verify and validate required DoD IA RMF documentations and artifacts using DoDI 8510.01/DoD Risk Management Framework).
  • Must have experience using the Agile Framework or experience applying Agile fundamentals in engineer-focused work.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Firewall experience with Juniper firewalls is a must and McAfee Sidewinder or Fortinet Fortigate
  • A solid understanding of JUNOS
  • Candidates with prior C2BMC network experience
  • Candidares with knowledge, skill sets or experience using protocol analyzers/sniffers (such as Wireshark)
  • Industry vendor certifications from Juniper (JNCIA, JNCIS, JNCIP, JNCIE) a must and from Cisco (CCNA, CCNP)
  • Authentication & encryption methods/capabilities (TACACS+, Active Directory, GEM-X), Virtualization technologies such a VMWare vCenter and vSphere
