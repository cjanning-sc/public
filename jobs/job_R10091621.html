Description

The Specialized Warfighter Development Contract (SWDC) Modeling and Simulation Software (MSS) at Northrop Grumman Space Systems is looking for you to join our team as a Network Engineer on the SWDC Shared Services Platform (SSP) Agile Release Train (ART) located at Schriever Space Force Base, Colorado Springs, CO. 

Primary duties include:

  • Designing and planning network communications systems
  • Providing specifications and detailed schematics for network architecture
  • Providing specific detailed information for hardware and software selection, implementation techniques and tools for the most efficient solution to meet business needs, including present and future capacity requirements.
  • Conducting testing of network design
  • Documenting, designing, and managing implementation of network compliance requirements.
  • Maintaining technical expertise in all areas of network and computer hardware and software interconnection and interfacing, such as routers, multiplexers, firewalls, hubs, bridges, gateways, etc
  • Evaluating and reporting on new communications technologies to enhance capabilities of the network.

Additional responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Identifying and leading network innovation across the program
  • Supporting lab operations during events with rapid response
  • Debugging configurations as part of DevSecOps for custom modeling and simulation software

This requisition may be filled at either a Principal Network Engineer or a Sr. Principal Network Engineer level.

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Principal Network Engineer Level 03 requires: Bachelor’s degree and 5+ years of related experience or a Master's degree and 3 years of experience. Additional 9 years of experience required in lieu of a degree
  • Sr Principal Network Engineer Level 04 requires: Bachelor’s degree and 9+ years of related experience or a Master's degree and 7 years of experience. 13 years of experience required in lieu of a degree
  • Must possess an active Secret clearance
  • Basic knowledge of network testing methodologies using routers, switches, and firewalls
  • Knowledge of TCP/IP behavior, network communications and routing protocols, network traffic analysis and troubleshooting experience
  • Work under minimal direction and independently determine and develop approach to solutions
  • Current Security+ CE or DoD 8570 IAT Level II certification or ability to obtain within 6 months

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with configuration and integration of cyber security tools like eMASS and HBSS
  • Experience deploying systems into an operational network with CM measures
