We have an opening for a Systems Administrator located in Columbus, OH. The selected candidate will be responsible for maintaining smooth operation of classified multi-user computer systems.
 
Duties will include interaction with users to determine hardware/software requirements and installation and configuration of hardware, operating systems and COTS. Ancillary duties will include, but are not limited to, evaluation of vendor products, providing recommendations to purchase hardware and software, providing backup recovery, maintaining system documentation, tuning system performance, and computing asset management.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Current DoD Top Secret Clearance and ability to obtain SCI clearance
  • Bachelor's degree with 2 years of relevant experience, or a Master's degree; an additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of degree
  • Systems Administration of Windows and Linux Operating Systems
  • Ability to obtain and maintain Security+ certification

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with remote and local system deployments
  • Familiarity with NAS and SAN technologies (NetApp SAN, EMC SAN, HP NAS)
  • CISSP or Microsoft OS Certification
  • SCI clearance
