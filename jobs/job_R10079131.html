Northrop Grumman Defense Services sector is currently seeking a Mgr Network Communications 1 to join our growing Subsystems & Software Sustainment (S&SS) Operating Unit Systems Support Group (SSG) team

in Warner Robins, Georgia. In this position, the selected candidate will take an active role in information security governance protecting the men and women that fly our nation’s aircraft in harm’s way.

Maintains smooth operation of local area and virtual private networks (VPN). Plans, evaluates, and coordinates installation and/or reconfiguration of hardware and software elements of data communication network. Maintains technical expertise in all areas of networks and computer hardware and software interconnection and interfacing, such as routers, multiplexers, firewalls, hubs, bridges, gateways, etc. May propose solutions to management to ensure all communications requirements based on future needs and current usage, configuring such solutions to optimize cost savings. Prepares or ensures that appropriate network documentation exists, including operational instructions. Provides regular monitoring and network analysis regarding short- and long-range planning for in-house systems. May coordinate third-party maintenance for network equipment. May design network or portion of network that include selection of hardware and software packages. Manage a team between 5-10 IT System Administrators ensuring daily work assignments are executed to meet program requirements through completion.

Basic Qualifications:
Qualified candidate must have the following:

- Must have 7-9 years related network communications experience with HS diploma/GED. Will consider an Associates degree or equivalent with a minimum of 5 years of electronics/related experience or a Bachelors degree in Computer Science or Information Technology with 3 years of related experience, or a Masters Degree in Computer Science or Information Technology with 1 year related experience

- Experience in a Lead or Managerial role

- Must have a U.S. Department of Defense (DOD) Interim Secret Security Clearance or higher
- Must be able to work on-site in Warner Robins, Georgia

- No remote work available

Preferred Qualifications:
- A Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or Information Technology

- Demonstrated success in performing in a fast-paced depot/sustainment environment
- Experience in managing classified and controlled unclassified information of data systems and test equipment

- Experience working with hardware setups, configuring of systems, and networks

- Experience in establishing policy and procedures to meet FAR 52.204-2, FAR 52.204-21, DFARS 252.204.7012, DFARS 252.204.7020, and DFARS 252.204.7021 

- Current CompTIA Security+ CE, CompTIA Network+ CE, CompTIA A+ CE, CISSP or equivalent certification(s)

- Experience managing a team between 5-10 employees

- Experience creating and working with an Integrated Master Schedule (IMS)

- Have a current U.S. Department of Defense (DOD) Secret Security Clearance or higher
