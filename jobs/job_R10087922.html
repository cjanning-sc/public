    We are looking for a Mechanical Engineer to join our team. This position may be filled out of Goleta, CA or Oxnard, CA.

    This position may be filled at either a level 2 or a level 3

    Job Summary

    Role and Responsibilities:

    • Establish and maintain the master geometry 3D Solid Model Database.
    • Prepare conceptual layouts and designs of tools and fixtures based on engineering definition of requirements and constraints.
    • Establish critical dimensions and tolerances; specify material, finishes and other data necessary for other drafters to prepare simple layouts or detail and assembly drawings.
    • Coordinate all activities of the project in the area of CAD information exchange, maintain project standards and guidelines.
    • Coordinate project tasks for other Designers and CAD Drafters.
    • Operate within the Product Data Management (PDM) system.
    • Understand and implement any departmental procedure manuals and procedures.
    • Create illustrations and animations for proposals, presentations and documents.
    • Create design modifications to existing hardware.
    • Perform basic analysis and make adjustments to drawings or specifications.
    • Create Manufacturing Instructions to plan and meet the requirements of the design.
    • Utilize MRP system to obtain material and parts to meet schedule.
    • Track progress of tools and fixtures from project proposal to hardware integration completion.

    Basic Requirements for a level 2:

    • B.S. Degree in STEM and or equivalent work experience and 2+ years’ experience in mechanical or electro/mechanical design/drafting
    • General knowledge of manufacturing practices and comprehensive working knowledge of ASME Y14.5, ASME Y14.100-200 and MIL-DTL-31000
    • Experience in CAD systems; NX CAD, SolidWorks or CREO
    • Knowledge and experience with MS Office (Word, Excel, Power Point, Outlook)
    • Knowledge and experience with a MRP system writing purchase orders and manufacturing orders
    • Good time management and prioritization in completing tasks on schedule and on budget

    Basic Requirements for a level 3:

    • B.S. Degree in STEM and or equivalent work experience and 5+ years’ experience mechanical or electro/mechanical design/drafting
    • Practical knowledge of manufacturing practices and comprehensive working knowledge of ASME Y14.5, ASME Y14.100-200 and MIL-DTL-31000
    • Experience in CAD systems; NX CAD, SolidWorks or CREO
    • Experience with MS Office (Word, Excel, Power Point, Outlook)
    • Experience with a MRP system writing purchase orders and manufacturing orders
    • Excellent time management and prioritization in completing tasks on schedule and on budget

    Preferred Requirements

    • BS Degree in Mechanical Engineering
    • Experience in aerospace structures and mechanisms
