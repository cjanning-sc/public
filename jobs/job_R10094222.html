ACME Engineering Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Principal Systems Software Analyst – C2BMC Planner Verification Test to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located out of Indianapolis, CO.

Position Overview:

As member of the Mission Applications Planner Product Team of the Missile Defense National Team for Command and Control, Battle Management, and Communications (C2BMC) (aka C2BMC NT), this position will participate in software design and peer reviews, develop test specifications and infrastructure, execute manual and automated test and perform analysis of C2BMC implementation in an integrated environment or to test specific capabilities for interfaces or infrastructure in support of operations (e.g., Internal Messaging, control and monitoring, message and display recording and playback, failover processing). Responsibilities also include the coordination of lab resources, test results and verification status with the C2BMC program and customers.

Basic Qualifications:

If applicable, please note your updated security clearance and IAT/relevant certifications on your resume.

  • An active Secret clearance is required.
  • 5 Years with Bachelor’s degree in a related field; 9 years’ experience in lieu of a degree.
  • The ideal candidate will possess experience in testing and evaluation of a software-intensive system in a variety of test environments/venues, utilizing Agile development.
  • This position will require a strong, self-starting individual capable of diving into problems with assistance from other test engineers and software developers.
  • Candidate experience should include: use of DoD test methodologies; functional capability related test/analysis planning and execution; planning and execution of test data collection; and test data analysis and reporting.
  • The analysis experience should involve characterizing system, software, or component performance using quantitative methods; reporting the characterization to subsequent users; and contributing to performance assessment by program system engineers. This experience may come from independent efforts and/or from participation within test teams.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with an Agile development process; Automated testing for requirement verification and performance assessment Requirement and verification analysis; Conducting post-processing and analysis of collected data; Development of action plans based upon analysis of data; Documenting software deficiency reports; and Presenting analysis results and plans of action orally and/or in written format (e.g., slides, annotated slides, test report document).
  • Experience in a DevSecOps environment
  • Security+ certification
