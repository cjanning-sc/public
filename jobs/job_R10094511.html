ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Principal Mechanical Engineer to join the System Test Team. This position is located in Detroit, MI and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will join a team researching, planning, designing, and developing the mechanical sub-systems for test data instrumentation systems. This will include development of engineering layouts and drawings. Specific roles and duties include:

  • System and component level analyses of complex systems

  • Component engineering and testing

  • Component and instrumentation system manufacturing and testing

  • Research, analysis, system design and development of systems, hardware procurement, and technical oversight with suppliers

  • Applying research to the design, development, and testing of mechanical and/or electromechanical systems, instruments, controls, engines, and/or machines.

  • Supporting testing and flight operations as well as authoring procedures and other engineering instructions for test or operating engineers or customer use.

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) discipline and 5 years of experience; 3 years with Master’s Degree; 0 years with PhD

  • Experience using CAD design tools (i.e. NX, CATIA, etc.)

  • Experience with electro-mechanical or instrumentation system development and design

  • Experience with MATLAB or similar engineering tools.

  • Must have US Citizenship with an Active DoD Secret clearance, granted or re-investigated within last 6 years.

  • Must have the ability to obtain, and maintain access to Special Access Programs (SAP)  as a condition of continued employment.

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

  • Direct experience with designing instrumentation systems including review of relevant analysis, manufacturing drawings, authoring test procedures or hands on pressure or hot fire, engine test support.

  • Masters’ Degree in Mechanical or Aerospace Engineering

  • Experience leading spacecraft instrumentation testing

  • Experience leading hardware procurement/supply chain operations

  • Possesses strong leadership qualities, as well as strong verbal and written communication skills
