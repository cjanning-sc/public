ACME Engineering Defense Systems sector is seeking a Logistics Support (2) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals for a position located at Sandusky, CA.

You will provide support to the logistics function preparing logistics documentation to establish the range and depth of spares, tools, test equipment and related manuals to be deployed and stocked. You will determine maintenance and repair materials required for deployment, as well as modify maintenance records such as repair standards for more complex equipment or components. You will develop requisitions and manage records of equipment, components, repair parts and related manuals; track logistics materials and parts for proper allocation of storage facilities; review configuration changes to evaluate impact on logistics deployment; document equipment, component and parts conditions; and identify logistic support documentation to be included such as drawings, technical manuals, and maintenance requirement cards to support life-cycle of product.

  • Basic computer skills; Excel, Word, PowerPoint required.
  • Must be willing to work a flexible work schedule that may include extended shifts, holidays, and weekends and support on call status for 24 hours-a-day, 7 days-a-week, 365 days a year.
