Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Principal/Senior Principal Software Quality Assurance Engineer.  This position will be located in Roy, UT or Huntsville, AL and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrence (GBSD) program. 

This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What You'll Get To Do:


Principal/Senior Principal Engineer, Software Quality Assurance - The selected candidate will be responsible for one or more software development teams (IPTs) on the GBSD or other SDS programs. Our focus is on ensuring process execution and efficiencies in the development, testing and release of the software deliverables.
Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Support individual scrum/sprint teams by attending meetings, creating Jira dashboard tools to help monitor the progress and execution efficiency. Offer suggestions for improvement to software leadership.  

  • Create process health, progress and stability metrics when required

  • Become fluent in the use of tools; Jira, Confluence, Jazz, DOORS or others as needed

  • Be a constructive advocate for Mission Assurance and relentlessly articulate our Value Proposition to our stakeholders

  • Ensure GBSD software is in compliance with industry guidelines and contractual requirements for development of military weapon systems. Maintain a keen focus on employee engagement, teaming and collaboration, process improvement, rework reduction, and continuous improvement.

  • Develops, modifies, applies, and maintains standards for software quality operating methods, processes, systems and procedures.

  • Conducts software inspection, testing, verification and validation. Implements software development and maintenance processes and methods.

  • Ensures measures meet acceptable reliability standards.

  • Develops overall operating criteria to ensure implementation of the software quality program according to project, process and contract requirements and objectives.

  • Ensures that project and process control documentation are compliant with requirements, objectives and/or contract.

  • Reviews software design, change specifications, and plans against contractual and/or process requirements.

  • Reviews include applicable specifications, materials, tools, techniques, and methodologies.

  • Performs or directs verification of software requirement allocations, traceability, and testability.
