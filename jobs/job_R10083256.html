1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman is looking for an Industrial Engineer / Principal Industrial Engineer to support the El Segundo, CA site.

  • Helps determine the most efficient sequence of operations and workflow and provides finite scheduling to manufacturing cost centers and IPTs
  • Maintains datasets and generates metrics that provide for the measurement of program & functional schedule & cost performance.
  • Develops and deploys plans that ensure the timely availability and effective utilization of labor, facilities, equipment, & tooling resources enabling the achievement of program delivery and cost milestones.
  • Develops and summarizes analyses for the acquisition, maintenance & enhancement of capital assets and miscellaneous equipment.
  • Plans plant, office, and production facilities and equipment layouts
  • Provides capacity planning and rate tool analyses
  • Conducts studies pertaining to cost control, cost reduction and inventory control

Basic Qualifications: Industrial Engineer

  • Bachelor Degree with 2 or more years related related experience in industrial, data or engineering; or Master’s Degree with 0 years of experience
  • Ability to obtain and Maintain a DoD Clearance

Basic Qualifications: Principal Industrial Engineer

  • Bachelor Degree with 5 or more years related related experience in industrial, data or engineering; or Master’s Degree with 0 years of experience
  • Ability to obtain and Maintain a DoD Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Military Aircraft Operations Experience
  • Documented experience working in a robust IPT environment
  • At least 1 year of demonstrated manufacturing experience
  • Strong expertise using the full suite of Microsoft Office Tools
