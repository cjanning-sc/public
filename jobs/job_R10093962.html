Are you an engineer with a passion for space? Are you driven to push the limits of human innovation?

Northrop Grumman's campuses in Goleta and Oxnard, CA will be hosting an online information session on Tuesday, February 28 to discuss our products and what it's like to work at Northrop Grumman. Please apply today to express your interest in joining us!

An organization within Northrop Grumman’s Payload and Ground Systems (PGS) division, Spacecraft Components is a leading designer, producer, and supplier of spacecraft components that power and enable satellites of all classes.

Spacecraft Components' campuses in Goleta and Oxnard are the satellite industry's premier source for mission-enabling, space-qualified deployable booms, masts and backing structures for commercial, military, civil and scientific space programs. You may learn more about the technologies at these campuses under the Deployables section of our website: https://www.northropgrumman.com/space/spacecraft-components/.
