Payload Infrastructure Subsystem Engineer/ Principal Payload Infrastructure Subsystem Engineer
R10067363
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems is seeking an Electronics Engineer/Principal Electronics Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Palmdale, CA. The successful candidate will be part of a payload infrastructure engineering team that manages and maintains critical pieces of payload infrastructure vital to program success.

This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

The Electronics Engineer will be responsible for:

  • Managing and supporting system performance in an operational environment during critical program testing
  • Maintaining sensor management hardware across the enterprise to ensure configuration management per released program documentation
  • Analyzing subsystem performance and addressing anomalous behavior in post test data logs
  • Reporting subsystem status to payload infrastructure team lead for upward program communication

This requisition may be filled at either a Electronics Engineer level or a Principal Electronics Engineer level.

Basic Qualifications (Electronics Engineer):

  • Bachelor's Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university with 2 years of related experience OR a Master's Degree in a STEM discipline with 0 years of related experience
  • Active DoD Secret security clearance or DoE equivalent and ability to acquire SAP clearance (SAP not required to start)
  • Up to 25% travel

Basic Qualifications (Principal Electronics Engineer):

  • Bachelor's Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university with 5 years of related experience OR a Master's Degree in a STEM discipline with 3 years of related experience OR a PhD in a STEM discipline with 1 years of related experience
  • Active DoD Secret security clearance or DoE equivalent and ability to acquire SAP clearance (SAP not required to start)
  • Up to 25% travel

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience in electronics integration and testing
  • Familiarity with common industry standards related to digital communications, such as MIL-STD-1553, RS-422, RS-232, IPv4/IPv6
  • Experience with Networking infrastructure at both the Layer 2 and Layer 3
  • Active DoD Top Secret security clearance and active SAP clearance
