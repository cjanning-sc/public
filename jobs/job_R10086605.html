Northrop Grumman Defense Systems is seeking a Principal Engineer Systems. This position will be located in Orlando, FL and will support our Ground Satellite Communications Systems (GSCS) Operating Unit (OU) design, development, delivery, and sustainment of next-generation, enterprise-level satellite ground systems and software

Roles and Responsibilities

Performs technical planning, system integration, verification and validation, cost and risk, and supportability and effectiveness analyses for total systems. Analyses are performed at all levels of total system product to include: concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal. Ensures the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints. Performs functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications.

Position is Contingent awaiting contract award

Basic Qualifications

  -- Bachelors and 5 years' experience OR Masters and 3 years' experience

   -- Experience working in an Agile environment

   -- Technical writing experience

   -- Working knowledge of systems engineering principles and processes

  -- Ability to pass an Office of Personnel Management (OPM) background check for High Risk Services contract

--Ability to obtain and maintain a public trust clearance

Preferred Qualifications

   -- Familiarity with Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) and associated tools (eg, Cameo)

   -- Experience in developing, managing, and decomposing requirements

   -- Experience developing system architecture or CONOPs

   -- Experience developing ICDs

   -- Experience with DOORS, Confluence, and JIRA

   -- Experience with satellite ground station systems

   -- Experience comprehending and analyzing complex problems, developing solutions, and making decisions that impact the organization’s credibility, operations and services.

   -- Experience interpreting numerical data for reporting purposes and preparing and analyzing data and figures

   -- Experience performing trade analysis as a function of performance, SWaP, and network topology

-- Experience developing RMA products: FRACAS, FMECA, Reliability Block Diagrams (RBDs) Probabilistic Reliability Analysis (PRA), Fault Tree Analysis
