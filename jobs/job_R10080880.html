Northrop Grumman Space Systems has an opening for a Planning Control Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This individual will be part of a team of Material Control Analysts, developing and deploying specialized knowledge and skills necessary to facilitate material procurement, tracking and reporting.  This position will be located in Dulles, VA or Gilbert, AZ. Some hybrid teleworking options may be available.

Roles and responsibilities will include, but not be limited to:

  • Providing material management and analysis support of complex programs.
  • Coordinating and supporting development of material requirements among multiple subsystem Control Account Managers (CAMs) and programs.
  • Developing program integrated Bills of Material, support definition and validation of program material requirements, and support material/production planning to enable the efficient approval of purchase requisitions.
  • Interacting with Engineering, Purchasing, Stock Room, Receiving Inspection, Operations, and other departments to provide accurate status of material availability requirements for meeting program integration schedules.
  • Developing subject matter expertise in the Material and Subcontract Management aspects of the Earned Value Management System guidelines.
  • Supporting program Earned Value Management processes through development and maintenance of material cost and schedule forecasts from initial planning through execution.
  • Developing baseline cost and schedule plans, including definition of material work packages and planning packages including material categorization and assignment of applicable earned value measurement techniques.
  • Supporting integration of material requirements into program integrated Master Schedules.
  • Providing reporting of program material status and analysis of material variances to plan and other applicable metrics.
  • Implementing internal and customer driven budget changes and contract change implementation.
  • Providing process training and support internal and external EVMS surveillance.
  • Supporting Material Management and Accounting and Government Property Management System processes through development of subject matter expertise, delivery of training, and oversight of material related business and procurement processes to ensure conformance with program contractual requirements.
