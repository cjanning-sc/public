ACME Engineering Space Systems is currently seeking a Staff Molding and Winding Process Engineer to support our Lansing Case Value Stream Process Engineering team at our Lansing, Michigan Plant.

Role Description & Responsibilities:
•    Developing, testing, and improving mold, WEI, and wind manufacturing processes
•    Supporting rocket motor case machining processes on the shop floor
•    Performing cause and corrective action activities to permanently eliminate defects using root cause analysis tools
•    Working with concurrent engineering team to create engineering, planning and other process support documents
•    Authoring, updating and reviewing shop floor planning
•    Identifying tooling needs and working with tooling group to design, qualify, and implement new tools
•    Preparing and presenting technical presentations to the VSET, leadership, review boards, and customers for review and approval
•    Performing Peer and Table Top reviews for various processes
•    Using SPC tools to ensure process control
•    Troubleshooting production/tooling/facility issues and implementing improvements and solutions

Education/Certifications
•    B.S. degree in Mechanical, Aerospace Engineering, or other technical engineering discipline with 14 years’ experience or M.S. degree with 12 years’ experience.

Skills & Qualifications:
•    Molding, WEI and Winding experience (hands-on or engineering experience)
•    Model based engineering
•    Self-starter who takes initiative to complete tasks and assignments with little supervision 
•    Good problem-solving skills and strong oral and written communication skills
•    Good organization and multi-tasking skills
•    Experience in lean manufacturing techniques
•    Basic Computer skills (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
•    Experience interpreting drawings, solid models, and specifications 
•    Experience writing shop planning (preferred)
•    Strong interpersonal and communication skills
•    Process Controls (SPC)
