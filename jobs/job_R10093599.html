Strategic Space Systems Division has an opening for a Sub-Project Manager / Electronics Engineering Manager 2) to support the Sub-Project Manager team within the Module Assembly and Test Center (MA&T). This position is located in Dayton, OH.

The selected individual is responsible for cost and schedule performance and execution for specific hardware package within the Module Assembly and Test Center (MA&T).  MA&T is a world class facility capable of assembling and testing a wide array of microelectronic modules from DC to W-Band for space, air and ground applications.  The MA&T department handles all operations from kitting through final screens.  Processes include: component attach using conductive/non-conductive epoxy, wire and ribbon bonding, eutectic mount, laser sealing, seam sealing, RF & DC Tune & Test, and MIL-STD-883 screens.

The selected individual will be the Point of contact for manufacturing through entire product life cycle on that package; will participate in proposal, IDRs, fab, assembly, test, through delivery; will have demonstrated ability to lead and provide direction to a technical cross functional team and exceptional communication skills are required. The individual shall demonstrate leadership qualities and ability to motivate and engage cross discipline teams to execute efficiently.   Candidate has advanced knowledge with Module Production procedures and manufacturing processes.  Candidate will set up a program from beginning to end and plan and coordinate a schedule with all of the required organizations including material. 

​In this role you:

  • Task manager of cross functional team of Engineers and exempt employees

  • Serves as the primary interface to programs for cost, schedule, and technical performance.

  • Accountable for cost and schedule variance and Earned Value methodology. 

  • Participates in proposal writing, proposal review, design reviews, module assembly issues, and module test issues from product inception through delivery.

  • Leads various root cause investigation teams.

  • Oversees as Cost Account Manager packages of ~$30M of sales.
