Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems is seeking a Software Engineer/Principal Software Engineer in Oklahoma City, OK.  The successful candidate will be assigned to develop and deliver software products for various classified programs.


This position involves maintaining current implementation of software pipeline automation, configuration, and execution, as well as integration of new capabilities as required and metrics generation. It will require being able to pick up new tools and technology quickly, knowledge of software development processes, and knowledge of software testing.

This position may be filled at either the Software Engineer OR Principal Software Engineer level.

Basic Requirements for Software Engineer:
• Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field with 2 years of Software engineering experience; OR a Master's Degree in a STEM field with 0 years of experience.
• Basic C++ OR Python knowledge
• Ability to obtain and maintain Secret Clearance AND ability to obtain and maintain Program Access (PAR). Secret clearance and PAR must be obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.


Basic Requirements for Principal Software Engineer:
• Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field with 5 years of Software engineering experience; OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field with 3 years of Software engineering experience; OR a PhD in a STEM field with 0 years of experience.
• C++ OR Python knowledge
• Ability to obtain and maintain Secret Clearance AND ability to obtain and maintain Program Access (PAR). Secret clearance and PAR must be obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

These positions require reliable means to get to work on time every workday - taxis or ridesharing services not permitted. Worksite is not readily accessible by public transportation.
