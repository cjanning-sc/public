We have an opening for a Digital Architecture Lead to join our qualified, diverse team in Columbus, CA.

What you’ll get to do?

The individual will support the Digital Architecture Lead and will be used to analyze the system design and enable technical trades to be performed at various levels.  Generate Digital Engineering artifacts to inform, define, and run engineering processes and to present data for technical cost, schedule, and performance.  This project will heavily emphasize state-of-the-art Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE), maintaining all requirements, descriptive models, analytical models, cost models, and management and support functions in one common, integrated suite of MBSE tools. 

Job duties include:  refining MBSE standards, style guides, and conventions; coordinating  MBSE infrastructure development; assisting MBSE change management and version control; communicating MBSE processes among teams, organizations, and management; creating and maintaining SysML models in Cameo EA; liaising with subject matter experts, analysts, architects, designers, system engineers, specialty engineers, and management to capture structure and behavior using accurate SysML representations according to OMG standards.
