About Your Opportunity:


As a Principal Cyber Engineer within our Active Cyber account, you will lead at the forefront of technical innovation and acting as a role model for our junior engineers to aspire to.

In today’s ever uncertain world, Northrop Grumman are successfully helping our customer’s meet their challenges head on in helping keep the United Kingdom secure.

Using your broad experience you will lead delivery of successful technically complex software projects, by being hands on and guiding a multi-disciplined and skilled team. Furthermore, you will concurrently champion your specific areas of expertise in workforce development and wider third-party assurance of critically important operational projects delivered by your colleagues and peers.

Your Benefits:

  • Flexible working schedules - we offer flexible and hybrid working arrangements and full time and part time roles. Talk to us at the application stage about any scheduling preferences you may have.

  • Flexible Benefits Package – choose which NGUKL benefits you want to satisfy your personal needs. Core Benefits provided for you are Healthcare, Dental, Life Assurance and Pension. Benefits you can flex include Critical Illness Cover, Health Cash Plan, and Health Assessments.

  • Employee Incentive Programme – exceptional performance is recognized through our annual incentive programme which is awarded to top performers who excel

  • Career Development – opportunity for ongoing professional development and career growth opportunities

Your Responsibilities:

  • Technical Leadership across the Cyber Engineering Workforce.

  • Informing and influencing customer’s technical strategic decisions.

  • Providing technical guidance to business development bids realising our UK strategy.

  • Internal assurance, ensuring commonality of technologies and validating our QA/QC processes.

  • Successful hands on delivery of high-assurance complex software and/or AWS cloud projects.


Your Experience:

  • Proficiency across a variety of languages. Our main focus remains on C++, C and Python, however, demonstrable aptitude to adapt to new languages is welcomed.

  • In-depth experience of leading agile software development teams, embracing DevSecOps to enable high assurance deliverables maximising the utility of well-defined CI/CD pipelines.

  • Eagerness to tackle complex integration issues when faced with legacy or black box systems, using your problem solving ability and experience to deliver sustainable solutions.

  • Excellent communication skills and stakeholder management to retain open dialogue with customers and assist in informing/influencing project trajectory and milestones.

  • Demonstrable agility, where you are keen to continue developing yourself in areas outside of your comfort zone.


Desirable:

  • Knowledge of modern desktop or mobile operating system internals.

  • Experience in embedded systems and RF communications. If you have experience in these areas, we are eager to hear from you.

  • AWS cloud certifications, having delivered secure and assured networked solutions.

  •  Previous experience working with the UK Government and/or MOD.
