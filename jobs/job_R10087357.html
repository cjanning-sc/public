You will need a strong understanding of the basic principles of statics, flexible body dynamics, finite element method, and mechanics of materials, strength analysis, stress, kinematics, and mass properties.

Development of structural dynamics design and analysis using advanced numerical solutions, establishing parameters for dynamics testing, dynamics test monitoring and data processing, correlation of test results to model predictions, and preparation of technical documents and presentations.   • Experience in structural dynamic analysis and testing of aerospace hardware
