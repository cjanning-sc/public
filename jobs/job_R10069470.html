Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is an industry-leading provider for prime satellite and payload capabilities and directed energy and electronics solutions for national security, military, and civil customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible.
 

Northrop Grumman Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) has an opening for a Structural Design, Analysis & Integration (SDAI) Section Manager - 2. This position will be located in Redondo Beach, California.
 

As a Manager, you will lead a group of approximately 15 engineers responsible for the structural design, analysis, or routing and installation of mechanical subsystems and mechanical design integration ranging from T01 to T06 levels. You will provide support to multiple space programs in both unclassified and classified environments.
 

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Provide leadership and direction to team members
  • Ensure members have sufficient statements of work
  • Manage employee performance (including engagement strategies for higher performers and corrective actions for lower performers)
  • Coordinate with programs to ensure appropriate staffing/de-staffing actions are developed and executed relative to their section
  • Ensure employees are familiar with, and use required engineering processes and tools.
  • Resolve technical challenges on programs involving section members.
  • Make decisions regarding required escalation and resources required for resolution.
  • Responsible for day-to-day operation of the organization including employee movement and balancing of resources among programs; and resolving critical/urgent program/technical issues.
     

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) discipline with 5 years of space design, analysis, subsystems design and integration experience, or mechanical design integration space experience.
  • Experience with industry standard design or analysis tools such as: CATIA V5, NX, Teamcenter, Engineering Central and Geometric Dimensioning & Tolerance applications (GD&T), FEMAP, Nastran, Enovia
  • Candidate must be able to obtain and maintain an active DoD Secret or Top Secret clearance with the ability to obtain and maintain an SCI clearance
     

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Prior functional management experience or engineering team lead experience
  • Master’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering or Aerospace Engineering
  • Excellent communicator and work well in a team environment
  • Active Top Secret and/or SCI clearance with an SSBI within the last 6 years.
