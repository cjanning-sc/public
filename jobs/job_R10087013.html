Support Operations and Maintenance by providing proactive and reactive maintenance on large antenna platforms and associated equipment in the generation of Radio Frequency (RF) signals and communications systems. Maintain, service and repair tracking antennas, antenna drive trains, gear boxes/motors, motor controllers/servo amplifiers, synchros, encoders, RF switches, and feeds. Troubleshoot and repair Traveling Wave Tube (TWT) and Solid State amplifiers, custom RF switching units, fiber optic transmitters and receivers and other electronic signal generation and verification equipment. Operate and use test equipment to include: spectrum analyzer, oscilloscope, power meter, signal generator, pulse generator, multi-meter, Digital Volt Meter (DVM), fiber optic testers, Network Analyzer, Peak Power Analyzer. Responsibilities include analyzing equipment problems, isolating causes and taking proper actions to resolve problems. Perform preventive maintenance and integrate approved equipment modifications into the existing customer baseline.

- Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite and technical writing skills
