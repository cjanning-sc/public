ACME Engineering Defense Systems sector (NGDS) is seeking a Systems Engineer or Principal Systems Engineer to join our growing team of diverse professionals.  The selected candidate will be motivated, energetic, and willing to learn.  The candidate will work on a small team supporting the Mission Planning and Decision Support Operating Unit (OU) where they will support Agile development of cloud-based solutions in the areas of User Story development, UI/UX development, external system interface design, system architecture and design, system development, integration, and test.

Daily responsibilities include:

  • Support development of system architecture and design
  • Support development of User Stories and Acceptance Criteria for the mission planning products
  • Support development of Interface Design between mission planning products and external systems
  • Support iterative development of UI/UX concepts and designs with customer feedback
  • Ensure the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints.
  • Support functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications. 
  • May include travel to other locations

This position is open as a dual level to be filled as either a Systems Engineer or a Principal Systems Engineer per the qualifications below.

Basic Qualifications for Systems Engineer:

  • One of the following:
    • Bachelor’s degree in a technical engineering or related discipline with a minimum of 2 years of related engineering experience
    • Master’s degree in a technical engineering or related discipline
  • Active U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) Secret security clearance investigated within the last 5 years
  • Ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance
  • Experience working in Agile software development environment
  • Experience with Azure Cloud infrastructure technology
  • Ability to work on-site in Elgin (IL), Philadelphia (PA), or Dayton (OH)

Basic Qualifications for Principal Systems Engineer:

  • One of the following:
    • Bachelor’s degree in a technical engineering or related discipline with a minimum of 5 years of related engineering experience
    • Master’s degree in a technical engineering or related discipline with a minimum of 3 years of related engineering experience
  • Active DoD Secret security clearance investigated within the last 5 years
  • Ability to obtain and maintain SAP clearance
  • Experience working in Agile software development environment
  • Experience with Azure Cloud infrastructure technology
  • Experience with containerization, including Docker and Kubernetes
  • Ability to work on-site in Elgin (IL), Philadelphia (PA), or Dayton (OH)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD SAP Clearance
  • Experience with containerization, including Docker and Kubernetes
  • Exposure to Infrastructure as Code (Iac) development
  • Experience with UI/UX development
  • Experience with MBSE Tools
  • Experience in scripting/programming languages such as Python, PowerShell, Visual Basic, .Net, Java, C, and C#
  • Knowledge of military mission planning systems
  • Work well in a fast-paced small team environment
