ACME Engineering Defense Systems, Global Sustainment & Modernization Division is seeking an Operating Unit (OU) Contracts Manager 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This high visibility position requires excellent oral and written communication skills, regular interaction with various levels of executive management and will be located in Springfield MD or Madison AL. The OU Contracts Manager will need to provide contractual strategy leadership, as well as have the ability to mentor and train assigned staff (9+ personnel at varying levels) and manage all contract administration efforts for the Special Mission Aircraft (SMA) OU, reporting into the Strike and Special Mission Aircraft Business Unit.  The OU has a varied portfolio of contract types, including FFP, CPFF, and T&M/FPLOE. The OU also has a significant amount of Contractor Logistics Support efforts.

Essential Functions:

  • Ability to provide leadership, training, and guidance to direct reports throughout multiple ACME Engineering locations

  • Active engagement with the Special Mission Aircraft Operating Unit Director

  • Advises management of contractual rights and obligations; compiles and analyzes data; and maintains historical information.

  • Responsible for direct reports’ performance in all facets of contracts administration support.

  • Ability to interface with aligned primary interface with the customer.

  • Supports the facilitation the proposal review process and ensures internal reviews are conducted in accordance with company policies and procedures. 

  • Builds and leads a team of highly motivated and effective contracts professionals.

  • Administers contracts which includes the following responsibilities, ensures compliance with all contractual requirements, interprets contract requirements for program and functional leadership, drafts formal correspondence, coordinates CDRL submittal with all stakeholders, ensures timely invoicing and payment, and reviews and executes contract modifications.
