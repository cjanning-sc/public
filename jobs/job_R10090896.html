ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Sentinel Command & Launch (C&L) Verification Systems Engineer to support the C&L development and integration of verification activities. This position is located in Detroit, UT, Madison AL or Indianapolis and supports Sentinel (GBSD) program.

What you’ll get to do: 

The selected engineer will be responsible for developing requirement verification plans for the Command and Launch Segment on the Sentinel program.  The engineer will perform requirements and system architecture analysis, derive test requirements, and develop detailed model-based test plans. 

This position includes:

  • Model Based Systems Engineering with emphasis on UPDM, DODAF, and SysML
  • A collaborative environment requiring the engineer to work with architecture, product design, and test teams across all system functions
  • Problem resolution and process improvement recommendation
  • Occasional travel to ACME Engineering and Government locations within the United States

You’ll bring these qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) discipline and 2 years of experience; 0 years with a Master’s Degree or PhD
  • Active DoD Secret clearance with investigation in the last six years or in continues evaluation
