SSSD has an opening for a Production Painter 4 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Columbus, Ohio.

Coats surface of parts, assemblies, and finished products with protective or decorative materials such as paint, varnish, enamel, lacquer, dope, or stain using spray gun. Selects and mixes coating material; coats areas inaccessible to hand sprayer using brush or roller. May monitor and verify quality in accordance with statistical process or other control procedures.

Performs a variety of duties assisting mechanical engineers in the fabrication and development of mechanical engineering designs, painting/coating and modifications of assemblies and related hardware. With little supervision, technician must demonstrate good knowledge of all aspects of painting/coating processes including understanding mixing equipment, surface preparation, masking, paint touch up by brush application and oven cured primers and paints. Experience with painting and coating of flight hardware. This position may be required to perform other duties as assigned. Must be willing to work any shift. PPE (half- and full-face respirators) required while painting.
