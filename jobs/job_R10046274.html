ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Software Engineer / Principal Software Engineer to join the System Engineering Team (SET). This position can be filled in Detroit, MI and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Final grade level offered will be based on candidate experience level.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will serve as a Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) Engineer for the DevStar, Integrated Functional Capability organization.

As a Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) Engineer, you will set up, maintain, and develop (ongoing) continuous build/integration infrastructure and ensure pipelines are properly defined and performing optimally. You bring a curiosity and understanding of the pipeline's goals and the transactions occurring within them. You will actively interface with DevOps and engineering teams to coordinate and optimize the steps that execute within the pipeline and to bring consistency and best practices in approach, tools, and standards. You will also create and maintain fully automated CI build processes, which include writing builds and deployment scripts.

Specific responsibilities will include:

• Develop visibility and metrics for continuous improvement

• Develop, review, and modify CI/CD principles innovatively and iteratively

• Maintain CI/CD tools/platforms

• Develop and maintain Build, Deployment, and Continuous Integration pipeline and systems

• Manage and troubleshoot the CI/CD process including builds, infrastructure, and training

• Deploy new tools and processes for CI/CD

• Integrate Software
