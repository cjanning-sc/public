ACME Engineering Mission Systems is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security.  Our organization is chartered with providing the skills and innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter.

We are looking for you to join our team as an Electrical Engineer based out of Rockford, MD. As an Electrical Engineer at ACME Engineering, you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation. Through the use of Model-based Engineering, DevSecOps and Agile practices we continue to evolve how we deliver critical national defense products and capabilities for the warfighter. Our success is grounded in our ability to embrace change, move quickly and continuously drive innovation. The successful candidate will be collaborative, open, transparent, and team-oriented with a focus on team empowerment & shared responsibility, flexibility, continuous learning, and a culture of automation.

What You'll Get to Do:

We are seeking a motivated Electrical Engineer to join a dedicated, highly engaged hardware engineering team.  You will have the opportunity to support a variety of unique electrical, electronic, and electromechanical systems designed for maritime applications.  We are specifically looking for candidates that have experience with electric motors and/or marine propulsion systems.

Experience in the following disciplines is required:

  • Electric Motors and Motor Controllers
  • Battery and energy storage, as it relates to powering electric motors
  • Motor control and telemetry, along with associated system communications
  • Connectors & Cabling
  • Electrical/electronic modeling utilizing MATLAB/Simulink, or related software

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Power and propulsion system modeling and integration
  • Developing robust specifications for vendors, as well as overseeing vendors
  • Generating formal engineering documentation (drawings, procedures, etc.)
  • Hands-on integration, testing and troubleshooting
  • Able to apply extensive technical expertise, yet have knowledge of other related disciplines
  • Able to communicate effectively and clearly present technical approaches and findings

Basic Qualifications for a Senior Principal Electrical Engineer:

  • B.S. in Electrical Engineering or a related field with 9 years of experience, a Master's degree with 7 years of experience, or 4 years with a PhD in a related field.
  • Experience with electric motors and motor controllers
  • Experience with batteries and/or energy storage systems
  • Experience in electrical/electronic modeling utilizing MATLAB/Simulink, or related software
  • Eligibility to obtain a security clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with marine propulsion systems
  • Technical writing skills, to include procedures and technical manuals
  • Experience with analog and digital circuit design and layout
  • Experience designing, integrating and testing power conditioning and battery circuits
  • Strong familiarity with underwater systems, including cabling and connector interfaces
  • Professional background on systems deployed on Naval platforms
  • Active or Current DoD security clearance
