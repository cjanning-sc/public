Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Principal / Senior Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Sentinel/Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

What you’ll get to do: 

  • Develop and report metrics on engineering design readiness.
  • Work with multiple Integrated Product Teams (IPTs) to understand the metrics and improve the product.
  • Maintain the integrity of the requirements database through governance.
  • Work with tools to extract and report data from our databases to leadership and customers.
  • Identifying gaps within our processes to enable stakeholders to fix issues.
  • Brief status regularly to key stakeholders.
  • Develop processes related to governance and discover ways to automate metrics being pulled from various databases.

Basic Qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either a Principal level or level Sr. Principal level.

  • Bachelor's degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics)
  • A minimum 5 years of related experience with a Bachelor’s degree; 3 years with a Master’s; or 0 years with PhD. (Sr. Principal level requires 9 years with a Bachelor’s degree; 7 years with a Master’s degree; or 4 years with a PhD)
  • U.S. Citizenship with an active DoD Secret Clearance with an investigation date within the last 6 years
  • Ability to obtain Special Access Program Clearance
  • Minimum 3 years utilizing Systems Engineering skills
  • Minimum 1 year of experience with Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) and Requirement management
  • Experience with Dynamic Object-Oriented Requirement System (DOORS)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with ICBM programs
  • Extensive MBSE experience (3+ years)
  • Experience with CAMEO, Tableau, Microsoft SQL Server  tools
  • Experience with software languages (C+, python, DXL,  other related languages) -  DXL is the DOORS script language
  • Experience with Agile Developments
  • Experience developing procedures
  • Good leadership and communication skills with the ability to brief Segment, Program and Customer leadership
