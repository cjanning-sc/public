Systems Engineer /Principal Systems Engineer
R10051981
1st Shift (United States of America)

The Melbourne Manned Aircraft Design (MAD) Center of Excellence (CoE) is seeking a Systems Engineer/ Principal Systems Engineer under Systems Engineering to support key program/s in the Aeronautics Systems Sector (NGAS). The selected candidate will perform system engineering activities for all levels of a total system product, including concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, and maintenance. This position will be located in Melbourne, FL.

Essential Functions:

·    Performs systems integration, and verification for total systems.

·    Performs functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation, and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications.

·    Support system requirements development, management, analysis, and traceability, to translate customer requirements and CONOPS into systems requirements, and further allocate to lower level requirements.

·    Generation and updating of System and sub-system level specifications in DOORS, including creation of Verification Cross Reference Matrices (VCRM) and verification traceability (tracing requirements to test plans/procedures/results)

·    Define and execute overall system verification plan and associated products for FCA. Coordinate verification with sub-systems to ensure proper verification roll-up. Manage overall verification products in support of system sell-off

This is a dual level requisition that can be offered at either the Systems Engineer OR Principal Systems Engineer title and level based upon the chosen candidate's education, experience, and skillset.

Basic Qualifications:

·     (Engineer Systems) Bachelor's Degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) discipline with 2 years experience OR Masters Degree in STEM discipline.

·     (Principal Engineer Systems) Bachelor's Degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) discipline with 5 years experience OR 3 years experience with a Masters Degree in STEM discipline

·     Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance.

·     Must have an active DoD Secret clearance or higher (with a background investigation completed within the last 6 years or currently enrolled into Continuous Evaluation)

Preferred Qualifications:

·    Experience with system engineering tools such as IBM Rational DOORS

·    First-hand experience with requirements management

·    Experience with verification of system requirements

·    Familiarity with system documents such as System/Subsystem Specifications, Verification Cross Reference Matrices (VCRM), etc.

·    Experience with Agile / Scrum / DevOps
