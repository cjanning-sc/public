ACME Engineering is seeking Production Technicians for an internship to hire opportunity. This position will be located at our Mission Systems Sector in Milwaukee, WI.

The qualified candidate will become part of ACME Engineering’s Manufacturing and Production Organization. The selected candidate will work in a project environment where he/she will interact with users, customers, and other developers to define the Mission Systems procedures for the quality of production and manufacturing operations.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Use precision measuring instruments to ensure conformance to specified tolerance.
  • Assemble precision electromechanical products such as gyros and gear train mechanisms.
  • Performs tasks such as drilling, reaming, burnishing, and tapping
  • Assembles, disassembles, repairs, and reassembles precision assemblies and corrects malfunctions.
  • May assemble and modifies prototype precision units for engineering personnel.
  • May monitor and verify quality in accordance with statistical process or other control procedures.

Basic Qualifications

A candidate, regardless of age and hiring source must meet ALL of the below criteria. The candidate must:

  • Be a student who is enrolled full-time and pursuing an associate degree from an accredited college/university
  • Have an overall cumulative GPA of 3.0/4.0 or higher (unofficial academic transcripts must be provided at time of application by uploading the documents to your application or profile)
  • Be majoring in Electronics Technician, Manufacturing, Mechanical Technician, Machining, or any other closely related discipline
  • Be available to work part-time (20 - 40 hours per week) for 12 weeks during the fall/spring semesters  
  • U.S. Citizenship is required

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience in a manufacturing environment 
  • Experience with small assemblies requiring high dexterity
  • Self-motivated, willing to learn, and interested in working in a team environment

Intern Pay Range:

  • Associate’s: $17.00 to $18.50

At ACME Engineering, our internship pay rates and standardized and take into account three main factors: the anticipated graduation date of the individual, the cost of labor of the geographic location, and whether the internship is considered technical or administrative.
