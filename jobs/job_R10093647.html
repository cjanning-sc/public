Northrop Grumman Space Systems is currently seeking an Associate/Engineer Systems to join the Launch Vehicles business unit to support the Sentinel Program in our Chandler, AZ office.


The selected candidate will work to maintain the architectural model, manage subsystem/component/assembly level requirements, participate in verification/validation planning, and support of integration and test. This position will require candidates to work with a team of systems engineers from a broad array of technical backgrounds as well as component design IPTs responsible for hardware development and integration activities. To succeed in this role the ideal candidate should bring a positive attitude, enjoy collaboration across teams and disciplines, and showcase a strong technical background.  Candidate should have strong technical skills and interpersonal skills with project experience to demonstrate that these requirements are met.

 
System Engineer Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Requirements Management

  • Architecture Management

  • Risk/Opportunity Management

  • Test Planning, Coordination, Execution

  • Verification Planning

  • System Verification Execution

This position may be offered at the Associate Engineer Systems Level 1 or may be offered at the Engineer Systems Level 2.

  • Bachelor of Science in STEM Engineering plus 0 years of experience.
