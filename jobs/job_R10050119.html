Northrop Grumman Mission Systems (NGMS) is seeking Associate Systems Engineering, Integration and Test (SEIT) engineers at our Rolling Meadows / Arlington Heights, IL location.  The selected candidates will report to the SEIT Department in the Infrared Countermeasures (IRCM) Department.

In Integration & Test, you will conduct integration testing and evaluate complex electronics systems from hardware/software design through aircraft installation.  Responsibilities for an Integration & Test Engineer may include generation of test plans, test procedures, test automation, test reports, and execution of integration test activities in System Integration Labs (SILs) or offsite locations.

As a part of the Systems Engineering Integration & Test (SEIT) team, an Integration & Test engineer gains an understanding of the Systems Engineering process across a product's lifecycle and gains a full appreciation for how the systems operate. Your key responsibilities will be to ensure that the systems we make work – and work well.   You’ll be able to apply your knowledge and experience in EOIR systems, optics, detection and tracking.

Basic Qualifications for Systems Engineer:

  • BS degree in Electrical Engineering, Systems Engineering, or related technical degree with 0 plus years of related experience.
  • Demonstrated proficiency in analytical thinking, problem solving skills and Systems Thinking mindset
  • Excellent decision making and communication (written and oral) skills
  • Strong work ethic and the ability to work in an integrated product team (IPT) environment
  • Must have ability to obtain Interim Secret clearance prior to start date. Once started, must have the ability to obtain full secret clearance and SAP clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Working knowledge of one of more of the following specialty areas for integration and test related to EOIR systems, optics, detection and tracking
  • Experience in an integration and test environment for complex electronic systems evaluation
  • Experience performing system verification and validation
  • Recent experience creating integration and test, design verification test and flight test plans and procedures
  • Electronic Warfare experience
  • Experience in development and earned value management of cost accounts and schedules as related to Systems Engineering tasks
  • Knowledge of MATLAB, C, C++, Java, Python, or other scripting languages
  • Working knowledge of DOORS and ClearCase/ClearQuest
  • Agile development experience a plus
  • Ability and willingness to travel 25%
  • Active Secret or Top Secret clearance and the ability to obtain a SAP (Special Access Program) clearance
