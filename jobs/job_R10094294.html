The qualified candidate will provide Mission Assurance consultation and guidance to program leadership to identify risks, tailor process requirements, and share best practices.  They will lead mission assurance assessments of process quality, supplier quality, and escalate critical or recurring issues to appropriate levels of management.  The selected individual(s) must be self-starters, have experience in risk management, root cause analysis/corrective action and process mapping/process building.  They will be familiar with continuous improvement methodologies such as Lean, Six Sigma, and Agile.  They must be strongly self-directed and result driven accustomed to working autonomously and with teams.  

Additional Responsibilities include:

  • Understand the requirements of a company policy, standard or process, and determine how best to apply it to quality/ compliance review recognizing when requirements are not being satisfactorily met and have the courage to challenge upward. 

  • Audit systems and processes for deficiency identification and lead implementation of corrective action ensuring that all corrective measures are effective and timely.  

  • Partner with program leadership to foster effective teaming and continuous improvement.

  • Facilitate problem resolution, root cause analysis and operational efficiencies applying best practices/lessons learned.

  • Assist with supplier quality, service management and escalate critical and/or recurring issues to appropriate levels of management. 

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree and 9 years of relevant experience OR Master's Degree and 7 years of relevant experience (additional experience will be considered in lieu of education).

  • Strong leadership, assertiveness, facilitation, interpersonal and virtual communication skills. Comfortable working with senior leadership and driving actions to completion. 

  • Self-directed and result driven team member accustomed to working autonomously and with teams to facilitate solutions. 

  • Experienced problem solver comfortable leading process decomposition/process mapping, problem definition, root cause analysis and risk management including maintenance of risk registries.

  • Experience in compliance auditing of systems processes. 

  • Experience with creating and maintaining process owner documents (plans, procedures, work instructions, training bulletins, templates, workflows, etc.)

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a security clearance. 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Science, Technology, Engineering or Math (STEM) related discipline and 8 years of relevant professional experience.

  • Knowledge of Project Management life-cycle principles

  • Lean Six Sigma Trained or Certified

  • Experience with Agile framework

  • Experience in large software deployments including 

  • ITIL Training/Certification

  • Experience as a project manager
