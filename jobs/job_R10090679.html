Overview

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking Manager of Software Engineering 3. The selected person or this position will be the section lead of a Software team in the Software and Digital Technologies functional organization. Position requires being on-site at NG office in Bethpage, NY; hybrid work environment.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • This role will focus on two primary areas of responsibility: team leadership and management, and team technical performance
  • Responsibilities include the oversight and addressing of cost, schedule, and technical quality for large system development contracts as well as software performance on major systems integration-type contracts
  • There is additional functional oversight of approximately 80+ software engineers of varied disciplines working multiple programs, IRAD initiatives, and new business capture opportunities with strategic importance for significant downstream value
  • Also responsible for staffing and workload forecast; support and adherence to standard software processes; Agile Framework; DevSecOps; lessons learned; and employee engagement, mentoring and career development, including training and identification and training of skills required to execute on programs
  • This includes the recruiting, sourcing, onboarding, and contract maintenance for the contractors working in the business area
  • Work closely with the directors and managers of other engineering disciplines, program directors, and program managers
  • Other duties as required

Basic Qualifications:

  • Typically requires 14+ Years with Bachelors in Science; 12 Years with Masters; 9 Years with PhD.
  • Expertise in embedded C/C++ Software development, and SDLC
  • Experience in Agile, and DevSecOps
  • Previous experience managing large-scale SW development projects, and 8+ direct reports
  • Demonstrate strong leadership skills and will have a track record as an effective communicator and problem solver who is able to develop and maintain good working relationships with internal and external stakeholders.
  • Able to demonstrate a broad skill set that is balanced among interpersonal, business, and technical capabilities
  • Demonstrate the ability to operate in a team environment and collaborate across the sector business areas as required to accomplish the team goals
  • US Citizenship and an active Security clearance, Secret or higher

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience in the electromagnetic maneuver warfare domain
  • Experience managing a function team, and 1st level managers
  • Experience in proposal writing, and Basis of Estimates (BOE)
  • Active TS Security clearance with SCI eligibility
