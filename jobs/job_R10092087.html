Northrop Grumman’s Space Systems sector is seeking a Sr Principal Systems Engineer Product Design Team Lead on the Command and Launch System Integration team.  This position is in our Strategic Deterrent Systems Division to execute the Engineering and Manufacturing Development phase of the Sentinel Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program in support of the United States Air Force.  This position will be located on campus in Roy, UT and may require on-site support at product manufacturing and integration sites.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

The qualified candidate will work within an Integrated Product Team (IPT) environment, interfacing with IPT leadership, suppliers, customers, and other technical disciplines to engineer design solutions to challenging problems through the development of Product Definition Packages (PDP). PDPs consists of 2D drawings and 3D models (NX), various design analyses/assessments (structural/thermal), bill of materials and parts lists (ASME format), and requirements (DOORS).

Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Serve as the main focal point to develop and baseline the developed PDPs
  • Drive the development of the System Integration owned the PDPs by utilizing the PDP development engineering team
  • Manage the tasking and workload for the PDP team ensuring work products are delivered on time and adequate support is provide to the various engineering teams for successful integration
  • Develop collector, assembly, and installation PDPs owned by the System Integration team
  • Implement program processes and engineering board approval processes for developed PDPs
  • Deliver required PDPs on time to meet program objectives
  • Integrate System Integration PDPs with the various element teams to proactively eliminate disconnects and design clashes
  • Identify gaps and holes within the design and/or requirements and provide a path forward for resolution that is cost effective, innovative, and has minimal impact to others while filling the gap/hole
  • Ensure the developed PDPs meet the design requirements from the various element/configured item specifications
  • Determine technical solution in accordance with product specifications and recommend actions to representatives for coordinated product solution
  • Support digital transformation initiatives relating to PDP development and release
  • Provide oversight and reviews of System Integration PDPs to drive quality and compliance to both technical and formatting requirements
  • Provide status and communicate blockers to IPT leadership
