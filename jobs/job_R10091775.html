ACME Engineering Mission Systems (NGMS) is looking for you to join our Undersea Systems Manufacturing and Test team as a Test Department Manager in Rockford, Illinois.  In Rockford, our technical and business leaders develop and manufacture advanced underwater systems, sensors, and vehicles designed to excel in volatile and ever-changing undersea environments.

The Test Department Manager will supervise Test Engineers and Test Technicians who perform test and evaluation of underwater acoustic sensors and systems, and electrical, mechanical, fiber optic, and electro-mechanical assemblies, sub systems and systems. These assemblies include potted and oil filled cables and acoustic sensors for undersea use, bonded acoustic sensor arrays, chassis and vessel wiring, dry cable soldering and wiring, and hydraulic and coolant components and systems (test and cleaning of).

The Test Department Manager will manage exempt and non-exempt employees performing test and test engineering functions and is responsible for the direct supervision/management of the staff. The ideal candidate will have experience with electrical and mechanical testing and ESD controls.

What You'll Get to Do:

  • Direct the work of employees, support safety initiatives, and train/cross train employees.   
  • Provide leadership direction of process development and implementation; as well as department compliance.
  • Monitor adherence to master plans and schedules, cost performance to budgets, identify process problems and develop solutions. 
  • Manage and present status on cost, schedule and performance for projects within their area. 
  • Utilize SAP and Material Requirements Planning (MRP) systems

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree plus 9 years of relevant experience; or Master’s degree plus 7 years of experience 
  • Developmental environment and/or production experience 
  • Experience with electrical and/or mechanical testing 
  • Experience leading a team and/or a project 
  • U.S. citizenship with the ability to obtain and maintain a Department of Defense Secret security clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current, active Secret clearance
  • Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering or related STEM discipline
  • Operations or Engineering Program Management experience and/or experience supervising non-exempt and exempt employees
  • Underwater acoustics testing experience
  • Fiber optics experience
  • Hydrostatic pressure testing experience
