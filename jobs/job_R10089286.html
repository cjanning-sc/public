ACME Engineering's Strategic Deterrent Division (SDSD)located in Detroit Michigan is seeking a Principal/Senior Principal Program Planning and Scheduling Earned Value Management EVMS Analyst 3/4 to support the Sentinel and other Programs. This EVMS Analyst will report directly to the Division EVM Manager and assist them in their role as trusted partners with the Division programs and their Business Management Teams. This Division Team is responsible for Earned Value Management training and mentoring as well as ensuring EV compliance and implementation of Earned Value guidelines.

This EVMS Analyst will be responsible to support surveillance events including reviewing DCMA Earned Value Compliance Metrics (DECM) analysis results with Strategic Deterrent programs. This role also includes coaching, training and mentoring individuals in compliant implementation of Earned Value rules and processes. In addition, this team reviews program artifacts and reporting.

This role requires the candidate to have or be able to secure an active Department of Defense (DoD) Top Secret (TS) and/or eligibility to acquire a Sensitive Compartmentalized Information (SCI) Clearance.

This position will be primarily located in Detroit, Michigan at the beautiful new ACME Engineering Detroit Innovation Center however, partial telework is available.

Responsibilities include:

  • Provide support for program cost and schedule analysts on implementation of EVM practices and established program processes
  • Provide EVMS training opportunities for all appropriate program personnel including Control Account Managers (CAMs)
  • Analyze program data and artifacts for compliance to the corporate standard
  • Review and analyze program schedules and scheduling practices including DCMA 14-Point Assessment, Schedule Risk Assessment (SRA), Critical Path Analysis, and Schedule Health Metrics
  • Assist in EVM compliant program set up and start-up
  • Analyze and review EVMS-DECM Metrics and provide analysis and guidance to programs
  • Support of Program Reviews, and IBRs

Basic Qualifications:

  • An active DoD TS and/or SCI Clearance or ability to secure one
  • Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited university with 3+ years (Level 3)/7+ years (Level 4) of a combination Business, Finance, or related field experience that includes program cost control and planning OR a master’s degree from an accredited university with 2+ years (Level 3)/6+ years (Level 4) of a combination in Business, Finance, or related experience
  • Working knowledge of program management including EVM Systems, and cost and schedule management
  • Experience in auditing compliance including data review and analysis
  • Expert proficiency in Microsoft Office tools (Excel, Word and PowerPoint)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Database access and data analysis
  • Experience working in MS Power BI
  • Experience training groups
  • Cobra (Cost Management Tool) Experience
  • Experience working in scheduling tools e.g. Primavera, MS Project Server
  • Current or previous experience working with restricted customers
