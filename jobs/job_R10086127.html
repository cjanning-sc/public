ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Project Manager. This position will be located in Detroit, MI and will support the Sentinel program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What you’ll get to do: 

  • Complete requirements analysis and data entry as required.

  • Serve as an overall integration function for the program engaging in many ad hoc activities required to ensure streamlined cadence for program operations and execution.

  • Collaborate across all functions within the program to develop advanced concepts, techniques and requirements for new applications and tools.

  • Coordinate schedules, requirements, and performance visibility to both the Program Office and the Customer while maintaining quality, cost, and schedule requirements.

  • Facilitate internal and external meetings to support the program and represent program management in appropriate program reviews or boards.

  • Identify and mitigate project risks throughout project life cycle.

  • Support and generate proposal documentation as needed to foster new work.

  • Develop and deliver high level briefings to Program Leadership and/or customer.

  • Provide solutions to complex problems that are consistent with the organization’s objectives.

  • Identify opportunities for change and champion needed changes in the organization.


 

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80A. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off.

  • 3 years of experience with a Bachelor's Degree; OR 1 Year with Masters. Four additional years of experience may be substituted in lieu of a degree. 

  • Must have U.S. Citizenship  with a current or active Secret level clearance.

  • Experience in project management.
