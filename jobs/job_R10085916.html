Installs, tests, troubleshoots and repairs aircraft avionics and electrical system components in accordance with testing, engineering, maintenance and installation directives. Avionics technician will support on and off-aircraft troubleshooting, maintenance and operational check-out of the Adaptable Communications Suite (ACS) system. Technician will directly assist AF maintainers and pilots to perform repairs, isolate problems, remove components and turn in for contractor maintenance. Must follow established procedures for handling classified and COMSEC equipment, materials and computer systems. Will coordinate efforts and communicate problems with a team of maintainers, pilots, engineers and logistics analysts to address system problems and capture failure causes and trends. Once hired, can successfully complete all tasks identified within the ACS training plan.
