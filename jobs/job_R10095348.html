Northrop Grumman is currently seeking a Mission Assurance Engineer to provide support to our Case Manufacturing Quality Team for our Clearfield location. (Must be able to obtain Secret Clearance)

  • Role description & Responsibilities:

  • Day-to-day activities will include side-by-side participation with operations, engineering, and inspection personnel to ensure high quality product and processes while removing non-value added waste.

  • Will work in an industrial environment and support solving complex engineering problems. Will effectively interact with Design engineering teams, Operations, Materials and Process experts, Process engineering, and Tool engineering to provide support across the Case Value Stream.

  • Provide and support nonconformance analysis for product and processes.

  • Concur with material review board dispositions for product non-conformances. Lead and participate in non-conformance investigations to analyze and determine root cause, define corrective actions, preventative actions, and ensure corrective/preventive action implementation and effectiveness Coordinate, identify part non-conformance history, and communicate trends. Participate in program and quality assurance reviews.

  • Track and report monthly quality performance metrics

  • Train or assist with training of new hires, and refresher training of current technicians

  • Review aerospace drawings and specifications and have sol id requirement interpretations

  • Routinely coordinates technical issues with various programs and customers. Able to work independently, be a self-starter, and capable of Multi-tasking

  • Have strong problem solving skills and be a critical thinker, - Have excellent computer, presentation, and communication skills.

  • Focus on developing, establishing and maintaining Mission Assurance policies and procedures, processes and controls thereby ensuring both performance and quality of manufactured products conform to established company, international (AS9100), and governmental requirements, as well as lasting customer satisfaction. Ensure customer requirements are our first priority

  • Understand the design intent, failure modes, requirements and specifications of the product as well as the manufacturing processes, process controls, statistics, SPC, as well as first article and quality inspections.

  • Provide expertise and guidance in interpreting governmental regulations, agency guidlines and internal policies/procedures to assure compliance and will serve as liaison between the Case Quality Team and various other organizations including some governmental agencies.

  • Work directly with operating entities to ensure that inspections, statistical process control analyses and audits are conducted on a continuing basis as specified to enforce requirements, meet specifications and improve processing. Conduct internal audits of processes and procedures and act as an escort for internal and external auditors
