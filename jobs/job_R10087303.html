ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking an EHS Coordinator 4. This position will be located at Santa Maria, OH with the majority time being spent supporting activities on Vandenberg Space Force Base (VSFB), OH, in support of the Strategic Deterrent Systems (SDS) Division.

What You’ll Get to Do:
• Protect the Company and your fellow employees from loss and injury due to accidents.
• Interpret, recommend, and comply with environmental, health and safety regulations and company policies.
• Responsible for reinforcing site and/or program safety protocols, maintaining documentation, ensuring compliance with OSHA and ACME Engineering policies and procedures
• Monitoring of the facility for safety hazards and responding accordingly.  May be directed as needed to support site safety and operations.
• Conduct inspections, audits, and incident investigations, and drive corrective actions.
• Organize and equip spill-control teams. 
• Prepare applications for any new required EPA permits and renewals.
 

Basic Qualifications
• Bachelors degree with 2 years’ experience or High School diploma or equivalent with 6 years’ experience
• Ability to leverage experience/background to bring alternative ideas/solutions to the team.
• Ability to Obtain and Maintain DOD Security Clearance and program access, as required.
 

Preferred Qualifications
• In-scope active DOD Clearance Secret Level or Above
• Experience supporting ACME Engineering or government contracting/DoD facilities
• Experience in an Aerospace Manufacturing and Test environment
• Experience in Hazardous Waste/Materials Management and Shipping
• Demonstrated ability execute in accordance with an Ammunition and Explosive (A&E) site plans
• EM 385-1-1 Experience is a plus
