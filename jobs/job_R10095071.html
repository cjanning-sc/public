Associate Industrial Security Analyst
R10095071
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking an Associate Industrial Security Analyst in our Redondo Beach, CA location.

The responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:

  • Develops, and administers security programs and procedures for classified or proprietary materials, documents, and equipment.
  • Studies and implements federal security regulations that apply to company operations.
  • Obtains rulings, interpretations, and acceptable deviations for compliance with regulations from government agencies.
  • Prepares manuals outlining regulations, and establishes procedures for handling, storing, and keeping records, and for granting personnel and visitors access to restricted records and materials.
  •  Conducts security education classes and security audits.
  • Investigates security violations and prepares reports specifying preventive action to be taken.

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree. An additional 4 years of applicable work experience may be substituted for Bachelor’s degree.
  • Candidate must be able to obtain a final U.S. security clearance at the Secret level or higher.
  • Candidate must be able to obtain and maintain appropriate Eligibility and access of program supported.
  • Must be proficient in Microsoft Office applications such as Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook
  • Must have excellent oral and written communication skills.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Working knowledge of NISPOM
  • Experience with DD Form 254s
  • Experience with inventory management and tracking
  • Experience with Security Information Management System (SIMS) Software
  • Current, active Top Secret security clearance is preferred
