ACME Engineering Defense Systems is currently seeking a Capture Management Manager 3 that will report to the Director of Advanced Weapons Strategy & Business Development and will perform the duties of Business Development Lead for various opportunities. 

This position can be located in any of the following geographical areas:

* Eglin AFB, FL

* Nellis, NV

* Waterloo, VA

The Business Development lead will work closely with the Advanced Weapons BD & Strategy Director, various Program Managers and lead engineers to provide the company with new business development briefings.

Job Responsibilities

  • Work with the both the Advanced Warfare Systems (AWS) & Advanced Programs (AP) operating units and Program teams to develop customer relationships with existing and new customers, including senior company and government agency leaders to ensure products and company are well known and respected.
  • Support capture managers on various pursuits; lead customer relationship development (US Government customer) to understand need, budget, timelines, and competitor capability.
  • Assemble and execute new AP business strategies in line with the current business overarching strategy.
  • Make strategic adjustments to the AP Business Development Plans as necessary to adapt to product updates and evolving customer’s interest.
  • Provide inputs to internal M&S, B&P and IRAD resource allocation decisions based on knowledge of future customer needs.
  • Support Proposal Preparation, Review and Approval Process.
  • Develop and Perform to a Strategic Plan including multi-year sales and marketing strategic plans that identify key products and markets that will enable the company to achieve its goals of growth and profitability.
  • Create, update, and maintain SalesForce records to support new business opportunity pursuits, and gate reviews.
  • Build and execute marketing and communications plans to support the specific opportunities.
  • Regularly report to the company’s Management Team the status of all major Advanced Programs opportunities assigned, and the status toward achieving the awards plan.

Required Education and Experience

  • Bachelor’s Degree in STEM related field, General Business or Marketing.
  • Ten years of overall experience in the aerospace/defense industry or Air Force service.
  • Strong background in DoD weapons programs.
  • Must hold (or have recently held and can reestablish) a DoD Secret Clearance.
  • Proven experience working with the Weapons Schools ( USAF Fighter Weapons School, HAVOC, and TOPGUN)

Preferred Education and Experience

  • Master’s Degree in a STEM related field, General Business or Marketing.
  • 10+ years of overall experience in the aerospace/defense industry or US Navy or Air Force service.
  • 5 years experience executing missions for Suppression of Enemy Air Defenses (SEAD) or Destruction of Enemy Air Defenses (DEAD), or other weapons engagement missions.
  • Recent retirement from the US Air Force (within the last 6 months)
  • Current DoD Top Secret clearance
  • Knowledge of the Business Acquisition Process or similar new business opportunity process.
  • Proven experience working multi service user communities on adoption of new weapons programs.
