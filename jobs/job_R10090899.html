ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for Manager, Programs 3 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in our Columbus, Ohio facility.

In this role, the selected candidate should have experience with suppliers and helping suppliers be successful. Leading large group discussions or short seminars. Solving strategic as well as detailed tactical problems within your team or for suppliers.

The selected individual will be responsible for acquisition strategies and execution of contracted work to include the bid and proposal process and working with the business manager to finalize negotiation strategies and closing, in a favorable way, for ACME Engineering Corporation.

The F-35 Support Equipment (SE) Program Manager is responsible for Profit and Loss (P&L) of the Support Equipment portfolio and leads all aspects of program execution. SE, also known as ground support equipment, includes an extensive variety of equipment and tools required to launch, recover, maintain, and repair the F35 aircraft. The SE PM will be responsible for the end-to-end management of SE including new item development, contract acquisition, program execution, quality, and delivery management, as well as the sustainment of SE which includes repairs, spares, calibration, etc. The SE PM will drive business capture and portfolio growth. Ensure program execution objectives are achieved in the areas of quality, schedule, cost, margin, and cash as well as customer satisfaction. Manage and lead technical teams charged with improving designs to enable increased reliability, utility, and affordability.

The SE PM will also be responsible for partnering with the Global Supply Chain (GSC) function to manage a large supply chain. The SE PM will serve as a key interface to the customer and end user as well as suppliers and sub-tier suppliers. The SE PM will be required to understand and manage risk and drive outcomes in alignment with program and company objectives. The SE PM will need key communication skills to lead cross-functional teams and effectively communicate with program and division leadership. Execute innovative Product Support program plans that focus on enabling mission success; development of operator/maintenance training, operational support, domestic and international site activation plans.

Key Tasks Include:

  • Managing the material baseline
  • Owning delivered quality, being responsible to ensure equipment works in the field
  • Managing the reports from users and feeding information back to suppliers for real time end item improvement
  • Leading the team and being responsible for their personal and professional success
  • Supporting the Director of Sustainment, act as DOA if necessary
  • A passion for sortie generation and the support equipment that contributes to mission success
     

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree with 9 years of experience OR a master’s degree with 7 years’ experience in either Supplier, Program, and/or Business management
  • Experience managing technical baselines as well as understanding internal (make) vs. buy strategies.
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret security clearance within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science or Master of Science degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math (STEM) discipline
  • The ability to obtain and maintain DoD Secret clearance must be obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.
  • Current SCI clearance
