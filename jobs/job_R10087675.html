Material Process Engineer / Principal Material Process Engineer - 3/10, 1st shift

ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Material Process Engineer or a Principal Material Process Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in Cincinnati, CA within the Cincinnati Aircraft Integration Center of Excellence (PAICoE) organization.

This position is a 3-10, 1st shift position (workdays - Friday, Saturday, Sunday). You will work 30 hours but get paid for a 40-hour work week.

Join ACME Engineering on our continued mission to push the boundaries of possible across land, sea, air, space, and cyberspace. Enjoy a culture where your voice is valued and start contributing to our team of passionate professionals providing real-life solutions to our world’s biggest challenges. We take pride in creating purposeful work and allowing our employees to grow and achieve their goals every day by Defining Possible. With our competitive pay and comprehensive benefits, we have the right opportunities to fit your life and launch your career today.

The selected candidate will work in a team environment with other engineering organizations performing detailed technical reviews of drawings, specifications and manufacturing plans. You will be responsible for analyzing, researching, and developing materials and their related application processes for use in engineering design of and/or application in structures, systems and subsystems, and also also apply principles of chemistry, physics, and material science to investigate material issues and provide solutions.

Basic Qualifications for a Material Process Engineer:

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Chemistry, Material Science or other STEM degree with 2+ years of relevant experience (Master’s degree may count towards an additional 2 years of experience).
  • Experience working with composite pre-pregs, honeycombs, and adhesive materials; Fabrication, assembly, and repair processes for aircraft composite structures.
  • Must have an active DoD Secret clearance (with a background investigation completed within the last 6 years or currently enrolled into Continuous Evaluation).
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to the commencement of employment.

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Material Process Engineer:

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Chemistry, Material Science or other STEM degree with 5+ years of relevant experience (Master’s degree may count towards an additional 2 years of experience).
  • Experience working with composite pre-pregs, honeycombs, and adhesive materials; Fabrication, assembly, and repair processes for aircraft composite structures.
  • Must have an active DoD Secret clearance (with a background investigation completed within the last 6 years or currently enrolled into Continuous Evaluation).
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to the commencement of employment.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced degree in Engineering, Chemistry, or Material Science
  • Relevant experience with working on military aircraft
  • Experience with preparing and presenting oral and written reports
  • Knowledge in material testing and lab equipment
  • Experience in writing and maintaining material and process specifications
