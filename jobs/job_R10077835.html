Develops, modifies, applies and maintains quality evaluation and control systems and protocols for processing materials into partially finished or finished materials product. Collaborates with engineering and manufacturing functions to ensure quality standards are in place. Devises and implements methods and procedures for inspecting, testing and evaluating the precision and accuracy of products and production equipment. Designs and analyzes inspection and testing processes, mechanisms and equipment; conducts quality assurance tests; and performs statistical analysis to assess the cost of and determine the responsibility for, products or materials that do not meet required standards and specifications. Audits quality systems for deficiency identification and correction. Ensures that corrective measures meet acceptable reliability standards and that documentation is compliant with requirements. May specialize in the areas of design, incoming material, production control, product evaluation and reliability, inventory control and/or research and development as they apply to product or process quality. May be certified in lean and six-sigma quality engineering methodologies.

This role will manage our Supplier Quality Team. This team includes a total of 50 plus direct reports derived of six different disciplines. They include Supplier Quality Engineering (SQE) - Electrical, SQE - Propulsion Ordnance and Pneumatics, SQE - Machined Hardware, Supplier Quality Systems, Suppliers supporting our Advanced Design Group products, and Receiving Inspection. Manager will be responsible to ensure that we meet all customer quality requirements, including commercial and government contracts. Manager will ensure that all Quality metrics are measured and monitored and report progress on a monthly basis. These metrics will include items such as Supplier Hardware Acceptance Review statuses, Product Escapes, First Articles statuses, Drawing reviews/approvals, non conformance reporting, corrective action reporting, continuous improvement activities, and performance review management. This function will also hold the key role to represent Launch Vehicles division in supporting all corporate Supply Chain/Supplier Quality Initiatives. The manager selected will interface with our Supplier Quality Mission Assurance group to ensure that new programs select suppliers balancing past quality data along with company value to meet our company's overall metrics.

Minimum of 9 year experience with Bachelors degree. 7 years with Masters degree
