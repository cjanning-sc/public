ACME Engineering is seeking a Compliance Desktop Support Technician 3 - CMMC.  This position will be located at our Space Systems Sector in Columbus, Ohio.

The position is responsible for ensuring that the computers in Materials & Science Laboratories department operate in compliance with all applicable security regulations.  The successful CMMC Compliance Desktop Support Technician 3 will be capable and comfortable working independently, collaboratively, and proactively with customers and stakeholders. The successful candidate will have the skills to evaluate and solve a broad range of IT challenges, both technical and compliance driven. This individual must have effective communication and social skills and be able to interface with a variety of disciplines. 

Responsibilities:

  • Ensuring CMMC compliance of laboratory computers

  • Working with scientists and engineers to troubleshoot IT issues and interface with other IT personnel across the company

  • Effectively communicating with stakeholders at all levels
