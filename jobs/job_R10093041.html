We are seeking a Test Engineer to support operations in our Oxnard, CA location. Occasional visits to the Goleta, CA site will be required throughout various times of the year based on site project goals & needs. This position is available at the Northrop Grumman facility at Oxnard/Goleta CA  

Overview

Reporting to the Test Engineering Manager, the Principal Test Engineer will be responsible for creating and implementing test plans, test requirements, test tooling, test setup and execution procedures, as well as performing post-test data processing and reporting to ensure product contractual and derived requirements. The qualified candidate will have ownership of their work through all phases of product development, production, testing, and integration supporting internal and external teams as required.

 

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Leadership of product testing, working in close collaboration with all parts of the Operating Unit for the design, development, planning & writing of test plans, test requirements, and test setup & execution procedures.
  • Leadership of test tooling, software, and instrumentation design, development, and selection.
  • Support test data collection, post-test data review, reporting, and test requirements verification.
  • Technical writing to produce test documentation.
  • Lead, participate, and present in internal and external (customer) reviews.
  • Leverage modern tools and methods to reduce cost and schedule of product testing.
  • Accountable for management and tracking of multiple priorities while maintaining a focus towards common project goals of the program team, matrix organization, and customers.
  • Accountable to the Operating Unit Test Engineering Manager for meeting the Test Engineering safety, quality, delivery and cost expectations of internal and external customers within entire product value streams.

 

Basic Qualifications for a level 2:

  • 2 years of relevant experience with a Bachelor's degree; 0 years of relevant experience with a Masters' degree; an additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of degree
  • Minimum of two (2) years of experience in testing, systems engineering, mechanical design, or other applicable experience
  • Demonstrated technical writing and/or procedure development skills
  • Demonstrated mechanical computer aided design (CAD) software skills
  • Ability to travel between the Oxnard, CA and Goleta, CA sites and travel to support customers
  • US Citizenship is a bona fide requirement

 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 3+ years test engineering experience in the aerospace/aeronautics industry
  • Demonstrated proficiency in test automation software (i.e., LabVIEW or other) for product testing
  • Demonstrated proficiency in measuring and controlling various electronic components such as motors, actuators, positional sensing devices, load transducers, etc.
  • Demonstrated problem solving skills with hands-on project experience
  • Excellent communication skills – presentation, oral, written, and listening
  • Self-motivated with the ability to organize and prioritize job assignments and tasks
  • Ability to work independently as well as in an integrated team and adapt to change and dynamic requirements
  • Working knowledge of applicable manufacturing processes such as machining, finishing, and rapid prototyping
  • Experience Leading Teams.
