SSSD has an opening for a Tool Cutter Grinder 4 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be in Columbus, Ohio.

The ideal candidate will have experience performing various types of precision grinding responsibilities which may include but are not limited to the following:

Must be capable of manufacturing complex close tolerance and grinding material such as Tungsten, carbide, tool steel, etc. Must be able to grind with diamond, ceramic, and Silicon aluminum oxide grinding wheels and dress and operate both Madison Tool grinder and Monoset Tool & cutter grinder.

Candidate must be able to setup grinding machines to grind and sharpen drills, milling cutters, reamers, broaches, tool bits, and punches. Must be able to modify tools involving special contours, compound angles, and radii to the required specifications.

The applicant should also be well versed in Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerance per ANSI 14.5M 1982/1994 specifications to interpret actual position and tolerance requirements. Also, to be able to work by written instructions and sketches.

  • AA\AS Degree plus five years of experience as a tool cutter grinder or High School Diploma/GED and ten years of experience as a tool cutter grinder.

  • Experience in setup and producing parts with minimum supervision
  • Experience in operating tool grinders and monoset grinders with needed attachments.
  • Experience in setup an Optical comparator and inspecting tools.

  • Proven ability to work in a team environment.
