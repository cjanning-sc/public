Northrop Grumman is seeking a responsible Custodian to maintain our facilities at our Promontory Utah facilities.

The external and internal appearance of a building reflects on the occupants. It is important for a custodian, or janitor, to be careful and thorough in working, cleaning and tidying the premises. The ideal candidate will be experienced in a custodian role focused on building cleaning and upkeep. They will have the physical endurance required to cover a large space. A keen eye for detail and diligence are also imperative in custodial maintenance jobs.

Responsibilities:

  • Ensure spaces are prepared for the next day by emptying and cleaning trash receptacles, tidying furniture and dusting surfaces

  • Sweep and mop floors and vacuum carpets

  • Wash and sanitize toilets, sinks and showers and restock disposables (e.g. soap, sanitary products etc.)

  • Wipe mirrors and windows

  • Cleaning entrances, including glass doors and floors

  • Report any safety, sanitary, or fire hazards to the appropriate department

  • Other duties as assigned.

Requirements:

  • Previous custodial experience required

  • Knowledge of use of industrial cleaning equipment and appliances

  • Possess working knowledge of the methods, technique, and materials used in custodial work

  • Knowledge of safe use and disposal of chemical liquids and other hazardous components

  • Attention to detail and conscientiousness

  • Ability to climb ladders and work from heights

  • Ability to understand and follow verbal and written instructions

  • Ability to lift and carry up to 50 pounds

  • Good physical condition and strength

Education/Experience Requirements:

  • High school diploma or GED required

  • Able to obtain a DOD clearance “Secret” level

  • 1-2 years custodial work experience
