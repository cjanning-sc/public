Northrop Grumman is in need of a Principal or Sr. Principal Research Scientist to work at its Rocket Center, West Virginia location.

Position Summary:

  • Manage solid rocket propellant and explosive/warhead development from subscale through production
  • Formulate and tailor both existing and novel compositions of: propellants, explosives, rocket case bond liners, thermal rubber insulations, and adhesives
  • Proactively interact within cross-functional program teams as a materials expert
  • Interface with internal and external customers
  • Design experiments and perform statistical based analysis of physical and chemical technical data
  • Document work in technical reports, prepare presentations, and effectively communicate results to technical peers, program teams, and customers
  • Perform duties within industry specific regulations regarding safety, product and material requirements, and plant operations standards
  • Take responsibility for assuring quality, cost effectiveness, and timely completion of projects
  • Perform technical planning, cost estimation, and general proposal support for new business captures
