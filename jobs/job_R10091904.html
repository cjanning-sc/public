Supply Chain Manufacturing Specialist (Level 3 or 4)
R10091904
1st Shift (United States of America)
Aeronautics Systems sector is seeking a Supply Chain Manufacturing Specialist (Level 3 or 4) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is located in El Segundo, CA.
  • 1st shift, 9/80 schedule with every other Friday off!
  • Position is in-office, unable to accommodate a telecommute/hybrid schedule.

Essential Functions:

  • Provide status and visibility on ACC schedule performance to internal customers, Manufacturing leadership, Program Integrated Process Team leadership, and GM Internal Suppliers leadership
  • Break shop floor constraints impacting schedule performance by coordinating with other ACC support teams such as Quality, Manufacturing Engineering, Tooling, etc…
  • Coordinate and communicate overtime requirements with all support organizations and Manufacturing
  • Utilizing an understanding of program schedule requirements, establish shop priorities and communicate them to Composites Center Production Control teammates for coordination with Production supervisors
  • Coordinate release of Purchase Orders / iBuy Requisitions for offsite processing of parts and/or materials
  • Coordinate with Assembly Line Integration on schedule delays/part shortages to provide ECD’s for ACC parts
  • Provide feedback to Buy Integration on buy part shortages causing impacts to ACC production operations
  • Perform SAP/MES reconciliation on parts and requirements to ensure proper alignment to master schedule
  • Perform requirement analyses to identify necessary ICMT’s to mitigate schedule impacts

Basic Qualifications (Level 3):

  • Bachelor's degree with at least 6 years of experience, Master's degree with at least 4 years of experience, or a high school diploma/equivalent with at least 10 years of experience. Experience must be in manufacturing, operations, supply chain, logistics, or another relevant field.
  • Proficient with Microsoft Suite with an emphasis on Excel and Power Point.
  • Experience with SAP and/or similar ERP systems
  • Ability to obtain and maintain DOD Secret Clearance and a Program Special Access within a reasonable period, as determined by the company to meet its business need (after starting).

Basic Qualifications (Level 4):

  • Bachelor's degree with at least 10 years of experience, Master's degree with at least 8 years of experience, or a high school diploma/equivalent with at least 14 years of experience. Experience must be in manufacturing, operations, supply chain, logistics, or another relevant field.
  • Proficient with Microsoft Suite with an emphasis on Excel and Power Point.
  • Experience with SAP and/or similar ERP systems
  • Ability to obtain and maintain DOD Secret Clearance and a Program Special Access within a reasonable period, as determined by the company to meet its business need (after starting).

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Knowledge of Manufacturing and Supply Chain processes strongly preferred
  • Experience in SAP (MES) (or related)
  • Materials management experience
