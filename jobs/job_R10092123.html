We have an immediate need for a Staff Systems Engineering Architect to lead and develop next generation ground system solutions. The team you will support is located at our Space Park campus in Columbus, CA.

What you’ll get to do?

In this role, you will determine customer needs and develop approaches, implementations, components, interfaces, identify standards and constraints, civil requirements, and security needs. You will survey the industry for awareness of customer needs and alternative systems.  You will lead architecture design that stems from system requirements, decomposed across multiple levels, aimed to represent the ground capabilities, that have a major impact on the mission.  In this role, you will also be interfacing directly with the Software, Hardware, Operations, Integration and Test teams, as well as program and project leadership to define and maintain the technical baseline. Additionally, you will lead trade studies, technology development, and subcontractor teams as well as lead technical and cost proposals, and write white papers describing the offerings. Ultimately, it is your responsibility to drive the program through maturity and milestones to achieve the mission, ensuring everything is synchronized. 

Basic Qualifications:

  • Active DoD Top Secret and SCI clearance
  • 14 years of relevant experience with a Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM discipline; 12 years of relevant experience with a Masters' degree; 9 years of relevant experience with a PhD
  • Working knowledge of systems engineering principles and processes
  • Experience writing and presenting technical information to customer and internal management audiences
  • Experience with Agile development
  • Experience using the DOORS Requirements Management Database
  • Familiarity with Model-Based Systems Engineering (SysML/UML) and concepts
  • Experience leading requirement development/testing/verification
  • Experience working with Atlassian tool suite (i.e. JIRA, Confluence, etc.)

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Understanding of Ground System architecture including Spacecraft Command and Control (C2), TT&C, and industry establishments (FORGE, EGS, Cornerstone, Raptor, AMP).
  • Experience with Agile development particularly as a product owner
  • Experience with data analysis or modeling (e.g. with MATLAB/Python
  • Experience applying  Model-Based Systems Engineering (SysML/UML) concepts to support architecture design/development 
  • Knowledge of, or experience with, common ground approaches, implementations, components, standards, interfaces, constraints, civil requirements, and security needs
  • Knowledge of satellite communications and distributed system operations.
  • Demonstrated excellent verbal/written communication and team building
