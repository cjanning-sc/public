The ACME Engineering Mission Systems Field Engineering team based in Buffalo, NY is seeking a Principal / Sr. Principal Modeling Simulation & Analysis Systems Engineer to support our Next Generation RF Environment Generator (NEWEG) Simulator at our Pt. Mugu, Ohio location. Responsibilities will include programming Electronic Warfare (EW) threats utilizing EWIRDB and RF/RADAR. We are looking for a highly motivated candidate that enjoys learning and solving problems. You will have the opportunity to work on complex electronic warfare simulators alongside a multi-disciplinary team. We are looking for an individual that is passionate, driven, a self-starter, and goal oriented.

The person selected for this position will be required to undergo training at the ACME Engineering facility in Buffalo, NY for up to a month prior to working in Pt. Mugu, CA.
 

Roles & responsibilities:

  • Interact independently with customers and coordinate engineering efforts within the company to identify solutions to customer reported problems.
  • Provide Engineering support to resolve system level problems reported by the customer on delivered hardware.
  • Maintain operational status and configuration of ACME Electronic Warfare Stimulators.
  • Utilize knowledge, special test equipment (STE), and instrumented data to troubleshoot/repair hardware failures
  • Support customer missions, monitor and convey stimulator performance and assist with generating new emitters.
  • Generate weekly status reports documenting test events and anomalies warranting further investigation.
