Position requires coordination and collaboration with key stakeholders to include, but not limited to, Contracts, Engineering, Quality, Strategic Sourcing, Planning, Operations and Program Management. Prior hands-on experience as a Buyer of supplies under US Government programs.

  • Performance of Pre-Award Reviews of cross section of files over the Simplified Acquisition Threshold

  • Performs and supports additional spot audits

  • Assists with identifying need for conflicts with lower level PrOP (Division of BU)

  • Assists in identifying/supporting supplemental training needed at Divisions/Bus

  • Supports CPSR Audits
