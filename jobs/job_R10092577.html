Northrop Grumman's Enterprise Server Storage and Networks team is seeking an experienced Network Engineer to perform network configuration and network management efforts, integrating and supporting the current established enterprise network at our Baltimore locations. These efforts constitute significant impact to the business on key programs. The successful candidate will bring in depth technical knowledge of enterprise level experience with managed network equipment.

This individual will represent the organization as a technical contact on multiple projects. Interacts with Team Lead, peers, and our business partner on various technical matters often requiring coordination between organizations.

Responsibilities include detailed engineering plan, design and implementation of network installations in accordance with standard policies and procedures for design review, installation, maintenance, and documentation. Provide support of technical analysis of project requirements, prepare design review package and execution of approved design.  Execution of corrective action to support problem resolution of infrastructure hardware and software elements to ensure availability of firewall infrastructure and components.

Additional Duties include:

  • Manage multiple efforts of short and long-term duration that are of significant influence to the network, storage and server infrastructure at the site. Maintains smooth operation of local area, world wide and virtual private networks (VPN).

  • Plans, evaluates, and coordinates installation and/or reconfiguration of hardware and software elements of data and/or voice communication network. Maintains technical expertise in all areas of networks and computer hardware and software interconnection and interfacing, such as routers, multiplexers, firewalls, hubs, bridges, gateways, etc.

  • May propose solutions to management to ensure all communications requirements based on future needs and current usage, configuring such solutions to optimize cost savings.

  • Prepares or ensures that appropriate network documentation exists, including operational instructions.

  • Provides regular monitoring and network analysis regarding short- and long-range planning for in-house systems.

  • May coordinate third-party maintenance for network equipment.

  • May design network or portion of network that include selection of hardware and software packages

Basic Qualifications

  • Must have a Bachelors degree and at least 5 years of relevant IT professional experience. (Appropriate combination of education and experience will be considered in lieu of the degree requirement)

  • Proven ability to define large-scale project scope, required tasks to achieve stated goal, project deliverable and artifacts definition, project timeline development, scheduling, budget creation and resource management.

  • Demonstrated ability to interface directly with vendors at a high technical level on existing enterprise solutions and new services technical requirements/deliverablesand develop technical solutions to complex problems, requiring the regular use of their ingenuity and creativity.

  • Demonstrated performance in working in a fast-paced environment delivering a high degree of quality and customer satisfaction. 

  • Flexible hours are required. 

Preferred Qualifications

  • Candidate must possess excellent communication skills, both verbal and written, and good interpersonal skills and has a strong willingness to learn.

  • Demonstrated performance in working in a fast-paced environment delivering a high degree of quality and customer satisfaction.
