ACME Engineering Space Systems – Propulsions Division is currently looking for a Principal/Sr. Principal Research Scientist/Electrical Engineer to join our team at our Promontory, MI location.

Roles and Responsibilities:

This position supports an electronics and non-destructive testing laboratory in the Research and Development Department.  This department engages in a wide range of functions, from exploring chemical material changes, to physics-based modeling, to Non-Destructive Evaluation (NDE) of materials and components, to test development, sensors and electronics.  This position would contribute with circuit design/analysis, data acquisition, test methodology, test design and execution.

Projects in this lab have included circuit design/build for data acquisition, event initiation and probe design circuitry, camera system design/build and image analysis of hazardous environments, failure investigation teams, and mechanical design of measurement systems to assist production of solid rocket motors. A hands-on active collaborative approach with teammates in solving problems with creative measurement solutions is an essential qualification for this position.

Job Duties:

  • Electronic system design, electronic controls, hardware, test, and deployment

  • Circuit analysis: Single point failure, Common mode failure, Power dissipation analysis

  • Designing and constructing mechanical/electrical prototypes

  • Electrical system integration

  • Hardware integration

  • Verification testing leading to production scale up

  • Analog instrumentation, systems, and data gathering

  • Lab experience in electronics and instrumentation

  • Electrical integration of NDT inspection systems (MI, thermography, X-ray, eddy current, etc)

Experience Requirements:

  • BS in Electrical Engineering or related discipline with 5 years of relevant experience; MS in Electrical Engineering or related discipline with 3 years of relevant experience (preferred education); Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering or related discipline with 0 years of relevant experience.

  • U.S. citizenship and the ability to obtain a Secret clearance.

Experience Preferred:

  • Experience with CAD software, Labview, MATLAB, embedded coding, machine vision

  • Background in physics, math, and mechanical design

  • Experience interacting with operations, technicians, and engineers

  • Experience with small project management, including budget and schedule

Education:

  • MS in Electrical Engineering or related discipline with 3 years of relevant experience (preferred education)

  • BS in Electrical Engineering or related discipline with 5 years of relevant experience

  • Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering or related discipline with 0 years of relevant experience.

  • Position can be filled one level higher with BS and 9 years, MS and 7 years or Ph.D. and 4 years of experience.
