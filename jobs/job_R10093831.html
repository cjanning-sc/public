ACME Engineering’s Aeronautics Systems Sector is currently hiring a Supply Chain Manager 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse, individuals in the GSC Planning, Requirements & Delivery organization. This position ideally will be located in Palmdale, CA; but may be able to be based in our Akron, CA office.

In this role, the selected candidate will:

  • Ensure vertical and horizontal integration of Global Supply Chain Schedules through strategic planning and execution
  • Provide early visibility into industry constraints and supplier performance hindering our ability to meet/exceed our contractual commitments
  • Integrate metrics requirements for the Requirements Planning Team
  • Communicate program schedule deviations and ensure all impacts are coordinated with internal/external customers prior to implementation and execution
  • Identify industry constraints driving schedule impacts in our supply base and incorporate mitigation plans to strategically partner with our external suppliers to be successful
  • Lead a team of individuals in a dynamic, fast-paced Program environment with responsibilities including rapid requirements execution, standard parts ordering, scrap/spare/replacement planning and serve as a GSC liaison to the Program Operations and GSC Execution IPTs

The successful candidate must be able to:

  • Deal effectively with competing priorities and deadlines and manage multiple tasks in a fast paced environment while demonstrating a professional attitude
  • Demonstrate excellent business acumen, problem solving, communication and organizational skills 
  • Lead and influence internal and external customers
  • Model and promote a strong sense of engagement, teamwork and cooperation

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, and collaborative/team settings across all levels.

As a leader, you always act with integrity, and the highest ethical standards characterize everything you do.
