SSSD has is seeking a Business Management Manager Level 1 or Level 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals.

The Business Management Manager is responsible for providing all aspects of financial planning and control services through a professional staff, including analyzing the portfolio of Operating Unit (OU) financial results, applying accepted accounting methods, controlling to budgets, and generating reports on sales, costs, profits, etc. This role is also responsible for the Cost Management portion of this portfolio.  This position will be located in our Redondo Beach CA offices and will report to the Director of Business Management for Directed Energy and Strategic Products. 

Roles and Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Strategic Planning – Partnership with the Portfolio Program Managers and Functional leadership for the development of the Annual Operating Plan (AOP) for Non-Contractual Technical Activities (NCTA) and Capital
  • Performance Measurement System implementation and program execution, including EVMS compliance under a validated business system
  • Integrate and synchronize a multi-disciplined staff of business and technical leads in support of EVMS compliant program
  • Provide analysis and recommendations to business and program leadership in support of monthly Business Unit (BU) and Division cost/schedule reviews
  • Lead independent estimate at complete (EAC), and baseline reviews
  • Supervise compliance to internal business system procedures and processes in support of program requirements and government EVMS implementation guidelines
  • Lead cost, schedule, and technical risk assessment events to identify and mitigate program risk
  • Develop and implement EVMS process improvements through collaboration with technical, cost, schedule, and program management leadership
  • Interdisciplinary coordination in support of program and department execution requirements
  • Attention to detail to support contract setup and execution in complex contract environment
  • Strong communication and relationship skills with internal, cross-functional colleagues and external customers
  • Collaborate across program leadership to develop business case analysis for investment and portfolio growth 

THIS ROLE CAN BE FILLED AT EITHER A LEVEL 1 OR LEVEL 2.  THE CANDIDATE MUST MEET THE BASIC QUALIFICATIONS AS OUTLINED BELOW. 

This role is a hybrid work from home role. The incumbent selected must work on-site 2 days a week.

  • Bachelor’s degree
  • 5+ years of experience in financial planning/reporting, strategic planning, cost proposals, project execution/management, contracts, pricing and/or budget controls and analysis
  • Strong understanding of government contracting and regulations, to include FAR and CAS
  • Experience with standard business systems (Costpoint/SAP, Cognos/IFAS, Cobra/MPM, MS Excel)
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a TS and/or SCI DoD security clearance
