Systems Engineer [AHT]
R10081283
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman is seeking a motivated professional to join our team as Engineer Systems. The selected candidate will be motivated, energetic, and willing to learn. The candidate will work on a small team supporting the Mission Planning and Decision Support Operating Unit where they will support Agile development of cloud-based solutions in the areas of User Story development, UI/UX development, Cybersecurity, external system interface design, system architecture and design, system development, integration and test.

Daily responsibilities include:

  • Support development of system architecture and design
  • Support development of User Stories and Acceptance Criteria for the mission planning products
  • Support development of Interface Design between mission planning products and external systems
  • Support iterative development of UI/UX concepts and designs with customer feedback
  • Ensure the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints
  • Support functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications. 
  • Contribute to implementation and verification of cybersecurity requirements in a cloud environment
  • May include travel to other locations

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a technical Engineering, Computer Science or related discipline with 2 years of related experience
  • Active DoD Secret Clearance investigated within the last 5 years.
  • Ability to obtain and maintain SAP Clearance
  • Experience working in Agile software development environment
  • Experience in Systems Engineering, e.g., CONOPS development, requirements analysis, decomposition and allocation, interface definition, and trade studies.
  • Familiarity with IA/Cybersecurity practices
  • Ability to work onsite in either Hollywood (MD), Melbourne (FL), Manhattan Beach (CA).

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD SAP Clearance
  • Experience with IA/Cybersecurity and industry platform hardening practices, such as DISA STIG Implementation
  • Current DoD 8570 IAT Level II certification (such as, Security+)
  • Experience with Cloud infrastructure technology such as Azure.
  • Experience with containerization, including Docker and Kubernetes
  • Exposure to Infrastructure as Code (Iac) development
  • Experience with UI/UX development
  • Experience with MBSE Tools
  • Experience in scripting/programming languages such as Python, PowerShell, Visual Basic, .Net, Java, C, and C#
  • Knowledge of military mission planning systems
  • Work well in a fast-paced small team environment
