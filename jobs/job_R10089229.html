The Mission Management / Mission Control (MM/MC) Thrust team is looking for a high performing Sr Principal SW or Systems Engineer to perform as the Principal Investigator (PI) for their Space On-board Systems (SOS)- Mission Execution Enterprise (MExE) Independent Research & Development (IRAD) effort.  MExE covers Mission Control (platform and payloads), Mission Management, and Mission Processing.

Tasks include:

  • Lead cross-functional team through IRAD design/development efforts;
  • Develop initial SOS-to-Ground core architecture (including the Mission Operations (MOPs));
  • Identify initial SOS testbed/environment, including prototyping with high fidelity digital twins and existing COTS/internal frameworks;
  • Perform UX analysis and establish testbed facility to support edge node usage; and Build initial prototypes.
  • PI will oversee a small team to move Ground-based MExE capabilities on-board and provide real-time on-board control of new space-based resources.  The goal of the IRAD is to initiate the transition of MExE functions to SOS.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree with 9 years of related experience; Master's degree with 7 years of related experience; PhD with 4 years of related experience; or an additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of degree
  • Proficiency in software development, software architecture, or systems engineering
  • Current SCI access or current DoD Top Secret clearance with the ability to obtain SCI access

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Previous on-board software architecture and development expertise
  • Previous experience working on an IRAD; self-starter
  • Ability to lead a small team of engineers and collaborate with subject matter experts (SMEs)
  • Demonstrated problem solving and decision-making skills
  • Excellent communication, interpersonal skills, with the ability to interface with all levels of employees and management
