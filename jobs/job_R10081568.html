The Fort Wayne Integrated National Systems (INS) Engineering organization seeks an innovative and motivated Senior Systems Engineering Manager 3 to lead the Systems Engineering, Integration & Test (SEIT) department of 250 engineers in support of ground technology programs and pursuits.  This position will report on-site daily, providing SEIT leadership through a team of Section Managers.  The position reports directly to the Engineering Site Lead in Fort Wayne, Indiana.

The selected candidate will assist the INS engineering organization in multiple activities and requires a broad engineering knowledge base in SEIT and Cyber Engineering.  The ideal candidate will have experience in Chief Engineer and SEIT Lead roles, and a strong background in engineering management, as well as the ability to work collaboratively across several Operating Units.

 Duties and Responsibilities include:

  • Functional oversight of a large engineering organization working multiple programs, IRAD initiatives, and new business capture opportunities

  • Develops a fundamental understanding of technical execution on key programs

  • Understanding internal and external customer needs and roadmaps.

  • Responsible for ensuring process compliance and supporting functional department initiatives.

  • May be called upon to support proposals; program evaluation; and other strategic initiatives.

  • Identify skill gaps within the department and create a training roadmap to improve engineering excellence

  • Staffing and workload forecasting

  • Oversight of recruiting, hiring, and onboarding new and experienced employees

  • Training new section managers

  • Employee inclusion, engagement, and mentoring

  • Performance management and career development

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Strong people leadership skills are required, including the ability to identify and develop critical skill capabilities, support career development, evaluate and manage performance, identify and recognize achievement, and plan staffing support for programs.

  • Strong understanding of SEIT engineering excellence, digital technologies, tools, and processes in a space systems context to establish strong systems engineering rigor on programs. 

  • An active SSBI investigation and the ability to obtain and maintain an active TS/SCI security clearance

  • Bachelor’s degree in a STEM discipline with a minimum of 12+ years of progressive technical experience in engineering.

  • 5+ years of leadership experience in engineering or related technical fields.

  • Strong understanding of the engineering development lifecycle across multiple engineering disciplines (SEIT, SW, HW, O&S)

  • Knowledge and experience with digital transformation initiatives and development tools.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree in a STEM discipline with a minimum of 12 years of progressive technical experience in engineering.

  • Previous experience with satellite payloads and ground systems is strongly preferred.

  • 10+ years of leadership experience in engineering or related technical fields.

  • Understanding of business development and capture strategies, Earned Value Management/Cost Account Management (EVM/CAM), program risk management, and technical, cost, & schedule impacts as it relates to managing engineering projects.
