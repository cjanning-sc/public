ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal/Sr. Principal Systems Engineer - Capture Operations to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

The ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems (NGAS) Systems Engineering Integration (SEI) Integrated Product Team (IPT) is seeking a Principal / Sr Principal Systems Engineer to support Capture activities for an upcoming proposal. The selected candidate will assist the SEI IPT Capture Lead in drafting Capture artifacts, such as Technical and Cost volumes, for a Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) effort. The candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, and collaborative/team settings across all levels.

Essential Functions:
- Act as Capture integrator for the SEI IPT
- Support requirements allocation and interface definition support to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications.
- Performs Control Account Manager (CAM) duties for SEI Integration
- Generates Basis of Estimates (BOEs) and Task Assessments (TAs) to support development of Cost Volume
- Supports Risk/Opportunity Management for SEI IPT, as required
- Communicate effectively and clearly present technical approaches and findings at Capture reviews and meetings  
- Champions NCTA/Capital projects in support of tech development and/or risk reduction
- Applies extensive technical expertise in the development of technical solutions to complex problems which require the regular use of ingenuity and creativity.  
- Provide cross-IPT support for Capture efforts, SSOW/SDRL/CDRL, and trade studies as required
- Work closely with peers and managers of other engineering disciplines, program managers, and IPT leads


This requisition may be filled at either a Principal Systems Engineer level or Sr. Principal Systems Engineer level.

Basic Qualifications:
- The basic qualifications for the Principal Systems Engineer are a bachelor's degree in STEM with 5 years professional/military experience to include a background in systems engineering OR Master's Degree in STEM with 3 years professional/military experience to include a background in systems engineering
- The basic qualifications for the Sr. Principal Systems Engineer are a bachelor's degree in STEM with 9 years professional/military experience to include a background in systems engineering OR Master's Degree in STEM with 7 years professional/military experience to include a background in systems engineering
- Working knowledge of Integrated Master Schedule (IMS) and Integrated Master Plan (IMP) development
- Working knowledge of Microsoft Office applications, including Word, Excel, and PowerPoint
- Must have an active DoD Secret clearance or higher (with a background investigation completed within the last 6 years or currently enrolled into Continuous Evaluation)
- Must adhere to Program security requirements while performing work
- Must be able to effectively communicate with engineers and management from multiple disciplines

Preferred Qualifications: 
- Degree in Systems Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or Electrical Engineering with MBSE experience
- Applied knowledge of aircraft design
- Experience in the military aerospace development environment is preferred.
- Working knowledge of MBSE tools such as Cameo, Magic Draw, Rhapsody, Sparx EA, and/or Enterprise Architect
- Strong understanding of common SE process, experience writing ICD’s
- Applies wide-ranging technical expertise and has knowledge of other related disciplines. 
- Able to communicate effectively and clearly present technical approaches and findings
- Demonstrated ability to work in a diverse, multi-disciplinary team environment. 
- Excellent leadership, communications, and interpersonal skills.  
- Ability to interface with all levels of the workforce and management. 
- Develop technical solutions to complex problems which require the regular use of ingenuity and guides the successful completion of major programs and may function in a project leadership role.  
- Proven experience with team engagement experience in a leadership/Team
- Perform work with no appreciable direction. Exercises considerable latitude in determining technical objectives of assignment.
- Proficiency using Microsoft Office applications, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint and MS Project.
