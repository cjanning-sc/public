About Your Opportunity:

As part of Northrop Grumman’s EMEA (Europe, Middle East and Africa) IT Solutions team working within the Infrastructure team, this role will be a pinnacle part of supporting and developing the EMEA IT infrastructure and service offering. The future growth of this role will encompass the development of systems across on-premise, hosted, and cloud environments. This role includes but not limited to monitoring, troubleshooting of issues, and build out of future IT based solutions maintaining and implementing procedural documentation.

  • Employee Incentive Programme – exceptional performance is recognized through our annual incentive programme which is awarded to top performers who excel
  • Career Development – opportunity for ongoing professional development and career growth opportunities

Your Responsibilities: 

1. Day to day operational activities to install, configure, administer, troubleshoot, and resolve system issues as required

2. Participate as directed by the Infrastructure Manager in on call rosters and other arrangements necessary for the delivery of 24/7 services

3. Ensure BAU activities are accounted for and kept up to date, including tickets, vulnerabilities, and patching

4. Work as part of a team to research, develop, and build upon IT offerings in the business

5. Actively contribute to the training and development of your skills and that of the Service Desk

Your Experience

1. Excellent IT troubleshooting experience in dealing with a wide range of different processes and systems

2. Experience working within a team-oriented, collaborative, distributed environment

3. 3rd line support with architecting experience

4. Windows and Linux operating systems. On-prem and cloud based infrastructure such as Exchange, Azure, and M365

5. Certification such as ITIL, Microsoft, Cloud, Storage, Virtual etc
