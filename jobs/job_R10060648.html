Duties and Responsibilities may Include:

  • Performing structural analysis on aerospace, shipboard, ground based, and space radar systems and structures. 
  • Loads development, stress analysis (static and fatigue), and vibration analysis of metallic and composite structures. 
  • Performing classical structural analysis, as well as, finite element analysis is essential. Utilizing excellent written and verbal communication skills are important for clear communication of results through reports and presentations. 
  • Collaborate in a team environment, mentor less experienced personnel, prioritize and organize tasks to support program milestones.

  • Bachelor of Science in Mechanical, Aerospace, or Civil Engineering or related technical field
  • Typical minimum structural analysis experience / education: 5 years with Bachelor of Science; 3 years with Masters
  • Experience with classical structural and finite element analysis
  • Software experience: Finite Element Analysis (FEA), and Microsoft Office applications
  • Experience developing Finite Element Models (FEM) from CAD geometry
  • U.S. Citizenship with the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance per business requirements
     

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced degree in Mechanical, Aerospace, Civil Engineering or a related field.
  • Active Secret/Top Secret/SCI clearance.
  • Software experience: NX.CAE (SimCenter 3D) / NX.Nastran, Ansys Mechanical, or MSC Patran
  • Experience with DOD and/or FAA program structural load environmental requirements.
  • Previous MRB Experience/ Authority
