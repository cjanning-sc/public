ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Staff Life Cycle Cost Engineer in the Change Integration Group. This position will be located in Detroit, Michigan and will support the GBSD Ground Base Strategic Deterrent program. Learn more about the GBSD program here.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

This position is responsible for performing Life Cycle Cost (LCC), Design to Cost (DTC), Cost as an Independent Variable (CAIV) and Cost Reduction Initiative (CRI) analyses. Analytical tools/approaches leveraged include commercial/Gov’t parametric models, such as PRICE and SEER, as well as custom developed parametrics determining correlation between operational or technical requirements and cost. This position requires experience developing complex cost models using advanced cost estimating techniques as well as excellent interpersonal skills required to solicit input, synthesize data, and communicate results to various levels of leadership. Leadership skills are required to lead and mentor more junior team members and interns. The analysis performed is done in a non-advocacy manner representing the organization as an “honest broker” providing credible cost analysis.
