Sr. Principal Military Trainer - Targeting Facilitator (Fires), Joint Targeting Team (AHT)
R10089688
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Defense Systems sector (NGDS) is seeking a Sr. Principal Military Trainer - Targeting Facilitator (Fires), Joint Targeting Team to join our growing team of diverse professionals at Fort Leavenworth in Kansas.  This position is in support of the Mission Command Training Program (MCTP) and trains Army Corps, Divisions, and Brigades in the Army Mission Command Systems and Operations Process.  MCTP facilitates Army simulation exercises replicating a robust Operating Environment (OE) for units to conduct Large Scale Combat Operations.

Targeting Team:

This team is part of Scenario Design and works with Plans and CUOPS to develop the HICOM targeting plan.  This includes development of the JTL, Joint Integrated Prioritized Target List (JIPTL), and electronic target folders.  The team travels to exercises and will be split between the HICOM and ECG to maintain the appropriate situational awareness and control for the exercises.

  • In planning, serves as part of the Plans Team to develop operational environments, scenarios, and HICOM orders for execution

  • In preparation, the team develop and refines electronic target folders at the Corps (as required), LCC, and JFC level.  This is done using the MIDB, JTT, and the scenario being used

  • In execution, facilitates the targeting process at the HICOM level and above as a core member of the HICOM TWG and TDB IOT ensure the exercise is creating the desired training conditions

  • During the ELC (Exercise Life Cycle), the team serves as a conduit and liaison between Scenario Design, MCTP, and the respective training unit fires sections.  This includes participating and facilitating the Exercise Design Targeting Working Group as appropriate

  • This team works with SDD, Strategic Effects Group, 505th DET 1, 505th CTS, and AJST IOT replicate the holistic fires WfF for the WFX

Responsibilities:

  • Assists in developing Corps / LCC / JFC targeting input to include TST, CCTL, and JIPTL development

  • Develops the HPTL, TSS, AGM, and TSM

  • Assists training audience in nesting targeting efforts at the ASCC, Corps, and Division by overseeing the targeting process

  • Conducts Joint Target List management using MIDB and JTT

  • Provides input to the NSL and RTL

  • Recommend desired effect to achieve JFC level intent and ensures that training objectives are met

  • Assists the training audience on ensuring target nominations and ASR timelines are met

  • Assists in JIPTL development by conducting de facto JTCB

  • Assists MCTP Intel Coordination Division in conducting BDA

  • Updated electronic target folders as required using JTT

Basic Qualifications:

  • One of the following:

    • High school diploma or GED with a minimum of 14 years of professional and/or military experience

    • Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 10 years of professional and/or military experience

    • Master’s degree with a minimum of 8 years of professional and/or military experience

  • U.S. Army Command and General Staff College (CGSC), Warrant Officer ILE, or service equivalent

  • U.S. Military service as a Field Grade Officer (MAJ, LTC, COL) or Warrant Officer Equivalent (WO3, WO4)

  • Familiar with current Army and Joint doctrine

  • Must possess and active U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) Secret security clearance

  • Familiar with AFATADS, JADOCS, JTT, MIDB

  • Must be able to travel as needed (up to 25% of the time)

  • Must be able to work on-site at Fort Leavenworth in Kansas

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 15 years of military experience

  • Mastery of current Army and Joint doctrine

  • Should have experience as an OCT (Observer, Coach, Trainer) and as a WFX participant

  • Experience targeting using MIDB and JTT
