Seeking a Network Engineer to support to a large classified program.  This multi-faceted position will assist with the design, implementation and maintenance of a multitude of systems, and possess technical expertise in all areas of network hardware and interconnection interfaces such are routers, multiplexers, firewalls, hubs, bridges, gateways etc.  Provide support to specific areas such as design, implementation, integration, and testing as well as support of the network development life cycle with peer reviews, documentation and configuration management adhering to the project level process plans. 

This position may be filled at a Principal Engineer Network or Sr Principal Engineer Network level.

Potential for sign-on bonus in addition to base salary.

Basic Qualifications:

  • For a Principal Engineer Network: 5 Years with Bachelor’s degree, 3 years with a Master's degree in a related field; an additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a degree
  • For a Sr. Principal Engineer Network: 9 Years with Bachelors in Science; 7 Years with Masters; 4 Years with PhD; an additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
  • Must possess a DoD 8570 certification at IAT Level II or higher (Security+, GSEC, SCNP, SSCP, CISSP, CISA, GSE, SCNA)
  • Working knowledge of network architecture, engineering, design, implementation, testing, network communication protocols, network routing protocols (BGP, OSPF, and EIGRP) and IP network troubleshooting.
  • Working knowledge of TCP/IP behavior, network communications.
  • Experience with operating monitoring/analysis tools and test equipment (ie Fireberd, Fluke OptiView, HP Openview., WhatsUp Gold, Protocol Analyzer, Oscilloscope, etc).
  • Familiarity with DISA, Commercial, SCN Comm systems and topology
  • Familiarity with cyber security STIGs compliance and Security Standards/Protocols
  • Must be an effective communicator with excellent writing and presentation skills using Microsoft PowerPoint and Visio drawing tools.
  • Must be able to work collaboratively in a team environment with other highly motivated network engineers.
  • Working knowledge of network communication products including router, switches, firewalls, and Domain Name Servers (DNS).
  • Basic system Administration skills/certifications a plus (UNIX, Linux, Windows)
  • Candidate must have a current Top Secret security clearance and the ability to qualify for an SCI to be considered
  • Candidate must be able to obtain a Counterintelligence Polygraph (CI-Poly)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Cisco and/or Juniper certifications such as CCNP or JNCIP
  • Extensive knowledge of Cisco and Juniper networks and firewalls
  • Cyber security certification
  • Knowledge of technical control operating records such as circuit history folders, master station logs, trouble tickets, outage and restoration records.
  • Circuit restoration, fault isolation, quality control testing, trend analysis, performance monitoring, and status reporting experience.
  • Experience using design, drawing, and engineering software (ie AutoCAD, MS Visio, etc)
  • Strong interpersonal/leadership skills and experience with a good attitude and courteous demeanor, and strong verbal and written communications skills.
  • Ability to mentor and train coworkers.
