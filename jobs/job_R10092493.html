The Northrop Grumman Mission Systems (NGMS) Sector Supply Chain Procurement function is seeking a
highly motivated individual to lead a team of buyers, responsible for executing all aspects of the procurement life
cycle for various commodities as the Procurement Manager at its Spectrum Center location in San Diego, CA. As a leader in developing strategic signal processing and direction finding systems, Northrop Grumman provides integrated real-time intelligence that aids decision making. This position will open up a challenging Global Supply Chain career with our commitment to provide required training and leadership for your management career at Northrop Grumman in the Mission Systems (MS) Sector where your team will engage in supporting large, complex, and challenging national defense programs that secure our nation and its allies.


This individual will be responsible for the direct supervision of a staff responsible for providing objective-direction to subordinates utilizing management guidelines and general policies and for the development, mentoring, coaching and engagement of the Procurement Specialists, reviewing work for adequacy in meeting objectives and meeting the needs of the business, foster proper engagement with the program and other functional teams, facilitating communication with programs and business area stakeholders. Functioning as an advisor regarding tasks, projects and operations and becomes actively involved in daily operations when required to meet schedules or resolve complex issues.

Activities may include developing strategy for long range procurement agreements, sourcing partnerships, continuous improvement initiatives, and identification of opportunities to

Reviews proposals, negotiates prices, selects or recommends suppliers, analyzes trends, follows up orders placed, verifies delivery, approves payment, and maintains necessary records. May prepare bid packages.
