Job Description

ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal Engineer Structural to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in the Aircraft Loads Section. This position will be located in Columbus, CA. As an engineer supporting the Aircraft Loads section, the selected candidate will support the analysis needs of unclassified and classified programs in Columbus, Ohio.  This position will support a variety of programs performing analysis and integration activities. Candidate will collaborate cross-discipline to define and perform study areas for analysis.

Essential Functions:

o    Provide external aircraft loads analysis (flight, ground, fatigue, miscellaneous) to programs

o    Perform subsonic and supersonic aircraft loads analysis

o    Interact with internal customers, assisting in defining system requirements, defining study areas, and providing status on current programs and technology

o    Develop and present materials for internal program related briefings

o    Support new business generation and prepare proposal material

o    Strong verbal and communication skills to interact with key cross-discipline stakeholders to achieve program objectives in a timely manner

o    Apply and contribute to the development of advanced technical principles, theories, and concepts.

o    Work on unusually complex technical problems and provide solutions which are highly innovative

o    Work under consultative direction toward predetermined long-range goals and objectives.

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Engineer:

o    Bachelor's degree in a STEM discipline (Science, Technology, Engineering or Math) with 5 years of related engineering experience; OR a Master’s degree in STEM with 3 years of related engineering experience; OR a PhD in STEM with 0 years of related engineering experience.

o    Ability and willingness to travel 10% of the time.

o    A current active in-scope U.S. Government (DoD) Department of Defense issued Secret or higher security clearance is required to start with the ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access (SAP/PAR).

Preferred Qualifications:

o    Three plus (3+) years of experience in Aircraft Loads and Flight Sciences engineering disciplines.

o    Experience in external aircraft loads analysis flight, ground, fatigue, and miscellaneous loads

o    Experience in Aircraft Loads processes and products throughout the program life cycle.

o    Experience with NASTRAN, FEMAP, Zonair, Zaero, Matlab

o    Understanding of Systems Engineering Principles and Model Based Systems Engineering tools (MBSE) with a practical focus for relevant use cases in propulsion integration.

o    A current active in-scope U.S. Government DoD Top Secret clearance with current or previous Special Program Access (SAP/PAR).

Basic Qualifications for a Senior Principal Engineer:

o    Bachelor's degree in a STEM discipline (Science, Technology, Engineering or Math) with 9 years of related engineering experience; OR a Master’s degree in STEM with 7 years of related engineering experience; OR a PhD in STEM with 4 years of related engineering experience.

o    Experience in external loads analysis flight, ground, fatigue, and miscellaneous loads

o    Ability and willingness to travel 10% of the time.

o    A current active in-scope U.S. Government (DoD) Department of Defense issued Secret or higher security clearance is required to start with the ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access (SAP/PAR).

Preferred Qualifications:

o    Seven plus (7+) years of experience in Aircraft Loads and Flight Sciences engineering disciplines.

o    Experience in technical leadership and guidance to Aircraft Loads discipline areas.

o    Experience in Aircraft Loads processes and products throughout the program life cycle.

o    Demonstrated ability to lead an engineering or cross-functional team.

o    Experience with NASTRAN, FEMAP, Zonair, Zaero, Matlab

o    Understanding of Systems Engineering Principles and Model Based Systems Engineering tools (MBSE) with a practical focus for relevant use cases in propulsion integration.

o    A current active in-scope U.S. Government DoD Top Secret clearance with current or previous Special Program Access (SAP/PAR).
