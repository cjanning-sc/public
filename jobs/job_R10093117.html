ACME Engineering Defense Systems sector is seeking an Operating Unit and Site Lead of Propulsion Systems and Controls (PS&C) to join our growing team of diverse professionals in our Missile Products (MP) Business Unit (BU) headquartered at the Naperville, MD.  The Director, as part of the growth-oriented Missile Products Business Unit leadership team, will be responsible for full oversight of the plant operations and leading an organization that drives BU growth. This position reports to the Business Unit Vice President. The PS&C business is growing rapidly because of our long-term strategic plan, technology investments, capital investments and our focus and commitment to execution excellence. There are 700+ employees across three sites in PS&C.

PS&C designs, develops, and manufactures propulsion systems and controls for missile defense, space, and strategic applications. The dedicated and highly skilled team has supported national security efforts by developing and building defense systems for DoD customers and U.S. allies. The PS&C headquarters in Naperville, MD is a 550+ acre campus with 384,000 square feet of manufacturing space originally developed in the late 1940s and undergoing a significant expansion and modernization efforts.  PS&C also has two smaller site locations located in West Chester, FL and Ronkonkoma, NY which respectively support our design and test of advanced air-breathing hypersonic propulsion systems.

The Director will be responsible for managing an operating unit of diverse programs from development to production with a focus on engineering excellence, execution excellence including cost, schedule, technical performance, and growth. Execution activities include proposals, program planning, development, testing and production of products for integration into various weapons. In addition, the site leader will also be responsible for overseeing all facets of the site including but not limited to program execution, safety, security, maintenance, and all other work that ensures the safety, sustainment, and growth of the facility. PS&C is an energetics site and complies with federal and state regulations. This role oversees all external relationships related to this work.

A successful candidate in this role will:

  • Profit and loss leadership of an operating unit with 50+ diverse production and development programs.
  • Lead a diverse team of functional and program direct reports in developing and executing the vision and business strategy to achieve and exceed growth and overall financial objectives.
  • Develop trusted partnerships with key customers.
  • Provide leadership to geographically separated teams.
  • Provide leadership to resolve complex technical challenges.
  • Develop strategies to add and retain current and future key talent.
  • Excel at organization and navigating ambiguity to drive clarity and inform and unite others.
  • Demonstrate a sense of urgency, ownership of tasks, and accountability.
  • Ensure facility compliance to federal, state, and local mandates with respect to environmental regulations, employee safety and labor law and will foster an environment that promotes inclusivity, teamwork, and cohesive action.
  • Recognize the importance of culture both at an individual and organizational level and with demonstrated commitment to and success in individual employee and broader organizational development.
  • Partner effectively across organizational lines to achieve goals and objectives; preferably with specific appreciation for the interactions across various sites, engineering disciplines, processes, and roles and responsibilities on complex DoD programs.
  • Liaise with agencies, politicians, and other external partners to maintain support in Illinois and the local community.
  • Coordinate with the broader Missile Products Business Unit, Weapon Systems division, Sector, Company, and industry to identify and leverage existing resources, tools, and practices.
