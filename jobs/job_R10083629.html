Put your skills to the test by pushing the boundaries of what’s possible.  From global defense to sustainment and modernization to mission readiness, your experience and ability will make it a reality.  Our programs are built on equal parts of curiosity and collaboration.  Our combined efforts means our customers can connect and defend millions of people around the world.  With Northrop Grumman, you’ll have the opportunity to be an essential part of projects that will define your career, now and in the future. 

Northrop Grumman is seeking a Production Planning and Scheduler 2 for our Promontory, Utah. location.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Responsible for supporting production activity and making sure business objectives are met.  
  • Duties include controlling production plans, identifying risks, reporting to management, preventing shortages and excesses, handling shipments, and maintaining quality standards.
  • Develop schedules that clearly show the flow of work through various departments or workstations.
  • Coordinate and expedite the flow of work and materials within or between departments of an establishment according to production schedule.
  • Work with Management to plan and manage production tasks.
  • Review production plans to identify and report potential risks to Management.
  • Develop inventory control techniques to avoid shortages and excesses.
  • Assist in processing customer orders and shipments according to the established schedule.
  • Assist Management in controlling production costs while maintaining the quality standards.
  • Review job orders and accordingly establish priorities and schedules.
  • Monitor execution of job orders and adjust schedules for timely delivery.
  • Maintain up-to-date knowledge on company objectives, production strategies and support activities.
  • Plan and monitor material movement in production cycles to ensure continuous operations.
  • Plan equipment, material and manpower needs for job order.
  • Coordinate with Management in resolving production planning and control issues.
  • Work with Management to prepare job order documents and give timely approvals.
  • Work with cross-functional teams to complete job orders on-time and within customer specifications.
  • Communicate production updates to Managers and customers on regular basis.

Basic Qualifications:

  • High school diploma and a minimum of seven (7) years of related experience. Will consider a Bachelor's degree and 3+ years of related experience or a Master's degree and 1+ years of experience.
  • Knowledge of ISO9001 quality standards and ESD-safe procedures
  • Use of both hands for repetitive wrist, hand, and finger motions
  • Ability to lift 25 pounds regularly and up to 50 pounds a few times per shift
  • Must be able to complete walking, standing, sitting, squatting, kneeling, twisting, bending, and reaching
  • Effective communication skills and the ability to communicate with employees of all levels.
  • Applicants must be detail oriented, calm under pressure, and well organized.  Applicants must have the ability and strong desire to learn.  

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Excellent problem-solving, time-management and analytical skills.
  • Lean Manufacturing Training and Experience
