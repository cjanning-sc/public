Northrop Grumman, Aeronautics Systems, has an opening for an Administrative Assistant 3 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located onsite El Segundo California.

Successful candidate will be a vital member of a program and capture team and perform in an administrative leadership role, which by nature of the position involves high-level contacts and exposure to sensitive program, new business, personnel and technical information necessitating considerable use of tact, diplomacy, discretion, and judgment.  

In the administrative assistant role, the selected candidate will perform diverse administrative responsibilities for multiple programs.
