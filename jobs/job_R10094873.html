Northrop Grumman, Strategic Space Systems Division, has an opening for a Senior Principal Engineer Material Process to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Manhattan Beach, California.

Analyzes, researches, designs and develops materials and their related fabrication and application processes to develop and optimize materials for use in engineering design of and/or application in structures, systems and subsystems. Applies principles of chemistry, physics, and material behavior to develop metallic, non-metallic and composite material and processing specifications, fabrication and assembly processes. Develops, analyzes and applies material properties and design allowables, processing processes and quality engineering specifications. Material analyses may include failure analysis of components, systems and subsystems, life predictions, and definition and requirement specifications. May review and approve subcontractor material processing procedures.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

Reviews and provide design input for microelectronic and microelectronic assembly designs for compliance with heritage processes and space design standards. Creates microelectronic part, material, and process qualification plans based on customer, internal, and MIL-STD requirements. Uses critical thinking to evaluate major design discrepancies and manufacturing and process deviations from MIL-STD-883, MIL-PRF-38534. Perform failure analysis of components, microelectronic systems and subsystems, life predictions. Understands and implements electrostatic discharge (ESD) sensitivity handling and safeguarding requirements. Performs audits and evaluations of material and special process suppliers.

Has excellent communication (written and verbal) and presentation skills. Provides mentorship and subject matter expertise for new hires, junior engineers, and collaborates closely with suppliers, procurement, design, analysis, manufacturing, quality and integration engineers, as well as functional management.
