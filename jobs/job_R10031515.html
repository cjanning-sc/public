**THIS IS A 2ND SHIFT ON SITE POSITION**

Northrop Grumman Strategic Space Systems Division has an opening for a Maintenance Plumber 3 (2ND SHIFT) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Redondo Beach, Ca.

Essential Functions:

  • Determines defects in, repairs, and maintains heating, water, and drainage systems in industrial or commercial establishments. 

  • Inspects high-pressure, air, steam, water, and gas systems to ascertain malfunctions

  • Repairs or replaces plumbing fixtures such as sinks, commodes, water heaters and hot water tanks.

  • Replaces washers

  • Mends or replaces burst or leaky pipes

  • Tests joints and pipe systems for leaks

  • Opens clogged drains

  • Measures, cuts, threads, bends, reams, and installs pipe and pipe fittings

  • Uses plumbers tools such as snakes, wrenches, plungers, pipe threaders, etc. 

  • Maintains knowledge of local codes.

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School Diploma or equivalent and four (4) years additional plumbing related experience

  • Ability to understand various piping system (air, steam, water and gas systems)

  • Ability to inspect, identify and provide solutions to repair systems

  • Experience using basic plumbing tools (such as basin wrench, pipe wrench, compression sleeve puller, drain auger, tongue-and-groove pliers, etc)

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret clearance if/when applicable

  • Ability to work 2nd shift hours (2:30PM-11:00PM PST)


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DOD Secret Clearance

  • Large/Multi-Site Facilities plumbing experience

  • Certified in backflow
