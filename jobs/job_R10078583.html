Northrop Grumman Defensive Systems has an opening for a Principal Electrical Engineer on our test team. In this role, you will be involved in all aspects of test station development.

Roles and responsibilities will include:

  • Review, track, and modify test station design documents as necessary

  • Generate and track Bill of Materials (BOMs), schedule, and execution of test station development

  • Coordinate with a multi-discipline team (supply chain, operations, QA, etc.) to ensure a quality product is built and remains on schedule and within budget

  • Develop and perform Acceptance Test Procedures for test stations

Basic Qualifications: 

  • A Bachelors in Science, Technology, Engineering or Math (STEM Degree) with a minimum of 5 years of applicable experience with a BS or 3 years with a MS

  • Experience with test equipment, such as, o-scope, spec-an, power supplies, etc.

  • Experience reading electrical schematics and design documentation

  • Understanding of test engineering concepts

  • The ability to obtain and maintain a US Secret level clearance is required

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Experience with test station design and development

  • Experience with creating and maintaining BOMs

  • Experience developing and executing ATPs
