Preferred Qualifications:

Candidate must have a thorough understanding of mechanical systems including but not limited to: 

  • Conveyor Systems
  • Automated Production Machines
  • Perform Preventative Maintenance on plant equipment
  • The ability to use CMMS, Computerized Maintenance Management System

  • All types of HVAC/Refrigeration systems
