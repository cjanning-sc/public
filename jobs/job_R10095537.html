Strategic Space Systems Division has an opening for an Electronic Technician 1 – DC Harness to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Columbus, Ohio.

ACME Engineering is one of the leaders in providing first class products to the world. The Electronic Technician 1- performs a variety of duties assisting mechanical and electrical engineers in the development of mechanical and electro mechanical engineering designs, tests, fabrications, modifications, harness assemblies and components. The selected candidate will utilize detailed engineering drawings for fabrication and test fixture setup.


Essential Functions:

• Set-up, Assemble and Tests complete flight harnesses assembly and sub-assemblies under operational conditions. 
• Works from schematics, diagrams, written and verbal descriptions, layouts or defined plans to perform routine testing, checkout, troubleshooting, prototype building, environmental testing, and simple developmental engineering tasks.
• Analyzes data and recommends modifications to components or test procedure to meet desired specifications.
• Excellent verbal and written communication skills.

Basic Qualifications: 
• High School Diploma or General Education Diploma with 0 to 2 years of experience as an Electronic Technician.
• Must have experience working with Engineering drawings and written work instructions.
• Knowledge of DC harness wiring using various connectors including D-Sub, Micro-D and Circular connectors
• Must be flexible and able to support weekend work when necessary

Preferred Qualifications: 
• Ability to interpret wire lists, perform continuity check-out to ensure correct wiring, splicing wires, wire prepping using thermal tweezers, spot tying, and performing other various tasks related to wire routing and wire dressing.
• Experience using basic tools (Torque wrenches, Wire Crimpers, etc.)
• Knowledge and/or experience working in aerospace
• Ability to take direction and follow company procedures and processes
• Active DoD Secret Clearance OR SCI Access.
