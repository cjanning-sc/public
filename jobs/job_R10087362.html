Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Sr. Principal Network Communication Subject Matter Expert (SME) -SMTPC to join the Systems Test Team (STT).   This position is in Promontory, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.   

What You’ll Get To Do:

The primary is Promontory travel will be to Roy and VSFB

Northrop Grumman Space Systems sector has an opening for a Network Communications Administrator to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This job will be located in Promontory, UT, with travel to Roy Utah and VSFB in support of the integration and maintenance of the ground systems for GBSD flight test program. This includes network and domain administration, mechanical, electrical, and RF systems.

The Network Communication Subject Matter Expert (SME) will maintain smooth operation of Advanced and Specialized Information System Hardware and Software. They will plan, evaluate, and coordinate installation and/or reconfiguration of hardware and software elements of several complex and specialized information systems. Maintains technical expertise in all areas of information systems hardware/software, radio frequency and SATCOM communications systems, and computer interconnection and interfacing, such as routers, multiplexers, firewalls, hubs, bridges, gateways, etc. May propose solutions to management to ensure all communications requirements based on future needs and current usage, configuring such solutions to optimize cost savings. Prepares or ensures that appropriate network documentation exists, including operational instructions. Provides regular monitoring and analysis regarding short- and long-range planning for in-house systems. May coordinate third-party maintenance for equipment. May design systems or portions of systems that include selection of hardware and software packages
