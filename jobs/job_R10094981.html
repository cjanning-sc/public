Northrop Grumman Mission Systems (NGMS) Advanced Processing Solutions is working to develop next generation high performance computing systems to meet the mission demands of the NGMS Networked Information Solutions (NIS) business. Superconducting electronics forms the core of our technology, with a focus on energy-efficient computation including our patented superconducting digital logic technology, Reciprocal Quantum Logic (RQL). On a gate for gate basis, RQL consumes orders of magnitude less power than CMOS while running at significantly higher clock speeds. The Advanced Technology Lab of Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking Cyber Systems Engineers. The qualified candidate will become part of Northrop Grumman’s Transformational Technology Business Area. Transformational Technology is focused on developing state-of-the-art next generation superconducting electronics (SCE) and advanced computing technologies.

Qualified Cyber Systems engineers will have experience with some of the following:

Responsibilities:

• Demonstrated project management skills
• Championing process improvement activities
• Establishing and analyzing test procedures
• Devises and implements methods and procedures for evaluating the accuracy of test stand installs and qualification
• Devises and implements methods and procedures for tracking test assets and develops a plan for periodic review of assets activity (e.g., in use, idle, at calibration, needs repair/upgrade, awaiting final disposition, etc) across multiple labs.
• Provide a monthly report on the status of all assets to the program offices as required

This position requires onsite work at our Linthicum, MD and Annapolis Junction, MD locations.

This position may be filled at the Associate Cyber Systems Engineer or Cyber Systems Engineer Level. Qualifications for both levels can be found below.

  • Strong problem solving and analytical skills.
  • Ability to communicate effectively to team members both verbally and in writing.

  • Cabling and Rack installation/cyber system integration experience

Preferred Qualifications:
