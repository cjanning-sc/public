Northrop Grumman Innovation Systems is currently seeking a Quality Control Technician to join its team at the Bacchus (Magna), Utah facility.  This is a full-time weekend shift (Friday-Sunday/12-hour days)

JOB DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

•  Successful candidate will work independently to perform a wide range of quality inspection tasks. 
•  Perform visual, in-process and final dimensional inspections on tightly tolerance piece parts, subassemblies and final assemblies in the production area.   
•  Perform Quality Process Assessments  
•  Performs required inspections in accordance with procedures and planning documentation. 
•  Performs required inspections using standard measurement instruments.   
•  Routinely coordinates and interfaces inspections with Customer and/or Government Representatives.
•  Must be able to work shift work, weekends, holidays, and overtime as required
•  Able to acquire and maintain Employee Possessor (EP) status under the Safe Explosives Act (SEA).

POSITION REQUIREMENTS:

•  4+ years of relative experience.

• Candidate should have technical strengths in processing, operations, and inspection.  Possess the ability to plan and organize work in order to accomplish assigned tasks. 
•  Candidate should have the ability to work multiple programs while maintaining attention to detail.
•  Interpret drawings and specifications.   Working knowledge and understanding of GD&T.
•  Be strategic in the identification of technical needs and solutions coupled with a strong program execution focus.
•  Possess excellent communication skills and effective interface with Mission Assurance department members, suppliers, customers, all other departments within the organization and both technical and non-technical personnel. 
•  Ability to record detail information accurately, timely and follow instructions.

  • This is a full-time weekend shift (Friday-Sunday/12-hour days)

EDUCATION:

High School Diploma or GED required; A.S. Degree or equivalent preferred
Excellent shop math skills and advanced trigonometry.  

SKILLS REQUIRED: 

•  Must be familiar with standard Mechanical Inspection techniques and tools.
•  Experience with ISO 9000/AS9100 Quality Management Systems.
•  Experience with Continuous Improvement/PES techniques.
•  Must have good computer skills -  MS Office suite.
•  Self Starter and ability to work through an issue to closure

CLEARANCE REQUIREMENTS:

Ability to acquire a Security Clearance is required, which requires US Citizenship as a pre-requisite.
