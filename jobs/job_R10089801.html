Provides secretarial/administrative support to nonexecutive employees or groups in the organization. Uses business software applications (e.g., word processing, presentation and spreadsheet) to prepare correspondence, reports, presentations, agendas, minutes, etc.. Receives, screens and directs incoming calls, visitors, mail and email. Maintains files, records, calendars and diaries. May arrange business travel, coordinate meeting arrangements, and/or track expenses.

Responsibilities include, but not limited to:

  • Providing administrative support to the sector P&E Director
  • Answers and logs phone calls
  • Coordinates and maintains calendars
  • Establishes and facility setup for meetings
  • May support other direct report management when required
  • Plans and supports internal and external business conferences, meetings, and other events
  • Coordinates, facilitates, and administers travel expenses and other administration duties associated with support to the AS Pricing & Estimating Director

The successful candidate will possess the following:

  • Ability to work in a fast paced environment with minimal supervision while exercising good judgment, demonstrating flexibility and agility, prioritizing multiple tasks with competing deadlines, managing schedule conflicts, while providing high quality products and excellent follow through
  • Will be pro-active, self-motivated, well organized and detail oriented
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work with all levels of management and their assistants

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School Diploma/GED
  • 6 years of administrative experience and/or additional education

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced degree
  • Fluent in the use of Microsoft Office applications
  • Experience in reviewing and editing written material for correct spelling and grammar
  • Experience working with travel, expensing systems (iTrips, Concur or similar)
  • Experience supporting senior level management/customers in a dynamic program environment
  • Candidate must be able to obtain and maintain an active U.S. Government security clearance at the DoD Secret level
