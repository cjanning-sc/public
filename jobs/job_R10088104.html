SSSD has an opening for a Mechanical Technician 4 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be in Redondo Beach, California.

The ideal candidate will have experience performing a variety of machinist responsibilities which may include but are not limited to the following:

Must be capable of manufacturing complex close tolerance machined hardware in various exotic materials, from Titanium, Invar, Kovar, Inconel, Macor, Fiberglass Epoxy, Aluminum, etc. Programming in Mastercam version 2020 or higher, creating geometry, or using Solid Models, IGES, STEP, DXF files, and 3, 4, and 5-dimensional advanced programming techniques is a must. Must have a high-speed machining background with the ability to work a two-axis trunnion.

The applicant should also be well versed in Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerance per ANSI 14.5M 1982/1994 specifications to interpret actual position and tolerance requirements. Capable of operating FADAL, DMG, Mazak, and HAAS CNC 3, 4, and 5-axis machines.  Also, able to program and operate turning machines with live tooling. A strong background in conventional manual equipment and can use inspection tools to inspect manufactured hardware.  Able to work with little or no direct supervision. Willingness to work overtime when needed to satisfy customer requirements. Experience in wire and plunge EDM is a plus.
