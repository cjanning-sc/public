Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems is seeking a Supply Chain Manager 1 to lead a team of qualified, diverse individuals support the Special Programs Global Supply Chain (GSC) Goods Movement organization. This position is in Palmdale, California.

  • 2nd shift, 4/10s schedule Monday – Thursday
  • Position is in-office and a production environment, unable to accommodate a telecommute/hybrid schedule.

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Plans, schedules and manages the efficient movement of material from receiving and/or the warehouse to production. Supports the fulfillment of material and scheduling requirements. May use Material Requirements Planning (MRP) systems. Coordinates efforts between cross functional departments to ensure issues are resolved, mitigated and/or communicated in support of successful program execution.
  • Manage the daily operations of an 8 to15 person team or work unit comprised of experienced and non-experienced employees exercising minimal latitude and independence in their assignments to accomplish required statement of work. Daily efforts will include and are not limited to:
  • Monitor work operations of exempt and/or non-exempt employees on a daily basis and actively assists or provides direction to subordinates as required.
  • Provide direction to all subordinates using established policies and precedents and ensure subordinate work is reviewed for adequacy in meeting cost and quality objectives.
  • Manage effort to consolidate part delivery data sets for use in ensuring parts are received, stored, retrieved, kitted and delivered to MFG.
  • Assist shop-floor supervision and senior management of the kit status of kits in progress, material availability, and potential production problems to ensure that personnel, materials and services are provided as needed.
  • Support ad hoc requests associated with attrition, stock purges, SMO’s, ETC…
  • Coordinate interdepartmental activity with quality assurance, manufacturing, purchasing, engineering, inventory control, traffic, etc.; to expedite the movement of parts by means of move orders, stock transfers and requests for shipping orders.
  • May perform on-going operational tasks of organizational groups or be assigned to teams working on sector level tasks, projects or process changes/improvements.
  • Frequent contacts with internal personnel and outside customer representatives at various management levels concerning operations or scheduling or parts management issues specific to the build of the product lines in Palmdale.
  • Conduct status briefings and participate in meetings for internal and/or external representatives concerning parts management issues, manufacturing caused constraints, program specific issues discovered utilizing analytical and data mining skills.
  • Data mining and statistical analysis of material requirements, production schedules, material delivery schedules and assessment of historical manufacturing anomalies are critical requirements for this position.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree with 3 years of experience or High School Diploma with 7 years of experience.
  • Prior experience and knowledge of Global Supply Chain processes and procedures
  • Working Knowledge of SAP specifically associated with purchasing, scheduling, parts management, shop floor control and MBoM structures
  • Experience executing daily SoW associated with Supply Chain Management
  • Proficient with MS Office (specifically MS Excel, Outlook), MES and SAP or equivalent MRP system
  • Current DoD Secret clearance to apply and start, with the ability to obtain a special program access clearance within a reasonable timeframe to meet business needs/objectives
  • As needed to support overall operations and team may be required to perform production, shop floor, warehouse, and part integration responsibilities. May include use of forklift, and ability to lift, push, pull up to 30 pounds, or stand for long periods of time.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Oral and written communication skills capable of communicating a wide variety of information to subordinates, peers and senior management.
  • 1yr of Leadership experience as a lead or similar capacity working in a manufacturing/production environment or warehousing
  • Proficient analytical and data mining skills
  • Parts management experience for large developmental and/or production programs
  • Ability to deal effectively with competing priorities and deadlines, and able to manage multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment while demonstrating a professional attitude.
