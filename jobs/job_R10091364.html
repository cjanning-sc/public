Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems is seeking a Level 2 and Level 3 Budget Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located on site in Melbourne, FL with partial telework available.

Essential Functions:

  • Compiles and reviews budgets for corporate or organizational units using actual performance, previous budget figures, estimated revenue, expense reports and other data sources to control funds and provide for proper financial administration
  • Prepares financial plans
  • Monitors implementation of financial policies
  • Prepares regular and special-purpose reports
  • Maintains historical records
  • Analyzes trends
  • Establishes cost rates and personnel forecasts
  • Recommends and interprets budgetary policies and procedures
  • Prepares comparative evaluation of actual costs against budgeted funds
  • Determines rationale for variances between costs and budget

This requisition may be filled as either a Level 2 Budget Analyst or a Level 3 Principal Budget Analyst.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Level 2 Budget Analyst: Bachelor’s Degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, Business Administration or related field with 3 years of related experience OR a Master's Degree with 1 year of related experience
  • Level 3 Principal Budget Analyst: Bachelor’s Degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, Business Administration or related field with 6 years of related experience OR a Master's Degree with 4 years of related experience
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Highly organized
  • Solid oral and written communication skills
  • Knowledge of SAP/Business Warehouse (BW) a plus
  • May Require some travel <5%
  • Knowledge of financial reporting and accounting principles
  • Demonstrated ability to effectively participate on team projects
