Job Summary

ACME Engineering Space Systems is searching for a Sr. Principal Mechanical Engineer to be part of our Integration (Build) and Test team in Launch Vehicles at our Louisville, AZ facility.

Job Responsibilities

The candidate will serve as an Integration and Test Sr. Principal Mechanical Engineer for the Sentinel Program, requiring the candidate to function in a dynamic multi-disciplinary environment. Daily responsibilities may require direct contact with sensitive launch vehicle flight hardware and ground support equipment. The candidate may be expected to help develop, plan and/or perform integration and testing activities associated with the assembly of flight hardware, testing of assembled systems and components, or execution/support of live fire tests (static and/or flight) at various facilities or launch ranges. Specific duties may include:

  • Hands-on integration or testing

  • Developing/maintaining procedures

  • Incorporating hardware/tooling design changes

  • Developing/planning/coordinating integration activities

  • Coordinating with suppliers and procurement office

  • Creating digital/physical mockups and tools

  • Complying with strict aerospace standards

  • Supporting live fire static testing and flight test events
