Job Description

Primary function is to lead all phases of ICBM targets program (part of broader IRBM/ICBM targets program) from inception through completion. Responsible for the cost, schedule and technical performance of program and for working directly with Program Engineering Managers, Program business management and IRBM/ICBM Program Director to ensure program objectives are met. Participates in the negotiation of contract and contract changes. Coordinates the preparation of proposals, business plans, proposal work statements and specifications, operating budgets and financial terms/conditions of contract. Acts as primary customer contact for ICBM program activities, leading program review sessions with customer to discuss cost, schedule, and technical performance. Establishes design concepts, criteria and engineering efforts for product research, development, integration and test. Develops new ICBM business or expands the product line with the customer. Establishes milestones and monitors adherence to master plans and schedules, identifies program problems and obtains solutions, such as allocation of resources or changing contractual specifications. Takes on general program management responsibilities outside directly related ICBM efforts as required.

  • Proven experience successfully managing technical project requirements.

  • Proficient in Microsoft Office suite
