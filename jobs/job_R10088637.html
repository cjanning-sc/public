Sr Principal Engineer Supplier Quality

1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman is seeking a Sr. Principal Supplier Quality Engineer specialized in PWB (Printed Wire Board) commodities to join our Supplier Performance Management Field Engineering team. The team supports programs with suppliers across the United States and Internationally. This position works a 9/80 schedule and will travel up to 75% of the time.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Collaborate in a team environment, negotiate and integrate different viewpoints, prioritize and adjust tasks to accomplish the project results
  • Represent the company with credibility, confidence, and professionalism
  • Perform Compliance Reviews, First Article Inspections, and Source Inspections on PWB hardware or other commodities as required
  • Initiate Corrective Action Plans and work with suppliers, manufacturing and engineering personnel to identify root cause and ensure robust corrective actions are implemented
  • Analyze and interpret data to identify supplier’s process issues and develop solutions
  • Perform process and product audits along with capacity assessments to ensure the supplier’s processes are robust and compliant to all requirements
  • Perform supplier surveillance activities designed to manage and improve supplier performance, productivity, and process validation
  • Apply knowledge to analyze and audit supplies processes and capabilities
  • Mitigate supplier quality risk to prevent/minimize business impact from material defects
  • Lead on-site supplier engagements, including performance reviews and QMS (Quality Management System) audits
  • Analyze and present data to all levels of leadership
  • Establish positive working relationships with suppliers and internal teams
  • Develop and mentor new engineers
  • Represent SPM Field Quality on the Distribution Global Supply Chain Category Management Team
  • Attend and present monthly KPI (Key Performance Indicator) data for all category managed distributors
  • Perform new supplier/distributor quality audits to assess candidacy for inclusion on the Approved Supplier List
  • Act and advise as a subject matter expert on Material Authenticity

Basic Qualifications

  • STEM degree with 9+ years of experience in Quality, Manufacturing, or Design Engineering
  • Must be able to obtain and maintain a Secret Clearance. US Citizenship required
  • Knowledge of ISO 9001 and AS9100 requirements
  • Must be able to travel up to 75% of the time
  • Experience in root cause and corrective action
  • Demonstrate skill to lead teams and projects
  • Knowledge in Material Authenticity

Preferred Qualifications

  • Possess active Secret Clearance
  • Experience working in the defense industry
  • Process knowledge/experience in CCA’s, microelectronics, or RF devices
  • Certification in J-STD-001, IPC-A-600, 610, 620, or Mil-STD-883
  • Lead Auditor experience
  • Subject matter expert in Material Authenticity
