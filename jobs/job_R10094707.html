Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems is seeking a Regulatory Compliance Analyst (Level 2) OR a Principal Regulatory Compliance Analyst (Level 3) to join our qualified and diverse Government Relations & Compliance team. This position will be virtual and can be performed anywhere in the continental United States.  

  

Essential Functions: 

Develops, maintains, and establishes audit compliance of financial systems, considering allocations, costing techniques, and supports various DCAA audits. This position prepares incurred cost proposals, billing & quick closeout rate proposals, overhead allowability risk, prior period adjustments, and CAS cost impact proposals. In addition, the position will provide responses to DCAA audited findings, support closure of open rate segments with DCMA, make recommendations to management, provide analysis on alternative cost structures, and monitor business compliance performance. The position requires excellent communication skills to resolve complex financial issues through coordination with multiple functions. 

Responsibilities include but are not limited to: 

  • Analysis of large volumes of data using worksheet and database skills to manipulate, summarize, and select into meaningful information for decision making 

  • Detail analysis and reconciliations for prior period adjustments 

  • Support financial and SOX audits by internal, external, and government auditors 

  • Provide financial information required for quarterly reporting

  • Work with their team to ensure strong processes and controls are in place and will provide value added services to our partner departments 

  • Partner across the Controller organization and Sector to ensure success of business priorities and outcomes 

 

 Basic Qualifications for Level 2: 

  • A Bachelor's degree in accounting, finance, business or other related field with 3 years of relevant experience; OR a Master's degree with 1 years’ experience with relevant experience. 

  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Office including Outlook, Excel, PowerPoint and Word 

  • Strong verbal and written communication skills 

Basic Qualifications for Level 3: 

  • A Bachelor's degree in accounting, finance, business or other related field with 6 years of relevant experience; OR a Master's degree with 4 years’ of relevant experience 

  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Office including Outlook, PowerPoint Word, and Excel (Including Pivot tables and V-Look up) 

  • Strong verbal and written communication skills 

 

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Experience with SAP Accounting System (PS/FI/CO modules) and Business Warehouse OLAP (AO/BW) 

  • Knowledge of overhead calculations, bases, allocations, costing sheets, overhead keys and Interdivisional Work Orders (IWO) 

  • Excel power user (pivot tables, vlookups, macros, advanced formula writing) 

  • Excellent organizational, prioritization and process improvement skills 

  • Accounting experience in government contracting 

  • GAAP, Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR), and Cost Accounting Standards (CAS) knowledge 

  • Resourceful with strong analytical and problem solving skills 

  • Ability to interface with multiple disciplines and varying levels of management 

  • Defense industry experience 

  • Ability to be granted DoD Secret clearance in future 

  • CPA certification
