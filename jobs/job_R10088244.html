The Payload and Ground Systems organization within the Northrop Grumman Space Systems pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter. Come be a part of our mission.

As a Radar Systems Engineer on this team, you will apply physics and engineering principles, mathematical analysis, and modeling and simulation to support development and analysis of advanced sensor systems, technologies, and algorithms. You will perform research, analysis, evaluation, development and test of technologies from industry, academia, and government organizations, to include Commercial Off-The-Shelf (COTS), Government Off-The-Shelf (GOTS), and open source technologies. You will also consider custom approaches to solve challenging problems in sensing. Additionally, you will provide systems engineering recommendations and assist with the planning and execution of development efforts for complex sensor systems, and systems of systems. Ideal candidates will apply their analytical skills to a broad range of systems engineering activities in support of Northrop Grumman projects and programs.

In short, the position requires a flexible, versatile, and innovative individual who is well-versed across a range of technical subjects and disciplines related to radar, mastery of a few, and an eagerness for continued learning and collaboration with team members.

Basic Qualifications for a Senior Principal Radar Systems Engineer:

  • 9 years of relevant experience with a Bachelor's degree in Physics, Mathematics, Electrical Engineering or other applicable STEM field; 7 years of relevant experience with a Masters' degree in a corresponding field; 4 years of relevant experience with a PhD

  • Active DoD Secret clearance to start and the ability to obtain and maintain a Top Secret once employed.

  • Experience in Radar signal processing

  • Experience with MATLAB/PYTHON or equivalent programming/analysis languages

  • Familiar in using a requirements management tool, such as DOORS

  • Familiar in using a discrepancy management tool, such as Jira, Bugzilla, etc.

Preferred Qualifications for a Senior Principal Radar Systems Engineer:

  • TS/SCI security clearance, or ability to obtain and maintain one after employment

  • Experience with MATLAB, Python, C/C++, Fortran and equivalent programming languages

  • Substantial domain subject matter expertise and extensive experience in multiple areas:

    • Systems Engineering

    • Radar principles

    • Radar system modeling, simulation, and performance analysis

    • Radar waveforms and digital signal processing (DSP) methods

    • Radar imaging and processing techniques

    • Radar antenna and active electronically scanned array (AESA) system principles, digital beamforming, and array processing

    • Electronic Protection (EP) and clutter mitigation principles

    • Principles of object detection, tracking, classification and related algorithms

    • Algorithm development and testing

    • High Performance Computing /Parallelization techniques

    • Multi-static radar principles

    • Business development

    • Innovative technology concept development
