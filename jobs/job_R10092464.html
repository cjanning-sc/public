ACME Engineering Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) has an opening for a Senior Principal Mechanical Engineer specifically supporting structural analysis within the Mechanical Ground Systems Engineering team. This position will be located in Columbus, OH at Space Park.

The Mechanical Ground Support Equipment (MGSE) team supports the design, fabrication, and use of Mechanical Ground Support Equipment (MGSE).  MGSE is non-flight hardware used to support the integration, test, and launch of spacecraft and its various stages of assembly. This includes, but is not limited to, lift slings, transportation systems, robotics, zero-g deployment fixtures, pneumatics, propellant loading systems, etc. In this position, the candidate will be responsible for structural analysis of MGSE and have experience in some of the following areas:

  • Design and Modeling in 3D CAD (Computer-Aided Design) software

  • Fundamental structural analysis techniques

  • Drafting using CAD software

  • Fabrication support at vendors including acceptance testing and inspection

  • End-user support during integration operations

  • Conceptual design

  • Requirements definition

  • Integration & Test process flow development

  • Applicants must have extensive structural analysis experience.

  • On-site support is expected for the following position.

As a Senior Principal Mechanical Engineer, a qualified candidate is expected to be proficient in a majority of design and analysis principles, processes, and tools. The candidate is expected to mentor and coach less experienced project personnel in MGSE design, structural analysis, and other technical concepts.  This senior level position requires acting independently to uncover and resolve issues associated with the overall strength of the MGSE designs to ensure personnel and hardware safety.
