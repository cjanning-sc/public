The Northrop Grumman Mission Systems, Processing Technologies Group, is seeking Associate/FPGA design engineers familiar with digital logic circuit design to work as Field Programmable Gate Array (FPGA) Design Engineers to be involved in full life-cycle product development of Radio Frequency Electronic Warfare (RFEW), Infrared Counter Measures (IRCM) and Advanced Target & Surveillance (ATS) Systems. You will be involved in Hardware Description Language (HDL) coding, test bench development, verification, synthesis, place and route and lab testing using state of the art tools and techniques.

This position is located at our Rolling Meadows, IL location

This position can be filled at the Associate FPGA or FPGA Engineer level:

Basic Qualifications for Associate FPGA design engineer:

- Bachelor’s Degree in STEM field

- Understanding of digital logic fundamentals

- Ability to read and understand Integrated Circuit (IC) data sheets

- Basic familiarity with engineering lab equipment, e.g. oscilloscope

- Good attention to detail and self-motivated to learn

- Ability to work in teams and communicate clearly across various levels of engineers

- Must have interim secret clearance prior to start. Once started, must have the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD secret clearance.

Basic Qualifications for FPGA design engineer:

- Bachelor’s Degree in STEM Field with 0-2 years of relevant professional work experience OR Master's degree in STEM field.

- Understanding of digital logic fundamentals

- Ability to read and understand Integrated Circuit (IC) data sheets

- Basic familiarity with engineering lab equipment, e.g. oscilloscope

- Good attention to detail and self-motivated to learn

- Ability to work in teams and communicate clearly across various levels of engineers

- Must have interim secret clearance prior to start. Once started, must have the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD secret clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:

- Some experience in HDL (VHDL/Verilog) for FPGA or Programmable Logic Device (PLD)

- Some understanding of Digital Signal Processing or Image Processing

- Some experience with high level programming languages (C/C++)

- Some experience with scripting languages (Python)

- A strong team player who also has the ability to work independently

- Active DOD Secret Clearance
