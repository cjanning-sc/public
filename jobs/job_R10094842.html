Northrop Grumman Defense Systems is seeking a Logistics Manager for our Yorktown, VA location.

Responsibilities:

  • Supervises employees who use a variety of equipment such as forklifts, hand carts, and power trucks to move materials, parts, assemblies, and finished products or goods within a plant, office, or warehouse

  • Oversees the processing, packaging, and storage of supplies, materials, and equipment

  • May assist in selecting appropriate containers and wrapping methods for materials to be shipped or stored

  • Responsibilities are stock control, inventory management, and warehouse functions to include requisitioning, receiving, storing, issuing, shipping, and management of defective material

  • Management of material is to include Depot Level Repairable, Pre-Expended Bin material, shelf-life material and HAZMAT material in accordance with Navy policies and procedures

  • Coordinate with team and Government oversite to schedule daily tasks, perform inventories, track process of material, dispose of excess material, and provide technical support for obsolete material or inaccuracies of inventories

  • Perform other qualified duties as assigned

Basic Qualification:

  • Has a minimum a High School degree or diploma with a minimum of 10 years' recent experience in a logistics role

  • Three plus years in Naval logistics support or in performing inventory management activities

  • Ability to pass a forklift drivers physical and be granted a license

  • Computer literacy in operation of MS Office products such as excel and Word

  • Experience in continues process improvement efforts to improve work place procedures, processes and cost effectiveness

  • Ability to obtain a Secret Clearance, Interim Secret required to start

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Individual very familiar with military logistics processing for a minimum of 3 years of current 10 year work history in a supervisory role

  • 4 year degree from an accredited school in logistics management

  • Currently possesses an active clearance
