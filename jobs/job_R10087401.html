ACME Engineering is seeking Data Scientists for an internship opportunity. This position will be located in our Corporate Headquarters in Falls Church, IA. In this role, you will apply modeling and simulation concepts to the people analytics domain by further developing a simulation architecture to project the impacts of human capital strategies. This will involve building and refining models and integrating them into a modular code architecture. There may also be opportunities to deploy models in a cloud computing environment. 

The qualified candidate will become part of ACME Engineering's People Analytics, Insights & Technology organization.

Basic Qualifications

A candidate must meet ALL of the below criteria. The candidate must:

  • Full-time student pursuing a graduate degree from an accredited college/university majoring in Data Science/Analytics, Statistics, Computer Science, Math, Aerospace Engineering, or other quantitative disciplines
  • Experience with modeling and simulation
  • Experience with Python
  • Experience collaborating on projects, managing relationships, and communicating findings to audiences
  • Be available to work full-time (40 hours per week) for at least 10 weeks during summer 2023.
  • Be able to obtain a U.S. Government security clearance (U.S. citizenship is a pre-requisite)
  • Have an overall cumulative GPA of 3.0 out of 4.0 or higher (unofficial academic transcripts must be provided at time of application by uploading the documents to your application or profile)

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience working with SQL and structured databases
  • Have an overall cumulative GPA of 3.70/4.0 or higher
