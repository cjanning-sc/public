Are You ready to put your experience to work at ACME Engineering? If so, our Platform Laboratory Systems & Design (PLSD) group wants YOU to join our team as a Principal / Senior Principal Structural Engineer (Level 3/4) and join our team of qualified and diverse individuals in Oklahoma City, OK.

You will become part of a ACME Engineering team of professionals who are actively supporting the development of Launch Vehicles, Space Systems, and Advanced Aerospace Structures.

Your Responsibilities include:

  • Under minimal direction, perform a variety of structural engineering tasks of an analysis, design, or test nature

  • Apply theoretical knowledge and engineering techniques to the solution of analytical engineering problems

  • Select and/or modify proven methods, mathematical formulas, previous design and test information, handbook data or other available information related to the assignment

  • Stress and bolted joint analysis of ground support equipment, test fixtures, compliance structures, and flight structures

  • Take direction and address issues using skills obtained in the field of engineering, or related structural integrity sciences as it relates to missile and space systems

  • Use classical stress and strain methods and software to predict structural integrity and failure and design flight components and ground equipment to survive worst-case loading conditions

  • Learn and develop proficiency with structural analysis software (Femap, NX Nastran, MathCAD, etc.)

  • Interpret, select, and apply company/commercial/military specifications and customer requirements to accomplish specific analyses

  • Produce engineering documentation and technical reports/analyses to support design, integration, and testing efforts

  • Gather, compile, and interpret technical data such as flight data, test data, or material test reports

Basic Qualifications:

This position may be hired at a Principal Structural Engineer or Sr. Principal Structural Engineer.

  • Principal Structural Engineer: A High School diploma or equivalent and a minimum of 9 yrs of related engineering experience. Bachelor's degree in a STEM discipline (Science, Technology, Engineering or Math) with 5 years of related engineering experience; OR a Master’s degree in STEM with 3 years of related engineering experience, or a PhD in STEM with no experience may also be considered

  • Sr. Principal Structural Engineer: A High School diploma or equivalent and a minimum of 13 yrs of related engineering experience. Bachelor's degree in a STEM discipline (Science, Technology, Engineering or Math) with 9 years of related engineering experience; OR a Master’s degree in STEM with 7 years of related engineering experience, or a PhD in STEM with 4 years of related experience may also be considered

  • Ability to obtain a DoD SECRET security clearance & specific program access

  • Strong structural analysis background with demonstrated work experience and knowledge as a structural analyst in Aerospace industry

  • Ability to read engineering drawings and specifications

  • Familiarity with finite element (FEM) analysis, classical stress and strain methodology, engineering statics, and structural mechanics including hand calculations

  • Computer skills with standard word processing/spreadsheet experience

  • Ability to listen effectively, take direction, and possess initiative to accomplish assigned tasks and complete work in a timely fashion


Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Master’s Degree in Mechanical/Aerospace Engineering

  • Active DoD SECRET or higher security clearance with investigation in the last five (5) years

  • Experience working on, and analyzing, different materials including metallic (steel, aluminum, titanium, etc.) and composite

  • Experience with Dassault Abaqus, Siemens NX Nastran or equivalent

  • Experience with Altair HyperWorks, Siemens Femap or equivalent

  • Experience with Python, MATLAB, MathCAD, or equivalent
