Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Staff Data Management Analyst. This position will be located in Roy, Utah and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. Learn more about the GBSD program here. 

What You’ll Get To Do: 

In this role, you will serve as consultant to management, contribute to the development of new concepts, techniques, and standards, and develop solutions to complex problems, which require the regular use of ingenuity and innovation. Most assignments performed independently without appreciable direction and will be responsible for leading and training others.

  • As a Data Management Analyst, you will support the implementation of Configuration and Data Management activities using our Product Data Management tools such as SharePoint, JIRA, and Common Product Lifecycle Management (CPLM). Assist in building and managing program Configuration and Data Management systems and tools, libraries, workflows, and processes.
  • Strong communication skills and be able to support the Government Customer as well as all levels of management and the Integrated Program Teams (IPT).
  • The Data Management analyst is responsible for ensuring program data has been approved, distributed, and managed appropriately in accordance with sector and program approved processes, as well as any customer deliverables such as Data Accession Lists (DAL).
  • Data Planning, Scheduling and Management requires the Analyst to have the skillset to utilize the DD Form 1423, Data Item Description (DID), Integrated Master Schedule (IMS) and the Statement of Work (SOW), scheduling and managing each deliverable based on the contractual delivery requirement.
  • The Analyst is responsible for validating data and verifying that the data is consistent with existing procedures and guidelines, as well as security markings.  Analysis and validation of project deliverables and non-deliverables, contract and non-contract data requirements, and customer-supplied data are to be performed.
  • Provide inputs into program level metrics to benchmark effectiveness of Configuration and Data Management performance.
  • Support and work closely with the program, customer representatives, functional organizations, Global Supply Chain (GSC), Mission Assurance (MA), Sub-Contracts, and suppliers to ensure that program Configuration and Data Management processes are accurately implemented.
  • Coordinate with Integrated Program Teams (IPT), Contracts and, Supplier Management Teams (SMT) on deliveries and schedules of Contractor Data Requirement List (CDRL) and Subcontractor Data Requirements List (SDRL) and creation of Data Responsibility Matrixes.
  • Administer Data Management on Contractor Data Requirement List (CDRL) and Subcontractor Data Requirements List (SDRL) information within SharePoint and Common Product Lifecycle Management (CPLM).  Ensure data integrity through audit processes.
  • Facilitate the review and disposition of supplier data, through the creation of the Supplier Data Review Notice (SDRN).

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off.

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

  • 15 Years of experience with Bachelors; 13 Years with Masters; 4 additional years of relevant experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
  • Active DOD Secret Clearance with a reinvestigation date occurring in the last 6 years.
  • U.S. Citizenship with the ability to obtain access to Special Access Program (SAP).
  • Knowledge and understanding of:
    • Data management practices, procedures, GEIA-859, and EIA 649-1 standards.
    • Development of Data Management products (i.e., Data Accession Lists (DAL), metrics, Contractor Data Requirement List (CDRL) schedules or Subcontractor Data Requirements List (SDRL) schedules.
    • Develop, write, and implement best practices, processes, procedures.
  • Must be able to support core hours of 9:00 – 4:00 and occasional surge operations.
