ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Principal Reentry Systems Field Engineer. This position will be located in Vandenberg Space Force Base, CA and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. Learn more about the GBSD program here.  This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

  • Conduct installation, preparation, and check out of ICBM or SLBM reentry vehicles and payload reentry systems.

  • Will support Flight and Ground Test system level testing as a Configuration Analyst for payload reentry systems.

  • Providing the documentation, technical implementation, and configuration for payload reentry systems as part of a missile launch support system

  • Performing reentry vehicle build up, general maintenance, test and checkout

  • Supporting mass properties testing of reentry vehicles

  • Performing integration of reentry vehicles as part of a payload reentry system and preparing for flight test

  • Participating in technical exchange meetings, interfacing with customers (internal/external) and contributing to a broad range of program deliverables.

  • The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

Basic Qualifications: 
- Bachelors degree waived with Associates degree in related field with 4 years ICBM or SLBM Reentry Vehicle maintenance
- Bachelor's degree and 10 years of relevant experience; or 8 years experience with an Associates degree in field related to Reentry Vehicle maintenance, nuclear weapons maintenance or related similar field.
- Must be have a DoD Secret level security clearance adjudicated in the last 5 years prior to commencement of employment 
- Excellent communication, interpersonal skills, and the ability to interface with all levels of employees and management
- Works well in a team environment
- Proficiency in MS Office

- Typical Minimum Education or Experience: 6 Years with Bachelors; 4 Years with Masters

Preferred Qualifications: 
- Experience leading maintenance teams in build-up, preparation, test
- Experience in ICBM or SLBM flight test
- Test report writing and documentation experience
- A current DoD Top Secret or DoE Q security clearance, to include a Single Scope Background Investigation, a periodic reinvestigation, or an original adjudication date completed within the last 5 years
