RF & Digital Technologist - Engineer RF Microwave
R10093628
1st Shift (United States of America)

The RF & Digital Category Technologist is a unique role which will support technical projects under the leadership of the Category Manager. Rather than a detail design role, this position would be good fit for an RF engineer looking to be in a higher-level advisory role. The selected candidate will have strong knowledge on emerging technology and technology road maps.

The candidate will apply advanced technical principles, theories, and concepts. The candidate should have experience and understanding with RF design and components such as: up/down-converter, amplifier, power splitter/divider, couplers, mixers, filters and their applications. The selected candidate must have the ability to work with a make/buy source board and risk review board to ensure the category management organization considers capabilities and risk mitigation plans to help support our supply chain base and ensure suppliers are successful in program execution. The selected candidate will apply supply chain principles to the category management supply chain base and contributes to the development of new principles and concepts to support the supplier’s technical capabilities and offerings.

The candidate will ensure problem solving work on unusually complex technical problems and provide solutions which are highly innovative and ingenious to support excellent supplier performance. The selected candidate will support the Quarterly Business Reviews by ensuring the technical roadmaps and program needs are clearly summarized for their assigned suppliers. Additionally, the selected candidate will collaborate with the strategist to analyze suppliers’ competitive landscape, market trends to influence supplier competition and diverse supply chain base.  The selected candidate will interface with design engineering on supplier capabilities, capacity, and design producibility analysis.

The RF & Digital Category Technologist will work under consultative direction toward predetermined long-range goals and objectives. The selected candidate’s assignments are often self-initiated and should pursue courses of action necessary to obtain desired results. The selected candidate will work through consultation and agreement with others rather than by formal review of supervisor. This individual will serve as organization spokesperson on advanced projects and/or programs. Acts as advisor to management and internal customers on advanced technical studies and applications.

The selected candidate will understand program execution framework to enable advocacy. The selected candidate will have the ability to provide a technical evaluation of supplier’s capabilities during the business acquisition process (BAP). The selected candidate will be the technical point of contact when responding to NG requests (RFPs, RFI, RFQ.) Additionally, the selected candidate will have the ability to work on competitive captures, program execution, strategy development process, Strategic Gap Analysis, Internal Investment discussions, business growth discussions, strategic studies, cross division business insight and maintain a process on how to look for similar to parts, technology grouping.

The position can be filled out of Redondo Beach, Rolling Meadows, or Baltimore. Full-time remote will be considered.

Basic Qualification:

  • Bachelor's degree in electrical engineering with 9+ years of experience. or 7+ years with Master's degree
  • The ideal candidate would have experience in the following:  RF design background, Digital design background, Design process or related experience.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Defense and Military electronics design experience
  • Technical Project Management
  • Supplier Interface/Management experience

Experience in the following is a plus:  global supply chain, project management, contracts management, business management, supplier diversity, strategic sourcing, customer service, or operations.
