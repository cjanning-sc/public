Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Staff Systems Test Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Oklahoma, OK

As a test engineer you’ll be responsible for level I and level II system verification efforts.  You’ll utilize your understanding of system test to create test procedures based on Level I and Level II requirements, develop an expert understanding of your assigned subsystems, and utilize test infrastructure to dry run and execute system level verification.  

  • A Laboratory System Test Engineer synthesizes customer contractual needs and requirements into system test solutions that acknowledges technical, schedule and cost constraints.  
  • Develops and directs preparation and execution of comprehensive test plans, procedures and schedules for completing systems.
  • Perform requirements analysis and decomposition including developing derived requirements as needed to test the system under test. Support Test Equipment Checkout and System Integration. Support Test Execution as First Line Troubleshooter. Leadership Characteristics, including ability to mentor and lead technical execution with a team of system test engineers.
  • Develop test equipment, environment, and simulation requirements.
  • Acts as advisor to management and customers on advanced technical topics.  Perform as the hardware responsible engineer for the subsystem under test. Perform technical interactions with the system test team to ensure system requirements verification. Write Test Reports and Post Test Review’s.
  • Understand level I and level II system test (SYS III). Utilize Python to write automated system test
