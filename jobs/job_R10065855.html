Northrop Grumman Space Systems Sector is looking for you to join our team as a Staff Configuration Management/Data Management Analyst based out of either, Fairfax VA or Linthicum MD,

What You’ll Get to Do:

As a Configuration Management/Data Management Analyst, you will be helping to develop and sustain systems using current technologies and others yet to come. We are seeking team members who have a thirst for knowledge, approach each day with optimism, and have a relentless drive to solve complex problems. Creativity and innovation are imperative for this role, because at Northrop Grumman, many of the problems we work on are impossible… until they’re not.

  • Support program Configuration Management and Data Management (CM/DM) requirements.

  • Assist in planning, coordinating, implementing, and administering program data management systems, data bases, and confirmation management tools to support program CM and DM requirements.

  • Assist in building and managing program CM/DM systems and tools, libraries, workflows, and processes.

  • Provide inputs into program level metrics to benchmark effectiveness of CM and DM performance.

  • Lead the CM and DM efforts and will work closely with the program, customer representatives, functional organizations, and suppliers to ensure that program CM and DM processes are accurately implemented.

  • Assist in the coordination and preparation of project documentation, such as engineering drawings, production specifications and schedules, and contract modifications, to ensure customer contract requirements are met.

  • Create and implement CM and DM processes that are accomplished within the bounds of the contract.

  • Utilize integrated tools for accomplishing Configuration and Data Management Team mission needs.

  • Facilitate Engineering Review Board and Configuration Control Board.  This includes tracking engineering changes through the lifecycle, supporting the boards as a voting member, and creating and distributing agendas and meeting notes, along with announcements to baseline updates

  • Prepares work Products to support CM audits

Basic Qualifications:

  • 14 Years with Bachelors; 12 Years with Masters; 12 years with PhD, + 9 years of relevant experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.​

  • Active TS/SCI or TS with SCI eligibility, and willingness to sit for a polygraph exam

  • Familiar in managing deliverables, documentation, and data libraries

  • Familiar in managing government customer deliveries and submissions

  • Familiar in managing CM/DM file repositories

  • Familiar in establishing and following CM/DM processes throughout project life cycles to maintain consistent configuration control, developing, operating, and maintaining CM baselines

  • Experience facilitating CCBs

  • Strong communication and organizational skills

  • Ability to interface well with program management/customers

Preferred Qualifications

  • Active/current SCI access and poly

  • SharePoint management experience

  • Atlassian toolset experience

  • Teamcenter Unified Architecture tool experience
