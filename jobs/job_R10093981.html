Northrop Grumman is seeking a creative graphic designer with a deep expertise in data visualization and infographics, who specializes in presenting complex data in visually compelling, understandable, and informative ways to join our communications team here at Space Park. The role will report directly to the Product Marketing Manager of our communications team.  This position will be located at our Redondo Beach CA campus.

In this job, you will:

  • Produce various printed and digital graphic design work from rough sketches, notes, or verbal instructions.
  • Design and develop easy-to-understand, eye-catching data visualizations, infographics, logos, brochures, info flyers, presentations, etc.
  • Use a variety of graphic software applications with great latitude for creativity and independent judgement.
  • Ensure a consistent look and feel across the work being developed for each of our customers that also stays within our branding guidelines.
  • Prepare routine graphic design work as digital files for web or offset printing.
  • Design and develop materials for events and conferences (virtual or in-person), including physical environmental displays and/or posters.
  • Gather design feedback and exhibit flexibility and willingness to integrate changes to improve the work.
  • Work alongside a multi-talented team of communications strategists and visual storytellers.

The candidate must be detail oriented, have exceptional communication, and people skills, a positive attitude and demeanor, a self-starter with the ability to build strong working relationships with colleagues across the Northrop Grumman enterprise.
