Job Description

The Payload and Ground Systems organization within the ACME Engineering Space Systems pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter. Come be a part of our mission!

We offer phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly collaborative workplace. We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal-oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do. Does this sound like you?

Our Employee Resource Groups (ERGs) provide benefits for the member, our leaders and the company. Our ERGs offer opportunities to be a friend, be active, be a volunteer, be a leader, to be recognized and to be yourself! Every ERG is inclusive of all employees!

Our diverse portfolio of programs means there are endless paths to cultivate your career. We are well-known for our inclusive environment, as well as our excellent work/life balance. We also offer exceptional benefits/healthcare, a 9/80 schedule, and a great 401K matching program.

We have an opening for a Manager of Systems Engineering to lead our qualified, diverse team. We are seeking a team member who wants to solve interesting, complex problems to help protect our nation. We are a trusted mission payload and ground system provider with extensive experience providing end-to-end mission solutions from concept through operations and support. The team you will support is located at our Space Park campus in Columbus, OH.

The candidate must possess excellent leadership, interpersonal communication, and presentation skills, and work closely with the ISG COAST IPT Lead. The candidate will be responsible for directing aspects of the Sub-Project’s tasking from design through requirement verification, including Control Account Manager (CAM) responsibilities. This position requires coordination and interfacing with the other Sub-Projects, Systems Engineering, external customers, and site personnel. Candidate will work in a dynamic team environment, ensuring personnel follow well-defined processes. The candidate must have the ability to complete tasks with minimal direction and oversight, and mentor junior engineers. The candidate will also be expected to fill a 20% part-time functional Section Manager role and will be responsible for 10 – 12 systems administrators, network engineers, software engineers, systems engineers, and/or cyber/information assurance engineers. The responsibilities of the p/t section manager role includes: Support functional management activities such as staffing and workload forecast; employee engagement, mentoring and career development; identification and training of skills required to execute on programs; Participate in sourcing, recruiting, onboarding, and performance management; and Support Business Strategy Efforts (including proposals, gate reviews, independent review team, NAR/ICE, as necessary)

Core Responsibilities:

  • Work closely with the ISG COAST IPT Lead, APM, DAPM, and Subproject Managers (SPMs)
  • Day to day operations of the technical, schedule, and cost; staffing; providing staff during site installations; supporting organic growth proposals (including CROMs & ECPs), authoring TD/BOEs and proposal development; interfacing frequently with internal and external stakeholders, including cross-program counterparts, Tech Support, and the customer
  • Setting and operating to program objectives and providing guidance to employees
  • Planning, risk management, and project performance
  • Direct team members to perform system buildups, meet deadlines and work within budget
  • Work in a dynamic team environment, ensuring personnel follow well-defined processes and will be responsible for managing all software development tasks from inception through completion

Basic Qualifications:

  • Current SCI access or current DoD Top Secret clearance with the ability to obtain SCI access prior to start date
  • Bachelor's Degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics (STEM) discipline with 9 years of relevant work experience; Master's Degree in STEM with 7 years of relevant work experience; PhD in STEM with 4 years of relevant work experience
  • Previous ground systems experience with technical background in ground system requirements development, design, software development, software integration & test
  • Excellent leadership and interpersonal communication skills with the ability to interface with all levels of employees and management
  • Demonstrated ability to develop effective processes, implement process improvements and streamline activities

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Mission 1 experience
  • Experience working with internal and external customers
  • Experience with software and network architecture
  • Control Account Management (CAM) experience
  • Experience with Agile and DevSecOps methodologies
  • Demonstrated problem solving and decision-making skills
  • Previous software experience working in a C++/RHEL environment
  • 5+ years of previous management experience as a Software Manager, SPM, or Technical Lead a plus
  • Master's degree in a relevant technical or management specialty
  • Leadership role as a Technical Lead, SPM, or Deputy SPM preferred
