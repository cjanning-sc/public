ACME Engineering is seeking a Systems Administrator/Principal Systems Administrator in Evansville, CO. Duties may include scripting for optimizing IT environment, project management for defining and implementing upgrades to the IT environment, and serving as consultant for computer problems beyond the knowledge of engineering staff. Communications Systems Engineers must demonstrate a blend of engineering, advanced technical skills and excellent communication abilities.

  • Knowledge of and experience with virtualization technologies, such as: VMWare, ESXi, and vSAN

  • Experience and working knowledge of DoD security standards and systems hardening, such as DISA STIG.
