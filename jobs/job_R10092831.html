An organization within Northrop Grumman’s Payload and Ground Systems (PGS) division, Cutting Edge Optronics is a leading provider of high-power laser diode arrays, high power pump modules and diode-pumped solid-state (DPSS) lasers. The Quality Systems Assistant 2 is responsible for performing a variety of tasks related to Quality Control to assure the quality and integrity of incoming products and the maintenance and calibration of monitoring and measuring devices.

 

Key Responsibilities:

•   Verifies/Inspects Quality of mechanical/electronic parts/assemblies from suppliers

•   Interacts with suppliers to resolve quality issues quickly

•   Documents and maintains Quality and Calibration records

•   Utilizes measurement equipment and coordinates/performs Calibration of equipment

•   Supports generation of monthly KPI data

•   Identifies and solutions for improvements in quality, productivity, product cost, and safety

•   Communicates non-conforming Quality issues with Purchasing, Engineering, and Suppliers

•   Coordinates Supplier Corrective Actions with suppliers

•   Models/follows Safety practices and policies

•   Document and communicate non-conformances to suppliers

•   May perform inspections across various stages of production

•   Performs calibration and recordkeeping activities

•   Coordinates all calibrations from outside sources

 

Minimum Qualifications and Education:

•  High school diploma or GED and at least 2 years of additional education and/or related experience 

•  2 years previous experience in a related area with extensive knowledge of quality inspection and calibration processes and procedures

•  Specific vision abilities required by this job include concentrated attention, close vision, far vision, depth perception, color vision, and the ability to bring object into sharp focus

•  Proficient in the use of SPC

•  Excellent prioritization, interpersonal, organizational, and multitasking skills

Preferred Skills/Education:

•   ASQ Certified C.M.I, C.Q.T., C.Q.A. and CIT IPC A 610 and 620 (CIS) a PLUS but not required

•   Must possess the ability to read blueprints and schematic diagrams

•   High level of accuracy and attention to detail

•   Excellent analytical/problem solving, troubleshooting and teamwork skills

•   Good oral and written communication skills are required

•   Proficient in the use of measuring devices such as CMM, torque analyzers, pressure calibrators, calipers, micrometers, height gauges, thread gauges, optical comparator, etc.  

•   Experience with ISO 9001 Quality Management Systems

•   Prior internal auditing experience

•   Proficient in Microsoft Office and the ability to learn various databases and systems in performance of job duties.
