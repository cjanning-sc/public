Capital Infrastructure Project Manager III
R10092513
1st Shift (United States of America)

The Capital Infrastructure Project Manager III will lead classified area initiatives and operations for multiple designated classified facilities within Launch Vehicles Programs in Chandler, AZ. This role will report to the Infrastructure Program Integration Manager and will work closely with other LV Program Managers and cross-functional teams. The successful candidate will influence the direction of the classified area activities to meet the company’s strategic objectives, and balance programmatic needs to deliver results in a classified work environment. This person will be responsible to manage complex problems, projects, and have functional responsibilities.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Submit, manage and track CAPEX for all classified infrastructure builds
  • Conduct walkthroughs with customer, contractors, and cross-functional teams
  • Write Classified Infrastructure Scope of Work (SOW), rough order of magnitude (ROM) estimates
  • Manage Projects with cross-functional teams to meet closed area requirements including facilities, security, and hardware/ software needs.
  • Ensures successful execution of Closed Area operations.
  • Act as primary contact for required classified operations items.
  • Drives operational efficiencies that improve Closed Area program operations.
  • Integrates between Program Management, Program Customer, Cybersecurity, Industrial Security, and classified IT
  • Clearly presents technical approaches and findings for initiatives.
  • Develops and drives solutions to complex problems consistent with organization objectives.
  • Serves as consultant to management and customers on policies, plans, and objectives.
  • Manage program relationships to ensure all security requirements are met and in compliance.
  • Manage the classified infrastructure and classified space for Programs.
  • Manage and deliver any required deliverables pertaining to the secure area or program requirements

Qualifications:

Basic Qualifications

  • Active Top Secret clearance in-scope (granted or renewed in the last 6 years)
  • BA+6 years of experience: MS+4 years of exp; PhD+ 0 years of exp OR Equivalent years of experience in lieu of degree
  • Ability to obtain and maintain SAP clearance.    
  • Proven experience writing processes and procedures for data transfer Understanding of JSIG guidelines
  • Proven understanding and experience of program life cycles and program management fundamentals
  • Proven experience successfully managing technical project requirements
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office suite
  • Must be able to perform occasional lifting up to 10 lbs

Preferred Qualifications

  • Active Top Secret clearance adjudicated within the last 5 years
  • Experience in Leadership roles within the Aerospace and Defense industry, or within the military.
