Industrial Security Manager 2

1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman is seeking an Industrial Security Manager 2 for the RISE business unit based out of Redondo Beach, CA.

The candidate will be responsible for leadership and management of a team of security professionals. They will direct activities that support SAP & SCI level programs and will provide guidance to assure compliance with applicable National Industrial Security Program (NISP) and other security requirements.

As the Security leader, the candidate will be the primary security liaison between Northrop Grumman and the government customer. The Security Manager oversees all security activities and provides guidance to all personnel, to include supporting program managers in achieving key goals. The selected candidate must be able to work collaboratively in a team environment with program management, program personnel, cyber security personnel, external security professionals, and government customers.

In addition to this functional expertise, the successful candidate will possess strong people leadership skills and collaborative attitude with an ability to provide challenging assignments and foster an inclusive work environment.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Active Top Secret SCI security clearance with CI Polygraph
  • 10 Years of security experience with Bachelors; or 8 Years of security experience with Masters; an additional 4 years of applicable work experience may be substituted for a Bachelor’s degree.
  • Experience in leading/managing Industrial Security programs
  • Comprehensive knowledge of the National Industrial Security Program, DoD Manuals, Intelligence Community Directives, and related training
  • Effective verbal and written communication skills
  • Strong skills in team building
  • Strong skills in educating and mentoring team members

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Previous security management experience
  • Knowledge of financial planning and budgeting within a team environment
  • Ability to effectively engage with team members on task completion
  • Ability to recognize opportunities and take independent action at resolving challenges
