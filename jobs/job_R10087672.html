ACME Engineering Space Systems has an opening for a Principal Electrical Test Engineer to support the Ground Systems Development Department. This position is based in Dayton, OH.

The ideal candidate has experience with hands on hardware test and integration and is comfortable with racking units, being hands on in the assembly lab with hardware such as commercial test equipment, custom electronics (simple to very complex), PCBs, Units, Test Sets, subsystems, and systems.

This person will be working on programs of various sizes ranging from small to large teams.  This position has opportunity to travel both domestically and internationally after obtaining security clearance.

 

The responsibilities include supporting satellite system ground-based hardware testing, flight electronics test systems, site support, procurement, logistics, material tracking and disposition.

Basic Qualifications:

  • 5 years of engineering experience with a Bachelors degree in Electrical or Computer Engineering, or another STEM degree from an accredited institution; or 3 years of experience with a STEM Masters degree; or a STEM PhD. degree

  • 2+ years of electronic engineering test experience in an engineering environment

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s Degree in Electrical or Computer Engineering from an accredited institution

  • 2 years of Electronic Engineering test experience in an Aerospace Environment

  • Electronic Design experience in and engineering environment

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

  • Fast learner and have a strong interest in hardware integration and test

  • DOD TS or SCI clearance
