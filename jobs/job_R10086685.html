ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Sr Principal Data Scientist to join the Program Integration Team (PIT).   This position is in Detroit, MI and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.   

What You’ll Get To Do:

Experienced data scientist with strong problem solving skills and ability to create business intelligent analysis and data visualizations to support leaderships in decision making

The data scientist will work with product owner to develop analytics strategy and apply machine learning and modeling in business intelligence analytics

Strong candidate should meet the following expectations:

  • Ability to communicate data and engineering processes
  • Ability to present data reports to support investigations and root-cause analysis
  • Ability to lead team in technical development of tools to improve pipeline efficiency, eliminate repetitive tasks, and implement new data modeling and machine learning methodologies
  • Possess solid communication skills (written, verbal, and visual)
  • Comfort with requirements-analysis and stake-holder engagement.
