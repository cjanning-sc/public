Northrop Grumman Aerospace Systems has an opening for a Lab Operations Principal OR Sr Principal Cyber Systems Engineer to join our team of qualified and diverse individuals. This position will be located in Palmdale, CA.

This position is for a Lab Operations Principal Cyber Systems Engineer in Palmdale, California. This position will entail maintaining smooth operation of multi-user computer systems, including coordination with network administrators. Interacting with users and evaluating vendor products. Making recommendations to purchase hardware and software, coordinating installation and providing backup recovery. Develops and monitors policies and standards for allocation related to the use of computing resources. Ancillary duties may include setting up administrator and service accounts, maintaining system documentation, tuning system performance, installing system wide software and allocating mass storage space.  Lab Operations Cyber supports multiple shifts.  Training will occur on first shift; however, successful candidate may be placed on second shift or weekend shift additional compensation applies to alternate shifts.

Essential Functions:
•    Maintain availability of Test Station and Software Development Environment 
•    Ensure systems adhere to program configuration and data management plans
•    Ensure Lab Operations Policy and Procedures as being executed and updated 
•    Establish strict program control processes to ensure mitigation of risks and support certification and accreditation of systems
•    Periodically conduct a complete review of system audits and monitor corrective actions

Basic Qualifications for Principal (level 3):

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 5+ years of cyber and/or systems administration experience; OR Master’s Degree and 3+ years; OR 9+ years of experience  in lieu of degree

  • DoD 8570 Level 2 Certification, such as Security+ CE 

  • A current in scope DoD Secret security clearance is required to start

  • Must be able to obtain and maintain program access prior to commencement of employment

  • Requires experience with setting up administrator and service accounts, maintaining system documentation, tuning system performance, installing system wide software and allocating mass storage space

Basic Qualifications for Sr Principal (level 4):

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 9+ years of cyber and/or systems administration experience; OR Master’s Degree and 7+ years; OR 13+ years of experience in lieu of degree

  • DoD 8570 Level 2 Certification, such as Security+ CE 

  • A current in scope DoD Secret security clearance is required to start

  • Must be able to obtain and maintain program access prior to commencement of employment

  • Requires experience with setting up administrator and service accounts, maintaining system documentation, tuning system performance, installing system wide software and allocating mass storage space

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Systems Administration experience, to include Windows, Linux, and VM environments

  • Cybersecurity/Information Assurance experience 

  • Understanding of enterprise-level computing platforms involving virtualization, distribution and high availability

  • Experience implementing storage allocation, reclamation, replication and migration

  • Experience operating and configuring security relevant tools such as Splunk and Nessus
