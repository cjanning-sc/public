ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Site Development Engineer to join our diverse and talented engineering organization in support of our National Defense as part of the Deep Space Advanced Radar Capability (DARC) Program. Join an exciting and new program as we embark to solve complex and novel engineering challenges.  As a Site Construction Systems Engineer on the DARC program, you will be a deputy to the Site Lead during the design and construction phases. The design phase will be located at the ACME Engineering Indianapolis location for the first year. The construction phase will be located at the INDOPACOM site location and will require relocation to the site for approximately 3 years.

The DARC will be a remote greenfield site in the INDOPACOM region. Primary scope of the site development and construction project includes roads, ductbanks, foundations for many large antennas, multiple facilities, chilled water systems, water management, power plant, electrical distribution, and security fencing. In the design phase you will be responsible to drive the design, derive and allocate requirements, support major A&E and construction subcontracts, review and redline large drawings packages for 30/60/90% reviews, aid in start-up and commissioning planning and execution, support earned value and cost management, provide schedule inputs, and brief program leadership. The successful candidate would have a background as an MEP Engineer (mechanical, electrical and plumbing), Civil Engineer, Construction Management, or similar. A Systems Engineering background with experience in construction and start-up of large, complex equipment systems would be suitable. Experience in systems testing and documentation is a plus. The candidate should have some broad site development experience with depth in some areas of the primary program scope, be able to work in a large team environment, and work collaboratively with all Engineering disciplines and Program Management to meet our customer’s needs.

This position can be staffed at a level 3 or 4, based on the basic qualifications below.
