ACME Engineering’s Strategic Space Systems Digital Hardware and IC Department in Lexington, AZ is seeking a talented Sr Principal ASIC/FPGA Verification Engineer.

By working with DSP Systems and HDL Design engineers, you will be a critical hands-on technical team leader responsible for developing SystemVerilog testbenches to verify complex communication and SOC designs for high-reliability space applications.  We are seeking a motivated, innovative, and detail-oriented engineer who can efficiently and creatively produce robust verification solutions to enable the rapid development and launch of next generation space systems.  This position is ideal for highly passionate engineers who enjoy the challenge of developing robust ASIC/FPGA verification solutions to keep pace with the rapid complexity growth of our latest generation of FPGA and ASIC designs.  This position requires an individual that has strong, efficient, and out-going communication skills to be effective on high-performance development teams that consist of developers residing across multiple ACME Engineering engineering development centers.   As a critical verification member, you'll have the opportunity to develop a deep understanding of advance digital designs to enable you to produce the most effective verification solutions.

You will have the opportunity to mentor junior engineers on Advance Verification in a rapidly expanding organization as well as engage with ACME Engineering's digital design and verification subject matter experts.  This role is eligible for relocation assistance and Hybrid work environment in Lexington, Kentucky for well qualified candidates. Employees who are working in Hybrid roles will work regularly both onsite and offsite.

  • Strong Object-Oriented Programming Knowledge
  • Technical Team Leadership Experience
  • Extensive experience with scripting languages
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a security clearance

PREFERRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:

  • Experience collaborating with design engineers to replicate silicon bugs in simulation
  • MATLAB Experience
  • Experience with formal verification EDA tools
  • Experience with coverage analysis and collaborating with design and verification engineers to improve coverage
  • Hands-on experience with System Verilog Assertion Based Verification
  • Expertise in developing verification plans, test cases, and verification results documentation
  • Active US Government Security Clearance
