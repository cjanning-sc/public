ACME Engineering Tactical Space Systems is seeking a Systems Engineer/Principal Systems Engineer. This position is located in Indianapolis, CO and will support an IRAD program for a physics-based simulation environment focused on satellite operations.

What you’ll get to do:

  • Develop space system architecture models using Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE)
  • Derive, allocate, update, and maintain requirements and specifications
  • Research other modeling and simulation (M&S) systems to define a future integration solution
  • Define high-level ConOps that provides a representation of a future integration solution
  • Support cost estimation to perform future integration solutions
  • Support all phases of the systems lifecycle including concept, design, development, test, operation, and maintenance.
  • Maintain design documentation
  • Work with orbital dynamics; guidance, navigation, and control.

Required Qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at Systems Engineer level (T02) or Principal Systems Engineer Level

(T03)

  • Systems Engineer (T02) requires a minimum 2 years of related experience with a Bachelor’s degree; 0 years with Masters in Science degree
  • Principal Systems Engineer (T03) requires a minimum of 5 years of related experience with a Bachelor’s degree; 3 years with a Master in Science or 0 years with a PhD
  • Understanding of Systems Engineering principles and process
  • Experience in Cameo EA including logical, physical, and functional architecture
  • Ability to work in a multi-disciplinary team: software engineers, orbital analysts and test engineers
  • Must have U.S. Citizenship with current or active Top Secret Clearance with the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current or active Top-Secret Clearance with Special Access Program
  • Prior experience with RPO, Satellite Ground Station Software, Distributed M&S, and/or wargaming
  • Experience in deriving, allocating, updating, and maintaining requirements
  • Experience in ConOps development
  • Experience in Python
  • Experience in Agile Development
  • Experience working in an Agile Team
