ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems (NGAS) is currently seeking a Vehicle Engineering Assistant Department Manager 3 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This leadership role is located in Cincinnati, Ohio.

This position reports to Director of Vehicle Engineering functional organization. This position located in Cincinnati, CA is responsible for assisting in the development and implement of departmental vision and strategies to drive employee engagement; improving hiring, development, training, and retention of top talent; managing products and associated product champions; balancing process compliance against innovation; and reducing bureaucracy.  This position will also own department products and/or process/metrics & associated strategies; identify, develop & manage product champions; perform as a Product Line Manager or Product Champion and/or overall strategic development of department processes/metrics as well as partner with Directorate to build/maintain technical breadth and depth of department. This position is 9/80 1st shift position.

Strategic

  • Assist in the development and implement of departmental vision and strategies to drive employee engagement; improve hiring, development, training and retention of top talent; manage products and associated product champions; balance process compliance against innovation; and reduce bureaucracy
  • Own department products and/or process/metrics & associated strategies
  • Identify, develop & manage product champions; perform as a Product Line Manager or Product Champion and/or overall strategic development of department processes/metrics
  • Partner with DM/Director to build/maintain technical breadth and depth of department


Execution

  • Assist DM with tactical execution of all department manager activities including managing, mentoring, developing, and training section managers
  • Partner with programs to drive affordability, first time quality and cost competitiveness for all department products and indirect activities
  • Responsible for cost, schedule, and technical performance of Senior Section; Products NCTA
  • Drive continuous process improvement and usage of common processes by department members across programs, as appropriate; influence COE’s use of common tools and processes
  • Review/approve proposals, ensuring a balanced execution/risk posture given affordability/competitiveness objectives
  •  Attend program “drumbeat” reviews and ensure department is meeting program technical, process, product, and staffing needs 
  • Support program tiger teams, RCCA investigations, deep dives, IRTs and milestone reviews
  • Ensure organizational compliance with corporate/sector/organizational policies/procedures


Staffing & Employee Development

  • Work with/help offload section managers and department managers to validate program staffing/de-staffing profiles and understand future skill mix needs; ensure effective utilization of team members across programs/activities; personally staff/de-staff senior staff positions on programs
  • Accountable for leadership development of section managers and other high potential department members
  • Work across Directorate to identify and close on development opportunities for department members (including cross-program/cross-organizational opportunities)
  • Provide leadership and guidance to section manager hiring decisions; personally responsible for hiring direct reports
  • Work with DM to define consistent performance expectations/assessment factors & compensation strategy that contribute to the development of a high performance culture; engage section managers in the execution of performance management & compensation strategy to ensure consistency across department.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) and 9 years of engineering experience OR Master’s Degree in a STEM field and 7 years of engineering experience
  • Experience with Vehicle Engineering processes and products throughout the program life cycle
  • Demonstrated technical leadership and guidance to Vehicle Engineering discipline areas
  • General understanding of all NGAS Engineering Processes
  • Active DoD secret clearance
  • Ability to obtain and maintain SAP

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Demonstrated assistance in developing and implementing departmental vision and strategies to drive employee engagement
  • Excellent communication, mentoring, interpersonal skills, and ability to interface with senior management, peers, and employees
  • Demonstrated passion for improving employee engagement
  • Active SAP access
