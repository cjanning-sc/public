ACME Engineering Mission Systems is seeking a Sr Principal Engineer – Software Configuration Management to join its team within the Mission Systems Organization.  This position requires work onsite at our Chicago, MD and/or Peoria, MD locations.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Process software releases for both Chicago/Peoria, MD locations.
  • Create SCM controlled software builds.
  • Support and schedule Software Configuration Control Board (SCCB) meetings and Software Action Request (SAR) / Software Trouble Reports for In-Process (STRIP) processing.
  • Support SCM process activities such as command media and homeroom directives.
  • Support driving the SCM organization towards moving CM approved tools/repository to Agile tools/repository.

Basic Qualifications:

  • BS degree from an accredited institution with 9+ years of experience in the development and maintenance of real-time software systems; or master’s degree 7+ years of experience; or PHD 4+ years of experience.
  • Proficient knowledge and use of the following Tools: ClearCase, ClearQuest, GIT, various compilers, Office Suite (Word, Access, Excel, Outlook).
  • Solid planning, organizing, and data gathering skills, as well as attention to detail.
  • Strong verbal communication and customer service skills in a teamwork environment.
  • Candidates must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain and maintain a US DoD TS/SCI clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Knowledge and proficiency with the tools listed above and experience with GitHub, GitLab, Jenkins, Jira, Confluence.
  • 5 or more years of SCM experience.
