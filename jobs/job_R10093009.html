Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal Test Conductor/Sr Principal Test Conductor to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is located in Palmdale, Ca, Mojave, Ca and Edwards AFB, Ca .
 

PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Responsible to ensure engineering tests serve mission objectives and mechanisms are in place to execute the tests.
  • Responsible for testing all customer samples.
  • Oversees the operational aspects of engineering tests and serves as liaison to program, engineering, and line management.
  • Reviews status of test requirements, manages schedules, and prepares reports.
  • Selects and supervises technicians during test.
  • Assesses anomalies and develops resolutions to documented procedures in conjunction with Test and Quality Engineering.
  • This position requires outstanding written and oral communications skills.
  • Individual will be required to serve as a principal subject matter focal point between maintenance, test operations - contractor and customer.
  • Individual must be able to work without direction to develop solutions to complex problems; must be able to work both within a team environment and independently; must be self-motivated, thorough, and detailed; and must be willing to learn additional engineering discipline areas.

“This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.”

Basic Qualifications:

“This requisition may be filled at either a principal or a Sr. Principal Test Conductor"

Principal Test Conductor:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 5 years of industry-related experience OR a HS Diploma with 9 years industry-related experience        
  • Ability to work non-standard hours and multiple shifts on a part-time basis
  • Active DoD Secret Clearance within scope of five years
  • Experience working in a production or test environment.
  • Must be able to work from extended work platforms at elevated heights and for an extended period of time
  • Basic computer proficiency (Microsoft Word, Excel, Outlook)
  • Must have experience with aircraft maintenance, systems check-out, pre and post-flight processes.
