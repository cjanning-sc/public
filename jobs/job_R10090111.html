Engineer Digital 5 - Responsible Design Engineer - Top Secret Clearance required
R10090111
1st Shift

The Digital Products Department (part of ACME Strategic Space Systems) in Dayton, OH is seeking a talented Engineer Digital 5 - Responsible Design Engineer. This person will be required to develop, test, and integrate complex digital hardware and support test systems for spacecraft flight programs.

The ideal candidate will be an experienced digital design engineer who has worked on the design and development of a space digital unit containing multiple digital boards.

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Unit level requirements development, management, and flow down, and coordination with system engineering

  • Unit level architecture development

  • Support unit level manufacturing, assembly and test, engineering model and flight hardware

  • Support unit verification, test-set development, environmental testing

  • Support subsystem level Integration & test

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in STEM from an accredited institution and 14+ years of experience in an Engineering environment; OR, a minimum of 12+ years of experience with a STEM Master's degree; OR a minimum of 9+ years of experience with STEM PhD. degree

  • Previous experience as a responsible design engineer at board or unit level for Aerospace applications

  • Hands-on lab experience with electronic hardware

  • Candidate must have, and be able to maintain, at least ONE of the following active U.S. Government security clearances:

Dept. of Defense Top Secret level with a background investigation date within 6 years

OR SCI access with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI) completed within 5 years

OR Dept of Energy Q clearance with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI) completed within 5 years

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 10+ years of experience in digital board design and analysis  for space applications

  • Experience with digital signal processing and/or FPGA design experience

  • Experience with parts selection and managing parts procurement

  • Experience with engineering support to manufacturing including hardware troubleshooting

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

  • Master’s Degree in Electrical or Computer Engineering from an accredited institution
