1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman's Mission Systems (MS) is seeking a Senior Principal Chief Engineer - Project Lead (SAP/PEO MES) to implement a new sector application for our Manufacturing Execution System (MES).

The Project Lead will establish the strategic direction for the MES selection, development, and deployment and ensure that the tool is applicable for business and manufacturing processes throughout the sector.

This position allows for full time telecommuting.


What's Great About Working for Northrop Grumman:
**Be part of a culture that thrives on intellectual curiosity, cognitive diversity and bringing your whole self to work.
**Use your skills to build and deliver innovative tech solutions that protect the world and shape a better future.
**Enjoy benefits like work-life balance, education assistance and paid time off.
**Many sites have 9/80 days, telework and flexible scheduling options, plus generous paid time off.
**Indeed named Northrop Grumman a top 15 company for work/life balance in 2019. We were rated #1 by Millennials on Indeed as the Best Company to work for in 2018.


What's Great About This Job:
**You will be part of the Manufacturing Digital Technology and Strategy team that is pursuing sector digital transformation and Industry 4.0/Digital Thread efforts. This includes interfacing with the teams developing the Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) system and our Plant Connectivity and Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) infrastructure.

**You will use your innovation and creativity to design business automation solutions that will revolutionize manufacturing execution and have a sector level impact.


The Engineering Project Lead responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the tasks outlined below:
• Develop the Business Case, Return on Investment and the Implementation Roadmaps for the future MES solution(s) that align with the other MS strategic digital transformation programs. Provide guidance and future vision of how MES fits into the larger enterprise tools, landscape and architecture.
• Lead, stand up and coordinate MES Pilot(s) with MES vendors, strategic consultants, operations and enterprise functional organizations. Coordinate and set priorities on requirements, use cases and integrations.
• Provide subject matter expertise of discrete COTs MES or Manufacturing Operations Management (MOM) solutions in electromechanical assembly, Surface Mount Technology, Machine Fabrication, Microelectronics and PCB Fabrication as needed to drive this strategic initiative forward.
• Provide direction on architecture and architectural impacts in partnership with Subject Mater Experts (internal/external and other domains).
• Lead the interface between our Enterprise Services and Manufacturing Operations organizations and other MS sector tool leaders to gather and help translate MES/MOM requirements between the organizations and solutions.
• Establish the business value and priority for capability development by working across multiple manufacturing sites, functional and process areas; earn customer satisfaction via effective implementation of the business objectives.
• Lead the project management and communication to ensure successful execution to project objectives; work with leadership to identify objectives and to acquire and track budget.
• Brief Sector Operations and Enterprise Leadership on projects and digital transformation concepts.
 

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's of Science degree or higher in Engineering or related STEM area plus 9 years of relevant professional experience (7 years with an MS degree/9 with a PhD)

  • Knowledge and understanding of discrete Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) use, functionality and fit in manufacturing

  • Knowledge and understanding of IIOT solutions and use cases in a manufacturing environment 

  • Experience with development and/or implementation of an enterprise manufacturing solution (PLM/ERP/MES/IIoT/etc.)

  • Must have fluency in MS Office software applications

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience implementing Discrete Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) in Electromechanical Environment with integrations to other key enterprise solutions (PLM, ERP, MRO, etc.)

  • Experience implementing IIOT solutions in Electromechanical Environment with integrations to other key enterprise solutions (PLM, ERP, MES, LIMS, MRO, etc.)

  • Experience with the deployment and integration of enterprise tools and applications

  • Experience with planning/running organizations and events

  • Experience implementing Low Code/No Code Enterprise Solutions

  • Business Process Engineering and Organizational Change Management for MES implementations

  • Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) certifications: Agilist, Practitioner, Product Owner, Scrum Master

  • Experience with Agile management tools (e.g., TFS or Jira)

  • Project Management Professional (PMP)
