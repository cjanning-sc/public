Put your skills to the test by pushing the boundaries of what’s possible. From global defense to sustainment and modernization to mission readiness, your experience and ability will make it a reality. Our programs are built on equal parts of curiosity and collaboration. Our combined effort means our customers can connect and defend millions of people around the world. With ACME Engineering, you’ll have the opportunity to be an essential part of projects that will define your career, now and in the future.

Are you ready to put your experience to work at ACME Engineering? If so, our Defense Systems Sector needs you to join our team as a Software Engineer and be a part of our AN/ALR-56M program. The AN/ALR-56M is a Radar Warning Receiver installed on our F-16 and C-130J aircrafts. This position is located at Kansas City, MO.

In this position you will develop, integrate, test, document and deliver software upgrades in support of system integration testing. You will have the opportunity to take ownership of difficult problems and drive to a solution while working in a supportive team environment. 

Your specific responsibilities include:

* Develop, modify, maintain, and test embedded software using software engineering best practices and standards.

* Participate in the full life cycle of software development, to include requirements development, application development, modular testing, integration, formal system testing, release, installation, and upgrades.

* Apply technical expertise in solving complex integration problems. 

* Interact frequently with Program Management, external suppliers and/or government customers.

This position may be filled at either Level 1/Associate OR Level 2 / Software Engineer.

Basic Qualifications:

* Associate: A minimum of 4 years of software engineering experience and a High School diploma or equivalent. A Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or Computer Engineering from an accredited university may also be considered.

* Software Engineer:  A minimum of 6 years of software engineering experience and a High School diploma or equivalent. A Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or Computer Engineering from an accredited university and a minimum of 2 years of systems or software engineering experience OR a master’s degree in one of the above listed majors.

* Experience developing software in C/C++

* Ability to obtain/maintain a US Department of Defense Secret level security clearance within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Preferred Qualifications:

* Current secret or higher security clearance

* Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or Computer Engineering

* Experience in C/C++ programming within a Linux/VxWorks environment

* Experience developing custom displays and user interfaces

* Familiarity with Qt Integrated Development Environment

* Familiarity with Electronic Warfare (EW) principles

* Familiarity with software configuration management tools (JIRA, Bitbucket, Redmine, Subversion, etc.)

* Familiarity with Software integration and test techniques

* Familiarity with integration of avionics interfaces (Ethernet, MIL-STD-1553B, ARINC-429, etc.)
