ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking an Electrical Engineer / Principal Electrical Engineer to join the System Test Team. This position is located in Detroit, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Grade level offered will be based on candidate level of experience.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will support the design, development and integration of hardware and software for the System Integration Labs (SILs) used to validate the ground-based Command and Launch equipment.  Typical duties to include:

  • Test aid design, fabrication, test stations/set build-up and verification and validation activities

  • Equipment acceptance test procedure (ATP) development, coding and/or scripting

  • Create validation procedures, user manuals, drawings

  • Work with mechanical and test engineering teams to build, certify, and operate the SILs

  • Create system models in CAMEO and Siemens NX

  • Regular customer interaction and coordination

  • Experience with creation and release of engineering hardware drawings, interface diagrams, wiring diagrams
