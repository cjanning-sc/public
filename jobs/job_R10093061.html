Principal Military Mission Command Information Systems (MCIS) and Battle Space Workstation (BSWS) Trainer
R10093061
1st Shift (United States of America)

Put your skills to the test by pushing the boundaries of what’s possible.  From global defense to sustainment and modernization to mission readiness, your experience and ability will make it a reality.  Our programs are built on equal parts of curiosity and collaboration.  Our combined effort means our customers can connect and defend millions of people around the world.  With Northrop Grumman, you’ll have the opportunity to be an essential part of projects that will define your career, now and in the future.

Northrop Grumman Defense Systems sector (NGDS) is seeking a Principal Military Mission Command Information Systems (MCIS) and Battle Space Workstation (BSWS) Trainer to join our growing team of diverse professionals supporting our III Corps Mission Command Training (MTC) program at Fort Sill in Lawton, Oklahoma (FSOK). The MCIS & BSWS Trainer will supervise a team of Trainers at Fort Sill Mission Training Complex (MTC) and ensures that students receive world-class instruction in accordance with approved Programs of Instruction (POIs).

Responsibilities:

  • Ensures all Mission Command Information Systems (MCIS) are loaded with the latest software version, that all classrooms are simulation driven and demonstrating multi-MCIS integration; also responsible for ensuring all individual training equipment is properly maintained and serviced

  • Manages the scheduling of courses, the enforcement of standards required for students to graduate, and the cross-training and professional development of the Team

  • Prepares MCIS briefing material for the supported Mission Command Training audience.  Ensures the team is prepared to support various simulation exercises using War Simulation and other Simulation systems

  • Responsible for supporting various exercises with “over the shoulder” MCIS support

  • Prepared to assist in the training of all supported classes

Basic Qualifications:

  • One of the following:

    • High school diploma or GED with a minimum of 10 years of military training and Command Post Operations experience

    • Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 6 years of military training and Command Post Operations experience

    • Master’s degree with a minimum of 4 years of military training and Command Post Operations experience

  • Must possess an active U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) Secret security clearance

  • Must have analytical subject matter expertise on Command Post Computing Environment (CPCE) and MCIS Integration in support of Mission Command Training

  • Must be familiar with managing and integration of various MCIS including CPCE, Advanced Field Artillery Tactical Data System, Air and Missile Defense Workstation, Tactical Airspace Integration System, Global Command and Control System-Army, Joint Automated Deep Operations Coordination System, Command Web, Tactical Messaging, Web Automated Verification System, and Joint Battle Command-Platform  

  • Must have a working knowledge of the following simulation systems used to support MCIS course instruction and exercise support, Joint Land Component Constructive Training Capability and Division Exercise Training and Review System

  • Recent (last 2 years) experience with MCIS or BSWS

  • May be required to obtain Network+ certification depending on Mission Command Information System expertise

  • Must be willing to work on-site at Fort Sill in Lawton, Oklahoma

  • Must be willing to travel as required (up to 10% of the time)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced degree
