Northrop Grumman, Strategic Space Systems Division, has an opening for a Manager Engineering 3 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Space Park Redondo Beach, California.

Responsibilities:

The selected candidate will manage the Materials & Sciences Laboratories Department (MSL) in Redondo Beach and Manhattan Beach, CA.  The Materials and Sciences Laboratories department performs laboratory analysis and testing, and provides expert consulting services related to chemistry and Materials & Processes (M&P) to internal and external Northrop Grumman customers. MSL is responsible for performing chemical analysis, material testing and characterization, mechanical and electrical parts testing, Purchased Component Work Order (PCWO) processing, receiving inspection, shelf life extension, Research and Development (R&D), monopropellant rocket engine (MRE) hot-fire testing, explosives and other hazardous materials testing, conducting engineering-scale tests and rapid prototype fabrication.

The primary responsibilities of the MSL department manager ensure that MSL laboratories successfully perform to the department’s statement of work. The department manager is responsible for ensuring laboratory operations are performed safely.  Responsible for ensuring laboratory operations are in compliance with all Local, State and Federal Regulations and Northrop Grumman Ethics and Policies.  The role requires providing technical leadership and oversight for the department as well as technical representation to both internal and external customers.  The department manager is responsible for overseeing staffing for the department to ensure adequate personnel with the required skills and clearances are on staff to perform the department’s statement of work.  The department manager is responsible for managing the annual indirect budget (labor and OIE) along with requesting and justifying capital acquisitions for required test equipment to run the laboratories.  Finally, the department manager is responsible for the department’s performance management and discipline.
