Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal Project Management/Sr. Principal Project Management to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Melbourne, FL.

The selected candidate will support the capture operation lead. Position requires oversight and management the capture projects.  The candidate will serve as liaison between capture team and IPT leadership, as well as, control project actions, track budgets, manage schedules, prepare status reports, assess risks and issues, and develop resolutions to meet productivity, quality, and client-satisfaction goals and objectives. The selected candidate will also develop processes and tools for monitoring project progress and for intervention and problem solving with program integration team, project team leads, and program support functions.

Oversees and manages the operational aspects of ongoing projects and serves as liaison between project management and planning, project team, and line management. Reviews status of projects and budgets; manages schedules and prepares status reports. Assesses project issues and develops resolutions to meet productivity, quality, and client-satisfaction goals and objectives. Develops mechanisms for monitoring project progress and for intervention and problem solving with project managers, line managers, and clients.


Essential duties include, but are not limited to:

Act as capture focal for integration to the PIT organization for all PIT services needed to support the capture team
Be the capture team interface between Integration IPT and other program IPTs and stakeholders
Serve as consultant between Program integration and other IPTs across the Program on integration projects
Collaborate across all functions within the program to develop advance concepts, techniques and requirements for new application and tools
Facilitate and lead process improvement projects, implement improvement initiative, and develop mechanisms to audit improved processes
Assist in the identification and documentation of risks, issues, and opportunities


Manage Capture Risks and Issues, track mitigation and corrective action plans
Develop and deliver presentations to Capture and Program Leadership
Provide solutions to complex problems that are consistent with the organization’s objectives
Identify opportunities for change and champion needed change in the organization
Actively track and report capture status on a regular interval basis
Develop and analyze performance metrics


The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, and collaborative/team settings across all levels.  Candidate should also have strong organization and communication skills, the ability to champion actions, and complete tasks with little supervision.  

Please note that this position can hired at either a Principal Project Management or Sr. Principal Project Management level.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Project Management:

Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited university and 6 years of experience in Project Management, Program Operations, or Program Integration OR Master’s Degree and 4 years of related experience in Project Management, Program Operations, or Program Integration, and Microsoft Office skills (PowerPoint, Word, Excel)
Experience preparing and presenting presentations
Current and active DoD Secret security clearance


Preferred Qualifications:

PMP certification
Familiar with proposal capture
Knowledge of project management principles and practices
Bachelor's or Master’s degree in Science, Technical, Engineering or Math (STEM) related field

Basic Qualifications for Sr. Principal Project Management:

Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited university and 10 years of experience in Project Management, Program Operations, or Program Integration OR Master’s Degree and 8 years of related experience in Project Management, Program Operations, or Program Integration, and Microsoft Office skills (PowerPoint, Word, Excel)
Experience preparing and presenting presentations
Current and active DoD Secret security clearance


Preferred Qualifications:

PMP certification
Familiar with proposal capture
