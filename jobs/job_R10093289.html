Our Cleveland, CA site is in search of an Associate Metrology Engineering Technician to join our growing team.  

 

Roles and Responsibilities 

 

  • Support internal and external customers’ needs. 

  • Identify, troubleshoot and solve tooling problems and provide assistance to manufacturing, inspection and engineering issues. 

  • Metrology support: tooling/production/inspection. 

  • Tooling: setup per tool drawing or cad model 

  • Production: setup shop aids, align flight hardware per customer’s drawings or CAD model 

  • Inspection: support inspections needs for in-process and End Item Data Pack deliverables

  • General knowledge with spatial analyzer software or similar software 

  • General knowledge and experience using CAD software 

  • Experience with MS Excel 

  • Ability to read drawings/blueprints and interpret GD&T 

 

Preferred Qualifications 

  

  • Certification in engineering or metrology disciplines 

  • Some college preferred. 

  • Familiarity with working around delicate spacecraft hardware 

  • Availability to work overtime and weekends as needed. 

  • Must be able to work on feet for extended periods of time.
