ACME Engineering manufactures some of the world’s most technically advanced products. Our manufacturing plants are highly automated, cutting-edge centers of excellence where employees leverage the latest technology, including the strategic use of automation, 3-D printing and robotics. There is a myriad of job functions associated with the manufacture of our products that range from machinists, engineers, operation program managers, and quality inspectors. The Operations, Planning, and Control (OPC) team at ACME Engineering Mission Systems (NGMS) is looking for you to join our team as a Production Controller 2 based out of Racho Carmel, Cleveland facility. This position will be on a 9/80 2nd shift, 3:30pm-12:30am.

Job Description:
• Maintain schedules for material requirements and availability for the manufacture of industrial and commercial products to support production control.
• Compile records concerning quantity, cost, and type of material received, shipped, in stock or inventory, and/or in production.
• Expedite flow of materials, parts, and assemblies within or between departments in accordance with production and shipping schedules.
• Assist in determining possible and actual shortages may initiate action to correct these deficiencies.
• Use knowledge of production, procurement, and engineering departments to alert proper authorities regarding potential problems and appropriate remedial action.
• Estimate and log production rate and time expenditures and may be required to establish sequence and lead time of each operation to meet shipping dates.

 

Basic Qualifications: 

• High School Diploma/GED and 2 years additional education and/or experience.
• Functional understanding of Microsoft Office Products.
• Strong work ethic.
 

Preferred Qualifications: 

• Extensive knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite.
• Prior experience with SAP.
