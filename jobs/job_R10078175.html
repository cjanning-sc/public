Northrop Grumman Space Systems Division designs, builds and delivers space, defense and aviation-related systems to customers around the world. Our main products include launch vehicles and related propulsion systems; missile products, subsystems and defense electronics; precision weapons, armament systems and ammunition; satellites and associated space components and services; and advanced aerospace structures.

Northrop Grumman Space Systems Division is seeking an experienced Mechanical Engineer to assist a team of mechanical engineers to perform Mechanical Ground Support Equipment (MGSE) design engineering tasks on one or more spacecraft systems or subsystems.

Responsibilities may include the following:

  • Work on design of Mechanical Ground Support Equipment (MGSE) hardware from requirements all the way through integration and test. 
  • Perform detailed design tasks using Computer Aided Design (CAD) and other software tools to develop and document designs. 
  • Interfacing and communicating with customers, program, Integration and Test (I&T), functional management, other engineering disciplines and component suppliers. 
  • Perform cost and schedule planning for MGSE projects and proposals. 
  • Be self-motivated and provide technical solutions to a wide range of difficult problems under limited supervision.
  • Skills include, but not limited to, Computer Aided Design (CAD), knowledge of Geometric Dimension and Tolerancing (GD&T), 3D Printing, welding, Design for Manufacturing (DFM) and Mechanics of Materials.

This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

Basic Qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either a Principal Mechanical/MGSE Engineer 3 or Sr. Principal Mechanical/MGSE Engineer 4.

Basic Qualifications: Principal Mechanical/MGSE Engineer 3:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Aerospace Engineering, Mechanical Engineering or related area and 5 to 9+ years of experience.
  • Must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain government clearance.
  • Experience in performing engineering tasks associated with design, manufacturing and integration of MGSE.
  • Experience with Computer Aided Design (CAD) software.
  • Experience with Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T) standards and preparation of manufacturing documentation.
  • Experience with cost and schedule management.
  • Knowledge of structural members, including weldments and other material joining and subtracting techniques. 
  • Ability to proactively seek out requirements from engineering, manufacturing and I&T.
  • Ability to document assumptions and requirements.  Ultimately verify validity before proceeding with MGSE design and manufacturing.
  • Basic understanding of analysis and simulation methods and results.

Basic Qualifications: Sr. Principal Mechanical/MGSE Engineer 4:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Aerospace Engineering, Mechanical Engineering or related area and 9 to 14+ years of experience.
  • Must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain government clearance.
  • Experience in performing engineering tasks associated with design, manufacturing and integration of MGSE.
  • Experience with Computer Aided Design (CAD) software.
  • Experience with Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T) standards and preparation of manufacturing documentation.
  • Experience with cost and schedule management.
  • Knowledge of structural members, including weldments and other material joining and subtracting techniques. 
  • Ability to proactively seek out requirements from engineering, manufacturing and I&T.
  • Ability to document assumptions and requirements.  Ultimately verify validity before proceeding with MGSE design and manufacturing.
  • Basic understanding of analysis and simulation methods and results.
  • Ability to lead a team of engineers and designers to close complex design solutions.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with large and small structure design.
  • Experience with CREO Parametric software.
  • Experience with hands-on hardware fabrication.
  • Demonstrated leadership experience.
  • Proficiency with analysis and simulation tools.
