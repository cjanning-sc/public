Northrop Grumman Defense Systems is seeking a talented Quality Analyst to join our team in Huntsville, AL. The selected candidate will perform the following:

Position Overview:

The Program Quality Analyst team monitors program production and testing activities processes to ensure the mission success of the program and works with the various teams to promote continuous improvements. Our team is exceptionally detail oriented. Each team member has individual program assignments, but we work as a united team to identify and resolve potential issues before timelines are impacted. We feel strongly about treating each other with respect and working with integrity. Do you enjoy executing to established processes/procedures and working in a team environment? Come join our team!

Role and Responsibilities:

  • Reviews, analyzes, and reports on quality discrepancies related to assembly, process, mechanical, electrical, and electro-mechanical systems.
  • Liaison between the customer and Program Management Team, engineering, and manufacturing personnel to ensure requirements are met.
  • Investigate problems, establish disposition and corrective actions for recurring discrepancies.
  • Recommend corrective actions, dispositions, and modifications to processes.
  • Manage the creation and disposition Non-conformances and Quality Notifications
  • Assure compliance in quality protocols in accordance with contractual requirements and NG policies and procedures.
  • Participate in program audits and weekly meetings.
  • Communicate openly and effectively with teammates and customer representatives.
  • Demonstrate problem solving skills/critical thinking in a manufacturing environment.
  • Demonstrates a timely and highly focused approach to document reviews and details
  • Witness testing activities
  • Perform Internal audits

 

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels of the organization.
