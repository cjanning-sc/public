Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security.  Our organization is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter. 

We are looking for you to join our team as a Field Technician 3 based out of Oxnard, CA. As a Field Technician 3 at Northrop Grumman you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation. Through the use of Model-based Engineering, DevSecOps and Agile practices we continue to evolve how we deliver critical national defense products and capabilities for the warfighter. Our success is grounded in our ability to embrace change, move quickly and continuously drive innovation. The successful candidate will be collaborative, open, transparent, and team-oriented with a focus on team empowerment & shared responsibility, flexibility, continuous learning, and a culture of automation.

What You'll Get To Do:

The position requires a Field Technician with a high level of understanding in mechanical principles. Candidate must be able to interpret schematics, engineering notes, and work from oral and written instructions. Also, it is desirable for candidate to have strong background in mechanical troubleshooting and repair. Candidate must be capable of working alone or in a team environment. Duties will be assigned according to operational requirements and will be distributed between office, industrial, shipboard, and remote locations.

Installs, operates, maintains, repairs and modifies equipment. Performs a variety of maintenance and technical support on products such as equipment, integrated systems and subsystems, and software at customer and/or field locations. Analyzes and evaluates products and related performance. Troubleshoots and diagnoses malfunctions to eliminate problem in minimum time. Installs, upgrades and removes products ensuring coordinative engineering field change. Maintains effective customer communications and relations. May provide onsite training of customer support personnel.

Job tasks frequently require the candidate to stand, walk, and climb in industrial facilities where it is necessary to bend, stoop, reach, and lift moderately. As a field team member, the employee may be assigned to work in areas and be assigned duties not related to electronics technician tasks to support site operations. These may include painting, assembly, repair, and equipment relocation activities. Work area assignments may be in proximity to certain potentially harmful physical and chemical agent areas. These may include, but are not limited to: fumes, dust, noise, heat, ionizing and non-ionizing radiation, chemicals, etc. To ensure employee safety and health, the field site maintains personnel protection programs consistent with applicable laws and regulations and may require the candidate to complete training in work procedures, use of personnel protective equipment, and medical surveillance. As part of medical surveillance programs, the employee may be required to take medical examinations to assess his/her medical qualification for duty involving work with these agents.

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Hydraulics
  • Pneumatics
  • Mechanical Assembly
  • Use of test equipment
  • Troubleshooting/Repair
  • Schematic reading
  • Attention to detail

Basic Qualifications for a Field Technician 3:

  • High School diploma or equivalent and a minimum of 4 year’s related experience.
  • Formal training in Electronic Technology or equivalent to 2 years technical trade school or military technical school.
  • Must have an active/Current Top Secret Clearance with the ability to obtain and maintain SCI access if selected. Top Secret Clearance with SCI is required to start work.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with maintaining, modifying, and operating equipment in a marine environment.
