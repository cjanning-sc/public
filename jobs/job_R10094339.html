Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is an industry-leading provider for prime satellite and payload capabilities and directed energy and electronics solutions for national security, military, and civil customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible.

 

ACME Engineering, Strategic Space Systems Division, is looking for you to join our team as a Department Manager/Assistant Department Manager or our Space Manufacturing & Engineering (SM&E) directorate to support satellite programs in Columbus, CA.

 

This department manager/assistant department manager position is responsible for managing a team of engineers and/or technicians in support of space manufacturing. Position requires intimate knowledge of satellite sub assembly manufacturing, lean concepts, digital transformation and production metrics suites to manage day to day production.  In addition, project management techniques, planning and process tool sets, functional management of large teams is desired. Position requires cross function collaboration to support significant program demands. Responsible deliveries include: Cost, schedule and quality commitments, staffing, affordability strategy and execution. Team support from proposal phase through initial concepts, development, engineering models and flight product delivery.

Responsibilities Include:

  • Leading high profile teams that enable new solutions and resolve issues for low volume high complexity space and air products.

  • Collaborating with program to develop effective strategies to maintain cost and schedule, mentoring, and developing mentoring relationships, assessing and developing a comprehensive staffing and training strategy to support multiple programs concurrently.

  • Managing a function within the larger organizational unit, setting & operating to objectives & providing guidance to subordinates based on goals, objectives & company policy

  • Coordinate workload in all manufacturing facilities to deliver value and ensure schedules of customer base are met.

  • Work with manufacturing and process suppliers to improve manufacturing processes and ensure successful delivery of hardware.

  • Promoting the Space Systems vision and principles in their area of responsibility

  • Building a culture that empowers and motivates team performance, establishing functional shared accountability with the programs and new business pursuits

  • Working closely with the other Department Managers, Fellows and section managers to define and implement the department strategy

  • Staffing critical skills and developing retention strategies

  • Labor Resource and Planning (LRAP)

  • Setting goals and providing performance feedback

  • Overseeing leadership development and knowledge retention efforts

  • Managing indirect and capital projects and budgets

  • Improving and enforcing processes and product early development engagement

  • Promoting metrics-based management across the organization and Digital Transformation

  • Leading affordability initiatives and reviewing and supporting proposals and program milestones presentations

  • Candidate should be technically diverse in multiple disciplines of manufacturing and assembly. Develop and manage process improvement in advanced manufacturing and digital transformation initiatives to improve performance, reduce cost and drive schedule accuracy.
