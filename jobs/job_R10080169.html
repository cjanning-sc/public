Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security.  Our Engineering and Sciences (E&S) organization pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter.  Come be a part of our mission!

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is actively seeking an experienced IT Professional to fill a role that requires a broad array of knowledge and skills working with team members to support a Cyber Security Program as a Scrum Master in Tampa, FL.

We're looking for a highly motivated individual with an impeccable work ethic and a strong ability to work in a collaborative fast-moving dynamic team environment. The primary team is located in Tampa, Florida with distributed team members embedded directly with the customer.

Responsibilities are in more than one of the following information systems and/or related areas: personal computer applications training, data control and scheduling coordination, systems administration, data security administration, and associated fields.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Will independently coordinate, audit, and participate in Configuration Control Board (CCB) processes and meetings.  This requires infrequent Continental United States (CONUS) travel requiring direct coordination with Program Manager (PM) on budgetary constraints and limitations
  • Assist Configuration Control Board, coordinate change requests, and validate documentation and software/hardware changes to verify compliance with Configuration Management Plans (CMP)
  • Frequent collaboration with internal company and external contacts for quality check inspections and create and maintain documentation. Represents organization on specific projects and tracking metrics. May make presentations to small groups
  • Develops solutions to a variety of problems of moderate scope and complexity.   
  • Facilitate, conduct and document quarterly audits, system baseline reviews, release planning and scheduling program protection, critical analysis reviews.
  • Review and understand technical and programmatic documentation for accuracy, implementation and completeness
  • Work with cross-discipline team members and must have excellent verbal and written communication skills, presentation experience and the ability to work with a diverse community of engineers and managers to achieve the goals of the program team.
  • Serve as a central point of general information for projects to interface and communicate information to a vast audience
  • Facilitate Daily Scrum, Sprint Planning, Sprint Review, Sprint Retrospectives and Quarterly Releases while mentoring Product Owners, Scrum teams, and stakeholders
  • Assist with product backlog refinement and scheduling
  • Works under only very general supervision. Work is reviewed for soundness of judgment and overall adequacy and accuracy ​

This requisition may be filled at either a Info System Admin Generalist or Principal Level.

Basic Qualifications for the Info System Admin Generalist Level:

  • 3 Years with Bachelors; 1 Year with Masters / an additional Four (4) years of experience can be substituted in lieu of college degree
  • US Citizenship is required with an active DoD Top Secret/SCI security clearance which was active in the last 24 months
  • Must possess or be able to obtain DoD 8570 Certification for IAT Level II or higher within two (2) months of starting

Basic Qualifications for the Principal Level:

  • 6 Years with Bachelors; 1 Year with Masters / an additional Four (4) years of experience can be substituted in lieu of college degree
  • US Citizenship is required with an active DoD Top Secret/SCI security clearance which was active in the last 24 months
  • Must possess or be able to obtain DoD 8570 Certification for IAT Level II or higher within two (2) months of starting

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience in development of engineering processes and documentation, configuration control and related processes  
  • Bachelor's or higher degree in a technical field highly desired
  • DoD 8570 Certification for IAT Level III
  • Experience with Agile and Scrum methodologies, processes, and procedures
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office software suite and Atlassian Jira and Confluence
  • Must practice exceptional organizational skills
  • Must be able to establish and maintain documentation and deliverables
  • General knowledge of Cyber Systems Engineering and Cyber Security
  • Experience with military / DoD practices
  • Experience with Atlassian Jira and Confluence (certifications a plus)
  • Experience as a Scrum Master (certifications a plus)
  • Experience as a Project Analyst and CCB member preferred and able to mentor others
  • Possess an understanding of, and be able to manage the scheduling, coordination, planning, collaboration, and documentation of multiple ongoing projects, meetings, and Scrum sessions on a daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly basis
  • Demonstrated success working individually or as part of a team requiring little supervision
  • When required, have the ability to work outside of normal working hours and weekends as needed to support the customer’s needs
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Must be able to work in a fast-paced environment
