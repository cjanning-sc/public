ACME Engineering Fellows are recognized experts in their fields who solve the hardest technical problems while ensuring we retain the hard-won insights needed to perform complex tasks successfully.  These leaders define possible in our most important technical capability areas, identifying and cultivating essential people, processes, and tools.  They are deployed against critical programs and captures aligned with our sector’s top business priorities, advancing our products and solutions to meet customers’ missions every day.

As an ACME Fellow in Advanced Weapons, you will serve as the primary sector domain expert for this significant business/technical area.  You will conduct complex scientific or technical analyses that require the synthesis of diverse disciplines and alternatives; solutions may be developed under conditions of uncertainty or where little precedent exists. 

As the primary domain expert for this technical area, you will set and drive strategy for a broad set of technical initiatives and will be empowered to make decisions and be held accountable for results that significantly impact the annual operating plan (AOP) and long-range strategic plan (LRSP) outcomes and other major initiatives. You will also act as a thought leader representing the business within the customer community and within industry groups.   

ACME Engineering Defense Systems in Toledo, CA is currently seeking to fill a ACME Engineering Fellow position to support the Advanced Programs Operating Unit.  As a recognized leader in their technical domain, the qualified candidate will join Engineering, Program and Business Development leadership in architecting, developing, maturing and integrating Missile Systems employing breakthrough technology innovations, new development/production opportunities and maintaining the future growth of the Advanced Weapons business.

The selected candidate can work in our Toledo, CA location, or remotely, as long as they have access to a classified ACME Engineering work location as required. The selected candidate will also need to be able to travel to Toledo, CA as required. Relocation assistance will only be provided for candidates able and willing to work in Toledo, CA.

In this role, you will:

  • Serve as the primary domain expert for Missile Prime Integration, system architectures and system level performance supporting Advanced Programs business objectives

  • Develop information which extends knowledge in technology domains covering state of the art, autonomous systems with subsystems that include flight sensors, flight computers, communications, propulsion, payloads, sensors and air vehicle integration

  • Conduct very complex analyses that require the synthesis of diverse disciplines and alternatives and achieve efficient, effective, and innovative engineering solutions

  • Introduce and develop new critical technologies for advanced missile system concepts and architectures

  • Plan R & D programs and recommend technological application programs to accomplish long-range objectives.

  • Influence business objectives by developing advanced applications of technology, and attracting and capturing new business consistent with the long range strategic planning       

  • Serve as a thought leader for the organization within the customer community and within industry groups.

  • Interfacing with design and implementation engineers to ensure technical excellence and shape the development of robust and compliant designs

  • Provide key insights and inputs in the development of proposals for technology and product development
