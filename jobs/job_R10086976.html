Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Structural Engineer / Principal Structural Engineer to join the System Engineering Team. This position is located in Roy, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Final grade level for position will be based on selected candidate experience level.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will perform basic to advanced structural analysis and support the development of damage tolerance criteria across the program. Typical duties and responsibilities to include:

  • Perform structural analysis including static, dynamics, and stability analysis.

  • Create Finite Element Analysis (FEA) models and perform systems and sub-systems structural analysis.

  • Compute margins using appropriate analysis methods.

  • Review of requirements for materials, structure systems & subsystems.

  • Participate and present in internal and external (customer) design reviews.

  • Manage and track multiple priorities while maintaining focus on common project goals
