Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Sr Principal Operational Technology Cybersecurity Engineer. This position will be located in Huntsville, Alabama and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. 

What You’ll Get To Do: 

Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Identify and assess cyber security risks to ICS and critical infrastructure networks 
  • Helping to create and manage security monitoring, event triage, and threat detection capabilities for OT systems 
  • Analyzing potential threats to mission and possibly joining teams to evaluate OT sensor deployment 
  • Collaborating with site engineers and senior cybersecurity leaders to suggest actions to address incidents and known vulnerabilities 
  • Design and implement security measures, such as firewalls, intrusion detection and prevention systems, and encryption 
  • Monitor and analyze security events and incidents to identify trends and potential threats 
  • Implement and maintain security policies and procedures to comply with industry standards and regulations 
  • Provide technical guidance and training to other team members and stakeholders 
  • Stay current with emerging cyber threats and technologies to improve the security posture of the organization.

Job Qualifications: 

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Science, Technology, Engineering or Math (STEM) with 9 years’ experience; 7 years’ experience with a Master’s degree; or 4 years’ experience with a PhD 
  • Must be a US Citizen 
  • Active DoD Secret Clearance with ability to obtain Special Access Program (SAP)  
  • Knowledge of control system design, programming, and troubleshooting. 
  • Knowledge of information security technologies as well as architectures and programs for IoT and/or Operational Technology (OT) Security, Governance, Risk and Compliance 
  • Knowledge of Operational Technology/SCADA protocol analysis, including common protocols throughout industry (e.g., BACNet, Ethernet/IP, Modbus, etc.) 

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

  • Working knowledge of the Agile Development Framework 
  • General knowledge of industry standards and best practices (e.g., NIST SP 800-82, ISA/IEC 62443, ISO/IEC 27001, IEC 61508, IEC 61511, NERC CIP, etc.)  
  • Thorough knowledge of security technologies, such as firewalls, intrusion detection and prevention systems, and encryption 
  • Thorough knowledge of instrumentation (e.g., flow meters, transducers, densimeters, controllers, etc.) 
  • General knowledge of electrical schematics/drawings 
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team. 
  • Strong verbal and written skills to communicate effectively both with customers and internal project teams 
  • General knowledge of operational technology environments (e.g., Power & Energy, Manufacturing, Oil and Gas, etc.) 
  • General knowledge of containerization and virtualization technologies 
  • General knowledge of programming languages, such as Python and C++ 
  • Passion for learning (technical and professional skills); implementing practices from others; trying, failing, and learning from both successes and failures; sharing practices and knowledge for others’ benefit. 
  • Ability to communicate results effectively
