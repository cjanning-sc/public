Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking a Principal/ Sr. Principal Manufacturing Engineer to join the Test and Assembly team, at our advanced manufacturing and design facility located at the Navy Yard in Philadelphia, PA. 

The Manufacturing Engineer will support Manufacturing Operations Engineering and will have design/process and hands-on manufacturing responsibilities, spending approximately 50% of the time or more on the factory floor with hours that may span multiple shifts.

The Manufacturing Engineer is responsible for taking the product design from the drawing and converting it into a sequence of detailed assembly/ manufacturing instructions.  The activities involved as part of this role include:

  • Create new and modify existing assembly/ manufacturing instructions with necessary operations to support build sequence, while also including specifications/quality requirements

  • Develop tooling / fixtures to conduct the operations steps and to serve as manufacturing aids for technicians

  • Provide guidance to Design Engineering during drawing reviews regarding design concepts and specification requirements to best utilize equipment and manufacturing techniques; and to identify any manufacturability challenges or changes required ahead of drawing release

  • Provide guidance to technicians on the factory floor to assist in any technical questions regarding the product build; and to resolve issues regarding non-conformances

  • Investigate and initiate corrective action to ensure product quality; resolve quality issues that arise during the build process to ensure all parts conform to the design configuration

  • Inspect performance of machinery, equipment, and tools to verify their efficiency

The ideal candidate will be able to work individually as well as collaborate with a diverse group of individuals, work tactically, but with an eye towards future strategy, and exhibit solid communication skills with internal management personnel and clients.   

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Manufacturing Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering or similar Engineer degree plus 5 years of relevant experience, or Master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering or similar Engineer degree plus 3 years of relevant experience  

  • Experience with solid modeling tools such as Siemens NX or Solidworks/Pro E 

  • Experience working in a Manufacturing environment

  • U.S. citizenship with the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance

Basic Qualifications for a Sr. Principal Manufacturing Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering or similar Engineer degree plus 9 years of relevant experience, or Master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering or similar Engineer degree plus 7 years of relevant experience  

  • Experience with solid modeling tools such as Siemens NX or Solidworks/Pro E 

  • Experience working in a Manufacturing environment

  • U.S. citizenship with the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications for both levels:

  • Mechanical Engineering degree

  • Experience with Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerance (GD&T)

  • Manufacturing or engineering background in a marine discipline (e.g., Rotating Machinery engineering or manufacturing)

  • Experience with Large Mechanical Assembly and Heavy Lifting Principles

  • Experience with machine tool equipment and machine shop processes (Vertical and/or Horizontal Machining centers

  • Experience in implementing new designs and processes into a factory environment

  • Working knowledge and past experience with equipment and tool suppliers

  • Understanding of welding processes

  • Fluency in MS Office Tools with a focus on documentation  

  • Active Secret Security Clearance
