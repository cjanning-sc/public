JOB DESCRIPTION: 

The successful candidates shall review, analyze, prepare, and modify provisioning data, and provide guidance in the preparation of Provisioning Technical Documentation (PTD) and related data in accordance with Northrop Grumman procedures. This provisioning data will be used to identify, select, catalog and determine support material requirements, and in the development of the following: Provisioning Parts List and design Change Notifications. The candidate shall also provide support for modifications, reviews, and updates to any of the above existing lists. The candidate shall attend and support provisioning meetings and conferences and perform other functions as directed. Position requires an organized, detail-oriented, self-starting individual who can work both, independently and in a team-oriented environment. The candidate must be proficient in Microsoft Office Suite. 

 

NG Skills 

 

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS: 

  • Must have 10 years of relevant experience. Will consider 6 years with Bachelor's Degree; or 4 year of experience with Master's Degree. 

  • Must have basic understanding of aviation maintenance processes and aircraft systems of a modern military aircraft. 

  • Must be capable and willing to follow strictly defined processes. 

  • Must be able to read and interpret engineering drawings and system description documents. 

  • Must possess a current DoD Secret Security Clearance and be able to maintain.  The security background investigation granting that clearance must have been completed within the last 5 years. 

  • Obtain special program access on or before official employment start date. 

  • Applicants must be willing to work a flexible work schedule that may include extended shifts, holidays, and weekends and support on call status for 24 hours-a-day, 7 days-a-week, 365 days a year. 

 

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS 

  • Master’s degree with 4 year relevant experience. 

  • 3-5 years’ experience as a qualified mechanic, technician, weapons, or operator in a technical field such as aircraft, ship, missile, or space systems highly desired. 

  • Ability to verify parts list and read drawings, understand the DOD Provisioning systems and how they function, have provisioning database (EAGLE, SLIC, Mil-STD-1388) experience, basic knowledge of how to use all Microsoft Office products, able to navigate in Haystack, FEDLOG, or WEBFLIS or similar government tools, understand maintenance concepts related to provisioning documentation and have experience developing an indentured PPL and DCN for submission to a customer. 

  • Have experience using 3D CAD modeling experience in NX, CATIA, or similar CAD programs. 

  • Have experience with Common Product Life Cycle Management (CPLM) programs. 

  • Have experience utilizing Raytheon EAGLE Database.
