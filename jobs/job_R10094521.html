ACME Engineering employees have incredible opportunities to work on extraordinary systems that impact people’s lives around the world. We’re curious and committed people, passionate about innovating technology to connect and protect the world by solving our most challenging problems. We look for people who have bold new ideas, courage and a pioneering spirit to invent the future…and have fun along the way.

ACME Engineering Mission Systems is looking for a savvy, strategic Communications manager to shape and deliver compelling communications to support our Airborne Multifunction Sensors business. Reporting to the director of Communications for the Airborne Multifunction Sensors division, the division communications manager is responsible for leading a talented team, and helping shape and lead communications strategy across earned, owned and paid channels and measuring results.

Join our team of dedicated professionals and define possible with ACME Engineering. Your communications expertise will play a critical role in continuing to position the company as a technology innovator. This position may be based in Chicago, Md., or Northern Iowa, though the right candidate can work from anywhere.  Travel to ACME Engineering facilities will be required periodically.

If you are a collaborative communicator who enjoys a dynamic work environment, have a strong executive presence, an innovative spirit and a passion for strategic thinking, we’re looking for you.

You’ll make a difference by:                                                       

  • Leading a priority portfolio designed to elevate company thought leadership on important next generation technologies and emerging markets
  • Overseeing the development and execution of an omni-channel, integrated business communications plans to achieve business and communication goals
  • Partnering with functional colleagues to develop and edit internal and external communications, including announcements, webcasts, videos, speeches, presentations, media engagements and more
  • Leading, preparing and positioning subject matter experts for strategic communications opportunities across internal and external channels
  • Partnering with communications colleagues to develop a holistic and integrated communication strategy to achieve business and communication goals 
  • Preparing and sharing metrics on communications efforts and leveraging those results to evolve strategies and approaches to achieve business objectives

You’ll be a great fit if you:

  • Are experienced in developing and executing strategic communications plans to achieve business objectives and advance thought leadership
  • Effectively manage multiple projects and priorities and deliver high-quality work within deadlines
  • Can confidently and independently interact with executive leaders, and all levels of employees
  • Enjoy working in a fast-paced environment and are comfortable leading through change
  • Are a strategic thinker, strong problem solver who is wildly creative and collaborative and pays attention to every detail
  • Are committed to delivering quality work and taking accountability for results
  • Are able to digest complex concepts and communicate them in simple, effective and compelling ways
  • Are adept at identifying and telling stories, and a strong editor

Basic qualifications:

  • Demonstrated knowledge and experience developing and deploying strategic communications campaigns and measuring business impact 
  • Experience building teams and leading people, formally or informally
  • Bachelor’s degree and minimum of 8 years of communications, public relations, marketing or journalism experience or master’s degree and minimum of 6 years of communications, public relations, marketing or journalism experience.
  • Ability to travel up to 25% to the Chicago, Illinois site in support of the business, as needed 

Preferred qualifications:

  • Experience working in a large, matrixed and/or geographically dispersed organization 
  • Experience developing productive relationships with executives
  • Experience in technology, aerospace, defense or other science-related business or government fields 
  • Demonstrated judgement in decision making and leadership actions with capabilities to drive change through creative and/or other disruptive methods, ideas & approaches 
  • Understanding of AP Stylebook
  • Experience with measurement tools and analyzing data
