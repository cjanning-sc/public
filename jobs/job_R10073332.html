Level 1/2 Associate Industrial Security Analyst
R10073332
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman has an opening for either a level 1 or level 2 Industrial Security Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals! This position will be located on site in Melbourne, FL.

Responsibilities: Develops and administers security programs and procedures for classified or proprietary materials, documents, and equipment. Studies and implements federal security regulations that apply to company operations. Obtains rulings, interpretations, and acceptable deviations for compliance with regulations from government agencies. Prepares manuals outlining regulations, and establishes procedures for handling, storing, and keeping records, and for granting personnel and visitors access to restricted records and materials. Conducts security education classes and security audits. Investigates security violations and prepares reports specifying preventive action to be taken.

  • Ensure that the programs maintain compliance with company and government regulations

  • Oversee the activities and priorities of the security program including physical security and access control

  • Field daily security requests/issues

  • Assist with investigations, information security, and personnel security measures at the facility

  • Establish security processes and procedures that satisfy customer expectations

  • Assist with providing overall site Security in addition to supporting Special Access Programs, to include OPSEC and Security Awareness

  • Conduct security reviews of technical papers, Foreign Travel and DD 254s

  • Administer security indoctrination and debriefings, assessments, visitor control; process requests for investigations; and prepare personnel access request (PAR)

  • Other security-related duties as assigned

This position may be filled as either a level 1 Associate Industrial Security Analyst or a level 2 Industrial Security Analyst.

Basic Qualifications for a level 1:

  • High School Diploma/GED with additional 4 years of applicable work experience

  • Candidate must have an be able to maintain an Active FINAL U.S. Government security clearance at the DoD Secret level with a background investigation date within the 6 years

  • Experience in file/data management or Personnel Security

  • Strong attention to detail

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills

  • Ability to work in a rapid and dynamic work environment

  • Ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Programs

Basic Qualifications for a level 2:

  •  High School Diploma/GED with additional 4 years of applicable work experience

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Secret Clearance

  • Strong attention to detail

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills

  • Ability to work in a rapid and dynamic work environment

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree

  • Active Security Clearance

  • Minimum 1 year of Security experience

  • Ability to work independently with minimum supervision

  • Working knowledge of the NISPOM and DoD SAP Manuals

  • Working knowledge of JPAS/DISS and Northrop Grumman Security Business Systems

  • Experience working on SAP programs
