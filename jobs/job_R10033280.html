1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Aerospace Systems has an opening for an Engineering Manager 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This 4/10 1st shift position will be located in Palmdale, CA.

This position is for the Section Manager (SM) of Liaison Engineering for the PAICoE F-35 Program, reporting to the Vehicle Engineering (VE) Liaison Engineering Department Manager, Palmdale Aircraft Integration Center of Excellence.  The SM will lead a growing team of 10-15 Liaison Engineers. SM will be responsible for the disposition of nonconforming metallic and composite parts and assemblies as well as subsystem nonconformance.

For functional tasking, approximately 15% of the SM’s time will be focused on recruiting, staffing, retaining talent, weekly team meetings, bi-weekly 1:1 meetings, and holding performance review discussions. Supporting the VE Liaison Engineering Department Manager on the NGAS Liaison Engineering Community of Practice is expected.

SM is expected to be thoroughly versed, and preferably a Subject Matter Expert (SME), for the Liaison Engineering discipline and the MRB process.


Basic Qualifications: 
• Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering from an ABET institution. 
• Minimum of 9 years of engineering experience. 
• Experience in a technical leadership/management roles.
• Material Review Board (MRB) Authority.
• Ability to obtain and maintain DOD Secret Clearance and special program access.


Preferred Qualifications: 

• Master’s Degree or PhD in ME, AE, or Civil Eng with (Structures) Design emphasis.
• MES experience is strongly preferred.
• Experience in staffing forecasting.
• Current Active In-Scope DOD Secret Clearance with the ability to obtain and maintain special program access.
