We are Northrop Grumman Australia. Part of an 85,000 strong global Defence organisation, over 800 talented employees in Australia are leveraging our global resources to deliver a superior Australian Defence capability. As we build our business and our capabilities, we are also building a reputation as a great place to work. Help us define what is possible for our nation, and for your career.

Your role:

Our Business Management team is critical to Northrop Grumman Australia’s success and sustained growth. We are looking for a Pricing Analyst to support our new business pursuits as well as existing programs to develop accurate and thorough pricing and financial responses and modelling. The aim of maximising profitability and cash flow for viable commercial decisions will be central to your success.

Working closely with a broad range of stakeholders including senior leadership and external stakeholders you will take ownership of developing sound commercial and financial strategies to increase our probability of winning new business and securing steady growth into the future.

About you:

Key to success will be your ability to collaborate effectively and courageously, collecting key data points across cost, schedule and technical elements within project-based environments.

With your previous experience working in a fast-paced environment along with your ability to identify problems and take a solutions focused approach, you will quickly become an integral part of our high performing team based in Brisbane.

Along with qualifications in accounting, finance, business management (or similar) you will have had a strong understanding of pricing/estimation principles and extensive analytical and problem solving skills. Experience using pricing tools such as ProPricer will be beneficial and familiarity with bids and proposals within Defence industry is highly desirable.

What’s in it for you:

Whatever your role, life stage or background, you can shape your career here in your way with highly challenging work, great colleagues and career development, plus a range of flexible benefits and working patterns.

  • Professional Development – further education, leadership development, professional industry memberships and unlimited access to a range of online training
  • Options for flexible & hybrid work including 9 day fortnight
  • Purchased annual leave
  • Salary packaging including novated car leases
  • Generous paid parental leave
  • Volunteer Leave
  • Health & Wellbeing program
  • Employee Assistance Program
