Sentinel (GBSD) - Supplier Quality Engineer/Principal Supplier Quality Engineer - 7270*
R10065781
1st Shift

Responsible for Supplier Quality risk management. Develops and implements supplier quality evaluation and control systems. Assures supplier process and product quality, including contractual, regulatory, and technical compliance. Interfaces directly with supplier personnel and must be knowledgeable in and capable of assessing both supplier quality and manufacturing processes and procedures. Conducts product and process verification activities, monitors supplier quality performance, and drives root cause corrective action analysis for supplier quality issues. Supports the maintenance of the approved supplier list and periodically reviews supplier performance to determine whether suppliers should remain on the approved list. Manages supplier requests for waivers, deviations and discrepancy reporting process. Keeps management apprised of supplier performance, quality status, and risks. Coordinates and oversees Supplier Quality plan implementation through all program phases. Actively engages in supplier evaluation, qualification and selection as part of the Business Acquisition Process (BAP) and as new procurements are required on existing programs. Manages implementation of Supplier Quality processes, including supplier approval activities, source selection evaluations, supplier classification designation (Critical, Key and Standard), development and execution of supplier quality risk assessments and mitigation plans, and supplier quality requirements development and flow-downs.

Defines and monitors mission assurance program specifications and processes to ensure mission success of programs. Performs or assures quality, risk management, safety, reliability and maintainability of program achievements, subcontractors, and suppliers in accordance with contractual requirements. Assesses program performance and risks, and determines resources to ensure mission assurance.

The selected candidate will be responsible for supporting documentation that implements the Sentinel Enterprise and Program Supplier Quality Assurance Plan, policies, procedures, & tools required to support Program Tasks as well as infrastructure needs. Additionally, the selected candidate will be support for SQA oversight activities for suppliers and subcontractors of hardware developed/manufactured/assembled/tested for Sentinel.


Roles and responsibilities:
•    Prepare documentation that implements the Sentinel Enterprise and Program Supplier Quality Assurance Plan, policies, procedures, & tools required to support Program Tasks as well as Enterprise infrastructure needs.
•    Create process health, progress and stability metrics
•    Supplier Quality Assurance oversight activities of hardware developed/manufactured/assembled/tested for Sentinel.

Basic Qualifications:
•   Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM-related field of study. Level 2 Supplier Quality Engineer will require a BS in STEM with at least 2 years of experience. Level 3 Principal Quality Engineer will require a BS in STEM with at least 5 years of experience.
•   Demonstrated ability prioritizing tasks and meeting deadlines.
•   Excellent liaison between local personnel, suppliers, and customer workforce
•   Experience in evaluation, trace, and analysis of requirements
•   Ability to obtain a Secret (S) security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:
