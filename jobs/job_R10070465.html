SSSD is seeking a Principal or Senior Principal Business Process Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in Columbus, CA. Specifically, this position will join the Cost Management Home Room Team and will report to the Manger of Program Control.

In this role, the Business Process Analyst will analyze business and technical processes to formulate and develop new and modified business information processing systems, such as production and inventory control systems, financial tracking systems, marketing, and human resources systems. The incumbent will represent the business unit to define requirements and business cases for the technology developments and coordinate with business and technology teams, ascertaining system requirements, such as program functions, output requirements, input data acquisition, and system techniques and controls.  This role requires knowledge of e-commerce tools, computer system capabilities, business processes, and workflow.  A preferred candidate will have a thorough understanding and working knowledge of Business Systems, MPM and/or COBRA, SAP, and Earned Value and its implementation as it pertains to program business rhythms.

This position requires the incumbent to possess a Department of Defense (DoD) active Top Secret (TS) security clearance and/or access to Special Compartmentalized Information (SCI). The stated clearance level must have been updated within the last 6 years.

Responsibilities include:

  • An understanding of Micro-Frame Project Manager (MPM) and/or COst BReAkdown (COBRA), Earned Value Management (EVM), as well as broad knowledge of industry systems and procedures, including a working knowledge of PowerBi
  • Use of program business systems and coordinating monthly business processes to enable the delivery of successful products to the program and customer
  • Facilitating improvements in processes, software and influence architecture requirements to improve efficiencies
  • Process/procedure/compliance, and Ad Hoc program activities

This position may be filled at a level 3 or level 4 as outlined below. You must meet the basic qualifications of either level to be considered.

Principal Business Process Analyst- Level 3

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree with 5 years of experience in Business Administration to include Program Cost Control and Planning or 3 years of stated experience and a Master's Degree
  • Active Top Secret (TS) and/or Special Compartmentalized Information (SCI) Access 
  • Experience working in Micro-Frame Program Manager (MPM) and/or COst BReAkdown (COBRA), MS Office Tools

Senior Business Process Analyst- Level 4 

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree with 9 years of experience in Business Administration to include Program Cost Control and Planning or 7 year of stated experience with a Master's Degree
  • Active Top Secret (TS) and/or Special Compartmentalized Information (SCI) Access 
  • Experience working in Micro-Frame Program Manager (MPM) and/or COst BReAkdown (COBRA), MS Office Tools

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master's Degree
  • Active Top Secret (TS) and/or Special Compartmentalized Information (SCI) Access 
  • Working knowledge of Earned Value Management (EVMS)
  • Experience working in Power BI
  • Current or previous experience working with restricted customers
