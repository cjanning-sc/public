ACME Engineering Aerospace Systems has an opening for a Principal Mechanical Design Engineer/ Sr. Principal Mechanical Design Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is in Philadelphia, FL.

**This position may be filled by either Principal Mechanical Design Engineer/ Sr. Principal Mechanical Design Engineer level based on the qualifications listed. **

The selected candidate will contribute to the research, design, analysis, manufacture, and testing associated with aircraft Support Equipment.

Essential Functions:

  • Mechanical design of test, measurement, and handling equipment used on military aircraft.

  • Gathering of data, requirements development, interfacing with suppliers, and performing basic stress and tolerance analysis

  • Acting as manufacturing liaison during build process of your project to ensure overall quality, delivery, and cost objectives are achieved

  • Development of test plans and performing environmental testing of Support Equipment

  • Testing and integration of manufactured Support Equipment on aircraft

  • Proficient with design, drafting, and stress analysis of mechanical systems. Drafting skills shall include considerable knowledge of solid modeling software, (NX, Pro Engineer, or Catia), or CAD based computer drafting software.

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels. Candidate must also be willing to travel to various site locations to demonstrate the function of the designed equipment.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Mechanical Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) discipline with 5 years’ experience; or a Masters’ Degree in a STEM Discipline with 3 years’ experience; or a PhD in a STEM Discipline with 0 Years’ experience

  • Proficient using CAD software in generating detailed engineering drawings used for manufacturing of mechanical and/or electrical assemblies

  • Ability to obtain a DoD Secret or higher Security Clearance

  • Must have the ability to obtain additional Special Access Program (SAP) access

Basic Qualifications for Sr. Principal Mechanical Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) discipline with 9 years’ experience; or a Masters’ Degree in a STEM Discipline with 7 years’ experience; or a PhD in a STEM Discipline with 4 Years’ experience

  • Proficient using CAD software in generating detailed engineering drawings used for manufacturing of mechanical and/or electrical assemblies

  • Ability to obtain a DoD Secret or higher Security Clearance

  • Must have the ability to obtain additional Special Access Program (SAP) access

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current, active in-scope DoD Secret or higher security clearance

  • Proficient using FEA software in performance of stress analysis of mechanical and/or electrical assemblies to include static and dynamic analysis to MIL-STD-810 requirements

  • FAA A&P Certificate or equivalent aircraft maintenance background

  • Experience developing manufacturing drawings to GD&T standards
