Northrop Grumman is currently seeking a Principal / Sr. Principal Structural Engineer. This position will be located in Northridge, CA.

  • 4 x 10 work scheduled (Monday - Thursday Friday off)

  • Must be on site.

Description:

We are looking for Engineers who might have specific structural engineering specialties which may include dynamics and loads, stability and stress fatigue and DaDT analysis. Your day-to-day activities will be to analyze, research and develop structural engineering analysis packages involving metals, non-metallic or composite structural materials for R&D, product design, or operation of product. This might include structures, mechanical, You will analyze damage tolerance, durability, design allowables and structural modeling.

Basic Qualifications:

For a Principal Structural Engineer (Level 3):

BS and 5 years of experience or MS and 3 years of experience in related field.

For a Sr. Principal Structural Engineer (Level 4)

BS and 9 years of experience or MS and 7 years of experience in related field.

  • Bachelors Degree in Mechanical Engineering, or Aerospace Engineering

  • Experience in Structural Analysis Methods and documentation, including hand-sheet analysis, as well as the development and manipulation of large Finite Element Models.

  • Must have the ability to obtain a DoD  clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD clearance preferred, not necessary on first day

  • Advanced degree in Mechanical Engineering or Aerospace Engineering

  • Aerospace structural analysis experience using NASTRAN, PATRAN, ABAQUS or FEMAP.

  • Experience in Metallic or / composite Structural Analysis of primary and secondary aircraft/ Missile structures

  • Experience with analysis of composite and/or metallic structure or repair methods

  • Experience in structural testing (building block or full-scale test)

  • Experience as a Lead Engineer managing a small group of analysts

  • Experience working on military aircraft
