Classified Area Engineering Tech (Level 2/3)
R10087875
1st Shift

The Classified Area Engineering Tech (Level 2 or 3) will lead be responsible to execute daily tasks related to maintaining the classified infrastructure for Launch Vehicles Programs in Louisville, AZ. This includes both the physical and digital footprint. The successful candidate will be able to work with cross-functional teams to complete their tasks and coordinate with other functions that their tasks impact or may impact them.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Execute daily tasks related to maintaining the classified infrastructure directed by their project lead(s) which include the following examples:
    • Manage IT tickets as needed
    • Take inventory of and manage IT assets
    • Responsible for ensuring classified equipment is properly dispositioned during off-cycle and annual disposition runs
    • Responsible for coordinating the pickup of classified laptops in support of classified meetings with program and customer as needed
    • Responsible for properly configuring rooms (wall dividers, unplugging unclassified phones) for classified meetings with the program and customer as needed
    • Maintain standard set of all hardware and software used by engineering disciplines to ensure that engineers have the resources needed to perform their jobs
    • Procure hardware and software as required
    • Attend and bring items to Change Management Board as required
    • Perform Data Transfer Agent duties as required
  • Ensures successful execution of Closed Area operations
  • Work with cross-functional teams to meet closed area requirements including facilities, security, and hardware/ software needs

Qualifications:

Basic Qualifications

  • Active Secret clearance in-scope (granted or renewed in the last 6 years).
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Understanding of IT and Cybersecurity
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office suite
  • Must be able to perform occasional lifting up to 10 lbs

Level 2

  • Must have a High School diploma or equivalent and 2 years additional education and/or related experience.

Level 3

  • Must have a High School diploma or equivalent and 4 years additional education and/or related experience.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Active Top Secret clearance adjudicated within the last 5 years
  • Experience in Leadership roles within the Aerospace and Defense industry, or within the military.
  • Experience working IT projects
  • Experience in cybersecurity
  • Experience maintaining facilities for classified work
  • Experience operating in a SAP environment
