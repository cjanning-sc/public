What you’ll get to do?

Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking Engineers at all levels and in numerous disciplines to become a pivotal part of the implementation of Digital Designs, Test & Communications for space applications. Specifically, we are looking for experienced individuals to lead flight digital, electrical, RF, RF microwave designs, mechanical and hardware developments for space applications at the Space Park campus located in Redondo Beach and Manhattan Beach, CA. The selected candidates will collaborate with Systems Engineers, Mechanical Engineers, Manufacturing Engineers and other Electrical Engineers.

Tasks include:

Perform design trades, requirements flow down, generate interconnection drawings, perform electrical analysis, perform I&T with verification engineering support, to ultimately deliver designed electrical board/units. In addition, Engineers have the opportunity to develop architecture design, detailed design, performing customer briefings/reviews, and hardware test for flight electronics, test systems and satellite system for operational ground based hardware.

Desired skillsets include:

- Conceptual Design

- Circuit Design

- Schematic Development

- Worst Case Analysis

- Brassboard/Engineering Model production

- Board/Unit Integration & Test

- Digital Hardware Design

- RF Hardware Design

- Design/analysis/testing of radio frequency (RF) and/or microwave antenna systems

- Design/analysis using electromagnetic theory, electromagnetic analysis, and performance budgeting/allocation

- Design/analysis of antenna hardware solutions

- Electronic Circuit Design

- Test Engineering

- Electronics Manufacturing Engineering

- Mechanical Design

- FPGA Development

- Ground Based Space Systems

- VHDL design of ASIC and/or FPGA logic

- FPGA/ASIC verification via simulation and emulation (lab testing)

- Synthesis, timing analysis, and place and route for FPGA

- FPGA/ASIC design for Space environment or Space Application
