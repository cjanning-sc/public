ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking Engineers at all levels and in numerous disciplines to become a pivotal part of the implementation of Digital Designs, Test & Communications for space applications. Specifically, we are looking for experienced individuals to lead flight digital, electrical, RF, RF microwave designs, mechanical and hardware developments for space applications at the Space Park campus located in Columbus and Dayton, OH. The selected candidates will collaborate with Systems Engineers, Mechanical Engineers, Manufacturing Engineers and other Electrical Engineers.

Tasks include:

Perform design trades, requirements flow down, generate interconnection drawings, perform electrical analysis, perform I&T with verification engineering support, to ultimately deliver designed electrical board/units. In addition, Engineers have the opportunity to develop architecture design, detailed design, performing customer briefings/reviews, and hardware test for flight electronics, test systems and satellite system for operational ground based hardware.
