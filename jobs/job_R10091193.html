ACME Engineering Mission Systems (NGMS) sector is seeking a Manager 3, Capture Manager.   This individual will lead capture-related activities for medium- to large-sized programs. The successful candidate will have a chance to lead some of the most exciting new capture opportunities in voice/data communications & networking and platform agnostic Resilient Multi-Domain Gateways.     

This individual will work closely with ACME Engineering’s leadership team and other industry and government partners to run all aspects of his/her assigned pursuits. The successful candidate will be a self-starter with experience in captures and proposals that will lead an overall capture effort, including investment strategy, supplier and contract partnerships, technical development, and customer relationships and alignment of priorities. Must be adept at quickly understanding what is required to win, developing compelling value propositions, and preparing strong business cases to leadership. Must have proven strong team leadership skills and writing abilities.  Candidate must be comfortable with briefing at the executive level and establishing NDAs, working with GSC, teaming, and sourcing.

Primary job responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Capture Leadership
  • Customer relationship management and development; Customer acquisition approach analysis and shaping; Government Relations collaboration on engagement strategies and execution
  • Developing capture strategy and tactics to produce capture products such as competitive assessments, teaming strategies, and prices-to-win analysis results applied to improve competitive posture.
  • Developing clear and concise Win Themes and Value Propositions for the capture.
  • Maintain relationships with internal stakeholders and functional organizations involved in program pursuit.
  • Developing relationships with teaming partners and industry associations to help drive partnerships.
  • Coordinating and collaborating with line of business management, strategy, business and finance, contracts and global supply chain, technology and engineering offices and functions.
  • Development and management of investment capture project funds, schedule, and execution.
  • Proposal development including managing process, writing proposal responses and developing pricing to deliver compelling and compliant proposals.
  • Clear, concise and actionable briefings to the ACME leadership team

Ethics

  • Ensuring new business activity is carried out in accordance with government, customer, and ACME Engineering policies, procedures, and processes.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited university.
  • 10+ years' experience with capture management, program management, and/or business development.
  • Self-directed leader with strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Experience leading and directing activities of team members through execution of a formal capture process
  • Technical understanding of military communications, networking and tactical data links (TDLs)
  • Experience interfacing with senior management, peers, and employees
  • Aptitude to develop and execute to a strategy
  • Demonstrated experience with establishing customer intimacy
  • Experience with DoD & international customers and/or partners
  • Active Secret Clearance
  • This position will require up to 25% travel.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Science, Technology, Engineering or Math (STEM).
  • Experience managing competitive captures of $100M in value or greater.
  • Experience leading and managing geographically dispersed teams.
  • Experience in program planning, budget development and execution, workforce planning, risk management, and the implementation of project processes and controls.
  • Knowledge and understanding of the U.S. Government’s competitive acquisition process including federal procurement regulations (FAR/DFAR).
  • Current user and acquisition customer relationships, specifically in the US Air Force Air Mobility Command (AMC), Air Force Reserve Command and/or Air National Guard.
  • Current/active TS/SCI security clearance.
