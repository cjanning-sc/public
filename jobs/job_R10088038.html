Principal Systems Engineer/Sr. Principal Systems Engineer - Test and Evaluation
R10088038
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems sector is seeking a highly motivated Principal Systems Engineer/Sr. Principal Systems Engineer to primarily support the E-2D Mission System programs as part of the Test and Evaluation Team. This position will be based in Melbourne, FL.

Candidate will provide support for all system test activities, to include test planning, creation of required test procedures, post test data analysis, creation of problem reports, and reporting on test results. Candidate shall display professionalism, leadership, have a strong work ethic, work well with others, and be able to function as member of the E-2D System Test Team.

Key Responsibilities:              

  • Assist in development, execution and peer review of test procedures

  • Participate in both Formal and Non-Formal Test execution events

  • Participate in test planning, problem report validation testing, and post testing data analysis

  • Provide support in the creation of required test artifacts used for official PCM and SRM releases to our customer

  • Attend and participate in all E2D System Test meetings

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

We offer flexible work arrangements, 9/80 work schedule with every other Friday off, phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly team environment. We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal-oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do.

Basic Qualifications (Principal Systems Engineer):

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) discipline AND 5 years of relevant experience OR Master’s Degree in a STEM discipline AND 3 years of relevant experience OR PhD Degree in a STEM discipline AND 0 years of relevant experience.

  • Must have an active DoD Secret or higher clearance (with a background investigation completed within the last 6 years or currently enrolled into Continuous Evaluation

  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to the commencement of employment

  • Must have experience generating technical documents and working with various software/data processing tools such as MS Word, MS Excel, PowerPoint, and other various programming languages

  • Must have an understanding of common Test Engineering test methodologies and practices

Basic Qualifications (Sr. Principal Systems Engineer):

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) discipline AND 9 years of relevant experience OR Master’s Degree in a STEM discipline AND 7 years of relevant experience OR PhD Degree in a STEM discipline AND 4 years of relevant experience.

  • Must have an active DoD Secret or higher clearance (with a background investigation completed within the last 6 years or currently enrolled into Continuous Evaluation

  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to the commencement of employment

  • Must have experience generating technical documents and working with various software/data processing tools such as MS Word, MS Excel, PowerPoint, and other various programming languages

  • Must have an understanding of common Test Engineering test methodologies and practices

Preferred Experience: 

  • Master’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) discipline

  • Software test automation experience

  • DoD Top Secret Clearance

  • Current E2 program clearance

  • Previous E-2D Hawkeye experience

  • Experience leading a small team or test effort
