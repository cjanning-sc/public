Mechanical Engineer - Propulsion System RE/Principal Mechanical Engineer Propulsion System RE
R10095369
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems is seeking a Mechanical Engineer - Propulsion System Responsible Engineer OR a Principal Mechanical Engineer - Propulsion System Responsible Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals.

Responsibilities include becoming the subject matter expert for a specific propulsion subsystem, defining and championing system improvement initiatives as they become necessary, serving as an engineering representative in the supplier management team for all suppliers providing components of the subsystem, performing system analysis as needed to serve specialty engineering needs, defining and documenting changes and revisions to the subsystem, and providing direct response to subsystem needs as they arise from the user, the customer, and/or program leadership. Additional tasks might include needing to be able to prepare and present effective technical briefs on all of the above for all levels of consumption (i.e. user, customer, program leadership, etc.)

This position may be filled at either the Mechanical Engineer - Propulsion System Responsible Engineer OR the Principal Mechanical Engineer - Propulsion System Responsible Engineer level.

Basic Qualifications for Mechanical Engineer - Propulsion System Responsible Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field with 2 years of related engineering experience OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field with 0 years of related engineering experience.
  • Experience working with aeronautic propulsion systems
  • Experience working with diverse technical teams.
  • Proficient at locating and examining technical data from Computer Aided Design and Product Lifecycle Management tools
  • Must have an active DoD Secret clearance (with a background investigation completed within the last 6 years or currently enrolled into Continuous Evaluation).
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to the commencement of employment. This SAP must be obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Mechanical Engineer - Propulsion System Responsible Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field with 5 years of related engineering experience OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field with 3 years of related engineering experience OR a PhD in a STEM field with 0 years of related engineering experience.
  • Experience working with aeronautic propulsion systems
  • Experience working with diverse technical teams.
  • Proficient at locating and examining technical data from Computer Aided Design and Product Lifecycle Management tools
  • Must have an active DoD Secret clearance (with a background investigation completed within the last 6 years or currently enrolled into Continuous Evaluation).
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to the commencement of employment. This SAP must be obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Preferred Qualifications for both levels:

  • DOD Top Secret clearance
  • Experience working on Special Access Programs
  • Experience applying Siemens AMESIM, MATLAB/Simulink, or similar analysis tools for controls and subsystems
  • Experience using CATIA V5 to examine 3D models
  • Experience using TeamCenter Engineer and Enterprise to locate and examine technical data
