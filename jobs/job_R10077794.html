ACME Engineering Space Systems (NGSS) designs, builds and delivers space, defense and aviation-related systems to customers around the world. Our main products include launch vehicles and related propulsion systems; missile products, subsystems and defense electronics; precision weapons, armament systems and ammunition; satellites and associated space components and services; and advanced aerospace structures.

Working Environment
The position is available at the ACME Engineering facility in Goleta CA, part of the ACME Engineering, Space Systems (NGSS) - Space Components Business Unit, which specializes in deployable systems for spacecraft such as solar arrays, instrument booms, and various extendible support structures, as well as antennas for space and aerospace applications such as satellite communication, radar systems, and electronic warfare (EW).

As a Senior Principal RF Microwave Design Engineer at NGSS – Goleta you will be responsible for designing, manufacturing, testing and delivering RF antennas for various applications in a leadership role on a team working across multiple ACME locations to deliver communications, radar and EW systems at the cutting edge of space and aerospace technology.  You will have ownership of your work from initial iterative studies through the design and analysis phases and may also participate in the manufacturing, testing, and integration of the systems.

Responsibilities

  • Design, develop and refine antenna components utilizing design skills, hand calculations, 3D design tools, or other modeling tools as needed.
  • Generate CAD drawings and related documents.
  • Participate and present in internal and external (customer) design and program reviews.
  • Manage and perform manufacturing and testing of a variety of different antennas and antenna systems for airborne and space applications.
  • Operate and maintain on-site antenna test facility.
  • Document assembly and test plans and procedures.
  • Manage and track multiple priorities while maintaining focus towards common goals of the program team, matrix organization, and customers.

Basic Qualifications:

To be considered for this position, you must minimally meet the knowledge, skills, and abilities listed below:

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering or Physics or many years of experience working at an engineering level. (Electrical Engineering degree is highly desired)
  • Minimum 9 years of relevant product development, test, or delivery experience
  • Strong background in RF microwave and/or antenna theory and suitability to be trained upward
  • Experience in RF microwave and/or antenna hardware build and test
  • Familiar with common RF lab test equipment such as microwave network analyzers and computer aided test equipment
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office tools suite
  • Strong communication skills (oral and written)
  • Ability to work independently as well as in a team environment
  • Ability to multitask and organize/prioritize job assignments in a fast-paced development environment
  • Active DoD Secret Security Clearance.  

Preferred Qualifications:

Candidates with these desired skills will be given preferential consideration:

  • Background in antenna testing and antenna range operations
  • Quasi-optical antenna systems and concepts (i.e. frequency selective surfaces, polarization selective surfaces, microwave lens design)
  • Reflector based antenna design and analysis
  • Broad band/narrow band feed horn design and analysis
  • Familiarity with antenna alignment and bore-sighting techniques
  • Familiarity with analysis tools such as MathCAD, MATLAB, GRASP10, CHAMP, HFSS, CST Microwave Studio.
  • Familiarity with CAD software, preferably PTC Creo Parametric 4.
  • Experience with RF test instruments such as network analyzer, spectrum analyzer, power meter, signal generator, noise figure analyzer, etc.
  • Experience in space-borne products
  • Cost Account Management (CAM) Experience
  • Demonstrated experience leading a technical team
