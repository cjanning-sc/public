Air Vehicle Performance Engineer (level 3/4)
R10095687
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems is seeking an experienced Principal or Sr Principal Air Vehicle Performance Engineer with demonstrated capabilities to join our Vehicle Engineering team in Melbourne, FL. In this role, you will perform Aircraft Performance analysis within a Flight Sciences team working with related disciplines including Aerodynamics, Propulsion, Mass Properties, Configuration, GNC, and others. CURRENT CLEARANCE IS PREFERRED BUT NOT REQUIRED.

Responsibilities Include:

  • Supports high-paced engineering manufacturing development through entry into service in a collaborative environment

  • Applies advanced engineering principles to the design and development of air vehicle, to ensure safe, operational efficiency while maintaining defined program goals

  • Works with highly motivated team responsible for all aspects of air vehicle performance to include including oversight of performance analyses generated by junior engineers in specific phases of flight, including takeoff, climb, descent, approach, and landing, as well as overall mission analysis using computer-based tools

  • Defines and validates flight test specifications

  • Recommends flight test procedures and reviews flight test data

  • Leads timely development and flight test maturation of aircraft flight manuals reflecting the developing performance database

  • Contributes to the modification of performance models and derivatives of current models based on evolving flight test data

  • Provides technical guidance for mission analysis, trade studies and development, air vehicle performance data synthesis, and performance assessments substantiating program requirements

  • Prepares release plan for air vehicle performance tech order and flight manual to support program milestone and delivery schedule

  • Provides technical guidance for substantiation data reports and other airworthiness artifacts required to support flight clearance

  • May provide technical support to junior team members

  • Develops air vehicle performance database deliverables to internal customers supporting their delivery schedules

  • Communicates effectively and clearly present technical approaches and findings

  • Develops technical solutions to complex problems

Position may be filled as a Principal Engineer (level 3) or Senior Principal Engineer (level 4) depending on the qualifications below.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Principal Vehicle Performance Engineer: Bachelor of Science (BS) in a relevant Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) discipline and 5+ years of relevant experience; OR Master of Science (MS) in a relevant STEM discipline and 3+ years of relevant experience; OR Ph.D. in a relevant STEM discipline and 1+ years of relevant experience

  • Sr Principal Vehicle Performance Engineer: BS in a relevant STEM discipline and 9+ years of related experience; OR MS in a relevant STEM discipline and 7+ years of related experience; OR a Ph.D. in a relevant STEM discipline and 4+ years of related experience

  • Understanding of fundamental aircraft aerodynamic and propulsion parameters and aircraft performance analysis

  • Effective communication and presentation skills

  • Must be able to obtain a DoD Secret clearance and Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to the commencement of employment

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Degree in Aerospace Engineering

  • Current, in-scope DoD Secret clearance or higher

  • Familiarity with MIL-STD-3013A

  • Experience in performing aircraft performance analysis, including mission performance, turn performance, kinematic maneuvering

  • Experience in take-off and landing performance

  • Experience with flight test data reduction for vehicle performance

  • Familiarity with Matlab and Linux Operating System
