MAJOR RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Primary responsibility will be for fabricating rods, slabs, or other geometries from single crystal laser materials in a job-shop environment. This includes:
    • Blocking, squaring, milling, grinding, beveling, polishing, and optically contacting
    • Cleaning, measuring, and inspecting parts for geometrical, optical, and surface quality.
    • Interpreting in-process optical and mechanical characteristics, decision making, and applying best practices in the realm of optical fabrication
    • Operating machinery, maintaining polishing laps, and performing preventive maintenance on area equipment
  • Follow technical work instructions, understand engineering blueprints, and verify conformance to specification within given tolerances.
  • Maintain a safe, clean, and organized work area.
  • Utilize the electronic Shop Floor Control system for data entry and scanning parts through applicable routing steps.
  • Perform work in a manner complying with our ISO 9001 quality and EHS management systems.
  • May provide training to less experienced team members.

EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE, AND SKILLS REQUIRED:

  • Required:
    • High School Diploma or GED
    • Basic computer skills using Microsoft Excel and Word
    • Effective communication skills with peers, engineering, and management are essential.
    • Ability to communicate in English, verbally and written, in a technical environment.
    • Manual dexterity, mechanical aptitude, strong work ethic, ability to multitask, positive attitude, and willingness to learn.
    • Good visual acuity, strong basic math skills, and attention to detail
    • Ability to use measurement equipment, work in metric or English units, read detailed instructions, interpret engineering prints, maintain written records, and keep an organized work environment using 5S principles.
  • Preferred:
    • An associate degree or two-year technical degree
    • Experience in a technical manufacturing environment including measuring production parts, interpreting process output, and troubleshooting.
    • Basic machine setup, programming, and operation (e.g., rotary surface grinder)
    • Experience in optical fabrication is strongly preferred, including experience with microscopes and mechanical or optical metrology equipment.
