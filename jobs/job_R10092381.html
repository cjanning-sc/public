As an integral part of our Systems Engineering team in Mission Systems you will be fulfilling the role of a Maintainability Engineer.

Roles and Responsibilities: 

  • Develop and implement a sound maintainability program

  • Provide technical leadership in the Maintainability Engineering discipline and work to drive sound systems engineering on programs.

  • Conduct modeling and analysis with an emphasis on Maintainability

  • Develop Maintainability predictions in support of quantitative requirements such as MTTR and MaxTTR, to include detailed task analyses

  • Determine component repair times, crew size, support equipment, skill levels and maintenance tasks required to restore a component to an operational condition.

  • Conduct Design for Maintainability assessments using criteria from MIL-STD-1472

  • The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

Basic Qualifications:

Bachelor’s degree with 14 years of experience, a Master’s degree with 12 years of experience or a PhD with 9 years of experience in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Applied Physics, Applied Mathematics, or related technical fields.

  • U.S. Citizenship and ability to obtain or maintain active secret DoD clearance

  • Fluency with MIL-HDBK-470 and 472 guidelines and processes

  • Knowledge of MIL-STD-1472 Design for Maintainability criteria

  • Experience developing Maintainability requirements

  • Experience conducting Maintainability allocations, predictions and assessments

  • Experience presenting results at major design reviews and interfacing with Customers

  • Experience developing Maintainability plans and analysis reports

  • Ability to support multiple programs

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Advanced degrees in engineering, science, software, or business administration

  • Active DoD Top Secret or TS/SCI Clearance

  • Reliability Centered Maintenance experience

  • Conduct/support Maintainability Demonstrations

  • Knowledge of Radar products

  • Knowledge and/or experience with Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE)

  • Experience supporting proposals (technical write-ups and Basis of Estimates)
