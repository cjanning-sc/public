NGMS is seeking an Associate Engineer Systems to join our SEIT & Product Support organization in Springfield, MD.  This position seeks an Engineer to support the Diminishing Manufacturing Sources and Material Shortages (DMSMS) Logistics team. The primary focus of this role will be reviewing product BOM's for DMSMS risks and providing appropriate mitigation solution based on comprehensive logistics analysis. Additional duties within the organization include: perform technical planning, system integration, verification and validation, cost and risk, and supportability and effectiveness analyses for total systems. Analyses are performed at all levels of total system product to include: concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal. Ensures the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints. Performs functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications. The successful candidate will apply experience in data analysis, maintenance processes and product support to serve as a Systems Engineer with a focus on logistics engineering in a variety of product areas supporting US Army, US Air Force, US Navy, US Marine Corps and other Customers.

The candidate selected will be responsible for:

•Reviewing handbooks for technical adequacy

•Assisting in the development of maintenance engineering and logistics support.

•Contributing to proposal efforts

•Interacting with team leads to interpret Customer needs and requirements

•Participating in and presenting technical information in team and customer meetings

•Performing logistics product support analyses to identify parts, equipment, activities and skillset necessary to maintain systems and retain operational availability

•Coordinating with other team members to integrate sparing and level of repair analyses results into Logistics Support Analysis.

•Coordinate with lab engineers and FSRs to locate and identify assets.

•Work with team to identify DMSMS items.  Work on mitigation and solutions.  Track inventory of DMS items, and recommend LTB of DMS items to customer.

•Manage system items for major customers, and multiple sites.  CONUS and OCONUS.  Provide spares status and locations to all Operating Unit members.
