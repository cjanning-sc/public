Configuration Management/Engineering Liaison

Job Description

Represents the configuration and data management function managing engineering administrative matters. Recommends, interprets, and/or implements configuration management and engineering company and internal administrative policies and procedures. Performs a combination of configuration and data management administrative tasks for the mechanical, electrical, and special test equipment engineering functions as required.

NGDS is looking for a Configuration Management/Engineering Liaison to assist Engineering with Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) System support.

  • Assist document authors with document creation within the PLM System
  • Assisting Engineers with creation of Change Request and Change Notices
  • Review metadata and items within the PLM System for completeness as required
  • Respond to PLM Break/Fix tickets
  • Provide PLM navigation tutorials
  • Assist document authors in the upload of documents to the PLM System
  • Assist document authors in establishing the correct relationships within the PLM System
  • Assist with Commercial-off-the-Shelf (COTS) part creation in MRP/ERP System
  • Interpret PLM error messages and assist document authors in resolving

Basic Qualifications (Level 2):

  • High school diploma/GED and a minimum of seven (7) years of relevant experience. Will consider a Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 3 years of relevant experience or a Master’s degree with a minimum of 1 years of relevant experience.
  • Experience following documented rules and guidelines to ensure technical content meets internal and customer requirements
  • Candidate must have solid written and verbal skills to effectively interact with other functional organizations
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) Secret security clearance within a timeframe set forth by management. US Citizenship required

Basic Qualifications (Level 3):

  • High school diploma/GED and a minimum of ten (10) years of relevant experience. Will consider a Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 6 years of relevant experience or a Master’s degree with a minimum of 4 years of relevant experience.
  • Experience following documented rules and guidelines to ensure technical content meets internal and customer requirements
  • Candidate must have solid written and verbal skills to effectively interact with other functional organizations
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) Secret security clearance within a timeframe set forth by management. US Citizenship required

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD Secret Security Clearance Secret (or higher)
  • Working knowledge of EIA-649 or MIL-HDBK-61A
  • Experience working in a configuration management product life cycle system (i.e., Agile PLM, Teamcenter, CMPro, etc.)
  • Working knowledge and understanding of engineering documentation (i.e., models, drawings, specifications, parts, change notices, manufacturing plans, and manufacturing procedures)
  • Experience in military, government, commercial aerospace industry (e.g., aviation/equipment maintenance, aircraft operations, or commercial industrial technology companies)
  • Experience working in an Manufacturing Resource Planning (MRP) or Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) System (i.e., Deltek, Oracle, SAP, etc.)
