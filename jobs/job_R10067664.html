Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Principal/Sr. Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be with our Payloads team located in Roy, UT and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

 

What you’ll get to do:   

  • Develop models to simulate and evaluate hardware performance
  • Perform model validation through test and analysis
  • Develop, perform and document tests, includes test planning, test conduction, data analysis and documenting test results
  • Participate in technical planning, system integration, verification and validation, cost and risk, and supportability and effectiveness analyses for missile system payloads
  • Verify specifications and designs through modeling and simulation, test data processing, and performance analysis
  • Support all phases of the systems lifecycle including concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal
  • Maintain design documentation and follow configuration management requirements
  • Create technical reports and present test and analysis results
  • Interface with AF customer

Basic Qualifications:

  • Principal Engineer:  Bachelor of Science degree and 5 years of experience performing in a Systems Engineering or related technical discipline; 3 years with a Master’s degree
  • Senior Principal Engineer: Bachelor of Science degree and 9 years of experience performing in a Systems Engineering or related technical discipline; or 7 years of experience with a Masters
  • Active DoD Secret Security Clearance with the ability to obtain a Top Secret Clearance
  • US Citizen with ability to obtain SAP and SCI accesses (clearance adjudicated in the last 5.5 years)
  • Experience with Modeling and Simulation, including programming experience (MATLAB, Python, C++, or similar)
  • Experience with Radar Modeling & Simulation

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DOD Top Secret Security Clearance
  • Special Program Access
  • Experience with ICBM Weapon Systems
  • Experience with Command and Control (C2) or Battle Management
  • Experience with Electro-Optical and Infrared Sensor Modeling
