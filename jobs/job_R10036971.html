Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package. This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

What you’ll get to do: 

The selected candidate will join the Command and Launch Team as a Physical Security Systems Engineering Architect; responsibilities includes but not limited to:

  • Support systems engineering architecture activities including use case development, functional decomposition, interface definition, and data flow development
  • Develop and maintain descriptive system models in UAFP and SysML using MBSE tools to define interfaces, physical/functional block diagrams and behaviors
  • Coordinate with integrated product teams for establishing content within the system architecture.
  • Interface with customers, program management, and technical staff.

As a full-time employee of Northrop Grumman Space Systems, you are eligible for our robust benefits package including:

  • Medical, Dental & Vision coverage
  • 401k
  • Educational Assistance
  • Life Insurance
  • Employee Assistance Programs & Work/Life Solutions
  • Paid Time Off
  • Health & Wellness Resources
  • Employee Discounts 

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80A. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off.

You’ll bring these qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either a Principal level or level Sr. Principal level.

  • Principal level requires 5 years with a BS – Science, Technology, Engineering or Math (STEM)  degree; 3 years with a Master’s degree; or 0 years with a PhD
  • Senior Principal requires 9 years with a BS – Science, Technology, Engineering or Math (STEM) degree; 7 years with a Master’s degree; or 4 years with a PhD )
  • A minimum 2 years of Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) experience, demonstrating broad technical experience across multiple platforms, and encompassing the entire product development lifecycle
  • Active Top Secret Security Clearance
  • Able to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance.
  • Detailed, hands-on experience using one or more of the following common MBSE tools in the areas of Architecture, Design, and V&V: Cameo, Rhapsody, Sparx EA, DOORS, Teamcenter, etc

These qualifications would be nice to have:

  • Experience with Defense Architecture Framework (DoDAF), Systems Modeling Language (SYSML), and Unified Architecture Framework (UAF)
  • Experience defining systems architecture for developing and decomposing requirements and system functions, developing functional and physical system architectures, and defining interfaces
  • Experience developing large, complex systems and holding cross-functional architecture reviews
  • Experience with physical security systems
  • Previous Systems Engineering experience and understanding of the Systems Engineering Process
  • Experience with Agile Development
  • Experience using Requirement’s repository tools (i.e., DOORS).
