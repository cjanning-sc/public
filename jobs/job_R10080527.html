Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Logistics Goods Movement Integrator. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) Sentinel program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package. This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

What you’ll get to do: 

Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Provide experience-based suggestions to the design teams with Logistics and Goods Movement in mind.
  • Act as the Logistics and Goods Movement focal point and subject matter expert, supporting the Segment team.
  • Support CDRL development and content required by the Segments.
  • Review and provide feedback to SDRL deliveries, work with Supplier Management Team lead to ensure execution requirements are being met and reviewing SDRLs for completeness.
  • Product movement, tracking, and visibility with the IMS (G/R).
  • Work with Segment teams to define requirements for Packaging, Handling, Storage, and Transportation of all product movements, storage and inventory management across all classification and compliance categories (HAZMAT, NWRM, etc.).
  • Prepare and manage schedules and prepares status reports on the health of the projects.
  • Identify risks and opportunities, and coordinate with Segment teams to mitigate and retire risks.
  • Develop and maintain trade studies to include reviewing & updating assessments by preparing Basis of Estimates (BOEs), performing quality checks on all proposal submittals, and attending design reviews.
  • Evaluate issues and develop resolutions to meet productivity, quality, and customer satisfaction goals and objectives.
  • Ability to handle multiple tasks in a dynamic environment, work autonomously under general direction and will demonstrate critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Review and align tasks to Program Statement of Work
  • Understand and adhere to master program and project plans and schedules
  • Identify, distribute, track, and complete program requirements
  • Adhere to all internal processes, policies, and applicable industry standards
  • Track Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) work challenges to mitigate KPIs
  • Develop and adhere to budget baselines utilizing Earned Value Management (EVM) or similar cost & schedule control
