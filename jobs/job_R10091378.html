Systems Engineer / Principal Systems Engineer – Requirements and System development on Missile Products W/ SECRET CLEARANCE
R10091378
1st Shift (United States of America)

We are currently seeking a Systems Engineer / Principal Systems Engineer - Requirements and System development to work with our talented Advanced Weapons team in Northridge, CA. The ideal candidate will be supporting one of our Stand-in Attack Weapon (SiAW) for analyzing, developing, and managing the system and subsystems requirements for our missile weapon system. This position will require close collaboration with team members of other projects to leverage on existing technology as well as working with the program team to innovate system architecture to adapt to the industry and customer’s needs.


In this role, the selected candidate will (but not be limited to):

  • Support system requirements development, management, analysis, and traceability, to translate customer requirements and CONOPS into systems requirements, and further allocate to lower-level requirements
  • Perform functional analysis and systems trade studies for technical solutions with respect to concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, and maintenance
  • Assist with the generation of well-defined and executable system and subsystem requirements documents, interface documents, and test and verification documents
  • Support system architecture development and system/subsystem allocations and designs
  • Collaborate cross-functionally to ensure system/subsystem designs align with system requirements
  • Support system/subsystems integration, test, and requirements verification activities
  • Support system technical performance measurement definition and tracking
  • Support the preparation of system engineering materials during System Requirements Review, System Functional Review, Preliminary Design Review, Critical Design Review, System Verification Review, Test Readiness Review, and other program reviews as needed
  • Interface with inter-organizational management and external Customer to communicate technical briefing and program status
  • Support Program Management activities to ensure cost and schedule objectives are met
  • Support the management of critical subcontractors developing and testing tactical missile technologies and delivery of quality products.

This position can be filled as a Systems Engineer (T02) or Principal Systems Engineer (T03) level. 

Basic qualifications for each level are listed below.

Relocation assistance, while not guaranteed, may be available.

 

Basic qualifications for a Systems Engineer (T02) are: 

  • Active DoD Secret Clearance
  • Bachelor of Science in an Engineering discipline and a minimum of two (2) years of systems engineering experience; will consider a Master of Science in an Engineering discipline
  • Exposure to any requirements development, analysis, and management
  • Experience working in a team setting
  • Experience in technical writing
  • Experience in communicating with peers in a methodical, detailed and process-oriented fashion
  • Must have demonstrated experience in providing solutions to a variety of technical problems under general supervision.

Basic qualifications for a Principal Systems Engineer (T03) are: 

  • Active DoD Secret Clearance
  • Bachelor of Science in an Engineering discipline and a minimum of five (5) years of systems engineering experience; will consider a Master of Science in an Engineering discipline with a minimum of three (3) years of systems engineering experience
  • Some experience in requirements development, analysis, and management
  • Experience working in a team setting
  • Experience in technical writing
  • Experience in communicating with peers in a methodical, detailed and process-oriented fashion
  • Must have demonstrated experience in providing solutions to a variety of technical problems with minimal supervision.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD Secret Clearance with periodic reinvestigation within the last five (5) years
  • Experience working on missile products
  • Experience with systems designs with Model-Based Systems Engineering
  • Experience in digital engineering
  • Experience with system/subsystem integration, test, and verification
  • Experience working in the defense industry
  • Must have experience with DOORS - (Engineering Requirements Management Tool)
