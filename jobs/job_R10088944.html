Supply Chain Tech Coordinator (Level 1 or 2)
R10088944
1st Shift

Aeronautics Systems sector is seeking a Supply Chain Technical Coordinator (Level 1 or 2) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals supporting the F-35 Program with the Global Supply Chain Goods Movement organization. This position is in Cincinnati, CA.

  • 1st shift, 4/10 schedule Monday – Thursday
  • Position is onsite supporting production, unable to accommodate a telecommute/hybrid schedule.

​Essential Functions:

  • Supports and assists activities within multiple disciplines across the Supply Chain process.
  • Ability to support the movement of goods, parts, hardware, and chemicals critical to production activity.
  • Loads, unloads, and conveys materials and parts to multiple work sites, and performs a combination of tasks under specific directions which may include shipping and receiving.
  • Opens containers using hand tools; counts and weighs materials and records information.
  • Loads and unloads materials onto or from pallets, trays, racks, and machines by hand; loads or unloads materials into or from truck trailers by hand.
  • Conveys materials using hands, truck, forklift, or other devices; stacks or assembles materials; aids by lifting heavy objects by hand.
  • Works closely with the Supply Chain Planning Specialist Production Integration team and shop floor production personnel.
  • Delivers kits and chemicals and verifies quantities and part numbers.
  • Wraps and packs kits to send to on and offsite locations.

Basic Qualifications (Level 1):

  • High School Diploma or equivalent.
  • Ability to obtain forklift certification.
  • Ability to lift 25 lbs. or more and walk or stand for extended periods.
  • Valid driver’s license, or ability to obtain one.

Basic Qualifications (Level 2):

  • High School Diploma or equivalent and 2 years of experience in logistics support, shipping/receiving, warehousing, and/or inventory control.
  • Ability to obtain forklift certification.
  • Ability to lift 25 lbs. or more and walk or stand for extended periods.
  • Valid driver’s license, or ability to obtain one.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Prior experience using a forklift/reach lift/pallet jack.
  • Prior experience using SAP/ MES or production based software.
  • Hazmat or chemical handling experience.
  • Experience in Aerospace Manufacturing.
