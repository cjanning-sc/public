Job Posting Title: Manufacturing Technician 1/Manufacturing Technician 2 – Ceramics

Job Profile: K99801/K99802

Job Description:

An organization within Northrop Grumman’s Payload and Ground Systems (PGS) division, Spacecraft Components is a leading designer, producer, and supplier of spacecraft components that power and enable satellites of all classes. Products include spacecraft panels, bus structures, precision optical structures, deployable structural systems and mechanisms, solar arrays, and antenna reflectors. Our products are on virtually every U.S. satellite built in the last 20 years.

Learn more about Spacecraft Components at our website: https://www.northropgrumman.com/space/spacecraft-components/.

This position may be located in San Diego, CA.

COI Ceramics (COIC), a part of Northrop Grumman’s Space Components Business Unit, leads the industry in the development, fabrication and production of High Temperature Ceramic Matrix Composite Components and test articles. 

(Wet Layup/Tooling Technician)

Successful candidate will be responsible for the following:

Primary duties:

  • Use of vacuum bagging processes.

  • Lay-up of composite structures

  • Handling and managing fragile composite materials.

  • Report data and interface with other employees and departments

  • Tooling preparation and release application.

  • Loading autoclave cure vessels. (Max 150 psi)

  • Loading operating oven(s).

  • Loading operating high temperature furnaces

  • Utilize measurement tools such as calipers, rulers, and tape measures for fabricating hardware.

  • Assist in maintaining overall shop appearance and cleanliness.

 Basic Qualifications for Manufacturing Technician 1:

  • High School diploma or equivalent and 0 years additional education and/or related experience.

  • Demonstrated mechanical ability.

  • Ability to lift/push/pull up to 50 pounds.

  • Ability to be on feet for up to 8+ hours each day.

  • Ability to work overtime and weekends as needed.

  • Ability to obtain a DOD Security Clearance, which requires US Citizenship as a pre-requisite.

  • Must be flexible to work 2nd Shift as needed

Basic Qualifications for Manufacturing Technician 2:

  • High School diploma or equivalent and 2 years additional education and/or related experience

  • Composites manufacturing experience required; ceramic composites materials industry experience preferred.

  • Demonstrated mechanical ability.

  • Ability to lift/push/pull up to 50 pounds.

  • Ability to be on feet for up to 8+ hours each day.

  • Ability to work overtime and weekends as needed.

  • Ability to obtain a DOD Security Clearance, which requires US Citizenship as a prerequisite.

  • Must be flexible to work 2nd Shift as needed

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Fabrication of composite layup tooling

  • Machining experience

  • Knowledge and experience with equipment maintenance, particularly ovens and furnaces

  • Proficient use of basic shop arithmetic

  • Experience or familiarity with ISO requirements, standards, and compliance issues

  • Knowledge and experience with Lean Continuous Improvement and 5S Principles

  • Currently hold a DOD Security Clearance
