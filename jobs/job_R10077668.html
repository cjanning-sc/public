ACME Engineering Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is seeking an Administrative Assistant 3 for to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in the Vehicle Products Center. This position will be based out of Columbus, CA. 
 

In this role, the selected candidate will be highly motivated, collaborative, detail-orientated, assertive (as necessary), and friendly in the performance of the Administrative Assistant role. The selected candidate must be able to professionally and effectively communicate both written/verbally with internal and external representatives of all levels, demonstrating tact, diplomacy, discretion, and judgment when conveying, gathering, and sharing relevant information.
 

The Administrative Assistant 3 will be responsible for, but not limited to the following:

  • Serve as a focal point for the organization and communicates with internal and external customers.
  • Perform office functions such as arranging meetings/conferences, preparing material and setting up for meetings; answering phone calls and making proper referrals; receiving, referring, or answering mail.
  • Follow up on open actions to ensure deadlines are met, and gathers, compiles, and reports on information relevant to the supervisor’s assignments.
  • Make domestic and international travel arrangements and prepares expense reports.
  • Assist in event planning and activities of organization initiatives and reviews. 
  • Operate independently to meet the requirements of the leadership team.
  • Provide operational support for special projects; researches and obtains information, helps plan and ensure follow-through of assigned tasks.
  • Must be proficient in the use of Microsoft Office suite and other software productivity tools and can work with some supervision while exercising good judgment, demonstrating flexibility and agility, prioritizing multiple tasks with competing deadlines, managing schedule conflicts, and providing excellent follow-through.
     

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School Diploma/GED with four years of administrative support experience
  • Prior experience in organizing, maintaining, and de-conflicting schedules, meetings, and travel arrangements
  • Candidate must have an active DoD Top Secret OR SCI access with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI)
     

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Associate’s degree
  • Experience in Concur/ITRIP, I-Buy, ACME My IT, SAP
  • Experience in reviewing and editing written material for correct spelling and grammar
  • Experience in Microsoft Office Suite including Word, PowerPoint, Outlook, Excel
