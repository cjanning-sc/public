Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package. This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

What you’ll get to do:

The selected candidate will implement and manage system requirements in the Requirements Management databases. Tasks includes but not limited to:

•                  Facilitate stake holder reviews

•                  Adjudicate and resolve comments; support review board release process

•                  Revise and manage both original and revised specification/interface definition document and expedite documents through signature approval process.

•                  Works closely with Architecture team to ensure requirements and architecture models are in alignment

•                  Participate in team agile work management process

•                  Provides data to risk management (identifies potential risks) and trade study processes.

•                  Supports system design, data analyses, interface verification, testing, failure/anomaly resolution and mission operations.

•                  Attends and prepares materials for presentation during technical reviews. (System Requirement Review, System Functional Review, Preliminary Design Review, Critical Design Review and System Verification Review).

•                  Supports trade studies, system sensitivity studies, design optimization studies, system performance assessments, system design assessments, Operations Analysis work such as mission analysis and timelines and concept of operations development
