  • Perform troubleshooting, problem solving, and system integration.

  • Become a subject matter expert on the intricacies of a complex system while mentoring more junior software engineers.

  • Lead critical projects while coordinating with customers, multiple sites, and teammates.

  • Develop and maintain software using Perl and TCL as well as Python, Java, and C++.

  • Work with a small team that is highly energized and has a significant software,

  • maintenance and development effort on a growing program with a large program impact. 

  • Perform testing of new software builds at the unit and system level.

  • Perform checkout of new software builds delivered form factory to ensure discrepancy report (DR) fixes are valid.
