Manager 3 Supply Chain Programs (Cincinnati, OH, and Dallastown, PA)
R10038094
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Manager 3 Supply Chain Programs. Candidate must be able to relocate/commute to Cincinnati, OH, and Dallastown, PA, areas (will require coverage of both, so can choose which area from which to base); estimate 50% travel.

Responsibilities:

  • Manages and coordinates with supplier teams composed of engineers, program management, production, quality and procurement personnel
  • Defines scope requirements for each service to be procured and ensures alignment of content and supplier capabilities
  • Defines and manages cost and schedule targets to achieve program and business objectives
  • Performs as the on-site total integrated product team (IPT) lead for two key subcontract suppliers and the associated subcontractor procurements and production
  • Monitors daily supplier operations and actively assists/provides in-scope, technical direction to suppliers as required
  • Determines the risk basis and value for the contracted products and manages mitigation plans
  • Coordinates/leads all supplier design, manufacturing, and program reviews, including the necessary levels of Northrop Grumman Space Sector (NGSP) participation
  • Functions as supplier Change Board interface for all anticipated, proposed, and released changes
  • Ensures supplier compliance with all elements of the relevant SOWs and subcontracts
  • Ensures recovery plans are put in place for any / all erosion to schedule milestones
  • Ensures accurate Estimates at Complete (EACs) and material receipt forecasts
  • Leads solicitations for proposals, review of proposals, and supports supplier negotiations

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in technical, manufacturing, supply chain or business field
  • At least 10 years of experience managing suppliers in an industrial manufacturing environment
  • Must be able to relocate/commute to Cincinnati, OH, and Dallastown, PA, areas (will require coverage of both, so can choose which area from which to base); estimate 50% travel
  • Experience leading a project and/or driving performance against cost and schedule
  • Must be able to analyze, comprehend, clearly communicate and help resolve technical, cost and schedule issues
  • Able to provide a clear example of self-directed work activity in a location remote from the rest of the IPT
  • At least two years of experience as a buyer, contracting agent or materials planner
  • Familiar with FAR/DFAR related to supplier management
  • Capable of leading Subcontract Management Team (SMT)
  • Willingness/ability to travel to Roy, Utah, for program reviews, etc.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Masters in technical, manufacturing, supply chain or business field
  • Experience with & robust knowledge of Earned Value Management Systems (EVMS)
  • Existing DoD clearance
  • Familiarity with managing aerospace/defense manufacturing suppliers
  • Experience developing, implementing and managing supplier quality at a supplier site
