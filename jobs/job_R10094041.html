1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking a Manufacturing Analyst.  This position will be located at our site in Annapolis, Maryland where technology and teamwork come together.  The qualified applicant will become part of Northrop Grumman's Manufacturing Operations Team working in a collaborative environment while promoting a positive and proactive teamwork environment.

The Manufacturing Analyst will use a hands-on approach and act as the production focal point for the management of bill of material and production planning within the Mission Systems Sector supporting Maritime/Land Systems & Sensors. The selected candidate will support production, business, and other processes in a team-based manufacturing environment.

What You’ll Get to Do:

  • Coordinate production workflow for one or multiple programs and products
  • Plan and prioritize operations to ensure customer schedule and requirements are met
  • Create production work order documentation for release to the shop floor
  • Assist in determining manpower required to meet schedule demands
  • Work with materials planning to determine and schedule materials needed to cover production demand
  • Monitor jobs to ensure they will be completed on schedule
  • Identify potential issues or deal with current disruptions to minimize delays in the value stream
  • Obtain output information (number of finished products, percentage of defectives etc.)
  • Prepare and submit status and performance reports
  • Collaborate with Quality Assurance, Materials Purchasing, Program Management and other staff
  • Revision Notice incorporation
  • Creation and releasing of purchasing requisitions to Procurement.

We offer phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly team environment.  We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal-oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do. 

The successful candidate will have strong organizational, analytical, presentation, problem resolution, and process improvement skills.  He/she will be comfortable communicating with internal and external customers, as well as working in cross functional teams.

*This position is on-site in Annapolis, Maryland and cannot be performed remotely full time.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree plus 3 years of relevant experience; or Master's degree plus 1 year of relevant experience; or an Associate’s degree plus 5 years of relevant experience; or 7 years of relevant experience without a degree.
  • Experience with SAP/ERP or Similar Material Resource Planning tool
  • Experience supporting a Manufacturing Environment (or military equivalent)
  • U.S. citizenship with the ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret level clearance, including Special Access Program within a reasonable period, as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Preferred Qualifications:             

  • Degree in Industrial Engineering, Business Administration, Supply Chain Management, Mathematics, or other closely related area
  • Current, active Secret clearance
  • Experience in Materials, MRP, Production Control, Supply Chain, Logistics or a similar role.
  • Strong communication, presentation, and analytical skills
  • Self-Motivated Individual with strong organizational, communication, interpersonal, and PC computer skills
