An organization within Northrop Grumman’s Payload and Ground Systems (PGS) division, Cutting Edge Optronics is a leading provider of high-power laser diode arrays, high power pump modules and diode-pumped solid-state (DPSS) lasers. We are a vertically integrated manufacturer capable of supplying unmounted diode bars, packaged laser diodes, DPSS modules, laser diode drivers and complete DPSS laser systems. Our diode laser-based products have become industry standards and are used in a wide variety of commercial and military applications. 

  

Learn more about Cutting Edge Optronics at our website: https://www.northropgrumman.com/who-we-are/cutting-edge-optronics/. 

Northrop Grumman - Cutting Edge Optronics (CEO) is looking for a highly motivated Assembler for wafer processing. The Semiconductor Wafer Fabrication Technician performs operations associated with the fabrication of semiconductor laser wafers. All work takes place in a team-oriented, mobile phone-free, fragrance-free cleanroom. Responsibilities are subject to change dependent upon your personal experience/strengths and specific needs of Cutting Edge Optronics.

Responsibilities:

  • Perform wafer production operations including photolithography, wet and dry etching, and metallization.
  • Measure and inspect wafers using a microscope, profilometer, and/or ellipsometer.
  • Handle chemicals used in the production process, including sulfuric acid, hydrogen peroxide and acetic acid
  • Mount and thin wafers using a slurry-based lapping and polishing equipment.

Basic Qualifications:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Excellent fine motor skills, including the ability to handle small, fragile materials using a vacuum wand or tweezers
  • Comfortable wearing full cleanroom attire and working under yellow lighting
  • Good oral communication and interpersonal skills
  • Able to follow written operational procedures
  • Willingness and capability to learn new tasks
  • Six months to one year of relevant experience and/or training.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 2-year technical associate's degree
  • Experience with Microsoft Office software (Outlook, Word, Excel and PowerPoint)
