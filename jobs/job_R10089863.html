ACME Engineering Mission Systems Sector is seeking a leader to manage a team of highly qualified, diverse individuals in Digital Technology. Digital Technology is responsible for creating complex FPGA, ASIC, System on the Chip, Circuit Card Assembly and Digital Subsystem designs for all electronic systems applications from undersea to outer space. The individual will be responsible for managing a digital design group with staff located in Springfield, IL and Morrisville, NC.

Roles and responsibilities:

  • People leadership for an engineering design organization, to include performance management, career development, staffing, and merit planning 
  • Provide project and technical oversight for design activities performed by the group
  • Provide ownership of and direction to the design processes used in the group
  • Provide proposal inputs and review proposal content for both internal and external customers
  • Support department initiatives, recruiting, technology roadmap creation, cross-campus/sector involvement, and NCTA project leadership

  • Must be able to obtain and maintain a high level security clearance

 
Preferred Qualifications - Candidates with these desired skills will be given preferential consideration:

  • Master’s degree in engineering, business management, engineering leadership, or equivalent
  • 10 years of experience in a multi-disciplined engineering environment
  • Position prefers a candidate with a current Secret Clearance or TS/SSBI
