Associate Engineering Design/Engineering Design
R10089075
1st Shift (United States of America)

Designs major components or major portions of a functional system to develop or improve products and facilitate manufacturing operations. Develops basic data and makes preliminary layouts, models, sketches, and notes necessary to present design proposals. Investigates pertinent design factors such as: ease of manufacture, availability of materials and equipment, interchangeability, replace ability, strength-weight efficiency, and contractual specification requirements and cost. Coordinates with other organizations that are affected by the design development. Creates layouts, models and engineering drawings of complex electro-mechanical assemblies, detail parts, devices, mechanisms, and structures using parametric modeling software and various computer aided design (CAD) tools and software. Checks completed layouts, models and drawings for clarity, completeness, conformity to standards, procedures, specifications, and accuracy of calculations and dimensioning. Identifies design errors, omissions, and other deficiencies, and recommends revisions and/or improvements in design layout to responsible engineers or designers. Utilizes computer-aided design equipment and/or graphic tools such as, AutoCAD, Solid Works and NX. Gathers information and prepares original rough layouts, sketches to present design proposal

  • Familiar with Microsoft operating systems
