ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Program Control Manager 1 or 2 to join our team of qualified and diverse individuals. This position will be located in Des Moines, VA or Akron, CA.

Roles and Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Providing a primary interface between Program Business Operations and Program Management, including the Customer Program Office.
  • Acting as a schedule subject matter expert (SME) in the development and statusing of mid-sized technically complex schedules. Acting as a manager for a small number of schedulers supporting a product line.
  • As a Scheduling Manager, directing the development, implementation, and execution of the program business rhythm through training, coordination, and facilitation of Control Account Managers (CAMs) and Program Planning personnel.
  • Supporting integrated program reporting and variance analysis for explaining and interpreting impact of results against program baseline plans/forecast and facilitate the tracking and implementation of corrective/preventative actions, including Baseline Change Requests (BCRs).
  • Working in conjunction with the ACME Engineering Earned Value Management Systems (EVMS) Teams to assist in the maintenance of cost and schedule baselines and forecasts that meet the program and company requirements.
  • Leading the CAMs in preparing for and participating in Integrated Baseline Reviews (IBRs) and follow-up surveillance and audits.
  • Supporting the execution of an Earned Value Management System compliant with ACME Engineering internal system description standards to include organizing the work, developing cost and schedule baselines, updating forecasts, performing monthly analysis, and managing scope change control.
  • Assist in supporting program EVMS status to program leads as well as supporting internal management financial performance reviews.
  • Supporting proposal efforts and other duties as required.

This role may be filled at as a Manager 1 or Manager 2 based on the qualifications outlined below.

Basic Qualifications for Manager 1:

  • Bachelor's degree from accredited university with a minimum of 5+ years related experience– OR – a Master’s degree with a minimum of 4+ years related experience as outlined above. Additional 4 years of experience accepted in lieu of a degree.

Basic Qualifications for Manager 2:

  • Bachelor’s degree with 7+ years of related experience - OR - a Master's degree with 5+ years of related experience. Additional years of experience accepted in lieu of a degree.
  • Experience in a lead or supervisory role.
  • Experience with building and maintaining schedules in systems development life cycle using MS-Project 2016.  Minimum of 3 years of experience.
  • Experience in forecasting and estimating, and/or cost/schedule integration techniques, including subcontractor integration in a program’s Integrated Master Schedule (IMS).
  • Experience in critical path analysis, program management reports, risk management tracking and/or corrective action plans.
  • Ability to obtain a DoD security clearance.  US Citizenship is a prerequisite.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current and Active Top Secret/ SCI security clearance.  US Citizenship is a prerequisite.
  • Experience using scheduling tools such as MS-Project 2016 in developing and maintaining large schedules/IMS. Working with Control Account Managers (CAMs) to develop and maintain task data.
  • Integrated Baseline Review (IBR) materials preparation and presentation.
  • Experience with writing baseline change requests (BCRs).
  • Understanding of Earned Value Management System (EVMS).
  • Supporting program surveillance reviews and oversight.
  • Controlling major subcontractors with compliant earned value systems requirements and Integrated Program Management Report (IPMR) Deliverables.
  • Familiarity with industry standard accounting, and cost management software tools such as Cobra, Open Plan, Acumen, Costpoint, and/or Cognos.
