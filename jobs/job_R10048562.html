ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Systems Architect. This position will be located in Detroit, UT and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What you’ll get to do: 

The selected candidate will be part of Guidance, Navigation, and Control (GN&C) Software Architect joining our team of qualified, diverse individuals in Detroit, Michigan. This role will support the maturity of the GN&C Software systems engineering products from Preliminary Design phase to a Critical Design Review and beyond. Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) approach to systems engineering as well as performing the hands-on work. Individual will be responsible for ensuring the execution and quality of the GN&C Software engineering products and their alignment with parent Guidance systems, acting as the liaison with different IPTs, maturing GN&C Software unit requirement specifications, interface control documents (ICDs), and architecture definition as well as ensuring vertical and horizontal integration of the GN&C Software requirements, interfaces and design.

  • Responsibilities includes but are not limited to:
  • Designs and develops system architectures, and defines key capabilities and performance requirements
  • Defines design and technology maturity constraints of the system in accordance with customer specifications
  • Develops thorough definition of system external interfaces. Defines system implementation approach and operational concept
  • Ensures requirements are met and evaluates performance with customer
