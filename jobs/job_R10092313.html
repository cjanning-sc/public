Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Program Integrator to join our Program Operations Homeroom team. This position will be located onsite in Palmdale, CA.

This individual contributor will work with the Program Office on all program reporting and executive level presentations. The candidate will be required to improve their knowledge of industry practices and standards and demonstrate the skill and ability to perform professional tasks. This individual should possess leadership and team building capabilities and have strong interpersonal skills. Must possess solid organization abilities and be a motivated, flexible team player. Some travel may be required.

Responsibilities for this position include (but are not limited to):

  • Preparing for, maintaining current status of reporting data, and running meetings, typically with a high level of Power Point content.
  • Programmatic operation and integration directly supporting the Program Office in day-to-day execution
  • Assess project issues, and support development of resolutions to meet productivity, quality and customer satisfaction goals and objectives
  • Development, implementation, and maintenance of tools/templates for monitoring project progress
  • Preparation of status reports and presentations for NGC internal and Customer meetings
  • Enterprise Risk and Opportunity management

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree from an accredited university with 3 years of Project Management experience; 1 year with a Master's Degree or 7 years of experience may be considered in lieu of Bachelor's degree requirement.
  • Experience with Microsoft Office tool suite; the ability to create, manage, and manipulate the tools, especially PowerPoint, Word, and Excel
  • Active secret or top secret clearance last investigation completed within last 5 years
  • Ability to attain and maintain program access (PAR)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 4+ years of experience in general business environment with preference in project management, program integration
  • Master’s Degree from an accredited university
  • Intermediate to advance skills with Microsoft Office tool suite
  • Knowledge of Project Management principles and experience utilizing them professionally
  • Familiarity with the Integrated Management Framework
  • Proven success managing multiple projects simultaneously
  • Experience with interfacing with all levels of employees and Leadership
  • SharePoint experience

The selected candidate shall adhere to NG processes. This individual should have demonstrated Project Management experience and strong interpersonal skills, be organized, flexible, and be a team player. Some travel may be required

We offer flexible work arrangements, 9/80 schedule with every other Friday off, phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly team environment. We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do.
