Are you ready to take your career to the next level by challenging your knowledge and expertise of Digital Designs?

The Digital Products Engineering Team is looking to grow and expand the team with skilled digital design engineers seeking to push the boundaries of board/unit design and development.

If your passion and career lies within digital design for digital communications, signal processing, network switching/routing, and have experience with digital hardware for spacecraft applications apply now!

We currently have opportunities to performing circuit design and analysis, prototype hardware testing and troubleshooting.

As part of this team, you will have the opportunity to leverage your knowledge and expertise.

  • To aid in the design and development of digital technologies

  • Perform conceptual digital hardware designs

  • Develop digital board/unit architectures

  • Requirements development and management with systems engineering

  • Supporting space hardware through manufacturing, assembly & test

We value and promote growth and development of our teams by providing opportunities to support other areas of interest to our employees.

If you are interested in learning more, apply for your chance to be considered for one of our many digital design engineer openings within the digital products team.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Minimum Bachelor’s/Master's degree in electrical/electronic engineering, computer engineering or related digital electronics experience in lieu of the electrical/electronic engineering degree

  • At least two (2) years of combined design experience in one or more the following areas: digital communications, signal processing, network routing, embedded processors or switching applications

  • The ability to obtain and maintain a security clearance and/or program access

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with digital circuit analysis tools such as: Hyperlynx and Mentor Graphics Expedition

  • Experience with PCB design and worse case analysis

  • Experience working on digital hardware for space flight applications

  • Experience with engineering support to manufacturing including hardware troubleshooting

  • Experience with digital signal processing and/or FPGA design experience

  • Experience with parts selection and managing parts procurement
