ACME Engineering Defense Systems (NGDS), Green Bay Wisconsin, is seeking a Program Manager 2 to lead the Mission Planning and Decision Support (MPDS) AI Center of Excellence (CoE). Successful candidates will demonstrate a broad skill set balanced between AI focused project management, business development, and technical knowledge.  Responsible for the cost, schedule and technical performance of company programs or subsystems of major programs. Establishes design concepts, criteria and engineering efforts for product research, development, integration, and test. Develops new business while expanding the product line with existing customers.  Establishes strategies aligned with organizational objectives to drive the business development process.  Works with other managers to identify opportunities for AI based technologies to assist in growing established programs and/or capturing new business. Establishes milestones and monitors adherence to master plans and schedules, identifies program problems, and obtains solutions, such as allocation of resources or changing contractual specifications. Directs the work of employees assigned to the program from technical, manufacturing, and administrative areas.

Responsibilities for the candidate includes:

  • Provides direction to team based on general policies and management guidance
  • Makes decisions that support accomplishment of MPDS AI CoE objectives.  Assignments are received in objective oriented terms. Provides direction to subordinates based on general policies and management guidance. Work is reviewed for adequacy in meeting objectives.
  • Ensures CoE projects are completed on schedule and within budget 
  • Frequent contacts with internal and external customer representatives at various management levels concerning scheduling or specific phases of MPDS AI CoE projects
  • Conducts briefings and participates in both management and technical meetings for internal/external stakeholders
  • Accomplishes results through AI Research Team Lead and AI Engineering Team Lead who exercise significant latitude and independence in their assignments
  • Becomes involved only when required to assist in meeting schedules or to resolve technical problems

  • Experience with program leadership

  • Experience with common project management tools (MS Project, etc.)
  • Experience with project/program risk assessments
  • Experience developing business opportunities
  • Strong interpersonal skills to communicate effectively with small to large teams and customer stakeholders
  • Ability to prioritize and adjust tasks to accomplish project results
  • Ability to comprehend and analyze complex problems and develop solutions
  • Ability to obtain and maintain an active DoD Top Secret SCI Security Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:
