We have an opening for a Principal Integration Test Engineer to join our qualified, diverse team in Redondo Beach, CA.

What you’ll get to do?

The System Integration and Test Engineer will assist with developing an integration and test strategy to successfully sell-off and verify the resilient system at System AI&T. The approach shall include necessary test assets and resources required to achieve success. The engineer will maintain processes and procedures for the assembly and test of spacecraft, Coordinate integration activities with supervisors, program engineers and various customer organizations. Document and disposition hardware discrepancies and pursuing root cause and corrective actions. Design and developing tooling to aid in the integration and test process.

  • Ability to travel 10%

  • Experience with MilSatCom, and Ground C2 systems.

  • Experience with continuous integration and deployment (DevOps)

  • Experience with cross-functional teams and working in an Agile environment

  • Active DoD Top Secret security clearance
