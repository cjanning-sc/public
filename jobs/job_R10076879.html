Northrop Grumman’s Enterprise Services organization is seeking a qualified Computer Systems Architect to perform key assignments for Mission Systems off-net Engineering and Manufacturing network. Primary location preference would be Linthicum, MD or Rolling Meadows, IL to support closed areas. This position does not offer the ability to telecommute full time, however

when not requested to be onsite for customer support, telework will be available for supporting unclassified resources.

This position can be hired as a ​Principal Engineer Software 3 or 4, depending on the individuals experience and education.

The incumbent will support software development teams through configuration management tools onsite, primarily with ClearCase and ClearQuest support. This technical role requires an understanding of concepts including software support, system administration, cyber security, and information assurance and requires the ability to support a diverse user community in a dynamic environment.

This position will report to the EMSI Infrastructure Engineering Development and Applications Support (EDAS) Manager but will be expected to work independently onsite, directly supporting programs. Primary duties will include providing onsite customer support for CM Systems (primarily ClearCase/ClearQuest but also Bitbucket, GitHub, and other Git applications), working with DevOps tools including but not limited to Atlassian tools, (Jira, Bitbucket, Confluence, Crucible / Fisheye), Jenkins, databases (SQL), and maintaining system documentation, developing infrastructure support procedures, and providing security support.  The incumbent will also be expected to support programs directly upon request. 

Basic Qualifications:

Requirements for a Principal Engineer Software (Level 3) are:

• At least 5 years of relevant experience with a BS Degree in Computer Science or Information Systems field; 3 Years with Masters

• Familiarity with Windows and Linux OS administration

• Familiarity with ClearCase and ClearQuest

• Familiarity with other Configuration Management tools/services

• Proven experience in a customer supporting role

• Demonstrated proficiency in analytical thinking, problem solving skills, and Systems Thinking mindset

• Willingness to learn and perform request tasks, respond quickly, work onsite when necessary, and make recommendations based on customer needs

• Ability and willingness to work independently with little oversight or direction in an Agile environment

• Must be a US Citizen and must have the ability to obtain Interim Secret clearance prior to start date.  Once started, must have the ability to obtain full secret clearance

• Candidate will be trained on the server-side installation of ClearCase/ClearQuest in a timely fashion

Requirements for a Senior Principal Engineer Software (Level 4) are

• At least 9 years of relevant experience with a BS Degree in Computer Science or Information Systems field; 7 Years with Masters

• Familiarity with Windows and Linux OS administration

• Experience with ClearCase/ClearQuest

• Experience with other Configuration Management tools/services

• Proven experience in a customer supporting role

• Willingness to learn and perform request tasks, respond quickly, work onsite when necessary, and make recommendations based on customer needs with little or no training

• Ability and willingness to work independently with little oversight or direction

• Must be a US Citizen and must have the ability to obtain Interim Secret clearance prior to start date.  Once started, must have the ability to obtain full secret clearance

• Candidate will be expected to perform server-side installation of ClearCase/ClearQuest in a timely fashion

Preferred Qualifications:

• Experience with Linux OS system

• Experience with ClearCase/ClearQuest

• CompTIA Security+ certification

• Working experience in ClearCase/ClearQuest

• Familiarity with database administration (MySQL)

• Experience using Perl, PowerShell, and Windows batch to automate manual tasks

• Understanding of server configuration and infrastructure

• Superior written and oral communication skills

• Ability to work flex and overtime hours if necessary

• Active Secret clearance or has been active within the last two years
