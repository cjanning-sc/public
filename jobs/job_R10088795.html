ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Project Management professional to support Advanced Programs, an emerging business portfolio. This position will be located in any of the following locations:

  • Madison, AL (preferred location)
  • Detroit, UT

The Project Management position involves managing and overseeing the operational aspects of ongoing Advanced Programs projects. Serves as liaison between project engineers, internal and external organizations, suppliers, and management team. Liaises with engineering and suppliers to understand and resolve issues associated with supporting Advanced Programs projects.

Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Provide financial control and maintenance of material/subcontracts budgets
  • Proficient and knowledgeable in managing material dollars across multiple projects. General understanding of Cost and Material accounting including knowledge of General Ledger accounts, Purchase Requisitions and Purchase Orders, networks and Material Master is preferred.
  • Support material/subcontracts schedules and prepare status reports on the health of the associated contracts or orders.
  • Identify risks and opportunities and coordinate with project teams to mitigate and retire risks to subcontract and material delivery.
  • Develop and maintain proposals to include reviewing & coordinate updates to Supplier Statements of Work (SSOW), preparing Requests for Proposals (RFPs), coordinating needed technical evaluations and PCARs on supplier proposals, performing quality checks on all proposal submittals, and attending proposal related reviews.
  • Prepare heading check executive level briefings for management and develops mechanisms for monitoring project progress and tools for intervention and problem solving with project managers, line managers, and customers.

Must possess the ability to handle multiple tasks in a dynamic environment, work autonomously under general direction and will demonstrate critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
