Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal Engineering Design / Sr. Principal Engineering Design to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Clearfield, UT.

Responsibilities:

Designs major components or major portions of a functional system to develop or improve products and facilitate manufacturing operations. Develops basic data and makes preliminary layouts, sketches, and notes necessary to present design proposal. Investigates pertinent design factors such as ease of manufacture, availability of materials and equipment, interchangeability, replace ability, strength-weight efficiency, and contractual specification requirements and cost. Coordinates with other organizations affected by design development. Makes layouts of complex assemblies and details parts of devices, mechanisms, and structures. Checks completed layouts and drawings for clarity, completeness, conformity to standards, procedures, specifications, and accuracy of calculations and dimensioning. Identifies design errors, omissions, and other deficiencies, and recommends revisions and/or improvements in design layout to responsible engineers or designers.​

Work schedules can vary, but we strive for a 9/80 work week, which gives us every other Friday off to pursue interests outside of the office.

This position may be filled at the Principal Engineering Design or Senior Principal Engineer Design level based on the qualifications below.

Basic Qualifications:

Principal

  • Bachelors degree and 5 years of experience; 3 years with Master’s Degree; 0 years with PhD. Note: An additional 4 years of relevant work experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
  • Multiple years experience in design and fabrication of special tooling or support equipment
  • Significant number of hours (approx.10,000) CAD modeling and drafting experience
  • Experience with Siemens NX/CATIA CAD tools
  • Solid verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret Clearance

Senior Principal

  • Bachelors degree and 9 years of experience; 7 years with Master’s Degree; 4 years with PhD. Note: An additional 4 years of relevant work experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
  • Multiple years experience in design and fabrication of special tooling or support equipment
  • Significant number of hours (approx.10,000) CAD modeling and drafting experience
  • Experience with Siemens NX/CATIA CAD tools
  • Solid verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret Clearance
     

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience in checking and standardization of models and drawings
  • Experience in design automation via macros and power copies
  • Active DoD Secret clearance granted or re-investigated within last 6 years
