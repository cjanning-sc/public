Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems Sector, Strike Division has one opening for a Contracts Manager 2 to Contracts teams for a Strike position in our Palmdale location. The selected candidate will report directly to the Senior Contracts Manager with dotted-line reporting to the Director of Business Management for the respective portfolio.

Roles and Responsibilities include:

  • Provide guidance, mentoring and counsel to staff in all areas of contracts administration and contracts management including: capture strategy, solicitations, proposal development, contract negotiations and contract execution in multiple contract types (e.g., Cost Plus, FFP, FPI).
  • Administers contracts which includes the following responsibilities: ensures compliance with all contractual requirements, interprets contract requirements for program and functional leadership, drafts formal correspondence, coordinates CDRL submittal with all stakeholders, ensures timely invoicing and payment, and reviews and executes contract modifications.
  • Review and approve contractual documents for protection of company’s contractual posture, adherence to company policy, and provide advice/recommendations to various levels of leadership regarding contractual rights and obligations and interpretation of terms and conditions.
  • Develop, maintain and foster highly effective customer relationships; serves as the primary interfaced with the customer
  • Work directly with program and business teams, functional and IPT leads in analyzing and developing risk mitigation strategies, protecting the contract baseline by employing strong change control oversight, developing strong contract language and various contracting approaches and strategies.

Be responsible for a contracts team that has an active role in leading the pre-award and post-award activities for a variety of contract types and customers.

  • Partners closely with Pricing & Estimating (P&E) and the Capture team to develop and implement proposal strategies.
  • Reviews and provides feedback on customer RFIs, draft RFPs, and RFPs.  Guides the program and direct reports on the implications of model contract terms and conditions
  • Facilitates the proposal review process and ensures internal reviews are conducted in accordance with company policies and procedures.  Partners with P&E and the Capture team to create briefings for internal reviews
  • Builds and leads a team of highly motivated and effective contracts professionals.
  • Provides day-to-day management, oversight and direction of staff, goal setting and tracking performance, coaching, training and development of staff, and performance evaluations.

We offer flexible work arrangements, phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly team environment. We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal-oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do.
