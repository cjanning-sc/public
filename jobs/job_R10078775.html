ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems in Ohio is currently seeking a Manager, Software Engineering 1 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals within our Software organization. This role can be located in Cleveland or Cincinnati, Ohio.

This candidate will be part of a product team working closely with Software Leadership and other Product Owners.  This is a vital position to a classified program in support of software design and development.  Candidate will be responsible for leading a product team through software architecture, requirements, design, and development phases. Candidate will provide leadership to the product team (6-8 engineers) by flowing down system level architecture/requirements and setting the technical direction for the team to execute with the assistance of the software lead.  Candidate will be responsible for the effective execution of an AGILE-focused software lifecycle process at the product level, to include managing a product Configuration Control Board (CCB), and tracking and reporting all relevant metrics to the software lead. Candidate will be responsible for both cross-product coordination within the various IPTs coordination within the program, allocating developer resources where necessary.

The program is a multi-site, multi-sector effort. The Candidate shall be willing to travel to other sites to support technical meetings, integration efforts, and other related tasking. The Candidate shall also be willing to adjust work schedule as required to meet needs of east coast coordination.

The candidate will also be responsible for functional duties including conducting performance assessments, staffing, employee development, communication meetings, disciplinary actions, compensation adjustments, training & educational oversight/ approval and other routine personnel issues. This position will be responsible for establishing common processes and practices in cooperation with colleague managers across the sector.

Essential Functions:

  • Responsible for managing staffing and reporting status and metrics to the software leadership and Functional leadership
  • Provide functional management to direct reports, including career guidance, staffing, interviewing/onboarding, disciplinary actions, and direct engagement
  • Manage all product activities for a team of developers, employing an AGILE-focused process
  • Coordinate the activities of the product-level CCB and ensure its efficient functioning
  • Coordinate all product-level software verification and release activities within the larger program schedules
  • Primary product representative for product integration in on-site labs and at off-site integration facilities

  • Experience developing in C/C++ for real-time, flight critical systems, multi-channel systems

  • Experience with MIL-STD-1553B, IEEE-1394b, RS-232/422/485 data bus integration
  • Experience designing, developing, integrating, verifying and qualifying safety-critical real-time embedded computing systems including experience with software and systems engineering.
