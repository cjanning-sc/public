The Northrop Grumman Propulsion Systems and Controls (PS&C) Northrop Grumman Defense Systems is currently seeking a Manager 2 of Pricing Cost Estimating for our Elkton, Maryland location. The Elkton, Maryland site began solid-propellant rocket operations in 1948. Today, the Elkton PS&C facility designs, develops, and produces solid-propellant propulsion and ordnance systems in programs professionally managed for a wide variety of Government and commercial customers.  The facility also serves as the hub for offsite engineering and test facilities in Ronkonkoma, NY and Palm Beach Gardens, FL.

Responsibilities

  • Responsible for the direct supervision and management of the Proposal Management, Price Estimating and Publications teams to ensure we submit timely, accurate, and compliant proposals.

  • Interacting with other functional areas (Business Management, Business Development, Contracts, Global Supply Chain, Program Management, Engineering, Manufacturing Operations, etc.) to develop strategies to maximize win probability

  • Work collaboratively to establish and review contract assumptions, terms and conditions, evaluation of risks and opportunities, cash flow, return on investment, and pricing budgets

  • Interface with internal business development representatives and customer representatives to clarify customer requirements and selection standards

  • Develop and lead teams as well as leadership reviews of final proposal presentations

  • Lead internal and external fact-finding activities and audits

Success in this role will require sound technical judgment, innovative thinking, and excellent interpersonal/management skills in order to plan, motivate and accomplish goals through your staff.
