ACME Engineering Global Operations Security is seeking a Cybersecurity Support Technician to assist the Information System Security Team with classified cybersecurity.

Responsibilities may include but not be limited to the following:

  • Perform hardware/software inventory actions of all assigned accountable classified assets.

  • Perform actions associated with introduction and release of hardware into/out of the restricted spaces.

  • May assist in administrative actions to maintain a configuration management baseline, to include processing entrance / exit forms, assisting with the execution of a configuration control board, and other tasks associated with maintaining a hardware and software baseline.

  • Perform system auditing using various security auditing tools according to published procedures. Record, maintain, and update records in the continuous monitoring system; assist the Information System Security Officer/Manager with analysis of anomalies, and work with System Administrators to resolve problems and issues.

  • Perform file transfers across various security domains in accordance with published Assured File Transfer procedures.

  • Support the continuous monitoring of information systems by performing administrative tasks related to the testing of technical controls applied to the system.  This may include administrative tests of security controls, collecting and reporting test results, and monitoring corrective actions until all actions are closed.

  • Maintain accurate internal records.

Basic Qualifications:

To be considered for this position, you must minimally meet these knowledge, skills, and abilities:

  • A High School diploma or equivalent with 2 years of additional education and/or experience.

  • Be prepared to use written, oral, and organizational skills to communicate in a professional workplace with peers, supervisors, and program leadership effectively.

  • Have strong Microsoft Office skills.

  • Must be able to obtain a DoD Secret security clearance within a reasonable period of time as determined by the company and also by program and/or customer requirements, and maintaining the required security clearance, will be a condition of continued employment. U.S. citizenship is a pre-requisite.

  • Have the ability to obtain, and maintain, a Special Access Programs (SAP/SAR) access within a reasonable period of time as determined by the company and also by customer and/or program requirements; maintaining SAP/SAR will be a condition of continued employment.

Preferred Qualifications:

Candidates with these desired skills will be given preferential consideration:

  • Current DoD Secret security clearance.

  • Current DoD 8570 IAM Level 1 (ex., Security+) certification.
