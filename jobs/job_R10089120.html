Senior Manager, Talent Development
R10089120
1st Shift

Reporting to the Director of Talent Development, the Senior Manager will help shape and execute a talent development strategy and related processes in alignment with business and workforce needs to unleash the full potential of our people and business. This individual will be responsible for identifying, assessing and developing talent development solutions aimed at developing and growing the capabilities and skills of our people who are focused on defining possible each and every day.

This position can be performed by as a virtual, telecommuter.

The Senior Manager will define the learning and talent development strategy for key talent segments to drive continued growth, development and engagement of our people.  The individual will take a data-driven approach to lead the assessment and analysis of development needs and partner with key stakeholders to prioritize, develop, deliver and scale those development solutions.  This person will leverage technology and modern approaches to help transform the learning and development experience for our people. 

Additionally, this Senior Manager will take an integrated approach to learning and development by applying learning and development subject matter expertise (e.g., instructional design) to ensure solutions appropriately demonstrate alignment to the company strategy, talent strategies and talent management disciplines, such as skills/competencies, succession management, performance management, and culture assessments.  The ideal candidate will manage a team capable of meeting the learning and development needs of a large and complex aerospace and defense company in an ever-changing talent landscape and be able to lead others in a matrix environment.

The successful candidate will effectively communicate and partner with internal and external stakeholders and will be responsible for creating strategic concepts and translating them to execution to produce business outcomes.

Responsibilities:

  • Support the shaping of an enterprise talent development strategy and drive execution within specific talent segment(s) and related processes in alignment with business and workforce needs (including needs assessment and validation, measurements/ROI).

  • Drive the identification, assessment, development and delivery for talent development solutions for the enterprise, integrating competency frameworks, company core values and leadership behaviors and tying focus areas to business strategy, priorities and needs.  Apply practical stakeholder, change and project management disciplines to execute and deliver programs.

  • Provide thought leadership and subject matter expertise in the talent development domain including line of sight to internal and external best practices and adopt for the enterprise as appropriate.

  • Partner with Organizational Effectiveness in the business to ensure talent development strategies align with business needs. Identify common talent development needs across the organization to inform strategic priorities in alignment with overarching Talent strategy. Consult on business specific talent needs.

  • Ensure diversity strategy is integrated for talent development to ensure talent is skilled and motivated to reskill to meet the needs of our business and ensure a pipeline of diverse talent. Partner with the Diversity and Inclusion and People Analytics teams to align talent development efforts and identify metrics to measure impact.

  • Identify talent development intersection points across functional partners (inside and outside of HR) to deliver an integrated approach and employee experience across our learning and development programs and processes.

  • Partner with Technical Development and Talent Management teams to ensure needs align with offerings and learning experiences are consistent.

  • Support talent development organizational design including services and delivery model, organizational roles and talent requirements.

Expertise Needed:

  • Bachelor’s degree in business, management, organizational development, human resources, or closely related field is required.

  • Minimum of 8+ years of mixed experience in an HR Business Partner role, or a role at the leadership level demonstrating expertise in learning and development, organizational development, succession planning, strategizing and implementing organizational and/or talent management solutions to include change management, talent management and development, rotational programs, or leadership / functional development.

  • Experience in a technology/manufacturing or aerospace and defense organization

  • Demonstrated aptitude for learning with the flexibility to adapt to shifting priorities and navigate ambiguous situations.

  • Demonstrated experience leading medium-large teams of direct and indirect leadership teams to drive successful business and functional outcomes

  • Experience collaborating with and influencing senior leaders (VPs) to ensure client-satisfaction regarding project and/or process goals and objectives and strategic initiatives

  • Must be willing to travel (25%) of the time

  • Outstanding organization and project management skills with the ability to manage the delivery of high-quality, on-time programs in a dynamic environment.

  • Exceptional interpersonal, collaboration, communication and relationship-building skills with an ability to manage multiple clients with multiple needs in an efficient and effective manner.

  • Systems and strategic thinking.

  • Executive level presentation experience and proven proficiency

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Desired experience includes Learning & Development or line of business experience to include organizational effectiveness / organization development, with strong knowledge and understanding of talent management, and internal consulting practices.

  • Experience leading and developing learning solutions in a digital environment, leveraging nudges, gamification and other modern techniques to enhance the learning experience and improve ROI.

  • Experience in consulting / coaching senior leadership and expertise in change management and strategic communication strongly preferred.

  • Demonstrated capability in process improvement implementation.

  • Vendor management, project management experience strongly desired.

  • Master’s degree or advanced certification in OE/OD preferred.
