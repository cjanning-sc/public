Oversees and manages the operational aspects of ongoing projects and serves as liaison between project management and planning, project team, and line management. Develops mechanisms for monitoring project progress and for intervention and problem solving with project managers, line managers, and clients.

  • Bachelor's degree plus 10 years of progressive experience in manufacturing, engineering, and/or program management. 8 years with master’s degree. In lieu of formal degree, High School Diploma or equivalent (GED) and at least 14 years of relevant experience.

  • Ability to develop solutions to complex problems requiring the use of ingenuity, creativity, and participation of internal and/or external personnel & teams.

  • Strong communications skills, both verbal and written, interpersonal skills and good problem-solving mindset.

  • Ability to effectively communicate with internal and external customers.

  • Ability to compose materials such as detailed reports, work-related manuals, publications.

  • Proficiency in Risk and Opportunity management.
