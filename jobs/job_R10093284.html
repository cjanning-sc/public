We are seeking either a Configuration Analyst or a Principal Configuration Analyst to support the planning and implementation of a comprehensive Configuration Management (CM) process on a major program, in accordance with established CM and Data Management (DM) practices that are based on DoD and customer contract requirements. This role is part of a Space Force Command, Space Systems Command (SSC) program – the Enhanced Polar System (EPS) Control and Planning Segment (CAPS) Recapitalization Contract.  The EPS-R program will provide Extremely High Frequency (EHF) protected satellite communications (SATCOM) capability to forces in the North Polar Region. The position will be located in Redondo Beach, CA.

What you’ll get to do?

Plans and coordinates preparation of project documentation, such as engineering drawings, production specifications and schedules, and contract modifications, to ensure customer contract requirements are met. Reviews contract to determine documentation required for each phase of project, applying knowledge of engineering and manufacturing processes. Analyzes proposed changes of product design to determine effect on overall product and system. Coordinates modification records for management control. Establishes change orders and prepares for change authorization and documentation by company and subcontractor. Reviews and analyzes released engineering change data and coordinates changes with engineering, quality, support, manufacturing, and engineering data control activities.

Core Responsibilities:

  • Develop and Maintain CDRL schedule, submittal requirements, metrics
  • Submit CDRLs according to schedule adhering to data requirements and Data Item Descriptions (DIDs)
  • Perform Change Management activities to a technical baseline:  System Engineering documents via an Engineering Review Board; Software Products (Software Change Control Board); Generate Board Metrics, Agenda and Minutes material
  • Baseline documents in a controlled system
  • Support Change Management using ClearQuest
  • Support program meetings during CDM Lead Absence
  • Onboard new employees to program processes and utilities

This requisition may be filled at either a Configuration Analyst level or a Principal Configuration Analyst level

Basic Qualifications for Configuration Analyst:

  • Bachelor's degree with 3 years of relevant experience, or a Master's degree with 1 year of relevant experience; an additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of degree
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office applications
  • Experience in CDRL management, document generation, and change control board (CCB) preparation and administration
  • Familiarity with change tracking tools and generating reports
  • Active Secret Clearance

Basic Qualifications for Principal Configuration Analyst:

  • Bachelor's degree with 6 years of relevant experience, or a Master's degree with 4 years of relevant experience; an additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of degree
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office applications
  • Experience in CDRL management, document generation, and change control board (CCB) preparation and administration
  • Familiarity with change tracking tools and generating reports
  • Active Secret Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience supporting milestone events (e.g. preliminary design reviews (PDRs) and critical design reviews (CDRs), functional configuration audits (FCAs), physical configuration audits (PCAs), etc.)
  • Experience with TopVue, Defense Cost and Resource Center/Cost Assessment Data Enterprise (DCARC-CADE) and SharePoint
  • Experience with and comprehensive understanding of software development and configuration management tools (i.e., Linux, Jira, Bitbucket, Jenkins, Confluence, ClearQuest, Git, Collaborator), including current SCM industry standards and practices
  • Familiarity with AS9100D, EIA-649, EIA-859, and MIL-HBK-61A guidance and standards.
