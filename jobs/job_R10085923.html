ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal Supplier Quality to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is located in Columbus, Ohio.

Collaborates with program management, engineering, subcontracts/procurement functional management and supplier management teams to ensure that all supplier products meet or exceed the requirements of external and internal customers. Defines the Supplier MA program within the scope of contract requirements and NGMS policies and processes. Manages implementation of Supplier Mission Assurance processes, including, supplier quality requirements development, supplier classification designation (Critical, Key and Standard), supplier pre-award capability assessments, source selection evaluations, supplier quality audits and supplier performance assessments. Supports the maintenance of the approved supplier list and periodically reviews supplier performance to determine whether suppliers should remain on the approved list.

Manages supplier requests for waivers, deviations and discrepancy reporting process (Material Review Board). Evaluates supplier-non-conformances, negative performance trends and failures and determines root cause and implements corrective and preventive actions. Manages source inspection processes.

Utilizes supplier performance data bases and supplier quality management system assessment tools to provide objective evidence of supplier’s capability to perform. Keeps management apprised of supplier performance, quality status and risks. Supports program Mission Assurance in development and maintenance of SAP Vendor and Part Master databases.

Essential Functions:

  • Leads supplier quality portion for managing non-conforming product issues using established systems and processes.
  • Support F-35 Production/Ground support/Spares equipment by investigating supplier caused issues and provide solutions on resultant actions to mitigate escape and impact on the production line, customer. Work with other ACME sites, Cincinnati, Redondo, Akron etc. for impact and reach across
  • Works effectively with suppliers for problem resolution using RCCA tools
  • Issues, monitors and maintains corrective actions for supplier related product/process issues.
  • Proactively analyze supplier performance data to determine continuous improvement opportunities to improve supplier performance.
  • Lead risk mitigation efforts/improvement plans using Zero Defect plan (ZDP), FMEAs, Process/product audits & reviews, monitoring key characteristics & reporting, SPC, scorecard reviews etc.
  • Review and report on First articles per AS9102 for compliance and robustness.
  • Interface with program, MRB and customer on supplier reported issues, impact and resolutions strategies.
  • Consult and advise the Sourcing team to select and manage assigned suppliers.
  • Review and have solid understanding of the relevant product, Engineering drawing / specifications, flow down documents, procedures, and regulating agent body requirements that apply to the products and suppliers assigned.
  • Mentors less experienced SQE's. as required.
  • Leads, directs and manages multiple projects effectively

  • Bachelor's Degree and 5 years of experience in Quality, Manufacturing or Engineering within aerospace industry OR a master's degree with 3 years of experience in Quality, Manufacturing or Engineering within aerospace industry.
  • A minimum of 3 years' experience with AS9100 and AS9102 standards.
  • A minimum of 3 years' experience in Microsoft Office applications.

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD security clearance per business requirements.
