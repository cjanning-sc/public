ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems is seeking a Principal Program Cost Schedule Control Analyst (Level 3) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position requires the ability to work on site weekly in Rancho Bernardo with a hybrid telework schedule.

The Program Cost Control Analyst position will work with a small group of analysts at the division level coordinating and managing the NCTA and Capital.

This position will be responsible for actuals, budgets, change notices, variance explanations, updating forecasts, and coordinating and collaborating with the technical leads to make sure the monthly rhythm flows smoothly. They will handle the day-to-day rhythm, collaborate on process improvements, and use the tools analysis in reporting to management and sector teams. This position will be responsible for ensuring that the weekly/monthly data reports are accurate and reviewed with NCTA and Capital Teams in a timely manner.

This position will be responsible for a challenging program portfolio of pursuits and will work closely with the Resource Manager to ensure all budgets and forecasts align.
