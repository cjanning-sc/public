SSSD has an opening for a Manager Pricing / Cost Estimating 3 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Columbus, Ohio.

In this role, the Manager Pricing / Cost Estimating 3 will manage and develop a team of analysts responsible for the development of cost products supporting the Directed Energy and Strategic Products Business Unit and the Resiliency Technology and Products Operating Unit of the Assured Interoperable Missions BU.  This includes providing appropriate staff and oversight to the development of cost volumes, ROMs, BPE, cost models, or other analysis required to support the objectives of the business unit. 

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Manage the department staffing, resources, tools, and training as needed
  • Engage with business unit leadership to develop estimating strategies needed to successfully capture new business
  • Understand and adhere to internal and external policies and procedures as it pertains to the role
  • Establish strategic relationships both internally with business unit leadership, and externally, with government counterparts

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree with 10 years of experience in Cost Estimating related business management OR 8 years of stated experience with a Master’s Degree
  • Experience directly or indirectly in a leadership role
  • Proven ability to evaluate, integrate, and communicate cost issues and risks to senior and executive management
  • An active in-scope DoD Top Secret or SCI clearance, investigation updated within the last 6 years  

 
Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Persistence and tenacity to meet deadlines in a challenging, multi-tasking environment
  • Excellent communications skills and leadership competencies
  • Strategically focused
  • Experience developing life cycle cost and budgetary planning estimates for space systems
  • Experience working in a special access environment
  • Ability to grasp math, science, and engineering concepts
