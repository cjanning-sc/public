Core Responsibilities:

  • Interaction with users to determine hardware/software requirements and installation and configuration of hardware, operating systems and COTS
  • Ancillary duties will include, but are not limited to: evaluation of vendor products, providing recommendations to purchase hardware and software, providing backup recovery, maintaining system documentation, tuning system performance, building of systems and computing asset management
  • Applicant must be experienced in STIG configuration, classified processing, and security procedures

This requisition may be filled at either a Principal Systems Administrator level or a Senior Principal Systems Administrator level

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Systems Administrator:

  • Current DoD Top Secret clearance or SCI access
  • Ability to obtain and maintain Security+ Certification
  • Bachelor's degree with 6 years of related experience, or a Master's degree with 4 years of related experience; an additional 4 years of related experience may be considered in lieu of a degree
  • Experience with systems administration in a Linux environment

Basic Qualifications for a Senior Principal Systems Administrator:

  • Current DoD Top Secret clearance or SCI access
  • Ability to obtain and maintain Security+ Certification
  • Bachelor's degree with 10 years of related experience, or a Master's degree with 8 years of related experience; an additional 4 years of related experience may be considered in lieu of a degree
  • Experience with systems administration in a Linux environment

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Virtualization experience such as ESX
  • Experience managing a highly virtualized testing lab
  • Experience with remote and local system deployments
  • Familiarity with NAS and SAN technologies (NetApp SAN, EMC SAN, HP, NAS) - CISSP or Microsoft OS Certification
  • Experience with Ansible
