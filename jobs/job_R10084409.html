Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Sr Principal Software Engineer to join the Systems Engineering Team (SET).   This position is in Roy, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.   

What You’ll Get To Do:

  • This role will lead a small team of software engineers who will create software engineering products and modeling governance from Preliminary Design phase to a Critical Design Review and beyond.

  • The role includes leading a team performing a fully immersed, iterative Model Based Engineering approach to software engineering as well as performing the hands-on work.

  • Individual will be responsible for the Computer Systems & Software Architecture Model (CSSAM) and Unified Modeling Language (UML) governance and guidance to a large, distributed software team developing the software design and capabilities for the GBSD program.

  • Individual will be the primary point of contact (POC) to the US Air Force (USAF) Software Architecture Office (SAO) and supporting organizations for the development activities for the program’s Software Architecture.

  • This role involves collaboration and responsibility across multiple organizations to ensure the CSSAM Software Architecture & Design products successfully meet key program milestone objectives, software deliverables, and the statement of work.

  • This role involves working with various certification authorities (e.g. Nuclear Surety, Cybersecurity, Safety) to ensure the CSSAM Software Architecture & Design products support key program certification reviews.

  • Individual will be responsible for validating Software Architecture & Design products meet CSSAM model governance rules and properly flow-down from systems engineering products to the software design and implementation

  • Individual will have the opportunity of leading a team in a highly dynamic environment, managing multiple priorities.
