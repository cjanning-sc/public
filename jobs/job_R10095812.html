SSSD has an opening for a Microelectronic Technician 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be based in Manhattan Beach, CA supporting the Module Assembly & Test department on 1st shift.

This position is vital in maintaining the highest quality standards on the cutting edge of the microelectronics industry. The microelectronic technician performs the following assembly tasks in a collaborative team environment:

  • Wire or ribbon bonding including setup and manual operation of thermocompression and thermosonic wedge bonding, ball bonding, and welding equipment in order to build, rework, or repair hardware.

  • Screening of assemblies including destructive and non-destructive bond pull testing, visual inspection using microscopes, examination against drawings and process specifications.

  • And or tracking and moving microelectronic hardware, maintaining shop floor metrics, troubleshooting basic computer issues.

Basic Qualifications:

  • A High School diploma is required with 2+ years of lab experience.

  • Experience that demonstrates manual dexterity and use of hand tools (work with tweezers, blades, scribes and probes)

  • Computer & communication skills (Microsoft Office)


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Ability to interpret drawing and work instruction within Solumina and Enovia.

  • Familiarity with epoxy dispensing either manually or automated.

  • Familiarity with bond/welding machines such as West Bond, Miyachi Unitek, TPT, K&S.

  • 1-3 years of experience manually epoxy mounting components such as substrates and chips.

  • 1-3 years of experience wire/ribbon bonding or welding onto components such as substrates and chips.

  • Experience in a ESD and FOD controlled lab environment.

  • Familiarity with a shop order or manufacturing execution systems (MES)

  • Experience as a lead or trainer

  • Demonstrated implementation of process improvement
