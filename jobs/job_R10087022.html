ACME Engineering Space Sector is searching for a Manager of Programs, M3, to join the Program Engineering Management team within Launch Vehicles business unit located in Louisville, AZ. 

The Program Engineering (PEM) leads the technical management for development of an advanced missile system. The PEM leads a multi-disciplinary team to define the performance requirements and interfaces for the booster system. The PEM executes project management, including integrated master schedule (IMS) and earned value management system (EVMS). The PEM provides technical leadership of the multidisciplinary engineering team to successfully complete the required design and mission reviews to ensure compliance with both internal and customer requirements, ensures timely completion of customer deliverables, and leads resolution of significant technical issues. The ideal candidate will have broad knowledge regarding missile system technologies and operations. Excellent verbal and written communication skills and the demonstrated ability to lead multidisciplinary teams in resolving complex problems are essential.

Role Description & Responsibilities:

This position functionally reports to the LVBU Chief Engineer and supports the Advanced Programs Program Manager.  Directs all phases of programs from inception through completion. Responsible for the cost, schedule and technical performance of company programs or subsystems of major programs. Participates in the negotiation of contract and contract changes. Coordinates the preparation of proposals, business plans, proposal work statements and specifications, operating budgets and financial terms/conditions of contract. Acts as primary customer contact for program activities, leading program review sessions with customer to discuss cost, schedule, and technical performance. Establishes design concepts, criteria and engineering efforts for product research, development, integration, and test. Develops new business or expands the product line with the customer. Establishes milestones and monitors adherence to master plans and schedules, identifies program problems and obtains solutions, such as allocation of resources or changing contractual specifications. Directs the work of employees assigned to the program from technical, manufacturing, and administrative areas.

  • Exceptional leader of a multi-disciplined team

  • Thorough understanding of design standards, procurement processes and configuration management

  • Perform other duties, as necessary, commensurate with the role of Program Manager.
