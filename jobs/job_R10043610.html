ACME Engineering is currently seeking experienced R&D Engineering Technicians for our Core Engineering Team in both Grand Rapids & Lansing, UT locations.

Job Description:

  • We are seeking highly motivated individuals with experience in composite structures and assemblies
  • Successful candidates will be considered subject matter experts and responsible for developing and implementing complex composite components and assemblies using a wide range of materials and processes 
  • Direct the safe execution of tasks required to support research and development and production for commercial, military and classified programs, as the demand requires.
  • Proficient with interpretation of Engineering models / drawings
  • Maintain inventory (tooling, equipment, NEI material, direct material, etc.)
  • Material handling & preparation (storage, thawing, cutting, kitting, inventory, traceability, etc.)
  • Use of chemicals with proper PPE
  • TC weld & verify / Vacuum port assembly / Vacuum leak check
  • Solid lamination / Bagging / Oven & A/C cure
  • Sandwich panel construct / Bag / Cure (perimeter dams or tapered core)
  • Film adhesive and past adhesive bonding
  • Expert with SMI (Standard Measuring Instruments)
  • Machining of tight tolerance Assemblies
  • Willing to work unconventional shifts occasionally to complete assignments
  • Design and fabricate complex production aids
  • Execute Work Orders / Project assignments
  • Track team metrics and report status frequently to management
  • Manage / Mentor less senior Technicians to successfully complete programs
  • Coordinate and oversee process readiness reviews
  • Promote corrective actions and process improvements
  • Promote Cost improvement initiatives
  • Review procedures for accuracy and completeness
  • Mentor early career Technicians and Engineers

Basic Requirements:

  • Minimum of 16 years of related composites aerospace manufacturing experience
  • In depth knowledge and proficiency with composite materials and manufacturing processes
  • Excellent interpersonal communication skills
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Security Clearance and special program access
  • Possess excellent problem solving skills as well as demonstrated experience in leadership of teams in resolving complex manufacturing issues
  • Proficient with Microsoft Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Demonstrated ability to lead effectively and mentor less experienced technicians
  • Highly regarded by management and peers for composite knowledge and expertise

Preferred Skills:

  • Active DoD Secret Security Clearance
  • SME (Subject Matter Expert) of Composite material and manufacturing technologies (including bonded and fastened assembly methods) experience
  • Ability to operate and troubleshoot complex CNC controlled equipment

 Competencies for Success:

  • Desire to work in a “team first” environment
  • Ability to work in a dynamic, fast paced environment as part of a multi-discipline team
  • Ability to prepare sound presentations in support of a technical position
  • Effective leadership of failure investigations to achieve root cause and corrective action
  • Ability to work independently and use judgment to make appropriate decisions that support overall project objectives
  • Ability to drive projects to completion
  • Ability to mentor Jr level technicians and engineers for success
  • Ability to partner with leadership at all levels
  • Able to coordinate and assemble a team to meet program objectives
  • Able to deal effectively with stress and conflict
  • Ability to trouble shoot problems and resolve issues quickly
