Principal /Sr Principal Configuration Analyst- Top Secret/SCI Clearance (Space/Satellite Systems)
R10088680
1st Shift (United States of America)

The Configuration Analyst supports Configuration Management (CM) activities of the Satellite System Division’s Civil/ Commercial Satellite, and National Security System business units. The Configuration Analyst performs several essential functions of the CM department.

Job Duties:

  • Analyzes and enters hardware part information into the material management system (Costpoint)
  • Controls and releases drawings and documentation using a Product Data Management (PDM) system (Windchill)
  • Responsible for analyzing drawing change requests, understanding the impact to the baseline configuration and next higher assemblies
  • Works closely with CM staff, engineering, flight assurance, and program office personnel to ensure that all CM processes are adhered to when carrying out the above job duties
  • Other CM activities as directed by configuration management staff

Skills & Qualifications:

  • Broad understanding of hardware Configuration Management Systems in an aerospace and defense environment
  • Knowledge of drawing types and engineering drawing practices
  • Strong skills in database use and data entry
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills
  • Excellent time management and organizational skills
  • Detail oriented but able to understand the big picture

This position can be filled as a Principal (level 3) or a Sr Principal (level 4) depending on experience

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree (years of experience may be considered in lieu of a degree)
  • Minimum of 6 years (level 3) or a minimum of 10 years (level 4) of related experience preferably with hardware Configuration Management experience in an aerospace and/or defense environment
  • Active or current Top Secret/SCI clearance
  • U.S. Citizen
  • Ability to obtain a SAP
  • Experience with the following concepts: Configuration Identification, Configuration Change Management, and Configuration Status Accounting
  • Experience with PDM systems
  • Experience with material management systems, Costpoint a plus
  • Literacy in Microsoft Office Suite

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active TS/SCI clearance with SAP
  • Applying Configuration Management requirements and concepts related to change effectivity a plus
  • Experience using Windchill PDM system a plus - CM experience in a manufacturing environment
