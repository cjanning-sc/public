ACME Engineering Mission Systems has an opening for a Manager Subcontracts Administration 3 to join our team of qualified and diverse individuals in Beavercreek, Ohio. The position may have the opportunity to work remote/hybrid for a highly qualified/experienced candidate. This exciting role supports the Maritime/Land Systems & Sensors division, performing on ACME Engineering’s existing programs and platforms while also exploring exciting and new opportunities to shape ACME Engineering’s portfolio of the future. The position reports to the Director, Global Supply Chair for the Marine Systems Business Unit with sites in Beavercreek, CA and Philadelphia, PA.

Essential Functions as a Leader:

As a leader, you always act with integrity, and the highest ethical standards characterize everything you do. You enable your team to achieve predictable and balanced results, while satisfying the needs of internal and external partners. You treat all people with respect, and you model personal growth and continuous development. You know how to build an inclusive environment that attracts, retains, and inspires a diverse and engaged team. You develop trusted and valued customer relationships and communicate effectively, so that people throughout the organization feel engaged and connected to their work. You deliver excellence, strive for continuous improvement and respond vigorously to change.

You ensure your team has the information and tools needed to perform at their best. You demonstrate our committed pursuit of applying innovation to meet the requirements of our customer. You craft and execute strategies that result in sustainable value creation that delivers measurable results.

** This role provides an opportunity to lead and manage a team of 5 or more subcontract specialists and managers who are responsible for the essential functions listed below. **

Essential Functions of the team:

  • All phases of the Subcontract from initiation of the business case for make or buy decisions, market research, single/sole source determinations, request for proposals, commercial item determinations, source selection decision, proposal evaluations, negotiation strategy, awarding subcontracts, and execution of technical, cost and performance requirements.
  • Coordinates and collaborates with key partners such as contracts, engineering, quality, supplier development, planning, operations, program and end-users.
  • Manages preparation of bid packages, supports bidders’ conferences, analyzes and evaluates proposals, negotiates subcontract provisions, selects or recommends subcontractors, writes awards, and administers resulting subcontracts.
  • In conjunction with quality organization, evaluates and monitors supplier quality and reliability, as well as supplier ability to support required delivery schedules.
  • Identifies strategies and opportunities to reduce cost, improve efficiency and manage risk through strategic and targeted negotiations.
  • Manages performance of the portfolio, ensures compliance with all public law requirements as well as company (and business) policies and procedures including import/export regulations.
  • Employs sound business practices and assesses supplier performance as part of follow-up activities.
  • Supports management and development of strategic/key suppliers.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree with at least 9 years’ experience, or a Master’s Degree with at least 7 years’ experience in any of the following: Subcontracts Management, Procurement, Program Management, or Contracts Management
  • Experience managing Programs and/or a Team of 5+ people
  • Leadership experience – Manager or Team Lead experience is acceptable
  • Experience with FAR and DFARS and/or similar environments supporting programs that range from research & development to full rate production
  • Experience with SAP or related purchasing software, in addition to Excel, PowerPoint, & MS Project
  • Excellent communication skills, be self-motivated and collaborate well in a team environment across all levels.  Highly effective interpersonal skills and a commitment to fostering an engaging environment for others. Demonstrated leadership ability with the skills to influence others, interact successfully with peers, and with all levels of management in the organization.
  • US Citizenship and ability to obtain government security clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master Degree in Business, Global Supply Chain or Project Management
  • Solid understanding of the Systems, Applications and Processes (SAP) procurement system, Supplier Scorecards and performance management, ACME Principles and Operating Practices, and metric analysis
  • Strong organizational skills with attention to detail and leadership characteristics
  • Internal ACME Engineering Subcontracts experience
  • Experience conducting presentations to and collaborating with Senior Leadership
  • Experience in negotiating Long Term Pricing Agreements and/or Risk and Opportunity Management  
  • Demonstrated experience assuring compliance with company command media and government regulations
  • Experience managing Programs and/or a Team of 5+ people in support of major defense programs
