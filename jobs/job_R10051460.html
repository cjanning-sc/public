Northrop Grumman Enterprise Services is seeking an Information Technology Manager 1 within the Digital Manufacturing, Engineering, and Quality Program Management Organization; this position will be located in Clearfield, UT. The selected candidate will be responsible for the day-to-day management of Costpoint MES and Solumina application support and service delivery for the Manufacturing, Design, Validation, and Product Data Management domains. The statement of work (SOW) focuses on applications and systems for internal use, including the design, architecture, deployment, and support of Commercial Off The shelf (COTS) digital manufacturing systems and the integration to other systems and applications. Additional responsibilities will include establishing and executing  program/project goals, forecasting and managing SOW, labor budget, and actuals against those established objectives, and oversight of various activities including the planning and execution of the integration of hardware and software for complex systems utilized on critical sector and corporate programs. The IT Manager documents and delivers performance evaluations and accomplishes merit increase planning. The successful candidate will provide their staff with professional development recommendations, mentoring, and discipline as necessary.

This position requires collaboration and team-building across multiple domains and a demonstrated ability to independently prioritize, plan, and execute work tasks in a rapidly changing, fast-paced environment while maintaining high quality results.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree with 5 years of progressive IT experience, or Master's degree with 3 years of progressive IT experience.
  • 2+ years of proven technical leadership or project management experience in a team environment.
  • Proven experience managing multiple simultaneous projects in various stages of the development lifecycle.
  • Proven experience in customer relationship management.
  • Experience with supporting applications and users within a large enterprise.
  • Experience with desktop, user acceptance testing, client/server, and web architecture end-user support and incident resolution.
  • Current CompTIA Security+ CE certification OR the ability to obtain this required certification within 6 months of start date

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Costpoint MES, Solumina, eQube, Teamcenter development, administration, and maintenance.
  • Familiarity with the functions, processes, and capabilities of the manufacturing processes in Northrop Grumman as they apply to MES Software development environments.
  • Experience in Project or Program Management with the ability to support multiple projects concurrently based on priority and criticality.
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills (written, verbal, and presentation).
  • An active DoD Secret security clearance.
