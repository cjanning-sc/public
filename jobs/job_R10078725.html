1st Shift (United States of America)

Aeronautics Systems sector is seeking a Supply Chain Operations Coordinator to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is located in El Segundo, CA.

  • 1st shift, 9/80 schedule with every other Friday off!
  • Position is in-office, unable to accommodate a telecommute/hybrid schedule.

Essential Functions:

  • Utilize WIP reports to expedites flow of materials, parts, and assemblies within or between departments in accordance with production and shipping schedules
  • Coordinate with Manufacturing to assist in prioritizing WIP to ensure contractual schedule dates are met
  • Utilize knowledge of production requirements to coordinate with other shop support teams (Manufacturing Engineering, Quality, MRB, Industrial Engineering, Shipping, Distribution, etc.) to initiate actions to remove any constraints to production
  • Perform necessary SAP and MES system transactions to release work orders for Manufacturing, receive parts into inventory, close out manufacturing work orders, and issue parts up to next higher assemblies
  • Create and prepare shippers/manifests for parts leaving the facility and being transported to the customer or other internal sites
  • Assists in determining possible and actual shortages may initiate action to correct these deficiencies
  • Estimates and logs production rate and time expenditures and may be required to establish sequence and lead time of each operation to meet shipping dates

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School diploma or equivalent and 4 years additional education and/or related experience
  • Ability to work in MS 365, Excel, Word, Power Point
  • Excellent communication skills dealing with customers and suppliers
  • Must have the ability to lift up to 25 lbs.
  • Valid driver’s license or ability to obtain one
  • Ability to obtain and maintain DOD Secret Clearance and a Program Special Access within a reasonable period of time, as determined by the company to meet its business needs (after starting).

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience working in SAP
  • Experience working in a manufacturing setting
  • Experience working in a warehouse environment
  • Active DOD Secret level clearance.
