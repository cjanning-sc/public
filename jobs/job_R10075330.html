Performs technical planning, system integration, verification and validation, cost and risk, and supportability and effectiveness analyses for total systems. Analyses are performed at all levels of total system product to include: concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal. Ensures the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints. Performs functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications.

Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Manager Systems Engineering to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in San Diego, CA.

   

The selected candidate will lead the RQ-4 Integrated Functional Capability Team across the Enterprise, providing strategic direction and oversight of software and hardware development across all RQ-4 programs.

Essential Functions:

  • Define and roadmap Integrated Functional Capability (IFC) efforts across the RQ-4 Enterprise, driving reuse and commonality while ensuring appropriate configuration identification and management.
  • Define and drive the execution of a strategic IFC roadmap driving multiple technical baselines toward commonality while establishing software points of departure and accounting for resource utilization across programs.
  • Utilize Agile methodologies which will enable rapid capability insertion and reduce time to field Implement tools, technologies, and procedure to enable more efficient integration. 
  • Manage and lead IFC teams for the RQ-4 Enterprise.  
  • Monitor and advise the creation and modification of software to meet IFC objectives.
  • Provide guidance and required documentation to maintain a current compatibility baseline.
  • Ensure consistent vertical and horizontal integration across the Global Hawk Enterprise teams with respect to managing software and hardware compatibility and changes.

Basic Qualifications:

  • BS degree in engineering or science and 9 years of related experience or 7 years with a Masters
  • Demonstrated ability to lead IFC processes and procedures.
  • Experience utilizing Earned Value Management System. 
  • Active DoD Secret Clearance.
  • Experience leading a team of 10+ individuals in a technical environment.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • MS degree in a STEM Field
  • Aerospace flight and ground software development experience
  • Previous experience as IFC Lead
  • Experience in management of multiple technical baselines
  • Experience in strategic roadmap development
  • Experience in Agile Development
  • Experience in Autonomous Systems Programs
  • Experience working on US Air Force Programs
  • Experience working on FMS or Export Controlled Programs
  • Active TS/SCI.
