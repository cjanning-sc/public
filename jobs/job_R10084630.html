We have an opening for a Sr Principal Systems Engineer to join our diverse Integrated Support Group (ISG) as a Consolidated Operations and Anomaly Support Team (COAST) Cost Account Manager (CAM).

The candidate must possess excellent leadership, interpersonal communication, and presentation skills, and work closely with the ISG COAST IPT Lead. The candidate will be responsible for directing aspects of the Sub-Project’s tasking from design through requirement verification, including Cost Account Manager (CAM) responsibilities. This position requires coordination and interfacing with the other Sub-Projects, Systems Engineering, external customers, and site personnel. Candidate will work in a dynamic team environment, ensuring personnel follow well-defined processes. The candidate must have the ability to complete tasks with minimal direction and oversight, and mentor junior engineers.

Core Responsibilities:

  • Work closely with the Integration Support Group Lead, APM and DAPM
  • Day to day operations of the technical, schedule, and cost; staffing; providing staff during site installations; supporting organic growth proposals (including CROMs & ECPs), authoring TD/BOEs and proposal development; interfacing frequently with internal and external stakeholders, including cross-program counterparts, Tech Support, and the customer
  • Setting and operating to program objectives and providing guidance to employees
  • Planning, risk management, and project performance
  • Direct team members to perform system buildups, meet deadlines and work within budget
  • Work in a dynamic team environment, ensuring personnel follow well-defined processes and will be responsible for managing all software development tasks from inception through completion

Basic Qualifications:

  • Current SCI access or current DoD Top Secret clearance with the ability to obtain SCI access prior to start date
  • Bachelor's Degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics (STEM) discipline with 9 years of relevant work experience; Master's Degree in STEM with 7 years of relevant work experience; PhD in STEM with 4 years of relevant work experience
  • Previous ground systems experience with technical background in ground system requirements development, design, software development, software integration & test
  • Excellent leadership and interpersonal communication skills with the ability to interface with all levels of employees and management
  • Demonstrated ability to develop effective processes, implement process improvements and streamline activities

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Mission 1 experience
  • Experience working with internal and external customers

  • Cost Account Management experience

  • Demonstrated problem solving and decision-making skills
  • Previous software experience working in a C++/RHEL environment
