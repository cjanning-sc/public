Computer Systems Analyst – Live-Virtual-Constructive Integrating Architecture (LVC-IA) Simulations Lead
R10092567
1st Shift (United States of America)

Put your skills to the test by pushing the boundaries of what’s possible.  From global defense to sustainment and modernization to mission readiness, your experience and ability will make it a reality.  Our programs are built on equal parts of curiosity and collaboration.  Our combined effort means our customers can connect and defend millions of people around the world.  With Northrop Grumman, you’ll have the opportunity to be an essential part of projects that will define your career, now and in the future.

Northrop Grumman Defense Systems sector (NGDS) is seeking a Computer Systems Analyst – Live-Virtual-Constructive Integrating Architecture (LVC-IA) Simulations Lead to join our growing team of diverse professionals supporting our III Corps Mission Command Training program at Fort Bliss in El Paso, Texas. The Live-Virtual-Constructive Integrating Architecture (LVC-IA) Simulations Lead will be responsible for the scheduling and management coordination of all technical, networking, system integration, scenario design, and technical support of the installation's LVC-IA Team at the Fort Bliss, Texas (FBTX) Mission Training Complex.

Responsibilities:

  • Integrates the LVC environments in support of operational force simulation-driven exercises

  • Manages all aspects of the LVC-IA program integration tasks and provides ongoing direction in the performance of the assigned technical support program

  • Integrates LVC-IA into Army Games for Training, Joint Land Component Constructive Training Capability (JLCCTC), and other Core Systems and associated Mission Command Information Systems (MCIS). Works directly with units that employ LVC-IA to meet training objectives

  • As needed maintain, troubleshoot, and correct system issues

  • Be familiar with integration of multiple systems into a single simulation when required by unit training events

  • As required, able to support other training requirements within the Mission Training Center team

Basic Qualifications:

  • One of the following:

    • High school diploma or GED with a minimum of 6 years of Computer Science (CS), Information Technology (IT), Cybersecurity & Information Assurance (IA), or related technical experience

    • Bachelor of Science degree in CS, IT, Cybersecurity & IA, or related discipline with a minimum of 2 years of CS, IT, Cybersecurity & IA, or related technical experience

    • Master of Science degree in CS, IT, Cybersecurity & IA, or related discipline

  • Military leadership experience at platoon or higher level

  • Familiar with Advanced Field Artillery Tactical Data System, Joint Battle Command-Platform, Command Post Computing Environment, Data Distribution System, Extensible C4I Instrumentation System

  • Possesses the ability to work multiple projects /tasks, interface with multiple contractors, Government, and US military personnel

  • Active U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) Secret security clearance

  • Must be willing to work on-site at Fort Bliss in El Paso, Texas

  • Must be able to travel as required (up to 10% of the time)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • A minimum of 5 years simulation training experience

  • Combat Arms Senior non-commissioned officer (NCO) experience
