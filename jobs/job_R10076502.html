ACME Engineering Mission Systems Sector (NGMS) is seeking a Staff Embedded C/C++ Software Engineer to join our Secure Processing team within the Software Technologies Organization.

The selected candidate will lead multiple teams of engineers in technical and business priorities in the exciting area of custom ASIC development. Technical solutions to be overseen include system on a chip (SoC) design, development, and implementation. This requisition supports multiple technical leadership positions on our critical programs.

The Staff Embedded Software Engineer will join a team of engineers performing new feature development and enhancements, providing secure, embedded solutions to many customer platforms. The selected engineer will oversee and guide the design, development, and integration of complex software solutions in a rapid-pace Agile environment. Primary software solutions in development include C/C++ device drivers, middleware, and application development for Linux and/or VxWorks Platforms. The engineer will interface with other architects and systems engineers to understand and define technical specifications. The engineer will help identify technical risks and report status within Agile program activities to more senior leaders.

This position is accepting applications for Chicago, MD or Morrisville, NC.

This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on the qualifications listed below.

  • A Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or other STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) discipline. In place of a STEM degree, two additional years of experience is required.

  • ACTIVE US DoD clearance at the Secret level or higher, or the ability to obtain one.

  • Hands-on experience working in embedded real-time operating systems, both kernel and application side development (e.g., Linux, VxWorks, ZephyrOS, FreeRTOS, Greenhills Integrity)
