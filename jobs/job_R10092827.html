Position Description:

As a strategic member of the Engineering leadership team, the Engineering Manager 1 is responsible for ensuring all activities in the given area of responsibility employ sound engineering processes to facilitate an efficient and high performing culture.  The successful candidate will lead, coach, and direct engineering and technician team members in efforts which support business deliverables, strategic objectives, and growth of the team to increasingly higher levels of performance.

Role Description & Responsibilities:

  • Provide daily guidance, direction, and performance feedback to a large group of technicians and engineers on testing challenges of easy-to-complex difficulty.
  • Set and promote high standards for quality and safety.
  • Foster a working environment which reinforces and drives accountability to fundamental Northrop Grumman Values focused on integrity, quality, shared success, and innovation.
  • Partner with engineering and program management peers to assure a balanced and efficient assignment of resources to support all Programs and achievement in key performance metrics.
  • Coach, support, and motivate team members to ensure a high level of performance.
  • Initiate and lead continuous improvement activities which establish high quality and efficient testing processes.
  • Lead and/or oversee investigations and failure analyses related to product or process performance deficiencies to ensure definitive root causes are determined and corrective actions are implemented.
  • Collaborate with peers and direct management in resource planning and hiring activities.

  • B.S. in Engineering or related technical field.

  • 2+ years of project, program, or people leadership experience
  • Strong mechanical skills and exposure to a testing environment
  • Ability to network and negotiate at all levels within an organization to accomplish objectives.
  • Strong leadership, influence, and project management skills
  • Excellent communication skills – written and oral
  • Working knowledge of MS Office
  • Must be a U.S. Citizen.
