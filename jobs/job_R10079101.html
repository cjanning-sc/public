Join ACME Engineering on our continued mission to achieve excellence and provide world class expertise in the execution of Army Warfighter Exercises (WFX) executed across multi-domains including land, sea, air, space, and cyberspace. With our competitive pay and comprehensive benefits, we have the right opportunities to fit your life and launch your career today.

ACME Engineering Defense Systems sector is seeking a motivated professional to join our team as a Principal Military Trainer – Aviation Work Cell Instructor/Controller (WIC) in support of the Army’s Operations Group B, Mission Command Training Program (MCTP).  The selected candidate will join our growing team of diverse professionals at Fort Leavenworth, Kansas. The Principal Military Trainer – Aviation WIC will serve as an Aviation Warfighting Function (WfF) subject matter expert and supports tactical and operational aviation, and as required other WfFs, as a cell lead for a team of contractors supporting MCTP.

Responsibilities:

  • Trains military personnel on the Warfighter Simulation (WARSIM) and Aviation WfF specific simulation processes and procedures

  • Enforces realism of exercise through role playing and conflict adjudication and resolution

  • Supervises the work cell mission of translating training audience rehearsals, operations and plans into simulation inputs/tasking’s and scripting requirements

  • Trains and supports work cell augmentees in reporting, role playing elements subordinate to the training audience

  • Ensures that the work cells WfF units/assets are simulated IAW current doctrine and unit SOP

  • Supports the After Action Review (AAR) process by communicating significant events using the Workstation Reporting System (WRS)

  • Maintains awareness and understanding of current and emerging Army and Joint Aviation doctrine for Army Brigades, Divisions, and Corps

  • Works with the MCTP Observer/Coach/Trainers (O/C/T) toward a common understanding of Aviation force organization and structure

  • Performs additional duties as required:

    • Conducts exercise design and scenario development

    • Performs pre-exercise planning by conducting Site Surveys and participating in planning conferences

    • Serves as an Aviation WfF SME for the development of the simulation Rules and Workarounds book

    • Assists with certification training in the Aviation WfF

    • Maintains the WARSIM WfF Data Base

    • Serves as a point of contact for various additional administrative duty positions

Basic Qualifications:

  • One of the following:

    • Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 6 years of experience

    • Master’s degree with a minimum of 4 years of experience

  • Minimum Military Education Level (MEL) 4 CGSC or Warrant Officer or SGM’s equivalent

  • Graduate of a related Advanced Course

  • Proficient in current Army Aviation doctrine and synchronization processes

  • Proficient with MS Office suite of software (PowerPoint, Word, Excel, Access)

  • Familiarity with current Army and Joint Doctrine

  • Ability to work on-site at Fort Leavenworth, Kansas

  • Ability to travel as needed (up to 25% of the time)

  • Must currently possess an active U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) Secret security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Graduate of the Aviation Advanced Course

  • Experience as an Aviation Battalion/Brigade Operations Officer or Executive Officer

  • Graduate degree

  • Brigade or higher staff officer experience
