We are looking for a Systems Engineer in the Cedar Rapids, VA location.

The successful candidate for this position will work in a technical role with PGS’s Atmospheric Effects Group supporting the deployment of the Atmospheric Monitoring System (AMS) and the development of atmospheric models and prediction systems to help understand and mitigate atmospheric effects on optical communications in support of multiple NASA missions. This position will expose the candidate to equal parts technical competency, forward thinking ideas, and execution focus. The successful candidate will be responsible for: supporting the ongoing development of the Atmospheric Monitoring System (AMS); development of atmospheric models and tools using Machine Learning; interpreting large amounts of data with advanced approaches using sophisticated analytical and data visualization software and tools; communicating advanced concepts and information in an understandable manner; and maintaining an up-to-date knowledge of technology trends by attending and presenting at relevant professional conferences. 

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have U.S. Citizenship with ability to receive a Secret level clearance 
  • BS degree in Atmospheric Science or related technical field and at least 2 years of applicable experience, or an MS Degree and 0 years of applicable experience.
  • Proficient knowledge of Python
  • Experience working in a Linux environment
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Must be willing to work in person 

Preferred Qualifications (one or more areas desired):

  • Experience with NOAA GOES satellite data
  • Experience with Numerical Weather Prediction models
  • Experience with Machine Learning concepts
