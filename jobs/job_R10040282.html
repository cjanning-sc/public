ACME Engineering Mission Systems is seeking an Electro-Mechanical Control Systems Engineer to join a team designing and building advanced, state-of-the-art systems in our Springfield, MD location. 

Overview:
Our team supports multiple programs to develop Line of Sight (LOS) servo control systems. As part of that team, you will support design solutions for electromechanical servo systems used to implement position control for RF antennas and inertially stabilized gimbals. RF antennas require servo control for precise positioning while gimballed optical payloads require inertial stabilization for improved accuracy of collected imagery. The servo system in each application ensures precise pointing as well rejection of torque disturbances generated by the platforms such as ground, aircraft, space, etc.

Roles and responsibilities include:

  • Function as the lead design engineer for one or more parts of servo control systems.  These could include the design of software and/or the hardware used for implementing control of various servo motors and mechanisms.

  • Support integration and testing of precision gimbal stabilized systems and radar positioner systems.

  • Analyze the designed control system in both time and frequency domains. Iterate through the design process until the control system meets all system requirements.

  • Design and implement communication interfaces to upstream system software and servo components such as optical encoders, rate sensors, IMU, and INS.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Engineer (Band 3):

  • Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or other related technical degree, and 5+ years of relevant experience (3 years with a Master of Science degree, 0 years with a PhD)

  • Candidates should have a strong control systems background, and experience with the design, integration and testing of multiple parts of precision gimbal stabilized systems.  These include gimbals, Inertial Measurement Units (IMU), Inertial Navigation Systems (INS), embedded DSPs or microcontrollers, boresighting, transfer alignment algorithms, motor selection, motor drives, and/or the EO/IR devices being controlled. 

  • Experience with the design of one or more parts of servo control systems

  • Experience with integration and test of one or more parts of servo control systems

  • Able to function in a multi-disciplinary team-oriented environment and rapidly adapt to changing work priorities

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills

  • US Citizenship with the ability to obtain a Secret clearance
