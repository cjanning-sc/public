Northrop Grumman Corporation is seeking a Financial Analyst to work in the Corporate Accounting department with a focus on benefits accounting.  The financial analyst will report to the Corporate Manager of Benefits Accounting and will be responsible for the following accounting/reporting activities:

  • Assist with preparation of financial statement analytics and other management reports.
  • Prepare monthly Intercompany Accounting Transfers.
  • Prepare account reconciliations and research and resolve reconciling items.
  • Prepare weekly, monthly, and/or quarterly wires and funding entries and the related analytics
  • Assist with preparation of quarterly pension & OPEB roll forward for external auditors, Corporate Financial Reporting and Tax.
  • Assist in populating the Form 10-Q and 10-K footnote disclosures and the applicable MD&A language.
  • Assist with preparation of year-end financial reporting packages and tax packages for corporate entities.
  • Assist with SOX, DCAA and IRS audits.
  • Develop and maintain documentation to support statement of work. 

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in accounting or related field, and 3 years of relevant work experience; 1 year of relevant work experience with a master's degree. Master’s degree or CPA may be traded for 2 years of experience. Master’s degree and a CPA may be traded to a total of 3 years of experience. Public Accounting IF directly relevant may count for up to an additional year of experience for each year of relevant experience.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Public accounting firm experience
  • CPA or actively pursuing licensure
  • Prior experience with SAP and/or HFM
