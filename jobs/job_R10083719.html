ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking an DevSecOps Software Engineer. This position will be located in Detroit, Michigan and will support the Strategic Deterrent Systems Division to include the Sentinel (GBSD) program.

What You’ll Get to Do:

This position will support our program as a DevSecOps Software Engineer. In this role, the successful applicant will design, develop, document, test and debug applications software and systems that contain logical and mathematical solutions. Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Conduct multidisciplinary research and collaborate with equipment designers and/or hardware engineers in the planning, design, development, and utilization of electronic data processing systems for product and commercial software.
  • Determine computer user needs
  • Analyze system capabilities to resolve problems on program intent, output requirements, input data acquisition, programming techniques and controls.
  • Prepares operating instructions
  • Designs and develops compilers and assemblers, utility programs, and operating systems.
  • Ensures software standards are met. 
  • Participate in the standup and growth of multiple large scale software factory used by software team
  • Integration of continuous build, continuous integration, continuous test, and continuous deployment pipeline systems
  • Utilize strong communication skills to work with Software IPT, chief engineers, and development teams
  • Maintains smooth operation of multi-user systems, including coordination with network administrators. 
  • The selected candidate should thrive in an environment with, diverse assignments and collaborative/team settings across all levels
  • Support Agile Software/Hardware teams on pipeline builds, software installation, troubleshooting, and performance monitoring of the Software build *Integration*
