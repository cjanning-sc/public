ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking an Electrical Engineer to join the Command and Launch Team. This position is located in Detroit, MI and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will join the Launch Equipment (LE) team and support the development, production and deployment of LE hardware and systems for each weapon system facility. The selected candidate will work in a dynamic people-focused environment where he/she will interact with users, customers and other engineers.

This includes the following duties and responsibilities:

• Research, plan, design, analyze and test electrical components, equipment, systems, and networks

• Investigate electrical equipment, facilities, components, products, and systems

• Design printed wiring boards and circuit card assemblies

• Preparation and presentation of reports, briefings, design documents (drawings and specifications) and interface with customers.

• Work cooperatively with professionals representing multiple engineering and non-engineering discipline
