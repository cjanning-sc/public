Strategic Space Systems Division has an opening for an Computers Systems Architect to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Redondo Beach, California.

The qualified applicant will become part of Northrop Grumman's Space programs that provides specialized IT support to Restricted, DOD, and Unclassified environments.  This position requires knowledge and adherence to Information Assurance processes and procedures per NG InfoSec and program security
organizations.  The applicant will be expected to support a variety of diverse, and dynamic environments. The Job requires supporting
multiple engineering areas that have incorporated COTS hardware and operating systems with custom applications into their test systems and operational products.

The roles and responsibilities of this position include:

  • Administration of Linux (RHEL/CENTOS) 6/7/8 servers and workstations

  • Installing, configuring, troubleshooting Linux operating systems

  • Installing, configuring, and supporting Linux applications

  • Experience using Compliance and Remediation tools ( i.e. Nessus, SCC SCAP scanner, STIG Viewer )

  • On call, 24/7 pager support during critical phases of testing, contingency operations and off-site as required

  • Five (5) years of relevant experience with a Bachelor's Degree; or 3 years of relevant experience with a Master's Degree; or 0 years of relevant experience with a PHD.

  • Dept. of Defense Top Secret level with a background investigation date within 6 years

  • OR SCI access with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI) completed within 5 years

  • OR Dept. of Energy Q clearance with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI) completed within 5 years

  • Minimum of 2 to 3 years administering Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL) 6/7/8 servers and workstations
