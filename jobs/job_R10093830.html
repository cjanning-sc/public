The Manufacturing Operations Project Management (OPM) homeroom has an opening for an OPM Manager 1, “G/ATOR OPM M1” Supporting Maritime / Land Systems & Sensors Division

The OPM Manager position is both a functional management position with direct reports and a Project Leadership position. The Manufacturing Operations Project Managers are the interface between the Business Division and Manufacturing. This position is responsible for schedule management and cost accountability of Manufacturing activities for this Business Division.

Responsibilities Include:

  • Manage the production execution for Business Unit programs
  • Manage Manufacturing cost and schedule performance
  • Serve as Manufacturing lead for all proposals in the Business Unit
  • Create and lead teams to resolve the issues adversely impacting the manufacturing execution of the programs
  • Understand and report on Manufacturing Operations status at the required level of detail to the Program Manager, Sector Executive Management and customers
  • Ensure Manufacturing has the needed resources (resources Manufacturing needs as well as allocation of resources from Manufacturing) to execute the programs
  • Serve as Functional Manager and direct the work of your reporting Operations Project Managers
  • Perform manpower planning and execute hiring to support the Business Division
  • Mentor, develop, and assess direct reports
  • Provide strategic leadership to ensure all Program and Manufacturing objectives are achieved
  • Provide Manufacturing expertise to support the strategic vision of the Business Division

The chosen candidate must have demonstrated ability in working with cross organizational teams and customers; the ability to set and clearly articulate goals/priorities and successfully communicate them to key staff; strong leadership skills; and a track record of building high-performance teams, with a particular focus on employee development, engagement, inclusion, and retention.

TAGS: MANUMS

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor's degree and 5 years of relevant experience (3 years with a Master's degree)
  • Experience leading people and/or projects
  • Experience working in Manufacturing, Engineering, Test, Production, Manufacturing or Industrial Engineering, Project Engineering, IPT, Operations Project Management (OPM), or similar roles
  • Working knowledge of SAP and Material Requirements Planning (MRP) systems
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Secret clearance. This requires US citizenship.

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field
  • Master’s Degree
  • Demonstrated expertise in the following systems and concepts: - MRP and IMS Scheduling - SAP production planning and material management tools - ISE/CADSTAR - Earned Value Management - Continuous Improvement, Lean Manufacturing and Manufacturing
  • Experience leading Manufacturing proposal efforts that include labor quoting, schedule and capacity analysis, investment planning, and descriptive writing contributions
  • Excellent presentation and communication skills
  • Program execution experience
  • A current Secret clearance
