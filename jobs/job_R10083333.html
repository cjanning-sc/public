The Tactical Space System Mechanical Engineering organization is seeking a a senior level Staff Mechanical/Structural Analysis Engineer to assist an experienced team of mechanical engineers performing mechanical and structural analysis on one or more spacecraft systems or subsystems.

Responsibilities may include the following:

  • Performing static and dynamic structure analysis.

  • Reviewing requirements for materials, structure systems & subsystems.

  • Selecting appropriate technical information and data from applicable products.

  • Determining the technical principles involved, identifying the design path forward and computing the engineering characteristics to ensure consistency with the specifications design needs and requirements of space applications.

  • Releasing results as “technical documents” after review by the senior members of the team.

  • Developing innovative solutions and elaborating on design concepts.

  • Evaluating completed designs with supervisor and other technical groups, and making modifications necessary to achieve the most ideal design. 

  • Developing, coordinating, and conducting test programs required to evaluate design work.

Basic Qualifications for Staff Mechanical/Structural Engineer 5:

  • Bachelor’s degree in STEM - Science, Technology, Engineer, Math or related area (Mechanical Engineering or Degrees with a focus in Structural Analysis highly preferred) and 14+ years of experience or Master’s Degree and 12+ years; 9 years of experience with PhD.

  • Experience performing structural engineering tasks associated with analysis, design, and/or testing of structures.

  • Experience with at least one of the following Mechanical Analysis tools such as NX FEMAP/NASTRAN, ABAQUS, HYPERMESH. (Equivalent spacecraft structural analysis tools may be considered on a case by case basis).

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with spacecraft or subsystem static/dynamic testing

  • Experience utilizing MATLAB
