NGMS is seeking a Digital Engineer with the desire to learn new technologies to join our innovative Digital Technologies Organization to help develop, enhance and maintain FPGA and/or ASIC designs on cutting edge products and systems. As a Digital Engineer at ACME Engineering you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation. The selected individual will work on FPGA and ASIC Design across the full product life cycle process. In this capacity, you will utilize your working knowledge of digital signal processing and digital interfaces.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science (BS) degree in science, engineering, or related discipline with 2 years of experience; or 0 years of relevant experience with a Masters (MS) degree;

  • Working knowledge of full product life cycle (requirements, design, implementation and test) of FPGA Design and/or ASIC Design.

  • Knowledge of System Verilog and/or VHDL.

  • Clearance: Must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain and maintain a Top Secret clearance.

Preferred Qualifications include experience with any of the following:

  • Industry standard FPGA design implementation tools for IP integration, PnR, CDC such as Xilinx Vivado, Intel Quartus, and QuestaSim.

  • Industry standard ASIC front-end design tools for synthesis, LEC, CDC

  • STA constraints generation and timing closure

  • MATLAB, Mentor Graphics design tools, Synopsys or similar tool

  • Xilinx and Intel FPGA technology

  • Active Top Secret security clearance with SCI
