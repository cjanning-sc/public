Sr. / Principal RF Electrical Engineer (Top Secret) - Space / Satellite Systems (Des Moines, IA)
R10077751
Days

We have an opening for an RF Electrical Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in the Tactical Space organization. This position will be located in Des Moines, IA.

In this role, you will work as an RF/Analog design engineer developing SDR and other components for satellite communications applications. As a senior principal engineer, you will have a senior role in a small team to define and design robust RF solutions while working within system constraints on mass, power, and volume.

To be successful you should have:

  • Strong technical skills coupled with the ability to operate within a fast-paced environment
  • Ability to work with others from engineering and non-engineering disciplines in a fast-paced team environment
  • Good oral presentation skills and comfort with customer interaction. Ability to write technical documents clearly and concisely
  • Well organized and able to keep track of and make progress on multiple simultaneous tasks
  • Strong skills in Microsoft Office (particularly Excel, Word and PowerPoint)

In particular, you will be involved in:

  • Design of software defined radio (SDR) and other RF components for satellite communication systems
  • Design and analysis of communications systems for satellite and interplanetary applications. You must be familiar with RF analyses such as link budgets, digital modulation/coding, gain/loss budgets, noise figure, etc.
  • Requirements flow-down from top-level customer document through subsystem-level definition to component-level specification. You will be expected to produce clear, concisely written documents that will be used as the technical basis for high dollar value procurements
  • Prepare and present design reviews for internal and external customers. You will have regular interaction with the internal engineering and program management team as well as the external customer
  • Requirements verification based on customer’s top-level requirements compared to subcontractor and prime contractor actual implementation. You will need to be able to concisely summarize findings and present them to the end customer
  • Subcontractor management (some travel required) and manufacturing interface definition including performance specification, interface control drawing, and statement of work generation. You will work with potential subcontractors to ensure any technical solution meets the top-level customer's needs while satisfying system and program constraints
  • Support integration and test of the communications system including unit-level functional and environmental testing, spacecraft level testing, and in-orbit checkout (some travel and occasional shift work required)

This position can be filled at the Principal or Sr. Principal level.

Basic Qualifications:

Principal Engineer

  • BSEE (or related field) with some combination of the following course work: microwave theory, satellite communications, radio-wave propagation, electromagnetics, antennas. Good understanding of communications and microwave theory
  • 5 years of experience with a Bachelors; or 3 years with a Masters; or a PhD
  • Experience with RF circuit card design of software defined Radios
  • Experience with performing RF analysis and trade studies to meet RF performance parameters including (Gain, noise figure, Intercept point, stability, power consumption, etc).
  • Hands on experience with RF test equipment such as spectrum analyzers, signal generators, oscilloscopes, digital multimeters, arbitrary waveform generators, etc.
  • Experience with digital waveforms such as WCDMA, BPSK, QPSK, and DVB-SX. Additionally, experience with and understanding of RF designs and validation test to support these waveforms
  • Experience with RF simulation tools such as Microwave office, ADS, or other simulation tools
  • US Citizenship with an active Top Secret security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • MSEE  (or related field) a strong plus
  • TS/SCI security clearance
  • Experience leading small engineering teams in development or production of RF systems
  • Familiar with engineering analysis tools such as MATLAB or Mathcad, and support tools such as Visual Basic for Applications
  • Experience with cost account management for engineering portion of multi-year program, including cost and labor hour estimating, task scheduling and earned value tracking
  • Experience or understanding of antenna design
  • Experience with HFSS
  • Experience with analog design of various circuits such as Opamps, regulators, feedback loops, power supply, etc.

The ACME Engineering Tactical Space Division is a strategic partner specializing in commercial and classified partnerships with the design, delivery, operation and sustainment of satellites and human spacecraft.  We support science and space exploration through our various partnerships, including NASA’s Artemis program with the goal to return humans to the Moon in 2024 and the TESS (Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite) program that has discovered more than twenty confirmed plants.  Recognized as an industry leader, we also develop highly specialized space and satellite components.
