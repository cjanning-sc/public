The eQube Developer researches, designs, develops, and/or modifies enterprise-wide interfaces and reports using eQ Technologic DaaS tools. Involved in the planning of system and development deployments as well as responsible for adhering to software compliance standards. Evaluates interfaces between systems, provides rough order of magnitude estimates, and supports all required operational requirements. Documents DaaS solutions, test use cases, and all required reporting/status updates.

Primary accountabilities are associated with eQube reporting (BI) and integration (MI) development, guidance, and support for ACME Engineering business units. Performs developmental plus support for all DaaS eQube solutions for enterprise programs and projects. Ensures proactive focus on details of complex solutions to ensure highly successful implementations. Able to effectively deal with challenges from the base technical perspective of high complexity. Engage with all Teamcenter eQube planning activities, as required.

Responsibilities include:

  • End-user support for eQube BI/MI solutions.
  • Planning, development, code reviews, testing, implementation, and knowledge sharing of eQube BI/MI solution customizations and upgrades.
  • Root cause analysis, debugging problems with the eQube BI/MI solutions and working with the appropriate technical teams to develop, test, and implement fixes.
  • Monitor, address, and resolve production eQube BI/MI solution incidents, reports, interfaces, collaboratively with the other development and administration teams.
  • Support System Integration Testing and User Acceptance Testing activities with business partners and end users, as required.
  • Support the mentoring, training, and/or demos to end-users, as required.
  • Support business readiness and change management activities through go-live and production implementations.
  • Involved in planning of system and development deployments, as well as responsible for meeting software compliance standards.
  • Develop and maintain technical, installation guidelines, and operational guideline documents.
  • Primary locations Columbus CA., Detroit UT., and Philadelphia FL.
