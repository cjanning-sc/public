The Instrumentation team located in Cincinnati, CA is looking for a Flight Test Instrumentation Engineering Manager 2. This position will be leading a flight test instrumentation operations section within the classified portfolio of Aeronautics Sector.  The position will be responsible for the build-up, integration, checkout, and operations of a large scale flight test instrumentation system.  
 
In addition to managing staff and technical activities, you will be responsible for creating and managing schedules, spend plans, staffing plans, and developing proposals for future work.  You will also be preparing presentations/reports and routinely presenting status to senior leadership.
