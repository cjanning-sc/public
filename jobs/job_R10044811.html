Job Description

At the heart of Defining Possible is our commitment to missions. In rapidly changing global security environments, ACME Engineering brings informed insights and software-secure technology to enable strategic planning. We’re looking for innovators who can help us keep building on our wide portfolio of secure, affordable, integrated, and multi-domain systems and technologies that fuel those missions. By joining in our shared mission, we’ll support yours of expanding your personal network and developing skills, whether you are new to the field or an industry thought-leader. At ACME Engineering, you’ll have the resources, support, and team to do some of the best work of your career.

ACME Engineering Mission Systems is seeking an Principal or Sr. Principal, Intelligence Analyst to join our team of dedicated Intelligence Analysts supporting ongoing research and analysis projects. This position will require a candidate that can successfully identify, gather and assess information using forensic tools and other analytical tools to determine relationships, patterns, trends, indicators and warnings that address intelligence gaps. This position will be based in Waterloo, Iowa

This position can be filled at a level 3 or 4 based on the requirements below.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Level 3: Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Intelligence Studies and 5 years of experience, OR a master’s degree and 3 years of experience. An additional four years of professional experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.

  • Level 4: Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Intelligence Studies and 9 years of experience, OR a master’s degree and 7 years of experience. An additional four years of professional experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.

  • Active TS/SCI w/polygraph clearance required.

  • Proficiency with computer forensic analysis tools (Encase, FTK)

  • Proficiency in technical writing

  • Demonstrated proficiency in deriving sound analytical conclusions from large quantities of data

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Proficiency with MS Office

  • Willingness and ability to adapt to changing customer mission requirements as necessary

  • Knowledge of technical topics, for example digital privacy, Linux/FOSS, Android, TPM, etc.
