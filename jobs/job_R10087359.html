Northrop Grumman Defense Systems (NGDS) is seeking a Manager 2, Capture Management. This individual will lead capture-related activities for small- to medium-sized programs with a focus on satellite ground station. The successful candidate will have a chance to lead some of the most exciting new capture opportunities in the areas of human and scientific exploration, space mobility and logistics, and advanced telecommunications.

This individual will work closely with Northrop Grumman’s leadership team and other industry and government partners to run all aspects of his/her assigned pursuits. Specific areas of focus include market research and data analytics, customer relationship management and shaping, and capture strategy development and execution.

Capture Leadership

  • Market research and data analytics
  • Opportunity identification and qualification
  • Pipeline data analytics
  • Business case analysis
  • Customer relationship management and shaping
  • Customer acquisition approach analysis and shaping
  • Marketing plan development and execution
  • Professional relationship development and maintenance with key decision makers
  • Government Relations collaboration on customer engagement strategies and execution
  • Future customer requirements analysis and planning
  • Capture strategy development and execution
  • Customer and competitor assessments
  • Congressional budgetary and appropriation analysis
  • Price-to-Win analysis
  • Offer design
  • Workforce planning
  • Teammate/supplier selection and relationship management
  • Threat identification and mitigation
  • Internal briefings to the NGDefense leadership team

Ethics

Preferred Qualifications:
