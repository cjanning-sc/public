Northrop Grumman is preparing to partner with the 705 Combat Training Squadron (CTS) Infrastructure, Development and Engineering (IDE) program to develop and maintain simulation software (SW) and hardware (HW) solutions, build, and maintain network infrastructure, design, and develop simulation SW applications, and evolve and conduct Live-Virtual-Constructive (LVC) Distributed Mission Operations (DMO) activities for the 705 CTS and Distributed Mission Operations Center (DMOC).

Northrop Grumman Defense Systems (NGDS) is seeking a part-time Logistics Support (level 2) to join our diverse team of professionals located in Kirtland AFB Albuquerque, NM.

As a Logistics Support member, you will:

  • Provide support preparing logistics documentation to establish the range and depth of spares, tools, test equipment and related manuals to be deployed and stocked.
  • Determine maintenance and repair materials required for deployment
  • Modify maintenance records such as repair standards for more complex equipment or components
  • Develop requisitions for and manage records of equipment, components, repair parts and related manuals
  • Track logistics materials and parts for proper allocation of storage facilities
  • Review configuration changes to evaluate impact on logistics deployment
  • Document equipment, component, and parts conditions
  • Identify logistic support documentation to be included such as drawings, technical manuals, and maintenance requirement cards to support life cycle of products

 Basic Qualifications:

  • High School diploma or equivalent and 2 years additional education and/or related experience.
  • Experience in warehousing, packaging, handling, shipping & receiving
  • Must be able to operate and maintain standard warehouse equipment/vehicles (e.g., forklift, warehouse tug, and bobtail)
  • Experience using MS Excel, Word, PowerPoint required
  • Must have an active Secret DoD clearance with ability to maintain that clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Must be willing to work a flexible work schedule that may include extended shifts, holidays, and weekends and support on call status for 24 hours-a-day, 7 days-a-week, 365 days a year
