The Structures and Acoustics Group of Northrop Grumman Marine Systems (NGMS) is looking for you to join our team based out of Sunnyvale, CA.

What You’ll get to Do:

  • Develop component and system level finite element models using Hypermesh and post process analysis results using Hyperview, Matlab and Excel.  
  • Perform linear finite element static, vibration, modal and direct frequency response, and response spectra analyses using Nastran and Abaqus.
  • Perform boundary element and statistical energy analysis based vibro-acoustic simulations using SimCenter (LMS Virtual Lab) and Wave6.
  • Collect, validate, and analyze acoustic test data
  • Develop / apply noise mitigation technologies

Basic Qualifications:

  • Experience working in a structural dynamics, noise, or acoustic engineering role
  • Demonstrated skills communicating technical and/or programmatic ideas and information both to peers and up to engineering management and manufacturing customers
  • Experience in performing a variety of classical and analysis software solution types. 
  • US citizen with the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret security clearance.

You’ll Bring These Qualifications for the Principal Structural Engineer role:

  • Bachelors of Science in a Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics (STEM) discipline with 5 or more years applicable experience; or a Masters of Science in relevant engineering discipline with 3 or more years applicable experience.

You’ll Bring These Qualifications for the Sr. Principal Structural Engineer role:

  • Experience working in a manufacturing support engineering role
  • Bachelor's degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics (STEM) discipline with nine or more years applicable experience; Master's degree in a STEM discipline with seven or more years applicable experience. 
  • Demonstrated skills communicating technical and/or programmatic ideas and information both down to less experienced team members and up to engineering management and manufacturing customers.
  • Experience in performing a wide variety of classical and finite element analysis solution types. 
  • US citizen with the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret security clearance.

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

  • Experience with the following software tools: NX, Hypermesh, Nastran, Abaqus, Matlab. SimCenter (LMS Virtual Lab), Wave6, Romax. 
  • Experience supporting a US government customer. 
  • Engineering experience with complex systems, vehicles, or machinery.
