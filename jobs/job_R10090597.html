Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems sector is currently hiring an Engineering Manager 2 (Integrated Product Team Lead) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals within our Product Support directorate. This leadership role can be in either Palmdale, CA; El Segundo, CA; or Rancho Bernardo, CA.

Notable mentions:

  • The primary objective will be the delivery of all contractual requirements on cost and on schedule, while achieving all technical requirements.

  • The individual will lead a matrixed cross-functional team aligned to common program performance goals through program baselining to completion of scope.

  • This position requires a comprehensive understanding of restricted and DoD customer communities, program management skills, proven strategic planning and business execution capabilities, as well as the ability to work collaboratively across the enterprise.

  • The selected candidate must have a comprehensive knowledge of program management standards, policies, and guidelines. The selected individual must be able to interact with and influence executive level management and customer’s and communicate effectively with both oral and written presentations.

Key Program Responsibilities:

  • Development of Customer and stakeholder relationships and cultivation of Customer intimacy

  • Adherence to all internal processes for Program Management

  • Manage and monitor the work of the various functional organizations supporting the Program

  • Development and adherence to budget baselines

  • Establish, manage, and track milestones, product roadmaps, inter- and intra-program dependencies, and ensure adherence to master plans and schedules.

  • Development and delivery of presentations to Customers, leadership, and program stakeholders

  • Support the various internal and external program reviews throughout the organization.

  • Conduct regular meetings with team to discuss issues, concerns, and resolution

  • Effectively coordinate and collaborate with functional and Segment leadership to ensure required staffing, processes, tools, training, infrastructure, etc. are provided to support successful project execution. 

  • Build and sustain an effective, engaged, and integrated team in a complex, geographically dispersed, and dynamic environment.

  • Work in compliance with company policies and procedures

Key Engineering Responsibilities:

Tech Data and Training IPT Lead

  • Lead development and sustainment support activities with responsibilities including, but not limited to, achieving customer vision of having safe, accurate, timely and user-friendly TD&T products with production, storage, and distribution concepts and processes compatible with the Northrop Grumman architecture for digital technical data

As a leader, you always act with integrity, and the highest ethical standards characterize everything you do. You enable your team to achieve predictable and balanced results, while satisfying the needs of internal and external partners. You treat all people with respect, and you model personal growth and continuous development. You know how to build an inclusive environment that attracts, retains, and inspires a diverse and engaged team. You develop trusted and valued customer relationships and communicate effectively, so that people throughout the organization feel engaged and connected to their work. You deliver excellence, strive for continuous improvement, and respond vigorously to change.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have a Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM discipline AND 9 years of related professional/military experience that includes a background in engineering, program management, or related field involving government programs, OR a Master of Science degree in a STEM discipline AND 7 years of related professional/military experience that includes a background in engineering, program management, or related field involving government programs.

  • At least 1 year of team lead and/or management experience.

  • Strong understanding of technical, cost, and schedule impacts on a program.

  • At least 3 years’ experience with Earned Value Management processes.

  • An active DoD Top Secret clearance that has been granted or renewed within the last 6 years or is enrolled in continuous evaluation.

  • Ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access (SAP).

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Demonstrated ability to create trusted customer relationships

  • Experience leading the performance of tasks on schedule, at cost and achieving all requirements as either project lead, integrated program team or cost account manager.

  • Ability to lead complex and geographically dispersed program teams comprised of engineering, business, contracts, supply chain, and mission/quality assurance

  • Experience managing suppliers and/or subcontractors.

  • Excellent interpersonal skills and a collaborative management style

  • Excellent oral, written, and electronic communication skills

  • Proven ability to effectively work across multiple organizations to form teams, gain internal support, act with speed, and make tough decisions

  • Open to direction and collaborative work style; commitment to get the job completed

  • Experience with Jira and Confluence tools

  • Experience with MBSE and Agile methodology, preferably in a lead role

  • Prior clearance to a Special Access Program (SAP) or Special Compartmentalized Information (SCI)
