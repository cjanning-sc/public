The Aeronautics Systems - Sector Cost Management Financial Team is looking for an energetic and highly seasoned financial analyst to join the team. The Sector Financial team directly supports the AS Cost & Schedule Management Divisions and Programs supporting common tools and processes along with being a direct advisor.  This is a remote opportunity.

Essential Functions:

  • Develops, maintains, and updates common processes; supports the development of tools directly related to the Cost Management Financial Teams
  • Effectively liaise with stakeholders across multiple functions, particularly communicating financial information to a broad audience with differing needs
  • Leads, coordinates, facilitates special projects related to financial functions
  • Develops and provides interactive and hands-on and training to the Cost Management Finance teams
  • Acts as an advisor to the Cost Management Finance Teams; provides guidance, problem solves, and conducts in-depth research on complex financial related topics
  • Briefs financial related topics, recommendations, and solutions at multiple levels (CFOs, VPs, Controllers, Peers)
  • Coordinates and supports audits between Sector and the Divisions (SOX, Internal Corporate Audits, etc.)

Basic Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree with 7 years’ experience or Bachelor’s Degree with 9 years' proven experience in business, finance, accounting, program control and/or similar industry related fields
  • Experience with financial forecasting and modeling related to the key financial planning metrics: awards/backlog, income statement, balance sheet, statement of cash flows
  • Understands the financial estimates at complete / profitability package process
  • Understanding of labor accounts (Operations/Manufacturing and Engineering), subcontract management, and general procurement
  • Advanced excel skills to support the development of complex tools and reports for analysis and deep dives

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 3+ years experience in financial forecasting and modeling related to the key financial planning metrics: awards/backlog, income statement, balance sheet, statement of cash flows
  • 3+ years experience developing Long Range Strategic Planning (5 Year planning) related to the key financial planning metrics
  • Experience in developing and reviewing financial estimates at complete / quarterly profitability packages
  • Experience in Earned Value Management and working with external customer reports (IMPR/IPMDAR, Contracts funds status reports, and other cost/financial customer deliverables)
  • Experience with budget, rates, and overhead management
  • Basic understanding of taxes
  • Detailed orientated with ability to spot financial trends and anomalies
  • Ability to perform under minimal direct guidance and achieve job objectives with significant degree of independence
  • Excellent written and communication, interpersonal, and diplomatic skills
  • Experience in SAP and/or Oracle Hyperion Planning/Essbase for Financial forecasting (IFAS), Hyperion Financial Management (HFM)
