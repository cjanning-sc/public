Sr Principal Supply Chain Planning Specialist

1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking a Sr Principal Supply Chain Planning Specialist to join our F-35 Radar Production Operating Unit (OU). This position will be based in Baltimore, MD or remote or hybrid work can be considered.

Specific duties to include but are not limited to:

Defines and develops material strategy for active production contracts. Participates in make/buy process, long-term capacity, and resource planning; product phase-in/phase-out, manufacturing ramp-up, end-of-life management and product-line management. Analyzes and aggregates supply / demand requirements with support from MRP (focusing on On-Time Requisition release).  Analyzes purchase material supply/demand position using Material Requirements Planning (MRP) tools. Plans inventory levels based on dependent and independent Bill-of-Materials (BOM) requirements. Assesses rough-cut capacity for current and short-term manufacturing requirements. Creates/reduces supply requirements based on inventory adjustments. Participates in long-term capacity and resource planning to ensure proper scheduling of new product line phase-ins as well as existing product line phase-outs. Interfaces with other Supply Chain functions and Manufacturing leads as needed. Creates/releases requisitions. Receives/reviews requirements for non-standard parts. Analyzes lead times and identifies critical path procurements. Leads and supports Supply Chain Program execution and supports proposal activity.  Provides updates and presents critical information to functional areas, managers, and directors on the status and issues affecting program cost and schedule performance. Recognizes and communicates subcontract issues; collaborates with Subcontracts and Category Management leads; facilitates meetings with suppliers to ensure areas of concern are being addressed and performance issues resolved.

Basic Qualifications for Sr. Principal Supply Chain Planning Specialist (SCPS) 4:

  • Bachelor’s degree and 10+ years of supply chain, business management, finance and/or business experience, or a Master's Degree and 8+ years of supply chain, business management, finance and/or business experience (e.g., cost account management, supplier financial reporting/forecasting and supplier/program/product scheduling).
  • U.S. Citizenship is required with the ability to obtain a Department of Defense (DoD) Secret Clearance
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word)
  • Demonstrate success in managing complex problems and driving solutions related to program execution

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s Degree in Business, Supply Chain or Project Management
  • Knowledge of Earned Value Management System (EVMS)
  • Previous Control Account Manager (CAM) experience or training
  • Risk mitigation experience and Demonstrated leadership skills
