Propulsion Systems Group is recruiting for an Electronic Technician 03 to join our team at the Promontory, Utah facility. 

Role description & Responsibilities:

An electronics maintenance technician is responsible for the repair, modification and maintenance of a variety of electrical and electronic equipment. The technician is required to perform tasks like wiring circuits, replacing components and repairing electronic devices. The technician performs routine electronic preventative maintenance on plant equipment including x-ray systems, CNC machines, auto claves, overhead bridge cranes, various PLC controlled systems, etc. An electronic maintenance technician also performs test readings using several different instruments, including semiconductor testers, curve tracers, oscilloscopes, signal generators and digital multimeters.

  • Maintenance activities to troubleshoot and repair equipment malfunctions, conducts preventative maintenance to prevent equipment failures and reduce downtime

Basic Requirements:

  • H.S diploma or equivalent and 4+ years working in industry as an electronic technician

  • Valid driver license

  • Working on equipment, including climbing ladders to access equipment on roofs in all weather, using man lifts and scissor lifts when necessary

Education/Certifications

  • H.S. diploma or equivalent and 4 years additional education and/or related experience or equivalent training in Electronics Technology

This position may be filled at a higher level requiring a High School diploma or equivalent and 6 years additional education and/or related experience or equivalent training in Electronic Technology

Preferred skills:

  • Must be able to understand and follow electrical schematics proficiently without supervision

  • Familiar with machine control systems

  • Familiar with AC/DC drives and servo systems

  • Ability to troubleshoot (complex) machine control systems including PLCs 

  • Experience working with 480 Volt / 3 phase equipment

  • Retrofit obsolete components and systems including controls

  • Familiarity of NDT systems, CNC machines, process/instrumentation controls, camera systems and fire alarm systems

  • Knowledge and experience using oscilloscopes and various high voltage measurement test equipment

  • Ability to interpret PLC logic as it relates to its application to process and equipment for troubleshooting purposes
