ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Process Engineer to join the Rocket Motor Case Engineering team in Lansing, Michigan.

This is a swing shift position.

Role Description & Responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain detailed work instructions for assigned products/processes

  • Provide day to day support of manufacturing personnel

  • Coordinates with Design Engineering on design and manufacturability issues

  • Coordinates with production on day to day activities. Assists in the resolution of processing issues

  • Evaluate and aid in the disposition of discrepant hardware

  • Troubleshoots existing processes to improve safety, quality, cost, and efficiency

  • Analyze processing data to highlight trends and implement corrective actions

  • Maintains manufacturing bill of materials (MBOM)

Basic Qualifications:

  • Good organizational skills and ability to work with minimal direction

  • Excellent computer skills. Skilled in working with Microsoft Office applications and business database systems

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills

  • Strong problem solving skills

  • Ability to build strong, working relationships with operations personnel

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience in working with aerospace specifications and drawings, both hard copy and model based

  • Experience in working with composite materials

  • Experience with insulation / rubber processes

  • Experience with composite winding processes

  • Experience with machining processes

  • Experience with hardware installation processes

  • Experience supporting an aerospace manufacturing process with the necessary materials, tooling, and equipment

  • Experience authoring, modifying, and auditing manufacturing procedures

  • Knowledge of root cause / corrective action processes and techniques

Education/Certifications

  • B.S. degree in Mechanical, Aerospace, Manufacturing, Chemical Engineering or related engineering degree; 2-years or more of applicable experience with Bachelor’s or 0-years with Master’s degree.

  • Position may be filled at a  higher level requiring the following:

    • B.S. degree in Mechanical, Aerospace, Manufacturing, Chemical Engineering or related engineering degree; 5-years or more of applicable experience with Bachelor’s or 3-years with Master’s degree.
