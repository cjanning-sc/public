ACME Engineering Mission Systems is seeking Software Engineers to be responsible for database engineering, visualizations and analytics as well as creation and support of computer-based tools used in Manufacturing. This position will involve internal interfacing and customer service, requirements documentation, coding, design and validation testing in SQL, and Visualization platforms with occasional C#. Develop, upgrade and maintain database driven applications in a client/server environment and support various graphical user interfaces. Maintain and enhance a web hosted application and support MS Office forms using visual basic for applications. Extensive contacts with internal personnel at factory floor, peer and various management levels concerning manufacturing operations. Presents and performs other related tasks as assigned.


We are interested in highly motivated candidates with great interpersonal communication skills and a willingness to learn.


Primary responsibilities will include but are not limited to:

  • Interfacing with customers to document needs and turn them into finished Tableau dashboards or C#/ASP.net and/or windows forms applications
  • Creating data-driven tools that simplify efforts related to a wide variety of functional areas
  • Connecting to, analyzing, and visualizing information from several disparate data sets in support of providing greater visibility on various areas of program performance
  • Automating reports and processes through software development
  • Must possess strong organizational and interpersonal skills to communicate effectively verbally and in writing and be able to observe and connect physical practices on the manufacturing floor with the data available in the system
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills.
  • Creating, upgrading and maintaining software that may interface with equipment and with graphical user interfaces in multiple languages
