Northrop has an opening for a Manufacturing Expeditor 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals.

Northrop Grumman Space Systems Sector is seeking a Manufacturing Expeditor (Area Coordinator) to join our Customer Support and Logistics (CS&L) team.  The Area Coordinator serves as the primary customer interface of the CSL section, as well as the Engineering Asset Management (EAM) department, executing recall and disbursement of Measuring and Test Equipment (M&TE).  Working daily with internal customers, strategic organizational partners, external vendors/suppliers and Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM), the Area Coordinator ensures equipment is managed and transported safely, in the most effective manner possible, between customers/end-users and metrology services activities.

The selected candidate’s responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Expediting, routing, and dispatching test equipment, materials and ancillary components in support of specified Areas of Operation within their purview

  • Responsible for test equipment accountability, control, pick-up and delivery to and from end-users (customers)

  • Consistent communication with customers for status and recall of test equipment and ancillary components

  • Coordination of pick-up/deliveries and all applicable processing of and database updates in the Test Equipment Asset Management and Metrology System (TEAMMS), Calibration Management System

  • Accountable for reviewing and managing workload based on daily Coordinator Performance Reports, ensuring performance metrics remain within specific thresholds

  • Maintain excellent data integrity via inventory reconciliation of equipment between TEAMMS database, Pool/Stock Inventory and customer Areas of Operation

Basic Qualifications:

  • H/S Diploma or equivalent and Associate of Arts (AA) or other 2-year technical degree; 1-2 years of relevant experience considered in lieu of AA or technical degree

  • Ability to frequently move and position/reposition objects weighing up to 50lbs

  • Military Logistics, Maintenance Management or Aerospace industry inventory and/or Supply Support experience required, e.g. warehousing, stockroom, customer service support

  • Valid California State Driver’s License

  • Willingness to change physical work locations based on operational need


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current/Active SCI Clearance

  • Metrology and/or Calibration (M&TE) experience

  • Aerospace and Defense industry experience

  • Military Occupational Specialty (MOS) classification codes: USMC-0431 or 0411 series; USAAF-2S0 or SC0 series, USA 92 or 91 series
