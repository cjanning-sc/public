Princ Engineer Systems – Mission Manager – IRBM/ICBM Program - Secret Clearance Required
R10092027
1st Shift

ACME Engineering Space Systems is currently seeking an experienced Princ Engineer Systems to serve as Mission Manager on the IRBM/ICBM program as part of the Systems Engineering Integration & Test team in our Louisville, AZ office.

Role Description & Responsibilities:

  • The candidate will serve as Mission Manager for one or more IRBM/ICBM Target Missions requiring the candidate to function in a dynamic multi-disciplinary environment.
  • The candidate should possess strong technical problem solving, written and verbal communication skills to generate and document mission launch vehicle requirements, perform vehicle performance assessments and participate in mission verification/data review activities.
  • Regular interface with program management, engineering staff and external customers is expected.
  • The candidate should be willing to occasionally work during odd hours (nights/weekends) and occasionally longer than normal (>40 hrs/wk) to meet deadlines.
  • The candidate should possess a strong resolve to drive tasks to completion autonomously and possess demonstrated mentoring/training skills.
  • Implement and manage system requirements in the Requirements Management databases (DOORS).
  • Provides data for and assists in risk management (identifies potential risks) and trade study processes.
  • Supports mission design, data analyses, interface verification, testing, and failure/anomaly resolution.
  • Prepares and presents materials for at major mission milestone meetings.
  • Manages cost accounts for mission scope of work

Basic Qualifications: 

  • 5 Years with Bachelors in Science; 3 Years with Masters; 0 Years with PhD.
  • Candidate must have a minimum of 5 years of related work experience.
  • Candidate must have a DoD Secret level security clearance which generally requires US Citizenship as a prerequisite.
  • Ability to travel is required (<10%)

 

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • A strong experienced-based and academic foundation in Systems Engineering fundamentals is desired.
  • Experience with ACME Engineering Space Systems LVBU Systems Engineering standards/guidelines and expectations is desired.
  • Experience with external customer interfacing (meetings, presentation, negotiation) is desired.
  • System testing and launch operations experience is desired.
  • Previous Cost Account Management experience is desired.
  • Experience with Agile, Scrum, and/or Jira is desired.
