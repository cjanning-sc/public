ACME Engineering, Strategic Space Systems Division, has an opening for a Principal Engineer Test to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Columbus, Ohio.

Responsibilities:

Designs, develops, and implements testing methods for Cryocooler Manufacturing and Production. Plans and arranges the labor, schedules, and equipment required for testing and evaluating standard and special devices. Provides test area with parameters for sample testing and specifies tests to be performed. Compiles data and defines changes required in testing equipment, testing procedures, manufacturing processes, or new testing requirements. Document and disposition hardware discrepancies and pursuing root cause and corrective actions. Responsible for testing all customer samples and for special tests that cannot be performed in the test area.
