ACME Engineering, Payload and Ground Systems Division - Space Components & Strategic Business Unit, specializing in deployable systems for spacecraft such as solar arrays, instrument booms, and various extendable support structures is seeking a Mechanical Engineer for our facility in Oxnard, CA.

Reporting to the Design Engineering Manager, the Mechanical Engineer will be responsible for creating and implementing deployable structures and mechanical mechanisms at the cutting edge of space technology.  You will have ownership of your work from the concept creation through the design and analysis phases and may also participate in the manufacturing, testing, and integration of the systems.
 

Responsibilities

  • Create, prototype, design, and analyze structures and mechanisms for moving mechanical assemblies such as solar arrays, booms, and other support structures and mechanisms for satellite subsystems.

  • Develop and refine components and subsystems utilizing design skills, hand calculations, and finite element analysis or other modeling tools as needed.

  • Perform detailed 3D designs, generate CAD drawings and related documents, and support manufacturing and assembly of components.

  • Document test plans and procedures requirements.

  • Lead, participate, and present in internal and external (customer) design reviews.

  • Strive for the highest possible structural performance that is also cost effective and assures complete reliability.

  • Be accountable for, manage, and track multiple priorities while maintaining a focus towards common project goals of the program team, matrix organization, and customers.

This position may be filled at either the Principal Level (Level 3) or Senior Principal Level (Level 4)

Basic Qualifications - Principal Mechanical Engineer (Level 3):

  • Bachelor or Master of Science or PhD in Engineering (Mechanical, Aeronautical, or Aerospace)
  • 5 years with BS, 3 years with MS or 0 years with PhD experience in manufacturing, design and drawing practices including geometric dimensioning and tolerancing
  • Experience in CAD systems: NX CAD, SolidWorks or CREO
  • Knowledge and experience with MS Office (Word, Excel, Power Point, Outlook)
  • Ability to work independently as well as in a team environment.
  • Ability to multitask and organize/prioritize job assignments in a fast-paced and gated-release engineering development environment
  • US Citizenship Required

Basic Qualifications - Senior Principal Mechanical Engineer (Level 4):

  • Bachelor or Master of Science or PhD in Engineering (Mechanical, Aeronautical, or Aerospace)
  • 9 years with BS, 7 years with MS or 4 years with PhD experience in manufacturing, design and drawing practices including geometric dimensioning and tolerancing
  • Experience in CAD systems: NX CAD, SolidWorks or CREO
  • Knowledge and experience with MS Office (Word, Excel, Power Point, Outlook)
  • Ability to work independently as well as in a team environment.
  • Ability to multitask and organize/prioritize job assignments in a fast-paced and gated-release engineering development environment
  • US Citizenship Required

Preferred Qualifications

  • Master's degree in applicable area.

  • Proficiency with mechanical computer aided design (CAD) software, preferably design and analysis experience with PTC Creo Parametric 4.0.

  • Practical understanding of GD&T (ASME Y14.5-2009)

  • Detailed mechanical design knowledge of structures, mechanisms, release and drive actuators, and material selection.

  • Spacecraft/aerospace materials experience in metals, adhesives, thermoplastics, etc.

  • Working knowledge of applicable manufacturing processes such as machining, finishing, and rapid prototyping.

  • Demonstrable problem solving skills with hands-on project experience.

  • Excellent technical communication skills and strong analytical skills.

  • Self-motivated with the ability to organize and prioritize job assignments and tasks.

  • Ability to work independently as well as in an integrated team and adapt to change and dynamic requirements.

  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Office suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint…)
