ACME Engineering is seeking a Linux Systems Administrator/ Associate Linux Systems Administrator to maintain and enhance Linux Systems and Infrastructure for the Air & Cyberspace Intelligence Group (AC) and Space, Missiles, and Forces Intelligence Group (SM) at the National Air and Space Intelligence Center (NASIC) at Wright Patterson Air Force Base. NASIC is responsible for creating all-source intelligence defining the present and future air, air defense, cyber, space and ballistic missile threats to enable military operations, force modernization, and policy making. Successful candidates will clearly demonstrate strong technical skills to achieve high availability, security, and integrity for the operating environment. They should have a track record as an effective communicator and problem solver who is able to develop and maintain good working relationships with internal and external stakeholders. This Linux Systems Administrator will be an active part of a team of Systems, Network, and Storage Administrators who collaborate to support two production networks and a R&D environment to include Linux and Windows. As part of a broader team, successful candidates will have the opportunity to gain experience in Windows and Linux Administration and grow their skills in network and hardware management.

This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.


Basic Qualifications for an Associate Systems Administrator:

  • Active Department of Defense Top Secret/Sensitive Compartmented Information security clearance
  • A High School Diploma and a minimum of 4 years of relevant experience or a Bachelor's degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering or Math discipline
  • A Minimum of three years of experience with Systems Administration for Linux Operating Systems such as RedHat Enterprise Linux (RHEL), CentOS, or Rocky Linux
  • Able to obtain IAT Level II certification such as CASP+ or Security+ within 90 days
  • Able to obtain a Linux Administration Certification within 90 days

Basic Qualifications for a Systems Administrator:

  • Active Department of Defense Top Secret/Sensitive Compartmented Information security clearance
  • One of the following:
    • High school diploma or GED equivalent with 7 Years relevant experience may be substituted in lieu of a degree
    • Bachelor's degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering or Math discipline with 3 Years related experience
    • Master’s degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering or Math discipline with 1 Year related experience
  • Minimum of three years of experience with Systems Administration for Linux Operating Systems such as RedHat Enterprise Linux (RHEL), CentOS, or Rocky Linux.
  • Able to obtain IAT Level II certification such as CASP+ or Security+ within 90 days
  • Able to obtain a Linux Administration Certification within 90 days

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Windows Systems Administration
  • Experience with security compliance and related security scanning products.  
  • Experience in enterprise backup and restore solutions, storage area network (SAN), and network attached storage (NAS)
  • Experience with scripting using tools such as Perl, Bash, PowerShell, etc.
  • Experience with network administration and related infrastructure
  • Experience with hardware installation and maintenance to include firmware upgrades
  • Candidate must demonstrate strong troubleshooting and problem-solving skills
