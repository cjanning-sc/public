Staff Air Vehicle Proposal Engineer - Flight Sciences

1st Shift

ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Staff Air Vehicle Proposal Engineer – Flight Sciences to support our Vehicle Engineering team in Philadelphia, FL.  Our ideal candidate will be a self-starter that flourishes in a fast-paced, collaborative team environment with minimal direction.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Provides technical guidance and coordinate a small team of engineers in proposal activities supporting a Corporate Priority Win Effort

  • Leads a team responsible for producing the engineering artifacts to support capture efforts

  • The team will be engaged in estimating, cost reduction opportunities, hardware trade studies associated with sourcing, and Integrated Master Plan (IMP) / Integrated Master Schedule (IMS) activities

  • Verifies the proposal offering meets requirements and is capable of being executed within the specified contract limits

  • Ensures that the proposal is technically sound and meets all budgetary and schedule requirements

  • Attends internal program, customer, and supplier technical exchange meetings and/or formal program milestone reviews to provide and obtain status updates

  • Reports to both the Air Vehicle IPT Leader and to the Air Vehicle Proposal Manager

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree and 14+ years of relevant engineering experience; OR Master’s degree and 12+ years of relevant engineering experience; OR Ph.D. and 9+ years of related engineering experience

    • At least one degree must be in an applicable STEM discipline

    • Must have some experience in a Flight Sciences discipline: Aerodynamics, Vehicle Performance, Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), Configuration Design, Propulsion, Mass Properties, Loads & Dynamics, and/or Guidance Navigation & Control (GNC)

  • Must have working knowledge of Air Vehicle fields (Airframe, Subsystems, etc.)

  • Active DoD Secret clearance or higher (background investigation completed within the last 6 years or is under continuous evaluation)

  • Must be able to obtain and maintain Special Access Programs (SAP) prior to the commencement of employment

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Degree in Mechanical or Aerospace Engineering

  • Familiarity with NX CAD software and Teamcenter PLM

  • Experience leading a project or team

  • Earned Value Management (EVM) experience

  • Experience with proposals and program captures within the Aerospace and Defense industry

  • Excellent written and oral communication and presentation skills

  • Proficient with Microsoft Word and Excel
