Northrop Grumman Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) has an opening for a Sr. Principal Tool Engineer within the Tooling & Hardware Acquisition Section of the Mechanical Engineering & Ground Support Department. This position will be located at Space Park in Redondo Beach, CA.

Candidate will use a systems engineering approach to analyze requirements for and design mechanical solutions by creating 3D models and drawings utilizing CATIA V5 & 3DX. In particular, the candidate will create layout drawings, fabrication drawings and engineering change orders, give design review presentations and perform some fundamental types of analysis (e.g. strength of materials). Other tasks might include fabrication engineering liaison support, hardware testing, and supporting Integration and Test (I&T) operations as related to Tooling/MGSE usage and processing.

This position also supports offsite tooling efforts including: managing tooling suppliers, solving technical fabrication concerns at suppliers, traveling to suppliers to ensure NGC tooling requirements are met, coordinating with the analyst team for supplier bids and schedules.


Senior Principal Tool Designers are expected to provide technical solutions to a wide range of complex and difficult problems. They are problem solvers who have the ability to provide results that are innovative, thorough, practical, and consistent with organization objectives. We seek Senior Principal Tool Designers that use a unique skill set that includes a hands-on approach and act as a technical representative voice for the Tooling organization.

  • Bachelor's of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) discipline plus 9 years of engineering experience OR Master's of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) discipline plus 7 years of engineering experience OR High School Diploma/G.E.D plus 13 years of engineering experience in lieu of degree

  • Proven leadership experience on a project and/or team

  • Experience with design modeling and/or drawing efforts using 3-D CAD modeling tools such as CATIA

  • High level of experience and comfort with Geometric Dimensioning or Tolerancing (GD&T)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Proven record of tooling design capability and innovation at all levels of complexity

  • Project management experience with tool design

  • Root Cause Analysis/CPATS experience in tooling and working directly with shop floor personnel to identify root causes

  • Experience with machine shop fabrication best practices, processes, and capabilities
