Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is an industry-leading provider for prime satellite and payload capabilities and directed energy and electronics solutions for national security, military, and civil customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable, and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible.

Northrop Grumman Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) has an opening for a Deputy-Chief Architect/ Engineer - Digital Transformation to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Redondo Beach, CA.

As a Deputy-Chief Architect / Engineer for Strategic Space Systems Digital Transformation, you provide strategic architecture and technical baseline leadership to project leads and our programs on large and complex digital transformation, product lifecycle systems integration projects for spacecraft and space electronics product development. You must understand the business imperatives tied to these strategic projects. You provide technology leadership and are the technical expert on strategic projects, drive the release strategy and guide the delivery of strategic projects aligned to business strategy on innovative model based everything (systems engineering, design, analysis, data management, procurement, manufacturing, integration, & test) and Dev* Ops. You maintain awareness of dependencies between enterprise, sector, and division projects and their delivery vision, while ensuring engineering excellence. You build the strategy for digital transformation teams to architect, design and deliver the next generation product development lifecycle for space.

Essential Functions:

  • Develop and maintain a roadmap for digital program initiatives (Model Based System Engineering, Model Based Design, Dev*Ops, Industry 4.0, Model Based Manufacturing), consistent with SSSD’s strategy, and in collaboration with functions, programs and BU’s

  • Design, develops, and maintains system architectures and the technical baseline for cross team / cross product development projects developing the digital thread through the Space product development lifecycle

  • Defines key capabilities and performance requirements for a digital thread through the product development lifecycle.

  • Define design and technology maturity constraints of the system based on specifications.

  • Develop thorough definition of system external interfaces.

  • Define system implementation approaches and operational concepts.

  • Ensure requirements are met and evaluates performance with SSSD leadership.

  • Align SSSD specific performance management metrics across business units and programs to understand progress against digital efforts and manage monthly readout sessions

  • Lead transformational change across SSSD functions, business units, and programs to a digitally connected product development lifecycle

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in STEM with 14 years of experience in engineering and/or operations; Master’s Degree in STEM with 12 years of experience in in engineering and/or operations; PhD in STEM with 9 years with of experience in in engineering and/or operations

  • Demonstrable experience with PLM (Product Life Cycle Management) systems

  • Excellent communication skills and ability to collaborate with technical and non-technical stakeholders

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Secret and SCI security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with interfacing with engineering and operations tools to support a digital thread through the product development lifecycle (i.e.: Cameo, DOORs, STK, Mentor Expedition, Cadence, Atlassian, Catia, CREO, Enovia, Windchill, 3Dx, Teamcenter, SAP, Solumina, Eyelit)

  • Demonstrated experience in developing and executing comprehensive change management plan

  • Experience collaborating across organizational boundaries to understand best practices or to share learnings from local initiatives

  • Proven knowledge of successful design, architecture, and development

  • Experience in model-based product development

  • Active Top Secret and SCI security clearance
