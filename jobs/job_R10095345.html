Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Manager of Programs - Communications Systems 3. The selected candidate will lead the Communications Systems for the Command and Launch segment. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. 

What You’ll Get To Do:

The Communications Systems Manager is responsible for the cost, schedule, and technical management of all phases from design through early manufacturing of communication systems used for the execution of the GBSD mission. This includes, but may not be limited to, hard-lined communications and RF communications in benign and contested environments. The selected candidate will lead a team with Intercompany Work Orders, complex subcontracts with large and small businesses, and general procurements across the United States.

Participates in the negotiation of contract and contract changes. Coordinates the preparation of proposals, business plans, proposal work statements and specifications, operating budgets and financial terms/conditions of contract. Acts as primary customer contact for program activities, leading program review sessions with customer to discuss cost, schedule, and technical performance. Establishes design concepts, criteria and engineering efforts for product research, development, integration and test. Develops new business or expands the product line with the customer. Establishes milestones and monitors adherence to master plans and schedules, identifies program problems and obtains solutions, such as allocation of resources or changing contractual specifications. Directs the work of employees assigned to the program from technical, manufacturing and administrative areas.
