The Payload and Ground Systems organization within the ACME Engineering Space Systems pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter. The NKW program is seeking an Integration and Test engineer to support our test team transforming from traditional manual testing to creating automated tests to support V&V of system requirement in a continuous integration/continuous deployment pipeline.

The successful applicant will be part of a team developing automated testing tools and tests to ensure compliance requirements baseline. In addition, responsibilities may include coordinating and conducting system engineering activities (e.g. SRR, SDR, PDR, CDR, etc.) and other ground segment support activities. Tests will be developed using modern testing tools which may include technologies such as Behave, Zephyr, Jenkins, and Python.

This is an opportunity to help a customer and team take advantage of leading-edge Integration and Test practices. If you have found yourself saying on more than one occasion “Hey folks, maybe we should really try <insert modern best practice>!” Then this might be the job for you!

This requisition may be filled at a higher job grade based on the qualifications listed below.

This requisition may be filled as either a Principal or Sr. Principal Systems Test Engineer.

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Systems Test Engineer: 

- Bachelor’s degree with 5+ years of relevant experience; 3 years with a Master's; 0 years with a PhD. An additional 4 years of relevant experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
- Strong knowledge of/experience creating automated test to sell off and validate requirements.
-Strong knowledge of/experience with CI/CD operations.
-Ability to obtain a Top Secret/SCI clearance

Basic Qualifications for a Senior Principal Systems Test Engineer:

- Strong knowledge of/experience creating automated test to sell off and validate requirements.
-Strong knowledge of/experience with CI/CD operations.

-Ability to obtain a Top Secret/SCI clearance
