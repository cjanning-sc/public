ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Program Integration Manager 2 to join its team of qualified and diverse individuals in support of the Sentinel (GBSD) program. This leadership role is located in Detroit, Michigan. Learn more about the Sentinel (GBSD) program here.  ACME Engineering supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What you will get to do:

Responsible for leading teams that may include project managers and other individual contributors on the Sentinel program to support program management processes, implementation and driving discipline to ensure program commitments are met. The selected candidate will also support developing the strategic and tactical objectives. Functional Manager for assigned project managers/program integrators across the Support and Training Product Segment. This position will be responsible for coordinating with the Program IPTs and various stakeholders to integrate various program management products/documents, provide oversight across the Segment, and support the planning and implementation of any assigned ad hoc projects.  Eligible candidates must have an emphasis on qualities such as analytical thinking, people leadership, managerial skills, networking, excellent presentation skills, and a passion of excellence.  They should have experience with project management, and Program Management processes and enabling tools.

Additional responsibilities include:

  • Develop deep and enduring Customer relationships by working closely with them and understanding their needs (both internal and external)
  • Develop strong relationships with the team by working closely with them, understanding their roadblocks, and resolving issues.
  • Ensure integrity, quality, coordination, and provide oversight for program projects
  • Continual focus on decision making based on engineering efficiency for increased performance
  • Experience in leadership skills to plan, organize, integrate and drive program activities across multiple engineering disciplines, along with the ability to effectively motivate people to work as an integrated team across locations, companies and cultures.
  • Oversees projects and task assigned to the organizational group performed by non-exempt and/or exempt employees. Becomes involved as needed to ensure the success of the program. Makes decisions that support accomplishment of discipline objective.

As a leader, you always act with integrity, and the highest ethical standards characterize everything you do. You enable your team to achieve predictable and balanced results, while satisfying the needs of internal and external partners. You treat all people with respect, and you model personal growth and continuous development. You deliver excellence, strive for continuous improvement, and respond vigorously to change.
