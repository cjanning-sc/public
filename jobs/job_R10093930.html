SSSD has an opening for a Principal or Senior Proposal Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in defining the future at the Redondo Beach, CA location. 

The Principal or Senior Proposal Analyst will report to the Price Cost Estimating Manager.  The candidate for this role will be persistent, tenacious, analytical, and able to meet deadlines in a challenging, multi-tasking environment. 

This position requires a Department of Defense (DoD) Top Secret (TS) clearance and/or Specialized Compartmented Information (SCI) Access.

Responsibilities include:

  • Partner with innovators to secure mission critical technology for military and defense
  • Lead and develop small to large new business cost proposals, including coordination, collection and analysis of cost, schedule, and technical elements from multi-functional departments
  • Publish cost proposals in accordance with request for proposal (RFP) specifications and other pricing/cost requirements
  • Design and develop Cost Engineering Models and Cost Estimating Relationships (CERs) for proposals, company investment, and design to cost tasks 
  • Create and present cost issues and risks to senior and executive leadership
  • Participate in customer fact-finding meetings.
  • Develop recommendations for should-cost and pricing reviews with management and customer representatives
  • Lead and support DCAA audit reviews for proposals
  • Promote affordability and quality initiatives
  • Represent the pricing organization in contract negotiations, drive improvements to the negotiation process, and advise leadership on our cost and risk position
  • Strategic focus oriented, including ability to evaluate, integrate, and communicate cost issues and risks to senior and executive management
  • Must have persistence and tenacity to meet deadlines in a challenging, multi-tasking environment
  • Excellent communications skills and leadership competencies

This requisition may be filled at a Principal - 3 or Senior Principal - 4 Level.  You must meet the basic qualifications as outlined below to qualify. 

Basic Qualifications – Level 3:

  • 6 years of work experience in any of the following: Pricing or Cost Estimating, Proposal Development, Project Management, Accounting/Budgeting, Finance, Economics, or Business Administration, or Master’s Degree with 4 years of stated experience
  • Strong analytical, presentation, and leadership skills
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel (pivot tables, Vlookups, nested formulas, macros), PowerPoint, and Word
  • Must have current DoD TS and/or SCI clearance

Basic Qualifications – Level 4:

  • 10 years of work experience in any of the following: Pricing or Cost Estimating, Proposal Development, Project Management, Accounting/Budgeting, Finance, Economics, or Business Administration, or Master’s Degree with 8 years of stated experience
  • Strong analytical, presentation, and leadership skills
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel (pivot tables, Vlookups, nested formulas, macros), PowerPoint, and Word
  • Must have current DoD TS and/or SCI clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s Degree
  • Excellent problem solving and organizational skills
  • Pricing and Proposal Analyst Experience
  • Knowledge of FAR/DFARS and other Government Regulations
  • Proficiency in ProPricer
  • Experience working for a cleared client
  • In-depth knowledge of FAR/DFARS/CAS and other government regulations
  • Experience in applying cost accounting, pricing, and estimating concepts and principles
