At the heart of Defining Possible is our commitment to missions in today’s rapidly changing global security environments. We’re looking for innovators who can help us keep building on our wide portfolio of secure, affordable, integrated, and multi-domain systems and technologies that fuel those missions. By joining in our shared mission, we’ll support yours of expanding your personal network and developing skills, whether you are new to the field or an industry thought-leader. At Northrop Grumman, you’ll have the resources, support, and team to do some of the best work of your career.

 

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking a Principal Research Development Engineer to work with our team of qualified, diverse individuals in Northern Virginia. The selected candidate be responsible for executing strong research, design, test, and evaluation, as well as prototype and development/fabrication of the resulting products for our customers. This individual will provide technical expertise and consultation on a variety of material issues, including material selection, supply chain support, troubleshooting, prototyping, production, and operations and maintenance of lab equipment. This position is focused but not limited to supporting research and development of specialize products for military application.

What qualifications you’ll need to have to be considered:

  • An active TS/SCI with current Polygraph is required.

  • Bachelor’s degree plus 5 years of experience in digital design and high-resolution 3D modeling and sculpting (4 years of additional experience in lieu of degree). Or, 3 years of related experience with a Master’s degree. Or, 0 years with a PhD.

  • Experience working with 3D models across multiple applications (e.g., ZBrush, Maya, Substance Painter, Rhino, Artec Studio 16, and Adobe Creative Suite including Illustrator and Photoshop).

  • Experience managing multiple SubTools, managing topology with Dynamesh and Zremesher, creating custom alphas for textures and brushes, and working with UV Master.

  • Applying an understanding of lighting, color, material, orientation, depth, visual perspective, and composition, including a good feeling for proportions, anatomy, musculoskeletal function, as well as finite details in human skin, hair, and clothing texture.

Our Preferred Qualifications:

  • Strong interpersonal, written, and verbal communications skills.

  • Ability to coordinate interdepartmental activities across multiple projects to meet deadlines.

  • Able to give and receive constructive feedback of co-worker and/or a team’s work.

  • Willingness to learn and keep current with new products, materials, software, and techniques.
