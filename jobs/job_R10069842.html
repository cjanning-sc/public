Aeronautics Systems Sector is looking for a well-qualified Flight Inspector 4. This position will be located in Cincinnati, CA.

Essential Functions:

  • Inspects demonstrator and production aircraft systems, equipment and parts for operation and conformance to government and company standards and specifications.
  • Inspects the installation and operation of electrical, electronic, hydraulic, pneumatic, fuel, oil, and emergency systems and other parts and equipment of aircraft.
  • Performs preflight and post-flight inspections in accordance with standard procedure on flight-proven aircraft.
  • Approves non-flight-proven aircraft for flight. Inspects, adjustments, repairs, or modifications made on the aircraft.
  • May monitor and verify quality in accordance with statistical process or other control procedures.

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School diploma or GED equivalent with at least 6 years of experience in aerospace with emphasis in aircraft manufacturing and quality assurance. Or an Associate degree with at least 4 years of experience in aerospace with emphasis in aircraft manufacturing and quality assurance.
  • Must be capable of performing mechanical measurement, test witness, processing of nonconforming material into the MRB and verification of reworked or repaired product.
  • Must have a Secret to start and within scope in the last 6 years and ability to obtain an approved PAR​
  • Must be able to pass medical/respiratory exams.


Preferred Qualifications:

  • 12 years’ experience working with production, flight operations, Flight Test, engineering and the customer.
  • An A&P license is a plus.
  • Excellent coordination and communication skills, with the ability to communicate technical issues clearly to all levels of leadership.
  • Current TS clearance and active PAR
