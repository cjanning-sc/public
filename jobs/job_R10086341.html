Program Cost & Schedule Control Analyst/Principal Program Cost & Schedule Control Analyst (level 2/3)
R10086341
1st Shift (United States of America)

AOA Xinetics (AOX), a wholly-owned Northrop Grumman Space Systems, Payload and Ground Systems Division Business Venture, is a leading supplier of complex electro-optic and photonic systems and subsystems that control and manipulate light waves for a variety of applications. We are seeking a Program Cost Schedule & Control Analyst (Level 2 or 3) to support our Special Programs Team. This position may be located in either East Hartford, CT or Devens, MA.

Overview

Reporting to the Manager of Program Planning & Control, the Program Control Analyst will provide financial analysis, pricing and program, planning & control support. Our ideal candidate will possess program cost control experience and collaborative people skills with the proven ability to work within a technically dynamic, high-growth, fast-paced work environment.

Responsibilities:

  • Provide Business Management, Finance/Accounting, Program Planning and Control, and proposal support

  • Support Program Management by providing recurring and ad hoc project reporting and analyses

  • Generate customer cost reports as required by CDRLs/SDRLs including EVM reporting

  • Monitor projects to ensure that costs are within budget and negotiated contract and funded values and performance is within contractual scope

  • Assist in preparation of budgets and forecasts and perform analyses of actual costs and estimates to complete

  • Prepare Government and Intercompany cost proposals that are consistent with Request for Proposal (RFP) requirements; assemble and coordinate all cost proposal input and support proposal managers in generating adequate costing for internal review and external submission; provide fact-finding, negotiation and price certification support

  • Provide analysis of business area and program financials

  • Support, interact and communicate effectively with Senior Management, Business Area Management and functional organizations

This position may be filled at either a level 2 or a Level 3

Basic Qualifications for the Level 2:

  • Bachelor's degree in Business Management or business related field

  • 2+ years of Program Planning and Control or Business Management experience

  • Proficient MS Excel skills and basic knowledge of MS Word, PowerPoint and Project

  • Understanding of FAR, DFAR, TINA, CAS and EVMS

  • Ability to multi-task and meet demanding deadlines with focus on attention to detail and accuracy

  • Ability to work independently with minimal supervision and work well in a team environment

  • Good oral/written communication and organizational skills

  • U.S. Citizenship is a requirement for employment

Basic Requirements for the Level 3:

  • Same as above, but with 5+ years of Program Planning and Control or Business Management experience

Preferred Qualifications:

  • MBA

  • Experience working in a manufacturing, DoD/Defense Contractor, Aerospace or related industry
