ACME Engineering is currently seeking an experience Supplier Quality Engineer who can lead Quality activities at our suppliers. The position will report into our Quality Organization located within our Owensboro, Kentucky facility.

Job Functions:

  • Responsible for multiple components commodities.
  • RCCA (Root Cause and Corrective Action) review, approval, and vetting for all NC’s written against your suppliers
  • Leads audits, continuous improvement activities and periodic performance reviews at component suppliers
  • FAIR (First Article Inspection Report) review and approval for FAIR’s written by your suppliers
  • Resolve issues with suppliers arising out of Receiving Inspection, including but not limited to paperwork issues, dimensional issues, packaging issues and other part defects
  • Manage and support the Source Inspection process including source inspection of parts at your suppliers, supporting 3rd party source inspection at your suppliers, and AS13001 approval of your suppliers as applicable
  • Work hand in hand with the ACME Buyer to resolve any quality issues that arise with your suppliers
  • NPI (New Product Introduction) support for your suppliers (new suppliers as well as new parts to an existing supplier)
  • Input SRMRB (Supplier Requested Material Review Board) NC’s (Non-Conformances) into the TipQA system and follow up to closure – also support MLE (Manufacturing Liaison Engineering) Engineering with any additional questions or follow up that may be needed to close the NC 
  • Provides internal and external QMS compliance audit support
  • Analyze overall supplier quality performance information to identify risks through vendor scorecard evaluations and manage return to green activities as required for your suppliers.
  • Assures supplier process and product quality, including contractual, regulatory, and technical compliance.
  • Interfaces directly with supplier personnel and must be knowledgeable in and capable of assessing both supplier quality and manufacturing processes and procedures.
  • Supports the maintenance of the approved supplier list and periodically reviews supplier performance to determine whether suppliers should remain on the approved list.

Basic Qualifications:

  • This position emphasizes skills first
  • Bachelor of Science in a STEM field (Preferably Mechanical or Electrical Engineering) plus 5 years of quality engineering experience in a manufacturing environment, or a master's degree plus with 3 years of experience.
  • In the absence of a bachelor’s degree, 9 years of quality engineering experience in a manufacturing environment, can be considered.
  • Supplier quality engineering experience
  • Excellent command of the 8D process for effective root cause determination and corrective actions
  • Strong interpersonal and communications skills, presentation skills and an excellent statistical analysis ability
  • Excellent people skills to work with internal customers and suppliers to achieve quality improvement activities
  • Familiarity with AS9100 or ISO 9100 Quality Management Systems.
  • High Energy, results oriented, self-motivated/self-reliant, team player
  • Solid Understanding of Design Failure Mode Effect Analysis (DFMEA) and Process Failure Mode Effect Analysis (PFMEA).
  • Computer and software expertise specifically MS Office products; Word, Excel and PowerPoint

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Six Sigma Green or Black belt formal certification
  • Good understanding of the Six Sigma concept.
  • ASQ Quality Engineer Certification (CQE)
