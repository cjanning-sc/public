This position can be located in East Hartford, CT or Devens, MA.

Overview

As a designer of printed circuit boards at AOX, you will create multilayer high-density printed circuit board designs using Siemens/Mentor Graphics tools (PADS PCB Layout, PADS Professional, Autorouter, etc.) per electrical and mechanical engineering input. You will be part of a team of electrical engineers and designers developing electronics for R&D projects, engineering prototypes, and production ready designs.

The ideal candidate will have experience laying out mixed high speed digital and analog circuits, power supplies, FPGAs and microcontroller circuits.

Role and Responsibilities:

  • Perform detailed layout design and documentation of printed circuit boards

  • Establishes all electrical functionality (current carrying, isolation requirements, stack-up, etc.)

  • Prepares all necessary artwork /electronic data required for complete board fabrication

  • Produce 2D CAD drawings for board fabrication and assembly

  • Produce 3D models of boards for mechanical integration

  • Work from schematics, net lists, part lists, and basic description of the board function, constraints, configuration, and arrangement as defined by engineering

  • Create and maintain electronic library parts, geometry, decals etc. per IPC standards

  • Work under minimal supervision

  • Interface with EE and ME staff for design development and solving board level related issues

Basic Requirements:

  • Associates Degree in electronics, electronics engineering, electrical engineering, or a related discipline and 5+ years PCB design experience, or an additional 2 years experience may be substituted in lieu of a degree

  • Experience in designing HDI PCBs

  • IPC CID certification, with CID+ preferred

  • Experience with Analog, RF, High Speed, and Switch Power Mode supply design layout techniques

  • Impedance control techniques

  • Experience in the design of boards to IPC-6011/6012, Class 2 and Class 3 standards

  • Experience in the PCB assembly of J-STD-001 and IPC-A-610, Class 2 and Class 3 standards

  • Excellent organization and communications skills

  • US Citizenship is a bona fide requirement

​

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience using Siemens/Mentor Graphics layout tools including PADS Pro

  • Experience using AutoCAD or SolidWorks

  • Experience managing parts libraries
