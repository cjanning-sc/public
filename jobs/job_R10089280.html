Northrop Grumman Space Systems is looking for you to join our team as a Capture Manager based out of Colorado Springs, CO. The Capture Manager is responsible for the capture strategy, campaigns, product line application, and strategic direction and execution of all aspects of the capture within the Space Surveillance and Environmental Intelligence (SSEI), Operating Unit (OU) Business Development and Strategy activities.  Duties include strategic, capture campaign identification, management, and execution in line with Business Development and Annual Operating Plan roadmap, goals, milestones, and strategy.  The Capture Manager leads business development capture activities and teams to secure new or re-compete contracts, or task order business.  The Capture Manager develops the capture strategy including business solutions for pursuit of opportunities; identifies potential customers, competitive assessments, teaming suppliers, and prices-to-win; participates in bid and proposal activities and maintains on-going interaction with marketing and government relations entities.

What you’ll get to do:

The Capture Manager is responsible for daily operations of a capture team or capture work unit (direct supervision of the staff, assignment of work, schedules, day to day workflow, and operating costs).  Capture Manager responsibilities include: cost, schedule, and performance of a specific capture activity within all assigned capture and proposal activities.  Work is reviewed for adequacy in meeting business development and capture pipeline objectives. The Capture Manager ensures that all capture and proposal teams adhere to sound capture management and Business Acquisition Process (BAP) processes in accordance with company quality standards per Northrop Grumman business development procedures and practices.

Our ideal candidate will also report to the Business Development Manager the status and management of the capture, proposal, and systems engineering teams, and also serves as the capture manager to active program teams to ensure the broader Northrop Grumman capabilities are exploited and the active program’s needs for program growth are met.  The Capture Manager coordinates capture and proposal activities across the broader Integrated National Systems Business Unit (BU) to ensure collaboration within the Business Unit portfolio and associated Business Development, Capture, and Systems Engineering teams. 

Basic Qualifications:  

  • 14 Years with Bachelors STEM, or 12 Years with Masters, or 9 Years with PhD

  • Active DoD Top Secret clearance required to start

  • Minimum of 5 years’ experience working with Business Development or Capture Management execution and management

  • Experience leading capture and proposal teams 

  • Experience in systems engineering, development, integration, and test  

  • Domain expertise in Space Domain Awareness

  • Domain and mission expertise with Ground Based Radars

  • Domain expertise in Missile Warning/Missile Defense

  • Good communication and presentation skills 

Preferred Qualifications:  

  • Master’s degree in STEM or Business Administration/Management

  • Domain expertise in Electronic Warfare systems

  • Domain expertise in Environmental Sensing (e.g. Weather)
