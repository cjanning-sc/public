Armament Systems, a Business Unit of ACME Engineering - Defense Systems Sector, is seeking an Engineering Manager 2, to join our team in Plymouth, MN.

Responsibilities of this role:

Lead a team of Production engineers within the Production Engineering department.  This Production Engineer Manager role supports the ACME Engineering Armament System PGK programs and reports directly to the Director of Production Engineering.

Specific responsibilities and desired experience include:

  • Maintain and develop PGK production processes for products under development and support production at Plymouth, other ACME Engineering locations and subcontractors.

  • Drive Team technical excellence in Production Engineering. 

  • Balance PGK group workload and staffing requirements to meet current and future program needs. 

  • Coach direct reports in the areas of career development, performance, and teamwork.   Provide training and development opportunities.  Provide continual feedback and hold mid-year and end-of-year performance communications.

  • Effectively lead a diverse team of production engineers in a dynamic product production environment.

  • Have experience with fast paced agile team engagement on production programs.

  • Maintain a “learning organization” where each team member is expected to teach and learn.

  • Plan, execute and manage department budget

  • Failure Analysis and root cause determination.

Minimum Qualifications:

  • 9 years of experience with a Bachelor of Science in Engineering

  • 5 years of production engineering experience in both product development and production environments.

  • DoD Secret Security Clearance, or ability to obtain one

  • Team management and/or Integrated Product Team (IPT) leadership experience.

  • Excellent communication skills

  • Production of mechanical defense products including technical, manufacturing, cost, and schedule aspects.

  • Experience working with customer engineering (U S Government), vendors and subcontractors.

  • Proven track record of working with individuals, as well as teams, at various levels and disciplines.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Department team leadership, supervisory, and/or people management experience.
