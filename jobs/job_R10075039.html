Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking an Integration & Test (I&T) Lead (Manager 3) for the Post Boost Attitude Control Module (PBACM) Sub-IPT. This position will be located in either Roy, UT or Huntsville, AL with occasional travel to other hub locations and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program.  Learn more about the Sentinel program here.  Northrop Grumman supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What you will get to do:

Essential Functions:

  • Lead and direct a team of I&T engineers and technicians in the integration, testing, and deployment of the PBACM element during the Engineering and Manufacturing Development (EMD) phase of program.
  • Develop, release, and execute test approaches and formal test plans that maximize the utility of the test while meeting the stringent schedule and technical requirements.
  • Serve as the primary interface between the PBACM sub-IPT and both internal and customer test organizations.
  • Track and report on program performance and execution metrics for cost and schedule including Earned Value Management (EVMS).
  • Establish and track progress towards key project milestones in accordance with the Integrated Master Schedule (IMS).
  • Assist in developing and providing status for IPT inputs to the program risk and opportunity management system.

  • 3 years of formal management experience to include performance reviews for direct reports.
