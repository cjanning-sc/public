ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems is currently seeking a Level 2/Level 3 Property and Asset Control Support located on site in Iuka, Mississippi with partial telework available.


Essential Functions:
• Maintains records of property owned by government, company, and associate contractors in accordance with company procedure or government regulations

• Maintains records and logs of property received, transferred, allocated, held and disposed

• Coordinates property transfers

• Conducts periodic property inventories

• Prepares reports of property status

This position may be filled as either a Level 2 or Level 3 Property and Asset Control Support.

Basic Qualifications for a level 2:
•High School Diploma/GED with 2 years of experience in Asset Management life cycle compliance related outcomes (i.e. Acquisition, Shipping/Receiving, Physical  Inventory, Record keeping, Reports, Maintenance records, and Disposition of assets)

•Able to work independently with limited supervision and resolve problems not routine in nature

• Ability to accurately count and record data and compile/generate reports
• Plans and manages time to ensure property management information is collected and organized on or before required due date(s)
•Valid Driver’s license

•Ability to obtain and maintain a Secret Clearance

Basic Qualifications for a level 3:
• High School Diploma/GED with 4 years of experience in Asset Management life cycle compliance related outcomes (i.e. Acquisition, Shipping/Receiving, Physical  Inventory, Record keeping, Reports, Maintenance records, and Disposition of assets)

•Able to work independently with limited supervision and resolve problems not routine in nature

• Ability to accurately count and record data and compile/generate reports
• Plans and manages time to ensure property management information is collected and organized on or before required due date(s)
•Valid Driver’s license

•Ability to obtain and maintain a Secret Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:
•Experience in Asset Management life cycle compliance related outcomes (i.e. Acquisition, Shipping/Receiving, Physical Inventory, Record keeping, Reports, Maintenance records and Disposition of assets)

•Government contract knowledge and experience
