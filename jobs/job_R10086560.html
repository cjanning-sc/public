ACME Engineering Defense Systems is seeking a Senior Supply Chain Subcontract Specialist to join our GSC team to support the international business expansion Foreign Military Sales and Direct Commercial Sales of Defense related programs and work with international suppliers and program teams. 

As a Principal Supply Chain Subcontract Specialist, you will be responsible for managing subcontracts with suppliers that will provide products and services necessary for ACME Engineering to support production systems delivered to our Customers throughout the life cycle of the program. You work with a sense of a sense of urgency; proactively engaged with the internal program office/contracts group and acts as lead when dealing with matters of subcontractor cost, performance and schedule.

Key accountabilities and responsibilities include the following:

  • Administers existing subcontracts with minimum supervision 

  • Maintains, analyzes and updates subcontract records and supports financial forecasting

  • Maintains an awareness of regulatory changes and their impact on subcontract 

  • Engages in regular communications with subcontractors; communicates supplier status during sustainment and supply chain meetings 

  • Recognizes and communicates subcontract issues; collaborates with Subcontracts Management Team (SMT); facilitates meetings with subcontractors that to ensure areas of concern are being addressed and performance issues resolved 

  • Works to identify and negotiate additions, deletions or other modifications to subcontracts Statement of Works

  • Provide leadership during the execution of subcontract changes and follow-on subcontract proposal efforts and resultant negotiated subcontract agreements

  • Prepares, analyzes and evaluates bids and proposals to ensure they are compliant with company policy, program requirements and department operating procedures 

  • Provides leadership in the evaluation of single/sole source proposals as well as during competitive source evaluation and selection process.

  • Identify subcontract issues and actively participate in the development mutually acceptable solutions

  • Advise management of its contractual rights and obligations relating to terms and conditions and the incorporation of prime contract flow downs

  • Acts as lead negotiator in all subcontract actions assigned; Ensures procurement compliance with Corporate and Sector policies and procedures

  • Executes quick-react requirements identified by the program team in accordance with supply chain management direction

Basic Qualifications: 
 

  • Must have high school diploma or equivalent (GED) with at least 14 years of relevant experience

  • Will consider at least 10 years of relevant experience with bachelor’s degree or at least 8 years of relevant experience with Master’s

  •  Experience supporting different types of programs or product lines as well as contract types including cost types 

  • Experience working with subcontractors during all phases of program lifecycle 

  • Experience utilizing MS Office Suite, SAP and other business tools used in the execution of subcontracts management activities 

  • Strong organizational skills

  • Understanding of FAR/DFAR - ability to evaluate, negotiate and administer subcontract terms and conditions

  • Demonstrated ITAR compliance

Preferred Qualifications: 
 

  • Background working in Subcontract Management, with focus on subcontracting of international Goods and Services supporting US and International Prime Contracts.

  • Demonstrated subcontracting in Direct Commercial Sales and Foreign Military Sales environments

  • Knowledge of a program lifecycle and the sustainment support required from its supply base

  • Ability to solicit, evaluate and negotiate subcontract proposals

  • Experience with negotiation of international terms and conditions of contracts
