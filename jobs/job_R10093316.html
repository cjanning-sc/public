Principal Systems Engineer - (Civil and Commercial Space)
R10093316
1st Shift (United States of America)

Successful candidates for this position will be a Systems Engineer within the Tactical Space Division at Northrop Grumman Space Systems located in Dulles, VA. This position offers the opportunity to work on exciting space flight programs designed to further Civil and Commercial Space (CCS) technology to support human exploration, communication, science and research as well as support space logistics.

The Systems Engineer is responsible for supporting key Systems Engineering (SE) deliverables as part of an Integrated Team at various program levels i.e. Mission-, Element-, and Segment-level. The integrated team will incorporate the direction and results of a multi-disciplined engineering development of the space system architecture & elements. The Systems Engineer is expected to support key program deliverables as well as support and incorporate new company and industry driven technologies. This description applies to a number of open positions within the CCS Systems Engineering group within Northrop Grumman Space Systems Tactical Space Division.

The Principal Systems Engineer position is expected to complete work with general direction and minimal supervision for soundness of technical judgment, overall adequacy and accuracy. Works under minimal supervision. The Principal Systems Engineer is expected to primarily interact with internal company contacts, but is expected to be able to interact with inter- organizational and outside customer contacts on routine matters.

Duties/Responsibilities:

  • Provide support to all or selected segments for the SE Integrated Team

  • Support in development of space vehicle and/or ground segment design and analysis efforts

  • Support development of solutions to complex analytical, design, and test problems

  • Support contractual space segment and mission technical reviews

  • Proficiency in related engineering programs, Microsoft Office or equivalent document generation programs, Microsoft SharePoint or equivalent collaboration tool, Matlab or equivalent computational tool

  • Radio communication design and development

  • Proficient in the use of the requirement development and management tool DOORS

  • Experience in avionics system testing and evaluation

  • Knowledge in any of the following areas: Launch Vehicles, System Safety, System Reliability, Contamination Control, EMI, Space Environments (Radiation, Surface Charging, etc.), or Human Factors
