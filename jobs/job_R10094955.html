ACME Engineering is seeking a Senior Principal Supply Chain Cost/Price Analyst to support the Global Supply Chain (GSC) organization. We are seeking a highly motivated individual to act as a subject matter expert for cost and price analysis. The successful candidate will be able to independently evaluate supplier proposals for compliance to Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Part 15 Table 15-2 requirements, and prepare and provide written Price/Cost Analysis Reports (PCARs) detailing and evaluating the suppliers’ price/cost proposal and will have experience working in a classified environment.

This role is a hybrid role that requires the person to work onsite in Des Moines, VA as needed.

Job Duties:

  • Participating in fact-finding sessions via telecom or at suppliers’ site
  • Requesting and obtaining DCAA Assist Audits where applicable
  • Establishing supplier proposed vs. evaluated positions by major element of cost
  • Evaluating supporting data used and provided in support of proposals
  • Conducting market research, analyzing historical data, and developing cost estimating models and factors to evaluate price reasonableness
  • Evaluating material estimates and consolidated priced bills of material obtained by GSC
  • Training supply chain organization in the basics of cost and price analysis

Basic Qualifications for a Senior Principal Supply Chain Planning Specialist: 

  • A bachelor's degree from an accredited university and 10 or more years of supply chain, planning, or closely related experience; OR a master's degree and 8 or more years of supply chain, planning, or closely related experience; 4 additional years of supply chain experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
  • MS Office including Excel, Outlook, and PowerPoint
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a US Top Secret/SCI level security clearance.  US Citizenship is a prerequisite.

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Current Top Secret security clearance.  US Citizenship is a prerequisite.
