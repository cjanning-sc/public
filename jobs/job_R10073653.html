U103 ASSEMBLER MECHANIC C
R10073653
1st Shift (United States of America)

SHIFT:

1st/2nd/3rd available

PRIMARY FUNCTION:

Prepare and assemble various marine components and products where methods, sequences and tooling are normally established.

TOOLS AND EQUIPMENT:

Floor operated cranes, grinders, sanders, banders and saws; assembly jigs, fixtures and tooling, powered hand tools, expander, drill press, saws, pipe bender, power forklift, chainfalls, jacks. Hammers, wrenches, pliers, screwdrivers, drills, taps, reamers, torque wrenches, micrometers, feeler gages, calipers, squares, level, gages, tapes, safety glasses and gloves, marking and lapping stands, etc.

MATERIAL:

Turbines, gears, oil sumps, heat transfer apparatus, turbine generators, special products, rotors, diaphragms, blade rings, valves, main assemblies, component parts, and subassemblies, piping, flanges, reservoirs, pedestals, oil, steam, chemicals, solvents, water, rags, tape, etc.

DIRECTION OF OTHERS:

Direct others assigned to help.

WORKING PROCEDURE:

THE FOLLOWING ARE THE USUAL MAJOR JOB DUTIES BUT THIS DESCRIPTION DOES NOT PRECLUDE THE PERFORMANCE OF OTHER DUTIES BY THE INCUMBENT, NOR THE QUESTION BEING RAISED ON THE PROPER RATE FOR THE ASSIGNMENT.

Perform the duties of Assembly Helper and in addition perform the following:

1. Receives work assignment and instructions from supervision.

2.  Read, understand and follow safety regulations and procedures and MI package including drawings, change notices, operation sheets, specifications and work authorizations.

3.  Disassemble and position blade rings and diaphragms in base and clean and assemble various components, subassemblies, covers, panels, preformed and straight pipe, flanges, fittings, valves, gears and levers where parts are normally self-aligning and methods established. Clean and assemble bearing components and mark and remove mating surface interferences.

4.  Layout and mark reference lines on components where templates and/or detail drawings are provided. Set up and operate drills to drill, tap and ream holes. Measure, cut, bend and thread pipe from detail drawings or sample.

5.  Position and assemble components to test stand configuration

6.  Check work for conformance to specification.

7.  Sign off operation completed and maintain MI package in an organized manner.

8.  Make proper time charge entries and record test data as required.

9.  Maintain tools, equipment and work area in a clean and orderly condition.

10.  Perform other work of equal or lower classification as required.
