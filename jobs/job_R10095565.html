1st Shift

ACME Engineering Defense Systems sector (NGDS) is seeking a Military Trainer or Principal Military Trainer - Joint National Training Capability (JNTC) Mitigation Analyst to join our growing team of diverse professionals in Sioux City, Iowa.  The selected candidate will coordinate the mitigation of accreditation and certification issues for selected Service Joint Training Division (SJTD) programs and training sites.  The JNTC Mitigation Analyst will analyze and draft complex reports, incorporating input from SJTD liaison officers positioned with each branch of Service and United States Special Operations Command (SOCOM).  The selected candidate will conduct analyses to determine mitigation actions that increase joint context at Service programs, and directly interact with active military officers (O-5 through O-7), government civilians (GS 13 through GS 15) and numerous stakeholders within the Joint Staff, J7.

Essential Job Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Comprehend and analyze issues resulting from Accreditation and Certification visits for 37 service programs and 36 training sites

  • Enter program and site issues into Defense Intelligence Information Enterprise (DI2E), determine components required for specific queries, and produce detailed reports using Microsoft office tools

  • Support development of solutions, plan, schedule, and execute telephonic In Progress Reviews (IPRs) with service program leadership and SJTD Desk Officers

  • Develop a Mitigation Action Plan (MAP) consisting of objectives, key tasks, office of primary responsibility (OPR) and incorporates the MAP into a Program of Actions and Milestones (POAM)

  • Prepare materials, reports, and briefings for oversight bodies such as the JNTC Corporate Board, Executive Steering Committee, and Joint Integrated Process Team

  • Maintain mitigation related information in schedules, standard operating procedures, and other process documentation

  • Update Service Joint Training Division SharePoint portal sites

  • Enters program and site issues into JIRA determines components required for specific queries, and produces detailed reports using Microsoft office tools

  • Supports development of solutions, plans, schedules, and executes In Progress Reviews (IPRs) with service program leadership and SJTD Desk Officers

  • Prepares materials, reports, and briefings for oversight bodies such as the JNTC Service Board, Executive Steering Committee, and Joint Enablers Board

  • Provide expertise during JNTC ACM analysis and document analysis appropriate in the following areas:  Scenario Development, Interagency, Information Operations, Opposing Forces (OPFOR), Intelligence, Logistics, Observer/Trainer/Analysis, Joint Exercise Control Group (JECG), Joint Exercise Life Cycle (JELC), Joint Fires, Joint Doctrine and Tactics, Techniques and Procedures (TTP), Cyber, Space Based Operations, Electronic Warfare, Command and Control, Feedback, Instrumentation
