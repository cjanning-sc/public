Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Principal/Sr. Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, Utah and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. This position may be filled at a higher level based on qualification below.

As a leading global security company, we provide innovative systems, products and solutions to our customers worldwide. We are comprised of diverse professionals that bring different perspectives and ideas, understanding that the more experiences we bring to our work the more innovative we can be. As we continue to build our workforce, we look for people that exemplify our core values, leadership characteristics, and approach to innovation.

What you’ll get to do: 

  • Develop, perform and document tests, includes test planning, test conduction, data analysis and documenting test results
  • Participate in technical planning, system integration, verification and validation, cost and risk, and supportability and effectiveness analyses for missile system payloads
  • Perform CAM duties for the PLT Test Team
  • Verify specifications and designs through testing, test data processing, and support modeling and simulation and performance analysis
  • Support all phases of the systems lifecycle including concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal
  • Maintain design documentation and follow configuration management requirements
  • Create technical reports and present test and analysis results
  • Interface with AF customer

Basic Qualifications:

This position may be filled at a Principal or Sr. Principal level

  • US Citizen with an active Secret clearance awarded or reviewed within the last 6 years and eligible for Special Access Program (SAP) Clearance
  • Experience with mathematical modeling, statistics, and/or numerical optimization
  • Experience with scientific computer programming (MATLAB, C++, Python, R, etc.)
