ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Systems Engineer / Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Detroit, MI and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.

This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package. This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

What you’ll get to do: 

The selected engineer will be responsible for multiple tasks within requirements and verification for the AVE Segment.  The engineer will be responsible for performing tasks dealing with requirement decomposition, compliance tracking, verification planning and verification execution.

This position includes:

  • Requirement development, decomposition, and refinement based on knowledge of the functional architecture and performance budgets
  • Ensure architecture is consistent with requirements decomposition
  • Verification planning for the AVE segment requirements.  Verification planning will transition into verification execution which includes development and review of verification artifacts, requirement verification report generation, and management of the approval process
  • Support to environmental requirement development and test strategy
  • Support to incremental CDR execution eventually culminating in an AVE CDR (includes execution of CDR, RvC development, readiness assessment)
  • Support to flight design review (FDR)
  • Support element system engineers in the development of element level requirements, verification plans, and CDR/IDR execution
  • Work in a collaborative environment, across all system functions.  Work with teammates across a wide variety of disciplines and get intimately familiar with all aspects of the Missile

Basic Qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either a Systems Engineer or Principal Systems Engineer.

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering or a related technical discipline
  • A minimum 2 years of related experience with a bachelor’s degree; 0 years with a Master’s. (Principal level requires 5 years with a bachelor’s degree; 3 years with a Master’s degree; or 0 years with a PhD)
  • Experience using DOORS and MBSE (CAMEO)
  • U.S. Citizenship with an Active Secret Security Clearance or higher awarded or reviewed in the last 6 years (or in continues evaluation)
  • 1+ years working in requirements management tools (e.g. DOORS)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience modeling with Cameo Enterprise Architecture or MagicDraw (No Magic, Inc.) or other architecture modeling tools
  • Systems engineering experience
  • Experience with DoDAF and UPDM or UAF Profiles
  • Experience using DOORS (IBM Rational)
  • Experience with complex system development on large programs
  • Experience with flight hardware
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills, both verbal and written
