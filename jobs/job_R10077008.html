Roles and Responsibilities

As a member of the Enterprise Integrated Development Environment team, the selected candidate will research, design, develop, and modify solutions for the tools listed below.  

Primary responsibilities:

  • Administration eIDE Hosting Environment

  • Server support for all eIDE hosted Apps

  • Support of user registration for eIDE Apps

  • Understanding of network structure, ips and cloud computing

  • Ability to troubleshoot complex and non-complex technical problems

  • Ability to automate daily tasks

The scope will include integrations with other systems and data sources, deployment of custom capabilities, and administration of the eIDE servers.  Other responsibilities coordinating with other infrastructure teams, user requests, and incident resolution. Additional capabilities include planning of system deployments, meeting software compliance standards, and evaluating operational requirements and customer needs.

The eIDE team’s mission is to deliver tools and capabilities to US based users, customers, and partners, to support requests and incident resolution, and to facilitate technology evolution from old to new.

This position will be virtual and can be performed anywhere within the continental United States.

Basic Qualifications

  • At least 5 years of relevant experience with a Bachelor’s degree; 3 year of relevant experience with a Master’s degree (will consider additional experience in lieu of education i.e. Associate's Degree and at least 7 years of relevant experience)

  • At least 1 year of Server administration experience (M365)

  • At least 3 years of Technology support experience

  • Working knowledge of Federated Authentication

  • Ability to work in a virtual environment and be accountable to a support team and the team leads

Preferred Qualifications

  • Excellent customer service skills

  • Current or prior US DoD Secret clearance

  • Experience developing PowerShell scripts and automation

  • Systems Engineering experience (requirements analysis, tracking & validation)

  • Scaled Agile Framework methodology

  • Experience using ServiceNow
