DOORS Database Engineer (Space/Satellite Systems)
R10084427
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Space Systems – Tactical Space Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Principal DOORS Administrator to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located out of Gilbert, Arizona, AZ.

Position Overview:

Successful candidate will provide cross functional support for requirements management utilizing IBM Rational Dynamic Object-Oriented Requirements System (DOORS) as an administrator on multiple programs. Responsibilities include DOORS importing/exporting, updating training materials, and acting as an interface to other DOORS users (internal and external), managing account access, and creating archives.

Essential Functions:

  • Maintaining consistent practices within the DOORS environment, which includes data created internal to programs and modules received from the customer and subcontractors
  • Driving the completeness and consistency of attributes and linkages within programs’ DOORS environments
  • Establishing strong working relationships and integrating across programs and functions
  • Performing DOORS administration; creating modules for sites; synchronizing modules between sites; exporting DOORS reports (e.g., specifications traceability and metrics); and working user problems (e.g., installation, use of Change Proposal System)

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in STEM Science Technology Engineering Math with 2 years’ experience working with Requirements database
  • Independent problem-solving skills, strong analytical abilities, and creativity
  • Database archiving and user-access management to include importing data into a database
  • Training new users and creating training material on how to use the database
  • Must have US Citizenship with the ability to obtain a security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current or active Secret/TS Clearance
  • IBM DOORS Administration experience
  • IBM DOORS DXL scripting experience
  • Familiarity with Systems Engineering principles
  • Familiarity with PTC Windchill
  • Requirements and data management experience on large complex development programs
  • Experience coding in Microsoft Visual Basic for Applications (VBA)
  • Experience exporting DOORS data using IBM Rational Publishing Engine or Engineering Lifecycle Optimization Publishing
  • Experience with IBM DOORS NextGen
  • Experience working across multiple classification levels and program
