Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Systems Engineer/Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, Utah or Huntsville, AL and will support the Strategic Deterrent Systems Division to include the Sentinel (GBSD) program. The selected candidate does not need to have an active clearance, but must be able to obtain a security clearance prior to starting in this role. This position may be filled at a higher level based on qualifications listed below.

What you’ll get to do: 

  • Work with both Program Managers and technical teams to develop and mature architecture content of large programs design.
  • Help develop and incorporate the appropriate requirements for the system and ensure that they are properly represented in the model.
  • Develop systems architecture using Cameo Enterprise Architecture (behavioral, structural, analytical)
  • Collaborate with program IPT counterparts to develop supporting systems engineering and design artifacts
  • Contribute to system requirements development, management, and analysis
  • Develop unifying model techniques, procedures, and processes (for model development, tool integration, and team integration) 
  • Communicate and train other systems engineering teams on modeling process & procedures
  • Take content through the configuration management

Job Qualifications: 

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

•     A Bachelor's Degree and (appropriate years of experience based off level – education & experience below):

  • level 2: 2+ years of related engineering experience; 0+ years with Master’s degree (at least 1 degree must be STEM discipline)
  • level 3: 5+ years of related engineering experience; 3+ years with Master’s degree (at least 1 degree must be STEM discipline)
  • US Citizenship with ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret security clearance
  • Experience with requirements management/analysis/allocations using tools such as DOORS (IBM Rational)
  • Experience with complex system development on large programs
  • Knowledge/experience of Software and Hardware design 

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

  • Active DoD Secret/ Top Secret clearance, granted or re-investigated within last 6 years or in continues evaluation
  • Experience modeling with Cameo Enterprise Architecture, MagicDraw (No Magic, Inc.) or other architecture modeling tools
  • Experience with SysML, UML or DoDAF modeling languages
  • Experience with flight hardware
