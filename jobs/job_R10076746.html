ACME Engineering is seeking a software developer. This position is located in the Akron / Columbus OH facilities and is part of ACME Engineering’s Business Systems Products Portfolio Manufacturing and Logistics group, a team focused on developing and supporting software applications needed for our manufacturing programs.

The position researches, designs, develops, and/or modifies enterprise-wide systems and/or applications software. Involved in planning of system and development deployment as well as responsible for meeting software compliance standards. Evaluates interface between hardware and software, operational requirements, and characteristics of overall system. Documents testing and maintenance of system corrections. Primarily will support SAP but may also support other applications related to manufacturing.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Collaborating with team members and a variety of functional roles to deliver high quality software products

  • Design, develop, document, test, and implement software enhancements and fixes using SAP ABAP, HTML, JavaScript, CSS, jQuery, Python, Java, C#, SQL, and/or Windows/Linux Scripts.

  • Provide end user support of applications

  • Perform software installation, setup, maintenance, and patching / updates on Windows/Linux servers

  • Troubleshoot issues and determine resolutions

  • Support the users with defining requirements as needed.


Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline and 2+ years of IT experience OR Master’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline

  • Be able to obtain a U.S. Government security clearance (U.S. citizenship is a pre-requisite)

  • Working knowledge of software development life cycle

  • Understanding of structured and object-oriented development concepts

  • Ability to learn and become efficient in new development languages

  • Experience developing software with ABAP, Java, C#, or Python

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Secret or Top-Secret U.S. Government security clearance

  • Familiarity with web accessibility concepts, UX/UI and Agile practices

  • Experience with Windows Server and Linux

  • Experience using SAP

  • Experience with SAP ABAP or Fiori Development

  • Experience in Manufacturing

  • Strong verbal and written communication skills and the ability to work equally well in self-managed and team-based environment
