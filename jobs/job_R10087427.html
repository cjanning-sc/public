Northrop Grumman Mission Systems (NGMS) is seeking a Quality Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in Baltimore, Maryland.

 Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Execution of a quality evaluation and control system for processing materials into our products.
  • Establishing and analyzing inspection and testing processes
  • Adherence to contract compliance through root cause analysis & determination and corrective & preventative action
  • Develops, modifies, applies and maintains quality evaluation and control systems and protocols for processing materials into partially finished or finished materials product 
  • Collaborates with engineering and manufacturing functions to ensure quality standards are in place 
  • Devises and implements methods and procedures for inspecting, testing and evaluating the precision and accuracy of products and production equipment
  • Audits quality systems for deficiency identification and correction 

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree in a related STEM Field.
  • 0-2 years of experience reviewing, analyzing and reporting on quality discrepancies related to assembly, process, mechanical, electrical and electro-mechanical systems.
  • Must be able to travel up to 10% of the time.
  • Ability to obtain a DoD Secret clearance.  (US Citizenship required.)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Certifications in lean and six-sigma quality engineering methodologies
  • Statistical analysis and/or quantitative analysis  on project trends
  • Experience with CAD Software applications
