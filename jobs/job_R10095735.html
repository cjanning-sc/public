ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Supply Chain Risk Management (SCRM) Sr. Principal Engineer. This position will be located in Detroit, Michigan and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. 

The Mission Defense Team (MDT) is seeking a highly motivated and qualified system engineer to serve as a Supply Chain Risk Management (SCRM) Engineer. You will be responsible for performing penetration testing and vulnerability assessments for supply chain elements, processes, and actors and provide results. 

What You’ll Get To Do:  

Assessment and analysis of threats, vulnerabilities, and risk for identified mission-critical functions and critical components

Research and identify vulnerabilities on Suppliers’ systems and networks coupled with conducting penetration tests on networks and applications

Perform vulnerability analysis of applications and operating systems and evaluate system security configurations

Participate in a variety of working groups and customer meetings; ensure communication of risk environment with stakeholders

Execute completion Statement of Work requirements, Program Milestone Exit Criteria, and program maturity commitments

Self-starters compelled to take action in the workplace without requiring prompting from supervisors

Support MDT with other duties as assigned

Travel: 10%

In addition to technical skills, you will be a self-starter with strong time management skills. Your organizational skills and ability to anticipate future challenges will serve you well.
