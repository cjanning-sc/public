ACME Engineering Defense Systems is seeking a Ground Support Equipment Mechanic Level 2 based in Kansas City, Missouri.

The candidate will be responsible for accomplishing maintenance of all ground support and associated equipment to sustain aircraft maintenance operations necessary to meet all USAF E-11A mission requirements from its CONUS base location.

Job Description:

  • Conduct all ground support equipment (GSE) supporting for a fleet of Bombardier USAF E-11A (Bombardier BD-700 commercial derivative aircraft (CDA)) fleet in a CONUS location
  • Conduct progressive maintenance & inspections on ground support equipment at CONUS locations providing a maximum amount of GSE to the flightline and minimize aircraft downtime
  • Execute maintenance/upkeep, inventory, trouble shooting of all deployed GSE to include Ground Power Units, special purpose vehicles, aircraft stands, scissor/general lifts, aircraft jacks/tail stands, hydraulic servicing, generators, and other assigned equipment
  • Perform maintenance, disassembly, rework, repair, replacement, re-assembly, or adjustment of various ground support equipment systems in accordance with technical specifications and or engineering instructions
  • Conduct calibrations on aircraft jacks and other assigned GSE as necessary to meet mission requirements
  • Conduct diagnosis of malfunctions, including all electrical & mechanical troubleshooting, fault isolation & repair, parts replacement, line repairable unit (LRU) replacement, calibration tasks, testing, engine repairs and/or replacements & proper documentation
  • Use technical data to diagnose & solve maintenance problems on GSE, coordinating with original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) when necessary to ensure fully operational equipment
  • Maintain status of inspections and maintenance in CORRIDOR database inventory tool

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School Diploma
  • 10+ years aircraft Ground Support Equipment experience
  • Ability to pass a general medical physical
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Security Clearance
  • Ability to obtain US Passport
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Corporate Travel Card
  • Compliance with US Gov’t/Department of Defense medical and deployment requirements 
  • Applicants must meet medical requirements for worldwide travel, which include a physical

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current U.S. Passport
  • Active U.S. Secret Security clearance or ability to obtain a Secret clearance.
  • Working experience in CORRIDOR inventory database operations
