Description

The Principal Configuration Analyst. Position will perform the following responsibilities:

  • Planning and coordinating of project documentation, such as Contract Deliverables (CDRLs, Engineering drawings, program plans, procedures, and other contract related documentation)
  • Experience in working through critical schedules to meet contractual requirements and ensure customer satisfaction.
  • Perform technical reviews on project documentation in accordance with statement of works, contractual, and corporate/sector/operating unit requirements.
  • Coordination and establish agendas, publish minutes, and track meeting actions.
  • Process changes packages through lifecycle, including disposition and documentation for hardware and software configuration items.
  • Perform program data release functions via Product Lifecycle Management tools.
  • Develop and maintain various CDM reports to support program, including Configuration Status Reports, Engineering Change Request status, CDM performance metrics, etc.
  • Support internal program CDM audits against program SOW and functional Command Media mandates.
  • Provide aid to Functional Configuration Audits and perform Physical Configuration Audits
  • Document Management for deliverable CDRLs and non-CDRL documents

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have 10 years of relevant experience in lieu of degree. Will consider: a minimum of 6 years experience with a Bachelors degree or 4 years with a Masters degree.
  • Must have the ability to obtain an Active DOD Secret Clearance
  • Strong Microsoft Office skills (Word, PowerPoint, Excel)
  • Ability to work in a dynamic and agile environment.
  • Extreme attention to detail while managing a variety of tasks and ensuring complete accuracy.
  • Supporting deliverables while coordinating among other team members

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD secret clearance
  • Certification in EIA 649 Standard, Configuration Management Standard
  • Working knowledge of Configuration Management processes
  • Working knowledge of AS9100 or ISO 9001
  • Working knowledge of Statement of Work (SOW) details outlined in contracts.
  • Experience working in an environment & role that requires a sense of urgency to meet challenging and/or overlapping deadlines.
  • Previous CDM experience on international programs
