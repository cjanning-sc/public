Principal HW/SW Electronics Test Engineer – (Top Secret) – Space Satellite Systems (Des Moines, VA)
R10078194
Days

We have an opening for an Hardware/Software Test Electronics Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in the Tactical Space organization. This position will be located in Des Moines, VA.

In this role you will design, develop, and execute test procedures and automated test scripts using spacecraft commands, telemetry and electrical and RF ground support equipment.  You should have system level test experience working with spacecraft flight hardware and good computer skills.  The ideal candidate has a background in script scripting language and formulate automated test scripts is highly desired for this position but not required.

Daily responsibilities will require test preparation leading to direct contact with satellite and space flight hardware. You must be able to tackle issues utilizing skills obtained in the field of Electrical and RF engineering, or a related discipline, preferably as it relates to system level integration and test.  Required skills include the ability to communicate clearly, and to use critical thinking in troubleshooting of problems.

International and or domestic travel to support launch campaigns may be required. Shift and weekend work to support testing may be required periodically.

This position can be filled with either a Test Engineer or a Principal Test Engineer level. 

Basic Qualifications: 

Test Engineer

  • Bachelor's degree in a STEM field (ideally Aerospace or Electrical Engineering) and minimum of 2 yrs of experience
  • Possess system level test experience working with spacecraft flight hardware
  • Strong computer skills
  • Must have U.S. Citizenship
  • Active Top Secret Clearance

Principal Test Engineer

  • Bachelor's degree in a STEM field (ideally Aerospace or Electrical Engineering) and 5 yrs of experience; OR 3 yrs w/ a Masters; OR PhD
  • Possess system level test experience working with spacecraft flight hardware
  • Strong computer skills
  • Must have U.S. Citizenship
  • Active Top Secret Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active Top Secret clearance with SAP/SAR access highly desirable
  • Aerospace system test industry experience
  • Ability to use a scripting language and formulate manual or automated scripts

The ACME Engineering Tactical Space Division is a strategic partner specializing in commercial and classified partnerships with the design, delivery, operation and sustainment of satellites and human spacecraft.  We support science and space exploration through our various partnerships, including NASA’s Artemis program with the goal to return humans to the Moon in 2024 and the TESS (Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite) program that has discovered more than twenty confirmed plants.  Recognized as an industry leader, we also develop highly specialized space and satellite components.
