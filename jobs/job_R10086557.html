R10086557
1st Shift (United States of America)

Enterprise Services is looking for a Software Development Analyst with Robotic Process Automation development experience. This role is not location specific and can be supported virtually.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Work directly with stakeholders to capture business requirements and translate them into cost-effective technical solutions using robotic process automation (RPA) tools and/or other languages.

  • Collaborate with process owners to understand manual process steps, business purpose and associated business rules and recommend automated process improvements.

  • Design, develop, test, and deploy automation workflows actively participating in the entire software development lifecycle from requirements, design to implementation.  

  • Participate in project quality management tasks, such as peer and quality reviews of specifications, design documents and code reviews.

  • Perform defect resolution including debugging complex scenarios.

  • Create and maintain technical specifications for all automation development work.

  • Able to operate independently to complete assignments receiving little instruction on daily work.

  • Adhere strictly to security, compliance, audit, and reporting standards.

  • Continuously learn to remain current on robotic process automation/business intelligence technologies and emerging trends.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or other related discipline and at least 2 years of applicable experience (Appropriate combination of education and experience will be considered in lieu of the degree requirement)

  • Be able to obtain and maintain a U.S. Government security clearance (U.S. citizenship is a pre-requisite).

  • Minimum of 1-year hands-on experience designing and developing automaton solutions using Robotic Process Automation tools.  Blue Prism tool is highly preferred.

  • Actively participate in the analyzing existing business processes for improvements and recommend cost-effective workflow automation design to meet or exceed business requirements.

  • Basic programming knowledge of Microsoft C# or VBA.

  • Experience in developing and consuming APIs and/or web services.

  • Understanding of workflow-based logic.

  • Experience with Agile development methodology.  Scrum methodology using Azure DevOps tool is preferred.

  • Ability to understand business processes and create process flow diagrams.

  • Strong technical/analytical skills and problem-solving skills.

  • Ability to work through ambiguous situations.  

  • Demonstrate strong initiative to complete assignments within schedule and cost targets.

  • Must have great attention to detail and be able to successfully manage competing priorities.

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

  • Strong collaboration skills to work closely in cross-functional teams.

  • Demonstrated ability to drive innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction.

  • Self-motivated, desire to continuously learn and drive to contribute to a high-performing team.

  • Ability to multitask in a fast-paced environment and prioritize the most critical tasks and projects.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience or exposure with various RPA and No-Code/Low-Code tools.  Knowledge of Intelligent Process Automation is a plus.

  • Understanding of Process Design Documents and Technical/Solution Design Documents within the automation space.

  • Experience in troubleshooting issues that arise day to day in the running of RPA processes and provide timely responses and solutions as required.

  • Familiarity with Blue Prism’s Intelligent Automation Platform and Digital Exchange offerings and solutions.

  • Experience or understanding of multiple integration technologies such as API, REST, SOAP, and web services.

  • Hands-on experience and knowledge of relational databases (SQL, Oracle).

  • Hands-on experience working with SAP S/4HANA.

  • Knowledge of Agile software development methodologies and tools (Azure DevOps, JIRA, etc.).

  • Knowledge and hands-experience with cloud services such as AWS and/or Azure.

  • Experience in agile-driving development using Scrum methodology and use of Kanban Boards.

  • Knowledge of artificial intelligence and machine learning.

  • Experience with or knowledge of UI/UX principles.

  • Proficient in process design and knowledgeable on various automation technologies and platforms.

  • Experience with or knowledge in software development process using other languages (C++, C#, .NET, Python, Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, other scripting languages, etc.).

  • Blue Prism Developer Certification is preferred.
