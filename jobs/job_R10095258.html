ACME Engineering Mission Systems is currently hiring for a Principal or Senior Principal Supply Chain Procurement Specialist to join our Airborne Multifunction Sensors Division Global Supply Chain team, primarily supporting space programs.  This position is located in Springfield, MD .with a hybrid option(preferred). May also consider full time remote.

Job Duties:

  • Sources and procures goods and services of a commodity nature from many suppliers.
  • Initiates Requests for Quotations and Proposals and analyzes proposed pricing from potential suppliers.
  • In conjunction with quality organization, evaluates and monitors supplier quality and reliability, as well as supplier ability to support required delivery schedules.
  • Works with internal customers to ensure specifications are appropriate for the services or products required. Identifies opportunities to reduce cost, improve efficiency and manage risk through targeted negotiations.
  • Issues purchase orders and manages its life cycle.
  • Manages performance of purchase orders, ensures compliance with all public law requirements as well as company (and business) policies and procedures including import/export regulations.
  • Employs sound business practices and assesses supplier performance as part of follow-up activities. Ensures that appropriate documentation is provided to logistics/transportation& warehouse organization so that proper receipt is recorded.
  • Reviews and approves supplier payments as needed.
