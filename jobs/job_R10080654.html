Principal Configuration Analyst (Software Focus)
R10080654
1st Shift (United States of America)

Triton Systems Engineering, Integration & Test (SEIT) Configuration/Change Data Management (CDM) team has an opening for a Principal Configuration Analyst.

Responsibilities will include:

* Perform configuration management and release engineering tasks for all VMS (VMS IMMC/VTC Software, CLS Software, G&C, Avionics), including preparing, authoring, submitting, guiding the VMS Change Notice though the Teamcenter Enterprise Document Release Lifecycle.

* Generate weekly VMS Software Review Board agenda and document minutes. Produce software metric collection/reports based on VMS Software Review Board data.

* Facilitate Software Rework meetings.

* Able to audit software processes and procedures and provide training to the team. Participate as the focal point of contact to prepare for and support SQA/Sector audits.

* Track and manage software licenses, including managing licenses for lab assets, SITS, HB, dev station.  Adding automated build.

* Perform software media releases. Executes builds and releases per program schedules to ensure reproducible software configuration baselines. Prepare code packages for release and disks.

* Provide Change Integration support for CR/ECP.  Verify CR/ECP template is accurate and up to date. Assist with JIRA related tasks for TAM data extraction, reaching out to stake holders, etc.

* Act as the CDM and Contracts liaison.

* Update/verify documents are using up to date templates/ data rights markings to ensure Program and Customer requirements are met.

* Conduct SharePoint pre-team center peer reviews with IPTs and customer.

* Support ClearQuest WTS related activities and conversion to JIRA tickets

* Act as moderator on software reviews.

Basic Qualifications:

  • 6 Years with Bachelors; 4 Years with Masters.
  • Must be able to obtain a DoD Secret clearance or higher.
  • Knowledge of software/hardware configuration, data and change management principles and proficiency in the use of configuration management tools.
  • Must be skilled in Microsoft Word, MS excel.
  • Experience following documented rules and guidelines to ensure technical content meets internal and customer requirements.
  • This position is required to work on-site minimum one day a week (Wednesdays) and as needed.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with and comprehensive understanding of software development, including current SCM industry standards and practices.
  • Excellent communication, interpersonal skills, and the ability to interface with all levels of employees and management.
  • Previous experience in an Aerospace or DoD environment.
  • Experience developing macros and scripts to assist with automating tasks and gathering data for CDRLs.
