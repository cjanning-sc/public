The Northrop Grumman’s Space Sector is looking for a Manager, Programs Level 1 or Level 2 to support the GMD Weapon System (GWS) Program.

POSITION OVERVIEW:

The primary objective of this role will be leading the management of Risks, Issues, and Opportunities (RIOs) to help the program meet delivery of all contractual requirements on cost and on schedule, while achieving all technical requirements and creating customer intimacy to support value creation strategies and actions.

PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Educating and leading the program in conducting thorough risk, issue, and opportunity management practices including identification, analysis, and handling
  • Incorporation of key suppliers into the program risk, issue, and opportunity process
  • Coordination with customer stakeholders in the elevation and management of risks, issues, and opportunities
  • Providing guidance to the program leadership on identifying and managing key risks, issues, and opportunities
  • Analyzing the current risk, issue, and opportunity registers to ensure the execution plan is being followed and implement corrective action where needed.
  • Identifying and incorporating emerging risk associated with contractual changes.
  • Producing and delivering reports and presentations to customers, company leadership, and program leadership on the program risk, issue, and opportunity posture
  • Assist the program cost account managers (CAMs), project managers, and the business office in ensuring risks, issues, and opportunities are correctly represented in the program earned value management (EVM) and financial reporting.
  • Ensuring program team understands and adheres to the risk, issue, and opportunity execution plan scope, and manages risks, issues, and opportunities through risk board activities.
  • Additional Program Management duties as needed to support the GWS Program Office

This requisition may be filled at either a level 1 or a level 2 Program Manager

Basic Qualifications:

  • Manager, Programs Level 1: Bachelors’ degree and 5 years’ experience or Master’s degree and 3 years’ experience supporting U.S. Government contracts and customers and/or project management in other industries.
  • Manager, Programs Level 2: Bachelor’s degree and 9 years’ experience or a Master’s degree and 7 years’ experience supporting U.S. Government contracts and customers and/or project management in other industries.
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Secret Clearance
  • Familiar or experienced with risk management, to include an understanding of risk management principles and techniques.
  • Demonstrated success leading teams/and or organizations to achieve a common goal.
  • Demonstrated self-initiative and conceptual management skills.
  • Experience with earned value management (EVM).
  • Strong communication skills.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Proven extensive experience with risk management, to include developing risk management plans and implementing risk management processes.
  • Engineering, business, or programmatic experience supporting Ground-based Midcourse Defense (GMD) or Missile Defense Agency (MDA) programs.
  • Active Secret Clearance.
  • Agile development methodologies experience.
  • Financial forecasting experience.
  • Producing and presenting senior executive level briefings experience.
  • Experience developing and maintaining direct customer relationships.
