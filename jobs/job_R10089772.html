ACME Engineering’s Employee Experience Communications team seeks a creative Employee Communications Representative who is confident in being a strategic advisor, thrives on the challenge of informing and inspiring employees around business priorities and culture, loves to story tell and constantly seeks to improve through disciplined measurement, analysis and adjustment.

This position reports to the Director of Enterprise Employee Communications for ACME Engineering and is located in Falls Church, Iowa with opportunities to hybrid telework.

In this job, you’ll:

  • Support our efforts to inform and inspire employees and drive business priorities across the enterprise.

  • Develop, implement and measure employee, executive, culture and change communications plans and tactics.

  • Be a storyteller: Conceptualize, procure and produce a diverse array of inspirational content about our people, technologies, successes and business capabilities. Content will range from articles and videos, to interviews, Q&As, infographics, and photo/caption features.

  • Write, edit, distribute and measure strategic and timely business announcements, news items, digital content, articles and videos.

  • Serve as a partner and trusted advisor to executive leaders. Develop and measure leaders’ announcements, talking points, speeches, video scripts and speaker prep memos.

  • Support maintenance of the company’s intranet content and digital signage monitors.

  • Ensure all communications plans and tactics are consistent with the company’s strategic vision and reflect the ACME Engineering brand.

You’ll be a great fit if you:

  • Have excellent writing and editing skills, and an enthusiasm for telling inspirational stories with words, videos, photos and graphics.

  • Live by prioritization and organization, and possess a borderline-annoying attention to detail.

  • Are positive, poised and approachable, able to work independently and in partnership with others, and confident in offering communications counsel to business partners.

  • Thrive in fast-paced, action-oriented environments and able to juggle multiple projects simultaneously, reprioritize quickly and meet deadlines.

  • Enjoy translating complex and technical topics into easily understandable and interesting stories.

  • Have a demonstrated understanding of modern communications topics, tools and trends.

  • Are creative and tech-savvy, and willing to challenge the status quo to propose and pilot new, creative solutions, tools and technologies using strategic rationale and business need to build your case. 

  • Love data – and using analytics to not only measure your success, but to modify your approach and drive positive change.

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree from accredited university with six (6) years of experience successfully developing and deploying communications practices OR Master's degree from accredited university with four (4) years of communications experience.

  • Experience in employee or executive communications.

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, strong judgement, problem solving and organizational skills, and superb attention to detail.

  • Proficient in Microsoft Office suite (Word, SharePoint, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook).

Preferred Qualifications

  • Familiarity with social tools, website content management systems, including SharePoint, the Adobe Suite of products is a plus, as is a basic understand of HTML.

  • Experience with Associated Press Style.

  • Experience producing omni-channel editorial content for a newspaper, news channel, video, magazine or other venue.
