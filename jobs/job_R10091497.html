ACME Engineering Tactical Space Systems Division is seeking a well-qualified Tier 1 Red Hat / Windows Systems Administrator providing 50-70% On-Site and on Call 24x7 support to join its dynamic team of technical professionals in Indianapolis, CO.

Roles and responsibilities will include but may not be limited to:

 

  • Provide assistance to fellow team members and end users in investigation, diagnoses, and

    problem resolution in support of real-time spacecraft operations.

  • Work across disciplines to isolate issues between software, firmware, and hardware, between

    software and operating systems, between networks and applications, and between one-time

    events and recurrent systematic problems

  • Communicate effectively verbally and in writing to include, but not limited to, creation of

    process documentation and How to Guides.

  • Install, configure, troubleshoot and maintain network services.

  • Provide feedback to the development and sustainment teams leading to improvements in

    product stability and functionality.

  • Perform overall administration of Windows client workstations and the associated LINUX

    servers

  • Perform as a Windows System Administrator for a classified government contract.

  • Maintain smooth operation of multi-user computer systems, including coordination with

    network, software, and system engineers, PC desktop technicians, project managers, end

    users, and customer and IT management

  • Install, configure, and perform maintenance of virtual environments to include virtualized

    servers and VDI Desktops.

  • Analyze internal or external customer requirements and determine equipment and software

    requirements for solutions.

  • Create and maintain user and computer accounts and modify file permissions and security

    access lists.

  • Adhere to strict Information Systems security guidelines in all cases.

  • Work after hours and weekends as needed

  • Oversee daily maintenance and Provide Tier 1 troubleshooting and Support in OpenStack

  • environment

  • Assisting ISSM/ISSO with running security scans (ex ACAS)

  • Assisting ISSM/ISSO with reviewing Splunk Security logs

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s IT degree with minimum of  6 years of experience OR Master's degree with minimum of 4 year of experience

    or Associate's IT degree and minimum 8 years of experience

  • Must have U.S. Citizenship with a current or active Top Secret level clearance and the ability to obtain SCI level access.

  • Experience in LINUX (RHEL 7) Server Administration, OpenStack Administration, and VMWare administration.

  • Experience with Unix Power Scripting (BASH)

  • Ability to follow a checklist or an established troubleshooting routine for connection or software issues

  • Familiar with the concepts of server and desktop virtualization, or experience with low level administration of an environment

  • Ability to document system configurations, create logical visio diagrams of physical or network layout, write procedures

 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Red Hat Certified Systems Administrator (RHCSA) certification.

  • Extensive experience with DNS, Active Directory, GPO's, and LDAP configuration.

  • Experience with Backup systems and methods.

  • Experience supporting real-time operations, particularly spacecraft operations.

  • Current Security + Certification
