  • Job Description

    Northrop Grumman has an opening for a Level 2 Proposal Analyst or a level 3 Principal Proposal Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is in Rancho Bernardo, California or Palmdale, California . This position is 100% on-site.

    Essential Functions:

    • This role will be a key member of proposal teams that support a variety of proposal activities (Firm Proposals, Rough Order of Magnitude (ROMs), pricing exercises) to meet program goals
    • Candidates for this position will lead/support proposal activities of diverse and inclusive teams with high levels of engagement and collaboration with other team members
    • They will demonstrate a broad skill set that is balanced across interpersonal, business, and proposal-based capabilities; a commitment to ethics and integrity; and the capability to innovate in a challenging and dynamic environment
    • Develops, plans and schedules proposals
    • Compiles cost, schedule, and technical elements in the development of proposals in accordance with request for proposal (RFP) specifications
    • Coordinates the collection and accumulation of cost, scheduling, and performance elements from functional departments
    • Researches and analyzes historical data, develops cost models to support proposal efforts
    • Coordinates activities and participates in fact-finding meetings
    • Participates in should-cost and pricing reviews with management and customer representatives
    • Participates as a member of the contract negotiating team

    Basic Qualifications for a Level 2 Proposal Analyst:

    • Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited university and 3 years of experience in Pricing & Estimating, Business Management, Contract Management, or Proposal experience; OR a Master's Degree with 1 year of stated experience
    • Ability to obtain/maintain Secret Clearance

    Basic Qualifications for a Level 3 Principal Proposal Analyst:

    • Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited university and 6 years of experience in Pricing & Estimating, Business Management, Contract Management, or Proposal experience OR a Master’s Degree from an accredited university and 4 years of stated experience
    • Ability to obtain/maintain Secret Clearance

    Preferred Qualifications:

    • Experience working within Proposals
    • Knowledge of the government regulations FAR and DFARS
    • Experience working with Basis of Estimate (BOE) and analyzing BOE quality
    • Experience with Excel Pivot Tables, VBA coding and Macros
    • Experience with parametric modeling (such as SEER-SIM, PRICE True Planning, etc.)
    • Proficient in using ProPricer
    • Experience conducting cost analysis and risk modeling
    • Excellent oral and written communication skills
    • Ability to effectively manage competing priorities and deadlines
    • Ability to handle multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment
    • Excellent problem solving and organizational skills
    • Ability to obtain a DoD Secret or Top Secret/SCI level security clearance, to include a Single Scope Background Investigation, a periodic reinvestigation, or an original adjudication date completed within the last 6 years
