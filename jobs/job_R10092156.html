Heating, Ventilating, Air Conditioning, and Refrigeration Mechanic
R10092156
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking a Heating, Ventilating, Air Conditioning, and Refrigeration Mechanic to join our team. This position is located in Linthicum, MD. The mechanic will:

 

Perform a variety of skilled maintenance tasks including but not limited to installation, operation, maintaining, diagnosis, and repair of all heating, ventilating, air conditioning, refrigeration, cooling systems, and  related electrical work.

1. Contact management, engineering, suppliers, or equipment manufactures to obtain work orders, drawings, wiring diagrams, schematics, manufacturers specifications, catalogs, and oral or written instructions to perform the tasks required for this job. Verify information and report any discrepancy to management.
2. Operate, maintain, and repair all equipment related to heating, ventilating, air conditioning, cooling, and refrigeration systems including environmental test chambers and related equipment.
3. Modify and repair associated piping/tubing by bending, cutting, threading, soldering, brazing or gluing.
4. Analyze and repair electrical circuits and electronic malfunctions on all heating, ventilating, air conditioning, and refrigeration systems including environmental test chambers.
5. Modify existing air conditioning and refrigeration equipment including Environmental Test Chambers to utilize the new Environmental Protection Agency approved refrigerants.
6. Check and adjust all systems for proper operation after repairs or modifications.
7. Utilize the computer to make adjustments or changes to the Energy Management System.
8. Repair and maintain all pneumatic and electronic controls related to this job description.
9. Maintain equipment and work areas in a clean and orderly condition.
10. Work along with contractors or vendors to accomplish the goals set forth by management.
11. Must possess applicable licenses as required by management.
12. Follow all Northrop Grumman safety rules and regulations including but not limited to lock out tag out procedure, confined space entry, wearing steel toe shoes, driving company vehicles, and other safe practices and personal protective equipment to insure a safe working environment for all employees.

This is an IBEW Union Represented position.

Basic Qualifications:

- Education requirement: High School graduate or recognized equivalent

- Experience requirement: Must possess Journeyman HVAC license. Must pass a formal, structured interview.
