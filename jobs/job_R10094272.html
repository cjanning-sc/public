Northrop Grumman Defense Systems sector (NGDS) is seeking a Business Development Director to join our growing team of diverse professionals at our sector headquarters in Linthicum, Maryland, Falls Church, VA, or as a telecommuter. This role will be accountable for leading our business development team and successfully growing the sector by focusing on market expansion opportunities, shaping captures and opportunities, developing robust business development strategies aligned to our three business divisions and providing a synthesized and integrated approach to maximize our portfolio’s capabilities.

The ideal candidate will Identify and implement business development projects relating to new products and/or industries, technologies, or services. Identify markets, licensing opportunities, and determines the feasibility of product development. Performs economic analyses on new or improved product opportunities and/or business models and coordinates with internal departments in identifying/implementing business development projects.

The candidate will work closely with strategy, our technology development team, communications and business development and execution leadership in all three Sector divisions and the Sector staff.

The candidate will also build strong, enduring and mutually supportive relationships with other Northrop Grumman Sectors and the Corporate CGBO office. The candidate will additionally work closely with the three businesses and their assigned business development and strategy professionals.

The candidate must have the knowledge and the collaborative skills to ensure each market is optimally oriented for growth and aligned to how we engage with our customers to meet their demands and grow our business. This person will also be responsible for driving the Sector’s growth and marketing objectives by increasing our pipeline resiliency, velocity to qualify opportunities, and the ability to maximize our response to include a full-portfolio perspective.

Responsibilities include:

  • Leadership of diverse team of business development and capture professionals
  • Develops and executes a business development strategy and plan for the Sector with the objective of growing our relationship and business pipeline
  • Identify new markets combined with either existing or new product offerings and work closely with the business to develop and shape the requirements and opportunities to maximize profitability
  • Interact comfortably and effectively with senior strategic customers, decision makers, third party partners, and officers within Northrop Grumman
  • Collaboratively executes the sector’s strategic growth plan
  • Manages multi-division priorities and pipeline coordination, and lead determination efforts throughout the sector
  • Assesses opportunities available within and beyond existing markets, offerings, and business models
  • Scan the environment for business opportunities, maintains surveillance over market, and work with the Strategy team to perform financial analysis
  • Understand, shape, and monitor profitability, productivity, and growth targets
  • Prepare acquisition proposals, recommend financial and nonfinancial strategic objectives and alternatives, and implement and maintain strategic and operational plans

Basic Qualifications:

  • One of the following: Bachelor's degree in business, engineering, sciences or related field with a minimum of 10 years of related experience, or a Master’s degree in business, engineering, sciences or related field with a minimum of 8 years of related experience
  • A minimum of 5 years of experience in C4, weapons and/or logistics business, or other areas across military and government domains
  • Experience in systems/services contracting with U.S. DoD programs and customers
  • Proven relationships and credibility in the federal contracting industry
  • Must have demonstrated skills in leadership and developing a talent pipeline
  • Must have or be able to obtain and maintain a U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) security clearance up to Top Secret
  • The ability to work on-site at a sector location in Maryland or Northern Virginia (up to 50% of the time)
  •  The ability to travel as needed (up to 50% of the time)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree in business, engineering, sciences or related field or related discipline
  • Experience working within the Department of Defense, Military branches, or civilian government roles
  • Program management and/or operational experience
