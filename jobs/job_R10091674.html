ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a CAD CAM Engineer / Principal CAD CAM Engineer to join the Command and Launch Team. This position is located in Detroit, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. The position may be filled at either level.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will plan, lay out, draw, and revise detailed part, assembly, and installation engineering drawings from basic layouts, established references, and standard engineering data. Candidate will apply basic knowledge of design and analysis tools and employ the application of general standards, principals, theories, concepts, and techniques to perform professional tasks.  Candidate will utilize computer-aided design equipment and/or graphic tools such as Siemens NX, CATIA, Creo, AutoCAD. Candidate will also complete details of major layout drawings prepared by others in accordance with standard engineering drafting practices.  The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.  Candidate must be able to collaborate closely with design engineers. Specific responsibilities include:

  • Determine scaling

  • Insert necessary reference planes, centering and dimension lines, hidden line interpolations, and sectioning

  • Prepare specifications for parts, assemblies, and installations

  • Prepare complete production detail drawings

  • Contribute design modifications to improve quality of product or facilitate manufacturing operations.

This positions standard work schedule is a 9/80. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off. Relocation assistance may be available.
