National Security Space (NSS) programs include technical roles at the mission, segment, subsystem and component levels.  They span the entire program life cycle from early concept studies, design development, system integration/test, launch and early operations, mission operations, sustainment and system disposal.  Programs range from small feasibility studies to medium-sized on orbit technology demonstrations and large-scale national system programs. 

Systems Engineering responsibilities include performing technical planning and analyses at all levels of total system product to include: concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal. The Systems Engineer ensures the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints.  

This role is for a production systems engineer for the Ammolite program. Typical job duties will include:

  • Assisting in production tool development(DES and 3D CAD Simulation Tools)

  • Coordinating Facility Development and Readiness

  • MGSE tracking

  • Supporting Test Documentation Development

  • Developing serialization plan for high yield production and identifying opportunities to streamline and find efficiencies.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have U.S. Citizenship and be eligible for a Top Secret Clearance
  • Bachelor's degree in an applicable technical field and 5 years systems engineering experience

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current or Active TS/SCI Clearance
  • Experience with a space development program
  • Experience working with a cross-discipline team
  • Experience with Jira, TipQA, Failure investigations and non-conformance processing
