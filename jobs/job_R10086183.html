ACME Engineering Space Systems is Principal Data Scientist/ Sr Principal Data Scientist to join the Command and Launch Team (CLT).   This position is in Madison, WI and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.   Final grade level offered will be based on candidate level of experience.

What You’ll Get To Do:

As a Data Scientist with Sentinel, you will be part of the team that is responsible for designing, developing, and validating analytical models and algorithms to support Mission Analysis and Design (M&D). Your responsibilities will include:

  • Using mathematical and statistical methods to analyze data and make recommendations
  • Developing data-driven models to support M&D activities
  • Staying current on data mining/machine learning/artificial intelligence (AI) techniques and software tools
  • Serving as an expert on the analysis and interpretation of data to support the development of products, processes, or systems
  • Conducting trade studies related to projects, processes, products, or services
  • Construct models in TensorFlow or PyTorch and adapt existing models to address our stakeholder's problem space
  • Assist with the development of an end-to-end machine learning pipeline and manage and support maintenance and lifecycle management throughout the pipeline's lifecycle
  • Support technical leadership by mentoring junior engineers in the areas of machine learning, data science, and artificial intelligence
  • Network with other ACME Engineering data science professionals to modernize our team's AI/ML methodologies and keep pace with industry best practices

  • US Citizenship with active DoD Secret clearance with a reinvestigation date occurring in the last 5 years. and the ability to obtain Special Program Access (SAP)
  • Knowledge of data gathering, cleansing and transformation techniques
  • Proficiency with Python

  • Proficiency with R and SQL

  • Previous experience with containerized environments (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes, OpenShift)

  • Experience with version control software such as Git and TortoiseSVN
