The Global Surveillance Division at Northrop Grumman has an opening for a Senior Manager (Manager Program Control 3) located in San Diego, CA. This Senior Manager position leads a talented team of cost management individuals that support the Triton program. Overall responsibilities include financial planning & reporting management, cost and schedule performance management, earned value management, risk and opportunity management, and all strategic and indirect management associated with the Triton program. Additional key responsibilities include (but are not limited to):

  • Manage staffing needs, including: hiring/training/developing employees, assigning work scope to employees, and providing daily direction to employees
  • Collaborate across other functional teams to ensure the success of program priorities
  • Interface with a multitude of internal and external customers, including: sector/division/program leadership (e.g. VPs/Directors), government customers (e.g. USAF), and auditing agencies (e.g. DCMA and Deloitte).
  • Oversee the development, implementation, and management of program budgets to enable programs to manage performance
  • Maintain compliance to internal and external policies and procedures (e.g. ANSI/EIA-748 32 guidelines, FAR, SOX, etc.)
  • Assist in financial planning/reporting/analysis, including: profitability assessments, financial forecasting, and cash management.

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution
  • At least 9 years of Business Management work experience or 7 years of experience with a Masters degree.
  • Prior managerial experience required
  • Aerospace industry experience with ANSI/EIA-748 Earned Value Management (EVM) guidelines
  • Working knowledge of EVM systems, including: Cobra, MPM, wInsight, or similar systems
  • Proficient in Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint and Word applications
  • Ability to obtain/maintain a DoD secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications

  • 10+ years Business Management work experience, and an MBA or other related master’s degree
  • Familiarity with cost and schedule management processes and tools
  • Advanced ability in Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint and Word applications
  • Experience developing and presenting business strategies, financial information, and risk assessments to leadership teams
  • Experience interfacing with government audit agencies such as DCMA and DCAA, as well as accounting auditing agencies such as Deloitte
  • Experience utilizing SAP, Hyperion (or related financial software), MPM/Cobra, and wInsight
  • Current DoD Top or Secret Clearance
