ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Supplier Management Team Principal Project Management/ Supplier Management Team Sr Principal Project Management. This position will be located in Detroit, Michigan and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. 

What You’ll Get To Do: 

Candidate will facilitate ongoing projects and serve as a liaison between Program Management, Supplier Management, Engineering, Cost Management and other disciplines in support of C&L/LE.

Serves as part of the C&L Supplier Management Team contributors for current and future projects. This team acts as the main point of contact for technical decision making for suppliers within Launch Equipment for the Command and Launch Segment of the Sentinel Program. Provides financial control and maintenance of budgets including EVMS metrics and Variance Explanations as a member of the Control Account Manager team.

Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Prepares and manages schedules and prepares status reports on the health of the projects. Interprets schedules from suppliers to understand impact on program schedule and milestones.
  • Identifies risks and opportunities and coordinates with project teams to mitigate and retire risks.
  • Develops and maintains Requests for Proposal (RFP) to include reviewing & updating Supplier Statements of Work (SSOW), reviewing Basis of Estimates (BOEs), performing quality checks on all proposal submittals, attending proposal related reviews.
  • Evaluates issues and develops resolutions to meet productivity, quality, and customer satisfaction goals and objectives.
  • Prepares higher level management briefings and develops mechanisms for monitoring project progress and tools for intervention and problem solving with project managers, line managers, and customers.
  • Candidate must be an independent self-starter able to work with multi-disciplined teams (internal and external) to analyze and solve problems in order to meet contractual requirements
  • Must possess excellent interpersonal, written, and oral communication skills

Job Qualifications: 

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

  • Principal Project Management - 6 Years of experience with Bachelor’s degree; 4 Years with Masters.  4 additional years of experience maybe considered in lieu of the degree.
  • Sr Principal Project Management - 10 Years of experience with Bachelor’s degree; 8 Years with Masters.  4 additional years of experience maybe considered in lieu of the degree.
  • Active DoD Secret Clearance with a reinvestigation date occurring in the last 6 years.
  • US Citizenship and the ability to obtain Special Program Access (SAP) within 365 days of submission.
  • Experience leading a team in successful completion of projects, which may include program management/ project scheduling, working experience in a manufacturing, production, facilities or engineering role.
  • Experience in a role with oversight or review of a subcontract or supplier.
  • Project / Program Management skills (proposal creation, scheduling, budget tracking).
  • Experience managing multiple projects concurrently in meeting customer requirements/expectations.

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

  • 8-10 years of experience in a similar role or a Master’s degree in Management, Project Management or Business Administration.
  • Sufficient knowledge of company project management standards, policies, procedures and guidelines to include supplier management and employee management.
  • Natural relationship builder able to earn the trust of colleagues and suppliers.
  • Proven ability to deliver effective communication verbally, written, and through well-crafted presentations across all levels of an organization.
  • Experience in a role with oversight or review of a subcontract or supplier worth more than $10M.
  • Experience on an Engineering and Manufacturing Development.
  • Ability to demonstrate independent creative thinking and problem-solving capabilities.
