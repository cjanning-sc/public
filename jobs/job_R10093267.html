The Mechanical Engineering & Ground Support Department (ME&GS) within Integration, Test & Launch (IT&L) is looking for a project manager who will provide technical leadership to space programs.

RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:

Oversees the execution of the RT&P AOP/LRSP capital projects implementation, including:

  • Serves as primary interface between Operations/Facilities and RT&P Operating Unit

  • Develops and provides the RT&P technical and facility requirement set for each capital project, including validation that plans and designs meet RT&P requirements

  • Is responsible for opening and managing the RT&P capital projects within the CCAR system, including all required paperwork, approvals / cost accounting structure in accordance with the BU AOP/LRSP.

  • Supports facilities / capital project trades on behalf of RT&P (cost, schedule & risk development/assessment)

  • Coordinates facility access requests through RT&P channels

  • Coordinates facility access for project execution through RT&P channels

  • Drives facilities capital projects forward, including timely clearing of roadblocks and elevation to leadership (as required)

  • Provides weekly project schedule & budget updates & status to the OU (& BU as required)

  • Prepares content for weekly and monthly operating unit rhythm (& executive reviews as required)
