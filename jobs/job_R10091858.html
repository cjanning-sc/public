Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Systems Engineer Architect / Principal SE Architect. This position will be located in Roy, UT, or Huntsville, AL, and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

Position Responsibilities:

The selected job candidate will be joining the Model Based - Requirements, Integration, and Architecture (MB-RIA) team. MB-RIA has opportunities to work in eleven different weapons system focus areas. This team is responsible for verification, architecture, and model governance of the GBSD weapon system. The verification portion of the team is responsible for system and segment requirements, requirements management and updates, and verification. The architecture portion of the team provides oversight for integration and delivery of architecture models and develops weapon system architecture. While the model governance portion of the team directs the system model methodology definition, model best practices documentation, and model configuration management. This team will provide candidates the opportunity to integrate complex design elements across multiple Product Teams of the GBSD weapon system and work with all levels of the weapon system to ensure a comprehensive weapon system design.

The selected candidate will be supporting the governance team within MB-RIA to develop and implement program-wide model governance.

  • Support MBSE strategy and implementation over the various Architecture Model
  • Implement and enforce model governance at all levels of the architecture
  • Coordinate with sub-integrated product architecture teams for compliance with contractual SOW and established program model governance
  • Coordinate with integrated product teams for establishing content within the architecture
  • Define and execute model integration processes, tools, and techniques
  • Be key stakeholder in the development and implementation of model governance throughout the GBSD program
  • Engage in formal and informal mentoring to support knowledge transfer and talent development.
