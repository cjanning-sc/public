Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible.

ACME Engineering Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) has an opening for a Staff System Architect Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position can be based out of Columbus, CA., or 100% remote within the United States.

The Staff Systems Architect Engineer will concurrently lead, support, and facilitate strategic operational improvement and transformational initiatives across SSSD. In this role, you will help achieve corporate business goals and strategic objectives by devising creative solutions and innovative ideas, creating supporting documentation and coordinating implementation. You will independently lead and support these initiatives from inception to completion by leveraging methods and tools consistent with industry standards. You will analyze business process documentation to identify opportunities for streamlining and improvements. Help design and develop process architecture, and define key capabilities and performance requirements. Defines design and technology maturity constraints of the system. Defines system implementation approach and operational concept. Ensures requirements are met and evaluates performance with customer. You will lead technical meetings with subject matter experts to capture best practices and lessons learned, and integrate those into existing processes and future state processes. You will use process mapping software (such as Nimbus) to develop and maintain process and data models; specifically to define “current state” and transform into the “future state”.

Essential Functions:

  • Lead in the facilitation of technical meetings to establish future state process architecture and details

  • Architect and map/draw process diagrams, using business modeling tools (Nimbus), while discussing with subject matter experts

  • Conduct business process analysis in order to identify areas of improvement

  • Analyze existing process documentation to parse out content according to process model needs and SSSD document hierarchy

  • Develop hierarchical and sequential process modeling concepts/techniquesTrain and lead
