ACME Engineering’s Aeronautics Systems sector has an opening for an Electronics Engineering Manager 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in Cincinnati, CA. This position will manage the Avionics Integration Engineering home room as well as support one of ACME’s program efforts.

Essential Functions:

  • Ensuring members have sufficient statement of work, making decisions regarding awards, balancing resources among programs, and verifying proper charging practices are followed
  • Managing member performance, creating and delivering performance evaluations, overseeing the salary review process, and supervising employee development and mentoring efforts (including engagement strategies for higher performers and corrective actions for lower performers)
  • Coordinating with programs to ensure appropriate staffing/de-staffing actions are developed and executed relative to their section, and managing employee movement between programs
  • Making sure members are familiar with and use required engineering processes and tools
  • Meeting indirect budget targets and interpreting higher level guidance for implementation within the organization.

The people management responsibilities described above, are expected to account for anaverage of 20% of the candidate’s time. The remaining percentage of the candidate’s time is to perform project assigned engineering responsibilities within the Special Programs Communications IPT. For candidates currently assigned to the Special Programs Communications IPT, any tasking to be reassigned, (if any), must be coordinated with candidate’s current project lead.

As a leader, you always act with integrity, and the highest ethical standards characterize everything you do. You enable your team to achieve predictable and balanced results, while satisfying the needs of internal and external partners. You treat all people with respect, and you model personal growth and continuous development. You know how to build an inclusive environment that attracts, retains, and inspires a diverse and engaged team. You develop trusted and valued customer relationships and communicate effectively, so that people throughout the organization feel engaged and connected to their work. You deliver excellence, strive for continuous improvement, and respond vigorously to change.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM discipline with 9 years of relevant experience; OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM discipline with 7 years of relevant experience.
  • Demonstrated experience leading and motivating a team
  • Demonstrated experience in avionics with a focus on payload, RADAR, or communication systems and subsystems.
  • Ability to obtain and maintain DoD Secret clearance within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.
  • Ability to obtain and maintain Program Access (PAR) within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs. This PAR is required to be obtained prior to the first day of employment.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s Degree in Engineering discipline
  • Highly proficient in technical team management of avionics engineers and direct customer relations. Demonstrated experience in leading complex technical projects to successful completion by meeting technical, cost and schedule requirements and satisfying customer expectations.
  • Current DoD Secret clearance or Interim DoD Secret clearan
  • Current active Program Access
