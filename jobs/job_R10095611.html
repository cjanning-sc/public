Physical Security Systems for Access Control Intrusion Detection & Video - ISA2/3
R10095611
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman is seeking a Physical Security Systems for Access Control Intrusion Detection & Video - ISA2/3 to join the Technical Security team member. Daily duties will include programming, troubleshooting, and running reports for DMP Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS), Lenel Physical Access Control Systems (PACS), various Camera systems and MASterMind alarm monitoring software.  Additional duties will include identifying and implementing technical solutions for these various systems. Also included will be the review and completion of physical security requirements and assessments as they pertain to Northrop Grumman facilities.

This position can be filled at either a level 2 or level 3.

Basic Qualifications Industrial Security Analyst 2:

3 Years of Security or related experience with Bachelor’s; or 1 Year with Masters; an additional 4 years of applicable work experience may be substituted for Bachelor’s.

                                      

Top Secret Clearance with ability to obtain SCI.

2+ Years of Experience with the Microsoft Office Suite; including MS Word, MS EXCEL and/or MS Project.

2 years experience working with Access Control Systems.

6 Years with Bachelor’s; or 4 Year with Masters; an additional 4 years of applicable work experience may be substituted for a Bachelor’s degree.

Top Secret with ability to obtain SCI.

3+ Years of Experience with the Microsoft Office Suite; including MS Word, MS EXCEL and/or MS Project.

3 years experience working with Access Control Systems.

TS/SCI Clearance.

2+ Years troubleshooting Lenel Access Control, IDS, Digital Camera Systems issues.

Ability to lead and manage projects related to IDS, Lenel, Video Systems and other physical/technical security related systems.
