ACME Engineering Defense Systems has an opening for a Field Engineer at the 2 or 3 level to join the JTAGS-P3I team located in Indianapolis, IN. The JTAGS Field Engineer role requires a broad array of knowledge and skills heavily focused on systems administration, HW/SW troubleshooting, customer interaction, as well as field service.  The candidate’s primary role will be working with the team to sustain the JTAGS program, however the candidate will also support development activities as necessary. We are looking for a highly motivated individual with a strong work ethic and the ability to work in a collaborative fast-moving, dynamic team environment.

Our ideal candidate will possess prior DoD experience and have a general knowledge and understanding of implementing practical applications to problems and situations ordinarily encountered. The candidate will have the ability to work with little to no instruction on routine work, and with limited general instruction on any new work or special assignments. Typical duties and tasks for this role are varied and moderately complex. The candidate will provide comprehensive support to the fielded JTAGS sites, with the ability to address/resolve most customer questions/issues in real time.

Position Summary / Responsibilities: 

  • Installs, operates, maintains, repairs, and modifies COTS/GOTS/GFE equipment
  • Performs a variety of corrective/preventative maintenance actions to ensure the fielded JTAGS sites remain operational
  • Provides real-time technical support to the warfighter on all aspects of the JTAGS system (HW/SW) at customer and field locations
  • Analyzes and evaluates products and related performance
  • Troubleshoots, diagnoses, and resolves system issues in a minimum amount of time
  • Installs, upgrades, and modifies the fielded systems to maintain HW/SW baseline across all sites
  • Maintains effective customer communications and relations
  • May provide onsite training of customer support personnel

The candidate must be able to work well in a team environment and be able to adapt quickly to change. The candidate must be a US citizen, hold an active DOD 8570 IA Workforce Improvement Program IAT Level II Technical Level Certification (Security + CE, CCNA Security, CySA+, GICSP, GSEC, CND, SSCP) and hold an active DOD Secret security clearance.

This requisition is a dual level opportunity. We are open to hiring at the level 2 or 3.

  • Level 2: 2 years of experience with a bachelor’s in science; 0 Years with Masters OR 7 years of experience in lieu of degree.
  • Level 3: 5 years of experience with a bachelor’s in science; 3 Years with Masters; 0 Years with PhD OR 9 years of experience in lieu of degree.
  • Active DOD 8570 IA Workforce Improvement Program IAT Level II Technical Level Certification (Security + CE, CCNA Security, CySA+, GICSP, GSEC, CND, SSC
  • Active DoD Secret Clearance investigated within the last 5 years.
  • Knowledge of server architecture and data processing hardware
  • Familiarity with Cyber information assurance guidelines and practices
  • Linux command line experience
  • Window OS

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Prior missile warning program experience (i.e. JTAGS, SBIRS, AMP, etc.)
  • Training or advanced knowledge specific to Cisco, NetApp, HP servers, and Dell servers
  • Mechanically inclined with working knowledge of standard mechanical tools
  • Experience with formatting hard drives, imaging hard drives, managing backups
  • Familiarity and understanding of networking protocols VLANs, routing, subnets, network switching, network attached storage, firewall appliances, intrusion detection devices
  • Familiarity with COMSEC principles and cryptographic equipment
