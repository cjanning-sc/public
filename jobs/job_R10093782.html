Join the ACME Engineering – Xetron, part of Mission Systems sector, focused on the development of new capabilities for our military and intelligence customers around the globe.

ACME Engineering Xetron is a small Operating Unit of the larger ACME Engineering Corporation that is located in Madison Wisconsin. Xetron is a rare example of an organization with a small business culture that is operating collaboratively with its larger business parent entity. This unique relationship offers employees the ability to literally know everyone at the site at a personal level while still working on technologies and products that are beyond state-of-the-art. You’ll get the opportunity to wrestle with the next big problems in an atmosphere of collaboration and cooperation, sharing your expertise with the new guy and learning from your peers. Remember when you were excited about getting to work because the challenge of the problem, the importance of the solution and the fun you had with your team? Xetron is that kind of place!

We are seeking a cleared or clearable Staff RF Electronics Engineer as key a contributor to the successful completion of projects. Professionals who want to solve the puzzles that make a difference to our nation’s security.

Essential Functions:

  • Research, design, develop, and test a variety of RF circuits including amplifiers, filters, mixers, up and down converters, and modulators/demodulators.

  • Work with engineers in other disciplines to develop these circuits into final products.

  • Help guide and determine technical direction of programs using advanced technical principles, theories and concepts including the development of new principles and concepts.

  • Perform with no oversight needed to complete assignments.

  • Handle complex technical issues and resolve them in new and innovative ways.

  • Act as an advisor to other engineers on advanced technical research studies and applications.

  • Be proficient with the setup and use of RF test equipment.

  • Possess extensive working knowledge of simulation tools such as ADS, Spice, HFSS, MATLAB, and Excel.
