  • Perform as Linux Systems Administrator for a large classified government contract.

  • Maintain efficient operations of the entire IT infrastructure including desktops and potentially servers. 

  • Support multiple networks and ensure necessary IT administration tasks are completed under supervision as necessary.

  • Assist testing new releases of products to ensure compatibility and minimize user and impact.

  • Associate’s degree with 7 years of experience, or a Bachelor’s degree with 5 years of experience, or a Master's degree with 3 years of experience; a High School diploma or equivalent with 9 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a completed degree

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret level security clearance as a condition of employment within a reasonable period of time as determined by the company; maintaining the required security clearance will be a condition of continued employment.

  • Experience working in team of IT professionals under direct supervision.

Preferred Qualifications:
