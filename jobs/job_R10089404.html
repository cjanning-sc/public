Northrop Grumman’s Defense Systems sector is looking for a Program Manager 3 to support the S&SMA business unit. The position will be in Melbourne, FL.

Essential Functions:

Leads and directs all phases of a classified program from inception through completion. Responsible for the cost, schedule and technical performance of program and participates in the negotiation of contract and contract changes. Coordinates the preparation of proposals, business plans, proposal work statements and specifications, operating budgets and financial terms/conditions of contract. Acts as primary customer contact for program activities, leading program review sessions with customer to discuss cost, schedule, and technical performance. Establishes design concepts, criteria and engineering efforts for product research, development, integration and test. Develops new business or expands the product line with the customer. Establishes milestones and monitors adherence to master plans and schedules, identifies program problems and obtains solutions, such as allocation of resources or changing contractual specifications. Directs the work of employees assigned to the program from technical, manufacturing and administrative areas.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have 11 years of relevant job experience in supporting U.S. Government contracts and customers and/or project management in other industries. Will consider a Bachelor’s degree and 7 years of relevant Program Management experience; Or Master’s degree and 5 years of relevant experience.
  • 5 years prior experience leading organizational, intermediate, or depot level logistics and sustainment operations of major defense weapons systems.
  • 5 years prior experience in planning and executing a quality management system to satisfy program/project quality requirements.
  • Must be a US citizen and hold a minimum active DoD Secret security clearance.
  • Ability to obtain and retain Special Program access clearance within 365 days from submission is a condition of employment.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Knowledge of and experience in an Earned Value Management System (EVMS).
  • Top Secret Security Clearance.
