ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Software Engineer. This position will be located in Detroit, Michigan and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program 

What You’ll Get To Do: 

What you will get to do, specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

The qualified applicant will become part of ACME Engineering’s team supporting the Air Force’s Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Duties of this position include deployment and sustainment of network equipment hardware, software and systems for the system test team. The selected candidate will work in a dynamic people-focused environment where he/she will interact with users, customers and engineers across the program.

Essential Functions include:

- Provide support for hardware and software installation and maintenance such as routers, multiplexers, firewalls, hubs, bridges, gateways, etc

- Responsible for installing security patches and adhering to any changes in program security directives

- Assist in user account creation, password unlocks and various day-to-day system operational tasks.

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.
