ACME Engineering Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) has an opening for a Principal Mechanical Engineer within the Mechanical Ground Systems Engineering team. This position will be located in Columbus, OH at Space Park.

As a Principal Mechanical Engineer, a qualified candidate is expected to be proficient in design and analysis principles, processes, and tools. Mechanical Ground Systems Engineers support the design, fabrication, and use of Mechanical Ground Support Equipment (MGSE). MGSE is non-flight hardware used to support the integration, test, and launch of spacecraft and its various stages of assembly. This includes, but is not limited to, lift slings, transportation systems, robotics, zero-g deployment fixtures, pneumatics, propellant loading systems, etc.

Mechanical Ground Support Equipment Designers are responsible for the following:

  • Design and Modeling in 3D CAD (Computer-Aided Design) software

  • Fundamental structural analysis techniques

  • Drafting using CAD software

  • Fabrication support at vendors including acceptance testing and inspection

  • End-user support during integration operations

  • Conceptual design

  • Requirements definition

  • Integration & Test process flow development
