ACME Engineering is looking for a system software integration engineer for the Ruby program to develop and operate a continuous delivery software pipeline and work with developers, system test and external mission users.   
 
Job Responsibilities
-Perform system software integration into a virtualized software integration lab (VSIL).
-Integrate space vehicle software into simulators
-Develop interoperability tests into an automated test suite iteratively with software developers.
-Learn and validate operational procedures for the ground and space system with demonstrations and development of a continuous integration test suite.
 
Candidates must be hands-on engineers in system and software testing and development. Successful candidates will have proven ability to work in a collaborative environment with other team members, adapt quickly, solve problems, and resolve conflict. 
 
Basic Qualifications
-Software development and test experience
-Programming experience  e.g. (C, C++, Ruby, Go)
-Developing and maintaining an automated testing system
-Used git source control

-Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline and 5+ years of engineering experience OR Master’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline and 3+ years of engineering experience OR PhD in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline

-Candidate must have, and be able to maintain, at least ONE of the following active U.S. Government security clearances:
-Current DoD Secret clearance
-Ability to obtain SCI clearance
 
Preferred Qualifications (some of the following and the rest will be learned on the job)
-Continuous Integration experience 
-User experience with Gitlab Ci or Jenkins
-Experience with end-to-end system testing and demonstrations
-Experience with Hardware test bed or system integration lab (SIL)  
-Experience with ground or flight systems software as developer, tester or end-user.
-Simulation software development experience 
-Current SCI clearance
