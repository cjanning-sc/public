Put your skills to the test by pushing the boundaries of what’s possible. From global defense to sustainment and modernization to mission readiness, your experience and ability will make it a reality. Our programs are built on equal parts of curiosity and collaboration. The Aircraft Maintenance and Fabrication Center within Northrop Grumman Defense Systems pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in aircraft maintenance and modification. Our team specializes in platform stewardship for high value, Low Density/High Demand (LDHD) aircraft fleets where quality and schedule drive performance. Come be a part of our mission.

AMFC is part of the System Sustainment and Software Business Unit that designs, manufactures, operates and maintains airborne platforms, sensors and electronic warfare systems for US military services and our international allies.

The Northrop Grumman Aircraft Maintenance and Fabrication Center is seeking a Proposal Manager 1 to join our team.  You will work with teams creating compliant, compelling business proposals that result in winning new business with U.S. and international customers.

This position will be located in either Lake Charles, LA.

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Develops and executes proposal plans, estimates resources (people, schedule, and budget), analyzes RFPs, creates outlines and compliance matrices
  • Coordinates the collection and accumulation of cost, scheduling, and performance elements from functional departments
  • Compiles cost, schedule, and technical elements in the development of proposals in accordance with request for proposal (RFP) specifications
  • Researches and analyzes historical data, develops cost models to support proposal efforts
  • Engages with Capture Manager to craft proposal strategy and develop compelling messaging that addresses the strengths of the Northrop Grumman offering
  • Creates compliant, compelling proposals that clearly and succinctly address the RFP requirements
  • Ensures on-time delivery of proposals and document lessons learned to improve processes.
  • Performs volume lead and writing assignments in support of other proposal managers
  • Works closely with Program Management and Supply Chain Management to ensure supplier quotes comply with requirements.
  • Researches and critically analyzes large volumes of data using spreadsheet and data mining skills to isolate and prioritize key data.
  • Summarizes and presents detailed information in internal management reviews and customer fact-finding meetings.
  • Highly organized, detail oriented and self-motivated with demonstrated abilities to prioritize work and meet tight deadlines.
  • Team player with strong interpersonal, written, and verbal communication skills; ability to present concepts and recommendations to all levels within the organization.
  • Demonstrated ability to think analytically, solve problems and convert complex data to actionable information.
  • Detail oriented, with ability to understand the big picture.
