Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Nondestructive Testing (NDT) Technician 4 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Clearfield, Utah facility. Our team conducts complex NDT inspection processes utilizing automated inspection systems and various manual inspection techniques on composite parts and assemblies. We support multiple prime contractors for military and commercial programs. Requires Ultrasonic certification in accordance with NAS410.

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School Diploma or equivalent GED (education will be verified) and Level 2 Certification per NAS410 in Ultrasonics (certification will be verified).
  • Minimum of 6+ years experience operating automated Ultrasonic systems and manual inspection techniques
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Clearance
  • Ability to obtain a special program access clearance

Preferred Qualifications

  • Efficient at MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and other office systems
  • Must have the skills and knowledge to interpret codes, standards, and other contractual documents that control NDT method(s)
  • Capable of selecting the method and technique for a specific inspection
  • Capable of preparing and verifying the adequacy of procedures and work instructions
  • Capable of approving NDT procedures and work instructions for technical adequacy
  • Capable of conducting NDT for the acceptance of parts and document the results
  • Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to work with a team
  • Excellent communication skills are required
  • Self-Starter and ability to work through an issue to closure
