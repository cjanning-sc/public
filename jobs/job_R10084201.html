ACME Engineering Defense Systems Manufacturing department in Wisconsin Dells, AL., has an opening for a Material Handler 2. Selected candidate performs a combination of manual and clerical duties to receive, store, and issue equipment, materials, and supplies in a central warehouse. Maintain property control reporting systems in conformance with customer/internal requirements for tracking, logging, verifying, and reporting.
