Are you ready to put your experience to work at Northrop Grumman? If so, our Defense Systems Sector wants you to join our Team as an SCA Stock Clerk for inventory, control and movement of property related to the Northrop Grumman Corporation in Rolling Meadows, Illinois.

Key Responsibilities:

- Perform material inventories, general packaging, and shipping related responsibilities.

- Communicate with a diverse group of supervisors, managers, and peers from multiple disciplines and organizational levels.

- Periodically lift and move heavy items.

- Validate all property against receipt documentation; stock items, assign locations and verify physical balance.

- Pick and issue material as necessary to satisfy in-house demands or outbound shipments; assure proper stock rotation using First In-First Out (FIFO) inventory system.

- Accurately transact all movement of property using a computer-based inventory control program.

- Preform physical inventory and input data into the inventory control program; perform general research of material transactions to resolve out of balance discrepancies.

- Communicate with a diverse group of management and peers from multiple disciplines and organizational levels.

- Perform packaging and un-packaging related responsibilities, including loading and unloading trucks sing pallet jacks or a lift truck.

Qualifications – External

Basic Qualifications:

1. High school diploma or equivalent and a minimum of 3 years of experience in inventory or warehousing. Will consider candidate with Bachelors Degree in related field

2. Basic computer knowledge.

3. Ability to safely lift a minimum of 50 lbs. from the floor to waist level.

4. Ability to read, correctly comprehend, and follow written English instructions.

5. Ability to obtain/maintain a Secret Clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:

1. Working knowledge of Microsoft Outlook, Access, and Excel.

2. Experience with general carpentry power and hand tools used to fabricate wooden shipping crates.

3. A current Secret Clearance.
