The DARC Program is currently seeking a Senior Principal Software Engineer to join our team.  The qualified candidate will become part of ACME Engineering’s Space Surveillance & Environmental Intelligence Division within the Space Sector. 

As a Software Engineer you will be Designing, developing, documenting, testing, and debugging software applications and systems that contain logical and mathematical solutions for radar systems.

Responsibilities:

  • Software Model Based Design using Cameo, Rational, or similar product.
  • Write scripts (bash) to move data between components and will often have to debug a disconnected system (data cannot be moved off).
  • Write parsers to translate binary data into standard formats.
  • Develop code and logic to send/receive JMS messages to start/stop processors execution.
  • Debug and troubleshoot at a system level.
  • Perform analysis on systems to determine bottlenecks and latency issues.
  • Quickly prototype potential solutions by coding logic in: script, Java, Python and/or C/C++).

Basic Qualifications Senior Principal Software Engineer:

  • Bachelor's degree with 9 years of related experience, a Master’s degree with 7 years of related experience; a PhD with 4 years of experience; 13 or more years of experience may be considered in lieu of a degree
  • Active/Current Secret Security Clearance or higher
  • Ability to obtain a DoD Top Secret Clearance
  • Proficiency in one or more of the following: Java, C/C++, Python
  • Java, Docker Experience
  • Experience working in an Agile Environment
  • Knowledge of Radar Systems

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in STEM
  • Java, Metlab experience
  • Experience with Radar communications
  • Experience with Agile development and continuous integration and test
  • Demonstrated ability to implement process improvements, streamline activities, and develop efficiencies
  • Previous in-depth domain knowledge of program software design and code
  • Proficiency with DevOps technologies including Jenkins, Fortify, Ansible, Atlassian suite
