ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Staff Structural Engineer to join the Command and Launch Team. This position is located in Detroit, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Final grade level will be based on experience.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The qualified candidate will lead and perform structural analysis on and define structural requirements for the GBSD Launch Equipment (LE) Mechanical Systems (MS).  The role will include the following tasks and responsibilities:

  • Development and capture of engineering specifications for metallic and non-metallic structures for all phases of the product like cycle, from preliminary design to sustainment

  • Conduct structural analysis including loads evaluation and static/dynamic conditions, stress, stability, fatigue and thermal loading

  • Analysis performed using hand calculation or finite element software packages such as ABAQUS and ANSYS

  • Provide instruction/guidance to junior engineers and handle multiple tasks in parallel

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) discipline and 14 years of experience; 12 years with Master’s Degree; 9 years with PhD

  • Experience running static and dynamic structural analysis

  • Proficient post-processing data using MATLAB and EXCEL

  • Proficient technical writer and able to verbally communicate technical content

  • US citizen with ability to obtain a DoD clearance

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

  • Proficiency with ABAQUS, LS-DYNA, and ANSYS

  • Experience with structural test applications

  • Experience with missile programs & with propulsion

  • Active DoD Secret clearance granted or re-investigated within last 6 years
