Develops, plans and schedules proposals. Compiles cost, schedule, and technical elements in the development of proposals in accordance with request for proposal (RFP) specifications. Coordinates the collection and accumulation of cost, scheduling, and performance elements from functional departments. Researches and analyzes historical data, develops cost models to support proposal efforts. Coordinates activities and participates in fact-finding meetings. Participates in should-cost and pricing reviews with management and customer representatives. Participates as a member of the contract negotiating team.

Northrop Grumman has an opening for a Level 3 Principal Proposal Analyst our team of diverse, qualified individuals! This position will be located on site in Melbourne, FL.  There are no options for remote, telework, or flexible work arrangements.

Essential Functions:

  • Develops, plans and schedules proposals
  • Compiles cost, schedule, and technical elements in the development of proposals in accordance with request for proposal (RFP) specifications
  • Coordinates the collection and accumulation of cost, scheduling, and performance elements from functional departments
  • Researches and analyzes historical data, develops cost models to support proposal efforts
  • Coordinates activities and participates in fact-finding meetings
  • Participates in should-cost and pricing reviews with management and customer representatives
  • Participates as a member of the contract negotiating team

Required Qualification (Contingency):

  • Active DoD Secret Clearance

Basic Qualifications for Level 3 Principal Proposal Analyst:

  • Bachelor's degree with 6 years of experience or a Master's Degree with 4 years of experience in the following areas: Proposal Development, Business Administration, Accounting, Finance, Economics, Pricing, Estimating and/or related experience
  • Experience with government proposal process
  • Knowledge and understanding of labor/material estimating, proposal analysis, and proposal adequacy as prescribed in DFARS
  • Experience with Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word & PowerPoint)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • MS Excel with Pivot tables, VLOOKUP and Macros
  • Experience with Propricer, Basis of Estimate Development Software, SAP
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills
  • Ability to effectively manage competing priorities and deadlines
  • Ability to handle multiple tasks in a fast paced environment
  • Excellent problem solving and organizational skills
  • Expertise in financial analysis
  • Knowledge of FAR/DFARS
