ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Supply Chain Technical Coordinator (Clearance Required) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Cincinnati, CA.

Shift: 1st shift on a 4/10 schedule Monday - Thursday from (5:00am to 3:30p.m.)

Essential Functions:

The role supports and assists activities within multiple disciplines across the Supply Chain process. Provides technical, tactical and execution support in various aspects of the industry standard supply chain business process areas primarily Supply Chain Manufacturing and Supply Chain Logistics, as well as Supply Chain Business Operations. Primarily responsible for the manual movement, shipping and receiving of materials.

  • Loads, unloads, and conveys materials and parts to multiple work sites, and performs a combination of tasks under specific direction which may include shipping and/or receiving

  • Opens containers using hand tools and counts and weighs materials and records information

  • Loads and unloads materials onto or from pallets, trays, racks, and machines by hand; loads or unloads materials into or from trucks by hand

  • Works closely with the integration team and shop floor

  • Delivers kits and chemicals and verifies quantities and part numbers

  • Wraps and packs kits to send to on and offsite locations

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School Diploma/GED and 2 years of experience in logistics support, shipping/receiving, manufacturing shop floor, warehousing, and/or inventory control

  • Ability to obtain a forklift certification

  • Ability to lift 25 lbs and walk or stand for extended periods of time

  • Active DOD Secret Clearance with investigation completed in the last 5 years (to apply/start) with the ability to obtain and maintain a Program Special Access within a reasonable period of time, as determined by the company to meet its business needs (after starting)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Prior experience using a forklift/reach lift/pallet jack

  • Prior experience using SAP/MES

  • ACME manufacturing shop floor experience

  • Ability to work overtime as needed to support the business
