ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Maintenance Operations Manager 3 (Manager, Programs 3) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Cincinnati, OH.

In this role, the selected candidate will be responsible for the implementation and execution of maintenance operations and support for manned and/or unmanned systems. Will provide leadership to an operations organization responsible for maintenance training, strategic planning, resource allocation, and system effectiveness metrics. Plan, direct, manage and control the cost, schedule and technical performance of the organization working with cross-functional teams from other ACME Engineering entities. Develop and forecast maintenance operations and execution plans for manpower, training, and support, as well as additional Integrated Logistics Support, including maintenance training concepts, technical publications, system deployment activities, and the strategic plans for the standup of various systems. Analyze maintenance performance indicators, identify trends/root cause and implement corrective actions. Coordinate provisions for maintenance facilities, personnel, and support equipment. Implement repair and resupply concepts and detailed execution plans.

You treat all people with respect, and you model personal growth and continuous development.
