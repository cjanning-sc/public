Northrop Grumman Space Systems is looking for you to join our team as an Integrated Product Team (IPT) Lead/ Cost Account Manager (CAM) based out of Colorado Springs, CO. This position will serve on The Enhanced Polar System Control and Planning Segment (EPS CAPS) Sustainment (ECS) Hardware & Logistics program. The Integrated Product Team (IPT) Lead/ Cost Account Manager (CAM), is responsible for executing the IPT schedule, ensuring the cybersecurity posture, and directing on-site sustainment activities to support mission operations of the EPS system.

This position will lead an engineering team, providing Level 2 sustainment supporting space-enabled, secure communications capabilities to polar users in support of national objectives in the arctic region. 

The ideal candidate will manage engineers and technicians who provide System Administration, Cybersecurity, Ground System Engineering (GSE), and Payload System Engineering support, as well as trainers to support the certification of Level 1 Operations Staff. 

This position is in Colorado Springs, CO supporting the customer’s operational site, reporting to the program office in Redondo Beach, CA. 

What You’ll Get to Do:

  • Serve as IPT Lead on the EPS CAPS program and work with program personnel in sharing technical expertise
  • Support the EPS-R CAPS IPT in sharing and managing resources
  • Perform mission owner coordination and support activities
  • Ensure 24/7 response to payload and ground system anomalies
  • Provide technical peer review and ensure timely CDRL deliveries
  • Ensure adherence to processes and procedures
  • Manage schedules and prepare status reports
  • Perform Control Account Management (CAM) duties using Earned Value Management (EVM) methodologies and tools. Provide status, variance reports, EV metrics, and Estimate At Complete (EAC) on a regular basis per program requirements
  • Work with the technical staff to specify tasking and develop and execute Integrated Master Schedules (IMS). Work with the technical staff to status the IMS
  • Work to targeted cost, schedule, and staffing requirements. Review labor resource and cost actuals on a weekly basis, taking action on any discrepancies
  • Support the development of packages for Change Requests for cost and schedule and present at the Configuration Control Board (CCB)
  • Support and generate staffing requirements, documentation, requisitions, forecasts, and reporting. Facilitate internal meetings to support staffing, ensuring the staffing needs meet the staffing baseline
  • Support RFP, ROM, and ECP
  • Provide functional oversight for a team of systems engineers, integrators, and testers of varied disciplines
  • Provide staffing and workload forecasts; support employee inclusion, engagement, mentoring, and career development; identification and training of employee skills required to successfully execute programs; and execution of employee performance review processes
  • Perform recruiting, sourcing, onboarding, performance management, and contract maintenance for contractors working in the business area as required
  • Demonstrated abilities to build diverse and inclusive teams with high levels of engagement, including mentoring and coaching, are a must
  • 15% CONUS and OCONUS travel is required

Basic Qualifications:

  • 9 Years with Bachelors; 5 Years with Masters; 2 Years with PhD
  • Active Top Secret DoD security clearance
  • Proficient in cost and schedule and have CAM, IPT, or Deputy IPT experience
  • Experience in Satellite/Payload Operations & Ground System Sustainment
  • Certified Cost Account Manager (CAM) experience
  • Experience with IMS and shop schedules
  • Experience w/ MILSATCOM sustainment processes
  • Experience managing diverse teams of hardware, software, technicians, cyber, Ground System Engineers, Training engineers

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD TS/SCI 
  • Familiar with DoD standards, instructions and FAR clauses
  • Experience in Site Manager duties at customer sites
  • Experience with US Space Force operations and processes
  • Experience with ROM and RFP responses
