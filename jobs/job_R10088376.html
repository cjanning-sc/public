ACME Engineering Mission Systems is seeking a General Manufacturing Manager- 2nd shift to join its Mechanical Design and Fabrication Cell team in the Finishing Department within the Manufacturing Organization. This position is located in Chicago, Illinois.

What You'll get to Do:

The General Manufacturing Manager 2 is responsible for the direct supervision of employees and processes in a manufacturing environment. The Manager provides direction to employees utilizing management guidelines and general policies.

Overall responsibilities include:

  • Driving employee efficiency.
  • Meeting product cost and schedule targets.
  • Managing the area to produce quality products.
  • Engaging employees to achieve their highest potential.

This Manager will be responsible for the direct supervision of approximately 15-20 represented (union) employees in a manufacturing environment.

The Manager is expected to work independently across organizations; manage the use of consumables to achieve budget targets; assign and manage employees to maximize efficiency and achieve target productive yields; implement process improvements to improve affordability, quality, and schedule; and perform required SAP/MES transactions to manage Operators/Finishers.

Other Responsibilities include:

  • The Manager will direct area in finishing to include plating, painting, bonding and miscellaneous processes and methodologies to produce quality hardware assemblies.   • Manage the use of consumable to achieve budget target
