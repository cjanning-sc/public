ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Staff Circuit Design Engineer to join the Flight Systems Team. This position is located in Detroit, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will provide technical oversight and leadership for all electrical design activities for the Guidance Computer (GC). The GC is a high reliability, radiation hardened flight controller for the GBSD Aerospace Vehicle (AVE). The GBSD Guidance Computer contains digital processing, digital I/O, and analog actuator/power I/O electronics functions.  The selected individual will be responsible for all aspects of the Guidance Computer electrical design.  The scope spans requirements derivation, design, development, analysis, verification, and production of hardware, plus laboratory and test lab support.  The engineer will work with a subcontractor providing processing hardware and will lead a team of electrical designers performing detailed board, FPGA, ASIC, and test set designs.  This position reports directly to the Guidance Computer IPT manager.
