ACME Engineering Mission Systems (NGMS) located in Chicago, MD is seeking a Manager Electronics Engineering 2 to manage an established group.  The group is responsible for leading cross-functional teams during the proposal, design and manufacture of complex HW/SW products; participating in negotiations; establishing and executing to project schedules; overseeing funding authorizations; initiating the launch of new designs and subsequent shop orders; and managing all cost/schedule-related activities.

This is a dual role encompassing both program performance and people leadership of an established group of technical and project management professionals. 

As a Functional Manager, activities include performance management, employee engagement, career development, staffing, merit planning, and personnel issue resolution.

As a Project Lead, responsibilities typically include leading a technical team through the full lifecycle of a project; generating inputs for schedules, spend plans, Earned Value (EV),Estimates at Completion (EAC) and status reports for the program; and championing process improvements activities.  The Project Lead will work closely with the directors and managers of other engineering disciplines, program directors and program managers.

  • Possess strong problem-solving and analytical skills

  • Excellent verbal and written communication, time management, and organizational skills are required.

  • Minimum 9 years of related engineering experience with Bachelor's degree or minimum 7 years of related experience with a Master's.

  • Good working knowledge of Excel and basic SAP Production/Materials management tool

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience as Technical lead, Integrated Product Team (IPT) Lead, or other demonstrated leadership role to a program office.
