ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Software Engineering, Targeting and NC3 Manager 3 within the Command and Launch IPT for the GBSD program. This leadership role will be located in Detroit, UT.


The successful candidate will:

The Software Engineering Manager will provide leadership and direction within the Command and Launch IPT serving as the Targeting and NC3 Lead.   This manager position is dual-role; directing all phases of select software products from inception through completion and oversight of subcontractor engineering scope. The manager is responsible for the cost, schedule and technical performance of assigned software products and supplier subcontracts. The manager will participate in the negotiation of subcontracts and subcontract changes. Coordinates the preparation of contract deliverables, schedules, and budgets. Acts as primary customer contact, leading program review sessions with program and customer stakeholders to discuss cost, schedule, and technical performance.  Establishes milestones and monitors adherence to master plans and schedules, identifies program problems and obtains solutions, such as allocation of resources or changing contractual specifications.  Directs the work of employees and also serves as a functional engineering manager to a team of software engineers. Develops new business.
