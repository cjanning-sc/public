Your role:

In this new position as a critical member of the Strategy and Future Business team, this is a great opportunity to shape and deliver strategic campaigns to increase the visibility and awareness of our key pursuits and captures. You will develop cohesive, integrated marketing and communications plans utilising a range of channels, contribute to trade shows and events, whilst working with the team to develop content, collateral and assets for use throughout the business. In this highly collaborative role, the ability to build relationships working closely with our Australian leads and teams in the US, industry partners, agencies and customers alike will be key.  

About you:

With a background working in a complex, regulated environment and relevant qualifications, you will have a strong understanding of how to effectively grow brand awareness and drive key messages across online, social, event and print media. Adept as collecting key data points across the organisation to produce meaningful and timely content, you will be comfortable delivering the detail whilst making sure the strategy and key themes are clear.

The role is based in Canberra (preferred) or Brisbane.
