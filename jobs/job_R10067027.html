Northrop Grumman Space Systems is searching for a Engineer Systems to join our Digital Engineering (DE) team of qualified, diverse individuals to support Digital Transformation within the Launch Vehicles business unit in Chandler, AZ.

The ideal candidate shall perform two main systems roles: Methodology Developer and Product Owner for User Experience and Documentation.

In this role, the candidate will work with a team to develop model-based solutions to support program execution and govern the uniformity and cohesion of User Experience and Documentation.

Methodology Development includes:

  • Executing a DevOps process to create solutions that integrate with the current methodology.

  • Understanding stakeholder needs and transforming said needs into model constructs.

  • Explain to stakeholders any issues/concerns, for managing the information in a model.

  • Strive for continuous improvement/efficiencies in the approach.

User Experience and Documentation:

  • Identify the scope of releases and approve, all additions/modifications to Documentation solutions.

  • Review and confirm that all added or modified capabilities are well-formed, documented and follow template criteria.

  • Track documentation tasks, and work with document owners, to ensure document efforts are on plan.

  • Perform document releases, as needed. Inclusive of updating the Revision History for completeness.

  • Collaborate with Customers and Product Owners to study user experiences, and monitor usability, to further improve both tool and program experiences

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science Engineering degree plus 5 years of experience; Master’s degree plus 3 years of experience.

  • U.S. Citizenship is required.

  • Ability to obtain DoD Secret Security Clearance

Preferred, but not Required Qualifications:

  • Experience with Agile Framework

  • Experience with Jira

  • Experience with Confluence

  • Experience with MagicDraw/Cameo Systems Modeler

  • Experience with Systems Modeling Language (SysML)
