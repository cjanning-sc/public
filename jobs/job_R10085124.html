Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a talented Staff Engineer Mission Assurance to join the Mission Assurance (MA) team within Strategic Space Systems Division in Redondo Beach, CA.

The successful candidate will be a member of the Mission Assurance Program Leadership team and will act as a program MA lead for a smaller space program or in a lead support role to the MA Manager within a larger space program.  As the program MA representative, you will have to wear multiple hats across the entire program lifecycle and interface with all levels of program and customer leadership. Helps define, flow down and monitor the MA program specifications, requirements, and processes to ensure mission success of the program. Performs or assures quality, risk management, safety, reliability and maintainability of program achievements, subcontractors, and suppliers in accordance with contractual requirements. Assesses program performance and risks, and determines resources to ensure MA. Is responsible for MA task delegation, coordinating staffing and establishing budgets and spend plans.

Ensures Mission and Quality Assurance support throughout the development, review, delivery, and maintenance cycles for program SDRL deliverables, internal plans, Gate Reviews, and risk-based process audits. Works with the extended MA team and program management teams to define, gather, evaluate, and effectively present process and quality metrics, as applicable, to assist with program efficiency, effectiveness, and continuous improvement in support of current and future Rapid Development Programs.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for the implementation of Mission Assurance Principles across the program-life cycle to ensure compliance with Contractual, Regulatory and NG SSSD requirements.

  • Provide the Program Managers with an objective identification and assessment of program risks and opportunities and an evaluation of the efficacy of the risk management approach throughout the program lifecycle.

  • Communicate risk and opportunities to the Program Managers based upon credible knowledge supported by quantitative and predictive analysis when possible.

  • Establish an integrated and executable mission assurance plan for the program along with supporting plans that align with contractual requirements and stakeholder goals prior to contract start; maintains the planning throughout program life cycle to ensure proper alignment with requirements and goals.

  • Ensure correct and effective quality metrics are utilized on programs.

  • Advocates and implements continuous improvement opportunities, providing leadership and guidance in identifying and executing tasks to increase the efficiency and effectiveness of NG SSSD processes.

  • Investigates quality problems, issues, and failures through root cause investigation and corrective action implementation.

  • Must be able to verify Mission Assurance requirements, determine Mission & Quality Assurance resource needs 

  • Interaction with both internal and external customers as well as all levels of employees including: NG SSSD Program Mission Assurance Managers, Integrated Project Team leads, and representatives from Reliability, PM&P, Configuration Management, System Safety, and Software Quality.

  • The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

  • Leads and supports all mission assurance and quality engineering functions in support of meeting all contractual, functional and program requirements in support of multiple, diverse customer deliverables primarily in our space business area.
