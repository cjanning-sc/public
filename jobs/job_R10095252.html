We have an opening for an Administrative Assistant to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in the Tactical Space organization. This position will be located in Des Moines, IA.

In this role you will:

  • Support an organization of ~450 employees made up of Software and Aerospace engineers
  • Work side-by-side our other Admin, supporting our managers/engineers to ensure they have the resources they need to do their job
  • Submit travel/expense reports, provide support the director to manage various excel and power point packages for the organization, and help to make sure new hires have all the resources they need on their first day of work
  • Perform normal office functions such as setting up and maintaining files; interviewing callers and making proper referrals; arranging meetings and conferences; and receiving, referring, or answering mail
  • Review drafts and finished documents for appropriate grammatical usage
  • Answer questions relating to office operations and established policies and procedures
  • Gather, compile, and report on information relevant to supervisor’s assignment
  • May take and transcribe dictation
  • Assist in getting new hires set up for their first day of work
  • Assist staff in their resource needs
     

To be successful you should:

  • Be able to work with limited supervision and minimal instructions for routine work.  General instructions will be given on new lines of work or special assignments
  • Be comfortable with duties & tasks that are varied and moderately complex. You should be able to resolve most questions and problems, and refer complex questions to higher levels
  • Have a strong understanding that errors may be difficult to detect and could result in loss of customer business, material, or equipment
  • Be comfortable interacting with individuals representing other departments, and/or representing outside organizations
  • Have experience handling confidential information, contacts, or data
  • Be organized, proficient in Excel/Power Point, and have a working knowledge of Concur Travel & Expense
  • Have excellent communication and writing skills

Basic Qualifications

  • High School diploma or equivalent and 4 years additional education and/or related experience
  • Working knowledge in Excel/Power Point
  • Experience in the coordination & support of meetings
  • Ability to be on-site no less than 4 days a week
  • US Citizenship

Preferred Qualifications

  • Working knowledge of editing and managing a SharePoint site
  • Business analyst experience

The ACME Engineering Tactical Space Division is a strategic partner specializing in commercial and classified partnerships with the design, delivery, operation and sustainment of satellites and human spacecraft.  We support science and space exploration through our various partnerships, including NASA’s Artemis program with the goal to return humans to the Moon in 2024 and the TESS (Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite) program that has discovered more than twenty confirmed plants.  Recognized as an industry leader, we also develop highly specialized space and satellite components.
