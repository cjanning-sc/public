Job Description

As a Mission Assurance professional on the F-35 CNI Quality and Mission Excellence team you are first and foremost committed to treating all people with respect. You model personal growth and are a feverish champion for continuous development both personally and for your peers. You are someone that always chooses to act with integrity and you believe that high ethical and moral standards characterize who you are by the things you choose to do. You are someone that chooses to enable your team to achieve predictable and balanced results all while also satisfying the needs of both your internal and external customers. You have a track record that proves you know how to support a diverse, equitable and inclusive environment that will attract, retain, and encourage diverse and engaged talent. You choose to build trusted and valued partner relationships and you're effective at verbal and written communications. You choose to deliver perfection, strive for continuous improvement and respond vigorously to change. You lastly, but certainly not least consistently work to ensure that your team and the teams you support are armed with the information and tools needed for optimal performance. If this description fits who you are as a person and you wish to join a fast paced team that builds and executes strategies that result in sustainable value creation then we need you on our team! So read on....

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems (NGMS) is seeking a Mission Assurance Engineer 4 located in our San Diego Rancho Carmel facility!

Responsibilities include:
•    Help define and monitor mission assurance program specifications and processes to ensure mission success of responsible programs.
•    Assist with supporting the development, implementation and maintenance of quality policy and procedures within the Quality Management System (QMS) including helping to perform internal audits to assess conformance.
•    Awareness of lessons learned process, facilitation and tools to help leverage lessons of the past for future program success.
•    Assist with the review of practices, procedures and processes seeking avenues to improve efficiency and effectiveness and reduce risk to product quality.
•    A basic knowledge of interpreting customer technical and MA requirements, and ensure these requirements are integrated into program plans, specifications and product drawings.
•    Familiar with Root Cause and Corrective Action (RCCA) analysis, and Problem Solving activities on program-specific, quality system and business system deficiencies.
•    Represent Mission Assurance at various program reviews and review boards including but not limited to Configuration Control Board (CCB), Material Review Board (MRB), Program Management Reviews (PMR), Design/Peer Reviews, Manufacturing/Test Readiness Reviews and Corrective Action Board (CAB).
•    Collaborates with engineering and manufacturing functions to ensure quality standards are in place based on the guidance and needs of the team
•    Responsible for developing Basis of Estimates to ensure sufficient Quality oversight on key Development and Sustainment captures and programs.
•    Open to learning new concepts like developing Basis of Estimates, Cost of Quality, FMEA and similar measures designed to help drive improvement.
•    Familiarity with metrics and statistical analysis to help the team to use data to drive improvements.

Basic Qualifications:
•    Sr. Principal Engineer Mission Assurance T4: Bachelor’s degree and 7+ years of experience in Quality, Quality Engineering, Program Management, Engineering Management or Manufacturing (5+ years of experience with a Master’s degree), or 9+ years of relevant experience in lieu of degree.
