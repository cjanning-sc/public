Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security.  Our Engineering and Sciences (E&S) organization pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter.  Come be a part of our mission!

Northrop Grumman is seeking an Associate Electronic Engineer or Electronic Engineer to join our Mission Systems sector site in Apopka, FL. This position is to support the design, integration, test, and production of laser and electro-optical systems for handheld, ground, and airborne products.

What you’ll get to do:

The new team member will possess design, test, and documentation experience in areas of:

  • Embedded Micro-Controllers,
  • Low Power Architecture,
  • FPGA Image/Signal Processing,
  • Electro-Optic Sensor Interface
  • Distributed Network Systems,
  • Programming/Understanding of VHDL source code and FPGA code simulation

The new team member must be able to work independently as a member of a multi-disciplinary design team and have excellent written and verbal communication skills.  Past experience should include root cause corrective action (RCCA) determinations as well as the ability to generate engineering change notices (ECNs), update models / drawings, and release of appropriate documentation using TeamCenter applications. The ability to work in a fast-paced environment is critical.   

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • FPGA and Micro-controller programming.
  • VHDL source and Test bench code.
  • Design and documentation of video/image/signal processing electronics.
  • Design and documentation of low power architectures utilizing FPGA’s and micro-controllers.
  • Generating detail electronic schematics, CCA drawings, and BOM’s high-speed digital electronic designs.
  • Implementation and test of image/signal processing algorithms.
  • Debug, test, and integration of high-speed digital electronic circuits in complex electro-optic systems.
  • EMI / EMC design and test.
  • Generating ECNs and entering documentation into TeamCenter databases.

"This role may be filled as an Associate Electronic Engineer or an Electronic Engineer based on the qualifications below."


Basic Qualifications for an Associate Electronic Engineer:

  • B.S. Degree in Electrical Engineering and a minimum of 6 months related experience.
  • FPGA and Micro-controller circuit design and programming (or equivalent college coursework)
  • FPGA code simulation (or equivalent college coursework)
  • General analog and digital circuit design and troubleshooting
  • Circuit Analysis using software tools such as PSPICE
  • Electrical Component Selection and Obsolescence
  • US Citizenship is required with the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Clearance

 
Basic Qualifications for an Electronic Engineer:

  • B.S. Degree in Electrical Engineering and 2+ years engineering experience,
  •   or M.S. in Electrical Engineering
  • FPGA and Micro-controller circuit design and programming (or equivalent college coursework)
  • FPGA code simulation (or equivalent college coursework)
  • General analog and digital circuit design and troubleshooting
  • Circuit Analysis using software tools such as PSPICE
  • Electrical Component Selection and Obsolescence
  • US Citizenship is required with the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Image/Signal processing algorithm development experience
  • QuestaSim FPGA code simulation experience
  • Electro-Optic circuit design and test
  • Inertial Measurement Sensor interface experience
  • Mixed analog, power, and digital signal CCA experience
  • EMI / EMC design and test to MIL-STD-461
  • Test, measurement, and control software experience
  • Mathematic and modeling analysis software using tools such as MATLAB, MathCad, and Mathematica
  • Experience with Military Vehicle and Aircraft Specifications
  • Experience supporting the integration of complex laser and optical-mechanical prototypes
  • Experience with TeamCenter Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) applications
  • Cadence Concept Schematic Capture Experience
  • Cadence Allegro Printed Circuit Board Layout Experience
  • Hand soldering and light assembly experience
