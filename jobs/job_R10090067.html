Northrop Grumman Space Systems (NGSP) seeks to fill a Space Principal Systems Engineer or Senior Principal System Engineer SEIT role  in the Payload and Ground Systems Division Engineering organization supporting Integrated National Systems (INS) Business Unit (BU). This position could be located in Morrisville, NC or Aurora, CO.

This individual will clearly demonstrate strong leadership skills that achieve sustainable, top performance. They will have a proven, track record as an effective communicator and problem-solver who is able to develop and maintain strong working relationships with both internal and external shareholders. In addition, they will demonstrate a commitment to ethics and integrity, and will have the capability to innovate while challenging traditional ways of doing business. This candidate will demonstrate broad abilities that are balanced between interpersonal, business, and technical capabilities. There will be opportunity to operate in a team environment and to collaborate across division business areas, as required, in order to accomplish team goals.

Overview:

Performs technical planning, system integration, verification and validation, cost and risk, and supportability and effectiveness analyses for total systems. Analyses are performed at all levels of total system product to include: concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal. Ensures the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints. Performs functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications.

This requisition may be filled at the higher grade based on the qualifications listed below. 

Basic Qualifications:

  • Principal Systems Engineer : Bachelor's degree in STEM with 5+years of relevant experience, OR Master's degree with 3+ years, or PhD with 0+ years; 9+ years of experience may be considered in lieu of a degree requirement.

  • Senior Principal Systems Engineer: 9 years of relevant experience with a Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM discipline; 7 years of relevant experience with a Masters' degree; 4 years of relevant experience with a PhD

  • Active TS/SCI and willingness to sit for a poly

  • Experience with Doors and Cameo (or other model based systems engineering tool such as Enterprise Architecture, SPARX)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Knowledge of the Space or Ground Systems mission and development

  • Breadth of engineering experience on large and small programs

  • Experience with documenting systems engineering work doc (i.e., ICD, Requirements, work documents)

  • Experience with JIRA, S2P, or Confluence

  • Experience performing cloud-based design, architecture, integration, and test of software systems using Amazon Web Services (AWS)
