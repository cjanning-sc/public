Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems sector is seeking a Principal Training Representative Level 3 / Training Representative Level 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is in Clearfield, UT. 

Essential Functions:

Designs and delivers programs to train all levels of personnel. Develops tests and visual aids, conducts training classes, and develops criteria for evaluating the effectiveness of training activities. Keeps abreast of training and development research: learning theory, motivation theory, and new materials, methods, and techniques. Develops in-house programs and practices to identify developmental needs, including preparing personnel for more responsible positions and increasing effectiveness in present assignments. May evaluate and/or oversee contractors or vendors develop and/or administer training programs. May be responsible for the development of e-learning programs.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Training Representative Level 3

  • Bachelor’s Degree with 6 years of Aerospace experience, Master’s Degree with 4 years of Aerospace experience, OR High School Diploma/ G.E.D plus 10 years of Aerospace experience
  • Experience with hydraulic parts, assemblies, components, and systems in an aerospace, automotive, military, manufacturing and/or maintenance environment 
  • Experienced in/familiarity with training systems or programs
  • Ability and experience in analyzing, designing, and developing publications and training materials
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook, and PowerPoint), technical writing, and strong verbal communication skills
  • Must be a customer-oriented team player, able to interact with staff members in a team environment productively
