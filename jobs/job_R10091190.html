ACME Engineering Space Systems is currently seeking a Systems Engineer/Principal Systems Engineer to support the Antares Launch Program. The Antares launch program supports the efforts for resupply of the International Space Station (ISS).

The Systems Engineer will support numerous system engineering tasks:

  • Implement and manage system requirements.

  • Support data review. (This may require updating of scripts)

  • Revise and manage both original and revised specification/interface definition document and expedites documents through signature approval process.

  • Supports system design, data analyses, interface verification, testing, failure/anomaly resolution and mission operations.

  • Supports the testing and/or evaluation/validation of one or more components of a system or subsystem analytical model or actual system implementation.

  • Attends and prepares materials for presentation for critical reviews.

This position may be offered at the Engineer Systems Level 2 or may be offered at the Principal Engineer Systems Level 3.

Basic Qualifications: 

If offered at the Engineer Systems Level 2 requires:

Candidate must have a Bachelors of Science in Engineering degree plus 2 years of experience; or Masters plus 0 years of experience.

If offered at the Principal Systems Engineer Level 3 requires:

Candidate must have a Bachelors of Science in Engineering degree plus 5 years of experience; or Masters plus 3 years of experience; or PhD with 0 years of experience . 

  • U.S. Citizenship is required

  • Ability to travel is required 

  • Engineering experience

  • Strong oral and written communication skill 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Scripting in Matlab and Python is preferred
