ACME Engineering’s Space Systems - Propulsion Division is recruiting for a Metallurgist/Principal Metallurgist to join our Research & Development Metals and Thermal team in our Promontory, UT offices.   

Role description & Responsibilities:

The Metallurgist is responsible for providing technical expertise and consultation on all metallurgical issues, including alloy selection, processing, supply chain support, failure investigation, tooling processing, and production/facilities support.  You will also be expected to perform hands-on metallurgical analysis on a variety of parts and hardware using our lab instruments.  Because of this, experience using a variety of lab instruments (SEM, optical microscopes, etc.) is expected.   

Job Duties:   

  • Leading or supporting failure investigations

  • Metallurgical design support and evaluation of flight hardware and production parts

  • Material selection and characterization

  • Review of material and production specifications

  • Review of design certifications

  • Assist in developing tooling concepts and selection of materials for tooling   

  • Presentation of data and recommendations to internal and external customers

  • Support a variety of organizations and programs as metallurgical expert

Experience Requirements: 

A qualified candidate for this position should have some experience relevant to this position, including:

  • Experience with failure analysis, corrosion engineering, metallography, and microstructures

  • Previous experience using standard metallurgical lab instruments (SEM, optical microscopes, hardness testers, etc.)

  • Candidate must be able to obtain a clearance

Experience Preferred: 

·Familiarity in fabrication, welding, and heat treatment of forged stock and castings  

  • Experience with aerospace metallic materials and processing specifications  

  • Ability to work well with a multi-disciplinary team

Skills & Qualifications:

  • Broad understanding of metals and processing methods, physical metallurgy, and material structure/property relationships  

  • Experience with laboratory procedures and knowledge of safety requirements

  • Excellent written and oral communication skills

  • Excellent time management and organizational skills

Education/Certifications

  • BS in Metallurgy, Metallurgical Engineering, or Material Science (with 2 years metallurgical experience)

  • Position can be filled one level higher, 5 years with BS, 3 years with MS or 0 years with Ph.D.

  • Advanced degree preferred
