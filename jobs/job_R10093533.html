ACME Engineering Space Systems has an opening for a Business Management Analyst 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Fort Wayne, CO and will support the Integrated National Systems (INS) Business Unit.

Roles and Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Responsible for month end accounting close activities and financial reporting for the Business Unit
  • Program resource management, including cost estimation, allocation, tracking, and analysis
  • Assisting with monthly IF/ Forecast actions and discussions
  • Assisting with quarterly Estimates at Complete (EACs).
  • Assisting with Annual Operating Plan (AOP) development and reporting for the BU
  • Preparation and presentation of financial data to internal and external customers, including variance analysis and forecasts
  • Assisting with NCTA and Indirect budget support for the INS BU
  • Internal and External support of compliance audits
  • Collaboration with Directors and Program Leadership
  • Cash Management to ensure timely invoicing and collections
  • Supporting Operating Units (OU) with adhoc requests and deliverables
  • Comfortable with multiple customers, deliverables, and enjoy a high-paced environment.

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution with 3 years of related professional experience - OR - a Master's degree with 1+ years of related professional experience. An additional four years’ experience accepted in lieu of a degree
  • Experience in cost management, finance management, or pricing & estimating
  • Experience successfully managing and producing data with strict deadlines
  • Experience with financial analysis and/or reporting
  • Advanced PC skills and proficient knowledge with Microsoft Office Suite, including MS Excel
  • Advanced analytical ability
  • Proficiency in MS Office Suite, specifically a high level of proficiency in Microsoft Excel (pivot tables, lookups, chart generation, etc.)
  • Demonstrated leadership, critical thinking, innovative behaviors, and the ability to multi-task in a challenging environment
  • Ability to work in a team environment and build relationships with others
  • Be able to obtain and maintain a U.S. Government security clearance (U.S. citizenship is a pre-requisite)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Professional experience in a regulated industry such as Aerospace & Defense, Insurance or Financial Services
  • Experience with SAP ERP 1
  • Experience with Cognos TM1 financial forecasting system
  • Earned Value Proficiency including ONE ACME System Description.
  • Knowledge of Earned value management systems (EVMS)
