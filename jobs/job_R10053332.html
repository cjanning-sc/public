ACME Engineering’s Enterprise Services organization is seeking a Principal Software Development Analyst at its Detroit, Michigan location. Opportunities also available in Madison, Wisconsin and Indianapolis, Indiana.

This position does not allow for virtual work.

The selected candidate supports, maintains, and/or resolves incidents on enterprise-wide Product Lifecycle Management systems and/or applications software. Involved in planning of system maintenance and upgrade deployments as well as responsible for meeting software compliance standards. Provides operational end user support, guidance, and incident management to agreed service levels. Performs or supports testing and maintenance of system corrections.

Responsibilities include providing IT-focused end-user support in areas of best practice development, operations support, and problem resolution for the engineering community and serving as a Subject Matter Expert in Siemens products including Teamcenter Unified. This includes addressing end-user incidents and requests for assistance within agreed service level guidance in support of business and IT priorities.

Additional responsibilities:

- End-user support of TeamCenter PLM software including, but not limited to TcU, Multi-CAD Data Management, Integrations, ProductData/Change/DocumentManagement, Account Management, and Workflows in production environments.

- Under supervision will perform root cause analysis, debugging problems with the Teamcenter environment, and then work with the appropriate technical teams to develop, test, and implement fixes to prevent recurrence.

- Monitor, address, and resolve Teamcenter production environment incidents, service requests, workflows, client install packages, etc., collaboratively with the PLM development and administration teams.

- Document all aspects of system support such that knowledge is readily shared among all those tasked with supporting Teamcenter.

- Support SIT and UAT with business users as necessary.

- Provide demos to users as and when required.

- Under minimal supervision will support business readiness and change management activities prior to go-live and in production.

Basic Qualifications:

• Bachelor in Science degree and at least 5 years of experience

• 2-4 years PLM support experience with Teamcenter or applications with similar functions and capabilities.

• Knowledge of Teamcenter 4-Tier, 2-Tier, or web architecture end-user support and incident resolution.

• Current Comptia Security + certification, maintaining a current Security+ certification will be a condition of continued employment.

• US Citizenship is required as well as the ability to obtain a clearance following hire.

• Current DOD Secret Clearance,  maintaining the required security clearance will be a condition of continued employment.

Preferred Qualifications:

• Familiarity with the functions, processes, and capabilities in Teamcenter as they apply to mechanical, electrical, or systems product development cycles across PLM modules in use for TcR, Tc4Sim, CMM, PDM, TcMfg, and integrations with other systems like SAP and IBM Rational Suite.

• Experience with Teamcenter Aerospace & Defense.

• Knowledge with and flexibility to support other applications associated with enterprise PLM such as NX,CATIA, ENOVIA, eQube, HarnesSys, and custom applications.

• Able to apply engineering techniques and analytical methods to make recommendations for improvements and provide information and assistance to end-users.

• Work effectively within teams where other team members may be located throughout the country and influencing others to action who are not direct reports.

• Excellent written and verbal English communication skills.

• Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

• Familiarity with AWS Cloud
