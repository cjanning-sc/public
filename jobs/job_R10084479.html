ACME Engineering is seeking a Control Account/Project Manager to join our team supporting the SMORS program.  The position is based at a facility in Indianapolis.

This role will work with a technical team, Program Operations leadership business management, and Scheduling team to gather necessary information for cost control and forecasting the Control Account financial data, and delivering monthly program reports. The CAM will update Earned Value Management (EVM) tools and produce required data products, review monthly Earned Value (EV) data and provide detailed cost account status, and support program requirements.

Job responsibilities will include, but not be limited to, the following:

  • Coordinate with technical leadership a cross-functional Integrated Program Team (IPT) to meet program cost, schedule, and technical objectives.
  • Assist DO Lead, PMO Lead, and WS Leads in executing on-contract activities ensuring proactive monitoring of dependencies, risks, and key milestones.
  • Develop and deliver presentations to customers, executive management, and other program stakeholders.
  • Define, plan, perform, monitor, control, and complete a variety of project tasks simultaneously.
  • Lead the development and maintenance of the budget EAC, Change Request, Variance Reports, and Contract Performance Report (CPR) monthly performance reporting that is submitted to the Government ensuring they clearly address status, explain significant variances, and outline correction plans. Present these reports to program management.
  • Track budget actuals against predictions and financial performance against the EV performance management baseline, participate in CAM status calls, prepare status for/attend a weekly status meeting, provide/enter weekly Integrated Master Schedule (IMS) status, support stakeholder meetings, review Actuals Hours reports for time charging accuracy and alignment with IMS status.
  • Coordinate with the scheduling team to develop and execute to an integrated Master Schedules (IMS).
  • Coordinate Change Requests with the Business Management and Scheduling team as needed.
  • Work directly with the engineering and program management staff to forecast the budget and meet the needs of executing contractual requirements while operating within available funding. Facilitate internal meetings to support staffing, ensuring the staffing needs meet the staffing baseline.
  • Process and approve all travel requests.
  • Coordinate the procurement of materials and other direct costs with the Global Supply department.
  • Interact directly with various team members to provide appropriate labor charging authorization distribution, charging practices, and Labor Forecast Tool (LFT) adjustments including the incorporation of Resource Manager (RM) and Functional Leads inputs.
  • Execute tasks in support of the Business Rhythm Calendar (BRC).
  • Support the development of packages for Proposals and Change Requests for cost and schedule and present them to Leadership.
  • Facilitate internal meetings to support staffing, ensuring the staffing needs meet the staffing baseline
  • Adhere to all internal processes for Program Management.
  • Perform schedule risk assessments to identify and mitigate program cost and scheduling risks.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree with 10 years of related experience, 8 years of experience with a Masters’ Degree; 14 or more years of experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
  • Must have U.S Citizenship with active/current Secret Clearance or higher.
  • Hands-on experience in project management, control account management, or technical leadership.
  • Experience working within an Integrated Product Team (IPT).
  • Experience with CAM and EVM methodologies.
  • Proficient with MS Office applications including Excel, Word, Project, and PowerPoint.
  • Ability to communicate with multiple levels of the organization having excellent presentation skills.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD Top Secret clearance or higher.
  • Experience with systems engineering process, from requirements analysis through verification testing, and manufacturing engineering and their interaction with associated tooling.
  • Experience with Risk Management, Return to Green (RTG) plans, and Scheduling cross-functional activities.
  • Experience in contracts executing earned value management.
  • Experience in Agile earned value management.
  • Previous experience working on Agile Development projects.
