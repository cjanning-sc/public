ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Communications Equipment Senior Principal Field Engineer. This position will be located in Vandenberg Space Force Base, OH and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. Learn more about the GBSD program here.  This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

  • Maintain, troubleshoot, and repair ICBM communications, command, and control systems.
  • Responsible for integration, assembly and test of ICBM aerospace vehicle and ground support equipment. 
  • Provides technical support to engineers on a variety of technical tasks.
  • Gathers, maintains, formats, compiles, and manipulates technical data, such as laboratory or material test results and engineering design changes.
  • Read and understand engineering documentation that applies to laboratory setup and integration.
  • Uses personal computer in performance of analyses and development of documentation/reports.
  • May conduct tests and record data to assist with engineering evaluation or analysis.
  • Implement best practices in demonstrates good hardware configuration management and tool accountability.
  • Able to read drawings, blueprints, diagrams and create work instructions/procedures 
  • Utilize MS products; Word, Excel, and Visio.
  • Effectively communicate issues and help to provide solutions.

Basic Qualifications:

  • 9 years of experience with Bachelor’s Degree; 7 years with Masters; 4 Years with PhD. An additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a degree. 
  • US Citizen with an active secret clearance
  • Active Secret Clearance with the ability to obtain a PAR
  • Experience with common hand tools and test equipment found in an integration, production, manufacturing, or test environment

Preferred Qualifications

  • Prior experience in an integration, production, manufacturing or test environment or like type work in a military setting.
  • Active Top Secret Clearance with completed investigation within the last 6 years
  • ICBM Maintenance or aerospace integration, assembly, and test experience
