ACME Engineering Tactical Space Systems is seeking multiple Software Engineers to support Tactical Space Systems Division in their development and deployment of satellite ground software to customer resources while implementing Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) pipelines in an agile environment. The location for this role will be in Des Moines, VA or Indianapolis, CO.  This work will primarily occur in a SCIF environment so onsite work is required.

To be successful in this role, you should enjoy learning and learn quickly, be motivated by the opportunity to solve hard problems with challenging boundary conditions, work well with a motivated and mission-focused team, enjoy working with both speed and accuracy, and take great pride in your work and the work of the team(s).  You should have demonstrated experience in technical problem solving, to include decomposition, root cause analysis, solution development, implementation, and monitoring. Software Engineers will be a critical member of one or more Agile Scrum Teams. After hours or extended work week hours may be required.

What You’ll Have an Opportunity to:

  • Produce applications that span languages and toolsets including Python, Postgres, Ansible, cloud infrastructure provisioning, PKI, and static code analysis
  • Locate and remediate problems in code, testing, and deployment
  • Take program software to the next level by using automation frameworks
  • Learn cutting edge technologies that take full advantage of a cloud environment
  • Use tools like Jenkins and Ansible to set up automation steps that fast-track code from development, testing, and deployment
  • Be a part of the enabling technology at ACME Engineering that puts satellites and spacecraft into space that catapults the Space Protection Mission to the forefront of capability and effectiveness

This position can be filled at the Principal Software Engineer, or the Sr. Principal Software Engineer level.

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Software Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Software Engineering, Computer Science, or closely related discipline plus 5 years of development experience (3 years with a master’s degree, 0 years with a PhD). Experience can be considered in lieu of degree
  • Experience with the design, build, and deployment of software products
  • Experience in producing software architecture documentation
  • US Citizenship with an active Top Secret clearance

Basic Qualifications for a Sr. Principal Software Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Software Engineering, Computer Science, or closely related discipline plus 9 years of development experience (7 years with a master’s degree, 4 years with a PhD). Experience can be considered in lieu of degree
  • Demonstrated expertise with the design, build, and deployment of software products
  • Experience in producing software architecture documentation
  • Clear understanding of secure coding practices and quality orientation
  • US Citizenship with an active Top Secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications for All Levels:

  • Familiarity with Agile framework or Scaled Agile Framework
  • Familiarity with DevOps practices and how it compliments Agile software development
  • Experience working with Python preferably on Linux operating systems
  • Experience working with tools like Docker, Kubernetes, Ansible, PostgreSQL, SonarQube
  • Familiarity with Cloud Infrastructure provisioning

The ACME Engineering Tactical Space Division is a strategic partner specializing in commercial and classified partnerships with the design, delivery, operation and sustainment of satellites and human spacecraft.  We support science and space exploration through our various partnerships, including NASA’s Artemis program with the goal to return humans to the Moon in 2024 and the TESS (Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite) program that has discovered more than twenty confirmed plants.  Recognized as an industry leader, we also develop highly specialized space and satellite components.
