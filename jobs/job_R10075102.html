Contract Administrator - Senior Principal - CLEARANCE NEEDED
R10075102
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Contracts Administrator – Senior Principal to join its team of qualified and diverse individuals supporting its Launch and Missile Defense Systems Division, specifically the Advanced Mission Solutions (AMS), Operating Unit (OU).

The successful candidate will be a direct report to AMS OU Contracts Manager and will work closely on a day-to-day basis with the Program Managers/Directors, Business Managers, Operations Managers, Capture Managers, and functional teams.

The successful candidate will be responsible for all aspects of a contract portfolio from the business development phase through closeout, delivering customer satisfaction, building customer relationships, and providing timely responses to both external and internal customers.

This position will be in Boulder, CO. and a hybrid work schedule will be considered.

Primary Job Duites:

  • Provide contracts support to major capture and proposal efforts
  • Help with customer communications and correspondence, support writing of Contracts Management proposal sections, prepare representations and certifications, OCI sweeps, and review terms and conditions, technical data rights and assertions and provide the administrative support needed to guide the proposal through the proposal approval process
  • Administer, extend, negotiate and terminate standard and nonstandard contracts
  • Prepare proposals, conduct contract negotiations, perform contract administration, and execute customer contact activities to provide for proper contract acquisition and fulfillment
  • Examine estimates of material, equipment services, production costs, performance requirements, and delivery schedules to ensure accuracy and completeness
  • Prepare bids, process specifications, progress, and other reports
  • Advise management of contractual rights and obligations
  • Compile and analyze data; maintain historical information

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree with 9+ years relevant experience in contract administration/management or related field – OR - Master’s Degree with 7+ years relevant experience in contract administration/management or related field
  • Knowledge of working large competitive Department of Defense (DoD) proposals
  • Knowledge of negotiating and administering various contract types of varying sizes and types of agreements with the DoD
  • Understanding of the requirements of applicable government regulations (FAR, DFAR)
  •  Ability to travel 10% of time
  • Current, active US government clearance at the Secret level with the ability to obtain and maintain a Top Secret Sensitive Compartmented Information (SCI) clearance. MUST be willing to accept all conditions of gaining access to restricted programs, which would include signing consent to polygraph.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current Top Secret Clearance.

Skills:

  • Dynamic self-starter with strong attention to detail and the ability to function independently
  • Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, with the ability to use them in informing, presenting, and negotiating to all levels of management
  • Team player, independent, proactive, and possess excellent problem solving, oral and written communication, and organizational skills
  • Willingness to work collaboratively as part of a dynamic leadership team
