ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems is an Industrial Engineer (Simulation) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. 

Responsibilities will include:

  • Analyzes lead times, queue times, work-in-process, cycle times, and throughput to meet customer demand most effectively and efficiently

  • Builds scalable and sustainable models to optimize product flow, while incorporating cost, schedule, and resource constraints

  • Develops optimized scheduling tools and aligns SAP/MRP systems with an executable plan given various capacity constraints

  • Incorporates complex interdependencies within a system to account for the interaction of multiple cost centers, programs, equipment, and tools

  • Communicates requirements planning and systems framework with IT, ERP, business analytics, and data science teams

  • Works with developers to design an interactive user interface that will be effective at scheduling jobs across cost centers

  • Guides digital transformation efforts to align with sector and enterprise strategies

  • Leads and drives positive change using iterative and agile methodologies

  • Creates new metrics and data visualizations to monitor shop floor performance

  • Creates Industrial Systems Simulation models and analyzes resulting data to support manufacturing decision making

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor's Degree in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Math) with 2 years of engineering, manufacturing, or data analysis experience OR Master's degree in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) with 0 years

  • Must have ability to obtain and maintain DoD secret or top secret clearance within a reasonable amount of time as required by the business

  • Ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access (PAR) within a reasonable amount of time as required by the business

  • MRP/MES or equivalent experience

  • Expertise using excel, including macros, data mining, and pivot tables.

  • Experience with data analysis, or data visualization software applications


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience working with cross-functional teams in a manufacturing environment.

  • Experience with improving manufacturing workflow using optimization, modeling, or simulation methods.

  • Computer programming and industrial systems simulation experience (i.e Plant Sim, Simeo, etc.)

  • Familiarity with mixed model manufacturing operations.

  • Experience with agile methods of project management or development.

  • Strong experience designing visualizations in Tableau in a professional work environment.

  • Experience with SQL, Python, HTML, VBA, JavaScript, or other programming languages.

  • Active Secret DoD clearance investigated within the last 5 1/2 years

What We Offer:
