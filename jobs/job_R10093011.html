Position Summary / Responsibilities

The Category Management Planning Specialist will be supporting multiple programs with the RF Distribution and PWB Category Suppliers across the Mission Systems Sector by aligning program needs and Sector Strategies.

  • Negotiates, manages, and foster strong relationships with suppliers
  • Defines and develops material strategy to align it to Sector and Program needs
  • Implements metrics to ensure that Sector goals are realized, and performance requirements are achieved
  • Aligns suppliers to the Supplier Performance Management Plan
  • Works with cross-functional teams to analyze supply base risks & opportunities by supporting program needs
  • Responsible for the assessment of supplier capacity resources
  • Aggregates and prioritizes demand requirements
  • Analyzes and aggregates supply / demand requirements
  • Analyzes purchase material supply/demand position using Material Requirements Planning (MRP) tools
  • Plans inventory levels based on dependent and independent Bill-of-Materials (BOM) requirements
  • Assesses rough-cut capacity for current and short-term manufacturing requirements.
  • Participates in long-term capacity and resource planning to ensure proper scheduling of new product line phase-ins as well as existing product line phase-outs
  • Analyzes lead times and identifies critical path procurements

This position can be worked from the Redondo Beach, Woodland hills, or San Diego location. Or may consider fulltime Teleworking.
