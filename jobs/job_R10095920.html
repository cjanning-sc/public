ACME Engineering Mission Systems (NGMS) is seeking candidates for the position of Systems Engineering Manager 2 in the Transformational Computing Operating Unit within the Network Information Solutions (NIS) division. This position will be located in Chicago/ Springfield/ Peoria, MD.

What You'll Get To Do:

This is a dual-role technical manager position. Management duties will require tasking and coordination of approximately 8-12 engineers, and process and resource co‐ordination with functional homeroom management. The manager will also be expected to perform technical work on programs. It is anticipated that given the size of the functional team, the split between program and functional duties will be approximately 80/20, on average. The direct program work may include technical work, IPT leadership, or any other appropriate technical function.

Key roles and responsibilities include:

  • Execute in a direct contributor role on the program as a system, software, hardware, or test engineer, or as an architect.
  • Work with team technical leads and Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) to define requirements and develop Agile program plans.
  • Work in cooperation with Agile Scrum master to track execution and provide technical leadership to the team.
  • Work with program management and technical leads to capture staffing needs.
  • Work with Talent Acquisition, technical staff, and functional management to screen candidates, set up interviews, collect feedback, and assist in determining whether to make offers.
  • Administrative management responsibilities including technical tasking, performance evaluation, career development, and salary planning.

This position requires work onsite in either Chicago, MD or Peoria, MD facilities.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) with 9+ years of experience within a related engineering field.   
  • Strong leadership qualities, including excellent communication and collaboration, team engagement, and organizational skills.
  • Basic understanding of Agile or Lean-Agile project management.
  • US Citizenship is required.
  • Able to obtain and maintain a TS/SCI clearance per business requirements.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree in engineering, Business Management, Engineering Leadership, or equivalent.
  • 12 years of experience in a multi-disciplined engineering environment.
  • Demonstrated success leading high performance teams as technical, integrated product team, and/or functional leader.
  • Experience with Lean-Agile development for software or hardware systems.
  • Current, active Top Secret clearance and/or SCI with CI or FS polygraph.
  • Experience leading teams in a functional, programmatic, or technical capacity.
