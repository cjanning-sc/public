Northrop Grumman Space Division designs, builds and delivers space, defense and aviation-related systems to customers around the world. Northrop Grumman Tactical Space Systems Division (TSS) designs, manufactures and operates small and medium-class spacecraft for communications, imaging, scientific, exploration and national security space applications. The Group is a leading supplier of space components that power and enable satellites of all classes, provides technical engineering services to government agencies and laboratories, and is developing exciting new satellite servicing and advanced space flight technologies.


Northrop Grumman Tactical Space Systems Division is seeking to fill multiple Staff Positions in support of the Integration & Test (I&T) Department located in Gilbert, Arizona.

These are Staff positions supporting Program management roles on one or multiple programs across the portfolio’s supported at the Gilbert site. Successful candidates will clearly demonstrate strong leadership skills to achieve a sustainable top performance. She/He will have a track record as an effective communicator and problem solver who is able to develop and maintain good working relationships with internal/external stakeholders and customers. This candidate will demonstrate a commitment to ethics, integrity, and the capability to innovate while challenging traditional ways of doing business. Demonstrated abilities to build diverse and inclusive teams with high levels of engagement, including mentoring and coaching, are a must.

  • Assist the I&T Program Manager in:

    • Support CAM roles & responsibilities (includes EVMS)
  • Support all review boards, tiger teams, RCCA investigations, deep dives, and pre-milestone reviews.
  • Serve on and participate in program MRR, PDR, CDR, TRR as required
  • Provides Problem Solving Expertise and capability to mentor the team in problem solving skills
  • Support Metrics for Quality, Schedule, Cost and Productivity for the Test Area. Continuous Process Improvement
  • Delegate of Authority to I&T Program Manager in His/Her absence

  • 5+ years minimum in Space system/Satellites industry
  • Minimum of 3-5 years of proven demonstrated leadership skills and the ability to work effectively in a team environment.
  • Experience working within Integration & Test organizations
  • Excellent written and oral communications skills and technical presentation experience
  • This position requires the applicant to be a U.S. citizen and have the ability to obtain and hold a Top Secret/SCI clearance
