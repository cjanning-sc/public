AOA Xinetics (AOX), a wholly-owned Northrop Grumman Space Systems, Payload and Ground Systems Division Business Venture, is a leading supplier of complex electro-optic and photonic systems and subsystems that control and manipulate light waves for a variety of applications. We are seeking a Product Engineer to support our Special Programs team in East Hartford, CT.

https://www.northropgrumman.com/who-we-are/aoa-xinetics  

Overview

Reporting to the Manufacturing Engineering Manager, the Product Engineer will perform Manufacturing Engineering support to Special programs production. this includes sustaining engineering and new product transition from design engineer to the production group

Role and Responsibilities:

The Product Engineer will be responsible for production support engineering with respect to testing methods, procedures, problem solving, electro-optic assembly specification and yield issues, minor redesign of assemblies, analysis of customer returns, and optimizing device production relative to cost constraints. Assumes responsibility for device transfer from engineering to production and usually responsible for supporting the entire device manufacturing operations.

This position may be filed as either a Product Engineer (Level 2) or Principal Product Engineer (Level 3)

Basic Qualifications for the Product Engineer (Level 2):

  • Bachelor's degree in Mechanical or Electrical Engineering, or other applicable degree; with two years related experience, 0 years' experience with a Masters. Four year additional experience may be substituted in lieu of a degree

  • Experience with alignment, assembly, bonding, and testing of precision opto-mechanical, fiber optic, micro-optic, and small mechanical components. Experience in Cable harness and Electronics/PCBs testing a plus

  • Detail oriented and with the desire to constantly improve the production environment, processes, and documentation

  • Experience assembling bills of materials and writing engineering change orders

  • Some experience working with a materials requirements planning (MRP) system

  • Good written and verbal communication skills

  • Be comfortable and work well as part of an integrated team including technicians, engineering peers and principal level engineers

  • Ability to read mechanical drawings schematics and understand basic Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing

  • Absolutely must be a self-starter and able to prioritize multiple tasks in a fast paced, highly technical work environment

  • Basic experience with SolidWorks or equivalent drawing package

  • Proficient Microsoft Office skills

  • U.S. Citizenship is a requirement for employment

  • Occasional domestic/international travel up to 10% of the time

  • U.S. Citizenship is a bona fide requirement as applicants selected will be subject to a government security investigation

  • Ability to obtain a DoD Top Secret Clearance

Basic Qualifications for the Principal Product Engineer (Level 3):

  • Same as above, but with 5+ years of experience with a Bachelor's degree, or 3 Years with Masters; 0 Years with PhD. four years additional experience may be substituted in lieu of a degree

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Masters Degree and 2 plus years’ experience in related field

  • Active Top Secret Security Clearance
