Northrop Grumman Space Systems Sector is looking for you to join our team as a Sr. Principal Software Engineer based out of Morrisville NC, Fairfax, VA. 

We are looking for an experienced Embedded Software Engineer to lead an FPGA team.  This person should have experience with modeling & simulation.  The successful candidate will lead/implement the creation of an FPGA VHDL and embedded ARM development framework inside a US+ FPGA. Our small agile Scrum team is highly energized and has a significant effort on a growing program with a large program impact. If this is your type of environment and you love fast-paced development, please consider joining our team.
 

This is a great opportunity for a flexible self-starter to work in a dynamic environment with real world technical content supporting several tools and systems. Plenty of opportunity for career advancement into leadership positions.

The Sr. Principal Embedded Software Engineer designs, develops, operates and maintains software and firmware components and computing systems software to be applied to and integrated with engineering, scientific and manufacturing requirements. Applies the appropriate standards, processes, procedures and tools throughout the system development life cycle to support the generation of such engineering applications and products such as laboratory simulation systems, aircraft flight control and display systems, pacemakers, defibrillators, mechanical and electrical systems. Interfaces with customers, suppliers, application users and other technical and support personnel.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Sr. Principal Software Engineer:  Bachelor’s degree with 9 + years of software development experience; 7 years with a Master's; 4 years with a PhD; or 14 years of software development experience in lieu of a degree
  • Candidate must have a current/active Top Secret/SCI security clearance to be considered
  • Candidate must be able to obtain a Counterintelligence Polygraph (CI-Poly)
  • Experience Embedded Software development
  • Experienced with Xilinx FPGA ARM processor architecture

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Agile development
  • Experience with Matlab simulation and modeling
  • Current Counterintelligence Polygraph (CI-Poly) completed within the last 5 years
