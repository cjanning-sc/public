NG Space Systems Payload & Ground Systems Division (PGSD) is looking for a Manager, Software/Systems Engineering 2 (Grade S02) to support its Ground Element (GE) Software IPT as their New Business Lead for the Advanced Mission Programs (AMP) in Redondo Beach, CA.

What You’ll get to Do:

Responsibilities will include:

  • Represent Ground Software for New Business Activities as the Software New Business Lead to support business development activity that directly impacts the company’s core business; handling contracts valued in the millions of dollars.
  • Work with SW Subproject Managers (SPMs) to develop cost estimation and write Task Descriptions/Basis of Estimates (TD/BOEs) for each SW development WBS area, including SW Development management
  • Assist in the development of the Cost Volume (creation of Pricing Green Sheets), Period of Performance, Staffing, Schedule, and Technical Volume
  • Assist in the cost modeling, including use of the Galorath SEER SEM parametric estimation tool, research of historical data
  • Represent Ground SW in customer meetings and coordination with other project teams
  • Identify Bill of Material (BOM) and interface with Global Supply Chain (GSC) Material Pricer with vendor quotes
  • Participate in cost and pricing reviews with management and functional representatives
  • Develop and present sign-off briefing packages to program and functional leadership
  • Oversee all reviews for proposal artifacts. Provide support to red team review, non-advocate review (NAR), and independent cost estimate (ICE)
  • Candidate will also be expected to fill a 20% part-time functional Section Manager role and will be responsible for 10 – 12 software engineers, systems engineers, and/or cyber/information assurance engineers. The responsibilities of the p/t section manager role includes: Support functional management activities such as staffing and workload forecast; employee engagement, mentoring and career development; identification and training of skills required to execute on programs; Participate in sourcing, recruiting, onboarding, and performance management; and Support Business Strategy Efforts (including proposals, gate reviews, independent review team, NAR/ICE, as necessary)

Basic Qualifications:

  • Current DoD Top- Secret clearance with the ability to obtain an SCI clearance prior to start date
  • 9 years of Engineering experience with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science or a related engineering field a STEM discipline; 7 years of Engineering experience with Masters; 4 Years with a PhD
  • Experience in ground systems deliverables and processes

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Self-starter who completes tasking with minimal supervision
  • Strong diagnostic/problem solving skills
  • Strong organizational skills to manage multiple priorities effectively
  • Previous experience working on proposal TD/BOEs is a plus
  • Works well in a team environment
  • Strong interpersonal and analytical skills
  • Demonstrated strong leadership skills as well as the ability to multi-task in a fast-paced environment
  • Working knowledge of Galorath SEER for Software (SEER-SEM) is a plus
  • Active SCI clearance
