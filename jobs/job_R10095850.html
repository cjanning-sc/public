Northrop Grumman Space Systems is currently seeking Principal Administrative Assistant 3 to support the Human Exploration and Operations OU in Dulles, VA.

·         Coordinating a broad variety of events, meetings, and teleconferences

·         Managing multiple priorities and actions simultaneously

·         Managing calendars and scheduling meetings, to include all support necessary to facilitate meetings, often in close coordination with executive assistants across the company

·         Arranging travel and processing expense reports in a timely manner

·         Supporting the creation of presentations and site communications

·         Ordering and managing refreshments, breakfasts, and luncheons – for meetings held in the office or at off-site locations

·         Working closely with the security team to ensure security protocols and processes are followed

·         Ordering office supplies and working with building management and NGC facility representatives to handle general office repairs and maintenance requests, communicate building and facility announcements, handling of mail and shipping/receiving, printer maintenance, janitorial oversight, and other incidents as needed

·         Maintaining copy/supply/breakroom and general office organization and appearance

·         Leading new hire onboarding and in-processing activities for the site, and acting as a point of contact for guidance and support during their first week

·         Supporting the site by handling routine administrative duties, greeting and escorting guests, and creating a friendly and professional atmosphere for all employees to work in

·         Acting as a site liaison for external civilian, military, government guests and customers
