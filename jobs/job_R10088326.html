ACME Engineering Enterprise Services is seeking an Accounting Assistant to support the Global Travel Accounting team. The selected individual will report to the manager of Travel Accounting and assist the Team with administering the Corporate travel program. The Accounting Assistant should have excellent people skills, organizational ability and a great deal of communication skills.

This role will offer telework, but candidates must be local to one of our ACME Engineering offices (preferably our office in Irving, TX) and able to go on site for training or special project purposes.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Process and audit expense reimbursements and credit card transactions for compliance to ACME policies
  • Utilize Concur, internal governing documents, and external guidance to perform research of expense reports
  • Perform accounting activities such as payment reconciliations, general ledger account reconciliations, and ad hoc reporting
  • Demonstrate analytical and problem solving skills to help resolve complex travel inquiries
  • Monitor and work to departmental and individual processing metrics
  • Clearly and professionally communicate related travel, accounting policies and procedures
  • Develops solutions to a variety of problems of moderate scope and complexity 

Successful candidates will possess the following leadership qualities:

  • Exercises high level of confidentiality
  • Strong analytical, communication, and creative problem-solving skills
  • Commitment to results, leadership, teamwork, and strong interpersonal/customer service skills
  • Develops solutions to a variety of problems of moderate scope and complexity
  • Desire to receive constructive feedback; work will be reviewed for soundness of judgment, adequacy & accuracy
  • Able to communicate effectively and clearly present technical approaches and findings
  • Excellent time management skills, ability to meet deadlines, and work in a fast-paced environment
  • Demonstrated ability of working with multiple levels of management/stakeholders and effectively communicating information to all levels of the organization
  • Self-motivated, team-player who works well with all employees and third parties

Basic Qualifications: 

To be considered for this position, you must minimally meet the knowledge, skills, and abilities listed below:

  • High School diploma or equivalent and 2 years additional education and/or related experience
  • Intermediate Excel skills
  • Concur and SAP experience
  • Demonstrate ability to apply travel and accounting policies decisively and methodically
  • Excellent customer service skills 
  • Local to one of our ACME Engineering offices and able to go on site for training or special project purposes.

Preferred Qualifications:

Candidates with these desired skills will be given preferential consideration:

  • Local to our office in Irving, TX
  • Demonstrate an understanding of travel and expense, imputed income, general ledger account reconciliations, and internal/external audits
  • Have an in-depth understanding of business processes, especially ACME Engineering segments and work structures
  • 1 to 3 years Travel and Expense experience
  • Bachelor's Degree in Accounting/ Finance or related field
  • Experience in a financial services environment
  • Advanced Excel Skills
  • Understanding of FAR, GAAP, CAS, and SOX requirements
