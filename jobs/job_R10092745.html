ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems (NGAS) is seeking a Principal / Sr. Principal Quality Analyst to join our Iuka, Mississippi location.

We are a world-class manufacturer of large composite rocket structures for commercial and military applications. Our team supports plant-wide manufacturing improvements in a low volume, highly technical environment. These roles will be integral to plant wide continuous improvement efforts and audit compliance. Our work is mission critical and directly contributes to science and exploration, national security, and the betterment of people’s everyday lives.

Responsibilities may include the following:

  • Manage plant wide corrective action improvements
  • Conduct and coordinate audits
  • Lead continuous improvement initiatives
  • Work with Operations to implement Blue Sheet suggestions
  • New hire orientation training
  • Plant wide root cause and corrective action training
  • Help develop and maintain plant wide work area instructions
  • Coordinate monthly plant Quality review
  • Generate monthly key performance metrics and charts

Our focus is on the end user – ensuring that our products work the first time and every time without defect – because failure is not an option.  We are seeking detail-oriented problem solvers to make an impact.  If that sounds like you, apply today!

We offer phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly team environment. We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal-oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do. The candidate selected for this role will be working either a 9/80 schedule with every other Friday off, or a 4/10 schedule.  Travel is very minimal, if ever, (less than 10%) and within the US.

Available Shifts:

NOTE: These shifts include a shift differential in addition to base pay.

  • B Shift Production: 7PM –530 AM, Monday - Thursday (4x10’s)
  • C Shift Production: 7AM - 730 PM, Friday - Sunday (3x12's)
  • D Shift Production: 7PM - 730 AM, Friday - Sunday (3x12's)

Basic Qualifications:

These roles may be filled at the Principal or Senior Principal level depending on the qualifications below.

Principal Analyst

  • Bachelor’s degree and 6 years of quality work experience; or Master’s degree and 4 years of quality work experience.  Note: an additional 4 years of relevant work experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
  • Demonstrated understanding of the non-conformance and corrective action process
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret clearance
  • Ability to work any shift as needed by the business.

Senior Principal Analyst

  • Bachelor’s degree and 10 years of quality work experience; or Master’s degree and 8 years of quality work experience.  Note: an additional 4 years of relevant work experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
  • Demonstrated understanding of the non-conformance and corrective action process
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret clearance
  • Ability to work any shift as needed by the business.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with composites or manufacturing environment
  • 6+ years of audit experience
