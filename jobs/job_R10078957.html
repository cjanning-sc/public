Northrop Grumman Defense Systems in Northridge, CA is currently seeking a Assembler 3 or 4 to join our team.

The primary responsibility in this role is to assemble subassemblies in accordance with written procedures per quality standards using a variety of tooling.  Documentation will be completed to support customer requirements. The selected candidate will work in a manufacturing environment where specific, required, production goals will need to be adhered to, and ESD and FOD procedures followed; attention to detail is paramount.

Due to the nature of the work, the selected candidate will be part of a team that works closely together, so ethical and respectful team interaction and communication is of the utmost importance.  Even though this position is in a team environment and set up for team success, the selected candidate will be required to work independently with little supervision. Occasional heavy lifting and occasional overtime may be required.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must be able to obtain and maintain a US Government Secret clearance; US citizenship required.
  • High school diploma or GED.
  • 4 years of experience in electronics testing or related field.
  • Experience using hand and small power tools.
  • A good mechanical aptitude, and problem solving skills.
  • Ability to interpret and execute assembly requirements provided in written specifications.
  • Experience working independently and in a team environment, and interfacing with individuals at multiple levels in the organization.
  • Ability to balance and lift fixtures up to 35 lbs.
  • Ability to read technical publications and drawings
  • Familiarity with systems within a production environment.
  • Basic computer literacy; ability to interface with computers.
  • Willingness and ability to assist with assembly instructions.
  • Experience with any of the following:
    • Soldering
    • Hand tools including torque wrenches
    • Excel data entry
    • ESD & Grounding
    • FOD prevention
    • Tool control

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience working in an aerospace industry.
  • Exposure to quality systems.
