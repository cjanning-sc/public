Embark on a career putting innovative, reliable, and agile products and ideas into orbit, and beyond. Our space systems connect and protect millions of people on earth every day, now and for the future. Explore your future and launch your career today.

ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Detroit, UT and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What you’ll get to do: 

  • Coordinate with systems engineers from various teams to validate design maturity for internal and external design reviews
  • Assist team to orchestrate internal and external design reviews
  • Work as a liaison between Integrated Product Team (IPT) stakeholders and external stakeholders
  • Manage the process to resolve comments and actions pertaining to the design review process.
  • Have an integral role in tracking completion of Design Review requirements and activities
  • Lead a collaborative Jira tracking environment project for use internally and Government colleagues.
  • Candidate will upload documents through CPLM for design reviews and other related events

As a full-time employee of ACME Engineering Space Systems, you are eligible for our robust benefits package including:

  • Medical, Dental & Vision coverage
  • 401k
  • Educational Assistance
  • Life Insurance
  • Employee Assistance Programs & Work/Life Solutions
  • Paid Time Off
  • Health & Wellness Resources
  • Employee Discounts 

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80A. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off.

You’ll bring these qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics)
  • A minimum 5 years of related experience with a Bachelor’s degree; 3 years with a Master’s; or 0 years with PhD.
  • U.S. Citizenship with an active DoD Secret Clearance with an investigation date within the last 6 years, and the ability to obtain Special Access Program Clearance

These qualifications would be nice to have:

  • Previous Systems Engineering experience and understanding of the systems engineering ‘V’
  • DoD Top Secret Security Clearance
  • Special Program access
  • Experienced in consolidating stakeholder needs across multiple Integrated Product Teams (IPTs)
  • Knowledge of Project Management principles and experience, ability to utilize them proficiently through EMD and Production program phases
  • Experience as a Scrum Master
  • Experience applying Agile Engineering principles and methodologies
  • Proficient using IBM Rationale Dynamic Object-Oriented Requirements System (DOORS)
  • Experience with Interface Control Documents (ICDs)
  • Experience with Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) and Systems Engineering
  • Experience using Common Product Lifecycle Management (CPLM)
  • Proven success managing multiple projects simultaneously
  • Experience with Atlassian suite (JIRA, Confluence, etc.)
  • Experience developing, implementing, and updating project schedules
  • Experience with Interfacing with various stakeholders to drive to a consensus, conclusion, or action plan
  • Experience developing program procedures and processes
  • Experience with design reviews
  • Excellent decision making and communication (written and oral) skills
