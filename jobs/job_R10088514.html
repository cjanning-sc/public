ACME Engineering Defense Systems is currently seeking a Test Engineer for our Naperville, Illinois office. 

The ACME Engineering Defense Systems Propulsion Systems and Controls (PS&C) facility in Naperville, Illinois began solid-propellant rocket operations in 1948. Today, the Naperville PS&C facility designs, develops, and produces solid-propellant propulsion and ordnance systems in programs professionally managed for a wide variety of Government and commercial customers.  The facility also serves as the hub for offsite engineering and test facilities in Ronkonkoma, NY, West Chester, PA and San Jose, OH.

This position can be filled at the Associate level 1 Engineer or the level 2 Engineer.  The level to be hired depends upon qualifications and exhibited experience.

Job Summary:

Under broad direction, develop system testing scope, plan and estimates in collaboration with QA. Evaluate and recommend changes and/or tools to improve processes. This position supports physical hardware testing, this is not a software testing position.

Job Responsibilities:

Provides technical support in the office and field as the mechanical test engineer. Review testing requirements, develop Test Plans and write Test Procedures. Executes tests, manages and prioritizes problems/failures. Reviews and evaluates test results and provides summary reports. Works with cross functional, multi‐discipline test teams. Interfaces with technicians, customers, vendors and management teams to resolve problems. Works directly with energetic test arrangements in the field and provides onsite mechanical and electrical/instrumentation troubleshooting support. Participate in an Integrated Product Team (IPT) environment.

Basic Qualifications Associate Test Engineer (level 1):

  • Requires Bachelors In STEM, preference is Aerospace, Mechanical or Industrial Engineering
  • Proficient ability to use Microsoft Office applications
  • Ability to read and understand manufacturing drawings and standards
  • Must have good communication skills and be able to write and deliver presentations.
  • Ability to obtain a Secret Clearance which requires US Citizenship as a prerequisite
  • Work well as a member of a project team

Basic Qualifications Test Engineer (level 2):

  • Requires Bachelors In STEM, preference is Aerospace, Mechanical or Industrial Engineering and 2 years of experience.  Will also consider those with a Masters degree and 0 years of experience.
  • Experience working in a manufacturing environment
  • Experience with laboratory or manufacturing test system design, hardware integration and support.
  • Proficient ability to use Microsoft Office applications
  • Ability to read and understand manufacturing drawings and standards
  • Must have good communication skills and be able to write and deliver presentations.
  • Ability to obtain a Secret Clearance which requires US Citizenship as a prerequisite
  • Work well as a member of a project team

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Environmental testing experience: thermal conditioning, shock and vibration testing
  • Experience working with explosives and/or hazardous environments
