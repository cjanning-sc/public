ACME Engineering Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems have exciting career opportunity for a Sr Principal Network Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position location is in Madison, WI.

JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Provide specific detailed information for hardware and software selections, implementation techniques and tools for the most efficient solution to meet business needs, including present and future capacity requirements.
  • Conduct testing of network design.
  • Maintain technical expertise in all areas of network and computer hardware and software interconnections and interfacing, such as routers, switches, firewalls, load balancers, multiplexers, hubs, bridges, gateways, etc.
  • Evaluate and report on new communication technologies to enhance capabilities of the network.
  • The positions are normally day shift hours, but there will be periods where off hours work will be required.  The Candidate will be required to be part of an on-call rotation.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Network/Communications Engineering or related STEM discipline with 5 years of experience, Master’s Degree with 3 years of experience, or 0 years of experience with a PhD.  4 additional years of experience in lieu bachelor’s degree.
  • Experience with IT and Data Center Infrastructure, including planning, designing, and implementing/installing network solutions for Data Centers and/or customer sites.
  • Solid understanding of applicable commercial technologies including hardware acceleration, and commercial best practices. Solid understanding of the OSI or TCP/IP model.
    Understanding of common commercial implementations of IP networks; including experience with network routing protocols and tunneling control plane protocols found in IP networks, and out-of-band control networks found in SDH and OTN overhead bytes.
  • Experience with Network configuration and troubleshooting, to include routing, access lists, firewalls, sub-netting, CIDR and load balancers. 
  • Experience with securing network systems (including commercial IP networks) by establishing and enforcing policies; defining and monitoring access; configuring network equipment to include access lists/firewall rules.  Ability to ensure systems are secure in accordance with customer standards.
  • Strong verbal/written communication skills, along with the ability to work well in a government facility within a multi-contractor (badgeless) team environment.  Ability to communicate with and support local as well as remote customers.
  • U.S. Citizenship required.
  • The ability to obtain and maintain an active Secret clearance.
  • Travel 25% Domestic & International

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Industry certifications, such as Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP), Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA), Network+, Security+, and/or Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP)
  • Experience administering Linux and Windows.
  • VMWare or other virtualization experience.
  • Experience with Kickstart using Red Hat Linux.
  • Ability to design complex network collection systems.
  • Experience performing system validation and verification on complex networks.
  • Ability to objectively assess architectural limitations or shortcoming in such areas as scaling, speed and throughput.
  • Strong interpersonal skills: ability to effectively interact with a variety of stakeholders including factory engineers, management, sub-contractors, trainees and the customer.
  • Active a Secret Clearance
