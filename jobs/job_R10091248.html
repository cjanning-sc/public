ACME Engineering is currently seeking a Sr. Principal Quality Engineer to join our team in Promontory, Michigan.

ACME Engineering Space Systems designs, builds and delivers space, defense and aviation-related systems to customers around the world. Our main products include launch vehicles and related propulsion systems; missile products, subsystems and defense electronics; precision weapons, armament systems and ammunition; satellites and associated space components and services; and advanced aerospace structures.

Role description & Responsibilities:

Candidate will be responsible for supporting manufacturing and test activities for electronic assemblies used in various space launch vehicles. Specific duties may include: supporting development of processes and procedures for the assembly and test of mechanical/electronic assemblies; reviewing and approving drawings; documenting and dispositioning hardware discrepancies and pursuing root cause and corrective actions; supporting required MRB processing; perform inspection of components and assemblies; supporting manufacturing activities with supervisors, program engineers and various customer organizations; reviewing design changes on WIP or completed assemblies; analyzing data for trends and recommending changes to improve process and product quality, interfacing with suppliers for auditing and corrective action/continuous improvement related to component assemblies and parts.

Education

  • 9 Years with Bachelors in Science; 7 Years with Masters; 4 Years with PhD.

Experience and Required Skills

  • Ideal candidate shall possess related work experience working with mechanical/electrical assemblies, pneumatic systems, structures, and tooling. 
  • Should have a demonstrated ability to work with minimal oversight and in a multi-discipline team environment, providing insight and solutions to complicated issues both technical and procedural.
  • Work related experience should demonstrate increasing levels of responsibility. Extensive Root Cause and Problem-Solving experience is highly desirable.
  • Discrepancy Reporting and MRB is highly desirable.  System integration and range safety experience a plus.
  • Experience working with TipQa, MES, and CostPoint software is a Plus
  • Proficient at planning /organization, and decision-making skills.

Security Clearance

*Requires current /active secret clearance minimum
