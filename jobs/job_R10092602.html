ACME Engineering Aeronautics has an opening for a Manager Supply Chain 2, to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in St Augustine, Pennsylvania. This position will be on-site at a variety of our warehouse and office locations to support multiple functions within Global Supply Chain. There will be little to no option for telework or remote work for this position due to program and function need.

At ACME Engineering, we are on the cutting edge of innovation-- building the next generation of sophisticated aircraft to protect our country. Our diverse portfolio of programs means there are endless paths to cultivate your career. We are well-known for our inclusive, family environment, as well as our excellent work/life balance. We also offer exceptional benefits/healthcare, robust total rewards options, diverse and engaging teams, career and growth development opportunities, and a great 401K matching program. This position will support our 4/10 schedule with every Friday Off!

This position will be responsible for activities and knowledge within multiple disciplines across the Supply Chain process. Participates in various aspects of supply chain process areas: Supply Chain-Plan, Supply Chain-Source, Supply Chain-Make, Supply Chain-Deliver as well as Supply Chain-Operations. Exhibits end-to-end supply chain competency and manages one or more Supply Chain functions (Planning, Sourcing, Small Business/Disadvantaged Programs, Supplier Quality, Warehouse & Transportation, Analytics, Compliance, Systems, etc.).

Manage the daily operations and lead a 10–19-person team or work unit (direct supervision of the staff, assignment of work, schedules, day to day workflow, and operating costs) comprised of lower-level experienced exempt/non-exempted employees exercising minimal latitude and independence in their assignments to accomplish required statement of work.

Daily efforts will include and are not limited to:

  • Oversee the coordination and movement of material throughout the site while ensuring process/procedural compliance safeguarding both personnel and product.

  • Monitor work operations of exempt and/or non-exempt employees on a daily basis and actively assists or provides direction to subordinates as required.

  • Provide direction to all subordinates using established policies and precedents and ensure subordinate work is reviewed for adequacy in meeting cost and quality objectives.

  • Schedule equipment and personnel to manage material supply and demands for the production or purchase of components or parts based on a master production schedule, shop load and inventory requirements.

  • Coordinate interdepartmental activity with quality assurance, manufacturing, purchasing, engineering, inventory control, production control, etc

  • Plans for and executes activities in support of production schedules and coordinates with internal and external departments to ensure materials and requirements support production requirements.

  • Advises management of the status of work in progress, material availability, and potential production problems to ensure that personnel, equipment, materials and services are provided as needed.

  • May perform on-going operational tasks of organizational groups or be assigned to teams working on sector level tasks, projects or process changes/improvements.

  • Frequent contacts with internal and external personnel and outside customer representatives at various management levels concerning operations or scheduling or parts management issues specific to the build of the product lines in St Augustine.

  • Conduct briefings and participate program/ homeroom meetings for internal and/or external representatives concerning material issues, supplier performance, and manufacturing/ programmatic risks and/or constraints.

  • - Regular interfacing with manufacturing supervision and site leadership to support program/ site needs, process improvements, and resource management.

  • Warehouse/Kitting/Distribution Manager supporting Full Rate production programs as well as small to medium size spare programs.

  • Managing the daily site operations for Shipping, Receiving, Distribution, Kitting, Chemical and JIT Hardware Operations
