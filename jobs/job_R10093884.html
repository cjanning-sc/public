ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Manufacturing Engineer / Principal Manufacturing Engineer. This position will be located in Detroit, UT and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package. Final grade offered will be determined based on qualified experience.

What you’ll get to do: 

  • Candidate will have sound technical experience and understanding of Production Processes for Assembly, Fabrication, Planning & Tooling. For day-to-day tasks, must possess strong organizational, communication, interpersonal, and PC computer skills. Understanding of tooling strategies for high-rate production, and document control. Possess a can-do attitude and willingness to learn, advise and guide team members in the challenging environment of a startup program

  • Supports production, business, and other processes in a team-based manufacturing environment.

  • Performs activities in a variety of cross-functional areas including Manufacturing Readiness Assessments, total quality management, manufacturing equipment, facilities engineering, process reengineering and establish sector business area procedural documentation.

  • May facilitate self-directed work teams, provide training in core skill areas to teams and peers, and interact with internal and external customers with respect to product tooling, problem resolution, and process improvement.

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80A. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off.

You’ll bring these qualifications:

  • Manufacturing Engineer: Bachelor’s Degree in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math) with 2 years of experience OR Master's Degree with 0 years of experience - experience in medium to large scale tooling fabrication and/or production fabrication and assembly of composites, metallic, coatings application, and/or production manufacturing operations.

  • Principal Manufacturing Engineer: Bachelor’s Degree in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math) with 5 years of experience OR Master's Degree with 2 years of experience - experience in medium to large scale tooling fabrication and/or production fabrication and assembly of composites, metallic, coatings application, and/or production manufacturing operations.

  • Must have U.S. Citizenship with the ability to obtain interim Secret level clearance prior to start, and ability to obtain and maintain Secret level clearance within a reasonable timeframe after starting per the needs of the business.

  • Must have the ability to obtain, and maintain access to Special Access Programs (SAP)  as a condition of continued employment.

These qualifications would be nice to have:

  • Experience with CPLM (Common Product Life Cycle Management) and or Manufacturing Execution System (MES).

  • Experience with Inter Continental Ballistic Missiles (ICBM) or other missile systems

  • Experience managing/coordinating technical projects (Cost, schedule and technical performance).

  • Experience identifying and mitigating producibility requirements

  • Experience in overseeing sub-tier suppliers

  • Knowledge of AS6500 requirements.

  • Experience leading or participating in the definition and execution of Manufacturing Plans/ CDRLs, MRA (Manufacturing Readiness Assessments) and MRL’s (Manufacturing Readiness Level).

  • Experience reading and applying GD&T to a blueprint & interfacing with 3D CAD e.g., NX, etc.

  • Experience determining risk mitigation and developing alternative work around plans

  • Experience coordinating and documentation Condition of Supply Requirements

  • Understanding the process for the coordination, design and fabrication requirements for tools and fixtures with customers, both internal and external.
