LMDS has an opening for a Manufacturing Technician 2  to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Chandler, Arizona.

Job description:

  • Using work instructions, engineering drawings, and technical manuals, assembles aerospace cables/harnesses to specifications.
  • Cut wire, crimp contacts, pin connectors, apply shielding, and torque hardware.
  • Documents actions by completing production and quality verification.
  • Good understanding of engineering drawings, instructions, parts lists, and schematics
  • Proficient in the use of hand tools
  • IPC-A-620 certification preferred
  • Soldering experience preferred but not required
  • High School diploma or equivalent
