​The Payload and Ground Systems organization within the ACME Engineering Space Systems pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. ACME Engineering Space Systems Sector in Kent, CA is seeking Satellite Systems Engineers (Level 2), OR Principal Satellite Systems Engineer (Level 3) to join our team. This role will report on-site to our Kent campus daily

Selected candidate will conduct mission and program specific real time spacecraft and satellite flight operations during readiness, execution and evaluation phases. Develops detailed plans and procedures to provide maximum mission support in areas such as orbit dynamics, satellite performance analysis, command and control of on-orbit satellites. Provides technical direction for on-orbit mission support, plans for critical vehicle malfunctions and recommends alternate operating techniques to continue maximum mission support. Conducts failure analysis of the performance of the orbiting satellites via telemetry data.

The successful candidate will be expected to:

  • Provide mission operations support to the SBIRS program.
  • Support system maintenance activities such as procedure updates, architecture upkeep, and formal program milestones.
  • Work with systems engineers to conduct anomaly review board investigations as necessary.
  • Support testing using simulators and resolve discrepancies.
  • Support development and test command procedures, telemetry pages, and other products
  • Support operations readiness activities including customer meetings.
  • Support operations, including shift work.
  • Monitor vehicle state of health across all subsystems and perform short and long-term telemetry trending.
  • Provide status reporting through both the functional and program reporting branches.
  • Provide mentoring, guidance and manage tasks for the operations team.
  • Support internal and external customer meetings through proficient verbal and documented (MS PowerPoint, MS Word, MS Excel) communications.

Basic Qualifications: (Level 2)

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) & 2 years of related engineering experience, OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field
  • Experience working with MATLAB
  • Active & Current DoD Secret security clearance 
  • Prior experience conducting on-orbit mission operations and resolving satellite and ground system anomalies.

Basic Qualifications: (Level 3- Principal)

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) & 5 years of related engineering experience, OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field & 3 years of related engineering experience
  • Experience working with MATLAB
  • Active & Current DoD Secret security clearance 
  • Prior experience conducting on-orbit mission operations and resolving satellite and ground system anomalies.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master's Degree or higher specifically in Aerospace or Electrical Engineering
  • Unix environment experience.
  • Experience with engineering tools such as STK
