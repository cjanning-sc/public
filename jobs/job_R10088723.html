Are you ready to put your experience to work at Northrop Grumman? If so, our Defense Systems wants you to join our team as an Avionics Technician (level 3) and join our team of qualified, diverse individuals for a rotational OCONUS position based out of Palmdale, CA.

Your duties include:

  • Install, test, troubleshoot, and repair avionics systems wiring in aircraft in accordance with technical specifications, engineering instructions, and FAA regulations.

  • Fabricates, installs and tests installation, routing, clamping and wiring harnesses.

  • Reads wiring diagrams and blueprints.

  • May receive additional training and be cross utilized into aircraft low observable maintenance to fulfill the daily activities and duties required to meet contract requirements. Low Observable tasks include repair of composite or metallic structures, and sanding, stripping, testing, masking and other preparation tasks. Temporary assigned work location can be at Edwards AFB, CA.
