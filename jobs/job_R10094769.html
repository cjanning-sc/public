Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking Sr Principal Financial Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is located in Roy, UT.

Roles and Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • This position will partner with the Rates and Budgets and Compliance teams to manage financial aspects of the business to achieve the Division’s financial and strategic objectives.  

  • The successful candidate must have the ability to foster a collaborative culture that sustains the momentum around ethics and integrity, affordability, innovation, and continuous improvement.

  • The candidate will maintain the highest level of commitment to quality, compliance, and internal controls in adherence to company values and ethics. 

  • Roles and responsibilities may include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Provide financial analysis and reporting with the ability to clearly communicate with senior management as it relates to rates performance and compliance financial status and analyses.

  • Mentor junior Business Management analysts within the team.

  • Support the preparation and quality control of Forward Pricing rate proposal packages to ensure timely and accurate submittals to the Defense Contract Management Association (DCMA) and Defense Contract Audit Association (DCAA).

  • Build and maintain positive working relationships with internal and external customers, including DCMA and DCAA, and provide timely responses to requests for information in support of various audits or ad hoc requests.

  • Provide support for SOX controls, Forward Pricing, Estimating System, and Accounting System related audits by internal and external auditors (DCMA/DCAA and D&T).

  • Support unallowable testing, analysis, and cost corrections by enforcing policies and procedures, ensuring rate segment compliance with internal SOX controls, and educate employees on compliance guidelines.

  • Support Disclosure Statement updates and ensure consistency in Forward Pricing.

  • Assist with the preparation, submission, and audits for the segment’s incurred cost proposal.

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off.

RELOCATION ASSISTANCE MAY BE AVAILABLE FOR THIS OPPORTUNITY.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree with 10 years of related professional experience in Accounting, Finance, Business Administration - OR - a Master's degree with 8 years of related professional experience in Accounting, Finance, Business Administration An additional four years’ experience accepted in lieu of a degree.

  • Proficiency in MS Office Suite, specifically a high level of proficiency in Microsoft Excel (pivot tables, lookups, chart generation, etc.)

  • Prior experience in the aerospace and defense industry

  • Detail and results oriented; ability to deliver timely and accurate results under time sensitive conditions.

  • Ability to obtain and maintain DoD Secret level security clearance. US citizenship is a prerequisite.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD security clearance. US citizenship is a prerequisite.

  • Ability to effectively manage competing priorities and deadlines.

  • Ability to handle multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment.

  • Excellent problem solving and organizational skills.

  • Aerospace/Defense Industry experience.
