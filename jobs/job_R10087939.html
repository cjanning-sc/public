Northrop Grumman Space Systems - Support and Training Segment Operations & Planning team is seeking a Principal / Sr. Principal Support and Training Operations and Planning Project Management / Agile Scrum Lead to provide project management and Agile Scrum Lead facilitation.  This position will be permanently located in Roy, Utah and will support the Sentinel program Training Devices team. Northrop Grumman supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What You'll Get To Do:

  • Provides support to the Support and Training Segment 

  • Leads for Operations & Planning on Agile execution across sub-IPTs.

  • Supports design, updates, and maintenance of dashboards tracking technical and program performance through Agile schedule and cost metrics.

  • Builds communications and visualization of Agile metrics to support complete understanding of the Segment's IMS and Control Accounts (CAs).

  • Provides data analytics inputs to Segment artifacts, products, and services that facilitate program execution as part of Knowledge Management for the Segment.

  • Leads facilitation of Agile Program Increment Planning on a quarterly cadence with tracking and status on progress

  • Ensures successful Program Increment (PI) Planning in a Scrum Master role for PI Events.

  • Supports creation and implementation of tools for Sub-IPT standardized approaches for data metrics visualization weekly performance capture.

  • Risk Management at Sub-IPT levels for input into the Risk and Opportunity process.

  • Supports Sub-IPT collaboration through the Segment Ops & Planning team and to / from the Segment Program Integration Team (PIT).

  • Technical process and progress facilitation in support of the Segment Chief Engineer.
