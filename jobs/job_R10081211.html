Northrop Grumman Mission Systems Sector (NGMS) is a leading global provider of secure software-defined, hardware enabled mission systems. Our company is pioneering capabilities in a wide variety of sectors that keep our nation and our allies safe from undersea to space and cyberspace. NGMS is seeking a Systems Engineer and Principal Systems Engineer to join out Secure Processing team within the Systems Organization. As a Systems Engineer at Northrop Grumman you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation. Through the use of Model-based Engineering, DevSecOps and Agile practices we continue to evolve how we deliver critical national defense products and capabilities for the warfighter. Our success is grounded in our ability to embrace change, move quickly and continuously drive innovation. The successful candidate will be collaborative, open, transparent, and team-oriented with a focus on team empowerment & shared responsibility, flexibility, continuous learning and a culture of automation.

The selected Systems or Principal Systems Engineer is responsible for testing device drivers, middleware, and applications developed by software engineers for use on Linux and/or VxWorks Platforms.  They will help with the development of system and software requirements, and technical specifications.  They will help identify risks, suggest mitigation, track defects, develop resolutions, and report status.  They will collaborate with engineers to integrate, debug, and verify the developed software and drivers. They will help with writing and executing system and software unit tests to verify and validate requirements and other assigned duties.

This position is located in Linthicum, MD.
