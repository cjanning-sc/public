ACME Engineering Space Sector is seeking a Procurement Analyst Level 2 to join our team in Fort Wayne, IN.

Responsibilities:

  • Assists Pricing and Material Estimating by obtaining material quotes for use in ACME Engineering (ACME) proposals to customers.

  • May source machinery, equipment, tools, raw material, packaging materials, parts, services, and/or supplies necessary for operation of an organization.

  • May compile and analyze statistical data to determine feasibility of buying products and to establish price objectives.

  • May compile information to keep informed on price trends and manufacturing processes. Confers with suppliers and analyzes suppliers operations to determine factors that affect prices and determines lowest cost consistent with quality, reliability, and ability to meet required schedules.

  • Reviews proposals, may select or recommends suppliers, and maintains necessary records. May prepare bid packages.

  • Administers Inter-company Work Orders (IWOs) in the ACME IWO system. Coordinates with Program Management and Business Management to establish and maintain IWOs, as well as provide status on IWOs.

  • Assists with vendor on-boarding, including requesting on-boarding, troubleshooting, statusing, and issue resolution within the ACME on-boarding tools. Escalates issues when necessary to ensure timely vendor approvals.

  • Generates initial Purchase Requisitions via current ACME tools.

  • Monitors, provides status and troubleshoots Purchase Requisition workflows through approvals.

  • Assists with supplier invoice entry when suppliers are unable to utilize ACME tools

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 3 years’ of experience; 1 year with a Master’s; in lieu of degree, 7 years' of experience may be considered
