ACME Engineering’s Enterprise Services organization is seeking a motivated and energetic IBM Planning Analytics (TM1) developer, with a background in financial planning and forecasting to work in the ACME Engineering Enterprise Systems Project Management Office, supporting the Space Systems sector.

The selected candidate will design, build, test, deploy and maintain multiple IBM Cognos Planning Analytics based models to meet financial planning and forecasting business requirements throughout the monthly and annual financial cycles. The incumbent will be required to work with internal customers to solicit and analyze requirements and manage the overall development process from start to finish.


This position can be hired at the Principal or Sr. Principal Level (level 3 or level 4), depending upon years of experience and education of the candidate.

Basic Qualifications for the Principal Level : 
 

To be considered for this position, you must minimally meet the knowledge, skills, and abilities listed below:

• Bachelor's degree from an accredited university with 5 years of relevant experience – OR – a Master’s degree with 3 years of related experience
• IBM Planning Analytics (TM1) development background
• Skilled in all aspects of the system development lifecycle for development and implementation of IBM Cognos Planning Analytics models
• Ability to apply ingenuity, innovation and advanced technical expertise to find solutions to complex business needs within the functionality of the IBM Business Analytics product set; develop new design concepts when needed
• Possess expertise in Financial and Accounting practices and standards; Translate Finance and Accounting knowledge into the technical objectives required to meet business modeling needs
• Determine objectives and approaches to projects and operational duties with minimal direction

Basic Qualifications for the Sr. Principal Level :

• Bachelor’s degree from an accredited university with 9 years of related experience – OR – a Master’s degree with 7 years of related experience
• IBM Planning Analytics (TM1) development background
• Skilled in all aspects of the system development lifecycle for development and implementation of Planning Analytics models
• Ability to apply ingenuity, innovation and advanced technical expertise to find solutions to complex business needs within the functionality of the IBM Business Analytics product set; develop new design concepts when needed
• Possess expertise in Financial and Accounting practices and standards; Translate Finance and Accounting knowledge into the technical objectives required to meet business modeling needs
• Determine objectives and approaches to projects and operational duties with minimal direction


Preferred Qualifications: 

Candidates with these desired skills will be given preferential consideration:


• Ability to deliver quality solutions that are consistent with the organization’s need and objectives; while avoiding rework.
