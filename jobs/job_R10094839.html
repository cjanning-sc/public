Northrop Grumman Space Division offers exciting opportunities for individuals looking to take part in the development and deployment of cutting-edge space technologies. With a focus on innovation, the Space Division is a leader in designing, building, and launching satellites, space vehicles, and related systems for government and commercial customers. Working in this dynamic environment requires a team of highly-skilled and motivated professionals who are eager to explore the possibilities of space exploration.

Northrop Grumman is seeking an Associate Supply Chain Procurement Specialist. This position will be located in Redondo Beach, CA. The qualified candidate will become part of the Northrop Grumman’s Strategic Space Systems Division, Global Supply Chain team.

 

Responsibilities:

  • Interface with Suppliers and Accounting for payment resolution.
  • Interface with Suppliers for quoting in support of proposals and PO placement.
  • Process and negotiate Non-Disclosure Agreements with Suppliers
  • Facilitate new supplier onboarding and interface with Vendor Administration and Suppliers to oversee completion of registration processes.
  • Work with Suppliers to maintain regular vendor maintenance and annual certifications.
  • Collaborate with Suppliers and Cyber Security team to ensure supplier compliance
  • Interface and collaborate with Programs and other organizations within and outside of Global Supply Chain.
  • Ability to plan, schedule and arrange activities to accomplish objectives.
  • Person should possess outstanding written and verbal communication skills and be extremely organized.

 

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree from an accredited university in Business Administration, Supply Chain, or related field; may consider 4 years of related work experience in lieu of a degree.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office including Word, PowerPoint, and Excel.

 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Minimum 6 months (internship or otherwise) of experience in supply, chain, purchasing/procurement, operations, and/or business
  • Previous internship/co-op or other work experience
  • Experience within the Aerospace or Aviation industry
  • Experience with ERP systems, preferably SAP
