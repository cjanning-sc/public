Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Manager Business Management 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Melbourne, Florida, Palmdale, CA, San Diego, CA or Redondo Beach, CA.

Essential Functions:

Responsibilities include oversight of Business Area Capital, NCTA, & business base development processes for Strike Vice President and CFO.  Capital planning and budgeting responsibilities include coordination with business area leadership in developing capital requirements and priorities, budget management, reporting and analysis. AOP/LRSP development, and evaluation of property accounting issues. Interface with sector capital planning as required. NCTA responsibilities include budget management, reporting and analysis, coordination with business area leadership, as well as coordination of LRSP development activities. The Resource Manager will be responsible for all reporting and analysis, all related briefing development  and briefing to division leadership.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree from an accredited university and 7+ years of related Aerospace Industry experience, or a Masters degree and 5+ years.
  • 4+ years’ experience in US Government contracts along with 2+ years of Leadership experience, either leading direct reports or integrated product teams
  • Experience with all aspects/phases of Business Management in developing and managing financial goals and overseeing monthly financial reporting
  • Experience coordinating and leading a business functional area (i.e., Contracts, Cost & Schedule, Pricing & Estimating, Compliance, etc.)
  • The ability to travel 10+% of the time
  • Active Secret Security Clearance and ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access


 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree and 5+ years of related aerospace industry experience
  • Experience with proposal development and negotiation is desired.
  • Working level knowledge of the Strike program is preferred
