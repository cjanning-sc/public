The Integrated Missions Operating Unit (within the Space Security Business Unit and the Tactical Space Systems Division) is seeking a motivated and talented Resources, Operations, and Projects Lead (Business Process Analyst or Principal Business Process Analyst). This position will report directly to the Program Portfolio Manager and will be responsible for:

  • Workforce and resource planning, to include providing inputs to the division level

  • Team member on-boarding and off-boarding

  • Facility and infrastructure planning and provisioning

  • Create, maintain, and execute operations processes in support of program execution

  • Integrate, analyze, synthesize, and communicate task order performance data and metrics

  • Lead and execute the development, implementation and enhancement of operating policies, processes and procedures that affect the organization's short- and long-range goals and strategies

  • Plan and coordinate operations and special projects in multiple areas (e.g., short- to long-term planning, joint ventures, business development, internal communications, budgeting)

  • Diverse and varying responsibilities according to the organization's evolving and growing needs

  • Contractor Data Requirement List (CDRL)/ Subcontractor Data Requirement List (SDRL) management utilizing the new Data Delivery Management System (DMDS) tool

A successful candidate is solution oriented, motivated, can-do, organized, and enjoys working with a diverse team creating complex and challenging solutions critical to our nation’s security. 

This position is hiring for either a level 2 (Business Process Analyst) or level 3 (Principal Business Process Analyst). To be considered, you must meet the qualifications as outlined for either one of these levels.

Locations:  Des Moines, VA

Basic Qualification for Business Process Analyst- Level 2

  • Bachelor's Degree with 3 years of experience in Operations and Projects or 1 year of stated experience with a Master's Degree

  • Experienced user of JIRA, Confluence, MS Office Tools (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), MS Teams, and/or SharePoint

  • Active Top Secret security clearance.

Preferred Qualifications for Business Process Analyst- Level 2

  • Advanced Education and/or Relevant Certifications

Basic Qualifications for Principal Business Process Analyst - Level 3

  • Bachelor's Degree with 6 years of experience in Operations and Projects or 4 year of stated experience with a Master's Degree; or PhD.

  • Active Top Secret security clearance.

  • Expert user of JIRA, Confluence, MS Office Tools (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), MS Teams, and/or SharePoint

Preferred Qualifications for Principal Business Process Analyst - Level 3

  • Advanced Education and/or Relevant Certifications

  • Active Top Secret/SCI security
