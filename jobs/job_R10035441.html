ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Systems Architect Engineer / Principal Systems Architect Engineer to join the Systems Engineering Team. This position is located in Detroit, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Final grade offered will be based on candidate experience level.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected job candidate will be joining the Model Based - Requirements, Integration, and Architecture (MB-RIA) team. MB-RIA has opportunities to work in multiple weapons system focus areas. This team is responsible for verification, architecture, and model governance of the GBSD weapon system. The verification portion of the team is responsible for system and segment requirements, requirements management and updates, and verification. The architecture portion of the team provides oversight for integration and delivery of architecture models and develops weapon system architecture. While the model governance portion of the team directs the system model methodology definition, model best practices documentation, and model configuration management. This team will provide candidates the opportunity to integrate complex design elements across multiple Product Teams of the GBSD weapon system and work with all levels of the weapon system to ensure a comprehensive weapon system design.

Specific tasks include:

• Coordinate with integrated product teams for establishing content within the architecture

• Coordinate across multiple integrated product teams to ensure development of an integrated GBSD architecture model

• Support oversight of the Architecture Model customer deliveries

• Conduct integration readiness reviews with segment architecture teams to gauge model governance achieved

• Define and execute model integration processes, tools, and techniques

• Engage in formal and informal mentoring to support knowledge transfer and talent development

• Coordinate across multiple integrated product teams to ensure development of an integrated GBSD architecture model
