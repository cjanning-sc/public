Northrop Grumman is currently seeking an experienced Sr. Millwright / Plumber Pipefitter to provide technical support to our Promontory Maintenance team at our Promontory Utah Facilities.

Role description & Responsibilities:

This Maintenance Craft position will perform preventative maintenance, troubleshooting and repair, as well as new construction on plant facilities, and equipment. This position will have an emphasis on supporting the AP Enterprise Facilities. The candidate may also be expected to perform other mechanical skills to maintain and repair industrial plant processing equipment such as, mixers, pumps, air compressors and cranes. Candidates with experience in other mechanical disciplines such as metal fabrication, welding, plumbing and pipefitting are preferred.  Shift work and overtime are required and the successful candidate must pass a background check for the Federal Safe Explosives Act (SEA).  

Minimum Requirements:  

  • 4 plus years of recent work experience in the millwright field.

    • Position may be filled at higher level requiring 6 plus years of recent work experience in millwright field.

  • Knowledge and experience with reading and interpreting blueprints and drawings.

  • Knowledge and experience diagnosing, troubleshooting and repairing precision tooling and/or processing equipment. 

Preferred:

  • Experience with welding and metal fabrication.

  • 6G GTAW certified

  • Experience with plumbing and pipefitting

Skills & Qualifications:

  • Safety First attitude and personal work behavior  

  • Actively engage in maintenance continuous improvement efforts.

  • Demonstrates initiative and ambition to become proficient in all job duties

  • Able to work to performance standards with little or no supervision

  • Ability to work in a team environment

Education/Certifications:

  • High School Diploma or GED.
