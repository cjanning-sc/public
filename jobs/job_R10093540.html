Northrop Grumman Business Management is unit is seeking an Administrative Assistant 4 to support the team based in Falls Church, VA. The successful candidate will be a self-starter with experience supporting fast moving businesses.  Must have proven strong organization skills and ability to build relationships.

Job responsibilities include:

  • Provides general administrative support to a staff to several Directors & Managers
  • Arranges meetings and conferences, manages calendars and conflicts for group of Directors
  • Understand the business and adhere to an agile, fast-paced business cadence
  • Present a professional and positive impression
  • Performs normal office /clerical functions such as setting up and maintaining files
  • Books domestic and international travel and prepares expense reports
  • Receives, re-directs, and answers mail
  • Compiles information received from staff on a weekly and monthly basis
  • Reviews and provides edits for drafts and finished documents for appropriate grammatical usage and routes, collects signature
  • Answers questions relating to office operations and established policies and procedures
  • Provides administrative support for meetings, conferences, training activities, and visitors to the site

Basic Qualifications:

  • High school diploma or GED with a minimum of 6 years of additional education and/or related administrative experience
  • Proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel, Microsoft Access, PowerPoint, and Outlook to produce and distribute email, letters, memoranda, and reports
  • Must be able to work on-site in Fall Church, VA

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience maintaining multiple calendars
  • Demonstrated ability to perform detail oriented work without close supervision
  • Must be able to independently assess priorities and work with confidential information
  • Demonstrated ability to manage multiple administrative projects and initiatives
  • Experience scheduling meetings for senior management and coordinating logistical support for internal and external meetings, including offsite events
