The TTS(Technical Team Supervisor) is responsible to:

Ensure proper execution of daily operations in a test environment, working with energetic materials and/or hardware, to meet organizational objectives.  The TTS is responsible to work with Engineering, Quality, Operations, Program Management, Safety and external customers to identify and make recommendations to solve technical issues.  This position will be dedicated to the energetics testing  group.

Job Duties:

  • Daily scheduling of technicians.
  • Development and revisions of policies and procedures.
  • Participate in technician reviews, hiring, and recognition decisions.
  • Building ownership including maintenance, building PMs and facility management, and overall building cleanliness.
  • Provide input to management on daily operations and processes.
  • Cover off shift operations, on call, and weekend work.

Skills and Qualifications:

  • Bachelors Degree or 3-5 years of experience working in an energetics area.
  • Proven ability to communicate with multiple function groups across ACME Engineering
  • Excellent written communication and presentation skills
  • Ability to drive process improvement in a dynamic environment.
  • Excellent time and organizational management
  • Demonstrated ability to lead, coach and develop technicians.
  • Experience in an energetics testing and manufacturing environment including:
    •  Ingredient ordering and preparation
    • Working knowledge of Solumina planning and data entry
    • Maintenance permitting and workorder submission in Dispatch
    • Material processing and preparation
    • Data analysis and entry
