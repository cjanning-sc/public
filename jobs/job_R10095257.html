ACME Engineering Mission Systems is seeking a highly motivated individual to lead a team of Global Supply Chain Planning Specialists.  This individual will lead a team responsible for executing all aspects of Global Supply Chain in support of the Airborne Battle Management (ABM) Operating Unit under the Airborne Surveillance Business Unit. The candidate must understand the all aspects of GSCM - materials management, procurement, subcontracts, cost budgets and supplier quality to support cross functionally with program office, business management engineering, production, logistics in the performance of their roles and responsibilities. This position will be in Chicago, MD with a hybrid option.

Develops and implements long-term supply chain strategies and networks that address capacity issues, production location decisions, etc. in support of business growth. Supports business execution of new programs and initiatives that include promotional activities, forecast and coordination for key events, new product launches, and network redesigns. Monitors key performance indicators, analyzes cost-to-serve performance data, and coordinates cross-functional work teams to increase effectiveness of supply chain initiatives.

Additionally, this individual will be responsible for the development, mentoring, coaching, and engagement of the Global Supply Chain Planning Specialist team and their respective workload to ensure the Business Unit’s requirements are appropriately prioritized and executed to meet the needs of the division, fostering proper engagement with the program functional teams and to facilitate communication of requirements and status to program and business area stakeholders.

Responsibilities also include, but are not limited to, conducting a rhythm of material forecasting, program reviews, shop order critical path analysis, coordination of sourcing strategies with procurement, proposal support, and supplier oversight and status reporting on material shortages.  This position also requires technical leadership competencies to form strategies to construct various contracting types for major subcontract activities to meet the requirements of the programs and needs of the business to include long range procurement agreements, sourcing partnerships, continuous improvement initiatives, and identification of opportunities for enhanced efficiencies. 

This position will require the candidate to support Global Supply Chain compliance standards, strategies and initiatives.  The successful candidate will hold strong leadership skills, excellent oral and written skills, solid communication skills, knowledge of terms and conditions, an aptitude for DoD contracting and its related requirements and the ability to lead a diverse team with varying requirements and priorities in support of the business. This individual will monitor and report on the cost and schedule and quality performance for the program.

MANDATORY QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and work experience of a minimum 9 years in Supply Chain Management, planning, procurement, Program Management, contracts, subcontracts/procurement or related field

  • 3+ years of experience as a team lead and/or a management of people role

  • Experience working with multiple contracting types, including Firm Fixed Price and Cost Reimbursable, and process knowledge relating to special payment provisions (progress or performance based payments)

  • Strong leadership, communication, and writing skills

  • Expertise in Microsoft Word and Excel

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Masters Degree in Business, Supply Chain Management, Finance, or related field
  • US Government supply chain experience

  • Knowledge of SAP, CAM Experience (Budget Management, Contract proposal support, cost Estimating)

  • Knowledge of Earned Value Management
