The ACME Engineering Space Systems Sector has an immediate need for several Engineering Operations Project Management (OPM)/Principal Engineering Operations Project Management (OPM) - Technical in Chicago, MD.

The OPM candidate must possess excellent communication skills - both verbal and written, strong interpersonal skills, a strong problem-solving mindset, and the ability to present program visibility at internal/external meetings (to include all levels of management- including executive level). The OPM must be a self-starter and self-motivated individual who can work in a fast paced environment.

The responsibilities of an OPM include, but are not limited to:

• Managing and maintaining the day to day interfaces and communications between the assigned Program Management Office (PMO), Manufacturing, Test, Manufacturing Resource Planning (MRP), Mission Assurance (MA), Supply Chain (SCM), Engineering, and Shipping.

• Achieving targeted cost and schedule requirements.

• Facilitating internal meetings in a way to support design, production, and problem resolutions.

• Reporting Earned Value Management System (EVMS) and serving as the Cost Account Management (CAM) for manufacturing projects.

• Representing manufacturing as a subject matter expert at Non Advocate Reviews / Independent Cost Evaluations (NARICE).

• Leading or participating on a product team, responsible for product-level requirements analysis, engineering and manufacturing of the products assigned, product tracking, and product visibility.

• Representing manufacturing at the program Risk Review Board (RRB) meetings and creating / managing any applicable manufacturing risks and/or opportunities from the program risk register.

• Supporting and generating proposal documentation as needed to foster new work.

This is not a Manager job. This is an individual contributor role.

This position can be filled as a Level 2 or a Level 3 based on the qualifications below.

Basic Qualifications for Level 2:

• Bachelor's degree with 2 years of relevant work experience; a Master's degree; or 6 years of related experience

• Experience in engineering, manufacturing, testing, and/or design

• Ability to obtain and maintain a Top Secret Clearance per business requirements (US citizenship is required)

• Experience with SAP or other Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system and MS Office applications including Excel, Word, Project, and Power Point

Basic Qualifications for Level 3:

• Bachelor's degree with 5+ years of relevant work experience; Master's degree with 3+ years of relevant work experience; or 9 years of related experience

• Demonstrated experience in leadership and/or project management

• Experience in engineering, manufacturing, testing, and/or design

• Ability to obtain and maintain a Top Secret or SCI security clearance per business requirements (US citizenship is required)

• Experience with SAP or other Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system and MS Office applications including: Excel, Word, Project, and Power Point

Preferred Qualifications:

• Proven experience in leadership and project management

• Master’s degree in Engineering or Business Management

• Experience with cost, schedule, project leadership, and/or process improvement

• Experience with bases of estimates, shop order creation, and Earned Value Management Systems (EVMS)

• Active Secret clearance
