ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has multiple openings for Principal Structural Design or Senior Principal Structural Design Engineers to join our team of qualified diverse individuals. In Vehicle Engineering, we develop manned and unmanned aircraft platforms for the programs in the Aeronautics Systems Sector.

These positions will be located in Cincinnati, CA and have the possibility to work multiple shifts:

  • 9/80 work scheduled (week 1, Monday - Friday, week 2, Monday - Thursday Friday off)
  • Monday - Thursday on a 4 day - 10 hours/day schedule. Alternate hours or shifts may be discussed
  • Friday, Saturday, Sunday on a 3 day - 10 hours/day schedule (you will be paid for a 40-hour work week). Alternate hours or shifts may be discussed

We are looking for Engineers to develop “design for producibility” solutions to difficult problems that are thorough, imaginative, practical, and consistent with program objectives. In the Structural Design group, we develop solutions to difficult problems that are thorough, imaginative, practical, and consistent with program objectives. In your day-to-day activities you will participate in the development and review of completed models, drawings, and repairs for accuracy, clarity, and completeness, in order to provide evaluation of engineering products for conformity to standards, procedures, and specifications. You will also supervise design solutions from initial concept to final implementation and give concise presentations and explanations of structural issues/solutions to internal management and customer representatives.

This position may be filled at either the Principal Structural Design Engineer or the Senior Principal Structural Design Engineer level.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Structural Design Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field with 5 years of related engineering experience OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field with 3 years of related engineering experience OR a PhD in a STEM field with 0 years of related experience.
  • Experience in the design of bonded composite primary and secondary aircraft structures.
  • High level of proficiency in using Unigraphics Nx or CATIA V5 in the development of aircraft structures and components.
  • Must have the ability to obtain a DoD security clearance. This clearance must be obtained prior to starting employment and within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to the commencement of employment. This SAP must be obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Basic Qualifications for Senior Principal Structural Design Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field with 9 years of related engineering experience OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field with 7 years of related engineering experience OR a PhD in a STEM field with 4 years of related experience.
  • Experience in the design of bonded composite primary and secondary aircraft structures.
  • High level of proficiency in using Unigraphics Nx or CATIA V5 in the development of aircraft structures and components.
  • Must have the ability to obtain a DoD security clearance. This clearance must be obtained prior to starting employment and within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to the commencement of employment. This SAP must be obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Preferred Qualifications for both levels:

  • Advanced degree in Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or Aerospace Engineering
  • Experience working on Special Access Programs
  • Experience working on military aircraft
