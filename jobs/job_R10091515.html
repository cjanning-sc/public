Staff Structural Engineer
R10091515
1st Shift (United States of America)

Perform linear and non-linear structural analyses of individual components and complex underwater assemblies using a combination of hand-calculations, Finite Element Analysis (FEA), and bolted joint calculations. Ability to lead a small team of structural analysts to support program’s analysis needs. Ensure that designs meet program requirements and are consistent with program schedule and budget objectives.

The candidate must be able to collaborate in a team environment, prioritize and adjust tasks to accomplish the project result, prioritize multiple responsibilities and/or accomplishing them simultaneously, and comprehend and analyze complex problems and develop solutions. Excellent verbal and written communication skills are required.

Basic Qualifications for a Staff Engineer Structural:

  • BS degree in Engineering (Aerospace, Mechanical or equivalent STEM) and a minimum of 14 years of experience in structural analysis or MS degree with 12 years of experience

  • Proficiency in FEMAP/Nastran and/or ANSYS FEA tools for performing linear and non-linear analyses

  • Experience with bolted joint analysis and structural hand calculations

  • Proficiency in using common engineering design, production, analysis, and office tools.

  • US citizen and ability to obtain clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Able to work in a fast-paced environment where multiple projects are being developed and deployed simultaneously

  • Demonstrated skills/experience in the following multi-disciplined areas: shock, vibration, and fracture analysis.

  • Active TS/SCI clearance
