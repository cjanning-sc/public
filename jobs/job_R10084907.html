Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support Sentinel (Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD)) program.

What you’ll get to do:

  • Design, develop, and analyze models to simulate and evaluate effectiveness of missile system payloads
  • Design, develop, and analyze models to support system design decisions and requirements derivation
  • Design and develop models to simulate and evaluate hardware performance
  • Perform model validation through test and analysis
  • Support test activities to collected model validation data, including supporting test planning, test execution, and data analysis, including Ground and Flight Test data
  • Verify specifications and designs through modeling and simulation, test data processing, and performance analysis
  • Create technical reports and present model, test, and analysis results to both internal and external stakeholders
  • Interface with AF customer.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering or a related technical discipline
  • A minimum 2 years of related systems engineering experience with a Bachelor’s degree; 0 years with a Master’s degree
  • Active DoD Secret clearance granted/investigated in the last six years or in Continues Evaluation
  • U.S. Citizenship with ability to obtain Special Access Program Clearance
  • Experience designing, developing and performing Modeling and Simulation, including programming language experience (MATLAB, Python, C++, or similar)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Special Access Program Clearance
  • Experience analyzing test data, either for requirements verification, model validation, or root cause analysis.
  • Experience with ICBM Weapon Systems or other missile systems.
