ACME Engineering Space Systems (NGSP) is looking for you to join our team as a Space Principal Systems Engineer/Sr Principal Systems Engineer based out of either, Cedar Rapids IA or Chicago, IL.

What You’ll get to Do:

Performs technical planning, system integration, verification and validation, cost and risk, and supportability and effectiveness analyses for total systems. Analyses are performed at all levels of total system product to include: concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal. Ensures the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints. Performs functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications.

Basic Qualifications for Sr Principal

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or other STEM related degree with 9 + years of experience; 7 years with a Master's; 4 years with a PhD; or applicable experience in lieu of degree.

  • Secret clearance with the ability to obtain a TS/SCI

  • Experience with JIRA

  • Experience in gathering requirements, leading exercises or demonstrations.

  • Familiarity with Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE)   

  • Maintain existing operations procedures up-to-date to also include lessons learned from real-time performance. 

  • Develop new procedures as required. 

  • Interface with program and customer personnel. 

  • Hands on experience developing and performing real-time executable mission plans. 

  • Familiarity with Mission Operations Centers architectures and interfaces.

  • Strong written and oral communications skills

  • Travel to support deployments and site surveys

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active TS/SCI

  • Experience in working with multiple subcontractors

  • Execution lead of exercises and demonstrations

  • Proficient using STK software (Systems Tool Kit, formerly called Satellite Tool Kit).

  • Detailed familiarity with space system operational documentation.

  • Mentor newer members of the operations team.
