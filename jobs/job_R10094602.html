Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Staff Logistics Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) Sentinel program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What you’ll get to do: 

The candidate will perform logistics related analysis and maintenance planning of the GBSD platform. The candidate will influence system design to enhance supportability and utilize engineering data from various sources to determine logistics requirements.

Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Act as an advisor to management and customers, mentor and train junior engineers.
  • Frequent inter-organization and outside customer communication is required and the candidate represents the organization in providing solutions to difficult technical issues associated with complex projects
  • Includes identifying risks and opportunities and coordinates with project teams to mitigate and retire risks
  • Develop SE proposals to include reviewing & updating Supplier Statements of Work (SSOW), performing technical evaluations on all supplier proposal submittals, and attending proposal related reviews
  • Evaluates issues and develops resolutions to meet productivity, quality, and customer satisfaction goals and objectives
  • Must possess the ability to handle multiple tasks in a dynamic environment, including critical thinking and technical problem-solving skills

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80A. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off.

You’ll bring these qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 14 years of relatable work experience.
  • 5 Years of experience authoring/reviewing Logistics SDRLs and CDRLs
  • 8 years of Logistics Planning and Support experience.
  • Experience with Systems Engineering requirements analysis, trade studies, Support Equipment Requirements Documentation (SERD) generation, design solutions, acquisition, test, and validation
  • U.S. citizen
  • Active DoD Secret clearance
  • Must possess excellent oral and written communication skills
  • Must be knowledgeable in Microsoft Office Suite - Project, PowerPoint, Excel, Word

These qualifications would be nice to have:

  • Proficient with 3D design / 2D drawing CAD in NX
  • Proficient with Teamcenter
  • Hands on experience in equipment maintenance, fabrication, assembly, power, hand and measuring tools
  • Military service experience with Support Equipment and ICBM maintenance
