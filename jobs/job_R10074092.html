Northrop Grumman IT Solutions team is seeking a Systems Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is located in Melbourne, FL in support of the E2 program.

This requisition may be filled at either a level 3 or a level 4, depending on the candidate’s education and experience.

Responsibilities include (but are not limited to):

  • Develop / deliver of the overall Information Technology (IT) technical baseline and system design, including delivery to the customer. This includes, but is not limited to, planning, design, development, integration, testing, production release to the business, operations / sustainment.
  • Interface with project managers, architects, technical specialists, customers regularly.
  • Communicate both written and verbal with a technical team and a customer team throughout the project lifecycle.
  • Ability to travel as business needs dictate.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Possess an active DOD Secret or higher security clearance or Active DOD within the last 24 months and must be able to obtain a Top Secret clearance. Further, the candidate’s clearance must be able to meet the government customer’s criteria for working on their program.
  • Experience in the System Engineering role providing written and oral presentations to customers, vendors, and technical team.
  • Demonstrate strong foundational expertise in Information Technologies with subject matter expertise in at least two of the following areas:
    • Virtualization
    • Data Center designs and solutions, including on-premises and co-location solutions.
    • Data Center Backup overall design
    • Software as a service;
    • Cloud technologies including external public / private cloud solutions / on-premises solutions
    • Containerization

Basic Qualifications for T03:

To be considered for this position, the candidate must possess 5 years of IT Project Management, System Engineering or related IT experience with a BS degree in a STEM or Business focused discipline; also acceptable is 3 years or experience with a related Masters; or 0 years with PHD. In lieu of the education requirement, 9 years of relevant IT experience with at least 4 years in System Engineering.

Basic Qualifications for T04:

To be considered for this position, the candidate must possess 9 years of IT Project Management, System Engineering or related IT experience with a BS degree in a STEM or Business focused discipline; also acceptable is 7 years or experience with a related Masters; or 4 years with PHD. In lieu of the education requirement, 13 years of relevant IT experience with at least 4 years in System Engineering.

Preferred Qualifications:

Candidates with these desired skills will be given preferential consideration:

  • Technical experience with Northrop Grumman SAP, Manufacturing Execution Systems, Engineering Solutions, Product Line Engineering, TeamCenter Enterprise, Mobile Applications, Extranet Solutions, or Collaboration Solutions.
  • Excellent communication skills, including good customer relationship management.
  • Ability to work well in a team environment, including strong leadership / team building skills.
  • Ability to effectively manage multiple assignments and deadlines simultaneously.
