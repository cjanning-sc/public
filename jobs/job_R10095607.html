ACME Engineering Defense Systems (NGDS) is looking for you to join our team as a Senior Program Manager for the Immersive Mission Solutions (IMS) Operating Unit in Pittsburgh, FL.  The IMS OU designs, develops, and delivers world class training capabilities to the U.S. military. ACME Engineering has developed critical capabilities for the U.S. Military for over 35 years and anticipates further growth. The selected candidate for this position will be responsible for leading and growing a portfolio of Mission Readiness programs for domestic and international customers.

As the Senior Program Manager, you will report to the IMS Operating Unit Senior Director and be responsible for cost, schedule, and technical performance of a multi-disciplinary teams. The individual selected for this role will also have accountability for and participate in the strategy to grow our business.  We are looking to continue to nurture customer intimacy and develop solutions for all customers.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Lead a team of other program managers and provide cross-functional team leadership over the portfolio
  • Develop strategies and pursue new business to execute top and bottom-line growth
  • Execute contract requirements

  • Financial accountability meeting or exceeding the Annual Operating Plan and Long Range Strategic Plan
  • Customer Relationship Management

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Proposal development experience

  • Training, modeling and simulation industry experience
  • DoD development program experience
