Northrop Grumman's Enterprise Services Sector is seeking a Director of Enterprise Wide Apps 1.


This position will offer some regular telework, but is NOT fully virtual. Candidates must be local to one of the following Northrop Grumman office sites:

  • Irving, Tx
  • El Segundo, CA
  • Falls Church, VA
  • Baltimore, MD


Main responsibilities include:

  • Managing a team of SAP business analysts with the specialties in Manufacturing and Logistics, which include but not limited to : MRP, Inventory Management, Warehouse Management, Shop Floor Control, MRO, Manufacturing Quality and Logistics Sustainment
  • This job will require you to manage a team of 30-40 people.


Other duties include, but are not limited to:

  • Day to Day People and team management (including monitoring backlog).
  • Making sure that 1 on 1 is being executed.
  • Goals are being set for people, midyear is being done and PMP is being done at year end.
  • Monitoring various budgets, which include team budget, recurring budget, travel budget, training, etc.
  • Ensuring an up to date manpower plan, at least 1/quarter.
  • Deconflict resources when necessary and making sure that the people assigned to the different projects are staying on top of their assignments
  • Nurturing the customer relationship and ensuring a smooth working relationship with our partners (NextGen, Controllers, and L2/BP)
  • Developing and maintaining business systems strategic roadmap by working with the multiple stakeholders


Basic Qualifications:

  • Minimum Bachelor’s degree with at least 20 years relevant experience or Master's with 15 years of relevant experience
  • Experience with at least 2-3 Full Cycle SAP Implementations
  • Experience with SAP either from technical or functional areas
  • Experience of managing a medium size team successfully (at least 20-30 people)
  • Experience managing a team budget of at least 2-5 million dollars
  • Experience as a People Leader/Engagement Leader
  • History in working with customers in creating strategic roadmap
  • Ability to work with the suppliers
  • Experience as a program manager, managing multiple projects


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master Degree
  • Experience with SAP S/4.
  • Experience with S/4 PEO module in S/4
  • Experience with iMRO solutions
  • Familiarity with Industry 4.0 concept – digital thread, additive manufacturing, IIOT
  • PMP certified
