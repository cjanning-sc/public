1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Composite Manufacturing Software Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Clearfield, UT.  You will be part of an Integrated Product Team supporting production by creating manufacturing software using 3D model based definition.

Responsibilities:

  • Creating, maintaining, supporting and continuing development of software that supports:
    • Automated manufacturing machines and systems
    • Manual manufacturing processes
  • Understand customer product/technical requirements and ensure successful project development and execution
  • Maintains technical standards for project/programs
  • Support lean manufacturing and process flow optimization activities
  • Supporting production in using manufacturing equipment and assist maintenance personnel as needed when troubleshooting and/or repairing
  • Assist R&D programs to improve system capabilities and bring new ideas and technology into projects and pursuits
  • Stays informed of technical advances through education, training, etc.

Our team enjoys a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, and collaboration across all levels.  We work independently but interact constantly with other engineering groups and manufacturing to achieve expectations. We’re empowered to make decisions, but can draw upon corporate resources to support us when needed. Each member of the team is tasked with identifying ways to improve efficiency in the team in the pursuit of continuous improvement. We’re looking for a fellow self-starter who enjoys assessing and understanding risk to join us.

This role is day shift with occasional off hour support. Travel is estimated to be less than 5%. This team adheres to our customers’ work schedule.  Flexibility is provided to accommodate personal work schedules.  Northrop Grumman provides a comprehensive suite of benefits, and we have won awards for our work-life balance!

This role may be filled at an Associate Engineer or Engineer level based on the qualifications outlined below. 

Basic Qualifications:

Associate Software Engineer 

  • Bachelors Degree.  Note: An additional 4 years of relevant work experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
  • Experience in 3D modeling
    • Solid model and complex geometry
  • Troubleshooting manufacturing processes
  • Windows and MS office skills required
  • Ability to obtain a DoD Secret clearance and special program access
     

Software Engineer 

  • Bachelors Degree and 2 years of engineering work experience; or a Masters degree and 0 years of engineering work experience.  Note: An additional 4 years of relevant work experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
  • Experience in 3D modeling
    • Solid model and complex geometry
  • Troubleshooting manufacturing processes
  • Windows and MS office skills required
  • Ability to obtain a DoD Secret clearance and special program access

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Working knowledge of NX and/or CATIA
  • Working knowledge of Siemens FiberSIM
  • Laser Projection Experience (Virtek LaserEdge preferred)
  • Pattern Cutting Nesting Experience (TruNest preferred)
