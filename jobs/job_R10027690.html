ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Principal/Sr. Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Detroit, UT and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program on the AVE (Missile) Architecture and Interfaces team. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package. This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

What you’ll get to do: 

  • Work with both the Program and lower-level Element Teams to develop and mature the AVE architecture content.
  • Help develop and incorporate the appropriate requirements for the system and make sure that that they are properly represented in the model.
  • Work with teammates across a wide variety of disciplines and get intimately familiar with all aspects of the Missile.
  • Develop missile flight systems architecture using Cameo Enterprise Architecture (behavioral, structural, analytical)
  • Collaborate with program IPT counterparts to develop supporting systems engineering and design artifacts
  • Contribute to system requirements development and analysis
  • Present at milestone internal and customer CDR reviews
  • Represent AVE at the program-level model governance working group
  • Communicate and train other systems engineering teams on modeling procedures
  • Audit AVE Architecture model for compliance with model procedures
  • Take content through the configuration management
  • Develop unifying model techniques, procedures, and processes (for model development, tool integration, and team integration)

You’ll bring these qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either a Principal level or level Sr. Principal level

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering or a related technical discipline
  • A minimum 5 years of related engineering experience with a Bachelor’s degree; 3 years with a Master’s; or 0 years with PhD. (Sr. Principal level requires 9 years with a Bachelor’s degree; 7 years with a Master’s degree; or 4 years with a PhD)
  • Comfortable spending substantial portion of time using software tools
  • U.S. Citizenship and an Active Secret Security Clearance or higher awarded or reviewed in the last 6 years

These qualifications would be nice to have:

  • Experience modeling with Cameo Enterprise Architecture or MagicDraw (No Magic, Inc.) or other architecture modeling tools
  • Systems engineering experience
  • Experience with DoDAF and UPDM or UAF Profiles
  • Experience using DOORS (IBM Rational)
  • Experience with complex system development on large programs
  • Experience with flight hardware
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills, both verbal and written
