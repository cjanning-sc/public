SSSD Business Management is seeking a Senior Principal or Staff Schedule Risk   Assessment Lead. This position will be located at our Space Systems Sector in Redondo Beach, CA and will report directly to the ARIS Business Unit Schedule Manager.

Working in a classified environment, this Lead will be the SME (Subject Matter Expert) for Schedule Risk Analysis (SRA) across multiple programs. This role will report directly to Program Management Office and will have direct customer and program leadership interactions/visibility.  The SME will train Schedule Analysts on SRA and inform program management of schedule risk implications. The candidate will provide technical leadership for the Integrated Master Schedule (IMS) and risk management, working closely with the Program Integration Team as the representative for Schedule Management risk.

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Conduct and analyze SRA’s for multiple programs, monthly
  • Generate/Present Program Management Office Briefings and Customer Briefings on SRA
  • Conduct Schedule (IMS) what-if scenarios
  • Standardize SRA process across Business Unit
  • Schedule Health SME
  • Conduct/Lead SRA Training sessions
  • Document SRA best practices
  • Manage RMOF risk and schedule integration

This position requires the incumbent to possess a Department of Defense (DoD) active Top Secret (TS) security clearance or access to Special Compartmentalized Information (SCI). This position can be filled at the Senior Principal (Level 4) or Staff Level (Level 5).

Basic Qualifications (Level 4):

  • BA/BS degree with a minimum of 9 years work experience to include any combination of experience in Business Management, Systems Development or Administration OR 7 years of stated experience with a Master’s Degree OR a PhD and 4 years of stated experience
  • An active DoD TS and/or SCI Security Clearance

Basic Qualifications (Level 5):

  • BA/BS degree with a minimum of 14 years work experience to include any combination of experience in Business Management, Systems Development or Administration OR 12 years of stated experience with a Master’s Degree OR 9 years of stated experience with a PhD
  • An active DoD TS and/or SCI Security Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Ability to interact with multiple layers of leadership and cross functional teams
  • Super User SRA software OPRA and Deltek Acumen
  • Experience with Risk Management frameworks
  • Experience with data collection and analysis
