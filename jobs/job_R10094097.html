Job Description

The Payload and Ground Systems organization within the ACME Engineering Space Systems pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter. Come be a part of our mission!

We offer phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly collaborative workplace. We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal-oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do. Does this sound like you?

Our Employee Resource Groups (ERGs) provide benefits for the member, our leaders and the company. Our ERGs offer opportunities to be a friend, be active, be a volunteer, be a leader, to be recognized and to be yourself! Every ERG is inclusive of all employees! 

Our diverse portfolio of programs means there are endless paths to cultivate your career. We are well-known for our inclusive environment, as well as our excellent work/life balance. We also offer exceptional benefits/healthcare, a 9/80 schedule, and a great 401K matching program. 
 

The Consolidated Operations and Anomaly Support Team (COAST) is a restricted Operations and Maintenance (O&M) program with several Integrated Project Teams (IPTs). The Software Engineering IPT focuses on the sustainment of several mission operational product lines, as well as the development of new capabilities as coordinated with the Customer.

 

The Software Engineering IPT is a large team comprising multiple subsystems, each with their specialized software focus. We are looking for an energetic and passionate Software Engineering Manager to join our team as we work to provide innovative solutions to complex issues in support of our Customer’s national security mission.

 

The selected candidate will lead a team with diverse skillsets including software development, system administration, and cybersecurity. They will provide the technical and people leadership skills needed to establish and maintain a world class Software Engineering organization and have overall responsibility for the development, design, implementation, application, training, and product support of state-of-the-art solutions.

 

Core responsibilities include:

  • Supporting functional management activities such as staffing and workload forecast; employee engagement, mentoring and career development; and identification and training of skills required to execute on the program
  • Supporting Business Strategy Efforts (including proposals, gate reviews, and independent review teams as necessary)
  • Providing the processes and tools required to meet the current and future needs of program

Basic Qualifications:

  • Active Top Secret clearance with the ability to obtain SCI access prior to start date
  • 9 years of relevant experience with a bachelor’s degree; 7 years of relevant experience with a Masters' degree; 4 years of relevant experience with a PhD. An additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of degree
  • Previous ground systems experience with technical background in ground system requirements development, design, software development, software integration & test
  • Demonstrated ability to develop effective processes, implement process improvements and streamline activities

 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree in a relevant technical or management specialty
  • Prior leadership role as a technical lead or project manager
  • Experience with Agile software development practices, including leadership roles such as Scrum Master, Product Owner, etc.
  • Knowledge of latest software development trends and technologies, operating systems, networks, and cyber security
  • Experience managing an IT support (or similar) team
  • Experience managing multiple projects concurrently
  • Active TS/SCI security clearance
