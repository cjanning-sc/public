Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Mission Assurance Engineer (Level 2 or 3) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Iuka, MS.

As quality engineering, we define and monitor mission assurance program specifications and processes to ensure mission success of programs. We assure quality, risk management, safety, reliability and maintainability of program achievements, subcontractors, and suppliers are in accordance with contractual requirements. We assess program performance and risks, and help to determine resources to ensure contract compliance.

Do you enjoy solving problems? Come join our team!

Responsibilities include the following:

  • Authoring documentation to instruct operations on successful execution of quality requirements
  • Manage and coordinate non-conformance containment and corrective action implementation activities
  • Lead data collection, analysis, and customer interface
  • Write and manage risk mitigation actions and assuring their implementation
  • Contribute to design change control reviews and implementation
  • Support internal audits, process observations and area assessments
  • Establish and maintain collaboration partnership across functions and programs, customers, program management, and other support organizations

Our team is exceptionally detail-oriented. We work on sophisticated projects in the aerospace industry, producing high-quality work. Each member has individual assignments, but we work as a united team to identify and resolve potential issues before timelines are impacted. Our team pursues inclusivity, diversity, and balance in both workload and work-life. We feel strongly about treating each other with respect and working with integrity.

Work schedules can vary, but we strive for a 9/80 work week, which gives us every other Friday off to pursue interests outside of the office. Northrop Grumman provides a full suite of benefits, and we are regularly mentioned as a top company for work life balance.

This role may be filled at an Engineer or Principal Engineer Mission Assurance depending on the qualifications below.

Basic Qualifications:

Engineer:

  • Bachelors in Science and 2 years of experience in Quality and/or Mission Assurance work experience; Master's Degree. Note: an additional 4 years of relevant work experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
  • Knowledge of Inspection Plans and First Article Inspections
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance.

Principal Engineer:

  • Bachelors in Science and 5 years of experience in Quality and/or Mission Assurance work experience; Master's Degree and 3 years of experience in Quality and/or Mission Assurance work experience; PhD. Note: an additional 4 years of relevant work experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
  • Knowledge of Inspection Plans and First Article Inspections
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Prior manufacturing experience
  • Ability to read and interpret GD&T drawings
  • Knowledge of Root Cause and Corrective Action methods
  • Knowledge of continuous improvement principles
  • Background in Composites
  • Active DoD Secret Clearance
