Roles and Responsibilities:

Establishes and maintains compliance with Quality Management System (QMS) policies, procedures, and processes to ensure the performance and quality of products conform to established standards, specifications, and customer requirements. Ensures compliance through establishment and execution of internal surveillances and audits. Supports and facilitates external audits for continued certification of AS9100. Collaborates with Sector, Site Management, Manufacturing, Program Quality Engineering, Design Engineering, Supplier Performance Management/Supplier Quality, and other support Teams to ensure quality is inherent and compliant. Serves as site representative to sector QMS organizations and to internal and external audit entities. Provides performance reviews and periodic feedback for all direct reports. Develops and operates within departmental budgets. Ensures calibration of equipment. Provides overall management of the Compliance and Calibration team by setting and operating to objectives, and providing guidance to direct reports based on goals, objectives, and company policy.
