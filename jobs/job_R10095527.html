Defines and monitors mission assurance program specifications and processes to ensure mission success of programs. Performs or assures quality, risk management, safety, reliability and maintainability of program achievements, subcontractors, and suppliers in accordance with contractual requirements. Assesses program performance and risks, and determines resources to ensure mission assurance.

This manager will lead a high performing Quality team covering Internal Audits, Compliance, and Calibration. This key leader will be supporting Mission Systems San Diego Sites (Rancho Carmel and Spectrum) and the Quality and Mission Excellence (QME) team.  Through Continuous Improvement and collaboration, the leader will influence optimization of efficient process and procedural compliance. The leader ensures that corrective measures address acceptable solutions. The leader must be committed to employee development, fostering a collaborative culture, and must be committed to achieving high levels of customer satisfaction.

As a leader, you always act with integrity, and the highest ethical standards characterize everything you do. You enable your team to achieve predictable and balanced results, while satisfying the needs of internal and external partners. You treat all people with respect, and model personal growth and continuous development. You know how to build an inclusive environment that attracts, retains, and encourages a diverse and engaged team. You build trusted and valued customer relationships and communicate effectively, so that people throughout the organization feel engaged and connected to their work. You ensure that your team has the information and tools needed to perform at their highest. You demonstrate our committed pursuit of applying innovation to meet the requirements of our customer. You build and execute strategies that result in sustainable value creation that delivers measurable results.

Roles and Responsibilities:

Establishes and maintains compliance with Quality Management System (QMS) policies, procedures, and processes to ensure the performance and quality of products conform to established standards, specifications, and customer requirements. Ensures compliance through establishment and execution of internal surveillances and audits. Supports and facilitates external audits for continued certification of AS9100. Collaborates with Sector, Site Management, Manufacturing, Program Quality Engineering, Design Engineering, Supplier Performance Management/Supplier Quality, and other support Teams to ensure quality is inherent and compliant. Serves as site representative to sector QMS organizations and to internal and external audit entities. Provides performance reviews and periodic feedback for all direct reports. Develops and operates within departmental budgets. Ensures calibration of equipment. Provides overall management of the Compliance and Calibration team by setting and operating to objectives, and providing guidance to direct reports based on goals, objectives, and company policy.

Basic Qualifications:

•    Bachelor’s degree and 9+ years of experience in Quality, Quality Engineering, Program Management, Engineering Management or Manufacturing (7+ years of experience with a Master’s degree), or 13+ years of experience in lieu of degree.
•    Demonstrated understanding of quality engineering, inspection methods, production control, manufacturing operations, product test & evaluation, reliability, root cause analysis, defect analysis, control of nonconforming material, product sell off, shipping and product support as they apply to quality.
•    U.S. citizenship with the ability to obtain and maintain a Department of Defense Secret clearance
•    Familiarity with ISO 9001 / AS9100 Quality Management System certification process.
 

Preferred Qualifications:

•    Experience with ISO 9001 or AS9100 based quality management systems; Lead Auditor certification

•    Experience conducting or managing internal audits

•    Six Sigma Black Belt or equivalent

•    Extensive prior work experience in the application of quality principles and practices (e.g., quality engineering, inspection, material review, etc.) related to systems engineering, integration, and manufacturing.

•    Working knowledge of calibration of equipment

•    Experience with supplier quality and risk reduction

•    Active/Current DoD Secret clearance
