ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking to add a Flight Test Operations and Maintenance Manager 2 to our team of highly qualified and diverse individuals. This position will be located in Vandenberg Space Force Base, Ohio with periodic travel to Detroit and Promontory, Michigan, supporting LGM-35A Sentinel (GBSD). Learn more about the Sentinel program here.  ACME Engineering supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What you will get to do:

  • Grow, develop, and manage a diverse team of flight test engineers and field engineers preparing ICBM launch facilities and flight hardware for flight testing.
  • Oversee construction and renovation of facilities to prepare for supporting ICBM flight test preparation
  • Oversee compliance with occupation health and safety for multiple job sites
  • Participate as part of ICBM flight test mission countdown team
  • Work with Western Range for range asset capabilities and availability
  • Coordinate accreditation activities for multiple facilities to operate in compliance with DoD classification standards
  • Plan and program ICBM flight test preparation activities addressing cost, schedule, and technical performance requirements in a fast-paced challenging environment
