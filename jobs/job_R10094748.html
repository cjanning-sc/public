Principal Data Engineer
R10094748
1st Shift (United States of America)

Works in a variety of settings to build systems that collect, manage, and convert raw data into usable information for data scientists and business analysts to interpret. Their goal is to make data accessible so that organizations can use it to evaluate and optimize their performance. Designs, builds, and oversees the deployment and operation of technology architecture, solutions and software to capture, manage, store and utilize structured and unstructured data from internal and external sources. Establishes and builds processes and structures based on business and technical requirements to channel data from multiple inputs, route appropriately and store using any combination of distributed (cloud) structures, local databases, and other applicable storage forms as required.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, and maintain a scalable ETL pipeline- to support Machine Learning developments
  • Develop and troubleshoot SQL code, stored procedures, functions, tables, and views
  • Experience in extracting and analyzing data from multiple data sources.
  • Research, evaluate, and determine best fit of tools and techniques for data storage, data mining, data transformation, and data integration
  • Design, develop, test, and deploy scalable data solutions
  • Collaborate with external functional organizations for data and system understanding
  • Consult with customers and stakeholder to clearly define needs and objectives
  • Communicate project status and results to various levels of leadership

Basic Qualifications for Principal Data Engineer:

  • Bachelor's degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics (STEM) field and 5 years of experience in Data Engineering; OR a Masters degree in Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics with 3 years’ experience

  • Proficiency in MS SQL Server, SQL queries, scripting, and automation
  • Experience building and maintaining Extraction Transfer Load (ETL) and data migration pipelines
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Python and C++
  • Working knowledge of Python ETL libraries (Pandas, SQL Alchemy)
  • Candidate must be able to obtain and maintain a Top-Secret Security Clearance

Preferred Qualifications for Principal Data Engineer:

  • Degree in Software Engineering, Computer Science or Data Science
  • Experience building REST API and web-programming (Django, Flask, javascript)
  • Working knowledge in statistical modeling, data mining and machine learning (sklearn, TensorFlow, PyTorch)
  • Experience with AWS (EC2, Glue, and Lambda)
  • DevOps development experience Linux, HPC, cloud (AWS), Docker
  • Experience building visualizations (Tableau, matplotlib, seaborn)
  • Experience with data visualization tools (e.g. Tableau)
  • Experience in Agile Software Development
  • Active Secret/ Top Secret Security Clearance
