Senior / Principal Electrical Engineer: Avionics Advanced Analyses – Space / Satellite Systems (Nationwide)
R10063904
1st Shift (United States of America)

We have an opening for an Avionics Advanced Analyses Electrical Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in the Tactical Space organization. The primary need for this position is in Dulles, VA and Gilbert, AZ; however, we will consider any NG location nationwide.


In this role, you will get the opportunity to conduct analyses and provide design inputs on components ranging from circuit cards to whole spacecraft buses. Your inputs will be valued throughout the design process to confirm that the systems operate as designed and meet requirements.

Job Responsibilities

  • Work on a design team and be responsible for advanced analyses at several levels.
  • Use Saber for most of the advanced analyses along with LT Spice and MATHCAD.
  • Support the test and debug for flight and non-flight spacecraft electronic circuit card assemblies (CCA) and boxes
  • Ensure that technical requirements, cost, and schedule for the CTE/ATE system development is achieved
  • Work closely with the spacecraft avionics Design Engineer to ensure the CTE/ATE system will validate the circuit cards and/or electronics box under all test environments
  • Perform subsystem definition, preliminary and detailed design, design implementation, subsystem and system integration, and test for a subsystem
  • Frequent inter-organizational and outside customer contacts
  • Analyze software requirements to determine feasibility of design within time and cost constraints
  • Make frequent contact with customers both internal and external concerning requirements, design and maintenance of software

This position can be filled at the Principal or Sr. Principal level.

Basic Qualifications:

Principal Engineer

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field (ideally in Electrical Engineering) with 5 or more years of experience; or a Master’s degree with 3 years of experience; or a PhD
  • U.S. Citizenship
  • Hands-on experience utilizing electrical design, modeling, simulation and/or prototyping tool Saber
  • Hands-on experience utilizing mathematical calculation and analyzation tools (i.e. Mathcad, Matlab, Solidworks, etc)
  • Experience designing, mapping and/or testing subsystems and related components

Sr. Principal Engineer

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field (ideally in Electrical Engineering) with 9 or more years of experience; or a Master’s degree with 7 years of experience; or a PhD with 4 years of experience
  • U.S. Citizenship
  • Hands-on experience utilizing electrical design, modeling, simulation and/or prototyping tool Saber
  • Hands-on experience utilizing mathematical calculation and analyzation tools (i.e. Mathcad, Matlab, Solidworks, etc)
  • Experience designing, mapping and/or testing subsystems and related components

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Hands-on experience utilizing electrical design, modeling, simulation and/or prototyping tools (i.e. Cadence, Siemans, LT Spice, etc)
  • Strong Stability Analysis skills
  • Experience using Saber
  • Experience using Mathcad
  • Strong custom hardware interface design skills
  • Strong ATE/CTE system design skills
  • Experience defining and mapping operational requirements to functional implementations
  • Experience developing test application SW to interface with Test Equipment along with instrument driver suites
  • Experience developing test applications for data protocols, high speed serial interfaces, and memory algorithms.
  • Experience in the test and debug of hardware and software systems
  • A high level understanding of general and specialized test equipment to include: Data Bus Analyzers, Signal Generators, Logic Analyzers, and Power equipment
