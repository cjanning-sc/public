Analyzes and designs sequence of operations and work flow to improve efficiencies in plant and production facilities and equipment layouts; and establishes methods for maximum utilization of production facilities and personnel. May establish or assist in establishing accident prevention measures and may manage training programs for personnel concerning all phases of production operations. Conducts studies pertaining to cost control, cost reduction, inventory control, and production record systems. On the basis of these studies, develops and implements plans and programs for facility modifications and revisions to operating methods. May assist facilities engineers in the planning and design of facilities.

Aeronautics Systems sector is seeking a Principal Industrial Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will support the B-2 and will be located in Palmdale, CA.

  • Analyzes the B-2 manufacturing process for opportunities to minimize waste, reduce cost, and optimize schedule.
  • Provides support to the manufacturing floor by working proactively with cross-functional teams to understand frontline problems and implement cost-saving, impactful solutions.
  • Works with program leadership to assess what-if scenarios and guide long-term strategic decision making
  • Generates metrics and conducts studies pertaining to efficiency, cycle times, work-in-progress, staffing, performance, and product delivery.
  • Partners with IPT leadership and manufacturing to develop and track recovery plans that instill confidence with the program and customers, even through unforeseen challenges.
  • Supporting the 2nd shift Air Vehicle Manager to include metric creation, data-driven recommendations, and be able to host meetings with multiple support groups.

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Industrial Engineer:

  • Bachelor of Science Degree in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Math) with 5 or more years of engineering or manufacturing experience; OR a Master’s Degree in STEM with 3 or more years of engineering or manufacturing experience.
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Clearance with program access.
  • Strong expertise using the full suite of Microsoft Office Tools
  • Critical Path identification

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience working with cross-functional teams in a manufacturing environment
  • At least 1 year of demonstrated experience leading project teams
  • Experience with shop floor control systems
  • Experience/certifications in lean/process improvement
  • Experience with Python
  • CAM Experience
