Northrop Grumman Enterprise Services is seeking a Principal / Sr Principal (Level 3 or Level 4) Systems Software Analyst in Clearfield, UT location. This position can be hybrid onsite and part-time work from home.

Job responsibilities will include, but not be limited to, the following:

  • Analyze, enhance, install, test, and modify enterprise-wide operating systems for both new and existing systems.
  • Develop requirements, workflow, and systems analysis.
  • Correct program errors, prepare operating instructions, compile documentation of program development, and analyze system capabilities.
  • Establish standards for the development and maintenance of new and existing products.

Basic Qualifications for Principal (Level 3) System Analyst:

  • Bachelors degree with 5 years of experience, OR Masters degree with 3 years of experience OR an Associate's Degree with 7 years of experience OR a High School diploma or equivalent with 9 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a completed degree.
  • Basic SQL knowledge is a plus
  • Working in a manufacturing environment is a plus
  • The selected candidate will be required to obtain a DoD Secret security clearance within a period of time as determined by customer and/or program requirements; maintaining the required security clearance will be a condition of continued employment. US Citizenship will be required.
  • The selected candidate may be required to obtain a Special Access Programs (SAP/SAR) clearance, depending on customer and/or program requirements; maintaining SAP/SAR will be a condition of continued employment.
  • Knowledge of software verification processes and techniques, test reporting, regression testing, and test data analysis.
  • The selected candidate will be required to analyze and validate the performance and validity of interfaces and supporting system software to specified system requirements.

Basic Qualifications for Sr Principal (Level 4) System Analyst:

  • Bachelors degree with 9 years of experience, OR Masters degree with 7 years of experience OR an Associate's Degree with 11 years of experience OR a High School diploma or equivalent with 13 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a completed degree.
  • Advanced SQL knowledge is a plus
  • Working in a manufacturing environment is a must
  • Knowledge of Manufacturing Execution Systems
  • The selected candidate will be required to obtain a DoD Secret security clearance within a period of time as determined by customer and/or program requirements; maintaining the required security clearance will be a condition of continued employment. US Citizenship will be required.
  • The selected candidate may be required to obtain a Special Access Programs (SAP/SAR) clearance, depending on customer and/or program requirements; maintaining SAP/SAR will be a condition of continued employment.
  • Knowledge of software verification processes and techniques, test reporting, regression testing, and test data analysis.
  • The selected candidate will be required to analyze and validate the performance and validity of interfaces and supporting system software to specified system requirements.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with MES (Manufacturing Execution Systems) preferably Solumina applications.
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Strong communication skills
  • Development of action plans based on data analysis.
  • Current DoD Secret security clearance.
