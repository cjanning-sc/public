Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has multiple openings for Mechanical or Principal Mechanical Engineers to join our team of qualified diverse individuals. In Vehicle Engineering, we develop manned and unmanned aircraft platforms for all of the programs in the Aeronautics Systems Sector

These positions will be located in Palmdale, CA and have the possibility to work multiple shifts:

  • Monday - Thursday on a 4 day - 10 hours/day schedule. Alternate hours or shifts may be discussed
  • Friday, Saturday, Sunday on a 3 day - 10 hours/day schedule (you will be paid for a 40-hour work week). Alternate hours or shifts may be discussed

We are looking for Engineers to become members of our multi-disciplined teams responsible for providing liaison engineering support to our multiple programs. In your day-to-day activities you will be working with subsystem installations and composite structures, performing timely dispositions for nonconforming parts, assemblies, and installations, as well as working as a part of a Material Review Board (MRB)

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelors of Science Degree in Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering or Aerospace Engineering with 2 – 9 years of directly related engineering experience (Masters degree may count towards an additional 2 years of experience)
  • Knowledge of the MRB process / experience with the MRB process
  • Experience with CATIA v5, NX, Visualization Pro, TeamCenter, MES, or other CAD programs
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced degree in Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering or Aerospace Engineering
  • Experience working in an aircraft manufacturing facility
  • Experience interfacing with a military customer
  • Experience working hands-on in a production assembly line environment
  • Experience with aircraft subsystem design
  • Previous MRB/LE Authority for Engineering
