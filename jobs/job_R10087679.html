The Successful candidate will be experienced in structural analysis methods including large NASTRAN models of metallic and advanced composite structures, as well as hand analysis.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Aerospace, Mechanical, or Civil Engineering with 2 to 9 years of experience to include a background in structural analysis (Master's degree may count towards an additional 2 years of experience)
  • Experience in Structural Analysis Methods and documentation, including hand analysis, and Finite Element Models.
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced degree in Aerospace Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or Civil Engineering
  • Aerospace structural analysis experience using NASTRAN, PATRAN, or FEMAP.
  • Experience with analysis of composite and / or metallic structure and repair methods
  • Analysis experience in a MRB role.
  • Experience working on military aircraft
  • Experience with durability and damage tolerance (DaDT) of aircraft structures
