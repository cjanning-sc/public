ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Manager, Manufacturing Engineering. This position will be located in Detroit, UT and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program Supplier Manufacturing Engineers (Supplier ME). Learn more about the GBSD program here. 

 

This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

 

What You’ll Get To Do: 

You will lead the ME Team as a member of the Sentinel Manufacturing leadership team, interfacing with other Manufacturing and Program team members, suppliers and customers. As the lead for this team of MEs, you will be responsible for the execution of Supplier integration by assisting the Supplier’s understanding of NG’s design, manufacturing and quality requirements. Supplier MEs work closely with Supplier Management Teams to ensure that the Supplier of hardware conforms to ACME requirements. Supplier ME provides help with the resolution of manufacturing issues when those requirements present a difficulty for the Supplier’s conformance. Internal and external processes exist to capture these issues and Supplier MEs assist with resolution of issues through the SRCI (Standard Request for Change/Information) process. The individual will have working knowledge of ME related processes for performing producibility analysis, creating manufacturing plans, creating the Manufacturing Bill of Material (MBOM), creating work instructions, assessing supplier capabilities in conjunction with IPT MEs utilizing MRA processes as defined by the DOD MRA handbook as well as having the ability to assimilate / collate the metrics for internal and customer reporting.

This position has the following duties and responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with the Sentinel IPT Manufacturing Leads, Engineering and Global Supply Chain personnel to assess SOW requirements for the execution of Sentinel program requirements related to Manufacturing for the various Supplier build and delivery SOWs

  • Develop and co-ordinate accurate and executable resource plans, assist with creation of Integrated Master Schedules, work plans and associated status tracking metrics

  • Provide guidance and direction for the team of Manufacturing Engineers who are executing the Supplier ME SOW requirements

  • Collaborate across the ME Team to ensure consistency in the use of Digital Engineering processes, tools and methods for accomplishing the development work leading to Production

  • Develop team members for growth in work scope capability and job satisfaction

  • Establish milestones and monitor adherence to master plans and schedules, identify program opportunities for improvement and obtain solutions as applicable

  • Conduct assessments of team member’s performance related to team and individual goals

  • The candidate will need to demonstrate exceptional organizational and interpersonal skills, and to have the ability to multi-task and communicate effectively with varying levels of Customer, ACME, Supplier employees and leadership
