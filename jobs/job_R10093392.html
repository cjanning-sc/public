Your future:

ACME Engineering Mission Systems is seeking a Software Asset Manager to join our highly skilled team of results driven and customer focused masterminds. We support a rapid environment of market relevant technology trends that demand critical thinkers and detail-oriented operators. This opportunity will entrust you to make real time decisions with real world significance.

Our team works diligently around the clock with passion for our mission but benefits most from the compassion we receive from top down. Health and wellness are an important part of our lives while we define a new protocol for customer success.

Your daily opportunities:

  • Manually create or modify IT software asset records and associated contract records in appropriate software repository.
  • Update IT software license asset records, software license contracts and software maintenance agreements and associated relationships
  • Process software related requests (new requests, changes, upgrades, renewals)
  • Partner with vendors to fully understand licensing agreements and offerings
  • Contributes to the team’s understanding, tracking, and forecasting of software licenses by collaborating with the teams and clearly explaining the software license agreements and management processes and procedures.

Typical Minimum Education / Experience for Software Asset Manager: Six (6) years of relevant experience with bachelor’s degree in Science, Technology, Engineering and/or Mathematics (STEM); Four (4) year with Masters. NOTE: Four (4) years of additional relevant experience can be substituted in lieu of degree.

To be considered you’ll need:

  • US Citizenship is required with an active DoD Top Secret/SCI security clearance which was active in the last 24 months

  • Must possess or be able to obtain DoD 8570 Certification for IAT Level II or higher within two (2) months of start date.

You will be a great fit if:

You have strong working knowledge in three or more of the following areas:

  • Understanding of various Tier 1 vendor license models: Microsoft, Atlassian, Splunk, Teradici, Dell, Cisco, RedHat, Gitlab, VMWare

  • Familiarity working with Value Added Resellers (VARs) for software acquisition and receipt of entitlements

  • Perform data analysis in support of license reclamation and reallocation efforts to optimize costs and reduce software overspend

  • Understanding of license types and license counter calculations (PVUS, CALS, cloud, subscription, perpetual, user, concurrent, etc.)

  • Generate Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), and Technique Tactics and Procedures (TTPs) relating to storage, updating, and reporting on the IT software licenses

  • Certified Software Asset Management (CSAM) or equivalent
