Gun Mission Module Technician (Field Technician II) based out of Mayport, FL.
R10073797
1st Shift (United States of America)

We are looking for you to join our team as a Gun Mission Module Technician (Field Technician II) based out of Mayport, FL.

What you’ll get to do:

Maintain and provide technical support for Mission Module (MM) equipment located at Naval Station Mayport, with some travel to Navy Base San Diego (NBSD), other Navy bases and deployed sites worldwide.  

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Analyze and evaluates equipment relative to specified performance parameters
  • Perform hands-on scheduled maintenance on MM equipment
  • Perform hands-on corrective maintenance, to include fault isolation
  • Install and removes MM equipment on US Navy ships
  • Provide logistics support; collaborates with customers/suppliers for technical and supply support 
  • Support the assigned SKED/Maintenance Planner with planned maintenance system (PMS) management and execution
  • Able to operate independently when supporting flyaway team maintenance and troubleshooting at various sites, both CONUS and OCONUS   

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School diploma or equivalent and a minimum 2 years additional education and/or related experience
  • Knowledge of tools and equipment required to conduct all repairs to include general hand tools, power tools, and torque wrenches
  • Must have US Citizenship and at minimum possess an Interim Secret Clearance
  • Must be able to pass basic DOT physical exam to obtain forklift license and pass proficiency tests
  • Must be able to perform physically demanding work, to include working in tight, restricted access spaces; must be able to climb 20 ft ladders and stand for long periods of time; must be able to lift 50 lbs
  • Must be willing to travel up to 25% both CONUS (continental U.S.) and OCONUS (outside the continental U.S.)

Preferred Qualifications:  

  • Experience operating and maintaining Gun Weapon Systems on US Navy/Coast Guard Vessels, and USMC/Army vehicles
  • Experience on LCS Class ships highly desired
  • Experience with Ship’s Maintenance and Material Management (to include using the 3M system), Preventive Maintenance and Corrective Maintenance (MFOM)
  • Experience with 30mm guns on the LPD-17 Class, USMC Advanced Amphibious Assault Vehicle (AAAV) or similar chain guns is highly desired
  • Knowledge of Surface Warfare (SUW) and Mine Countermeasure (MCM) operations and systems
  • Active Secret clearance
