Northrop Grumman Defense Systems Sector has a need for a Software Engineer / Principal Software Engineer at its Subsystem and Software Sustainment (S&SS) organization in Warner Robins, GA. This engineering position is for a role on our Application Software Engineering Team. In this role you will be supporting the APN-241 Radar Program. You will be an integral part of an interdisciplinary team of engineers focused on the modernization and sustainment of the APN-241 system. Our ideal candidate will possess technical expertise and collaborative people skills with the proven ability to work within a technically diverse and fast-paced team. This position is located in Warner Robins, GA

This position may be filled at Level 2 or Level 3 Embedded Software Engineer.

Your responsibilities will include:

  • Develop, modify, and maintain software using software engineering best practices and standards. 

  • Software development including automated testing to facilitate and analyze large data sets.

  • Participate in the full life cycle of software development, to include requirements development, modeling and design, software development, unit to CSCI testing, integration, formal system testing, release, installation, and maintenance.

  • Apply technical expertise in solving complex integration problems.  

  • Frequent opportunities to interact with program management, external suppliers and/or government customers. Therefore, excellent verbal, written, and interpersonal skills are essential to succeeding in this role
     

Basic Qualifications for Embedded Software Engineer:

  • Minimum of 2 years of direct relevant experience with B.S degree; No direct relevant experience necessary with a Masters degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or an equivalent STEM related discipline

  • ​Bachelor of Science (B.S) degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or an equivalent STEM related discipline

  • Proficient in C/C++

  • Proficient in all areas of formal software lifecycle process from requirements to testing

  • Must currently hold or be able to obtain and maintain a U.S Department of Defense (DoD) Secret level security clearance
     

Basic Qualifications for Principal Embedded Software Engineer:

  • ​Bachelor of Science (B.S) degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or an equivalent STEM related discipline

  • Minimum of 5 years of direct relevant experience with B.S degree; Minimum of 3 years of direct relevant experience with Masters degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or an equivalent STEM related discipline; Minimum of 1 year of direct relevant experience with PhD

  • Proficient in C/C++

  • Proficient in all areas of formal software lifecycle process from requirements to testing

  • Must currently hold or be able to obtain and maintain a U.S Department of Defense (DoD) Secret level security clearance
     

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Knowledge in RADAR systems and concepts

  • Experience in object-oriented analysis and design

  • Experience using JIRA/Bitbucket and Git/Mercurial/Bazaar version control tools

  • Experience with Java programming language

  • Experience using LabVIEW

  • Experience working in a fast pace, Agile environment with the ability to work on a cross-functional Scrum team focused on quality and capability delivery

  • Experience with Electronic Warfare (EW) principles

  • Active Secret Security Clearance
