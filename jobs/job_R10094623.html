Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for Senior Principal Mechanical Engineer to lead our team of qualified diverse individuals in the Subsystem Design group. In Vehicle Engineering, we develop manned and unmanned aircraft platforms for all programs in the Aeronautics Systems Sector. This position will be in Palmdale, CA and support the 9/80 1st Shift.

We are looking for Engineers to develop “design for producibility” solutions to difficult problems that are thorough, imaginative, practical, and consistent with program objectives. In the Subsystems Design group, we develop solutions to difficult problems that are thorough, imaginative, practical, and consistent with program objectives. In your day-to-day activities you will participate in the development and review of completed models, drawings, and repairs for accuracy, clarity, and completeness, in order to provide evaluation of engineering products for conformity to standards, procedures, and specifications. You will also lead a team and supervise design solutions from initial concept to final implementation and give concise presentations and explanations of subsystem design issues/solutions to internal management and customer representatives.

Basic Qualifications:
•    Bachelor's Degree in a STEM field with 9 years of related experience; OR a Master's Degree in a STEM field with 7 years of related experience; OR a PhD in a STEM field with 4 years of related experience.
•    Working knowledge of mechanical installation of subsystems, such as: electrical, hydraulics, fuels, fluids or propulsion systems.  
•    Experience in a combination of ECS, Fuel, Powerplant, Landing Gear, Harness, and Hydraulic installations.
• Experience and ability to work with the manufacturing team, support A/V testing, and support A/V integration as needed.
• High level of proficiency using CATIA V5 in the development of aircraft Subsystem Installations.

•    Must have the ability to obtain an active DoD Secret or Top Secret clearance. This clearance must be obtained in a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.
•    Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance. This SAP must be obtained in a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Preferred Qualifications:
•    Active DoD clearance

•   Advanced degree in Mechanical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, or Electrical Engineering and experience using Unigraphics Nx or CATIA V5

•    Experience with Model Based Engineering (MBE) or 3-D model engineering definition.

•    Experience working on Special Access Programs
•    Experience working on military aircraft
