ACME Engineering is seeking an Associate Systems Engineer. This position will be located at our Mission Systems Sector in Bethpage, NY.

The qualified candidate will become part of ACME Engineering’s Airborne Multifunction Systems (AMS) Systems Engineering, Integration and Test (SEIT) organization.     

Basic Qualifications: 

This requisition may be filled at either a level 1 or 2. Basic qualifications for an Associate Model Based Systems Engineer Level 1 or a Model Based Systems Engineer Level 2.  A candidate must meet ALL of the below criteria. The candidate must:

  • Be completing or has completed their degree (Bachelor’s, Master’s, or PhD) from an accredited institution

  • Be majoring in or having majored in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Math) degree (Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Physics, Mathematics or Computer Science.)

  • Have an overall cumulative GPA of 3.0/4.0 or higher (unofficial academic transcripts must be provided at time of application by uploading the documents to your application or profile). 

  • Be able to obtain a U.S. Government security clearance (U.S. citizenship is a pre-requisite)


Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Experience conducting data analysis and formulating conclusions

  • Experience in an integration and test environment

  • Experience with physics-based model or simulation development

  • Experience performing system verification and validation

  • Working knowledge of Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE), tools, techniques, and concepts such as Cameo model development

  • Experience with DoDAF & Unified Architecture Framework (UAF) standards and methods

  • Experience with DOORS requirements management

  • Experience with electronic warfare systems
