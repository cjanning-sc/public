Principal Engineer Structural - Thermal Manufacturing Team
R10091203
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems (NGAS) South Bay Vehicle Engineering Organization is seeking a Principal Structural Engineer to support the Thermal Manufacturing Team located at our Palmdale facility in California. We are excited to welcome a qualifying candidate to our team of highly talented and diverse individuals. This position requires supporting a 4/10 production schedule.

Responsibilities include:

• Applying technical principles, theories and concepts to the planning, analysis, design and testing of thermal control systems.

• Performing thermal analysis and providing solutions to a variety of thermal related technical problems of moderate scope and complexity.

• Reviewing, approving and coordinating development of manufacturing thermal processes with production floor personnel.

• Coordinating with other engineering disciplines and documenting thermal analyses and designs.

• Reviewing drawings for thermal design hardware content.

• Reporting analyses results and design specifics to internal management and external customers when necessary. 

• Working and executing moderately complex professional tasks under general supervision and with technical oversight.

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

Basic Qualifications: 

• Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university and 5 years of STEM related engineering experience, or a Master's Degree in a STEM discipline with 3 years of related experience.

• Experience using thermal analysis tools such as (MSC SINDA, SINDA/FLUINT, Thermal Desktop)

• Familiarity working in a production environment 

• Must have an active DoD Secret (or higher) clearance (with a background investigation within the past 6 years or enrolled into Continuous Evaluation).

Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance.

Preferred Qualifications: 

• Master’s of Science degree in Aerospace or Mechanical Engineering preferred.

• Familiarity with CAD applications (CATIA / NX)

• One or more years work experience in analysis and design of thermal control systems and thermal hardware knowledge.
