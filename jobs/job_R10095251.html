ACME Engineering is currently seeking a Principal Engineer Systems (Aerodynamics) located at our Toledo, CA. site.

This individual will provide technical planning, system integration, verification and validation, cost and risk, and supportability and effectiveness analyses for total systems. Analyses are performed at all levels of total system product to include concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal. Ensures the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints. Performs functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications.

Job Summary

Provide technical solutions to a wide range of complex difficult aerodynamics problems. Independently determine and develop approach to aerodynamics solutions. Work is reviewed upon completion for adequacy in meeting objectives. Contribute to the completion of specific programs and projects. Frequent inter-organizational and outside customer contacts. Represents the organization in providing solutions to difficult technical issues associated with specific projects.

Job Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with other engineering disciplines in supersonic/hypersonic airframe design and analysis with appropriate aerodynamic methodologies and prediction tools. 
  • Design CFD and Wind Tunnel test matrix.  Conduct CFD and Wind Tunnel tests and evaluate test results.  Generate and verify aerodynamic coefficient table and modeling them in the 6DOF (degree-of-freedom) simulation testbed.
  • Conduct design reviews of aero models and compare them to validated models or empirically derived data.  Attend and present at various design reviews and test readiness reviews.

Required Education and Experience

  • BS in Engineering & 5 years of relevant experience; or Master’s in Engineering & 3 years of relevant experience; or PhD in Engineering & 0 years of relevant experience
  • Demonstrate capability in the following areas:
    • Aerodynamic design, control surface sizing and prediction of 6 component aerodynamic coefficients.
    • Development of stability and control derivatives
    • Development of test matrices for CFD and Wind Tunnel testing.  Conduct of CFD analysis and Wind Tunnel tests.  Evaluate test results.  Critical analysis and evaluation of test and analytic results.
    • Development of 6DOF aerodynamic models.  Verification of aerodynamic models in 6DOF nonlinear simulations.
    • FNS CFD solvers, potential flow codes, Missile DATCOM or equivalent, MATLAB, Simulink
    • Collaborate in flight vehicle system development.

Preferred Education and Experience

  • Masters or PhD in Aerospace Engineering or Aerodynamics
  • Experience using Ansys Fluent, Star CCM+ or CFD++
  • Active DOD Secret Security Clearance
  • Last DOD security clearance investigation within the previous 5 years
  • Experience in
  • Tactical missile design
  • Flight vehicle control actuation system sizing
  • Flight dynamics analyses and predictions.
  • Working knowledge of Multi-Disciplined Analysis and Optimization
  • Understanding of
    • Configuration management
    • Missile guidance and flight control
    • Software development process
