About Your Opportunity:

Play a key role on Northrup Grumman UK’s Highest Profile programmes!

As the senior member of the support team you will be the pivotal role between our customer base, our product engineering teams and engineering management.    You will help provide our UK and international Defence and security customers with innovative, multi-domain C4ISR solutions. Our software is used throughout the world for military operations, homeland defence, crisis management and conflict resolution.

  • Career Development – opportunity for ongoing professional development and career growth opportunities

Your Responsibilities:

  • Manage the service desk, service requests, incidents, problems and change requests in line with the relevant quality processes and SLA.
  • Establish strong working relationships between customers and Northrop Grumman teams so each understands the others’ activities, needs and priorities.
  • Collate support-related statistics and status reports and provide these as requested;
  • Supervise junior team members
  • Maintain the service desk tools and customer reference systems

Your Experience:
