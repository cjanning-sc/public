The Ground Systems Development Department based in Manhattan Beach, CA is seeking talented Sr. Principal Test Engineer interested in testing and integrating Digital and RF hardware focused on ground based applications.

The responsibilities include satellite system ground-based hardware testing, flight electronics test systems, site support, procurement, logistics, material tracking and disposition.

The ideal candidate has experience with hands on hardware test and integration and is comfortable with racking units, being hands on in the assembly lab with hardware such as commercial test equipment, custom electronics (simple to very complex), PCBs, Units, Test Sets, subsystems, and systems. This person will be working on programs of various sizes ranging from small to large teams. This position has opportunity to travel both domestically and internationally after obtaining security clearance.
