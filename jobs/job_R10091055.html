Level 4 Sr Principal Proposal Analyst
R10091055
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman has an opening for a level 4 Sr Principal Proposal Analyst to support efforts associated with proposal development, planning and scheduling during the supplier acquisition process. This position will be located in on site in Melbourne, FL.

The selected candidate will perform cost estimating/pricing related duties and analysis in accordance with request for proposal (RFP) specifications and in compliance with regulatory requirements. They will coordinate with functional departments to facilitate the collection and accumulation of cost, scheduling, and performance elements and research and analyze historical data and develop cost models to support proposal efforts. Additionally, the selected candidate will coordinate activities and participates in fact-finding meetings and in should-cost and pricing reviews with management and customer representatives.

Essential Functions:

  • Submits written supplier price/cost analysis reports (PCARs) detailing the suppliers’ price/cost proposal as required by FAR Part 15 and the NGAS negotiation objective position
  • Prepares Procurement Review Board (PRB) briefings showing supplier proposed vs. negotiation objective positions by major element of cost to attain formal Supply Chain Management approval for supplier negotiations
  • Prepares materiel estimates and provides a consolidated bill of material to include in cost volumes as a support function to NGAS Pricing/Estimating
  • Prepares and supports presentation and approval of materiel estimates at the required value level as part of the proposal team
  • Supports supplier negotiations and represents the supplier price/cost analysis position to customers as required to support NGAS Contracts and NGAS Pricing/Estimating organizations
  • Responds to miscellaneous and external Global Supply Chain Business Management request for information regarding assigned suppliers
  • Estimating, accounting, and billing system review and compliance
  • Maintain and update program bill of material, actual cost records, and supporting data
  • Supports DCAA and DCMA audits as required
  • Material/Subcontract EAC and Variance Analysis
  • Analyze, Forecast and Track Supplier Cash/Expenditures
  • Make/Buy Analysis Support
  • Support Global Supply Chain with assigned suppliers from Change Board, RFP process, proposal evaluation, negotiation, affordability, and close out

Basic Qualifications for a Sr Principal Proposal Analyst:

  • Bachelor's degree with 10 years of pricing/estimating, finance, contracts, proposals, and/or business-related experience, OR a Master's Degree with 8 years of relevant experience
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain a Secret Clearance and Special Program Access prior to start
  • Proficiency with MS Office Suite, specifically Excel and Word

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Ability to perform and apply cost accounting, pricing and estimating concepts and principles
  • Ability to work as an integral member of the Subcontract/Procurement team to achieve Global Supply Chain, Program, and/or NGAS objectives, while maintaining compliance during all stages of the proposal/acquisition process
  • Ability to meet deadlines in a challenging, multi-tasking environment
  • Active Secret Clearance
