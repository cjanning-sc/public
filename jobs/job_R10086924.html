The Payload and Ground Systems Division within Northrop Grumman Space Systems is a strategic partner specializing in delivering intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance solutions for classified and unclassified missions in space.  Headquartered in Linthicum, Maryland, PGS RSP has offices, manufacturing, and world-class assembly, integration, and test facilities.  We do it all here, on-site, from sensors to payloads to full up spacecraft, supporting Class A to Class D missions.  Our key capabilities include Active RF, Passive RF, and Electro-Optical payloads, space solar power, cross domain missions, and space resilience payloads & solutions.

 

This position is for a Principal/ Sr Principal Systems Engineer to join our Remote Sensing Programs, Systems Engineering Integration and Test (RSP SEIT) team.  As a member of this team, you will have the opportunity to support efforts on both concept and product development to deliver key solutions to our customers.  We cover a wide variety of expertise within the systems engineering domain, including CONOPS development, mission engineering, requirements development and management, architecture, modeling and simulation, integration and test, verification and validation, and on-orbit operations and support.

 

This position will be responsible for:

Electromagnetic Interference/Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMI/EMC) planning

  • Development and updates to test documentation, i.e., test plans, procedures and configuration management of test results

  • Procurement and verification of required testing items

  • Interface with various facets of the Ammolite team, including, but not limited to Electrical & Mechanical Ground Support Equipment (EGSE/MGSE), Environmental, Thermal Vacuum, Maximum Power and Vibration Testing for Space

  • Status any remaining procured and/or manufactured parts

 

Some initial work will be unclassified while waiting on a security clearance if not already active, but a TS/SCI clearance will be needed to continue and perform all duties for this position.

This requisition can be filled at either a Principal or Sr Principal Level based on the qualifications below.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Principal Engineer: Bachelor's in Electrical, Computer, Systems or other STEM related degrees + 5 years of exp; Master's + 3 years of exp; PhD + 0 years of exp

  • Sr Principal Engineer: Bachelor's in Electrical, Computer, Systems or other STEM related degrees + 9 years of exp; Master's + 7 years of exp; PhD + 4 years of exp

  • Active DoD TS clearance with ability to obtain an SCI.

 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active TS/SCI clearance

  • Space experience

  • Sensor/payload experience, especially with RADAR or RF Systems

  • Experience in EMI/EMC activities

  • Integration and Test for RADAR or RF Systems and/or Space Payloads

  • Atlassian Tool Suite:  Jira, Bitbucket, and Confluence
