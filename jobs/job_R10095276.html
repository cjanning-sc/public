Staff Team Lead - Analysis

1st Shift (United States of America)

We are looking for you to join our team as a Staff Team Lead - Analysis based out of Colorado Springs, CO. In this position, you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be our Staff Team Lead – Analysis for a Mission Level Modeling & Simulation Internal Research and Development (IRAD) project, solving challenging problems and supporting internal and external initiatives throughout various DoD customers and across NG. Through the use of Mission Level Modeling and Simulation techniques, DevSecOps and Agile practices, we continue to evolve how we deliver impactful and meaningful analysis, data analytics/products and capabilities for the warfighter, our government customers and also to our internal NG customers.

Our success is grounded in our ability to embrace change, move quickly and continuously drive innovation across the kill chain in the various land, sea, air & space domains. The successful candidate will be collaborative, open, transparent, and team-oriented with a focus on team empowerment & shared responsibility, flexibility, continuous learning, and a culture of automation.  The Staff Team Lead – Analysis will serve as a mentor and guide other teammates to innovate and solve the nation’s hardest analysis problems across the kill chain.

This position will require onsite work however for this role we are able to offer flexible work arrangements and some remote work will be possible.

As the Staff Team Lead – Analysis on this program, you will

  • Support leadership meetings and large team meetings
  • Develop solutions for hard, high priority projects within our mod/sim space and work with a team to implement the solutions
  • Research new and novel ways to solve our hardest problems
  • Advance the analysis capabilities of a 50 person team through mentorship and brown bag sessions
  • Lead and grow an analysis team
  • Work with various stakeholders to define MS&A objectives
  • Assist in the Design of Experiments and set quantitative analytical approaches
  • Oversee and help establish scenarios that achieve the defined objectives with an analysis first perspective

Candidate is expected to operate independently with little oversight in a dynamic environment, to be highly self-motivated. The individual is expected to handle diverse assignments with efficiency, finding unique solutions and working in a very collaborative and fast paced environment that continues to grow in size.

Basic Qualifications for Staff Team Lead – Analysis:

  • Active Secret Clearance
  • 14 years of relevant experience with a Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM discipline; 12 years of relevant experience with a Masters' degree; 9 years of experience with a PhD
  • Deep understanding and experience with Python and AFSIM
  • Domain expertise in defense, especially Missile Defense, sensors, and satellite systems
  • Familiarity and direct experience with Model-Based Systems Engineering languages (SysML/UML) and concepts
  • Deep understanding of DoD systems engineering requirements
  • Experience in modeling across multiple engineering areas such as Space Sensors, Ground Sensors, Weapons
  • Demonstrated experience quickly learning new concepts and approaches
  • Effectively communicate and present to project and program management, other technical managers and customers
  • Experience as SME, Technical lead, Integrated Product Team (IPT) Lead, or other engineering leadership role on a modeling and simulation project

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Work experience and/or exposure to software engineering, modeling and simulation, and analysis
  • Experience in business capture engagement and proposals
  • Experience in systems development, integration, and test 
  • Experience with Agile development
  • Excellent technical writing and team building skills
  • Working knowledge of software engineering principles and processes
