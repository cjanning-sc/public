ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Command & Launch Element Production Manager 2 to join its team of diverse professionals in support of the Sentinel (GBSD) program. This leadership role is located in Detroit, Michigan. Learn more about the Sentinel (GBSD) program here.  ACME Engineering supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What you will get to do:

The Command & Launch Element Production Manager 2 will report to the Command & Launch Segment Production Lead. Responsibilities include:

  • Lead and direct a team in Engineering & Manufacturing Development (EMD) environment for C&L (Command and Launch) segment IPT Teams (Integrated Product Team).
  • Provide leadership, draw upon technical expertise in Manufacturing Engineering disciplines (Engineering to Manufacturing design for excellence integration, producibility, tooling concepts development; etc.) across the IPT
  • Manage SOW within Program (IPT) and Budget.
  • Develop and coordinate accurate and executable resource plans, Integrated Master Schedules, work plans and associated Visibility Metrics
  • Experience working with Digital Environment integrating Engineering Systems to the MES (Manufacturing Execution Systems)
  • Outline specific objectives and processes that are focused on generating innovative solutions to moderately complex business problems
  • Lead optimization activity, studies on affordability and efficiency, and EVMS Goals
  • Maintain open communications with all Enterprise functions including Production, Engineering, Quality, GSC, Logistics, and Integrated Product Teams (IPTs)
  • Development and execution of long-range plans and strategies while providing the most effective resources by skillsets to the program
  • Awareness of Manufacturing Engineering and Industrial Engineering best practices and on operating models, proposals and new business pursuits
  • Collaborate the technical solutions across the IPT, functions and program leadership

As a leader, you always act with integrity, and the highest ethical standards characterize everything you do.
