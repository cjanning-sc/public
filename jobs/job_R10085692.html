Principal Systems Engineer - GEO Communications (Space/Satellite) TS/SCI Clearance
R10085692
1st Shift (United States of America)

The successful candidates for this position will work in a lead technical role for a space program in Dulles VA, in the NSS business area within the Space Systems Sector that include technical roles at the mission, segment, and component levels. They span the entire program life cycle from early concept studies, design development, system integration/test, launch and early operations, mission operations/sustainment, and system disposal.

This position will expose the candidate to equal parts technical competency, forward thinking ideas, and execution focus. This position requires a functional understanding of systems engineering as well as a basic understanding of space systems.

This Systems Engineering role is responsible for working the Systems Engineering technical team, which includes the following duties: systems analysis and trades, coordinating Vehicle Systems engineering activities, interface management, requirements/V&V management, subcontractor technical oversight, preparing/presenting SE material. The responsibilities include both individual-contributor and leadership roles, within a multi-disciplined engineering team.  In this role, the Systems Engineer is expected to be self-directed using previous experience to assist in design, analysis, and execution efforts.

The candidate will work with cross-discipline team members and must have excellent verbal and written communication skills and the ability to work with a diverse community of engineers and managers to achieve the goals of the program team.

Duties/Responsibilities:

  • Analyze and interpret requirements and apply them to the design and development of a power architecture

  • Apply in-depth understanding of Geo-communication Satellites product line of NG or equivalent GEO-communication satellites.

  • Supports program staff in the analysis, investigation, and resolution of engineering electrical systems problems, subsystem requirements development, tasking, and schedule management as required by the program.

  • Complete subsystem trade studies for best application from a technical, schedule and cost

  • Cross-Collaborate with multiple development projects and team members to reach common solutions

  • Support Integration and Test at a spacecraft level

  • Support Mission Operations during launch of spacecraft

  • Develops and manages schedule and resources as required by the program

  • Effectively operate within a multi-discipline engineering team

  • Provide technical oversight to ensure the electrical system is compliant and verification

  • Evaluates and coordinates electrical system designs to ensure system- level requirements are met

  • Provides documentation and makes technical presentations as required

  • Develops space vehicle level interface designs

  • Assistance in providing solutions to technical issues associated with specific projects

Basic Qualifications:

  • B.S. in STEM - Science, Technology, Engineering, Math or related area and 5 years of experience; M.S. in STEM or related area and 3 years of experience; or a PhD in STEM or related area with systems engineering experience in either Geo-communication Satellites product line of NG or equivalent GEO-communication satellites

  • Experience with a space development program

  • Must have US Citizenship with an Active or Current TS/SCI Clearance

Preferred Experience (one or more areas desired):

  • Knowledge of various mission analysis techniques and software tools.

  • Understanding of Agile software development practices.

  • Requirements management using DOORS
