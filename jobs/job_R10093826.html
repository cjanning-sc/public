SP Launch and Missile Defense Systems (LMDS) is an industry-leading provider for launch systems capabilities serving national security, military, civil and commercial customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible.
 
LMDS has an opening for a Principal Subcontracts Administrator to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Chandler, Arizona.

Roles and Responsibilities:

·       Sources and procures goods and services of a category nature from many suppliers.
·       Initiates Requests for Quotations and Proposals and receives same, analyzing proposed pricing from potential suppliers.
·       Establishes agreements with suppliers using a mix of contract types including firm fixed price and cost reimbursable.
·       In conjunction with quality organization, evaluates and monitors supplier quality and reliability, as well as supplier ability to support required delivery schedules.
·       Works with internal customers to ensure specifications are appropriate for the services or products required.
·       Identifies opportunities to reduce cost, improve efficiency and manage risk through targeted negotiations.
·       Issues purchase orders.
·       Manages performance of purchase orders, ensures compliance with all public law requirements as well as company (and business) policies and procedures including import/export regulations.
·       Employs sound business practices and assesses supplier performance as part of follow-up activities.
·       Ensures that appropriate documentation is provided to logistics/transportation & warehouse organization so that proper receipt is recorded.
·       Reviews and approves supplier payment as needed.
·       Utilizes process tools such as Lean Six Sigma with suppliers to drive quality, efficiency and continuous improvement.


The ideal candidate will have the following qualities:

·       Works positively in a high-energy and fast-paced environment 

·       Self-motivated

·       Highly organized

·       Delivers quality work on time

·       Communicates quickly and thoughtfully through integrated team structures 

·       Strong interpersonal skills with a best-in-class customer service mindset

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree or 4 additional years of related experience

  • 8+ years’ experience in purchasing, sales, contracts, or related field –

  • Knowledge of government contracting requirements and regulations (e.g. FAR, DFARS, ITAR, etc.) - Comfortable leading negotiations and preparing price analyses of supplier proposals

  •  US citizenship and ability to obtain clearance

 

 

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience writing and negotiating Long Term Agreements

  • Experience negotiating Terms and Conditions

  • Comfort level working with MRP/ERP systems

  • Strong MS Office skills
