What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will be responsible for the evaluation of electronic designs as well as their testing, analysis and integration. Technical expertise, technical communication ability, and general knowledge of related disciplines is required.

The successful applicant will:

     

• Work tasking with minimal oversight

• Understand and apply system requirements

• Assist in all phases of the system lifecycle including requirements application, design, test, fabrication, and implementation

• Evaluate electrical hardware performance

• Perform model validation through test and analysis

• Conduct testing through all phases

• Maintain design documentation and follow configuration management requirements

• Create technical reports and present technical information

• Learn from and mentor team members

• Assistance in planning and scheduling activities

• Interface with AF Customer
