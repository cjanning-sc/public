Configuration Analyst/Principal Configuration Analyst (DOORS administration)(Philadelphia, FL)

1st Shift

ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Configuration Analyst/ Principal Configuration Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania and will be focused on change management within the IBM DOORS Application.

Position Overview:

Successful candidate will provide cross functional support for requirements management utilizing IBM Rational Dynamic Object-Oriented Requirements System (DOORS) as an administrator on multiple programs. Responsibilities include creating DOORS processes and procedures, training materials, and acting as a technical interface to other working level counterparts (internal and external).

Essential Functions:

•    Development and execution of scripts and other database automation

•    Maintaining, streamlining, and establishing consistent practices within the DOORS environment, which includes data created internal to programs and modules received from the customer and subcontractors

•    Investigation of transition into forward looking tools and Agile/MBSE requirements management processes

•    Development and reporting of requirements management metrics to IPT and program leadership

•    Development of database management best practices for classified/unclassified merges and database synchronization with NAVAIR

•    Implementation of Change Request across IPTs

•    Identifying and analyzing technological problems or risks related to DOORS and system requirements processes

•    Performing DOORS administration; creating modules for sites; synchronizing modules between sites; creating DOORS reports (e.g., specifications traceability and metrics), successfully addressing user problems (e.g., installation, use of Change Proposal System).  

•    Providing technical leadership for processes and best practices across IBM Rational DOORS

•    Establishing strong working relationships across program and functional organizations

Basic Qualifications:

• Configuration Analyst: Master’s degree and 1 year of professional work experience in DoD configuration and/or data management; a Bachelor’s degree and 3 years of professional experience as a configuration analyst, OR 7 years of professional work experience in a DoD configuration and/or data management may be considered in lieu of degree requirement

• Principal Configuration Analyst: Master’s degree and 4 years of professional work experience in DoD configuration and/or data management; a Bachelor’s degree and 6 years of professional experience as a configuration analyst, OR 10 years of professional work experience in a DoD configuration and/or data management role may be considered in lieu of degree requirement

•  Must have the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance, must have an Interim DoD Secret clearance to start•  

•  Must have Knowledge of System engineering lifecycle process

•  Must have Knowledge of requirements change control process

•  Must have Knowledge of MS Office applications, and experience working in a technical environment

•  Must have Knowledge/experience in writing DXL scripts, Python, XML, or Java

•  Must have knowledge/experience importing data into DOORS and exporting DOORS data into Microsoft Excel/Word

Preferred Qualifications:

•  Advanced DOORS administrator skills

•  Training new users on how to utilize the DOORS tool, and document/update DOORS training material

•  DOORS eXtension Language (DXL) programming skills to modify existing or create DXL scripts to manipulate DOORS data

•  Familiarity with Systems Engineering principles

•  Experience exporting DOORS data using IBM Rational Publishing Engine or Engineering Lifecycle Optimization Publishing

•  Experience working across multiple classification levels and programs
