What You’ll get to Do:

  • Synthesize customer contractual needs and requirements into system test solutions that acknowledges technical, schedule and cost constraints.
  • Develop and direct preparation and execution of comprehensive test plans, procedures, and schedules for complete systems. This includes designing a formal qualification strategy to qualify new system designs utilizing Microsoft Word, Excel, DOORS.
  • Using scripting knowledge to develop test scripts to automate test campaigns.
  • Coordinate integrated testing activities.
  • Review and evaluate test requirements to ensure completeness of test program.
  • Perform technical analysis of complete systems and prepares comprehensive system level evaluations.
  • Assess performance of Navigation Systems and Mission Computers by analyzing data using MatLab or similar software.
  • May develop System Models using Simulink or Cameo.
  • May be part of an Agile SCRUM team.
