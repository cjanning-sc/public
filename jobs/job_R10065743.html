Northrop Grumman is seeking a Supply Chain Intern.  This position will be located at our Mission Systems Sector in Rolling Meadows, IL. The qualified candidate will become part of Northrop Grumman's Global Supply Chain organization.

Essential Functions:

  • Actively monitor supplier delivery and quality performance tracking

  • Actively support functions to provide comprehensive business forecasting.

  • Interfaced with Business Operations Manager to pull and analyze data for GSC Division.

  • Coordinate with internal partners to develop competitive and non-competitive sourcing strategies.

  • Understand and compare bids and proposals and place Purchase Orders

  • Understand and evaluate multiple supplier proposals to place compliant purchase orders.

  • Extract, monitor, analyze and validate data and information from multiple sources.

  • Develop engaging and collaborative relationships with suppliers and internal partners / customers to drive problem resolution.

Basic Qualifications

A candidate, regardless of age and hiring source, must meet ALL of the below criteria. The candidate must:

  • Be enrolled full-time and pursuing an undergraduate or graduate degree from an accredited college/university AND graduating after August 2023

  • Be majoring in Supply Chain Management, Business Management, Logistics, Operations Management, or closely related discipline

  • Have an overall cumulative GPA of 3.0/4.0 or higher (unofficial academic transcripts must be provided at time of application by uploading the documents to your application or profile)

  • Be available to work full-time (40 hours per week) for at least 10 weeks during summer 2023

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Have an overall cumulative GPA of 3.25/4.0 or higher

  • Previous internship/co-op or other work experience, leadership & teamwork capabilities, interpersonal communication skills, and activities (professional/community/extracurricular)

Intern Pay Range:

  • Bachelor’s: $22.75 to $29.25

  • Master’s: $34.50 to $36.50

At Northrop Grumman, our internship pay rates and standardized and take into account three main factors: the anticipated graduation date of the individual, the cost of labor of the geographic location, and whether the internship is considered technical or administrative.
