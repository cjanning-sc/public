  • Managing multiple priorities and actions simultaneously
  • Managing calendars and scheduling meetings, to include all support necessary to facilitate meetings, often in close coordination with executive assistants across the company
  • Arranging travel
  • Executing expense reports
  • Support the creation of presentations

  • Computer skills required include advanced expertise in Microsoft Office 365 software: Word, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Excel.
  • Experience participating and hosting in-person and remote video teleconference meetings (such as Zoom, Skype, MS Teams).
