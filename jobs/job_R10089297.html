ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Manager Procurement 1 to support our Global Supply Chain Procurement team. The selected candidate may be located in Iuka, MS.

The leader in this role will be responsible for the direct supervision and management of their delegated staff. The leader’s team will consist of various professionals who support in procurement activities across various locations.

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off.

In this role, the selected candidate will be expected to:

  • Must be Proficient in the procurement process, including but not limited to source selection, price analysis, negotiations, risk management
  • The preferred candidate should possess outstanding written and verbal communication skills and be extremely organized
  • Can quickly identify and communicate risk and opportunity
  • Have a clear understanding of established processes and procedures
  • Possess the ability to align team goals to Sector and Division objectives
  • Effectively manage a supply base: Evaluate supplier delivery, quality, and cost performance
  • Develop and maintain Strategic Supplier relationships
  • Supervise release of RFQs for LTA and high-dollar reqs
  • Execute negotiations of LTAs and high-dollar contracts
  • Identify opportunity for cross Divisional strategies
  • Build and develop a geographic diverse and remote team
  • Ability to understand engineering practices and work within the technology arena to further the connection between the technology roadmaps and Global Supply Chain
  • Ability to plan schedule and arrange activities to accomplish objectives

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree and a minimum of 5 years’ experience in a procurement related field
  • Understanding of Federal Acquisition Regulations, DFARs and terms and conditions
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Security Clearance and special program access within a reasonable period of time as determined by the needs of the business

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD Secret Clearance
  • Previous Management experience
  • Project Management experience with a clear understanding of establishing and maintaining schedules
  • Experience building and/or leading teams, interacting with multifunctional roles/department
  • Leadership and people management skills with the ability to model and promote a strong sense of engagement, teamwork and cooperation
  • Budgeting experience and EVM knowledge
  • Solid understanding of engineering/technical requirements.
