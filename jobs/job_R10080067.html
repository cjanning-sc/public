Northrop Grumman's Life Cycle Sustainment Operations group is seeking a Testing & Repair Engineer to support the repair and testing operations of airborne radars and test sets.  The engineer will apply Radio Frequency (RF) engineering principals to support analysis and process support for legacy and emerging technologies. Familiarization with a wide variety of systems including radars would be a positive.

The position consists of engineering support in the form(s) of:

  • Troubleshooting and performance evaluation of older test set. This includes test set modification with documentation
  • Design and integration of new test sets
  • Unit under test evaluations
  • Documenting test procedures
  • Interface with program offices
  • Generate technical reports, track, and report on performance
  • Estimate work efforts (hours and schedule) and other ongoing tasks in support of an operational system

Mature verbal and written skills are required, and work efforts are in a variety of areas including office spaces and testing areas. The position is hands on working with the commodities and test sets, includes interface with vendors, in house prime mission equipment engineering, test design mechanical/electrical engineering as well as Purchasing, Operations and Manufacturing functional groups.

Basic Qualifications

  • BS degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering or closely related technical degree with 3 years related experience or a Master's with 0.5years related experience.
  • Requires a strong technical background (electrical and software); with experience testing RF circuitry and troubleshooting to the component level by following schematics.
  • Team oriented individual with strong communication skills and the ability to effectively interface with various internal and external customers and functional groups including: vendors, engineering, purchasing, operations and manufacturing.
  • Proficiency in MS Office is required (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).
  • The ability to obtain a US Secret Level security clearance is required, US Citizenship is a prerequisite.  An interim Secret level security clearance will be required soon after start.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current Secret or higher clearance
  • Prior knowledge of the following program languages
  • C++
  • Lab Windows /CVI

Prior experience with SAP or other Material Requirements Planning/ Manufacturing Execution too.
