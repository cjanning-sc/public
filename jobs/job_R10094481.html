ACME Engineering Defense Systems in Toledo, CA is currently seeking an Associate Supply Chain Procurement Specialist (level 1) / Supply Chain Procurement Specialist (level 2)   to join our team.

This position may be hybrid after the first three (3) months. The selected candidate will be required to work on-site in Toledo, CA and must be able and willing to be in the office full-time to start.

In this position, the selected candidate will:

  • Source and procure several commodities such as electronic components, COTS, test equipment on development and production programs.
  • Proficiency in the procurement process, including but not limited to price analysis, negotiations, policies and procedures, including the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR)
  • Ability to place and oversee procurement packages
  • Initiates Requests for Quotations and Proposals and receives same, analyzing proposed pricing from potential suppliers.
  • In conjunction with quality organization, evaluates and monitors supplier quality and reliability, as well as supplier ability to support required delivery schedules.
  • Identifies opportunities to reduce cost, improve efficiency and manage risk through targeted negotiations.
  • Manages performance of purchase orders, ensures compliance with all public law requirements as well as company (and business) policies and procedures including import/export regulations.
  • Review, reconcile and approve supplier invoices
  • Interfacing and collaborating with Programs and other organizations within and outside of Global Supply Chain
  • Ability to plan schedule and arrange activities to accomplish objective
  • Person should possess outstanding written and verbal communication skills and be extremely organized

  • Ability to work an amazing work schedule (4/10 shift schedule - Monday- Thursday)

Position to be hired based on qualifications and experience of selected candidate.

​Basic Qualifications for an Associate Supply Chain Procurement Specialist (Level 1): 

  • High School diploma/equivalent with 4 years' experience in supply chain, procurement, purchasing, supplier management, and/or related industry experience OR a Bachelor’s Degree with 0 years experience.
  • Effective oral and written communication and interpersonal skills
  • Experience with using Microsoft Office Products including Word, PowerPoint, Excel
  • Self-starter who is able to work with minimal direction
  • Procurement experience in Government environment
  • Ability to obtain a US Government issued Secret clearance; US Citizenship is required.

Basic Qualifications for a Supply Chain Procurement Specialist (Level 2): 

  • High School diploma/equivalent with 7 years' experience in supply chain, procurement, purchasing, supplier management, and/or related industry experience, OR a Bachelor’s Degree with 3 years experience OR a Master's degree with 1 years' experience
  • Effective oral and written communication and interpersonal skills
  • Experience with using Microsoft Office Products including Word, PowerPoint, Excel
  • Self-starter who is able to work with minimal direction
  • Procurement experience in Government environment
  • Ability to obtain a US Government issued Secret clearance; US Citizenship is required.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree
  • FAR/DFAR or government contracts experience
  • Current/active Secret clearance
