Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support Sentinel (Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD)) program.

What you’ll get to do:

  • The selected candidate will be a Systems Engineer for the Major Maintenance Vehicle Team. This position will be responsible for development of requirements/architecture/interfaces for the GBSD system. This engineer will use DOORS for requirements documentation and CAMEO for Architectural decomposition, and Verification Planning. The position includes working with the internal MMV team, external Sentinel teams, our suppliers, as well as the customer.
  • Responsibilities may include but not limited to:
  • Developing vehicle level architecture models and requirements.
  • Supporting supplier development of lower level requirements and interfaces
  • Supporting supplier design reviews / ensuring presented designs meet requirements
  • Supporting development of verification plans and supporting vehicle test efforts
  • Working on a small, high performance team / mentoring other systems engineers
  • Provide technical teams guidance and oversite with the applicable requirements and specifications
  • Work at a point of contact for other teams to ensure that applicable requirements are documented and flowed down to the responsible engineering team

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering or a related technical discipline
  • A minimum 5 years of related experience with a Bachelor’s degree; 3 years with a Master’s; or 0 years with PhD
  • U.S. Citizenship with active DoD Secret Clearance
  • Eligible for Special Access Program Clearance (DoD clearance awarded or reviewed in the last 6 years)
