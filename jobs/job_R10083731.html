The Cincinnati Lab Operations, Engineering and Test Department is looking for a passionate and dedicated Integration and Test Engineer 4 to join our Cincinnati, CA team.  


Our Integration and Test team works directly with Vendors, Responsible Engineer (RE) and Software Developers to integrate subsystems into a larger family of systems.  As the integration and test engineer, you’ll be responsible for integrating systems, writing detailed test procedures, performing dry runs and running certification tests with quality assurance.  This statement of work is performed within our numerous Cincinnati labs but might require a hand full of visit to other locations throughout the year.  Are you ready for a new challenge and interested in supporting the future of technology at ACME Engineering?  If yes, then this position is for you.

This position will require occasional overtime and cross-training efforts.
