ACME Engineering’s Space Systems – Propulsions Division is currently seeking an Administrative Assistant 3/Administrative Assistant 4 to support the Promontory Value Stream at our Promontory, UT facility.

Job Description:

The Administrative Assistant in the Propulsion Systems group provides a critical role in providing the organizational and administrative support required for the effective operation.

Job Duties/Responsibility:

The primary responsibilities of the position include but are not limited to:

  • Write and distribute email, correspondence memos, letters, faxes and forms

  • Assist in the preparation of regularly scheduled reports

  • Track and ensure that all training is complete and current

  • Answers and directs incoming calls

  • Books complex and changing domestic and international travel, prepare and submit expense reimbursement reports

  • Schedules meetings and arranges for catering

  • Deals with a diverse group of internal employees and external customers

  • Order, organize, and monitor inventory for office and kitchen supplies

  • Interfaces with data management tools and personnel to retain critical communications, files and records

  • Provides general support to visitors and customers

  • Manages multiple Outlook calendars

  • Produces and edits correspondence and presentations

  • Independently coordinates and manages a variety of projects 

  • Builds working relationships with a diverse group of internal employees and external customers

  • Schedule celebration lunches and track Rewards and Recognition budget

  • Submit P-card orders, receive, and reconcile orders

  • Coordinate approvals and submit FFI-0010/PRs

  • Other duties as assigned

Basic Requirements:

  • Minimum of 4 years of experience as an administrative assistant

  • Strong written and oral communication skills

  • Strong computer skills including working knowledge of Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Outlook and use of the Internet. 

  • Ability to learn new programs and databases.

  • Ability to exercise discretion and respect confidentiality concerning employee information and company records

Competencies for Success:

  • Articulate and possess excellent verbal and written communication skills

  • Must possess strong interpersonal skills and be able to work amicably with a diverse work force

  • Must possess a high level of initiative

  • Excellent time management skills and the ability to prioritize work

  • Ability to adapt to a changing environment

  • Must be organized and detail oriented.

  • Ability to work well independently and as part of a team.

  • Ability to effectively handle multiple tasks.

  • Must be customer focus oriented

Education/Certifications:

  • High School diploma or equivalent and 4 years additional education and/or related experience

  • Position can be filled one level higher requiring High School diploma or equivalent and 6 years additional education and/or related experience
