Principal or Sr. Principal Mechanical Engineer - Commodity Engineering - Roy, UT
R10091584
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Space Systems has an opening for a Principal Engineer or Senior Principal Engineer to join the Global Supply Chain Engineering Team of qualified, diverse individuals.

This position will be located in Roy, Utah and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.

The Team

The Global Supply Chain Engineering Team is responsible for supply base technical management related to the acquisition of Space mission electronic and mechanical hardware. The candidate will work within a high performing organization that values its employees and has a strong sense of purpose tied to the vision of Northrop Grumman: To be the most trusted provider of systems and technologies that ensure the security and the freedom of our nation and its allies. The candidate will be able to grow personally and professionally in an ethical and inclusive environment, while being an important part of a great team.  Collaboration, learning, and growth are core to the team’s success. 

The Role

This high visibility position requires a motivated, self-starter with an engineering and hardware background and excellent interpersonal skills to lead efforts in response to program customer product needs and concerns.  The engineer should be able to readily apply mechanical engineering fundamentals to creatively solve problems and learn quickly while managing their projects independently. 

Candidate will be expected to work effectively with Materials & Processes (M&P) and other Engineering organizations to drive technical solutions for supplier and manufacturer activities in support of Global Supply Chain efforts and Space related missions.

Responsibilities include:

  • Perform product capability and producibility reviews
  • Perform technical evaluations of supplier quotes
  • Support internal processes associated with program part/product selection and qualification driven by supplier engineering change requests. 
  • Work closely with project engineers, QA and Suppliers to ensure compliance to flow down requirements from customers.
  • Possess ability to work both independently and as a team player and must demonstrate the ability to multi-task and communicate effectively with varying levels of program customers and functional organization leadership.

Some travel to supplier facilities and other Northrop Grumman facilities, may be required.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Science, Technical, Engineering, or Math (STEM)
  • 5 years of experience in an engineering or manufacturing role
  • Must be able to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance

Basic Qualifications for Senior Principal Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Science, Technical, Engineering, or Math (STEM)
  • 9 years of experience in an engineering or manufacturing role
  • Must be able to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance

Basic Qualifications for both Principal Engineer and Senior Principal Engineer:

  • Demonstrated experience to formulate and execute technical solutions to electronic part, assembly, and process engineering challenges
  • Demonstrated experience reviewing engineering specifications, schematics and drawings with the ability to translate customer driven requirements into specifications
  • Demonstrated application of innovative solutions to complex problems that arise within the design, development, and production phases

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Electro-Mechanical and/or Electronic Component/Commodity Engineering experience
  • Experience and understanding of standards for the production of high reliability connectors, cable/harnesses, or printed wiring board fabrication and assembly
  • SAP or other ERP system experience
  • Project Management experience
  • Knowledge of FAR & DFAR requirements
  • Knowledge of ISO 9001/AS9100 requirements
