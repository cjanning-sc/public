Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Staff Electrical Engineer to join the Division Engineering Design Team. This position is located in Roy, UT and supports Strategic Deterrence Systems Division Engineering function.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will report to the manager of Engineering Design within the SDS Engineering Design Directorate and will be a project lead, a contributing team member, and mentor as required:

•Support multiple programs within the Strategic Deterrent Systems (SDS) Division for development of Processes and Governance for their application and use

•Utilize their high degree of understanding of Electrical Design Best Practice and Processes for Missile Systems, to include communications design and test

•Lead the Community of Practice (CoP) for the Electrical Engineering Homeroom

•Provide oversite review of Parts, Materials, and Processes (PM&P) for new/revised specifications related to Mechanical parts, components, materials and processes prior to release for engineering design

•Provide Subject Matter Expertise (SME) for all SDS programs for new proposals, Critical Design Reviews, and Electrical Design Analysis Reviews

•Support Common Product Lifecycle Management (CPLM) design questions in the digital environment (NX products)

•Provide training support for the Electrical Engineering Homeroom

•Coordination to ensure Standards and Processes are being observed for governance and compliance with Corporate, Sector, Division and Gov’t regulations for mandatory procedures, manuals, forms and work instructions

•Collaborate closely with other IPT Subject Matter Experts

•You will have the opportunity to interact with many cross functional organizations

•Provide mentor guidance to Homeroom Engineers and support staff

•Staff Engineers are expected to work towards long term organizational goals, by improving processes

•Broad understanding and application of aerospace electrical engineering technical standards

•Knowledge of Electrical test and evaluation principles

•Broad technical expertise and working knowledge of other related engineering disciplines (i.e. Systems Engineering, Mechanical, Analysis)
