We have an opening for either a Senior Principal Network Engineer (level 4) or a Staff Network Engineer (level 5) to design, perform installation and configuration of network products: switches, routers and firewalls. Installation activities include initial setup following standard configuration guidelines, security hardening, cabling, labeling, documentation and checkout. Ongoing tasks include problem resolution, firewall changes, connection additions, moves and changes, monitoring.

Core Responsibilities:

  • Provide assistance in all aspects of network management from network design through implementation, maintenance, and upgrading of existing systems
  • Implement a communication system requirement to support distributed functionality of a software engineering environment
  • Analyze network characteristics
  • Troubleshoots network and system problems

Basic Qualifications for Senior Principal Network Engineer:

  • Active SCI clearance with adjudicated poly or TS clearance with ability to obtain SCI access and adjudicated poly prior to start date
  • Bachelor’s degree with 9 years of related experience, or a master’s degree with 7 years of related experience; an additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a degree
  • Ability to obtain a Cyber Security certification

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current Cyber Security Certification
  • Information Assurance experience related to Certification and Accreditation
  • System Admin knowledge for Linux or Windows is beneficial
  • Experience in developing networks from design through implementation
  • Experience with CISCO IOS or NX-OS
  • Experience with network test gear to resolve technical issues at all layers of OSI model
  • Experience configuring and maintaining Multicast networking
  • Experience configuring routers for EIGRP, OSPF, IP Multicast Protocols
  • Experience configuring firewalls
