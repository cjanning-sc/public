ACME Engineering Mission Systems is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security.  Our organization is chartered with providing the skills and innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter.

We are looking for you to join our team as an Electrical Engineer based out of Rockford, IL. As an Electrical Engineer at ACME Engineering, you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation. Through the use of Model-based Engineering, DevSecOps and Agile practices we continue to evolve how we deliver critical national defense products and capabilities for the warfighter. Our success is grounded in our ability to embrace change, move quickly and continuously drive innovation. The successful candidate will be collaborative, open, transparent, and team-oriented with a focus on team empowerment & shared responsibility, flexibility, continuous learning, and a culture of automation.

What You'll Get to Do:

We are seeking a motivated Electrical Engineer to join a dedicated, engaged, and collaborative hardware engineering team supporting various undersea vehicles and systems. The Electrical Engineer will support a broad range of design activities including defining requirements, completing trade studies, developing engineering documentation and test plans, and performing hardware test and system integration. We are specifically looking for candidates that have experience with fiber-optic cabling-based system design.

The following knowledge/experience is required:

  • Knowledge of relevant fiber-optic cabling design and communication standards
  • Experience with fiber-optic cabling, connector, and penetration selection
  • Experience with design of traditional copper cables and connector selection
  • Well-versed in testing and/or troubleshooting of fiber optic systems
  • Experience with optical instrumentation, such as optical spectrum analyzers, laser sources and power meters
  • Understanding of fiber optic transmission, communication theory, advanced optical signal coding and modulation formats, and network architecture

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Cabling and connector design including fiber-optic communication system design
  • Generating formal engineering documentation (drawings, procedures, etc.)
  • Hands-on testing and troubleshooting
  • Able to apply extensive technical expertise, yet have knowledge of other related disciplines
  • Able to communicate effectively and clearly present technical approaches and findings

**This position can be filled at the Principal or Sr. Principal level.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Electrical Engineer:

  • B.S. in Electrical Engineering or a related field with 5 years of experience, a Master's degree with 3 years of experience, or a PhD in a related field.
  • Experience in fiber-optic cabling design
  • Experience with fiber-optic cabling, connector, and penetration selection
  • Experience testing and/or troubleshooting of fiber optic systems
  • Knowledge of Zuken E3
  • Eligibility to obtain a Secret clearance

Basic Qualifications for Sr. Principal Electrical Engineer:

  • B.S. in Electrical Engineering or a related field with 9 years of experience, a Master's degree with 7 years of experience, or 4 years with a PhD in a related field.
  • Experience in fiber-optic cabling design
  • Experience with fiber-optic cabling, connector, and penetration selection
  • Experience testing and/or troubleshooting of fiber optic systems
  • Knowledge of Zuken E3
  • Eligibility to obtain a Secret security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Technical writing skills, to include procedures and technical manuals
  • Strong familiarity with underwater systems, including cabling and connector interfaces
  • Professional background on systems deployed on Naval platforms
  • Knowledge of LabView and MATLAB
  • Active or Current DoD Secret clearance
