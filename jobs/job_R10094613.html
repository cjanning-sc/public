Supply Chain Planning Specialist
R10094613
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking a Supply Chain Planning Specialist to join our Category Management Unit. This position will be based in Rolling Meadows, IL or can be 100% remote.

Defines and develops material strategy. Responsible for the assessment of supply resources; aggregates and prioritizes demand requirements; plans inventory, distribution requirements, production, material and rough-cut capacity for all products. Participates manufacturing ramp-up, end-of-life management and product-line management. Analyzes and aggregates supply / demand requirements. Analyzes purchase material supply/demand position using Material Requirements Planning (MRP) tools. Plans inventory levels based on dependent and independent Bill-of-Materials (BOM) requirements. Assesses rough-cut capacity for current and short-term manufacturing requirements. Creates/reduces supply requirements based on inventory adjustments. Interfaces with Source process area regarding inventory levels, working capital and other areas as needed. Receives/reviews requirements for non-standard parts. Analyzes lead times and identifies critical path procurements. Frequent use and general knowledge of industry practices, techniques, and standards. Develops solutions to a variety of problems of moderate scope and complexity. Demonstrated leadership skills with projects, planning and scheduling.

Basic Qualifications for Supply Chain Planning Specialist (SCPS) 2:

  • Bachelor’s degree and 3+ years of supply chain, business management, finance experience and/or program management or related, a Master's Degree and 1+ years of supply chain, business management, finance experience (e.g. cost account management, supplier financial reporting/forecasting and supplier/program/product scheduling).
  • Proficient in the use of MS Office Suite with expertise in MS Excel
  • Must be eligible to obtain and maintain government secret clearance
  • Demonstrate success in managing complex problems and driving solutions related to program execution

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Manufacturing Operations Experience
  • 6 Sigma Knowledge
