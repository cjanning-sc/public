The IBCS Synchronization Cell Lead directs the efforts of the Synchronization Cell and maintains overall responsible for all efforts toward IBCS program synchronization. As a result, this role will perform the following tasks and functions:

  • Develop deep and enduring Customer relationships by working closely with them and understanding their needs (both internal and external)
  • Develop strong relationships with the team by working closely with them, understanding their roadblocks, and resolving issues
  • Review and update the synchronization cell organization and processes to align with overarching program and customer goals and schedules
  • Identify organization and process weaknesses to drive efficiencies and/or quality and the establishment of program priorities and prepare/present status to IBCS and customer leadership
  • Oversees subcontractor performance, provides monthly subcontractor performance evaluations, and develops corrective actions as necessary.
  • Supports various program level boards/meetings including the IBCS control boards
  • Supports daily, weekly, and monthly internal meetings and reviews to assess organization progress against program goals and milestones
  • Serves as the primary interface to external coordination activities with the customer as well as internal coordination activities among the IBCS team
  • Supports weekly Integrated Master Schedule (IMS) reviews
  • Continual focus on decision making based on engineering efficiency for increased performance
  • Experience in leadership skills to plan, organize, integrate and drive program activities across multiple engineering disciplines, along with the ability to effectively motivate people to work as an integrated team across locations, companies and cultures.
  • Management of configuration items, documentation, policy/process, training, change management and policy enforcement. 
  • Focal point for all configuration control activities related to hardware and software configuration items and documentation which requires collaboration with customers, program management, manufacturing, quality, supply chain, suppliers and other partners.
  • Development, implementation, and maintenance of hardware, software and system configuration management processes and procedures in development, production and sustainment environments

Additionally, this task includes the synchronization management effort to provide EVM support for the synchronization cell in association with the scope of the IBCS LRIP effort. Specific activities include:

  • Owns responsibility for all Earned Value (EV) and Quantifiable Backup Data (QBD) associated with the synchronization cell organization
  • Supports various program level EVM boards/meetings
  • Supports daily, weekly, and monthly internal meetings and reviews to assess organization progress against program goals and milestones
  • Supports weekly IMS and QBD reviews
  • Participates in the following:
    • Monthly Estimates to Complete (ETC) and Variance Analysis Report reviews
    • Monthly and Quarterly PMRs
    • Weekly PDBs
    • Bi-Monthly Risk and Opportunity Boards

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree with 9 years of relevant engineering experience, OR Master's Degree with 7 years of relevant engineering experience, OR PhD in with 4 years of relevant engineering experience.
  • Knowledge of Subsystem processes with demonstrated technical ownership experience in requirements analysis, architecture development, integration, test, or verification.
  • Familiarity with IBCS design and verification processes including hardware and software development, preliminary and detailed design, MRP, Planning, Manufacturing, Procurement and Maintenance.
  • Active DoD Secret clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:    

  • Master's degree in a relevant technical or management specialty
  • Excellent communication skills and experience producing executive level presentations
  • Prior management experience
  • Prior experience in a lead role in a technical engineering capacity
  • Knowledge of DoD acquisition process
  • Proven demonstrated leadership skills and the ability to work effectively in a team environment.
  • High proficiency in technical problem solving.
