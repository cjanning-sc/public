ACME Engineering Corporation is offering a challenging and rewarding career as a Principal/Sr. Principal Mechanical Engineer within the Mission Systems Sector located at the Milwaukee - Wisconsin facility.

This position will support the development, build, and test validation of ruggedized electronic equipment for military applications. The candidate will work within the Mechanical & Optical Technology organization. It is a high performing organization that values its employees and has a strong sense of purpose tied to the vision of ACME Engineering: To be the most trusted provider of systems and technologies that ensure the security and the freedom of our nation and its allies. The candidate will be able to grow personally and professionally in an ethical and inclusive environment, while being an important part of a great team. The engineer will be responsible for solving challenging technical problems with innovative solutions that meet performance, cost, and schedule requirements. The engineer must have demonstrated expertise in at least one of the following specific technical skills: advanced thermal analysis, advanced structural analysis, microelectronics design, aircraft modification (Group A) design, or opto-mechanical design. The expertise will be applied at any phase of the product life cycle, including requirements analysis, concept design, detailed design, manufacture, and environmental test.  The engineer will serve on engineering teams, and interface heavily with related disciplines and customers/stakeholders.  Strong communication skills are required for success in all phases of the role.

The engineer will also contribute to overall knowledge base and skill level of the organization via coaching, knowledge transfer, and process improvement activities.

This position can be filled at the Principal or Sr. Principal Level:

Basic Qualifications for Principal Mechanical Engineer:

- Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical or Aerospace Engineering with a minimum of 5 years of experience in electronics packaging or a Master’s degree with 3 years of experience in Electronics Packaging.

- Extensive experience with hardware development and electro-mechanical packaging, including at least one of the following subjects: thermal analysis, structural analysis, microelectronics design, opto-mechanical design, environmental testing

- Proficiency with 3D CAD Tools (Siemens NX is preferred) and product data management tools

- Demonstrated leadership of small to moderately sized teams during the product development process including the review of technical work products

- Demonstrated application of innovative solutions to complex problems that arise within the design, development, and production phases

- Strong written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills including experience presenting at design reviews and customer/stakeholder briefings

- Displayed ability to exercise latitude in determining most appropriate technical approach of assignments

- U. S. citizen with the ability to obtain and maintain a U.S. Government Secret security clearance 

Basic Qualifications for Sr. Principal Mechanical Engineer:

- Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical or Aerospace Engineering with a minimum of 9 years of experience in electronics packaging or MS degree in Mechanical or Aerospace Engineering with a minimum of 7 years of Electronics Packaging.

- Demonstrated expertise in at least one of the following subjects: advanced thermal analysis, advanced structural analysis, microelectronics design, group A design, or opto-mechanical design

- Expertise in mechanical design and/or analysis tools

- Demonstrated application of innovative solutions to complex problems

- Experience reviewing technical work products of peers

- Experience presenting at design reviews and customer/stakeholder briefings

- Strong written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills

- U. S. citizen and ability to obtain and maintain a U.S. Government security clearance 

Preferred Qualifications:

- Master of Science degree in Mechanical or Aerospace Engineering

- Expertise in NX and/or ANSYS

- Familiarity with opto-mechanical engineering

- Leadership in process improvement or knowledge transfer (i.e. publication) activities

- Experience contributing to development of junior engineers
