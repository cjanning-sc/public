The position will oversee B-2 Form Fit Function (F-Cube) Projects supporting NG Aeronautics Sector (NGAS) or NG Defense Services (NGDS) Statement of work (SOW) and will report directly to the Manager, B-2 Product Support (PS) Program Management, Integration, and Analysis.  Position responsibilities will include managing ongoing technical projects and budgets, managing project schedules, developing status reports, and ensuring IPT leadership is efficiently executing enterprise processes.  Additional responsibilities include identifying deficiencies and implementing updates to the enterprise processes and assessing enterprise project risk, issues, and opportunities to develop resolutions to meet cost, schedule and quality goals and objectives. This position will be located in Oklahoma City, OK.

The selected candidate should thrive independently in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, and collaborative/team settings across all levels.

  • Candidate will work closely with upper management and project team members to establish project requirements, schedules, milestones, and budgets.
  • Candidate’s primary skills include planning, organizing, and communicating project requirements, priorities and objectives then motivating your team to execute.
  • A Project Manager must possess both hard and soft skills in leadership, interpersonal skills, decisiveness, problem-solving, delegation and time management skills.

Primary Responsibilities:

Job duties include, but are not limited to:

  • Review and align tasks to Program Statement of Work to meet program cost, schedule, and technical performance.
  • Obtain and understand status from functional teams to measure and report program performance and adhere to master project plans and schedules.
  • Identify, distribute, track, and complete project requirements.
  • Adhere to all internal processes, policies, and applicable industry standards.
  • Track Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).
  • Conduct thorough risk & opportunity management practices including identification, mitigation, and realization.
  • Ensure program team understands and adheres to contract scope, and manages change through control board activities
  • Develop and adhere to budget baselines utilizing Earned Value Management (EVM).
