Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking to hire a Test Engineering Manager 2 to its team of diverse and qualified individuals. These positions will be located in either Roy, UT or Colorado Springs, CO and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Learn more about the GBSD program here.  Northrop Grumman supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What you will get to do:

  • Develops and execute the integrated test strategy of the program through development of the Integrated Test Plan CDRL A328
  • Synthesize test requirements into verification requirements, test objectives, success criteria, and required resources for system level tests and demonstrations. 
  • Orchestrate and coordinate the development of test requirements and detailed integration test plans, test information sheets (TIS), test procedures, test readiness reviews (TRRs), test data and test reports.
  • Execution of qualification and acceptance testing per system level requirements, plans and procedures.
  • Develop test enabling product requirements (i.e. MGSE, EGSE, test instrumentation, test facilities).
  • Creation of test ConOps flows that maximizes the utility of test resources while meeting schedule and technical requirements and/or constraints
  • Effectively communicate and present to project and program management, technical stakeholders, peers, and customers.
  • Participate in internal Engineering Review Boards (ERB), internal and customer test planning working groups.
  • Support program reviews and customer delivery (CDRL) development
  • Lead test planning campaigns through major program milestones and/or mission threads
