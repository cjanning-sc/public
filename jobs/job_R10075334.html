Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is an industry-leading provider for prime satellite and payload capabilities and directed energy and electronics solutions for national security, military, and civil customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible.

ACME Engineering Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) has an opening for a Capture Manager 3 in the OPIR & Geospatial Systems Business Unit (OGS BU) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Columbus, OH.

The successful candidate will be a self-starter with experience in captures/proposals that will lead an overall capture effort, including investment strategy, supplier and contract partnerships, technical development, and customer relationships and alignment of priorities. Must be adept at quickly understanding what is required to win, developing compelling value propositions, and preparing strong business cases to leadership. Must have proven strong team leadership skills and writing abilities.  Candidate must be comfortable with briefing at the executive level and establishing NDAs, working with GSC, teaming, and sourcing.

Primary job responsibilities include:

  • Understanding of and engagements with the OPIR marketplace and stakeholder customer organizations and budgets in order to identify market drivers and trends and develop strategies.

  • Developing capture strategy and tactics to produce capture products such as competitive assessments, teaming strategies, and prices-to-win analysis results applied to improve competitive posture.

  • Developing clear and concise Win Themes and Value Propositions for the capture.

  • Maintain relationships with internal stakeholders and functional organizations involved in program pursuit.

  • Developing relationships with teaming partners and industry associations to help drive partnerships.

  • Coordinating and collaborating with line of business management, strategy, business and finance, contracts and global supply chain, technology and engineering offices and functions.

  • Development and management of investment capture project funds, schedule, and execution.

  • Proposal development including managing process, writing proposal responses and developing pricing to deliver compelling and compliant proposals.

Basic Qualifications:  

  • Bachelor's degree in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) with 7+ years of experience in Aerospace and Defense

  • Current DoD Secret Clearance

  • Prior management experience leading a team


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced Degree in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) or Business discipline

  • Excellent interpersonal skills

  • Experience interfacing with senior management, peers, and employees

  • Aptitude to develop and execute to a strategy

  • Self-directed leader with strong verbal and written communication skills

  • Knowledge and experience within the government Space-based Overhead Persistent Infrared (OPIR) missile warning and missile tracking

  • Knowledge of the DoD acquisition processes and Customer organizations (SSC, SDA, MDA, IC)

  • Active Top Secret / SCI clearance
