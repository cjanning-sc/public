eQube Integration and Business Intelligence Application Administrator 2
R10076807
1st Shift (United States of America)

The eQube Applications Administrator researches, designs, develops, and/or modifies enterprise-wide systems and/or applications software. Involved in planning of system and development deployment as well as responsible for meeting compliance standards. Evaluates interfaces between hardware and software, operational requirements, and characteristics of overall system. Documents standup, testing and maintenance of the systems assigned.

Primary accountabilities are associated with Application Administration of eQube reporting (BI) and integration (MI) environment’s, guidance, and support for Northrop Grumman business units. Performs deployments plus support for all eQube solutions for enterprise programs and projects. Ensures proactive focus on details of complex deployments to ensure highly successful implementations. Able to effectively deal with challenges from the base technical perspective of high complexity. Engage with all eQube planning activities, as required.


Responsibilities include:

  • Manage the configuration, installation, maintenance, and support of the eQube application tools, Integrations, and Reports/Portals for development and production environments. 
  • Maintain the solution architecture and configuration activities with systems like DOORS, SAP, Teamcenter, Cameo and Jira.
  • Plan, test, and deploy system updates and upgrades collaborating with various business functions to coordinate outages as necessary.
  • Collaborate with Database Administrators in the ongoing maintenance and structure of eQube data
  • Support, test, and maintain disaster recovery plans as required
  • Perform root cause analysis debugging problems with the eQube environment and then work with the appropriate technical teams to develop, test, and implement fixes to prevent recurrence.
  • Provide End-user support for eQube BI/MI solutions.
  • Root-cause analysis, debugging problems with the eQube BI/MI solutions and working with the appropriate technical teams to develop, test, and implement fixes.
  • Document all aspects of system support such that knowledge is readily shared among all those tasked with supporting eQube BI/MI instances.
  • Support System Integration Testing and User Acceptance Testing activities with business partners and end users, as required.
  • Support the mentoring, training, and/or demos to end-users, as required.
  • Support business readiness and change management activities through go-live and production implementations.
  • Develop and maintain technical, installation guidelines, and operational guideline documents.
  • Primary location Redondo Beach CA., Roy UT., and Melbourne, FL.

  • General understanding of Software Lifecycle Process.
