Are you a systems or software or hardware engineer interested in designing solutions for space missions? Did you know that the ACME Engineering Space Systems site located in Chicago/Springfield MD has over 50 years of experience defining what is possible in space? 

Want to find out what all the buzz is about for this site, the work we do here, and the roles we are looking to staff? Look no further as we have a great information session to address just that! ACME Engineering will be hosting an invite only virtual hiring/information session on Friday March 24th from 12 to 1 PM EST. Attending this event, you will get the opportunity to learn about our space payload and ground systems team, current and future programs, and what it’s like to work at our Springfield/Chicago site.

This event will be targeting engineers that have a background in systems or software or hardware.

We are hiring for various roles/levels across our engineering teams as outlined below. We will have manager and director representation from our software and systems and hardware engineering teams to talk about their department needs.

Positions/Skillsets we are actively hiring for:

Systems Engineers (Active Top Secret Clearance OR ability to obtain TS Clearance with relevant Space Systems Experience Required)

The position will be responsible for supporting and leading systems engineering tasks for space subsystems, payloads and/or vehicles. These may include, but are not limited to:

  • Requirements analysis, derivation, and management
  • Mission Engineering, modeling, and analysis
  • Architecture and CONOPs development
  • Systems modeling and simulation
  • Functional analysis
  • Timeline analysis
  • Trade studies
  • Technical planning
  • Cost and risk analysis
  • Interface definition
  • System design
  • System integration and test
  • Verification and Validation planning and execution
  • Supportability and effectiveness analyses for total systems.
  • Some tools you could expect to use are: Cameo, STK, DOORS, MATLAB, Python, Modelcenter

Software Engineers (Can start without a clearance, but must be eligible for a security clearance)

The position will be responsible for supporting and leading software engineering tasks for space subsystems, payloads and/or vehicles. These may include, but are not limited to:

  • Embedded Software
  • Flight Test Software
  • Integration & Test Software
  • Experience with one or more of the following languages: Java, C, C++, Python, TCL.
  • Design and development of software in a multi-processor environment for real-time embedded Linux
  • Experience in an Agile Scrum Environment
  • Experience with the Atlassian tool suite (Jira, Confluence, Bitbucket)

RF and Design Engineers (Can start without a clearance, but must be eligible for a security clearance)

This position will be responsible for supporting and leading hardware/RF/Design engineering tasks for space subsystems, payload and or/vehicles. This may include, but are not limited to:

  • RF/Microwave & Mixed Signal Design Engineering - RF/Microwave Design, Mixed Signal Custom IC Design, CMOS SoC Design, Test
  • Digital Design Engineering - ASIC/FPGA and Digital Circuit Design, Simulation/Verification, DSP Architecture/Implementation, Systems Level Validation
  • Antenna Design Engineering - Antenna Systems Mechanical and Electrical Design and Analysis, Antenna Range Integration and Test
  • Power Conversion and Control Design Engineering - High Speed, High Frequency Power and Analog Systems Design, Modeling and Simulation
  • Experience translating customer requirements and/or functional block diagrams into realized FPGA/Digital Logic Designs or Circuit Card Assemblies
  • Strong experience with Matlab and Simulink modeling and programming.
  • Subsystem requirements (hardware and software) derivation, flow-down, and configuration

Electrical Engineers (Must need a secret clearance to start)

  • Panel & cable design, electrical interconnects & schematics, developing acceptance criteria & certification procedures, and hardware checkout/troubleshooting.
  • Root cause determination, problem resolution and documentation is expected.
  • Interface with subsystem test engineers, test operators, technicians, systems administrators, security team, and vendors to provide support for various test set tasks as required.
  • Full design cycle of EGSE, from conception through design to fabrication and integration/test.

FPGA Engineers (Must be able to obtain/maintain a TS Clearance)

  • Develop digital logic designs for space applications such as satellite command and control and sensor data processing
  • Design, development, creation and modification of digital logic designs implemented with Xilinx and Microsemi FPGA IDEs
  • Simulation and verification of FPGA logic designs, compliance requirements, and supporting validation at the system level
  • Work with members of multi‐functional teams to develop innovative solutions
  • Documentation of design and analysis reports as well as test reports
  • Working knowledge of FPGAs and HDL (Verilog and VHDL) code
