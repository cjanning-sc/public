ACME Engineering is looking for a Material Program Manager 2 or 3 that is responsible and accountable for the execution of material schedule and budget for Advanced Weapons Business Unit.

The Material Program Manager (MPM) is a unique position that requires strategic thinking as well as tactical execution. A successful MPM can work with minimal supervision to solve complex problems to ensure both short term and long term success of their respective program. An MPM would need to possess strong hard skills to execute plans to completion, as well as soft skills to appropriately work with multiple disciplines, and communicate challenges to multiple stakeholders. It is a rare position to stretch your skillsets and test your abilities; making real impact on the organization.

Responsibilities include:

  • Ability to support financial requirements including EAC’s, material sales forecasting, and accruals
  • Ability to confidently present and represent ACME Engineering Global Supply Chain in Program Management Reviews, leadership briefings, and customer briefings
  • Experience working with electronic components, CCA’s, and complex electronic assemblies
  • Ability to creatively solve complex supply chain problems with data driven strategies
  • Demonstrated aptitude in identified Risks and Opportunities to the program
  • Consistently work on supply and demand to proactively identify gaps and work to eliminate them
  • Escalate critical material shortages immediately upon identification and own the efforts to resolve them
  • Implement solutions to overcome current & future Material Shortages Coordinate and optimize Delivery schedules with critical Suppliers
  • Challenge business impediments to excellence and continuous improvement
