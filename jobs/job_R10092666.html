ACME Engineering Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems have an exciting career opportunity for a (GCN) GMD Communications Network Technician 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals located in Fort Greely, Alaska

Position Overview:

Supports the operation of the GMD Communications Network (GCN) through effective monitoring and management of Local Area Network (LAN) and Wide Area Network (WAN) devices.

Responsibilities include:

  • Using SolarWinds and other network management tools to monitor hardware devices, virtual machines, and network connections.
  • Configuring router and switch interfaces in accordance with written documentation
  • Detecting and reporting faults
  • Assisting Field Engineers with troubleshooting activities
  • Participating in asset and system transitions
  • Maintaining archives of system logs
  • Accurately recording events in shift logs and trouble tickets
  • Strictly adhering to all applicable processes, approved procedures, and management directives
  • Effectively responding to work schedule changes as needed to support mission requirements.

Basic Requirements:

  • High School diploma or equivalent and 2 years additional education and/or related experience
  • Holds an IAT Level II (CompTIA Security+) certification or higher IAW DoD 8570 requirements.
  • Possesses (at a minimum) a DoD Secret clearance with eligibility to obtain a DoD Top Secret clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:  

  • Basic understanding of networking fundamentals and Cisco IOS commands
  • Basic understanding of Linux/UNIX commands
  • Experience using Microsoft Windows, SolarWinds Orion Platform, VMWare ESXi and/or Active Directory
  • Knowledge of Information Security best practices such as centralized authentication and multi-factor authentication
  • STEM Degree
