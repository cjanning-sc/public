Functions as the AH-6i Mechanic under the direction of the AH-6i Mechanic Lead. The AH-6i Mechanic will perform aircraft maintenance, repairs, modifications, and inspections in accordance with approved publications and directives. The AH-6i Mechanic will also provide technical direction and training to mentor Saudi Arabia National Guard (SANG) maintenance personnel when needed. Ensures that all related maintenance operations are conducted IAW safety regulations and directives.

This expat position will include other compensatory fringes such as Cost of Living Allowances, Housing Allowances and other allowances for employment in Saudi Arabia

Basic Qualifications:

  • High school diploma or equivalent required
  • 4 years' experience on Saudi Arabia military rotary wing aircraft
  • Formal Saudi Arabia (SA) military rotary wing aircraft repairer training (MOS producing school, for example, 15R, 15T, 15S)
  • Must be proficient in written and spoken English
  • Must be able to communicate effectively with host nation personnel and company management
  • Must possess a valid driver's license.

  • Must be a US Citizen

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced degree or equivalent. 
  • Possess an A&P or equivalent with SA military rotary wing aircraft repairer training and AH-6i aviation maintenance course
  • Six years’ experience on SA military rotary wing aircraft. Experience should be within the eight most recent years. Minimum MDS experience may be waived if you have completed the AH-6i On-the-Job (OJT) program.
