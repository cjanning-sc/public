Software Engineer/Principal Software Engineer (Scrum Master)
R10094881
1st Shift (United States of America)

Work at the Northrop Grumman Corporation Rolling Meadows, IL facility as a Software and Digital Technologies (SWDT) as a scrum master. 

Utilize existing agile processes, tools (Jira) and infrastructure to ensure engineering success driven programs.

Lead product scrum teams using best practices across multi-functional scrum teams to support customer deliverable hardware and embedded systems.

Responsible for developing, maintaining, and continuously improving standard agile methodologies, policies, and procedures for an Agile, iterative solution life cycle, from an executable architecture, concept development, iterative design, production, delivery, deployment, and maintenance.

Responsibilities for this position will include:

•            Serving multiple Scrum teams as Scrum Master

•            Facilitating Agile ceremonies (PI / Sprint Planning, Review, and Retrospectives; Daily Stand ups, Product Backlog Grooming, Scrum of Scrums, Product Owner Sync).

•            Analyzing and improving on relevant metrics for your teams and programs.

•            Raise the level of your teams’ Agile knowledge and discipline above current levels.

•            Help other roles such as the Product Owner understand and optimize their roles.

•            Help tailor processes as needed to apply software-only based principles into an environment of large engineering team of systems / software / hardware

•            Lead efforts of developing Agile engineering and management best practices, metrics, and audit

•            Maintain scrum management tool Jira database, create, and brief agile metrics.

•            Lead the efforts of developing Agile engineering and management best practices, metrics, and audit practices.

This position can be filled at either Software or Principal level:

Basic Qualifications for Software Engineer (Scrum Master):

- Bachelor's degree in STEM field and 2+ years of experience or MS degree in STEM field.

- Must have Agile experience leading teams

- Must be a Certified Scrum Practitioner; or Certified Scrum Master/Professional Scrum Master 1 with PMI-ACP certification, or similar.

- US Citizen and must have interim secret clearance prior to start.  Once started, must have the ability to obtain and maintain a Secret security clearance.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Software Engineer (Scrum Master):

- Bachelor's degree in STEM field and 5+ years of experience or MS degree in STEM field with 3+ years of experience.

- Must have Agile experience leading teams

- Must be a Certified Scrum Practitioner; or Certified Scrum Master/Professional Scrum Master 1 with PMI-ACP certification, or similar.

- US Citizen and must have interim secret clearance prior to start.  Once started, must have the ability to obtain and maintain a Secret security clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Security Clearance (Secret, Top Secret)
- Demonstrated ability to earn and maintain the respect of strong technical and intelligence mission-oriented staff
- Advanced (MS and/or PhD) technical degree desired
- Effective communicator, capable of effectively translating and presenting complex concepts to both technical and non-technical management and customers, through presentations and written media
- Mastery of systems engineering discipline, to include proven practical experience in the following, preferably for large programs
- Requirements interpretation, definition, and allocation
- Risk identification and mitigation
- Ability to teach, mentor and coach highly skilled technical staff
- Experience with Earned Value Management Systems (EVMS) and their application to agile programs
- Experience with transitioning teams from waterfall to agile methods and practices
- Certification in one or more of these designations is desired:
- Certified Agile Coach
- Certified facilitator
- Certified Product Owner (CPO) ,
- Project Management Professional (PMP)®
