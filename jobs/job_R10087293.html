ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems is seeking a Sr. Manager Supply Chain - Level 3 to join our team in Cincinnati, OH. Be a part of a team of qualified, dedicated, and diverse professionals designing, developing, building, and supporting some of the world's most advanced products. This position will have limited opportunity to work remote and primarily works from our Cincinnati, OH sites. Operating on our 9/80 schedule, this position will have the opportunity to have every other Friday off!

This position is dynamic and challenging, requiring ingenuity, collaboration, and an insatiable drive to make the impossible possible. We’ll support you with training, employee resource groups, and robust benefits as we work together to defend and define the future.

This position reports to the Cincinnati GSC Goods Movement & Logistics Director for the Aeronautics Systems Sector. The Manager will serve as the Goods Movement Cincinnati Site focal point for Warehousing and Distribution, Shipping and Receiving, and will be the Site manager for Site 8 Warehouse. Candidate will be the focal point for Program Managers, IPTs and Production Operations leadership within the Cincinnati Site, OH AS group. The candidate will be responsible for developing and executing GM strategies to support all of the appropriate programs, with focus on achieving affordability, operational efficiency, safety and first-time quality targets. Candidate responsible for activities and knowledge within multiple disciplines across the Supply Chain organization. Exhibits end-to-end supply chain competency.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Defines and develops warehouse and inventory strategies to meet Site/Program timelines and requirements.
  • Responsible for developing, implementing, and influencing the future course of storage and inventory management for AS Sites.
  • Leads long-term capacity analysis and resource planning to ensure proper scheduling of new product line phase-ins as well as existing product line phase-outs.
  • Serves as consultant to management and external spokesperson for the organization on policies, plans, and objectives.
  • Acts as an advisor to senior management and customers by defining and developing storage and material handling strategy for implementation cross-functionally.
  • Develop solutions to complex problems which require the regular use of ingenuity and innovation. Ensures solutions are consistent with organization objectives.
  • Contributes to the development of new concepts, techniques, and standards.
  • Able to communicate effectively and clearly present approaches and findings to senior leadership.
  • Supports proposal plans and writing assignments.
  • Implements and integrates scheduling systems and processes.
  • Leads Affordability Programs for all responsible sites
  • Operate company-owned vehicles to execute pick-up and delivery of parts between ACME Engineering sites/buildings, and offsite locations at times. 
  • Any Ad Hoc projects assigned as required

Basic Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree with 5 years’ experience OR a Bachelor’s degree with 7 years of experience; 11 or more years of experience may be substituted for degree requirements
  • 3 or more years' experience leading a large team to effectively execute a broad statement of work
  • Experience with manufacturing and supply chain operations with demonstrated and proven leadership skills and ability to collaborate effectively at all levels
  • MS Office (PowerPoint, Excel and Word) & knowledge of SAP
  • Familiarity with MMAS/DCMA/ISO 9001 requirements
  • This position requires a current DOD Secret clearance at a minimum to begin/start

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Ability to prepare and present executive-level presentations
  • 4 or more years' proven track record building a robust team; provide people leadership, with a focus on acquiring, developing and retaining talent; respectful of others & understand and embrace the value of an inclusive team; empower each team member to perform at the highest level of productivity; be empathetic and listen for understanding
  • Proven ability to set a vision; assess external and internal factors to determine long-term business direction; create strategies that shape the future and inspire the team, resulting in sustainable value and performance; critical thinking
  • Proven ability to lead with courage; make bold moves, willingness to speak up and to take on new challenges; take calculated risks, with appropriate mitigation plans; self-aware, transparent and authentic; seek feedback from others
  • Proven ability to act with speed; understand the need for urgency to meet the needs of the business and stakeholders; decisive, drive timely decision-making; empower others with clear accountability and appropriate support
  • Experience as an accomplished problem-solver and change-agent whose unique mix of technical and business expertise lends itself to developing innovative Supply Chain strategies
