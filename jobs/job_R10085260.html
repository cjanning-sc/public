Global Supply Chain Business Management (GSCBM) Manager 2 has supervisory or management responsibilities for Global Supply Chain (GSC) labor and material estimating, price/cost analysis reporting, and other supplier financial support functions. This position allows for strategic alignment of resources and staffing in support of Global Supply Chain and Business Management organizations, most notably Pricing and Estimating. Support Price/Cost Analysis of Subcontractor Proposals and ensure compliance to sector and FAR policies and procedures. Develop and support GSC Cost Estimates and obtain appropriate GSC approvals. Support proposal reviews and approvals. Support the NAR ICE process including the development of floor positions for negotiation approval.  Develop and submit adequate GSC Cost Volumes. Support Customer proposal reviews, Customer Fact-findings and requests for information responses, DCAA audits, Customer negotiations, and sweeps. Provide guidance and support relative to GSC and Business Management Processes and procedures (Process Architecture). Ensure compliance with these procedures and to Federal Acquisition Regulations and cost accounting standards (FAR, DFARS & CAS )
