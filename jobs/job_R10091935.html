Our Defense Systems Sector is seeking a Material Analyst to join our growing team in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma.

  • Perform activities in a variety of cross-functional areas including materials planning, total quality management, procurement, supplier quality assurance, finance, materials systems, customer service, etc.

  • The incumbent may facilitate self-directed work teams, provide training in core skill areas to teams and peers, and interact with internal customers and suppliers with respect to product planning, problem resolution, and process improvement

  • Incumbent must be experienced in one or more areas of the material function

  • Control the efficient flow of goods, services, and information between points of origin through customer placement in order to meet customer requirements

  • Compile and maintain material and parts inventory and status information to expedite movement of material and parts between production areas, according to predetermined production schedules and order priorities

  • Assist with Equipment Control, Disposition, and Calibration

  • Perform physical count, cycle count and periodic material inventory verification

  • Facilitate return of merchandise authorizations from focalized users to Global Supply Chain POC's

  • Record and maintain inventory of quantity and type of materials and parts received, stocked, and distributed, manually or using computer

  • Expedite or delegate critical material (Requisition creation, Po-issuance, Delivery and Receipts internal material movement)

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor degree and 4 years of experience in Procurement and/or Property Management

  • Experience working with Hardware Databases and Tools

  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite

  • Proficient in SAP

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s Degree and 1 Year of experience in Procurement and/or Property Management

  • Working Knowledge of FARS/DFARS

  • Experience working with DCMA

  • Experience with AssetSmart

  • Active Security Clearance
