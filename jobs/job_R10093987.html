What You’ll Get To Do: 

The Manufacturing Plan is used by the Operations organizations personnel as the tool for developing all elements of the Produce Product Package. The elements of the Manufacturing Plan will enable the accuracy and completeness of the execution and risk mitigation phases of the program. The Manufacturing Plan will also serve as an instrument to ensure that ‘best manufacturing processes” are utilized. These Manufacturing Plans will facilitate our ability to achieve manufacturing process commonality and synergy throughout the Division. Manufacturing Plans are an integral part of the Program Concept of Operations and will reflect all program execution elements necessary for the Operations Department to execute the program.
