Principal Electronics Engineer/Sr. Principal Electronics Engineer
R10080010
1st Shift

The Principal Electronics Engineer/Sr. Principal Electronics Engineer position requires a mix of Test Fixture and Software design skills. Experience with DC, Analog, Digital & RF test & measurement equipment; and a hands-on approach to troubleshooting complex electronic systems. Typical Roles and Responsibilities include:

  • Understanding design to cost, scope, and schedule

  • Requirements definition

  • Design of electromechanical test fixture & software test solutions

  • Manufacturing support during the hardware build process

  • Integration of test fixtures & software into a functioning test solution

  • Test equipment & software certification per customer requirements

  • Test system deployment and factory support

  • Software maintenance and/or hardware fixture update initiatives

**This opening can be filled at a level 3 OR level 4

  • Basic Qualifications Principal Electronics Engineer:

    You must satisfy one of the following education/experience criteria to be considered for this position:

    • Bachelor’s degree in STEM with 5 years’ experience, Master's degree in STEM with 3 years’ experience, Ph.D degree in STEM with 0 years’ experience

     

    Plus:

    • The ability to read and interpret specifications, schematics and block diagrams

    • Mix of hardware and software skills

    • Experience in text based programming (e.g. C / C++)

    • Background and working knowledge in RF theory and concepts

    • Effective verbal and written communication

    • Experience with test & measurement equipment

    • Ability to obtain a Secret Clearance

    Basic Qualifications Sr. Principal Electronics Engineer:

    You must satisfy one of the following education/experience criteria to be considered for this position:

    • Bachelor’s degree in STEM with 9 years’ experience, Master's degree in STEM with 7 years’ experience, Ph.D degree in STEM with 4 years’ experience

    Plus:

    • The ability to read and interpret specifications, schematics and block diagrams

    • Mix of test fixture and software design skills

    • C/C++ software programming experience

    • Background and working knowledge of RF theory and concepts

    • Effective verbal and written communication

    • Experience with test & measurement equipment e.g. o'scope, Spectrum Analyzer

    • Ability to obtain a Secret Clearance

     

    Preferred Qualifications:

    • Advanced degree in Engineering or related STEM discipline

    • Test design and integration background

    • Ability to work with minimal direction and guidance

    • Experience with LabWindows CVI, C/C++ programming

    • LabView programming

    • Experience with schematic design tools (e.g. Mentor Graphics, Zuken, etc.)

    • Proficiency with digital, analog, RF test equipment, RF theory and high speed digital design

    • Familiarity with differential digital interfaces like LVDS - PXI/PXIe and VME bus experience

    • Hands-on troubleshooting of electronic hardware

    • Active clearance
