AOA Xinetics (AOX), a wholly-owned ACME Engineering Space Systems, Payload and Ground Systems Division Business Venture, is a leading supplier of complex electro-optic and photonic systems and subsystems that control and manipulate light waves for a variety of applications. We are seeking an Opto-Mechanical Test Engineer for our Special Programs team in East Hartford, CT.

Overview

The Opto-Mechanical Test Engineer is an integral part of our team of engineers and scientists in supporting various tasks related to implementation, use, and setup of electro-optical and photonics based work stations and test systems.   Stations and systems include, but are not limited to optical assemblies, mechanical assemblies, measurement equipment, electronic boards, and various other sub-systems.  The engineer will support technical problem solving, test definition, test result data compilation, equipment selection, and integration.

The position will work in support of research, development, engineering, and business development efforts under general supervision.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree in a technical field, or 4 years equivalent experience in lieu of degree

  • Minimum of two (2) years work related experience with electro-optic instruments and/or opto-mechanical test benches

  • Experience with computer operated test setups and systems

  • Experience with National Instruments hardware products, equipment, and drivers

  • Experience working in multi-discipline teams

  • Hands-on approach to problem solving and troubleshooting

  • Good verbal and written communications skills related to technical approaches, work performed, test results, and findings

  • Valid U.S. Drivers License

  • US Citizenship is a bona find requirement as applicants selected are subject to a government security investigation

  • Active Top Secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Physics, Optical, Mechanical, or Electrical Engineering

  • Experience with National Instruments LabVIEW programming

  • Experience with National Instruments TestStand programming

  • Familiarity with SQL Server, MS-Access, or other database utility

Physical Requirements:

  • Ability to sit or stand for extended periods of time and light physical effort required
