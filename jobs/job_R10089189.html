ACME Engineering Defense Systems sector is seeking a Principal Configuration Analyst (level 3) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals located in Edwards, CA. 

 

The Configuration Analyst will plan and coordinates preparation of project documentation, such as engineering drawings, production specifications and schedules, and contract modifications, to ensure customer contract requirements are met. Reviews contract to determine documentation required for each phase of project, applying knowledge of engineering and manufacturing processes. Analyzes proposed changes of product design to determine effect on overall product and system. Coordinates modification records for management control. Establishes change orders and prepares for change authorization and documentation by company and subcontractor. Reviews and analyzes released engineering change data and coordinates changes with engineering, quality, support, manufacturing, and engineering data control activities. 

 

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor's degree and at least 6 years of configuration analyst experience; Master's degree and 4 years of relevant experience. In lieu of formal degree, High School Diploma or equivalent (GED) and at least 10 years of relevant job experience. 

  • Experience in specifying, collecting, processing and presenting information from potentially several disparate data sets 

  • Experience with technical data and electronic technical manual change processes 

  • Experience in using MS Word, PowerPoint and Excel products.

  • ​Must be willing to work a flexible work schedule that may include extended shifts, holidays, and weekends and support on call status for 24 hours-a-day, 7 days-a-week, 365 days a year.       

  • Must possess an active DoD Secret security clearance with last investigation completed within 6 years and be able to maintain that clearance.

  • Ability to obtain Special Program access clearance within 365 days from submission and able to retain.

​

Preferred Qualifications:  

  • DoD Top Secret Clearance. 

  • Experience supporting aircraft mechanical and avionics systems. 

  • Experience evaluating engineering changes for implementation.
