Staff Mission Architect Engineer - Space Security Missions - Top Secret (Space/Satellite Systems)
R10094412
1st Shift (United States of America)

Successful candidates for this position will work in a technical role in Dulles VA.  This Systems Engineering role is responsible for leading the mission engineering efforts for a space program (Phoenix) in the NSS business area within the Space Systems Sector.

The Mission Engineer will support critical national security mission engineering activities to lead, plan, and manage mission utilization of complex space systems and capabilities.  A successful Mission Engineer must have a proven ability to understand and communicate operational mission requirements to engineering teams.  The Mission Engineer will perform system analysis, design optimization, system performance assessments, and operations analysis work in support of integrated mission analysis for current and potential customers.  The Mission Engineer will track Measures of Performance, Measures of Effectiveness and define other metrics to ensure capabilities meet customer objectives.  Successful candidates will demonstrate an attitude and approach founded in the following key characteristics:

  • Customer Intimacy
  • Mission Objective Driven
  • Simultaneous Focus on Short Term Objectives and Long Term Imperatives
  • Can-Do, Solutions Oriented, High Energy, Problem Solver
  • Information and Team Integrator
  • Decisive, Closure-Driver

The candidate will work with cross-discipline team members and must have excellent verbal and written communication skills and the ability to work with a diverse community of engineers and managers to achieve the goals of the program team.

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Must have U.S. Citizenship with a current or active Top Secret level clearance with ability to obtain SCI clearance
  • This position requires a BS degree in Engineering or related technical field and at least 14 years of applicable experience, or an MS Degree and 12 years of applicable experience
  • 5 years’ experience with Satellite Ground Systems in the US National Security arena
  • Expertise in space vehicle mission planning, command and control, and operations

Preferred Experience (one or more areas desired):

  • U.S. Citizenship with a current or active Top Secret/SCI level clearance
  • 10 years’ experience with Satellite Ground Systems in the US National Security arena
  • CONOPS development for Space Based programs
  • Knowledge of various mission analysis techniques and software tools
  • Expertise with space and ground product lines
