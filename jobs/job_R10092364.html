ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Mechanical Engineer / Principal Mechanical Engineer to join the Infrastructure and Deployment Team. This position is in Detroit, UT and can also be filled in Madison, AL supporting the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Final grade level offered will be based on candidate experience.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will work within an Integrated Product Team (IPT) environment, interfacing with IPT leadership, suppliers, customers, and other technical disciplines to engineer design solutions to challenging problems through the development of Product Definition Packages (PDP). PDPs consists of 2D drawings and 3D models (NX), various design analyses/assessments (structural/thermal), bill of materials and parts lists (ASME format) and requirements (DOORS). Candidate must be able to work independently, function in a dynamic environment, and be flexible to changing direction. Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Create and implement deployable mechanical mechanisms that are verified through test/analysis

  • Develop CAD models visualizing various design options

  • Support the PDP lead in designing mechanical equipment, components, products, and systems.

  • Drive the design and development of the mechanical portion of the System Integration owned the PDPs

  • Implement program processes and engineering board approval processes for developed PDPs

  • Deliver required PDPs on time to meet program objectives

  • Integrate System Integration PDPs with the various element teams to proactively eliminate disconnects and design clashes

  • Identify gaps and holes within the design and/or requirements and provide a path froward for resolution that is cost effective, innovative, and has minimal impact to others while filling the gap/hole

  • Ensure the developed PDPs meet the design requirements from the various element/configured item specifications

  • Determine technical solution in accordance with product specifications and recommend actions to representatives for coordinated product solution

  • Support digital transformation initiatives relating to PDP development and release
