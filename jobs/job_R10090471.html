The Emerging Capabilities Development group of ACME Engineering Mission Systems is seeking a Microelectronics Product Engineer for our Advanced Technology Lab (ATL) - located outside of Springfield, Illinois - where we design, manufacture, and test semiconductor products for internal and commercial production customers as well as emerging technology programs. ACME Engineering’s ATL semiconductor foundry is a unique capability supporting a range of production microelectronic devices (Silicon, Gallium Arsenide, Gallium Nitride, Silicon Carbide, Carbon Nanotubes) and providing leading edge technology development in superconducting electronics. Our devices enable a number of ACME Engineering’s ground-based radars, avionic radars, and space systems. Join us for the chance to work with an amazing, experienced, and talented team while helping serve your country.

The qualified candidate will have a general knowledge of and experience in semiconductor fabrication, digital or RF circuit design and test. Candidate will join a production team working closely with multiple programs and area managers to support on-time delivery of products derived from a variety of Si and Compound Semiconductor technologies. As a Product Engineer, you will be responsible for developing product backend (assembly, test, and/or reliability testing) process flow, making on-time deliveries, and ensuring program requirements compliance. On daily basis, you will interface with representatives from design, assembly, test, inspection, product integration, supply chain, operations, and program management. You will also be responsible for identifying product yield and quality issues, and leading a cross functional team in an effort to identify root cause and implement corrective measures. Candidates should have a strong sense of ownership and urgency, cross functional team leadership, and also have an aptitude for data manipulation and analysis via tools such as JMP and/or custom programming via languages such as Python.
