Description

An organization within ACME Engineering’s Payload and Ground Systems (PGS) division, Spacecraft Components is a leading designer, producer, and supplier of spacecraft components that power and enable satellites of all classes. Products include spacecraft panels, bus structures, precision optical structures, deployable structural systems and mechanisms, solar arrays, and antenna reflectors. Our products are on virtually every U.S. satellite built in the last 20 years.

Learn more about Spacecraft Components at our website: https://www.acmeengineering.com/space/spacecraft-components/.

Payload and Ground Systems is looking to add a Manufacturing Engineering Supervisor Level 1 to the Deployables team. This role will be managing the functional activities of Manufacturing Engineers at the site in Goleta, CA.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design manufacturing processes, procedures and production layouts for assemblies, equipment installation, processing, machining and material handling.
  • Design arrangement of machines within plant facilities to ensure most efficient and productive layout.
  • Design sequence of operations and specifies procedures for the fabrication of tools and equipment and other functions that affect product performance.
  • Adapt machine or equipment design to factory and production conditions.
  • Incorporate inspection and test requirements into the production plan.
    • Inspect performance of machinery, equipment, and tools to verify their efficiency, and investigates and initiates corrective action of problems and deficiencies to ensure product quality.
  • Develops manufacturing processes that are applicable to statistical process control, and may develop those techniques.
    • Ensures processes and procedures are in compliance with regulations.
  • Provide guidance to engineering regarding design concepts and specification requirements to best utilize equipment and manufacturing techniques.
  • Manage the functional activities of Manufacturing Engineers.
  • In addition to functional Section Manager duties, will perform IPT engineering tasks, which will comprise most of the statement of work.
    • Note: Internal candidates aligned to Vehicle Engineering will typically continue to perform their existing IPT engineering tasks.
  • Process oversight, participation in the performance review process, management of personnel records and participation in corrective or disciplinary actions.
  • Staffing responsibilities may include hiring, recruiting of personnel, and working to ensure that engineers with the appropriate skills and experience are deployed.
  • Mentor, provide career guidance and coaching, and leading others.
  • Use excellent verbal and written communication skills.

Basic Qualifications:

  • 5 years of relevant experience with a Bachelor's degree; 3 years of relevant experience with a Masters' degree; 0 years of relevant experience with a PhD; an additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of degree
  • Must be willing and able to travel to other ACME Engineering and/or customer sites as needed.
  • Must be a U.S. Citizen.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Demonstrated technical leadership and management experience.
  • At least 5 years of applicable Engineering Manufacturing experience on space hardware. 
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to effectively interact with the customer and program management.
  • Proficiency with MS Office software (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Project).
  • Experience managing people preferred
