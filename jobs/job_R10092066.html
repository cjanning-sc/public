Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Senior/Principal Testing Engineer-Infrastructure & Deployment.  This position will be located in [Roy, UT and will support the Sentinel – Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.  This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

Help us blow stuff up!  The selected candidate, as part of the Analysis Group within the Infrastructure and Deployment team, will be responsible for managing test related planning and execution for facility nuclear hardness, unauthorized entry, and other required test programs.  This will include support of test flights for the new Sentinel missile.  This selected candidate will also develop or manage the development of the technical documents associated with this testing and present those documents to various stakeholders within Northrop Grumman and the AF.


Roles and Responsibilities: 
•    Coordination with AF and other Northrop Grumman test teams
•    Oversight of testing performed by Northrop Grumman or subcontractors
•    Creation and Review of Test Plans
•    Management of test execution, including schedule and cost
•    Coordination with Systems Engineering Requirements Verification Activities
•    Coordination with Modeling and Simulation Validation Activities
•    Equipment Check-Out and Start-Up Planning
•    Due to the complex nature of the systems analyzed, the position requires working closely with multiple engineering disciplines including but not limited to Civil Design, System Engineering, and Mechanical Design to achieve program objectives in a timely manner.
•    Additional work responsibilities may include task planning, conducting technical peer reviews, and mentoring junior engineers. 
•    Interface with AF customer
