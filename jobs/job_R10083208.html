Northrop Grumman Mission Systems (NGMS) sector delivers a decisive advantage to its customers through software-defined, hardware-enabled solutions. The Electrical Assembly Design & Verification section in Linthicum, MD enables these hardware solutions by providing RF microwave and mixed signal architecture, design, verification, and support expertise across a wide variety electronic sensor products. Our engineers work on complex, leading edge, high reliability electronics supporting maritime, ground, airborne, and space platforms.

What You Will Do:

NGMS is seeking engineers with experience and interest in software development and database design including:

  • Designing, modeling, and verifying RF, microwave, and mixed signal subsystem assemblies
  • Support verification efforts for RF, Analog, and Digital hardware designs
  • Create/manage automated test benches for prototype and low production rate systems
  • Develop/Optimize customer facing software applications for hardware design support
  • Develop design for testability strategies and methodologies
  • Collaborating with peer functions such as Mechanical, Manufacturing, Quality, Supply Chain, and Test
  • Deriving and trading requirements with subsystem engineers and component level designers
  • Working directly with component level design peers developing custom RF integrated circuit designs to meet assembly-level requirements
  • Supporting products throughout the product life cycle
     

Basic Qualifications:

This position may be filled at either the RF Engineer, Level T02, or RF Principal Engineer, Level T03

  • Level T02 requirements: Electrical Engineering or related STEM field degree: Bachelor of Science and 2 years of experience, or Master's degree and 0 years of experience.
  • Level T03 requirements: Electrical Engineering or related STEM field degree: Bachelor of Science and 5 years of experience, or Master's degree and 3 years of experience.
  • Proficiency with software programing languages such as MATLAB, Python, Java, C#
  • Understanding of fundamentals of electronics, mechanical requirements, and schematic drawings
  • Hands on experience utilizing test instrumentation such and power supplies, network analyzers, Oscilloscopes, spectrum analyzers, High speed digital IO
  • US Citizenship is required
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Security Clearance
