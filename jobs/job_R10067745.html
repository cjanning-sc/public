ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking Software Development Analyst / Principal SW Development Analyst to join the Digital Systems Team (DST). This position is in Detroit, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.   Final grade level offered will be based on candidate level of experience.

What You’ll Get To Do:

Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • The selected candidate supports, maintains, and/or resolves incidents on enterprise-wide Product Lifecycle Management systems and/or applications software

  • Involved in supervised planning of system maintenance and upgrade deployments as well as responsible for meeting software compliance standards

  • Provides operational end user support, guidance, and incident management to agreed service levels

  • Performs or supports testing and maintenance of system corrections

  • Responsibilities include providing IT-focused end-user support in areas of best practice development, operations support, and problem resolution for the engineering community and serving as a Subject Matter Expert in Siemens products including Teamcenter Unified

  • This includes addressing end-user incidents and requests for assistance within agreed service level guidance in support of business and IT priorities

  • End-user support of TeamCenter PLM software including, but not limited to TcU, Multi-CAD Data Management, Integrations, ProductData/Change/Document Management, Account Management, and Workflows in production environments

  • Under supervision will perform root cause analysis, debugging problems with the Teamcenter environment, and then work with the appropriate technical teams to develop, test, and implement fixes to prevent recurrence

  • Monitor, address, and resolve Teamcenter production environment incidents, service requests, workflows, client install packages, etc., collaboratively with the PLM development and administration teams

  • Document all aspects of system support such that knowledge is readily shared among all those tasked with supporting Teamcenter

  • Support SIT and UAT with business users as necessary

  • Provide demos to users as and when required

  • Under supervision will support business readiness and change management activities prior to go-live and in production

  • Work effectively within teams where other team members may be located throughout the country and influencing others to action who are not direct report
