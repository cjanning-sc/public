ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Engineer Supplier Quality. This position will be located in Detroit, Michigan and will support the Sentinel program.  

 

This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

 

What You’ll Get To Do:  

  • Defines and monitors mission assurance program specifications and processes to ensure mission success of programs.

  • Performs or assures quality, risk management, safety, reliability and maintainability of program achievements, subcontractors, and suppliers in accordance with contractual requirements.

  • Assesses program performance and risks, and determines resources to ensure mission assurance.

  • The selected candidate will be responsible for supporting documentation that implements the Sentinel Enterprise and Program Supplier Quality Assurance Plan, policies, procedures, & tools required to support Program Tasks as well as infrastructure needs.

  • Additionally, the selected candidate will be support for SQA oversight activities for suppliers and subcontractors of hardware developed/manufactured/assembled/tested for Sentinel.

Additional roles and responsibilities:

  • Prepare documentation that implements the Sentinel Enterprise and Program Supplier Quality Assurance Plan, policies, procedures, & tools required to support Program Tasks as well as Enterprise infrastructure needs.

  • Create process health, progress and stability metrics.

  • Supplier Quality Assurance oversight activities of hardware developed/manufactured/assembled/tested for Sentinel.
