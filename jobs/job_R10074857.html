We are looking for you to join our team as a Test Equipment Coordinator based out of Rolling Meadows, IL. As a Test Equipment Coordinator at Northrop Grumman you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation.

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking Test Equipment Coordinator to join our Rolling Meadows Engineering Laboratories Management team. The qualified applicant will be responsible for managing test equipment, tools, and government hardware.  Additional responsibilities include maintaining laboratory compliance requirements and providing support to hardware, software, and systems test engineers.

What You’ll get to Do:


Roles and Responsibilities:


• Executes property control program to ensure accountability of all engineering test equipment and government furnished property
• Works with Equipment Standards Lab to maintain calibration of assets, while maximizing equipment uptime
• Maintains property accountability records and conducts periodic property audits
• Determines condition and arranges disposal of surplus property

• Supports laboratory compliance requirements

• Supports day-to-day laboratory operational needs

Basic Qualifications:
• Associate degree
• Experience using computers and Microsoft Office products
• Ability to lift and move heavy equipment up to 35 lbs.
• Must be able to obtain and maintain interim Secret Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:
• Bachelor’s degree

• Previous experience as an Equipment Custodian
• Familiarity with electronic test equipment, including oscilloscopes, signal generators, and spectrum analyzers
• Experience handling government owned property
• Current Secret Clearance
