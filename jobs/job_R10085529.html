Northrop Grumman Space Systems sector delivers a decisive advantage to its customers through software-defined, hardware-enabled solutions. The Electrical Design and Analysis (EDA) section in Linthicum, MD enables these hardware solutions by providing technical expertise in the areas of architecture development, design, verification, and test across a wide variety of electronic sensor products. Our engineers work on complex, leading edge, high reliability electronics supporting different space platforms.

NG Space Sector engineers share a great sense of pride knowing their contributions have a direct effect on the safety and security of the country and its allies. Our culture thrives on intellectual curiosity, cognitive diversity, teamwork, and bringing your whole self to work to accomplish the mission while learning and having fun along the way. Our department is fertile ground for all experience levels to become part of our growing team and contribute to our goals. The department offers opportunities to grow on a technical track both broad and deep as well as explore project leadership or management positions.

This position will require the candidate to be 100% on site. 

EDA is seeking a Principal or Senior Principal Electronics Engineer with experience and interest in analog/digital/mixed signals electronic test and verification.  Typical responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Troubleshooting hardware based on schematics and component design documents

  • Troubleshooting mixed-signal assemblies utilizing various test equipment

  • Supporting Low-Rate Space Production

  • Supporting some systems level electrical test and performance analyses

  • Leading testing and reporting activities

  • Managing technician workload and priorities

  • Developing test plans and managing its execution

  • Maintaining and coordinating calibration of test equipment

  • Interfacing with customer and providing support as needed

  • Hands on integration and test support

  • Collaborating with cross functional groups to achieve desired outcome (management, engineering, reliability, mission assurance and production personal)

This position can be filled at the Principal level (level 3) or Sr. Principal (level 4)

Basic Qualifications Principal Electronics Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s degree in STEM with 5 years’ experience, Master's degree in STEM with 3 years’ experience, Ph.D. degree in STEM with 0 years’ experience. In lieu of a degree 9 years of experience will be considered

  • Ability to read and interpret schematics, assembly drawings, parts lists

  • Troubleshoot hardware based on schematics and component design documents

  • Utilize basic lab test equipment to troubleshoot mixed-signal assemblies

  • Open to occasional shift support during Pre-Environmental testing (PET) and Thermal vacuum testing (TVAC) for system test

  • Effective verbal and written communication

  • Active Secret clearance required to start and must have the ability to obtain and maintain a TS/SCI

Basic Qualifications Sr. Principal Electronics Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s degree in STEM with 9 years’ experience, Master's degree in STEM with 7 years’ experience, Ph.D. degree in STEM with 4 years’ experience. In lieu of a degree, 13 years of experience will be considered

  • Ability to read and interpret schematics, assembly drawings, parts lists

  • Troubleshoot hardware based on schematics and component design documents

  • Utilize basic lab test equipment to troubleshoot mixed-signal assemblies

  • Open to occasional shift support during Pre-Environmental testing (PET) and Thermal vacuum testing (TVAC) for system test

  • Ability to manage technician workload and priorities

  • Ability to develop test plans and manage its execution

  • Effective verbal and written communication

  • Active Secret clearance required to start and must have the ability to obtain and maintain a TS/SCI

Preferred Qualifications for both Principal and Sr Principal Electronics Engineer:

  • Active TS/SCI

  • Ability to assess and generate reports for electrical over stress analysis

  • Test Coordination

  • Experience with standard test equipment (STE) design or support

  • Experience with environmental stress screening (ESS)

  • Ability to troubleshoot Test bench issues from H/W and S/W documentation

  • Experience with electro optical (EO) System/CCD

  • Willing to support new design efforts to promote future EO system business

  • Experience with Design & Test Requirement documents and Operational Test Specifications
