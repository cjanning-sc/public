SCA Computer Operator 4 (AHT)
R10084098
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman’s Defense Systems sector is seeking an experienced SCA Computer Operator 4 interested in professional and personal advancement supporting the U.S. Army Mission with our growing team of diverse professionals at Fort Leavenworth in Kansas.  Mission Command Training Program (MCTP) SCA Computer Operators will help define the future by working side by side with U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) civilians, U.S. military personnel, and representatives from other Governmental organizations.  The team supports U.S. Army simulation-based training out of Fort Leavenworth, Kansas, and employs the Army's Warfighters' Simulation (WARSIM) system.

Responsibilities:

  • Prepares WARSIM and its components for operation during MCTP Warfighter Exercises (WFX)

  • Prepares simulation components for shipment to and from training exercise sites across the United States and overseas

  • Builds and distributes hard drive images

  • Installs, configures, and administers servers and client workstations

  • Loads software patches and security updates

  • Sets up, tests, and monitors the performance of both Microsoft and Red Hat Linux-based servers and workstations, and responds to trouble calls at the exercise training site

  • Other duties include the installation, configuration, and set-up of exercise control and collaboration client computers, routine maintenance of MCTP systems hardware and software, and assistance with troubleshooting and repairing local area networks

Basic Qualifications:

  • One of the following:

    • High school diploma or GED with a minimum of 3 years of computer system administration experience

    • Associate's degree in an IT or technical discipline

  • Must have or be able to obtain and maintain a U.S. DoD Secret security clearance (a valid interim Secret clearance will be required before starting work)

  • Must have or be able to obtain a valid Security+ CE certification and a Computing Environment certificate in a manager-approved systems area within 6 months of hire

  • Continuing education in Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist (MCTS)/Microsoft Certified Information Technology Professional (MCITP) or Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL) computing environments is an ongoing requirement

  • Must be able to lift equipment containers weighing up to 50lbs

  • Must be able to travel CONUS and OCONUS to support simulation-based Army training exercises as needed (up to 50% of the time)

  • Must be able to work on-site at Fort Leavenworth in Kansas

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Military experience and knowledge of US Army organizations, systems and operations

  • CompTIA, Microsoft and Red Hat Linux computing environment certifications

  • Familiarity with US Army organizations, systems and operations

SCA hourly rate: $25.66; H&W as specified in the applicable wage determination
