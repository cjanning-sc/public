Northrop Grumman Space Systems is currently recruiting for a Facilities Engineer to join our team at the Promontory, Utah location.

Job Description:

  • Manage projects and coordinate all procurements and installation to meet the program cost and schedule requirements for capital upgrades.

  • Provide engineering design as needed to support the Facilities Engineering Department.  This could include:

    • Design mechanical piping and instrument diagrams (P&IDs) for chemical plant systems including boiler feed and condensate, water treatment,  hydraulic, heat transfer systems, conveying systems, and air and gas systems.

    • Prepare design calculations and specifications for major plant related equipment including, but not limited to heat exchangers, industrial chillers, cooling towers, boilers, condensers, and pumps.

    • Prepare mechanical engineering designs, including layout, design sketches, and conceptual design.

    • Check design deliverables in accordance with industry codes and standards, contract requirements, and company standards.

    • Develop schedules, work assignments and staffing requirements to ensure successful project completion.

    • Coordinate with other engineers and craftsman assigned to the project or a task, and reviews work progress and results

    • Providing detailed cost estimates for project design and implementation

    • Preparing specifications for the procurement of pumps, heat exchangers, valves, compressors, tanks, cranes, and other miscellaneous of plant equipment.

    • Review engineering drawings and designs for manufacturability and functionality.

  • Troubleshoot industrial mechanical equipment.  Resolve equipment problems in the field through technical assistance with Maintenance Craftsmen.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering

  • 2+ years industrial facility mechanical design experience

  • Industrial process equipment/facilities design:

    • Chemical processing preferred

    • AutoCAD drafting ability

    • Solid modeling experience a plus; not a requirement

    • Ability to work in the field, climb ladders, work outside and work in congested areas.

  • May be hired at a level higher with a Bachelor’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering and 5+ years’ experience

Technical Skills:

  • Strong candidates for this position will possess:
    •     Desire, i.e. passion, to build and manufacture support hardware.
    •     Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
    •     Strong organizational skills in managing time, projects and resources.
    •     Leadership ability.
    •     Technical attention to detail.
