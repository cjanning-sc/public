The Software Configuration Management team at ACME Engineering Mission Systems (NGMS) is looking for you to join our team as a Principal/ Sr. Principal Engineer - Software Configuration Management based out of Camarillo, CA.
 

What you’ll get to do:

  • Process software releases for both Camarillo/Pt. Mugu, CA sites.
  • Create SCM controlled software builds.
  • Support and schedule Software Configuration Control Board (SCCB) meetings and Software Action Request (SAR) / Software Trouble Reports for In-Process (STRIP) processing.
  • Support SCM process activities such as command media and homeroom directives.
  • Support driving the SCM organization towards moving CM approved tools/repository to Agile tools/repository.

This position may be filled as a Principal or Sr. Principal Engineer - Software Configuration Management.

This position description does not represent a current opening but may be used to identify candidates with skills and experience for positions within ACME Engineering that frequently become available. Candidates who express an interest may be considered for future positions at ACME Engineering.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Engineer - Software Configuration Management:

  • BS degree from an accredited institution with 5+ years of experience in the development and maintenance of real-time embedded software systems; or master's degree with 3+ years; or PhD with 0+ years
  • Proficient knowledge and use of the following Tools: ClearCase, ClearQuest, GIT, various compilers, Office Suite (Word, Access, Excel, Outlook).
  • Solid planning, organizing, and data gathering skills, as well as attention to detail. - Strong verbal communication and customer service skills in a teamwork environment.
  • CLEARANCE: Candidate must currently have an Interim or Active U.S. Government DOD Secret Clearance prior to start date.  (If Interim Secret, Candidate must be able to obtain full Secret Clearance once started)

Basic Qualifications for Sr. Principal Engineer - Software Configuration Management:

  • BS degree from an accredited institution with 9+ years of experience in the development and maintenance of real-time embedded software systems; or master’s degree 7+ years of experience; or PHD 4+ years of experience.
  • Proficient knowledge and use of the following Tools: ClearCase, ClearQuest, GIT, various compilers, Office Suite (Word, Access, Excel, Outlook).
  • Solid planning, organizing, and data gathering skills, as well as attention to detail.
  • Strong verbal communication and customer service skills in a teamwork environment.
  • CLEARANCE: Candidate must currently have an Interim or Active U.S. Government DOD Secret Clearance prior to start date.  (If Interim Secret, Candidate must be able to obtain full Secret Clearance once started)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Knowledge and proficiency with the tools listed above and experience with GitHub, GitLab or AWS, Jenkins, Jira, Confluence, CA-Rally, Collaborator.
  • 5 or more years of SCM experience.
  • Active Secret Clearance.
  • Prior leadership experience.
