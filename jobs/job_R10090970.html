Northrop Grumman is seeking a Global Project Manager. This role is not location specific, and can be supported virtually, but there may be up to 25% domestic travel.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Project Manage small to large efforts in either Agile, Waterfall, or Activate project methodology
  • Build, maintain, and execute project schedules and track performance to budget and schedule
  • Responsible for discovery, prep, and execution of project through go live and stabilization, including required project documentation, project costs, resource allocation, risks/issues logs, milestone data.
  • Manage a team of highly skilled geographically dispersed analysts to ensure the overall technical quality and performance of solutions meets the business requirements and objectives.
  • Ensuring the team adheres to sound technical processes and approaches in accordance with company policies, procedures, quality standards and Project protocols.
  • Communicate and prepare presentation status regularly and timely to leadership
  • Work closely with the Global ERP Recurring Service Line Manager to Manage the Global ERP5 year Roadmap.
  • Coordinate with leadership, homeroom managers, and sectors to identify, plan, implement, and sustain ERP solutions in meeting program strategic and tactical objectives.

Basic Qualifications – to be considered for this position, you must minimally meet the knowledge, skills and abilities listed below:

  • Bachelor’s degree and a minimum of 2 years related experience
  • Experience building relationships and understanding the needs of stakeholders
  • Ability to prioritize and multi-task when there are competing demands on you
  • Ability to formulate, communicate and execute project plans and initiatives
  • Excellent interpersonal and critical thinking skills - ability to effectively communicate with all levels of leadership, level 1 employees, and support function employees
  • Proven experience with constant communication in a quickly changing environment
  • Working knowledge of MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, SharePoint)
  • High level of understanding in ERP functions and interdependencies of modules
  • Experience in planning, organizing, and executing large/small scale events

Preferred Qualifications – candidates with these desired skills will be given preferential consideration:

  • 5 years of relevant experience including prior experience
  • Master's degree
  • Experience in ERP SAP modules in MM, FICO,GPD
