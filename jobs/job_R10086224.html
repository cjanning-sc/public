Basic Qualifications for Principal Digital Engineer (FPGA/ASIC):

  • Bachelor of Science (BS) degree in science, engineering, or related discipline with 5 years of experience; or 3 years of relevant experience with a Masters (MS) degree; or 0 years with a PhD

  • Working knowledge of full product life cycle (requirements, design, implementation and test) of FPGA Design and/or ASIC Design.

  • Knowledge of System Verilog and/or VHDL.

  • Clearance: Must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain and maintain a Top Secret clearance

Basic Qualifications for Sr. Principal Digital Engineer (FPGA/ASIC):

  • Bachelor of Science (BS) degree in science, engineering, or related discipline with 9 years of experience; or 7 years of relevant experience with a Masters (MS) degree; or 4 years with a PhD;

  • Working knowledge of full product life cycle (requirements, design, implementation and test) of FPGA Design and/or ASIC Design.

  • Knowledge of System Verilog and/or VHDL.

  • Clearance: Must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain and maintain a Top Secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications include experience with any of the following:

  • Industry standard FPGA design implementation tools for IP integration, PnR, CDC such as Xilinx Vivado, Intel Quartus, and QuestaSim.

  • Industry standard ASIC front-end design tools for synthesis, LEC, CDC

  • STA constraints generation and timing closure

  • MATLAB, Mentor Graphics design tools, Synopsys or similar tool

  • Xilinx and Intel FPGA technology

  • Active Top Secret security clearance with SCI
