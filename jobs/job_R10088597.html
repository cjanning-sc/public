Principal Cloud Systems Engineer (AHT)

1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Defense Systems sector (NGDS) is seeking a Principal Cloud Systems Engineer to join our growing team of diverse professionals in Manhattan Beach, California.  The selected candidate will take a leading role in migrating legacy applications to the cloud and work with our engineering team to develop, implement, optimize, and maintain native cloud-based solutions in accordance with best practices.

Responsibilities:

  • Supports standup of Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment pipeline
  • Provides guidance and educates teams on the implementation of new cloud technologies and initiatives
  • Reviews existing systems and makes recommendations for improvements
  • Ensures performance capabilities and security of new systems and architectures are consistent with mission requirements
  • Communicates effectively with small technical teams
  • Prioritizes and adjusts tasks to accomplish project results
  • Comprehends and analyzes complex problems and develops solutions

Basic Qualifications:

  • One of the following:
    • Bachelor’s degree in a technical engineering or related discipline with a minimum of 5 years of relevant engineering experience
    • Master’s degree in a technical engineering or related discipline with a minimum of 3 years of relevant engineering experience
  • Must have an active U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) Secret security clearance investigated within the past 5 years
  • Experience with cloud hosting and the migration of legacy systems to a cloud environment
  • Experience designing, deploying and administering cloud-based solutions
  • Experience with Azure Cloud infrastructure technology
  • Knowledge of containerization, including Docker and Kubernetes
  • Knowledge of software configuration management tools such as: Git and Jira
  • Experience working in Agile software development environment
  • Experience in Systems Engineering, e.g., CONOPS development, requirements analysis, decomposition and allocation, interface definition, and trade studies
  • Ability to work on-site in Manhattan Beach, California

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience/exposure to Infrastructure as Code (Iac) development and administration, developing or using tools such as Terraforn, YAML, Ansible, Cloudformation, or equivalent
  • Experience working within complex or high security environments
  • Experience with DevSecOps service/system delivery model
  • DoD 8570 IAT Level II certification or greater (e.g., Security+)
  • Cloud Service Provider certification(s) such as Azure (Solutions Architect, Developer, Administration, DevOps, Cloud Security) a plus
    • Experience with MBSE Tools
    • Experience in scripting/programming languages such as Python, PowerShell, Visual Basic, .Net, Java, C, and C#
