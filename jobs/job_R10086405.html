AOA Xinetics (AOX), a wholly owned Northrop Grumman Space Systems, Payload and Ground Systems Division Business Venture, is a leading supplier of complex electro-optic and photonic systems and subsystems that control and manipulate light waves for a variety of applications. We are seeking a Calibration Coordinator to support our Quality efforts in Devens, MA.

This position may be filled at either a level 3 or a level 4

Overview

The Calibration Coordinator calibrates electronic test measuring equipment and signal generating equipment to conform to set standards. Tests, calibrates, adjusts, and maintains electromechanical, mechanical, optical and opto-mechanical measuring and indicating instruments. Sets up calibration sequences, methods and procedures according to detailed specifications, blueprints, drawings, and requirements. Disassembles, cleans, repairs, and replaces defective parts of test equipment such as pressure gauges and tension meters. May monitor and verify quality in accordance with statistical process or other control procedures.

Role and Responsibilities:

  • Effectively solve problems related to measurement equipment

  • Acquire, analyze, document and report data relating to calibration activities

  • Communicate with internal customers to convey calibration equipment activities

  • Create and update procedures as necessary

  • Identify and execute processes related to calibration for improvement opportunities to drive effectiveness of calibration management

  • Support calibration related activities such as the processing of various measurement equipment and in accordance with AOX internal calibration procedures and policies

  • Track and trend data on equipment that fall under AOX calibration requirements

  • Support internal audits, external audits, product, and process verification

  • Notify responsible personnel/user/work area of upcoming calibration due dates

  • Ensure calibration is performed on time as required per calibration procedure/policy, so that schedules are always met

  • Contact calibration and service providers and initiate purchase orders for the service

  • Plan to deliver calibration equipment received back from service providers to respective owners, function, and work centers after verifying calibration report is acceptable, and pertinent information has been documented into the database

  • Initiate Non-Conforming Material Report if equipment is found to be out of tolerance during calibration or any time during use

  • Create calibration and reports on a regular cadence and communicate any reportable observations to your manager

  • Review change orders related to calibration services request

  • Support verification/validation activities for new equipment or based on need

  • Report unsafe conditions observed while equipment is in use

  • Support projects or activities as required by the company

  • Perform other duties or special projects as assigned by management

  • Accountable to meet or exceed the Quality Assurance, and support the Operational, safety, quality, delivery, employee, and cost (SQDEC) expectations of external and internal customers across all AOX programs supported by (either Quality Assurance or Quality Engineering)

  • Perform other duties or special projects as assigned by management

Basic Qualifications for a level 3:

  • High School Diploma, or equivalent and four years applicable technical experience

  • Understanding basic calibration process

  • Ability to interpret OEM specifications related to test and measurement equipment

  • Ability to multitask while upholding attention to detail and Quality

  • A working knowledge of MS Outlook, Excel, Word, PowerPoint etc.

  • Knowledge and application of AS9100

  • Ability to collaborate effectively with managers, and peers to achieve business and organizational goals

Basic Qualifications for a level 4:

  • Same as above, but with six years applicable technical experience

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Associates degree in a technical discipline

  • Experience working in a similar coordination role

Physical Requirements:

  • Ability to sit for extended periods of time and light physical effort required

  • Ability to lift 10lbs
