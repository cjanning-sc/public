Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

Responsibilities includes but not limited to:

  • Modeling, Simulation & Analysis for specific aspects of the system.
  • Support requirement decomposition and traceability.
  • Support MBSE strategy and implementation.
  • Coordinate with sub-contracted product teams for compliance with contractual SOW and established program model governance.
  • Contribute content for presentations to internal and external stakeholders.
