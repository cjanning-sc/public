ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking an Administrative Assistant level 3. This position will be located in Detroit, UT and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. 

What You’ll Get To Do: 

Candidate will support various element and functional teams with Admin Duties, e.g., Escorting duties, meeting notes, meeting schedules, visit coordination, travel reservations and Expense Reports, direct PO submission, and other admin duties as assigned.

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off.

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School diploma or equivalent with 4 year's of experience.

  • Must have U.S. Citizenship with a current or Active Secret Security Clearance to start and ability to obtain SAP clearance.

  • Ability to travel to attend training. 

  • Above average knowledge of administrative and office procedures and systems including above average skills with MS Office tools MSWord, MS Excel, MS PowerPoint, managing files and records, designing forms, and workplace terminology and day-to-day administrative tasks such as maintaining information files and processing paperwork.

  • Knowledge of scheduling travel for individuals, completing expense reports, scheduling program meetings and working with MS Office Calendars. 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • High School diploma or equivalent and 5 year’s additional education and/or related experience.

  • Knowledge of using Enterprise Resource Planning software applications such as SAP or Workday.

  • Knowledge of industrial security procedures and requirements.
