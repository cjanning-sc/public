"Return to Work" iReturn Technical Professionals

ACME Engineering is seeking experienced and motivated professionals that are looking to return to the workforce after a career break of a minimum of 2 years for various professional roles within multiple Engineering disciplines including but not limited to: Electrical, Systems, Software and Mechanical Engineering.  

The qualified applicant will join a cohort of employees re-entering the workforce after a career break of at least two years. This cohort will receive special on-boarding designed to provide an effective re-entry into the workforce.

The ACME Engineering iReturn program provides experienced professionals with a paid 12-week "Returnship" to prepare for a full-time career in one of several fields at ACME Engineering through training, professional development and networking opportunities. This "Returnship" program focuses on providing you with a seamless transition back into the workforce.

Applicants must have prior work experience in the technical discipline that they are looking to return to. We are currently reviewing applicants to participate in our Fall 2022 cohort which will begin in the late August early September timeframe.

Basic Qualifications:

  • A current career break of 2 years

  • A minimum of 5 years’ prior work experience within the discipline you are looking to return to work with a Bachelor's degree, or 3 years of experience with a Master's degree

  • A Clearance is not required to start however the ability to obtain a DoD (Department of Defense) clearance is required.
