Senior Principal System Engineer - Enterprise Defect Manager (Secret Clearance or Higher Required)
R10083887
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Aerospace Systems has an opening for a Sr. Principal Engineer Systems – Enterprise Defect Manager to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals within our System Engineering Organization. This position will be located in San Diego, CA.

The selected candidate will assume an Enterprise Defect Manager role as part of the Systems Engineering Integration and Test (SEIT) team on the RQ-4 Enterprise. As part of day-to-day operations, the selected candidate will interface with various engineering functions including engineering leadership to perform risk assessment and defect management.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Work with IPTs to develop and track formal Software, Hardware, and Infrastructure anomalies and Defects at an enterprise level from identification through disposition and closure.
  • Provide engineering technical rigor to the adjudication and disposition of defects, working directly with Chief Engineering staff and senior management to ensure proper level of risk acceptance and documentation.
  • Ensure supplier defects are appropriately adjudicated and reported via a standard process

We offer flexible work arrangements, 9/80 work schedule with every other Friday off, phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly team environment. We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal-oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do.

Basic Qualifications for Sr. Principal Systems Engineer

  • BS degree in a STEM discipline with 9 years engineering experience; MS degree in STEM discipline with 7 years engineering experience; PhD degree in a STEM discipline with 5 years of engineering experience
  • An active DoD Secret (or higher) clearance
  • Familiarity with JIRA software tool (and/or similar) for issue tracking and resolution
  • Experience in full lifecycle of Systems Engineering, including requirements decomposition, test planning, requirements verification and requirements management.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Software development and defect management
  • Experience with Infrastructure development and defect management
  • Experience with Hardware design and defect management
  • Experience with root cause investigations, fielded issue investigation, and operational missions
  • Experience with multiple disciplines across Systems Engineering, incl. requirements, integration, technical configuration management (CM or Technical Baseline), cyber
  • Familiarity and understanding of system requirements (validation and verification) and interface control documents (ICDs), and statements of work (SOW)
  • Experience with USAF programs
  • Experience with International or FMS customers
  • Experience supporting Aircraft Systems
