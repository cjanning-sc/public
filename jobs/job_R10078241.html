Northrop Grumman Aerospace Systems has an opening for a Principal/Sr. Principal Test Conductor to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals within our Integration and Test Organization. This position will be located in Melbourne FL but may be remote or hybrid.


In this role, you will be responsible for supporting planning and coordinating all test requirements on a capture program.  Candidate shall display professionalism, motivation, strong leadership skills, and ability to work independently.  Should be able to function as a key technical subject matter expert of the test team and will interface directly with Program Management, the Chief Engineer, and the customer to coordinate test requirements, test planning, and test execution.  The candidate should be comfortable with making real-time decisions in a high-risk fast-paced test environment. Able to collaborate in a team environment and prioritize multiple responsibilities and/or accomplishing them. Candidate will have strong mission systems including extensive radar and mission comms ground and flight testing.  They will help evaluate threat scenarios and develop testing to validate requirements and assess capabilities.

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels. Relocation assistance may be available.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Designs, develops, and tests a variety of power plants, aircraft, missiles, and parts, taking into consideration the most efficient use of the natural laws affecting flight
  • Establishes structural specifications, performs stress analysis, and conducts performance tests.
  • Responsible to ensure engineering tests serve mission objectives and mechanisms are in place to execute the tests
  • Responsible for testing all customer samples
  • Oversees the operational aspects of engineering tests and serves as liaison to program, engineering and line management
  • Reviews status of test requirements, manages schedules and prepares reports
  • Selects and supervises technicians during test
  • Assesses anomalies and develops resolutions to documented procedures in conjunction with Test and Quality Engineering

We offer flexible work arrangements, phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly team environment. We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal-oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do.

  • Must be able to travel Internationally under current COVID restrictions and beyond (e.g. must be able to obtain a passport and VISA if required; must have appropriate vaccinations to enter and conduct business Internationally without excessive quarantine).
