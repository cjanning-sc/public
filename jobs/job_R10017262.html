Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Systems Under Test, Manager of Test Engineering 2, to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals.

This position will be located in Palmdale or Edwards, AFB, CA.

Candidate must have a significant background in leading people and teams in high-paced demanding technical environment. Must be able to drive team to maintain schedule, cost and program goals. Will manage a diverse team of technicians and engineers.

  • The selected candidate will provide technical products and planning coordination for the Test IPT as the manager for the system under test.

  • Will fill in as needed as the Cost Account Manager overseeing a substantial operating budget producing budget forecasting and analysis.

  • Position will be the lead point of contact for the test team and will coordinate and oversee all maintenance and test activities for the system under test.

  • Maintain and specify test article configurations and coordinate all test modifications.

  • Ensures all test article activity has the required authorization, schedules activities, and assigns priorities.

  • Coordinate with local and remote test facilities / ranges, other Special Projects IPTs, subcontractors, and government agencies.

  • Reports on article status and provides detailed reports to Senior Management as required.

Position requires domestic travel to various location across the CONUS. Travel periods may be of significant duration. Candidate must be willing to work extended shifts, weekends and holidays as required to meet program schedule and objectives.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics)

  • A minimum of 9 years of engineering experience

  • Experience in Systems Test Environment

  • Active In-Scope Secret Clearance (investigation within last 3 years) with ability to obtain special program access

  • Preferred Qualifications:

  • Excellent communications skills to include ability to author technical reports and correspondence

  • Significant experience in the developmental/operational test maintenance environment

  • Experience briefing managers, senior program leadership and customers.

  • Previous experience with team engagement and minimum of 7 years experience in a leadership/management position
