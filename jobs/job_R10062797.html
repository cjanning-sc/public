Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Staff Engineer Systems to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in San Diego, CA.

The selected candidate will be responsible for coordination and collaboration with diverse engineering design teams to provide imaginative, technical solutions to a wide range of difficult problems in establishing and managing the technical baseline in support of programmatic milestones. The candidate will perform technical planning, system integration, verification and validation, cost and risk, and supportability and effectiveness analyses for total systems. Analyses are performed at all levels of total system product to include: concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal. Ensures the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints. Performs functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications.

The position requires system thinking and extensive knowledge of development life-cycle processes. 

This role can take on a Requirements and Verification (R&V) lean, or an Integrated Product Team lean, among other smaller specialties that an experienced Systems Engineer would possess. If you are confident in your Systems Engineering abilities and have an active or interim DoD clearance of Secret or higher, we would love to chat!

Basic Qualifications:

  • BS degree in a STEM discipline with 14 years engineering experience; MS degree in STEM discipline with 12 years engineering experience; PhD degree in STEM discipline with 9 Years engineering experience.
  • Experience in troubleshooting system issues and identifying root causes
  • Active or Interim DoD Secret clearance or higher

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with DOORS, Confluence, and JIRA.
  • Experience in ICD development and integration
  • Experience with Systems Engineering V-Model of development
  • Experience with multiple disciplines across Systems Engineering, incl. requirements, integration, technical configuration management (CM or Technical Baseline), cyber
  • Experience with Agile Design Methodology
  • Programming Skills in C/C++/Python/VBA
  • Familiarity with Global Hawk architecture and interface control documentation
  • Experience supporting field operations including maintenance and mission support
  • Familiarity with Systems Engineering and Design processes
  • Experience testing space/US military products
  • Familiarity working with military specifications/standards
  • Ability to develop task specific test tools
  • Demonstrated communication and technical writing skills
  • Active TS/SCI clearance
