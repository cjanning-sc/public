The ACME Engineering Mission Systems Field Engineering team based in Buffalo, NY is seeking a skilled Field Technician to support our Combat Electromagnetic Environment Simulator (CEESIM) Electronic Warfare product line at customer locations. The Field Technician installs, operates, maintains, repairs, and  modifies equipment. Performs a variety of maintenance and technical support on products such as equipment, integrated systems and subsystems, and software at customer and/or field locations. Analyzes and evaluates products and related performance. Troubleshoots and diagnoses malfunctions to eliminate problems in minimum time. Installs an upgrades products ensuring coordinative engineering field change. Maintains effective customer communications and relations. May provide onsite training of customer support personnel.

The successful candidate will have a background in the operation and maintenance of RADAR, or Electronic Warfare (EW) systems, or commercial digital and RF equipment, or other electronics background, and will have experience testing, troubleshooting, and performing repairs with minimal supervision. Knowledge in the proper usage of RF/Digital test equipment such as a multi-meter, oscilloscope, spectrum analyzer, and power meter is desired.

This position may be filled as a Field Technician 2 or Field Technician 3 based on the qualifications listed below.

Basic Qualifications for a Field Technician (2):

  • 2 years of relevant experience or an Associate's degree in a technical field.
  • Ability to travel domestically and internationally.
  • Ability to frequently move and position objects weighing 50 lbs.
  • Current, active Department of Defense (DoD) Secret clearance with an investigation date within the last 5 years.
  • Ability to travel (up to 50%).

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Field Technician 3:

  • 4 years of relevant experience; or an Associate's degree in a technical field plus 2 years of relevant experience.
  • Ability to travel domestically and internationally.
  • Ability to frequently move and position objects weighing 50 lbs.
  • Current, active Department of Defense (DoD) Secret clearance with an investigation date within the last 5 years.
  • Ability to travel (up to 50%).

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Associate's degree in a technical field and related experience.
  • U.S. military electronic school in Electronic Warfare or RF Systems.
  • Experience with RADAR, Electronic Warfare systems, RF Engineering, or other electronics background that includes experience testing, troubleshooting, and performing repairs.
