ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal or Senior Principal Program Cost Schedule Control Analyst - Financial Planner to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position can be located in Cleveland, Cincinnati, or Akron, CA.

This requisition is for a high-performing Program Financial Planner position within the Strike Division. Responsible for all aspects of Financial Reporting for an entire Program Business Area. The chosen candidate is expected to partner with Program Business Manager in order to ensure financial objectives are thoroughly understood and being achieved. Recurring duties include: weekly Cost of Sales/RWA reporting, bi-weekly Invoicing/Accounts Receivable analysis, and monthly Pre-Close activities. In addition, the planner is also responsible for quarterly Profitability analyses for all Business Area efforts, including compliance with ASC606 Revenue Recognition Standard. This person will help develop the Annual Operating Plan & Long-Range Strategic Planning (AOP & LRSP). Works closely with Business Area Cost & Schedule Analysts, Material Cost Analysts, Global Supply Chain, and Accounting to understand/mitigate potential financial impacts.
