Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Configuration Analysis Manager 1. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Ground Subsystems Support Contract (GSSC) program.

Plans and coordinates preparation of project documentation, such as engineering drawings, production specifications and schedules, contract modifications, and internal process manuals to ensure quality team output and customer contract requirements are met. Reviews contract to determine documentation required for each phase of project, applying knowledge of engineering and manufacturing processes. Analyzes proposed changes of product design to determine effect on overall product and system. Coordinates modification records for management control. Establishes change orders and prepares for change authorization and documentation by company and subcontractor. Reviews and analyzes released engineering change data and coordinates changes with engineering, quality, support, manufacturing, and engineering data control activities.

Section Manager within the Department.

Configuration Data Management (CDM) representative to perform one or more of the following responsibilities:

  • Drawing Change Management
  • Perform Technical Review Board and Configuration Control Board activities
  • Establish agendas, publish minutes & track meeting actions for owned meetings and interchanges
  • Support proposal development by tracking department actuals and providing Basis of Estimate inputs. This includes task descriptions, hours proposals and justifications content.
  • Support engineering teams in processing change packages through lifecycle include disposition & documentation
  • Perform program data release functions via Product Lifecycle Mgmt. tools
  • Develop & maintain various reports for program support. Example, Indentured Parts Lists, Conflict Resolution Matrix, Program Drawing Status List
  • Provide Drawing and Document Verification & Audits
  • Support internal program audits against program SOW & Functional Command Media mandates
  • Provide aid to Functional and Physical Configuration audits
  • Document Management for deliverable CDRLs and non-CDRL documents
  • Coordinate and manage access to shared CAD and CPLM tools license access for direct reports as well as greater ICBM-S team
  • Conduct periodic career planning and progress tracking to guide team of direct reports to successful execution on existing tasks as well as establishment and progression toward career goals.
  • Support hiring process to include identification of position/qualification requirements, develop requisitions, interviews, candidate selection and onboarding.
