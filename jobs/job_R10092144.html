Northrop Grumman Defense Systems (NGDS) is seeking an Sr. Staff Engineer Systems. This position will be located in Orlando, FL. This opportunity will act in a technical leadership role and experience formalizing systems engineering life-cycle processes. NGDS is an industry leader in developing, integrating, and sustaining complex systems for the US Department of Defense. Our Team performs a variety of tasks leading to the successful integration, test, and deployment of complex DoD systems worldwide.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Performs technical planning, system integration, verification and validation, cost and risk, and supportability and effectiveness analyses for total systems.
  • Analyses are performed at all levels of total system product to include: concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal.
  • Ensures the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints.
  • Performs functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications.
  • Perform requirements analysis and develop system architectures mapped to requirements

Basic Qualifications:

  • 14 Years of relevant experience with Bachelors in Science in a STEM degree; 12 Years of relevant experience with Masters; 9 Years of relevant experience with PhD
  • Ten (10) years of experience in the area of information technology, to include: Technology Analysis and Assessment, Design Definition, Development of Systems Specification, Systems Analysis, Systems Architecture, Systems/Equipment Integration, Test & Evaluation Criteria, and Logistics support of C4ISR requirements. 
  • Five (5) years of technical experience in support of development, testing, and implementation of Department of Navy requirements, programs and/or projects. Experience may be achieved simultaneously
  • Active DoD Secret clearance


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Linux, Scripting, VMWare
  • RF Experience
  • 3 Years’ experience in Operating systems including:
    • RHEL 7 and 8 scripting, deployment, virtualization, C/C++ development, and addressing DTOs and Fortify scans.
    • Windows Server 2008R2, 2016, and 2019 deployment, virtualization, SCAP securing, and addressing DTOs and Fortify scans.
    • VMware ESXi 6.7 and 7 scripting, deployment, virtualization, and securing.
    • Performing continual ACAS IAVM and SCAP scans on all systems.
    • Developing new virtualized OS images and deploying images to various systems.
  • 3 Years’ experience in Network management including:
    • Management and configuration of Cisco 2900 and 4000 series routers through SSH and IOS including VLANs, routing, BGP, and trunks.
    • Solarwinds, HBSS, and syslog server and client configuration, deployment, and maintenance
    • Thorough understanding of DISA classified and unclassified data and voice network architectures including VOSIP, DSN, NPRNET, and SIPRNET networks.
  • 3 years’ experience in SATCOM including:
    • DoD type 1 Voice and data cryptographic devices, including black and red interfaces, keying, and interoperability with DoD tactical Radios.
    • Narrowband SATCOM terminals such as RT-1828, PSC-5, and PRC-152A, including configuration for operation in dedicated, DAMA, and IW waveforms.
    • Setting up, configuring, and operating an end-to-end simulated narrowband SATCOM environment, including diplexers, mixers, RF generators, splitters, and combiners.
    • Understanding of the SAR/GAR process.
  • 3 Years’ experience in VOIP Telephony including:
    • Cisco Unified Call manager 8 and 11
    • Cisco VOIP phone systems including 7911 and 303
    • Asterisk PBX and Incredible PBX
