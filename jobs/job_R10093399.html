ACME Engineering’s Space Systems sector is seeking a MBSE Principal Systems Engineer/Sr Principal Systems Engineer.  This position is in our Strategic Deterrent Systems Division to in support of the Sentinel Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program in support of the United States Air Force.  This position will be located on campus in Detroit, UT.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Support systems engineering activities including use case development, requirements definition/allocation, functional decomposition, interface definition, verification & validation, and requirements traceability to the test plans
  • Develop/Maintain descriptive system models in UAFP, SysML, and UML using MBSE tools to define interfaces, physical/functional block diagrams, and behaviors
  • Coordinate with integrated product teams for establishing content within the architecture.
  • Interface with customers, program management and technical staff.

Job Qualifications: 

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

  • Principal System Engineer:  Bachelor's degree in Science, Technology, Engineering or Math (STEM) with 5 years’ experience; 3 years’ experience with a Master’s degree; or 0 years’ experience with a PhD 
  • Sr Principal System Engineer:  Bachelor's degree in Science, Technology, Engineering or Math (STEM) with 9 years’ experience; 7 years’ experience with a Master’s degree; or 4 years’ experience with a PhD 
  • Must be a US Citizen with ability to obtain Special Access Program (SAP) Clearance  
  • Active DoD Secret clearance with investigation in the past 6 years.
  • Broad technical and MBSE experience, across multiple platforms, and encompassing the entire product development lifecycle.
  • Experience with DOORS
  • Experience developing models with Cameo or equivalent tools
  • Experience with Systems Modeling Language (SysML)

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

  • Previous Systems Engineering experience and understanding of the Systems Engineering V
  • Experience with physical security systems
  • Experience with Agile Development
  • Experience with Unified Profile for DoDAF and MoDAF (UPDM), Unified Architecture Framework (UAF), and/or the Department of Defense Architecture Framework (DoDAF).
  • Experience using Requirements repository tools (i.e., DOORS).
  • Active DoD Top Secret clearance
