Northrop Grumman Space Systems is currently seeking an early career systems engineer in support of the Strike Program in our Chandler, AZ office.

The selected candidate will support the full system development cycle from requirement identification/derivation to system verification. This position will require candidates to work with a team of systems engineers from a broad array of technical backgrounds, component design IPTs, and Program Management. To succeed in this role the ideal candidate should bring a positive attitude, enjoy collaboration across teams and disciplines, and showcase a strong technical background. The candidate should have strong technical and interpersonal skills with project experience to demonstrate that these requirements are met.

Systems Engineering Responsibilities:

  • Support development of design review content

  • Technical Problem Solving

  • Develop and Derive new System Requirements to meet customer capability needs

  • Manage requirements changes and updates within Requirements Management Databases

  • Creation and management of specification/interface definition documents

  • Participates in the creation of and manages changes to system or subsystem analytical models utilizing MBSE constructs.

  • Supports trade studies, system sensitivity studies, design optimization studies, system performance assessments, system design assessments, Operations Analysis work such as mission analysis and timelines and CONOPS development

  • Supports anti-tamper capability development

This position may be offered at the Associate Engineer Systems Level 1 or maybe offered at the Engineer Systems Level 2.

Basic Qualifications:

If offered at the Associate Engineer Systems Level 1 Requires BS in Engineering plus 0 years of experience.

If offered at the Engineer Systems Level 2 Requires BS in Engineering with a minimum of 2 years related experience, Master’s degree with 0 years of related experience

  • U.S. Citizenship is required

  • Active Secret clearance is required for this position

  • Strong oral and written communication skill 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • A strong experienced-based and academic foundation in Systems Engineering fundamentals

  • System testing and launch operations experience is desired

  • Certifications/Experience in System Security

  • Certifications/Experience in Anti-Tamper

  • Experience with Agile and Scrum is desired

  • Experience with IBM Rationale DOORS is desired

  • Experience with SysML (specifically Cameo) is desired
