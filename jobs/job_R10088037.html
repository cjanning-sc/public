ACME Engineering Mission Systems Sector is seeking a Senior Prinipal Contract Administrator to support the Land and Maritime Sensors Business Unit with its Power and Control Systems (PC/S) pursuits.

The preferred location for this position is on-site in Sykesville, MD with hybrid work schedule available at the discretion of the hiring manager. This position may also be offered as a remote schedule at the discretion of the hiring manager.

The Contract Administrator is responsible for supporting proposal preparation, contract negotiation, contract administration and customer contact activities to provide and ensure proper contract acquisition and fulfillment in accordance with company policies, legal requirements, and customer requirements. You will be an embedded member of the program management team, supporting program success and new growth opportunities across the portfolio. This position will require a thorough understanding of contracting and proposal experience negotiating complex terms and conditions. The position will be primarily focused on continued execution of various direct commercial sale procurement contracts , post production support and warranty. USG contracts are included within the portfolio as well as the requirement for extensive review and administration associated with select new business pursuits across the Business Unit.

Roles and responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Act as primary interface with customer for contractual issues; Monitor contract and associated program to ensure that contractual obligations are communicated and understood.
  • Participate in contract proposal preparation, contract negotiations, contract administration, and customer contract activities to manage assigned contracts in accordance with company policies, legal requirements, and customer specifications.
  • Work under general supervision to develop solutions to both routine and complex contractual matters to ensure company compliance with contract requirements on assigned programs.
  • Ensure fulfillment of contract requirements, manage contract changes and maintain historical information.
  • Review terms and conditions to address provisions and risks of financial terms, acceptance criteria, delivery requirements, warranty, intellectual property, termination provisions, indemnification and all other potential risk areas and recommend risk mitigation strategies.
  • Assist management in achieving financial goals related to cash management, EBIT and sales.
  • Prepare and manage written communications with internal and external customers for assigned contractual matters.
  • Enter and maintain contract data into Contract Management System and/or Enterprise Accounting System.
  • Ensure timely delivery of all contractual deliverables and submission of invoices, proposals, and data calls.

This role will have significant visibility within key strategic programs, including Division and Sector submittals and reviews. Candidate must manage tight deadlines in a complex environment and have the ability to meet high performance expectations in a dynamic environment.  Excellent communication is a must as the selected candidate will be directly involved in negotiations with the customer as a strategic team member. Our team is looking for someone who wants to grow and experience different contract types and resolve complex issues.

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree - 10 years of experience in a contracts related field - OR - Master's Degree with 8 years' experience as described.
  • Working knowledge of commercial and Government contracting.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite, (specifically Excel and PowerPoint), Adobe
  • Willingness and ability to travel domestically up to 10%
  • Current, active US Secret level security clearance.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Working knowledge of FAR/DFARS and/or government regulations
  • Law, business, or relevant advanced degre
  • 1ERP, SAP, and Teams 365
  • Experience/familiarity with large federal and/or commercial international programs
