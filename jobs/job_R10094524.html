There is currently an opening for an Associate Facilities Engineer (level 1) or Facilities Engineer (level 2) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position falls under one of Northrop Grumman's Small Business Units, Sonoma Photonics, Inc. and will be located in Santa Rosa, CA.

We are looking for candidates with knowledge and experience with high vacuum deposition equipment, component, and process operational theory.  Responsibilities include overseeing the design, build, and upgrade state-of-the-art manufacturing equipment and processes, supporting general maintenance of vacuum and electro-mechanical equipment, assisting with troubleshooting, problem analysis, and data collection, and maintaining detailed documentation of procedures and systems.

This position may be filled as an Associate Facilities Engineer (level 1) or Facilities Engineer (level 2)

  • Working knowledge of tools, common appliances and devices

  • Familiar with Solidworks

  • Experience with Machining and operation of CNC Mill

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance
