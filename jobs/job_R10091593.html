ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking an MBSE Systems Engineer/Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Detroit, Michigan and will support the Strategic Deterrent Systems Division to include the Sentinel (GBSD) program. The selected candidate does not need to have an active clearance, but must be able to obtain a security clearance prior to starting in this role. This position may be filled at a higher level based on qualifications listed below.

In this role, you will be responsible for ensuring and enforcing consistency on the Model-Based implementation of the architecture design.  You will work with the System Engineering & Integration (SE&I) team, as well as with Program and Element Systems Engineering teams. 

Responsibilities (may include any or all of the following):

  • Maintain structure and arrangement of the Cameo Model and remove inconsistent architectural artifacts
  • Create new and adapt existing models, methods, and analysis capabilities to conduct studies to support the design, development, and assessment of advanced system design concepts and technologies
  • Perform routine audits and provide findings to delinquent architects on the team
  • Remove all deprecated and duplicative design elements and errant relationships
  • Prepare model for final Major Design Review deliveries including construction of Architectural Overview diagrams and all MDR dashboards
  • Responsible for initial and subsequent synchronizations of data into the model from external databases.
  • Identify areas and methods for improvement on overall model organization and performance
  • Serve as direct point of contact to the System and other IPT-level model governors and model governance boards. Ensures architecture team consistency to overall Program Style and process guides

Basic Qualifications:

(This position may be filled at a Systems Engineer level or Principal level)

  • Bachelor's degree or higher in a Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) discipline
  • A minimum 2 years of related engineering experience with a Bachelor's degree; 0 years with Master’s degree (Principal level requires a minimum 5 years with a Bachelor's; 3 years with a Master's; or 0 years with a PhD)
  • Experience with Model-Based Engineering / Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBE/MBSE) practices, languages and/or tools such as Cameo, Rhapsody or MagicDRAW
  • US Citizenship and ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance (required to obtain prior to start)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD Secret/ Top Secret clearance, granted or re-investigated within the last 6 years or in continues evaluation
  • Experience modeling with Cameo Enterprise Architecture or MagicDraw (No Magic, Inc.)
  • Experience with requirements management/analysis/allocations using tools such as DOORS
  • Experience with architecture modeling language such as SysML, DoDAF or UML
  • Experience with complex system development on large programs
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills, both verbal and written
