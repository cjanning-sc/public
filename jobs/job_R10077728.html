The Financial Services Material Accounting team is looking for a motivated individual to join our team as a Principal Accountant.  This individual will serve as an integral team member in support of the Company's inventory and material accounting and will interact and collaborate with various sectors, divisions, and technical staff to solve complex inventory/material issues and questions.

This position is not location specific but must be within commuting distance to one of these main campuses: El Segundo, CA / San Diego, CA / Palmdale, CA / Melbourne, FL. Working remotely will only be allowed on a part-time basis. Candidate must have the ability to obtain Government Secret Level Clearance to support Classified programs (if not currently active).

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Review and collect data to prepare reports in accordance with the company's cost accounting system (SAP GPD) in accordance with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP), corporate policies and procedures, disclosure statements and government regulations, including MMAS and Cost Accounting Standards (CAS)
  • Prepare and present various reports/analyses, which could include material cost and inventory accuracy, price variance, allocations, and excess
  • Research, analyze, and interpret current and historical cost using Product Actual & Cost Estimating (PACE) Reports for activities such as: material and labor cost shifts, grouping/pegging/distribution, and goods receipt/invoice receipts (GR/IR)
  • Perform Material Master data maintenance, such as: Valuation Class, Material Group, and Material Type updates.
  • Prepare and/or Review documentation to post journal entries for various material accounting transactions.
  • Perform monthly general ledger account reconciliations for material accounts using Blackline.
  • Assist auditors with understanding of SAP GPD, PACE, and material accounting processes, as well as fulfill audit requests.
  • Drives continued success by finding and communicating opportunities for efficiencies, process improvement, and/or automation of processes, in support of the Company's digital transformation initiatives.
  • Supports other initiatives as needed.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in accounting, finance, business or related field and 5 years related experience; or 2 years related experience with a Masters degree
  • Knowledge of material management and cost transactions
  • Experience with SharePoint, AO/WEBI, SAP, Excel and Word
  • Strong knowledge of material costing systems and processes

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Secret Level clearance
  • Knowledge of compliance audit standards for MMAS
  • Strong ambition with the intent to work quickly and effectively, can get up to speed with business processes quickly and enjoys a dynamic environment
  • Experience in various business process and system capabilities in manufacturing and project management (i.e., cost accounting/MRP/Inventory/Production)
