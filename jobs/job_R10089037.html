What You’ll get to Do:

ACME Engineering Marines Systems specializes in the development, manufacture assembly and testing of propulsion, and power generation systems for naval surface ships and submarines. The Final Assembly Supervisor 2 will supervise the factory hourly assembly mechanics, electricians and production support team in the assembly, test and shipment of high quality, low volume, large electro-mechanical fabricated structures with complex controls for the Marine Propulsion and Power Generation SBU. This position will also interface with Sr. Operations Leadership, a variety of engineering disciplines, and end customers for the products their team produces and delivers.

The Business Operations Manager for the Operations team within Marine Systems will support the Senior Director of Manufacturing, other Manufacturing Directors, and site IPT Operations Directors (Quality & Missions Excellence and Global Supply Chain), as well as liaison with the division leadership team to manage all business operational aspects of the MLSS Operations organization.  The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, and collaborative/team settings across all levels. The position will report directly to the Senior Director of Manufacturing and will have a limited number of direct reports supporting specific assignments.
