Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Systems Engineer - Principal Cyber Security/ Sr. Principal Systems Engineer - Cyber Security to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in Melbourne, Fl. This position will be responsible for System Security Engineering (e.g. Cybersecurity, Anti-Tamper (AT), Program Protection). This position is located in Melbourne, FL.

The selected Cyber Systems Engineer will have experience with Cybersecurity engineering and implementations based on DoD, National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), Committee on National Security Systems Instruction (CNSSI), and Risk Management Framework (RMF) processes, policies and guidelines.

This position can be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

Essential Functions:

• This position requires knowledge and experience using systems security engineering methodologies and techniques.

• Requirements development and analysis, including supporting trade studies.

• System Architecture development and Modeling

• Must be able to prepare technical documents, provide briefings, support ongoing authorization efforts including development of supporting documentation, develop procedures, and work in a fast changing dynamic environment with minimal direction.

• Responsible for System Security Engineering duties as assigned.

• Interface with other cyber functional areas, internal company management and executives with a focus on effective program execution and mission assurance.

Basic Qualifications:

- Principal Cyber Systems Engineer: Bachelor’s Degree and 5+ years Cybersecurity experience, a Master’s degree and 3 years of experience, a PhD  and 0 years of experience, or in lieu of a degree 9 years of relevant experience may be considered.

- Sr. Principal Cyber Systems Engineer: Bachelor’s Degree and 9+ years Cybersecurity experience, a Master’s degree and 7 years of experience, a PhD and 4 years of experience, or in lieu of a degree 13+ years of relevant experience may be considered.

- Experience with DoD System Security Engineering (SSE) activities

- Experience with Risk Management Framework (RMF), Security Technical Implementation Guide (STIG) and requirements development from security controls

- Must have an active DoD Secret security clearance (with a background investigation completed within last 6 years or currently enrolled in Continuous Evaluation)

- Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance before establishing a start date (contingency)

Preferred Qualifications:

- Possess a DODD 8570 certification, such as CompTIA Security+, CISSP, or similar network/security certifications

- Experience with embedded computing environments and technologies

- Experience with cloud computing environments and technologies

- Experience working with and providing oversight and guidance to different IPTs and Suppliers/Subcontractors

- Experience with Model Based Systems Engineering tools such as Cameo or other similar system modeling tools

- Experience working with Agile methodologies and development tools (Jira, Confluence, etc.)

- Experience developing cybersecurity architectures

- Experience validating security assessments and reviews

- Familiarity with U.S Government Anti-Tamper (AT) processes and implementations

- Experience with NSA Type-1 certification process or Cross Domain Solution implementation and certification

- Experience working on a multidisciplinary team and writing technical proposals

- Excellent communication, interpersonal skills, and the ability to interface with all levels of employees and management

- Ability to obtain a DoD Top Secret security clearance to include a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI)
