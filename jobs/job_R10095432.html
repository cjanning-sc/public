ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Regulatory Compliance Analyst in support of the audit readiness for the Six DFARS Business Systems and to provide full spectrum of regulatory compliance support to the ACME Engineering Space Systems sector. The Regulatory Compliance Analyst will interface with senior level management and other company personnel to ensure organizational compliance with the DFAR Business Systems requirements, internal procedures, Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR), Cost Accounting Standards (CAS) and other government policies and procedures. This will require the ability to work effectively as a team member within both the Government Compliance function and the overall ACME Engineering organization.

  

The selected candidate will report to the Manager of Regulatory Compliance.  This role is located in Des Moines, Iowa.

Roles and Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Support business systems and other compliance activities as we seek opportunities to streamline and harmonize compliance activities
  • Support the continued execution of the Annual DFAR Business System Self-Assessment Plan.
  • Work with the DFARS business systems Leads to   ensure that all deficiencies are documented, Internal Corrective Action Reports (ICARS) established, Corrective Actions Implemented and closed.
  • Lead in business systems pre-audit activities in support of internal and external audits.
  • Coordinate with internal customers for audit responses and tracking.
  • Coordinate the scheduling of DCMA/DCAA meeting in support of business systems audits as needed.
  • Assist with Compliance Principles and Operating Procedures (PrOP) and Maintenance of Sector Compliance Technical Manual and Associated Procedures.
  • Contributes to other Government Compliance tasks, projects, and process improvement activities
  • Will continually demonstrate knowledge of relevant FAR and CAS rules/regulations to assist in assignments.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree
  • 9 years of relevant experience in regulatory compliance analysis, accounting, finance, internal audits, billing, contracts or a related discipline
  • Experience working with DCAA auditors and basic understanding of the Defense Contract Audit Manual (DCAM)
  • Basic knowledge of U.S. Government regulations, including Cost Accounting Standards (CAS), TINA and DFARS)
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office suite, with added emphasis on Excel and PowerPoint
  • Well-developed oral and written communication skills to successfully work across business units at various levels and disciplines.
  • Ability to obtain a security clearance.  US citizenship is a prerequisite

Preferred Qualifications:

  • An overall understanding of accounting, business systems, reporting systems, budgeting, estimating, internal controls
  • A practical knowledge of FAR, CAS, and other related rules, regulations, laws, or issued guidance (i.e. DCAM, DFARS, etc.)
  • Strong interpersonal communication, problem solving, conflict resolution, time management, writing, and team building skills.
  • Ability to learn all ACME policies and procedures required to interpret issues, determine solutions, and evaluate their impact on the ACME business environment.
