Northrop Grumman Aerospace Structures Business Unit (ASBU) has an opening for a Manager General Manufacturing 1 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Clearfield, UT.

The General Manufacturing Manager 1 is responsible for the direct supervision of employees and processes in a manufacturing environment. The Manager provides direction to employees utilizing management guidelines and general policies. Overall responsibilities include: driving employee efficiency, meeting product cost and schedule targets, managing the area to produce quality products, and engaging employees to achieve their highest potential.


The Manager is expected to work independently across organizations; manage the use of consumables to achieve budget targets; assign and manage Operators to maximize efficiency and achieve target productive yields; implement our process improvements to improve affordability and quality.

Responsibilities include:

  • Directs and coordinates the activities to plan, prepare, issue, and control production schedules, in conjunction with Material Requirements Planning, to meet production/delivery requirements
  • Oversees the status of work in process, material availability, and potential production problems to ensure that equipment, materials, and services are available, as needed
  • Assigns and manage staff to maximize efficiency and achieve schedule and target productive yield.
  • Provides specific direction and training, as required
  • Ensures 5S practices within cell areas
  • Utilizes both internal and external resources, and coordinates cell and functional activities with quality assurance, manufacturing, supply chain, engineering, inventory control, transportation, etc

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree or higher and prior experience in a manufacturing, production, or engineering environment; OR at least 5 years of work experience in a manufacturing, production, or engineering environment may be considered in lieu of a degree
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Security Clearance
  • Ability to work any shift

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD Secret Security Clearance
  • 5 or more years of aerospace manufacturing experience
  • Prior leadership experience in a managerial, lead, or supervisory capacity
  • Prior experience managing in a manufacturing environment
