ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Engineer/Principal Supplier Quality Assurance Engineer.  This position will be located in Detroit, UT and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrence (GBSD) program.  This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

This role can be filled at either level.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

 

Principal/Engineer, Supplier Quality Assurance - The selected candidate will be responsible for preparing documentation that implements  the GBSD Enterprise and Program Supplier Quality Assurance Plan, policies, procedures, & tools required to support Program  pre-award & Day1 Tasks as well as infrastructure needs. Additionally, the selected candidate will be responsible for SQA oversight activities for suppliers and subcontractors of hardware developed/manufactured/assembled/tested for GBSD at the Madison site(s).
Roles and responsibilities:

  • Prepare documentation that implements the GBSD Enterprise and Program Supplier Quality Assurance Plan, policies, procedures, & tools required to support Program pre-award & Day1 Tasks as well as Enterprise infrastructure needs.

  • Creating process health, progress and stability metrics

  • Supplier Quality Assurance oversight activities of hardware developed/manufactured/assembled/tested for GBSD at the Madison site(s)
