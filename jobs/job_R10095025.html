ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking Staff Process Software Engineer.   This position is in Detroit, UT and supports the Software Directorate and the SW Process & Governance Manager in developing processes for the SDS Division. We may also consider remote work. 

What You’ll Get To Do:

You are a Staff Process Software Engineer working for the SDS Division. You will review and further adapt SDS processes to ensure they are relevant to the SDS portfolio.  This individual would also assist in creating new processes to fill in any gaps that are identified as it relates to software development (for example a rapid prototype SDP, metrics best practices, NIMBUS, CPLM, etc.).

The division is partnering with the Sentinel program to increase the awareness of the software processes.  A subtask of this activity is to work with the SQA team to increase their scope and their personnel.  The individual in this role will be the focal point that will assist with:

  • ensuring SQA alignment to program objectives.
  • assessment of SQA processes and procedures.
  • identifying process gaps
  • assist with making process updates to close the gaps identified. 

Additionally, as it relates to SQA, this individual will review training materials to ensure we have comprehensive training that is properly aligned with the program SDPs.  This individual will also support risk assessments and/or TRB/CCB package development that recommends staffing augmentation and any new processes activities necessary to ensure compliance (i.e. an SQA sign off for all software peer reviews, etc.)  Develop and administer training to communicate the updates to the existing SQA team, recommend staffing augmentation where necessary, and monitor to ensure compliance.

In coordination with the SDS Software Process and Governance manager, this role will have full responsibility for ensuring the software related processes are clear, documented, and sufficient to meet program objectives.  This would include Software Quality Assurance processes.  The individual in this role is expected to bring relevant experience to lead, guide and coach the rest of the team.

In this case the initial customer would be the SDS programs and extended customers would be program customers (i.e., the government).  The interaction with the SDS programs would be significant to make sure that the division is getting the information needed to develop processes that make since for advanced programs or IRADS, EMD development programs and sustainment programs.  This individual may present our processes to government counterparts to assist in alignment of processes.  This individual will also present progress of process development to division and program leadership.
