ACME Engineering Defense Systems Sector has a need for a Senior Principal Embedded Software Engineer / Staff Embedded Software Engineer at its Subsystem and Software Sustainment (S&SS) organization in Kansas City, MO. This engineering position is for a role on our Application Software Engineering Team. In this role you will be supporting the APN-241 Radar Program. You will be an integral part of an interdisciplinary team of engineers focused on the modernization and sustainment of the APN-241 system. Our ideal candidate will possess technical expertise and collaborative people skills with the proven ability to work within a technically diverse and fast-paced team. This position is located in Kansas City, MO

Your responsibilities will include:

  • Serve as a software and systems subject matter expert in a wide range of technical areas that include algorithm development, data analysis, system troubleshooting, system testing, and software development

  • Develop, modify, and maintain software using software engineering best practices and standards. 

  • Software development including automated testing to facilitate and analyze large data sets.

  • Participate in the full life cycle of software development, to include requirements development, modeling and design, software development, unit to CSCI testing, integration, formal system testing, release, installation, and maintenance.

  • Apply technical expertise in solving complex integration problems.  

  • The candidate must have the ability to lead, communicate, support, and collaborate on technical issues for the program

This position may be filled as either a Sr. Principal Embedded Software Engineer or Staff Embedded Software Engineer.

Basic Qualifications for Sr. Principal Embedded Software Engineer:

  • Bachelor of Science (B.S) degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or an equivalent STEM related discipline

  • Minimum of 9 years of direct relevant experience with B.S degree; Minimum of 7 years of direct relevant experience with Masters degree; Minimum of 5 years of direct relevant experience with PhD.

  • Expert in C/C++

  • Exceptional experience in all areas of formal software lifecycle process from requirements to testing

  • Proven leadership experience on software development programs

  • Experience in object-oriented analysis and design

  • Must currently hold or be able to obtain and maintain a U.S Department of Defense (DoD) Secret level security clearance

Basic Qualifications for Staff Embedded Software Engineer

  • Bachelor of Science (B.S) degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or an equivalent STEM related discipline

  • Minimum of 14 years of direct relevant experience with B.S degree; Minimum of 12 years of direct relevant experience with Masters degree; Minimum of 10 years of direct relevant experience with PhD

  • Expert in C/C++

  • Exceptional experience in all areas of formal software lifecycle process from requirements to testing

  • Proven leadership experience on software development programs

  • Experience in object-oriented analysis and design

  • Must currently hold or be able to obtain and maintain a U.S Department of Defense (DoD) Secret level security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Expert knowledge in RADAR systems and concepts

  • Expert knowledge in DSP

  • Expert knowledge in embedded systems and RTOS

  • Experience in Java

  • Experience using JIRA/Bitbucket and Git/Mercurial/Bazaar version control tools

  • Experience using LabVIEW

  • Experience using MATLAB

  • Experience working in a fast pace, Agile environment with the ability to work on a cross-functional Scrum team focused on quality and capability delivery

  • Experience with Electronic Warfare (EW) principles

  • Experience in Automated Testing Techniques

  • Active Secret Security Clearance
