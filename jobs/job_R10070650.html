What you’ll get to do:

Northrop Grumman Marine Systems specializes in the development, manufacturing, assembly, and testing of missile launching and handling systems, propulsion, and power generation systems for naval surface ships and submarines.  The Reliability and System Safety Engineering Department ensures reliability and system safety are key considerations throughout the program life cycle starting with design and development through system retirement. 

The Principal/Sr. Principal Reliability Systems Engineer we are seeking will be an individual who thrives in a diverse work environment, has outstanding communication skills and possesses a strong sense of engagement and continuous process improvement. The Principal/Sr. Principal Reliability Systems Engineer performs technical planning, requirements development, system integration, verification and validation.  Analyses are performed at all levels and include: concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal. The Principal/Sr. Principal Reliability Systems Engineer ensures the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule and cost constraints. A materials engineering oriented systems engineer capable of developing and conducting electrical, mechanical and non-destructive test procedures with the ability to assess material/component service life is preferred. A fundamental knowledge and/or the interest in developing skills in Reliability and Systems Safety Engineering is also preferred.
