ACME Engineering Enterprise Systems is looking for a Security Coordinator 3 to join our team of diverse and talented individuals at our Louisville, AZ location.

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Supporting the Lenel based Enterprise Physical Access Control System (PACS) and SLOPACS infrastructures within the Enterprise Security Control Center (ESCC).

  • Assist with troubleshooting network and hardware issues affecting ESCC operations.

  • Produce employee and guest badges, identification cards, and security reader cards. Processes security information and data for employment records, security clearances, and property control. Maintains lock and key records as required.

  • Processes visit authorizations for employees and guests to gain access to restricted areas.

  • Verifies accuracy of information prior to issuing accesses. Maintains controlled security documents and information.

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School diploma or equivalent and 4 years of additional education and/or experience related to Security

  • Active DoD Secret clearance (Eligible for DoD TS & SAP & SCI)

  • Knowledge of MS Windows servers and client application connections

Preferred Qualifications:

  • In-scope SSBI / Current SCI and/or SAP program access

  • Preferred experience in Lenel OnGuard or similar physical security Access Control System

  • Technical aptitude and experience in application support and troubleshooting

  • Knowledge of WAN\LAN networks and IP addressing

  • Experience presenting project overviews and providing user training

  • Ability to maintain application standardization requirements

  • Ability to communicate technical information between departments

  • Able to organize and schedule multiple projects simultaneously.

  • A positive and outgoing personality
