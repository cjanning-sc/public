Northrop Grumman Mission Systems (NGMS) is looking for you to join our team as a Steam Turbine Testing Supervisor based in Sunnyvale, CA.

What you’ll get to do:

  • Manage the Test Facility Operating Team and oversee Test Facility Operations in support of Test Campaigns and individual component testing for quality; and to maximize schedule and cost performance.
  • Manage the support for all aspects and phases of Test operations from inception through completion.
  • Responsible for the cost and schedule performance of Operations programs and/or subsystems of major programs in the Test areas.
  • Monitors and drives adherence to master plans and schedules, cost performance to budgets. Provides cost and schedule performance data/status as requested.
  • Identifies process problems and working with Manufacturing Test Engineering develops solutions.
  • Directs the work of assigned Employees.  Interfaces with Employee representatives as required.  Conducts performance reviews with Employees and provides timely feedback on performance.
  • Coordinate, Maintain and track Employee Training/Certifications as required.
  • Work with Manufacturing Test Engineering of the Preparation, Maintenance and Operation of the Test Facilities.  Assist in facility planning, maintenance and improvements.
