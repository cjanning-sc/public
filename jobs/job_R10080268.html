ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Contractor Logistics Support (CLS) Manager 3 (Manager, Programs 3) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Cincinnati, OH with frequent travel to CONUS and OCONUS locations.

In this role, the selected candidate will be responsible for the CLS operations and support for manned and/or unmanned systems. Will provide leadership to an organization responsible for maintenance training, strategic planning, resource allocation, and system effectiveness metrics. Control the efficient flow of goods, services, and information between point of origin through customer placement to meet customer requirements. Plan, direct, manage and control the cost, schedule and technical performance of the organization working with cross-functional teams from other ACME Engineering entities. Develop and forecast maintenance operations and execution plans for manpower, training, and support, as well as additional Integrated Logistics Support, including maintenance training concepts, technical publications, system deployment activities, and the strategic plans for the standup of various systems. Analyze maintenance and logistic performance indicators, identify trends/root cause and implement corrective actions. Coordinate provisions for maintenance facilities, personnel, and support equipment, spares, repairs, consumables and implement repair and resupply concepts with detailed execution plans.

As a leader, you always act with integrity, and the highest ethical standards characterize everything you do. You enable your team to achieve predictable and balanced results, while satisfying the needs of internal and external partners. You treat all people with respect, and you model personal growth and continuous development. You know how to build an inclusive environment that attracts, retains, and inspires a diverse and engaged team. You develop trusted and valued customer relationships and communicate effectively, so that people throughout the organization feel engaged and connected to their work. You deliver excellence, strive for continuous improvement, and respond vigorously to change.


Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree with 9 years of related CLS; OR a Master’s Degree with 7 years of related aircraft CLS.

  • At least 5 years of experience managing an organization with 20+ individuals as well as multi-million dollar budgets.

  • Current and active Secret clearance with the ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access is required. This Special Program Access must be obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science or Master of Science degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math (STEM) discipline

  • Experience in the military leading tactical and maintenance teams with increasing levels of responsibility, including recent (past 5 years) manned/unmanned system leadership and/or combat experience.

  • Current Top Secret clearance
