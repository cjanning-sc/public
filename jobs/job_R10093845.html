The Tactical Space Systems Mechanical Engineering organization is seeking a mid-level Structural Analysis & Test Engineer to assist an experienced team of mechanical engineers performing stress/dynamic analysis and testing of one or more spacecraft systems or subsystems.

Responsibilities may include the following:

  • Performing static and dynamic structure analysis.
  • Reviewing requirements for materials, structure systems & subsystems.
  • Selecting appropriate technical information and data from applicable products.
  • Determining the technical principles involved, identifying the design path forward and computing the engineering characteristics to ensure consistency with the specifications design needs and requirements of space applications.
  • Releasing results as “technical documents” after review by the senior members of the team.
  • Developing innovative solutions and elaborating on design concepts.
  • Evaluating completed designs with supervisor and other technical groups, and making modifications necessary to achieve the most ideal design. 
  • Developing, coordinating, and conducting test programs required to evaluate design work.

This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.


Basic Qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either a Structural Analysis & Test Engineer 3 or a Structural Analysis & Test Engineer 4.

Basic Qualifications for Structural Analysis & Test Engineer 3:

  • Bachelor’s degree in STEM - Science, Technology, Engineer, Math or related area (Mechanical Engineering or Degrees with a focus in Structural Analysis highly preferred) and 5+ years of experience or Master’s Degree and 3+ years or a PhD.
  • Ability to perform structural engineering tasks associated with analysis, design, and/or testing of structures.
  • Experience with at least one of the following Mechanical Analysis tools such as NX FEMAP/NASTRAN, ABAQUS, HYPERMESH. (Equivalent spacecraft structural analysis tools may be considered on a case-by-case basis).
  • Experience utilizing MATLAB

Basic Qualifications for Structural Analysis & Test Engineer 4:

  • Bachelor’s degree in STEM - Science, Technology, Engineer, Math or related area (Mechanical Engineering or Degrees with a focus in Structural Analysis highly preferred) and 9+ years of experience or Master’s Degree and 7+ years or a PhD and 4+ years of experience.
  • Active Top Secret security clearance
  • Ability to perform structural engineering tasks associated with analysis, design, and/or testing of structures.
  • Experience with at least one of the following Mechanical Analysis tools such as NX FEMAP/NASTRAN, ABAQUS, HYPERMESH. (Equivalent spacecraft structural analysis tools may be considered on a case-by-case basis).
  • Experience utilizing MATLAB

Preferred Qualifications Principal Structural Analysis & Test Engineer 3 & Sr Principal Structural Analysis & Test Engineer 4:

  • Experience with Structural Analysis of Dynamic Test Fixtures & Mechanical Ground Support Equipment (MGSE)
  • Experience with one or more of the following types of testing: static load testing, sine/random vibration, mechanical shock testing, vibro-acoustic testing, and dynamics/kinematics testing
  • Experience with Static/Dynamics Test Planning & Execution (Test Director Role)
  • The ability to take initiative and coordinate planning of tests with multi-disciplinary teams and stakeholders
  • The ability to create and maintain detailed and accurate test schedules and to coordinate test support in a fast-paced and changing environment
  • Experience with preparing and presenting comprehensive Test Readiness Review (TRR) packages
  • Experience with troubleshooting efforts, failure investigations, and implementation of lessons learned related to testing
  • Test/Analysis Finite Element Model (FEM) correlation experience.
  • The ability to build strong working relationships with a diverse team. 
  • Strong communication and leadership skills
