Capture Affordability Senior Manager
R10090968
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems is looking for a Capture Affordability Sr. Manager to join a team of qualified and diverse individuals on a corporate priority program.  This position is a key role part of the strategy and competitiveness team. This position is located in Melbourne, FL.

This role is ideal for a leader and strategic thinker with a strong inclination for action and a proven ability to consistently model Northrop Grumman’s values across diverse, high performing, cross-functional teams. 

Responsibilities Include, but are not limited to:

  • Lead capture affordability within the competitiveness team to ensure strategy alignment across the organization
  • Lead team of cost and affordability engineers
  • Lead cost working group discussions with customer team, and serve as focal for capture/competitiveness team
  • Responsible for cost analyses to include Life Cycle Cost Analysis, and Design to Cost Analysis to all phases of program
  • Synthesize cost analyses clearly for multiple audiences, internally and externally
  • Deliver cost products on time, with high quality
  • Drive coordination and partnership with program and IPT leaders to implement affordability targets and objectives
  • Drive culture initiatives to engage and inspire engineering teams to meet affordability objectives
  • Identify opportunities to integrate affordability approach and analysis with GSC and industry teaming implementation
  • Ensure close coordination and integration with leaders within capture strategy and competitiveness team
  • Lead forums across program and capture IPTs to communicate affordability objectives, cost reduction initiatives and performance against plan
  • Lead collaboration across teams to receive regular inputs from engagements with customers, and other key engagements
  • Foster a culture that respectfully challenges critical assumptions, highlight blind spots, and actively seek multiple perspectives to inform and influence affordability approach
  • Enable operational cadence, structure, and rhythm for the affordability team

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 7+ years professional experience or Master's Degree and 5+ years professional experience managing complex projects, initiatives, or teams
  • Experience developing briefings and interfacing with executive leadership teams
  • Active DoD Secret clearance (to start/apply) with the ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access (PAR) within a reasonable amount of time as determined by business needs

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current Special Program access (PAR)
  • Demonstrated ability to zoom in and zoom out with detailed analyses
  • Experience establishing clear objectives and roadmaps to achieve objectives
  • Demonstrated depth in technical and/or business acumen
  • Demonstrated experience working on a large capture
  • Demonstrated experience leading people and teams
  • Demonstrated experience in delivering on commitments, working in cross-functional teams, and ability to adapt to changes in a dynamic environment
  • Demonstrated roles of increasing responsibility and progressive leadership experience
  • Demonstrated experience working in cross-functional roles in a matrix organization
  • Familiarity with enterprise ProP and industry best practices on affordability
  • Ability to successfully engage across all levels in the organization
