The Chief Engineer is responsible for the technical direction and execution of all aspects of engineering on a program including design, development, implementation, integration, testing, and delivery of product to the customer. The Chief Engineer is the technical leader for the program engineering team, and also serves as technical advisor to the program manager. The Chief Engineer is typically the primary technical point-of-contact to the customer. The Chief Engineer coordinates across the program’s Systems Engineering team and other engineering teams. The Chief Engineer ensures that all engineering teams adhere to sound engineering processes, in accordance with company policies, procedures, and quality standards.

  • Experience leading technical teams 

  • Experience in systems development, integration, and test  

  • Good communication skills 

  • 20+ Years with Bachelors in Science; 18+ Years with Masters; 15+ Years with PhD .

  • Active DoD Secret clearance
