ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking an Administrative Assistant 3 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Columbus, CA.

Where Technology and Teamwork come together...The qualified applicant will become part of ACME Engineering's Laser and Sensor Subsystems Department.

Roles and Responsibilities:
• Setting up and maintaining files including private/proprietary and other sensitive data
• Arranging meetings and conferences; receiving, referring, or distributing mail
• Coordinating calendars
• Arranging travel (domestic and international)
• Timely and accurate processing of expense reports
• Generation of visit requests
• Creating/editing various documents in Microsoft Office tools
• Assisting in maintaining the organization's collaboration websites
• Managing inventory of and placing/tracking orders for office supplies and general support equipment
• Managing and tracking department resources as cameras, computers and other equipment
• Maintaining seating assignments for personnel
• Upkeep of conference rooms
• Answering questions relating to office operations and established policies and procedures
• Collaborating with administrative professionals in related organizations as appropriate
• Coordinating support from IT focal points, facilities, and security as needed and escorting visitors as required
• Managing organizational announcements and communication bulletin boards

This position requires strong interpersonal skills in order to maintain contact and work effectively with all levels of management, employees, and internal/external stakeholders. The selected candidate must work effectively with others to ensure seamless operations for the department. This individual must be articulate, with excellent written and verbal communication skills and demonstrated proficiency of all Microsoft Office tools. This role will require the candidate to interface with senior-level personnel in ACME Engineering as well as external stakeholders and requires a high degree of professionalism at all times. The selected candidate must have a demonstrated ability to maintain confidentiality while performing a wide variety of sensitive administrative and support tasks. The successful candidate must be a self-starter, highly motivated, able to independently execute tasks, and will be expected to multi-task and balance daily activities. The use of good judgment, discretion and uncompromising standards of quality, integrity and performance are essential.
