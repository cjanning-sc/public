The Payload and Ground Systems organization within the Northrop Grumman Space Systems pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter. Come be a part of our mission.

 

The Space Components and Strategic Businesses Unit is seeking a Sr Principal Research & Development Engineer to join our Engineering team. This position will be based in San Diego, CA, but may be located at our other Operating Unit (OU) site locations in Devens, MA, Beltsville, MD, or Oxnard, Goleta, or Commerce, CA.

Overview

The Senior Principal Research and Design Engineer will develop plans to ensure a strategic, business unit-wide view of technology investment planning. The Senior Principal R&D Engineer will update, create, and manage strategic roadmaps for development of advanced technologies. This team member will perform project management duties for internal research and development (IRAD), including management of spending, scheduling, staffing, and budgeting. 

This team member will work closely with each OU to identify synergies between OU product lines for development projects, and prioritize projects which best align with higher-level business strategies to capitalize on emerging markets. A strong background in defense and space applications, as well as an awareness of current global conditions to identify and appropriately select investment opportunities is essential to this role.

This individual will participate in intellectual property evaluations and development of patent applications and may coordinate interdepartmental activities and research efforts.

The ideal candidate will demonstrate proficiency in the following areas:

  • Strong background in project management

  • Initiating, designing, developing, executing and implementing scientific research projects.

  • Developing theories for understanding, characterizing and organizing natural phenomena into a systematic and meaningful pattern for research into potential new products or inventions.

  • Ability to interpret and understand technical papers and presentations for a wide range of applications

  • Ability to carefully analyze complex problems and develop innovative solutions.

  • Broad knowledge of industry practices.

  • Ensures solutions are consistent with organization objectives.

  • Must be able to work independently and have strong organizational skills.

  • Skillfully perform complex professional tasks and apply regular use of ingenuity and innovation.

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills and ability to effectively and clearly present technical approaches and findings.

  

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM discipline from an accredited university and 9+ years of engineering, process development and/or project management experience OR Master’s Degree in STEM discipline with 7+ year experience OR PhD in STEM discipline with 4+ year experience

  • Minimum 2 years of experience working on R&D projects

  • Minimum 2 years of experience in project management

  • Skilled in Microsoft Office Programs (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, Project)

  • Ability to obtain DoD Secret clearance

 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Project Management Professional PMP certification

  • Experience with, and knowledge of hardware design and manufacturing processes for space & aerospace applications

  • Active DoD Secret or Top Secret Clearance
