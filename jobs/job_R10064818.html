We are seeking a Configuration Analyst to support operations in our Oxnard/Goleta location.  This position is available at the Northrop Grumman facility at Oxnard/Goleta CA (% allocation varying throughout year based on site project goals/needs, but remote work capable), as part of the Northrop Grumman Payload and Ground systems Division, Space Components and Strategic Business Deployables Operating Unit, which specializes in deployable systems for spacecraft such as solar arrays, instrument booms, extendible support structures, and reflector boom antennas/deployable booms/masts.

Overview:

Reporting to the Configuration Management manager, the Configuration Analyst will plan and coordinate preparation of project documentation, such as engineering drawings, production specifications and schedules, and contract modifications, to ensure customer contract requirements are met. Reviews contract to determine documentation required for each phase of project, applying knowledge of engineering and manufacturing processes. Analyzes proposed changes of product design to determine effect on overall product and system. Coordinates modification records for management control. Establishes change orders and prepares for change authorization and documentation by company and subcontractor. Reviews and analyzes released engineering change data and coordinates changes with engineering, quality, support, manufacturing, and engineering data control activities

Responsibilities:

  • Establish and maintain program documents and baselines through the lifecycle of the program

  • Perform document revision audits to verify compliance with contract requirements and/or internal engineering baselines

  • Perform regular documents configuration audits on established baselines

  • Data entry of part numbers or documents into MRP system (CostPoint)

  • Verify accuracy and completeness of external and internal drawings and specifications to ensure compliance with company CM standards before release into the appropriate program via CM Databases or PLM system (Enovia & Teamcenter)

  • Create documentation in support of internal customers

  • Route internal documentation for review/approval

  • Maintain procedures for storage, retention and destruction of company records

  • Issue, track and distribute engineering documentation to the shop floors

  • Other functions as assigned

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree and 2 years experience in Configuration Management, or an additional 4 years experience in lieu of a degree

  • Understanding of Configuration Management principals and application to the performance of Key Responsibilities

  • Demonstrated use Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access) to document and manage business processes

  • Excellent communication skills – presentation, oral, written, and listening.

  • Self-motivated with the ability to organize and prioritize job assignments and tasks.

  • Ability to work independently as well as in an integrated team and adapt to change and dynamic requirements.

  • Strong critical thinking and logical reasoning skills with the ability to solve issues

  • U.S. Citizenship is a bona fide requirement

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 2+ years experience in Configuration Management

  • Demonstrated knowledge of PLM systems such as Teamcenter or Enovia

  • Demonstrated knowledge of Configuration Management principles

  • Ability to read and interpret drawings and blue prints

Physical Requirements:

  • Ability to sit for extended periods of time and light physical effort required
