The Payload and Ground Systems organization within the Northrop Grumman Space Systems pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter. Northrop Grumman, Space Systems is looking to fill multiple Manager, Programs Level 2 roles to support the Resiliency and Advanced Programs (RAP) operating unit in the fast-growing Payload & Ground Systems (PGS) Division.  The primary objective will be the delivery of all requirements on cost and on schedule, while achieving all technical requirements.  The individual(s) will lead a cross-functional organization aligned to common performance goals related to investment projects and/or customer contracts.

Program Managers are responsible for coordination and management of all phases of program execution, from inception through completion.  In this position, the selected Program Manager may oversee one or more programs within each Operating Unit organization.  Based on the requirements of the programs, the responsibilities may include but are not limited to:

  • Adherence to all internal processes for Program Management
  • Leading and directing a cross-functional Integrated Product Team (IPT) to achieve technical objectives
  • Development and adherence to Integrated Master Schedules (IMS)
  • Development and adherence to budget baselines
  • Development and delivery of presentations to leadership and program stakeholders
  • Proposal development / capture planning and strategy
  • Creation, review and finalization of the program Statement of Work
  • Establishment of schedule milestones and spend-plan
  • Development of stakeholder relationships and cultivation of stakeholder intimacy

These positions may be based out of our Baltimore, MD and Rolling Meadows, IL.  Other PGS locations may be considered as well.

This requisition represents multiple opportunities in our organization and may be used to identify candidates with skills and experience relevant to our portfolio.  Candidates who express an interest may be considered for immediate or future positions.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree and 7 years (or Master’s degree and 5 years) experience working in engineering, business management, or another related field.  In lieu of a degree, an additional 4 years of relevant experience will be considered.
  • 2 years program or project management experience, leading the performance of tasks on schedule, at cost, and achieving all requirements as either project lead, integrated program team lead, or cost account manager
  • Demonstrated success aligning teams, organizations, or employee resource groups to achieve a common goal. 
  • U.S. Citizenship
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a TS/SCI clearance with a polygraph.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current TS clearance or higher is strongly preferred.
  • The ability to apply knowledge, insights and understanding of business and financial concepts, tools and processes to the benefit of program decisions, actions, and performance.
  • The ability to manage communications with stakeholders through organized processes to ensure that program information is defined, collected, shared, understood, stored and retrieved in a manner that effectively meets program and stakeholder needs.
  • The ability to identify and address program impacts through a systematic, proactive, approach to issue and problem resolution that identifies, communicates, monitors, and promptly resolves conflicts across all levels of the program.
