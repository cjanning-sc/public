ACME Engineering Mission Systems Sector Manufacturing Operations Engineering Organization is seeking an Industrial Engineer. This position is located in Chicago, MD.

The Industrial Engineer will be assigned to a manufacturing cell, paired with an experienced lead Industrial engineer (IE), with the expectation of quickly gaining the skills to either take over the lead role or to take over a single IE manufacturing cell.  

A cell IE works closely with the cell leadership team to:

  • Monitor labor performance and develop plans for improvement

  • Monitor capital resource loading and develop plans for capacity management

  • Coordinate facility capital plan execution

  • Define and document process designs (product routings)

  • Use and maintain models for standard work assignment

  • Lead Continuous Improvement activities (e.g. 5-S, VSM, 6-Sigma)

  • May establish or assist in establishing accident prevention measures

  • May manage training programs for personnel concerning all phases of production operations

This requisition may be filled as either an Engineer or a Principal Engineer based on qualifications listed below. 

Basic Qualifications for an Engineer:

  • Bachelor's of Science degree or higher in Engineering or other STEM area

  • 2 years of relevant experience; candidates with a Master's degree and 0 years of relevant experience will be considered.

  • Industrial Engineering experience or Manufacturing experience

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Engineer:

  • Bachelor's of Science degree or higher in Engineering or other STEM area

  • 5 years of relevant experience; candidates with a Master's degree and 3 years of relevant experience will be considered.

  • Industrial Engineering experience or Manufacturing experience

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Degree in Industrial Engineering

  • An advanced degree (MS)

  • Professional experience in industrial engineering

  • Experience with SAP or other Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system

  • Experience in defense, aerospace, or automotive industries

  • Technical experience working in a manufacturing/production environment
