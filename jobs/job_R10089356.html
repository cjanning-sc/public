The ACME Engineering Mission Systems (NGMS) Operations Organization is seeking an Operations Manager, Material Control 3 to join their team in Springfield, MD.

What You'll Get to Do:

The Operations Manager for Material Control reports to the Operations Director for Production and Material Control, and is responsible for Receiving, Receiving Inspection, Warehousing, Kitting and Internal Material Handling. The Manager has 4 direct reports: 3 managers and 1 professional. The Manager has direct responsibility for 45 ACME Engineering employees at the BWI campus, but also indirect responsibility for 100+ contractors at a Ryder Logistics facility in nearby Curtis Bay. The ACME Engineering team is a mix of union represented and non-represented employees. The Manager is responsible for maintaining the flow of material from vendors, through inventory and to the manufacturing shop floor. Much of this activity is newly performed by Ryder Logistics under ACME Engineering oversight. The Manager directs the sub-contract with Ryder, ensuring performance to prescribed Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), but also partners with Ryder leadership to improve Safety, Quality, Delivery and Cost of material operations. The Manager oversees the handoff of material from Ryder personnel to their ACME Engineering team for transport to the point of use. The Manager is responsible for compliance to all Material Management Accounting practices and reporting of quality control metrics to internal and Government auditors. This responsibility includes ownership of several internal quality control documents and procedures. Temporarily, the Manager will oversee completion of the transition of material operations from ACME Engineering facilities into a Ryder Logistics facility.  

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree with 9+ years of experience
  • Management and/or leadership experience
  • Experience in a manufacturing, production, logistics or repair environment
  • Demonstrated basic understanding of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)/Material Resource Planning (MRP) systems
  • Fluent in MS Office Software applications (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain a clearance

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience working with SAP
  • Experience in warehousing, logistics or supply chain operations
  • Lean six sigma, world class manufacturing or similar continuous improvement experience
  • Current Secret or Top-Secret clearance
  • Demonstrated excellent written and verbal communication skills, including the ability to effectively present to upper management
  • Working knowledge of Materials Management Accounting Systems (MMAS)
  • Experience managing Third Party Logistics Providers (3PL)
  • Experience working in a union represented environment
