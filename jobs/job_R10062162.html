Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Principal Mechanical Engineer to join the System Support Team. This position is in Roy, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will be responsible for creating and implementing deployable structures and mechanical mechanisms at the cutting edge of space technology.  You will have ownership of your work from the concept creation through the design and analysis phases and may also participate in the manufacturing, testing, and integration of the systems.  This role will utilize a Systems Engineering approach to establish Support Equipment functional and performance requirements and will include involvement in all phases of research and product lifecycle development starting with requirements, initial concepts, trade studies, early prime equipment design influence, design and development of the Support Equipment item, fabrication/procurement, engineering evaluation testing, verification, delivery of hardware, and support of delivered hardware.

Roles and responsibilities:

The candidate will be responsible for their projects, preparing reports, and briefing leadership.  Frequent inter-organization and outside customer communication is required, and the candidate represents the organization in providing solutions to moderate-to-difficult technical issues associated with specific projects.  Will utilize an ability to handle multiple tasks in a dynamic environment, including critical thinking and technical problem-solving skills
