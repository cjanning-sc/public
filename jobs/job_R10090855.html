ACME Engineering Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is seeking a Human Resources Project Analyst to drive and lead the planning and facilitation of various human resources initiatives and processes, such as employee engagement, succession planning, metrics reporting and data analysis. The HR Project Analyst will collaborate with a diverse set of stakeholders and cross-business team members to meet project deliverables. As a thought leader on the SSSD HR leadership team, this position will help develop talent priorities and generate ideas of how to execute them within the division. The role will play an integral role in project tracking and team execution, drive the communication flow with all constituents to ensure project success and seek out and share best practices for continuous improvement.  This role is located in our Columbus OH facility but telework may be considered.

As a HR Project Analyst, you will partner with the Division HR Director to drive strategic human capital plans that support sector and enterprise goals, providing HR guidance and counsel to leaders and managers regarding succession planning and talent development, reward and recognition, compensation, organizational effectiveness, performance management, staffing and employee relations.  

Keys to success include:

  • Ability to listen, communicate, collaborate, and influence effectively across levels and functions.
  • Strong project management skills (to include the ability to set and maintain accountability to execution schedules and commitments).
  • Someone who is energized by, and adept at, navigating ambiguous cultural and business sensitivities to drive some stability and clarity, and champion diversity and inclusion, performance, and employee engagement.
  • Working well under pressure.
  • Completing requirements in a fast-paced, multifaceted environment.

Specific duties and responsibilities include (but are not limited to) the following:

  • Creating and strategizing for division-level HR presentations.
  • Serving as a liaison to compensation, talent development, talent acquisition, employee relations, talent management, and organizational effectiveness.
  • Partnering with internal stakeholders and leaders managing human resource projects and logistics.
  • Providing monthly and quarterly people analytics to division reviews.
  • Mentoring and training HR team on annual HR activities (ex. performance management, compensation planning, succession management).
  • Serving as a subject matter expert for human capital analytics and reporting.
  • Managing HR team MS Teams Channel and SharePoint.
  • Supporting large-scale reorganizations, as needed.
  • Leading other projects and initiatives on behalf of the HR team.
