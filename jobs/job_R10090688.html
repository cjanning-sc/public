The Northrop Grumman Mission Systems Sector in San Diego, California has an immediate opening in the Communications Solutions Division, for a Manager 2, Project Management. 

The individual will be responsible for operating in a team environment with the operating unit leadership, program managers, customers, and peer organizations to lead our manufacturing teams to improve operational efficiency, implement optimal solutions and define and implement the OU end-to-end manufacturing, integration & test strategies.

The candidate must be knowledgeable of the OU business acquisition process, program life cycle, manufacturing capabilities, scheduling techniques, continuous improvement strategies, manufacturing readiness assessments, compliance requirements and financial practices to execute cost and schedule objectives and implement strategic initiatives.

The Manager 2 must possess strong leadership abilities, excellent communications skills and effective problem solving capabilities. The candidate will be responsible for recruiting and mentoring future leaders, directing training and skill development initiatives and promoting engagement and inclusion activities. Excellent written and verbal communication skills, and the ability to influence up and down the chain of command are required, as is a proven track record of innovation, problem solving and career development.

·       Lead multifunctional teams, including Operations functional support organizations, to ensure all program and manufacturing goals and objectives are met

·       Acts as the key point of contact for senior leadership on all contract and program related matters

·       Responsible for employee development, performance management and employee engagement activities

·       Effective communication skills both verbal and written

Preferred Qualifications:

·       Ability to apply systems methodology and lean applications to problem solving
