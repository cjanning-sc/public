The Systems Integration & Operations Engineering (SI&OE) Organization is looking for a Systems Engineering, Integration and Test (SEIT) lead to join our team of highly qualified, diverse individuals. The candidate will be responsible for the overall direction, leadership, and management of the Systems Engineering Products Team and the Systems Integration Team reporting directly to the SI&OE Assistant Program Manager (APM) and SI&OE APM Deputy. Candidates should have a thorough comprehension of systems engineering principles and products and understand how to apply them on an operations and maintenance contract. The candidate must possess excellent communication and presentation skills with experience presenting to senior leadership and customers during working groups, technical exchange meetings, and program reviews. Candidates must be able to work collaboratively with other Integrated Product Teams (IPT) across disciplines, departments, and multiple sites, including external customers.

We offer phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly collaborative workplace.
