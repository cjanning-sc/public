As an integral part of our Mission Modeling, Simulation & Analysis team in Mission Systems Engineering you will be responsible for development, implementing, and understanding modeling and simulation needs as a function of the systems engineering and mission engineering processes across the air, space, land, sea, and cyberspace domains.


Roles and Responsibilities: 

  • Use Object Oriented Systems Engineering Method (OOSEM) and Model-based Systems Engineering (MBSE) approach to understanding Concept Definition, CONOPS development, requirements definition and allocation, system and product definition, component (HW and SW) design and implementation, integration & test, verification, validation, and operations and sustainment

  • Accelerate the delivery of sensor capability to our customers through the use of advanced MBSE and agile systems engineering methods

  • Assist with System concept development, elicitation and specification of functional requirements, and decomposition and traceability of those requirements

  • Assist with performance analysis and specification, Architecture Trade Studies, synthesizing and extending architectures

  • Allocate functionality to components and define interfaces, and Interface Control Documents (ICDs)

  • Contribute to a Detailed Design Analysis; May also involve test design

  • Utilize modern model-based systems engineering approaches that make extensive use of Systems Modeling Language (SysML) skills; integrate M&S analysis models with descriptive models

  • Communicate technical capabilities and analyses with internal and external stakeholders

Basic Qualifications:

Bachelor’s degree with 2 years of experience, a Master’s degree with 0 years of experience in Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics, or related technical fields.

  • U.S. Citizenship and active Secret DoD clearance is required

  • General understanding of Requirements elicitation and generation.

  • General understanding of Architecture, Interface Definition, and Design.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active Top Secret DoD Clearance

  • Advanced degrees in Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics or related technical fields

  • Domain knowledge in RF sensors

  • Knowledge of MBSE and relative methodologies, e.g. OOSEM

  • Experience with physics-based model or simulation development

  • Experience working in an agile development environment
