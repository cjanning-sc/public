ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for an engineering technician to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. The position will be in Lansing, UT. Our team works with experienced engineers, technicians, supporting sophisticated structural analysis, manufacturing, and testing of both composite and metallic materials of aerospace structures. We engineer the latest structures for military/commercial aircraft and launch structures, and are leaders in next-generation structure development. Would you like to work on the cutting edge of aerospace? Come join our team!

Responsibilities:

  • Develop, maintain, and execute test plans for products ranging from components to sub-assemblies to vehicle-level systems.
  • Operate and/or support complex test facility systems (e.g MTS and Instron machines) and help direct a team of technicians to execute test processes
  • Plans sequence of fabrication, assembly, installation, and other manufacturing operations relating to specific portion of the product for guidance of production workers.
  • Plans detail operations from digital models, blueprints, engineering orders, change notices, and other design releases.
  • Analyze and review data to ensure that hardware is operating as expected utilizing numerical data analysis tools to interpret test results
  • Write test reports that include test results and conclusions based on the data collected 
  • Directly support testing, such as material preparation and testing, testing using an Instron and other material testing equipment, metallurgical lab sample cutting, mounting, polishing and evaluation
  • Provide support to the manufacturing floor by working proactively with cross-functional teams to understand frontline problems and implement cost-saving, impactful solutions.
  • Material preparation (storage, thawing, cutting, inventory, traceability, etc.) in support of material testing
  • Track individual metrics, maintain notebook, capture lessons learned and undocumented process details
  • Work across engineering and operations to clarify requirements and drive improvements
  • Lead process improvements working with cross functional teams
