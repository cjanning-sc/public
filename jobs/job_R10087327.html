ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Principal Subcontract Specialist for our Detroit, UT location. This position is in support of the Ground-Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) Engineering and Manufacturing Development (EMD) contract.

The purpose of the GBSD Program is to replace the current ICBM system, including the weapon systems command and control; flight, launch and ground systems; and cyber elements.

Responsibilities:

  • Sources and procures specialized goods and services. Owns contractual management for the supplier.
  • Responsible for source selection from contract proposal to execution phases.
  • Coordinates and collaborates with key stakeholders such as contracts, engineering, quality, supplier development, planning, operations, program and end-users.
  • Develops subcontract specifications, work statements, and terms and conditions for the procurement of specialized materials, equipment, and/or services.
  • Prepares bid packages, conducts bidders conferences, analyzes and evaluates proposals, negotiates subcontract provisions, selects or recommends subcontractors, writes awards, and administers resulting subcontracts.
  • In conjunction with quality organization, evaluates and monitors supplier quality and reliability, as well as supplier ability to support required delivery schedules.
  • Identifies strategies and opportunities to reduce cost, improve efficiency and manage risk through strategic and targeted negotiations.
  • Manages performance of subcontracts, ensures compliance with all public law requirements as well as company (and business) policies and procedures including import/export regulations.
  • Employs sound business practices and assesses supplier performance as part of follow-up activities.
  • Ensures that appropriate documentation is provided to logistics/transportation & warehouse organization so that proper receipt is recorded.
  • Maintain data in SAP related to Purchase Orders, Contracts, and Contract Messages
  • Manages and develops strategic/key suppliers.
  • Reviews and approves supplier payment as needed.
  • Utilizes process tools such as Lean Six Sigma with suppliers to drive quality, efficiency and continuous improvement.

Candidate will negotiate with both large businesses and small businesses on a variety of complex-type contracts including cost-plus-incentive-fee type.

Must have an understanding and application of principles, concepts, practices, and standards. Knowledge of industry practices. Independently demonstrates the skill and ability to perform complex professional tasks.  Able to be creative in finding solutions to resolve issues.

This position will help develop solutions to complex problems, which require the regular use of ingenuity and innovation. Ensures solutions are consistent with organization objectives.

Candidate will attend program meetings and may be required to present to groups.  Must be able to work independently and have strong organizational skills.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree and 5 years of experience in subcontracts, contracts, procurement, cost control, finance, or business management. Or in lieu of a degree, 9 years of experience in subcontracts, contracts, procurement, cost control, finance or business management.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Applications
  • Must be able to obtain and maintain a Secret clearance
