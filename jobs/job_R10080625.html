ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking an experienced Principal /Sr. Principal Network Engineer to support the Remote Ground Station program.

As a Network Engineer, you will; design, develop, configure, integrate, install, and test network systems.

 

The candidates will be responsible for (but not limited to) the following tasks:

• Design and plan network communication systems

• Provide specification and detailed schematics for network architecture

• Define and develop techniques and tools for the most efficient solution to meet business needs to include current and future capacity

• Conduct testing of network design

• Maintain technical expertise in all areas of network and computer hardware and software interconnection and interface such as routers, multiplexers, firewalls, switches, bridges, gateways, etc.

• Evaluate and report on new communication technologies to enhance capabilities of the network

• Troubleshoot, upgrade and implement improvements to the entire enclave, to include but not limited to Cisco and Juniper

• Perform Trade Study analysis to identify the network architecture solution

 

This requisition may be filled at either a Principal or Sr. Principal Level.

Basic Qualifications:

• Principal Engineer: 5 Years’ experience with Bachelors in Science; 3 Years with Masters; 0 Years with PhD; or 9 years of experience in lieu of a degree will be considered.

• Sr Principal Engineer: 9 Years’ experience with Bachelors in Science; 7 Years with Masters; 4 Years with PhD; or 13 years of experience in lieu of a degree will be considered.

• A current active Secret clearance

• Experience with, and knowledge of, Cisco IOS and Cisco Nexus Operating systems

• Experience with Cisco appliances, specifically Firepower firewalls and TACACS+ services

• Intimate knowledge and experience designing, maintaining, and troubleshooting Network switching and routing protocols.

• Ability to obtain a CompTIA Security+ CE certification (IAT II or higher) within the first 6 months of employment

• Experience with, and knowledge of, virtualization technology to include VMWare and virtual switching/routing

 

Preferred Qualifications:

• In depth understanding of IPV4 Network sub/super netting, CIDR, and DNS

• Understanding and experience designing and maintaining a large scale Multicast deployment, to include IGMP and PIM protocols

• Experience with ServiceNow submitting and tracking requests

• Understanding and experience with designing, writing, and implementing FCRs - Experience with SolarWinds or other Network Management tools

• Experience with WAN operations and protocols

• A current active TS w/ SCI eligibility

• A valid CompTIA Security+ CE certification (IAT II or higher)

• Cisco CCNA/CCNP certification
