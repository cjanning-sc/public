We are looking for you to join our team as a Principal or Senior Principal System Engineer based out of San Jose, CA.

What you’ll get to do:

As a Systems Engineer on this team, you will support a team of engineers to define, develop, decompose, analyze, verify, and validate requirements for the ground segment of a satellite system. In addition:

  • Support architecture design that stems from these system requirements, decomposed across multiple levels, aimed to represent the ground capabilities, that have a major impact on the mission.

  • Interact with the customer, subcontractors, Software, Hardware, Operations, Integration and Test, higher level systems engineering and integration teams, as well as program and project leadership to define and maintain the technical baseline.

  • Help drive the program through maturity and milestones to achieve the mission, ensuring everything is synchronized.

This role may be filled as a Principal or Senior Principal System Engineer based on the qualifications listed below.

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Systems Engineer:

  • 5 years of relevant experience with a Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM discipline; 3 years of relevant experience with a Masters' degree; 0 years of experience with a PhD

  • Active DoD Top Secret clearance with the ability to obtain SCI access or active SCI access

  • Proposal/Conops/Architecture Design

  • Requirements Dev: IBM Rationale Tools (DOORS/Clearquest) experience, ability to write/review requirements to define capabilities across subsystems

  • Software/Hardware Experience: particularly in support of interface definition and capability development/integration

  • Integration & Test Experience: particularly in support of Test Plan reviews, tool/script validation, dry runs, formal tests, etc.

  • Verification & Validation Experience: particularly in support of formal verification events (Run for Record), engineering summary development and customer sell-off

  • Experience with Atlassian Tool Suite (Jira, Confluence), MS Office Suite

  • Experience with at least ONE of the following:

–Understand/Develop wire frames, GUI widgets and other user interface/experience components/concepts (e.g. alarm monitoring, confirmation dialogs/windows, subsystem interfacing, etc)

–Similar experience to HCI/UX, command/control (C2), command generation/execution, logging/archiving, SubK/customer interfacing, etc.

–Distributed operations exposure: Cloud based architecture, file syncing, data point definition for situational awareness, etc

–Mathematical algorithm development, extensive Matlab/Simulink exposure, HW/SW component interfacing and modeling/simulation
