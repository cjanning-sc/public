ACME Engineering Defense Systems is seeking a Sr. Principal Supply Chain Business Operations Compliance Specialist to join our GSC team.

The successful candidate will be responsible for processes and activities within supply chain related operations and compliance such as regulatory compliance, systems and technology, data analytics and performance, international supply chain, operational excellence and supply base diversity. Supports strategic direction and tactical execution of governance (policy, procedure, processes, etc.), priorities, initiatives and related activities.

Candidate must have experience working in a Global Supply Chain organization and prior hands-on experience as a Buyer or Subcontract Administrator of DoD FAR based acquisitions.

The essential functions of this position are as follows;

  • Perform Internal PACE audits (compliance audit of Procurement files)

  • Support audits within the organization

  • Provide compliance support to the GSC organization

  • Review procedures for accuracy and needed clarification

  • Support the training team with compliance guidance and provide training as required to the organization

  • Remain up to date in FAR/DFAR regulations

  • Interface with Supply Chain Management processes and personnel at all levels

Will be able to support a hybrid telecommute/in-office schedule. Most in-office requirements would be for training and development activities

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Must have high school diploma or equivalent (GED) with at least 14 years of relevant experience

  • Will consider at least 10 years of relevant experience with bachelor’s degree or at least 8 years of relevant experience with Master’s

  •  Experience supporting different types of programs or product lines as well as contract types including cost types 

  • Experience working with subcontractors during all phases of program lifecycle 

  • Experience utilizing MS Office Suite, SAP and other business tools used in the execution of subcontracts management activities 

  • Strong organizational skills

  • Understanding of FAR/DFAR - ability to evaluate, negotiate and administer subcontract terms and conditions

  • Demonstrated ITAR compliance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Working knowledge of SAP ERP System

  • Current DoD Secret level or ability to obtain this level of Clearance
