ACME Engineering Space Systems (NGSP) is seeking a Capture Manager. The successful candidate will work closely with all of ACME Engineering’s functional organizations leadership team, internal marketing or government relations teams and other industry and government partners. In this role the ideal candidate will lead capture efforts to secure new opportunities, re-compete contracts, or task order business. Capture leads play a critical role in the organization by growing and leading teams that develop and execute capture plans including architecting the technical and business strategy; leading competitive assessments and prices-to-win; conduct gap assessments and define potential supplier or teaming approach; and lead bid and proposal activities.

This position will preferably be located in one of the following locations:

  • Des Moines, IA
  • Lexington, KY
  • Columbus, OH

Roles and Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

Capture Leadership

  • Opportunity identification and shaping
  • Opportunity qualification
  • Business case analysis
  • Customer relationship management
  • Customer acquisition approach analysis and shaping
  • Professional relationship development and maintenance with key decision makers
  • Government Relations collaboration on customer engagement strategies and execution
  • Future customer requirements analysis and planning
  • Capture strategy development and execution
  • Customer and competitor assessments
  • Price-to-Win analysis
  • Teammate/supplier selection and relationship management
  • Threat identification and mitigation

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree
  • 5+ years' experience with program management, customer management, capture, and/or business development
  • 4 years in a management role
  • Must have a current DoD TS and eligibility for SCI
  • US Federal Agency experience required.
  • Telework position (must be driving distance to one of the locations: Des Moines, IA; Lexington, KY or Columbus, OH) with 50% travel.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience in program planning, budget development and execution, workforce planning, risk management, and the implementation of project processes and controls
  • Current user and acquisition customer relationships
