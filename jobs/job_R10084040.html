ACME Engineering Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) has an opening for a Staff Engineer Mechanical Design Checker within the Mechanical Ground Systems Engineering team. This position will be located in Columbus, CA at Space Park.

Mechanical Ground Systems Engineers support the design, fabrication, and use of Mechanical Ground Support Equipment (MGSE). MGSE is non-flight hardware used to support the integration, test, and launch of spacecraft and its various stages of assembly. This includes, but is not limited to, lift slings, transportation systems, robotics, zero-g deployment fixtures, pneumatics, propellant loading systems, etc.

Mechanical Ground Support Equipment Designers are responsible for the following:

  • Design and Modeling in 3D CAD (Computer-Aided Design) software

  • Fundamental structural analysis techniques

  • Drafting using CAD software

  • Fabrication support at vendors including acceptance testing and inspection

  • End-user support during integration operations

  • Conceptual design

  • Requirements definition

  • Integration & Test process flow development

As a Staff Engineer Design Checker in this specific role, a qualified candidate is expected to examine engineering drawings and related documents for soundness, efficiency, and simplicity in design; completeness, clarity, and dimensional accuracy; correctness of materials and processes; conformity to design/drafting standards, design specifications, and practicability; and economy of fabrication, assembly, and installation. Types of hardware includes weldments, bracketry, mechanical fixtures, tooling.  The candidate is expected to have experience in design or analysis principles, processes, and tools.  In addition, the candidate is expected to be familiar with current fabrication/machine shop practices to provide guidance to engineers for design manufacturability and drawing clarity.
