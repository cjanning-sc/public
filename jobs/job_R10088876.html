ACME Engineering is hiring an Electron Technician 3 to support our tactical space systems division located in Des Moines, VA.

Primary Duties/Responsibilities:

  • Manual assembly of electronic and mechanical devices according to assembly instructions, customer specifications and quality standards

  • Hand soldering of various electronic devices (surface mount and through hole) following the J-STD-001 and IPC-A-610 standards

  • Reworking thru-hole and surface mount assemblies including fine pitch components and ensuring that IPC criteria have been met according to IPC standard

  • Component preparation (forming), placement or installation

  • Visual inspection of assemblies per the IPC standard and customer specific standards

  • Taking part in training and certification programs to ensure job knowledge and skills set requirements are updated regularly

  • Participate in team level quality improvement initiatives

  • In-process Inspection and touch up of thru-hole and surface mount components using microscope

  • Ability to demonstrate rework skills with de-soldering station and hot air gun

  • Communicate and take direction from Supervisor/ Manager

  • Other duties as assigned
