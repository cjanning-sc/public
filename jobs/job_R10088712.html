Job Description

Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is an industry-leading provider for prime satellite and payload capabilities and directed energy and electronics solutions for national security, military, and civil customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible

ACME Engineering, Strategic Space Systems Division, has an opening for a Principal Project Management to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Dayton, Ohio.

Oversees and manages the operational aspects of ongoing projects and serves as liaison between project management and planning, project team, and line management. Reviews status of projects and budgets; manages schedules and prepares status reports. Assesses project issues and develops resolutions to meet productivity, quality, and client-satisfaction goals and objectives. Develops mechanisms for monitoring project progress and for intervention and problem solving with project managers, line managers, and clients.

  • Schedule a sequence of fabrication, assembly, installation, and other manufacturing operations relating to a specific portion of the product for guidance of production work, material readiness and all aspects of scheduling related to meeting program needs:

  • Load, analyze and monitor demands in the ERP system to create robust & attainable schedules.

  • To support future capabilities for the sector, candidate should also have knowledge of capacity & resource planning to work in parallel with Industrial Engineering for level loading of schedules.

  • Project Management and analysis of manufacturing scheduling activities including coordination across multiple disciplines such as: production planning, quality assurance, manufacturing, purchasing, engineering, inventory control, material requirements planning and Program Scheduling.

  • Ensure production control and parts integrators expedite appropriate material based on master production schedule and critical path analysis.

  • Provides visibility into and advises management of the status of work in progress, material availability, and potential production risks to ensure that personnel, equipment, materials and services are provided as needed to meet production and Program needs.

  • Able to schedule and plan detailed operations from engineering drawings, engineering orders, change notices, and other engineering releases.

  • The ideal candidate will have strong verbal communication and customer service skills and able to work in a teamwork environment.

  • Presents schedules, power point presentations to leadership, leads schedule meetings with IPT’s.
