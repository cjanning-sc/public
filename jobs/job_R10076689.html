Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems is seeking an  Supplier Manufacturing Engineer and a Principal Supplier Manufacturing Engineer to provide support to our Global Supply Chain team in our Clearfield, Utah location.

  • Function as the primary technical point of contact for the supply base for assigned programs to address supplier manufacturing issues.  This includes working with suppliers to develop possible new manufacturing techniques, implement design changes for manufacturability, and aiding the suppliers to determine root cause for technical issues.
  • Manage supplier requests for waivers, deviations and discrepancy reporting process
  •  On-site resolution of technical issues for Procurement and Subcontracts at Domestic suppliers
  • Support/lead supplier manufacturing capability qualifications and supplier capacity assessments
  • Supplier Proposal Support (Technical Evaluations, spares requirements, labor hours estimate, parts obsolescence and supplier capability)

The ideal candidate thrives in a fast-paced work environment with diverse assignments. Our team is highly collaborative, working in team settings across all levels and subject areas including operations, engineering, and mission assurance. Our diverse portfolio of programs means there are endless paths to cultivate your career.
