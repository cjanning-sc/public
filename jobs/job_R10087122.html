ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Senior Principal Test Planner.  This position will be located in Detroit, MI and will support the Sentinel – Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.  This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

• Develop and execute the integrated test strategy at the system test level for the program. 
• Lead the development of detailed test plans at the system level. 
• Support the development of test procedures, test readiness reviews, test data methodology, and test reports based on program requirements. 
• Support execution of qualification and acceptance testing per the test plans and test procedures. 
• Participate in internal and customer test planning working groups. 
• Create test ConOps flow that maximizes the utility of test resources while meeting schedule and technical requirements and/or constraints. 
• Effectively communicate and present to project and program management, technical stakeholders, peers, and customers. 
• Support program reviews and customer delivery (CDRL) development. 
• Lead test planning campaigns through major program milestones and/or mission threads.

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

• 9 years of experience with Bachelors; 7 years with Masters; 4 years with PhD. An additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
• US Citizenship with an Active Secret Clearance or higher (awarded within the past 6 years)
• Demonstrated experience with system integration and test principles and processes. 
• Prior experience with planning test campaigns. 

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

• US Citizenship with an Active Top-Secret Clearance or higher (awarded within the past 6 years)
• Demonstrated knowledge and experience with Model Based System Engineering (MBSE) principles. 
• Demonstrated knowledge of SYSML and Cameo. 
• Demonstrated knowledge of DOORs as a requirements database tool. 
• Prior experience with test planning in a missile program.

• Understanding of ACME Engineering test processes and tools.
