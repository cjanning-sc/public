Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems sector has an opening for a Staff Weapons Integration Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This role will be located in Rancho Bernardo, CA.


In this role you will be responsible for developing, modifying, maintaining the Weapons Systems Mechanical Architecture using engineering practices and standards.  The selected candidate will have responsibility over the physical and 3D digital architectures for weapons carriage related hardware.


Key Responsibilities:
•    Primary role is to serve as a mechanical designer for weapons integration on a major program.
•    The candidate will be part of a small team of mechanical design engineers supporting the design, development and integration of weapons onto a military aircraft.
•    Candidate will develop requirements documentation to capture weapon carriage hardware types and quantities, weapon suspension and release equipment (S&RE), and weapon load crew equipment and adapters based on limited customer input.
•    The candidate will also develop the weapon/S&RE layout configurations, Interface Control Drawings (ICDs) and overall weapon carriage and release volume/clearance requirements, and coordinate them with other aircraft IPTs (such as structures and subsystems) to help define/develop hard points for S&RE attachment, weapon bay size, weapon bay door configuration, weapon bay door drive system, door up-locks, subsystem routing, and electrical harness routing, while also ensuring sufficient weapon bay volume exists for weapon loading crew personnel to physically and safely perform required weapon loading tasks.
•    As needed, candidate must also attend internal program, customer and/or supplier technical exchange meetings and/or formal program milestone reviews to provide and/or obtain progress status updates.


The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, and collaborative/team settings across all levels. The candidate must be a self-starter capable of flourishing in an environment with minimal direction.  The candidate will need to have excellent communication and writing skills with experience in Microsoft Word PowerPoint, and Excel. 


We offer flexible work arrangements, 9/80 work schedule with every other Friday off, phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly team environment. We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal-oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have a Bachelor’s degree in a STEM discipline and 14 or more years of related professional/military experience in mechanical/aerospace engineering that includes a background in weapons integration; OR a Master's degree and 12 or more years of related professional/military experience in mechanical/aerospace engineering that includes a background in weapons integration; OR a PhD and 10 or more years of related professional/military experience in mechanical/aerospace engineering that includes a background in weapons integration
  • Active DoD Secret or DoE equivalent required to be considered

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s Degree in Mechanical or Aerospace Engineering or related discipline.
  • Familiarity with NX CAD software and Teamcenter PLM.
