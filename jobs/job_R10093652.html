Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is an industry-leading provider for prime satellite and payload capabilities and directed energy and electronics solutions for national security, military, and civil customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable, and reliable aerospace defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible possible.

SSSD has an opening for a Staff Program Integration Systems Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Columbus, CA.

This role will support the ARIS Ground Director with logistics and oversight to enable program leadership to achieve operational excellence.

Duties include coordinating efforts across the Ground Element relating to:

-Affordability/Digital Transformation: Understanding the current processes, working with the Systems Engineering digital transformation team, and being able to communicate to the team effectively to be able to make change to how we do business.

-Metrics: Drive metrics to support affordability & digital transformation efforts

-Facilities/Open Close communication/Sharepoint

-Data Call from ARIS BU Tech Ops: Support succession planning, staffing & priorities, conference nominations, and management course nominations

-SEIT Support

-Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline and 14+ years of engineering experience OR Master’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline and 12+ years of engineering experience OR PhD in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline and 9+ years of engineering experience

-Dynamic self-starter with strong attention to detail and the ability to function independently.

-Demonstrated flexible approach to work and the ability to work collaboratively on a team.

-Strong problem solving and analytical skills.

-Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

-Experience with Windows-based MS Office tools, including MS Word, MS Excel, and MS PowerPoint.

-Dept. of Defense Top Secret level with a background investigation date within 6 years
