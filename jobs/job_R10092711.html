Propulsion Systems is currently seeking a Manufacturing Technician 2 to work at our Promontory facilities.

Role description & Responsibilities:

A Manufacturing Technician 2 performs hands on activities/tasks associated with manufacturing oxidizers for rocket fuel. Tasks may include but are not limited to Tooling/Assembly/Disassembly, Chemical Mixing, and Propellant Ingredient Handling, maintaining cleanliness of work area and facilities, and other duties as assigned by the supervisor. Participates as a team member to positively influence and enhance the safety, quality, cost and scheduling of their team.

Requirements:

• High School diploma or equivalent required

• 2 to 4 years of manufacturing (product assembly) experience.

• Must be a U.S. Citizen

• Able to obtain Employee Possessor designation from the Federal Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco and Firearms

• Must be willing to work shift work

Experience Preferred:

• Safe handling of hazardous materials

• Operating industrial equipment such as cranes, forklifts, precision measuring instruments and hand tools.

• Job function may require handling of live propellant related materials.

Skills & Qualifications:

• Must be willing to perform shift work and work on weekends and holidays

• Must be willing to work overtime as required

• Must possess a valid state driver’s license

• Able to successfully pass the Safe Explosive Act (SEA) background check

• Able to pass a respirator FIT test.

• Able to work a physically demanding day (stand for long periods; work from your knees; ascend and descend ladders; lift, bend, twist push and pull up to 50 lbs.) while wearing protective equipment (respirator, coveralls, gloves, eye and ear protection, etc.).

• Able to read, comprehend and follow established written instructions without deviation

• Perform basic math and measurement functions
