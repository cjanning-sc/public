Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking an Electrical Engineer / Principal Electrical Engineer to join the Flight Systems Team. This position is in Roy, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Final grade level offered will be based on candidate experience.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will report to the Air Vehicle (AVE) Design Integration lead and is responsible for artifact development for all AVE electrical interfaces. Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Vehicle level power management

  • Managing signal integrity

  • Signal to noise calculations

  • Work with signal types including RS 422, ethernet, and LVDS

  • Manage electrical interface characteristics

  • Develop vehicle block diagrams, schematics, wiring diagrams, and cables
