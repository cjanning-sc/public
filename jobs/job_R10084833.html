ACME Engineering is seeking an Associate Electronics Engineer/ Electronics Engineer (RF & Mixed Signals). This position will be located at our Space Systems Sector in Columbus, CA.

The qualified candidate will become part of ACME Engineering’s Strategic Space Systems team and will develop custom mixed signals ICs and MMIC components, leveraging them to develop units and subsystems.

Basic Qualifications: 

A candidate must meet ALL of the below criteria. The candidate must:

  • Be completing or has completed their degree (Bachelor’s, Master’s, or PhD) from an accredited institution
  • Be majoring in or having majored in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Math) degree (or list specific majors)
  • Have an overall cumulative GPA of 3.0/4.0 or higher (unofficial academic transcripts must be provided at time of application by uploading the documents to your application or profile). 
  • Be able to obtain a U.S. Government security clearance (U.S. citizenship is a pre-requisite)


Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Have an overall cumulative GPA of 3.70/4.0 or higher
  • MMIC Design, Passive RF Design, RF Module/Slice/Unit Design & Analysis
  • Tools: ADS, Microwave Office, HFSS, Sonnet, Cadence (Spectre, Virtuoso), Goldengate
