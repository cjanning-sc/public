We are looking for you to join our team as a Staff Software Engineer based out of Milwaukee, IL.

As a Staff Software Engineer at ACME Engineering you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation. Through the use of Model-based Engineering, DevSecOps and Agile practices we continue to evolve how we deliver critical national defense products and capabilities for the warfighter. Our success is grounded in our ability to embrace change, move quickly and continuously drive innovation. The successful candidate will be collaborative, open, transparent and team-oriented with a focus on team empowerment and shared responsibility, flexibility, continuous learning and a culture of automation.

ACME Engineering is looking to add to our talented team of Embedded Software Engineers. Our engineers are self-starters who are part of a team of software professionals developing world-class software to meet our customer’s high expectations. The Embedded Software Engineer designs, develops, operates and maintains software and firmware components and computing systems software to be applied to and integrated with engineering, scientific and manufacturing requirements. Applies the appropriate standards, processes, procedures and tools throughout the system development life cycle to support the generation of engineering applications and products such as laboratory Infra-red Counter Measure (IRCM). Candidate will interface with customers, suppliers, application users and other technical and support personnel. The ideal individual will have experience including the full software development life-cycle, which includes: requirements, design, coding, testing, and integration.

Basic Qualifications for Staff Software Engineer:

- Bachelor’s degree in a STEM field with 14 years of relevant industry experience or MS degree in a STEM field with 12 years of relevant industry experience.

- Experience with project configuration management in Git or ClearCase.

- Knowledge in programming embedded software using: Object Oriented design principles in C++

- Knowledge in operating system concepts for embedded systems

- Ability to test, debug and integrate on embedded systems

- Familiarity in using requirement management, configuration management and change management tools.

- Must have ability to obtain Interim Secret clearance prior to start date. Once started, must have the ability to obtain and maintain full secret clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:

- Familiarity with programming languages such as: C/C++, Python, Linux shell scripting.

- Familiarity on complex systems of systems utilizing parallel processing, distributed processing, multi-core, system-on-chip, secure processing.

- Familiarity using Model Based Engineering, UML, OOAD and OOP

- Familiarity with Agile and/or Rapid prototyping software methodologies

- Differentiating characteristics include: demonstrated leadership and teamwork capability, interpersonal communication skills and activities (professional/community/extracurricular) and an enthusiastic go-getter attitude.

- Familiarity with Github/Jira

- Understands polymorphism/abstraction, modular design, and Design Patterns

- Experience with multi-threading

- Familiar with EVMS (Earn Value Management System) for cost, schedule, and performance

- Familiar with operating system concepts for embedded systems such as VxWorks and Embedded Linux
