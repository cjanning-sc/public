As part of our maturity and growth, we’re looking for a Security Specialist to join our team.

Your role:

You will uphold Defence Industry security requirements, maintain security accreditations, manage security clearances and facilitate awareness training.

This role is based out of the Richmond RAAF base, in Richmond NSW.

About you:

The hiring manager’s mantra is: ‘hire for attitude, train for skills’.

You display a high degree of emotional intelligence, good at building relationships with a variety of stakeholders and are an effective communicator. You come with a good foundational knowledge of security, are a lateral thinker and know where to look when you don’t know the answer.

Added bonus if you have formal security education, Defence experience or knowledge of the Defence Security Policy Framework (DSPF).


What’s in it for you:

Whatever your role, life stage or background, you can shape your career here in your way with highly challenging work, great colleagues and career development, plus a range of flexible benefits and working patterns.

  • Salary packaging including novated car leases

  • Generous paid parental leave

  • Volunteer Leave

  • Health & Wellbeing program

  • Employee Assistance Program
