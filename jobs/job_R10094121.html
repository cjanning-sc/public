ACME Engineering’s Armament Systems Business Unit is currently seeking a Program Manager PM1 to support the Ammunition Operating Unit out of the Plymouth, MN facility. 

The position’s primary responsibility will be to manage the Medium Caliber AmmoBook.  The AmmoBook is a pre-costed tool used to provide medium caliber ammunition quotes to our international customers.  This allows for rapid responses to international requests for ammunition.  The selected candidate will need to work in close collaboration with the Medium Caliber Program Management team and International Business Development to support international opportunities to expand the medium caliber ammunition market space.

Primary responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Responsible for full management of the AmmoBook tool
  • Understanding of cost estimating methodologies to manage bi-annual tool updates
  • Preparing and delivering cost/price delegations to the Ammunition Operating Unit and Business Unit leadership
  • Cultivating customer relationships and intimacy to develop further opportunities within the international market
  • Adherence to all internal processes for Program Management
  • Conducting risk & opportunity management practices including identification, mitigation and realization
  • Potential for overseas travel to support customer reviews and meetings
  • Participating in the negotiation of contracts, contract changes, budgets, schedule requirements, and key terms and conditions
  • Drive timely and clearly communicated decisions and recommendations to the program management and business development teams.
  • Strategically develop competitive quotes by working across the medium caliber ammo portfolio

Job Qualifications:

  • 6 years of work experience required. Would consider a Bachelors Degree with 4 years of experience or a Masters Degree with 2 years of experience.
  • Demonstrated success leading teams/and or organizations to achieve a common goal
  • Basic competency in Project Management skills and toolsets
  • Excellent interpersonal skills with ability to manage a project team and communicate effectively with leadership
  • Ability to operate in a dynamic, fast-paced work environment and handle changes
  • Strategic thinker with excellent organizational skills
  • Competent in standard Microsoft Office Software Suite
  • Strong formal and informal communication skills with all levels of internal and external management
  • Familiarity with ITAR processes and requirements
  • Defense Industry and DOD contract experience desired
  • Ability to manage risk to reduce likelihood and severity of program impacts
  • Ability to apply knowledge, insights and understanding of business and financial concepts, tools and processes to the benefit of program decisions, actions, and performance.
  • Able to travel both international and domestically (25% travel requirement)
  • Able to obtain a secret security clearance. (US Citizenship is a requirement)

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • A Degreen in Science, Engineering or Business preferred.
  • Prior experience with large or medium caliber military armaments, or related systems
  • International business experience desired, including contract negotiation and program execution
