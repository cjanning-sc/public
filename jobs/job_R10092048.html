ACME Engineering Space Systems Sector, Tactical Space Division in Des Moines, VA, seeks a qualified and experienced Sr. Staff Systems Engineer. This candidate will serve as deputy to the program Chief Engineer for a space-to-ground communications network.

The candidate must be mission execution and end-user focused with extensive experience in practical systems engineering techniques and will immediately apply them to multiple US Government stakeholders. The candidate will provide both technical and programmatic leadership for networked communications systems that feature high-reliability communications support to multiple national security missions. Experience with both operations and sustainment of ground-based systems and space-based national security payloads and interfaces is strongly preferred.

The candidate must have experience with the following: architecture development, systems analysis, requirements and interface management, and Engineering Review Board representation over total systems lifecycle. Direct experience must include client collaboration with CONOPS, capability development, system design, design verification, system-of-systems integration, and system sustainment. On-orbit support is preferred. The candidate must have exceptional problem solving abilities, troubleshooting expertise, and be able to drive challenging technical issues to resolution to meet customer and mission-critical timelines. Leadership skills include the ability to help guide the program through a culture transition that will be based on systems engineering rigor.

This position requires ability to define/review electromechanical and RF & Digital communication designs and ensure that requirements are met for cost, schedule, and technical performance. This position will also require IPT leadership and coordination with multi-discipline functional organizations as well as internal and external stakeholders. The candidate must excel at preparation and presentation of mixed technical and project management topics to senior leadership and senior technical advisors. Superior written and verbal communication is mandatory. Broad systems development experience and understanding of the DoD, Intelligence Community, and their missions is desirable. Prior experience with Cost Account Management and EVMS oversight for multi-phased development projects is preferred.
