The Data Transfer Manager (M1) is responsible for daily operations of a dedicated Data Transfer Agent (DTA) Team. The dedicated DTA Team will conduct daily transfers between multiple classified networks supporting multiple programs. This is a working manager role which requires processes and procedures development, conduct data transfers, and manage the team.  

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Manages a six-person team responsible for executing data transfer in support of Launch Vehicle programs  
  • In accordance with organization goals, provides direction on activities and behaviors to increase morale
  • Identifies and analyzes problems, plans, tasks, and solutions
  • Provides guidance to team on development, performance, and productivity issues
  • Provide program schedulers with day to day support, guidance, troubleshooting, and satisfying programmatic requirements
  • Support Launch Vehicle program administering the Data Transfer Agent (DTA) duties for information systems processing National Security Information.
  • Perform mission critical data transfers across various security boundaries for multiple programs (Low-to-High, High-to-High, High-to-Low) and networks (NGPCN, SIPR, CCN)
  • Ensure DTA software (Purifile, Document Detective) is operating in accordance with RMF (JSIG)
  • Perform Assured File Transfer (AFT) related security education and training
  • Manage 1010 waiver
  • Conduct associated tasks as prescribed by the LV Data Transfer Manager
  • Provide weekly status of all file transfer actions to and from classified information systems
  • Coordinate handling, destruction, receipt and transferring of security related documentation
  • Ensure all DTA related documentation is current and accessible to properly authorized individuals
  • Participate in technical and security related training relative to assigned duties
  • Full working knowledge of procedures and practices.
  • Assist the ISSMs of each classified network with approved data transfer process and two-person integrity (TPI) policy execution for their areas of responsibilities.  
  • Report all security-related incidents to the ISSM
  • Ensure all users have the requisite security clearances, authorization, need-to-know, and are aware of their security responsibilities
  • Formally notify the ISSM when changes occur that may compromise performance of Assured  File Transfers
  • Understand Programs Security Classification Guide (SCG)
  • Support/coordinate internal and external surveillance audits and DCSA reviews, and integrated baseline reviews

Basic Qualifications:

  • BS Eng degree plus 5 years of experience; Master's plus 3 years
  • Ability to obtain and maintain SAP clearance.    
  • CompTia+ certification
  • 5 years experience in leading projects and teams
  • Proven experience with Data Transfers over multiple classifications
  • Proven experience writing processes and procedures for data transfer
  • Understanding of, DAPM and JSIG guidelines
  • Top Secret Clearance upon start
