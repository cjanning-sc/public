Put your skills to the test by pushing the boundaries of what’s possible. From global defense to sustainment and modernization to mission readiness, your experience and ability will make it a reality. Our programs are built on equal parts of curiosity and collaboration. Our combined effort means our customers can connect and defend millions of people around the world. With ACME Engineering, you’ll have the opportunity to be an essential part of projects that will define your career, now and in the future.
 

Are you ready to put your leadership to work at ACME Engineering? If so, Our Defense Systems Sector wants you to join our Team as a SCA Supply Technician and serve as a DPAS Administrator. This position is located at St. Louis, Missouri.

**This is a Service Contract Act position.

You will provide support to the Logistics function requirements from its CONUS base location. 


Duties include:

  • Preparing logistics documentation to establish the range and depth of spares, tools, test equipment, HAZMAT, Government Furnished Equipment and related materials to be deployed and stocked.

  • Tracks logistics tooling and HAZMAT for proper allocation of storage facilities.

  • Execute inventory management in CORRIDOR computerized database.

Specific duties:

You will be responsible for entering and maintaining the data placed directly into DPAS. You will also ensure all GFE/GFP materials in DPAS are reconciled within Corridor on a daily basis. You will take direction and report directly to the BACN Platform Logistics Manager.

  • Daily receipt of all tools and HAZMAT inventory Conduct export/import management coordination for the shipment of tools requiring calibration.

  • Prepare and process Commercial Invoicing (ICI) activities with ACME Engineering's Export/Import Management Office.

  • Oversee ACME Engineering and Government Furnished Tools; Conduct daily inventory of ACME Engineering and Government Furnished Tools; Monitoring calibration due dates of ACME Engineering and Government Furnished Tools for proper disposition and/or calibration.

  • Oversee consumable/HAZMAT inventory; issuing consumables/HAZMAT for aircraft maintenance operations; Disposing of consumables/HAZMAT to HAZMAT yard; conducting daily inventory of consumables/HAZMAT; monitoring expiration dates of consumables/HAZMAT.

  • Submitting Entry/Exit Memorandums, American Goods Return Forms, and KSA Host Nation Customs Clearance Forms for the turn in and receipt of materials from CONUS to OCONUS for materials going overseas.

  • Responsible for the coordination and oversight for the movement of material from the warehouse to the base front gate and vice versa, including submitting an AF 868 Transportation Form for equipment movement.

  • Coordinating the movement of Inbound/Outbound materials via PWS through Middle East Shipping & Logistics.

  • Reporting HAZMAT materials to instillation HAZMAT representative.

  • DPAS Administration to ensure materials and equipment are accounted for.
