ACME Engineering Mission Systems Manufacturing is seeking a Manager 2, Industrial Automation and Systems Engineering to join the Leadership Team.

The Industrial Automation and Systems Engineering Manager will be responsible for shaping and executing the automation strategy for Manufacturing and Operations within the AMS Business Division, the Springfield Campus, and the Mission Systems sector – achieving industrial and data automations that advance operational efficiency for the organization.

The position requires leadership of a technical team of approximately 14 total engineers that are responsible for:

  • Developing and applying design for automation standards
  • Establishing standard industrial automation applications, platforms and tools
  • Implementing industrial automation and connectivity solutions for a defined business value
  • Supporting and optimizing automated processes in the factory

The Industrial Automation and Systems Engineering Manager responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the tasks outlined below:

  • Establish the pipeline of automation and connectivity capabilities and processes required by the business; ensure business value and priority development align to leadership and strategic expectations; will require assessing requirements across multiple manufacturing sites, functional and process areas; customer satisfaction measured via effective implementation of the business objectives
  • Lead the management and communication of industrial automation project objectives to ensure successful technical and financial execution; work with leadership to identify objectives and to acquire funding; develop key suppliers that enable project execution 
  • Maintain Industrial Automation team affordability and advance team effectiveness; ensure team resources and skills are in place to meet both technical and financial objectives; promote career development and employee engagement; assess employee performance
  • Brief Sector Operations, Business Division and Enterprise Leadership on industrial automation strategy and projects, objectives

The Industrial Automation and Systems Engineering Manager must perform in a fast-paced, dynamic, and high visibility operation; work well in a team environment; and have excellent written and verbal communication skills. They must have a passion for people, innovation, and excellence and the ability to effectively collaborate with multiple disciplines to solve complex issues.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 9 years of relevant professional experience (7 years with a Master's degree) 
  • Demonstrated intermediate experience with project management and communication including associated tools and methods
  • Must have fluency in MS Office software applications
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a US Secret level security clearance is required. US Citizenship is a prerequisite.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field 
  • Experience with industrial automation and controls implementation and support 
  • Experience in a leadership position managing people and projects
  • Experience with factory data connectivity
  • Six Sigma Black belt/Green belt certification from an accredited program
