U107 Mechanical Assembly / Fabricator - Level 2 - 2nd Shift
R10088087
2nd Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking Mechanical Assembler/Fabrication Specialist to support the Mechanical Design and Fabrication manufacturing cell at the BWI facility in Linthicum, MD.

  • Determine, plan, develop and modify complex fabrication processes and methodology to produce prototype and complex electro-mechanical units and assemblies.

  • Assemble complete electro mechanical units consisting of chassis, power assemblies, frames and related units performing required welding, drilling riveting, cleaning, de-burring and the like.

  • Coordinate with limited supervision, fabrication work group activities to ensure proper development of products from the prototype to the production stage, utilizing latest available equipment and techniques.

  • Set up and operate punch presses, NC punch presses, etc. to reform a variety of fabrication operations.

This is an IUE Union Represented position on the 2nd Shift.

EDUCATION REQUIREMENT:

  • High School graduate or recognized equivalent

EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENT:

  • The Level 2 position will require a minimum of 3 years of relevant and verifiable experience in the field, which can be acquired either by time in the Level 1 occupation or by equivalent external experience. Must be able to obtain and maintain area skills and safety certifications.

  • Must be able to pass a structured interview and demonstrate the ability to perform all essential functions of the position.
