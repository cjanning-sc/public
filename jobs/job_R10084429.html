Pr. DOORS Database Engineer - Top Secret (Space/Satellite Systems)
R10084429
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Space Systems – Tactical Space Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Principal DOORS Administrator to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located out of Gilbert, Arizona, AZ.

Position Overview:

Successful candidate will provide cross functional support for requirements management utilizing IBM Rational Dynamic Object-Oriented Requirements System (DOORS) as an administrator on multiple programs. Responsibilities include creating DOORS processes and procedures, training materials, and acting as a technical interface to other working level counterparts (internal and external).

Essential Functions:

  • Maintaining, streamlining, and establishing consistent practices within the DOORS environment, which includes data created internal to programs and modules received from the customer and subcontractors
  • Identifying and analyzing technological problems or risks related to DOORS and system requirements processes
  • Driving the completeness and consistency of attributes and linkages within programs’ DOORS environments
  • Performing DOORS administration; creating modules for sites; synchronizing modules between sites; creating DOORS reports (e.g., specifications traceability and metrics), successfully addressing user problems (e.g., installation, use of Change Proposal System).  
  • Providing technical leadership for processes and best practices across IBM Rational DOORS
  • Establishing strong working relationships across program and functional organizations

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in STEM Science Technology Engineering Math with 5 years’ experience
  • Must have US Citizenship with a current or active Top Secret clearance with SCI eligibility
  • Experience working with complex system development on large programs
  • Database archiving, user access management, and creating module/project baselines
  • Importing data into DOORS and exporting DOORS data into Microsoft Excel/Word
  • Advanced DOORS administrator skills
  • Training new users on how to utilize the DOORS tool, and document/update DOORS training material
  • Experience using DOORS Change Proposal System (CPS) or equivalent Configuration Management within DOORS
  • DOORS eXtension Language (DXL) programming skills to modify existing or create DXL scripts to manipulate DOORS data

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Extensive IBM DOORS DXL scripting
  • Familiarity with Systems Engineering principles
  • Familiarity with PTC Windchill
  • Experience coding in Microsoft Visual Basic Editor in Microsoft Visual Basic for Applications (VBA) strongly desired
  • Experience exporting DOORS data using IBM Rational Publishing Engine or Engineering Lifecycle Optimization Publishing
  • Experience with IBM DOORS NextGen and java script
  • Experience working across multiple classification levels and programs
