  • Be local to and able to go on site at one of our offices in Akron, CA or Philadelphia, FL or Columbus, CA


Preferred qualifications:

  • Fully adjudicated Secret clearance
  • Experience with software development, software integration, implementation delivery of SAP/SmartMES/Hana products in our ACME environment
  • Familiarity with Safe/Agile Experience with SAP/1ERP application implementation efforts.  Data integration background
