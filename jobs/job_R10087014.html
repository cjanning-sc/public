Northrop Grumman’s Enterprise Services Sector is looking for a Senior Principal (Level 4) Enterprise Applications Analyst. This is an opportunity for an Application Analyst/Systems Integration professional who is an energetic, self-starter with knowledge and experience in converting requirements into systems/business process solutions.

The qualified candidate will be responsible for:

  • Acting as a consultant between the customer and technical community in support of GSC, Quality and Manufacturing business processes
  • Delivering aspects of reporting and analytic deliverables for projects on the 1ERP SAP S/4 HANA platform
  • Supporting our recurring/existing customer base and new project/program initiatives

This position will offer a hybrid of telework and onsite presence. Eligible candidates must be local to one of our offices in:

  • El Segundo, CA
  • Melbourne, FL
  • Redondo Beach, CA

Basic qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in STEM or related field and 9 years of experience is required; a Master’s degree in STEM or related field and 7 years’ experience.  In lieu of a Bachelor’s degree, one year of relevant experience may be substituted for each year of the 4 years of education
  • Must be able to obtain a Secret clearance, with the ability to obtain and maintain SAP/SAR security clearance, as necessary per business requirements
  • Functional/business process knowledge or expertise spanning MRP, procurement, shopfloor, and reporting/analytics activities
  • Leads recurring/existing customer base and new project initiatives
  • Experience in aspects of project management
  • Be local to and able to go on site at one of our offices in El Segundo, CA or Melbourne, FL or Redondo Beach, CA


Preferred qualifications:

  • Fully adjudicated Secret clearance
  • Experience with software development, software integration, implementation delivery of SAP/SmartMES/Hana products in our NG environment
  • Familiarity with Safe/Agile Experience with SAP/1ERP application implementation efforts.  Data integration background
  • Excellent leadership characteristics
  • Demonstrated experience presenting to leadership or government customer
  • Experience supervising or leading cross functional teams/IPT’s and/or individuals
  • Demonstrated experience presenting and working with all levels of the organization from individual contributor to IPT leadership to program leadership
  • BOM Development/Maintenance, SAP/ MRP, Inventory Management, and Quality Management knowledge and proficiency
