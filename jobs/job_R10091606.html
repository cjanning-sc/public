ACME Engineering Space Systems, Payload and Ground Systems Division is currently seeking a Staff Digital Design Engineer for our Remote Sensing Program (RSP) unit located in Chicago, Illinois.

In this role you will define our next digital subsystem architectures.

Roles and Responsibilities:

Digital Subsystem Architecture

  • Work with multidiscipline teams to define product architectures to meet customer requirements

  • Implement DSP algorithms in Matlab/Python or other higher-level languages

  • Create subsystem requirement specifications and direct design engineers during development process to ensure requirements are met on schedule and within budget

  • Developing integration and test plans for subsystems

  • Drive improvement of internal engineering processes

  • Support business capture activities as needed

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelors in STEM discipline with 14 years of related experience, or a Master’s Degree with 12 years of related experience, or a PhD with 9 years of related experience OR in lieu of a degree 18 years of experience

  • Experience designing, architecting, and using various FPGAs in processor designs (knowledge of VHDL or other high level language a plus)

  • Experience defining and executing development test plans and/or methods

  • Must be a U.S. citizen and able to obtain and maintain a DoD Top Secret Clearance

Preferred Skills:

  • Active DoD Top Secret Clearance

  • Experience translating customer requirements into functional block diagrams that can be realized as FPGAs or other Digital Logic Circuit Card Assemblies

  • Strong experience with Matlab and Simulink modeling and programming

  • Develops detailed specifications from high level design goals and performs analysis of the hardware, system environment and design goals to enable selection and implementation of the desired system topology

  • Can clearly present assigned work at program technical reviews, proposals, or other technical meetings

  • Possesses strong interpersonal skills to work effectively within a development team to understand system level inter-dependencies and design constraints

  • Conduct trade studies, identify and present alternate design options

  • Experience with Systems Engineering principles
