Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is an industry-leading provider of prime satellite and payload capabilities and directed energy and electronics solutions for national security, military, and civil customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable, and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible.

Northrop Grumman Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) has an opening for a Staff Electrical Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in the Vehicle & Software Engineering Department. This position will be located in Redondo Beach, CA.
 

The Staff Electrical Engineer’s responsibilities include (but are not limited to) the following:

  • Produce photovoltaic array electrical design-related engineering drawings and specifications
  • Planning and execution of design, analysis, test, and qualification and may include mechanical solar array structures
  • Provide status, problem resolution plans and provide input as an Integrated Process Team (IPT) member to a complete solar array mechanical/electrical design and production efforts
  • Support weekly subcontract telecoms, review in-process data, attend Manufacturing Readiness Reviews (MRRs) and Test Readiness Reviews (TRRs), review test data and support pre-shipment readiness review meeting
  • Develop cost estimates and track efforts to these estimates
     

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Electrical Engineering or in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university with 14 years of engineering experience OR Master’s Degree in STEM with 12 years of engineering experience OR Ph.D. in STEM with 9 years of engineering experience
  • A minimum of 5 years of experience with procurement of aerospace hardware design and/or development of components.
  • Hands on experience in mechanical/electrical troubleshooting of components and equipment
  • Must have a working knowledge of solar cells and experience with solar cell electrical testing
  • Technical writing experience for test procedures and technical documentation
  • Understanding of mechanical and electrical drawings including electrical schematics and assembly drawings
     

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active SSBI clearance
  • Working and programming knowledge with LabView
  • Self-motivated, work with minimal supervision and excellent communication skills
  • Has demonstrated multidisciplinary skills and ability to work with manufacturing and other engineering disciplines
  • Experience with Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Project
