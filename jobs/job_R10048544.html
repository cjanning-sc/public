ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Systems Engineer / Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Detroit, UT and will support the Sentinel/Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

What you’ll get to do: 

  • Track requirement allocations into specs, traceability (parent to child… vertical)
  • Track status and report on barrens and orphans
  • Track and report status of spec board approvals
  • Brief status weekly
  • Check provided CI spec list for accuracy
  • Tracking status for any new specs that get created not on the original list
  • Talk to specialties and report any roadblocks that are preventing requirement allocation and decomposition.
  • Help in development of tableau dashboard and then use “Tableau” metrics to identify and track requirements, barrens and orphans, report weekly status, etc.
  • You will be deployed to various engineering specialties to get documents correctly updated in DOORS.  This includes ensuring the following are correctly established in each file:
  • Tailored text – (filled out by the specialty engineers but could be populated in DOORS by this person)
  • Allocation
  • Develop processes, decompose processes into program plan, update RMMP, update compliance process, etc.

Basic Qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either a Systems Engineer level or Principal level.

  • Bachelor's degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline
  • A minimum 2 years of related engineering experience with a bachelor’s degree; 0 years with a master’s (Principal level requires 5 years with a bachelor’s degree; 3 years with a master’s degree; or 0 years with a PhD)
  • U.S. Citizenship with an active DoD Secret Clearance with an investigation date within the last 6 years
  • Ability to obtain Special Access Program Clearance
  • Experience with design specifications and requirement decomposition

Preferred Qualifications:

  • DoD Top Secret Security Clearance
  • GBSD Program Cleared (CJ1)
  • Experienced in consolidating stakeholder needs across multiple Integrated Product Teams (IPTs)
  • Knowledge of Project Management principles and experience
  • Experience applying Agile Engineering principles and methodologies
  • Experience with Interface Control Documents (ICDs)
  • Experience with Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) and Systems Engineering
  • Experience using Common Product Lifecycle Management (CPLM)
  • Experience developing program procedures and processes
  • Experience developing, implementing, and updating project schedules
  • Proficient using IBM Rationale Dynamic Object-Oriented Requirements System (DOORS)
  • Excellent decision making and communication (written and oral) skills
  • Experience coordinating between cross functional teams
  • Experience with Interfacing with various stakeholders to drive to a consensus, conclusion, or action plan
