Northrop Grumman, Strategic Space Systems Division, has an opening for an Administrative Assistant 4 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Redondo Beach, CA.

 

Successful candidate will be a vital member of the team in an administrative leadership role, which by nature of the position involves high-level contacts and exposure to sensitive program, new business, personnel and technical information necessitating considerable use of tact, diplomacy, discretion, and judgment.  

 

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Performing normal office functions such as setting up and maintaining files/content; vetting callers and making proper referrals; arranging meetings and tours; arranging for catering, maintaining Outlook calendars; and receiving, referring, or answering mail

  • Reviewing documents and communications for appropriate grammatical usage; answers questions relating to office operations and established policies and procedures

  • Gathering, compiling, and reporting on information relevant to organization, and for inputs into newsletters, communications, and business unit website; sometimes assists with creativity

  • Providing support to high volume travel (periodic) to include travel arrangements, expense reports, and all forms of correspondence

  • Maintaining and ordering supplies for the team

  • Supporting administrative functions for program initiatives and major milestone events

 

Essential Functions:

  • Maintains Director’s and next level Managers’ schedules and coordinates management meetings

  • Communicates with executive, administrative, management, team and customer personnel to gather or convey relevant information, and provide logistical support, interface for customer visits.

  • Coordinating internal and external briefings/presentations; coordinating agendas, logistically setting-up and “driving” key management and technical meetings; taking notes

  • Maintaining Operating Unit organizational charts and staffing/seating plans

  • Supports and maintains a positive and collaborative work environment

 

The ability to be collaborative, proactive, self-motivated, well-organized, detail oriented, and assertive as necessary and able to execute projects and tasks independently will be vital to the success of this role.
