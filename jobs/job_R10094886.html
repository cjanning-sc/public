Job Description

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking a Software Engineer to support the SNAP Department (Strategic Networks, Applications, and Products).  Our team focuses on Network Solutions, and supports a variety of technologies across our three departments; C3 Solutions, Strategic Networks, and Small Form Factors. C3 Solutions, specializes in Tactical Data Link processors, emulators, and simulators.  Our Strategic Networks team builds solutions to defending our country's nuclear assets.  And lastly, our Small Form Factors Department brings SWaP optimized solutions (Crypto, SDRs, Space Networks) to new domains, bridging us into Space, Weapons, and more.  This position will be located in the beautiful city of San Diego, CA, at one of our two locations; Rancho Carmel, or Kearny Mesa. 

At the heart of Defining Possible is our commitment to missions. In rapidly changing global security environments, Northrop Grumman brings informed insights and software-secure technology to enable strategic planning. We’re looking for innovators who can help us keep building on our wide portfolio of secure, affordable, integrated, and multi-domain systems and technologies that fuel those missions. By joining in our shared mission, we’ll support yours of expanding your personal network and developing skills, whether you are new to the field or an industry thought-leader. At Northrop Grumman, you’ll have the resources, support, and team to do some of the best work of your career.

This position will require onsite work however for this role we are able to offer flexible work arrangements and some remote work will be possible.

This position may be filled as a Software Engineer or Principal Software Engineer.

  • Bachelor of Science (BS) degree in science, engineering, or related discipline with 9 years of experience; or 7 years of relevant experience with a Masters (MS) degree; or 4 years with a PhD;
    - An additional 4 years of relevant experience/education can be considered in lieu of a     bachelor's degree
  • 1+ year of recent professional experience in C++
  • Experience working in a Linux Development Environment
  • Clearance: Candidate must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain an active U.S. Government DOD Secret Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Familiar with Atlassian Tool Suite tools such as GIT, JIRA, BitBucket
  • Familiar with additional programming languages: C, Java, Python, LabVIEW
  • Familiar with CI/CD Pipelines Tools: Jenkins, SonarCube, Fortify
  • Familiar with Ansible Scripting
  • Agile development experience
  • Device I/O experience.
