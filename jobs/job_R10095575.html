About Your Opportunity:

The European Business Development Support Officer is a key enabler for European Business growth supporting our Director of European Business Development to maximize the performance of our expanded BD team’s output through the provision of effective and efficient support. The European BD Support Officer will work collaboratively with teams and enabling functions across NG in UK, across Europe, the US, and also externally with customers, partners and suppliers, to drive growth in the European region.

The role will require close working across the London-based business development team and with other key stakeholders based in the region across the Corporation’s four Sectors (Space Systems, Aeronautics Systems, Mission Systems and Defence Systems).  There will also be significant contact with the Sector and Divisional BD teams and existing subsidiary businesses.

Your Benefits:

  • Flexible working schedules - we offer flexible and hybrid working arrangements. Talk to us at the application stage about any scheduling preferences you may have.
  • Flexible Benefits Package – choose which NGUKL benefits you want to satisfy your personal needs. Core Benefits provided for you are Healthcare, Dental, Life Assurance and Pension. Benefits you can flex include Critical Illness Cover, Health Cash Plan, and Health Assessments.
  • Employee Incentive Programme – exceptional performance is recognized through our annual incentive programme which is awarded to top performers who excel
  • Career Development – opportunity for ongoing professional development and career growth opportunities

Your Responsibilities:

  • Supporting the European Director on issues of strategy, planning, business development and government relations
  • Supporting the Annual Planning cycle with the European Director and wider European BD team as required
  • Conducting research and producing draft documents, briefing papers, reports and presentations as requested
  • Update and maintain customer relationship management data and produce reports when required
  • Build own network of peers within the International NG BD community to pull through best practice within the business
  • Coordinate, attend and support Company customer events and visits when required

Your Experience:

  • Degree-qualified in a relevant business subject or equivalent experience, preferably with experience of Business Development or Sales and Marketing
  • Ability to collaborate with the confidence and integrity to earn stakeholders’ confidence.
  • Excellent problem solving, influencing, negotiation and organisational skills and adept at dealing with competing priorities and deadlines at a senior level.
  • An action-orientated self-starter, with the ability to work with limited supervision, whom is looking to play a major part in supporting business growth and operational maturity.
  •  Strong written and verbal communication skills and highly developed interpersonal abilities
