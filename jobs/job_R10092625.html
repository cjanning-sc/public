ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a System Test Project Manager 1 to lead the internal and external working group team. This position will be located in Detroit, MI and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. Learn more about the Sentinel program here. ACME Engineering supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What you will get to do:

Responsible for leading a team that may include project managers and other individual contributors on the Sentinel program to support program management processes, implementation, Integration and driving discipline to ensure program commitments are met. The selected candidate will also support internal and external working groups requiring significant coordination across Sentinel segment and element teams and functional organizations as well as Government and Program leadership.  This position will be responsible for coordinating with the Program IPTs, Segments, Elements and various stakeholders to integrate various program management products/documents, provide oversight across the Segment, and support the planning and implementation of any assigned ad hoc projects.  Eligible candidates must have an emphasis on qualities such as effective communication skills, analytical thinking, people leadership, managerial skills, networking, excellent presentation skills, and a passion of excellence.  They should have experience with project management, and Program Management processes and enabling tools.

As a leader, you always act with integrity, and the highest ethical standards characterize everything you do. You enable your team to achieve predictable and balanced results, while satisfying the needs of internal and external partners. You treat all people with respect, and you model personal growth and continuous development.
