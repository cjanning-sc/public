Northrop Grumman Space Systems - Support Systems Team (SST) is seeking qualified individuals to design and develop simulation based training devices working closely with Systems Engineering.  This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent program (GBSD). Learn more about the GBSD program here. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

  • Support the Systems Engineering process,  testing methods, procedures, and problems; device specification and yield problems; minor redesign as needed; and analysis of reliability and maintainability for Training Systems. 

  • Assist in defining product definition for training requirements.

  • Understanding the wide application of technical principle, theories, and concepts in the field. 

  • Collaborates with Systems and Quality Engineers to ensure hardware requirements are met.

  • Assists in developing technical data packages for customer delivery.

  • Able to communicate effectively and clearly present technical approaches and findings.

  • Interacts with partners and suppliers on significant technical matters when required for coordination.

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's of Science in Engineering and a minimum of 5 years’ experience; 3 years with Masters; 0 years with PhD.

  • Must have U.S. Citizenship with a current or active Secret Clearance.

  • Experience troubleshooting hardware.

  • Engineering Process experience.

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:
