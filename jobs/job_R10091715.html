ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Senior Principal Test Engineer.  This position will be located in Detroit, UT and will support the Sentinel – Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.  This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

As a Sr. Principal Test Engineer, you'll be responsible for designing, developing and implementing testing methods/equipment in support of test execution. You'll achieve the aforementioned by/with/through first-hand experience, leveraging SME experience and/or referencing ACME PrOP. 

Specific roles and responsibilities will include:

- Identify and facilitate the resources needed to successfully execute test plans/procedures
- Oversee the completion of technical reports and test results in addition to ensuring that all required documentation is properly stored via a TDMS
- Will/may be responsible for supporting systems test for authoring test procedures in coordination with program requirements. Conduct and oversee execution of testing procedures on customer test articles per work instructions and training procedures
- Complete and/or support test readiness reviews
- Frequent collaboration with external IPTs is required
- Must possess the ability to handle multiple tasks in a dynamic environment, including critical thinking and technical problem-solving skills
- Technical Writing for test execution products
- Prepare and present reports, briefings, documents to leadership
