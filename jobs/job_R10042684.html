Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Contract Administrator to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located on site in Redondo Beach, CA with partial telework available.

Essential Functions:

  • Conduct proposal preparation, contract negotiation, contract administration, and customer contact activities to provide for proper contract acquisition and fulfillment in accordance with company policies, legal requirements, and customer specifications
  • Examine estimates of material, equipment services, production costs, performance requirements, and delivery schedules to ensure accuracy and completeness
  • Prepare bids; process specifications, progress, and other reports; advise management of contractual rights and obligations; compile and analyze data; and maintain historical information
  • Perform contract execution tasks
  • Administer, extend, negotiate and terminate standard and nonstandard contracts
  • Provide knowledge of the FAR and DFARs, general knowledge of pre and post-award federal government contracting industry practices, techniques, and standards
  • Demonstrate the skill and ability to perform moderately complex contracts administration tasks, to include proposal preparation, the development of change orders and processing of inter-company work orders
  • Develop solutions to a variety of problems of moderate scope and complexity

Basic Qualifications for a Contract Administrator:

  • Bachelor’s degree with 3 years of contracts, proposals, and/or business-related experience, or a Master's degree with 1 year of stated experience
  • Experience with Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel)
  • Knowledge of FAR, DFARS, and other U.S. Federal Government contracting regulations
  • Able to obtain/maintain a Security Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • An advanced degree in a relevant field (JD, MBA, etc.)
  • Current active DoD Secret/TS Clearance
  • Recent experience as a Contracts Professional in the defense industry
  • Strong knowledge of FAR, DFARS, and other U.S. Federal Government contracting regulations
  • Experience with various contract types including cost reimbursable, fixed price, time and materials
  • Experience with various types of agreements, including Non-Disclosure Agreements and Teaming Agreements
