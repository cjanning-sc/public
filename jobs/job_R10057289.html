The Payload and Ground Systems organization within the Northrop Grumman Space Systems pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter. Come be a part of our mission.

The Strategic Force Programs (SFP) Business Unit is seeking a Staff (Level 5) Special Test Equipment Electronics Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals.  This position will be located in Azusa, CA and report under our hardware engineering team. We are looking for someone who has the ability to report on-site, but we can accommodate partial teleworking based upon business/program need.

Exciting opportunity for a technical and functional deputy leader of an emerging Special Test Equipment (STE) organization focusing on the design, development, assembly, Integration & Test (I&T), and validation of Special Test Equipment (STE) for Space hardware. Responsibilities will include to further help evolve our technology and grow our business into new domains and markets. In this role you will be responsible for technical and program leadership of a multi disciplined STE team for the design, development, assembly, Integration & Test (I&T), and validation of Electrical Engineering STE for Space Hardware. The ideal candidate for this position is someone who is passionate about leadership, diversity & inclusion, advances in technology, has a creative mindset, dedicated to catalyzing continuous improvements, and enjoys proactive interaction and collaboration with multiple engineering teams and functional disciplines.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field & 14 years of related technical engineering experience, OR a Master’s Degree & 12 years of related technical engineering experience
  • Extensive technical and functional engineering leadership experience as an Integrated Product Team Lead responsible for program cost and schedule accountability
  • Proposal and program development experience, including: Earned Value Management Systems (EVMS), Integrated Master Schedule (IMS), Technical Description (TD), and Basis of Estimate (BOE) creation
  • Extensive team leadership experience in an Engineering laboratory transitioning from design to production / manufacturing performing Special Test Equipment (STE) hardware, software, & firmware integration and test activities through creating assembly procedures and test plans
  • Experience with presenting program status and program cost and schedule accountability reviews throughout the engineering product lifecycle for executive leadership, functional management, and/or senior level internal / external stakeholders
  • Experience with creating and presenting formal review packages
  • Experience with testing hardware and/or STE supporting testing activities
  • Experience developing and operating STE
  • Demonstrated problem solving, organization, coordination, and planning skills
  • Ability to obtain & maintain a DoD security clearance (US Citizenship Required)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current & active DoD security clearance
  • Experience in Space Systems
  • Experience with supporting formal reviews such as Test Readiness Review (TRR) and Failure Review Board (FRB)
  • Experience programming in LabVIEW and/or LabVIEW FPGA
  • Demonstrated test equipment trouble-shooting and failure diagnosis experience
  • Knowledge of environmental test methods and practices for Space Hardware
  • In-depth understanding of Commercial Off the Shelf (COTS) computer architecture, interconnect/interfaces, and standard communication protocols
  • Experience with standard industry requirements management tools like DOORS
  • Experience in generating Verification Compliance Requirements Matrices, Requirements Verification Plans, Test Requirements Documents, Test Procedures and Requirements Sell-off Documents
  • Knowledge of environmental test methods and practices
  • Experience working in an engineering laboratory and transition to production environments performing STE hardware, software, & firmware integration and test activities
