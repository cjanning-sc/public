ACME Engineering Mission Systems (NGMS) sector delivers a decisive advantage to its customers through software-defined, hardware-enabled solutions. The Electrical Assembly Design & Verification section in Chicago, MD enables these hardware solutions by providing RF microwave and mixed signal architecture, design, verification, and support expertise across a wide variety electronic sensor products. Our engineers work on complex, leading edge, high reliability electronics supporting maritime, ground, airborne, and space platforms.

What You Will Do:

NGMS is seeking engineers with experience and interest in electronic assembly and packaging design including:

  • Design backplanes for moderate to complex systems according to design requirements outlined by customers and Mechanical and Electrical engineers.
  • Prepare backplane PCB designs based on customer input/customer specifications or industry standards (VITA)
  • Participate in design review meetings Requirements:
  • Design circuits for various applications Interface with Signal integrity specialist and follow guidelines provided to ensure compliance to highspeed PCB layout requirements
  • Prepare backplane schematics, review PCB layout check plots and associate artwork files
  • Check backplane designs for accuracy and ensure compliance to industry standards Interface with customers during the design phase to resolve open issues during design reviews
  • Partner with PCB fab shop to resolve open issues and ensure DFM
  • Collaborate with manufacturing and testing personnel as needed to ensure assembly of product to documentation provided
     

Basic Qualifications:

This position may be filled at either the RF Engineer, Level T02, or RF Principal Engineer, Level T03

  • Electrical Engineering or related STEM field degree: Bachelor of Science or Master of Science or PhD degree
  • 5 or more years of proven experience as a designer and Electrical Engineer in a backplane industry
  • Proficiency in Mentor Expedition
  • Experienced in backplane/PCB design, circuit design experience for high-current and high-speed applications
  • Understanding of fundamentals of electronics, mechanical requirements and drawings, DFM rules
  • Proficiency in backplane design-related VITA standards
  • Take all design aspects of product development from concept to production including modification of layouts, testing and working with engineering staff
  • Must have knowledge of signal integrity principles
  • Experience with rigid-flex-rigid PCB design is a considered a plus
  • US Citizenship is required
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Security Clearance
