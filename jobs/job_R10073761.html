ACME Engineering Aeronautics has an opening for a Research & Advanced Design Special Program Development Manager. This person will need to be located near a ACME Engineering facility within the D.C. metro area.

This position, reporting to the Strategy & Growth (S&G) function, and supporting the Research & Advanced Design (R&AD) organization, will work with select customers to identify and pursue innovative technology development programs, create engagement strategies, and ultimately address USG customer needs and warfighting challenges.

The Manager, R&AD Special Program Development will be the primary research and technology interface for ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems to a broad range of US government organizations, with primary focus on restricted technology & prototype development organizations. This includes entities within AFRL, AFLCMC, ONR, NAWC, DARPA, AS&T, DS&T, SAF/AQ, the test community, and others. Additionally, the Manager will support interaction with other US government entities to include OUSD(R&E), SYSCOMs, MAJCOMs, COCOMs, SAF/AQ, Joint Staff, and others. The individual selected for this role will rely on their outstanding personal credibility, built upon a combination of strong technical expertise and exceptional interpersonal and communication skills, to build and sustain trusted relationships with customer R&D Program Managers, technical staff, and organization leadership. The Manager will work intimately with these customers on a day-to-day basis, inform customers of R&AD’s capabilities, facilitate connections between customers and members of R&AD, and provide the R&AD organization robust customer and technology understanding in support of development and implementation of a coherent research strategy to enable long-term ACME Engineering objectives and growth.

The R&AD Special Program Development Manager will work skillfully across US Government and ACME Engineering organizations to interface daily with customers, coordinate and support customer engagements, develop and implement customer engagement strategies, and support technology planning. Specific supporting responsibilities will include developing and executing comprehensive strategies, technology gap assessments, competitive assessments, make/buy analysis, and resource planning.  The Manager will flexibly respond to a wide range of strategic and tactical tasking from leadership in the R&AD organization and in the S&BD organization with consistently superb products, exceptional customer intimacy, and highly professional judgment.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree with 9+ years related experience or Master’s degree and 7+ years experience
  • Prior experience in program management, either as a program manager or principal investigator
  • Strong understanding of DoD and other agencies’ strategic planning processes, user communities, technology transition paths, and customer trends
  • Ability to travel 25-50% of the time
  • Active and Current DoD Top Secret clearance and experience with SAP/SAR and SCI


Preferred Qualifications:

  • STEM related degree
  • Basic understanding of relevant mission technologies, including Aerodynamics, Aircraft Design, Propulsion, Aerostructures, Survivability, Communications, Controls, Artificial Intelligence, Autonomy, and Thermal Management
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Broad and deep relationships with and knowledge of USG R&D stakeholders, portfolios, and priorities
  • Strong technical background, including Masters or PhD in Science, Engineering, or related technical discipline
  • Recent experience working with science and technology customers (such as AFRL, ONR, DARPA) and the classified test community
  • Experience working with SAF/HAF, MAJCOMs, COCOMs, SYSCOMs, and OUSD(R&E)
  • Strong understanding of national strategy, military roles and missions, organization, enterprise processes and business contacts
  • Proven experience in the research & technology and advanced design aeronautics arenas
  • Proven ability to interact skillfully and diplomatically with colleagues and stakeholders
  • Proven experience developing and sustaining customer relationships to drive program results
  • Enthusiastic willingness to conduct customer engagement, strategy development, and other analyses and to quickly produce exceptional reports and presentations across a broad range of topics simultaneously
