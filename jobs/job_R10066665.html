Northrop Grumman’s Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is an industry-leading provider of satellite payloads and directed energy solutions for national security, military, and human exploration missions. We are built on a strong heritage of innovative, affordable, and reliable aerospace and defense products our customers and nation rely on our team to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible. 

SSSD Business Management is seeking an Operations Research Analyst or Principal Operations Research Analyst to fill our critical and specialized technical needs. This position will be located at our Space Systems Sector in Redondo Beach, CA.

Working in both classified and unclassified networks, with up to 25% telework available, the selected applicant will work in a people-focused environment interfacing with users and customers on Department of Defense (DoD) systems.  This role participates in the entire software development lifecycle of web and windows-based applications using multiple high-level programming languages and technologies from concept to execution. The systems we develop enable the highest-quality decision making from a dynamic team of both technical and business management end-users.

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Designing, developing, documenting, testing, and debugging applications software and systems
  • Adhering to software standards and requirements
  • Multi-tasking and prioritizing multiple projects, deadlines, and responsibilities
  • Assist with analyzing complex problems and developing solutions

This position requires the incumbent to possess a Department of Defense (DoD) active Top Secret (TS) security clearance or access to Special Compartmentalized Information (SCI).

THIS ROLE MAY BE FILLED AT EITHER A LEVEL 2, OPERATIONS RESEARCH ANAYST OR LEVEL 3, OPERATIONS RESEARCH ANALYST AS OUTLINED ON THE BASIC QUALIFICATIONS.

Operations Research Analyst - Level 2 (no title)

Basic Qualifications:

  • BA/BS degree
  • 2 years of work experience to include any combination of Business Management, Systems Development or Administration OR a Master’s with 0 years of stated experience
  • An active DoD TS and/or SCI Security Clearance

 
Preferred Qualifications: 

  • DAX scripting experience and familiarity with MS PowerBI
  • Ability to acquire a CompTIA Security+ certification within 6 months of hire
  • SQL and/or VBA script writing experience
  • Familiarity with Html, CSS and JavaScript
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Project and/or other Scheduling Programs

Operations Research Analyst - Level 3 (Principal title)

Basic Qualifications: 

  • BA/BS degree with a minimum of 5 years work experience to include any combination of Business Management, Systems Development or Administration or 3 years of stated experience with a Masters Degree
  • An active DoD TS and/or SCI Security Clearance

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Ability to acquire a CompTIA Security+ certification within 6 months of hire
  • DAX scripting experience and familiarity with MS Power BI
  • Python programming experience
  • Familiarity with Django (or other) web framework
  • .Net programming experience
  • SQL and/or VBA script writing experience
  • Familiarity with Html, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Project and/or other Scheduling Programs
