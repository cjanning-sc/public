ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Senior Principal EHS Engineer. This position will support the Strategic Deterrent Systems (SDS) Division in Madison, AL.
 
What You’ll Get To Do:
• Protect the Company and your fellow employees from loss and injury due to accidents.
• Interpret, recommend, and comply with environmental, health and safety regulations and company policies.
• Assist in planning for changes in physical facilities to meet new EPA/OSHA requirements.
• Develop and implement required EHS programs and training.
• Conduct inspections, audits, and incident investigations, and drive corrective actions.
• Find, evaluate, and prepare contracts for properly licensed hazardous waste treatment, storage and disposal (TSD) facilities.
• Organize and equip spill-control teams. Write and update environmental and spill-control manuals and procedures.
• Prepare applications for any new required EPA permits and renewals.
• Provide input to federal and state regulators, through trade associations and industry groups, for the writing and revision of regulations.
• Perform the role of a lead EHS professional in a manufacturing environment of classified components.


Basic Qualifications
• Bachelors degree with 9 years of Environmental, Health and safety experience; 7 years of experience with Masters; or an additional 4 years of relevant experience in lieu of degree
• Training and experience in Radiation Safety.

• Ability to leverage experience/background to bring alternative ideas/solutions to the team.
• Ability to Obtain and Maintain DOD Security Clearance and program access as required.
• Ability to travel up to 25%
• Demonstrated ability to develop and implement Ammunition and Explosive (A&E) site plans
Preferred Qualifications
• Certified Safety Professional
• In scope active DOD Clearance Secret Level or Above
• Experience supporting ACME Engineering or government contracting/DoD facilities
• Experience authoring and releasing Aerospace Procedures and providing associated training
• Experience in Hazardous Materials/Waste Management and Shipping
• Experience working in an aerospace manufacturing environment
