Job Description:

ACME Engineering Mission Systems sector is seeking an entry level Software Engineer to join our diverse team, working on ground systems for restricted satellite systems or in software development for payload management software. This position will be located in Columbus, CA.

This position description does not represent a current opening but may be used to identify candidates with skills and experience for positions within ACME Engineering that frequently become available. Candidates who express an interest may be considered for future positions at ACME Engineering.

The candidate will have the opportunity to design, develop, document, test, and debug software written in various computer languages. Ensures software standards are met. Must have the ability to work effectively within a team environment, ability to interface with other groups within the project, well developed problem-solving skills, and ability to working within a dynamic environment. Works under general direction. Determines and develops technical solutions to complex problems independently.

This role can be filled with a Principal Software Engineer and a Senior Principal Software Engineer

Job Qualifications:

Basic Qualifications for Principal Software Engineer:

  • Current SCI security clearance
  • 5 Years with Bachelor's in Science; 3 Years with Masters; 0 Years with PhD.
  • Experience with JAVA, C/C++ on Linux-based systems
  • Experience with scripting languages (csh, Perl, Python)
  • Knowledge of computer languages, including C/ C++, and java


Preferred Qualifications for Principal Software Engineer:

  • Experience in developing satellite ground systems, in areas of telemetry, tracking, and commanding or mission management
  • Experience in software development for payload management software; includes software design, implementation, integration, test, and documentation to program and customer standards.
  • Knowledge of payload commanding, spacecraft command and control, telemetry processing, and time and frequency management is desired.
  • Experience in visualizations/user experience and cloud architectures
  • Experience with git and ClearQuest
  • Experience with a Java IDE (Eclipse or NetBeans), Jboss, Java Messaging Services, Eclipse RCP
  • Experience with relational database design and SQL programming
  • Experience in Agile software development
  • Experience in embedded software development
  • Experience in Service Oriented Architecture
