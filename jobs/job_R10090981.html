Northrop Grumman Aerospace Systems has an opening for an Aircraft Installer Structure to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in El Segundo, CA.

Position Summary:

This occupation requires the assembly, including necessary fabrication of aircraft mechanical and structural parts, of production aircraft.
This individual will work with jigs, fixtures, and tooling, or without them, when work operations do not require their use.

The Aircraft Installer Structures will be expected to:

  • Rivet and assemble repetitive type parts that have been assigned to specialized group, such as nutplate group, squeeze group, drilling & riveting group, etc.

  • Rivet and ream by hand and power; drills, countersinks, dimples, presses, squeeze rivets and fits component parts together.

  • May do layout for drilling holes for nutplates, clips, etc, not requiring precise measurements and markings.

  • Work with the assistance of higher classified workers.

  • Use arithmetic involving decimals and fractions.

  • May monitor and verify quality in accordance with statistical process or other control procedures.

  • May be required to work in confined space.

  • Maintained required certifications and qualifications on area of specialization.

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School Diploma/GED and 9 months of mechanical experience.

  • Ability to utilize computer based systems to perform duties.

  • Must be able/willing to work any shift or schedule.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Spartan College Certification
