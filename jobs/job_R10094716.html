Northrop Grumman Tactical Space Systems is seeking a Software Configuration Analyst to support the Tactical Space Systems Division in their development and deployment of satellite ground software to customer resources. The location for this role is in Dulles, VA; Gilbert, AZ; Colorado Springs, CO; or El Segundo, CA and has possible opportunities for full or part-time telecommuting.

What You’ll Have an Opportunity to Do:

  • Develop and maintain software configuration management tools to support configuration identification, control, reporting, and delivery of both internally developed and externally purchased commercial-off-the-shelf (COTS) software products
  • Perform configuration management and release engineering tasks to ensure new software product operating parameters are documented, comply with standard hardware configurations, and are logistically sustainable
  • Serve as program librarian.  Assign document numbers and maintain the document inventory
  • Serve as SharePoint Administrator, add and delete personnel to SharePoint groups as required
  • Maintain categories of documents as they are developed.  Deliver User Guides and other program documents as required
  • Maintain and update the program Configuration Management/Change Management Plan and program Software CM Plan
  • Review and comment on the CAMEO model tool CM and CAMEO baseline.  Participate in review and update in Program Documentation. Track and coordinate documents in process and for delivery
  • Participate in the Agile Increment Planning, daily Defect Review Board and bi-weekly Sprint Planning and Release Readiness Reviews as required.  Review bug and story tickets as required for accuracy and content. Coordinate updates to the Raptor User Guides.  Create and deliver Version Description Documents (VDDs) as required for formal software deliveries
  • Interface with DevOps Team for the generation of program Dependencies using the Syft tool.  Using Syft data create and conduct release audits on software releases
  • Create and maintain dependency documentation for program
  • Coordinate audit and documentation activities with Mission Assurance and Cyber Security
  • Support tracking of software licenses.  Create application accounts and serve as POC for the vendors delivering licenses and keys

Basic Qualifications

Principal SW Configuration Analyst

  • Bachelor’s in software engineering, Computer Science, or closely related discipline plus 5 years of configuration management experience (3 years with a Master’s Degree, 0 years with a PhD.)  Experience can be considered in lieu of a degree
  • Requires strong process background knowledge of Configuration Management and DevSecOps
  • Experience with Bitbucket, Jenkins, Ansible, Jira, Nexus, Microsoft Teams, SharePoint and Windchill
  • US Citizenship is required

Sr. Principal SW Configuration Analyst

  • Bachelor’s in software engineering, Computer Science, or closely related discipline plus 9 years of development experience (7 years with a Master’s Degree, 4 years with a PhD) with C++, Python.  Experience can be considered in lieu of degree
  • Requires strong process background knowledge of Configuration Management and DevSecOps
  • Experience with Bitbucket, Jenkins, Ansible, Jira, Nexus, Microsoft Teams, SharePoint and Windchill
  • US Citizenship is required

Preferred Qualifications:

  • An extensive knowledge of software / hardware configuration, data and change management principles
  • Proficiency in the use of company standard configuration management & Model Based tools such as CAMEO
  • Experience implementing a CMDB
  • Be able to administer and or maintain software configuration management tools to support configuration identification, control, reporting, and delivery of program products.
  • DoD Top Secret Clearance and/or SCI
