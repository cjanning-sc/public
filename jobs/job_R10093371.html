Principal Engineer Software Mission Assurance (Space/Satellite Systems)
R10093371
1st Shift

Software Mission Assurance is a unique function within the Tactical Space Systems, working on spacecraft programs, highly interactive with multiple subsystems, and knowledgeable of the overall spacecraft software development life-cycle process related to software planning, design, implementation, verification, validation, acceptance and delivery. To be successful in this role, you should have a strong understanding of the Agile software life cycle, software and firmware industry process, product compliance audits and non-conformance system, and a detail oriented approach to identify and capture noncompliance and risks throughout the software’s development life cycle and maintenance. Excellent people and technical skills are required to coordinate with various subsystems, conduct compliance analysis and audits, implement corrective actions, and interact with management, customers and subcontractors.

Note: This is not a software tester position.

In this role you will:

- Participate in bid and proposal efforts to provide cost estimates

- Derive software assurance requirements from standards, contract requirements, statement of work, processes and program plans. Develop Software Mission Assurance Plan, and implement tasks per the plan
- Participate in agile life cycle, product line development supporting a wide variety of Class A to Class D customer
- Participate in sprint planning and backlog refinement ensuring team's acceptance criteria for stories and tasks is appropriate and being met as planned
- Ensure build and verification testing of new features being added every sprint, as well as full regression testing for all previously implemented functionality in every sprint is performed per plans
- Be a member of the Software Configuration Control Boards, review software changes and approved implementation using related software problem tracking tools (e.g. TRAC, Razor, Jira, etc.)
- Witness software verification testing, software patches and software qualification to ensure compliance to requirements and procedures
- Participate in product peer review and approved product for configuration control and release before products are made available for internal or external customers
- Assist in collecting status reports and metrics. Provide weekly and monthly status to the management
- Perform process and product audits ensuring compliance to established standards
- Identify process and product non-conformances, determine root cause and support implement corrective and preventive action
- Demonstrate strong oral and written communication skills, with the ability to communicate technical topics to management and non-technical audiences

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Computer Science or a related field 
  • Minimum of 5 years of related experience
  • U.S. citizen
  • Knowledge of Software Quality Assurance standards
  • Knowledge of aerospace software development life cycles
  • Strong computer skills including MS Word, Excel, Access, and PowerPoint
  • Knowledge of software nonconforming product tracking systems

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Excellent writing communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to adapt to new challenges and ideas
  • Exceptional customer responsiveness
  • Ability to follow established processes and procedures
  • Be detail oriented and self-driven
  • Ability to successfully work multiple concurrent tasks
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills
  • Must be self-motivated and detail-oriented
  • Capable of working on multiple levels of compliance requirements
  • Work well under pressure and adapts well to unplanned tasks
  • Ability and willingness to continuously learn and implement new processes
  • Dedicated to teamwork and be able to work very well in a team environment
