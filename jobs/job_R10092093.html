Northrop Grumman’s Space Systems sector is seeking a Staff Systems Engineer.  This position is in our Strategic Deterrent Systems Division to support the Sentinel Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program in support of the United States Air Force.  This position will be located on campus in Roy, UT.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

The qualified applicant will become part of Northrop Grumman’s Space System team supporting the Air Force’s Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • This will include development, production, testing, and deployment of On-Board Test Equipment hardware. The selected candidate will work in a dynamic people-focused environment where he/she will interact with users, customers and other engineers.
  • Responsible for system requirements related to flight instrumentation, allocation of those requirements to unit level, verification planning, analysis planning, and execution in a Model based Systems Engineering (MBSE) environment.
  • Responsible for aiding in the design, test planning and test execution of flight instrumentation, proactively interfacing with other SE&I organizations and GBSD IPTs to ensure successful flow down, implementation and verification of requirements.  Significant customer coordination is also expected in this role.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Science, Technology, Engineering or Math (STEM) with 14 years’ experience; 12 years’ experience with a Master’s degree; or 9 years’ experience with a PhD

  • Ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) Clearance
