ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Preconstruction Services Manager 3 to its team of diverse and qualified individuals. This leadership position will be located in Detroit, Michigan or work remote/virtually and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.  Learn more about the Sentinel program here. ACME Engineering supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What you will get to do:

The Preconstruction Services Manager 3 will have experience delivering design-build and design-bid-build construction, renovation and environmental remediation projects for commercial and public-sector customers. Preconstruction Services Manager 3 is responsible for the leadership and successful completion of the preconstruction phase of projects to meet the cost objectives for planning, estimating, purchasing and bidding.  Projects are valued at $5-100 million.

Daily tasks include but not limited to:

  • Maintain a firm commitment to safety behaviors and protocols that lead to an incident and injury free workplace.
  • Develop high-performance teams through supervision, training, coaching, and mentoring.
  • Lead all preconstruction efforts, including proposal development, budgeting, planning, estimating, constructability reviews, value engineering, purchasing and bidding, and cost control for major construction and environmental remediation projects.
  • Provide leadership of risk evaluation, contract negotiations, fee and pricing decisions.
  • Monitor and assist in design progress for compliance with defined cost, schedule and quality criteria, provide and update design/construction project schedules.
  • Coordinate resolution of constructability issues during design and coordinate pricing of alternative design concepts.
  • Participate in development of the construction and logistics planning.
  • Develop and maintain long-lasting relationships with customers, the design community, and subcontractors to lay the foundation for successful project execution and future business development opportunities.
  • Support Business Development and Marketing Departments to secure new work for the business unit.
  • Ensure strict adherence to ethics and compliance requirements for all projects.
