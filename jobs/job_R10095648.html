Northrop Grumman, Strategic Space Systems Division, has an opening for a System Test Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Redondo Beach, California.

Essential Functions:

Synthesizes customer contractual needs and requirements into system test solutions that acknowledges technical, schedule and cost constraints. Develops and directs preparation and execution of comprehensive test plans, procedures and schedule for completing systems, and coordinates integrated testing activities. Reviews and evaluates test requirements to insure completeness of test program needs.

Electrical Test Engineer to support Communication System programs. Candidate will need experience in ATS (Automated Test Sequence) and procedure writing and help run day to day floor activities. Candidate will also work closely with Lead TE and system Engineering to develop new test cases and troubleshoot any anomalies. The Test Engineer will participate in all phases of integration and test of a payload subsystem.

Candidate must be willing to travel > 70% of the time starting Quarter 4 of 2023. DoD Secret clearance preferred. May also require to work overtime and weekends depending on the test phase.

Basic Qualification:

  • Bachelor of Science Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Math) discipline plus 2 years of engineering experience OR a Masters of Science Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Math) discipline plus 0 years of engineering experience.

  • Experience in Electrical System Level Testing, Integration or Test

  • Some shift work and travel might be required

  • Must be able to travel more than 75% of the time to support offsite testing

  • Candidate must be able to obtain and maintain a security clearance

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Electrical Test procedure writing, authorizing, and execution

  • Run day to day floor activities on Flight payloads including create, update, and execute Automated Test Scripts (ATS)

  • Work closely with Lead Test Engineer and Systems Engineering to develop new test cases, review test data, and troubleshoot any anomalies

  • Participate in all phases of the integration and test of a payload subsystem and cross-team collaboration

  • Active DOD Secret Clearance.
