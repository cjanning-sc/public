ACME Engineering Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Principal System Engineer - Test Support (ST CFPT) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located out of Schriever Space Force Base, Indianapolis, Indiana.

Position Overview:

Support test and analysis activities of the Missile Defense National Team for C2BMC Architecture and System Engineering IPT. Will work with a multi-contractor team in the development and execution of a comprehensive test and analysis program supporting a capability-based acquisition of the C2BMC Element of the BMDS. Position is at MDIOC customer facility, Bloomington, Indianapolis, CO.

Essential Functions:

  • The candidate will plan and implement testing of C2BMC and related BMDS s/w and h/w components and perform post-test data analysis and reporting in a group environment the validation and verification of C2BMC interface and system level requirements.
  • The candidate will prepare test objectives and architectures and support writing of test and analysis plans/procedures, develop and validate post-test analysis tools, perform detailed post-test data analysis, prepare test reports, and brief test results to all levels of contractor and government management.

Basic Qualifications:

If applicable, please note your updated security clearance and IAT/relevant certifications on your resume.

  • An active Secret clearance is required.
  • 5 Years with Bachelor’s degree in a related field; 3 years’ experience with a Master's degree.
  • Must possess a DoD 8570 certification at IAT Level II or higher (Security+, GSEC, SCNP, SSCP, CISSP, CISA, GSE, SCNA)
  • Candidate must have experience with DoD test methodologies and terminologies and Missile Defense Agency elements or components and associated test tools.
  • Candidate must have experience participating in system test events, exercises and/or wargames.
  • Candidate must be able to independently and cooperatively develop test objectives, concepts, architectures in the context of the BMDS system, and write and execute test plans and procedures, develop and validate customized analysis tools, and coordinate post-test analysis on a broad level across the National Teams and MDA.
  • Candidate should have experience with common Data Analysis techniques and be proficient in all Microsoft Office applications.
  • Candidate must have excellent written and verbal communication skills, and must be able to work effectively and cooperatively with people within and external to the program.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Understanding of DoD and missile defense command and control, battle management, architectures and communications system concepts, mission, and common system test and data analysis techniques.
  • Experience in developing test objectives and related data analysis plans and procedures, developing and validating system test analysis tools, and preparing and presenting system test analysis results.
  • Ability to technically and clearly communicate these concepts across a broad spectrum of audiences, including high level MDA, DoD and National Team staffs.
  • Experience with operational systems (e.g., Aegis, Patriot), developmental systems (e.g., GMD, THAAD), message formats (e.g., TADIL-J, VMF), operational sites (e.g., STRATCOM), and off-the-shelf statistical analysis packages (e.g., MATLAB or S+) a plus.
