ACME Engineering's Enterprise Services is seeking Metadata Management Analysts to join the Information Architecture and Governance (IAG) organization of the Chief Data Office (CDO).  The Metadata Management Analysts will coordinate with business and technical data stewards in sectors and functional organizations, including the CDO, to manage data elements, metadata and processes to drive consistent data governance, classification, and quality across the enterprise so that the data consumers can find, securely access and use data.  We are looking for self-motivated, enthusiastic people who are excited about taking on a variety of challenges as we continue to develop data and analytics solutions. Successful candidates will be able to work under only very general supervision.

We will consider fulltime, virtual telecommuting candidates for the role. Alternatively, this role can be located in Northern VA, Chicago, MD or other major ACME Engineering sites. Occasional travel may be required to engage with key stakeholders.  This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off. 

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Work with business data stewards in sectors and functional organizations to assist in conducting data lineage, deriving business rules (attributes, calculations, derivations, etc.), and understanding data attributes (columns) purpose and utilization

  • Enable cross-enterprise usage of data elements within the assigned sub-domain by coordinating cross-sector and functional organization’s definition and maintenance of data elements within the assigned sub-domain; drive enterprise level master data requirements to new and “in-flight projects”

  • Coordinate with business data stewards to create a quality data model, consistent across enterprise, of the data systems for the assigned domain

  • Help create enterprise level data quality metrics and reporting

  • Partner with sector and functional organization data administrators to ensure that standards and Principles and Operating Practices (PrOP) are understood and well implemented

  • Support sector and functional organization issue and resolution tasks for data issues for the data elements within the assigned domain

  • Serve as a liaison between Business and Functional areas and technology to ensure that data related business requirements for protecting sensitive data are clearly defined, communicated, well understood and considered as part of operational prioritization and planning

  • Develop & maintain inventory of the enterprise information maps of the domain or subdomain, including authoritative systems and owners

  • Support on-boarding of domain data sources to the data catalog

  • Serve as a liaison between sector and functional organizations in support of federated governance model

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree with 2 years of experience and/or master's degree. Equivalent experience will be considered in lieu of bachelor's degree.

  • Proficient with Microsoft Office tool suite (PowerPoint, Word, Excel)

  • Ability to be able to manage changing priorities and priorities from multiple stakeholders

  • Strong oral and written communication skills

  • Ability to obtain a DOD Secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Science, Technology, Engineering or Math is preferred

  • Experience with data, data analytics and/or data systems

  • Experience with Aerospace and/or Defense Industry Experience

  • Experience with one or more major commercial platforms such as Cognos, CostPoint, Salesforce, ServiceNow, Workday, etc.

  • Experience with Agile development and methodologies
