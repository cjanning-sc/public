Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Principal Supply Chain Subcontracts Specialist. This position is in support of the Sentinel Program aka Ground-Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) Engineering and Manufacturing Development (EMD) contract. 

This position may be located in one of the following locations:

  • Huntsville, AL
  • Roy, UT

Hybrid Work Location - 2 Days In Plant weekly with a 9/80 work schedule.

The purpose of the GBSD Program is to replace the current ICBM system, including the weapon systems command and control; flight, launch and ground systems; and cyber elements.

This position will perform the following functions:

  • Supports the development of subcontract specifications, work statements, and terms and conditions for the procurement of specialized materials, equipment, and/or services.
  • Responsible for source selection from contract proposal to execution phases.
  • Coordinates and collaborates with key stakeholders such as contracts, engineering, quality, supplier development, planning, operations, program and end-users.
  • Prepares bid packages, conducts bidders’ conferences, analyzes and evaluates proposals, negotiates subcontract provisions, selects or recommends subcontractors, writes awards, and administers resulting subcontracts.
  • In conjunction with quality organization, evaluates and monitors supplier quality and reliability, as well as supplier ability to support required delivery schedules.
  • Identifies strategies and opportunities to reduce cost, improve efficiency and manage risk through strategic and targeted negotiations.
  • Manages performance of subcontracts, ensures compliance with all public law requirements as well as company (and business) policies and procedures including import/export regulations.
  • Employs sound business practices and assesses supplier performance as part of follow-up activities.
  • Forecasts, reviews and approves supplier payment as needed.
  • Utilizes process tools such as Lean Six Sigma with suppliers to drive quality, efficiency and continuous improvement.
  • Executes teaming agreements and non-disclosure agreements, which may include several parties.
  • Negotiates and coordinates additions, deletions, or modifications to subcontracts.
  • Reads, interprets, and implements written procedures to maintain compliance

Role requirements:

  • Negotiate with both large businesses and small businesses on a variety of complex-type contracts including cost-plus-incentive-fee type. 
  • Must have an understanding and application of principles, concepts, practices, and standards. Full knowledge of industry practices. Independently demonstrates the skill and ability to perform complex professional tasks. 
  • Contributes to the development of new concepts, techniques, and standards.
  • Able to communicate effectively and clearly present technical approaches and findings. 
  • Able to be creative in finding solutions to resolve issues. 
  • Develop solutions to complex problems, which require the regular use of ingenuity and innovation. Ensures solutions are consistent with organization objectives.
  • Attend program meetings and may be required to present to groups.  Must be able to work independently and have strong organizational skills.
  • Must be knowledgeable of the FAR/DFARS and processes and/or procedures required to be followed in order to be in compliance with FAR/DFARS. 

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree and 5+ years of relevant experience managing high dollar program in subcontracts, contracts, or business/project management. Or, in lieu of a degree, 9+ years of relevant experience managing high dollar program in subcontracts, contracts, or business/project management
  • Strong knowledge of Microsoft Office Applications
  • Must be able to obtain and maintain a Secret clearance

Preferred qualifications:

  • Degree in Supply Chain, Business, or Finance preferred.
  • Active Secret Clearance
  • Experience with SAP
  • Change Management experience
