ACME Engineering Space Systems (NGSP) is looking for you to join our team as a Staff Communications Systems Engineer based out of either Cedar Rapids, VA

What You’ll get to Do:

Analyzes, models, designs and simulates communications systems, subsystems, and algorithms for communication networks, data links, digital radio, battle management, and command and control systems such as wide and local area networks, point-to-point systems, secure systems, signal coding and modulation schema, digital radio/communications technology and network architecture design.

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or other STEM related degree with 14 + years of experience;12 years with a Master's; 9 years with a PhD; or 18 years of experience in lieu of a degree.

  • Active TS/SCI or TS with SCI eligibility, and willingness to sit for a poly security clearance

  • Have RF communications domain expertise

  • Have an understanding of RF communications capabilities (waveforms, data-rates) and how to apply those requirements to the configuration of the RF chain

  • Experience with RF comms mission engineering

  • Experience using and configuring software defined modems

  • Experience with RF link budget analysis

  • Experience with Radio Frequency Interference analysis

  • Experience with creating and controlling engineering documentation for formal milestone reviews

  • Familiarity with Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) using SysML

  • Experience interfacing with program and customer personnel. 

  • Strong written and oral communications skills

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active TS/SCI with poly

  • Experience with Civil Engineering projects

  • Experience conducting trade-studies

  • Experience briefing at formal milestone reviews

  • Experience in working with multiple subcontractors

  • Test Engineering development for large scale Mission / Cross-Mission test campaigns
