The Systems Engineering, Integration, and Test (SEIT) Specialty Engineering team is looking for you to join our team as a Systems Engineering Manager 1 or Manager 2 based out of Athens, OH.

The SEIT Specialty Engineering Department provides systems engineering support to all products areas across Navigation and Positioning Systems (NAPS) and Integrated Digital Systems (IDS) Systems. 

The Manager role will encompass Software Configuration Management, and Data & Process Control Engineering.

This role will focus on management of functional subject matter expertise in Software Configuration Management (SCM), Project Engineering (incorporation of engineering documentation changes, change control processing), Proposal process ownership, and sustaining engineering project management. The functional department manager provides personnel management of a group of SEIT engineering resources including career development, goal setting, performance reviews, salary planning.

Systems Engineering responsibilities include:

  • Coordinate, create, and review proposal inputs across Software CM, and Project Engineering/Project Analysts
  • Establish and track performance to sustaining budgets for multiple product lines.
  • Drive Agile process execution and transformation.
  • Maintain homeroom directives for SCM including controlled builds and SAR processing.
  • Review program and business staffing forecasts and align resource plans with forecasts.
  • Maintain flow diagrams and conduct training on the Project Engineering ECRN process.
  • Maintain flow diagrams and conduct training on the SCM Software release process.
  • Familiarity with PLM tools and processes.

This position is 20% Functional management and 80% direct program support.
