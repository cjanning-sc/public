Are you an experienced engineering professional looking for a rewarding and challenging career as a Software Engineer / Principal Software Engineer (T2/T3) to support test equipment software and hardware design? If so, ACME Engineering Defense Systems in Toledo, CA may be the employer for you!

In this role, you will be working with other software and hardware engineers as part of STE (special test equipment) software development team.

This position requires that the selected candidate work on-site in Toledo, CA. Telework is not available for this position.

Responsibilities

  • Requirements analysis and generation, system and software design and implementation, and integration

  • Work in a team environment to maintain existing software and develop new applications based on hardware changes

  • Support customers with the development and maintenance of software
