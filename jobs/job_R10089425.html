ACME Engineering Defense Systems (NGDS) is seeking a Manager 3, Capture Management to Manage the Immersive Mission Solutions (IMS) Operating Unit’s (OU) Capture Team. This individual will lead capture-related activities to captures for medium- to large-sized captures, mostly valued at over $50M each. In addition, this individual will be responsible for overseeing the team of professionals working on medium- to large-sized captures. The successful candidate will have a chance to lead some of the most exciting new capture opportunities in the areas of distributed training, live training, synthetic test and training environments.

This individual will work closely with ACME Engineering’s leadership team and other industry and government partners to run all aspects of his/her assigned pursuits. The successful candidate will be a self-starter with experience in captures/proposals that will lead an overall capture effort, including investment strategy, supplier and contract partnerships, technical development, and customer relationships and alignment of priorities. Must be adept at quickly understanding what is required to win, developing compelling value propositions, and preparing strong business cases to leadership. Must have proven strong team leadership skills and writing abilities.  Candidate must be comfortable briefing at the executive level and establishing NDAs, working with GSC, teaming, and sourcing.

Primary job responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Leading and managing a team of capture management professionals.
  • Capture Leadership.
  • Customer relationship management and development; Customer acquisition approach analysis and shaping; Government Relations collaboration on engagement strategies and execution.
  • Developing capture strategy and tactics to produce capture products such as competitive assessments, teaming strategies, and solution-to-win analysis results applied to improve competitive posture.
  • Developing clear and concise Win Themes and Value Propositions for the capture.
  • Maintain relationships with internal stakeholders and functional organizations involved in program pursuit.
  • Developing relationships with teaming partners and industry associations to help drive partnerships.
  • Coordinating and collaborating with line of business management, strategy, business management, contracts, global supply chain, and engineering functions.
  • Development and management of investment capture project funds, schedule, and execution.
  • Proposal development including managing process, writing proposal responses, and developing pricing to deliver compelling and compliant proposals.
  • Clear, concise, and actionable briefings to the ACME Defense leadership team

Ethics

  • Ensuring new business activity is carried out in accordance with government, customer, and ACME Engineering policies, procedures, and processes.

  • 4+ years’ functional management experience.

  • Experience leading and directing activities of team members through execution of a formal capture process.
  • Experience interfacing with senior management, peers, and employees.
  • Aptitude to develop and execute to a strategy.
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Secret security clearance.
  • This position may require up to 25% travel.
