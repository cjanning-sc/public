ACME Engineering Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Quality Analyst - 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located at Fort Greely, AK.

Position Overview:

The Quality Analyst will develop and implement program quality plans, programs, and procedures using statistical quality control statistics, lean manufacturing concepts, and six-sigma tools and analyses. Ensures that performance and quality products conform to established company and regulatory standards. 

Essential Functions:

  • Reviews, analyzes, and reports on quality discrepancies related to assembly, process, mechanical, electrical, and electro-mechanical systems.
  • Interfaces with manufacturing, engineering, customer, vendor and subcontractor representatives to ensure requirements are met.
  • Investigates problems and develops disposition and corrective actions for recurring discrepancies.
  • Recommends corrective actions, dispositions, and modifications.
  • Work closely with Engineers, System Administrators, and Technicians within an operations and testing environment.
  • Assures compliance in program quality in accordance with contractual requirements.
  • Participates in program audit, metrics, and processes improvements.
  • Communicate openly and effectively with teammates and customer representatives.
  • Witness and inspect installations and testing activities

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels of the organization.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 3 years of related work experience, or a Master’s Degree and 1 years of related work experience, or 7 years of related work experience in lieu of a Bachelors Degree.
  • Ability to work flexible or extended hours and travel offsite as required to support business needs.
  • Self-starter with demonstrated problem-solving skills who can work with minimal direction.
  • Ability to obtain and maintain an in-scope U.S. Government DoD issued Secret Security clearance, and a minimum of an Interim Secret Clearance will be required to start.


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience as a liaison to engineering and site management.
  • GMD or related missile defense program experience.
  • Proficiency writing and following detailed setup and test procedures.
  • Demonstrated Quality experience.
  • A current active in-scope U.S. Government DoD Top Secret clearance with current or previous Special Program Access (PAR/SPA).
