1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal systems Engineer Test or Sr Principal Systems Test located at Palmdale, CA.

Duties include planning and managing work priorities and deadlines, overseeing adherence to company/government policies and procedures, and ensuring that quality and safety standards are met. This position is also responsible for managing technical risk, cost and reporting regularly to Flight Test leadership team on performance.  Daily responsibilities include supporting ground segment configuration, documentation, and scheduling to specific requirements.  Additional responsibilities include supporting annual procedure updates and audits to meet customer instructions. This engineer will have professional interaction with all levels of company leadership, as well as customers, concerning operations, scheduling, and specific phases of the test program.  Some travel may be required to support specific programs, ground operations, or special tests.   This position is on 1st shift but may occasionally support 2nd shift or 3/10 shift as necessary.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Ensuring ground segments serve mission objectives and mechanisms are in place to execute the tests.
  • Oversees the operational aspects of engineering tests and serves as liaison to program, engineering and line management.
  • Coordinate system checkout, maintenance, troubleshooting, technical engineering support, and anomaly resolution with system safety and quality to maintain the operational specifications of the subsystem.
  • Reviews status of test requirements, manages schedules, and prepares reports.
  • Selects and supervises system admins and subcontractor engineers during test.
  • Assesses anomalies and develops resolutions to documented procedures in conjunction with Test and Quality Engineering.

  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP).
