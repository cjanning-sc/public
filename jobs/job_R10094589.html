Northrop Grumman Defense Systems is seeking a Principal Flight Simulation Trainer (level 3) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals for a position based out of Palmdale, CA.

As a Subject Matter Expert (SME), the Flight Simulation Trainer will develop technical manual and training curriculum to be employed during procedures, simulation, and/or flight training. Writes, rewrites and/or edits technical documents such as technical procedure manuals, user manuals, operational specifications, and related technical publications to communicate clearly and effectively technical specifications and instructions to aviation related audiences. Evaluates curriculum materials based on trainee feedback and instructor input. Reviews simulator design and training effectiveness to provide maximum proficiency. Reviews mission planning processes and software for incorporation into training materials. Coordinates meetings and working groups with engineering support and SMEs to develop new, and expand already designed training curriculum. Conducts quality review of materials.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have ten (10) years related experience with HS diploma/GED. Will consider an Associates degree or equivalent with a minimum of 8 years related experience or a Bachelors degree with 6 years of related electronics/related experience, or a Masters degree with 4 years related electronics/related experience.

  • Must have a valid FAA Class III flight physical standards.

  • Must be able to complete initial qualification training within 365 days from program access.

  • Applicants should be able to demonstrate an understanding of Aviation and Aerospace technical data.

  • Must be experienced with technical writing, training development, and conducting briefings and/or presentations.

  • Ability to gain proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite, Adobe Captivate, Evolution Learning Content Management System (LCMS) and Learning Management System (LMS) or similar training development software, and an ability to learn and become proficient with related software used for training development, project tracking, and presentations.

  • Ability to research engineering data, technical manuals, and utilize subject matter experts, team members, and established processes to complete complex training materials, schedules, and related projects under very general supervision.

  • Must be willing to work a flexible work schedule that may include extended shifts, holidays, and weekends and support on call status for 24 hours-a-day, 7 days- a-week, and 365 days a year. 

  • Must be able to obtain a DoD security clearance.

  • The ability to obtain and retain Special Program access clearance within 365 days from submission.

     

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Flight experience and familiarity with commercial mission planning software.

  • Quality Assurance or performing quality reviews of technical publications or training documents.
