ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Principal/Sr. Principal Mission Assurance Engineer who brings space mission system leadership, engineering, capture, and proposal experience to a growth oriented organization. Prior experience in satellite design, development, integration, testing, operations, and/or ground support is desired. This role will leverage your knowledge of all elements of the space mission design process including initial concept development, system-level trades, requirements definition, spacecraft design, and performance assessment. Knowledge of Space, Ground, mission operations, and Launch segment inter-relationships to support quality assurance of a mission baseline is desired. In this position, the candidate will be responsible for defining the mission assurance approach and supporting payload manufacturing, spacecraft design, performance, and affordability analyses, as well as support for ground segment and O&M support.

This role can be filled at either levels posted below:

Basic Qualifications:

- Principal Engineer: Bachelor Degree in an Engineering discipline and 5 years of Mission Assurance or Systems Engineering (HW) experience OR a Master’s Degree in an Engineering discipline and 3 years of experience OR PhD Degree in an Engineering discipline and 0 years of experience; OR 8 years of additional experience in lieu of a degree

- Sr Principal Engineer: Bachelor Degree in an Engineering discipline and 9 years of Mission Assurance or Systems Engineering (HW) experience OR a Master’s Degree in an Engineering discipline and 7 years of experience OR PhD Degree in an Engineering discipline and 4 years of experience; OR 13 years of additional experience in lieu of a degree

- Active TS/SCI clearance.
- Experience in Mission Assurance HW/SW/Systems
- Experience in evaluation, trace, and analysis of requirements
- Experience in working with various elements/segments of space program performance

Preferred Qualifications:          

• 10+ years systems engineering experience with at least 5 years working space systems engineering
• Experience with requirements development, flow-down, analysis, verification and validation
• Quality Assurance experience with payload, spacecraft bus, antenna, integration and test, launch, ground systems, operations and maintenance activities
• Experience with development, integration, and testing of integrated space systems
• Leadership skills to develop proposals for Government and Commercial customers
• Ability to work independently, determine and develop approach to solutions
• Evidence of problem solving and time management skills
• Experience working with multi-disciplined teams
• Excellent presentation skills and negotiation skills
