Engineer Digital 3/4 - Unit Verification Engineer - Top Secret Clearance required
R10090097
1st Shift

The Digital Products Department (part of ACME Strategic Space Systems) in Dayton, CA is seeking a talented Engineer Digital 3/4 - Unit Verification Engineer. This person will be required to test, and integrate complex digital hardware and support test systems for spacecraft flight programs.

The ideal candidate will be an experienced digital design engineer who has worked on the verification and validation of a space digital unit containing multiple digital boards.

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Unit level requirements development, management, and flow down, and coordination with system engineering

  • Generation of verification and test plans for digital board and units

  • Generation of verification artifacts necessary to sell off space hardware

  • Lead unit verification efforts, test-set requirements development, environmental testing definition

  • Support subsystem level Integration & test

Basic Qualifications:

  • Level 3: Bachelor’s Degree in STEM from an accredited institution and 5+ years of experience in an Engineering environment; OR, a minimum of 3+ years of experience with a STEM Master's degree; OR STEM PhD. Degree

  • Level 4: Bachelor’s Degree in STEM from an accredited institution and 9+ years of experience in an Engineering environment; OR, a minimum of 7+ years of experience with a STEM Master's degree; OR a minimum of 4+ years of experience with STEM PhD. Degree

  • Previous experience in the verification of digital hardware for Aerospace applications

  • Hands-on lab experience with electronic hardware

  • Candidate must have, and be able to maintain, at least ONE of the following active U.S. Government security clearances:

Dept of Defense Top Secret level with a background investigation date within 6 years

OR SCI access with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI) completed within 5 years

OR Dept of Energy Q clearance with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI) completed within 5 years

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 5+ years of experience in digital board/unit verification

  • Experience with engineering support to manufacturing including hardware troubleshooting

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

  • Master’s Degree in Electrical or Computer Engineering from an accredited institution
