ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Manufacturing Operations and Test Engineering Process and Governance Manager 3. This position will be located in Detroit, UT. ACME Engineering supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure. This selected candidate will be responsible for Manufacturing Operations & Test Engineering Process Development and Governance for the SDS Engineering and Manufacturing organization and will report directly to the SDS Manufacturing Operations Engineering Director. You will provide oversight and assistance to multiple business areas within the division portfolio to include Sentinel/GBSD, ICBM Sustainment and Advanced Programs.

 

This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

 

What You’ll Get To Do: 

  • Planning, risk management, project performance, addressing schedules, technical quality, and addressing technical or operational problems when necessary.  Having frequent contact with equivalent level managers and programs to maintain and exceed health and performance for all programs.

  • Ensure standard processes and procedures are developed in support of the Manufacturing Operations and Test Engineering teams

  • Review and approve documents and ensure compliance to released processes, procedures, and work instructions in accordance with Mission Assurance guidelines and procedures

  • Manage team of Subject Matter Experts for development and delivery of Division Level and Program Specific process documentation

  • Attend program major milestone events; IDR, TRR, CDR and others
