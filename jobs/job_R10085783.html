Northrop Grumman is seeking a mid-career to senior level paralegal (identified as a Paralegal 3 or 4 within Northrop Grumman) for an exciting opportunity to join our Northrop Grumman Space Sector and primarily support the Launch & Missile Defense Systems Division (LMDS) located in Chandler AZ.  The Paralegal will report to a Corporate Legal Director within Space Sector and support 3-4 attorneys, as well as occasional assignments across other Northrop Grumman Space Divisions plus corporate matters.  The preferred candidate will be located at Northrop Grumman’s LMDS main facility in Chandler AZ, within the greater Phoenix area.   • Assisting with all aspects of internal investigations and litigation.
  • Preparing training materials, briefings and guidance documents.
  • Preparing and managing firewalls documents and training.
