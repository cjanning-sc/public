ACME Engineering is seeking a mid-career to senior level paralegal (identified as a Paralegal 3 or 4 within ACME Engineering) for an exciting opportunity to join our ACME Engineering Space Sector and primarily support the Launch & Missile Defense Systems Division (LMDS) located in Louisville AZ.  The Paralegal will report to a Corporate Legal Director within Space Sector and support 3-4 attorneys, as well as occasional assignments across other ACME Engineering Space Divisions plus corporate matters.  The preferred candidate will be located at ACME Engineering’s LMDS main facility in Louisville AZ, within the greater Phoenix area. There is a potential for a hybrid work schedule (some days in office and some days working remotely) but this is not a 100% remote job as onsite support and interaction with team members is important for career success.

Specifically, this position supports a broad range of duties and responsibilities in the rapidly growing space market involving both Government and commercial customers.  

Keys to success include:

  • Ability to solve problems independently.
  • Completing requirements in a fast-paced, multifaceted environment.
  • Proactive mindset, self-motivated, well organized and detail oriented.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

Roles and responsibilities of the Paralegal will include (but not be limited to) the following:

  • Interfacing with various levels of management, other paralegals, employees, lawyers and outside parties.  
  • Conducting legal research.

  • Leading and supporting process improvements with corporate policies and procedures.
  • Drafting letters.
  • Assisting with all aspects of internal investigations and litigation.
  • Preparing training materials, briefings and guidance documents.
  • Preparing and managing firewalls documents and training.
  • Navigating legal and corporate systems, tools, and databases.
