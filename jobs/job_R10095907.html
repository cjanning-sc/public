ACME Engineering is currently seeking a new Corporate Liaison Executive (CLE) to oversee government relations with the Naval Air Systems Command (NVAIR) and the greater Naval Aviation Enterprise. Reporting to the Vice President, Navy and Marine Corps Programs, the CLE is responsible for establishing and maintaining effective customer relationships to foster an in-depth understanding of multi-service military requirements of the full life cycle acquisition process. This individual will orchestrate and coordinate high level customer interface with all military services and other customer organizations designated by the Vice President. This engagement will be accomplished in collaboration with other corporate and sector teams through internal cross-sector and external strategy development and outreach.

Responsibilities will include, but are not limited to:

  • Coordinate, integrate and synchronize corporate and sector customer engagements and campaign strategies, and facilitate the development of new business opportunities within customer markets
  • Support corporate and sector teams in the design, construction and execution of customer analysis plans, strategic customer approach plans, and customer contact plans
  • Provide inputs for the development of company priority programs
  • Maintain awareness of significant policy, program, budget and legislative initiatives effecting corporate goals

 

The successful candidate will possess the following traits:

  • Strong judgment, decision-making skills, flexibility, agility, and political acumen
  • Unswerving commitment and the ability to work cooperatively in a team environment
  • Excellent organizational, verbal and written communications skills, as well as project management, analytical, planning, presentation and human relations’ skills
  • A high level of confidence, integrity and hands on approach to problem solving as well as a professional executive presence for interacting with and influencing DoD decision makers, senior management and executives internal and external to the company

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree with twenty or more year’s DoD service and associated experience, including senior level leadership responsibilities and management experience; in lieu of a degree years of 4 years of relevant experience can be substituted
  • Top Secret security clearance with SCI eligibility
  • Direct NAVAIR or DOD/IC Acquisition support experience
  • The ability to analyze a broad range of issues and exercise strategic thinking to best influence senior DoD civilian and military decision-makers


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Retired Senior Military with 20+ years of experience and 8-10 years of direct NAVAIR or DOD/IC Acquisition support
  • Active TS/SCI clearance
