Sr Principal Engineer Mission Assurance
R10083366
1st Shift (United States of America)

Defines and monitors mission assurance program specifications and processes to ensure mission success of programs. Performs or assures quality, risk management, safety, reliability and maintainability of program achievements, subcontractors, and suppliers in accordance with contractual requirements.

Assesses supplier performance and risks, and as a team member of the Supplier Quality Organization: Collaborates with program management, engineering, subcontracts/procurement functional management and supplier management teams to ensure that procurements meet or exceed the purchase order requirements.

Candidate will be responsible for providing technical support and quality oversight in the acquisition of various ordnance related products or systems for various launch applications.

This includes ensuring supplier quality requirements are met as well as assisting with supplier assessments.

The position will include evaluating waivers, deviations and discrepancy reporting process (Material Review Board) for product acceptance.

The candidate will evaluate supplier-non-conformances, negative performance trends and failures and determines root cause and implements corrective and preventive actions.

This requires knowledge of discrepant material procedures.

Manages or performs source inspection processes, Hardware Acceptance Reviews, and coordinates action with the supplier as required. This will also include in-process verifications at the supplier.

This also includes review of component drawings and specifications related to the hardware being procured. Experience in inspection planning a plus.

Knowledge of materials or material sciences preferred. Background in solid rocket motors is a plus as would be a familiarity with composite structures.

Travel is an essential function of this position (approximately 15-20%) and may be on short notice.

Work schedule includes 9/80 schedule (9 hours per day, 9 days every 2 weeks, every other Friday off). Basic Minimum Qualification:

9 Years with Bachelor’s in Science; 7 Years with Masters; 4 Years with PhD.
