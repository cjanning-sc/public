Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Senior Staff Aeronautical Engineer to join the Payload Team. This position is in Roy, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. This role may offer a competitive relocation package.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will be part of the Payload Team (PLT) as a technical leader in the design, build, and test of the Payload Reentry System (PRS) element of the Sentinel Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) such that it successfully meets requirements as proven by design reviews, detailed analyses, characterization testing, and qualification verification processes. Roles and Responsibilities include:

  • Has responsibility for controlling the PRS technical baseline. Coordinates requirements development, modeling and analysis, hardware/software design, verification, validation, and integration activities across elements contributing to design and delivery

  • Ensures that the program is adhering to sound engineering processes in the development, production, test and sustainment of products for our customers and is compliance with company policies, procedures, and quality standards

  • Maintains cognizance over technical activities within adjacent Aerospace Vehicle Segment and ensures horizontal integration of the best engineering practices across the company

  • Continually coordinates with the systems engineering and program integration team leadership to ensure the execution of program statement of work.

  • Extensive customer, and external stakeholder interfacing and some travel will be required.

  • Work collaboratively and with influence to gain commitment and performance from all organizations including the Aerospace Vehicle Segment and Program customers, Business Operations and Contracts, System Design, Integration and Test as needed to drive required business outcomes
