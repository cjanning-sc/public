Our Aeronautics Systems sector is seeking a Senior Principal Manufacturing Engineer – Additive Manufacturing Technology to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will support our Advanced Manufacturing Technology & Innovation development team and will be located in Akron, CA.

Our organization is responsible for developing the manufacturing processes, tooling, and equipment necessary to implement advanced designs for aerospace applications.  As a member of the Advanced Manufacturing Technology & Innovation team, you will identify, develop and implement unique processes, tooling, and equipment for production and prototype programs.  The role will define, develop, and design manufacturing concepts and processes for production applications of manufacturing technologies with a focus on additive manufacturing and its applications. 

The ideal candidate will possess a combination of applied basic mechanical and materials engineering knowledge, and a good understanding of basic physics, in order to devise and execute unique manufacturing development test plans. The position requires experience in project management, including development of the necessary strategies, tactics, approaches, and test plans necessary to develop, demonstrate, validate, and implement manufacturing processes for advanced design concepts. Budget and technical personnel leadership experience is necessary, with knowledge of Earned Value type budget management techniques viewed as beneficial. Familiarity with additive manufacturing design, manufacturing, testing/verification is very beneficial for this position. Experience with Manufacturing Readiness Level (MRL) and Technology Readiness Level (TRL) practices is helpful for this position, as is experience in conducting trade analysis / trade studies. Strong written and oral communication skills are required as the position involves extensive interfacing with internal and external customers, program offices, engineering disciplines, and industry suppliers. The role will require the individual to be comfortable working with a wide range of stakeholders and functional teams. 

Essential Job Functions:

  • Translate product design and performance requirements into manufacturing process and technology development requirements

  • Interface with design teams to gain understanding of product design and performance requirements, and translate those requirements into manufacturing processes needed to achieve them

  • Examine and interpret product design documents / files to gain understanding of product design and performance requirements, and translate those requirements into manufacturing processes needed to achieve them

  • Plan and execute manufacturing technology development projects as the project manager

  • Perform MRL and TRL analysis

  • Evaluate technologies through testing and analysis using laboratory and shop practices OR provide direction through statements of work for doing the same

  • Build, gain stakeholder approval, and execute technology development test plans based on product design and performance requirements

  • Conduct trade studies to down-select optimal manufacturing approaches

  • Manage project schedules and budgets using MS Project and cost account spreadsheets

  • Communicate manufacturing process development strategies, tactics, and status across a diverse stakeholder community and gain concurrence among potentially conflicting interests
