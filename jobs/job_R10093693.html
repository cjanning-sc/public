ACME Engineering Mission Systems is looking for a Program Quality Engineer for Ames, VA

The Program Quality Engineer:

  • Collaborates with engineering and manufacturing functions to ensure quality standards are in place.
  • Performs oversight and monitoring of program risks and opportunities
  • Develops and executes independent quality reviews to ensure compliance with contractual and customer requirements.
  • Identifies key product characteristics and works with Quality Analysts to design and analyze inspection and testing processes, mechanisms, and equipment to ensure delivered quality and customer satisfaction
  • Identifies applicable quality requirements to flow to vendors
  • Leads activities associated with Material Review Boards (MRBs) to include root cause and corrective action identification and developing required notifications to the Customer
  • Audits quality systems for deficiency identification and correction, assuring process and product quality. Ensures that corrective measures are effective, and that documentation is compliant with requirements.
  • May specialize in the areas of design, incoming material, production control, product evaluation and reliability, inventory control and/or research and development as they apply to product or process quality.Â 
  • Represent Quality function in discussions with Customer or Customer delegates
  • Responsible for ensuring overall program quality

This position may be filled at a level 2 or 3, depending on experience.

Basic Qualifications for a Level 2:

  • Bachelor's degree in a related STEM field
  • Relevant experience of 2+ Years with Bachelors in Science; 0+ Years with Masters; 0+ Years with PhD
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Secret clearance, US citizenship
  • Excellent written and verbal interpersonal, communication skills and confidence to brief senior management
  • Ability to interpret Military and Commercial specifications
  • Ability to interpret CAD data, blue prints and schematics
  • Foundation in Quality and Mission Excellence applications
  • Ability to work in cross-functional teams to resolve problems or issues

  • Ability to work in cross-functional teams to resolve problems or issues

Preferred Qualifications:
