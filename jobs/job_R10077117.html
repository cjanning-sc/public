ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems sector is seeking a Ground Support Equipment (GSE) Mechanic 2/3 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position is located in Cincinnati, CA.

Performs scheduled maintenance and in-depth repair as required on aircraft ground support equipment. Reviews maintenance records of periodic maintenance to ensure equipment is fully operational in support of daily equipment requirements. Uses an assortment of basic hand tools in addition to precision measuring devices. Works from complex written instructions such as formal test specifications, blueprints, drawings, schematics, and manuals. May instruct lower level mechanics in technical aspects of Ground Support equipment work. Exercises appropriate safety measures.

This position standard work schedule is a 3/10 1st shift Friday-Sunday.
