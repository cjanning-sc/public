The Missile Defense Solutions (MDS) Business Unit is seeking a Business Development Director for Missile Defense and missile warning markets focused on the Missile Defense Agency (MDA) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals.  The position is located in Huntsville, AL and will report to the Sr. Director of Strategy and Business Development within the Launch and Missile Defense Systems (LMDS) Division of Northrop Grumman’s Space Sector. The primary responsibility is to lead the MDS business development team in the creation and execution of a comprehensive strategy to capture new business opportunities and implement best practices across the BU to produce compelling capture approaches.  Programs of interest focus on Missile Warning (MW) and Missile Defense (MD) and adjacent opportunities to include both new starts and development/expansion of existing programs. 

The selected candidate will be responsible for the development, assessment, and cultivation of new opportunities for MDS mission capabilities and software development and maturation of contact plans with customer organizations and influencers. The individual is responsible for developing and implementing strategies for identifying and maturing initial capture targets, market/mission expansion, and effectively marketing the business unit’s products and capabilities to meet customer needs, thereby translating business opportunities into contact plans, strategic win themes, and supporting capture teams and wins. The individual will work and collaborate with a team of business developers, MDS and LMDS leadership, and other integrated stakeholders to implement win strategies, compliant with Northrop Grumman’s business acquisition process.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in technical area

  • Must have a current and active clearance; Top Secret, SCI security clearance preferred

  • A minimum of 15 years total experience with 8+ years being in Missile Warning, Missile Defense or Missile Tracking markets

  • An understanding of U.S. Government, US Space Force, Missile Defense Agency or Space Development Agency customer requirements, acquisition processes, and organizations.

  • Candidate should have proven experience in the establishment of new customer relationships with the objective of identifying customer needs and technology roadmaps to establish new opportunities for market/business expansion

  • Experience with ground system development for space-related applications

  • Strong written and verbal communication skills

  • U.S. Citizenship is required

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master of Science in STEM, or MBA Degree

  • A minimum of 20 years' experience in MW/MD markets with focus on strategic missions

  • Experience in U.S. Government customer requirements development, program management, and acquisition

  • Significant experience developing and executing capture strategies to win greater than $100M new business with the Intelligence Communities, DOD and Civil agencies.

  • Proven track record of business growth with the Missile Defense Agency community and DOD programs across a broad range of customers

  • Experience leading and managing multi-disciplined teams
