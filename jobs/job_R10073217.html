ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Senior Principal Software Engineer to join the System Test Team (STT). This position is in Detroit, UT and supports the Sentinel/Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.   

What You’ll Get To Do:

Responsibilities include network architecture development and implementation for program test facilities. Support the test team in deployment of network assets, sustainment of systems, network modification verifications, and helping to resolve complex networking issues.

Essential Functions Includes:

  • Responsible for building, integrating, and testing network configurations and or implementing and configuring network hardware in complex IT systems.
  • Create Build and Upgrade documentation along with instructions for lab and field engineers/technicians on how to configure and implement network devices within new or current systems.
  • Create and maintain network drawings or infrastructure diagrams
  • Act as a technical resource and provide tier 1-2 support in response to network issues and various support tasks.

Job Qualifications:

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

  • 9 years of relevant experience with Bachelors (4 additional years of experience may be used in lieu of a degree); 7 Years with Masters; 4 Years with PhD.
  • Active DoD Secret clearance awarded within the last 6 years
  • US Citizenship with the ability to obtain Special Program Access (SAP)
  • Prior experience working with IT systems in large networks (25-100+ users), deployment of hardware and software, patches, general system administrator duties
  • Demonstrated knowledge of network engineering principles and best practices

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

  • Working knowledge of datacenter design and management
  • Working knowledge of virtualization (e.g. VMware)
  • Working knowledge of network information & event management systems (e.g. ISE, Splunk, Solarwinds, Nagios)
  • Experienced in both Windows and Linux computer operating systems environments
  • Experienced with programming/scripting languages (e.g. C, C++, Java, Python, Ansible)
  • Working knowledge on cybersecurity (e.g. security standards, STIGs, RMF, ACAS scan)
  • Applicable certifications such as CCNA, CCNP or CCIE
