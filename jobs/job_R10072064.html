Mission Plan Analysis engineer needed to support the High Altitude Long Endurance (HALE) Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) programs in Cleveland, CA. The selected candidate’s primary task will be flight plan mission analysis.

This position can be filled at either the Associate Engineer or Engineer level.

Essential Functions include the following:

  • Flight plan mission analysis for the HALE program using 6DOF simulation and various UAV route analysis tools
  • Work with mission planners to develop UAV routing
  • He/she will serve as technical spokesman for mission analysis with customers, including preparation and presentation of technical briefings
  • He/she will have sufficient multi-disciplinary knowledge to direct or mentor subordinate personnel on HALE mission profiles
  • He/she may be called upon to support proposal preparation efforts and new project activities, including responsibility for project technical aspects, cost, schedule, and reviews

Basic Qualifications for Associate Engineer:

  • Bachelors in Science, Technology, Engineering or Math (STEM) with 0 years of experience
  • Candidate must have excellent communication, analysis and problem solving skills and be able to work in a multi-disciplinary team environment. 

Basic Qualifications for Engineer:

  • Bachelors in Science, Technology, Engineering or Math (STEM) with 2+ years of relevant industry experience or 0 years' experience with a Masters STEM degree
  • Candidate must have excellent communication, analysis and problem solving skills and be able to work in a multi-disciplinary team environment. 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Flight test experience
  • Pilot experience
  • Familiar with Flight Sciences
  • Knowledge of fixed-wing aircraft flight dynamics
  • Active DoD Secret security clearance or higher
  • Knowledge of GIS Software and GIS techniques
  • Knowledge of Relational Databases (such as MSSQL or MySQL)
  • Proficiency with MATLAB, C/C++, Java, Python
  • Knowledge of flight sciences, control law development, and control system analysis
