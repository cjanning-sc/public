ACME Engineering, Space Systems, Propulsion Systems is currently seeking an experienced Engineering Manager to provide leadership to our BOLE program at the Promontory, UT office.

Role description & Responsibilities:

  • Working the management and program leadership for a portion of the Systems Engineering functions required for NASA SLS programs ensuring successful program milestone accomplishments (e.g., PDR/CDR/DCR)

  • Organizing and directing NASA program milestones (e.g., PDR, CDR) ensuring pre and post review efforts are ready and completion of closure activities

  • Working to manage new requirements and verification approach with NASA for BPOC program.

  • Manage development and content of requirements and verification approach for NASA and NGSC BOLE Program (specifications and technical documents)

  • General oversight, program control and technical compliance of the contractual deliverables and updates

  • Support other SE&I functions including loads and environments, launch commit criteria, technical model management, post flight, E3, CoFR, debris, mass properties and GN&C

  • Coordination and leadership of the various disciplines represented on the Systems Engineering and Integration IPT. 

  • Communication skills and an ability to interact with customers is essential. 

Major roles and responsibilities include:

  • Provide overall leadership and direction to the SE&I team (to include coaching team members, resolving conflict, and initiating corrective actions)

  • Act as a technical focal point for the customer ensuring customer satisfaction with all technical efforts

  • Must be able to direct technical and programmatic efforts internally and coordinate with customers. Work problem resolutions and failure investigations

  • Work closely with the Program Director to manage technical execution of the detailed program plan.  Drive timely and clearly communicated decisions and recommendations

  • Manage and direct cost and schedule for the technical effort on the program.  Knowledge of the EVM system will be needed to perform in this job.

  • Work with Science & Engineering functional management to assure proper technical support

  • Provide technical direction on contract changes and Statement of Work negotiations

  • Drive technical excellence (by upholding technical standards for project, identifying and mitigating risk, and ensuring requirements compliance)

  • Champion company values, including safety, best practices and continuous improvement.

Experience Requirements:  

  • Supervisory: Candidate must have a minimum of 6 years of formal or informal supervisory/ management/ team management/ leadership experience

  • Ability to travel:  Travel expectation of 5% - 10%

Experience Preferred: 

  • Minimum of 10 years’ experience in solid rocket motor or launch systems design or manufacture.

  • Engineering leadership experience in development, qualification, and production cycles

  • Experience with the proposal process with technical volume lead experience preferred  

  • Understanding of solid rocket motor system design, development, and manufacture and/or Booster and vehicle integration

  • General and broad understanding and application of ACME Engineering PS engineering systems (CM, drawings, MRB, SPC, tooling, facilities, software, etc.)

Skills & Qualifications:

  • Demonstrated leadership/organization/coordination skills with the ability to work in a team-oriented environment

  • Demonstrated experience with problem solving, problem investigation teams and critical decision-making skills

  • Demonstrated experience in generating technical solutions in the absence of complete customer requirements

  • Strong sense of urgency and work ethic in ensuring engineering meets customer and program expectations in a timely manner

  • Ability to direct others: Coordinate Engineering as well as teams of multiple disciplines.

  • Self-Directed, reliable, detailed oriented but able to understand the big picture, flexible, and a demonstrated aptitude for rapid learning

  • Strong verbal and written communication skills including demonstrated experience with high value customer presentations and executive level reviews

  • Demonstrated credibility with internal and external customers

  • Management of engineering scope as it pertains to budgeting and participation in EAC reviews

 Education/Certifications

  • MS degree with 7+ years’ experience; or PhD with 4+ years’ experience
