Northrop Grumman Mission Systems in Woodland Hills, CA is currently seeking a Principal or Senior Principal Signal/Image Processing Engineer with a current/active Secret clearance to support radar exploitation activities in the Artificial Intelligence and Analytics department.

In this role, the selected candidate will join a group developing algorithms for a novel radar capability. In this position you will perform analyses at all levels of total system product to include: concept, design, prototyping, implementation, and test. The position requires familiarity with radar remote sensing and classical signal processing techniques.

Specific responsibilities include:

  • Determining reuse possibilities, or conceptualizing novel algorithms
  • Designing and rapidly prototyping implementation
  • Designing a robust test suite
  • Creating presentation material to explain and document work, and presenting to internal and external stakeholders
