Job Description

  • Responsible for daily coordination of Integrated Thermal Systems proposal activities.
  • Maintains opportunity trackers and ensures proactive communication with customers. 
  • Coordination of pre-RFP activities including ROMs.
  • Develops, plans and schedules proposals (both external and IWO).
  • Tracks and manages Bid and Proposal budgets.
  • Hosts proposal kick-off’s and actively coordinates with all contributing team members to ensure on time delivery / responsiveness.
  • Compiles cost, programmatic, and technical elements in the development of proposals in accordance with request for proposal (RFP) specifications.
  • Coordinates the collection and accumulation of cost, scheduling, and performance elements from functional departments.
  • Researches and analyzes historical data, develops cost models to support proposal efforts.
  • Coordinates with finance & pricing teams.
  • Coordinates activities and participates in fact-finding meetings.
  • Coordinates with contracts on extensions / exemptions / terms / Q&A / etc.
  • Review of inputs for adequacy in meeting RFP requirements / coordination with functional management to resolve discrepancies. 
  • Participates in should-cost and pricing reviews with management and customer representatives.
  • Coordinates delegation activities with the Operating Unit, Business Unit, and with Division / Sector as required.  Presents delegation packages.
  • Participates as a member of the contract negotiating team.
  • Coordinates and maintains new business manpower demand forecasts to the functions (supports staffing decisions)
  • Present at weekly Operating Unit meetings and Monthly / Quarterly Business Unit reviews.
  • Direct report to the Site Director with dotted line report to the Business Unit Business Development Director.

Competencies

  • Ability to work and manage a proposal team independently with minimal direct supervision to produce high quality “management ready” packages.
  • Polished presenter and communicator.
  • Excellent internal and external team building skills including the ability to interface effectively cross-functionally (technical, finance, contracts, operations, supply chain, executive management, etc.)
  • Excellent technical writing and general composition skills
  • Excellent organization skills (including technical, schedule, and financial data)
  • Full proficiency with MS Office
  • Assists with priority setting when conflicts are identified
  • Leads and participates in process improvement activities  
  • Occasional travel required (<10%)
