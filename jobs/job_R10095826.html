Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is an industry-leading provider for prime satellite and payload capabilities and directed energy and electronics solutions for national security, military, and civil customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible.

SSSD has an opening for a Deputy Operations Lead to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Columbus, Ohio.

What You’ll Get to Do: 

  • As a Deputy Operations Lead you will be responsible for helping to organize and synchronize general business operations across six different Operating Units and six Functional Groups within a $300M Business Unit.  Work with all OU and Functional Leads to ensure sufficient meetings are in place at the BU Level without impeding operations.  Ensure that meetings are planned, prepared, and executed effectively.  

  • Assist and facilitate preparation for any Division or BU briefings as required.  

  • Assist with liaison work with the Engineering Management Team.  Lead/guide that team to ensure that they are in a position to effectively support the BU without impeding operations.  Help plan, prepare, and execute a quarterly Engineering Management Team meeting with the BU General Manager. Support the rollout and sustainment of the McKinsey 7S Operations Model for each OU.  Help manage BU level Action Item list with BU General Manager.  

  • Ensure that all actions are addressed (completed or updated) in a timely manner. Become a knowledgeable user of the Salesforce Strategy and Capture Database.  Download and format BU data from this system regularly. Work with the Program Management team to ID programs in Salesforce that are nearing their award dates and facilitate the assigned PM with the PM Homeroom to start the Program Startup Checklist in a timely manner.  

  • Assist the Analytics Team to ID staffing needs and hire in an effective and timely manner.  Assist Ops Lead to work with all OUs and Functional Groups to perform effective and timely personnel evaluations at the end of the year.  Deconflict BU ratings with Functional ratings as needed.  Participate in the BU Talent Management Process as required.   Work with the Division as well as the OU/Functional Leads to Develop and Update Year End Goals via X-Matrix process with measurable quarterly milestones.
