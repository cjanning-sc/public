Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking Program Controls - Financial Analyst – Senior Level to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. Our analyst will be located at our Magna, UT (Bacchus, UT) facility and will be onsite, with a potential to work remotely 1-day per week.

Duties & Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Setting up cost control system, monitors and controls costs and schedules on contracts requiring validated cost schedule control system.
  • Performing analyses and preparing reports in order to ensure that contracts are within negotiated values.
  • Preparing budgets and schedules for contract and NCTA work and performing and/or assisting in financial analyses such as funding profiles, sales outlook, and variance analysis.
  • Preparing program plans to ensure program requirements and statement of work are captured and scheduled.
  • Ensuring adequate funding availability by maintaining accurate records of expenditures, directing preparation of expenditure projections, and submitting timely requests for additional funding to the customer.
  • Incorporating contractual changes into control systems by staying aware of outstanding work against each contract in order to maintain realistic contract cost and schedule baselines.

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree with 10+ years of experience in finance, accounting and/or industry related experience – OR – a Master’s degree with + years of experience in finance, accounting and/or industry related experience. May consider 4 additional years of experience in lieu of a degree.
  • Proven ability to prioritize multiple assignments to meet critical milestones.
  • Excellent analytical and reporting skills.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite; Excel, Word, PowerPoint.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Degree in Business, Finance or Accounting.
  • Cost control, budgeting, and variance analysis experience.
  • Experience with SAP ERP, Cognos reporting, ProPricer, and/or PowerBI.
  • Advanced Excel Techniques (i.e. v/h lookups, pivot tables, VBA/Macros).

This position’s standard work schedule is a 9/80. The 9/80 schedule allows employees who work a nine-hour day Monday through Thursday to take every other Friday off.
