Your Opportunity to Define Possible. Our Opportunity to Deliver the Nation’s Security. Together.

About Your Opportunity:

A unique opportunity to work on cutting-edge technology for large Cyber and Intelligence Projects.

You will contribute as part of a wider multi-disciplined team by writing well defined and thoroughly tested software which gives our customers their competitive edge. We represent a rapidly growing area for NGUK and we are continuously keen to expand and share our knowledge across the domain of cyber. 

Your role as a Cyber Engineer will see you assisting in the delivery of technical innovation of complex software deliveries, enabling our customers to define possible in their most important endeavours.

  • Flexible working schedules - we offer flexible and hybrid working arrangements and full time and part time roles. Talk to us at the application stage about any scheduling preferences you may have.
  • Flexible Benefits Package – choose which NGUKL benefits you want to satisfy your personal needs. Core Benefits provided for you are Healthcare, Dental, Life Assurance and Pension. Benefits you can flex include Critical Illness Cover, Health Cash Plan, and Health Assessments.
  • Employee Incentive Programme – exceptional performance is recognized through our annual incentive programme which is awarded to top performers who excel
  • Career Development – opportunity for ongoing professional development and career growth opportunities

Your Responsibilities:

  • To deliver well-designed, documented and tested code in Python, C or C++.
  • To ensure high assurance deliverables by writing verbose unit and integration tests.
  • Contribute to an Agile delivery team utilising Scrum or Kanban methodologies.
  • Write technical reports targeted to a variety of audiences (both technical and non-technical), and present to external stakeholders.
  • Integrate into CICD pipelines gaining experience of Jenkins, Containersation, IaC and automation tooling.

Your Experience:

  • Can demonstrate proficiency across one or more programming / scripting languages; with an aptitude to learn others. Preferably Python, C, C++, Java or C#.
  • Knowledge of at least more than one Operating System and how to setup requisite development environments, with a focus on Windows and Linux.
  • Demonstrate ability to adapt when faced with complex challenges in pursuit of innovative solutions.
  • Demonstrable experience taking ownership of assigned project deliverables through to completion and within agreed timeframes.
  • Experience working in agile teams with tools such as Confluence, Jira and BitBucket.
