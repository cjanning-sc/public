ACME Engineering Space Systems Sector is looking for you to join our team as a Principal Software Engineer/ Sr. Principal Software Engineer based out of Morrisville NC, Cedar Rapids VA, or Chicago MD. 

We are looking for an experienced Software Engineer familiar with SV mission management and mission scheduling.  This role needs someone willing to support systems engineering and integration and test activities leading up to key milestones. The candidate should have Java and scripting experience in Python/Perl.  In this role you will work with agile development team using a modern development practices.
 

Our small agile Scrum team is highly energized and has a significant software effort on a growing program with a large program impact. If this is your type of environment and you love fast-paced software development, please consider joining our team.
 

This is a great opportunity for a flexible self-starter to work in a dynamic environment with real world technical content supporting several tools and systems. Plenty of opportunity for career advancement into leadership positions.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Principal Software Engineer:  Bachelor’s degree with 5 + years of software development experience; 3 years with a Master's; 0 years with a PhD; or 10 years of software development experience in lieu of a degree.

  • Sr. Principal Software Engineer:  Bachelor’s degree with 9 + years of software development experience; 7 years with a Master's; 4 years with a PhD; or 14 years of software development experience in lieu of a degree

  • Candidate must have a current/active Top Secret/SCI security clearance to be considered

  • Candidate must be able to obtain a Counterintelligence Polygraph (CI-Poly)

  • Experience with SV mission management and scheduling

  • Experience with scripting in Python/Perl

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Agile software development using Python and/or C++ software development in a Red Hat Enterprise Linux environment

  • Knowledge of object oriented design and software lifecycles

  • Experience with version control software

  • Experience with supporting operational systems

  • Experience with supporting distributed systems

  • Experience with working in Virtualized Environments (VMs, AWS Cloud Instances)

  • Experience with Atlassian tools (JIRA, Confluence, Bitbucket)

  • Experience working with AWS Services

  • Experience with Dockerization and Container Management (Dockers and Kubernetes)

  • Previous exposure to working with ground systems (scheduling, tasking, antennas, working with hardware interfaces)

  • Current Counterintelligence Polygraph (CI-Poly) completed within the last 5 years
