Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking a Production Controller in Sykesville, Maryland.

This position will be responsible for the following:
• Timely movement of hardware between work cells and induction of field hardware by capturing condition status and updating the system of record.
• Utilize SAP and other internal tracking systems to manage all production and repair orders that support Material Requirements Planning (MRP).
• Collaborate with multiple organizations to ensure daily priorities support weekly/monthly sales plan. Including Supervisors, Engineering, Operations, Program management, Technicians, Operators, Mission Assurance, and
Stockroom personnel ensuring that the daily priorities support weekly/monthly sales plan.
• Expedite flow of materials, parts, and assemblies within or between departments and or work cells.
• Assists in determining shortages, and initiate action to correct these deficiencies.
• Uses knowledge of production, procurement, and engineering departments to alert leadership regarding potential problems and appropriate remedial action.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree with 1 years of manufacturing experience or 5 years of Production Control experience in lieu of a degree.
  • Microsoft Office knowledge.
  • Strong Excel background
  • General understanding of continuous process improvement 
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Security clearance
  • Ability to frequently move and position objects weighing up to 40 lbs
  • Must be able to move stock/inventory/materials around the manufacturing floor and various business units.

  

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Bachelors with 3 years related experience.
  • Knowledge of SAP similar ERP software.
  • Knowledge of manufacturing work flow and MRP systems or similar.
  • Ability to work in a fast paced environment.
  • Secret Clearance Special Program Access
