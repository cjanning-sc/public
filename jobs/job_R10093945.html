Northrop Grumman Aerospace Systems has an opening for an Electronic Technician 4 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Manhattan Beach, CA.

The selected candidate will primarily support test and checkout of flight level electronic hardware, in a production environment. The candidate’s initial assignment will be support of various types of electronic hardware developed for the EPS-R program.

Essential Functions:

  • Support RF, Power, and Digital, testing of flight Hardware

  • Support test activities on 1st, 2nd or 3rd shift

  • Perform test and checkout of assembly, circuit card, slice, and unit level hardware

  • May require test of experimental design circuitry, prototype models, or specialized test equipment

  • Must be able to work from schematics, diagrams, written and verbal direction, layouts, or defined test plans

  • Should be capable of conducting tests and collecting data, using standard test equipment; such as spectrum analyzers, oscilloscopes, DVMs, and signal generators

  • Should be able to mentor junior level technicians, and function as a Shift Lead Technician if required.

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.  Candidate must be comfortable in a lab environment and be able to collaborate closely with system engineers, hardware designers, test engineers, and various technicians.

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School diploma or equivalent and a minimum of 6 year’s related experience.

  • Associate of Arts Degree or equivalent Military training in Electronic Technology

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Associate of Science Degree in Electronic Technology or equivalent

  • 15+ years of electronic test experience, in a space flight or complex military hardware environment

  • The ability to obtain DoD, and SCI clearance
