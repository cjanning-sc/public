AOA Xinetics (AOX), a wholly owned ACME Engineering Space Systems, Payload and Ground Systems Division Business Venture, is a leading supplier of complex electro-optic and photonic systems and subsystems that control and manipulate light waves for a variety of applications. We are seeking an Assembler to support our manufacturing efforts in Devens, MA.

https://www.acmeengineering.com/who-we-are/aoa-xinetics       

Overview

Reporting to the Manufacturing Operators Manager, the Optical Assembler will perform a variety of tasks ranging from repetitive to non-repetitive production assembly operations on opto-mechanical, electro-optical and electronic and/or mechanical assemblies. Works from diagrams and drawings, makes initial layouts, and uses hand and/or power tools. Performs continuity checks on work in process and completed assemblies. May disassemble, modify, rework, reassemble, and test experimental or prototype assemblies and subassemblies according to specifications provided by a cognizant engineer.

This position is a great fit for someone who enjoys working in a team environment, can perform tasks under a microscope on very small assemblies; has a high level of attention to detail and quality. The chosen candidate must be able to multi-task and flex between multiple assignments.  

Basic Qualifications: 

  • High School Diploma or Equivalent

  • Ability to handle delicate optical and mechanical components

  • Ability to use basic hand tools

  • Ability to perform tasks from written directions and drawings

  • Ability to read and interpret drawings and blueprints

  • U.S. citizenship is a bona fide requirement


Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Assembly or electronic experience

  • Basic knowledge of optical and electrical test equipment
