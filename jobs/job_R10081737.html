ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Software Governance and Process Manager 3 for the Strategic Deterrent Systems (SDS) division Engineering and Manufacturing Directorate. This leadership role will be located in Detroit, UT, Indianapolis, CO or Madison, AL and will provide enabling support for all business areas within the SDS portfolio.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

ACME Engineering supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure. This selected candidate will be responsible for Software Governance and Process Development for the Software Engineering organization and will report directly to the SDS Software Engineering Director. You will provide oversight and assistance to multiple business areas within the division portfolio to include Sentinel/GBSD, ICBM Sustainment and Advanced Programs.

Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Planning, risk management, project performance, addressing schedules, technical quality, and addressing technical or operational problems when necessary.  Having frequent contact with equivalent level managers and programs to maintain and exceed health and performance for all programs.
  • Ensure standard processes and procedures are developed in support of the SW Engineering teams
  • Review and approve documents and ensures compliance to released processes, procedures, and work instructions in accordance with Mission Assurance guidelines and procedures
  • Manage team of Subject Matter Experts for development and delivery of Division Level and Program Specific documentation
  • Attend program major milestone events; IDR, TRR, CDR and others
