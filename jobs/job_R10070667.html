ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Principal/ Senior Principal Project Manager. This position will be located in Detroit, MI and will support the Sentinel Training Devices team and Scrum efforts. Learn more about the Sentinel program here. 


What You'll Get To Do:

  • Lead the efforts of developing Scaled Agile engineering and management best practices, metrics, and audit practices.

  • Conduct Scrum Team training, product backlog and retrospective training, Lean Agile development training for the Training Devices team as needed.

  • Support a range of technical disciplines from software engineering to hardware to cyber to testing automation on a Scrum Team.

  • Facilitate daily Scrum meetings and regular ceremonies.

  • Collect and analyze related metrics for trends and indicators, facilitate iteration/increment planning, provide Agile coaching and mentoring at all levels within the organization.

  • Lead the iterative solution lifecycle from executable architecture to maintenance, including: concept development, iterative design, production, delivery and deployment.

  • Have an understanding of Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe), either from training and certification or 1-2 years direct experience.

  • Provide regular interaction with the customer.

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

This position may be filled at either a Principal Level or Sr. Principal Level per the requirements below:

  • Principal Level: 6 years experience in project management with Bachelors, or 4 years experience in project management with Masters.

  • Sr. Principal Level: 10 years experience in project management with Bachelors, or 8 years experience in project management with Masters.

  • Experience managing communications with stakeholders through organized processes to ensure that program information is defined, collected, shared, understood, stored and retrieved in a manner that effectively meets program stakeholder needs.

  • Experience identifying and addressing program impacts through systematic, proactive, approaches to issue and problem resolution that identifies, communicates, monitors and promptly resolves conflicts.

  • Must have U.S. Citizenship with a current or active Secret level clearance and have the ability to obtain, and maintain access to Special Access Programs (SAP)  as a condition of continued employment.

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

  • 1 or more years experience as a Scrum Master (or similar relatable experience - i.e. Certified Scrum Practitioner, PMI Agile Certified Practitioner, etc.)

  • Bachelors or Masters degree in Project Management or related discipline from an accredited university. 

  • Active Top Secret Clearance

  • Familiarity with Missile Defense, Missile Warning and Space Situational Awareness missions.

  • Knowledge of System Engineering principles and requirements management.

  • Experience with transitioning teams from Waterfall to Agile methods and practices.

  • Certification in one or more of these designations is desired: Certified SAFe Program Consultant (SPC), Certified Scrum at Scale Practitioner, Certified Agile Coach, Certified facilitator, Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO), Certified Scrum Trainer, and Project Management Professional (PMP).

  • 2 years or more experience implementing Agile principles and practices within DoD or Federal government environments.
