ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal Project Manager to join our team. This position will be located onsite in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

Enjoy a culture where your voice is valued and start contributing to our team of passionate professionals providing real-life solutions to our world’s biggest challenges. We take pride in creating purposeful work and allowing our employees to grow and achieve their goals every day by Defining Possible. With our competitive pay and comprehensive benefits, we have the right opportunities to fit your life and launch your career today.

This position is for a Principal Project Manager, who will be supporting the Program Office and Operations and Integration team in a classified environment. The selected candidate will be part of a diverse organization of project integrators within the Operations and Integration organization. The candidate is responsible for oversight and management of the operational aspects of ongoing projects, and will serve as the liaison between program management, integrated product teams, and other functional areas. The selected candidate will also support infrastructure team and act as a liason between the Enterprise Services team and Program Integrated Product Teams (IPTs). This individual should possess leadership and team building capabilities and have strong interpersonal skills. Must possess solid organization abilities and be a motivated, flexible team player. Some travel may be required.

Responsibilities for this position include (but are not limited to):

  • Capturing, tracking, and providing visibility into/reporting on action items
  • Development and presentation of Executive level leadership briefings
  • Facilitation of meetings, resulting actions, and any associated deliverables, including senior leadership meetings
  • Assisting in the planning, development, and oversight of a classified network infrastructure
  • Development, implementation, and maintenance of tools/templates for monitoring project progress
  • Assessing project issues and developing resolutions to meet productivity, quality and customer satisfaction goals and objectives
  • Providing mentorship to junior-level employees
  • Work with internal customers to understand the business goals, objectives, rules and additional requirements
  • Work side by side with an IT counterpart to ensure tool development and infrastructure projects are on time and meet the needs of the program

This individual contributor should be able to work independently with little oversight and be able to receive requirements at a high level and derive lower-level requirements as required. Candidate should possess leadership skills and should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree with 6 years of experience OR Master’s Degree with 4 years of experience in Project Management or Program Integration.
  • 4 additional years of project management experience may be considered in lieu of bachelor’s degree.
  • Intermediate experience with Microsoft Office tool suite; the ability to create, manage, and manipulate the tools, especially PowerPoint, Word, and Excel.
  • Knowledge of Project Management principles and experience utilizing them professionally. (managing projects, scheduling, budget)
  • Active DoD Secret or Top-Secret clearance (last investigation completed within last 6 years).
  • Ability to obtain Special Program Access

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with IT infrastructure (both hardware and software) 
  • Experience with SharePoint Designer, InfoPath, and Jira
  • Experience with Designing, developing, and managing site content and capabilities
  • Experience with agile methodologies in software development or product owner responsibilities

We offer flexible work arrangements, 9/80 schedule with every other Friday off, phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly team environment. We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal-oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do.
