Northrop Grumman Defense Systems (NGDS) is currently seeking a Financial Analyst / Principal Financial Analyst with Earned Value Management Systems experience.

Role Description:

Provide direct support to the Program Management Office (PMO) and Control Account Managers (CAMs) on the planning, implementation, and monitoring of Earned Value Management Systems processes on the next phase of NGDS’s successful AARGM missile. This position will be responsible for developing and maintaining an Earned Value Management (EVM) baseline that interfaces with an MS Project resource-loaded schedule. This position will help drive effective program execution relative to cost performance, delivery performance, and effective program resource planning. Successful candidates will analyze program performance (historical and predictive), monitor the forecasted completion data, and support recommendations made to the program managers and leads.
 
Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Working with Program Management, IPTs/CAMs, and functional business analysts to perform detailed cost/schedule planning, earned value variance analysis, identification of cost/schedule problems, and developing corrective action plans.
  • Interfacing with program finance and program office for monthly financial forecasts, issues, concerns, and status
  • Strictly adhering to American National Standards Institute/Electronic Industries Alliance Standard 748 (ANSI/EIA-748), Earned Value Management Systems (EVMS) and associated internal company procedures
  • Developing and maintaining program artifacts and documentation
  • Integrating data from Microsoft Project to Cobra and ensuring accuracy
  • Using Cobra, and Winsight/Empower to create, analyze, and submit monthly Integrated Program Management Report (IPMR) to customer in accordance with Earned Value Management standards, as well as, preparing monthly EV metrics for internal/external meetings
  • Tracking spending to ensure adequate funding is maintained, financial metric development, & analysis and reporting in support of monthly program reviews
  • Fostering an environment of integrity, establishing relationships, and maintaining communication with stakeholders at multiple levels
  • Timely and accurate Estimate at Completion development, analysis, and reporting
  • Direct involvement in streamlining of processes, tools, and best practices
  • Facilitate and conduct EVM compliance reviews with DCMA and other government oversight agencies (Internal Baseline Reviews/Joint Surveillance Reviews), as well as other related audits
