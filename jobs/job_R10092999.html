ACME Engineering is seeking a 5th Gen Fighter or Bomber Pilot/SME at the Virtual Test and Training Center, 31CTS, Nellis AFB, NV. The VTTC is evolving into the world’s premier high-end Virtual Air Dominance Battlespace for future Large Force Integration events, and ACME Engineering’s team is working to make that future a reality at Nellis AFB.

The Joint Simulation Environment (JSE) is also right around the corner at Nellis AFB. The 31CTS and ACME is taking a leading role in accelerating future JSE capabilities now, to ensure Nellis’ Airmen are ready, and America’s Air Force is prepared for any high-end fight.

The candidate will assist in the creation of integrated 5th Gen virtual operations to ensure the best possible training environment for WPS, 422, and/or visiting units. NGC’s Virtual Battlespace Environment includes operational and test software to include Combat Environment Server (CES), Next Generation Threat System (NGTS), Advanced Simulation Combat Operations Trainer (ASCOT), just to name a few. With ACME Engineering fulfilling the role of Lead Systems Integrators (LSI), the candidate will fill a critical role to ensure seamless battlespace integration with current Fighter/Bomber assets on the 31CTS campus (F-22/F-35/F-15E/F-16/E-3/RJ/CRC), and future advanced capabilities on the near horizon.

If selected, your role with the VTTC will be a critical one. Advancing the world’s best Air Force by shaping and implementing a world premier high-end Virtual Air Dominance Battlespace synthetic 5th Gen environment is no easy task, but as many of us know, it needs to happen. If you’re ready to stay in the fight, and love the mission of Nellis AFB as we do, ACME is prepared to compensate well for your skill set and expertise.

Required Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree with 15 years' experience

  • Fighter or Bomber experience; 4 years minimum flying 5th Gen aircraft F-22 F-35 B-2 or similar, either operationally or in test.

  • Thorough knowledge of AFTTP 3-1 fighter/bomber employment

  • Operationally relevant experience within the past 5 years (simulator experience considered)

  • Active DoD Secret Clearance with ability to obtain to TS/SCI and SAP/SAR

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Mission Commander and/or Instructor Pilot/WSO/CSO/NFO experience

  • Weapons Officer and/or Test Pilot School graduate

  • Possess an active DoD Top Secret security clearance
