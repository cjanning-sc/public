ACME Engineering’s Launch Vehicles business unit designs, builds and delivers space, defense and aviation-related systems to customers around the world. Our main products include launch vehicles and related propulsion systems; missile products, subsystems and defense electronics; precision weapons, armament systems and ammunition; satellites and associated space components and services; and advanced aerospace structures.

The Launch Vehicles (LV) Sentinel Program Promontory General Manufacturing Manager 1 will provide daily leadership of the electrical and mechanical technician staff at the LV field site location in support of the Sentinel Program, in Promontory, UT.  The technician team supports the manufacture, assembly and test of target launch vehicles for the Sentinel program.   This role is responsible for implementing mechanical and electrical I&T engineering statement of work requirements and accounting for cost and schedule objectives.

Job Summary:

Candidate shall possess a general manufacturing background and have an understanding of system-level manufacturing concepts as they apply to integration of complex systems.  Duties will require daily contact with space launch vehicle hardware and solid rocket motors.  Candidate will be responsible for providing leadership and for guiding and mentoring mechanical and electrical technicians in the final system-level integration and test of air launched and ground launched target vehicles.  Specific duties may include: contribute to the development of detailed work instructions, execute processes and procedures directing the assembly and test of launch vehicle systems, ensuring consistent application of assembly processes and philosophies across all products; read and interpret complex engineering drawings and translate requirements into work instructions; coordinate integration activities and schedules with Integration and Test (I&T) engineers and field site management; document hardware defects and test anomalies, and develop and implement robust and effective corrective actions; identify opportunities for continuous process improvement and participate in implementation; support launch operations, as required.  Good interpersonal skills are required as well as the ability to communicate with field site leadership.  Knowledge of standard PC application software is required. 

Key Responsibilities include:

•     Supports Promontory Booster Integration Facility (BIF) Operations Manager in the integration, test and delivery of Sentinel program vehicles

•     Provides overall leadership and task assignments to diverse team of electrical and mechanical technicians

•     Responsible for overall Performance Management of the technician staff including performance evaluations, merit and compensation planning, professional development and hiring decisions.

•     Interfaces between the technician team and local Program Management, Quality, Planning and Logistics leadership to ensure necessary support is available to support BIF activities

•     Interfaces with government oversight representatives from the USAF and Defense Contractor Management Agency (DCMA) as necessary regarding daily activities and issues

•     Provides input and status for the BIF Operations Manager regarding daily activity and schedule progress

•     Ensures staff adheres to processes, procedures and standard practices, including include 5S, Foreign Object Debris (FOD) Control, iCard, Tool Control, among others

•     Contributes to ongoing Continuous Improvement initiatives

•     Occasional business travel may be necessary (10% or less)

Basic Qualifications:

BS degree plus 5 years of experience; Masters degree plus 3 years of experience is required.

  • Must be able to exercise influence and lead team to success
  • Ability to make independent decisions, be self-motivated, possess excellent communications skills, detail-oriented, and team player
  • Strong communication skills, both written and verbal, with an ability to effectively communicate to all levels of employees, management and customers
  • Demonstrated effectiveness in managing internal relationships, prioritization, and delegation.
  • Ability to be flexible, innovative, work with speed and agility to drive tasks to completion
  • Must be detail-oriented but able to grasp the big picture
  • Must possess a DoD Secret Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Energetic safety knowledge and experience
  • Bachelor’s degree in manufacturing or science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM)
  • previous supervisory/leadership experience is preferred
