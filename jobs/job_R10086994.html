The Environmental and Specialty Test department within the Materials, Manufacturing, Integration, Test & Launch (M2IT&L) directorate has a need for a Manager Engineering Technical Support 1 - Structures Test Operations to be a leading a team of individual contributors, and contribute directly to programs and other department activities. 

This is a highly dynamic, interactive position. You will interact with numerous stakeholders including program management, business team, systems engineering, flight hardware design engineers and logistical support services. Outstanding organizational and leadership skills are required to succeed in these roles.

RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:

  • Oversee floor operations being executed by Test Conductors and Technicians

  • Ensuring effective coordination between disciplines:  Structural Test Team, Program Leads, Test Engineering, Measurements, Mechanical Operations and Quality

  • Planning and reviewing procedures (Solumina, CAPE), engineering drawings, design reviews and program schedules to ensure customer requirements are met

  • Coordinating Structures Test staffing support to programs

  • Conducting periodic performance evaluations, including mid-year and annual reviews

  • Providing technical leadership and guidance to Test Conductors and Technicians

  • Developing and overseeing employee development and training

  • Supporting Functional Homeroom initiatives and projects which include process improvement and Corrective/Preventive Action implementation
