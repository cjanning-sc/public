We are looking for you to join our team as a Staff Systems Engineering Architect based out of San Jose, CA.

What you’ll get to do:

As a Staff Systems Engineering Architect you will lead and develop next generation ground system solutions. In addition you will get to:

  • Determine customer needs and develop approaches, implementations, components, interfaces, identify standards and constraints, civil requirements, and security needs.

  • Survey the industry for awareness of customer needs and alternative systems.

  • Lead architecture design that stems from system requirements, decomposed across multiple levels, aimed to represent the ground capabilities, that have a major impact on the mission.

  • Interface directly with the Software, Hardware, Operations, Integration and Test teams, as well as program and project leadership to define and maintain the technical baseline.

  • Lead trade studies, technology development, and subcontractor teams as well as lead technical and cost proposals, and write white papers describing the offerings. 

  • Drive the program through maturity and milestones to achieve the mission, ensuring everything is synchronized. 

  • Work closely with Chief Engineer, Sr Program Managers and Program Director to support proposals, CONOPS and overall architecture.

Basic Qualifications:

  • 14 years of relevant experience with a Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM discipline; 12 years of relevant experience with a Masters' degree; 9 years of relevant experience with a PhD

  • Working knowledge of systems engineering principles and processes

  • Experience writing and presenting technical information to customer and internal management audiences

  • Experience with Agile development

  • Experience using the DOORS Requirements Management Database

  • Familiarity with Model-Based Systems Engineering (SysML/UML) and concepts

  • Experience leading requirement development/testing/verification

  • Experience working with Atlassian tool suite (i.e. JIRA, Confluence, etc.)

  • Extensive System Engineering experience supporting all phases of project life cycle, inclusive of technical experience tied to other roles, and must have the ability to be a technical leader

  • Active DoD Top Secret and SCI clearance

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Understanding of Ground System architecture including Spacecraft Command and Control (C2), TT&C, and industry establishments (FORGE, EGS, Cornerstone, Raptor, AMP).

  • Experience with Agile development particularly as a product owner

  • Experience with data analysis or modeling (e.g. with MATLAB/Python

  • Experience applying  Model-Based Systems Engineering (SysML/UML) concepts to support architecture design/development 

  • Knowledge of, or experience with, common ground approaches, implementations, components, standards, interfaces, constraints, civil requirements, and security needs

  • Knowledge of satellite communications and distributed system operations.

  • Demonstrated excellent verbal/written communication and team building
