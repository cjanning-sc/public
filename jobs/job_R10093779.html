Join ACME Engineering Space Systems and be a part of the team of men and women who solve some of the world’s most complex technical challenges. The Space Security business unit within the Tactical Space Systems division (TSSD) is seeking a Business Development Manager 3 to join their team.  This individual may work in one of the following locations:

  • Des Moines, VA
  • Columbus, CA

Lead business development and strategy for a portfolio of programs in the Space Security business area within individual Operating Unit objectives for growth. This individual will provide direction on the sustainment and organic growth of existing business development targets/programs as well as developing and leading the future business capture strategy in the DoD, Intelligence Community and adjacent markets.

The successful candidate must possess the domain experience and ability to: develop early win strategies for customer outreach and targeted influence; devise detailed government relations and legislative advocacy plans; convene multi-functional teams to create mission discriminators through affordable, innovative technology and engineering, management strategies and cost strategies, and present to senior management in a concise fashion to enable early bid/no bid decisions.

Roles and responsibilities include:

  • Managing task order distribution and workflow, proposals associated with small to large, cross division and sector re-competes.
  • Translating customer needs into specific requirements, identifying/tailoring company services and solutions into offerings, and managing captures to effectively market the operating unit’s products and capabilities to meet those needs.
  • Oversight on all aspects of the Business Acquisition Process (BAP) that includes steps to develop and execute growth strategies, identifying new business opportunities, establishing pursuit priorities, gaining, and growing customer intimacy, and developing success capture plans.
  • Assisting capture/proposal managers with task order proposals and execution.
  • Participating in key task order capture pursuits

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree
  • 5 years of experience in capture management, business development, project/business/program management or engineering
  • 2 years of experience in a leadership or supervisory role
  • Must have a current and active Top Secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Domain expertise in space system command and control and/or space business integration expertise
  • Understand the DoD & intelligence budgets and marketplace
  • Working knowledge of U.S. Government procurement policies and regulations
  • Program management, hardware and/or software engineering skills
  • TS/SCI clearance
  • SAP clearance eligibility
