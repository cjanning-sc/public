Northrop Grumman's Sensing & Secure Solutions Operating Unit is actively seeking a talented System Engineering expert to join a newly awarded, Northrop Grumman primed program. This position's work location is in Annapolis Junction Maryland and is part of the Mission Systems Sector within Northrop Grumman. The new team member will be designing and developing new systems, applications, and solutions for external customer's enterprise-wide cyber systems and networks. We are seeking a person who is capable of being responsible for System design efforts, mentoring junior engineers, and demonstrating knowledge/experience of the latest System Engineering techniques to model system design driving Software Development efforts.  

  

Performs technical planning, system integration, verification and validation, cost and risk, and supportability and effectiveness analyses for total systems. Analyses are performed at all levels of total system product to include: concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal. Ensures the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints. Performs functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications.

- A current and active TS/SCI Clearance and Polygraph is required. 

- BS + 9 years of exp; MS + 5 years of exp; PhD + 4 years of exp is required. 

- Experience with SysML and Model based system Engineering concepts. 

- Proven disciplined System Engineering experience with requirement-driven design through formal V&V.   

- Experience working in Agile (SaFE) environments. 

- Experience working with Software Development teams utilizing MBSE artifacts to drive Software Development. 

- An ability to interface with external entities is preferred. 

- Demonstrated technical leadership experience is highly preferred. 

  

Preferred Qualifications:  

- Experience with designing and developing new systems using Model Based System Engineering tools including CAMEO . 

- Knowledge of SysML.  

- Experience with creating Use Cases, Activity Diagrams, BDDs, IBDs, Sequence diagrams, etc. in CAMEO to satisfy system requirements.
