Northrop Grumman’s Space Systems sector is looking for a Director 1, Systems Engineering in our SDS Division. This leadership role is located in Roy, Utah. Northrop Grumman supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What You Will Get To Do:

Drive core systems engineering execution across the SDS Division portfolio of programs while reporting into the Strategic Deterrent Systems Division’s Engineering and Manufacturing Operations organization.

  • Participate in establishing and direct the implementation of integrated system engineering process across the SDS Division, driving consistency and implementation across program/product teams at all national locations and multiple subcontractor locations.
  • Participate in establishing and direct the implementation of model-based digital engineering processes used in the design and development of the SDS Division, at all levels of decomposition.
  • Manage all aspects of process development, training and development across mission/weapon system engineering, subsystem/component systems engineering as well as nuclear surety and SDS program specific processes and enablers
  • Ensure Systems Engineering product quality and consistency and manage auditing and oversight functions, including metrics development and management.
  • Interface closely with the product/program team leadership and implement systems engineering priorities for the team

Additional responsibilities may include:

  • Horizontally integrate across System Engineering directorate as well as members of the Engineering and Manufacturing Operations organization to ensure consistency of systems engineering approach. This will help identify areas of opportunity to support upcoming challenges that are not currently being elevated.
  • Provide independent review of portfolio software, hardware and deliverables, focused deep dives in program and product teams, as well as driving compliance to processes and procedures. 
  • Identify opportunities for and support of engagement activities across all programs.
  • Identify and mentor high potential technical leaders.
  • Provide advisement to all program leads (as required) and embedded resources.
