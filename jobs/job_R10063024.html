Digital ASIC (RTL) Design Engineer
R10063024
Days (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems’ Electrical Devices and Integrated Circuit (EDIC) design organization is responsible for enabling discriminating system capability with the design of full-custom mixed signal ICs and RFICs. In support of that capability, we are seeking a Principal Digital ASIC Design Engineer for design and implementation of the digital cores of these circuits using a top-down automated design flow.

The ideal candidate will have experience of full custom ASIC design, but we would consider applicants with FPGA experience who would like to move into the IC field. The digital content of our chips is relatively small but growing so we need people who can take responsibility for the entire function from specification to production test. Must be comfortable working in a project team comprising multiple skill sets while being accountable for their individual contribution.

This position may be filled at an Engineer level (T02), or a Principal Engineering (T03):

Level T02

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Electrical or Computer Engineering

  • 2 years of relevant experience with B.S., none required with Masters

  • Writing and Maintaining Design Specifications

  • RTL Coding using VHDL or Verilog languages

  • Digital RTL design (Verilog, Innovus, ModelSim, QuestaSim )

  • Design and coding of test benches to verify correct functionality

  • Familiarity with Cadence tools would be advantageous including but not limited to:

    • Xcelium for simulation and verification

    • Jasper or Conformal for Formal verification

    • Genus for synthesis

    • Tempus for static timing analysis

  • Eligible for a Secret or higher security clearance

Level T03

  • Bachelor's degree in Electrical or Computer Engineering

  • 5 Years with Bachelors in Science; 3 Years with Masters; 0 Years with PhD.

  • Writing and Maintaining Design Specifications

  • Digital RTL design (Verilog, Innovus, ModelSim, QuestaSim )

  • RTL Coding using VHDL or Verilog languages

  • Our tool flow is Cadence based using

    • Design verification using Cadence tools such as Xcelium

    • Formal Verification using tools such as Jasper or Conformal

    • Synthesis using Genus

    • Static Timing Analysis using Tempus

  • Able to obtain and maintain a Secret clearance per business requirements. US Citizenship is a requirement of this.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Delivery of multiple verified digital IC designs

  • Experience following a formal design process with checkpoint reviews

  • Familiarity with revision control and EDA standard formats used in cell/library development and modeling

  • Strong design automation skills

  • Current security clearance
