Principal Systems Engineer - (Space/Satellite) Top Secret
R10082332
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Space Systems Sector, Tactical Space Systems Division, seeks a qualified Principal Systems Engineer to support a space-to-ground communications network. This position is located in Dulles, VA and Colorado Springs, CO.

Systems Engineering responsibilities include:

  • Performing technical planning and analyses at all levels of total system product including: concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance, and disposal.

  • The Systems Engineer ensures the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints.

  • The role will include functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications.

  • The role may require assisting in new business activities such as conceptual design and analysis for proposals and funded studies and may include authoring sections for both.

  • Successful candidates must be able to interface with clients, as well as interact regularly with internal engineering associates and program management.

#NOVASPACE

Basic Qualifications:

  • B.S. in STEM - Science, Technology, Engineering, Math or related area and 5 years of experience; M.S. in STEM or related area and 3 years of experience; or a PhD in STEM

  • Systems engineering experience in one of more areas, such as requirements, Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE), verification & validation, interface management, test planning, readiness reviews, etc.

  • Must be a U.S. Citizenship with an active/current TS clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Space industry experience - Space domain/space vehicles, subsystems, and components

  • Radiofrequency (RF) and/or digital communications experience

  • Space-ground communications experience

  • Experience working in an Agile program team using methodologies such as Kanban or Scrum

  • MBSE requirements and architecture management (using DOORS and Cameo Systems Modeler)
