Northrop Grumman Space Components has an opening for a Maintenance Technician 1 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in San Diego, CA.

Responsibilities:

  • Uses a variety of hand/power tools, and shop equipment.

  • Performs repairs and maintenance in plant or office facilities, or on machines and equipment.

  • Typically performs work in one or more of the maintenance trades: carpentry, plumbing, painting, machine and equipment repair, electrical, sheet metal fabrication and HVAC. 

  • Conducts routine, periodic, or special inspections to determine repair and maintenance work necessary to prevent breakdowns of facilities, machinery, and equipment.

  • Uses tests, inspects, troubleshoots, and repairs machines and equipment.

  • Uses blueprints, sketches, layouts, wiring diagrams, drawings, and specifications.

  • Maintain and repair plumbing, electrical, soft soldering and brazing of various metals and pipefitting; perform minor electrical installations and operate and troubleshoot chillers

  • Perform mechanical and electrical repairs and maintenance to the buildings; preventative maintenance on fan coils, air handling units, compressors, autoclaves, ovens and plant machinery and equipment

  • Various small odd jobs, minor pipe repairs, and installations of air, vacuum, and drainage systems; drive company vans, trucks or cars to load, haul and move equipment

  • Complete Facilities Service Requests as assigned by Department Manager  

  • Responsible for observing all laws, codes, federal, state and local regulations and other applicable obligations wherever and whenever business is conducted on behalf of the Company

  • Expected to work in a safe manner in accordance with established operating procedures and practices.

  • High School diploma or equivalent and 2 years related experience in Facilities Maintenance

  • Basic knowledge of computer operations and applications pertinent to the maintenance field

  • Troubleshooting / repairing machinery and equipment

  • Ability to work both independently and in a team environment.

  • Good interpersonal, verbal and written communication skills to work with all levels of personnel, including management and outside vendors and to accurately communicate, document and report.

  • Good analytical ability to recognize deviations from accepted practice

  • Ability to work from heights (ladders, scaffold, lifts)

  • U.S. Citizenship required

  • Valid driver's license

  • Ability to install and uninstall (lift) devices weighing 50 pounds

  • Installation and repair of door hardware
