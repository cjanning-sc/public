Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking an Antenna Products Sr. Principal Antenna Design Engineer Mechanical to join our team of qualified and diverse individuals in Redondo Beach, CA.

The selected candidate will have knowledge and experience with antenna hardware and design practices. The ideal candidate will possess knowledge of RF components and assemblies, and spacecraft structures including primary/secondary structures made of metallic materials, advanced composites & Honeycomb constructions.

This individual will be knowledgeable of material strengths and properties, design criteria, loading conditions and processes (e.g. non-destructive testing, composite assemblies, etc). Experience with environmental testing requirements and experience with mechanical testing such as: structural static test, thermal cycle test, random & vibration tests to demonstrate qualification / acceptance of spacecraft structures.
