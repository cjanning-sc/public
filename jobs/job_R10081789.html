Principal Mechanical Engineer or Sr. Principal Mechanical Engineer
R10081789
1st Shift (United States of America)

  

Northrop Grumman is seeking a Principal Mechanical Engineer or Sr. Prinicipal Mechanical Engineer to join our Mission Systems sector site in Apopka, FL. This position is to support the design, integration, test, and production of laser and optical systems for handheld, ground, and airborne products.  
 
The Principal Mechanical Engineer or Sr. Principal Mechanical Engineer will use ProEngineer to create CAD models and generate drawings and bill of materials (BOMs) for production and development projects. Candidate must be able to work within a multi-discipline team to support root cause corrective action (RCCA) determinations, generate engineering change notices (ECNs), update models and drawings, and release the appropriate documentation using TeamCenter applications. The ability to work in a fast-paced environment is critical.  

Roles & Responsibilities:


• Design/documentation of opto-mechanical and laser systems using CAD tools 
• Generating detail part drawings with tolerances to 0.001”  
• Generating assembly drawings and BOMs  
• Generating ECNs  
• Entering documentation into TeamCenter databases  

  

 
"This requisition is to be filled as a Principal Mechanical Engineer or Sr. Principal Mechanical Engineer based on the qualifications listed below."

 

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Mechanical Engineer:
• B.S. Degree in Mechanical Engineering plus 5 years engineering experience, or M.S. in Mechanical Engineering plus 3 years engineering experience. 
• Creo, SolidWorks, or NX CAD experience 
• Experience with ANSI Y14.5M GD&T specifications  

• US Citizenship is required with the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD  

• Mechanical design and drawings of electro-optical systems 

• US Citizenship is required with the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Clearance 

 
Basic Qualifications for a Sr. Principal Mechanical Engineer:
• B.S. Degree in Mechanical Engineering plus 9 years engineering experience, or M.S. in Mechanical Engineering plus 7 years engineering experience. 
• Pro-Engineer, SolidWorks, or NX CAD experience 
• Experience with ANSI Y14.5M GD&T specifications  

• US Citizenship is required with the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD  

• Mechanical design and drawings of electro-optical systems 

• US Citizenship is required with the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Clearance 

 
Preferred Qualifications:
• Experience with TeamCenter Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) applications  
• Experience with military specifications  
• Experience in ANSYS FEA 
• Experience supporting the integration of complex laser and optical-mechanical prototypes
