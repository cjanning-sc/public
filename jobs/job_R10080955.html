ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems is seeking an experienced Principal Strategy Analyst / Principal Project Manager to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals supporting a corporate priority capture.  The role will require both strategy development and driving strategy execution within the capture while supporting implementation of operations across the Competitiveness organization.  This position will be located onsite in Philadelphia, PA. 

In the role you will:

  • Support development, integration, maintenance, and communication of Competitiveness team schedule and roadmap
  • Facilitate and implement competitiveness operational rhythm
  • Deliver quick turn analytic products to support program leaders on military, modernization, customer, competitor, strategy, and market issues
  • Craft and deliver clear, concise, and impactful presentations for executive-level leadership that provide actionable intelligence and recommendations
  • Support capture strategy integration within the competitiveness team to ensure strategy alignment across the organization (in coordination with team lead)
  • Support coordination and partnership with IPT leaders to enable capture strategy deployment by aligning implementation of tactics to achieve win strategy (in coordination with team lead)
  • Support forums across program and capture IPTs to communicate strategy (in coordination with team lead)

Basic Qualifications for the Principal Strategy Analyst / Project Management:

  • Bachelor’s degree from an accredited university and 10 years of experience in Project Management, Program Integration/Execution; 8 years with Master's degree for a level 4 or Bachelor’s degree from an accredited university and 6 years of experience in Project Management, Program Integration/Execution; 4 years with Master's degree for a level 3
  • Demonstrated depth in technical and/or business acumen
  • Dynamic self-starter with strong attention to detail, strong work ethic, ability to handle multiple priorities, and the ability to function with minimal guidance
  • Experience developing briefings and interfacing with and briefing executive leadership teams
  • Active DoD Secret or Top Secret clearance with the ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access (PAR) within a reasonable amount of time as determined by business needs

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current Special Program access (PAR)
  • MBA
  • Strategy consulting and / or operations experience on high performing teams
  • Strong analytical, statistical, and critical thinking skills paired with the ability to visualize in a meaningful manner
  • In-depth knowledge of military aviation, the Aerospace and Defense industry, customers, and competitors (portfolio, business models, structure, and programs)
  • Experience with MS Project and JIRA or similar products
