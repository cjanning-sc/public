Northrop Grumman is currently seeking a Driver Heavy Vehicles 2 to join our team at the St. Augustine, FL facility.  

Job Responsibilities:

  • Operates heavy-duty truck (typically with either semi-trailer or heavy-duty trailer) to transport materials, merchandise, or equipment within an industrial area.
  • Coordinates the loading or unloading of trucks and trailers. Makes minor mechanical adjustments.
  • Accountable for all aspects of safe operations of vehicle and equipment used in transporting company and government owned assets.
  • Organize loads to maximize delivery times and reduce waste. 

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School Diploma plus 2 or more years driving experience.
  • Must have a current Class A CDL with the ability to obtain applicable endorsements, such as Haz-Mat, Passenger
  • Shall provide a current MVR with zero moving violations in the past year.
  • Must meet all DOT pre-employment requirements
  • Proven ability to work within a team environment and work well with customers.
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Prefer knowledge of FMCSR regulations and possession of a valid Class A CDL and Endorsements.
  • Must possess the ability to read in-plant handling manual instructions including special tie-down arrangements and general and special safety equipment.
  • Two-year driving transportation vehicles of up to vehicles with a GVWR of 26,001 or its equivalent in on-the-job training either as a medium equipment or forklift operator.
  • Functional with basic computer functions including Word, and ability to operate mobile devices (iPad).
