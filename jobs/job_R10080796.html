Cost Estimating Analyst - Facilities

Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems sector and Aerospace Structures business unit is seeking a Cost Estimating Analyst - Facilities to provide detailed cost and schedule estimates for facility projects that support both sustaining infrastructure and new program facility requirements at our Clearfield, UT location. 

Join Northrop Grumman on our continued mission to push the boundaries of possible across land, sea, air, space, and cyberspace. Enjoy a culture where your voice is valued and start contributing to our team of passionate professionals providing real-life solutions to our world’s biggest challenges. We take pride in creating purposeful work and allowing our employees to grow and achieve their goals every day by Defining Possible. With our competitive pay and comprehensive benefits, we have the right opportunities to fit your life and launch your career today.

Essential Functions:

  • Provide detailed cost estimates for site capital projects
  • Maintain a cost database using past actuals and industry intelligence
  • Collaborate with security, EHS, IT, facilities engineers, architects, contractors, and customers as needed to quantify the scope of projects
  • Create detailed statements of work and ground rule packages
  • Develop and/or analyze project schedules
  • Read blueprints and technical documents
  • Visit job sites to gather information on conditions and project considerations
  • Identify and quantify cost factors, such as time, materials, and labor expenses
  • Utilization of Estimating books and computer software to calculate estimates
  • Calculate demolished capital asset valuations
  • Ensures project requirements are defined, operating budgets established, and financial terms/conditions of contract are identified

This position may be filled as either a level 2 or level 3 Cost Estimating Analyst dependent on qualifications.

Basic qualifications for a level 2:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Construction Management, Architecture, Civil Engineering, Structural Engineering, or STEM with 3 years of relevant experience OR a Master’s degree with 1 year of relevant experience
  • Ability to read and interpret architectural and engineering plans, drawings and specifications for quantities take-off and cost estimates
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office products including Excel, Word and Power Point
  • Must be able to obtain/maintain a Secret DoD Clearance

Basic qualifications for a level 3:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Construction Management, Architecture, Civil Engineering, Structural Engineering, or STEM with 6 years of relevant experience OR a Master’s degree with 4 years of relevant experience
  • Ability to read and interpret architectural and engineering plans, drawings and specifications for quantities take-off and cost estimates
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office products including Excel, Word and Power Point
  • Must be able to obtain/maintain a Secret DoD Clearance

Preferred qualifications:

  • Experience preparing cost estimates for facility and/or construction projects and the use of construction cost data reference books
  • Experience maintaining cost estimating standards
  • Project management experience over facility and/or construction projects
  • Proficient in MS Project (schedule development)
  • Knowledgeable in AutoCAD and Revit
  • Demonstrated experience in effectively forecasting and tracking project costs
