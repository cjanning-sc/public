Northrop Grumman is seeking a Quality Inspector 3 to join our Defense Systems sector. This position will be in Madison, Alabama and support the Huntsville Manufacturing Center within Combat Systems and Mission readiness division.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Inspects, verifies and validates parts and assemblies of mechanical, electrical, structural components or systems meet engineering and program requirements.
  • Visually and physically inspects installations and rechecks work after rework, adjustment or repair.
  • Monitors and verifies that product quality is in accordance with engineering drawings, work instructions, and other control procedures.
  • Reads and interprets blueprints, diagrams, and specifications.
  • Basic understanding of AS9102 First Inspection Report, generating FAI package for external customers.
  • Maintains necessary inspection documentation, writes inspection reports, and enters inspection data and defect information into a computer database.
  • Frequently interfaces with internal teams across the organization and external customers and contacts.
  • Participates in team activities and provides expertise and guidance for effective problem-resolution.
  • Audits processes and activities in accordance with documented procedures.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have a High School Diploma with 4 years of related work experience in the inspection of one or more areas: electrical, mechanical fabricated parts, or cables and assemblies.
  • Experience with and the ability to read and interpret blueprints, diagrams, and specifications.
  • Experience maintaining necessary inspection documentation, writing inspection reports, and entering inspection data and defect information into a computer database.
  • Ability to follow both verbal and written instructions timely and with a high degree of quality.
  • Computer experience (MS Office/Excel).
  • US citizenship, Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret security clearance.
  • Must be able to work on-site in Madison, AL

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Dimensional Receiving Inspection of machined parts.
  • Soldering and Electrical Assembly Inspection.
  • Board Assembly Inspection with capabilities of some repair work.
  • Mechanical Assembly Inspection
