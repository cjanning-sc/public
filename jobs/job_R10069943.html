ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Modeling, Simulation, and Analysis Principal Engineer to join the AVE Modeling, Simulation, and Analysis (MS&A) Team. This position will be located in Detroit, UT and will support the Sentinel program.

What you’ll get to do: 

The AVE MS&A Verification and Validation (V&V) engineer will be responsible for providing oversight and technical support across multiple disciplines and will be reporting to the AVE MS&A V&V Lead.

Responsibilities includes but not limited to:

  • Help plan and coordinate the development and implementation of model V&V standards, plans, activities, and reports across multiple disciplines including: Fluids, Structures, GNC, Flight Performance, etc in accordance with MIL-STD 3022 and DOD V&V best practices.
  • Help architect and develop MBSE (Cameo) content in support of cross-discipline model V&V efforts. These responsibilities include verification and validation of analytical models, and analysis/simulation software tools.
