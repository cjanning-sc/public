Adaptive Optics Xinetics (AOX), a wholly owned ACME Engineering Space Systems, Payload and Ground Systems Division Business Venture, is a leading supplier of complex electro-optic and photonic systems and subsystems that control and manipulate light waves for a variety of applications. We are seeking a Principal Mechanical Engineer to support our Special Programs team in East Hartford, CT.

Position Description:

Reporting to the Special Programs Mechanical Engineering Manager, the Principal Mechanical Engineer will perform design and analysis for opto-mechanical systems, tooling, and test equipment, leading engineering design efforts of complex electro-optical and opto-mechanical systems, designing hardware that satisfies system requirements, documenting design approaches, detailing parts, and assembly drawings to support the development, manufacture, and test of optical systems. Additional expectations include support to new business opportunities and active leadership and development of junior engineers, designers and technicians.

Role and Responsibilities:

  • Perform mechanical design on multiple projects through all phases of development and manufacturing

  • Create part and assembly models, detailed drawings and associated parts lists, in accordance with AMSE Y14.5, ASME Y14.100 and AOX drawing standards

  • Conduct peer reviews of other engineer’s work products

  • Execute new designs with a focus on DFM

  • Perform analysis of mechanisms, detailed geometric tolerances, and manufacturability

  • Support transition to production and manufacturing engineering

  • Provide technical solutions to complex problems which require regular use of technical acumen, ingenuity and creativity

  • Perform trade studies, design of experiments, assembly and testing of parts, components, and assemblies

  • Estimate and track mechanical design costs, create schedule estimates, and make presentations to customers and company management

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering

  • 5 or more years of mechanical design, development, analysis and FEA experience working in a collaborative, highly technical multi-disciplined engineering work environment

  • Analytical problem solver with technical lead abilities; results and deadline driven; excellent oral and written communication skills including presentation skills; technical influencer

  • Proficient MS Word, MS Excel, MS PowerPoint software

  • Intermediate-Advanced SolidWorks (or equivalent) software skills

  • Active Top Secret Clearance

  • US Citizenship is a bona fide requirement

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s Degree plus 5 years mechanical design experience

  • Experience working with micro or small precision form factor mechanisms; opto-mechanical designs; optical assembly, interferometer and alignment, test equipment and tools

Working Environment & Physical Demands:

  • Travel to customer site as required (10%)

  • Ability to sit and/or stand for extended periods of times and light physical effort as required
