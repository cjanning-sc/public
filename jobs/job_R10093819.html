SCA Engineering Technician 4 (Quality)
R10093819
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Defense Services is seeking an experienced Flight Inspector to join its dynamic team of technical professionals in the Patuxuent River, MD area with the final destination to be in Sigonella, Italy. The candidate will provide quality support to the Triton maintenance team. Candidate will inspect test development and production Triton aircraft systems, equipment and parts for operation and conformance to company standards and specifications, which includes the inspection of installation and operation of electrical, electronic, hydraulic, pneumatic, fuel, oil, and emergency systems. Audit / monitor processes to ensure contractual requirements and AS9100D standards are being met. Develops processes, procedures and maintains metrics and trend analysis for those processes and procedures. Monitors and verifies processes in accordance with quality or other control procedures. Ensures the required quality assurance programs and policies are maintained. Serves as a liaison between the company and the customer. Must be able to use MS Word, EXCEL, PowerPoint and possess strong writing skills. Must have a working knowledge of aircraft systems and be able to read aircraft system wiring schematics and diagram, including naval publications and engineering blueprints. This position may work multiple shifts.

Basic Qualifications:
AA or other 2 year technical degree in a related field and/or 4 years experience. In lieu of formal education, 6 years of related experience may be considered.

Candidate must have a minimum of 6 years of aircraft maintenance experience preferably in unmanned aircraft. A minimum of 2 years of quality inspecting and auditing experience is required.

Working knowledge of DCMA Instruction 8210.1 and COMNAVAIRFORINST 4790.2

DoD secret clearance required to start.

Preferred Qualifications:

Global Hawk or Triton experience.

Optimized Organizational Maintenance Activity (OOMA)

Working knowledge of Lean Six Sigma practices

Working knowledge of Agile practices
