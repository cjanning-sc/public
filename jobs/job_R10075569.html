Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for an Sr. Principal Engineer Systems to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Melbourne, Florida.

Position Summary / Responsibilities

The Melbourne Manned Aircraft Design (MAD) Center of Excellence (CoE) is seeking a Sr. Principal Systems Engineer under Systems Engineering to support key program/s in the Aeronautics Systems Sector (NGAS). The selected candidate will perform system engineering activities for all levels of a total system product, including concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, and maintenance.

Essential Functions:

·    Performs systems integration, and verification for total systems.

·    Performs functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation, and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications.

·    Support system requirements development, management, analysis, and traceability, to translate customer requirements and CONOPS into systems requirements, and further allocate to lower level requirements.

·    Generation and updating of System and sub-system level specifications in DOORS, including creation of Verification Cross Reference Matrices (VCRM) and verification traceability (tracing requirements to test plans/procedures/results)

Basic Qualifications:
• Sr. Principal Engineer Systems: 9 years of professional work experience and a Bachelor's Degree in Science, technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM), OR 7 years of professional work experience with a Masters Degree in STEM discipline.

• An active Secret DoD Clearance with an investigation in the past 5 years.

• Ability to obtain additional Special Access Program (SAP) clearance before establishing a start date (contingency)

Preferred Qualifications:

·    Experience with system engineering tools such as IBM Rational DOORS

·    First-hand experience with requirements management

·    Experience with verification of system requirements

·    Familiarity with system documents such as System/Subsystem Specifications, Verification Cross Reference Matrices (VCRM), etc.

·    Experience with Agile / Scrum / DevOps
