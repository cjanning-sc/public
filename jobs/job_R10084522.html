ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Systems Engineer/Principal Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Detroit, MI and will support Sentinel (Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD)) program. This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What you’ll get to do:

Performing customer requirements elicitation leading to Requirements Development

Coordination & collaboration with the System Engineering and Command & Launch Segment System Engineering Teams

Supporting system, segment, element and unit level trade studies

Definition of high-level functional and performance requirements and verification methods, subsequent analysis, decomposition, and derivation of requirements, allocation of those lower-level requirements to physical and functional architectural elements, and tracking them through design, verification, integration, and operational validation

Supporting a team of engineers to create, mature and present comprehensive and cohesive technical charts representative of the appropriate level of maturation for any given milestone meeting and demonstrating team readiness in relation to achieving the milestone entrance and exit criteria

Identifying required contractual deliverables and non-contractual documentation and driving corresponding updates to closure

Coordinating and collaborating with diverse engineering design teams to assist in managing the technical baseline

You’ll bring these qualifications:

  • Engineer: Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Math) discipline from an ABET accredited university with 2 years of experience performing in a Systems Engineering or related technical discipline
  • Principal Engineer: level requires minimum of 5 years of related experience with a Bachelor’s degree; 3 years with a Master’s; 0 years with a PHD.
  • Understanding of systems engineering principles and processes
  • Demonstrated communication skills and flexibility
  • Proficiency in MS Office
  • U.S. Citizenship with active DoD Secret Clearance (awarded or reviewed in the last 5.5 years)
  • Eligible for Special Access Program Clearance
  • Requisition may be filled at T2 or T3 level

These qualifications would be nice to have:

  • A current DoD Top Secret clearance with capability for SAP access
  • Experience presenting to project/ program management, other technical managers and customers
  • Direct experience using the DOORS Requirements Management Database
  • Familiarity with Model- Driven Systems Engineering languages (SysML /UML) and concepts and CORE knowledge a plus
  • Excellent technical writing skills, and team building skills
  • Subcontractor technical oversight experience
