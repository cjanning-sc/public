ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking an Affordability & Capture (Manager 3) for the Aerospace Vehicle Equipment (AVE) Segment team in preparation for capturing additional Engineering Manufacturing Development (EMD) and Production business for the Sentinel (GBSD) program. This position will be in Detroit, UT with occasional travel to other spoke locations that support the Sentinel program.  Learn more about the Sentinel program here.  ACME Engineering supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

Specific duties to include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Develop Capture and Production Planning Strategy and integrate across multiple AVE element product teams for future Sentinel business positioning
  • Lead proposal development effort for AVE, utilizing developed strategies to feed the greater Sentinel Program
  • Develop Affordability / Cost per Unit Targets and Sustainability Strategy and implement/execute affordability program for the AVE Segment Team
  • Supplier proposal coordination and relationship building to a common ACME capture plan
  • Assist IPTs and the Functions in meeting affordability management goals and establish affordability progress metrics
  • Conduct cost-benefit-analysis needed for approval of IPT and Functions affordability initiatives and trade studies
  • Establish program “affordability culture” through affordability management events and recognition of successful affordability initiatives
  • Support cross-functional development and maintenance of Life Cycle Cost models and their integration into broader business operations
  • Assist in developing and providing affordability status for IPT inputs to the program risk and opportunity management system
  • Ability to lead a small team of analysts and tech writers to execute job functions
  • Maintains on-going interaction with Sentinel Business Development Leadership and Program Mgmt.

  • 3 years of formal management experience in product team leadership, or program management, or capture / proposal work, or business management, or manufacturing/industrial engineering leadership.
  • Must be a US Citizen with an active DoD Secret Clearance with an investigation date within the last 6 years.

  • Understanding or experience of full rate production economic strategies and supply chain management
  • Previous experience developing “price to win” targets and cost reduction initiatives in a large program affordability office
  • Ability to deal with ambiguity and meet program commitments in a highly fluid engineering environment
