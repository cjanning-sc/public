Associate Windows Systems Administrator

1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Global Operations Security is seeking a qualified Associate Windows Systems Administrator to join its dynamic team of technical professionals in Linthicum, MD.  The selected candidate will be required to work on-site, full-time, at our Linthicum campus. Roles and responsibilities will include but not be limited to the following:  

  • Perform as a Windows Systems Administrator for a large classified government contract.

  • Maintain efficient operations of desktop computers. 

  • Support multiple networks and ensure necessary IT administration tasks are completed under supervision as necessary.

  • Serve as the first point of contact for customers seeking technical assistance over the phone, via email, and/or in-person.

  • Provide answers to clients by identifying problems, researching answers, and guiding clients through corrective steps.

  • Record events, problems, and their resolution in logs.

  • Report project status as required for all recurring and non-recurring efforts.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Associate’s degree with 2 years of experience, or a Bachelor’s degree with 0 years of experience; a High School diploma or equivalent with 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a completed degree.

  • Current DoD Top Secret security clearance with SCI eligibility; the ability to maintain the required security clearance will be a condition of continued employment.

  • The selected candidate will be required to obtain a polygraph within a reasonable period of time, as determined by customer and/or program requirements; maintaining a current DoD Top Secret security clearance with the required polygraph will be a condition of continued employment.

  • Candidate may be temporarily assigned other duties, as needed, while awaiting completion of clearance requirements.

  • Current DoD Approved 8570 IAM Level I certification (i.e. Security+CE), or the ability to obtain the required certification within 6 months of hire date; customer and/or program requirements may expedite the 6 month time window; maintaining the required certification will be a condition of continued employment.

  • Ability to travel as needed.

  • Ability to lift equipment weighing up to 40 pounds.

  • Ability to work after hours and weekends as needed.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current DoD Approved 8570 IAM Level I certification (i.e. Security+CE).

  • Experience operating under and managing systems within current applicable information systems compliance standards.

  • Fundamental knowledge of Windows desktop support and Active Directory (AD) users and computers.

  • Helpdesk support experience. 

  • Experience working in team of IT professionals under direct supervision.
