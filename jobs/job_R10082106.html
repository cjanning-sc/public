SP Launch and Missile Defense Systems (LMDS) is an industry-leading provider for prime satellite and payload capabilities and directed energy and electronics solutions for national security, military, and civil customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible.
 
LMDS has an opening for a Manager 1 Procurement – Strategic Services – Rapid Procurement to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Louisville, Kentucky.


Job Description: The successful applicant for the post of Manager Procurement 1 will join a growing supply chain Rapid Procurement team as a people leader in the Launch Vehicles business unit of ACME Engineering’s Space Systems sector. The Manager 1 Procurement will initially oversee daily operations for the multi-functional Strategic Services team which serves the procurement groups responsible for the Electronics, Mechanical Hardware, Machined Hardware, and Structures.

The Manager Procurement 1 will be deployed into a compliance-oriented FAR/DFARS/NFARS environment and must have an appropriately high level of expertise in federal contracting. The Strategic Services team is initially expected to consist of employees implementing complex Long Term Agreements and other overarching agreements, Procurement Specialists (buyers) acquiring a variety of goods and services in support of both capital and direct expenditures, and hourly Procurement Coordinators performing a variety of support functions.

Job responsibilities may include but are not limited to:

  • Daily operations of a team or work unit (direct supervision of the staff, assignment of work, schedules, performance development, day to day workflows, escalations, reporting, and operating costs).
  • Ownership of cost, schedule, technical performance, and achievement of critical program and project needs relating to procurements and agreements residing in the Strategic Services Team.
  • Provide strong training, performance and career development, and mentorship to direct reports.
  • Provide subject matter expertise to direct reports, peers, and members of procurement commodity groups served as customers by Strategic Services.
  • Constantly engaged in the development and execution of business process improvements.
  • Receive general strategic direction from Senior Management and additional commodity-specific strategy from peers managing commodity procurement teams.
  • Receive and flows down performance goals tailored to the various functions on the team.
  • Establishes team objectives and provides tactical direction to subordinates using established policies and precedents.
  • Flexibility in work assignment within the people management function is desired; the successful candidate will likely be asked to assist in organizational transformation and change management with personal job scope shifting potentially multiple times during the re-organization process.
     

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree with 6 years or Master’s degree with 4 years of supply chain or business management experience.  Or, in lieu of a degree, 8 years of experience in supply chain or business management experience.
  • One year of experience in a lead or supervisory role
  • Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contracts, Blanket Purchase Agreements (BPAs), and Basic Ordering Agreements (BOAs).
  • Costpoint experience is highly desirable, however experience on other major ERP systems will be considered.
  • Demonstrated people management skills with associated soft skills that include especially strong communication skills, emotional intelligence, inspirational approach, boldness, and personal caring.
  • Strong Excel and Powerpoint skills are preferred.

     

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Strong expertise in FAR- based federal contracting, preferably including strong proficiency in DFARS and/or NFARS.
  • Strong proficiency in strategic sourcing agreements is desired, including but not limited to Long Term Agreements (LTAs), overarching terms and conditions
  • SAP proficiency
