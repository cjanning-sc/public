ACME Engineering, Classified Cybersecurity (CCS) has an opening for a security coordinator to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. The successful candidate will be a vital member of the team and perform in an administrative leadership role.  This position will include but not be limited to performing office functions such as arranging meetings, schedules, and calendars; supporting leadership travel; ordering supplies for the organization; and supporting operational tasks.  

Essential functions consist of:

  • Maintain professional demeanor and act with integrity with all levels of the organization

  • Maintain busy and dynamic calendar for CCS Director

  • Responsible for maintaining CCS calendar and coordinating management meetings

  • Coordinating travel arrangements, expense reports and all forms of correspondence for CCS Director and direct reports

  • Plan and provide logistical support to leadership and the leadership team, including scheduling internal and external meetings, coordinating lunch and learn meetings, and coordinating special events

  • Utilizing Microsoft Office Suite for communications, reporting, presentations and other various tasks (i.e. mail merges, coordinate calendars and meetings, assembling presentations, documenting processes and knowledge sharing)

  • Create briefings and reports to management using various information sources

  • Procure material and supplies to support organization using corporate card and internal purchasing systems

  • Strong interpersonal, organizational and problem-solving skills

  • Ability to troubleshoot and work with minimal supervision

  • Ability to be an exceptional team player, interface well at all levels in the organization

  • Ability to be collaborative, proactive, self-motivated, well-organized, detail oriented, and assertive as necessary and able to execute projects and tasks independently

  • Ability to exercise good judgment while demonstrating flexibility and agility, prioritizing multiple tasks with competing deadlines, managing schedule conflicts, and providing excellent follow-through

  • Demonstrates outstanding verbal, interpersonal and written communication skills

  • Ability to protect and maintain confidentiality

**This requisition may be filled as either a Security Coordinator 3 or a Security Coordinator 4.**

Basic Qualifications for a Security Coordinator 3:

  • High School Diploma or General Education Diploma (G.E.D) required

  • Minimum 4 years of experience as an administrative assistant or performing administrative-related work

  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook and PowerPoint)

  • Proficient in arranging travel and expensing reports in Concur

  • Proficient in ordering and processing item orders in iBuy and SAP

Basic Qualifications for a Security Coordinator 4:

  • High School Diploma or General Education Diploma (G.E.D) required

  • Minimum 6 years of experience as an administrative assistant or performing administrative related work

  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook and PowerPoint)

  • Proficient in arranging travel and expensing reports in Concur

  • Proficient in ordering and processing orders in iBuy and SAP

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Excel formula use

  • Advanced proficiency in business and travel expense system (Concur, Edge, or similar) with complex, frequently changing travel
