ACME Engineering Mission Systems (NGMS) is a leading global provider, manufacturer and integrator of advanced, secure and agile software-defined systems and solutions. Our products range from advanced sensing technologies to state-of-the-art targeting and tracking systems that are deployed in Infrared Countermeasures (IRCM) / Aircraft Survivability Systems (ACS), Electro-Optical Infrared (EOIR), and Radio Frequency Electronic Warfare (RFEW) systems. These systems are designed, developed, built, integrated, and tested by the capable folks at our company to protect the lives of U.S. and Allied warfighters in present and future conflicts.

We are looking for you to join our team as a Systems Engineering Manager based out of Milwaukee, IL. As a Systems Engineering Manager at ACME Engineering, you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation.

What You’ll get to Do:

This is a dual responsibility position, so the candidate with spend part of their time managing the team and part of their time working as a Modeling & Simulation Lead or contributor on programs within the area the team supports.

If selected as the Manager for the SEIT Modeling & Simulation, as Modeling & Simulation Lead, you will provide leadership in applying the core principles of model-based systems engineering in order to deliver innovative, affordable, highly reliable systems solutions that meet customer needs, key performance parameters, and program execution goals. The Modeling & Simulation Lead will lead multi-disciplined engineering teams (ranging from 1 to 30+ engineers) from business capture through system design and then integration and test of highly complex technical systems in a model-based environment while maximizing focus on reuse and ineroperability.  Responsibilities may include engineering leadership for system architecture development, requirements development, modeling & simulation, hardware/software design, verification and validation activities, and production program engineering support. A person in this position ensures that the program is adhering to sound engineering processes in the development, production, test and sustainment of products for our customers and that the program and product is compliant with company policy, procedures, and quality standards. 

The successful candidate will clearly demonstrate strong leadership skills to achieve sustainable top performance. He/she will have a track record as an effective communicator and problem solver who is able to develop and maintain good working relationships with internal and external stakeholders. The candidate will demonstrate a commitment to ethics and integrity, and the capability to innovate while challenging traditional ways of doing business. Demonstrated abilities to build diverse and inclusive teams with high levels of engagement, including mentoring and coaching, are a must. Excellent verbal and written communication skills are required.

The candidate will demonstrate a broad skill set that is balanced between interpersonal, business, and technical capabilities. He/she will have an ability to operate in a team environment and collaborate across the sector business areas as required to accomplish the team goals. The candidate will work closely with the managers of other engineering disciplines as well as program directors/managers. The ideal candidate will also be able to champion change within the organization, to achieve competitive advantage for the business.
