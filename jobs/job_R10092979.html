ACME Engineering Mission System's Navigation and Positioning Systems BU in Flint, UT is seeking a Supplier Quality Engineer/Principal Supplier Quality Engineer to join the Supplier Performance Management (SPM) team, reporting to the SPM site lead.  This position is instrumental in driving the supply base to provide consistently high-quality parts to support our customers.

Tasks include:

  • Review supplier quality history and drive activities to improve performance
  • Act as Supplier Performance Management POC on program teams
  • Coordinates and oversees supplier quality plan implementation through all program phases
  • Provide updates to senior management in Mission Assurance Critical Action Board
  • Liaison to field supplier quality engineers (FSQE) for program related actions and information
  • Engage in supplier selection for component procurement
  • Make decisions utilizing supplier quality risk appraisal tools
  • Assists with program cost estimate development related to supplier quality activities
  • Participates in customer and regulatory on-site quality audits, and manages supplier quality related corrective actions as necessary
  • Initiates and manages SCAR’s, CAR’s for non-conforming materials
  • Provides input to supplier scorecards
  • Supports the maintenance of the approved supplier list
  • Assist with development of entry level engineers to maximize team effectiveness

Basic Qualifications for Supplier Quality Engineer:

  • Bachelor's degree in a related STEM field and 2+ years of relevant experience.

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Supplier Quality Engineer:

  • Bachelor's degree in a related STEM field and 5+ years of relevant experience.

To be successful in this position, the candidate should possess the following characteristics:

  • Ability to prioritize and complete multiple tasks within critical deadlines
  • Ability to influence and drive results without direct authority of personnel
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with attention to detail and accuracy
  • Excellent organizational, verbal and written communication skills, as well as analytical, planning, and presentation skills are required
  • Demonstrated ability to build and foster effective relationships with others
  • SAP experience
  • Risk and opportunity management experience
