Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security.  Our Engineering and Sciences (E&S) organization pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter.  Come be a part of our mission!

 

We are looking for you to join our team as a Field Technician 3 based out of Chantilly, VA.

What You’ll get to Do:

As a uniquely qualified mission oriented individual with a background in electronics, Electronic System integration/maintenance, Airborne ISR, Electronic Warfare (EW) and some field experience you will get to:

  • Perform CONUS mission/maintenance support, integration/test of in-house and field-deployed EW products

  • Conduct training in both formal and informal environments

  • Closely interface with the customer and system operators in planning, maintenance and operation of fielded equipment

  • Support will include field-testing of selected equipment and software

  • Work with minimal supervision and support

  • Work on various types of electronic test equipment and diagnostic software packages

  • Showcase your excellent oral and written communications skills, as well as, your strong analytical, organizational, and interpersonal skills

  • Communicate concisely and effectively with all levels of program personnel and customers using verbal and/or written communications

  • Author, distribute and maintain formal and informal reports on all activities

  • Be a highly motivated, self-starter but also get to work with a team

  • Be part of a high pressure, no-fail, highly dynamic, rapidly changing, and challenging work environment; therefore, a highly professional demeanor and a positive mental attitude needed to satisfy these demands

  • Work extended hours during mission critical time periods

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School diploma or equivalent and 4 years additional education and/or related experience

  • Must have radio and/or computer troubleshooting and repair experience

  • Experience with Microsoft Office (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint)

  • Must have an Active DoD Top Secret / SCI

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Avionics Integration experience

  • Electronic Warfare experience

  • Military experience highly desirable

  • Antenna, RF cable/connector troubleshooting and repair experience
