Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Senior Principal Facilities Engineer/Reliability Engineer to support our Facility & Maintenance Team. This position will be located in Clearfield, UT.

Role Description & Responsibilities:

As the Principal Facilities Engineer / Reliability Engineer you will specialize in various types of automated mechanical/electrical components.  Reviews existing equipment and becomes completely familiar with the OEM’s manufacturing build protocol as it relates to machine function.  Provides emphasis to ensure that the correct preventative maintenance procedures are in place as it relates to the mechanical and electrical systems and recommend all necessary spare parts to keep the plant operational on a 24/7 basis. This position is a Senior Principal Level Reliability Engineer working to ensure that all equipment performs at their ultimate performance level and maintain a high level of uptime.  There is considerable latitude for independent action and decision making. You will support our current facility and allow it to grow to the next level of operations and production. You will prepare presentations and reports detailing the Operational Equipment Effectiveness on all machines assigned.  You will aid in developing standard specifications for all future equipment purchases.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science with 9 yrs related experience. Master’s degree with 7 yrs related experience, or P.H.D. with 4 yrs related experience in Mechanical or manufacturing engineering.
  • Experience in installing and commissioning industrial equipment.
  • Ability to prepare sound presentations and conduct meetings with the senior staff.
  • Proficient in Word, Excel, MS Project and PowerPoint.
  • Ability to obtain and maintain an Active DoD Secret Clearance
  • Experience in installing and commissioning industrial equipment.

Preferred Skills:

  • Experience in lean manufacturing
  • Experience in aerospace industry
  • Past experience working in high rate manufacturing facilities operating on a 24/7 schedule.
  • Basic understanding of Siemens 840D control systems.
  • Basic understanding of carbon composite machining

Competencies for Success:

  • Ability to manage time, and multi-task
  • Ability to work independently and use judgment to make appropriate decisions that support overall project objectives.
  • Ability to drive projects and processes
  • Ability to partner with leadership at all levels.
  • Ability to negotiate and consult with vendors as required to maintain and repair mechanical equipment.
  • Ability to prepare sound presentations and conduct meetings with the senior staff.
