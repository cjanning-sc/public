ACME Engineering Space Systems has an opportunity for a Mission Assurance (MA) Manager 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Fort Wayne, IN. Join the Payload and Ground Systems (PGS) Division and work with our team of experts ensuring the development and delivery of systems that keep our world safe.

This position may provide relocation assistance. Up to 25% travel may be required.

What you'll get to do: Lead a Mission Assurance Team, supporting multiple Operating Units (OU) within the Integrated National Systems (INS) Business Unit of the PGS Division. This includes functional management of a staff of MA/QA engineers; communicating and collaborating with OU directors, Program Managers and Program support personnel; and ensuring that the Quality Management System is effective at all levels. The MA team is responsible for assuring the on-time delivery of high-quality products and maintaining compliance across the organization and its suppliers.

The MA Manager 2 will also work directly with Programs within the OU(s) providing technical leadership and individual contributor support.  The MA Manager will be a cultural and technical change agent in the transformation of the INS Business Unit’s portfolio of Programs from exclusive software development to hardware development and manufacturing.  The MA Manager reports to the INS Business Unit MA Director.

Roles and Responsibilities: Supporting the OU Directors and their portfolio of Programs to ensure program effectiveness. Participation and monitoring of the Business Development process (proposals and writing of MA basis of estimates (BoE). Interfacing with customers to build trusted relationships and work through challenges. Directing and coordinating the application of mission assurance functional disciplines, including Quality Control, Quality Engineering, Supplier Quality, Quality Programs and Quality Systems per AS9100 standards and the PGS Division quality management plan. Functionally managing a team of mission assurance personnel across multiple Programs, including training, professional development, staffing, and performance evaluation. Establishing and maintaining a budget for your MA staff to include budget for various certifications and training required. Overseeing the proper implementation and execution of Continuous Improvement on assigned programs. Creating effective partnership across all the client leadership teams: Engineering, Manufacturing, Supply Chain as well as the Profit and Loss Organizations. Set the strategy to meet customer’s expectations and regularly communicating program status.  Assuring compliance in quality, risk management, safety, process improvement, subcontractor performance, and supplier quality in accordance with contractual requirements. Communicate and collaborate with various Site Leadership personnel of support the Fort Wayne site delivery of high-quality integrated software/hardware systems at the speed of need. This position requires a dedication to ethics and integrity, and the capability to innovate while challenging traditional ways of doing business.  Excellent verbal and written interpersonal skills are required.
