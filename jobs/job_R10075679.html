Northrop Grumman Space Systems is searching for experienced Principal/ Sr. Principal Engineer Electrical Nuclear Hardening & Survivability to join Launch Vehicles business unit in Chandler, AZ.

This requires the candidate to establish a thorough knowledge of the operation of the circuit and its use in the system application, using that insight to assure testing is conducted in a manner consistent with its use in flight.

- Job Knowledge/Technical Skills- Experience with circuit and/or system level design or test required
