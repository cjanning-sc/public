Cyber Systems Engineer/Principal Cyber Systems Engineer - Secret Clearance Required
R10065280
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Space Systems is currently seeking a Cyber Systems Engineer/Principal Cyber Systems Engineer as part of the Systems Engineering Integration & Test (SEIT) team in our Chandler, AZ office.

Role Description & Responsibilities:

  • The candidate will serve as a System Security Engineer for a Program requiring the candidate to function in a dynamic multi-disciplinary environment.
  • The candidate will perform system security engineering activities supporting all phases of system development
  • The candidate will define system architectures that are resistant to tamper and cyber-attacks
  • The candidate will support identifying critical program information (CPI) and writing of Anti-Tamper Plans
  • The candidate will support Anti-Tamper/Cybersecurity Integration & Test and executing Verification & Validation requirements
  • The candidate will generate and document system level and security subsystem level requirements, perform security system performance assessments and participate in system and subsystem level verification/data review activities.
  • The candidate is expected to regularly interface with program management, engineering staff and external customers.
  • The candidate should be willing to occasionally work during odd hours (nights/weekends) and occasionally longer than normal (>40 hrs/wk) to meet deadlines.
  • The candidate should possess a strong resolve to drive tasks to completion autonomously.
  • The candidate will implement and manage system requirements in the Requirements Management databases (DOORS).
  • The candidate will provide data for and assist in risk management (identifies potential risks) and trade study processes.
  • The candidate will support system security design, data analyses, interface verification, testing, and failure/anomaly resolution.
  • The candidate will support the testing and/or evaluation/validation of one or more components of a system or subsystem analytical model or actual system implementation
  • The candidate will prepare and present materials for program major milestone meetings.

This position may be offered at the Cyber Systems Engineer Level 2 or Principal Cyber Systems Engineer Level 3.

Basic Qualifications: 

If offered at the Cyber Systems Engineer Level 2 requires: 2 Years with Bachelors in Science; 0 Years with Masters; 0 Years with PhD. 

If offered at the Principal Cyber Systems Engineer Level 3 requires: 5 Years with Bachelors in Science; 3 Years with Masters; 0 Years with PhD.

  • Candidate must have a DoD Secret level security clearance which generally requires US Citizenship as a prerequisite.
  • Ability to travel is required (<10%)
  • Currently possess or ability to obtain the DoD 8570-M Certifications (Security+, CISSP, etc.)
  • Experience ensuring system security needs are established and maintained for operations development, security requirements definition, security risk assessment, systems analysis, systems design, security test and evaluation, certification and accreditation.
  • General knowledge of DoD based Platforms and Embedded Systems, including but not limited to: RMF, Cryptography and Systems Engineering design and implementation
  • Experience writing Cybersecurity related plans, procedures, and artifacts
  • Ability to work cross-functionally with program teams
  • Ability to take requirements and turn them into diagrams, architectures, or data flows
  • Possess strong technical problem solving, written and verbal communication skills

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Experience ensuring system security needs are established and maintained for real-time embedded mission critical systems design and development, including security requirements definition and allocation, security risk assessment, systems security analysis, and security test, evaluation, certification and accreditation.
  • Prior cybersecurity/systems security engineering experience working with DoD customers (e.g., Air Force, MDA, etc)
  • Experience with external customer interfacing (meetings, presentation, negotiation) is desired.
  • Experience with systems engineering tools (e.g., DOORS and Cameo) is desired.
