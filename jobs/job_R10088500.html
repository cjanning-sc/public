Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking a Senior Pipe Fitter to join our team. This position is located in Linthicum, MD.

Plan, install, modify, repair, and maintain all facility plumbing and piping systems, associated components and equipment. Perform various brazing and/or welding operations on all types of metals to fulfill the duties and responsibilities under this job classification.

1. Obtain all necessary information to perform job assignment from management, engineering, suppliers, or equipment manufacturers and report any discrepancies to management or engineering. Plan and layout work to be performed, check and take measurements, determine material requirements, and make calculations to suit conditions in the field.
2.Install and maintain size and type of piping required to provide services under different temperature and operating conditions which may involve numerous connections and control devices. Install, maintain, modify, and repair such items as steam, air, water, glycol, hot water, chilled water, plumbing in lavatories, kitchens, break areas, labs, sewer lines, tanks, machine and spray booth lines, and pumps.
3.Perform the following operations as needed; measure, cut, bend, thread, hang and insulate pipe; make threaded, flanged, welded, glued, soldered, or similar pipe joining methods; dismantle and replace equipment; fabricate brackets and hangers, cut and drill wall, floor, and ceiling openings; flame cut and burn items; erect scaffolding; excavate and back fill as needed.
4.Perform operations such as rigging and securing equipment, disconnect and reconnect existing electrical pumps, solenoids, etc. as necessary to perform required operations.
5.Perform various welding operations on all types of metals and plastics to fulfill the responsibilities of this job classification.
6.Follow all Northrop Grumman safety rules and regulations, including but not limited to; lock out tag out procedures, confined space entry, wearing steel toe shoes, and other safe practices and personal protective equipment to ensure a safe working environment for all employees.
7.Utilize the SAP system to maintain records on equipment preventative maintenance, repairs and time entry.
8.Assist higher classified maintenance personnel and perform other miscellaneous duties which may fall within the scope of this position.
9.Work along with contractors or vendors to accomplish the goals set forth by management.
10. Maintain equipment and work areas in a clean and orderly condition. Remove all surplus materials and trash at the completion of the job.
11. Must possess and maintain Journeyman Plumbing License.

This is an IBEW Union Represented position.

EDUCATION REQUIREMENT:

High School graduate or recognized equivalent.


EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENT:

Must possess Journeyman Plumbing License. Must pass a formal, structured interview.
