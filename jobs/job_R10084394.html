The Networked Information Solutions Division (NISD) is seeking a Business Development Infrastructure Manager 2 or 3 (depending on experience) to join the team in the Growth & Mission Solutions (G&MS) organization.  This individual will drive successful execution for the G&MS team, ensuring that digital tools are successfully leveraged to facilitate customer engagement and capture activities along with relevant reporting and analyses.

This role will report to the NISD VP of Growth and Mission Solutions. Job location is flexible, with preference for San Diego, Baltimore, Annapolis Junction, or Northern Virginia.

Job Description:

The Business Development Infrastructure Manager position requires a strong leader with the ability to drive execution at the tactical level. Responsibilities of the position will include but are not limited to:

  • Manage analytical team that builds the division Tableau portfolio dashboard, integrating data from multiple sources to provide quantitative insights that shape decision making and business outcomes
  • Perform data collection, analysis, planning, and forecasting to aid in leadership, financial and organizational decision making.
  • Develop analytical tools to track metrics in support of defined objectives
  • Manage the forecasting and reporting of business development operational goals and activities
  • Act as the G&MS Salesforce Administrator, managing accounts, leading training, defining standards and processes, and supporting data requests
  • Support execution of data analysis projects initiated by G&MS leadership by working with cross-functional teams and business leaders throughout the organization
  • Prepare leadership for meetings and events with data collection, analysis, and presentation materials as needed
