The GSC Program Manager 3 will lead a program or a function within the larger organizational unit, setting and operating to objectives and providing guidance to subordinates based on goals, objectives and company policy.

Responsible and accountable for the strategy, planning, and execution of all material and services content for a Program or collection of Programs.
