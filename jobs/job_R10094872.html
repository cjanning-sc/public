ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Field or Principal Field Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals within our Vehicle Engineering organization. This role is located in Edwards Air Force Base, CA.

 

In this role, the selected candidate will be responsible for assessing, coordinating, and dispositioning non-conformances, as they occur in a test environment. The selected candidate will author detailed engineering instructions by collaborating with a multi-disciplinary team of engineers ensuring the functional integrity of the product. The candidate must be willing to support a dynamic work schedule, willing to travel to potentially remote locations, and frequently respond to pressing needs on short notice. The candidate must be equally comfortable briefing a government customer, members of program leadership, and working closely with engineers and maintenance personnel.  Excellent verbal and non-verbal communication skills are essential in this role.

Key Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with a multi-disciplinary team of engineers to determine effective technical solutions that are innovative, practical, and consistent with organizational objectives.
  • Troubleshoot technical problems to determine solutions in accordance with product and customer specification.
  • Assess, coordinate, and disposition non-conformance as they arise in the test environment.

We offer flexible work arrangements, 4 day - 10 hours/day schedule (Monday - Thursday) on day shift, phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly team environment. We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal-oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s of Science degree in a STEM discipline with 2 - 9 years of directly related professional/military experience (4 additional years of relevant professional experience may be considered in lieu of a Bachelor's degree) (Masters degree may count towards an additional 2 years of experience)
  • Experience with the conceptual operation, and troubleshooting of, typical aircraft subsystems
  • Experience briefing leadership on team progress and goals
  • Must have an active DoD Secret clearance (with a background investigation completed within the last 6 years or currently enrolled into Continuous Evaluation)
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to the commencement of employment.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced degree in Mechanical Engineering or Aerospace Engineering
  • Experience with CATIA V5, NX, Visualization Pro, TeamCenter, MES, or other CAD programs
  • Experience working in an aircraft manufacturing facility
  • Experience interfacing with a military customer
  • Experience with aircraft subsystem design
  • Previous MRB/LE Authority for Engineering
