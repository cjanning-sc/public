The Networked Information Solutions Division (NISD) is seeking a Business Development Operations Manager 2/3 to join the team in the Growth & Mission Solutions (G&MS) organization.  This individual will drive successful execution for the G&MS team, ensuring that organizational vision is translated into operational reality and business outcomes.

This role will report to the NISD VP of Growth and Mission Solutions. Job location is flexible, with preference for Cleveland, Springfield, Peoria, or Northern Iowa.

Job Description:

The Operations Manager position requires a strong leader with the ability to operate at both strategic and tactical levels. Responsibilities of the position will include but are not limited to:

  • Manage execution across the G&MS team including calendar integration, task tracking, customer engagement coordination, and internal deliverables
  • Drive execution of strategic initiatives and projects initiated by G&MS leadership by working with cross-functional teams and business leaders throughout the organization
  • Serve as the focal point for communication, planning and collaboration for internal G&MS activities
  • Ensure strong collaboration across the entire division through effective coordination inside G&MS and with other division teams
  • Manage the reporting of business development goals and activities
  • Facilitate effective staff meetings with focused agendas, calendar reviews, task tracking, and appropriate analytics
  • Prepare leadership for meetings and events with agenda review, data collection, talking points, presentation materials, and logistical support 

Basic Qualifications for Level 2:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 9+ years of work experience or Master’s Degree 7+ years of work experience
  • Skilled in project management, analysis, communication, and presentation
  • Effective problem solver
  • Strong Microsoft Office skills
  • Experienced with dashboarding software such as Tableau
  • Ability to obtain and maintain Secret Level Clearance.
  • Willingness to travel up to 5%, depending on location

Basic Qualifications for Level 3:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 12+ years of work experience or Master’s Degree 10+ years of work experience
  • Skilled in project management, analysis, communication, and presentation
  • Effective problem solver
  • Strong Microsoft Office skills
  • Experienced with dashboarding software such as Tableau
  • Ability to obtain and maintain Secret Level Clearance.
  • Willingness to travel up to 5%, depending on location

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with digital collaboration tools such as Confluence and Jira
  • Experience with Salesforce
  • Active Secret Clearance
