Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Sr Principal / Principal Manufacturing Engineer. This position will be located in Roy, UT and will support the GBSD Ground Base Strategic Deterrent program. Learn more about the GBSD program here. 

 

This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package. 

 

What You’ll Get To Do: 

This position requires a highly motivated self-starter with preferred knowledge in the fabrication, assembly, system integration, and testing of missile hardware. The candidate will be involved in assisting other NG sectors and suppliers with developing a Manufacturing Plan, conducting Manufacturing Readiness Assessments (MRA), and executing the startup of a new program. The candidate will need to demonstrate exceptional organizational and interpersonal skills, have the ability task orient and communicate effectively with varying levels of Customer, NGC, and Supplier employees and leadership.


Primary responsibilities will include providing technical and administrative leadership and guidance to the program Integrated Product Teams (IPT) and Suppliers for developing the manufacturing planning and successful preparation and execution of MRA’s. This will include establishing a program project plan and schedule that is aligned with the IPT Integrated Master Schedule (IMS). Provide advanced technological ideas and Subject Matter Expertise (SME) to resolve technical questions and ensure compliance and execution to the Manufacturing Readiness Level (MRL) Deskbook, and Engineering and Manufacturing Development (EMD) Statement of Work (SOW) requirements. In addition, responsibilities will include providing technical input to the Manufacturing Plan.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Represents the organization as a technical contact, interacts with senior personnel on important technical matters, and helps with coordination between organization

  • Experience implementing Lean practices in a production and/or business environment

  • Acts as a leader and understands that erroneous decisions on their part will result in probable delay and failure of organizational objectives

  • Develops processes by studying requirements; researching, designing, modifying, and testing manufacturing methods and equipment

  • Collaborates with Design Engineering on design and manufacturability issues

  • Coordinates with the integration team on day-to-day activities. Resolves complex process issues.

  • Research and evaluate commercially available tools and equipment to support activities.

  • Design/procure specialized tooling, visual aid and fixtures.

  • Investigates root cause of discrepancies and implements corrective actions.

  • Troubleshoots existing processes to improve safety, quality, cost and efficiency.

  • Develops and maintains detailed work instructions for assigned products/processes

  • Educates/trains operators on new and existing processes.
