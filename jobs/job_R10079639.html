Job Description and Qualifications

Job Description:

We are seeking a Manager 2 to join us in the pursuit of top-tier financial performance.  The position will be based in Columbus, CA and will report to the Sector Director of Financial Planning & Analysis. Typical responsibilities include the following:

• Manage a small team of analysts and coordinate all aspects of the sector’s capital asset investments.

• Execute Monthly/Quarterly Reporting and Planning processes for capital investments.
• Lead development of capital investment plans and budgets to support the sector’s Long Range Strategic Plans and Annual Operating Plans.
• Manage capital investment forecast updates and provide performance updates.
• Execute and support Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) compliance and documentation.
• Work with the finance and business management teams to develop strategies to improve financial performance.
• Internal and external audit support
• Support the development of presentations for, and brief to senior leadership
• Interface with geographically disbursed business, program, and sector personnel.
• Support initiatives and ad hoc requests from Division, Sector, and Corporate leadership.

Job Qualifications:

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Economics, Finance, Accounting, Audit, or related discipline with a minimum of 8 years applicable experience, or Master’s degree and 6 years applicable experience.
  • Able to prepare and analyze data and figures
  • Ability to comprehend and analyze complex problems and develop solutions
  • Experience conducting oral presentations to executive leadership
  • Experience with SAP ERP system
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word and PowerPoint)
  • Ability to obtain a DOD Secret Security Clearance


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Full working knowledge of Capital Asset planning and budgeting processes and/or Financial Planning and Reporting including supporting monthly actuals and forecasts, Annual Operating Plans, and Long-Range Strategic Plans
  • Master's degree in Business Admin, Accounting, or related discipline
  • CPA License
