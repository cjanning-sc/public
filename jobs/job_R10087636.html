The Payload and Ground Systems organization within the ACME Engineering Space Systems pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter. Come be a part of our mission!

We have an opening for a Senior Principal Systems Engineer (level 4) to join our qualified, diverse team. We are seeking a team member who wants to solve interesting, complex problems to help protect our nation. We are a trusted mission payload and ground system provider with extensive experience providing end-to-end mission solutions from concept through operations and support. The team you will support is located at our Space Park campus in Columbus, OH.

What you’ll get to do?

As a Senior Principal Systems Engineer, you will directly support the Ground SEIT IPT lead in leading the systems engineering and integration and test team in the campaign to refine requirements in support of the verification campaign, coordinate with customers to ensure alignment to contractually required product deliveries and documentation, influence the program’s roadmap planning, and provide systems engineering guidance to the teams.

Some key tasks:

  • Provide technical expertise to the Program Management and Engineer staff

  • Work with product owners to ensure product roadmaps align with needed integration schedule and pipeline build plans

  • Support customer briefings on status of development, verification, and other technical subjects requiring closure.

  • Capability Lead to pull together the entire set of artifacts for a capability (requirements, OpsCon, system design, interfaces)

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in STEM with 9 years of relevant experience, or a Master's degree in STEM with 7 years of relevant experience

  • Working knowledge of systems engineering principles and processes

  • Working knowledge of satellite ground stations

  • Active DoD Top Secret/SCI Clearance with the ability to obtain Special Program Access

  • Ability to travel up to 10%

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Agile development

  • Familiarity with Model-Based Systems Engineering languages (SysML/UML), Cameo and concepts

  • Direct experience using the DOORS Requirements Management Database

  • Direct experience providing technical leadership, direction and mentoring to other technical employees to achieve practical end results

  • Experience with UI/UX

  • Direct experience with formally presenting technical solutions to senior leadership.
