Payload and Ground Systems Division (PGSD) is seeking a motivated Systems Engineering, Integration & Test (SEIT) Lead that will assist with leading the Ground Integrated Product Team (IPT) for the Next Generation OPIR Polar (NGP) program. The position will be located in Columbus, OH, and report on-site daily.

The NGP Ground SEIT Lead will lead a team of twenty (20) Systems Engineers embedded in various cross-functional Agile scrum teams through Preliminary Design Review (PDR) and on to Critical Design Review (CDR).  The Ground SEIT lead is part of the larger NGP Ground program leadership team and supports leading and managing a team responsible for Systems Engineering, Ground Software and NGP unique ground hardware. The SEIT Lead will interface daily with other internal and external organizations, including customers and their technical support in a complex multi-party development Customer Ground Enterprise. The SEIT Lead is responsible for identifying staffing demands, supporting the interview, and selection process. Additional responsibilities include planning, risk management, and project performance addressing cost, schedule, and technical quality for the program. The SEIT Lead will coordinate the preparation of cost estimates, business plans, work statements and specifications, Contracted Deliverable Requirement Line Items (CDRLs), design reviews PDR and CDR, and technical exchanges with the customer.

Additional responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:

  • Candidate is required to have experience in software development deliverables and processes
  • Provides management and technical direction to Ground Systems Engineers
  • Champions a scaled Agile framework and serves as a Product Manager of SE work products, supporting the individual scrum team POs
  • Provide leadership and review of proposal efforts (e.g., BPEs, CROMs, ECPs)
  • Cost account management CAM of SE organization, including forecast monitoring, variance explanations, and cost/schedule lien evaluation and preparation
  • Management of Risk and Opportunity processes
  • Management of verification activities and coordination with other Project, third-party stakeholders and the customer
  • Oversight of GE SE activities, including tracking product deliveries and milestone (PDR, CDR) preparation
  • Review of SE generated design reviews, test reviews, Engineering Summaries, I&T milestone chart packages and related documentation
  • Provide SE review disposition/impact assessment of change requests
  • Coordination with SEIT IPT leads, other GE Project and Program management, and third-party stakeholders
