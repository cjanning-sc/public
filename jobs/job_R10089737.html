Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal Configuration Analyst/ Sr. Principal Configuration Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Melbourne, Florida.

This position is responsible for the planning and coordinating preparation of project documentation, such as engineering drawings, production specifications and schedules, and contract modifications, to ensure customer contract requirements are met. Reviews contract to determine documentation required for each phase of project, applying knowledge of engineering and manufacturing processes. Analyzes proposed changes of product design to determine effect on overall product and system. Coordinates modification records for management control. Establishes change orders and prepares for change authorization and documentation by company and subcontractor. Reviews and analyzes released engineering change data and coordinates changes with engineering, quality, support, manufacturing, and engineering data control activities. Support program configuration and data management requirements.

Basic Qualifications:

• Principal Configuration Analyst: Master’s degree and 4 years of professional work experience in DoD configuration and/or data management; a Bachelor’s degree and 6 years of professional work experience as a configuration analyst, an Associate's degree with 8 years, OR 10 years of professional work experience in a DoD configuration and/or data management role may be considered in lieu of degree requirement

•  Sr. Principal Configuration Analyst: Masters with 8 years of professional work experience in DoD configuration and/or data management, a Bachelor's Degree and 10 years, an Associate's degree with 12 years, OR a total of 14 years of professional work experience in a DoD configuration and/or data management role may be considered in lieu of degree requirement

• Must have the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance, must have Interim Secret Clearance to start

• Understanding of configuration management principles / best practices to include management of contract and supplier data, Microsoft SharePoint 2013 designer and owner experience

Preferred Qualifications:

• Excellent organizational skills to include the ability to multi-task and meet deadlines

• Excellent communication, interpersonal skills, and the ability to interface with all levels of employees and management

• SharePoint experience and familiar with executing Configuration and Data Management based on MIL-HDBK-61 and/or EIA-649 standards
