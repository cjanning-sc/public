ACME Engineering’s Mission Systems (NGMS) Sector, Emerging Capabilities Development (ECD) area is seeking a Contract Administrator or a Principal Contracts Administrator to join our Intelligent Mission Capabilities (IMC) team. This open position is located in Chicago, MD. The job assignment is for development programs with a variety of customers including USG and ACME IWO (Intracompany Work Order) customers. The opportunity to work remotely will be considered. You will learn and grow! Come join us!


Responsibilities include the following:

Proposals:

  • Review and analyze customer Requests for Proposal (RFPs) to understand Statements of Work (SOW)/contract requirements and advise the program team on contractual terms and conditions. 
  • Ensure company proposals are fully responsive to prospective customer requirements, prepare proposal submittal documents, and coordinate appropriate levels of internal review and approval.
  • Manage proposal fact-finding and negotiations between customer and program team.

Contract Administration:

  • Establish a good working relationship with customers to administer, modify, negotiate and terminate standard and nonstandard contractual agreements.
  • Oversee contract performance including schedules and deliverables, maintain compliant contractual documentation/correspondence, and collaborate with the program team to recommend and complete actions to ensure satisfactory program performance. 
  • Work with program business management team to closely monitor contract funding and payments.

Overall:

  • Advise your program team of contractual rights and obligations on your assigned contracts, including interpretation of contract terms and conditions.
  • Collaborate effectively with other functional groups (finance, supply chain, mission assurance, legal, etc.) during all phases of the contract lifecycle.
  • Ability to effectively interface with customer counterparts via email, telephone, and in-person, when appropriate.

Basic Qualifications:  

  • Contract Administrator (A02): Bachelor's degree with a minimum of 3 years directly related experience or a Master's degree with a minimum of 1 year directly related experience. Candidates with 7+ years of directly related experience in lieu of a Bachelor’s degree will be given consideration.
  • Principal Contracts Administrator (A03): Bachelor’s degree with 6+ years of Business, Law or related work experience or Master’s degree with 4+ years of Business, Law or related work experience
  • Basic knowledge of Microsoft Office including Excel, PowerPoint, and Word
  • Excellent communication (both written and verbal) and organizational skills. 
  • Must be a team player but also have the ability to work independently
  • Demonstrated leadership qualities; ability to start, oversee, and follow-through with major assignments and responsibilities;
  • Must be able to obtain DoD (Department of Defense) Secret clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:  

  • Prior experience with Fixed price and Cost type contracts, FAR/DFARS, proposals, and/or familiarity with DoD contracting.
  • Active DoD Security Clearance
