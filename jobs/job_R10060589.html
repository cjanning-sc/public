Duties and Responsibilities:

This position reports to the GSC Integration and Affordability Strike Division Senior Manager for the Aeronautics Systems Sector. The Manager will serve as the Affordability and Operations focal point for the GSC Program Manager, IPT leadership. The candidate will be responsible for developing and executing GSC strategies to support the program, with focus on achieving affordability, operational efficiency, safety and first time quality targets. Candidate Responsible for activities and knowledge within multiple disciplines across the Supply Chain organization. Exhibits end-to-end supply chain competency.
