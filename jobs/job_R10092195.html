Are you ready to take your career to the next level by challenging your knowledge and expertise of Digital Verification?

 

The Digital Products Engineering Team is looking to grow and expand the team with skilled digital verification engineers seeking to push the boundaries of board/unit design and development.

 

If your passion and career lies within Verification or Emulation for digital communications, signal processing, network switching/routing, and you have experience with digital hardware for spacecraft applications apply now! We currently have opportunities performing design verification of space flight hardware.

 

As part of this team, you will have the opportunity to leverage your knowledge and expertise.

•To develop verification plans

•Architect and implement verification environments of prototypes to class A space hardware

•Leverage UVM throughout the verification process

•Generate verification and test plans for digital board/units

•Generation of verification artifacts necessary to sell off space hardware

•Support/Lead unit verification efforts, test-set requirements development, environmental testing definition.

•Resolves or oversees resolution of off-nominal test data

•Support/Lead subsystem level Integration & test

We value and promote growth and development of our teams by providing opportunities to support other areas of interest to our employees.

If you are interested in learning more, apply for your chance to be considered for one of our many unit verification engineering openings within the digital products team.

•Minimum Bachelor’s/Master's degree in electrical/electronic engineering, computer engineering or related digital electronics experience in lieu of the electrical/electronic engineering degree

•Must have 2 years of professional working experience in Verification and/or Emulation

•The ability to obtain and maintain a security clearance and/or program access

 

Preferred Qualifications:

•Experience with Board/unit verification.

•Experience in FPGA/Software design and development

•Experience in Universal Verification Methodology (UVM)/SystemVerilog.

•Experience performing formal verification of digital space flight hardware.

•Experience with emulation efforts.

•Knowledgeable of verification environment (architecture definition, development and maintenance.)
