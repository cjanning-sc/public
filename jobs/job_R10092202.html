ACME Engineering Space Systems sector, located in Dayton, Ohio, is seeking a motivated candidate to join the Electronics and Payloads Directorate. The selected candidate will become an ASIC/FPGA Design and Verification Engineer at the Principal Level. The qualified applicant will work on digital designs for cutting edge space applications.

Primary responsibilities may include, but not limited to:

  • Development of ASIC/FPGA architecture based on requirements

  • Development of RTL Design for digital systems

  • Perform simulation and emulation (lab integration), synthesis, timing analysis, logic equivalency check, etc

  • Development of verification plans and verification test benches using VHDL or System Verilog

  • Performing trace requirements as well as performing review and audits on the simulation test procedures and test environment

Basic Qualifications: 
- STEM Bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution with a minimum of 5 years of experience; OR STEM Master’s degree with minimum 3 years of experience; OR STEM PhD. degree

- 3 years of experience in digital logic design or verification

- Must have the ability to obtain and maintain an active Government clearance

Preferred Qualifications: 
- Hands-on lab experience with design and integration of digital hardware is highly desired

- Experience with RTL design using Xilinx Vivado or Microchip Libero

- Experience in SystemVerilog/UVM methodology for verification

- Active DoD clearance
