Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for an Engineer Systems Architect / Principal Engineer Systems Architect to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals.  This position will be located in Redondo Beach, CA.

The Principal Engineer Systems Architect or the Sr. Principal Engineer Systems Architect will be responsible for developing system architectures, defining key capabilities and performance requirements, decomposing functionality, and defining interfaces in a Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) environment.  This individual will collaborate with cross-functional engineering teams to establish architecture content and develop solutions that achieve customer intent.  A successful candidate will demonstrate good communication skills, systems thinking, and the ability to capture technical design in MBSE architecture products.

This role can be filled at the Principal or Sr. Principal level based on the qualifications below.

Basic Qualifications for the Principal Engineer Level:

  • Bachelor of Science degree from an accredited university with 5 years of engineering related experience; OR a Master's degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics (STEM) discipline with 3 years of experience.

Basic Qualifications for the Sr. Principal Engineer Level:

  • Sr. Principal Engineer Systems Architect requires 9 years engineering related experience with a Bachelor of Science degree, OR 7 years engineering related experience with a Master’s degree in a STEM discipline

Basic Qualifications for either level:

  • Ability to analyze architectures and develop MBSE views in DoDAF/UPDM or SysML
  • Ability to work in a collaborative, geographically dispersed team environment
  • Should have previous Systems Engineering or MBSE experience
  • Should be familiar with DoDAF/UPDM, SysML, or UAF
  • Active DoD Secret security clearance or DoE equivalent with investigation in the last 6 years and ability to obtain a special access program clearance.


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience developing MBSE artifacts such as behavior diagrams (activity, sequence, or state machine) and structure diagrams (block definition and internal block diagrams) in SysML, DoDAF/UPDM, or UAF
  • Experience with parametric diagrams and linkage between MBSE tools and Analytical models such as Matlab.
  • Experience using an MBSE tool, such as Cameo Enterprise Architecture or MagicDraw, IBM Rhapsody, PTC Integrity or Windchill Modeler, or Sparx Enterprise Architect
  • Experience with Systems Engineering activities, such as functional analysis, requirements development and decomposition, interface definition, or verification
  • Experience with DOORs or other requirements management tool and integration with MBSE software
  • Experience with military aircraft architectures and the military aerospace design environment
  • Interfacing and presenting to customers.
