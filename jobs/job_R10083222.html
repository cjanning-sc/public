The Payload and Ground Systems organization within the Northrop Grumman Space Systems pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter. Come be a part of our mission.

The Space Components and Strategic Businesses Unit is seeking a Process Improvement Analyst (Level 3/4) to join our Engineering team. This position will be based in San Diego, CA, but may be located at other major site locations in Devens, MA, Oxnard, CA, or Goleta, CA.

Overview

The Principal Process Improvement Analyst defines, designs and implements continuous improvement programs to ensure programs are integrated vertically and horizontally across the organization. This role will be responsible for defining improvement projects aligned with business strategies as well as analyzing improvement programs and potential results. Supporting engineering operations, this individual facilitates and coaches improvement teams, establishes and maintains performance metrics to measure program success, and installs process excellence tools for program governance, technical product creation, project reviews, communications, and training.

The ideal candidate will demonstrate proficiency in the following areas:

  • Ability to lead cross functional teams in Kaizen events and apply LEAN / Six-Sigma principles.
  • Must have an understanding of LEAN principles, concepts, practices, and standards and their practical application.
  • Ability to develop new techniques and applications based on professional principles and theories.
  • Ability to carefully analyze complex problems and develop innovative solutions.
  • Broad knowledge of industry practices in continuous improvement.
  • Ensures solutions are consistent with organization objectives.
  • Must be able to work independently and have strong organizational skills.
  • Effectively communicate and clearly present technical approaches and findings.

 

This position may be filled as a Principal Process Improvement Analyst​ (Level 3) or a Sr. Principal Process Improvement Analyst​ (Level 3) depending upon candidate education, experience and qualifications.

 

Basic Qualifications for a Level 3:

  • Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited university and 6+ years of engineering process development and/or project management experience OR Master’s Degree with 4+ years experience
  • Training and/or certification in Lean/Six Sigma
  • Minimum 2 years leading Lean Kaizen events
  • Skilled in Microsoft Office Programs (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, Project)
  • Ability to obtain DoD Secret clearance

 

Basic Qualifications for a Level 4:

  • Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited university and 10+ years of engineering process development and/or project management experience OR Master’s Degree with 8+ years experience
  • Training and/or certification in Lean/Six Sigma
  • Minimum 4 years leading Lean Kaizen events
  • Skilled in Microsoft Office Programs (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, Project)
  • Ability to obtain DoD Secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Industrial Engineering degree with focused studies in Lean / Six Sigma.
  • Comfort and experience presenting to a variety of audiences, including senior leadership as well as other internal and external stakeholders.
  • Ability to effectively work with Senior Management Team (SMT).
  • Experience with, and knowledge of hardware design and manufacturing processes for space & aerospace applications
  • Experience with PLM / PDM software
