The selected individual is responsible for cost and schedule performance and execution for specific hardware package within the Module Assembly and Test Center (MA&T).  MA&T is a world class facility capable of assembling and testing a wide array of microelectronic modules from DC to W-Band for space, air and ground applications.  The MA&T department handles all operations from kitting through final screens.  Processes include: component attach using conductive/non-conductive epoxy, wire and ribbon bonding, eutectic mount, laser sealing, seam sealing, RF & DC Tune & Test, and MIL-STD-883 screens.

The selected individual will be the Point of contact for manufacturing through entire product life cycle on that package; will participate in proposal, IDRs, fab, assembly, test, through delivery; will have demonstrated ability to lead and provide direction to a technical cross functional team and exceptional communication skills are required. The individual shall demonstrate leadership qualities and ability to motivate and engage cross discipline teams to execute efficiently.  Position is the Cost Account Manager for typically over $15M total hardware package. Is accountable for cost and schedule variance explanations and EV methodology.  Candidate has advanced knowledge with Module Production procedures and manufacturing processes.  Individual may be required to lead Tiger Teams and lead various proposal efforts within organization.  Candidate will set up a program from beginning to end and plan and coordinate a schedule with all of the required organizations including material.  Preferred Candidate will have production experience.
