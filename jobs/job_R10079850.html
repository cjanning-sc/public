As an integral part of our Mission Modeling, Simulation & Analysis team in Mission Systems Engineering you will be responsible for development, implementing, and understanding modeling and simulation needs as a function of the systems engineering and mission engineering processes across the air, space, land, sea, and cyberspace domains.

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • System concept development

  • Elicitation and specification of functional requirements, and decomposition and traceability of those requirements

  • Mentor and direct junior staff efforts to meet program deadlines

  • Performance analysis and specification

  • Architecture Trade Studies

  • Synthesize and extend architectures

  • Allocate functionality to components

  • Definition of interfaces, and Interface Control Documents (ICDs)

  • Detailed Design Analysis; May also involve test design

  • Use modern model-based systems engineering approaches that make extensive use of Systems Modeling Language (SysML) skills; integrate M&S analysis models with descriptive models

  • Determine task schedules and levels of effort needed to meet program goals

  • Communicate technical capabilities and analyses with internal stakeholders and external customers

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree with 9 years of experience, a Master’s degree with 7 years of experience or a PhD with 4 years of experience in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Applied Physics, Applied Mathematics, or related technical fields.

  • U.S Citizenship with an active DoD Secret Clearance

  • Academic or on the job demonstration of Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) and relative methodologies

  • Working knowledge and frequency use with Cameo Enterprise Architecture / Magic Draw 

  • Capable of mentoring technical professionals and/or advising programs on the use of Model-based Systems Engineering (MBSE)

  • General understanding of Requirements elicitation and generation.

  • General understanding of Architecture, Interface Definition, and Design.


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced degrees in engineering, science, software, or business administration

  • Active DoD Secret, Top Secret Clearance with SCI

  • Capable of standing up a Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) environment

  • Domain knowledge in RF sensors

  • Experience with DOORS and requirements/architecture linking

  • Experience with physics-based model or simulation development

  • Experience with Multidisciplinary Design Analysis Optimization (MDAO)

  • Experience working in an agile development environment

  • Experience with version control using e.g. Teamwork Cloud, SVN, GitHub
