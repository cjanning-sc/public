This is a Mechanical Engineering position supporting propulsion subsystem design, manufacture and test. This position requires the candidate to work independently with minimal guidance from senior lead engineers to perform general task completions, perform fluid flow analysis, sizing exercises, support trade studies, performance specification review or authoring, troubleshooting technical issues and supporting test operations.

Essential Functions:

  • Researches, plans, designs and develops mechanical products and systems such as instruments, controls, robots, engines, machines and mechanical, thermal hydraulic or heat transfer systems for production, transmission, measurement, and use of energy.
  • Applies research to the planning, design, development, and testing of mechanical and/or electromechanical systems, instruments, controls, engines, and/or machines.

This requisition may be filled at higher grade based on qualifications listed below.

Basic Qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either a Propulsion Mechanical Engineer 2 or Principal Mechanical Engineer 3.

Basic Qualifications for Propulsion Mechanical Engineer 2:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in STEM - Science, Technology, Engineering, Math or related area (Degrees in Aerospace or Mechanical Engineering highly preferred) and 2+ years of experience or Master’s Degree and 0+ years of experience.
  • Must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain government clearance.
  • Relevant course work and experience in fluid mechanics, compressible flow and/or thermodynamics of propulsion systems.
  • Experience with industry standard fluid/ thermal and/or mechanical analysis software, design standards and/or documentation related to propulsion system or subsystem design.
  • Experience with MATLAB or similar engineering tools.
  • Domestic travel may be required.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Propulsion Mechanical Engineer 3:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in STEM - Science, Technology, Engineering, Math or related area (Degrees in Aerospace or Mechanical Engineering highly preferred) and 5+ years of experience or Master’s Degree and 3+ years of experience; or a PhD.
  • Must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain government clearance.
  • Relevant course work and experience in fluid mechanics, compressible flow and/or thermodynamics of propulsion systems.
  • Experience with industry standard fluid/ thermal and/or mechanical analysis software, design standards and/or documentation related to propulsion system or subsystem design.
  • Experience with MATLAB or similar engineering tools.
  • Domestic travel may be required.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with designing propulsion systems or fluid systems including review of relevant analysis, manufacturing drawings, authoring test procedures or hands on pressure or hot fire, engine test support.
  • Experience in authoring performance specifications and/or statements of work required for the procurement of propulsion system fluid components, test support contracts or other related contractual technical documentation.
  • Current or active Top Secret/SCI clearance.
