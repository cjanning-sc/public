Principal Systems Engineer - Requirements & Verification (Space/Satellite) Top Secret
R10092417
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Space Systems Sector, Tactical Space Systems Division, seeks a qualified Principal Systems Engineer to support space and satellite programs. This position is located in Dulles, VA. Successful candidates for this position will work in a technical role for the National Security Systems (NSS) business area. This position offers the opportunity to work on exciting, cutting-edge programs and new mission areas.

Duties/Responsibilities:

  • Candidates should be knowledgeable of requirements flow down and management, validation and verification planning, interface definition, and expertise with various engineering tools such as DOORS and Cameo. 

  • The role will include validating the customer level requirements and pulling from multiple inputs to develop the program level requirements. 

  • The role will include support for major program reviews such as SRR, PDR, and CDR.

  • The role will include interface with clients, as well as regular interactions with internal engineering associates and program management.

 

#NOVASPACE

Basic Qualifications:

  • B.S. in STEM - Science, Technology, Engineering, Math or related area and 5 years of experience; M.S. in STEM or related area and 3 years of experience; or a PhD in STEM or related area with systems engineering experience

  • Experience with Model Based Systems Engineering for requirements and architecture management (DOORS and Cameo Systems Modeler preferred)

  • Experience with a space development program

  • Must have US Citizenship with current or active Top Secret Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Space industry experience - Space domain/space vehicles, subsystems, and components

  • Experience coordinating the efforts of diverse stakeholders.
