ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a GBSD Principal Training Systems Network and Cyber Systems Administrator​. This position will be located in Detroit, UT and will support the GBSD Ground Base Strategic Deterrent program. Learn more about the GBSD program here.

This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What You’ll Get To Do: 

  • Maintains smooth operation of multi-user computer systems, including coordination with network administrators.  

  • Interacts with users and evaluates vendor products.

  • Makes recommendations to purchase hardware and software, coordinates installation and provides backup recovery.

  • Develops and monitors policies and standards for allocation related to the use of computing resources.  

  • Ancillary duties may include setting up administrator and service accounts, maintaining system documentation, tuning system performance, installing system wide software and allocating mass storage space.

  • Develops and maintains Local Area Network (LAN) and Wide Area Network (WAN) based networks for training systems.

  • Identifies and applies cybersecurity controls, industry best practices, security control software, and scanning suites.

  • Collaborates with Cyber, Systems, and Software Engineers to ensure network requirements are met.

  • Performs network analysis, monitoring, troubleshooting, and repair

  • Performs system administration of IT assets, user accounts, network architecture.

You’ll Bring These Qualifications:

  • 6 years experience with a Bachelor's degree and/or 4 years with a Master's degree.  Four additional years of experience may be substituted in lieu of a degree.

  • Experience with Risk Management Framework (RMF) requirements and associated Systems Engineering.

  • US Citizenship with an Active Secret Clearance or higher (awarded within the past 6 years).

These Qualifications Would be Nice to Have:

  • Training systems / devices / simulation experience.

  • Military network experience.

  • Linux background and experience.

  • CSSP Incident Responder certification.

  • Network CCNA, Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) or equivalent.

  • DOD Approved 8570 Baseline Certification.

  • CISSP or equivalent certifications for an IASAE II.

  • Understanding of “as-a-Services” architecture and protocols.

  • Experience in Training Networks, Training Interfaces, Integrated Training Protocols and Security.

  • Working knowledge of Software Defined Network (SDN), network functions, and distributed networks.

  • Familiarization with Cloud and service architectures, Virtualization (NFV), and Containers.
