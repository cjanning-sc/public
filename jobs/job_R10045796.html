ACME Engineering Mission Systems Sector (NGMS) is a leading global provider of secure software-defined, hardware enabled mission systems.  ACME Engineering’s Bethpage location has served as a vital location throughout the history of the company. Opening in 1936, it served as the main headquarters for the Engineering Corporation - the central location for projects such as the Lunar Module and the F-14 Tomcat.  Our employees have made significant historical contributions and are continuing to shape the future. The selected individual will work a fully Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) approach for systems engineering and be responsible for supporting MBSE strategy and implementation over the Architecture model. 

What You Will Do:

  • Help to drive organizational change by driving Digital Transformation and Model-Based Everything (MB(X)) approaches.
  • Coordinate with integrated product teams for establishing content within the architecture.
  • Develop descriptive system models to include logical/physical/functional block diagrams at various abstraction layers to support Model-Based Engineering (MBE) and Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) efforts.
  • Collaborate with other members of the technical staff, program office, and government customers.
  • Work independently and across multi-disciplined/multi-site teams to architect, model, simulate, and analyze systems to predict/verify system performance against functional/capability requirements.
  • Demonstrate a Systems Thinking mindset.
  • Be capable of understanding a complex problem, developing an approach to solve it, and implementing that approach. 
  • Duties may extend to include one or more of: developing system specifications derived from customer requirements, data analysis, algorithm development, system issue resolution, test derivation/validation, as well as design and modeling tasks.
  • Travel will be required for meetings and program support, particularly at the start of program support.

Basic Qualifications:

Sr. Principal Engineer (9+ years of engineering experience with a bachelor’s degree, 7 years for MS degree, 4 years for PhD)

  • Experience with requirements development, systems requirements analysis and requirements management
  • Experience preparing and presenting technical content to internal and/or external customers
  • Ability to communicate effectively
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office suite (i.e., Word, PowerPoint, Excel)

  • U.S. Citizenship (cannot be dual citizen), with the ability to obtain and maintain a U.S. Secret Security clearance, and the ability to obtain approval for Special Access Program (SAP). A current active clearance is desired

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Working knowledge of Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE), tools, techniques, and concepts; e.g., Cameo model development
    • Experience in MBSE techniques including requirements management; interface control and verification; logical, physical and functional architecture; trade studies; and analysis of alternatives.
    • Understanding of System Modeling Language (SysML), Unified Modeling Language (UML), or other descriptive constructs/tools.
  • Experience with radar systems, particularly: data analysis, requirements analysis, mode design and development and/or signal and data processing.
  • Experience with techniques associated multiple target tracking (e.g. Kalman filtering, multiple hypothesis logic)
  • Extensive knowledge of Cameo Enterprise Architect or IBM Rational Rhapsody
  • Experience with DoDAF & Unified Architecture Framework (UAF) standards and methods
  • Experience with DOORS requirements management
  • Experience using tools to model large systems with multiple developers
  • Experience with Agile Development
  • Experience performing system verification and validation
  • Experience in an integration and test environment
  • Proficiency with engineering programming languages (e.g., C/C++, MATLAB, Python)
  • Experience with physics-based model or simulation development
