ACME Engineering is seeking a Principal Facilities Planner located in the Northern Iowa area and this position is a “roll up your sleeves” position responsible for supporting a full range of Facilities Support associated with a large 4-floor, 400,000 square foot building with an emphasis on protecting and supporting sensitive operations. 

Job Description:

The ideal candidate plans utilization of building space and facilities for company consistent with requirements of organizational efficiency, and available facility resources and funding.  Individual will be responsible for being the Program facilities POC and for leading the efforts of an assigned facilities planner in planning, directing, and managing all phases of equipment and machinery maintenance, facilities modification and building project upgrades from establishment of design concepts through development and implementation of final selected facilities solution(s).  Candidate will ensure project requirements are clearly defined and approved by the Program Manage prior to recommending to the customer.  Candidate will assume primary contact for facilities, and equipment and machinery maintenance, project activities and lead facility tasks for schedule and mechanical compliance with the Program Manager.  Candidate will work with the Program Manager to identify building issues and develop solutions to ensure no break to customer support.   May coordinate the work of employee(s) assigned to the project.  Assist the Program Manager establishes milestones and monitors adherence to master facility maintenance vendor schedules.  The Facilities Planner shall be responsible for supporting both internal and external customers in a dynamic and fast paced environment. 

Responsibilities include:

  • Overview and daily access the Program facilities database and accuracy track and monitor the status of submitted facility tickets

  • Provide status (either closure or update) each facility ticket each work day

  • Ensure daily facility rounds are completed with the purpose of heading off any potential issues before they are reported via the database

  • Works with the Program Manager to identify building facility issues and develop solutions, such as allocation of required resources or staffing potential project scope changes with the customer

  • Will coordinate the work of employee(s) assigned to the project

  • Will use Excel software for functional area, to assist PM compile and track facility tasks

  • Provides cleaning and other escort services when required

  • Reports directly to the Program Manager and performs job duties with minimal supervision

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 6 years of facility related experience, or a Master's Degree and 4 years of facility related experience or 10 years or more of facility or construction experience may be considered in lieu of degree

  • Must have active/current TS/SCI Clearance with Full Scope Polygraph

  • Must have experience and working knowledge of facility topics such as: HVAC, UPS and diesel generator

  • Must have in-depth electrical and mechanical knowledge, and overall maintenance experience associated with a large, several story building (electric outlets, garbage disposals, overhead lighting and ballast)

  • Must have experience and success interacting successfully with customers and building facility vendors as candidate will very often serve as facility liaison with vendors

  • Candidate will be required to perform hands-on building corrective maintenance as well as address emergency building/facility issues when they arise and must know what facilities tasks can be completed by Program Staff or by Vendor personnel

  • Ability to maintain flexibility to deal with changing priorities, deadlines, and emergent requirements and adjust work time schedule

  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Office (Word and Excel)
