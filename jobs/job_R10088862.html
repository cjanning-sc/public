Principal Program Planning and Scheduling Analyst/ Sr Principal Program Planning Scheduling Analyst
R10088862
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Defense Systems is currently seeking a Principal/Sr. Principal Program Planning Scheduling Analyst (Level 3/4) to join our Advanced Weapons group in Northridge, CA. 

Job Responsibilities:

  • Ensure major Projects and Programs Integrated Master Schedules (IMS) and plans are horizontally and vertically integrated across company functional groups including finance, engineering, material, manufacturing, and quality.
  • Monitor and measure program progress; ensure the accuracy of program planning data that is used in the execution of projects through the use of milestones, deliverables, and vertical and horizontal integration. Support integrated baseline reviews.
  • Perform monthly IMS status and conduct schedule integrity checks, correcting schedule when necessary.
  • Maintain Baseline IMS through change control process. Perform change analysis to determine the schedule impact to the program. Ensure proper documentation and traceability is in place.
  • Provide planning support for programs including budgeting, performance monitoring, and analysis reporting. 
  • Utilize Gantt, PERT, milestone charts, earned value management and other project management techniques to gauge progress and identify performance variances to facilitate focus and intervention on critical areas.
  • Engage with the Program Team to perform thorough project analysis showing knowledge and understanding of the program and the principles of EVMS and suggests/creates/implements work around to enhance cost and schedule performance.
  • Perform risk analyses and identify and resolve critical path and network logic conflicts.
  • Understand numerous facets of scheduling and contribute to the development of new scheduling concepts, techniques and standards.
  • Provide interface/support to program office, IPT leads/CAMs and less experienced schedulers to achieve program objectives.
  • Support new business proposal providing recommendations on schedule methodologies and proposal strategies and develop an IMP and IMS.

Basic Qualifications:

Principal Program Planning Schedule Analyst (Level 3):

  • Must have 10 years of related experience in lieu of degree. Will consider: Bachelor’s degree with 6 years professional related experience – OR – a Master’s degree with 4 years of professional related experience.
  • 6 years of project scheduling experience using Microsoft Project or other related scheduling tool.
  • 6 years of experience using MS Office Suite, specifically Excel, PowerPoint, and Word.
  • Working knowledge of EVMS guidelines and processes.
  • Must be able to obtain a Secret Clearance.

Sr Principal Program Planning Schedule Analyst (Level 4):

  • Must have 14 years of related experience in lieu of degree. Will consider: Bachelor’s degree with 10 years professional related experience – OR – a Master’s degree with 8 years of professional related experience.
  • 10 years of project scheduling experience using Microsoft Project or other related scheduling tool.
  • 10 years of experience using MS Office Suite, specifically Excel, PowerPoint, and Word.
  • Working knowledge of EVMS guidelines and processes.
  • Must be able to obtain a Secret Clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 6 years or more of project scheduling experience and expert proficiency in Microsoft Project as demonstrated by an understanding of the tool’s properties and functions, including: exports and imports, multi-project consolidation, inter-project linkage, ability to create a variety of views, tables, filters and reports.
  • Experience with resource loaded schedules.
  • Experience using Schedule Health Analysis tools and Schedule Risk Assessment tools.
  • Experience with Milestone Pro or other milestone tool.
  • Experience with of EVMS surveillance and validation reviews.
  • Experience with Jira and understanding of Agile/Scrum methodology and concepts.
