Northrop Grumman Classified Cybersecurity is seeking a Staff Cyber Security Architect to join our Cyber Operations and Architecture (COA) team. As a Cyber Architect, you will be responsible for the development, testing, and support of various security tools that are developed under the COA team. In addition to tool development and sustainment, you will also be responsible for designing secure architectures and engineering principles that will be used as classified corporate cybersecurity standard across all NG classified programs.

Job Responsibilities Include (but not limited to):

  • Work on a high performing team as a senior architect and engineer that will be responsible for the design, development, implementation and support on a wide variety of security tools such as: McAfee ePO, Symantec Endpoint Protection, Nessus Essentials and Nessus Pro, Tenable.sc, Splunk, ELK Stack, SolarWinds, Rapid7, and more.

  • Designing and developing security requirements that drive down risk while maintaining operational capability.

  • Building enterprise-wide cyber reference architectures, taxonomy, and related documentation.

  • Defining common workflows, automations, templates, inheritable cyber services and execution value streams that enable sustainable compliance across the corporation.

  • Define strategic cyber roadmaps with a focus on innovation and continuous improvement.

  • Employ best practices when implementing security requirements for information systems including software engineering methodologies, system/security engineering principles, secure design, secure architecture, and secure coding techniques.

  • Evaluate the latest industry trends in cyber security and bring those into the enterprise as applicable.

  • Collaborate closely with other corporate and classified cyber teams to ensure proper governance and adherence to cyber policies and procedures.

  • Provide security thought leadership at an enterprise level.

  • Establish and document security strategies, directions, and approaches.

  • Provide Cyber architecture consulting & guidance to project teams and other areas of the organization.

  • Track and monitor security product landscape, specific to relevant security technologies and vendors.

  • Perform and document security research with the potential for architectural modeling & prototyping of solution options.

  • Development of conceptual security architectures, operating models, reference architectures and security technology standards.

  • Serve as the ISSE subject matter expert (SME) to programs and leadership as required for all security engineering and architecture topics regarding security best practices.

  • Perform assessments of systems and networks within the networking environment or enclave and identify where those systems and networks deviate from acceptable configurations, enclave policy, or local policy.

The successful candidate will establish an inclusive culture within a diverse environment. Travel within the continental United States will be required.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have at minimum, a master’s degree with 12 years of relevant experience; OR a bachelor’s degree with 14 years of relevant experience; OR an associate degree with 16 years of relevant experience; OR a High School Diploma/GED with 18 years of relevant experience is required.

  • Candidates must have a current DoD Secret level security clearance, to include a closed investigation date completed within the last 5 years in order to be considered.

  • Must have the ability to obtain, and maintain, access to Special Access Programs (SAPs) as a condition of continued employment.

  • Must have a DoD 8570 IAM level III security certification (examples: CISSP, CISM, Etc.)

  • Demonstrated expertise in networking, security engineering, security architecture, and complex network architectures to include cross domain solutions.

  • Experience with implementing and managing security tools such as: McAfee ePO, Splunk Enterprise Security, Tenable SecurityCenter, SCAP, and SCC.

  • Experience with Linux & Windows system administration.

  • Excellent technical document preparation and writing skills.

  • Experience with the Risk Management Framework or similar cyber-security, risk-based frameworks (e.g., HIPAA, PCI/DSS, etc.).

  • Experience working on DISA Security Technical Implementation Guide (STIG) implementation.

  • Demonstrated expertise and SME level knowledge with JSIG/NIST 800-53 policies, DAAPM, and ICD 503 policies, & writing entire RMF Bodies of Evidence to obtain and maintain ATO for classified systems.

  • 8-10 years of experience configuring and securing systems to achieve compliance with Security requirements (Controls, STIGS, etc.).

  • Must have excellent organizational and written and verbal communication skills.

  • Must have experience in communicating with various levels of leadership.

  • Experience leading multiple project assignments.

  • Scripting with PowerShell, Bash, and Python.

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Active TS/SCI w/ CI Polygraph with SAP and SCI access.

  • 15 or more years of experience as an ISSE or System Administrator in classified environments (SAP, SCI, and collateral).

  • Master’s degree in computer science, Information Assurance, or Cyber Security.

  • CCSP, CEH, AZ-500, CCSK Certifications.

  • Strong knowledge with the following tools: Nessus, Tenable.SC, Splunk, Symantec Endpoint Protection, McAfee ePO, Rapid7 Nexpose, Winzip/Roxio Secure Burn, SCC, SCAP, Bitlocker, LUKs.

  • Strong knowledge with the following technologies: containers, infrastructure as Code (IaC), and cloud security design principles.

  • Strong knowledge of security architecture and engineering.

  • Strong knowledge in Microsoft and Linux operating systems.

  • Experience with DevOps methodologies and practices.

  • Highly organized with ability to manage multiple priorities and time sensitive deliverables. Hands on, self-starting, disciplined and detail-oriented individual with ability to work independently in a high-volume environment

  • Efficient, proactive, responsive team player with excellent written and oral communication skills; able to interface effectively with all levels of corporate management and government customers
