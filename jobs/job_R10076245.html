ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking an Antenna RF Microwave Design Engineer 7 to join our team of qualified and diverse individuals in Columbus, CA.

Responsibilities not limited to demonstrating consistent and exceptional degree of ingenuity, creativity, and resourcefulness, internal and external to the company, offering best value Antenna RF solutions to customers. Application and/or development of highly advanced and integrated Antenna RF technologies, scientific principles, theories and concepts, forming a basis to new concepts or theories, resulting in redefining the state of the art in RF Antenna technology domain. Viewed as expert within the field and technical community. Able to communicate effectively and efficiently at the highest levels in the company, serving as a consultant in the long range technology planning and new or projected areas of research and advancement.

Primary scope will involve development of mission-enabling Antenna products for space.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) field from an accredited institution and 20 years of experience with designing/analyzing radio frequency (RF) and/or microwave Antenna systems OR 18 years of experience with a Master's Degree OR 15 years of experience with a PhD.
  • Expertise with CST Microwave Studio, HFSS, GRASP, FEKO, or other computational electromagnetics software tools
  • Candidate must have the ability to obtain, and maintain a U.S. Government TOP SECRET security clearance
