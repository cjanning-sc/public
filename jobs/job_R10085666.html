ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Logistics Manager 3. This position will be located in Detroit, MI and will have overall management responsibility of the packaging, shipping and transportation operations for the SDS Division. Responsible for managing teams across multiple sites and supporting multiple programs.

What you will get to do:

  • Responsible for controlling the efficient flow of goods, services, and information between point of origin through customer placement to meet customer requirements.
  • Ensures that customer service and time objectives are achieved within existing financial constraints to meet program and financial objectives.
  • Ensures the execution and continuous improvement of standard logistics processes, such as the replenishment system, data interchange systems, demand management, electronic data systems administration and related functions.
  • Builds relationships with strategic customers through logistics initiatives.
  • Integrates learning from customers, competitors, operating entities, distribution, transportation, customer service, other industries, industry groups, and professional training to continuously improve competitive position.
  • Strategically plan and manage logistics, shipping, transportation, and customer services.
  • Keep track of quality, delivery times, transport costs and efficiency.
  • Maintain metrics and analyze data to assess performance and implement improvements
  • Responsible for 3PL carrier selections, rate and contracts negotiations with carriers, and keep up-to-date on shipping carriers, routes and rates and any changes to them.
  • Local and long-haul transportation responsibility.
  • Ensuring that compliance with all federal and state regulations, as well as security requirements, regarding shipping and transportation is met.
  • Functions as an advisor to the team regarding tasks, projects, and operations. Becomes actively involved in daily operations only when required to meet schedules or to resolve complex problems.
  • Responsible for the direct supervision/management of the staff. Providing objective-oriented direction to subordinates utilizing management guidelines & general policies. Responsibilities include planning and performance addressing cost, schedule, and quality of the transportation and packaging operations.
  • Participate in proposal and planning projects by analyzing transportation/fleet requirements and supporting the development of best processes for accomplishing objectives of transportation and delivery.
  • Manage direct and indirect budget requirements and all other related administrative, training and personnel issues.
  • Provide oversight in the packaging engineering and shipping functions, as well as material handling in a production environment.
