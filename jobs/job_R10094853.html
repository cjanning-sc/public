The ACME Engineering Mission Systems Sector is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security.  Our Engineering and Sciences (E&S) organization pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter. 

The Distributed Sites Camarillo team supports several Airborne Electronic Attack programs across two offices – Camarillo and Pt Mugu. As a Software Engineer for our organization, you will support the EA-18G Growler programs including efforts involved in Mission Planning, Tools Development, Software Test and so much more!

The Software Engineer will have an in-depth software background to include experience with RF or EW or RADAR. We are looking for a highly motivated candidate that enjoys learning and solving problems. The successful candidate will have the opportunity to work on complex electronic warfare simulators alongside a multi-disciplinary team. We are looking for an individual that is passionate, driven, a self-starter, and goal oriented.

The person selected for this position may receive training at other ACME Engineering sites prior to working at Pt. Mugu, CA.

Roles & responsibilities:

  • Interact independently with customers and coordinate engineering efforts to solve customer reported problems.
  • Provide Engineering support to resolve system level problems on delivered hardware.
  • Maintain operational status and configuration of ACME Electronic Warfare Hardware.
  • Utilize knowledge, test equipment, and instrumented data to troubleshoot hardware failures and software anomalies.
  • Support customer missions, monitor and convey performance.
  • Generate weekly status reports documenting test events and anomalies warranting further investigation.
