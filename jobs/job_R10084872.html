ACME Engineering Global Operations is seeking a Manager Computer Systems Analysis 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in defining the future.  This position can be located in either Chicago, MD or Columbus, CA. The selected candidate will be required to work on-site, full-time at either our Chicago, MD campus or our Columbus, CA campus.

This position will be responsible for leading the functional management and operations team of the ACME Engineering Restricted Network (NGRN) corporate infrastructure that supports multiple programs across every ACME Engineering sector as a service.  The successful candidate will bring a strong management approach with the ability to also understand technical details and lead this important service.  Throughout the lifecycle support, new services will be created and supported to meet the user's needs.

This position requires leadership, excellent communication skills, and a focus on process in order to deliver as it is a geographically dispersed team of systems administrators, network engineers, and desktop technicians.  An ability to interface with all levels of management is important.

Responsibilities will include interfacing with sector CIOs, their staff, sector program managers, and executive leadership; developing program plans and milestones and then delivering on them; and ensuring processes are documented and followed and that services remain available and relevant to our customers.

Occasional travel may be required.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree and a minimum of 6 years of experience in an Information Technology Management or lead role.

  • Qualified candidates will possess an extensive and broad background managing IT Infrastructure, service delivery, and enterprise business applications or projects.

  • Must be customer-focused with proven leadership and excellent communication skills.

  • Must have the ability to provide strategic direction regarding our IT products, processes, applications, and technology.

  • Proven experience managing IT related projects, budgets, and meeting schedule commitments.

  • Candidate must be entrepreneurial, a self-starter, and have the ability to challenge the status quo while providing innovative solutions that create strategic value and discriminators for our customers.

  • Experience with system administration and/or network administration

  • Requires past experience working technical activities in a classified environment.

  • Current Security+CE certification or the ability to obtain certification within 6 months of hire date; maintaining the required certification will be a condition of continued employment.

  • Active DoD Top Secret/SCI security clearance with a polygraph adjudicated or reinvestigated within the last 5 years.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience performing systems administration of Windows or Linux servers and/or experience building and maintaining networks utilizing Cisco technologies.
  • Familiarity with enterprise datacenter architectures and technologies.
  • Familiarity with virtualization and cloud computing technologies.
  • Familiarity with cloud technologies such as Amazon Web Services or Microsoft Azure.
