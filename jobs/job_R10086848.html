ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Product Trainer/ Principal Product Trainer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Philadelphia, PA for the Logistics and Modernization group, operating on our 9/80 schedule meaning you will get every other Friday off. The position has no opportunity for remote or telework.

 

The successful candidate needs to be experienced with the Instructional Systems Development (ISD) framework for analyzing, designing, developing, implementing, and evaluating (ADDIE) learning systems. Select appropriate instructional methods such as individual training, group instruction, self-study, lectures, demonstration-performance, problem-based learning, student-centered learning, and guided discussion. Support, build, and promote a positive and collaborative culture across all teams. Develop and maintain expert level knowledge. Strong written and verbal communication skills required, as well as strong organizational skills and an ability to work independently in a deadline-oriented environment.

 

Job Duties Include:

· Develops training curriculum, study outlines, and orientation materials to be employed during procedures-based maintenance training.

· Support the development of criteria for evaluating effectiveness of training activities.

· Continuously revises lesson plans to meet new training requirements and maintain updated technical information.

· Provide input to meet new training requirements and to keep technical information up to date.

· Demonstrate and use effective training analysis, project management, course development, and evaluation skills.

· Develop instruction materials – self-paced guides, case studies, job aids, evaluations; scenario-based course outlines.

· Develop training materials – classroom materials, instructor guides, student manuals, workbooks, related materials; curricula to support program implementation across target audiences.

· Collaborate with subject matter experts (SMEs) to identify specific training needs and develop solutions for satisfying unique skills-based training requirements.

· Create and analyze assessments to evaluate learning effectiveness and instructional improvement opportunities – e.g., pre-tests, post-tests, and quizzes.

· Use ISD current Learning Theory to solve complex learning and performance problems.

 

Basic Qualifications for Product Trainer

· Bachelor's Degree with 3 years’ relevant experience; OR a Masters’ Degree with 1-year relevant experience; OR 7 years’ experience in lieu of Degree

· Experience in courseware development, instruction, or industry training content development

· Must have familiarity with Microsoft Office products (Word, PowerPoint, and Excel)

· Must have the ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret Security Clearance

· Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program Access (PAR/SAP)

 

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Product Trainer:

· Bachelor's Degree with 6 years’ relevant experience; OR a Maters’ Degree with 4 years’ relevant experience; OR 10 years’ experience in lieu of Degree

· Experience in courseware development, instruction, or industry training content development.

· Must have familiarity with Microsoft Office products (Word, PowerPoint, and Excel)

· Must have the ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret Security Clearance

· Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program Access (PAR/SAP)

 

Preferred Qualifications

· Must have working knowledge of customer compliance documents, government instructions, and government standards

· 6 or more years of experience in courseware development, instruction, or industry training content development

· An Active, In-Scope, DOD Secret Security Clearance or higher that has been granted / renewed within the past 6 years

· Special Program Access

· 3 or more years of aircraft maintenance classroom instruction and course development at a Field Training Detachment (special considerations will be given for diverse development experience).

· 6 or more years of hands-on aircraft maintenance experience (special considerations will be given for more diverse experience).

· Experience in task analysis procedures and training systems products

· Basic understanding of Agile processes.

· Design and development of curriculum for advanced technologies.

· Experience using courseware development tools – LCMS and LM
