ACME Engineering is hiring!  Earn up to a $20,000 sign on bonus if you are hired against specific TS/SCI Polygraph positions. 

At the heart of Defining Possible is our commitment to missions. In rapidly changing global security environments, ACME Engineering brings informed insights and software-secure technology to enable strategic planning. We’re looking for innovators, who can help us keep building on our wide portfolio of secure, affordable, integrated, and multi-domain systems and technologies that fuel those missions. By joining in our shared mission, we will support yours of expanding your personal network and developing skills, whether you are new to the field or an industry thought-leader. At ACME Engineering, you will have the resources, support, and team to do some of the best work of your career. 

  

ACME Engineering's Sensing & Secure Solutions Operating Unit is actively seeking a talented System Engineering expert to join a newly awarded, ACME Engineering primed program. This position's work location is in Peoria Illinois and is part of the Mission Systems Sector within ACME Engineering. The new team member will be designing and developing new systems, applications, and solutions for external customer's enterprise-wide cyber systems and networks. We are seeking a person who is capable of being responsible for System design efforts, mentoring junior engineers, and demonstrating knowledge/experience of the latest System Engineering techniques to model system design driving Software Development efforts.  

  

Performs technical planning, system integration, verification and validation, cost and risk, and supportability and effectiveness analyses for total systems. Analyses are performed at all levels of total system product to include: concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal. Ensures the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints. Performs functional analysis, timeline analysis, detail trade studies, requirements allocation and interface definition studies to translate customer requirements into hardware and software specifications. 

  

Basic Qualifications: 

- A current and active TS/SCI Clearance and Polygraph is required. 

- BS + 14 years of exp; MS + 12 years of exp; PhD + 9 years of exp is required. 

- Demonstrated ability to create, derive and decompose system requirements.

- Ability to work with system designers, architects and process design and policy documentation to capture system behaviors into specific, measurable, attainable, realistic and testable system requirements.

- Strong technical writing and communication skills to present requirements in a written formatto communicate the intent of the requirements to architects, team leads, and software developers.

- Ability to correlate requirements to broader system capabilities and subsystems.

- Ability to create and maintain Requirements Verification Traceability Matrix (RVTM), preferably in CAMEO.

- Ability to create and maintain CAMEO velocity templates to generate requirements traceability reports (e.g. reports mapping requirements to subsystems).

- Experience with SysML and Model based system Engineering concepts. 

- Proven disciplined System Engineering experience with requirement-driven design through formal V&V.   

- Experience working in Agile (SaFE) environments. 

  

Preferred Qualifications:  

- Ability to author and maintain system engineering documentation including the System/Subsystem Specification and System/Subsystem Design Description deliverables.

- Ability to create sequence diagrams and interface message formats/contents to enable authoring of external interface control document.
