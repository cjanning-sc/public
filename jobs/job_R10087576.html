Northrop Grumman, located in Promontory, UT, is currently recruiting for a Facilities Power Systems Electrical Engineer.

Job Summary including Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Designs power, controls and process instrumentation per the NEC including hazardous classified locations.

  • Provide detailed cost estimates for project design and implementation.

  • Troubleshoot industrial electrical equipment.

  • Develop electrical maintenance guidelines for industrial equipment.

  • Knowledge of performing Arc Flash Analysis

  • Expertise to develop electrical schematics and One Line diagrams that are logical, well organized and require little to no redlines

  • Expertise in Power Distribution

  • Work with maintenance craftsmen to troubleshoot.

  • Project management and cost estimating.

  • Provide Detailed Project Designs in AutoCAD drawing format.

Position Requirements:

  • BS in Engineering with an emphasis in electrical/power.

  • 2 years minimum industrial facility electrical design experience.

    • Position may be filled at higher level requiring BS in Engineering with an emphasis in electrical/power and 5 years minimum industrial facility electrical design engineering

  • Industrial power and control design experience.

  • AutoCAD drafting ability.

  • Ability to work in the field, climb ladders, work outside and work in congested areas.
