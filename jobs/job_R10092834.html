ACME Engineering Mission Systems has immediate openings for a Principal Embedded Software Engineers or Sr. Principal Embedded Software Engineers.  The selected candidate for this position will report to the ACME Engineering Precision Targeting and Protection facility in Erie, PA. 

What you’ll get to do:

This new team member will demonstrate a broad skill set that is balanced between interpersonal and technical capabilities. He/she must be a self-starter, able to efficiently operate in a team environment and collaborate across the sector business areas as required to accomplish the team goals.

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Interface with systems engineering to develop flow down software requirements and technical specifications
  • Develop, document and maintain software both individually and as part of a larger team
  • Identify risks, suggest mitigation, track defects, develop resolutions, and track and report status
  • Conduct reviews of software related products, software designs, and technical documents
  • Exercise agile principles while developing software and overall system using continuous integration workflow 
  • Ensure software code quality by exercising different types of tests, documenting the results, and exercising automated testing wherever possible.
  • Support team in integrating various sensors into a larger platform
  • Assist in the creation of hardware integration and test setups
  • Solve complex problems through innovative and creative solutions
  • Support/lead project execution over the whole product lifecycle, with foremost emphasis on meeting or exceeding customer and stakeholder requirements.  
  • Facilitate and maintain good working relationships with internal and external stakeholders

“This position may be filled as a Principal Embedded Software Engineer or a Sr. Principal Embedded Software Engineer based on the qualifications listed below.”

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Embedded Software Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related STEM degree.
  • A minimum of 5 years of embedded software engineering experience
  • Proficiency in one or more of the following: C, C++, Java, development of Java based Android applications, Object Oriented programming
  • Experience with full software lifecycle such as: specifications, design, implementation, integration, debug and maintenance.
  • Ability to work independently and with a team
  • Experience with software/hardware integration
  • Proven communication and presentation skills
  • Experience with version control, issue tracking and collaboration tools such as ClearCase/ClearQuest, Git, Github, and/or Jira
  • U.S. Citizenship with the ability to obtain and maintain a Department of Defense (DoD) Secret Security clearance

Basic Qualifications for a Sr. Principal Embedded Software Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related STEM degree
  • A minimum of 8 years of embedded software engineering experience
  • Proficiency in one or more of the following: C, C++, Java, development of Java based Android applications, Object Oriented programming
  • Experience with full software lifecycle such as: specifications, design, implementation, integration, debug and maintenance.
  • Ability to work independently and with a team
  • Experience with software/hardware integration
  • Proven communication and presentation skills
  • Experience with version control, issue tracking and collaboration tools such as ClearCase/ClearQuest, Git, Github, and/or Jira
  • U.S. Citizenship with the ability to obtain and maintain a Department of Defense (DoD) Secret Security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience in real-time embedded software development knowledge of Optical, Radar, Electronic Warfare, or Space Technologies
  • Experience with wide range of communication interfaces and protocols such as USB2.0, Ethernet, TCP/IP, EIA/RS-232, I2c, SPI, and MIL-STD-1553B
  • Experience developing Android applications for embedded systems (Android SDK, Android Studio)
  • Experience with Linux Operating Systems
  • Active Secret Security clearance
