ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems is seeking a Senior Principal Communications/Networks Electronics Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Rancho Bernardo, OH.

  • Essential Functions:
    • Lead the a team of 10 or more through integration to meet program milestones
    • Lead the planning, designing, configuring, installing and troubleshooting network and communications infrastructure
    • Clearly and effectively discuss technical low-level details with engineers and provide high level impact statements and recommendations to senior management.  
    • Document issues,  justify and recommend solutions related to communications systems and network technical challenges. 
    • Provide ongoing mentorship to junior engineers.
    • Travel up to 25%

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university and 9 years’ experience with bachelor’s degree, 7 years with MS, or 5 years with PHD
  • Extensive experience working with RF Communications Systems (CDL, UHF LOS, SATCOM, tactical voice, and Link 16)
  • Experience working with IP networking topologies, quality of service implementation, network encryption methods (AES and Type 1), and routing hardware appliances
  • Active DoD Secret Clearance or DoE equivalent

Preferred Qualifications:

  • MS in Electrical Engineering with Computer Systems Engineering, Computer Science or related discipline
  • Active Top-Secret clearance
  • Experience Leading Teams of 10 or more through integration to meet program milestones
  • Experience as a subcontracts/supplier lead
  • Experience in end to end communications architectures (including the radios and their connection points or next hop points (satellites, ground entry, etc.))
  • Experience planning, designing, configuring, installing and troubleshooting network infrastructure and associated systems, to include routers, switches and firewalls, in support of customer requirements.
  • Experience in LAN/WAN technologies such as VLANs, Trunking (port tagging), Spanning-Tree, OSPF, DMVPN and Port Security.
  • Experience in red/black separation between classification environments and methods to employ those separations.
  • Knowledge of the operational employment of UAS communications links and capabilities
  • Experience handling COMSEC material
