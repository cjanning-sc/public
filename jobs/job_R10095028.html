Inventory Material Control Support 2 - (Space/Satellite)
R10095028
1st Shift (United States of America)

The Space Tactical Business Unit is seeking an Inventory Control Specialist 2 to join our team.  This position is located in Dulles, VA and will be on 1st shift. The Inventory Control Specialist 2 Compiles records concerned with quantity, cost, and type of material received, in stock, or issued. Requisitions needed supplies; verifies material received to determine irregularities in order; inspects articles and rejects defective ones. Prepares inventory records and reports and investigates discrepancies in stock counts.

Duties/Responsibilities:

  • Maintain track a daily spread sheet of activities 

  • Pick components according to daily spread sheet and priorities and prepare kits for manufacturing

  • Load reels to feeder and load to magazines in proper order and hand off to SMT operator

  • Label reels with inventory labels and setup kits according to daily/weekly work-center schedule

  • Cycle count components and perform daily kit audits

  • Responsible for keeping a neat, clean, and organized workspace

  • Responsible for reading all documents and historical data to make process more efficient

  • Responsible for handling materials and detailed data entry of items into systems

  • Used automated material identification scanner to move, stack, bins, and select storage locations and pick material per work order

  • Maintains outstanding records for documenting inventory

  • Maintains excellent customer relations and developed customer rapport

  • Process all inventory transactions including ready for stocks, location transfers, reservations, kits, inter/intra program transfers, returns to stocks, incoming customer furnished materials, and etc.

  • High School diploma OR GED and a minimum of 2 years’ of relevant experience

  • Post receipt against purchase order and deliver items to appropriate department or individual

  • Working knowledge of MS Office preferred

  • Must be detailed oriented and have good communication skills

  • Ability to work in a fast paced environment and be comfortable with change

  • Ability to solve problems
