Propulsion Systems Sector is looking for Operations Team Supervisors (OTS) to support our Case Value Stream operations at our Lansing facilities.

Job Description:

Successful candidates will be responsible for the frontline supervision of High Value rocket motor case manufacturing activities utilizing Machining Centers and Hand Operations.  This position is accountable for safety, quality, on the job training, productivity, and hardware delivery schedules within areas of responsibility.

The preferred candidate communicates expectations clearly and initiates necessary shift assignment actions proactively to ensure that organizational goals and business objectives are accomplished while ensuring company policy directives and operating procedures are strictly being followed.  

As a frontline leader, this position works side-by-side with a team of production machinists and technicians maintaining a presence on the production floor to influence safe operations and drives adherence to all safety rules, regulations, and standards.

Experience Requirements:

•  High School diploma or GED with 6 years of experience working in a manufacturing environment.
•  Two years of successful team leadership
•  Five years machining center experience
•  Skill in interpreting engineering drawings, specifications and operating procedures
•  Strong Root Cause Analysis and Problem Solving skills
•  Knowledge, experience and demonstrated strict compliance to Safety, Quality and Manufacturing policies and procedures.

•  Able to acquire and maintain Employee Possessor (EP) status under the Safe Explosives Act (SEA).

Preferred experience and skills:  

•  A.S. degree plus four years experience or a B.S. degree with two years of experience. 
•  Three years experience in machining processes for solid rocket cases.
•  Machinist Certification from an accredited Trade School
•  Experience with FANUC controllers
•  Advanced Root Cause Analysis and Problem Solving skills
