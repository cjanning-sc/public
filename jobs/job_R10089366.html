We have an opening for a Staff SoSI Digital Engineering/Architecture Lead to join our qualified, diverse team in Redondo Beach, CA.

What you’ll get to do?

The individual will oversee the development of the ESS System model that will be used to analyze the system design and enable technical trades to be performed at various levels.  Integration of Segment digital models.  Will provide an digital engineering strategy that meets the Government’s digital engineering methodology to establish and maintain a technical baseline, with automated technical impact tools, integrated modeling and simulation, and has traceability to the program technical solution.  Generate Digital Engineering artifacts to inform, define, and run engineering processes and to present data for technical cost, schedule, and performance.  This project will heavily emphasize state-of-the-art Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE), maintaining all requirements, descriptive models, analytical models, cost models, and management and support functions in one common, integrated suite of MBSE tools.  The candidate for this position must have significant, hands-on expertise in UPDM/DODAF and SysML. 

Job duties include:  refining MBSE standards, style guides, and conventions; coordinating  MBSE infrastructure development; assisting MBSE change management and version control; communicating MBSE processes among teams, organizations, and management; creating and maintaining SysML models in Cameo EA; liaising with subject matter experts, analysts, architects, designers, system engineers, specialty engineers, and management to capture structure and behavior using accurate SysML representations according to OMG standards.

Basic Qualifications: 

  • 14 Years with BS STEM Degree; 12 Years with Masters; 9 Years with PhD Experience with Model Based System Engineering (MBSE)
  • Experience with UPDM/DODAF and SysML
  • Active DoD Secret security clearance
  • Ability to travel 10%

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with Cameo EA.  Phoenix Integration a plus.
  • Experience with MilSatCom, and Ground C2 systems.
  • Experience with MBSE on System of Systems
  • Experience with cross-functional teams and MBSE in an Agile environment
  • Active DoD Top Secret security clearance
