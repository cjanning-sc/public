Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking an Electronic Warfare Systems Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals at our Linthicum/Baltimore, MD campus.

Candidates must be self-motivated systems thinkers that can work as part of a multidisciplinary engineering team.

  • Performance analysis

  • Data analysis

  • Support of firmware, software and hardware integration and test
