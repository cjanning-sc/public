Sr. Principal VMware Systems Administrator
R10074645
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Global Operations Security is seeking a well-qualified VMware administrator to join its dynamic team of technical professionals in Linthicum, MD. The selected candidate will be required to work on-site, full-time, at our Linthicum campus.  Roles and responsibilities will include but not be limited to the following:

  • Perform as primary VMware administrator for a classified government contract, supporting multiple VxRail/vSphere and Horizon view infrastructures.

  • Maintain smooth operation of multi-user virtual environments, including coordination with network, software and system engineers, project managers, end users, and customer and IT management.

  • Complete administration tasks as required to include patching, updates, security hardening and remediation, and management of VxRail/vSphere/ESXi platform and associated tool sets.

  • Assist in the implementation of virtual infrastructures and associated software packages and tool sets to maintain smooth functionality of the environment and improve service levels.

  • Install and configure monitoring solution and logging as required.

  • Manage virtual network switch infrastructure and file system/storage allocation and space.

  • Recommend and implement system enhancements that will improve the performance and reliability of the system including monitoring, proactive problem identification, and configuration management.

  • Analyze internal and external customer requirements and determine equipment and software requirements for solutions to problems by means of automation. 

  • Provide backup and recovery services.

  • Create and maintain administrative accounts and security access lists as required.

  • Test new releases of products to ensure compatibility and minimize user impact.

  • Interact, meet, discuss, and troubleshoot issues with vendors; evaluate vendor products, services, and suggestions.

  • Develop and document technical processes and procedures as needed.

  • Communicate effectively at all levels of the organization, with internal and external customers, in written and verbal format.

  • Adhere to strict Information Systems security guidelines in all cases.

  • Report project status as required for all recurring and non-recurring efforts.

  • Work under minimal direction and independently determine and develop proactive approach to solutions.

  • Participate in inspection activities and security events as required.

  • Lift equipment weighing up to 40 pounds.

  • Work after hours and weekends as needed.

 

Basic Qualifications:

  • Associate’s degree and 11 years of experience, or Bachelor’s degree and 9 years of experience, or Master’s degree and 7 years of experience; a High School diploma or equivalent with 13 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a completed degree.

  • Active DoD Secret security clearance; maintaining the required security clearance will be a condition of continued employment.

  • The ability to obtain a Special Access Program (SAP/SAR) clearance within a reasonable period of time as determined by the company and also by customer and/or program requirements; maintaining a SAP/SAR clearance will be a condition of continued employment.

  • IAT Level II certification (ex: Security+CE) or greater, or the ability to obtain the required certification within 3 months of start date; maintaining the required certification will be a condition of continued employment.

  • Experience building and administering VMware vSphere v7x or higher in a large environment.

  • Experience with VMware Horizon View and VDI infrastructures.

  • Experience managing Windows Server 2012 and higher or Linux Red Hat v6 and higher OS versions in a Windows domain environment.

  • Experience with virtual switch architecture and storage allocation in virtual environments.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree in an IT related discipline.

  • VMware certification in vSphere v6.0 or higher.

  • DoD 8570 Certification in a primary OS (Windows, Linux, Cisco).

  • Experience or strong familiarity with VxRail Platform.

  • VMware Identity Manager (now Workspace One Access) familiarity.

  • Experience with Windows domain architectures including but not limited to Active Directory, GPOs, Policies, and permissions.

  • Experience operating under and managing systems within NISPOM Chapter 8, DCID 6/3-ICD 503, RMF, STIG, JAFAN, or JSIG information system environments.

  • Currently valid Security+CE, CASP, CISSP or similar security certification.

  • Understanding of basic networking, including subnets, routing, and VLANs; Cisco experience or certification.

  • Familiarity with VMware NSX basic architecture.

  • Strong familiarity with SAN storage systems including NetApp and/or EMC products.

  • Scripting using PowerShell or PowerCLI command line tools.
