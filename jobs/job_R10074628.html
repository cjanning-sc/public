Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems is seeking candidates with outstanding leadership skills who would be responsible for Configuration Data Management as the Lead reporting to Configuration Management Section Manager reporting directly to the System Management Control Configuration Manager under PAICoE System Engineering & Electronics Payload (SE&EP) Director.  Position is located in Palmdale, CA or South Bay, CA.


Responsibilities: 

  • Serve as the lead HW CDM focal point, supporting, leading and training the HW CDM team and providing direct support to the manager
  • Responsible for maintaining all CDM program procedures, processes, and work instructions working with each discipline within the CDM group to ensure these document are maintained and kept current
  • Cross matrix and match up program procedures to Sector processes and get waivers, if needed
  • Support all HW CDM Audits and CPATs Actions
  • support proposal development for CDM support and assisting estimating efforts
  • Provide back-up support for change boards, CDRL assistance, and other CDM related tasks when needed
  • Serve as the focal point for control, storage, retrieval, and delivery of all CDM data 
  • Set up the CDM (document, software and hardware) processes and controls specifically for this program
  • Control engineering and document releases, including plan and coordinate preparation of project documentation (such as engineering drawings, production specifications, programmatic documents and others), to ensure requirements are met
  • Control change requests for configuration items.  Administer change configuration review boards.
  • Track and close changes and action items
  • Process and track program contract and supplier deliverables
  • Maintain the CM Library and baselines
  • Interact frequently with data originators, program managers, and customer representatives concerning project deliverables and deliverable data scheduling requirements
  • Plan and coordinate preparation of project documentation, such as engineering documentation and drawings, subcontractor specifications and schedules, and contract modifications, to ensure customer contract requirements are met. 
  • Review contract requirements to determine documentation required for each phase of project, applying knowledge of engineering and manufacturing
  • Coordinate modification records for management control. 
  • Establish change orders and prepare for change authorization and documentation by internal organizations and subcontractors. 
  • Review and analyze released engineering change data and coordinates changes with engineering, quality, support, manufacturing, and engineering data control activities. 
  • Prioritize requests and communicate schedule to all stakeholders including customers 
  • Collaborate with other team leads 
  • Perform baseline (product) management, hardware release and replication management, configuration item identification, software build releases, and configuration audits 
  • Control configuration items, product traceability matrices, documents, and software media, CM records, and software libraries 
  • Perform change management and status identification 
  • Manage/coordinate program strategic council, support PIT tasking related to Data Management and track work status/metrics in support of team activities.

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor’s degree with 10 years of Configuration Management experience or 8 years’ experience with a Master’s degree; or 14 years’ experience in lieu of a degree 
  • Active Top Secret clearance is required for this position 
  • Knowledge of software / hardware configuration, data and change management principles and proficiency in the use of configuration management tools 
  • Experience with software Configuration Management tools and software management to include baseline management and auditing 
  • Self-motivated and able to effectively work with all levels of the organization 
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills. 
  • Proficient in Windows, Microsoft Office Suite programs: Word, Excel & PowerPoint, Access tools, Adobe, TeamCenter, CMDL, Collaborator, and other related CM programs 

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Master’s degree with 10 years of Configuration Management experience or 8 years’ experience with a Phd degree; or 14 years’ experience in lieu of a degree 
  • Experience leading a CM team for program with multiple task orders/CLINs
