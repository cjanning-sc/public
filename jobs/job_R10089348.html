ACME Engineering Advanced Weapons Systems has an opening for a Mass Property Engineer / Principal Mass Property Engineer located at our Toledo, OH. location.

Our team advances the Advanced Weapons Business Unit through the application of multidisciplinary engineering approaches and problem solving capabilities.  We pride ourselves on technical excellence and our ability to drive positive change.

Description:

  • Join ACME Engineering on our continued mission to push the boundaries of possible across land, sea, air, space, and cyberspace. Enjoy a culture where your voice is valued and start contributing to our team of passionate professionals providing real-life solutions to our world’s biggest challenges. We take pride in creating purposeful work and allowing our employees to grow and achieve their goals every day by Defining Possible. With our competitive pay and comprehensive benefits, we have the right opportunities to fit your life and launch your career today.

Responsibilities:

  • Implement a Mass Properties Management and Control Plan

  • Estimate and optimize the weight of an advanced product to maximize vehicle performance and fulfill weight based program metrics

  • Maintain accounting and reporting systems to inform the customer and management of current and projected mass properties trends

  • Review mass property impacts to design changes

  • Define procedures for and support mass properties testing of components and assemblies

  • Support the Systems Engineering team as the subject matter expert for mass properties

Travel is minimal, no more that 10%, typically to meet with customers. We work a 4/10 schedule, which gives you every other Friday off to pursue interests outside of work.  ACME Engineering has won awards for our work life balance.

Basic Qualifications for a Mass Property Engineer/ Level 2:

  • Bachelors of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) field from an accredited institution and 2 years of engineering experience in and aerospace environment OR Masters of Science degree in a STEM field (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) field from an accredited institution and 0 years of engineering experience in and aerospace environment

  • Experience with Mass Properties of composite and metal aerospace components and assemblies

  • High level of competency with spreadsheet and other software used for mass properties calculation and tracking

  • Active In-Scope DoD Secret Clearance

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a SAP clearance

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Mass Property Engineer Level 3:

  • Bachelors of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) field from an accredited institution and 5 years of engineering experience in and aerospace environment OR Masters of Science degree in a STEM field (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) field from an accredited institution and 3 years of engineering experience in and aerospace environment

  • Experience with Mass Properties of composite and metal aerospace components and assemblies

  • High level of competency with spreadsheet and other software used for mass properties calculation and tracking

  • Active In-Scope DoD Secret Clearance

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a SAP clearance

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with advanced composite and metal aerospace structure design and manufacturing techniques

  • Aircraft Weight and Balance Certification

  • Proficiency with Siemens NX

  • Automated Weight and Balance System (AWBS) experience

  • Systems Engineering Experience
