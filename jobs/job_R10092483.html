IRBM/ICBM Chief Engineer

1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Space Systems is searching for a Chief Engineer, M3, to join the Program Engineering Management team within Launch Vehicles business unit located in Chandler, AZ. 

The IRBM/ICBM Chief Engineer (CE) is the technical authority for intermediate and intercontinental missile defense targets. The CE is responsible for technical execution for two different booster systems. The CE provides technical leadership of the multidisciplinary engineering team to successfully complete the required design and mission reviews to ensure compliance with both internal and customer requirements, ensures timely completion of customer deliverables, and leads resolution of significant technical issues. The ideal candidate will have broad knowledge regarding missile system technologies and operations. Excellent verbal and written communication skills and the demonstrated ability to lead multidisciplinary teams in resolving complex problems are essential.

Role Description & Responsibilities:

You support program execution including ongoing development activities, production, test, and flight operations for the IRBM/ICBM Targets Program. You support the Targets Program Chief Engineer in the technical oversight of the IRBM/ICBM program and ensure proper allocation and coordination of engineering resources through an IPT structure. You will co-lead the development and execution of technical reviews. You will partner with IPT leads to achieve efficient, effective, and innovative engineering solutions.  You will ensure subsystem and supporting technology development achieves program success and look for opportunities to leverage common design and products.

•Help the team achieve 100% mission success.

•Support Program Management team with broad technical experience and down-and-in technical guidance to the technical and operations teams.

•Maintain working relationships with the Customer, providing timely communication and engaging to resolve technical questions posed by the Customer.

•Guide teams preparing for flight operations with rapid review of change engineering, analysis, and test results.

•Deliver quick and thorough resolution of non-conformances and other challenges that might arise, including leading failure investigations and presentation of findings to internal and customer review boards.

•Bring mature engineering leadership to the coordination and performance of internal reviews like ERB, CCB, FRRB, IFRR and design reviews.

•Support flight operations as in coordination with program management.

•Ensure that designs and hardware builds meet all internal and Customer requirements, especially across engineering discipline boundaries. Provide justification and adjudicate through proper boards any deviations or waivers to such requirements.

•Perform other duties, as necessary, commensurate with the role of Chief Engineer.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science Engineering degree plus 9 years of experience; Masters degree plus 7 years of experience; PhD plus 4 years of experience.

  • U.S. Citizen

  • Candidate must have an active Secret Security Clearance.

  • Ability to Travel Occasionally (6-8 trips/year)

  • Candidate must have a minimum of 7 years directly related experience in project management

  • Position is located in Chandler, AZ and requires 100% on-site support.

  • Direct internal as well as external customer interface experience

  • Demonstrated verbal and written communication excellence with customers and all levels of management

  • Experience developing project cost and schedules and managing efforts via EVMS

  • Exceptional leader of a multi-disciplined team

  • Thorough understanding of design standards, procurement processes and configuration management

  • Ability to work under limited supervision and complete projects on time and on budget

  • Multi-disciplinary engineering experience -or- system engineering experience

  • Perform other duties, as necessary, commensurate with the role of Chief Engineer / Program Manager

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Candidate will have a minimum of 10 years of directly related experience in project management

  • Experience with missile systems including field operations

  • Interim Top Secret or Top Secret Clearance is preferred.
