ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Manager of General Manufacturing 1 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Cincinnati, OH.

The selected candidate has the responsibility, authority, and accountability for leading the manufacturing activities of a given cost center within restricted programs, including quality, cost and schedule objectives and factory accountability along with other duties.

Essential Functions:

  • Providing direction and coaching to technicians and other production personnel in the assembly and manufacturing areas.
  • Ensures team members receive the required training and prepares the daily work plans in support of overall program objectives.
  • Encourages and provides direction for career development of employees, including educational options for highly motivated employees.
  • Provides leadership/direction to ensure all affordability initiatives and opportunities for process improvements are systematically identified, pursued, and supported.

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels. 

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Minimum of a High School Diploma /equivalent with 5 years’ experience in an aerospace, automotive, military, manufacturing, maintenance and/or related environment. Additional education may be considered in lieu of experience.
  • 2 years’ experience in a leadership, supervisory and/or management role.
  • A current and active DoD Secret security clearance with a periodic re-investigation OR an original adjudication date completed within the last 5 years
  • The ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access within a reasonable period of time, as determined by the company to meet its business need
  • Must be available to work any shift

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor's Degree
  • Special Program Access
  • Leadership experience in aerospace or a related industry
  • Advanced knowledge of MS Office (Word, Excel & PowerPoint) and the ability to effectively communicate with various levels of leadership

Salary Range: $89,800.00 - $134,800.00
