ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Information Technology Project Manager 1. This position will be located in Detroit, UT and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. Learn more about the Sentinel program here. ACME Engineering supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What you will get to do: 

Responsibilities for this role will include:

  • Managing and leading the team for technical development, collaboration, integration, and deployment of program capabilities.
  • Responsible for leading teams that may include systems engineers and project managers of the GBSD capabilities to support process development, implementation and driving discipline to ensure the technical integrity of the integrated digital environment. 
  • Support developing the strategic and tactical objectives.
  • Functional and Technical Manager for MBx Tools across the domains of PLM, ERP, MES, Logistics, CAD, Manufacturing, Analysis and Simulation. Responsible for team of 10+ senior application SME’s along with tool adoption and deployment strategy across the Program.
  • Responsible for overall tool design and technical leadership.
  • Coordination with the Program IPTs and IT resources to define/document requirements, providing development oversight across the Program, and supporting the planning and implementation of any application integration solution. 
  • Must have an emphasis on qualities such as analytical thinking, people leadership, managerial skills, networking, excellent presentation skills, and a passion of excellence. 
  • Should have experience with a variety of application integration platforms, project management, and Engineering/Manufacturing processes and enabling tools.
  • Develop deep and enduring Customer relationships by working closely with them and understanding their needs (both internal and external)
  • Develop strong relationships with the team by working closely with them, understanding their roadblocks, and resolving issues.
  • Ensure technical integrity, quality, coordination, and provide oversight for keep program projects
  • Continual focus on decision making based on engineering efficiency for increased performance
  • Experience in leadership skills to plan, organize, integrate and drive program activities across multiple engineering disciplines, along with the ability to effectively motivate people to work as an integrated team across locations, companies and cultures.
  • Oversees projects and task assigned to the organizational group performed by non-exempt and/or exempt employees. Becomes involved as needed to ensure the success of the program. Makes decisions that support accomplishment of discipline objective.
