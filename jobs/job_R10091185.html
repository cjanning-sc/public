ACME Engineering Space Sector has an opening for a Supply Chain Planning Specialist to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Grand Rapids Michigan. The selected candidate will manage a program or a function within the larger organizational unit, setting and operating to objectives and providing support and customer service to their supported program(s).

Will operate on 1st shift

Position is in-office, cannot accommodate a telecommute/hybrid schedule

Essential Functions:

Responsible for the assessment of supply resources; aggregates and prioritizes demand requirements; plans inventory, distribution requirements, production, material and capacity for all products. Participates in make/buy process, capacity and resource planning, product phase-in/phase-out, manufacturing ramp-up, end-of-life management and product-line management.

  • Plans inventory levels based on dependent and independent Bill-of-Materials (BOM) requirements
  • Assesses capacity for current and short-term manufacturing requirements
  • Creates/reduces supply requirements based on inventory adjustments
  • Participates in long-term capacity and resource planning to ensure proper scheduling of new product line phase-ins as well as existing product line phase-outs
  • Collaborates with source process area regarding inventory levels, working capital, and other areas as needed and creates/releases requisitions
  • Receives/reviews requirements for non-standard parts
  • Analyzes lead times and identifies critical path procurements
  • Analyzes and aggregates supply/demand requirements
  • Analyzes purchase material supply/demand position using Material Requirements Planning (MRP) tools

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree with 4 years of experience, a Master’s degree with 2 years’ experience or a high school diploma (equivalent) with 8 years of experience. Experience in this case encompasses supply chain, production planning, materials planning, total quality management, procurement, supplier quality assurance and/or industry related fields
  • Ability to perform 50% desk work and 50% production floor integration responsibilities with the ability to lift up to 25 pounds
  • Knowledge of SAP or other similar ERP/MRP system and experience with data sets including analyzing large data sets
  • A current active DOD Secret Clearance that has been investigated in the last 5 years
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Special Process access within a reasonable period of time as required by the business to meet objectives

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Production floor experience in the aerospace industry
  • Knowledge of MES business system
  • Current ACME Special Program Access
