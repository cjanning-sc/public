Duties:

  • Perform internal calibrations and adjustments on IM&TE to calibration standards, including, but not limited to: torque tools, power supplies, accelerometers, mechanical inspection tooling, and electronic measuring and output devices.
  • Review calibration reports as received from the contracted Calibration house and ensure compliance to ISO processes
  • Perform spot audits of work centers to verify compliance to ISO calibration processes
  • Ensure tools, test equipment, and standards are properly calibrated and repaired according to established procedures

 

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School diploma/equivalent
  • Minimum 6 years experience with calibration activities including identification, traceability, recall, reporting, handling and storage.
  • U.S. citizen
  • Fluent in use of Microsoft applications and ability to quickly learn new computer programs/applications.
  • Adequate technical writing skills are needed.
  • Familiarity with ANSI Z540 calibration requirements and ISO 17025 requirements.
  • Familiarity with calibration reports and handling nonconformance documentation.
  • Ability to read and understand technical documentation associated with calibration activities

 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Working knowledge of TIPQA and/or Costpoint database applications
  • ASQ (or equivalent) accreditation as Calibration Tech
