ACME Engineering Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Principal Network Engineer - JEMINI to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located out of Schriever Space Force Base, Indianapolis, CO.

Position Overview:

  • The network engineer will produce designs including system, logical, and connectivity drawings using DoDAF standards and present them to different engineering review boards.
  • Conducts testing of network design.
  • Maintains technical expertise in all areas of network and computer hardware and software interconnection and interfacing, such as routers, switches, firewalls, gateways, etc.
  • Evaluates and reports on new communications technologies to enhance capabilities of the network.
  • The position involves hands on designing and troubleshooting networks to include Cisco and Fortinet technologies.
     

Basic Qualifications:

If applicable, please note your updated security clearance and IAT/relevant certifications on your resume.

  • An active Secret clearance is required.
  • 5 Years with Bachelor’s degree in a related field; 9 years’ experience in lieu of a degree.
  • Must possess a DoD 8570 certification at IAT Level III or higher (CISSP or equivalent)
  • Demonstrated experience for the design and execution of network engineering configuration changes and documentation in a complex IT environment.
  • Experience in reviewing work products produced by a team of engineers for quality, completeness, and adherence to established procedures and policies
  • Experience including configuring Cisco and/or Juniper routers/switches and troubleshooting routing protocols such as OSPF/BGP as well as experience with firewall technologies.
  • Experience with creating system level and connectivity diagrams is a must. Strong MS Visio skills are also required.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with WANs, Taclanes, VxLAN highly desirable.
  • Top Secret Security Clearance.
  • Knowledge of strategic and theater missile defense weapon systems (C2BMC, Aegis, GFC/C, etc.), sensor systems, message formats (VMF, TADIL), and communications (DISN, SIPRNet, MDA CNet, GCN, etc) environments.
  • Certifications such as Network+, SSCP, CISSP, JNCIA, CCNA and CCNP.
  • Understanding of the project lifecycle, related processes, and traditional systems engineering skills.
