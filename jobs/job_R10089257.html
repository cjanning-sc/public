The Advanced Mission Programs (AMP) Team, a large restricted development program, is seeking a highly motivated self-starter to perform as an Automated Data Processing Equipment (ADPE) and Software Engineer in their Ground Software (SW) Engineering Team.

As an ADPE and software/script developer on this team, you will have the opportunity to design, documentation, tests and perform some software application development and scripting.  The SW ADPE team is in the process of transitioning from a Waterfall methodology to a hybrid Agile sprint implementation. 

A DevOps environment is being created and utilized with an emphasis on Continuous Integration.  Team planning, collaboration, and development activities utilize the Atlassian product suite, including Jira, Bitbucket, Bamboo, and Confluence.  You will ensure that the ADPE is built to the documented design and takes into consideration best practices and security implications.  You will thrive on a team that is experienced, brilliant, and extremely supportive of one another.
