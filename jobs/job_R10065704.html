ACME Engineering is seeking a Supply Chain Specialist in planning, procurement, or subcontracts.  This position will be located at our Mission Systems Sector in Springfield, Chicago, or Schaumburg, IL.

The qualified candidate will become part of ACME Engineering's Global Supply Chain organization. This position will be in the Pathways rotational program where there will be three rotation opportunities within Global Supply Chain and other core areas of the business. 

Basic Qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either a level 1 or 2. Basic qualifications for an Associate Supply Chain Planning Specialist level 1 or a Supply Chain Planning Specialist level 2.

Basic Qualifications for an Associate Supply Chain Planning Specialist:

A candidate, regardless of age and hiring source, must meet ALL of the below criteria. The candidate must:

  • Be completing or has completed their Bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution

  • Be majoring in or having majored in a Supply Chain Management, Business Management, Operations Management, Data Analytics, or closely related degree

  • Have an overall cumulative GPA of 3.0/4.0 or higher (unofficial academic transcripts must be provided at time of application by uploading the documents to your application or profile)

  • Be able to obtain and maintain a U.S. Government security clearance (U.S. citizenship is a pre-requisite)

Basic Qualifications for a Supply Chain Planning Specialist:

A candidate, regardless of age and hiring source, must meet ALL of the below criteria. The candidate must:

  • Be completing or has completed their Master’s degree from an accredited institution or is completing their Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution and 2 years of supply chain planning experience

  • Be majoring in or having majored in a Supply Chain Management, Business Management, Operations Management, Data Analytics, or closely related degree

  • Have an overall cumulative GPA of 3.0/4.0 or higher (unofficial academic transcripts must be provided at time of application by uploading the documents to your application or profile)

  • Be able to obtain and maintain a U.S. Government security clearance (U.S. citizenship is a pre-requisite)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Have an overall cumulative GPA of 3.7/4.0 or higher

  • Previous internship/co-op or other work experience, leadership & teamwork capabilities, interpersonal communication skills, and activities (professional/community/extracurricular)

Salary Range: $53,800 to $80,600
