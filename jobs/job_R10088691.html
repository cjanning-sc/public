ACME Engineering Future Programs is seeking a Principal Systems Architect Engineer/Sr. Principal Systems Architect Engineer to join the Systems Engineering team. This position is located in Cleveland, CA and supports Classified efforts OR various FVL (future vertical lift) proposal efforts, RWP (request for white papers), executing studies awarded to ACME Engineering and its partners.  

What you’ll get to do:

The selected job candidate will be joining a Model Based System Engineering (MBSE) team and assist in modeling existing configurations and capabilities. The candidate will also assist in documenting and transforming new configurations and capabilities. Some tasks may include capturing future business and winning short and long term studies that will propel NGAS to obtain new programs in 2024 and beyond. The proposal efforts are responsible for developing the Global Surveillance Division Reference Architecture integrating open mission architecture (OMS), common OMS architecture (COMSA), Joint All Domain Command and Control (JADC2) and other enterprise capabilities. In addition, the candidate will also model mission threads, scenarios, capabilities, weapon integration interfaces, CONOPS related views, and related items. This is an exciting position whereby you will be exposed to state of the art technology and building the next generation unmanned air systems (UAS).

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) discipline and 6 years of experience; 3 years with Master’s Degree; 0 years with PhD
  • Experience with basic systems engineering toolset and languages (e.g. DOORS, Cameo, UML, SysML)
  • Experience with DoDAF standards and methods
  • Active Top Secret Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Knowledge/familiar with Mission Engineering, ABMS, JADC2, Autonomy, GRA, Common Products, COMSA, OMS/UCI, OAM, Digital Engineering
  • Knowledge/familiar with weapons integration, Air Vehicle, Fixed Wing, Tilt Rotor, VTOL, etc..
  • Familiar with airworthiness, cybersecurity, exportability, mission and vehicle systems
