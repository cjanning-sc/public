Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for an LO Applicator to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in El Segundo, CA. The selected candidate will paint the aircraft's interior, exterior, and parts. Essential Functions: 

Essential Functions: 

  • Prepares for painting by masking, stripping, and washing aircraft sections and components.
  • Blends and mixes paints, inks, and coatings to proper spraying consistency.
  • Must be able to perform detailed touch-ups.
  • Coordinates processes for painting production parts.
  • Required ability to be certified for a half & full-face respirator. 

This position's standard work schedule is a 9/80A schedule 1st shift 05:00am – 2:42 PM or 2nd shift 2:00 PM to 11:30 PM.

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School diploma or GED
  • Minimum of 9 months of experience painting/surface prep experience in an aerospace, automotive, military, manufacturing and/or maintenance environment OR Must have a minimum of 2.0 or higher GPA upon completion of A&P certificate program
  • Must be able to perform moderately strenuous physical and repetitious work, including: bending, stooping, kneeling, lifting to 25 pounds, and working overhead
  • Must be able to access small confined spaces and work in an enclosed environment
  • The ability to pass the Special Medical/Respiratory Exam.
  • Active DOD Secret Clearance with Background investigation completed within the last (5) Years
