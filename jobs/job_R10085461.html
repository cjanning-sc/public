ACME Engineering Space Systems, Payload and Ground Systems Division (PGSD) is seeking a Scrum Master with the desire to champion Scrum practices and continuous improvement to support our Remote Sensing Programs (RSP) Business Unit Software team to help mentor projects and programs in their adoption and execution of Agile practices.  The Scrum master will provide leadership and oversight of the Agile Framework to ensure operational excellence in product deliveries. 

The selected candidate will provide guidance, coaching, mentoring, and help educate the team in Scrum, ensuring Agile process is being followed.  The Scrum Master will help remove impediments and foster an environment for high-performing team dynamics, continuous flow, and relentless improvement. They will facilitate team events and functions such as team planning, team execution, and team demonstrations. This position is in Chicago, IL.

Most of the work will have to be conducted on-site.

While not all positions will require a clearance to start, all positions are contingent on the candidate’s ability to obtain and hold a Top Secret/SCI clearance.

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Lead the efforts of developing Scaled Agile engineering and management best practices, metrics, and audit practices

  • Conduct Scrum Team training, product backlog and retrospective training, Lean Agile development training

  • Support software technical disciplines on a Scrum Team

  • Facilitate Agile ceremonies

  • Collect and analyze related metrics for trends and indicators, facilitate iteration/increment planning, provide Agile coaching and mentoring at all levels within the organization

  • Lead the iterative solution lifecycle from executable architecture to maintenance, including concept development, iterative design, production, delivery and deployment

  • Understand Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe)

  • Regular interaction with the customer

Basic Qualifications Principal Scrum Master:

  • Bachelor's degree in STEM discipline with 5+ years of related experience, or Master's degree with 3+ years of related experience, or Ph.D.

  • Experience in Scrum Agile framework

  • Experience mentoring individuals on Agile practices

  • Minimum of 2 years working as Scrum Master

  • Practical experience of servant leadership within agile teams

  • Thorough knowledge of agile principles, practices, and methods, particularly Scrum

  • Excellent organizational and facilitation skills

  • Self-starting mentality and willingness to take ownership of difficult challenges

  • Desire to drive change, implement new ways of working and think "out of the box"

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Top Secret clearance with SCI

  • Experience Atlassian tool suite (Jira, Confluence)

Preferred Qualifications

  • Active DoD Top Secret clearance with SCI

  • Hold a recognized certification as a Scrum Master such as Professional Scrum Master I (PSM I) or Certified Scrum Master (CSM)

  • Experience administering Atlassian tool suite (Jira, Confluence)
