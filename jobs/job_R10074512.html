ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Systems Engineer. This position will be located in Detroit, UT and will support the Sentinel (GBSD) program. As a Systems Engineer on this team, you will support a team of engineers to define, develop, decompose, analyze, verify, and validate requirements for weapon system. In this role, you will be supporting program leadership and the customer to define and maintain the technical baseline. Ultimately, you will support the program through maturity and milestones to achieve the mission by ensuring teams are aware of the compliance requirements they will need to meet.

Candidates should be highly motivated engineers who can work both independently and in team environments.

What you’ll get to do: 

  • Define, manage, and track requirement decomposition through our requirements database.
  • Interface with multiple teams to help define successful processes and make recommendations for how to adapt.
  • Define metrics and help gather information to report up to leadership on the status of requirements flow-down.
  • Provide innovative solutions to complex technical problems
