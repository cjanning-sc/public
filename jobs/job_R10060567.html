As the Australian Defence Force and Government agencies explore new capabilities and technologies, there has never been a better time for collaborative innovators to join us developing our extraordinary portfolio of offerings in Satellite Communications Infrastructure, Space Technology and Battle Management Systems with the full backing and encouragement of our parent company.

  • System Architecture and Design Patterns
  • System Integration
  • Modelling and Simulation
  • Interface Design and Management
