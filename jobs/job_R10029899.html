ACME Engineering’s Aeronautics Systems sector is hiring a Manager 3, Capture / Proposal Management to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in either Philadelphia, PA or Columbus, OH.

Our Capture and Proposal Managers lead teams across the sector to ensure successful, compliant, and on time proposal submissions. The proposals we support include top sector priority wins. We are seeking an experienced proposal manager with the skills, vision, and people skills to execute highly effective proposal efforts with a multifaceted team of proposal participants. Capture / Proposal Managers are delegated decision authority over proposal deliverables and work in collaboration with leads for Technical and Programmatic offerings.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Developing, planning, scheduling, and leading proposal execution.
  • Defining RFP assignments and proposal deliverables to ensure comprehensive flow-down of customer proposal requirements to responsible authors.
  • Leading internal proposal quality reviews to ensure team is on-track to deliver a winning proposal.
  • Ensuring proposal compliance to RFP and configuration control of proposal baseline documents/information.
  • Conducting ongoing risk management addressing volatility in proposal requirements, deadlines, and our offering.
  • Managing development of proposal deliverables, proposal reviews, final proposal production, and on-time delivery to the customer.
  • Providing decision authority for “the book” that is delivered to the customer. 
  • Proposal planning during the pre-proposal phase, and cost-effective execution of the plan through proposal submission.

As a leader, you always act with integrity, and the highest ethical standards characterize everything you do. You enable your team to achieve predictable and balanced results, while satisfying the needs of internal and external partners. You treat all people with respect, and you model personal growth and continuous development. You know how to build an inclusive environment that attracts, retains, and inspires a diverse and engaged team. You develop trusted and valued customer relationships and communicate effectively, so that people throughout the organization feel engaged and connected to their work. You deliver excellence, strive for continuous improvement and respond vigorously to change.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have a Bachelor’s degree AND 9+ years of professional experience that includes a background in Capture Management and/or Proposals, OR a Master’s degree AND 7+ years of experience OR 13 years of experience in-lieu of a degree.
  • Prior leadership expierence
  • Must be able to attain and maintain an active DoD Secret clearance and Special Program Access within a reasonable amount of time as determined by business needs.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Knowledge and understanding of the U.S. Government’s competitive acquisition process
  • Experience conducting RFP review/analysis and developing a response strategy
  • Experience leading and directing the activities of team members through execution of a formal proposal process
  • Experience interfacing with and briefing senior leadership
  • Demonstrated ability in one or more of the following roles: (1) Proposal Manager on a $50M-class competitive proposal or $500M-class non-competitive proposal, (2) Deputy Proposal Manager on a $500M-class competitive proposal, or (3) Volume Captain on $500M-class competitive proposal.
  • Active DoD Top Secret clearance
  • Current Special Program Access (PAR)
