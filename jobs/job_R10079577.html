Job Description

Maintains schedules for material requirements and availability for the manufacture of industrial and commercial products to support production control. Compiles records concerning quantity, cost, and type of material received, shipped, in stock or inventory, and/or in production. Expedites flow of materials, parts, and assemblies within or between departments in accordance with production and shipping schedules. Assists in determining possible and actual shortages may initiate action to correct these deficiencies. Uses knowledge of production, procurement, and engineering departments to alert proper authorities regarding potential problems and appropriate remedial action. Estimates and logs production rate and time expenditures and may be required to establish sequence and lead time of each operation to meet shipping dates.

Responsibilities

  • Tracks inventory levels and monitors inventory in WIP, On-Hold, MRB & In-Inspection locations
  • Works with material planning to ensure materials are ordered in time to support Scheduling & Operations needs.
  • Issues scrapped and OBS material through MRP/ERP systems.

Basic Qualifications

  • High School diploma or equivalent and 2 years additional education and/or related experience
  • Material Expediting
  • Utilizes ERP (Costpoint) & EWI (Solumina) system to track parts through production to meet customer demand and set priorities for Work In Process (WIP)
  • Position requires 80% of time on shop floor coordinating and controlling product flow and 20% of time updating systems to ensure accurate records in systems.
  • This position requires the ability to perform moderate strenuous physical and repetitious work to include: bending, stooping, kneeling, carrying tools, lifting up to 35 pounds and working overhead
  • Ability to work B shift (6am-6pm Mon-Thurs) OR ability to work C shift (6am to 6pm Friday-Sunday)

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with the following software: CostPoint, Solumina, Sync Manufacturing
