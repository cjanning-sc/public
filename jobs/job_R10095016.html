The Program Hardware Tracking & Item Management (PHT&IM) Team located in Rolling Meadows, IL has a challenging opportunity for a driven, multitalented, team-oriented individual to support Program Hardware Tracking efforts for a specific program. This position is responsible for leading PHT efforts in coordinating and managing asset inventory, utilization, forecasting, and shipment facilitation in support of program requirements. The candidate must be able to effectively communicate with both internal and external customers.

Position is hybrid with option to work remotely. This will mostly be remote work, however candidate will be required to come into the office as needed to support business needs. Candidates must be located near the Rolling Meadows site in order to be considered for this role.

What We Can Offer You:

Northrop Grumman provides a comprehensive benefits package and a work environment that encourages your growth and supports the mutual success of our people and our company. Northrop Grumman benefits give you the flexibility and control to choose the benefits that make the most sense for you and your family.   Your benefits will include the following:

  • Health Plan

  • Savings Plan

  • Paid Time Off

  • Additional benefits

  • Education Assistance

  • Training and Development

  • 9/80 Schedule at Many Sites

Specific responsibilities include:

- Serve as liaison between internal and external customer for managing and communicating the status of assets

- Interact with lab user community to provide equipment necessary for engineering test activities

- Maintain accurate program asset records in supply database and deliver contractual output reports

- Track, manage, and report on internal assets used at NGC and assets shipped to external locations

- Facilitate external asset shipments to support customer needs

- Work closely with Program Demand Coordinator to assign supply to fulfill internal test asset demands

- Train asset users on asset management requirements and enforce adherence to tools and processes (Reservation Tool, Tableau Dashboards, Hardware Move Request Forms, Location Scanning, etc.)

- Support physical and data audit processes to drive data integrity and asset user accountability practices

- Support contract closeout activities

- Continuously work to improve PHT processes that align with contractual requirements

Basic Requirements:

- Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 3 years of related experience or Master’s degree with a minimum of 1 year of related experience

- Must have the ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret Security Clearance

- Experience with data processing, analysis, and metrics in MS Excel or other applications

- Experience with business communications

Preferred Requirements:

- Demonstrated experience with inventory analysis

- Demonstrated success with process improvement

- Demonstrated ability to collaborate cross-functionally

- Presentation experience - developing slides and presenting content to a group
