ACME Engineering Mission Systems (NGMS) is seeking candidates for the position of Systems Engineering Manager 2 in the Amherst Systems business unit within the Navigation, Targeting, and Survivability division.  This position is located in Buffalo, NY.

What You'll Get To Do:

This is a dual-role technical manager position.  Management duties will require tasking and coordination of approximately 6-10 engineers, and process and resource coordination with functional homeroom management. The position will also have 75% of his/her time allocated to technical responsibilities. This candidate will demonstrate a broad skill set that is balanced between interpersonal, leadership, and technical capabilities. He/she must be a self-starter, able to efficiently operate in a team environment, and collaborate across the sector business areas as required to accomplish the team goals. The candidate will work closely with other managers, and engineering disciplines.  The manager will be required to communicate with internal customers as well as external customers and suppliers. He/she must be able to work as part of a multifunctional team working within schedule and budget constraints.

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Support/lead project execution over the whole product lifecycle, with foremost emphasis on meeting or exceeding customer and stakeholder requirements  
  • Revises and manages both original and revised specification/interface definition document and expedites documents through signature approval process.
  • Identify risks, suggest mitigations, track defects, develop resolutions, and track and report status
  • Supports trade studies, system performance assessments, system design assessments, timelines and concept of operations development.
  • Supports system design, data analyses, interface verification, testing, failure/anomaly resolution and mission operations.
  • Supports the testing and/or evaluation/validation of one or more components of a system or subsystem analytical model or actual system implementation.
  • Provide technical contributions on assigned programs, including subject matter expert reviews for major milestone events (SRR, PDR, CDR, TRR, etc.).
  • Support MBSE and Agile implementation when applicable
  • Facilitate and maintain good working relationships with internal and external stakeholders
  • Oversight of cost, schedule, and technical quality for SEIT execution on supported programs
  • Technical lead for proposal (Formal and ROM) efforts including Basis of Estimate generation
  • Create and support department policies, procedures, and budgets
  • Provide flow-down and direction from upper management and the organization
  • Support to hiring, staffing and workload forecasting including identification and training of skills required to execute on programs
  • Provide employee mentoring, career development, and training opportunities
  • Management and execution of employee performance review processes
  • Creating an inclusive and engaging environment that promotes employee retention

Basic Qualifications for Systems Engineering Manager 2 :

  • Bachelor’s Degree in STEM, or related technical field and a minimum of 8 years of relevant engineering experience (6 years with a MS degree)
  • Demonstrated leadership experience
  • Prior experience in a lead role in a technical engineering capacity
  • Ability to work independently and within a team environment
  • Experience with system integration
  • High proficiency in technical problem solving
  • Proven communication and presentation skills.
  • U.S. Citizenship with the ability to obtain and maintain a minimum of a Department of Defense (DoD) Secret Security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Prior functional management experience (executing performance appraisals, staffing allocations, systems process leadership, and career goals development)
  • Master's degree in a relevant technical or management specialty
  • Familiarity with model-based engineering
  • Experience with creation of system specifications, requirements traceability and verification
  • Proven demonstrated leadership skills and the ability to work effectively in a team environment.
  • Demonstrated capability to make difficult decisions that impact team direction and team performance outcomes
  • Experience in activities related to Electronic Warfare systems development, use, testing, support
