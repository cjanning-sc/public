Duties:

  • Perform in-process and final inspections on product throughout the SMF work centers (Receiving, Manufacturing, or Integration and Test) and perform product build documentation review and end-item data package review. 
  • Perform inspections on both Electrical and Mechanical components at the piece part level as well as the assembly and sub-assembly level.
  • Identify discrepant conditions and generate nonconformance reports for discrepancies discovered during inspection processes
  • Practically apply process specifications and use applicable workmanship standards during everyday job functions.
  • Perform reviews of product build documentation for configuration audit to determine if as-built meets as-designed.
  • Act as safety representative during critical and hazardous operations for assigned spacecraft.

Basic Qualifications:

  • High school diploma/equivalent
  • U.S. citizen
  • Must have an active or current Top-Secret clearance.
  • Minimum of 3 years of related experience
  • Must be able to read engineering documentation and inspect to drawings and parts lists, inspection plans, and supplemental instructions.
  • Willingness to work off shift activities with occasional overtime, weekend, and Holiday support as required.
  • Willingness to support 2nd shift (if required), and occasionally (though not often) 3rd shift support for short periods of Spacecraft testing.
  • Willingness to travel (though not often) in support of S/C level activities is required.
  • Must be able to use Microsoft applications and have the ability to learn new computer programs/applications.
  • Basic technical writing skills are required.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience working in a TS secured environment utilizing an active TS/SCI.

  • Working knowledge of TipQA and/or Costpoint database applications.
  • Knowledge in how to read schematics and wirelists is a plus.
  • Previous experience working in ISO-certified company and using Process Specifications or Military Standards.
  • Active workmanship certifications from accreditation/standards companies, such as IPC
