Job Description

As part of Northrop Grumman’s EMEA (Europe, Middle East and Africa) IT Solutions team working within the Infrastructure team, this role will be a pinnacle part of supporting and developing the EMEA AWS infrastructure and service offering. The future growth of this role will encompass the development of an Azure and/or other cloud solutions. This role includes but not limited to being the first point of contact for any tickets and issues being logged, working with end users and senior engineers to quickly resolve issues, management of users and groups in AWS IAM/SSO, and deploying new AWS accounts using the AWS Service Catalogue.

Your Responsibilities: 

1. Day to day operational activities to support and develop our AWS environment and it’s end users

2. Work with the Senior Engineers to research and evaluate AWS services/solutions to enhance IT services and offering

3. Work in collaboration with the customer to develop ideas and requests for features within AWS

4. Work as part of a team to research, develop, and build upon AWS/Azure offerings in the business

5. Opportunity to review, recommend, build out cutting edge cloud technologies

Your Experience:

1. AWS Certified Solutions Architect or greater (essential)

2. Experience with AWS services such as VPC, Transit Gateway, Security Hub, Guard Duty, Inspector, automation services, IaaS

3. Experience with Azure services including M365, AD Sync, E3/E5 tenants

4. Ability to collaborate with key stakeholders and innovate recommended solutions

5. Keen to work towards AWS Specialist certification for Security or Networking
