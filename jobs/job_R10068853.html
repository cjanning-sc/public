Staff FPGA design engineer
R10068853
1st Shift (United States of America)

The Northrop Grumman Mission Systems, Digital Technologies Group, is seeking FPGA Design Engineers to be involved in full life-cycle product development of Radio Frequency Electronic Warfare (RFEW), Infrared Counter Measures (IRCM) and Advanced Target & Surveillance (ATS) Systems. You will be involved in requirements analysis and system architecture, coding, test bench design, verification, synthesis and place and route using state of the art tools and techniques.

This position is located at our Rolling Meadows, IL and will be filled at the Staff Engineer level:

Basic Qualifications

- B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering (Computer or Electrical), or related field

- Minimum of 14 years of relevant professional work experience OR Master's degree with 12 years of work experience.

- Knowledge of HDL (VHDL/Verilog) and HVL (SystemVerilog), SystemVerilog Assertions (SVA), UVM for FPGAs, CPLDs and/or ASICs

- Ability to work in teams and communicate clearly across various levels of engineers

- Ability to translate system performance and operational specifications into programmable logic requirements, design, and test specifications

- Must have interim Secret clearance prior to start. Once started, must have the ability to obtain and maintain a Secret DoD security clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:

- Experience in HDL (VHDL/Verilog) and HVL (SystemVerilog), SystemVerilog Assertions (SVA), UVM for PLDs, FPGAs, and/or ASICs

- Experience with Electronic Design Automation (EDA) Tools: Mentor Graphics ModelSim/QuestaSim, Synplicity, Xilinx ISE/ Vivado, Xilinx EDK, Xilinx System Generator, Altera Quartus, Altera SOPC Builder, Altera Qsys, DSP Builder

- Expertise achieving timing closure on FPGA designs and generating test benches

- Experience in Microprocessor (PowerPC, Intel–based, SOC) designs

- Implementation of Digital Signal or Image Processing algorithms on FPGAs

- Experience developing in MATLAB/Simulink

- Experience with high level programming languages (C/C++)

- A strong team player who also has the ability to work independently

- Active DOD Secret Clearance
