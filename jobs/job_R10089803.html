Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking a dynamic, energetic individual for the position of Principal Pricing Analyst (Level 3) or Senior Principal Pricing Analyst (Level 4) to support the Strategic Initiatives Team in the Pricing & Estimating Department.  Preferably, the position will be located in Baltimore, MD, but will consider remote location. This individual must be able to function in a fast-paced proposal environment, as well as collaborate with business management counterparts and capture/proposal teams to assist in the independent assessment of pricing and win strategies of high-visibility captures. In addition, the position will provide cost volume and pricing & estimating support to strategic proposals as required.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Participate in the Corporate & Sector level Independent Cost Evaluation (ICE) process

  • Support risk and opportunity assessment, quantification, and analysis during the proposal process

  • Identify and evaluate proposal risk issues in cost estimates, terms and conditions, and all other business management related functions

  • Provide strategic proposal support during the Pre-RFP stages, RFP execution, and RFP Cost Volume development

  • Develop cost models to support proposal efforts and overall pricing strategy

  • Research and analyze historical data to understand cost trends that can be applied to proposal estimates

  • Present and brief estimating and pricing issues and risks to senior and executive leadership

  • Grow the awareness and knowledge base of risk and confidence assessment training to multiple sector locations and program teams

  • Support pricing activities for sole source and competitive proposals as required

  • Support proposal Independent Assessment process

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Business/Finance/Accounting/Mathematics or related area

  • Principal Pricing Analyst (Level 3) - 6+ Years of combined experience in Pricing/Finance/Contracts/Business or 4+ years with a Master’s Degree

  • Senior Principal Pricing Analyst (Level 4) - 10+ Years of combined experience in Pricing/Finance/Contracts/Business or 8+ years with a Master’s Degree

  • Current Secret Level Security Clearance

  • Comprehensive knowledge of pricing, contracts, and business management

  • Ability to prioritize effectively and complete work timely in a deadline driven environment

  • Advanced MS Excel and PowerPoint skills

  • Knowledge of FAR/DFARS and other applicable government regulations.

  • Excellent communication and coordination skills

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Advanced Degree (Master's)

  • Risk and quantitative analysis skills

  • Active Secret Security Clearance

  • Experience supporting Cost Volume Teams, ensuring RFP compliance

  • Proficient in use of ProPricer

  • Familiarity with Northrop Grumman Policies and Procedures
