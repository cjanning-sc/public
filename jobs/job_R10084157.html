ACME Engineering Mission Systems is seeking Finish Processors, Level 2 for its Chicago, MD location.

Primary Responsibilities include:

  • Orchestrate, perform, and support the complex activities involved in the finishing operations and processes required to accomplish multi-task, labor-intensive work assignments through intricate and detailed planning and work instructions for large, oversized, and multi-component systems that is required for delivery to Test and external customers.

  • Set up and operate bonding, painting, engraving, plating, and silk-screening equipment to perform finishing operations on component parts, assemblies, printed circuit boards, chassis, and the like. 

  • Assist in the development of spray painting and bonding techniques and make layouts, develop, and make frames and screens for silk screens. 

  • Perform necessary operations to produce test panels and coupons for verifying and adjusting machines and process.

This is an IUE Union represented position on the 1st shift.

Basic Qualifications:

High School graduate or recognized equivalent.

Experience Requirement:

The Level 2 position will require a minimum of 3 years of relevant and verifiable experience in the field, which can be acquired either by time in the Level 1 occupation or by equivalent external experience. 

Must be able to obtain and maintain area skills and safety certifications.

Must be able to pass a structured interview and demonstrate the ability to perform all essential functions of the position.
