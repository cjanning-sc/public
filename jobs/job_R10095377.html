ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has multiple openings for Mechanical Engineers or Principal Mechanical Engineers to join our team of qualified diverse individuals. In Vehicle Engineering, we develop manned and unmanned aircraft platforms for all programs in the Aeronautics Systems Sector.

These positions will be in Cincinnati, CA and have the possibility to work multiple shifts:

  • Monday - Thursday on a 4 day - 10 hours/day schedule. Alternate hours or shifts may be discussed
  • Friday, Saturday, Sunday on a 3 day - 10 hours/day schedule (you will be paid for a 40-hour work week). Alternate hours or shifts may be discussed

We are looking for Engineers to develop “design for producibility” solutions to difficult problems that are thorough, imaginative, practical, and consistent with program objectives. In the Subsystems Design group, we develop solutions to difficult problems that are thorough, imaginative, practical, and consistent with program objectives. In your day-to-day activities you will participate in the development and review of completed models, drawings, and repairs for accuracy, clarity, and completeness, in order to provide evaluation of engineering products for conformity to standards, procedures, and specifications. You will also supervise design solutions from initial concept to final implementation and give concise presentations and explanations of subsystem design issues/solutions to internal management and customer representatives.

These positions can be filled at either the Mechanical Engineer OR Principal Mechanical Engineer level.

Basic Qualifications for Mechanical Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field with 2 years of relevant experience OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field with 0 years of relevant experience.
  • Proficiency in a 3D CAD related software in the development of aircraft parts and assemblies.
  • Working knowledge of one of the following subsystems: electrical, hydraulics, fuels, fluids, mechanisms, or propulsion systems and/or experience with mechanical routing.
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance. This clearance must be obtained prior to employment and must obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to starting. This SAP must be obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Mechanical Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field with 5 years of relevant experience OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field with 3 years of relevant experience OR a PhD in a STEM field with 0 years of experience.
  • Proficiency in a 3D CAD related software in the development of aircraft parts and assemblies.
  • Working knowledge of one of the following subsystems: electrical, hydraulics, fuels, fluids, mechanisms, or propulsion systems and/or experience with mechanical routing.
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance. This clearance must be obtained prior to employment and must obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to starting. This SAP must be obtained within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active Secret clearance highly preferred.
  • Advanced degree in Mechanical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, or Electrical Engineering.
  • Experience using Unigraphics Nx or CATIA V5
  • Experience with Model Based Engineering (MBE) or 3-D model engineering definition.
  • Experience with HarnesSys tool for schematic design.
  • Experience in the design of bonded composite primary and secondary aircraft structures.
  • Experience working on Special Access Programs
  • Experience working on military aircraft
