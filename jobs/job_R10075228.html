ACME Engineering, located in Promontory, UT, is currently recruiting for a Facilities Electrical Controls Engineer.

Job Summary including Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Designs power, controls and process instrumentation per the NEC including hazardous classified locations.

  • Provide detailed cost estimates for project design and implementation.

  • Troubleshoot industrial electrical equipment.

  • Develop electrical maintenance guidelines for industrial equipment.

  • Integrate and specify electrical equipment for robotics, PLC’s, servo drives, instrumentation and HVAC equipment.

  • Program Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC) to control a variety of processes, machine tools, and robotics.  Allen Bradley PLC and Rockwell Software programming experience preferred.

  • Expertise to develop electrical schematics that are logical, well organized and require little to no redlines.

  • Expertise designing panel layouts that minimize space and comply with manufacturer’s specifications.

  • Design, specify, and program variable frequency drives (VFD) for all types of motor control applications.

  • Work with maintenance craftsmen to troubleshoot.

  • Project management and cost estimating.

  • Provide Detailed Project Designs in AutoCAD drawing format.

Position Requirements:

  • BS in Engineering with an emphasis in electrical/automation.

  • 2 years minimum industrial facility electrical design experience.

  • Industrial power and control design experience.

  • PLC systems and programming experience.

  • AutoCAD drafting ability.

  • Ability to work in the field, climb ladders, work outside and work in congested areas.

  • Can be hired at a higher level with BS and 5+ years of experience
