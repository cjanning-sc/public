Northrop Grumman is seeking an Engineering Product Manager (PM) in Rolling Meadows, IL within the DevOps Solutions Group

The selected candidate will be responsible for leading the development of Software products and/or services for the organization. The position includes responsibility for all business aspects of the product/service, including business development, customer interfacing, project management and road mapping, in addition to managing cost and schedule. The position requires strong leadership and communication skills in addition to multi-discipline engineering understanding.

Responsibilities may include developing Product vision, Use Case and requirements development with customers.  In addition, the PM will be responsibilities for managing active contracts with customers which include project management responsibilities.  A person in this position ensures that the product is being architected to meet current and future customer needs, during the development, production, test and sustainment of products for our customers and that the program and product is compliant with company policy, procedures, and quality standards.  The PM will also be responsible for working with their teams to identify modernization and investment opportunities for their specific product/services.

This position can be filled at either the Software/ Principal Software level

Basic Qualifications for Software level:

- BS in STEM field with 2+ years of experience or MS degree in STEM.

- >2 years of experience in engineering development environment

- >2 years of experience leading/managing engineering development

- Strong communication skills

- Candidates should understand the various processes tailored to engineering disciplines.

- Candidates should have background and understanding in various tools and techniques associated with cost/schedule management and have experience leading efforts through the entire engineering lifecycle.

- Must have interim secret clearance prior to start. Once started, must have the ability to obtain and maintain DoD Secret clearance.

Basic Qualifications for Principal level:

- BS in STEM field with 5+ years of experience or MS degree in STEM with 3+ years of experience.

- >3 years of experience in engineering development environment

- >3 years of experience leading/managing engineering development

- Strong communication skills

- Candidates should understand the various processes tailored to engineering disciplines.

- Candidates should have background and understanding in various tools and techniques associated with cost/schedule management and have experience leading efforts through the entire engineering lifecycle.

- Must have interim secret clearance prior to start. Once started, must have the ability to obtain and maintain DoD Secret clearance.

Preferred Qualifications:

- Software experience

- MS, or higher in an engineering or closely related discipline, and/or BS followed by MBA, MPD (Masters, Product Development), or MEM (Masters, Engineering Management)

- Current Active secret or higher clearance

- Prior Engineering Product Development leadership experience

- EVMS, MS-Project, Risk Management, NG EPM Curriculum, or equivalents.

- Track record of successful project proposals, startups and completions.

- Experience with the development and/or test of electronic warfare, EO/IR, laser, or related defense systems

- Ideal candidates will have >5 years of experience in development engineering.
