ACME Engineering Aeronautics is currently seeking a motivated and execution-oriented leader to join the Triton Development Program Management team. This position will be located in Cleveland, Ohio. The Triton Enterprise falls within the Global Surveillance division of ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Directs all phases of programs from inception through completion.
  • Responsible for the product quality, cost, schedule and technical performance of contracted activities.
  • Coordinates the preparation of proposals, business plans, proposal work statements and specifications, operating budgets and financial terms/conditions of contract.
  • Participates in the negotiation of contract and contract changes.
  • Acts as primary customer contact for program activities, leading program review sessions with customer to discuss cost, schedule, and technical performance.
  • Establishes milestones and monitors adherence to master plans and schedules, identifies program problems and obtains solutions, such as allocation of resources or changing contractual specifications.
  • Directs the work of employees assigned to the program from technical, manufacturing and administrative areas.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in STEM with 9+ years experience in either program management or engineering leadership OR a Master’s degree with 7+ years’ experience
  • Experience with Earned Value Management and demonstrated financial acumen
  • Prior experience leading large multi-functional teams
  • An active DoD secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience managing engineering teams
  • An active DoD top secret clearance
  • Experience successfully managing programs of $50M or greater in the aerospace industry
  • Experience with preparing proposals and negotiating contract and financial terms/conditions
  • Experience managing classified programs
