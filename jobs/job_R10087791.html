Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Principal/Sr. Principal Supply Chain Subcontract Specialist to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in San Diego, CA.

Essential Functions:

  • In support of the Global Surveillance Division portfolio of programs, develops subcontract specifications, work statements, and terms and conditions for the procurement of specialized materials, equipment, and/or services.

  • Prepares bid packages, conducts bidders conferences, analyzes and evaluates proposals, negotiates subcontract provisions, selects or recommends subcontractors, writes awards, and administers resulting subcontracts.

  • Negotiates and coordinates additions, deletions, or modifications to subcontracts.

  • Responsible for source selection from contract proposal to execution phases. Coordinates and collaborates with key stakeholders such as contracts, engineering, quality, supplier development, planning, operations, program and end-users. 

  • In conjunction with quality organization, evaluates and monitors supplier quality and reliability, as well as supplier ability to support required delivery schedules. Identifies strategies and opportunities to reduce cost, improve efficiency and manage risk through strategic and targeted negotiations. 

  • Manages performance of subcontracts, ensures compliance with all public law requirements as well as company (and business) policies and procedures including import/export regulations.  

  • Employs sound business practices and assesses supplier performance as part of follow-up activities. 

  • Ensures that appropriate documentation is provided to logistics/transportation & warehouse organization so that proper receipt is recorded. Manages and develops strategic/key suppliers.

  • Reviews and approves supplier payment as needed. 

  • Utilizes process tools such as Lean Six Sigma with suppliers to drive quality, efficiency and continuous improvement.

This position may be filled at a level 3 or level 4

Basic Qualifications: (Principal)

  • Bachelor’s Degree with at least 6 years' experience in supply chain subcontract OR a master’s degree with at least 4 years in supply chain subcontract experience OR High School Diploma/equivalent with at least 10 years of combined experience in Subcontracts, Procurement, Supplier Management, Price Cost Analyst and/or Contracts

  • Experience with FAR (Federal Acquisition Regulations) and DFARS

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Clearance

Basic Qualifications: (Senior Principal)

  • Bachelor's Degree with 10 years subcontracts, procurement, supply chain experience OR a master's degree with 8 years of applied experience in subcontracts, procurement, contracts and/or other supply chain related field OR 14 years of supply chain subcontract experience in lieu of a degree

  • Experience with FAR (Federal Acquisition Regulations) and DFARS

  • Proficient with Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, and PowerPoint). 

  • Must be able to obtain and Maintain DOD Secret Clearance and Special Programs Access

Preferred Qualifications (Principal)

  • Working knowledge of FAR and DFARS regulations.  

  • Supplier selection/management experience on US Government contract(s). 

  • Subcontracting experience with aircraft production and/or sustainment programs.

  • Experience working within a government approved purchasing system.

  • Familiarity with NGAS Subcontracting terms and conditions.

  • Active DoD clearance Secret or Top Secret

Preferred Qualifications: (Senior Principal)

  • Subcontracting experience with aircraft production and/or sustainment programs.

  • Experience working within a government approved purchasing system.

  • Familiarity with placement and administration of Inter Company work orders 

  • Experience with SAP.  

  • Active DoD clearance at a Secret or Top-Secret level. 

  • Working knowledge of FAR and DFARS regulations.  

  • Supplier selection/management experience on US Government contract(s).
