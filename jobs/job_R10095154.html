The ACME Engineering teams in Commerce, CA has an opening for a Maintenance Technician Level 1 / 2 to perform repairs and maintenance on machines and equipment in a manufacturing environment. 

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Conducts routine, periodic, or special inspections to determine repair and maintenance work necessary to prevent breakdowns of facilities, machinery, and equipment
  • Perform all maintenance trades as needed to keep facility operational
  • Maintains Preventative Maintenance Database and is responsible for on time completion of defined tasks
  • Repair, construct, install, troubleshoot, and maintain routine electrical systems and equipment
  • Performs EHS inspections; examples include fire extinguisher/eye wash inspections and emergency lights
  • Install, repair, or replace switch boxes, light bulbs, plugs, sockets, light fuses, and extension cords
  • Disassemble motors, generators, and other equipment in preparation for repair
  • Troubleshoot electrical malfunctions, inspect circuits and wiring for shielding and grounding, and test electrical equipment for safety and efficiency
  • Assists in preparing capital projects
  • Close coordination with outside vendors and suppliers
  • Ensure snow removal from doorways and walkways in winter

This requisition may be filled at either a Maintenance Technician level 3 or a Maintenance Technician level 4
