About the role:

At Northrop Grumman, flexibility and performance are the foundation to our success in the numerous programs we run to support our defence and government clients. Our SPA (Special Purpose Aircraft) facility in Canberra is a prime example, and we are now seeking the services of an experienced B1 LAME to meet our expanding business requirements in Canberra.

Maintaining excellence on this highly visible fleet of aircraft is not an easy task and requires a proactive individual with a genuine “can do” attitude.

About you:

Experienced in line and heavy maintenance, you are an aviation professional committed to ensuring maintenance and safety requirements are met. You work well within a team but are happy to work autonomously during travelling engineer requirements. Customer service and communication are key as you work closely with and pivot between technical and non-technical stakeholders.

You are B1 licenced and ideally are B737 or Falcon 7x type rated, however, the willingness to learn is just as favourable.
