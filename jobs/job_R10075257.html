Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Sr. Principal Software Configuration Analyst. This position will be located in either Roy, UT or Huntsville, AL and will support the GBSD program.  This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What you'll get to do:

  • Perform configuration management function on MBSE tools CAMEO and requirements database DOORs.

  • Serves as the system administrator, revision control focal and verification auditor for all updates made within these tools.

  • Provides general Software CM support: develop and maintain software configuration management tools to support configuration identification, control, reporting, and delivery of both internally developed and externally purchased commercial-off-the-shelf (COTS) software products.

  • Performs configuration management and release engineering tasks to ensure new software product operating parameters are documented, comply with standard hardware configurations, and are logistically sustainable.

  • Designs, develops, automates, and maintains productivity tools using programming, database or scripting languages to improve software modeling and development.

  • Designs and implements build procedures that are used to support software product development and use.

  • May develop software configuration standards for company-wide use.

  • May train software developers in the use of configuration management tools and the implementation of software quality standards. Additional position responsibilities:

  • Plans and coordinates preparation of project documentation, such as engineering drawings, production specifications and schedules, and contract modifications, to ensure customer contract requirements are met.

  • Reviews contract to determine documentation required for each phase of project, applying knowledge of engineering and manufacturing processes.

  • Analyzes proposed changes of product design to determine effect on overall product and system. Coordinates modification records for management control.

  • Establishes change orders and prepares for change authorization and documentation by company and subcontractor.

  • Reviews and analyzes released engineering change data and coordinates changes with engineering, quality, support, manufacturing, and engineering data control activities.

  • Represents the organization as a prime technical contact. Interacts with senior internal/ external personnel on significant technical matters often requiring coordination between organizations.

  • Develops technical solutions to complex problems which require the regular use of ingenuity and innovation. Ensures solutions are consistent with organization objectives.

  • Contributes to the development of new concepts, techniques, and standards. Considered expert in field within the organization.

  • Demonstrates the skill and ability to perform complex tasks. Able to communicate effectively and clearly present technical approaches and findings.

  • 9 Years with Bachelors; 7 Years with Masters; 4 Years with PhD; or 4 additional years of relevant experience in lieu of degree.

  • Experience with CPLM and/ or Teamcenter.

  • Experience working in a classified environment.

  • Experience with JIRA and Confluence.
