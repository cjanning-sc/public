ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Structural Engineer / Principal Structural Engineer to join the Flight Systems Team. This position is in Detroit, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Final grade level offered will be based on candidate experience.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will perform basic to advanced structural analysis and support the development of On Board Test Equipment (OBTE). The OBTE package will be integrated into missile system for flight tests. Responsibilities include:

  • Perform structural analysis including static, dynamics, and stability.

  • Create Finite Element Analysis (FEA) models and perform systems and sub-systems structural analysis.

  • Compute margins using appropriate analysis methods.

  • Review of requirements for materials, structure systems & subsystems.

  • Participate and present in internal and external (customer) design reviews.

  • Manage and track multiple priorities while maintaining focus on common project goals.

  • Experience using Finite Element Analysis tools to perform structural and dynamic analysis
