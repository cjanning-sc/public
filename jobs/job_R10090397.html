ACME Engineering Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) has an opening for Maintenance Electrician 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Columbus, CA.

 Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Repairs, constructs, installs, troubleshoots, and maintain routine electrical systems and equipment.
  • Cuts, threads, bends couples, and straps conduit
  • Installs and repairs switch boxes, lighting, plugs, sockets, fuses, ballast, drivers, and extension cords
  • Disassembles motors, generators, and other equipment in preparation for repair
  • Troubleshoots electrical malfunctions, inspects circuits and wiring for shielding and grounding, and tests electrical equipment for safety and efficiency
  • Uses blueprints, drawings, layout, or other specifications to perform routine electrical work
  • Uses electrical hand tools and voltage-or-amperage measuring and recording devices

Qualifications - External

Basic Qualifications: 

  • High School Diploma or GED
  • 2 years of Commercial or Industrial electrical experience in service and repair

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Active SCI Clearance
  • Trade school certificate or degree in Electrical
