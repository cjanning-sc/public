ACME Engineering Defense Systems has an opening for a Sr. Principal Logistics Planner to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. The successful candidate will be a part of the WSTARS program located in the Satellite Ground Mission Planning & Analysis department of the Communications, Integration, and Asset Protection (CIAP) OU.

The candidate will perform logistics acquisition life cycle management for the WSTARS program; assist in the development of the logistics schedules and Logistics CDRLs; review and track logistics milestones, drafts briefings, and review logistics CDRL deliverables for accuracy; act as SCRUM Master for the logistics team.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Milestones include the following: Training Package, Technical Manual (TM), Safety Assessment Report (SAR), Instructor and Key Personnel (IKP) Training, Maintenance Engineering Evaluation (MEE), Validation/Verification, and Log Demo
  • Store and properly mark equipment with proper identification and safety labels; prepare shipping documentation that will be sent with the equipment to the sites, to include the Depot
  • Participate in the Technical Documentation In Progress Reviews (IPR)s
  • Possess the ability to apply these standard logistics engineering principles to complete assignments and handle challenging tasks.
  • Work closely with other Logistics IPTs (Integrated Product Teams), Design Engineering IPTs, Configuration Management, Systems Engineering, and Technical Publications teams.
  • Candidate must possess: Excellent interpersonal skills, with excellent verbal/written communications skills, self-motivated, and understand and exhibit a strong team work ethic and approach.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Functional area or related subject with a minimum of ten (10) years of experience in military acquisition logistics management (including development of technical manuals, training packages, equipment fielding planning and documentation) OR 8 Years with Masters.
  • Minimum of five (5) years’ experience in Military acquisition logistics management
  • Experience managing logistics CDRLs (i.e. development of technical manuals, training packages, equipment fielding planning and documentation)
  • Active DoD Secret Clearance
  • Ability to travel both CONUS & OCONU

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Military Acquisition Logistics experience in SATCOM
  • Experience in logistics support with knowledge of the ARMY regulations is a plus
  • DAU Life Cycle Logistics Level 1 Certification
  • Certified SCRUM Master
