The Payload and Ground Systems organization within the ACME Engineering Space Systems pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter. Come be a part of our mission!

We offer phenomenal learning opportunities, exposure to a wide variety of projects and customers, and a very friendly collaborative workplace. We are looking for self-motivated, proactive, and goal-oriented people to help us grow our services and become even better at what we do. Does this sound like you?

Our Employee Resource Groups (ERGs) provide benefits for the member, our leaders and the company. Our ERGs offer opportunities to be a friend, be active, be a volunteer, be a leader, to be recognized and to be yourself! Every ERG is inclusive of all employees! 

Our diverse portfolio of programs means there are endless paths to cultivate your career. We are well-known for our inclusive environment, as well as our excellent work/life balance. We also offer exceptional benefits/healthcare, a 9/80 schedule, and a great 401K matching program.

Support the activities of the Consolidated Operations & Anomaly Support Team (COAST) and Development Contract Software Program as a member of the Software Configuration Management (SCM) team’s Data Management activities. Tasks include:

  • Support SDRB/SCCB activities
  • Maintain and updated Discrepancy Report (DR) databases
  • Produce multiple weekly agendas and minutes
  • Manage meetings
  • Scheduling, running, sometimes flipping charts for others
  • Timely responsiveness to a high volume of email traffic
  • Formal document control and release
  • Assisting Software Project personnel in the release of their documents
  • Maintain and publish build plan weekly
  • Contribute to process improvement as we transition to new, more Agile, ways of accomplishing the goals of SCM
  • Contribute to training others in SCM Data Management tasks and in helping software program personnel better understand those processes

Ideal candidate is a self-starter who is capable of prioritizing to keep track of a variety of requests and tasks, and who knows when to ask for guidance. This team practices the “work-life-balance” concept, but there will be times when extended work hours will be necessary to stay ahead of deadlines. You will likely be challenged, but you will be joining a team of knowledgeable and dedicated professionals who will not hesitate to work with you to help you ensure the success of your efforts.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS

  • Current DoD Top Secret clearance with the ability to obtain an SCI clearance to start
  • Bachelor’s degree + 2 years related experience, master’s degree OR 6 years of experience in lieu of degree
  • Solid proficiency with Microsoft Office products (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Visio)
  • Advanced abilities in Word, able to comfortably work with tables, figures, automatic numbering, TOC, PDF conversion

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

  • SCI clearance
  • Hands on experience with Atlassian Tools - Jira and Confluence
  • Experience or familiarity with ClearQuest
  • Hands on experience with basic Linux commands
  • Previous experience on a Software development or O&M program
