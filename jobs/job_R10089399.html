Duties:

  • Perform in-process and final inspections on product throughout the SMF work centers (Receiving, Manufacturing, or Integration and Test) and perform product build documentation review and end-item data package review.

  • Uphold quality standards and workmanship practices in assigned work centers by learning/practicing process specifications and employing quality workmanship standards during everyday job functions.
  • Assist in developing training material for assigned work center QA group.

  • Perform in-depth reviews of product build documentation for configuration audit to determine if as-built meets as-designed.
