1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Defense Systems Sector has a need for a Software Engineering Manager 2 at its campus in Warner Robins, GA. The successful candidate will demonstrate strong technical ability and leadership skills to achieve sustainable top performance. The candidate will have a track record as an effective communicator and problem solver who is able to develop and maintain good working relationships with internal and external stakeholders. This candidate will demonstrate a commitment to ethics and integrity, and the capability to innovate. The candidate needs the ability to build diverse and inclusive teams with high levels of engagement, including mentoring and coaching.

This position will lead a functional team of software engineers that support a diverse portfolio of programs. Our engineering teams provide world-class engineering solutions to the sustainment and modernization of mission systems for our customers.  Our programs include development, sustainment, and IR&D programs for systems that include aircraft self-protection systems, radar, and avionics. This position is a dual role position encompassing both technical leadership and performance as well as people leadership.

Position Responsibilities:

Technical Leadership and Performance (~70% of candidate workload):

  • Candidate will contribute technically to our current software programs. Technical responsibilities will include:

    • Develop, modify, and maintain software using software engineering best practices and standards

    • Participate in the full life cycle of software development, to include requirements development, modeling and design, software development, unit to CSCI testing, integration, and formal system testing

    • Apply technical expertise in solving complex software and integration problems

  • Candidate will be a thought leader for software engineering practices and modern software strategies for both their team and the organization

  • Candidate will assist software engineering department manager in reviewing proposals, white papers, and other new business initiatives

People Leadership (~30% of candidate workload):

  • Lead a software engineering team that supports multiple programs

  • Assist software engineering department manager in providing financial oversight of engineering budgets assigned to your software team

  • Responsible for direct management of staff and providing mentorship and general direction

  • Assist software engineering department manager in executing, updating, and maintaining department procedures

  • Support organizational engineering initiatives

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science (B.S) degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or an equivalent STEM related discipline

  • Minimum of 9 years of direct relevant technical experience with B.S degree; Minimum of 7 years of direct relevant technical experience with M.S degree; Minimum of 5 years of direct relevant technical experience with PhD

  • Proficient in C/C++ and/or Ada programming language

  • Proficient in all areas of formal software lifecycle process from requirements to formal testing

  • Proven leadership skills as a people and technical leader, and ability to work effectively in a collaborative team environment

  • Able to obtain and maintain a Secret clearance per business requirements. US Citizenship is a requirement. Must be able to work at location in Warner Robins, GA.

Preferred Qualifications:  

  • Experience as an engineering manager or software engineering lead

  • Experience in object-oriented analysis and design

  • Experience programming in Ada

  • Experience using JIRA/Bitbucket and Smart Git

  • Experience working in a fast pace, agile environment with the ability to work on a cross-functional Scrum team focused on quality and capability delivery

  • Experience with Electronic Warfare (EW) principles

  • Active Secret Security Clearance
