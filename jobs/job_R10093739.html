We have an opening for a Staff Systems Engineering Network Architect to join our qualified, diverse team. We are seeking a team member who wants to solve interesting, complex problems to help protect our nation. We are a trusted mission payload and ground system provider with extensive experience providing end-to-end mission solutions from concept through operations and support. The team you will support is located at our Space Park campus in Redondo Beach, CA.

What you’ll get to do?

The Payload and Ground Systems Division (PGSD) has an immediate need for a Staff Systems Engineering Network Architect to lead and develop next generation ground system solutions. In this role, you will determine customer needs and develop approaches, implementations, components, interfaces, identify standards and constraints, civil requirements, and security needs. You will survey the industry for awareness of customer needs and alternative systems.  You will lead network architecture design that stems from system requirements, decomposed across multiple levels, aimed to represent the ground capabilities, that have a major impact on the mission.  In this role, you will also be interfacing directly with the Software, Hardware, Operations, Integration and Test teams, as well as program and project leadership to define and maintain the technical baseline. Additionally, you will lead trade studies, technology development, and subcontractor teams as well as lead technical and cost proposals, and write white papers describing the offerings. Ultimately, it is your responsibility to drive the program through maturity and milestones to achieve the mission, ensuring everything is synchronized. 

Network Architect Responsibilities:

  • Maintains smooth operation of local area, world-wide and virtual private networks (VPN).
  • Plans, evaluates, and coordinates installation and/or reconfiguration of hardware and software elements of data and/or voice communication network. Maintains technical expertise in all areas of networks and computer hardware and software interconnection and interfacing, such as routers, multiplexers, firewalls, hubs, bridges, gateways, etc.
  • May propose solutions to management to ensure all communications requirements based on future needs and current usage, configuring such solutions to optimize cost savings.
  • Prepares or ensures that appropriate network documentation exists, including operational instructions.
  • Provides regular monitoring and network analysis regarding short- and long-range planning for in-house systems.
  • May coordinate third-party maintenance for network equipment.
  • May design network or portion of network that include selection of hardware and software packages

Basic Qualifications:

  • Active DoD Top Secret and SCI clearance
  • 14 years of relevant experience with a Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM discipline; 12 years of relevant experience with a Masters' degree; 9 years of relevant experience with a PhD
  • Working knowledge of systems engineering principles and processes
  • Experience writing and presenting technical information to customer and internal management audiences
  • Experience with Agile development
  • Experience using the DOORS Requirements Management Database
  • Familiarity with Model-Based Systems Engineering (SysML/UML) and concepts
  • Experience leading requirement development/testing/verification
  • Experience working with Atlassian tool suite (i.e. JIRA, Confluence, etc.)
