ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Vehicle Management Systems (VMS) Systems Engineer or Principal Systems Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals within our South Bay Vehicle Engineering organization. This role is located in Akron, Ohio.

Primary Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Design and develop flight-critical and safety-critical systems for control of an aircraft
  • Define system key capabilities, performance requirements, and design and technology maturity constraints in accordance with customer specifications
  • Define, develop, and manage system interface definitions, implementation approach, and requirements
  • Perform technical planning, analysis, system integration, and verification and validation to ensure that system performance meets customer requirements.
  • Investigate and resolve system anomalies
  • Support laboratory, ground, and flight testing

Basic Qualifications for Systems Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) field from an accredited university with 2 years of related engineering experience OR a Master’s Degree (STEM)
  • Experience with engineering integration & test
  • Active DOD Secret or higher security clearance
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance

Basic Qualifications for Principal Systems Engineer:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) field from an accredited university with 5 years of related engineering experience OR a Master’s Degree (STEM) with 3 years of related engineering experience OR a PhD Degree (STEM)
  • Experience with engineering integration & test
  • Active DOD Secret or higher security clearance
  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with design, development, and/or integration of Air Vehicle systems and subsystems
  • Integrated hardware/software verification & validation experience
  • Experience with engineering modeling and simulation
  • Excellent oral, written, and interpersonal communication skills
  • Ability to work as part of a team, organize work, manage time, plan, and prioritize
