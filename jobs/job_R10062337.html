ACME Engineering Space Systems Sector is seeking a Global Supply Chain SAP Computer Systems Analyst (Level 5) for a new SAP Level 2 support organization.  This position may be located at one of Space Sectors key locations and will report directly to the Manager of Global Supply Chain (GSC) Business Operations.  

The Global Supply Chain Systems team at ACME Engineering Space Systems (NGSP) is looking for motivated individuals to join our team as a Computer Systems Analyst 5 / Staff Computer Systems Analyst.

The GSC Systems team bridges the gap between the business side of operations (day to day system users) and the Information Technology (IT) department. The team also manages the sector’s current and future portfolio of IT projects which includes budgets, schedules, and risk management.

The ideal candidate must be able to effectively communicate technical principles, theories, and concepts as well as develop innovative solutions to technical issues; demonstrate exceptional customer support skills; work well in a team environment and be able to complete assignments with minimal management supervision; effectively manage multiple tasks in a fast paced environment; and be able to learn new computer applications.

Primary responsibilities:

  • Gather, analyze and document user requirements, procedures, and/or problems to improve existing systems

  • Collaborate with business partners to develop solutions and correct system deficiencies

  • Test solutions to ensure optimal system functionality

  • Monitor integrated system interfaces for errors and address as necessary

  • Draft & publish instructional materials and provide system training

Occasional travel may be necessary (up to 25% of the time).

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree with 14 years experience in Global Supply Chain, STEM or IT field; or Master’s degree with 12 years experience; or equivalent work experience

  • Project Management or Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) engineering experience

  • Strong working knowledge of ACME Engineering’s SAP architecture

  • Must be willing and able to travel on short notice

  • Must be willing and able to occasionally work outside of normal business hours (including weekends) if necessary

  • Ability to obtain a DoD Secret level Security Clearance

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Experience with SAP S/4 HANA

  • Management Information Systems, Computer Science or related Technical Degree preferred

  • Prior involvement with integrating Digital Thread/Digital Twin capabilities

  • Experience in the Aerospace Industry or working on Defense Acquisition programs

  • Understanding of hardware and software requirements, design, development and testing of system integrations
