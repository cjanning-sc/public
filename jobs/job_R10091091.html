SSSD has an opening for a Principal Program Planning and Scheduling Analyst 3 for their Columbus, OH location and report to the Manager of Program Planning. The successful candidate must be able to work independently on multiple concurrent programs and proposals and will prepare, develop, and coordinate the Integrated Master Schedule (IMS) to meet all program objectives and ensures program schedules are horizontally and vertically integrated.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

Implements scheduling best practices as prescribed by industry to include, but not limited to:

  • DCMA 14-point
  • IPMR DI-MGMT-81861
  • Earned Value Management System (EVMS)
  • Critical Path Analysis
  • Schedule Risk Analysis (SRA)
  • Schedule Performance and Health Metrics and Analysis
  • Provides weekly status and reporting
  • Conducts Integrated Baseline Reviews (IBR) and Joint Surveillance Reviews (JSR) audits

 The successful candidate will possess the following:

  • Ability to perform detailed schedule, resource, work, and cost analysis and make recommendations to solve problems
  • Ability to create presentation content and present to various levels of leadership
  • Ability to effectively work with multiple functional teams, supplier teams, and the Program/Project Manager

Basic Qualifications:

  • 5 years of experience in any combination of the following: Program Planning & Scheduling, Finance, Pricing, Program Management or Engineering, Military Leadership; OR 3 years of stated experience with a Master’s Degree
  • Microsoft Office
  • Ability to obtain Department of Defense (DoD) Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree in Business, Supply Chain, and/or STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math) discipline
  • Experience with Microsoft Project Server 2010/2013
  • Knowledge with Integrated Master Plan (IMP) and Integrated Master Schedule (IMS) development; Schedule Risk Assessment (SRA) approach and execution
  • Excellent communication, interpersonal skills, and the ability to interface with all levels of employees, management, and executive leadership
  • DoD Secret security clearance
