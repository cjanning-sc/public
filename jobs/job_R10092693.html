ACME Engineering Defense Systems is seeking a Logistics Manager 1. This position will be located in Elk River, MN and will have overall management responsibility of the packaging, shipping and transportation operations for the site.

What you will get to do:

  • Responsible for controlling the efficient flow of goods, services, and information between point of origin through customer placement to meet customer requirements.
  • Ensures that materials and products are delivered to customers on time.
  • Ensures the execution and continuous improvement of standard logistics processes, such as the replenishment system, data interchange systems, demand management, electronic data systems administration and related functions.
  • Builds relationships with strategic customers through logistics initiatives.
  • Strategically plan and manage logistics, shipping, and transportation.
  • Keep track of quality, delivery times, transport costs and efficiency.
  • Maintain metrics and analyze data to assess performance and implement improvements
  • Local and long-haul transportation responsibility, i including international shipment.
  • Ensuring that compliance with all federal and state regulations, as well as security requirements, regarding shipping and transportation is met.
  • Functions as an advisor to the team regarding tasks, projects, and operations. Becomes actively involved in daily operations to meet schedules or to resolve complex problems.
  • Responsible for the direct supervision/management of the shipping/receiving team and logistics.
  • Manages the energetics inventory and maintain an accurate inventory system.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and/or 5 years of related experience or 4 additional years of experience in lieu of degree
  • At least 3 years of experience with ATF regulations and policies.
  • 3 years of experience in logistics and distribution functions.
  • Ability to obtain a Secret clearance and Special Access
  • Experience supporting complex shipping and logistical requirements, assuring full compliance with regulations and company policies governing transportation activities.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with regulations and company policies governing transportation activities and assuring full compliance.
  • Experience with material handling of destructive device and energetics.
  • Knowledge of booking shipments in Ryder Domestic Transportation Management System or similar system.
  • Ability to work across disciplines to influence change to improve operational efficiency
  • Ability to successfully manage multiple competing priorities.
  • Demonstrated ability to lead safety initiatives to drive a culture of a safety-first environment.
  • Experience in handling and managing transportation for unclassified, classified and energetic type materials
  • High degree of creativity and ability to solve a wide range of problems.
  • Basic computer skills, including MS Office (Word, Excel, Power Point, Project)
