Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Principal Systems Engineer to join the Command and Launch Team. This position is located in Roy, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program.

What You’ll Get To Do:

• Implement and manage system requirements in the Requirements Management databases.  Facilitate stake holder reviews; adjudicate and resolve comments; support review board release process.

• Revise and manage both original and revised specification/interface definition document and expedite documents through signature approval process.

• Work closely with Architecture team to ensure requirements and architecture models are in alignment

• Participate in team agile work management process

• Provide data to risk management (identifies potential risks) and trade study processes.

• Support system design, data analyses, interface verification, testing, failure/anomaly resolution and mission operations.

• Attend and prepare materials for presentation during Technical reviews. (System Requirement Review, System Functional Review, Preliminary Design Review, Critical Design Review and System Verification Review).

• Support trade studies, system sensitivity studies, design optimization studies, system performance assessments, system design assessments, Operations Analysis work such as mission analysis and timelines and concept of operations development
