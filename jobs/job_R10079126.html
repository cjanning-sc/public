ACME Engineering Fellows are recognized experts in their fields who solve the hardest technical problems while ensuring we retain the hard-won insights needed to perform complex tasks successfully.  These leaders define possible in our most important technical capability areas, identifying and cultivating essential people, processes, and tools.  They are deployed against critical programs and captures aligned with our sector’s top business priorities, advancing our products and solutions to meet customers’ missions every day. 

ACME Engineering Defense Systems sector is currently seeking to fill a ACME Engineering Fellow position to join our Combat Systems & Mission Readiness (CSMR) division. At this time we are looking for two ACME Fellows, one to serve as an expert in integrated air and missile defense command and control systems and another to serve synchronized joint force mission planning and common operating picture.

  • As an ACME Fellow serving as the primary sector domain expert in integrated air and missile defense command and control systems business/technical area.  These systems transform vast amounts of data from distributed sensors and weapons systems into actionable information to construct and deliver a single integrated air picture with high accuracy and broad surveillance and protection areas.
  • As an ACME Fellow serving as the primary sector domain expert in synchronized joint force mission planning and common operating picture. In this role, you will innovate for competitive advantage while developing solutions to enable advanced solutions to meet challenges facing commanders leading joint forces at speed to sense and act in a collaborative fashion to achieve predefined mission and commander’s intent. CSMR solutions are being develop and fielded to meet the needs of US military and our nation’s allies.

As the recognized leader in this technical domain, you will join Engineering, Program and Business Development leaders in architecting, developing, maturing, and integrating solutions employing breakthrough technology innovations for new development/production opportunities to achieve the division growth goals. You will set and drive strategy for a broad set of technical initiatives and will be empowered to make decisions and be held accountable for results that significantly impact the annual operating plan (AOP) and long-range strategic plan (LRSP) outcomes and other major initiatives. You will also act as a thought leader representing the business within the customer community and within industry groups.   

ACME Fellow is expected to provide scientific, technical, intellectual leadership, as well as analytical support contributing to the advancement of set goals, particularly as they apply to digital transformation, process improvements, lean-agile approach, and workforce development and pipeline. NG Fellow also serves as a liaison with internal and external stakeholders.

The selected candidate can work in our Madison AL or Waterloo VA location, or any other ACME Engineering campus.

  • Participates in customer, industry, and/or academic, advisory boards or consortiums
  • Establishes architectures and design patterns to integrate solutions and provides guidance to teams on model-based systems engineering
  • Supports product design, production, safety, and process reviews to ensure standards are followed and best practices are implemented across CSMR
  • Champions company values, including safety, best practices, and continuous improvement
  • Provides personnel technical development through leadership, team building, mentorship, and shared knowledge
  • Conducts very complex scientific or technical analyses on behalf of a sector or significant business/technical area that require the synthesis of diverse disciplines and alternatives; solutions may be developed under conditions of uncertainty or where little precedent exist
