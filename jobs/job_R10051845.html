ACME Engineering is currently seeking a Senior Principal Facilities Engineer to lead the execution and management of facility projects that support both sustaining infrastructure and new program facilitation requirements at our Lansing, Michigan location.

In this position you will plan, design and oversee the reconfiguration, maintenance, and alteration of equipment, machinery, buildings, structures, and other facilities.  Responsibility includes gathering, reviewing, and finalizing requirements data regarding facility or equipment specifications, required completion dates, and construction feasibility.

Additional Job Responsibilities:

  • Coordinate with architecture/engineering firms in developing design criteria and preparing layout and detail drawings
  • Prepare Scopes of Work (SOWs) and supporting documents for procurement of design and construction services
  • Reviews and estimates design costs, including equipment, installation, labor, materials, preparation, and other related costs
  • Inspect or direct the inspection of construction and installation progress to ensure conformance to established drawings, specifications, and schedules
  • Develop strategic replacement plans for outdated equipment to address excessive maintenance, energy efficiency and establish consistency of equipment utilized across the site
  • Design new facility layouts or alterations to existing structures or construction of new structures, this will include navigating building codes and permits
  • Check design deliverables in accordance with industry codes and standards, contract requirements, and company standards
  • Develop schedules, work assignments and staffing requirements to ensure successful project completion
  • Coordinate with other engineers and craftsman assigned to the project or a task, and reviews work progress and results
  • Develop plans to orderly remove old unused structures and equipment as well as plan for replacement of existing old structures to help meet evolving business needs
  • Provide detailed cost estimates for project design and implementation

Basic Qualifications:

  • 9 years’ experience with facility engineering experience and Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Civil, Mechanical, or Electrical engineering degree; or 7 years’ experience and MS Degree or 4 years’ experience and PhD
  • Must be a US citizen
  • Ability to obtain a DoD Secret Security Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Professional Engineering (PE) License
  • Experience supporting a large Manufacturing Facilit
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Strong organizational skills in managing time, projects and resources
  • Strong oral and written communication skills
  • Interpret building and utility drawings and make the revisions as required
  • Have applied knowledge of Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Microsoft Project) and AutoCAD capabilities
  • Demonstrated knowledge of Michigan Code and Regulatory requirements
  • Experience using a CMMS, Maximo, or other maintenance software packages
  • AutoCAD drafting ability
  • Solid modeling experience with NX, CATIA, Solid Works, or similar
  • Experience using BIM Software – Revit

Civil/Structural Engineering Qualifications:

  • Experience using common construction materials in designs (i.e. steel, concrete, wood, CMU)
  • Experience designing equipment pads and foundations including tank design
  • Must be familiar with building codes: IBC, IEBC, and ASCE 7
  • Familiarity with wood design including large timber, is preferred
  • Familiarity with NFPA is preferred
  • Proficiency in structural analysis tools, RISA or Revit, is preferred but not required
