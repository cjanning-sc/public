ACME Engineering in Akron, OH is seeking an Engineering Manager 2. The GSC Supplier Manufacturing Engineering Field Operations (SME-FO) Manager 2 will lead the SME-FO team, setting and operating to objectives and providing guidance to subordinates based on goals, objectives, and company policy.

Supplier Manufacturing Engineering Field Operations (SME-FO) is a technical group of Mechanical, Electrical, Aeronautical and Manufacturing Engineers who work to proactively resolve producibility and technical issues across the supply base. The SME-FO organization performs risk reduction and identification through proactive supplier engagement and critical part production oversight. The SME Field Operations team is ACME Engineering’s technical boots on the ground.

Duties and Responsibilities:

This position is a telework role and reports to the Director of GSC Programs for the ACME Engineering Aeronautics Sector (NGAS) who is located in Columbus, OH. In this role, the candidate will serve as the Manager of Supplier Manufacturing Engineering Field Operations supporting all NGAS programs. The candidate will be responsible for developing and executing GSC strategies with focus on achieving operational efficiency, safety and first-time quality targets. Candidate will be responsible for various activities, be knowledgeable of multiple disciplines across the Supply Chain organization, and exhibit end-to-end supply chain competency.

  • Manage a technical team of 15-20 remotely located direct-report engineers
  • Provide and coordinate supplier support for NGAS programs and the Delivery Management Team (DMT) organization
  • Provide up to daily communication guidance to the Procurement Organization and other IPT organizations regarding supplier base issues and activities 
  • Develop and track progress of GSC goals for the team and individuals
  • Create and manage functional documents such as PrOP(s), organizational charts, and team calendars.
  • Manage the SME-FO team labor and travel budgets provided by all supporting programs as well as seek out new opportunities to support NGAS and beyond
  • Generate weekly progress reports of all supplier-critical issues to management
  • Evaluate supplier’s capability(s) when called upon for capacity issues, process control activities and make needed recommendations regarding utilization of the ACME supplier base back to the GSC organization
  • Lead various supplier engagement activities and be a reliable and responsive POC for all GSC and ACME Engineering leadership
  • Create and implement team priorities based on GSC Program Management needs.
  • Ability to work various special projects in support of the greater organization needs
  • Develop mentorship and training plans and prioritize teammate individuals’ growth

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree AND 7+ years of related professional experience OR a Master's degree and 5+ years experience
  • Require strong aerospace manufacturing and supply chain background
  • Ability to understand/ interpret engineering documents/drawings and basic aerospace quality requirements
  • Ability to travel up to 50% including of out-of-state travel
  • Experience with manufacturing and supply chain operations with demonstrated and proven leadership skills and ability to collaborate effectively at all levels
  • MS Office (PowerPoint, Excel and Word) & knowledge of SAP

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active DoD Secret Clearance
  • Exposure to major programs including F/A-18, E-2 or F-35
  • Understanding of various special processing
  • Ability to prepare and present executive-level presentations
  • Excellent communications and leadership skills
  • Proven ability to lead with courage; make bold moves, willingness to speak up and to take on new challenges; take calculated risks, with appropriate mitigation plans; self-aware, transparent and authentic; seek feedback from others
  • Proven ability to act with speed; understand the need for urgency to meet the needs of the business and stakeholders; decisive, drive timely decision-making; empower others with clear accountability and appropriate support
  • Experience as an accomplished problem-solver and change-agent
