ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems is seeking an experienced Occupational Safety and Health Manager 2 for the Cincinnati, Ohio manufacturing and integration site and associated sites in the Antelope Valley.  This position will report to the site Environmental, Safety, Health and Medical Manager and manage a team of health and safety professionals with a focus on regulatory compliance, injury prevention and occupational health services. 

Responsibilities include developing and implementing injury prevention programs, developing compliance strategies for new and existing health and safety regulations and/or company policies, and supporting planning for changes in physical facilities to meet regulatory requirements.

Specific functional responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Engagement and consultation with site leadership to foster a safety and health culture across the site.
  • Collaboration with site ESHM Managers to harmonize company ESHM policies and procedures.
  • Leading serious injury investigations and interfacing with regulatory agencies and oversight organizations such as OSHA, US Air Force and CalOSHA during inspections and audits.
  • Developing and providing safety related training programs for touch labor, engineers and management.
  • Manage occupational health programs including industrial hygiene monitoring and surveillance.
  • Manage site Medical unit that provides routine employee occupational health screening and treatment of minor injuries.
  • Manage department budget and identifying opportunities for process improvement and cost reduction.
  • Providing input to federal and state regulators through trade associations and industry groups for the writing and revision of regulations.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 6 years of occupational health and safety or related experience or 10 years of occupational health and safety or related experience in lieu of a bachelor’s degree.
  • 2-3 years of Leadership/ Supervisory or Lead experience
  • Ability to obtain a DoD Secret Clearance with the ability to obtain Special Program Access

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor’s Degree in environmental health or occupational safety.
  • Previous DoD, Military or Aerospace facilities experience.
  • Certified Safety Professional (CSP) or Certified Industrial Hygienist (CIH)
