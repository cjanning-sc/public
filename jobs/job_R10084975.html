ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems located in Cleveland, CA has an opening for a Software Engineering Manager 2 DevSecOps  This is a dual-hat technical lead role and functional manager position that reports into the DevSecOps Software Department at the Autonomous Design Center of Excellence (COE).  This position is responsible for the people, processes, tools, and products to meet the current and future DevSecOps needs of unclassified and classified programs in Cleveland, CA.

The successful candidate will be working as a full-time DevOps technical lead across multiple programs, and carry functional responsibilities. The manager will coordinate staffing requirements with all IPTs and support all staffing processes, including reviewing resumes and conducting interviews.  She/he will support establishment and maintenance of DevSecOps specific processes, design manuals, and common tools for sector programs. The manager will facilitate and promote improvement initiatives, and sharing of lessons learned and best practices between programs.  She/he will support technical oversight across the sector, including independent reviews of major programs and support of proposals.  She/he will administer the performance evaluation reviews and salary review processes in conjunction with program IPT leaders and the software directorate functional organization to ensure timely, accurate input from the IPT leaders, and consistency across employees in the disciplines/skill areas. The manager will identify the capital assets, indirect, and training budgets required for the personnel to perform their statements of work.
