Northrop Grumman has opportunities waiting for you that play a vital role in human space exploration, national defense, and scientific discovery, supporting multiple programs across the universe. With us, you’ll discover a culture of curiosity and collaboration that will have you Defining Possible from the day you start. Our space systems connect and protect millions of people on earth every day, now and for the future. Explore your future and launch your career today. 

 

Northrop Grumman Space Systems – Launch & Missile Defense Systems organization is seeking a Financial Analyst – Senior Level – Financial Planning & Analysis, to support our Chandler, AZ site. This individual will support the Controller’s Office and help provide the financial outlook for our Launch Vehicles Business Unit.  This role will offer a hybrid working schedule and will be required to be in office 2-days per week.

 

Job duties and responsibilities, will include, but are not limited to, the following: 

  • Create financial forecasts budget and provide regular budget forecast updates
  • Assist with the LRSP Creation for the full 5-year plan to include Key Line Metrics, including balance sheet and cash flow
  • Analyze actual costs to budgets and forecasts
  • Present at various monthly Executive Reviews
  • Assist with consolidation of all other FP&A metrics for Management review
  • Support the testing and maintenance of the Financial Model
  • Support and perform ad-hoc what if modeling and reporting
  • Audit support with the government DCMA/DCAA
  • Making recommendations for change to optimize the profitability of the company

The ideal candidate will possess the following qualities:

  • Knowledge: Broad knowledge with techniques in collecting data, analyzing information, generating reports.
  • Problem-solving skills: Ability to identify problems or gaps in the financial or modeling systems, solve problems in a creative manner, and implement opportunities for improvement
  • Modeling skills: Must be able to implement complex modeling tools to improve the financial performance of the organization
  • Organizational and time management skills: Must be able to work within time constraints while managing multiple priorities
  • Communication skills: Must possess great oral and written communication skills to effectively execute job responsibilities
  • Collaborative skills: Must be able to work together with people from different units as required to help them address financial or system issues
  • Project management skills: Should have the ability to accomplish projects working with and through others including consistently documenting procedures to address problems.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 9+ years' work experience in a position that includes FP&A or Budget analysis - OR – Master’s Degree and 7+ years’ work experience in a position that includes FP&A or Budget analysis (an additional 4+ years of experience may be substituted for a Bachelor's Degree)
  • Advanced working knowledge of Microsoft Office, including expertise in Microsoft Excel using advanced formulas, pivots, charts, and graphs

 

Preferred Qualifications:

  • CAS and FAR knowledge
  • Cognos TM1 Planning Analytics, Cognos Reports and Costpoint experience
