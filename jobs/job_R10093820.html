Our Platform Laboratory Systems & Design (PLSD) group is seeking a Principal Systems Engineer to join our team of qualified and diverse individuals in Oklahoma City, OK.  You will become part of a Northrop Grumman team of professionals who are actively developing Training Systems for the Sentinel (GBSD) platform. These solutions consist of custom-designed operational, maintenance and cyber training systems and platforms.

Responsibilities:

  • Creates system requirements and manages requirement changes in the Requirements Management databases using established guidelines

  • Derive requirements through analysis of high-level requirements and interactions between various technical disciplines

  • Interface with customers, program management, and technical staff

  • Resolve problems and recommend improvements

Basic Qualifications:

  • A Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) discipline and 5 years of experience; 3 years with a Masters; 0 years with a PhD

  • Proficient ability to use Microsoft Office suite (Excel, Word, Power Point, Project)

  • Excellent interpersonal, communication, writing, presentation and critical thinking skills.

  • Must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain a DoD Secret security clearance & specific program access

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience in design and operation of training systems and/or simulators

  • Previous Systems Engineering experience in a hardware environment

  • Current Active DoD Secret security clearance

  • Experience with the Minute Man III weapon system or other ICBM systems 

  • Experience in DOORS and/or Cameo, utilizing a Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) approach

  • A background in an Agile development environment
