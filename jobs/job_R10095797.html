Security Coordinator 3

1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman is seeking a Security Coordinator 3 to support the Propulsion Systems business area at our Bacchus (Magna, Utah) facility.  The selected candidate will be responsible for providing multi-disciplined Security support for a variety of classified programs and customers.  This involves interaction with internal and external customers, and exposure to classified and sensitive unclassified information necessitating considerable use of tact, diplomacy, and discretion. 

This is an entry level position under the direction of the Industrial Security Manager.  The Security Coordinator shall support the management and execution of DoD security as it applies to the National Industrial Security Operating Manual (NISPOM) and Intelligence Community Directives (ICDs).  The candidate will provide direct assistance and support in areas of Program (FSO/CPSO), Personnel (PERSEC) and Physical Security. 

Primary Duties include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Security duties and responsibilities within NG facilities to ensure compliance with company and government regulations/requirements.
  • Conduct training sessions and administer clearance and special access briefings.
  • Maintain records and databases.
  • Coordinate and provide direction to contract security team for employee and guest badges, identification cards, and security reader cards.
  • Maintain lock and key records and combination changes for locks supporting classified programs.
  • Process visit authorizations for employees and guests to gain access to restricted areas.
  • Safeguard classified materials.
  • Oversee the daily operation of classified work areas.
  • Pick-up and drop-off classified mail.
  • Obtain fingerprints and process security clearance packages.
  • Monitoring, maintaining, and verifying information in various security databases to ensure systems are up to date and procedural/regulatory requirements are met.
  • Answering telephones; scheduling meetings and conferences.
  • Setting up and maintaining various files.
  • Conducting training and other briefings; and providing direct daily security support to classified programs. 
  • Other security related duties as assigned.

Preferred Skills:

  • Customer service oriented - dependable, responsive, and possess the qualities of diplomacy, discretion, and initiative.
  • Well organized, willing to work in a team environment, able to prioritize tasking, and perform in a multi-tasked and dynamic environment.
  • Demonstrated ability to effectively communicate information to various audiences and all levels of the organization both verbally and through written communications.
  • Make sound decisions, manage time, take independent action, and provide focused solutions.
  • Work with minimal direction and supervision to support the security program.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Minimum of High School Diploma / GED 
  • 4 years of experience in security, operations, or administration OR 4 years of additional education and/or experience
  • Eligibility for security clearance (Must receive and maintain a security clearance at the Secret level as a condition of continued employment.  Will ultimately be processed for a Top Secret security clearance)

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Current active Secret or Top Secret clearance
  • Bachelor's degree completion or in-progress
