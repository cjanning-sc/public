ACME Engineering Space Systems program has an opening for a Cost/Control Analyst that will interact with various business areas and division staff to support the Earned Value Management reporting requirements. The Analyst will interface closely with other Program Control Analysts, Control Account Managers (CAMs) as well as other management and Business/Finance to provide financial support and analysis. This position will be located in Des Moines, IA.

Roles and Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Leading all aspects of financial management on programs, including forecasting, planning, estimate-at-complete (EAC) maintenance, program control/Earned Value Management (EVMS), and ad hoc analyses and reporting.
  • Setting up and maintaining cost control system, monitor and control costs and schedules on contracts requiring validated cost schedule control system.
  • Performing analyses and prepare reports in order to ensure that contracts are within negotiated and agreed-upon parameters and government cost control guidelines.
  • Supporting the Control Account Managers (CAMs) in the Variance Analysis Reporting (VAR) process.
  • Leading the Baseline Change Control process by working with CAMs, Schedulers and other staff to document and implement change requests into the cost management system (Cobra).
  • Working with Control Account Managers to prepare budgets and schedules and forecasts for contract work and perform and/or assist in financial analyses such as funding profiles, sales outlook, and variance analysis.
  • Incorporating contractual changes into control systems by staying aware of outstanding work against each contract in order to maintain realistic contract cost and schedule baselines.
  • Performing monthly, quarterly and annual Estimates at Complete (EACs).
  • Setting up, prepare and follow up with all required financial reports.
  • Effectively communicating with leadership and customers regarding financial status, metrics and deliverables.
  • Participating in knowledge transfer and best practices sharing with peers.
  • Assisting in preparing for internal and external audits.
  • Establishing a budget/EAC, analyze capital expenditures and prepare reports for programs.
