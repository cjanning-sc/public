Basic Qualifications:

  • For a Level 2 Embedded Software Engineer, a bachelor’s degree in a Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics (STEM) field with 2 years of related software engineering experience, OR a master’s degree in a STEM field.
  • For a Level 3 Principal Embedded Software Engineer, a bachelor’s degree in a STEM field and 5 years of related software engineering experience, OR a master’s degree in a STEM field and 3 years of related experience.
  • Demonstrated experience developing software in object-oriented programming languages such as C++ or ADA.
  • Active and current DoD Secret clearance or higher.

Preferred Qualifications Either Level:

  • Experience with developing software in Real-Time Operating System (RTOS) for embedded applications (VxWorks or similar)
  • Knowledge of Agile development concepts, methodologies, and associated tools (Jira/Atlassian tools)
  • Excellent communication and organizational skills
  • Strong interpersonal and analytic skills
  • Thrive on colleague interactions and collaborations
  • Some experience with unit testing (googletest or similar framework)
  • Some experience with scripting languages such as Python or Ruby
  • Some experience with configuration management and version control tools
  • Some experience with software design using tools such as Cameo or Rhapsody
  • Some experience with Static Code Analysis (SCA) tools (Fortify, Understand, SonarQube)
  • Some experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes, OpenShift)
  • Some experience with Linux operating system development and test experience

There is no expectation that you will know everything we have listed although the higher the band the more may be expected. We’d love the opportunity to meet you and have a conversation. Let us facilitate the process to achieve your goals and dreams as a professional engineer!
