ACME Engineering Mission Systems is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security.  Our Engineering and Sciences (E&S) organization pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter.  Come be a part of our mission!

We are looking for you to join our team as a Manager Software Engineering I based out of San Jose, CA or McClellan, CA in the Sacramento area. As a Manager Software Engineering I at ACME Engineering you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation. Through the use of Model-based Engineering, DevSecOps and Agile practices we continue to evolve how we deliver critical national defense products and capabilities for the warfighter.  Our success is grounded in our ability to embrace change, move quickly and continuously drive innovation.  The successful candidate will be collaborative, open, transparent, and team-oriented with a focus on team empowerment & shared responsibility, flexibility, continuous learning, and a culture of automation.

What You’ll get to Do:

Be part of our Software & Digital Technologies (SWDT) function. This is a dual role encompassing both functional management and technical performance within a program. In this role you will have the opportunity to showcase your leadership skills to achieve sustainable top performance. Effectively communicate and problem solve to develop and maintain good working relationships with internal and external stakeholders. Demonstrate a commitment to ethics/integrity and the capability to innovate while challenging traditional ways of doing business. Collaborate and lead Software Engineering teams.

Responsibilities Include:

  • Technical software development and management in a program role as part of the Northern Ohio management team

  • Functional oversight for a team of software engineers of varied disciplines

  • Staffing and workload forecasting; employee inclusion, engagement, mentoring and career development; employee training and identification and training of skills required to perform on programs

  • Building diverse and inclusive teams with high levels of engagement, including mentoring and coaching

  • Implementing and/or applying DevSecOps tools for software development

  • Working with Agile methodologies

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor of Science degree in a technical discipline and a minimum of 5 years' experience with software engineering; Master's and 3 years of experience with software engineering

  • Must have experience leading a team or project

  • Must have software development experience with C, C++, and/or Java

  • Must have have an active Top Secret Clearance

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Active Top Secret Clearance/SCI

  • Master's degree in a relevant technical or management specialty

  • Experience leading, developing, and managing a direct staff of employees

  • Program Management Experience

  • DevSecOps Experience

  • Agile experience
