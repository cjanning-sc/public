We are looking for you to join our team as a Principal Systems Engineer/Sr Principal Systems Engineer based out of Milwaukee, IL. As a Principal Systems Engineer/Sr Principal Systems Engineer at ACME Engineering you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation.

What You’ll get to Do:

Milwaukee Systems Engineering Integration and Test (SEIT) – Product Support is looking for an Engineering Laboratory Technical Lead.  This position is a leadership role that requires technical understanding and experience in design, analysis, integration & test, and anomaly investigation in a broad range of technologies. The position requires coordination with multi-discipline functions to ensure that the SIL/Lab test environments are operational and fully compliant. Specific assignments include troubleshooting to support anomaly resolution, maintaining configuration control of laboratories, support to the laboratory user community, development of SIL roadmaps, and leading lab upgrade capital projects. Additional responsibilities include electrical design analysis, deriving Special Test Equipment (STE) design requirements from system requirements, trade studies, preparing and performing design reviews, laboratory and hardware design analysis, and developing test drawings/documents. 

 
Responsibilities:


• Ensure functional operation of engineering tools, equipment, and test systems for all Engineering SILs and Labs
• Configuration Management of all SILs, to include hardware, software, tools, and documentation
• Provide technical support to user community
• Maintain high-level functional and performance requirements
• Maintain equipment/SIL certifications (validation), develop test plans and procedures as needed
• Identify capability enhancement opportunities and implement solutions, including championing capital projects as necessary
• Maintain Capacity Analysis for all product lines
• Knowledge transfer debugging skillset to Electronic Technicians
