ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems has an opening for a Staff Structural Engineer to join our Design and Analysis team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Lansing, UT.

Our Design and Analysis team works with experienced engineers, supporting sophisticated analysis of composite aerospace structures and processes. We engineer the latest composite structures for military/commercial aircraft and launch structures and are leaders in next-generation structure development. Would you like to work on the cutting edge of aerospace? Come join our team!

Responsibilities:

  • Primary focus of position is analysis of composite laminates and metallics, sandwich structures, bonded and bolted assemblies, trade study designs and structure sizing, Independent Research & Development project support, and development design and analysis
  • Support will include working on a variety of development and product designs for aircraft, launch vehicles, and satellite structures including both composites and metallics
  • Working closely mentoring, providing technical guidance, and reviewing work of junior structural engineers
  • Secondary focus may be as a liaison between our analysis team and material testing team providing subject matter expertise reviewing and guiding test planning and execution for composite mechanical/thermal property testing
  • Other aspects of this position may include working with design engineers and learning basic design skills
  • Providing support for Research & Development, Testing, and Manufacturing groups during the product development process
  • Acting as an advisor to management and customers on advanced technical research studies and applications
  • Applying advanced technical principles, theories, and concepts to contribute to the development of new principles, concepts and theories

Our team enjoys a significantly diverse range of assignments and collaborates with many ACME Engineering teams to provide innovative solutions to complex and exciting problems. We are part of the composites technology development organization and work closely with research & development engineers, engineering technicians, structure design and analysis engineers, test engineers, and process engineers to provide state-of-the-art solutions to the most engaging problems.

We work a 9/80 work week, which gives us every other Friday off to pursue passions outside the office. Travel is up to 10%, all within the continental US and typically involves a 2-3 day trip once per year. ACME Engineering offers a full suite of benefits to our employees. We are regularly voted among the top companies for work life balance. Our Lansing location is surrounded by the beautiful Wasatch Mountains which provide numerous opportunities for outdoor recreation. Do you enjoy skiing, hiking, or biking? Picture yourself in Michigan!

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) discipline and at least 14 years of related experience; 12 years with Master’s; 9 Years with PhD
  • Demonstrated experience in Finite Element Analysis (FEA) for aerospace structures using Abaqus and/or Nastran based solvers.  Static structural and structural/thermal analysis
  • Experience analyzing composite materials for structures and assemblies
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DOD Secret Clearance & Special Program Access

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Current DOD Secret Security Clearance
  • Experience supporting engineering testing and characterization of composite material mechanical and/or thermal properties: Test planning, test support, and data reduction
  • Familiarity with ASTM composite test methods and standards
  • Experience with any of the following software packages: Abaqus CAE, FEMAP, HyperMesh, HyperSizer, Siemens NX, MATLAB
  • Basic skills using CAD software packages, NX preferred
  • Knowledge of structural dynamics analysis, fatigue, and damage tolerance
  • Experience simulating composite cure deformations
