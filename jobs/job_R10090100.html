ACME Engineering Aerospace Systems has an opening for a Principal Weapons Engineer / Sr. Principal Weapons Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Rancho Bernardo, CA.

**This position may be filled by either at the Principal Weapons Engineer level OR at the Sr. Principal Weapons Engineer level based on the qualifications listed.**

The Weapons Engineer is expected to be a highly-motivated self-starter with a demonstrated ability to operate effectively on a team of engineers and technicians. General understanding of Universal Armament Interface (UAI) compliant MIL-STD-1760 Stores Management Systems (SMS), MIL-STD-1553 and Suspension and Release Equipment (S&RE). Daily activities include architecture development and CAMEO modeling and/or requirements development for weapon integration for both the SMS and the Suspension & Release Equipment (S&RE).  Specific tasks performed in this role are requirements management, system integration, functional and physical architecture modeling, and test planning for vertical lift aircraft.   


The successful candidate must possess technical knowledge of SMS/S&RE-related hardware and software requirements and experience in the definition, design, integration, test, and certification (end-to-end development) and operation of SMS and S&RE.  The successful candidate will also possess knowledge of governing standards to include UAI Platform-Store ICD, MIL-STD-1553, MIL-STD-1760, AS5653, etc., and must possess some experience preparing detailed requirements specifications, statements of work, test plans and procedures, certification plans and procedures, performance reports, etc., related to these governing standards.
The successful candidate will be expected to thoroughly understand all system requirements, specifications, and interface control documents, prepare, monitor and update program schedules, conduct and participate in all related design reviews, and provide technical guidance to the cross-functional team.


The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Weapons Engineer:

  • Bachelor's Degree in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) related field with 5 or more years of related engineering experience, OR 3 or more year of related engineering experience with a Masters in STEM; OR 1 or more years of related engineering experience with a PhD in STEM
  • Must have experience working with weapons and payload systems
  • Active DoD Secret or DoE equivalent required to be considered

Basic Qualifications for Senior Principal Weapons Engineer:

  • Bachelor's Degree in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) related field with 9 or more years of related engineering experience, OR 7 or more year of related engineering experience with a Masters in STEM; OR 5 or more years of related engineering experience with a PhD in STEM
  • Must have experience working with weapons and payload systems
  • Active DoD Secret or DoE equivalent required to be considered

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master's in Engineering
  • Greater than 4 years of experience working with weapons and payload systems
  • Knowledge of CAMEO.  
  • In-depth knowledge of governing standards to include UAI Platform-Store ICD, MIL-STD-1553, MIL-STD-1760, AS5653
  • Active Secret clearance with access to Special Programs
  • Experience with fighter aircraft and air-to-air weapons a plus.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
