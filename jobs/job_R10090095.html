Northrop Grumman Networked Information Solutions (NIS) Division has an opening for a Quality Inspector 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals and maintain our world class capabilities in a dynamic environment.  This position is in Cincinnati, Ohio.

This position reports to the Quality Manager.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Perform in-process and final inspections on government equipment and control units to include a review of the associated product repair documentation.
  • Perform receiving inspection on electronic and mechanical parts and materials.
  • Confirm acceptable quality standards and workmanship practices in assigned work centers by learning/practicing process specifications and using workmanship standards during everyday job functions.
  • Identify discrepant conditions and generate nonconformance reports for discrepancies discovered during inspection processes.
  • Maintain records of inspection results.
  • The use of common tooling such as calipers, gauge pins and micrometers for inspection will be required for this position.

Basic Qualifications:

  • High School diploma or equivalent with 2 years additional experience in mechanical inspection, assembly, machining, or other equivalent experience.
  • Experience in electronic application
  • Certification to IPC-610 – Acceptability of Electronic Assemblies and/or J-STD-001
  • Must have Interim clearance or ability to obtain a Secret Clearance. U.S. citizenship required.
  • Strong work ethic and good attitude
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Basic understanding of Microsoft Office applications.
  • Must be able to read and interpret engineering documentation and inspect to specifications, drawings, parts lists, inspection plans, and supplemental procedures and instructions.
  • Must be able to work onsite in Rolling Meadows, IL
  • Previous experience working in ISO-certified company and using Process Specifications or Military Standards to perform inspection or work activities.
  • Ability to learn new computer programs/applications.
  • Ability to repeatedly lift 30 pounds from cart to table.
  • Ability to focus and communicate over ambient noise.  

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Associate degree /Bachelor's degree or higher
  • Active DoD Secret Clearance
  • Experience with SAP
  • Experience with ISO process audits
  • Experience with First Article Inspections
  • IPC 610/620 Workmanship Certification
  • J-STD-001 Certification
  • Fluent in use of Microsoft applications and ability to learn new computer programs/applications
