ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Sr Principal Engineer Supplier Quality. This position will be located in Detroit, Michigan and will support the Ground Based Strategic Deterrence (GBSD) program

This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What You’ll Get To Do:  

The selected candidate will be responsible for preparing documentation that implements  the GBSD Enterprise and Program Supplier Quality Assurance Plan, policies, procedures, & tools required to support Program  pre-award & Day1 Tasks as well as infrastructure needs. Additionally, the selected candidate will be responsible for SQA oversight activities for suppliers and subcontractors of hardware developed/manufactured/assembled/tested for GBSD.
 

Roles and responsibilities:

  • Prepare documentation that implements the GBSD Enterprise and Program Supplier Quality Assurance Plan, policies, procedures, & tools required to support Program pre-award & Day1 Tasks as well as Enterprise infrastructure needs.

  • Creating process health, progress and stability metrics

  • Supplier Quality Assurance oversight activities of hardware developed/manufactured/assembled/tested for GBSD

  • Defines and monitors mission assurance program specifications and processes to ensure mission success of programs.

  • Performs or assures quality, risk management, safety, reliability and maintainability of program achievements, subcontractors, and suppliers in accordance with contractual requirements.

  • Assesses program performance and risks and determines resources to ensure mission assurance.

  • Bachelors in Science degree with 9 years of related experience; OR a Master’s Degree with 7 years of related experience; OR a PhD with 4 years of related experience.
