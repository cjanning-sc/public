ACME Engineering is seeking an Operations Team Supervisor (OTS) position at our Bacchus, UT plant to manage a team of touch labor operators in the ACME/Binder area.

As an Operations Team Supervisor you will be responsible for direct supervision of manufacturing activities. This would include the following:

•  Able to lead a crew in the production of nitroglycerin (ACME) and Binder premix.
•  Must be able to process and apply basic math functions to ensure appropriate amounts of ingredients are used. 
•  Accountable for safety, quality, on the job training, productivity, and production schedules within areas of responsibility. 
•  Communicates expectations clearly and initiates necessary actions to ensure that organizational goals and business objectives are accomplished. 
•  Ensures company policy directives and operating procedures are being followed. 
•  Maintains a presence at the point of most criticality or difficulty to favorably influence the operations of a high-performance manufacturing team. 
•  Exemplifies adherence to all safety rules, regulations, and standards.

Requirements:

•  High School diploma or GED with 6 years of experience working in a manufacturing environment.
•  Two years of successful team leadership
•  Skill in interpreting engineering drawings, specifications, and operating procedures
•  Knowledge of MM&IR, Safety and Quality Assurance policies and procedures.

•  Able to acquire and maintain Employee Possessor (EP) status under the Safe Explosives Act (SEA).

Preferred Skills:

•  A.S. degree plus four years’ experience or a B.S. degree with two years of experience.
•  One year experience using visual daily management systems, standard work and coaching techniques.

•  Manufacturing experience including knowledge of supporting systems including MM&IR, OPCB, Safety and QA policies
