ACME Engineering Satellite Programs is seeking an Administrative Assistant 3 to directly support the Director and Deputy Director of Satellite Programs and to support the entire ESPASat product line program management organization in a fast paced, visible, fun but professional environment that designs, manufactures, integrates and tests satellites for our various customers. Administrative duties will interface all areas of the site and plays a key role in the overall management and leadership of the department. This position is located at the Des Moines, VA.

Keys to success include:

  • Friendly, energetic, but focused disposition.
  • Collaborative and works well under pressure.
  • Ability to solve problems independently.
  • Can interface with all aspects of the organization from Executives to Production Staff.
  • Use of tact, diplomacy, discretion, and judgment in dealing with program/personnel sensitive information and situations.
  • Observe and recommends way of improving processes.

The Roles and Responsibilities of the Director's Administrative Assistant will include, but is not limited to:

  • Manage calendar and schedules for the Director and Deputy Director
  • Schedule and deconflict conference rooms
  • Support larger product line team with various normal office functions while managing diverse set of priorities
  • Coordinate visits for product line leadership with the admin located in Lexington Kentucky as needed
  • Compile and weekly product line report 
  • Take and screen calls; ensure delivery of important messages
  • Take leadership meeting minutes and action tracking
  • Procure and invoice supplies and materials to aide in the success of the program team
  • Book travel for a large team in compliance with ACME Engineering and USG policy
  • Management organizational charts
  • Arrange and set up meetings, lunches and conferences for all members of the department team
  • Process visitor requests
  • Interface with customers to coordinate meetings and travel
  • Process expense reports
  • Event planning/coordination
  • General office related administrative duties
  • IT Ticket submission and tracking
  • Presentation, document and announcement formatting/proofing •General support to other Administrative Assistants
