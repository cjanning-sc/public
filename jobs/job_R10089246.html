An organization within ACME Engineering’s Payload and Ground Systems (PGS) division, Cutting Edge Optronics is a leading provider of high-power laser diode arrays, high power pump modules and diode-pumped solid-state (DPSS) lasers. We are a vertically integrated manufacturer capable of supplying unmounted diode bars, packaged laser diodes, DPSS modules, laser diode drivers and complete DPSS laser systems. Our diode laser-based products have become industry standards and are used in a wide variety of commercial and military applications. 

  

Learn more about Cutting Edge Optronics at our website: https://www.acmeengineering.com/who-we-are/cutting-edge-optronics/. 

ACME Engineering, Cutting Edge Optronics, is looking for a highly motivated Associate Accountant to work in our Saint Charles, MO facility. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication and interpersonal skills, strong organizational and analytical skills, and the ability to handle multiple assignments. Under the supervision of the Controller this individual will be responsible for the following: 

Job Duties:

  •  Conduct general-ledger account reconciliations, verifying accuracy of data and identifying errors
  • Support monthly, quarterly, half-year, and year-end financial closes, including assigned journal entries, accruals, and overall maintenance of general-ledger accounts.
  • Reconcile and tie out cash accounts with Bank Statements, ERP system and Corporate Treasury
  • Assist in providing schedules and commentary for operational and senior management including data for working-capital cash-flow reports
  •  Prepare financial reports when requested by managers, collecting, analyzing, and summarizing account information and trends
  • Perform Accounts Receivable and Cash Collection functions
  • Perform Accounts Payable functions
  • Process credit card payments
  • Reconcile and process intercompany billings and allocations
  • Maintain and update Purchase Order delivery dates in ERP system
  • Serve as a backup for Purchase Requisition processing
  • Provide accounting guidance to functional departments
  • Review travel & expense (T&E) reports and monthly statements; process T&E reimbursements
  • Identify cost savings opportunities and process efficiencies
  • Responsible for the adherence to and communication of accounting and auditing policies and procedures.
  • Support sign-in security at business office•    
  • Research of GAAP and tax law change impact
  • File accounting records (i.e. invoices, statements, journal entries, etc.)

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in related field; an additional 4 years of accounting experience may be considered in lieu of degree.

Preferred Education/Experience/Skills

  • Bachelors in Accounting
  • 2 years of experience in General Accounting
  • Prior experience with ERP Systems
