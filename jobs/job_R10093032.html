Staff Systems Engineer - GMD Program

Days (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Space Systems – Launch and Missile Defense Systems has an exciting career opportunity for a Staff Systems Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals located in Huntsville, AL for the GMD Program.

Job Responsibilities:

  • GMD Development and Sustainment Contract (DSC) Communications Node Project Manager Deputy.
  • Lead in the facilitation of product engineering, development, testing, and fielding for the GCN product team.
  • Clearly articulate and brief status, issues, and recommended COAs/Mitigations to senior level leaders to customer (Boeing and MDA), warfighter, NG management.
  • Support/brief to GMD DSC technical and program management reviews with internal and external stakeholders.
  • Support GCN management of suppliers to include sub-contract support, supply chain management, and facilities management at 13 geographic sites.
  • Oversee staffing, requisition development and review/hiring process.
  • Support Transition to GWS
  • Architect and map/draw process diagrams, using business modeling tools, while discussing with subject matter experts
  • Conduct process analysis in order to identify areas of improvement.
  • Develop hierarchical and sequential process modeling concepts/techniques.

Basic Qualifications:

  • 14 Years with BS; 12 Years with Masters; 9 Years with PhD
  • Active Department of Defense (DoD) Secret Security Clearance and ability to obtain a Top-Secret Security Clearance
  • Extensive technical leadership and development experience with Missile Defense systems.
  • Strong interpersonal skills to communicate effectively with teams and stakeholders to include excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Demonstrated ability to manage efforts in an Agile and DevSecOps process model.
  • Experience with customer and stakeholder relationship management
  • Proven presentation/briefing development experience
  • Ability to lead product team with organization and execution of tasks that acknowledges cost, schedule, and technical constraints.
  • Problem solving skills of technical, organizational, or personnel issues.
  • Supporting DoD proposal activities that includes BOE development, Integrated Master Schedule input, and writing contributions on multiple proposal activities.
  • Demonstrate a commitment to ethics and integrity, and the capability to innovate while challenging traditional ways of doing business.
  • Experience and understanding of Engineering Processes and Procedures.
  • Broad skill set that is balanced between interpersonal, business, and technical capabilities.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Strong knowledge of the Missile Defense System (MDS) and its elements.
  • Expertise with containerized software systems in accordance with DoD DevSecOps Reference Design
  • Experience delivering products and services on Missile Defense Agency (MDA) programs in accordance with MAP/PMAP standards.
  • Provide in-depth analysis of Missile Launch Equipment performance data.
  • Active Top Secret DoD Security Clearance
