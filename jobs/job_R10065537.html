We are looking for you to join our team as a CAD CAM Engineering Manager 2 based out of Beavercreek, CA. In this role at ACME Engineering you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide Hardware Engineering Organization. Through the use of Model-based Engineering, Agile practices and robust engineering, we continue to evolve how we deliver critical national defense products and capabilities for the warfighter.  Our success is grounded in our ability to embrace change, move quickly and continuously drive innovation.  The successful candidate will be collaborative, open, transparent, and team-oriented with a focus on team empowerment & shared responsibility, flexibility, continuous learning, and a culture of automation.

What you’ll get to do:

This position focuses on the management of the CAD (Computer Aided Design) drafting group within the Beavercreek Marine Systems Engineering Department. Primary responsibilities include managing up to 12 direct reports to create solid models and drawings using CREO with Windchill and NX with Teamcenter, develop 2D/3D layouts, create detailed manufacturing drawings and revise existing models, drawings and electronic images. You will apply knowledge of design, modeling and drawing practices and guide your team to solve design issues with the Engineering team.  The drafting group is a represented team (union), and onsite presence is required.

Your responsibilities include

  • Manage workflow in the team to meet cost budgets and schedule deadlines
  • Maintaining appropriate staffing levels that commensurate with dynamic workloads. Coordinate drafting needs with engineering managers, project engineers and programs managers
  • Establishing and maintaining standards and procedures for generation of models and associated drawings
  • Collaborate with configuration management to ensure proper data configuration control, managing CAD Software configuration
  • Plan and manage employee training and development, and employee performance evaluation
  • Develop and collect metrics to support cost estimating for future work
  • Develop plan to increase first time quality of group and conform to ASME requirements

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor of Science degree in a technical field with a minimum of 5 years of related experience or AA/AS in STEM field and 10 years of related experience.
  • Must have demonstrated leadership and teambuilding capabilities.
  • Fundamental Configuration Management Principles
  • Experience with CAD Modeling in CREO and NX. Experience with PLM system, Windchill and Teamcenter 
  • Thorough understanding of Model Based Engineering (MBE)
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Secret security clearance.
