Strategic Space Systems Division:
Strategic Space Systems Division (SSSD) is an industry-leading provider of launch systems capabilities serving national security, military, civil and commercial customers. We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable, and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible.

SSSD has an opening for a Principal Thermal Systems Engineer/Sr. Principal Thermal Systems Engineer to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Redondo Beach, California.

The ideal candidate will have a complete understanding and wide application of technical principles, theories, and concepts in the field of thermal engineering and general knowledge of other related disciplines.  
 

Candidate responsibilities include (but not limited to) the following:

  • Thermal systems requirements development
  • Perform thermal analysis to develop and optimize thermal control designs and provide solutions to a wide range of difficult problems
  • Coordinate with other engineering disciplines
  • Document thermal analyses and designs
  • Review and approve drawings for thermal design hardware content
  • Report analyses results and design specifics to internal management and external customers.
  • Work with test engineering to plan and execute subsystem level thermal tests, as well as perform associated analyses.
  • Provide feedback regarding impacts of possible cost and/ or schedule changes
  • Work and execute independently with minimal technical oversight and general direction
  • Mentor junior engineers and provide guidance on best practice

Basic Qualifications for a Principal Thermal Systems Engineer: 

  • Bachelor’s degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) from an accredited university with 5 years of engineering experience; OR Master’s degree in STEM from an accredited university with 3 years of engineering experience; OR Ph.D. in STEM from an accredited university with 0 years of engineering experience.
  • Candidate must be able to obtain and maintain an SCI access with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI) 

Basic Qualifications for a Sr. Principal Thermal Systems Engineer: 

  • Bachelor’s degree in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics (STEM) from an accredited university with 9 years of engineering experience; OR Master’s degree in STEM from an accredited university with 7 years of engineering experience; OR Ph.D. in STEM from an accredited university with 4 years of engineering experience.
  • Candidate must be able to obtain and maintain an SCI access with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI) 

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Design, analysis and integration experience of spacecraft thermal systems
  • Active SCI access clearance with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI) completed within 5 years
  • Experience using thermal analysis tools (SINDA/G, SINDA/FLUINT, TSS, Thermal Desktop)
  • Experience using aerospace standard design tools (NX, CATIA)
  • MS in Mechanical or Aerospace Engineering with 6 years of engineering experience
  • Experience with space vehicle thermal design and/or integration
  • Detail-oriented and have excellent verbal and written communications skills, leadership skills, and organization skills
  • Spacecraft and/or Payload background with experience through the entire system lifecycle from concept, design, production, integration, test, launch, to operation support.  
  • Experience working on thermal design and troubleshooting efforts.
