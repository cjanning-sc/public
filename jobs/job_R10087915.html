We have two openings for Level 3 or 4 Administrative Assistants to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals in the Remote Sensing Programs Business Unit. This organization designs, builds and operates cutting edge payloads that are critical to our national defense. These positions will each support one of the Operating Units within the BU. One position will be located in Baltimore, MD and the other will be located in Rolling Meadows, IL.

What You’ll get to Do:

We are seeking high energy, dependable teammates. As a selected candidate, you will work closely with OU director and support the OU’s program managers as well as collaborate with the staff working on programs in a variety of capacities including arranging meetings, setting up and maintaining files, arranging and setting up food orders, supporting office moves and associated equipment orders, arranging travel for personnel, processing associated expense reports and supporting special program and campus events.

We are seeking independent thinkers with track records of excelling in a fast-paced, multi-faceted, and collaborative team-based environment. The positions require excellent communication skills, an ability to organize and prioritize tasks independently, strong time management skills and strong office management and interpersonal skills. The selected candidates should have the ability to act with discretion and good judgment when handling or disseminating sensitive, confidential, or proprietary information. These positions will report to the Operating Unit Directors and provide support throughout the leaders in the Director’s organization as required.

Principal Duties:

  • Arrange meetings and teleconferences
  • Maintain and synchronize calendars
  • Arrange travel and prepare associated expense reports
  • Coordinate on-boarding of personnel
  • Answer questions and solve problems related to office operations and established policies and procedures
  • Coordinate office workspace planning, ordering office equipment with the facilities and IT organizations
  • Interface with other organization leaders
  • Support coordination and integration of meeting materials
  • Support and collaborate with other Administrative Assistants as needed
  • Support special event planning and catering, such as meal planning, ordering food from caterers, delivering food, clean up and expensing

This position may be filled as either a level 3 or a level 4 based on the qualifications listed below.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Level 3: High School Diploma/GED plus 4 years of additional education and/or experience
  • Level 4: High School Diploma/GED plus 10 years of additional education and/or experience
  • U.S. Citizen with a Top Secret or SCI clearance
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite including Word, PowerPoint, Outlook, Excel
  • Proficiency in editing written material for correct spelling and grammar
  • Experienced with organizing/maintaining/deconflicting schedules, calendars, meetings, and travel arrangements
  • Prior experience working as an Administrative Assistant or performing equivalent responsibilities

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Associates degree
  • Experience in Concur/ITRIP, I-Buy, NG My IT, SAP
