What You Will Get To Do:

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking a Systems Engineer to support the SNAP Department (Strategic Networks, Applications, and Products).  Our team focuses on Network Solutions, and supports a variety of technologies across our three departments; C3 Solutions, Strategic Networks, and Small Form Factors. C3 Solutions, specializes in Tactical Data Link processors, emulators, and simulators.  Our Strategic Networks team builds solutions to defending our country's nuclear assets.  And lastly, our Small Form Factors Department brining SWaP optimized solutions (Crypto, SDRs, Space Networks) to new domains, bridging us into Space, Weapons, and more.  This position will be located in the beautiful city of San Diego, CA, at one of our two locations; Rancho Carmel, or Kearny Mesa. 

  • Apply your Systems, Comms, and/or Network Modeling experience to cutting edge pursuits
  • Participation in architecture definition and complex system problem solving in support of advanced software defined multifunction mission systems
  • Creation of innovative concepts of operation and employment of the advanced mission system solutions
  • Development of detailed design requirements suitable for efficient hardware/software/firmware implementation and network deployment

This position will require onsite work however for this role we are able to offer flexible work arrangements and some remote work will be possible.

This is a dual level requisition which can be filled as a Systems Engineer or Principal Systems Engineer

Basic Qualifications Systems Engineer:

  • 2 years of relevant experience with a Bachelor of Science in STEM field; or a Master's Degree
  • System analysis and/or integration experience
  • Recent experience in requirements allocation & derivation and verification traceability
  • Experience with Requirements management tools such as DOORs 
  • Clearance: Candidate must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain an active U.S. Government DOD Secret Clearance

Basic Qualifications Principal Systems Engineer:

  • 5 years of relevant experience with a Bachelor of Science in STEM field; or 3 years of relevant experience with a Masters
  •  System analysis and/or integration experience
  • Recent experience in requirements allocation & derivation and verification traceability
  • Experience with Requirements management tools such as DOORs 
  • Clearance: Candidate must be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain an active U.S. Government DOD Secret Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Model Based System Engineering experience, especially using Cameo
  • Agile development experience, including use of Atlassian products
  • High proficiency with tools required for communication system and network modeling and simulation
  • Demonstrated depth and breadth in the physical through application layers and how the aggregation of these partitioned capabilities accomplish required missions
  • Ability and willingness to travel
  • Active DoD Top Secret clearance
  • Ability to obtain DoD Special Program Access (SAP)
