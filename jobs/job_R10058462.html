Principal Systems Engineer - IFC Manager (Secret Clearance Required)
R10058462
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Aerospace Systems has an opening for a Principal Engineer Systems to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in San Diego, CA.

The selected candidate will assume an IFC lead role as part of the Systems Engineering Integration and Test (SEIT) team on the RQ-4 Enterprise. As part of day-to-day operations, the selected candidate will interface with various engineering functions and suppliers both internal and external to Northrop Grumman.

Essential Functions:

  • Work with IPTs to develop and execute program IFC plans and metrics
  • Manage project execution rhythm, coordination, and reporting activities
  • Manage supplier activities and serve as primary project POC to customers and key suppliers
  • Technical liaison to the cross-functional team
  • Familiarity and understanding of system requirements (validation and verification) and interface control documents (ICDs), and statements of work (SOW)
  • Familiarity with JIRA software tool (and/or similar) for issue tracking and resolution

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelors in Science degree in a STEM discipline (Science, Technology, Engineering or Math) with 5 years of engineering experience; Master’s degree in a STEM discipline with 3 years of engineering experience; PhD degree in a STEM discipline with 0 years of engineering experience
  • An active DoD Secret Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience in Integrated Functional Capability (IFC) process
  • Strong leadership, excellent communication, and strong presentation skills
