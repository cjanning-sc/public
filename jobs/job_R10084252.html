The Launch Vehicles Business Unit is seeking an experienced Contracts Administrator (Senior Principal Level) to become a member of the Ground-Based Midcourse Defense (GMD) program team. The position will report directly to the Contracts Manager and will serve as the primary contact with an important strategic customer, ensuring the team holds NG’s favorable financial and programmatic baseline.

This position will be based out of our Louisville, AZ facility and will have potential for a hybrid working schedule. You will be a key contributor and empowered to make a difference!

Responsibilities include the following:

  • Help lead and coordinate the company response to customer Requests for Proposal (RFPs) to understand Statements of Work (SOW)/contract requirements and advise the program team on risk identification and mitigation strategies.
  • Ensure company proposals are fully responsive to prospective customer requirements, prepare proposal submittal documents, and coordinate appropriate levels of internal review and approval.
  • Coordinate and develop responses to fact-finding, evaluation notices, and requests for information (RFIs), to bolster priorities, strengthen support for the proposal, and identify customer key items or concerns.
  • Develop and execute negotiation strategy / plan to establish a framework to arrive at the most beneficial agreement possible between ACME Engineering and the customer in the quickest timeframe possible.
  • Maintain our excellent working relationship with customers to effectively communicate, administer, modify, negotiate and terminate standard and nonstandard contractual agreements.
  • Oversee contract performance including schedules and deliverables and complete actions to ensure satisfactory program performance.
  • Advise your program team of contractual rights and obligations on your assigned contracts, including interpretation of contract terms and conditions.
  • Enter and manage data in the Contract Management System (CMS).

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 9+ years of experience in any combination of the following: Contract Administration, Proposal Development, Contract Negotiation, Contract Risk Assessment and/or Contract Cost Performance - OR -  Master’s Degree and 7+ years of stated experience
  • Knowledge of FAR/DFARS

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Recent experience as a Contracts Administrator for Department of Defense contracts
  • Current Department of Defense (DoD) Secret Security Clearance
  • Excellent working knowledge of  Export Regulations
