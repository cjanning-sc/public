ACME Engineering Mission Systems is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security.  Our Engineering and Sciences (E&S) organization pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter.  Come be a part of our mission!

We are looking for you to join our team as a Staff Engineer / Deputy Chief Engineer - Integrated Avionics based out of Athens, OH.  As a Staff Engineer / Deputy Chief Engineer - Integrated Avionics at ACME Engineering you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation. Through the use of Model-based Engineering, DevSecOps and Agile practices we continue to evolve how we deliver critical national defense products and capabilities for the warfighter.  Our success is grounded in our ability to embrace change, move quickly and continuously drive innovation.  The successful candidate will be collaborative, open, transparent, and team-oriented with a focus on team empowerment & shared responsibility, flexibility, continuous learning, and a culture of automation.

What You’ll get to Do:

  • Work as a technical leader on a dynamic, fast-paced Agile engineering development environment using Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) methods and NoMagic Cameo, DOORS, Jenkins, and MatLab Simulink, analyzing and developing new capabilities and integrating new technologies into customer war fighters.
  • Support development and management of system requirements for the helicopter platform subsystems, suppliers, and ACME Engineering developed Mission Computer subsystem.
  • Support analysis and modeling of Helicopter usage scenarios, On-board weapons systems, Pilot and crew environments and related Human factors, the impact of Mission Computer changes on buses, interfaces and capabilities of the other components of the integrated helicopter cockpit.
  • Involved with developing and verifying system, interface, cyber, safety, reliability, maintainability and airworthiness requirements, and test planning, testing & integration, and validation activities.
  • Use MBSE and NoMagic Cameo Enterprise Architect (EA) to develop models of new technologies and capabilities for various helicopter platforms
  • Introduce and develop new technologies for concepts and designs of advanced systems
  • Communicate with the USMC customer, engineers and pilots to review, assess and clarify customer needs, and derive product hardware and software technical capabilities, function and features requirements.
  • Regular communication with program leadership, and participate in meetings and technical presentations with customers.
  • Importantly, you will create long-term career development opportunities for yourself on other helicopter platforms including Future Vertical Lift (FVL), as well as for ACME Engineering through developing your knowledge and skills in Integrated avionics, Helicopter platforms, and Systems Engineering, MBSE and Agile methods.

Basic Qualifications:

  • BS degree in STEM field with 14+ years related experience, MS degree in STEM field with 12+ years of related experience, or PhD in STEM field with 9+ years of related experience.
  • An active Secret DoD security clearance required.
  • Demonstrated ability to analyze high level needs utilizing use cases/scenarios and relate them to the requirements.
  • Well-versed, skilled and disciplined in system engineering processes.
  • Background in aerospace systems; basic understanding of aircraft systems, missile systems, avionics, software systems, and their design drivers.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Demonstrated experience managing wide array of senior technical engineers and managers.
  • Demonstrated experience in technical judgment with significant impact to program success.
  • Experience with / knowledge of MBSE or model-based engineering desired
  • Demonstrated experience negotiating with customers to convert customer needs into testable system requirements.
  • Software System Engineering Experience
  • Working knowledge of DOORS
  • Experience working as part of an Agile development environment as Product Owner or Architect
