Northrop Grumman Defense Systems is seeking a Strategic Planning and Business Development professional for the Mission Readiness and Protective Systems (MRPS) Business Unit.  The Manager is responsible for developing long-range objectives and strategic plans, business and market development, and customer engagements for the MRPS Business Unit.  This position will work closely with the MRPS Business Unit and Mission Planning and Decision Support (MPDS) Operating Unit.  The Manager will report to the MRPS Director for Mission Solutions and Strategy.

The ideal candidate will be proactive, organized and willing to help lead MPDS through its next phase of growth. The successful candidate will have outstanding communication and negotiation abilities in support of setting strategy and developing requirements with the warfighter

Responsibilities include:

  • Lead and coach the operating unit in the art of new business development
  • Provide thought leadership to development and synthesis of a cohesive and executable operating unit strategy
  • Manage operating unit opportunity pipeline and related budget; lead pipeline reviews and business acquisition process (BAP) gate reviews for the operating unit
  • Orchestrates new strategic business development activity to identify new business opportunities
  • Architect customer engagement strategies
  • Lead strategy development for new major business captures
  • Responsible for the fiscal management of the department’s multi-million new business and  internal research & development budget
  • Knowledgeable in Customer Relationships Management (CRM) tools (e.g. Salesforce) while following the process standards of the Northrop Grumman Business Acquisition Process (BAP)
  • Visionary leader. Aptitude to see the bigger picture, understand how the resources available across NGC can achieve a larger goal, and lead toward that goal creating MPDS business growth

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree with a minimum of 10 years of Aerospace and Defense experience
  • Working knowledge of DoD’s mission planning, command and control, and/or ISR tools
  • Demonstrated people leadership skills with the ability to assemble and lead teams
  • Experience developing briefings, interfacing with, and shaping executive leadership teams
  • Must be able to obtain and maintain and active DOD (Department of Defense) Secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 10+ developing DoD new business or program management where program growth is a KPI
  • Master’s Degree
  • Established relationships in the defense community, particularly the C2 communities
  • Experience deploying Mission Planning and Decision Support Tools
  • Military experience at senior command and/or staff levels
  • Experience/knowledge of business development, capture, or program management
  • TS/SCI Clearance
