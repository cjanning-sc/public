The selected candidate will Integrate across IPTs and Sub IPTs and act as the focal for the GSC organization.

Essential Functions:

•             Ensures consistency in GSC processes across all GSC disciplines

•             Supports execution of supply chain strategy in the Business Acquisition Process (BAP)

•             Acts as a GSC focal with various program IPTs / Sub IPTs and suppliers

•             Accurately builds/presents program-level leadership briefings focused on parts status and critical program milestones

•             Assists with different GSC affordability initiatives

•             Must have an Active, In-Scope, DOD Security clearance or higher that has been granted / renewed within the past 5-6 years.

•             Must have the ability to obtain program access

Preferred Qualifications:

•             Previous experience in Supply Chain, including supply/demand tracking and planning within a manufacturing environment

•             SAP Knowledge

•             CAM Experience

•             Excellent communication, interpersonal skills and the ability to interface with all levels of employee and management
