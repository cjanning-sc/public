Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems is searching for a Software Engineer (Level 2 or 3) to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will support our Clearfield, Utah location.

This position will support our B-shift on weekday nights, and work 4-10s.

Job Description:

As a Software Engineer, you will use CAD / CAM tools to generate or edit Machine Programs to support automated machining centers. Our team enjoys a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, and collaboration across all levels.  We work independently but interact constantly with other engineering groups and manufacturing to achieve expectations. We’re empowered to make decisions, but can draw upon corporate resources to support us when needed. Each member of the team is tasked with identifying ways to improve efficiency in the team in the pursuit of continuous improvement. We’re looking for a fellow self-starter who enjoys assessing and understanding risk to join us.

This position may be filled at a Level 2 or Level 3 based on the qualifications outlined below.

Basic Qualifications

Software Engineer (Level 2)

  • Bachelor of Science and 2 years of related experience; or 6 years of related experience may be considered in lieu of degree
  • Active DoD Secret Security Clearance and the ability to obtain special program access
  • Working knowledge of NX CAM 5-Axis Milling
  • Fanuc 31i experience
  • Investigate and resolve manufacturing machine problems and instigate corrective actions and follow-up on equipment i.e. CNC mills/lathes
  • Prepare solid model or complex geometry
  • Perform blue print reading
  • Troubleshooting manufacturing processes
  • Windows and MS office skills required

Principal Software Engineer (Level 3)

  • Bachelor of Science and 5 years of related experience; or 6 years of related experience may be considered in lieu of degree

  • Fanuc 31i experience
  • Investigate and resolve manufacturing machine problems and instigate corrective actions and follow-up on equipment i.e. CNC mills/lathes
  • Prepare solid model or complex geometry
  • Perform blue print reading

  • Windows and MS office skills required

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Siemens 840D experience

  • Aerospace or manufacturing experience
