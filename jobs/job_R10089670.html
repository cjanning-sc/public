ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Program Planning Scheduler - Staff Level to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This individual will serve as a process/tool Homeroom Subject Matter Expert for the Program Planning team at the Launch & Missile Defense Systems division level, specifically our Launch Vehicles group.  This position will be located in Louisville, AZ.  Partial telework will be considered.

Roles and Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Enabling high caliber, rapid response EVMS process, tools, and training.
  • Providing best practice program planning and scheduling team support.
  • Assisting the team in development of high quality IBR artifacts, SRAs, latest Health Metrics and deliverable products.
  • Aiding program planning to hit target team annual goals.
  • Ensuring team planning data, logical sequencing of activities, establishment of activity durations and loading resources in an Integrated Master Schedule network.
  • Implementing automated planning/scheduling software tools to perform program planning.
  • Disseminating guidance on schedule analysis and reporting, including critical path analysis and assessment, schedule risk assessments, and schedule health metrics analysis.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree with 15+ years of related experience - OR - a Master's degree with 13+ years of related experience. Additional 4+ years of experience accepted in lieu of a degree.
  • Proficient in developing and maintaining a program schedule in a scheduling software tool (MS Project).
  • Ability to understand and maintain a resource loaded network.
  • Extensive Earned Value Management Systems (EVMS) experience.
  • Strong PC skills and proficient knowledge with Microsoft Office Suite, including MS Excel.
  • Experience with DCMA audits and compliance rules and regulations.
  • Understand process and implementation of schedule risk assessments (using Acumen Fuse or other commercial SRA tools).
  • Demonstrated ability to work in a team environment with technical subject matter experts and business operations personnel.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • US Citizenship is a prerequisite.
  • Previous leadership experience.
  • Direct experience with automated cost and schedule integration (to include MS Project and Cobra).
  • DCMA certification.
  • Aerospace/Defense Industry experience.
  • FAR or DFAR experience.
