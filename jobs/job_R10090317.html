This requisition may be filled at a higher grade based on the qualifications listed below.

  • Principal Engineer DevOps: Bachelor’s degree with 5 + years of software development experience; 3 years with a Master's; 0 years with a PhD
  • Sr. Principal Engineer DevOps: Bachelor’s degree with 9 + years of software development experience; 7 years with a Master's; 4 years with a PhD
  • Active Top Secret/SCI clearance or higher with a willingness to obtain a poly
  • Experience with software systems deployment and Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD)
  • Knowledge of DevOps, i.e. Jenkins and Nexus

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with AWS
  • Knowledge of Kubernetes
  • Experience with Red Hat Linux administration
  • Experience developing Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, Helm, Puppet)
  • Willing to document key knowledge and share with team
  • Active TS/SCI clearance w/ Poly
