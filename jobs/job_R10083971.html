Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Analysis and Test Manager 2 to join its team of diverse professionals in support of the Command and Launch Design Integration team on the Sentinel (GBSD) program. This leadership role is located in Roy, Utah. Learn more about the Sentinel (GBSD) program here.  Northrop Grumman supports the Air Force’s sustainment, development, production and deployment of hardware and system modifications for Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Ground and Airborne Launch Control Systems, Launch Facilities and associated infrastructure.

What you will get to do:

The Analysis and Test Manager 2 will report to the Command and Launch Segment Design Integration Lead. Responsibilities include:

  • Lead and direct a team of Structural Analysts and Test Engineers responsible for developing and managing functional, performance, design, compliance, and verification/validation requirements for Weapon System infrastructure in a Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) environment
  • Provide people leadership for a diverse team of Analysts and Engineers, to include performance management, career development, staffing, and merit planning
  • Manage cost and schedule performance for Analysis and Test control accounts as a Control Account Manager (CAM), adhering to Earned Value Management principles and processes.
  • Manage engineering tools and applications for structural and other analytical models and develop the necessary engineering support team to sustain and evolve capabilities
  • Establish the processes that govern modeling, simulation, analysis, and test development and sustainment and participate in the enforcement of processes using modern model-based methods and practices in support of a full digital engineering transformation
  • Provide technical review authority, process audit and process enforcement across all program activities to ensure technical artifact integrity
  • Support planning, budgeting staff forecasting for contract direct and indirect budgets supporting process development.
  • The qualified candidate will be leading a team of engineers to create and present Analysis and Test products at the appropriate level of maturation for any given milestone and demonstrating team readiness in relation to achieving the milestone entrance and exit criteria. The candidate will be identifying required contractual deliverables and non-contractual documentation and driving corresponding updates to closure. They will be coordinating and collaborating with diverse engineering design teams to assist in managing the technical baseline as well as subcontractor engineering teams to help facilitate the integration of their products with the larger GBSD program engineering products.
