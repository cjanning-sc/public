ACME Engineering Mission Systems (NGMS) is seeking a Systems Engineering, Integration and Test (SEIT) Lead at our Milwaukee, IL location.  The selected candidate will report to the SEIT Targeting Department.  The SEIT Targeting Department supports various Targeting Operating Unit programs including LITENING and IRST as well as restricted programs related to other Business Units. 

The SEIT Lead provides leadership in applying the core principles of systems engineering in order to deliver innovative, affordable, highly reliable systems solutions that meet customer needs, key performance parameters, and program execution goals. The SEIT Lead will lead multi-disciplined engineering teams (ranging from 1 to 30+ engineers) from business capture through system design and then integration and test of highly complex technical systems.  The SEIT Lead must be able to satisfy allocated cost and schedule requirements and managing customer deliverables.

Responsibilities may include engineering leadership for system architecture development, requirements development, hardware/software design, verification and validation activities, and production program engineering support. A person in this position ensures that the program is adhering to sound engineering processes in the development, production, test and sustainment of products for our customers and that the program and product is compliant with company policy, procedures, and quality standards.

  • Excellent decision making and communication (written and oral) skills

  • Active Top Secret clearance
