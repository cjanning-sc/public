Northrop Grumman Space Sector - Payload and Ground Division is seeking a Principal/Sr. Principal Hardware Engineer to work in our Aurora, CO office.


The primary roles/responsibilities of this role include conducting research, design, development, and testing of information processing hardware such as computer chips, circuit boards, computer systems, and electrical components. Designing new or modifies existing products, develops technical specifications for production, analyzes hardware configuration and processing solutions, and tests hardware conformance to specifications. In this role, the selected candidate must have the ability to work within a group or independently where collaboration and attention to detail is critical to mission success. The selected must be able to work closely with contractors and government customers. The ability to work in varying weather conditions, and with hand tools and general construction practices is also needed. Basic Qualifications for a Principal Hardware Engineer:

- Bachelor’s degree and 5 years of related experience; 3 years with a Masters; 0 years with a PhD. An additional 4 years of related experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
- Must have an active Top Secret clearance.
- Ability to obtain a Passport.
- Must have prior experience troubleshooting technical problems and issues developing solutions.
- Must be able to travel ~25% of the time.
- Must be capable of performing hardware integration with minimal supervision.

Basic Qualifications for a Sr. Principal Hardware Engineer:

- Bachelor’s degree and 9 years of related experience; 7 years with a Masters; 4 years with a PhD. An additional 4 years of related experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
- Must have an active Top Secret clearance.
- Ability to obtain a Passport.
- Must have prior experience troubleshooting technical problems and issues developing solutions.
- Must be able to travel ~25% of the time.
- Must be capable of performing hardware integration with minimal supervision.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Master’s degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Physics, Math, or related discipline.
- Experience in leadership, leading teams, and providing technical guidance to other engineers.
- Position prefers a candidate with a current TS/SCI Clearance with polygraph.
