Northrop Grumman Space Systems is searching for multiple experienced Manager of Programs, M2, to join the Minotaur Program team within the Launch Vehicles business unit located in Chandler, AZ.  The Minotaur Program provides space launch services to the US Space Force and other DoD customers.  This position reports to the Minotaur Chief Engineer and works in concert with Minotaur Program team to support the entire Minotaur portfolio of launch vehicle configurations. Most Minotaur engineering efforts occur in Chandler, AZ; launch activities occur at various federal launch sites in the United States.

You support the Minotaur Chief Engineer in the technical oversight of the Minotaur program and the Minotaur Program Manager by ensuring proper allocation and coordination of engineering resources.
