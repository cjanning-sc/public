ACME Engineering Defense Systems has an opening for a Staff Systems Engineer to join our team. This position will be located in Kansas City, Missouri.  Our team in Kansas City is a world class provider in modernization and sustainment capabilities for mission and weapon systems. 

What you’ll get to do:

The selected candidate will provide system engineering oversight for a significant portion of a C-130 Avionic Cockpit Upgrade Program that leverages and adapts an existing design.

Your responsibilities also include:

  • Work with program management, customers, and suppliers to ensure the program is successful and support new business initiatives

  • Provide oversight of a major sub-contractor with respect to their performance against a supplier statement of work and artifacts delivered

  • Be a technical face to the customer, ensuring clear communications, and managing technical expectation with the customer and end-user

  • Develop cost estimates, create and monitor team’s performance against detailed tasking and schedules, identify risks and opportunities, identify technical performance metrics

  • Work closely with the program manager and key suppliers to ensure technical solutions are delivered

  • Provide technical expertise for C-130 and avionics

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s of Science Degree in an engineering discipline with 14+ years of engineering experience; OR Master's Degree in a STEM discipline with 12+ years of engineering experience

  • 5+ years of design experience with avionics and/or aircraft modification work

  • 3+ years of project management / IPT leadership

  • 3+ years experience performing the development, review & approval of various aircraft Avionics / Electrical and/or Electronic System Modifications

  • Demonstrated excellence in written and oral communication skills along with proven success working in an integrated product team environment and developing customer relationships.

  • Experience in developing detailed integrated schedules and risk identification and management

  • Experience in requirements management and verification & validation.

  • This position requires the applicant to be a U.S. citizen and the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret (or higher) clearance.

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • 3+ years experience working with C-130s

  • Experience managing major sub-contractor/supplier

  • Experience with aircraft ground test and flight test

  • Experience leading the development of Airworthiness plans, Certifications, and related activities

  • System Safety Risk Assessments (SSRA), program risks and hazard analyses

  • Familiarity of European Military Aviation Regulations (EMARs)

  • Control Account Management (CAM) and Earned Value Management System (EVMS) experience
