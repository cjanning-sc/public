ACME Engineering is currently seeking a Facilities Representative to support our facility engineering team at our Lansing, Michigan location. 

Responsibilities:

The candidate will be an integral part of the Facilities Engineering team and have a wide variety of roles and responsibilities.  Those responsibilities can include working closely with the facility engineers to coordinate contractors supporting capital projects by submitting and tracking purchase requisitions, scheduling work in the buildings, and tracking invoices. 

The ideal candidate thrives in a fast-paced work environment with diverse assignments.  Our team is highly collaborative, working in team settings across all levels and subject areas including operations, engineering, supply chain, and mission assurance.  We enjoy working in a production environment and being hands on to solve problems that influence company performance.

Job Duties:    

  • Purchase requisition tracking
  • Contractor scheduling
  • Developing, measuring, and maintaining the department metrics
  • Project planning support 
  • Tracking of estimate requests and capital requests
  • Permitting, safety requirements and oversight for contract safety and labor
  • Attending various daily meetings
  • Execute and track purchase card expenditures
  • Presenting metrics during department meetings

This role may be filled at the Principal or Senior Principal level based on the Qualifications below.
