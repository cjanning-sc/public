Join ACME Engineering – Xetron, part of Mission Systems sector, focused on the development of new capabilities for our military and intelligence customers around the globe. Remember when you were eager about getting to work because of the excitement of the challenge, the importance of the solution, and the fun you had with your team?  Xetron is that kind of place! 

As a small Operating Unit of the larger ACME Engineering Corporation located in Madison Wisconsin, Xetron is a rare example of a company with a small business culture operating collaboratively with its larger business parent. This unique relationship offers employees the ability to literally know everyone at the site at a personal level while still working on technologies and products that are beyond state-of-the-art. You’ll get the opportunity to wrestle with the next big problems in an atmosphere of collaboration and cooperation, sharing your expertise with team members at all levels. If you’re the type of person that likes to see a product through the full development lifecycle from concept to deployment, this is the place for you.

We are seeking cleared or clearable Cyber Test Engineer who are self-motivated, energetic individuals with a background in Information Technology/Computer Science, or a related technical discipline who are interested in joining our highly skilled Cyber Development teams to develop unique solutions critical to global security.

Successful candidates will participate in a broad range of applications with exposure to a variety of programs that provide critical solutions to U.S. government agencies and the Department of Defense.  Consequently, we are seeking Cyber professionals who already have security clearance, or those who are readily clearable. Our Cyber Test Engineer will lead the test of cyber operations products to ensure verification of requirements and validation that the system meets the Concept of Operations. 

Essential Functions:

  • Interface with the customer regarding test and verification activities of cyber operations products.

  • Work with multiple teams to develop stress, functional, acceptance, and ad-hoc tests based on the requirements and operational scenarios.

  • Automate the testing and characterization of cyber products to the extent possible given budgets, project time frame, and long-term benefit of the product.

  • Design, develop and configure system test components necessary to perform end-to-end testing of cyber products.

  • Develop scripts as necessary to integrate components, perform new capabilities, facilitate testing, etc.

  • Characterize cyber operations products under a multitude of configurations.

  • Monitor daily automated test executions and work with teams to analyze the cause and apply fixes for failures.

  • Generate and present to the end customer various milestone packages including Software Requirement Review, Test Readiness Review, and Acceptance Review.

  • Specify improvements to test and development network infrastructures.

This position can be filled at the Engineer or Principal Engineer level.

Basic Qualifications Engineer Level:

  • Computer/IT or STEM related BS degree and 2 years engineering experience OR Computer/IT or STEM related MS Degree with 0 years engineering experience

  • Exposure to developing test plans, procedures, and requirements documentation

  • US Citizenship and the ability to obtain and maintain a Top-Secret security clearance

Basic Qualifications Principal Engineer Level:

  • Computer/IT or STEM related BS degree and 5 years engineering experience OR Computer/IT or STEM related MS Degree with 3 years engineering experience OR Computer/IT or STEM related Ph.D. with 0 years engineering experience

  • Experience developing test plans, procedures, and requirements documentation

  • US Citizenship and the ability to obtain and maintain a Top-Secret security clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Review, revise, and modify documents prepared by others

  • Experience planning and performing ad-hoc test to uncover esoteric software errors by performing non-standard sequences

  • General understanding of databases

  • Experience with MySQL or other databases

  • Programing skills in a scripting language such as Python, Bash, Expect, PowerShell

  • Active Top Secret security clearance
