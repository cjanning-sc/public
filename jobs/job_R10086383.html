Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Staff Circuit Design Engineer to join the Flight Systems Team. This position is located in Roy, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. A competitive relocation package may be included with offer.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will fill the role of Guidance Computer Analog Board Design Engineer joining our team of qualified, diverse individuals. The individual in this role will develop Analog board designs utilized in the Guidance Computer (GC) flight Unit.  The GC is a high reliability, radiation hardened flight controller for the Sentinel Aerospace Vehicle (AVE).  The Sentinel Guidance Computer contains digital processing, digital I/O, and analog actuator/power I/O electronics functions. 

The scope of this role includes collaboration with systems engineers, mechanical engineers, manufacturing engineers, and other electrical engineers to perform design trades and requirements flow down, to create interconnection drawings, to perform electrical analysis, to develop test plans, and ultimately to deliver working hardware that meets program requirements. The qualified candidate will be an experienced analog design engineer who will work on the actuator drive board design and development with application in an embedded control processing function.  This position reports directly to the Guidance Computer Responsible Design Engineer (RDE).
