Sr Principal Engineer Systems - Space-to-Ground Communication Network Requirements & Verification Lead (TS/SCI Required)
R10085705
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Space Systems Sector, Tactical Space Systems Division, seeks a qualified Sr. Principal Systems Engineer to support a space-to-ground communications network. This position is located in Dulles, VA or Colorado Springs, CO. Successful candidates for this position will work in a technical role for the National Security Systems (NSS) business area. This position offers the opportunity to work on exciting, cutting-edge programs and new mission areas. The position is to lead the requirements definition effort for a spacecraft development program.

Candidates should be knowledgeable of requirements flow down and management, validation and verification planning, interface definition, and expertise with various engineering tools such as DOORS and Cameo.  The role will include validating the customer level requirements and pulling from multiple inputs to develop the program level requirements.  The role will include support for major program reviews such as SRR, PDR, and CDR. The role will include interface with clients, as well as regular interactions with internal engineering associates and program management.

  • B.S. in STEM - Science, Technology, Engineering, Math or related area and 9 years of experience; M.S. in STEM or related area and 7 years of experience; or a PhD in STEM or related area and 4 years of related experience

  • Experience with Model Based Systems Engineering for requirements and architecture management (DOORS and Cameo Systems Modeler preferred)

  • Must have US Citizenship with current or active Top Secret/SCI level Clearance

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Space industry experience - Space domain/space vehicles, subsystems, and components

  • Radiofrequency (RF) and/or digital communications experience

  • Space-ground communications experience

  • Experience coordinating the efforts of diverse stakeholders.
