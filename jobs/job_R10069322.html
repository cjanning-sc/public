The Tactical Space Systems I&T Mechanical Engineering organization seeks to fill an Engineering Manager position for our Gilbert, AZ location. This is a dual role position encompassing both functional management and technical performance supporting current programs and initiatives. The successful candidate will clearly demonstrate strong leadership skills to achieve sustainable top performance. He/she will have a track record as an effective communicator and problem solver who is able to develop and maintain good working relationships with internal and external stakeholders and customers. This candidate will demonstrate a commitment to ethics and integrity, and the capability to innovate while challenging traditional ways of doing business. Demonstrated abilities to build diverse and inclusive teams with high levels of engagement, including mentoring and coaching, are a must.

This manager will functionally lead the Mechanical Ground Support Equipment (MGSE) design engineering team. You will work alongside the lead engineer to ensure the success of preliminary, conceptual, and detailed design of MGSE components, including the creation of detailed models through drawing release, and the coordination of manufacturing, integration, test, and delivery to I&T.

 FUNCTIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:

  • Staffing support and workload forecasting
  • Conducting periodic performance evaluations, including written and 1 on 1 mid-year and annual reviews
  • Providing leadership and guidance to the staff
  • Managing indirect and capital needs
  • Training coordination and assessments of the Mechanical Engineering Team
  • Professional development and mentoring of direct reports
  • Supporting Functional Homeroom initiatives and projects which includes but is not limited to: process improvements, event response teams, corrective and preventive action implementation, safety initiatives, Capex, Career Goal planning, and associated business activities/processes.
  • Support Cross Functional, Cross Division and Community of Practice collaborative efforts.
  • Ownership for factory standard processes.

 PROGRAM RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:

  • Leading a team of Mechanical Engineers that support multiple spacecraft and/or product lines.
  • Perform requirements definition, conceptual design, preliminary / layout, and detail design, drawing release, design review presentations, and support to fabrication/integration
  • Lead and participate in system-level trades to ensure the design meets all physical, mechanical, and environmental requirements while accommodating the required payload, power, propulsion, thermal, communications, and attitude control system requirements
  • Providing mechanical operation status to upper management and the customer
  • Problem Solving, Root Cause/Corrective Actions
  • Providing Technical guidance to engineering staff
  • Support various Program Mechanical Activities
  • Supports/ Operational Metrics for Quality, Schedule, Cost and Productivity.

Basic Qualifications:

This requisition may be filled at either a Manager Engineering 1 or Manager Engineering 2.

Basic Qualifications – Manager 1:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university with 5 Years of engineering experience; OR Master's Degree with 3 Years of engineering experience
  • Experience within the Aerospace and/or Defense industry processes and standards 
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Top Secret Clearance or SCI access.

Basic Qualifications – Manager 2:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university with 7 Years of engineering experience; OR Master's Degree with 5 Years of engineering experience
  • At least 2 years of proven demonstrated experience leading a technical team or project
  • Experience within the Aerospace and/or Defense industry processes and standards 
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Top Secret Clearance or SCI access. 


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Previous experience in MGSE design, fabrication, and proof load testing
  • Experience in defining driving requirements in fluid design environment
  • Experience providing technical product support to a customer or a business.
  • Experience establishing goal driven deadlines.
  • Experience supporting PDR, CDR, MRR, and TRR reviews.
  • Experienced in all aspects of the engineering development lifecycle.
  • Experience in I&T for Satellite products
  • High proficiency in problem solving.
  • Effective communication and presentation skills
  • Experience in working technical and programmatic issues across a global team
