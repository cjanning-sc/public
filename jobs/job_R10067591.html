Our Joliet, MD location is seeking a Production Planner and Scheduler 2 to join our team our team of qualified, diverse individuals.

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Track material shortages for work-orders, kits, sub-assemblies based on bill-of-material or drawings or configuration. Highlight to management critical part shortages that will jeopardize ship schedule

  • Create and release work orders based on bill-of-materials and configuration. Close work orders after sub-assemblies, assemblies and integration are complete

  • Maintain materials, parts and bill of materials, ensuring bill of material supports efficient material flow and is aligned with assembly process

  • Planner must understand manufacturing capacity and allocate as required to meet build plan commitments. Expedites and reschedules open purchase orders to meet production-planning requirement

  • Must understand & execute material requirements planning (MRP), capacity planning and other advanced material planning needs

  • Coordinates and expedites flow of materials, parts, and assemblies between departments, according to manufacturing schedules or priorities, and compiles and maintains manual or computerized record

  • Effectively communicate and report material status, issues, and highlight

  • Prioritize Work Orders and program needs against overall company program need

  • Update Work Orders based on Engineering Change Orders (ECOs)

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor’s degree and 3 years' of relevant work experience in a production control and manufacturing environment utilizing an MRP system required. 

  • Working computer literacy using an ERP system, Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Outlook), spreadsheet and production control and work order procedures are required

  • Must have experience in rescheduling, canceling, opening and closing work orders per MRP

  • Must be experienced in understanding product structures, configurations and bill-of-material and parts specification

  • Must have experience in creating metrics for production demands, tracking part shortages, open PO’s, track due dates and inventory report, and line of balance

  • Strong analytical skills with the ability to work under pressure and very tight timelines

  • US Citizenship required

Preferred qualifications

  • Manufacturing, Operations and/or Program Management experience desired.

  • Reasonable knowledge of manufacturing, inventory, production, procurement and quality basics, engineering flows and ability to read technical data i.e., blue prints, schematics and other documents.
