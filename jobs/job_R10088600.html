ACME Engineering is seeking a Program Manager 1 for our location at Owensboro, AZ.

Job Scope
Responsible for leadership of Integrated Project Team (IPT) supporting the overall Legacy Guns Product Portfolio. Program Manager responsibilities include: cost, schedule, and technical performance of a program, including program performance on a delivery order.

Specific responsibilities include:

  • Leading and directing cross-functional Integrated Program Teams (IPT) to meet program cost, schedule and technical performance objectives

  • Cultivating customer relationships and intimacy to develop further opportunities within the customer community

  • Measuring and reporting program performance

  • Delivering presentations to customers, Guns Operating Unit and Business Unit leadership, and other program stakeholders

  • Participating in the negotiation of contracts, contract changes, budgets, schedule requirements, and key terms and conditions

  • Establishing objectives for engineering efforts

  • Identification, distribution, tracking, and completion of program requirements

  • Development of and adherence to budget baselines utilizing Earned Value Management (EVM) or similar cost control methodologies

  • Identifying, allocating and managing program resources

  • Managing Government/customer supplied property or information (GFE, CFE, etc.)

  • Managing suppliers to meet program objectives

  • Adherence to all internal processes, policies, and applicable industry standards

  • Ensuring program team understands and adheres to contract scope and manages change

  • Conducting risk & opportunity management practices including identification, mitigation and realization

Basic Qualifications:

  • 10 years of experience required. We would consider a Bachelor’s Degree and 6 years of relevant experience or MS and 4 year.

  • Experience supporting U.S. Government or commercial contracts and customers and/or project management in other industries

  • 2 years of experience leading the performance of tasks on schedule, at cost and achieving all requirements as either project lead, integrated program team or cost account manager.        

  • Demonstrated experience working with customers to ensure project success.

  • Strong formal and informal communication skills with all levels of internal and external management

  • Defense Industry and DOD contract experience desired

  • Familiarity with mechanical and electrical engineering processes and other specialty engineering activities within the product development process.

  • Ability to manage risk to reduce likelihood and severity of program impacts

  • Ability to operate in a dynamic, fast-paced work environment

  • Strategic thinker with excellent organizational skills

  • Experience writing statements of work and estimating project scope.

  • Be able to obtain a DoD Secret Clearance

  • Able to travel both international and domestically up to 25% of the time

  • Must be a US Citizen

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Master’s degree or MBA

  • Engineering or technical background

  • Prior experience with large or medium caliber military armaments

  • Prior management of Internal Research and Development (IRAD) programs
