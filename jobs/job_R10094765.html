Business Development (BD) & Strategy Director 2

1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Defense Systems sector is seeking to consolidate separate Business Development and Strategy roles into a Business Development (BD) & Strategy Director 2 to lead growth in the Global Sustainment and Modernization (GSM) Division.  The Director, as part of the growth-oriented and customer-focused GSM Division leadership team, will be responsible for leading BD & Strategy for the Division, providing a vision for growth and identifying opportunities to expand existing product lines and aggressively pursue new markets.  This leader will drive division growth and be responsible for delivering an extraordinary customer experience across our domestic and global customer environment infusing strategic focus and messaging into all elements of customer engagement.  

This position reports to the Vice President, General Manager for the GSM Division within the Defense Systems Sector. The selected candidate will preferably be located on-site with our team in McLean, Virginia but full-time teleworker will be considered.

 

Responsibilities include leading a team to:

  • Own the business development and strategy function, including its operation (budgeting, forecasting, etc.), strategic planning, market analysis, and pursuit and capture planning.
  • Develop and drive the strategic direction of the Division, both domestically and internationally, and in both DCS as well as FMS related opportunities.
  • Provide vision and leadership in the development and synthesis of a cohesive and executable division strategy.  Build new market capture strategies and initiate product expansion to drive organic growth. 
  • Lead, direct, coach, and develop business development, capture and strategy directors and managers.
  • Implement reliable business development and capture processes, that are consistent with Company, Sector and Division business strategy, values, and operating principles.
  • Customer Engagement - Responsible for the development of customer engagement strategies across the Division portfolio, in collaboration with the Division, Business Units (BU), Functions and Defense Systems Sector teams considering emerging markets, industries, and technologies
  • Create, maintain, and implement a customer engagement and senior customer visit plan across domestic and international military, government, and industry leadership.  Expand relationships both internally and externally, with key DOD customers, industry partners, as well as relationships with key international customers.
  • Understand and help shape requirements and expectations of key customers. Maintain and communicate an understanding of customer planning and budgeting processes.
  • Secure strategic alliances and teaming opportunities that strengthen market position and fill capability gaps critical to achieving market expansion strategies.  Lead Division portfolio assessment to identify and pursue advantageous strategic alliances, mergers and acquisition candidates.
  • Lead the pipeline new business development activity to define new business opportunities that ultimately achieve the AOP and LRSP growth goals for the division
  • Lead development of a comprehensive discretionary Investment Strategy & NCTA Management, to include Performance Tracking, to support the vision and growth objectives of the Division.
  • Responsible for the fiscal management of the department’s budget
  • Responsible for the accuracy and consistency of the Customer Relationships Management (CRM) tools (SalesForce) while following the process standards of the Northrop Grumman Business Acquisition Process (BAP)

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have one of the following: Bachelor’s degree in business or related discipline with a minimum of 15 years of Aerospace and/or Defense industry experience or Master’s degree in business or related discipline with a minimum of 10 years of Aerospace and/or Defense industry experience
  • 10+ years of industry experience in business development, capture leadership and/or program management
  • Demonstrated multi-disciplinary global business development, strategy development and capture experience
  • Ability to acquire a US Department of Defense (DOD) Secret security clearance
  • Must be able to travel as needed (up to 50% of the time)


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master’s Degree in technical/business field
  • Demonstrated people leadership skills; direct and indirect leadership of large teams; ability to work effectively in a team environment
  • Proven ability to provide independent critical assessments, that ultimately result in better outcomes for the enterprise
  • Proven highly effective executive communication, presentation, and influencing skills
  • Previous Profit & Loss (P&L) responsibility
  • Strategic planning, new business development, and investment planning
  • Strong customer domain knowledge and a proven track record of sustained business growth
  • Active DOD secret clearance
