ACME Engineering Mission Systems is a trusted provider of mission-enabling solutions for global security.  Our Engineering and Sciences (E&S) organization pushes the boundaries of innovation, redefines engineering capabilities, and drives advances in various sciences. Our team is chartered with providing the skills, innovative technologies to develop, design, produce and sustain optimized product lines across the sector while providing a decisive advantage to the warfighter.  Come be a part of our mission!

We are looking for you to join our team as a Principal or Sr Principal Technical Field Engineer Subject Matter Expert (SME) based out of Cleveland, CA (Spectrum Center facilities).

What you’ll get to Do:

Have an opportunity to travel to sustained operational locations overseas and develop your technical expertise across the engineering spectrum. Be part of our field engineering team that operates and maintains complex tactical communication systems worldwide.

 As the Hardware SME for our Field Engineering team, you will be supporting operations and maintenance of complex tactical communications systems and data links providing voice and data services in a sustained operation located overseas and CONUS (continental U.S.).  This technical SME position will report directly to the Field Engineering Manager. This is an exciting opportunity to join a dynamic team and develop technical expertise across the engineering spectrum.

While at the production facility:

  • Coordinating non routine maintenance and technical notices

  • Coordinating the Troubleshooting of OCONUS issues that require engineering assistance

  • Reviewing schematics and other engineering and maintenance process related documentation

  • Providing OCONUS operations technical expertise to factory engineering teams and the training department.

  • Tracking and advising the factory engineering team regarding the resolution of OCONUS discrepancies

  • Assisting Spectrum Software and Cyber engineering in development and delivery of software upgrades and service packs

  • Advising Systems engineering on the potential impacts of future design changes to OCONUS operations

  • Project Management installing and upgrading equipment in the field.

  • Will work closely with an Active Duty/Government customer as well as internal ACME Engineering Integrated Product Teams (IPTs).

While on travel OCONUS (outside the continental U.S):

  • Work on a flight line configuring, maintaining and testing payloads

  • Troubleshoot Hardware/Software issues

  • Perform system and computer maintenance

  • Operate and control data links during operational missions

  • Perform Linux System Administration and Account Management

  • Perform network troubleshooting and monitor IP Network health and status

  • Perform as COMSEC Responsible Officer (CRO)

  • Perform as a Technical Lead of the OCONUS Tech Team

 

This position may be filled as a Principal or Sr Principal Technical Field Engineer.
