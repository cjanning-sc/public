GBSD Principal Supply Chain Subcontract Specialist (Roy, UT, Huntsville, AL, or San Diego, CA)
R10090885
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Space Systems (NGSP) has openings in support of the Ground-Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) Engineering and Manufacturing Development (EMD) contract and the Ground Subsystems Support Contract (GSSC).  The purpose of the GBSD Program is to replace the current ICBM system, including the weapon systems command and control; flight, launch and ground systems; and cyber elements.

We are seeking a Principal Supply Chain Subcontract Specialist. This position will be based in one of these locations:

  • Roy, UT
  • Huntsville, AL
  • San Diego, CA

The selected candidate will perform the following functions:

  • Supports the development of subcontract specifications, work statements, and terms and conditions for the procurement of specialized materials, equipment, and/or services.
  • Responsible for source selection from contract proposal to execution phases.
  • Coordinates and collaborates with key stakeholders such as contracts, engineering, quality, supplier development, planning, operations, program and end-users.
  • Prepares bid packages, conducts bidders’ conferences, analyzes and evaluates proposals, negotiates subcontract provisions, selects or recommends subcontractors, writes awards, and administers resulting subcontracts.
  • In conjunction with quality organization, evaluates and monitors supplier quality and reliability, as well as supplier ability to support required delivery schedules.
  • Identifies strategies and opportunities to reduce cost, improve efficiency and manage risk through strategic and targeted negotiations.
  • Manages performance of subcontracts, ensures compliance with all public law requirements as well as company (and business) policies and procedures including import/export regulations.
  • Employs sound business practices and assesses supplier performance as part of follow-up activities.
  • Forecasts, reviews and approves supplier payment as needed.
  • Utilizes process tools such as Lean Six Sigma with suppliers to drive quality, efficiency and continuous improvement.
  • Executes teaming agreements and non-disclosure agreements, which may include several parties.
  • Negotiates and coordinates additions, deletions, or modifications to subcontracts.
  • Reads, interprets, and implements written procedures to maintain compliance

This position will negotiate with both large businesses and small businesses on a variety of complex-type contracts including cost-plus-incentive-fee type. 

This position must have an understanding and application of principles, concepts, practices, and standards. Full knowledge of industry practices. Independently demonstrates the skill and ability to perform complex professional tasks.  Contributes to the development of new concepts, techniques, and standards. Able to communicate effectively and clearly present technical approaches and findings.  Able to be creative in finding solutions to resolve issues. 

This position will develop solutions to complex problems, which require the regular use of ingenuity and innovation. Ensures solutions are consistent with organization objectives.

This position will attend program meetings and may be required to present to groups.  Must be able to work independently and have strong organizational skills.

This position must be knowledgeable of the FAR/DFARS and processes and/or procedures required to be followed in order to be in compliance with FAR/DFARS. 

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree and 5+ years of relevant experience in subcontracts, contracts, supply chain or business/project management. Or in lieu of a degree, 9+ years of relevant experience in subcontracts, contracts, supply chain or business/project management
  • Functional knowledge of FAR & DFARS
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Applications
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Supply Chain Management
  • Active Secret Clearance
  • Experience with SAP
  • Subcontract negotiation experience with contracts >$100M
  • Experience managing multiple contract types from contract award through contract closeout
