Northrop Grumman Space Systems (NGSP) is looking for you to join our team as a Systems Administrator/Principal Systems Administrator based out of Albuquerque, NM.

Responsibilities/Duties:

  • Work in an agile based team consisting of systems administrators, systems engineers, hardware engineers, and software engineers

  • Builds large, integrated hardware systems from bare metal including racking, cabling, powering, initializing systems

  • Supports the integration and test of hardware and software subsystems

  • Recommends and implements system enhancements that will improve the performance and reliability of the system; including installing, upgrading/patching, monitoring, problem resolution, and configuration management.

  • Updates Windows WSUS servers for monthly patching and virus scanning

  • Creates and maintains administrator and user accounts through Windows Domain Controller

  • Creates and maintains system documentation

  • Performs tuning system performance,

  • Installs system wide software and allocates mass storage space

  • Performs backups and recovery as required

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have a Bachelor's degree from an accredited college, with 6 years of related experience in systems administration; 4 years with a Master’s degree; 10 years of related experience in lieu of a degree

  • Experienced Linux System Administration for Red Hat/CentO with Red Hat Certified System Administrator or no less than 3 years’ experience

  • Windows Systems admin experience

  • Demonstrated understanding of and experience with virtualization technologies, including VMware within a virtualized environment.

  • Good communication skills

  • Must be a US citizen with an active TS/SCI  clearance (required to start)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with satellite ground station hardware system administration

  • Experience with docker containers

  • Experience with ansible or other configuration management tools

  • Experience with cloud providers or hypervisor experience

  • Security+ certification
