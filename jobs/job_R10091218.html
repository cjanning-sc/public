Propulsion Systems has an Operations Team Supervisor position at our Promontory, UT plant to lead a team of operators in the Composite winding area. Some of the time could be spent working off shifts.

As an Operations Team Supervisor (OTS) you will be responsible for direct supervision of manufacturing activities in M179.  The OTS is accountable for safety, quality, on the job training, productivity, and hardware delivery schedules within areas of responsibility.  The preferred candidate communicates expectations clearly and initiates necessary actions to ensure that organizational goals and business objectives are accomplished and ensures company policy directives and operating procedures are being followed.  An OTS maintains a presence at the point of most criticality or difficulty to favorably influence the operations of a high performance manufacturing team and exemplifies adherence to all safety rules, regulations, and standards.

Requirements:

  • 6 -10 years of manufacturing experience

  • Two years of successful team leadership

  • Skill in interpreting engineering drawings, specifications and operating procedures

  • Knowledge of Safety and Quality policies and procedures.

  • Must be able to work flexible and changing shift schedules; day or night shifts, including weekends

 Selection Criteria:

  • Demonstrated experience leading a work team to achieve organizational goals.

  • Basic computer skills to include Word, Access, Excel and PowerPoint.

  • Demonstrates skill and initiative in resolving functional or process related problems or issues.

  • Skill in interpreting engineering drawings, specifications and operating procedures

  • Familiarity with composite procedures and processes.

  • Manufacturing experience including knowledge of supporting systems including ESI, ePIC, and Maximo.

  • Engaged in continuous improvement initiatives.  Utilizes employee cooperation and involvement to achieve area objectives.

  • Effective written and verbal communication skills.  Makes sound business decisions.
