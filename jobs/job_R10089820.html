Contract Data Management responsibilities include: 

*This is a part time 20 hour per week position

  • Performs overall contract review/interpretation and keeps up-to-date with approved contractual requirements, including Statement of Work (SOW), contract modifications, Points of Contact (POCs), approval/distribution obligations, and any other information that will assist with the organized management and accountability of Contract Data Requirements List (CDRL) data items.
  • Creates and maintains a Contract Data Status Report (CDSR) via an Excel spreadsheet for establishing, recording and reporting a Program’s CDRL identification, requirements and data submittal forecasts/occurrences.
  • Works with the assigned Contracts Administrator and/or Program Management, as needed, to ensure the timely delivery of CDRL data items and/or resolution of any contractual or data submittal issues/concerns.
  • Composes contract submittal letters and delivers to the customer along with applicable CDRLs via customer-sponsored tools/methods.
  • Gathers and maintains monthly metrics of on-time, late and early CDRL submittals per program.

Technical Writing/ Editing responsibilities include:

  • Supports documentation formats, review, edit, configuration control and “release” requirements via internal processes/tools and contractual needs which involves.
    • A-size drawings (including DOORS documents) and Technical Manuals (TMs) which also comprises major Program briefing TMs (PDRs, CDRs, etc.).
    • Coordinating with Word Processing to achieve consistent, quality documentation (e.g., appropriate template usage, formatting, spell check, acronyms, etc.).
    • Ensuring contractually required data markings/statements are specified on relevant Program documentation.
    • Ensuring appropriate Integrated Product Team (IPT) review, signature and “release” via an internally generated Product Data Management (PDM) tool.
  • Communicates and maintains sound business relations with Program team, CDM team, Government Agencies and Customers alike, showing responsiveness, professionalism and cooperativeness at all times.
  • Maintains accurate document identification and status accounting records via various Excel logs.
