Your role:

Due to significant investment and progress, we are seeking an International Trade Compliance Analyst to join our International Trade Compliance team to manage Australian, U.S. and other International regulatory and policy requirements for imports / exports for NGA programs. 

Your primary focus is to manage the day-to-day export control operations and export licencing requirements, deliver export control induction and training to build awareness and keep up-to-date with knowledge of export controls, ITAR and EAR regulations.

The position is preferably based in Fortitude Valley, Brisbane, however we are open to Canberra or Adelaide as well.

About you:

You are a detailed-oriented professional and come from either a compliance or legal background.  You pride yourself on your communication abilities begin able to tailor your language (both verbal and written) to suit your audience. An added bonus if you have a good understanding of Australian and other international export and import regulations for the licencing of commodities, technical data and defence services.
