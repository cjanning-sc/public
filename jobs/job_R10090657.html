Northrop Grumman has an immediate opportunity for a Field Engineer or a Principal Field Engineer. As a Field Engineer or Principal Field Engineer at Northrop Grumman, you will have a challenging and rewarding opportunity to be a part of our Enterprise-wide digital transformation. Through the use of Model-based Engineering, DevSecOps and Agile practices we continue to evolve how we deliver critical national defense products and capabilities for the warfighter. Our success is grounded in our ability to embrace change, move quickly and continuously drive innovation. The successful candidate will be collaborative, open, transparent, and team-oriented with a focus on team empowerment & shared responsibility, flexibility, continuous learning, and a culture of automation.

What You’ll get to Do:
 

Installs, operates, maintains, repairs and modifies equipment. Performs a variety of maintenance and technical support on products such as equipment, integrated systems and subsystems, and software at customer and/or field locations. Analyzes and evaluates products and related performance. Troubleshoots and diagnoses malfunctions to eliminate problem in minimum time. Installs, upgrades and removes products ensuring coordinative engineering field change. Maintains effective customer communications and relations. May provide onsite training of customer support personnel. work remotely close to customer sites. The Field Engineer or Principal Field Engineer will primarily work from the Rolling Meadows area but will be expected to travel to other customer sites as required (post DoD SkillBridge Program Participation). (Minimal OCONUS travel possible)

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Troubleshoot AN/AAQ-28 LITENING pod hardware/software issues
  • Perform hardware/software upgrades, supporting flight tests, monitoring and engaging with the customer support line
  • Provide onsite training of customer support personnel
  • Coordinating maintenance and training activities with squadron operations and maintenance staff
  • Other duties include but are not limited to, supporting any engineering investigations and product improvement activities, preparing test reports and summary reports, and creation of procedures.
  • Will be expected to work alone at a field site but within a team structure consisting of members located at other sites.
  • Travel to other customer sites as necessary (typically 5-7 days, but occasionally up to 16 days).
  • Think critically to resolve problems.
  • Effectively communicate and utilize good organizational skills to manage hardware in the field and maintain good customer relations.

"This position may be filled as a Field Engineer or Principal Field Engineer based on the qualifications listed below."

Basic Qualifications for Field Engineer:

  • BS Degree and a minimum of 2 years experience. An additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
  • Basic knowledge of EO/IR fundamentals
  • Experience with military flightline operations, support systems and maintenance
  • Effective verbal and written communication, and ability to work in a team environment
  • Ability and willingness to travel to customer locations that include business trips and short term travel to customer facilities at CONUS locations and occasional OCONUS locations. (Up to 75% travel may be required)
  • Must have a minimum of an active interim Secret Clearance. Must be able to obtain and maintain a Secret clearance if selected.

Basic Qualifications for Principal Field Engineer:

  • BS Degree and a minimum of 5 years experience . An additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of a degree.
  • Basic knowledge of EO/IR fundamentals
  • Experience with military flightline operations, support systems and maintenance
  • Effective verbal and written communication, and ability to work in a team environment
  • Ability and willingness to travel to customer locations that include business trips and short term travel to customer facilities at CONUS locations and occasional OCONUS locations. (Up to 75% travel may be required
  • Must have a minimum of an active interim Secret Clearance. Must be able to obtain and maintain a Secret clearance if selected.


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Active Secret clearance obtained or investigated within the last 5 years
  • Knowledge of Microsoft Operating Systems
  • Knowledge of military logistical systems
  • Experience in troubleshooting and maintenance of military EO/IR systems and avionics
  • Experience with test and evaluation process
  • Experience with testing of software applications
