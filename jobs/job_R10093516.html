Job Description:

The candidate will perform job duties as a Windows Systems Administrator on a wide array of different technologies supporting multiple lab environments in support of the WISŁA IBCS Program in Madison, Wisconsin. Specific responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:

  • Support day-to-day lab efforts ensuring that all technologies are operational.
  • Provide expert-level solutions for Windows Domain architecture
  • Conduct Windows Server administration and management tasks as required including patching, updates, hardening, and remediation of multiple Windows Domains  
  • Work with Product Teams in support of secure operating systems, applications, networks, hardware, databases, etc.
  • Work on multiple simultaneous tasks.
  • Mentor and provide guidance to junior systems administrators. 

Basic Qualifications (Level 3):

  • Current DoD Secret Security Clearance
  • Current DoD 8570 IAT Level II Certification (i.e., Security+, etc.)
  • Associate degree and 7 years of experience, or bachelor’s degree and 5 years of experience, or master’s degree and 3 years of experience; an additional 4 years of related experience may be considered in lieu of degree.
  • Experience architecting and maintaining Windows Active Directory in a large mixed Operating Systems environment
  • Ability to maintain all Windows Domain components i.e., Active Directory, Group Policy, DNS, DHCP, WSUS, etc. in accordance with DCSA RMF SP 800-53 Standards.  
  • General understanding of networking to include subnets, routing, VLANs, etc.
  • Mid-level experience administrating Windows Domain environments
