Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems is looking for a Principal or Senior Principal Engineer Guidance Navigation Controls to support the Systems Engineering organization in Redondo Beach, CA.

Position Overview:

The selected candidate will support research and development of flight control algorithms for manned and unmanned air vehicles.  This is a dynamic and fun organization involved in deploying forward-leaning avionics, guidance, navigation, and control systems for many of the newer concepts in development for air vehicles, weapons, and many more platforms.

Essential Duties: 

  • Stability and control analysis and preliminary control design synthesis.
  • Perform Control Sizing, and Control Power analysis.
  • Support inner-loop and outer-loop control development.
  • Perform modeling and analysis using Matlab/Simulink.
  • Develop high-fidelity 6 Degree of Freedom simulations to verify performance.
  • Lead and manage projects accomplished by small, highly skilled teams plus the abilities to communicate clearly with customers and project management, and manage subcontractors.

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced work environment with high expectations, significantly diverse assignments, collaborative/team settings across all levels.

***This requisition may be filled at either a Principal Engineer Guidance Navigation Controls level or Senior Principal Engineer Guidance Navigation Controls Engineer level ***

Basic Qualifications Principal Engineer Guidance Navigation Controls: 

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university and 5 years of related engineering experience, or a Master's Degree in STEM and 3 years of related engineering experience, or 0 years of related engineering experience with a PhD in STEM.
  • Candidate must be able to work independently and be knowledgeable of system architectures, flight control and autonomy design requirements, and simulation, and modeling techniques. 
  • Perform analysis using C/C++ or Matlab/Simulink.
  • Demonstrated proficiency in MS Office with strong technical writing skills and the ability to communicate and present technical material to various stakeholders.
  • Active U.S. Government in-scope DoD Secret security clearance or higher is required, with the ability to obtain Special Program Access (PAR/SPA).

Basic Qualifications Senior Principal Engineer Guidance Navigation Controls: 

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics) discipline from an accredited university with 9 years of related engineering experience; OR a Master’s degree in STEM with 7 years of related engineering experience, or a PhD in STEM with 4 years of related engineering experience.
  • Candidate must be able to work independently and be knowledgeable of system architectures, flight control and autonomy design requirements, and simulation, and modeling techniques. 
  • Perform analysis using C/C++ or Matlab/Simulink.
  • Demonstrated proficiency in MS Office with strong technical writing skills and the ability to communicate and present technical material to various stakeholders.
  • Active U.S. Government in-scope DoD Secret security clearance or higher is required, with the ability to obtain Special Program Access (PAR/SPA).

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • A Master's Degree in Aerospace Engineering discipline from an accredited university
  • A current active in-scope U.S. Government DoD Secret clearance with current or previous Special Program Access (PAR/SPA).
