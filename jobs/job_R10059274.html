Northrop Grumman Space Systems – Propulsion Division has a need for a Sr. Principal Project Engineer to support the Vision for Innovative Propulsion and Enabler Roadmaps (VIPER) Internal Research and Development (IRAD) program. Primary work locations will be at our Bacchus (Magna) & Promontory, UT offices.

Roles & Responsibilities:

The qualified engineer will serve as a project engineer supporting various IPTs for the VIPER Program.  The selected candidate will perform analysis and lead projects in service of:

  • Monitors schedule control points and support adjudication of pressures between IPTs.

  • Partners with IPT Leads and CAMs to ensure schedule execution to plan.

  • Monitors GFx dependencies with the government and G/R dependencies across IPTs within their MPE.

  • Continuously assesses executability of the plan and identifying, characterizing, and managing risk

  • Champions opportunity identification and proactively look for opportunities to gain schedule margin.

  • Participates in and supporting baseline change board activities relative to their respective MPE Supporting/brokering cross-IPT MPE integration.

  • Participates in agile engineering activities in planning, execution, and retrospectives

  • Supports/attends major supplier activities for critical MPE drivers.

  • Monitors and supports IMP execution. 

  • Facilitates enabling smooth and integrated collaboration and communication among product teams and program

  • Assists with project reporting, planning, forecasting, supporting proposals, metrics creation, analysis, process creation, documentation, and regular status briefs for major milestones

  • Provide technical integration support across IPTs and across IWOs to ensure all major milestones and programmatic requirements are met

  • Supports creating metrics and enhancements to present program status

Skills & Qualifications:

A candidate qualified for this position should have the following demonstrated skills and experience:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics) discipline with 9 years of related experience; OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM discipline with 7 years of experience; OR a PhD in a STEM discipline with 4 years of experience.

  • 2+ years’ experience in Program Planning or engineering working on program planning activities for complex programs.

  • Experience assimilating information from multiple sources, creating coherent plans and experience executing those plans.

  • Project management and earned-value management (EVM) experience and experience applying EVM application to complex projects.

  • Basic understanding of rocket propulsion, fluid mechanics, and structural analysis sufficient to facilitate system integration with technical SMEs

These qualifications are nice to have:

  • Experience in a government contractor manufacturing or engineering development environment. 

  • Experience leading program teams through baseline planning activities.

  • Experience managing givers/receivers across subcontractors, IPTs, and customers.

  • Experience with IMS structure design and integration process across a complex program with multiple subcontractors and IPTs. 

  • Experience with Monte Carlo schedule risk assessments and analysis.

  • Experience managing givers/receivers across subcontractors, IPTs, and customers.

  • Experience with baseline planning activities.

  • Experience applying integrated program planning and management methodologies within an Earned Value (EVMS) environment on a complex program with multiple IPTs and subcontractors.

  • Experience with Oracle Primavera P6 software. 

  • Experience in Aerospace and/or Defense programs and familiarity with Integrated Program Management Report (IPMR) or Integrated Program Management Data and Analysis Report (IPMDAR).

  • Excellent interpersonal, communication and organizational skills.

  • Strong critical thinking and logical reasoning skills with the ability to solve issues and predict potential programmatic and technical risks.

  • Operate independently with limited supervision, and the ability to establish a solid working relationship with technical staff, peers, and customers.

  • Experience working in agile engineering programs or agile engineering training
