This individual will reside in an Aerospace Systems west coast location and report to the Aerospace Sector Manager of Independent Cost Evaluation & Estimating Analytics. 

The selected candidate is responsible for creating and maintain a certifiable historical / analytical data base for all AS program proposals; leading/coordinating in the development/review of cost estimating products & capabilities (ROMs, parametrics, models).   

The successful candidate will provide leadership & support for estimating, cost analysis & database development.  Stakeholder/Functional Coordination, Cost Data Base Operation,   Design & Plan Development,  Tool Selection, Data Collection & Analysis.  User Communications/Training, and maintain an integrated, real-time accessible NGAS cost history database to enhance cost estimating/analysis/modeling and cost proposal development for the sector.

Effectively integrate the AS Cost History Database into the sector’s proposal development and estimating processes. Establish a cost analytics/estimating team focused on implementation of the NGAS CHDB and leverage this expertise in development of cost estimates (ROM), analytics (should cost/comparative analysis) and modeling (LCCE) for NGAS acquisitions and other business needs. 

Effectively integrate the cost analytics/estimating team capabilities and products into the sector proposals and acquisitions

Travel as required

Specific responsibilities include:

  • Database design & plan development
  • Cost analysis
  • Process and procedures
  • Stakeholder/Functional Coordination and integration
  • Tool Selection, Data Collection & Analysis. 
  • User Communications/Training, and maintain an integrated, real-time accessible NGAS cost history database (CHDB)
