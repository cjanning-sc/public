Northrop is looking for top-notch, focused, and dedicated software engineers to join our team of engineers on the AN/ALQ-161A B-1B Countermeasure System. The AN/ALQ-161A system is an integrated RF electronic countermeasures system designed specifically for the B-1B bomber aircraft. This position is located in Warner Robins, GA

Essential Functions:

Assist with the creation, integration, troubleshooting and testing of implemented software changes. Ideal role for someone who has the ability to take ownership of ambiguous hard problems and drive to a solution, while working in a team environment. 

  • Software development including automated testing to facilitate and analyze large data sets.

  • Develop, modify, and maintain embedded software using software engineering best practices and standards. 

  • Participate in the full life cycle of software development, to include requirements development, modeling and design, software development, unit to CSCI testing, integration, formal system testing, release, installation, and maintenance.

  • Apply technical expertise in solving complex integration problems.  

Frequent opportunities to interact with Program Management, external suppliers and/or government customers, therefore excellent verbal, written and interpersonal skills are essential to succeeding in this role.

Basic Qualifications for Software Engineers 

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or STEM related discipline with at least 2-years of software engineering experience

  • OR a master’s degree in Computer Science or STEM related discipline

  • 4 additional years of related experience may be considered in lieu of degree

  • Must currently hold or be able to obtain and maintain a U.S Department of Defense (DoD) Secret level security clearance within a reasonable amount of time as determined by the company to meet its business needs. 

  • Experience programming in a high-level language software language such as C#, C++, JAVA, etc.

This requisition is open for additional multiple levels:

  • Level 3: Bachelor’s degree and at least 6 years of experience OR 10 years in lieu of degree

  • Level 4: Bachelor’s degree and 9 years of experience OR Master’s degree with 7 years of experience OR 13 years in lieu of degree.
