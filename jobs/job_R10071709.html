Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems sector is seeking am Manufacturing Analyst/ Principal Manufacturing Analyst to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals.  This position will be located in Palmdale, CA where technology and teamwork come together.  The qualified applicant will become part of Northrop Grumman's Manufacturing Engineering / Tooling organization working in a collaborative environment while promoting a positive and proactive teamwork environment.

Manufacturing Analyst/ Principal Manufacturing Analyst job duties involve the following: structuring and maintaining bill of material, develop and maintain work instructions (including 3D visual work instructions) that meet manufacturing processes and procedures that are in compliance with customer requirements, implement detailed sequence of operations and specify procedures needed for product builds, investigate and initiate corrective actions and improvements relating to work instructions and identify improvement opportunities through sequence optimization with Manufacturing Engineering concurrence. Supports production, business, and other processes in a team-based manufacturing environment. Performs activities in a variety of cross-functional areas including production planning, continuous improvement, material management, process optimization and liaison. We seek Manufacturing Analysts that use a unique skill set that includes a hands-on approach and act as the production focal point for management of bill of material and production planning within the Aerospace sector.

Basic Qualifications Manufacturing Analyst:

  • Bachelor’s Degree with 3 years of experience OR Master's Degree with 1 year experience in an inventory control or manufacturing/production role.
  • A current and active DoD Secret security clearance with a periodic re-investigation OR an original adjudication date completed within the last 5 years
  • The ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access within a reasonable period of time, as determined by the company to meet its business need

Basic Qualifications Principal Manufacturing Analyst:

  • Bachelor’s Degree with 6 years of experience OR Master's Degree with 4 year experience in an inventory control or manufacturing/production role
  • A current and active DoD Secret security clearance with a periodic re-investigation OR an original adjudication date completed within the last 5 years
  • The ability to obtain and maintain Special Program Access within a reasonable period of time, as determined by the company to meet its business need.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Active Strike Program Clearance
  • Experience in subsystem and structure fabrication.
  • Experience in a manufacturing environment.
  • Experience in an engineering environment.
  • SAP knowledge or experience.
