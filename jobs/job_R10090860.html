Manufacturing Technician 1 & 2

ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems (NGAS) has several openings for a Manufacturing Technician 1 & Manufacturing Technician 2 to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals supporting our Green Bay (Dayton), OH. facility. This position requires onsite presence due to the nature of the role.  We are a world-class manufacturer of composite structures, radomes, and antenna systems for military applications.  We design and manufacturer solutions for our war fighters to keep our country safe and maintain peace on a global scale.

The Manufacturing Technician 1 & 2 will be responsible for fabricating composite components and tools to include bond prep, tool prep, ply kit generation, lamination, vacuum bagging, de-molding, trimming, finishing, and core fabrication.

The ideal candidate for this role must be interested in technical growth. He / She will enjoy a culture where teamwork and collaboration drive success and must be able to work in a process-centric environment.

*This is a first shift position working a 9/80 and/or 4/10 schedule.

**This position requires an active DoD Secret Clearance. While you are waiting for clearance, you could be performing non-cleared first shift work for other groups in support of our mission.  Once you receive your clearance, you will begin working your assigned position.

Essential Functions / Responsibilities:

  • Troubleshoot and solve unique problems working with engineering and other composite technicians
  • Handle perishable materials, paying close attention to expiration dates and out time life, and traceability information
  • Translate blueprints, technical specifications, and operating procedures into fabrications of structural assemblies to include precision drilling, countersinking, and the use of calibrated tool
  • Operate handheld tools. (e.g.) Scissors, razor knives, grinders, drills, sanders, cutting wheels, saws, routers, etc.
  • Utilize lean manufacturing principles and disciplines to enhance quality, cost, and delivery
  • Excels in a team environment
  • Support other departments or completes other duties as necessary
  • Be able to operate autoclave and curing ovens
  • Work in both (FOD) Foreign Object Debris critical and Foreign Object Debris sensitive environments and observe all related safety procedures
  • Responsible for completing and passing job specific qualification programs required to perform task.  Maintain a clean and safe working environment
