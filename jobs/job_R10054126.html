Description

Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking a Senior Principle Engineer Circuit Design (Power and Control) to join our team of creative and diverse engineers within the Electronics and Payloads directorate. The position will be located in Redondo Beach, CA.

Responsibilities will include:

  • Designing, analyzing, and testing high-reliability power and control electronics

  • Conduct circuit design, analysis, and simulations

  • Perform Hardware testing and validation

  • Provide manufacturing test support on deliverable hardware

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in an engineering or technical discipline from an accredited institution

  • 9 related experience with development and delivery of hardware through lifecycle of programs (proposal, architecture, design, production and test); 7 years with Masters; 4 years with PhD.

  • Knowledge of designing and testing analog and power circuits

  • Proficiency with PSpice or Simplis/Simetrix or LTSpice and MathCAD

  • Knowledge of control systems and feedback theory

  • Ability to obtain and maintain an SSBI/SCI security clearance

Preferred Qualifications

  • Masters Degree in Electrical Engineering with a concentration in Control Systems or Communications and 7 years of engineering experience in an aerospace environment

  • Active In-Scope DoD Top Secret or SSBI/SCI clearance

  • Experienced in power converters/electronics development

  • Experienced in Earned Value Management Systems
