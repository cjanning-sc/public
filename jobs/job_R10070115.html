ACME Engineering Mission Systems is seeking a talented Quality Analyst to join our team in Madison, AL. The selected candidate will perform the following:

  • Responsible for supporting Contractor System Integration Laboratory (CSIL) Quality and Mission Assurance (QMA) activities
  • Collaborate with CSIL personnel to ensure quality standards are in place and are routinely accomplished via quality checks
  • Support fielded major end items (MEI) integration of hardware/software
  • Develop, modify, apply, and maintain quality evaluation and control systems and protocols for processing materials into partially finished or finished products
  • Provides QMA support to preparation of presentation material, review of documentation, sign-off on technical review materials and readiness, attendance at meetings, and support to closeout of review action items 
  • Participation in risk management process and support to team in identification of risks
  • Performs test witnessing to include monitoring/ witnessing preparations for test events and sign-off on final test reports, surveillance of integration and test labs, buy-off/sell-off support, support to test tool development, verification of calibration of monitoring and measuring devices, test hardware, and test software
  • Corrective action review and reporting to include monitoring status of corrective and preventive actions and tracking through closure, reviewing/analyzing defect data, monitoring test problem report data and closure status, monitoring causal analysis and resolution
  • Support hardware control to Bill of Materials (BOM), First Article Inspections, close-out and delivery inspections and control of end item data packages
  • Able to conduct/support internal process evaluations and develop process evaluation reports

Qualifications

Basic Qualifications:

  • Must have 7 years of related experience in lieu of degree. Will consider: Bachelors degree with 3 years of industry experience or 1 year of experience with a Master's degree within one or more areas: quality, auditing, manufacturing, engineering and/or software-related field
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Secret DOD Secret Security Clearance
  • Must be able to work on-site in Madison, Wisconsin
  • Must be able to travel up to 25% of the time.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Integration Lab experience
  • SAP experience
  • Familiar with ISO 9001, AS9100, AS9102, and AS6500
  • Proficient with Microsoft tools (Word, Excel, Outlook, and PowerPoint)
  • Aerospace and Defense Industry experience
