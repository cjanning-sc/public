Northrop Grumman Space Systems Strategic Deterrence Systems (SDS) Division is seeking a Staff Systems Engineer in the Engineering Process and Governance Team. This position will be located in Huntsville, AL, or Roy, UT, and will support Division engineering processes as well as multiple programs across SDS.  This role may offer a competitive relocation assistance package.

What you’ll get to do:

SDS Division Staff Engineer will support roles include, but are not limited to, the following:

Coordinate with multiple IPTs/programs, functional technical teams, technical management, change management, risk management, specifications, SE process, procedures, and governance to develop and implement engineering processes, governance and training from division level to across multiple programs.

Implement Process Ecosystem architecture in the System Architecture Model, including modeling integration processes, tools, and techniques, Support development of process training.
