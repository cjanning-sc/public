NGIS is currently seeking an experienced Millwright to join our Maintenance Team at our Bacchus facility in Magna, Utah.

Role description & Responsibilities:

The Millwright position performs preventative maintenance, troubleshooting and repair, as well as new construction on plant facilities, and equipment.  While this position will have an emphasis on performing typical Millwright activities, the candidate will be expected to perform other Mechanical disciplines to maintain and repair industrial plant processing equipment and vehicles.

 Candidates possessing skills and experience in other mechanical disciplines are preferred.  Some shift work and overtime is required and the successful candidate must pass a background check for the Federal Safe Explosives Act (SEA).

Minimum Requirements:    

 •    Journeyman Millwright.
 •    Five years recent work experience as a Journeyman Millwright with skills in maintaining and repair of industrial plant processing equipment. 
 •    Must be able to obtain a security clearance.

Experience / Skills Preferred:  

 •    Plumber / Pipefitter
 •    Heavy Equipment Mechanic
 •    Certified Welder
 •    Ability to work with little or no supervision

Education/Certifications:

 •    High School Diploma or GED 
 •    Certification as a Journeyman Millwright, having completed apprenticeship and applicable training from an accredited institute.
