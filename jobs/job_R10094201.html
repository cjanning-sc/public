ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Mechanical Engineer / Principal Mechanical Engineer to join the Command and Launch Team. This position is located in Madison, WI and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Final grade level will be based on candidate level of experience.

What You’ll Get To Do:

The selected candidate will be responsible for designing equipment associated with the Operational Command and Control sub-system.  This will include layout design in NX CAD of all computer based networked rack systems and additional components related to the overall system design and the gathering of product information and the eventual submission of packages for program and government review & acceptance. This role will require candidate to work collaboratively with multi-disciplinary teams to support hardware development, have a proactive nature, exhibit analytical thinking and problem-solving skills, and be able to quickly understand tasking. Tasks and responsibilities will include, but not be limited to:

  • Design and Modeling in 3D CAD (Computer-Aided Design) software

  • Creating released engineering drawings using CAD software

  • End-user support during integration operations

  • Possible Conceptual design(s)

  • Requirements definition and refinement

  • Integration & Test process flow development
