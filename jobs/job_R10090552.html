ACME Engineering Space Components is seeking a Quality Inspection Manager to join our team.  The Process Center Leader is responsible for compliance through delivery to internal customers and secondarily to external customers. This position will be located in Louisville, AZ.

Overview

Our team is responsible for various quality inspection responsibilities including in-process inspection of product and final acceptance of product.  The Quality Inspection Manager will also be responsible for reviewing quality inspection processes, work with operations on schedule completions, assist with customer audits.

Role and Responsibilities:

  • Support all operation inspection needs and communicate quality issues as appropriate.
  • Assure technicians are working tasks that align with the schedule.
  • Works with Quality Inspectors on career development and training.
  • Resolves employee and/or resource priorities across Operations and Production Control
  • Coordinate within the Quality team or functions as needed to address obstacles preventing technicians from completing their work.
  • Responsible for implementation of proactive quality initiatives or projects to support program affordability and technical compliance.
  • Accountable to meet or exceed the Quality Assurance, and support the Operational, safety, quality, delivery, employee and cost (SQDEC) expectations of external and internal customers.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree and minimum of 5 years’ experience (such as schedules, labs, quality, manufacturing)
  • 6+ years experience in lieu of degree will be accepted
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Secret clearance.
  • US Citizenship required

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience in an Aerospace or Composite Materials manufacturing environment
  • Experience with Quality Management Systems e.g., AS9100
