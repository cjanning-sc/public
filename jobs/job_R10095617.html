Loads Engineer (level 3/4)
R10095617
1st Shift (United States of America)

Northrop Grumman Aeronautics Systems is seeking an experienced Principal or Sr Principal Loads Engineer specializing in vehicle flight / ground loads to support an advanced development program within our Vehicle Engineering team. This position is in Melbourne, FL and relocation assistance is available. CURRENT CLEARANCE PREFERRED BUT NOT REQUIRED

Job Summary:

The Loads Engineer acts as a subject matter expert on vehicle flight / ground loads in support of an advanced development program and is responsible for developing loads for miscellaneous conditions including canopy, door, pressurization, engine mount, external store, tie-down/mooring, and aerial refueling.

The successful candidate will be a highly motivated self-starter. In addition, the successful candidate should be proactive, forward-thinking, and able to lead a small team to rapid resolution of complex technical loads problems.

Requisition may be filled as a Principal Engineer (level 3) or Sr Principal Engineer (level 4) depending on the qualifications below.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Principal Engineer: Bachelor of Science (BS) in a relevant Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics (STEM) discipline with 5+ years relevant experience; OR Master of Science (MS) in a relevant STEM discipline with 3+ years relevant experience; OR Ph.D. in a relevant STEM discipline with 1+ years of relevant experience

  • Sr Principal Engineer: Bachelor of Science (BS) in a relevant Science, Technology, Engineering or Mathematics (STEM) discipline with 9+ years relevant experience; OR Master of Science (MS) in a relevant STEM discipline with 7+ years relevant experience; OR Ph.D. in a relevant STEM discipline with 4+ years of relevant experience

  • Experience with aircraft loads (flight/aero, ground)

  • Must have the ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret clearance and Special Access Program (SAP) clearance prior to the commencement of employment

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Degree in Mechanical, Aerospace, or Structural Engineering with an emphasis on aircraft loads (flight loads, ground loads, flutter and aeroelasticity, etc.)

  • Current, in-scope DoD Secret or higher clearance

  • Advanced knowledge of detailed ground and flight loads and load mitigation

  • Experience in vehicle loads analysis with emphasis on flight / ground loads to include steady state maneuvers, abrupt maneuvers, gust response, and control surface hinge moments

  • Experience developing loads for miscellaneous conditions including canopy, door, pressurization, engine mount, external store, tie-down/mooring, and aerial refueling

  • Experience in the use of industry standard loads and dynamics analysis software such as NASTRAN, PATRAN, FEMAP, ZONAIR, ZAERO

  • Experience with MATLAB and CAD Tools (NX)

  • Previous experience leading a team or project
