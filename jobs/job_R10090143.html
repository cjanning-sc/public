We are built on a heritage of providing innovative, affordable, and reliable aerospace and defense products that our customers rely on to achieve mission success. Join the Space revolution and make the impossible, possible.

Northrop Grumman Space Systems is seeking an Antenna RF Microwave Design Engineer 6 to join our team of qualified and diverse individuals. This position will be located in Redondo Beach, CA. Responsibilities will span antenna system architecture development, antenna system requirements generation/flowdown, antenna system design/analysis, and antenna system performance verification including range testing. Primary scope will involve development and demonstration of mission-enabling antenna products for both space and airborne platforms. 

Essential Functions: 

  • Technical Lead to manage antenna design developments including architecture, components, circuits, or products, and performance of Radio Frequency (RF) and/or microwave systems testing with a cross-disciplinary engineering & production team 

  • Responsible for the design & analysis, development, production and sustainment of antenna RF systems in support of the antenna programs 

  • Proficiency in electromagnetic theory, electromagnetic analysis, and performance budgeting/allocation 

  • Incorporation of lessons learned and experiences in design/analysis, innovative thinking, and familiarity with the state-of-the-art 

  • Communication & information flow to project team and functional homeroom management, as well as fulfilling reporting requirements to customer & program management 

  • Identifying and realizing opportunities to reduce cost and improve processes 

The selected candidate should thrive in a fast-paced and collaborative work environment with high expectations and diverse assignments. Candidate must also be able to collaborate closely with system engineers, manufacturing engineers, hardware designers, and integration/test engineers.

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math) field from an accredited institution and 14 years of experience with designing/analyzing/testing radio frequency (RF) and/or microwave antenna systems OR 12 years of experience with a Master's Degree OR 9 years of experience with a PhD. 

  • Proficiency with CST Microwave Studio, HFSS, GRASP, FEKO, or other computational electromagnetics software tools 

  • Candidate must have, and be able to maintain, at least ONE of the following active U.S. Government security clearances: 

Dept of Defense Top Secret level with a background investigation date within 6 years. OR SCI access with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI) completed within 5 years OR Dept of Energy Q clearance with a Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI) completed within 5 years. 

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering with a concentration in Electromagnetics from an accredited institution and 15 years of engineering experience in an aerospace environment producing flight hardware. 

  • Experience with antenna system architecture development, antenna system requirements generation/flowdown, antenna system design/analysis, and antenna system performance verification including range testing. 

  • Technical breadth across any of the following areas: highly-integrated antenna systems, phased arrays, digital beamforming, reflector antennas, reflect-arrays, aperture antennas, wire antennas, antenna/RF system performance analysis, RF electronics, digital electronics, power electronics, CAD modeling, statistics, systems engineering, antenna range testing, measurement equipment automation, and data post-processing/reduction.
