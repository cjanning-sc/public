As the Principal or Sr. Principal Software Configuration Analyst, you will manage, all software and documentation assigned to the program. This is exciting time to join our growing team!  This position will be based in CO Springs, Colorado.

In this role, you will:

  • Design, develop, maintain, coordinate the configuration management and document management processes to include process improvement.

  • Documentation and Software Inventory Management.

  • Perform spot checks to ensure process and system quality.

  • Actively use knowledge of Systems Engineering and Agile Development Methodologies to support the software development lifecycle.

  • Use Configuration Management tools and system auditing to support a fast-paced software delivery program.

  • Experience with Systems Engineering that can support the software configuration management needed on our program

This position can be staffed at a level 3 or 4 based on the basic qualifications below.

Level 3 Basic Qualifications:

  • 5 or more years of Document Management experience with Bachelor’s degree or 3 Years of Document Management experience with a Master’s degree

  • Software configuration database management experience

  • An active TS/SCI & Polygraph

Level 4 Basic Qualifications:

  • 9 or more years of Document Management experience with Bachelor’s in Science OR 3 Years of Document Management experience with a Master’s degree

  • Software configuration database management experience

  • An active TS/SCI & Polygraph

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Inventory system tracking experience

  • Experience with software build procedure creation

  • Experience with software configuration management tools

  • Experience with Systems Integration and System/Software Testing, including virtualized environments, test environments and operational environments

  • Working knowledge in test plan/procedure preparation and test execution

  • Knowledge of DevOps pipeline implementation
