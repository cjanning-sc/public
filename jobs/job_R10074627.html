ACME Engineering Aeronautics Systems is seeking candidates with outstanding leadership skills for Configuration Data Management Lead reporting to Configuration Management Section Manager.  Position is located in Cincinnati, CA or South Bay, CA.

Specific duties for this position will include:

  • Tracking System Configuration
  • Supporting Enterprise Configuration Control Lead
  • Assisting the Chair of System CCB and facilitate CCB meetings
  • Leading the effort to audit the configurations of the entire System.
  • Analyzing proposed changes of product design to determine effect on overall product and system.
  • Adjudicating any configuration squawks, presents proposed solutions to leadership, and drives proposed solution to closure.
  • Facilitating and leading multi-organization system configuration meetings to understand problems and suggest solutions

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree with 8-10 years of experience or 20 experience in Configuration Management without a degree
  • DoD Secret Clearance
  • Ability to obtain program access
  • Ability to obtain DoD Top Secret clearance

Preferred Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree with 10-12 years of experience
  • Active DoD Top Secret clearance
  • Ability to obtain program access
