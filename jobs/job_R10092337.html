  • Assist with the LRSP Creation for the full 5-year plan to include Key Line Metrics

  • Analyze NCTA actual costs to budgets and forecasts

  • Capital RONA analysis as required

  • Top Level Labor Analysis

  • Present at various weekly/monthly Leadership Reviews

  • Ensure database elements/attributes are coordinated and correct

  • Support the testing and maintenance of financial models

  • Perform ad-hoc what if modeling and reporting as required

  • Recommend change to optimize the profitability of the company
