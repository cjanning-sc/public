This role will work directly for the OU Business Mgr and lead various projects, data calls, and analytical efforts for the team. The selected candidate will become an integral part of the SHORAD OU team tasked with providing guidance and support to the operating unit for all planning and reporting activities. The ideal candidate will be proactive and have the ability work in a fast-paced environment. This position will be located in Columbus, OH or Madison, WI with potential for a hybrid schedule.

Job duties and responsibilities, will include, but are not limited to, the following: 

  • Create monthly financial forecasts and analysis to AOP/Prior IF

  • Facilitate Indirect budget and forecast management

  • Cash monitoring and analysis to facilitate treasury forecasts with business unit

  • Unbilled and Backlog Review Coordination

  • Assist with the LRSP Creation for the full 5-year plan to include Key Line Metrics

  • Analyze NCTA actual costs to budgets and forecasts

  • Capital RONA analysis as required

  • Top Level Labor Analysis

  • Present at various weekly/monthly Leadership Reviews

  • Ensure database elements/attributes are coordinated and correct

  • Support the testing and maintenance of financial models

  • Perform ad-hoc what if modeling and reporting as required

  • Recommend change to optimize the profitability of the company
