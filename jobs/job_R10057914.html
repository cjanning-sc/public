ACME Engineering Space Systems is seeking a Principal / Senior Principal Systems Engineer to join the Payload Team. This position is located in Detroit, UT and supports the Ground Based Strategic Deterrent (GBSD) program. Final position offered will be based on candidate level of experience.

Responsibilities:

The selected candidate will join a team of engineers and project managers supporting the development and test of Re-entry Vehicle systems. Tasks for this role include, but are not limited to:

  • Facilitate internal and external stake holder reviews
  • Adjudicate and resolve comments, action items, support review board release process
  • Participate in revision and management of both original and revised specification/interface definition document and expedite documents through signature approval process.
  • Participate in team agile work management process
  • Contribute to risk management (identifies potential risks) and trade study processes
  • Supports system design, data analyses, interface verification, testing, failure/anomaly resolution and mission operations
  • Attends and prepares materials for presentation during Enterprise, Subcontract Management Team, Industry technical reviews
  • Supports trade studies, system sensitivity studies, design optimization studies, system performance assessments, system design assessments, Operations Analysis work such as mission analysis and timelines and concept of operations development
  • Apply the principles of engineering to design components and systems using applicable standards and specifications
  • Verify specifications and designs through modeling and simulation, test data processing, and performance analysis
  • Support all phases of the systems lifecycle including concept, design, fabrication, test, installation, operation, maintenance and disposal
  • Maintain design documentation and follow configuration management requirements
  • Assist in Basis of Estimates (BOEs) reviews for the Subcontract Management Team (SMT)
  • Utilize abilities to communicate clearly and effectively; presenting/interacting with all levels of leadership
  • Interface with AF customer

Basic Qualifications:

This position may be filled at a Principal or Sr. Principal level

  • Bachelor of Science degree in a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) discipline and 5 years of experience; 3 years with a Master’s Degree; or 0 years with PhD (Sr. Principal requires 9 years with a BS; 7 years with a Master’s; or 4 years with PhD)
  • Experience with Project Management including project scheduling fundamentals
  • Experience with Agile fundamentals and practice
  • Experience with environmental test lab operations and conditions
  • Experience in Test, including test report review and assessment, test witnessing for compliance, etc.
  • Experience with mechanical and electrical applied skills
  • Active DoD Secret clearance granted or re-investigated within last 6 years

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 5 or more years of ICBM experience
  • Experience reading and analyzing CAD Drawings
  • Lab Testing experience: planning, testing, and reporting background
  • Active DoD Top Secret Clearance
