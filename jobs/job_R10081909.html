Northrop Grumman’s TSSD sector is looking for a Manager Programs Level 3 to support the Space Security organization/business unit, geographically located in Dulles, VA.

The TSSD Space Security (SpS) Business unit provides end-to-end mission capabilities related to space as a warfighting domain. SpS is seeking a senior manager or technical expert in the area of mission integration. The selected candidate will be responsible for customer engagement and technical leadership regarding the end-to-end mission integration of multi-capability or multi-payload satellites, inclusive of ground command and control and ground mission execution.  Responsibilities will be dynamic and managed at a high level – with the understand that all Space Security missions and customers are unique, the candidate must have a combination of a flexible mindset and the ability to understand customer goals. Team building, both with the customer community and internal NG stakeholders is paramount. The position will report to the Senior Director of Mission and Technology for the SpS BU.

Primary responsibilities are the management of cost, schedule, and technical performance of company programs or subsystems and include, but are not limited to:

  • Cultivating customer relationships and intimacy to develop further opportunities within the customer community
  • Developing new business opportunities through long term strategic planning, capture planning, and development of proposals and business plans
  • Establishing a program organization that effectively addresses customer requirements and incorporates the necessary internal and external sub-organizations
  • Leading and directing cross-functional Integrated Program Teams (IPT) to meet program cost, schedule and technical performance objectives
  • Measuring and reporting program performance
  • Delivering presentations to customers, executive management and other program stakeholders
  • Participating in the negotiation of contracts, contract changes, specifications, operating budgets, schedule milestones, and key terms and conditions.
  • Establishing design concepts, criteria, and engineering efforts for product research, development, integration and test.
  • Creation, review and finalization of the program Statement of Work
  • Identification, distribution, tracking, and completion of program requirements
  • Identifying, allocating and managing program resources, including workforce planning
  • Managing Government/customer supplied property or information (GFE, CFE, etc.)
  • Managing suppliers to meet program objectives.
  • Adherence to all internal processes, policies, and applicable industry standards

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree and 10 years, or Master’s and 8 years’ experience supporting U.S. Government contracts and customers and/or project management in other industries
  • 5 years of experience managing or technically leading space hardware or ground sites
  • Active Top Secret or SCI clearance
  • Ability to obtain and maintain Special Access Program (SAP) accesses

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience managing or technically leading space missions covering both space and ground
  • Experience leading subcontractor management teams
  • Active TS/SCI with SAP accesses
  • Experience in multi-mission programs
  • Experience with multiple customer communities, especially multi-customer programs
