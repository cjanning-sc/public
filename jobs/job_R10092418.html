The ACME Engineering Space Sector is seeking an Associate Electronics Engineer in our Integration and Test facility located in Des Moines, VA.

In this position we are seeking a creative and energetic engineer to design, develop, and execute test procedures and automated test scripts using spacecraft commands, telemetry, and electrical ground support equipment during 2nd shift Satellite testing operations.  The ideal candidate has system level test experience working with spacecraft flight hardware and good computer skills.  Daily responsibilities will require test preparation leading to direct contact with satellite and space flight hardware.  Candidate must be able to tackle issues utilizing skills obtained in the field of electrical engineering, or a related discipline, preferably as it relates to system level integration and test of flight hardware.  Required skills include the ability to communicate clearly, and to use critical thinking in troubleshooting of problems.  The ability to use a scripting language and formulate automated test scripts is highly desirable.  Domestic and international travel may be required to support launch campaigns.  Shift and weekend work to support testing will be required periodically.  

The employee will be compesated a 10% increase shift differential for working the second shift.

  • U.S Citizenship with the ability to obtain a DoD Top Secret Security clearance
  • Excellent writing skills
  • 2nd shift

Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Active Top Secret clearance with SAP/SAR access highly desirable
  • Aerospace industry experience
  • Familiar with software:  Matlab, PERL, Python, C++, and Linux
