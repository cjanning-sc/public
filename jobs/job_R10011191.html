ACME Engineering Strategic Force Programs (SFP) is looking for a qualified Consulting (Level 7) Chief Engineer with architecture and design development expertise and major procurement, assembly, integration, test and on-orbit experience for space-qualified payloads.  This person will be supporting new business captures and proposals as well as supporting the existing OPIR and/or Remote Sensing programs. The candidate will be working out of our Kent, CA (primary) and Columbus, CA (secondary) locations.

Position Summary / Responsibilities include:

  • Supports business captures toward predetermined long-range goals and objectives
  • Supports pursuit/proposal efforts by leading the development of innovative system payload concepts and architectures to meet mission objectives, applying new principles and theories where applicable
  • Serves as organization spokesperson on advanced projects and/or programs. Acts as advisor to management and customers on advanced technical research, studies and applications
  • Supports program execution as definitive technical authority for all aspects of engineering on all program elements (hardware/software/support/test).
  • Leads a multi-disciplined engineering team to ensure compliant technical performance
  • Maintains cognizance over technical activities within and across program IPTs for requirements development, hardware/software design, payload integration and verification/validation activities
  • Solves program unusually/complex technical problems and provides solutions that are highly innovative and achievable
  • Provides technical status/updates and assessment to PMO. Supports PMO in assessing cost and schedule impacts of technical decisions or customer requests
  • Ensures program is adhering to engineering processes and best practices in design, development, fabrication/assembly, test and sustainment of products for our customers and is compliant with company policies, procedures, and quality standards
  • Coordinates with functional organization to ensure execution of program statement of work and technical program requirements are met
  • Serves as the program technical point of contact with the customer

Basic Qualifications: 

  • Bachelor’s Degree in a STEM field & 20+ years of related technical experience, OR a Master’s Degree in a STEM field & 18+ years of related technical experience, OR a PhD in a STEM field & 15+ years of related technical experience
  • Experience with OP/IR and/or remote sensing space qualified payload concept/architecture expertise with design AI&T and on-orbit experience
  • Experience within business captures/proposals and program execution
  • Current & active DoD secret security clearance


Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Master’s in a STEM field & 20+ years of related technical engineering experience
  • Current & active DoD TS/SCI security clearance
