Northrop Grumman Defense Systems Sector is seeking a Business Management Manager 1 for the Subsystems Maintenance and Software Sustainment Operation Unit. This position will be located in Warner Robins, GA.

Roles and Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Partner with the Program Managers for selected programs to manage all financial aspects to achieve the financial and strategic objectives

  • Responsible for developing the long-range strategic plan of certain programs and annual operating plan

  • Promote the professional development of the business management workforce and foster a collaborative culture that sustains the momentum around employee engagement, affordability, innovation, value growth and continuous improvement

  • Lead a business management team, responsibility to orchestrate all business management functions including contracts, pricing, proposal development, program planning & financial control, financial planning, program scheduling, direct and indirect rate management, etc.

  • Use broad experience in financial planning and analysis, program finance, variance analysis, and strategic planning

  • Oversee forecasting, work authorization, EACs/ETCs, Indicated Finals, financial reporting, cash modeling, etc.

  • Responsible for the daily operations of a team (direct supervision of the staff to include job requirements / schedules, and provide direction to subordinates using established policies and procedures)

  • Maintain the highest level of commitment to quality, compliance, and internal controls in adherence to company values and ethics

  • This position requires occasional business travel

Basic Qualifications: 

  • This position emphasizes skills and experience. Will consider a high school diploma or GED with a minimum of 10 years professional experience in finance, accounting, or program control

  • Bachelor's degree in Business Management, Accounting, Finance, or related field with 5 years of relevant experience, or a Master’s degree with 3 years relevant experience

  • In depth financial reporting experience

  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite

  • Excellent communication skills with the ability to present business strategies, financial information, and risk assessments to Program Managers and Business Unit Management.

  • Demonstrated ability to perform independent risk analysis and cost/schedule performance assessments leveraging solutions that create business value to the customer and company

  • Must be able to obtain and maintain a U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) Secret security clearance

    
Preferred Qualifications: 

  • Complete understanding of contract types and terminology

  • Experience developing EACs and AOP / LRSPs

  • In-depth knowledge of DOD financial business models

  • Proven skills and knowledge of financial management and analysis

  • Experience with Cobra, MPM, and reporting tools

  • A solid understanding of FAR, CAS and Disclosure Statements

  • Experience with similar and relevant financial experience as described in the job description

  • Active Secret clearance
