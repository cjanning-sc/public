Northrop Grumman Propulsion Systems is currently seeking an Operations Site Manager 1 to provide leadership to our Nozzle Value Stream, machining operations team at our Promontory, Utah plant.


The Operations Manager will work under the guidance of the Sr. Operations Manager and use long range planning methods, tools and facilities in the execution to meet multiple Customer requirements.  The successful candidate will work with a group of talented Technical and Operational Team Supervisors in coordinating the day to day activities of Operations Employees, in processing and assembling precision steel forgings and composite/rubber phenolic materials to exacting engineered requirements.  The process includes, machining of Aerospace flight and manned flight components, strategic DOD components, critical process tooling, and composite winding and tape wrapping of phenolic materials as well as rubber formulations.
